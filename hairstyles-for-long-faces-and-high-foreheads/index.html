Hairstyles For Long Faces And High Foreheads The Ultimate Guide

Are you tired of constantly feeling self-conscious about your long face and high forehead? Well, you're not alone. Many people with these features struggle to find the perfect hairstyle that flatters their face shape. But fear not, because we've got you covered. In this article, we'll be sharing with you some of the best hairstyles for long faces and high foreheads that will make you look and feel your best.

Description

Long faces and high foreheads are often considered to be less desirable features in the beauty world. However, with the right hairstyle, you can accentuate your best features and minimize the ones you're not so fond of. The key is to find a hairstyle that balances out your face shape and highlights your best features.

How to Determine Your Face Shape

The first step in finding the perfect hairstyle for your long face and high forehead is to determine your face shape. There are six main face shapes: oval, round, square, heart, diamond, and long. To determine your face shape, use a measuring tape to measure the width of your forehead, cheekbones, and jawline. Then, use the following guidelines:

  • Oval: forehead is slightly wider than the chin, and the length of the face is about one and a half times the width
  • Round: forehead, cheekbones, and jawline are all about the same width
  • Square: forehead, cheekbones, and jawline are all about the same width, with a sharp jawline
  • Heart: forehead is wider than the cheeks and jawline, with a pointed chin
  • Diamond: forehead is slightly wider than the cheeks, with a pointed chin
  • Long: forehead, cheekbones, and jawline are all about the same width, with a longer face length

Step-by-Step Guide for Choosing a Hairstyle

Once you've determined your face shape, it's time to choose a hairstyle that flatters your features. Here's a step-by-step guide:

  1. Determine your face shape
  2. Research hairstyles that are recommended for your face shape
  3. Consider your hair texture and thickness
  4. Think about the amount of maintenance you're willing to do
  5. Consult with a hairstylist for their professional opinion

Tips for Choosing the Right Hairstyle

Here are some tips to keep in mind when choosing a hairstyle for your long face and high forehead:

  • Choose a hairstyle that adds width to your face, such as layers or waves
  • Avoid hairstyles that add height to your forehead, such as bangs or updos
  • Consider your hair texture and how it will affect the style
  • Think about the amount of time and effort you're willing to put into styling your hair

FAQs

Q: Can I still wear bangs if I have a long face and high forehead?

A: While it's not recommended to wear straight-across bangs that add height to your forehead, you can still wear side-swept bangs that add width to your face.

Q: What is the best haircut for a long face and high forehead?

A: The best haircut for a long face and high forehead is one that adds width to your face, such as layers or waves.

Hair Care Tips for Long Faces and High Foreheads

When it comes to hair care, it's important to keep your hair healthy and moisturized. Here are some tips:

  • Use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends
  • Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a UV protection spray

Hairstyles for Long Faces and High Foreheads

Now, onto the fun part: hairstyles! Here are some of the best hairstyles for long faces and high foreheads:

Updos for Long Faces and High Foreheads

When it comes to updos, it's important to choose a style that doesn't add height to your forehead. Here are some options:

  • Messy bun
  • Low ponytail
  • Loose chignon

Bobs for Long Faces and High Foreheads

Bobs are a great option for long faces and high foreheads because they add width to your face. Here are some styles to consider:

  • Layered bob
  • Asymmetrical bob
  • Long bob (aka "lob")

Layers for Long Faces and High Foreheads

Layers are also a great option for long faces and high foreheads because they add volume and width to your face. Here are some styles to consider:

  • Long layers
  • Face-framing layers
  • Choppy layers

With these hairstyles and tips in mind, you're sure to find the perfect hairstyle for your long face and high forehead. Remember to embrace your unique features and rock your new 'do with confidence!
