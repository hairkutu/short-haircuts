How To Slim Square Face Tips And Tricks

Written by William Jul 27, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Square faces are characterized by a strong jawline, broad forehead and cheekbones, and a straight hairline. If you have a square face, you might feel that your facial features are too angular or harsh. However, with a few simple tricks, you can slim down your square face and create a more balanced and flattering look.

How to Slim Square Face: Step by Step

Here are some step-by-step tips on how to slim square face:

1. Contouring

Contouring is a makeup technique that can help you create the illusion of a slimmer face. Use a contouring powder or cream to shade the areas of your face that you want to slim down, such as your jawline, temples, and cheekbones. Apply a highlighter on the high points of your face, such as your forehead, nose, and cheekbones, to create a more sculpted look.

2. Hairstyle

Your hairstyle can also work wonders in slimming down your square face. Opt for hairstyles that add height and volume on top of your head, such as a high ponytail, top knot, or layered haircut. Avoid hairstyles that are too flat or straight, as they can make your face look wider.

3. Accessories

Accessories can also help you slim down your square face. Wear earrings that are long and delicate, as they can create the illusion of a longer and slimmer face. Avoid earrings that are too round or bulky, as they can make your face look wider.

4. Clothing

Choose clothing that complements your square face. Opt for tops that have a V-neckline or scoop neckline, as they can elongate your face. Avoid tops that have a high neckline, as they can make your face look wider. Wear clothing that fits you well and flatters your body shape.

Tips to Slim Square Face

Here are some additional tips to slim down your square face:

1. Exercise

Exercising regularly can help you lose weight and tone your facial muscles. Focus on exercises that target the muscles around your jawline and cheekbones, such as facial yoga or chewing gum.

2. Diet

Eating a balanced and healthy diet can also help you slim down your square face. Avoid foods that are high in salt, sugar, and fat, as they can cause bloating and water retention. Instead, opt for foods that are rich in vitamins and minerals, such as fruits, vegetables, and lean protein.

3. Hydration

Drinking plenty of water can help you flush out toxins and reduce bloating. Aim to drink at least eight glasses of water per day, and avoid sugary or caffeinated drinks.

FAQs about How to Slim Square Face

1. Can I slim down my square face without makeup?

Yes, you can slim down your square face without makeup. By choosing the right hairstyle, accessories, and clothing, you can create the illusion of a slimmer and more balanced face.

2. How long does it take to slim down a square face?

The time it takes to slim down a square face depends on various factors, such as your genetics, diet, exercise routine, and skincare regimen. However, with consistent effort and dedication, you can see results in as little as a few weeks.

3. Are there any risks associated with slimming down a square face?

No, there are no risks associated with slimming down a square face. However, it's important to remember that beauty comes in all shapes and sizes, and that you should embrace your unique features.

Hair Care Tips for Square Faces

Here are some hair care tips for square faces:

1. Moisturize

Moisturize your hair regularly to keep it healthy and shiny. Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week, and avoid using hot tools or harsh chemicals that can damage your hair.

2. Trim regularly

Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends and breakage. Aim to get a trim every six to eight weeks.

3. Protect

Protect your hair from damage by wearing a hat or scarf when you're outside, and by using a heat protectant spray before using hot tools.

With these tips and tricks, you can slim down your square face and create a more balanced and flattering look. Remember to embrace your unique features and to love yourself for who you are!
