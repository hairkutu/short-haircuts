Long Hairstyles For Round Faces Over 50

Written by Petter Apr 02, 2023 · 6 min read

If you are over 50 and have a round face, finding the perfect hairstyle can be a challenge. However, long hairstyles that flatter your face shape are possible. In this article, we will share some of the best long hairstyles for round faces over 50, along with tips on how to style and care for your hair.

Description

As we age, our hair tends to thin out and lose volume. This can make it challenging to find long hairstyles that complement our round face shape. However, with the right cut and styling techniques, you can achieve a look that flatters your features.

How to

The first step in finding the perfect long hairstyle for your round face over 50 is to determine your face shape. To do this, use a measuring tape to measure the length and width of your face. If the measurements are roughly equal, you have a round face shape. Once you have determined your face shape, it’s time to choose a hairstyle that flatters your features.

Step by Step

Step 1: Choose a long layered haircut. Layers can help add volume and body to your hair, which is essential for a round face shape.

Step 2: Consider adding highlights. Highlights can help add dimension and depth to your hair, which can help elongate your round face shape.

Step 3: Style your hair with a side part. A side part can help create the illusion of a longer face shape.

Step 4: Use a curling iron or hot rollers to add loose waves to your hair. This can help create a soft and romantic look that flatters a round face shape.

Tips

When choosing a long hairstyle for your round face over 50, it’s important to consider your hair texture and thickness. Fine hair may require a different cut and styling approach than thick hair.

Another tip is to avoid hairstyles that add too much volume to the top of your head, as this can make your face appear wider. Instead, opt for styles that add volume to the bottom half of your hair.

FAQ

What are the best long hairstyles for round faces over 50?

Some of the best long hairstyles for round faces over 50 include long layered cuts, side-swept bangs, loose waves, and highlights.

How can I style long hair for a round face?

You can style long hair for a round face by adding loose waves, using a side part, and avoiding hairstyles that add too much volume to the top of your head.

Hair Care

To keep your long hair looking healthy and vibrant, it’s important to follow a regular hair care routine. This should include washing your hair with a gentle shampoo and conditioner, using a heat protectant before using hot tools, and getting regular trims to prevent split ends.

In addition, you may want to consider using a hair mask or deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair looking and feeling its best.

Overall, with the right haircut, styling techniques, and hair care routine, you can rock a long hairstyle that flatters your round face over 50. So go ahead and embrace your age and your beautiful features!
