Short Hairstyles For Round Faces And Wavy Hair A Guide

Are you someone who has a round face and wavy hair? Do you often find it difficult to choose a suitable hairstyle that complements your features? If yes, then worry not because we have got you covered! In this article, we will be discussing some of the best short hairstyles for round faces and wavy hair that you can try out in 2023.

  • How to

Description

Short hairstyles are a great option for those who have a round face and wavy hair. When choosing a hairstyle, it is important to consider the shape and texture of your face and hair. Round faces tend to have a circular shape, with full cheeks and a soft jawline. Wavy hair, on the other hand, has natural waves and curls that can add volume and texture to your hair.

Short hairstyles for round faces and wavy hair can help to balance out the roundness of your face and bring out your best features. There are many different styles to choose from, including pixie cuts, bobs, and shags. The key is to find a style that works well with your face shape and hair texture.

How to

When choosing a short hairstyle for round faces and wavy hair, it is important to consider the following:

  • Face shape: Round faces tend to look best with hairstyles that add height and volume on top, while keeping the sides and back shorter.
  • Hair texture: Wavy hair can add volume and texture to a hairstyle, so it's important to choose a style that works well with your natural waves.
  • Lifestyle: Consider your lifestyle and how much time you are willing to spend styling your hair. Short hairstyles can be low-maintenance, but some styles may require more upkeep.

Step by step

Here are some step-by-step instructions for creating some popular short hairstyles for round faces and wavy hair:

Pixie Cut

  1. Start by washing your hair and towel-drying it.
  2. Apply a styling product to your hair, such as mousse or gel, to enhance your natural waves.
  3. Part your hair to one side and use a comb to create a side-swept fringe.
  4. Use a pair of scissors to cut your hair to the desired length, leaving the sides and back shorter than the top.
  5. Use a blow dryer and a round brush to add volume to your hair.
  6. Finish off with a hairspray to hold your style in place.

Bob

  1. Start by washing your hair and towel-drying it.
  2. Apply a styling product to your hair, such as mousse or gel, to enhance your natural waves.
  3. Use a pair of scissors to cut your hair to the desired length, keeping the sides and back shorter than the front.
  4. Use a blow dryer and a round brush to add volume to your hair.
  5. Finish off with a hairspray to hold your style in place.

Shag

  1. Start by washing your hair and towel-drying it.
  2. Apply a styling product to your hair, such as mousse or gel, to enhance your natural waves.
  3. Use a pair of scissors to create layers in your hair, focusing on the crown and the ends.
  4. Use a blow dryer and a round brush to add volume to your hair.
  5. Finish off with a hairspray to hold your style in place.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when choosing a short hairstyle for round faces and wavy hair:

  • Consider adding highlights or lowlights to your hair to add dimension and depth.
  • Use a round brush when blow-drying your hair to add volume and shape.
  • Try using a diffuser attachment when blow-drying your hair to enhance your natural waves.
  • Invest in a good quality hairspray to hold your style in place without weighing it down.

FAQ

What are the best short hairstyles for round faces and wavy hair?

Some of the best short hairstyles for round faces and wavy hair include pixie cuts, bobs, and shags.

How do I choose a short hairstyle for my face shape?

When choosing a short hairstyle, consider your face shape and look for styles that complement your features. For round faces, hairstyles that add height and volume on top tend to work well.

How do I style my short, wavy hair?

You can style your short, wavy hair using a variety of products, such as mousse, gel, or hairspray. Use a round brush when blow-drying your hair to add volume and shape, or try using a diffuser attachment to enhance your natural waves.

Hair Care

To keep your short, wavy hair looking its best, it is important to follow a good hair care routine. This includes washing your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo and conditioner, using a heat protectant when blow-drying or styling your hair, and getting regular haircuts to maintain your style.

With these tips and tricks, you can find the perfect short hairstyle for your round face and wavy hair. Don't be afraid to experiment with different styles and products to find what works best for you. With the right hairstyle, you can look and feel your best every day!
