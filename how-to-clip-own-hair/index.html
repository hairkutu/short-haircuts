How To Clip Own Hair A Step By Step Guide

Written by Frank Mar 16, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Clipping your own hair can be a daunting task, but with the right tools and technique, it can be a great way to save time and money. In this post, we'll go over how to clip your own hair in a relaxed and easy-to-understand language. Here's what we'll cover:

Table of Contents

  • How to Clip Own Hair
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Clipping Own Hair
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care after Clipping Own Hair

How to Clip Own Hair

Before we get into the step-by-step guide, let's go over some basic tools you'll need:

  • Clippers with guards
  • Scissors
  • Comb
  • Clip
  • Mirror

Now let's get started.

Step-by-Step Guide

Step 1: Wash and Dry Your Hair

Start by washing and drying your hair. This will make it easier to clip and ensure a more even cut. Make sure your hair is completely dry before you start clipping.

Step 2: Start with the Guards

Attach the appropriate guard to your clippers. Start with a longer guard and work your way down to a shorter one. This will help you achieve the desired length without cutting too much off at once.

Step 3: Start Clipping

Start at the front of your head and work your way back. Use the clippers to trim the hair, moving in an upward motion. Make sure to go over each section multiple times to ensure an even cut.

Step 4: Blend the Sides and Back

Once you've clipped the top of your head, move on to the sides and back. Use a shorter guard and blend the hair from the top to the sides and back. Make sure to blend the hair evenly for a natural look.

Step 5: Trim the Bangs

If you have bangs, use scissors to trim them. Start by combing the bangs forward and cutting them at an angle. Make sure to cut a little bit at a time to avoid cutting too much off.

Step 6: Check for Evenness

Once you've finished clipping, use a mirror to check for evenness. Make any necessary adjustments to ensure a clean and even cut.

Tips for Clipping Own Hair

  • Start with a longer guard and work your way down to avoid cutting too much off at once.
  • Make sure to go over each section multiple times to ensure an even cut.
  • Use scissors to trim bangs and any stray hairs.
  • Check for evenness using a mirror and make any necessary adjustments.

FAQs

Can I clip my own hair without guards?

We do not recommend clipping your own hair without guards. This can result in uneven and choppy cuts.

How often should I clip my own hair?

This depends on your hair type and style. Generally, it's recommended to clip your hair every 4-6 weeks to maintain the desired length and style.

Can I clip my own hair if it's curly?

Yes, you can clip your own hair if it's curly. However, it's important to use the appropriate guard and technique to avoid cutting too much off and creating an uneven cut.

Hair Care after Clipping Own Hair

After clipping your own hair, it's important to take care of it to maintain the desired length and style. Here are some tips:

  • Use a quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and moisturized.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair to prevent damage.
  • Use a hair oil or serum to add shine and prevent frizz.

Clipping your own hair can be a great way to save time and money, but it's important to use the right tools and technique to achieve a clean and even cut. With this step-by-step guide, you'll be able to clip your own hair with ease.
