How To Make A Round Face Oval Tips And Tricks

Written by Aprilia Jun 07, 2023 Â· 7 min read

If you have a round face, you may be looking for ways to make it appear more oval. The good news is, there are several easy and effective ways to achieve this. In this article, we'll go over some tips and tricks for making a round face look more oval, as well as some hair care tips to help you achieve the look you want.

Table of Contents

  • Determining Your Face Shape
  • Hairstyles for a Round Face
  • Makeup Tips for a Round Face
  • Contouring Techniques
  • Accessories to Complement Your Face Shape
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care Tips for a Round Face

Determining Your Face Shape

Before we dive into tips and tricks for making a round face look more oval, it's important to determine your face shape. There are several ways to do this, but one of the easiest is to stand in front of a mirror and use a measuring tape or ruler to measure the width of your forehead, cheekbones, and jawline.

If your forehead, cheekbones, and jawline are all about the same width, you likely have a round face shape. Other common face shapes include oval, square, heart-shaped, and diamond-shaped.

Hairstyles for a Round Face

One of the easiest ways to make a round face look more oval is to choose a hairstyle that adds height and volume to the crown of your head. This can help elongate your face and make it appear more oval.

Some great hairstyles for round faces include:

  • A long, layered haircut with volume at the crown
  • A side-swept fringe or bangs
  • A high ponytail or bun
  • A short, layered bob
  • Curls or waves that start at the mid-length of your hair

Makeup Tips for a Round Face

When it comes to makeup, there are several tricks you can use to make a round face look more oval. One of the most effective is contouring.

Contouring Techniques

To contour your face, you'll need a contouring powder or cream that's a few shades darker than your skin tone, as well as a highlighter that's a few shades lighter.

Start by applying the contouring powder or cream along your jawline, temples, and under your cheekbones. Blend well to avoid any harsh lines. Next, apply the highlighter to the center of your forehead, down the bridge of your nose, and on the high points of your cheekbones.

By creating shadows and highlights on your face, you can help create the illusion of an oval-shaped face.

Accessories to Complement Your Face Shape

Accessories can also play a role in making a round face look more oval. Some great accessories for round faces include:

  • Long, dangling earrings to elongate your face
  • A long pendant necklace to draw the eye down
  • A wide-brimmed hat to add height to your face
  • Sunglasses with angular frames to balance out your face shape

FAQs

What if I like my round face?

That's great! The tips and tricks in this article are just suggestions for those who are looking to make their round face appear more oval. There's no right or wrong way to wear your face shape!

Do I need to use all of these tips and tricks?

No, you can pick and choose which tips and tricks work best for you. Remember, the most important thing is to feel confident and comfortable in your own skin.

Hair Care Tips for a Round Face

In addition to choosing the right hairstyle, there are a few hair care tips that can help complement a round face:

  • Avoid hairstyles that are too short or flat, as these can make your face appear rounder
  • Use volumizing products to add height and bounce to your hair
  • Avoid heavy, blunt bangs, as these can shorten your face
  • Use a round brush when blow-drying your hair to add volume and shape

By following these tips and tricks, you can make a round face appear more oval and feel confident and beautiful in your own skin. Remember to embrace your unique features and find what works best for you!
