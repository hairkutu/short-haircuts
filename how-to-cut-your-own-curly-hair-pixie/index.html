How To Cut Your Own Curly Hair Pixie A Step By Step Guide

Written by Aprilia Mar 26, 2023 Â· 8 min read

If you're looking for a new hairstyle that's low maintenance and stylish, a curly hair pixie cut may be just what you need. While going to the salon can be expensive and time-consuming, cutting your own hair can be a convenient and affordable alternative. In this article, we'll go over everything you need to know about how to cut your own curly hair pixie, from the tools you'll need to the step-by-step process.

Tools You'll Need

Before you begin cutting your own curly hair pixie, you'll need to gather a few tools. These include:

  • Scissors
  • Clippers (optional)
  • Comb
  • Sectioning clips
  • Spray bottle filled with water
  • Mirror

Once you have all of your tools ready, you can begin preparing your hair for the cut.

Preparation

Before you start cutting your hair, it's important to prepare it so that it's easier to work with. To do this, follow these steps:

  1. Wash your hair and let it air dry until it's damp.
  2. Comb your hair to remove any tangles.
  3. Section your hair into four parts: the top, left side, right side, and back. Use sectioning clips to keep the hair in place.

Section 1: Cutting the Back

The first section you'll want to cut is the back. Follow these steps:

  1. Start at the nape of your neck and work your way up.
  2. Using your scissors, cut a straight line across the bottom of your hair, making sure to keep the ends even.
  3. Continue cutting small sections of hair, working your way up the back of your head.
  4. Make sure to check your work in the mirror frequently to ensure evenness.

Section 2: Cutting the Sides

Now it's time to move on to the sides of your hair. Follow these steps:

  1. Start at the front of your left side and work your way back.
  2. Use your scissors to cut small sections of hair, making sure to keep the length even.
  3. Repeat on the right side of your hair.
  4. Check your work in the mirror frequently to ensure evenness.

Section 3: Cutting the Top

Finally, it's time to cut the top of your hair. Follow these steps:

  1. Start at the front of your hair and work your way back.
  2. Use your scissors to cut small sections of hair, making sure to keep the length even.
  3. For added texture, you can use clippers to create short layers on the top of your hair.
  4. Check your work in the mirror frequently to ensure evenness.

Finishing Touches

Once you've finished cutting your hair, you can make any final adjustments to ensure that it looks just the way you want it to.

  1. Use your scissors to trim any stray hairs that you may have missed.
  2. Create additional layers or texture as desired.
  3. Style your hair as desired.

Tips for Cutting Your Own Curly Hair Pixie

Cutting your own hair can be intimidating, especially if you're new to it. These tips can help make the process easier and more successful:

  • Start with small sections of hair and work your way up.
  • Check your work frequently in the mirror to ensure evenness.
  • Don't be afraid to take breaks if you need to.
  • For added texture, use clippers or thinning shears.
  • Experiment with different cutting techniques until you find what works best for you.

FAQ

Q: Can I cut my curly hair pixie when it's dry?

A: It's best to cut your hair when it's damp, as this will make it easier to work with and ensure evenness.

Q: Do I need to use clippers to cut my hair?

A: No, clippers are optional. You can use scissors alone to create your curly hair pixie cut.

Hair Care for Your Curly Hair Pixie

Once you've cut your hair, it's important to take care of it to keep it looking its best. Follow these tips for maintaining your curly hair pixie:

  • Use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair hydrated.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools, as they can damage your curls.
  • Use a diffuser when blow-drying your hair to enhance your curls.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.

With these tips and techniques, you can confidently cut your own curly hair pixie at home. Remember to take your time, check your work frequently, and don't be afraid to experiment until you find the perfect cut for you.
