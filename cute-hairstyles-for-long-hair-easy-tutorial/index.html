Cute Hairstyles for Long Hair Easy Tutorial

Description

Are you tired of wearing the same hairstyle every day? Do you want to switch up your look without spending too much time on your hair? Look no further! This article will provide you with step-by-step tutorials on cute hairstyles for long hair that are easy to do.

How to

Before we get started, make sure you have the following tools: a brush, hair ties, bobby pins, and a curling iron. Now, let's begin!

1. Half-Up Half-Down

Start by brushing your hair to get rid of any tangles. Then, take a section of hair from the crown of your head and tie it with a hair tie. Make sure to leave some hair at the front to frame your face. You can also curl the ends of your hair to add some texture.

2. Messy Bun

Gather all of your hair into a high ponytail and tie it with a hair tie. Twist your hair around the base of the ponytail until it forms a bun. Use bobby pins to secure the bun in place. Pull out some strands of hair around your face to create a messy look.

3. Braided Crown

Part your hair down the middle and braid both sides. Take one braid and wrap it around the crown of your head, securing it with bobby pins. Repeat with the other braid, tucking the ends under the first braid.

Tips

For a more polished look, use hairspray to keep your hairstyle in place. You can also add accessories such as headbands or clips to enhance your hairstyle.

FAQ

1. How long does it take to do these hairstyles?

These hairstyles should take no more than 10 minutes to do.

2. Can I do these hairstyles on shorter hair?

These hairstyles are best suited for long hair, but you can modify them to work for shorter hair.

Hair Care

Make sure to take care of your hair by washing it regularly and using conditioner to keep it hydrated. You can also use hair masks to nourish your hair and prevent damage.

Conclusion

With these cute hairstyles for long hair easy tutorial, you can switch up your look without spending too much time on your hair. Try them out and see which one works best for you!
