Short Hairstyles For Heart Shaped Face Over 50

Written by Robby Jul 28, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Are you over 50 and looking for a new hairstyle that suits your heart-shaped face? You're in luck! In this article, we'll discuss the best short hairstyles for heart-shaped faces over 50. From bobs to pixie cuts, we've got you covered.

Description

A heart-shaped face is characterized by a wide forehead, high cheekbones, and a narrow chin. The key to finding a flattering hairstyle for this face shape is to balance out the features. Short hairstyles, in particular, can help to elongate the face and draw attention away from the chin.

How To

Before you head to the salon, it's important to consider your lifestyle and personal preferences. Do you have time for daily styling? Do you prefer a low-maintenance look? Once you've determined your needs, you can choose a hairstyle that works for you.

Step by Step

Here are some step-by-step instructions for achieving three popular short hairstyles:

Bob

1. Begin by washing and blow-drying your hair.

2. Use a round brush to create volume at the crown of your head.

3. Use a straightening iron to smooth out any flyaways.

4. Finish with a light hairspray.

Pixie Cut

1. Start with clean, dry hair.

2. Use a comb to create a deep side part.

3. Use a scissors to cut your hair short on the sides and back.

4. Use a styling gel to create texture on the top of your head.

5. Finish with a light hairspray.

Layered Cut

1. Start with clean, dry hair.

2. Use a scissors to create layers throughout your hair.

3. Use a straightening iron to smooth out any kinks.

4. Finish with a texturizing spray.

Tips

When it comes to short hairstyles for heart-shaped faces over 50, there are a few tips to keep in mind:

  • Avoid hairstyles that accentuate the chin.
  • Choose hairstyles that add volume to the crown of the head.
  • Consider bangs to balance out the forehead.
  • Experiment with texture to add dimension to the hair.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about short hairstyles for heart-shaped faces over 50:

What is the best short hairstyle for a heart-shaped face?

The best short hairstyle for a heart-shaped face is one that adds volume to the crown of the head and balances out the chin. Bobs, pixie cuts, and layered cuts are all great options.

Can I still have long hair if I have a heart-shaped face?

Yes, you can still have long hair if you have a heart-shaped face. However, it's important to choose a style that doesn't accentuate the chin. Long layers or a side-swept fringe can help to balance out the face.

How often should I get my hair cut?

It's recommended to get a haircut every 6-8 weeks to maintain the shape of the style.

Hair Care

Proper hair care is essential for maintaining a healthy, shiny, and beautiful hairstyle. Here are some tips for caring for your short hair:

  • Wash your hair every 2-3 days to prevent dryness.
  • Use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often.
  • Use a heat protectant spray before using heat styling tools.
  • Get regular trims to maintain the shape of the style.

With these tips and the right hairstyle, you can look and feel your best at any age!
