How To Cut Short Choppy Hairstyle

Written by Robby Mar 31, 2023 Â· 6 min read

If you're looking for a hairstyle that's edgy, trendy, and low-maintenance, then a short choppy hairstyle may be just what you need. This style is perfect for those who don't have a lot of time to spend on their hair but still want to look fashionable. In this article, we'll show you how to cut a short choppy hairstyle step by step, including tips and tricks to make it look amazing.

How to Cut a Short Choppy Hairstyle

A short choppy hairstyle is all about layers and texture. The goal is to create a messy, undone look that's still stylish and chic. To achieve this, you'll need to follow these steps:

Step by Step Guide

  1. Start with clean, dry hair. This will make it easier to see where the layers should be.
  2. Section your hair into four parts: top, sides, and back.
  3. Start with the back section and cut a straight line across the nape of the neck. This will be the shortest layer.
  4. Working your way up, take small sections of hair and cut them at an angle, so the hair is shorter towards the back and longer towards the front.
  5. Repeat this process on the sides and top sections, making sure to blend the layers together so they look natural.
  6. Once you've finished cutting, use your fingers to tousle your hair and create more texture.
  7. If you want to add more dimension, you can use a razor or scissors to create choppy layers throughout your hair.
  8. Style your hair as desired, using products like mousse or texturizing spray to enhance the choppy look.

Tips and Tricks

To make sure your short choppy hairstyle looks amazing, follow these tips:

  • Don't try to cut your hair all at once. Take your time and work in small sections.
  • Use sharp scissors to create clean, precise cuts.
  • If you're not sure how short to cut your hair, start with a longer length and work your way down.
  • Don't be afraid to use a razor to create more choppy layers.
  • When styling your hair, use your fingers to create volume and texture.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about short choppy hairstyles:

Q. Who is a short choppy hairstyle best for?

A. Short choppy hairstyles are great for anyone who wants a low-maintenance, edgy look. They're especially popular among younger women.

Q. Will a short choppy hairstyle work for curly hair?

A. Yes, but you may need to have your hair cut differently to accommodate your curls. Talk to your stylist about the best way to cut a short choppy hairstyle for curly hair.

Q. How often should I get my short choppy hairstyle trimmed?

A. It depends on how fast your hair grows and how quickly the layers lose their shape. Generally, you should get it trimmed every 4-6 weeks to maintain the style.

Hair Care for Short Choppy Hairstyles

To keep your short choppy hairstyle looking its best, follow these hair care tips:

  • Wash your hair every 2-3 days to avoid stripping it of its natural oils.
  • Use a shampoo and conditioner specifically designed for short hair.
  • Avoid using heavy styling products that can weigh down your hair.
  • If you have colored hair, use a color-safe shampoo and conditioner to prevent fading.
  • Protect your hair from heat damage by using a heat protectant spray before using hot tools.

With these tips and tricks, you'll be able to cut and maintain a short choppy hairstyle like a pro. Whether you're looking for a bold new look or just want to switch up your style, this trendy haircut is sure to turn heads.
