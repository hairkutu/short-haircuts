Styling Curly Hair Products Your Ultimate Guide

Written by Petter Aug 17, 2023 · 7 min read

If you have curly hair, you know that styling it can be a challenge. But with the right products and techniques, you can achieve the perfect curly hairstyle. In this article, we’ll go over everything you need to know about styling curly hair products, from how to choose the right products to step-by-step instructions for styling your curls.

Table of Contents

  • How to Choose the Right Products
  • How to Style Curly Hair
  • Step-by-Step Instructions for Styling Curly Hair
  • Tips for Styling Curly Hair
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care Tips for Curly Hair

How to Choose the Right Products

The first step in styling curly hair is choosing the right products. There are a few key things to consider when selecting products for curly hair:

  • Moisture: Curly hair needs moisture to stay hydrated and healthy. Look for products that contain ingredients like shea butter, coconut oil, or glycerin.
  • Hold: Curly hair also needs hold to maintain its shape. Look for products that offer a medium to strong hold, like gels or mousses.
  • Frizz control: Curly hair is prone to frizz, so look for products that offer frizz control, like serums or creams.

Some popular curly hair products include:

  • Curly hair shampoo and conditioner
  • Curl defining cream
  • Curl enhancing gel
  • Curl refreshing spray
  • Leave-in conditioner

How to Style Curly Hair

Before you start styling your curly hair, it’s important to start with clean, wet hair. This will help your curls form better and last longer.

Once your hair is clean and wet, you can start applying your styling products. Here are some tips for styling curly hair:

  • Apply your products in sections, starting from the back and working your way forward.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to distribute the product evenly through your hair.
  • Scrunch your hair gently to encourage your curls to form.
  • Use a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer to dry your hair without disturbing your curls.

Step-by-Step Instructions for Styling Curly Hair

Here’s a step-by-step guide for styling curly hair:

  1. Start with clean, wet hair.
  2. Apply a curl defining cream or gel to your hair.
  3. Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to distribute the product evenly through your hair.
  4. Scrunch your hair gently to encourage your curls to form.
  5. Use a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer to dry your hair without disturbing your curls.
  6. Once your hair is dry, use your fingers to separate your curls and create volume.
  7. If desired, use a curl refreshing spray to revive any flattened or frizzy curls.

Tips for Styling Curly Hair

Here are some additional tips for styling curly hair:

  • Don’t touch your hair too much while it’s drying, as this can cause frizz.
  • Sleep on a silk or satin pillowcase to prevent frizz.
  • Avoid using a towel to dry your hair, as this can cause friction and frizz. Instead, use a microfiber towel or an old t-shirt to gently squeeze excess water from your hair.
  • Experiment with different products and techniques to find what works best for your hair.

FAQ

What’s the best way to dry curly hair?

The best way to dry curly hair is with a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer. This will dry your hair without disturbing your curls.

How often should I wash my curly hair?

It depends on your hair type and personal preference, but most people with curly hair find that washing their hair once or twice a week is sufficient.

What’s the best way to revive flattened or frizzy curls?

You can revive flattened or frizzy curls by using a curl refreshing spray, or by wetting your hair slightly and reapplying your styling products.

Hair Care Tips for Curly Hair

In addition to using the right products and styling techniques, it’s important to take good care of your curly hair. Here are some hair care tips for curly hair:

  • Avoid using hot tools like flat irons and curling irons, as these can damage your hair.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it’s dry, as this can cause frizz. Instead, use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your hair when it’s wet.
  • Apply a deep conditioning treatment to your hair once a week to keep it hydrated and healthy.

By following these tips and using the right products and techniques, you can achieve the perfect curly hairstyle. Happy styling!
