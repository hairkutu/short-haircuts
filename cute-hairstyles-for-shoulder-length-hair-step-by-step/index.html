Cute Hairstyles For Shoulder Length Hair Step By Step

Written by Aprilia Mar 22, 2023 Â· 8 min read

In 2023, shoulder length hair is still a trendy and versatile length that can be styled in a variety of ways. Whether you're looking for a quick and easy style or something more elaborate for a special occasion, there are plenty of cute hairstyles for shoulder length hair that you can try. In this post, we'll share some step by step tutorials to help you achieve some of these styles, as well as offer tips for hair care and answer frequently asked questions.

Table of Contents:

  1. How to Style Shoulder Length Hair
  2. Step by Step Tutorials
  3. Tips for Maintaining Shoulder Length Hair
  4. Frequently Asked Questions
  5. Hair Care for Shoulder Length Hair

How to Style Shoulder Length Hair

Before we dive into specific hairstyles, let's go over some general tips for styling shoulder length hair:

  • Start with clean, dry hair. If your hair is naturally oily, consider using a dry shampoo to absorb excess oil before styling.
  • Invest in quality hair styling tools, such as a curling iron, straightener, or hair dryer. These tools can make a big difference in achieving the look you want.
  • Use hair products to help hold your style in place. This can include mousse, hairspray, or styling cream.
  • Experiment with different partings to change up your look. A side part can create a more dramatic effect, while a middle part can be more classic.

Step by Step Tutorials

1. Messy Bun

Step 1: Start by brushing your hair to remove any tangles.

Step 2: Pull your hair back into a high ponytail and secure it with a hair tie.

Step 3: Twist your ponytail into a bun and secure it with bobby pins.

Step 4: Use your fingers to gently pull on the bun to create a messy, undone look.

Step 5: Finish with hairspray to hold your style in place.

2. Beach Waves

Step 1: Start with clean, dry hair.

Step 2: Divide your hair into sections and clip them up, leaving one section down.

Step 3: Use a curling iron to curl the section away from your face. Hold the curling iron for a few seconds, then release.

Step 4: Repeat this process on all sections of hair.

Step 5: Use your fingers to gently loosen the curls and create a beachy, textured look.

Step 6: Finish with hairspray to hold your style in place.

3. Half Up Half Down

Step 1: Start by brushing your hair to remove any tangles.

Step 2: Take a small section of hair from the top of your head and pull it back, securing it with a hair tie.

Step 3: Use a curling iron to curl the remaining hair in loose waves.

Step 4: Use your fingers to gently pull on the curls to create a more relaxed look.

Step 5: Finish with hairspray to hold your style in place.

Tips for Maintaining Shoulder Length Hair

  • Get regular trims to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.
  • Consider using a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair hydrated and nourished.
  • Avoid using hot tools on your hair every day, as this can cause damage and breakage.
  • Use a heat protectant spray before using hot tools to minimize damage.
  • Avoid pulling your hair back too tightly, as this can cause tension and breakage.

Frequently Asked Questions

What are some cute hairstyles for shoulder length hair?

Some cute hairstyles for shoulder length hair include messy buns, beach waves, half up half down styles, and braids.

How can I make my shoulder length hair look fuller?

You can make your shoulder length hair look fuller by adding layers, using volumizing products, and blow drying your hair upside down to create more volume at the roots.

How often should I wash my shoulder length hair?

This can vary depending on your hair type and lifestyle, but generally it's recommended to wash your hair every 2-3 days to avoid stripping your hair of natural oils.

Hair Care for Shoulder Length Hair

Some tips for hair care for shoulder length hair include:

  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner that is formulated for your hair type.
  • Avoid using hot water when washing your hair, as this can strip your hair of natural oils.
  • Consider using a leave-in conditioner to help detangle your hair and keep it hydrated throughout the day.
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it's wet, as this can cause breakage. Instead, use a wide-tooth comb to gently detangle your hair.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and harsh weather conditions by wearing a hat or using a UV-protectant spray.

With these tips and tutorials, you can experiment with different cute hairstyles for shoulder length hair and keep your hair healthy and looking its best. Have fun and don't be afraid to try something new!
