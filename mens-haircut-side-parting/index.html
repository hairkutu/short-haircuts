Mens Haircut Side Parting A Classic Look For The Modern Man

Written by Petter May 19, 2023 · 7 min read

Are you looking to upgrade your hairstyle with a classic and timeless look? Look no further than the mens haircut side parting. This hairstyle has been around for decades and is still popular today. In this article, we’ll cover the basics of the mens haircut side parting, including how to get it, styling tips, and hair care.

Table of Contents

  • How to Get the Mens Haircut Side Parting
  • Styling Tips for the Mens Haircut Side Parting
  • Step-by-Step Guide to Styling the Mens Haircut Side Parting
  • Tips for Maintaining the Mens Haircut Side Parting
  • FAQs About the Mens Haircut Side Parting
  • Hair Care Tips for the Mens Haircut Side Parting

How to Get the Mens Haircut Side Parting

The mens haircut side parting is a classic style that can be achieved with any hair type. It involves cutting the hair short on the sides and back while leaving some length on top. The hair on top is then combed to one side, creating a side parting.

To get this haircut, visit a barber or stylist who is experienced in classic men’s hairstyles. Ask for a tapered cut on the sides and back, with a longer length on top. Make sure to communicate your desired length and the location of your side parting.

Styling Tips for the Mens Haircut Side Parting

The mens haircut side parting is a versatile style that can be worn in many different ways. Here are some styling tips to achieve the look that suits you:

  • Use a comb to create a clean and defined side parting.
  • Apply a styling product such as pomade, wax, or gel to the hair on top to hold the style in place.
  • Experiment with different parting locations and angles to find the look that suits your face shape.
  • Add volume to the hair on top by blow-drying it with a round brush.

Step-by-Step Guide to Styling the Mens Haircut Side Parting

Here is a step-by-step guide to styling the mens haircut side parting:

  1. Wash and towel-dry your hair.
  2. Apply a styling product such as pomade, wax, or gel to the hair on top.
  3. Use a comb to create a side parting on one side of your head.
  4. Use a brush or your fingers to style the hair on top, sweeping it to one side.
  5. Add more product if necessary to hold the style in place.
  6. Use a hair dryer and a round brush to add volume to the hair on top.
  7. Finish with a light hairspray to hold the style.

Tips for Maintaining the Mens Haircut Side Parting

To keep your mens haircut side parting looking its best, follow these tips:

  • Visit your barber or stylist regularly for touch-ups and trims.
  • Use a high-quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy.
  • Avoid using too much product, which can weigh down your hair and create a greasy appearance.
  • Experiment with different styling products to find the one that works best for your hair type.

FAQs About the Mens Haircut Side Parting

Is the mens haircut side parting suitable for all face shapes?

Yes, the mens haircut side parting can be adapted to suit any face shape. Experiment with different parting locations and angles to find the one that flatters your features.

How often should I get my mens haircut side parting trimmed?

You should visit your barber or stylist every 4-6 weeks for touch-ups and trims to keep your mens haircut side parting looking sharp.

Can I style my mens haircut side parting in different ways?

Yes, the mens haircut side parting is a versatile style that can be adapted to suit different occasions and moods. Experiment with different styling products and techniques to find the look that suits you.

Hair Care Tips for the Mens Haircut Side Parting

Here are some hair care tips to keep your mens haircut side parting looking its best:

  • Use a clarifying shampoo once a week to remove buildup from styling products.
  • Avoid using hot tools such as flat irons and curling irons, which can damage your hair.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and wind by wearing a hat or applying a leave-in conditioner with SPF.
  • Eat a healthy diet rich in vitamins and minerals to promote hair growth and strength.

Now you have all the information you need to rock the mens haircut side parting with confidence. This classic hairstyle is a timeless look that will never go out of style.
