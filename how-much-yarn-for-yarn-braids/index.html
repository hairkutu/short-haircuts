How Much Yarn For Yarn Braids A Complete Guide

Written by Daniel Mar 30, 2023 · 7 min read

Yarn braids are a popular protective hairstyle that uses yarn instead of traditional braiding hair. Not only is it a cost-effective option, but it also allows for more creativity in terms of color and texture. However, one of the most frequently asked questions about yarn braids is how much yarn is needed to complete the style. In this guide, we will break down everything you need to know about how much yarn for yarn braids.

Table of Contents

  1. Description
  2. How To
  3. Step by Step
  4. Tips
  5. FAQ
  6. Hair Care

Description

Yarn braids are created by wrapping yarn around small sections of hair, resulting in a braided appearance. The amount of yarn needed depends on the length and thickness of your hair, as well as the desired length and thickness of the braids. Generally, you will need around 3-4 skeins of yarn for a full head of medium-sized braids.

How To

To determine how much yarn you will need for your yarn braids, you will need to take a few measurements. First, measure the length of your hair from the roots to the ends. Next, determine the thickness of your hair by measuring the circumference of a small section of hair. Finally, decide on the desired length and thickness of the braids.

Calculating Yarn Needed

To calculate how much yarn you will need, use the following formula:

(Length of hair x Number of braids x Desired thickness) ÷ Yarn length per skein = Number of skeins needed

For example, if you have shoulder-length hair and want to create 100 medium-sized braids with a thickness of 1 inch, and the yarn you are using comes in 200-yard skeins, the calculation would be:

(12 inches x 100 braids x 1 inch) ÷ 200 yards = 6 skeins

Step by Step

Here are the basic steps to create yarn braids:

  1. Divide your hair into small sections using a rat-tail comb.
  2. Cut a length of yarn that is three times the length of the section of hair.
  3. Fold the yarn in half and place it underneath the section of hair.
  4. Wrap one half of the yarn around the section of hair, then wrap the other half in the opposite direction.
  5. Tie the ends of the yarn together at the bottom of the section of hair.
  6. Repeat steps 2-5 for each section of hair.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when creating yarn braids:

  • Use a lightweight yarn to prevent heaviness and tension on the scalp.
  • Pre-stretch the yarn by wrapping it around a chair or table to prevent it from shrinking and becoming too tight on the scalp.
  • Apply a leave-in conditioner or oil to the hair before braiding to prevent dryness and breakage.
  • Wrap the ends of the yarn tightly around the hair to prevent it from unraveling.
  • Wear a satin or silk bonnet or scarf at night to protect the style and prevent frizz.

FAQ

How long do yarn braids last?

Yarn braids can last up to 8 weeks with proper care and maintenance.

Can you wash yarn braids?

Yes, you can wash yarn braids. However, it is important to use a sulfate-free shampoo and to avoid soaking the braids to prevent frizz and unraveling.

Can you swim with yarn braids?

It is not recommended to swim with yarn braids, as the yarn can become heavy and cause tension on the scalp.

Hair Care

To maintain the health of your hair while wearing yarn braids, it is important to:

  • Moisturize the scalp and braids with a light oil or leave-in conditioner.
  • Avoid pulling or tugging on the braids to prevent breakage and tension on the scalp.
  • Trim the ends of the braids periodically to prevent split ends.
  • Remove the braids after 8 weeks to prevent matting and tangling.

By following these tips and taking proper care of your hair, you can enjoy beautiful and long-lasting yarn braids.
