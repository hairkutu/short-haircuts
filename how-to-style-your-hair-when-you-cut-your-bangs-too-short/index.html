How To Style Your Hair When You Cut Your Bangs Too Short

Written by William May 24, 2023 Â· 8 min read

Have you ever gone for a quick trim and ended up with bangs that are just too short? It can be frustrating and make you feel like you're stuck with a hairstyle you didn't plan for. But fear not, there are actually several ways you can style your hair to make those too-short bangs work. In this article, we'll go over how to style your hair when you cut your bangs too short, step by step.

Description

When you cut your bangs too short, it can be tricky to figure out how to style them. But with a few tricks and tips, you can make them work for you. Whether you want to grow them out or just make them look more intentional, there are several ways to style your hair when your bangs are too short.

How to

Option 1: Pin Them Back

If your bangs are just too short to work with, the easiest solution is to pin them back. Use bobby pins or hair clips to secure your bangs to the side of your head. You can do this with just one or two pieces, or you can create a more intricate look by twisting or braiding your bangs before pinning them back.

Option 2: Wear a Headband

Another way to hide too-short bangs is to wear a headband. Choose a headband that matches your hair color or adds a pop of color to your outfit. Place the headband just above your bangs and push your hair back. This will give you a cute, retro look while keeping your bangs out of your face.

Option 3: Style Them Up

If your bangs are just a little too short, you can try styling them up. Use a small amount of hair gel or pomade to slick your bangs back. This will give you a sleek, modern look that works well for both casual and formal occasions.

Option 4: Add Volume

If your bangs are too short and flat, you can add volume to them by using a round brush and a blow dryer. Start by blow-drying your bangs forward, then use the round brush to curl them back. This will give your bangs some much-needed volume and texture.

Step by Step

Step 1: Wash and Dry Your Hair

Start by washing and drying your hair. Make sure your bangs are completely dry before you start styling them.

Step 2: Decide on Your Style

Choose one of the styling options we mentioned above, or come up with your own.

Step 3: Pin or Style Your Bangs

Depending on the style you chose, either pin your bangs back or style them as desired.

Step 4: Finish with Hairspray

Once you have your bangs styled the way you want them, finish with a light mist of hairspray to hold everything in place.

Tips

  • Experiment with different styling options until you find one that works for you.
  • Use bobby pins or hair clips that match your hair color for a more natural look.
  • Invest in a good round brush for adding volume to your bangs.

FAQ

What should I do if I cut my bangs too short?

If you cut your bangs too short, don't panic. Use one of the styling options we mentioned above to make them work for you.

Can I still grow out my bangs if I cut them too short?

Yes, you can still grow out your bangs if you cut them too short. It may take a little longer, but they will eventually grow out. In the meantime, use one of the styling options we mentioned above to make them look intentional.

Should I go to a professional to fix my too-short bangs?

If your bangs are just a little too short, you can probably fix them yourself using one of the styling options we mentioned above. If they're extremely short or you're not comfortable styling them yourself, it's a good idea to go to a professional hairstylist to fix them.

Hair Care

To keep your hair healthy and looking its best, make sure to use high-quality hair products and avoid using heat styling tools too often. Eat a healthy diet and drink plenty of water to promote hair growth and keep your hair looking shiny and strong.

In conclusion, cutting your bangs too short can be frustrating, but there are several ways you can style your hair to make them work for you. Experiment with different options until you find one that you love, and don't be afraid to ask a professional hairstylist for help if needed. With a little creativity and patience, you can turn your too-short bangs into a cute and stylish hairstyle.
