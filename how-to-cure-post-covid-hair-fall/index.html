How To Cure Post Covid Hair Fall Tips And Tricks

Written by Aprilia Jul 08, 2023 · 7 min read

If you have experienced hair fall after contracting Covid-19, you are not alone. Many individuals have reported hair loss as one of the post-Covid symptoms they have encountered. Fortunately, there are several ways to combat hair fall and get your luscious locks back. In this article, we will discuss how to cure post Covid hair fall, step by step, tips, and frequently asked questions. Let's dive in!

How to Cure Post Covid Hair Fall

Post Covid hair fall can be frustrating and stressful, but it is not permanent. Here are some ways to cure post Covid hair fall:

1. Consult a Doctor

Before starting any treatment for hair fall, it is essential to consult a doctor. They can determine the underlying cause of hair fall and suggest appropriate treatments.

2. Eat a Balanced Diet

A balanced diet can help improve hair health. Include foods that are rich in protein, vitamins, and minerals such as eggs, spinach, nuts, and fruits in your diet.

3. Use Hair Growth Products

There are several hair growth products available in the market, such as shampoos, conditioners, and serums. Look for products that contain ingredients such as biotin, keratin, and argan oil, which can promote hair growth.

4. Practice Stress-Relieving Techniques

Stress can contribute to hair fall. Practice stress-relieving techniques such as meditation, yoga, or deep breathing exercises to reduce stress levels.

Step by Step: How to Cure Post Covid Hair Fall

Here is a step-by-step guide on how to cure post Covid hair fall:

Step 1: Consult a Doctor

Visit a doctor to determine the underlying cause of hair fall.

Step 2: Change Your Diet

Incorporate hair-healthy foods into your diet, such as eggs, spinach, nuts, and fruits.

Step 3: Use Hair Growth Products

Use hair growth products that contain ingredients such as biotin, keratin, and argan oil.

Step 4: Reduce Stress

Practice stress-relieving techniques such as meditation, yoga, or deep breathing exercises.

Tips to Prevent Hair Fall

Here are some tips to prevent hair fall:

1. Avoid Tight Hairstyles

Avoid tight hairstyles such as braids, buns, or ponytails, as they can pull on the hair and cause breakage.

2. Use a Wide-Toothed Comb

Use a wide-toothed comb to detangle wet hair, as wet hair is more susceptible to breakage.

3. Avoid Heat Styling

Avoid heat styling tools such as curling irons, straighteners, or blow dryers, as they can damage the hair and cause breakage.

4. Wash Your Hair Regularly

Wash your hair regularly to keep it clean and healthy. Use a mild shampoo and conditioner that is suitable for your hair type.

Frequently Asked Questions

1. Is post Covid hair fall permanent?

No, post Covid hair fall is not permanent. With the right treatment and care, hair can grow back.

2. How long does it take for hair to grow back after Covid?

It can take several months for hair to grow back after Covid. The timeline can vary depending on the severity of hair fall and individual factors such as genetics and diet.

3. Can stress cause hair fall after Covid?

Yes, stress can contribute to hair fall after Covid. Practice stress-relieving techniques to reduce stress levels.

Hair Care: How to Keep Your Hair Healthy

Here are some tips to keep your hair healthy:

1. Trim Your Hair Regularly

Trim your hair every six to eight weeks to get rid of split ends and promote healthy hair growth.

2. Protect Your Hair from the Sun

Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a leave-in conditioner that contains SPF.

3. Avoid Over-Shampooing

Avoid over-shampooing your hair, as it can strip the scalp of its natural oils and cause dryness.

4. Use a Hair Mask

Use a hair mask once a week to nourish and hydrate your hair. Look for masks that contain ingredients such as coconut oil or avocado.

By following these tips, you can cure post Covid hair fall and keep your hair healthy and luscious. Remember to consult a doctor before starting any treatment, and be patient as hair growth can take time. With the right care and treatment, you can achieve the hair of your dreams!
