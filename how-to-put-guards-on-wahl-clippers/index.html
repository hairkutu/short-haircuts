How To Put Guards On Wahl Clippers A Step By Step Guide

Written by Luffy Aug 05, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Wahl clippers are a staple in the hair industry. They are known for their durability, versatility, and precision. One of the great things about Wahl clippers is that they come with guards that allow you to adjust the length of the hair you are cutting. In this article, we will be discussing how to put guards on Wahl clippers.

Description

Guards are attachments that are placed over the blades of Wahl clippers to adjust the length of the hair being cut. They come in different sizes, usually numbered from 1 to 8, with 1 being the shortest and 8 being the longest. Guards are essential for achieving the desired haircut length and creating a consistent look.

How to Put Guards on Wahl Clippers

Putting guards on Wahl clippers is a simple process that can be done in a few easy steps.

Step 1: Turn off the clippers

Before attaching the guards, make sure the clippers are turned off to avoid any accidents.

Step 2: Choose the right guard size

Choose the guard size that corresponds to the desired length of the haircut. The numbers on the guards correspond to the length of the hair in millimeters, so make sure to choose the right size.

Step 3: Attach the guard

Attach the guard onto the clipper by sliding it onto the blade until it clicks into place. Make sure the guard is securely attached before turning on the clipper.

Step 4: Turn on the clippers

Turn on the clippers and start cutting the hair. Make sure to hold the clippers at the right angle and to move them in the direction of the hair growth.

Step 5: Remove the guard

Once you are finished cutting the hair, remove the guard by sliding it off the blade. Make sure to clean the clippers and the guard before storing them away.

Tips

- Always make sure the clippers are turned off before attaching or removing the guards.
- Choose the right guard size for the desired haircut length.
- Make sure the guard is securely attached before turning on the clippers.
- Hold the clippers at the right angle and move them in the direction of the hair growth.
- Clean the clippers and the guard after each use to maintain their performance.

FAQ

Q: Can I use different brands of guards on Wahl clippers?

A: It is not recommended to use different brands of guards on Wahl clippers. Each brand has its own specifications and may not fit properly on other brands.

Q: Can I cut hair without a guard on Wahl clippers?

A: Yes, you can cut hair without a guard on Wahl clippers. However, it is not recommended for beginners as it requires a lot of skill and experience to do so.

Hair Care

Cutting hair with Wahl clippers is easy and efficient with the use of guards. Make sure to choose the right guard size for the desired haircut length and to clean the clippers and guards after each use. With proper care and maintenance, Wahl clippers can last for years and provide excellent performance.
