Mens Medium Length Hairstyles For Wavy Hair A Complete Guide

If you have medium length wavy hair and are looking for some inspiration for your next haircut, you're in the right place. In this guide, we will be discussing the best mens medium length hairstyles for wavy hair, how to achieve them, and tips for maintaining your hairstyle. Let's get started!

Description

Medium length hairstyles for wavy hair are a great option for men who want to keep some length while still having a manageable hairstyle. Wavy hair is naturally voluminous and adds texture to any hairstyle. With the right cut and styling products, you can achieve a variety of looks, from messy and casual to sleek and polished.

How to

The first step to achieving a great medium length hairstyle for wavy hair is finding the right cut. A layered cut is the most common and versatile option for wavy hair. It helps to reduce bulk and adds movement to the hair. A layered cut also provides more styling options, allowing you to create a messy, textured look or a more polished style.

Choosing the Right Length

The length of your hair will depend on your personal preference, face shape, and lifestyle. For a more casual look, you can opt for a longer, shaggy style. For a more polished look, a shorter, slicked-back style may be more suitable. Generally, medium length hair falls between 2-4 inches in length.

Styling Products

To achieve the perfect medium length hairstyle for wavy hair, you will need some styling products. A good quality hair gel or pomade can help to add texture and hold to your hair. You can also use a salt spray or texturizing spray to enhance your natural waves and create a messy, beachy look.

Step by step

Here is a step-by-step guide on how to style your medium length wavy hair:

  1. Wash your hair with a good quality shampoo and conditioner.
  2. Towel dry your hair until it is damp.
  3. Apply a small amount of hair gel or pomade to your hair, starting at the roots and working your way down to the tips.
  4. Use your fingers or a comb to create your desired style. For a messy, textured look, use your fingers to scrunch your hair and create waves. For a more polished look, use a comb to create a side part or slicked-back style.
  5. Finish with a hairspray to hold your style in place.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you maintain your medium length wavy hairstyle:

  • Regular trims every 4-6 weeks will help to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as this can damage your hair and make it dry and brittle.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair moisturized and healthy.
  • If you have a naturally oily scalp, use a dry shampoo to absorb excess oil and keep your hair looking fresh.

FAQ

What is the best haircut for medium length wavy hair?

A layered cut is the best option for medium length wavy hair. It helps to reduce bulk and adds movement to the hair. A layered cut also provides more styling options, allowing you to create a messy, textured look or a more polished style.

How do you style medium length wavy hair?

To style medium length wavy hair, you will need some styling products. A good quality hair gel or pomade can help to add texture and hold to your hair. You can also use a salt spray or texturizing spray to enhance your natural waves and create a messy, beachy look.

Hair Care

To keep your medium length wavy hairstyle looking its best, it is important to take care of your hair. Here are some tips for maintaining healthy hair:

  • Wash your hair with a good quality shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as this can damage your hair and make it dry and brittle.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair moisturized and healthy.
  • If you have a naturally oily scalp, use a dry shampoo to absorb excess oil and keep your hair looking fresh.

With the right cut and styling products, you can achieve a variety of mens medium length hairstyles for wavy hair. Whether you prefer a messy, casual look or a more polished style, medium length wavy hair is a great option for men who want to keep some length while still having a manageable hairstyle. Remember to take care of your hair and experiment with different styling products to find your perfect look.
