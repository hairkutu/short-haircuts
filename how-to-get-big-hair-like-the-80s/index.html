How To Get Big Hair Like The 80S A Step By Step Guide

Written by Daniel Apr 21, 2023 Â· 6 min read

If you're looking to channel your inner 80s icon, big hair is a must. From Bon Jovi to Madonna, big hair was the ultimate symbol of style and rebellion. But how do you achieve that iconic look today? Follow this step-by-step guide for big hair like the 80s.

Description:

The 80s was a decade of excess and big hair was no exception. From tight curls to voluminous waves, big hair was a fashion statement that could not be ignored. Whether you're heading to a costume party or just want to change up your look, big hair is a fun and playful way to channel the 80s.

How to:

Getting big hair like the 80s is all about volume and texture. Here's how to achieve the look:

Step 1: Start with Clean Hair

Wash your hair with a volumizing shampoo and conditioner. This will help add body and texture to your hair, making it easier to style.

Step 2: Use a Volumizing Mousse

Apply a volumizing mousse to your hair. This will help add volume and hold to your hair. Work the mousse through your hair, focusing on the roots.

Step 3: Blow Dry Your Hair

Blow dry your hair using a round brush. This will help add volume and shape to your hair. Start at the roots and work your way down to the ends, using the brush to lift and shape your hair.

Step 4: Tease Your Hair

Using a teasing comb, tease your hair at the crown. This will help add height and volume to your hair. Be gentle and work in sections, teasing the hair at the roots.

Step 5: Set Your Hair

Once you've teased your hair, use hairspray to set the style. This will help hold the volume and shape of your hair. Spray your hair from a distance, being careful not to overload it with hairspray.

Tips:

Here are some additional tips to help you get big hair like the 80s:

  • Use hot rollers to create big, bouncy curls.
  • Try backcombing your hair for even more volume.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair after teasing.
  • Invest in a good quality hairspray that will hold your style all day.

FAQ:

Here are some frequently asked questions about getting big hair like the 80s:

What kind of hair works best for big hair?

Big hair works best on hair that is medium to long in length and has some natural texture.

How do I keep my big hair from falling flat?

Use a good quality hairspray and avoid touching your hair too much throughout the day.

Can I get big hair without teasing?

Teasing your hair is the best way to achieve big hair, but you can also use hot rollers or a curling iron to add volume and texture.

Hair Care:

Getting big hair like the 80s can be tough on your hair. Here are some tips to keep your hair healthy:

  • Use a clarifying shampoo once a week to remove buildup from styling products.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair moisturized.
  • Avoid using heat tools too often and always use a heat protectant spray.
  • Don't wash your hair every day as this can strip your hair of its natural oils.

By following these tips and steps, you'll be able to achieve big hair like the 80s in no time. So crank up the tunes, grab some hairspray, and let's get styling!
