Are Cornrows Unprofessional Debunking The Misconception

Introduction

Cornrows are a popular hairstyle among Black women and men. They are versatile and can be worn for any occasion, including professional settings. However, there is a misconception that cornrows are unprofessional. This is often due to stereotypes and prejudices against natural Black hair. In this article, we will debunk this misconception and provide tips for wearing cornrows professionally.

How to Cornrow Your Hair

Cornrowing your hair is a simple and easy process that can be done at home or by a professional. Here are the steps:

  1. Start with clean, detangled hair.
  2. Section your hair into small parts using a comb.
  3. Take one section and divide it into three equal parts.
  4. Begin braiding the hair by crossing the right strand over the middle strand, then the left strand over the middle strand.
  5. As you braid, add more hair to each strand from the parted section.
  6. Continue braiding until you reach the end of the section.
  7. Tie the end of the braid with a hair elastic or thread.
  8. Repeat the process for each section until all of your hair is cornrowed.

Tips for Wearing Cornrows Professionally

While cornrows can be worn in any setting, there are some tips to keep in mind when wearing them in a professional environment:

  • Ensure that your cornrows are neat and well-maintained.
  • Keep your edges and parting neat and clean.
  • Avoid wearing overly long or intricate cornrows.
  • Choose a natural hair color or subtle highlights.
  • Avoid wearing loud or distracting hair accessories.
  • Consider wearing a head wrap or scarf to cover your cornrows if necessary.

FAQs About Cornrows in the Workplace

Here are some frequently asked questions about wearing cornrows in a professional environment:

1. Are cornrows unprofessional?

No, cornrows are not unprofessional. They are a legitimate hairstyle that can be worn in any setting, including the workplace.

2. Can I wear cornrows to a job interview?

Yes, you can wear cornrows to a job interview. Just make sure that they are neat and well-maintained.

3. Will my employer discriminate against me for wearing cornrows?

Employers should not discriminate against employees for wearing cornrows. However, if you experience discrimination or harassment, it is important to report it to HR or a supervisor.

Hair Care Tips for Cornrows

Caring for your cornrows is essential to maintain their longevity and neat appearance. Here are some tips:

  • Moisturize your scalp and hair regularly with a light oil or leave-in conditioner.
  • Avoid using heavy products that can weigh down your cornrows.
  • Wash your hair every 1-2 weeks with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid scratching or pulling at your cornrows, as this can cause breakage.
  • Keep your hair covered while sleeping to prevent frizz and tangling.

Conclusion

In conclusion, cornrows are not unprofessional. They are a legitimate hairstyle that can be worn in any setting, including the workplace. By following the tips and hair care advice provided in this article, you can confidently wear your cornrows to work and any other professional setting.
