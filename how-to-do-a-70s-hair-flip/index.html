How To Do A 70S Hair Flip

Written by Luffy Jul 27, 2023 Â· 6 min read

In the 70s, hair flips were all the rage. This classic hairstyle is still popular today and can be achieved with a few simple steps. In this article, we will teach you how to do a 70s hair flip, step by step.

Description

The 70s hair flip is a hairstyle where the ends of the hair are flipped outwards. It can be achieved on any hair length or texture, and it adds volume and movement to the hair. The hair flip was a popular hairstyle in the 70s and is still a favorite today.

How to Do a 70s Hair Flip

Step 1: Wash and Condition Your Hair

Start by washing and conditioning your hair with a volumizing shampoo and conditioner. This will help to add volume to your hair and make it easier to style. Rinse your hair thoroughly and towel-dry it.

Step 2: Apply a Styling Product

Apply a styling product to your hair, such as a volumizing mousse or a texturizing spray. This will help to add texture and hold to your hair. Apply the product evenly throughout your hair, focusing on the roots.

Step 3: Blow-Dry Your Hair

Blow-dry your hair using a round brush. Start at the roots and work your way down to the ends, using the brush to create volume and movement. Once your hair is dry, use a curling iron to curl the ends of your hair outwards.

Step 4: Flip Your Hair

To create the 70s hair flip, use your fingers to flip the ends of your hair outwards. You can do this by gently pulling the ends of your hair outwards and upwards, creating a slight bend in the hair. Use hairspray to hold the style in place.

Tips

  • Use a large curling iron to create loose curls at the ends of your hair.
  • Don't use too much product, as it can weigh down your hair and make it look greasy.
  • Use hairspray to hold the style in place, but don't overdo it. Too much hairspray can make your hair stiff and crunchy.
  • If you have fine hair, backcomb the roots to add volume.

FAQ

Can I do a 70s hair flip on short hair?

Yes, you can do a 70s hair flip on short hair. Use a curling iron to curl the ends of your hair outwards, then use your fingers to flip the ends outwards.

What kind of brush should I use to blow-dry my hair?

Use a round brush to blow-dry your hair. This will help to create volume and movement in your hair.

How long will the 70s hair flip last?

The 70s hair flip should last all day with the help of hairspray. However, if your hair is prone to falling flat, you may need to touch up the ends throughout the day.

Hair Care

To keep your hair healthy and strong, it's important to take care of it. Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner, and avoid using hot tools on your hair every day. Drink plenty of water and eat a healthy diet to promote hair growth and strength.

In conclusion, the 70s hair flip is a classic hairstyle that can be achieved with a few simple steps. By following the steps outlined in this article, you can create this timeless look and add volume and movement to your hair.
