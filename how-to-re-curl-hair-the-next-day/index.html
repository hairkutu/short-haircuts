How To Re Curl Hair The Next Day A Step By Step Guide

Written by Daniel Apr 15, 2023 Â· 6 min read

If you have curly hair, you know the struggle of making your curls last all day. However, sometimes you may not have the time or energy to re-curl your hair every day. That's why knowing how to re curl hair the next day is essential. In this blog post, we'll provide you with a step-by-step guide on how to re curl hair the next day so that you can enjoy your curls for longer.

Description

Re-curling your hair the next day is all about refreshing your curls without washing your hair. This is especially useful if you have naturally curly hair or if you have styled your hair using hot tools the day before. The goal is to revive your curls and make them look fresh again.

How to Re Curl Hair the Next Day

Here are the steps to follow on how to re curl hair the next day:

Step 1: Start with Dry Hair

Begin by making sure your hair is dry. If you have naturally curly hair, you can skip this step. However, if you have straightened your hair the day before, make sure it is completely dry before moving on to the next step.

Step 2: Apply Heat Protectant

Apply heat protectant to your hair to prevent any damage from hot tools. This is important because you are going to be using hot tools on your hair again.

Step 3: Section Your Hair

Section your hair into small sections using clips. This will make it easier to curl your hair and ensure that you don't miss any sections.

Step 4: Use a Curling Iron

Take a small section of hair and wrap it around a curling iron. Hold for 5-10 seconds and release. Repeat until you have curled all of your hair.

Step 5: Add Volume

To add volume to your curls, use a teasing brush to lightly tease the roots of your hair. This will create a more voluminous look.

Step 6: Apply Hairspray

Finish off by applying hairspray to your curls to hold them in place.

Tips

Here are some tips to ensure that your curls last longer:

  • Use a high-quality curling iron
  • Apply a leave-in conditioner to your hair before going to bed
  • Sleep with your hair in a loose bun or braid to prevent frizz
  • Use a satin pillowcase to prevent hair breakage

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about re curling hair the next day:

Can I re-curl my hair without a curling iron?

Yes, you can use hot rollers or flexi rods to re-curl your hair. Simply wrap your hair around the rollers or rods and leave them in for a few minutes before removing.

How often can I re-curl my hair?

You can re-curl your hair as often as you like, but it's important to give your hair a break from hot tools to prevent damage.

What should I do if my curls fall flat?

If your curls fall flat, try using a volumizing mousse or dry shampoo to add volume to your hair.

Hair Care

Remember to take care of your hair by using a heat protectant and moisturizing products. This will help prevent damage and keep your curls looking healthy and shiny.

Now that you know how to re curl hair the next day, you can enjoy your curls for longer. Follow these steps and tips to keep your curls looking fresh and beautiful.
