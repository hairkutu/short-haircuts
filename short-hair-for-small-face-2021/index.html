Short Hair For Small Face 2021 A Guide To Finding The Perfect Style

Written by William Jun 25, 2023 Â· 7 min read

If you have a small face, finding the perfect hairstyle can be a challenge. You want a style that will complement your features and enhance your natural beauty. Short hair is a great option for small faces, as it can help create the illusion of volume and dimension. In this article, we'll share some tips and tricks for finding the perfect short hairstyle for your small face in 2021.

Description

When it comes to short hair for small faces, there are a few key things to keep in mind. First, you want a style that will create the illusion of volume and dimension. This can be achieved through layers, texture, and movement. Additionally, you want a style that will flatter your face shape and enhance your natural beauty.

How to

When choosing a short hairstyle for your small face, start by considering your face shape. Oval faces have the most versatility when it comes to hairstyles, while round faces benefit from styles that add height and length. Square faces look great with soft, textured styles, and heart-shaped faces can benefit from styles that add volume to the top of the head.

Next, consider your hair texture and thickness. Fine hair benefits from styles that add texture and movement, while thick hair can handle more structured styles. Finally, think about your lifestyle and how much time you are willing to spend on styling your hair each day. Choose a style that is easy to maintain and fits your lifestyle.

Step by Step

Follow these steps to find the perfect short hairstyle for your small face:

  1. Determine your face shape
  2. Consider your hair texture and thickness
  3. Think about your lifestyle and how much time you are willing to spend on styling your hair each day
  4. Look for inspiration in magazines, online, and from celebrities with similar face shapes and hair textures
  5. Consult with a hairstylist to find the perfect style for you

Tips

Here are some additional tips for finding the perfect short hairstyle for your small face:

  • Consider a layered bob or a pixie cut with side-swept bangs
  • Add texture and movement with choppy layers or beachy waves
  • Avoid styles that are too flat or one-dimensional
  • Consider adding highlights or lowlights to add dimension to your hair
  • Use a volumizing mousse or texturizing spray to add body and volume to your hair

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about short hair for small faces:

What is the best short hairstyle for a small face?

The best short hairstyle for a small face depends on your face shape and hair texture. Consider a layered bob or a pixie cut with side-swept bangs for a flattering and dimensional look.

Will a short hairstyle make my face look smaller?

A short hairstyle can help create the illusion of volume and dimension, which can make your face appear smaller. However, it's important to choose a style that flatters your face shape and enhances your natural beauty.

How do I style short hair for a small face?

Styling short hair for a small face is easy with the right products and tools. Use a volumizing mousse or texturizing spray to add body and volume to your hair, and use a round brush and blow dryer to add movement and shape.

Hair Care

Short hair requires regular maintenance to keep it looking healthy and stylish. Use a high-quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair clean and nourished, and consider using a hair mask or treatment once a week to strengthen and repair your hair. Use heat protectant products when styling with heat tools, and avoid using too much product, which can weigh down your hair.

In conclusion, short hair is a great option for small faces, as it can help create the illusion of volume and dimension. By considering your face shape, hair texture, and lifestyle, you can find the perfect short hairstyle for your small face in 2021. Remember to consult with a hairstylist and take good care of your hair to keep it looking healthy and stylish.
