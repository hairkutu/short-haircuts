How To Cover Up Facial Hair A Step By Step Guide

Written by Luffy Jun 17, 2023 · 6 min read

Facial hair can be a source of embarrassment for many women. Whether it's an unwanted mustache or patchy beard, facial hair can make you feel self-conscious and affect your confidence. Fortunately, there are several ways to cover up facial hair and feel more comfortable in your own skin. In this article, we'll discuss how to cover up facial hair using makeup and other techniques.

Table of Contents

  • How to Cover Up Facial Hair
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Covering Up Facial Hair
  • Frequently Asked Questions
  • Hair Care Tips

How to Cover Up Facial Hair

There are several ways to cover up facial hair, including:

  • Makeup
  • Waxing
  • Threading
  • Shaving

In this article, we'll focus on using makeup to cover up facial hair.

Step-by-Step Guide

Follow these steps to cover up facial hair using makeup:

  1. Start with clean, dry skin. Make sure your face is free of oil and dirt.
  2. Apply a primer to your face. This will help your makeup stay on longer.
  3. Use a color corrector to neutralize any darkness or shadow caused by facial hair. For example, if you have a mustache, use a peach or orange color corrector to cancel out the blue tones.
  4. Apply foundation to your face. Use a full-coverage foundation to cover up any remaining shadow.
  5. Set your foundation with a powder. This will help it last longer and prevent it from rubbing off.
  6. Use a concealer to cover up any remaining facial hair. Apply the concealer with a small brush and blend it in well.
  7. Apply blush and bronzer to add some color and dimension to your face.
  8. Finish with a setting spray to help your makeup last all day.

Tips for Covering Up Facial Hair

Here are some tips to help you cover up facial hair more effectively:

  • Use a primer to help your makeup stay on longer.
  • Choose a full-coverage foundation to cover up any remaining shadow.
  • Use a color corrector to neutralize any darkness or shadow caused by facial hair.
  • Apply concealer with a small brush and blend it in well.
  • Set your makeup with a powder and finishing spray to help it last all day.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I use hair removal cream to get rid of facial hair?

Yes, hair removal cream can be an effective way to get rid of facial hair. However, make sure to choose a cream that is specifically designed for the face and follow the instructions carefully.

Is it safe to wax my face?

Waxing can be safe if done properly. However, it can also be painful and cause irritation or breakouts. If you're considering waxing your face, it's best to have it done by a professional.

Can I use a razor to shave my face?

Yes, you can use a razor to shave your face. However, it's important to use a clean, sharp razor and shave in the direction of hair growth to avoid irritation or ingrown hairs.

Hair Care Tips

Here are some tips to help you take care of your facial hair:

  • Keep your skin clean and exfoliated to prevent ingrown hairs.
  • Moisturize your skin regularly to keep it hydrated and healthy.
  • Consider using a hair growth inhibitor to slow down hair growth.

With these tips and techniques, you can effectively cover up facial hair and feel more confident in your own skin. Remember to choose the method that works best for you and take care of your skin and hair to prevent irritation or damage.
