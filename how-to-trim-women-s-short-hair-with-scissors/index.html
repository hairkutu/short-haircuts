How To Trim Women s Short Hair With Scissors A Step By Step Guide

Written by Christine Jul 08, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Are you tired of spending a fortune at the salon every time you need a trim? With the right tools and technique, you can easily trim your own short hair at home. In this article, we'll show you how to trim women's short hair with scissors in a few simple steps.

How to Trim Women's Short Hair with Scissors

Before we begin, gather the following tools:

  • Sharp hair-cutting scissors
  • Comb
  • Clips to section hair
  • Spray bottle with water

Step 1: Wash and Dry Your Hair

Start with freshly washed and completely dry hair. Wet hair is more difficult to cut, and you may end up cutting more than you intended if your hair is wet.

Step 2: Section Your Hair

Section your hair into four parts: the top, the sides, and the back. Use clips to hold each section in place.

Step 3: Start with the Sides

Begin with one side of your head. Take a small section of hair from the bottom and hold it between your index and middle fingers. Cut the hair straight across, using the length of your fingers as a guide. Repeat this process, working your way up the side of your head.

Step 4: Move to the Back

Once you've finished one side, move to the back of your head. Take a small section of hair from the bottom and cut it straight across, using the length of your fingers as a guide. Work your way up the back of your head.

Step 5: Trim the Top

Finally, trim the top of your head. Take a small section of hair from the middle of your head and lift it straight up. Use your fingers as a guide to cut the hair straight across. Work your way from the front to the back of your head.

Step 6: Check for Balance

Once you've finished trimming your hair, check for balance. Make sure both sides are even and adjust as needed.

Tips for Trimming Women's Short Hair with Scissors

  • Use sharp scissors. Dull scissors can damage your hair and make it harder to cut.
  • Don't cut too much at once. It's better to take small sections and work your way up.
  • Take breaks if you need to. Cutting your own hair can be tiring, so take a break if your hands get tired.
  • Invest in a good pair of scissors. A high-quality pair of hair-cutting scissors will last you for years and make trimming your hair easier.

FAQs

Can I trim my own short hair?

Yes, you can trim your own short hair with scissors. With the right tools and technique, it's easy to do at home.

How often should I trim my short hair?

It's recommended that you trim your short hair every 4-6 weeks to keep it healthy and prevent split ends.

Do I need to wet my hair before trimming it?

No, it's best to trim your hair when it's completely dry. Wet hair can be more difficult to cut and may cause you to cut more than you intended.

Hair Care After Trimming Your Hair with Scissors

Now that you've trimmed your hair with scissors, it's important to take care of it properly. Here are a few tips:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy.
  • Avoid heat styling tools as much as possible to prevent damage.
  • Get regular trims every 4-6 weeks to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.

With these tips, you can keep your hair looking healthy and beautiful after trimming it with scissors.
