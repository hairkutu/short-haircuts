What Side Should A Girl s Hair Part Everything You Need To Know

Written by Daniel Aug 11, 2023 Â· 8 min read

When it comes to styling your hair, one of the most important decisions you make is which side to part it on. Your hair part can greatly affect the overall look of your hairstyle, so it's important to choose the right side for your face shape and hair type. In this article, we'll take a closer look at what side should a girl's hair part and provide you with tips on how to create the perfect hair part.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to
  • Step by Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

Your hair part is the line that separates your hair on either side of your head. A hair part can be created in the center, on the left side, or on the right side of your head, depending on your preference. However, choosing the right hair part can make a big difference in how your hair looks and fits your face.

How to

The first step in choosing the right hair part is to determine your face shape. There are five basic face shapes: oval, round, square, heart, and diamond.

Once you've identified your face shape, you can determine which side your hair should be parted on to flatter your features the most. Here are a few guidelines to keep in mind:

Oval Face

If you have an oval face, you're in luck because you can pull off any hair part. Experiment with different parts to find the one that you like the most.

Round Face

If you have a round face, a deep side part is your best bet. A side part will help elongate your face and create the illusion of angles.

Square Face

If you have a square face, a side part is also your best option. However, instead of a deep side part, try a slightly off-center part. This will help soften your features and create a more feminine look.

Heart Face

If you have a heart-shaped face, a side part is also a good choice. Try a deep side part to balance out the width of your forehead.

Diamond Face

If you have a diamond face, a middle part is the most flattering option. This will help balance out your features and create a more symmetrical look.

Step by Step

Now that you know which side your hair should be parted on, here's how to create the perfect hair part:

  1. Start by washing and conditioning your hair.
  2. Towel-dry your hair until it's damp.
  3. Using a comb, create a straight line from the crown of your head to the back of your neck.
  4. Section off the hair on one side of the line and clip it away.
  5. Use the comb to create a part on the other side of the line.
  6. Use your fingers to smooth down any flyaways or bumps.
  7. Repeat on the other side of your head.
  8. Finish by styling your hair as desired.

Tips

Here are a few tips to keep in mind when creating your hair part:

  • Experiment with different parts to find the one that you like the most.
  • Use a tail comb to create a precise part.
  • Don't be afraid to switch up your part from time to time.
  • Use a texturizing spray or dry shampoo to add volume to your hair.

FAQ

What side should a girl's hair part for a round face?

For a round face, a deep side part is the most flattering option.

What side should a girl's hair part for a square face?

For a square face, a slightly off-center side part is the most flattering option.

What side should a girl's hair part for a heart-shaped face?

For a heart-shaped face, a deep side part is the most flattering option.

Hair Care

Proper hair care is essential for maintaining healthy, beautiful hair. Here are a few tips to keep in mind:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too frequently.
  • Use a heat protectant spray before using heat styling tools.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.
  • Use a leave-in conditioner to keep your hair moisturized.

In conclusion, choosing the right hair part is essential for creating the perfect hairstyle. Whether you have an oval, round, square, heart, or diamond face, there's a hair part that will flatter your features the most. Experiment with different parts and don't be afraid to switch it up from time to time. By following these tips and taking proper care of your hair, you'll be able to create beautiful hairstyles that enhance your natural beauty.
