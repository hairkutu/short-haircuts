Short Hair Styles For Round Faces Over 50 A Guide

Written by Daniel Sep 03, 2023 Â· 10 min read

As we age, our hair may start to thin or lose its luster, making it difficult to style. However, that doesn't mean we can't rock a fabulous hairdo! Short hair styles for round faces over 50 can be a flattering and low-maintenance option that can give you a fresh new look. In this guide, we'll provide tips and tricks on how to choose the perfect short hair style for your round face, as well as how to take care of your hair to keep it looking its best.

How to Choose the Perfect Short Hair Style for Your Round Face

Choosing the right short hair style for your round face is all about balance. You want to create the illusion of length and height to offset the roundness of your face. Here are some tips:

1. Consider Your Hair Texture

The texture of your hair can play a big role in determining which short hair style will work best for you. For example, if you have fine hair, a layered bob or pixie cut can add volume and texture. If you have thick, curly hair, a short, layered cut can help tame your curls and create a more manageable style.

2. Look for Length in the Crown

Adding length to the crown of your hair can help elongate your face. Try a short, layered cut with longer layers on top, or a pixie cut with a longer fringe.

3. Consider a Side-Swept Fringe

A side-swept fringe can help break up the roundness of your face and draw attention to your eyes. Try a short bob or pixie cut with a side-swept fringe.

4. Don't Be Afraid of Layers

Layers can add texture and movement to your hair, which can be especially helpful if you have fine or thinning hair. A layered bob or pixie cut can create the illusion of volume and thickness.

Step-by-Step Guide to Styling Short Hair for Round Faces Over 50

Once you've chosen the perfect short hair style for your round face, it's important to know how to style it to keep it looking its best. Here's a step-by-step guide:

1. Start with Clean, Dry Hair

Wash and dry your hair before styling. If you have thin or fine hair, use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to add texture and body.

2. Apply Heat Protectant

Before using any heat styling tools, apply a heat protectant spray or serum to your hair to prevent damage.

3. Use a Round Brush

Use a round brush to create volume and shape. Wrap small sections of hair around the brush and blow-dry, pulling the brush away from your face to create lift and height.

4. Add Texture

If you have fine or thin hair, use a texturizing spray or dry shampoo to add texture and body. For thicker hair, use a styling cream or pomade to add definition and control.

5. Finish with Hairspray

Finish with a light hold hairspray to keep your style in place throughout the day.

Tips for Maintaining Short Hair Styles for Round Faces Over 50

Short hair styles for round faces over 50 can be low-maintenance, but they still require some upkeep to keep them looking their best. Here are some tips:

1. Trim Regularly

Regular trims can help keep your short hair style looking fresh and healthy. Aim to get a trim every 6-8 weeks.

2. Use a Weekly Hair Mask

A weekly hair mask can help nourish and hydrate your hair, keeping it soft and shiny. Look for a mask that is formulated for your hair type.

3. Protect Your Hair from Heat

Use a heat protectant spray or serum before using any heat styling tools to prevent damage to your hair.

4. Embrace Your Natural Texture

Short hair styles for round faces over 50 can look great with natural texture. Try air-drying your hair and using a texturizing spray or sea salt spray to enhance your natural waves or curls.

Frequently Asked Questions

What short hair styles are best for round faces over 50?

Short hair styles that add height and length to the crown of your head are generally the most flattering for round faces over 50. Consider a short, layered bob or pixie cut with a side-swept fringe.

What should I look for in a hair stylist?

Look for a hair stylist who has experience working with mature clients and can recommend styles that are both flattering and low-maintenance. Don't be afraid to ask for references or to see photos of their work.

How often should I wash my short hair?

How often you wash your hair depends on your hair type and lifestyle. If you have oily hair, you may need to wash it more frequently. If you have dry hair, you may be able to go longer between washes. Aim to wash your hair every 2-3 days.

Hair Care for Short Hair Styles for Round Faces Over 50

Keeping your hair healthy and nourished is key to maintaining a beautiful short hair style. Here are some tips for hair care:

1. Use a Gentle Shampoo

Choose a shampoo that is gentle and formulated for your hair type. Avoid shampoos that contain harsh sulfates or detergents.

2. Condition Regularly

Condition your hair regularly to keep it hydrated and soft. If you have fine hair, use a lightweight conditioner. If you have thick, curly hair, use a hydrating conditioner or mask.

3. Protect Your Hair from the Sun

UV rays can damage your hair, causing dryness and breakage. Wear a hat or scarf to protect your hair from the sun, or use a styling product with UV protection.

4. Avoid Heat Styling Too Often

Heat styling can damage your hair over time, causing dryness and breakage. Try to limit your use of heat styling tools and use a heat protectant spray or serum when you do need to use them.

Short hair styles for round faces over 50 can be a great way to update your look and feel confident and stylish. By choosing the right style for your face shape and taking care of your hair, you can rock a fabulous short hair style at any age.
