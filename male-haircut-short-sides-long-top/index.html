Male Haircut Short Sides Long Top A Guide To Achieving The Perfect Look

Written by William Jul 26, 2023 Â· 6 min read

If you're looking for a versatile haircut that can work for various occasions, a male haircut short sides long top is your best bet. This type of hairstyle has been popular for a while now, and it's easy to see why. It is sleek and stylish, perfect for both casual and formal events. In this guide, we'll take you through everything you need to know about getting the perfect male haircut short sides long top.

Description

A male haircut short sides long top is a hairstyle that features short hair on the sides and back of the head, with longer hair on the top. The length of the hair on the top can vary, depending on your preference. This hairstyle is versatile and can be styled in many ways, making it perfect for any occasion.

How to

Getting a male haircut short sides long top is easy, and you can do it at home or visit a barbershop. Here are the steps to follow:

Step 1: Prepare your hair

Before you start cutting your hair, wash it thoroughly and dry it with a towel. You want your hair to be clean and free of any products.

Step 2: Cut the sides and back

Using clippers, trim the sides and back of your hair. Start with the longest guard and gradually work your way down until you achieve your desired length. Keep the hairline near your ears and neck clean and tidy.

Step 3: Cut the top

Using scissors, cut the hair on the top of your head. You can either cut it straight across or add layers for depth and texture. Make sure that the hair on the top is longer than the hair on the sides and back.

Step 4: Style your hair

Once you've cut your hair, you can style it in various ways. You can slick it back, add volume, or create a messy look.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you achieve the perfect male haircut short sides long top:

- Bring a picture of the style you want to your barber for reference. - Use a good quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy. - Experiment with different styles to find what works best for you.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about male haircut short sides long top:

What face shapes suit this hairstyle?

Male haircut short sides long top suits most face shapes, but it looks particularly good on square and oval-shaped faces.

How often do I have to cut my hair?

The frequency of your haircuts will depend on how fast your hair grows. On average, you should get your hair cut every four to six weeks to maintain the style.

Can I style this haircut differently?

Yes, you can style this haircut in many ways. You can slick it back, add volume, or create a messy look. Experiment to find what works best for you.

Hair Care

To keep your hair healthy, use a good quality shampoo and conditioner. Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as this can damage it. Use styling products sparingly, and make sure to wash them out thoroughly.

A male haircut short sides long top is a versatile hairstyle that can work for various occasions. Follow the steps above to achieve the perfect look, and don't forget to take care of your hair. With a little effort, you can rock this style with confidence.
