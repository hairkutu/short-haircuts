Description

Shoulder length curly hairstyles are perfect for those with long faces as they add volume and width to the face, balancing out the length. Curly hair is versatile and can be styled in different ways, from loose waves to tight curls. This length is also easy to maintain and can be styled in various ways for different occasions.

How to Style Shoulder Length Curly Hair for Long Faces

Styling shoulder length curly hair for long faces can seem daunting, but with the right techniques and tools, it can be easy and effortless. Here are some tips:

1. Use a curl-enhancing shampoo and conditioner

Curly hair tends to be dry and can lose its shape if not properly moisturized. Use a shampoo and conditioner that enhances curls and adds moisture to the hair.

2. Apply a curl-defining product

To define curls and reduce frizz, apply a curl-defining product such as a curl cream or gel. Apply the product evenly throughout the hair, scrunching the curls as you go.

3. Diffuse your hair

Drying your hair with a diffuser can help enhance curls and reduce frizz. Hold the diffuser at the ends of your hair and lift it towards your scalp, scrunching as you go.

4. Use a curling iron

If you want tighter curls, use a curling iron to define your curls. Wrap small sections of hair around the curling iron and hold for a few seconds, then release.

Step by Step Guide

Here's a step by step guide on how to style shoulder length curly hair for long faces:

1. Wash and condition your hair

Use a curl-enhancing shampoo and conditioner to moisturize your hair and enhance your curls.

2. Apply a curl-defining product

Apply a curl-defining product such as a curl cream or gel to define your curls and reduce frizz.

3. Diffuse your hair

Dry your hair with a diffuser to enhance your curls and reduce frizz. Hold the diffuser at the ends of your hair and lift it towards your scalp, scrunching as you go.

4. Use a curling iron

If you want tighter curls, use a curling iron to define your curls. Wrap small sections of hair around the curling iron and hold for a few seconds, then release.

Tips for Maintaining Shoulder Length Curly Hair

Here are some tips for maintaining shoulder length curly hair:

1. Use a wide-toothed comb

Use a wide-toothed comb to detangle your curls, starting from the ends and working your way up.

2. Don't over-wash your hair

Washing your hair too often can strip it of its natural oils, making it dry and frizzy. Aim to wash your hair every 2-3 days.

3. Sleep with a silk pillowcase

Sleeping with a silk pillowcase can help reduce frizz and prevent breakage.

4. Trim your hair regularly

Trimming your hair regularly can help prevent split ends and keep your hair healthy.

FAQs

1. Can I still straighten my hair if I have shoulder length curly hair?

Yes, you can still straighten your hair if you have shoulder length curly hair. Just make sure to use a heat protectant before using any heat styling tools.

2. How often should I deep condition my hair?

It depends on your hair type and how often you wash your hair. If you have dry hair, aim to deep condition once a week. If you have oily hair, you can deep condition once every 2-3 weeks.

3. Can I dye my hair if I have shoulder length curly hair?

Yes, you can dye your hair if you have shoulder length curly hair. Just make sure to use a color-safe shampoo and conditioner to prevent the color from fading.

Hair Care for Shoulder Length Curly Hair

Here are some tips for taking care of your shoulder length curly hair:

1. Use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner

Curly hair tends to be dry, so use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair hydrated.

2. Deep condition regularly

Deep conditioning can help restore moisture to your hair and keep it healthy. Aim to deep condition once a week.

3. Don't over-wash your hair

Washing your hair too often can strip it of its natural oils, making it dry and frizzy. Aim to wash your hair every 2-3 days.

4. Protect your hair from heat

Heat can damage your hair, so use a heat protectant before using any heat styling tools.

5. Trim your hair regularly

Trimming your hair regularly can help prevent split ends and keep your hair healthy.

In conclusion, shoulder length curly hairstyles are perfect for those with long faces as they add volume and width to the face. With the right techniques and tools, styling shoulder length curly hair can be easy and effortless. Use a curl-enhancing shampoo and conditioner, apply a curl-defining product, diffuse your hair, and use a curling iron if you want tighter curls. Remember to maintain your hair by using a wide-toothed comb, not over-washing your hair, sleeping with a silk pillowcase, and trimming your hair regularly.
