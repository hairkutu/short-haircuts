Mid Long Hairstyles For Boys Tips Tricks And Ideas

Written by Robby Jul 10, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Are you a mom or dad looking for the perfect mid long hairstyle for your little boy? Look no further because we've got you covered! In this article, we will be discussing everything you need to know about mid long hairstyles for boys. From how to style it to hair care tips, and frequently asked questions, this article has it all. So let's dive in!

What is a Mid Long Hairstyle for Boys?

A mid long hairstyle for boys is a haircut that falls between the chin and shoulders. It's not too long and not too short, making it the perfect length for boys who want something in-between. This hairstyle is versatile, trendy, and can be styled in various ways.

How to Style a Mid Long Hairstyle for Boys

There are several ways to style a mid long hairstyle for boys, including:

  • Side-swept bangs
  • Messy bedhead look
  • Slicked back
  • Half up-half down
  • Braids

To achieve any of these styles, you'll need the right tools, such as a good quality hairbrush, styling gel, and hair ties.

Step-by-Step Guide to Styling a Mid Long Hairstyle for Boys

Here is a step-by-step guide to styling a mid long hairstyle for boys:

  1. Start by washing your boy's hair with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  2. Towel dry the hair, leaving it slightly damp.
  3. Apply a small amount of styling gel to the hair, starting at the roots and working your way down to the tips.
  4. Use a hairbrush to style the hair, creating the desired look.
  5. Finish off with a light hairspray to hold the style in place.

Remember, it's important not to use too much styling product, as this can make the hair look greasy and unkempt.

Tips for Maintaining a Mid Long Hairstyle for Boys

To keep your boy's mid long hairstyle looking its best, follow these tips:

  • Regularly trim the ends to prevent split ends and maintain the shape of the haircut.
  • Use a good quality shampoo and conditioner to keep the hair healthy and hydrated.
  • Avoid using too much heat styling tools, such as hair dryers and straighteners, as this can damage the hair.
  • Brush the hair regularly to prevent tangles and knots.

Frequently Asked Questions

Here are some frequently asked questions about mid long hairstyles for boys:

Q: Can mid long hairstyles work for all hair types?

A: Yes, mid long hairstyles can work for all hair types, whether it's straight, curly, or wavy.

Q: How often should I trim my boy's mid long hairstyle?

A: It's recommended to trim the ends every six to eight weeks to maintain the shape of the haircut.

Q: Can mid long hairstyles be worn to school?

A: Yes, mid long hairstyles are appropriate for school, as long as they are well-maintained and not too extreme.

Hair Care for Mid Long Hairstyles for Boys

Maintaining a mid long hairstyle for boys requires proper hair care. That includes washing the hair with a gentle shampoo and conditioner, using a good quality styling gel, and regularly trimming the ends to prevent split ends.

In conclusion, mid long hairstyles for boys are versatile, trendy, and easy to style. By following the tips and tricks outlined in this article, you can keep your boy's hair looking its best.
