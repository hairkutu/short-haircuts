How To See If A Different Hair Colour Suits You

Written by Aprilia Apr 04, 2023 · 8 min read

Are you bored with your current hair colour and want to try something new? Changing your hair colour can be a great way to refresh your look and give you a confidence boost. However, it can be difficult to know if a different hair colour will suit you or not. In this article, we’ll show you how to see if a different hair colour suits you and provide you with some tips and tricks to make the process easier.

Table of Contents

  • How to See if a Different Hair Colour Suits You
  • Step by Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

How to See if a Different Hair Colour Suits You

The best way to see if a different hair colour suits you is to try it out. However, you don’t have to dye your hair to see if a new colour will work for you. There are a few things you can do to get an idea of how a different hair colour will look on you.

1. Use a Virtual Makeover Tool

There are many websites and apps that offer virtual makeover tools. These tools allow you to upload a photo of yourself and try on different hairstyles and colours. This can be a great way to see if a different hair colour suits you without having to commit to dyeing your hair.

2. Try a Temporary Hair Colour

If you’re not ready to commit to a permanent hair colour, try a temporary one first. Temporary hair dyes can give you an idea of how a different hair colour will look on you without the long-term commitment. You can find temporary hair dyes at most drugstores or beauty supply stores.

3. Wear a Wig

Wearing a wig is another way to see if a different hair colour suits you. You can find wigs in a variety of colours and styles, allowing you to try on different looks without having to dye your hair.

Step by Step

If you’re ready to dye your hair and want to see if a different hair colour suits you, follow these steps:

1. Do Your Research

Before you dye your hair, do some research on the different hair colours that would suit your skin tone and eye colour. You can find a lot of information online or consult with a hair stylist.

2. Choose the Right Shade

Once you’ve done your research, choose a shade that you think will suit you. Consider your skin tone, eye colour, and natural hair colour when choosing a shade.

3. Do a Strand Test

Before dyeing your entire head of hair, do a strand test to see how the colour will look on your hair. Follow the instructions on the dye package to do the strand test.

4. Dye Your Hair

If you’re happy with the results of the strand test, dye your hair according to the instructions on the package. If you’re unsure about dyeing your hair yourself, consult with a hair stylist.

Tips

Here are a few tips to help you see if a different hair colour suits you:

  • Start with a subtle change. If you’re not used to dyeing your hair, start with a subtle change to see how you like it.
  • Consult with a hair stylist. A hair stylist can give you advice on what hair colours will suit you best.
  • Consider your lifestyle. If you have a busy lifestyle, choose a hair colour that is easy to maintain.

FAQ

How long does it take for hair dye to fade?

It depends on the type of hair dye you use and how often you wash your hair. Generally, permanent hair dyes can last for several weeks to months, while temporary hair dyes can last for a few days to a week.

Can I dye my hair if I have sensitive skin?

If you have sensitive skin, it’s important to do a patch test before dyeing your hair. Follow the instructions on the dye package to do the patch test.

Hair Care

After dyeing your hair, it’s important to take good care of it to maintain the colour and keep your hair healthy. Here are a few tips:

  • Use a shampoo and conditioner formulated for coloured hair.
  • Avoid using hot tools on your hair too often.
  • Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a hair product with SPF.

By following these tips and tricks, you can see if a different hair colour suits you and give your look a fun and fresh update.
