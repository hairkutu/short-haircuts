How Long Are You Supposed To Keep Braids In Your Hair

Written by Daniel May 02, 2023 Â· 6 min read

When it comes to braids, many people wonder how long they can keep them in without causing damage to their hair. In this article, we will be discussing how long you are supposed to keep braids in your hair and some tips on maintaining them.

Table of Contents

  • How To Keep Braids in Your Hair
  • Step-by-step Guide on Braiding Your Hair
  • Tips for Maintaining Braids
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care After Braiding

How To Keep Braids in Your Hair

It is recommended that you keep braids in your hair for no longer than 8 weeks. After this time, you should take them out and give your hair a break for a few weeks before braiding again. If you keep braids in your hair for too long, it can cause damage to your hair and scalp.

When getting braids, make sure that your hair is clean and free of any knots or tangles. This will make the braiding process easier and less painful. It is also important to moisturize your hair and scalp before getting braids to prevent dryness and breakage.

Step-by-step Guide on Braiding Your Hair

If you are braiding your own hair, here is a step-by-step guide to help you:

  1. Start by sectioning your hair into small parts using a comb.
  2. Take one section and divide it into three equal parts.
  3. Cross the right section over the middle section, then cross the left section over the middle section.
  4. Add more hair to the right section, then cross it over the middle section.
  5. Add more hair to the left section, then cross it over the middle section.
  6. Repeat steps 4 and 5 until you reach the end of the section.
  7. Secure the braid with a hair tie.
  8. Repeat on the remaining sections of your hair.

Tips for Maintaining Braids

Here are some tips to help you maintain your braids:

  • Keep your scalp moisturized by using a leave-in conditioner or oil.
  • Avoid pulling or tugging on your braids as this can cause damage to your hair.
  • Wash your braids every 2-3 weeks to prevent buildup and keep your hair and scalp clean.
  • Avoid using heavy hair products as they can weigh down your braids and cause them to unravel.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about keeping braids in your hair:

Q: Can I keep braids in my hair for longer than 8 weeks?
A: It is not recommended to keep braids in your hair for longer than 8 weeks as it can cause damage to your hair and scalp. Q: How often should I wash my braids?
A: It is recommended to wash your braids every 2-3 weeks to prevent buildup and keep your hair and scalp clean.

Hair Care After Braiding

After taking out your braids, it is important to give your hair a break for a few weeks before braiding again. This will allow your hair to rest and recover from the tension of the braids. You should also deep condition your hair to restore moisture and prevent breakage.

In conclusion, it is recommended to keep braids in your hair for no longer than 8 weeks. Make sure to keep your hair and scalp moisturized and avoid using heavy hair products. With proper care and maintenance, braids can be a great protective style for your hair.
