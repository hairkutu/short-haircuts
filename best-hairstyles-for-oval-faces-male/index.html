Best Hairstyles For Oval Faces Male A Complete Guide

Written by William Aug 15, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Are you an oval-faced male looking for the perfect hairstyle? Look no further! In this guide, we'll be discussing the best hairstyles for oval faces male, including how to achieve them and tips for maintaining your new look. Let's get started!

Description

An oval face shape is considered to be the most versatile when it comes to hairstyles. With a balanced forehead, cheekbones, and jawline, almost any hairstyle can look good on an oval-faced male. However, there are certain hairstyles that can accentuate your features and make you look your best.

How to

When choosing a hairstyle for an oval face, it's important to consider your hair type and texture. Some hairstyles may work better for thick hair, while others may be better suited for thin hair. Here are some of the best hairstyles for oval faces male:

1. Short Buzz Cut

The short buzz cut is a classic hairstyle that looks great on oval-faced men. It's easy to maintain and requires little to no styling. Simply use clippers to trim your hair to a short length all around.

2. Side Part

The side part is a timeless hairstyle that adds a touch of sophistication to any look. To achieve this style, use a comb to create a deep side part and then sweep your hair to the side.

3. Quiff

The quiff is a popular hairstyle that works well for oval-faced men with thick hair. To achieve this style, use a hairdryer and a round brush to create volume at the crown of your head. Then, use a styling product to hold the hair in place.

4. Slicked Back

The slicked-back hairstyle is a classic look that never goes out of style. To achieve this look, use a styling product to slick your hair back and away from your face.

5. Messy Textured Hair

If you have wavy or curly hair, a messy textured hairstyle can look great on an oval face. To achieve this style, use a texturizing product to create volume and texture in your hair.

Step by Step

Here's a step-by-step guide on how to achieve the side part hairstyle:

Step 1:

Start with clean, dry hair.

Step 2:

Use a comb to create a deep side part on one side of your head.

Step 3:

Using a brush or your fingers, sweep the hair on the larger side of the part over to the other side of your head.

Step 4:

Apply a small amount of styling product to hold the hair in place.

Tips

Here are some tips for maintaining your new hairstyle:

1. Keep your hair clean:

Wash your hair regularly to keep it looking fresh and healthy.

2. Use quality styling products:

Invest in quality styling products that won't damage your hair or scalp.

3. Visit a barber regularly:

Visit a barber every 4-6 weeks to keep your hair looking neat and tidy.

FAQ

Q: Can oval-faced men pull off long hair?

A: Yes, oval-faced men can pull off long hair, but it's important to keep it well-maintained and styled.

Q: What if I have thinning hair?

A: If you have thinning hair, opt for a shorter hairstyle that will make your hair look thicker.

Q: Can oval-faced men wear a fringe?

A: Yes, oval-faced men can wear a fringe, but it's important to choose a style that complements your face shape.

Hair Care

Proper hair care is essential for maintaining healthy, great-looking hair. Here are some tips for keeping your hair in top condition:

1. Wash your hair regularly:

Wash your hair every 2-3 days with a gentle, sulfate-free shampoo.

2. Use a conditioner:

Use a conditioner after shampooing to keep your hair hydrated and nourished.

3. Protect your hair from heat:

Avoid using hot tools like straighteners and curling irons, as they can damage your hair.

In conclusion, the best hairstyles for oval faces male are those that complement your face shape and hair type. Whether you opt for a short buzz cut or a messy textured hairstyle, there's a style out there that will make you look your best. Follow these tips and take care of your hair to keep it looking healthy and great!
