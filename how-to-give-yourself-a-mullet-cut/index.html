How To Give Yourself A Mullet Cut A Step By Step Guide

Written by William Aug 05, 2023 Â· 6 min read

In the world of fashion and hairstyling, mullet cuts have made a comeback. This iconic hairstyle, made famous in the 80s, is now back in trend. If you're looking for a bold and edgy hairstyle, a mullet cut may just be what you need. In this article, we'll show you how to give yourself a mullet cut that will make heads turn. So, let's get started!

How to Give Yourself a Mullet Cut

Before we begin, ensure you have the following tools:

  • Scissors
  • Comb
  • Clippers
  • Hair ties
  • Hair spray

Step 1: Wash and Dry Your Hair

Start by washing and drying your hair. This will help to remove any dirt and oil buildup. Once your hair is clean and dry, brush it to remove any tangles.

Step 2: Create a Guide

Take a comb and create a guide for your cut. Start by parting your hair down the middle, from your forehead to the nape of your neck. Use the comb to create a straight line from the parting to the back of your head. This will serve as your guide for the cut.

Step 3: Cut the Top Section

Take your scissors and cut the top section of your hair using the guide you created in step 2. Cut your hair to the desired length, ensuring that it's longer at the front than at the back. Use hair ties to secure the sides and back of your hair to keep them out of the way.

Step 4: Cut the Sides and Back

Using your clippers, start cutting the sides and back of your hair. Begin from the bottom and work your way up. Ensure that you're cutting your hair in a straight line, following the guide you created earlier. Cut your hair to the desired length, ensuring that it's shorter at the back than at the front.

Step 5: Blend the Hair

Once you've cut the sides and back, use your scissors to blend the hair. This will help to create a seamless transition between the top section and the sides and back.

Step 6: Create Layers

If you want to add some texture to your hair, create layers by cutting the top section of your hair into different lengths. This will help to create a more dynamic and edgy look.

Step 7: Style Your Hair

Once you're done cutting your hair, style it according to your preference. You can use hair spray to keep your hair in place.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you achieve the perfect mullet cut:

  • Use sharp scissors and clippers to avoid uneven cuts.
  • Start with a longer length and gradually cut more to achieve your desired look.
  • Don't be afraid to experiment with different lengths and textures.

FAQ

1. Is it possible to give yourself a mullet cut?

Yes, it's possible to give yourself a mullet cut. However, it's recommended to seek the help of a professional hairstylist if you're unsure.

2. What face shapes suit a mullet cut?

Mullet cuts can suit various face shapes. However, they work best for oval, round, and heart-shaped faces.

3. How often should I trim my mullet cut?

It's recommended to trim your mullet cut every four to six weeks to maintain its shape and style.

Hair Care

To keep your mullet cut looking great, ensure you follow a hair care routine that includes washing, conditioning, and styling. Use hair products that are suitable for your hair type and avoid excessive heat styling. Additionally, protect your hair from the sun and other environmental factors that can damage it.

With this step-by-step guide, you can give yourself a mullet cut with ease. Remember to take your time and be patient, and you'll have a stylish and edgy hairstyle in no time. Happy styling!
