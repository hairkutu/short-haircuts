What Style Bob For Round Face The Ultimate Guide

Written by Oliver May 01, 2023 Â· 8 min read

Are you looking for a new hairstyle that flatters your round face? Look no further than the bob! This versatile cut can be tailored to suit any face shape, but there are a few key considerations to keep in mind when choosing the perfect bob for a round face. In this article, we'll cover everything you need to know to find the perfect bob for your face shape.

What to Consider

Before you choose a bob style, it's important to consider a few key factors. First and foremost, you'll want to think about the length of your hair. A bob can be short, medium, or long, and the length you choose will depend on your personal preference and the shape of your face. For a round face, a shorter bob that hits just above the chin is generally most flattering.

Another important factor to consider is the shape of your bob. A round face can benefit from a bob with angled or asymmetrical lines, as these can help to elongate the face and create a more balanced look. A layered bob can also work well, as it can add volume and movement to the hair.

How to Choose the Right Bob

Now that you know what to consider, it's time to choose the perfect bob for your round face. Here are some tips to help you make the right choice:

Consider Your Hair Texture

The texture of your hair can also play a role in choosing the right bob. If you have fine hair, a layered bob can add volume and create the illusion of more fullness. If you have thick hair, a blunt bob can help to control and tame your locks.

Look at Celebrity Inspiration

One great way to get inspiration for your new bob is to look at celebrities with round faces who have rocked the style. Some great examples include Emma Stone, Mila Kunis, and Selena Gomez.

Consult with a Stylist

If you're still unsure about which bob style is right for you, it's always a good idea to consult with a hairstylist. They can offer personalized advice based on your face shape, hair texture, and personal style.

Step-by-Step Guide to Styling Your Bob

Once you've chosen the perfect bob for your round face, it's important to know how to style it properly. Here's a step-by-step guide to styling your bob:

Step 1: Start with Clean, Dry Hair

Before you style your bob, make sure your hair is clean and dry. This will help your styling products to work more effectively.

Step 2: Apply a Heat Protectant

Next, apply a heat protectant to your hair to prevent damage from heat styling tools.

Step 3: Use a Round Brush

When blow-drying your bob, use a round brush to create volume and movement.

Step 4: Add Texture

To add texture to your bob, use a texturizing spray or pomade.

Tips for Maintaining Your Bob

Now that you know how to style your bob, it's important to know how to maintain it. Here are some tips for keeping your bob looking great:

Get Regular Trims

To keep your bob looking fresh and healthy, make sure to get regular trims every six to eight weeks.

Use a Silk Pillowcase

Using a silk pillowcase can help to prevent hair breakage and keep your bob looking smooth and shiny.

Protect Your Hair from Heat Damage

When using heat styling tools, always use a heat protectant and avoid using high heat settings.

FAQ

What length bob is best for a round face?

For a round face, a shorter bob that hits just above the chin is generally most flattering.

Can a round face pull off a bob?

A bob can be tailored to suit any face shape, including a round face. The key is to choose the right length and shape for your face.

What styling products are best for a bob?

Texturizing spray, pomade, and heat protectant are all great styling products for a bob.

Final Thoughts

Choosing the perfect bob for your round face can be a daunting task, but with these tips and tricks, you'll be well on your way to finding the perfect style. Whether you opt for a layered bob or an asymmetrical cut, remember that the most important thing is to choose a style that makes you feel confident and beautiful.
