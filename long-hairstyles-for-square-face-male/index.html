Long Hairstyles For Square Face Male A Guide To The Best Styles

If you have a square face shape, you may be wondering what the best long hairstyles are for you. A square face has a prominent jawline and forehead, so it's important to choose a hairstyle that complements these features. In this guide, we'll take a look at some of the best long hairstyles for square face males and provide tips on how to style and care for your hair.

Table of Contents

  • How to Choose the Right Long Hairstyle for Your Square Face
  • Step-by-Step Guide to Styling Long Hair for Square Face Males

How to Choose the Right Long Hairstyle for Your Square Face

When it comes to choosing a long hairstyle for a square face, there are a few things to keep in mind:

  • Choose a hairstyle that adds volume to the top of your head to balance out your jawline.
  • Avoid hairstyles that are too short on the sides, as they can make your face look even squarer.
  • Consider adding layers to your hair to soften your features.
  • Choose a hairstyle that complements your personal style and personality.

Some of the best long hairstyles for square face males include:

Long Wavy Hair

Long, wavy hair can add volume to the top of your head and soften your jawline. This hairstyle works well for those with thick hair.

Layered Hair

Adding layers to your hair can create movement and soften your features. This style works well for those with thinner hair.

Man Bun

A man bun can be a stylish and practical hairstyle for long hair. It works well for those with thick hair and can be styled in a variety of ways.

Step-by-Step Guide to Styling Long Hair for Square Face Males

Styling long hair can be a challenge, but with a few simple steps, you can achieve a great look:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair.
  2. Apply a styling product, such as mousse or gel, to add texture and hold.
  3. Blow dry your hair, using a round brush to add volume.
  4. Use a curling iron or flat iron to add waves or straighten your hair, depending on your desired style.
  5. Finish with a styling product, such as hairspray or pomade, to hold your style in place.

Tips for Maintaining Long Hair for Square Face Males

Maintaining long hair can be a challenge, but with the right tips and tricks, you can keep your hair looking great:

  • Wash your hair regularly to keep it clean and healthy.
  • Use a deep conditioner once a week to keep your hair moisturized.
  • Avoid using hot tools, such as a curling iron or flat iron, too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends and keep your hair looking healthy.

FAQs about Long Hairstyles for Square Face Males

What is the best long hairstyle for a square face male?

The best long hairstyle for a square face male is one that adds volume to the top of your head and softens your features. Consider hairstyles like long wavy hair, layered hair, or a man bun.

How do I style long hair for a square face male?

To style long hair for a square face male, start with clean, dry hair and apply a styling product. Blow dry your hair, using a round brush to add volume, and then use a curling iron or flat iron to style your hair. Finish with a styling product to hold your style in place.

How do I maintain long hair for a square face male?

To maintain long hair for a square face male, wash your hair regularly, use a deep conditioner once a week, avoid using hot tools too often, and get regular trims to prevent split ends.

Hair Care Tips for Square Face Males

In addition to styling and maintaining your hair, it's important to take care of it to keep it healthy and strong:

  • Eat a balanced diet that includes plenty of vitamins and nutrients.
  • Drink plenty of water to keep your hair hydrated.
  • Avoid smoking and excessive alcohol consumption, as they can damage your hair.
  • Avoid using harsh chemicals on your hair, such as bleach or hair dye.

With these tips and tricks, you can choose the best long hairstyle for your square face and keep your hair looking great.
