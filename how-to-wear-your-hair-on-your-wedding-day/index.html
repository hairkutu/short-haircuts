How To Wear Your Hair On Your Wedding Day Tips And Tricks

Written by Christine Aug 16, 2023 Â· 8 min read

Are you getting married soon and looking for some inspiration on how to wear your hair on your big day? Look no further! In this post, we'll share some tips and tricks on how to choose the perfect hairstyle that will complement your wedding dress and make you look stunning on your special day.

Table of Contents

  • How to Choose the Perfect Wedding Hairstyle
  • Step-by-Step Guide to Creating Your Wedding Hairdo

How to Choose the Perfect Wedding Hairstyle

Choosing the perfect wedding hairstyle can be overwhelming, but it doesn't have to be. Here are some tips to help you decide:

Consider Your Wedding Dress

Your wedding dress should be the main inspiration for your hairstyle. If you have a classic, elegant dress, consider an updo or a sleek bun to show off the neckline. If you have a bohemian or beachy dress, consider loose, romantic waves or braids.

Think About Your Venue

Your wedding venue can also influence your hairstyle. If you're getting married in a formal ballroom, a sophisticated updo can be a great choice. If you're having a beach wedding, loose waves or a messy bun can be a more relaxed option.

Consider Your Hair Type and Length

Your hair type and length can also influence your hairstyle. If you have thin or short hair, consider adding extensions or opting for a voluminous updo. If you have long, thick hair, consider wearing it down or in loose waves.

Step-by-Step Guide to Creating Your Wedding Hairdo

Now that you've decided on your wedding hairstyle, it's time to create it. Here's a step-by-step guide:

Step 1: Wash Your Hair

Start by washing your hair with a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner. This will help your hair look healthy and shiny.

Step 2: Blow Dry Your Hair

Blow dry your hair with a round brush to create volume and texture. If you have curly hair, use a diffuser to enhance your natural curls.

Step 3: Curl Your Hair

Using a curling iron or wand, curl your hair in small sections. Hold the curl for a few seconds before releasing it.

Step 4: Pin Your Hair

Once you've curled your hair, pin it up in the desired style. Use bobby pins and hair spray to secure your hair in place.

Tips and Tricks for Your Wedding Hair

Here are some additional tips and tricks to help you achieve the perfect wedding hairstyle:

Do a Hair Trial

Before your wedding day, schedule a hair trial with your stylist to ensure that you're happy with your hairstyle.

Use Hair Accessories

Hair accessories such as flowers, headbands, and hairpins can add a touch of elegance and glamour to your hairstyle.

Consider Updos

Updos are a classic and sophisticated choice for wedding hairstyles. They can also help keep your hair out of your face and off your neck.

FAQs on Wedding Hairstyles

What's the best way to prepare my hair before my wedding?

Start preparing your hair a few months before your wedding by getting regular trims and using moisturizing hair products. Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as they can damage your hair.

How long before the wedding should I get my hair done?

Plan to get your hair done a few hours before the wedding ceremony. This will give you enough time to get ready and take photos.

What should I do if it's raining on my wedding day?

If it's raining on your wedding day, consider wearing a protective hairstyle such as an updo or braids. You can also use hair spray to help keep your hair in place.

Hair Care Tips for the Bride-to-Be

Here are some additional hair care tips to help you prepare for your wedding day:

Avoid Heat Styling

Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as they can damage your hair. Instead, air dry your hair or use a diffuser to enhance your natural texture.

Use Hair Masks

Use a weekly hair mask to keep your hair healthy and moisturized. You can also try DIY hair masks using natural ingredients such as avocado, honey, and coconut oil.

Get Regular Trims

Get regular trims every 6-8 weeks to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.

With these tips and tricks, you're sure to find the perfect wedding hairstyle that will make you feel confident and beautiful on your special day. Happy wedding planning!
