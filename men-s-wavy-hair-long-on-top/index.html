Men s Wavy Hair Long On Top A Guide For Relaxed And Stylish Look

Written by Frank Jun 08, 2023 Â· 8 min read

Are you looking for a hairstyle that is both relaxed and stylish? Look no further than men's wavy hair long on top. This versatile hairstyle has been a popular choice for men in recent years, and for good reason. Whether you have naturally wavy hair or want to create waves with styling products, this look can work for you. Read on to discover how to achieve this look, step-by-step instructions, tips, frequently asked questions, and hair care advice.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to Achieve Men's Wavy Hair Long on Top
  • Step-by-Step Instructions
  • Tips for Styling Men's Wavy Hair Long on Top
  • Frequently Asked Questions
  • Hair Care Tips for Men's Wavy Hair Long on Top

Description

Men's wavy hair long on top is a hairstyle where the top of the hair is left longer and styled into waves or curls, while the sides and back are cut shorter. This look can be achieved with natural wavy hair or by using styling products like mousse, curl cream, or sea salt spray. This hairstyle is a great choice for those who want a relaxed, laid-back look that is still stylish and trendy. It is also versatile enough to work for any occasion, from casual to formal.

How to Achieve Men's Wavy Hair Long on Top

The first step in achieving men's wavy hair long on top is to have the right haircut. The top of the hair should be left longer, while the sides and back are cut shorter. It is important to communicate with your hairstylist to achieve the desired length and style. If you already have longer hair, you can skip this step.

The next step is to use styling products to create waves or curls. Mousse or curl cream can help to define natural waves or curls, while sea salt spray can create a beachy, textured look. Apply the product to damp hair and scrunch or twist the hair to create waves or curls. Use a diffuser attachment on a hair dryer to dry the hair without disrupting the waves or curls.

Step-by-Step Instructions

  1. Wash and condition your hair with products suitable for your hair type
  2. Towel dry your hair until damp
  3. Apply a styling product like mousse, curl cream, or sea salt spray to your hair
  4. Scrunch or twist your hair to create waves or curls
  5. Dry your hair with a diffuser attachment on a hair dryer
  6. Style your hair with your fingers or a comb to achieve the desired look
  7. Finish with a hairspray or hair oil for shine and hold

Tips for Styling Men's Wavy Hair Long on Top

  • Experiment with different styling products to find the one that works best for your hair type and desired look
  • Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to avoid disrupting the waves or curls while styling
  • Use a hair oil or serum to add shine and combat frizz
  • Visit your hairstylist regularly for trims to maintain the shape and style of your hair
  • Accessorize with a headband or hat for a trendy look

Frequently Asked Questions

Can men with straight hair achieve this look?

Yes, men with straight hair can achieve this look with the help of styling products like mousse, curl cream, or sea salt spray. Use a diffuser attachment on a hair dryer to create waves or curls.

Is this hairstyle suitable for all face shapes?

Yes, this hairstyle can work for all face shapes. However, it is important to communicate with your hairstylist to achieve the desired length and style that complements your face shape.

How often should I wash my hair?

It is recommended to wash your hair every two to three days to maintain the natural oils and prevent dryness.

Hair Care Tips for Men's Wavy Hair Long on Top

  • Use a shampoo and conditioner suitable for your hair type
  • Avoid using hot water when washing your hair to prevent dryness
  • Avoid using a hairbrush, which can disrupt the waves or curls
  • Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your hair
  • Avoid using heat styling tools like straighteners or curling irons, which can damage the hair
  • Apply a hair mask or deep conditioner once a week to nourish and hydrate your hair
  • Avoid using too much product, which can weigh down your hair and cause buildup

With these tips and tricks, you can achieve a relaxed and stylish look with men's wavy hair long on top. Whether you have natural waves or need to create them with styling products, this look can work for you. Don't be afraid to experiment with different products and styles to find the one that suits you best. With regular hair care and maintenance, you can keep your hair healthy and looking great.
