How To Make My Face Look Rounder With Makeup

Written by William Mar 16, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Do you have a square or angular face and want to make it look rounder? Don't worry, with the right makeup techniques, you can create the illusion of a rounder face. Here are some tips on how to make your face look rounder with makeup.

Description

Having a round face is considered to be a desirable trait in many cultures. A round face is usually associated with youthfulness and femininity. However, not everyone is blessed with a round face. If you have a square or angular face, there are makeup techniques you can use to create the illusion of a rounder face.

How to

The key to making your face look rounder with makeup is to create shadows and highlights. By using light and dark shades strategically, you can make your face appear rounder.

Step 1: Prep Your Skin

Before applying makeup, make sure your skin is clean and moisturized. Use a primer to create a smooth base for your makeup.

Step 2: Create Shadows

Use a contour powder or cream to create shadows on your face. Apply the contour product on the hollows of your cheeks, temples, and jawline to create the illusion of a rounder face. Blend the product well to avoid harsh lines.

Step 3: Add Highlights

Use a highlighter to add brightness to your face. Apply the highlighter on the high points of your face such as the cheekbones, brow bone, and the bridge of your nose. This will make your face appear rounder and more youthful.

Step 4: Use Blush

Blush is an essential makeup product to make your face look rounder. Apply blush on the apples of your cheeks and blend it towards your hairline. This will give your face a natural flush and make it appear rounder.

Tips

Here are some additional tips on how to make your face look rounder with makeup:

  • Use a lighter shade of foundation to make your face appear brighter and more youthful.
  • Use a matte finish foundation to avoid adding shine to your face, which can make it look flatter.
  • Use a beauty sponge to blend your makeup well and avoid harsh lines.
  • Avoid over-contouring, as it can make your face look too angular.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about how to make your face look rounder with makeup:

Can I use bronzer instead of contour powder?

Yes, you can use bronzer instead of contour powder. However, make sure the bronzer has a cool undertone to avoid making your face look too warm or orange.

What color blush should I use?

You can use any color blush that complements your skin tone. However, peach and pink shades are usually the most flattering for a rounder face.

Can I use a cream blush instead of powder blush?

Yes, you can use a cream blush instead of powder blush. However, make sure to blend it well to avoid a patchy finish.

Hair Care

Your hairstyle can also play a role in making your face look rounder. Here are some hair care tips:

  • Choose a hairstyle that has layers around your face to soften the angles.
  • Avoid hairstyles that are too severe or too long, as they can elongate your face.
  • Consider getting bangs to create the illusion of a rounder face.

By following these makeup and hair care tips, you can make your face look rounder and more youthful. Remember to experiment with different techniques and products to find what works best for you.
