How To Cut Your Own Hair Short Choppy Layers Youtube

Written by Eveline Mar 19, 2023 · 6 min read

Are you looking for a way to add some edge to your hairstyle? Short choppy layers can give you a trendy look without sacrificing too much length. And the best part? You can do it yourself! In this article, we’ll take you through the steps to cut your own hair short choppy layers using YouTube as your guide.

Description

Short choppy layers are a popular hairstyle that can give you a fresh and modern look. The style involves cutting the hair into different layers, with each layer being shorter than the one before it. This creates a textured and edgy look that is perfect for those who want a little bit of a change without going too extreme.

How to Cut Your Own Hair Short Choppy Layers YouTube

Before you start cutting your hair, make sure you have the right tools. You’ll need a pair of sharp scissors, a comb, and some hair clips. Once you have your tools ready, follow these steps:

Step 1: Wash and Dry Your Hair

Start by washing and drying your hair. This will help you see the natural texture of your hair and make it easier to cut. Make sure your hair is completely dry before you start cutting.

Step 2: Section Your Hair

Use a comb to divide your hair into sections. Start by parting your hair down the middle and then divide each side into two sections. Use hair clips to secure each section.

Step 3: Cut the Bottom Layer

Start by cutting the bottom layer of your hair. Use the comb to hold the hair in place and then cut the ends with the scissors. Make sure to cut each section evenly and to keep the length consistent.

Step 4: Cut the Middle Layer

Next, move on to the middle layer. Use the same technique as before, cutting each section evenly and keeping the length consistent.

Step 5: Cut the Top Layer

Finally, cut the top layer of your hair. This layer should be the shortest and create the most texture. Use the same technique as before, cutting each section evenly and keeping the length consistent.

Step 6: Blend the Layers

Use the scissors to blend the layers together. This will create a more natural look and help the layers flow seamlessly.

Step 7: Trim the Bangs

If you have bangs, trim them last. Use the scissors to cut them to the desired length.

Tips

  • Start with a little bit of hair and work your way up. You can always cut more, but you can’t put hair back once it’s cut.
  • Use sharp scissors to ensure a clean cut.
  • Take breaks if you need to. Cutting your own hair can be tiring and time-consuming.

FAQ

What if I make a mistake?

If you make a mistake, don’t panic. You can always trim it later or visit a professional stylist to fix it.

How often should I cut my hair?

It’s recommended to cut your hair every 6-8 weeks to keep it healthy and maintain the style.

Hair Care

After cutting your hair, make sure to take care of it properly. Use a good shampoo and conditioner, and avoid using too much heat on your hair. You can also use hair masks and oils to keep it healthy and shiny.

Cutting your own hair can be a fun and rewarding experience. By following these steps and using YouTube as your guide, you can achieve a trendy short choppy layers hairstyle in no time.
