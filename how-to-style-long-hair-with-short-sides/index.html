How To Style Long Hair With Short Sides A Guide For Men

Written by William May 09, 2023 Â· 8 min read

If you're a man with long hair and short sides, you might be wondering how to style your hair in a way that's both fashionable and practical. Fortunately, there are plenty of options available to you, whether you're looking for a sleek and polished look or something more casual and laid-back. In this guide, we'll walk you through the steps of how to style long hair with short sides, including tips, tricks, and FAQs.

Description

Long hair with short sides is a popular hairstyle for men that combines the best of both worlds: the length and versatility of long hair, and the clean, neat appearance of short hair on the sides. This look can be achieved in a variety of ways, from a classic undercut to a modern fade or taper.

How to Style Long Hair with Short Sides: Step by Step

Step 1: Decide on Your Haircut

The first step in styling long hair with short sides is to decide on your haircut. There are several options to choose from, including:

  • The undercut: This involves shaving the sides and back of your head, leaving the hair on top long.
  • The fade: This involves gradually tapering the hair on the sides and back of your head, creating a smooth, blended look.
  • The taper: This is similar to a fade, but involves a more gradual transition between the long hair on top and the short hair on the sides.

Once you've decided on your haircut, it's time to move on to styling.

Step 2: Wash Your Hair

Before you style your hair, it's important to wash it thoroughly. Use a high-quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair clean and healthy.

Step 3: Apply Product

Next, apply a styling product to your hair. Depending on your hair type and the look you're going for, you may want to use a pomade, wax, gel, or mousse. Work the product through your hair evenly, using your fingers or a comb to distribute it evenly.

Step 4: Style Your Hair

Now it's time to style your hair. There are several ways to do this, depending on the look you're going for:

  • Slick it back: Use a comb to slick your hair back, creating a polished, sophisticated look.
  • Messy and textured: Use your fingers to create a messy, textured look that's perfect for more casual occasions.
  • Half-up, half-down: Gather the hair on top of your head and tie it back into a half-up, half-down style, leaving the hair on the sides short and neat.

Step 5: Finish with Hairspray

Finally, finish your look with a light mist of hairspray to hold it in place. Be sure to choose a hairspray that won't leave your hair feeling stiff or crunchy.

Tips for Styling Long Hair with Short Sides

Here are a few tips to keep in mind when styling long hair with short sides:

  • Experiment with different products to find the one that works best for your hair type.
  • Don't be afraid to ask your stylist for advice on how to style your hair.
  • Invest in a high-quality comb or brush to help you achieve the look you want.
  • Consider getting regular trims to keep your hair looking neat and tidy.

FAQ About Styling Long Hair with Short Sides

Q: Is long hair with short sides a good look for men?

A: Yes! Long hair with short sides is a popular and stylish look for men of all ages.

Q: What products should I use to style my hair?

A: This depends on your hair type and the look you're going for. Pomade, wax, gel, and mousse are all great options.

Q: How often should I wash my hair?

A: This depends on your hair type and lifestyle. Generally, it's a good idea to wash your hair every other day or so to keep it clean and healthy.

Hair Care Tips for Men with Long Hair and Short Sides

Here are a few tips to help you keep your hair looking healthy and stylish:

  • Use a high-quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair clean and nourished.
  • Avoid using hot tools like hair dryers and straighteners too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Consider using a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair soft and moisturized.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair looking neat and tidy.

By following these tips and steps, you'll be able to style your long hair with short sides in a way that's both fashionable and practical. Don't be afraid to experiment with different products and styles until you find the perfect look for you!
