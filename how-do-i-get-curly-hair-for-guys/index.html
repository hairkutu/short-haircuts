How Do I Get Curly Hair For Guys A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Oliver Jun 19, 2023 · 10 min read

Are you tired of your straight hair and want to switch to a curly hairstyle? Well, you are not alone. Many guys are opting for curls these days, and it's not hard to see why. Curly hair can add a lot of volume and texture to your look, making it more interesting and appealing. But how do you get curly hair if you don't naturally have it? In this article, we will explore different methods on how to achieve curly hair for guys.

Table of Contents

  • How to Get Curly Hair for Guys
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Maintaining Curly Hair
  • Frequently Asked Questions
  • Hair Care for Curly Hair

How to Get Curly Hair for Guys

There are several ways to get curly hair for guys, and we will discuss them in detail below.

1. Perm

A perm is a chemical treatment that can give you permanent curls. The process involves applying a solution to your hair that breaks down the bonds in your hair shaft. The hair is then wrapped around rods and another solution is applied to fix the curls in place. Perms can last for several months, and the curls will gradually loosen over time.

2. Curling Iron

A curling iron is a quick and easy way to get temporary curls. All you need to do is heat up the iron, wrap your hair around it, and hold it in place for a few seconds. You can use a curling iron to create loose waves or tighter curls, depending on the size of the barrel.

3. Twist Out

A twist out is a natural way to get curls. This method involves twisting small sections of your hair while it's wet, and then letting it dry overnight. When you untwist your hair in the morning, you'll have beautiful curls. You can use a twist out method regularly to train your hair to curl naturally.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide on how to get curly hair for guys:

1. Choose a Method

Decide which method you want to use to get your curls. If you want permanent curls, go for a perm. If you want temporary curls, use a curling iron. If you want natural curls, try a twist out.

2. Get the Right Products

Depending on the method you choose, you'll need to get the right products. For a perm, you'll need a perm kit. For a curling iron, you'll need a heat protectant and a styling product. For a twist out, you'll need a leave-in conditioner and a styling product.

3. Prep Your Hair

Before you start, make sure your hair is clean and dry. If you're getting a perm, don't wash your hair for at least 24 hours before the treatment. If you're using a curling iron or twist out method, apply a heat protectant to your hair to prevent damage.

4. Follow the Instructions

Follow the instructions carefully for the method you choose. If you're getting a perm, make sure you follow the instructions on the kit. If you're using a curling iron, wrap your hair around the barrel and hold it in place for a few seconds. If you're doing a twist out, twist small sections of your hair while it's wet and let it dry overnight.

5. Style Your Curls

Once you've got your curls, style them the way you want. You can use a styling product to define your curls and add shine. Don't forget to maintain your curls with regular hair care.

Tips for Maintaining Curly Hair

Here are some tips for maintaining your curly hair:

1. Use a Moisturizing Shampoo

Curly hair tends to be dry, so use a moisturizing shampoo to keep it hydrated.

2. Condition Regularly

Condition your hair regularly to keep it soft and manageable. Use a deep conditioner once a week for extra hydration.

3. Don't Overwash

Don't wash your hair too often, as this can strip it of its natural oils. Aim to wash your hair once or twice a week.

4. Use a Wide-Tooth Comb

Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair, as this will minimize breakage and frizz.

5. Avoid Heat Styling

Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as this can damage your hair. If you must use a heat tool, use a heat protectant and keep the heat on low.

Frequently Asked Questions

1. Can I get curly hair if I have straight hair?

Yes, you can get curly hair even if you have straight hair. You can use a perm, curling iron, or twist out method to achieve curls.

2. How long does a perm last?

A perm can last for several months, depending on how fast your hair grows and how well you maintain it.

3. Can I wash my hair after a perm?

Avoid washing your hair for at least 24 hours after a perm, as this can affect the curls.

Hair Care for Curly Hair

Curly hair requires special care to keep it healthy and manageable. Here are some tips:

1. Use a Silk Pillowcase

Use a silk pillowcase to minimize frizz and breakage while you sleep.

2. Sleep with Your Hair in a Pineapple

Before you go to bed, gather your hair on top of your head and secure it with a scrunchie. This will help preserve your curls overnight.

3. Don't Brush Your Hair When It's Dry

Brushing your hair when it's dry can cause breakage and frizz. Instead, detangle your hair with a wide-tooth comb when it's wet.

4. Use a Leave-In Conditioner

Use a leave-in conditioner to keep your curls hydrated and defined.

5. Trim Regularly

Trim your hair regularly to get rid of split ends and keep your curls looking healthy.

Now that you know how to get curly hair for guys, it's time to try it out for yourself. Whether you opt for a perm, curling iron, or twist out method, make sure to maintain your curls with proper hair care. With a little bit of effort, you can rock a stylish and trendy curly hairstyle.
