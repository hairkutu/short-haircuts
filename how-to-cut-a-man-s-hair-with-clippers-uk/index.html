How To Cut A Man s Hair With Clippers Uk A Step By Step Guide

Written by Christine Apr 21, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Welcome to our ultimate guide on how to cut a man's hair with clippers in the UK! With the ongoing pandemic, many people are opting to cut their hair at home. With clippers, you can achieve a professional look without breaking the bank. In this blog post, we'll show you how to cut your hair with clippers, step-by-step. We'll also provide you with tips and hair care advice to ensure you get the best results. Let's get started!

How to Cut a Man's Hair with Clippers UK: Step-by-Step Guide

Step 1: Gather Your Tools

The first step in cutting a man's hair with clippers is to gather all the necessary tools. You'll need:

  • Clippers
  • Comb
  • Scissors (optional)
  • Cape or towel
  • Clipper oil
  • Brush

Make sure that your clippers are fully charged and oiled before you start.

Step 2: Decide on the Length

Before you start cutting, decide on the length you want to achieve. Use the clipper guards to determine the length you want. The lower the number on the guard, the shorter the hair will be. Start with the longest guard and work your way down until you achieve the desired length.

Step 3: Start Cutting

Start cutting at the sides and back of the head. Use the clipper in an upward motion, against the direction of hair growth. Use the comb to lift the hair and ensure an even cut. Move to the top of the head and work your way towards the front. Use the scissors to trim any longer hairs that the clippers may have missed.

Step 4: Blend the Hair

To blend the hair, use a lower guard than the one you used previously. Start from the bottom and work your way up, using a flicking motion to blend the hair.

Step 5: Trim the Neckline

Trim the neckline using the clippers. Start from one side and work your way to the other. Use a lower guard than the one used on the sides and back of the head.

Step 6: Clean Up

Use a brush to remove any loose hair from the head and neck. Wipe the clippers with a clean cloth and apply clipper oil to maintain them.

Tips for Cutting a Man's Hair with Clippers UK

  • Start with a higher guard and work your way down to avoid cutting too much hair at once.
  • Use the comb to lift the hair for an even cut.
  • Trim the hair in a well-lit area to avoid missing any spots.
  • Invest in a good quality set of clippers to ensure a clean and even cut.

FAQs

Can I Cut Wet Hair with Clippers?

It's not recommended to cut wet hair with clippers as it may damage the blades. It's best to cut dry hair to achieve the best results.

How Often Should I Oil My Clippers?

You should oil your clippers before and after every use to maintain them and ensure they last longer.

How Can I Achieve a Fade?

To achieve a fade, start with a higher guard and work your way down. Blend the hair using a lower guard, starting from the bottom and working your way up.

Hair Care after Cutting with Clippers UK

After cutting your hair with clippers, it's important to take care of your hair. Use a shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair clean and healthy. Avoid using hot tools such as hair dryers or straighteners as they may damage the hair. Trim your hair regularly to maintain the style.

That's it! Our ultimate guide on how to cut a man's hair with clippers in the UK. We hope you found this guide helpful and are now ready to achieve a professional look at home. Remember to maintain your clippers and take care of your hair to ensure the best results. Happy cutting!
