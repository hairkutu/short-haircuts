How To Maintain Twists On Natural Hair A Complete Guide

Written by Eveline Jul 19, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Twists are a popular hairstyle among those with natural hair. They provide a low-maintenance option that can last for weeks with proper care. However, maintaining twists can be a bit tricky, especially for those who are new to natural hair care. In this article, we will provide a step-by-step guide on how to maintain twists on natural hair, as well as some tips and FAQ.

How to Maintain Twists on Natural Hair

Maintaining twists on natural hair requires a few essential steps, including moisturizing, detangling, and protecting your hair at night.

Moisturize Your Hair

Keeping your hair moisturized is vital to maintaining twists on natural hair. Dry hair can lead to breakage, which can ruin your twists. To moisturize your hair, use a leave-in conditioner or a moisturizing spray daily. Be sure to focus on the ends of your hair, as they tend to be the driest.

Detangle Your Hair

Detangling your hair is essential before and after twisting your hair. Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to gently detangle your hair. Start at the ends and work your way up to the roots. Detangling your hair before twisting it will make the process smoother and prevent tangles from forming.

Protect Your Hair at Night

Protecting your hair at night is crucial to maintaining twists on natural hair. Use a satin bonnet or scarf to protect your hair while you sleep. Cotton pillowcases can cause friction, which can lead to frizz and breakage.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here is a step-by-step guide on how to maintain twists on natural hair:

  1. Moisturize your hair daily with a leave-in conditioner or moisturizing spray.
  2. Detangle your hair before and after twisting it.
  3. Protect your hair at night with a satin bonnet or scarf.
  4. Avoid excessive manipulation of your twists.
  5. Re-twist any loose or frizzy twists.
  6. Wash your hair every 1-2 weeks with a sulfate-free shampoo.
  7. Deep condition your hair every 2-4 weeks.
  8. Avoid using heavy oils or products that can weigh down your hair.
  9. Trim your ends every 6-8 weeks to prevent split ends.
  10. Take a break from twists every few months to allow your hair to rest and recover.

Tips for Maintaining Twists on Natural Hair

Here are some additional tips for maintaining twists on natural hair:

  • Avoid using too much product, as it can cause build-up and make your hair look greasy.
  • Avoid using heat on your hair, as it can damage your hair and cause breakage.
  • Avoid wearing your twists for too long, as they can cause tension on your hairline and lead to thinning edges.
  • Use a light oil, like jojoba or almond oil, to seal in moisture.
  • Experiment with different twist styles to keep your look fresh and prevent boredom.

FAQ

How long can I wear twists on natural hair?

You can wear twists on natural hair for up to 4-6 weeks with proper care. However, it's essential to listen to your hair and remove your twists if they start to feel heavy or uncomfortable.

Can I wash my hair while wearing twists?

Yes, you can wash your hair while wearing twists. Dilute your shampoo with water and apply it directly to your scalp. Be sure to rinse your hair thoroughly and avoid manipulating your twists too much.

How do I prevent frizz in my twists?

To prevent frizz in your twists, avoid excessive manipulation and use a light oil to seal in moisture. You can also re-twist any loose or frizzy twists as needed.

Hair Care for Natural Hair

Maintaining twists on natural hair is just one part of a healthy hair care routine. Here are some additional tips for caring for natural hair:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo to prevent stripping your hair of its natural oils.
  • Deep condition your hair regularly to keep it hydrated and healthy.
  • Avoid using heat on your hair, as it can cause damage and breakage.
  • Trim your ends regularly to prevent split ends.
  • Be gentle when detangling your hair to prevent breakage.
  • Protect your hair at night with a satin bonnet or scarf.

By following these tips and maintaining your twists regularly, you can keep your natural hair healthy and looking great!
