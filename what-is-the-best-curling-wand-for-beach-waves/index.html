How to Achieve Beach Waves with a Curling Wand

The key to achieving beachy waves with a curling wand is to use a technique called âwrap and hold.â This technique involves wrapping sections of hair around the wand and holding them in place for a few seconds before releasing. To achieve the perfect beachy waves, follow these steps:

Step 1: Prep Your Hair

Step 2: Section Your Hair

Next, divide your hair into sections using clips or hair ties. Start at the bottom and work your way up, clipping the top sections out of the way as you go. The size of the sections will depend on the thickness of your hair and the size of your curling wand.

Step 3: Curl Your Hair

Take a section of hair and wrap it around the curling wand, holding the end with your fingers to prevent it from slipping. Hold the hair on the wand for a few seconds before releasing. Repeat this process with all of the sections, alternating the direction of the curls for a more natural look.

Step 4: Finish and Texturize

Once all of your hair is curled, use your fingers to gently separate the curls and create a more tousled, natural look. You can also spritz some texturizing spray or sea salt spray for added texture and hold.

Tips for Choosing the Best Curling Wand for Beach Waves

When it comes to choosing the best curling wand for beach waves, there are a few things to keep in mind:

  • Size: The size of the curling wand will determine the size of your curls. For beachy waves, a larger barrel (around 1 1/2 inches) is ideal.
  • Material: Look for a curling wand made from ceramic or tourmaline, as these materials distribute heat evenly and reduce damage to your hair.
  • Temperature: Make sure the curling wand has adjustable temperature settings, as different hair types require different levels of heat.
  • Brand: Choose a reputable brand with good reviews to ensure the quality and longevity of your curling wand.

Q: Can I achieve beach waves on short hair?

A: Yes, you can achieve beach waves on short hair using a smaller curling wand or a straightener. Just make sure to adjust the size of the sections and the direction of the curls to suit your hair length.

Hair Care Tips for Maintaining Beach Waves
