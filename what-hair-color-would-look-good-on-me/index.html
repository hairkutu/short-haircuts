What Hair Color Would Look Good On Me

Written by Aprilia Jul 12, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Are you tired of your current hair color and want to switch things up? Or maybe you're looking for a new look that complements your features? Whatever the reason may be, choosing the right hair color can be a daunting task. In this article, we'll explore what hair color would look good on you and provide tips on how to choose the perfect shade.

Description

Choosing the right hair color can enhance your natural beauty and highlight your facial features. However, with so many hair color options available, it can be challenging to find the right one. The first step is to determine your skin tone and undertones. Your skin tone can be warm, cool, or neutral, and your undertones can be pink, yellow, or olive. Once you've identified your skin tone and undertones, you can choose a hair color that complements them.

How To Choose The Right Hair Color

Choosing the right hair color can be overwhelming, but with these tips, you'll find the perfect shade in no time:

Step 1: Determine Your Skin Tone

The first step is to determine your skin tone. To do this, look at the veins on your wrist. If they're blue, you have a cool skin tone. If they're green, you have a warm skin tone. If you can't tell whether they're blue or green, you have a neutral skin tone.

Step 2: Identify Your Undertones

Once you've determined your skin tone, the next step is to identify your undertones. If you have a warm skin tone, your undertones will be yellow or golden. If you have a cool skin tone, your undertones will be pink or blue. If you have a neutral skin tone, your undertones will be a blend of pink and yellow.

Step 3: Choose a Hair Color That Complements Your Skin Tone and Undertones

Now that you've identified your skin tone and undertones, you can choose a hair color that complements them. If you have a warm skin tone with yellow undertones, opt for warm hair colors like golden blonde, honey blonde, or copper. If you have a cool skin tone with pink undertones, go for cool hair colors like platinum blonde, ash brown, or burgundy. If you have a neutral skin tone, you have the flexibility to choose warm or cool hair colors.

Tips

Here are some additional tips to keep in mind when choosing a hair color:

  • Consider your eye color when choosing a hair color. Certain shades can make your eyes pop.
  • Think about your lifestyle and how much maintenance your new hair color will require.
  • Consult a professional hairstylist for advice on choosing the right hair color.

FAQ

What hair color looks good on warm skin tones?

Warm hair colors like golden blonde, honey blonde, and copper complement warm skin tones with yellow undertones.

What hair color looks good on cool skin tones?

Cool hair colors like platinum blonde, ash brown, and burgundy complement cool skin tones with pink undertones.

What hair color looks good on neutral skin tones?

Neutral skin tones can pull off both warm and cool hair colors. It's all about personal preference and what complements your features.

Hair Care

After coloring your hair, it's essential to take care of it to maintain the color and keep it healthy. Here are some tips:

  • Use a color-safe shampoo and conditioner to prevent fading.
  • Avoid washing your hair every day to prevent color from washing out.
  • Use a deep conditioner once a week to keep your hair moisturized.
  • Avoid using hot styling tools too frequently as they can damage your hair.

Conclusion

Choosing the right hair color can enhance your natural beauty and make you feel more confident. By following these tips and consulting a professional hairstylist, you can find the perfect hair color that complements your skin tone and undertones. Remember to take care of your hair to maintain the color and keep it healthy.
