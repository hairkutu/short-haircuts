How To Cut Short Hair With Clippers For Women

Written by Christine Mar 29, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Short hair has been a popular trend for women in recent years, and many women opt to cut their hair short for various reasons. One of the most convenient ways to cut short hair is by using clippers. Clippers are an excellent tool to use for cutting hair, as they are easy to control and can be used for any hair length, including short hair. In this article, we will discuss how to cut short hair with clippers for women.

Description

Cutting your hair with clippers can seem intimidating, especially if you have never done it before. However, it is a straightforward process that anyone can learn. By following a few simple steps, you can achieve a professional-looking haircut without having to spend money on a salon.

How To

Step 1: Prepare Your Clippers

Before you start cutting your hair, make sure your clippers are clean and oiled. This will prevent them from pulling or snagging your hair. You should also ensure that your clippers have the appropriate guard attached. The guard will determine the length of your hair, so choose the appropriate one for your desired length.

Step 2: Wash and Dry Your Hair

It's essential to start with clean, dry hair when cutting with clippers. This will help you see the natural texture and shape of your hair, making it easier to cut.

Step 3: Section Your Hair

Divide your hair into sections using hair clips. Start by sectioning the hair at the nape of your neck, then move upwards towards the crown of your head.

Step 4: Start Cutting

Start cutting at the nape of your neck and work your way upwards. Use the clippers to trim the hair evenly, moving in an upward motion against the natural growth of your hair. Use the guard to achieve your desired length.

Step 5: Blend the Hair

Once you have cut the hair, use the clippers to blend the hair from the crown of your head to the sides of your head. This will create a seamless transition between the different lengths of hair.

Step 6: Clean Up

Once you have finished cutting your hair, use a comb to remove any loose hairs. You should also clean your clippers and oil them before storing them away.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when cutting your hair with clippers:

  • Start with a longer guard and work your way down to your desired length. This will prevent you from cutting your hair too short.
  • Take your time and work in small sections. This will help you achieve an even cut.
  • Make sure your clippers are fully charged before you start cutting your hair.
  • Use a mirror to check the back of your head as you cut.

FAQ

Can I use clippers to cut curly hair?

Yes, clippers can be used to cut curly hair. However, it's important to use the appropriate guard and work in small sections to prevent uneven cuts.

Can I use clippers to cut my own hair?

Yes, you can use clippers to cut your own hair. However, it can be challenging to cut the back of your head, so it's essential to use a mirror to check your work.

How often should I oil my clippers?

You should oil your clippers after every use to prevent them from rusting and to prolong their lifespan.

Hair Care

Short hair is easy to maintain, but it's still essential to take care of it properly. Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner to prevent dryness and breakage. You should also avoid using heat styling tools, as they can damage your hair. Instead, try air-drying your hair or using a diffuser when blow-drying.

Cutting your hair with clippers is an excellent way to achieve a trendy and low-maintenance haircut. By following these simple steps and tips, you can cut your hair like a pro and save money on salon visits.
