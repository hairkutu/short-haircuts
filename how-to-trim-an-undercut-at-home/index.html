How To Trim An Undercut At Home A Step By Step Guide

Written by Daniel Jun 14, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Are you tired of spending money at the salon just to maintain your undercut hairstyle? Trimming your undercut at home can save you time and money. In this article, we will provide you with a step-by-step guide on how to trim an undercut at home.

How to Trim an Undercut at Home

Before we get started, let's gather the necessary tools:

  • Clippers
  • Scissors
  • Comb
  • Mirror
  • Hair tie

Step 1: Wash and Dry Your Hair

Start by washing your hair and drying it completely. This will make it easier to cut and style.

Step 2: Decide on Your Desired Length

Using your clippers, decide on the length you want your hair to be. It's best to start with a longer guard and work your way down to avoid cutting too much off at once.

Step 3: Start Cutting

Begin by cutting the sides and back of your hair. Use a lower guard for a shorter cut and a higher guard for a longer cut. Use the comb to brush the hair and keep it in place while cutting.

Step 4: Blend the Hair

Once you have cut the sides and back, blend the hair with the top using scissors. Take small sections of hair and cut at an angle to blend the two sections together.

Step 5: Create a Guide

To create a guide for the top of your hair, use a hair tie to pull the sides and back of your hair away from the top. This will allow you to see where you need to cut.

Step 6: Cut the Top

Using your scissors, cut the top of your hair to your desired length. Start by cutting small sections and gradually work your way through all of the hair.

Step 7: Check for Evenness

Once you have finished cutting, check for evenness by using your comb to see if there are any longer or shorter sections. If you notice any unevenness, use your scissors to trim those sections.

Step 8: Style Your Hair

Finally, style your hair as desired. You can use a styling product to add texture or hold.

Tips for Trimming an Undercut at Home

Here are some tips to keep in mind while trimming your undercut at home:

  • Start with a longer guard and work your way down to avoid cutting too much off at once.
  • Use scissors to blend the hair and create a natural look.
  • Check for evenness by using your comb to see if there are any longer or shorter sections.
  • Take your time and be patient.

FAQs

Can I trim my undercut with just scissors?

While it is possible to trim your undercut with just scissors, it can be difficult to achieve an even cut. Using clippers with guards can make the process easier and more precise.

How often should I trim my undercut?

It's recommended to trim your undercut every 2-4 weeks to maintain the style and keep it looking fresh.

Hair Care for Undercuts

To keep your undercut looking healthy and stylish, it's important to take care of your hair. Here are some tips:

  • Wash and condition your hair regularly.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, such as blow drying or straightening.
  • Use a styling product to add texture or hold.
  • Visit your hairstylist regularly to maintain the style and keep it looking fresh.

Trimming your undercut at home can be a great way to save money and maintain your style. With the right tools and techniques, you can achieve a professional-looking cut from the comfort of your own home.
