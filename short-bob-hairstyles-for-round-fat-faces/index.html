Short Bob Hairstyles For Round Fat Faces

Written by Aprilia Mar 26, 2023 Â· 9 min read

Are you tired of your old hairstyle and want to try something new? Short bob hairstyles are perfect for those with round, fat faces. They are easy to maintain and can give you a fresh, stylish look. In this article, we will discuss different short bob hairstyles for round fat faces, how to style them, tips, and hair care. Let's get started!

Table of Contents

  • How to Style Short Bob Hairstyles for Round Fat Faces

  • Tips for Short Bob Hairstyles for Round Fat Faces

Description

A short bob hairstyle is perfect for those with round, fat faces as it can help to create an illusion of a longer, slimmer face. There are different types of bob hairstyles such as the classic bob, stacked bob, asymmetrical bob, and inverted bob. These hairstyles can be customized to suit your face shape and hair type.

How to Style Short Bob Hairstyles for Round Fat Faces

The first step in styling your short bob hairstyle is to wash your hair and apply a leave-in conditioner. Use a round brush to blow dry your hair, starting from the roots to the ends. This will help to add volume and texture to your hair. Once your hair is dry, you can use a straightening iron to create sleek, straight hair or use a curling iron to add curls or waves to your hair.

Classic Bob

The classic bob is a timeless hairstyle that is perfect for those with round, fat faces. It is a simple, straight bob that is cut to chin-length. You can add layers to your bob to create texture and volume. You can also add bangs to your bob to frame your face.

Stacked Bob

The stacked bob is a layered bob that is shorter in the back and longer in the front. It is perfect for those with round, fat faces as it can help to create an illusion of a longer, slimmer face. You can add texture to your stacked bob by adding layers and using a curling iron to create waves.

Asymmetrical Bob

The asymmetrical bob is a trendy hairstyle that is perfect for those with round, fat faces. It is a bob that is cut shorter on one side and longer on the other side. This hairstyle can help to create an illusion of a longer, slimmer face. You can add texture to your asymmetrical bob by adding layers and using a straightening iron to create sleek, straight hair.

Inverted Bob

The inverted bob is a bob that is shorter in the back and longer in the front. It is perfect for those with round, fat faces as it can help to create an illusion of a longer, slimmer face. You can add texture to your inverted bob by adding layers and using a curling iron to create waves.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here is a step-by-step guide on how to style your short bob hairstyle:

  1. Wash your hair and apply a leave-in conditioner
  2. Blow dry your hair using a round brush
  3. Straighten your hair using a straightening iron or curl your hair using a curling iron
  4. Apply hairspray to keep your hair in place

Tips for Short Bob Hairstyles for Round Fat Faces

Here are some tips to help you style your short bob hairstyle:

  • Use a round brush to add volume and texture to your hair
  • Use a straightening iron to create sleek, straight hair
  • Use a curling iron to add curls or waves to your hair
  • Add layers to your bob to create texture and volume
  • Add bangs to your bob to frame your face

Frequently Asked Questions

Here are some frequently asked questions about short bob hairstyles for round fat faces:

Can I add highlights to my short bob hairstyle?

Yes, you can add highlights to your short bob hairstyle to add dimension and texture to your hair.

What type of hair products should I use for my short bob hairstyle?

You should use hair products that are specifically designed for your hair type. For example, if you have fine hair, you should use volumizing products to add volume to your hair. If you have curly hair, you should use products that will define your curls.

How often should I trim my short bob hairstyle?

You should trim your short bob hairstyle every 6-8 weeks to maintain its shape and style.

Hair Care for Short Bob Hairstyles for Round Fat Faces

Here are some hair care tips for short bob hairstyles for round fat faces:

  • Wash your hair every 2-3 days to prevent it from becoming dry and brittle
  • Use a leave-in conditioner to keep your hair moisturized
  • Avoid using hot tools such as straightening irons and curling irons too often as they can damage your hair
  • Trim your hair every 6-8 weeks to maintain its shape and style

Short bob hairstyles are perfect for those with round, fat faces. They are easy to maintain and can give you a fresh, stylish look. With the right styling techniques and hair care, you can rock your short bob hairstyle with confidence. Try out different types of bob hairstyles to find the one that suits your face shape and hair type the best.
