How To Not Have Long Face A Complete Guide

Written by Robby Apr 15, 2023 Â· 8 min read

If you're someone who feels self-conscious about your long face, you're not alone. Many people feel insecure about the length of their face, but the good news is that there are plenty of ways to address this issue. In this post, we'll discuss how to not have long face, including tips, tricks, and hair care recommendations to help you look and feel your best.

Table of Contents

  • What Causes a Long Face?
  • How to Determine if You Have a Long Face
  • How to Not Have Long Face: Tips and Tricks
  • Hair Care Tips for People with Long Faces
  • FAQs

What Causes a Long Face?

Before we dive into how to not have long face, it's important to understand what causes this facial shape. A long face is typically caused by genetics, which means that if your parents have long faces, there's a good chance you will too. Additionally, some medical conditions such as Marfan syndrome and acromegaly can cause a long face.

How to Determine if You Have a Long Face

So, how can you tell if you have a long face? One way is to measure your face from your hairline to your chin and compare it to the width of your face. If your face is significantly longer than it is wide, you may have a long face. Additionally, people with long faces tend to have prominent foreheads and narrow chins.

How to Not Have Long Face: Tips and Tricks

If you're looking for ways to address your long face, here are some tips and tricks to try:

1. Play with Your Hairstyle

Your hairstyle can have a big impact on the appearance of your face. Consider getting bangs or layers to help balance out the length of your face. Additionally, avoid hairstyles that are too long or too short, as these can make your face look even longer.

2. Use Makeup to Your Advantage

Makeup can also be a powerful tool for addressing a long face. Use contouring techniques to create the illusion of a shorter face, and highlight your cheekbones to draw attention away from the length of your face.

3. Choose the Right Accessories

Accessories like hats, glasses, and earrings can all help to balance out the length of your face. Consider choosing accessories that are wider instead of taller, as these can help to visually shorten your face.

Hair Care Tips for People with Long Faces

In addition to the tips above, there are also some specific hair care recommendations that can help people with long faces:

1. Avoid Too Much Volume

While volume can be great for adding dimension to your hair, too much volume can actually make your face look longer. Stick to styles that are sleek and smooth, and avoid teasing or backcombing your hair.

2. Consider a Side Part

A side part can help to balance out the length of your face, and can also add some volume at the crown of your head. Additionally, a side part can help to create the illusion of more width in your face.

3. Embrace Your Natural Texture

If you have curly or wavy hair, embrace it! These textures can be great for adding volume and dimension to your hair, which can help to balance out the length of your face. Additionally, curly or wavy hair can help to create the illusion of more width in your face.

FAQs

Q: Can I change the shape of my face?

A: While there are some cosmetic procedures that can alter the shape of your face, these should only be considered as a last resort. Instead, focus on ways to embrace and enhance your natural features.

Q: Is having a long face a bad thing?

A: Absolutely not! Having a long face is just one of the many unique features that make you who you are. Embrace your face shape, and remember that beauty comes in all shapes and sizes.

Q: Are there any hairstyles that are best for people with long faces?

A: While everyone's face and hair is different, some hairstyles that can be flattering for people with long faces include side-swept bangs, layered cuts, and chin-length bobs.

Q: Can I still wear glasses if I have a long face?

A: Of course! Just be sure to choose glasses that are wider than they are tall, as these can help to visually shorten your face. Additionally, consider trying on a variety of styles to find the ones that best suit your face shape.

By following these tips and tricks, you can learn how to not have long face and feel confident in your appearance. Remember, everyone's face is different, and there's no one "perfect" way to look. Embrace your unique features, and don't be afraid to experiment with different hairstyles, makeup looks, and accessories to find what works best for you!
