How To Do Cornrows Male Step By Step Guide

If you're looking for a low-maintenance hairstyle that's both stylish and practical, look no further than cornrows. This classic braiding technique has been around for centuries and is a popular choice for men of all ages and backgrounds. In this guide, we'll show you how to do cornrows male in relaxed English, with step-by-step instructions and helpful tips to make the process as easy as possible.

How to Do Cornrows Male

Cornrows are a type of braiding technique that involves weaving hair in tight, narrow rows close to the scalp. They can be done on any type of hair, but are particularly popular among men with textured or curly hair. To do cornrows male, you'll need a few basic supplies:

  • Wide-tooth comb
  • Rat-tail comb
  • Clips or hair ties
  • Braiding hair (optional)

Step-by-Step Guide

Follow these steps to create perfect cornrows:

  1. Start by washing and conditioning your hair. This will make it easier to work with and help prevent breakage.
  2. Using a wide-tooth comb, detangle your hair and part it into sections. The size of the sections will depend on the thickness of your hair and the desired size of the cornrows.
  3. Take a small section of hair at the front of your head and divide it into three equal parts.
  4. Cross the left section over the middle section, then cross the right section over the new middle section. Continue this pattern, adding a small amount of hair to each section as you go.
  5. Continue braiding until you reach the end of the section. Secure the braid with a hair tie or clip.
  6. Repeat steps 3-5 on the next section of hair, working your way towards the back of your head.
  7. When you reach the back of your head, continue braiding in a straight line or in a pattern of your choice.
  8. Once you've braided all the sections, tie off the ends with a hair tie or clip.
  9. If desired, add extensions or braiding hair to make the cornrows longer or thicker.
  10. Finish with a light styling product to keep the style in place.

Tips for a Great Result

Here are some tips to help you achieve the best possible result:

  • Start with clean, detangled hair to make the braiding process easier and prevent breakage.
  • Use a rat-tail comb to create clean, even parts.
  • Braid tightly to prevent the cornrows from unraveling or coming loose.
  • Use clips or hair ties to keep sections of hair out of the way while you braid.
  • Don't be afraid to experiment with different patterns and styles.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about cornrows:

How long do cornrows last?

Cornrows can last anywhere from a few days to several weeks, depending on how well they're maintained and how quickly your hair grows.

Can I wash my hair with cornrows?

Yes, you can wash your hair with cornrows, but be sure to use a gentle shampoo and don't rub the hair too vigorously. Pat it dry gently with a towel and let it air dry.

Can I style my cornrows?

Yes, you can style your cornrows in a variety of ways, such as pulling them into a bun or ponytail, or adding beads or other accessories.

Hair Care Tips

To keep your cornrows looking great, follow these hair care tips:

  • Keep your scalp clean and moisturized.
  • Avoid pulling or tugging on your cornrows, as this can cause breakage.
  • Wear a satin or silk scarf or bonnet at night to protect your hair while you sleep.
  • Avoid using heavy oils or greasy products on your hair, as this can weigh it down and make it look greasy.
  • Visit a professional stylist for maintenance every few weeks.

With these tips and techniques, you'll be able to create perfect cornrows male every time. Whether you're looking for a low-maintenance style for everyday wear or a unique and eye-catching look for a special occasion, cornrows are a great choice for men of all ages and backgrounds.
