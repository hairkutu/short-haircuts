How To Cut Pixie Short Hair A Step By Step Guide

Written by Petter Jun 08, 2023 · 8 min read

If you're thinking of cutting your hair short, a pixie cut is a stylish and low-maintenance option. This trendy haircut can be customized to suit your individual style and face shape. However, before you grab your scissors and start snipping away, it's important to know the basics of how to cut pixie short hair. In this article, we'll walk you through the process step-by-step, providing tips and advice along the way.

Table of Contents

  • How to Prepare for a Pixie Cut
  • Step-by-Step Guide to Cutting Pixie Short Hair
  • Tips and Tricks for Cutting Pixie Short Hair
  • FAQs About Cutting Pixie Short Hair
  • Hair Care for Pixie Short Hair

How to Prepare for a Pixie Cut

Before you start cutting your hair, it's important to prepare it properly. This will ensure that your haircut turns out well and looks great. Here are some tips on how to prepare your hair for a pixie cut:

1. Wash Your Hair

It's important to wash your hair before cutting it. This will remove any dirt or oil that could interfere with the cutting process. Use a mild shampoo and conditioner to avoid damaging your hair.

2. Detangle Your Hair

Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair. This will make it easier to cut and ensure that your hair looks neat and even.

3. Gather Your Tools

Before you start cutting your hair, make sure you have all the necessary tools. You'll need a pair of sharp scissors, a comb, and hair clips to section your hair.

Step-by-Step Guide to Cutting Pixie Short Hair

Now that you've prepared your hair, it's time to start cutting your pixie short hair. Follow these steps to get the perfect haircut:

1. Section Your Hair

Use hair clips to section your hair into four parts: the top, right side, left side, and back. This will make it easier to cut your hair evenly.

2. Cut the Back

Start by cutting the back of your hair. Use your comb to separate a small section of hair at the nape of your neck. Hold your hair between your fingers and cut it straight across. Work your way up your head, cutting small, even sections of hair.

3. Cut the Sides

Once you've cut the back, move on to the sides. Use your comb to separate a small section of hair at the temple. Hold your hair between your fingers and cut it at an angle, following the curve of your head. Repeat on the other side.

4. Cut the Top

Finally, it's time to cut the top of your hair. Use your comb to separate a section of hair at the crown of your head. Hold your hair between your fingers and cut it to your desired length. Work your way forward, cutting small, even sections of hair.

Tips and Tricks for Cutting Pixie Short Hair

Cutting pixie short hair can be challenging, but with the right tips and tricks, you can get a great haircut. Here are some tips to help you out:

1. Start Small

If you're new to cutting hair, start with a small section and work your way up. This will make it easier to get an even cut.

2. Use Sharp Scissors

Sharp scissors are essential for a good haircut. Dull scissors can damage your hair and create uneven cuts.

3. Cut in Small Sections

Cutting your hair in small sections will ensure that you get an even cut. It's better to take your time and cut small sections than rush and end up with a bad haircut.

FAQs About Cutting Pixie Short Hair

Here are some frequently asked questions about cutting pixie short hair:

1. How often should I cut my pixie short hair?

It's recommended to cut your hair every 4-6 weeks to maintain your pixie cut.

2. Can I cut my own pixie short hair?

Yes, you can cut your own hair. However, it's recommended to have a professional do it if you're new to cutting hair.

Hair Care for Pixie Short Hair

Here are some tips for taking care of your pixie short hair:

1. Wash Your Hair Regularly

It's important to wash your hair regularly to prevent buildup and keep it looking healthy. Use a mild shampoo and conditioner to avoid damaging your hair.

2. Use a Heat Protectant

If you use heat styling tools, use a heat protectant to prevent damage to your hair.

3. Trim Your Hair Regularly

Regular trims will keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends. It's recommended to trim your hair every 4-6 weeks.

With these tips and tricks, you can cut your pixie short hair like a pro. Remember to take your time and be patient, and you'll end up with a great haircut.
