Men s Hairstyles For Long Thick Curly Hair

Written by William Aug 25, 2023 Â· 7 min read

If you have long, thick, and curly hair, you might be overwhelmed with the different options available for styling your hair. In this article, we will discuss some of the best men's hairstyles for long thick curly hair. Whether you want to keep your curls long or short, there's a hairstyle that will suit your preferences.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How To
  • Step By Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

Long, thick, and curly hair can be challenging to style, but it's also a blessing because you can create various hairstyles with your hair. With the proper haircut, you can accentuate your curls, and with the right hair products, you can keep your hair healthy and shiny.

How To

The first step in styling your long, thick, and curly hair is to find the right barber or hairstylist who knows how to work with your hair type. You can show them some pictures of hairstyles you like and discuss what will work best for your hair. Once you have a good haircut, you can start experimenting with different styles.

Step By Step

Here are some of the best men's hairstyles for long thick curly hair:

1. Long Curly Hair with Undercut

This hairstyle is perfect for men who want to keep their curls long but still want a clean and polished look. The undercut will provide a nice contrast to the long curls on top. To style, apply a curl-enhancing product to damp hair and let it air dry. Use your fingers to shape your curls and add volume.

2. Curly High Top Fade

This hairstyle is a modern take on the classic high top fade. The curly hair on top will add texture and dimension to the style. To style, use a curl-defining product and a blow dryer to shape your curls. Use a trimmer to fade the sides and back.

3. Curly Quiff

The curly quiff is a versatile hairstyle that can be dressed up or down. To style, blow dry your hair while using a brush to shape your curls. Apply a medium hold product to keep your hair in place.

4. Curly Side Part

The curly side part is a classic and timeless hairstyle that works well with long thick curly hair. To style, part your hair to one side and use a curl-enhancing product to define your curls.

Tips

Here are some tips for maintaining your long, thick, and curly hair:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and moisturized.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as they can damage your curls.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your curls soft and manageable.
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it's dry, as this can cause frizz. Instead, use a wide-tooth comb when your hair is wet.

FAQ

Q: How often should I wash my long thick curly hair?

A: It depends on your hair type and lifestyle. If you have dry hair, you can wash it once or twice a week. If you have oily hair, you may need to wash it more often. If you exercise frequently, you may need to wash your hair more often to remove sweat and dirt.

Q: Can I straighten my long thick curly hair?

A: Yes, you can straighten your hair, but it's essential to use a heat protectant spray and not straighten it too often to prevent damage to your curls.

Hair Care

It's essential to take care of your long, thick, and curly hair to keep it healthy and manageable. Here are some hair care tips:

  • Use a shampoo and conditioner specifically designed for curly hair.
  • Apply a hair mask once a week to keep your hair soft and hydrated.
  • Avoid using hot water to wash your hair, as this can strip your hair of its natural oils.
  • Use a microfiber towel or t-shirt to dry your hair to prevent frizz.
  • Avoid using hair products that contain alcohol, as this can dry out your hair.

In conclusion, long thick curly hair can be challenging to style, but with the right haircut and hair products, you can create various hairstyles that suit your preferences. Remember to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and shiny.
