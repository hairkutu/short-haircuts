How To Find A Good Hairdresser Uk A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Oliver Aug 26, 2023 Â· 8 min read

Looking for a good hairdresser in the UK can be a daunting task, especially if you're new to the area or don't have any recommendations from friends or family. With so many salons and stylists to choose from, it can be overwhelming to find the right one. But fear not, we've put together a comprehensive guide to help you find the perfect hairdresser for your needs.

Ask for Referrals

One of the best ways to find a good hairdresser is to ask for referrals from friends, family, or colleagues. If you like someone's haircut, ask them where they got it done and who their stylist is. This way, you can get a personal recommendation from someone you trust.

It's also a good idea to ask for referrals from people who have similar hair types and styles to you. If you have curly hair, for example, ask someone with similar hair who they go to for a cut or style.

Research Online

If you don't have any personal referrals, the next best thing is to research online. Use search engines and social media platforms to search for hairdressers and salons in your area. Look at their websites and social media profiles to get an idea of their services, prices, and customer reviews.

Check Social Media

Social media platforms like Instagram and Facebook can be a great resource for finding a good hairdresser. Look for hairdressers and salons in your area and check out their profiles. Look at their photos and read their captions to get an idea of their style and approach to hairdressing.

Read Reviews

Reading customer reviews is an essential part of finding a good hairdresser. Look for reviews on the salon's website, social media profiles, and third-party review sites like Google and Yelp. Read both positive and negative reviews to get a well-rounded idea of the salon's services and customer satisfaction.

Visit Salons

If you have a list of salons that you're interested in, visit them in person before making an appointment. Take a look around the salon and observe the atmosphere, cleanliness, and professionalism of the staff. This will give you an idea of the salon's overall vibe and whether it's a good fit for you.

Ask Questions

When you visit a salon or speak to a hairdresser, don't be afraid to ask questions. Ask about their experience, training, and approach to hairdressing. Ask about their services and prices, and whether they offer consultations or hair care advice.

Look at Portfolios

Most hairdressers and salons will have portfolios of their work on their websites or social media profiles. Look at their portfolios to get an idea of their style and approach to hairdressing. If you like what you see, it's a good sign that they'll be able to give you the haircut or style you're looking for.

Consider Price

Price is an important factor to consider when choosing a hairdresser. While you don't want to skimp on quality, you also don't want to break the bank. Look for a hairdresser or salon that offers a good balance of quality and affordability.

Book a Consultation

If you're still not sure which hairdresser to choose, consider booking a consultation. Many hairdressers offer consultations for free or a small fee. This will give you the opportunity to meet the hairdresser, discuss your needs and preferences, and get a feel for their approach to hairdressing before committing to a full appointment.

FAQ

What should I look for in a good hairdresser?

A good hairdresser should have experience, training, and a good eye for detail. They should be able to listen to your needs and preferences and offer advice and recommendations based on your hair type and style.

How much should I expect to pay for a haircut?

The price of a haircut can vary depending on the salon, hairdresser, and location. On average, you can expect to pay between Â£30 and Â£50 for a haircut in the UK.

How often should I get my hair cut?

How often you should get your hair cut depends on your hair type, style, and personal preference. Generally, it's recommended to get a trim every 6 to 8 weeks to keep your hair healthy and looking its best.

Hair Care

Finding a good hairdresser is just one part of maintaining healthy, beautiful hair. To keep your hair looking its best, it's important to follow a regular hair care routine. This includes washing your hair regularly, using the right products for your hair type, and avoiding heat styling and chemical treatments that can damage your hair.

By following these tips and taking care of your hair, you'll be able to enjoy healthy, beautiful locks for years to come.
