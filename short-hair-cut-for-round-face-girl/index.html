Short Hair Cut For Round Face Girl A Guide For 2023

Written by Daniel Jul 20, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Are you a round-faced girl looking for a new haircut? Short hair can be a great option for you! In this guide, we'll give you tips on how to find the perfect short haircut for your round face. We'll also give you step-by-step instructions on how to style your new haircut and share some hair care tips.

Description: What is a Round Face?

A round face is characterized by a wide forehead, full cheeks, and a rounded chin. If you have a round face, you may feel like your face lacks definition. However, a short haircut can help to create definition and draw attention to your best features.

How to: Finding the Perfect Short Haircut for a Round Face

When looking for a short haircut, it's important to find a style that will complement your face shape. Here are some tips for finding the perfect haircut for a round face:

1. Consider the Length

The length of your haircut can make a big difference in how your face looks. Shorter haircuts tend to elongate the face, while longer haircuts can make the face look wider. For a round face, a short haircut that falls above the chin is a great option.

2. Look for Layers

Layers can help to add texture and movement to your hair, which can create a more flattering look for a round face. Consider a layered bob or a pixie cut with choppy layers.

3. Avoid Blunt Bangs

Blunt bangs can make a round face look shorter and wider. Instead, consider side-swept bangs or no bangs at all.

Step by Step: Styling Your Short Haircut

Once you've found the perfect short haircut for your round face, it's important to know how to style it. Here are some step-by-step instructions for styling your new haircut:

1. Start with Clean, Dry Hair

Wash and dry your hair before styling. Use a heat protectant spray to protect your hair from heat damage.

2. Use a Curling Iron

Use a curling iron to add volume and texture to your hair. Start at the roots and curl your hair away from your face.

3. Finish with Hairspray

Once you've curled your hair, finish with a light hold hairspray. This will help to keep your curls in place throughout the day.

Tips: Maintaining Your Short Haircut

Here are some tips for maintaining your short haircut:

1. Trim Regularly

Short haircuts require regular trims to keep them looking their best. Aim to get a trim every 4-6 weeks.

2. Use a Deep Conditioning Treatment

Short hair can be prone to dryness, so use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy and moisturized.

3. Protect Your Hair from Heat

Use a heat protectant spray before using hot tools on your hair. This will help to prevent heat damage and keep your hair looking healthy.

FAQ: Short Hair Cut for Round Face Girl

1. Can I still wear my hair up with a short haircut?

Yes, you can still wear your hair up with a short haircut. Consider a half-up, half-down style or a sleek ponytail.

2. What if I don't like my short haircut?

If you don't like your short haircut, don't panic! Hair grows back. Talk to your stylist about ways to adjust your haircut or try different styling techniques.

3. Can I still color my hair with a short haircut?

Yes, you can still color your hair with a short haircut. Talk to your stylist about the best color options for your hair type and skin tone.

4. Will a short haircut make my face look too round?

A short haircut can actually help to create definition and draw attention to your best features. Just make sure to find a style that complements your face shape.

Hair Care: Conclusion

A short haircut can be a great option for a round-faced girl. Just make sure to find a style that complements your face shape and use the right styling techniques and hair care products to keep your hair looking its best. With the right haircut and styling, you can show off your best features and feel confident and beautiful!
