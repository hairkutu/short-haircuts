Shoulder Length Hair With Bangs And Layers 2020 The Ultimate Guide

Written by Robby Jun 20, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Are you looking to switch up your hairstyle this year? Shoulder length hair with bangs and layers was a popular trend in 2020 and still going strong in 2023. It's a versatile style that can be dressed up or down, and works well for all hair types and face shapes. In this guide, we'll cover everything you need to know about this trendy hairstyle, including how to achieve it, tips for styling and maintaining it, and answers to frequently asked questions.

How to Achieve Shoulder Length Hair with Bangs and Layers

If you're starting with longer hair, you'll need to trim it to shoulder length first. A professional stylist can help you achieve the right length and layers for your face shape and hair type. If you're starting with shorter hair, you can still achieve shoulder length with the help of extensions.

Once you have the right length, it's time to add some layers. Layers will add movement, texture, and volume to your hair. Your stylist will use different cutting techniques to create layers that flatter your face shape and hair type. They'll also add bangs if that's the look you're going for.

Step-by-Step Guide

1. Start by washing your hair with a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to add body and texture.

2. Blow-dry your hair using a round brush to add volume and create a smooth finish.

3. Use a curling iron or straightener to add waves or curls to your hair for added texture.

4. Apply a texturizing spray or dry shampoo to the roots for added volume and texture.

5. Finish with a light hairspray to hold the style in place.

Tips for Styling and Maintaining Shoulder Length Hair with Bangs and Layers

1. Use a heat protectant spray before using hot tools to prevent damage to your hair.

2. Invest in quality hair products that are suitable for your hair type.

3. Trim your hair regularly to maintain the shape and prevent split ends.

4. Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy and moisturized.

5. Experiment with different styling techniques and accessories to switch up your look.

FAQs

1. Will shoulder length hair with bangs and layers suit my face shape?

Yes, this hairstyle works well for all face shapes. Your stylist will customize the layers and bangs to flatter your features.

2. Can I style this hairstyle in different ways?

Yes, you can style this hairstyle in different ways, such as wearing it straight, wavy, or curly. You can also experiment with different partings and accessories.

3. How often should I get my hair trimmed?

You should get your hair trimmed every 6-8 weeks to maintain the shape and prevent split ends.

Hair Care for Shoulder Length Hair with Bangs and Layers

To keep your hair healthy and looking its best, it's important to follow a regular hair care routine. Here are some tips:

1. Wash your hair every 2-3 days with a gentle shampoo and conditioner that's suitable for your hair type.

2. Use a wide-tooth comb or detangling brush to gently comb your hair when wet to prevent breakage.

3. Avoid using hot tools too often as they can damage your hair. If you do use them, use a heat protectant spray first.

4. Apply a hair mask or deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair moisturized and healthy.

5. Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a UV protection spray.

Conclusion

Shoulder length hair with bangs and layers is a versatile and trendy hairstyle that's perfect for anyone looking to switch up their look. With the right cut, styling techniques, and hair care routine, you can achieve a beautiful and healthy head of hair that will turn heads wherever you go.
