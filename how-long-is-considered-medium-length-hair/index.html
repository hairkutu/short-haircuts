How Long Is Considered Medium Length Hair

Are you wondering whether your hair length is considered medium length? If so, you're in the right place. In this article, we will discuss how long is considered medium length hair, as well as provide tips and hair care advice for those with medium length hair.

Definition of Medium Length Hair

Medium length hair is typically considered to be hair that falls between the shoulders and the collarbone. This length is often referred to as a "lob" or a "long bob". However, the exact length of medium length hair can vary depending on the individual and their hair type.

How to Measure Medium Length Hair

Measuring medium length hair is relatively simple. To determine whether your hair falls within the medium length range, measure the length of your hair from the base of your neck to the tips of your hair. If your hair falls between the shoulders and the collarbone, it's considered medium length.

Step-by-Step Guide to Determine Medium Length Hair

If you're having trouble determining whether your hair is considered medium length, follow these steps:

  1. Stand in front of a mirror with your hair down and untangled.
  2. Measure the length of your hair from the base of your neck to the tips of your hair.
  3. If your hair falls between the shoulders and the collarbone, it's considered medium length.

Tips for Styling Medium Length Hair

Medium length hair offers a lot of versatility when it comes to styling. Here are a few tips for styling your medium length hair:

  • Experiment with different haircuts to find a style that flatters your face shape.
  • Use styling products like mousse or hairspray to add volume and texture to your hair.
  • Try different updos and braids for a versatile look.
  • Use a curling iron or straightener to add waves or straighten your hair.

FAQs about Medium Length Hair

Q: Can I still wear a ponytail with medium length hair?

A: Yes, you can still wear a ponytail with medium length hair. However, you may need to use hair ties or bobby pins to keep your hair in place.

Q: Is medium length hair easy to maintain?

A: Medium length hair can be relatively easy to maintain, as it doesn't require as much upkeep as longer hair. However, it's still important to regularly wash and condition your hair, as well as trim split ends.

Q: Can I dye my medium length hair?

A: Yes, you can dye your medium length hair. However, it's important to use high-quality hair dye and to follow the instructions carefully to avoid damage to your hair.

Hair Care for Medium Length Hair

Proper hair care is essential for maintaining healthy medium length hair. Here are a few tips for keeping your hair in top condition:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as they can cause damage to your hair.
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair moisturized.

Conclusion

Medium length hair is a versatile and popular hair length that is easy to maintain and style. By following these tips and hair care advice, you can keep your medium length hair looking healthy and beautiful.
