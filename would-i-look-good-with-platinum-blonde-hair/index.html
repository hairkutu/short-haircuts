Would I Look Good With Platinum Blonde Hair

Written by Christine Jul 24, 2023 Â· 8 min read

If you're considering dyeing your hair platinum blonde, you may be wondering if this bold color would suit you. Platinum blonde hair can be a striking and attention-grabbing look, but it's not for everyone. In this article, we'll explore how to determine if platinum blonde hair is a good choice for you, and provide tips for maintaining your new look.

Description

Platinum blonde hair is a very light, cool-toned shade of blonde. It's a high-maintenance color that requires regular touch-ups and toning to keep it looking fresh. This shade of blonde is especially popular among celebrities, but it's not a look that everyone can pull off.

How to

If you're considering dyeing your hair platinum blonde, it's important to keep in mind that this is a drastic change that requires careful consideration. Here are some steps to follow:

Step 1: Consider your skin tone

Platinum blonde hair looks best on people with cool-toned skin. If you have warm-toned skin, this shade of blonde may clash with your complexion and make you look washed out.

Step 2: Think about your current hair color

If you have dark hair, achieving platinum blonde hair may require multiple rounds of bleach, which can be damaging to your hair. If you have light hair, such as blonde or light brown, it may be easier to achieve the desired shade of platinum blonde.

Step 3: Consult with a professional stylist

A professional stylist can help you determine if platinum blonde hair is a good choice for you, and can guide you through the process of achieving and maintaining your new look.

Tips

If you've decided to go for platinum blonde hair, here are some tips to keep in mind:

Tip 1: Invest in quality hair products

Platinum blonde hair requires special care to keep it healthy and vibrant. Invest in high-quality hair products that are specifically formulated for blonde hair, including shampoos, conditioners, and toners.

Tip 2: Protect your hair from heat damage

Heat styling tools can be damaging to your hair, especially when you have platinum blonde hair. Use a heat protectant spray before styling your hair, and avoid using hot tools too often.

Tip 3: Keep up with regular touch-ups

Platinum blonde hair requires regular touch-ups to keep the color looking fresh. Plan on visiting the salon every 4-6 weeks to have your roots touched up and your hair toned.

FAQ

Q: Will platinum blonde hair make me look older?

A: Platinum blonde hair can be a bold and striking look, but it won't necessarily make you look older. The key is to choose a shade of blonde that complements your skin tone and features.

Q: Can I dye my hair platinum blonde at home?

A: It's possible to dye your hair platinum blonde at home, but it's a difficult and potentially damaging process. We recommend consulting with a professional stylist to achieve the best results.

Q: How can I prevent my platinum blonde hair from turning yellow?

A: Platinum blonde hair can sometimes turn yellow over time, especially if you expose it to chlorine or sunlight. Use a purple toning shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair looking cool-toned and vibrant.

Hair Care

Maintaining platinum blonde hair requires a lot of care and attention. Here are some tips for keeping your hair healthy:

Tip 1: Deep condition regularly

Platinum blonde hair can be dry and brittle, so it's important to deep condition your hair regularly. Use a hydrating hair mask once a week to keep your hair soft and moisturized.

Tip 2: Avoid over-washing

Washing your hair too often can strip it of its natural oils and cause it to become dry and brittle. Aim to wash your hair no more than every other day, and use a dry shampoo in between washes to keep your hair looking fresh.

Tip 3: Protect your hair from the sun

UV rays can be damaging to your hair, especially when you have platinum blonde hair. Wear a hat or use a UV protectant spray when you're spending time in the sun to prevent your hair from becoming dry and brittle.

In conclusion, platinum blonde hair can be a striking and bold choice, but it's not for everyone. Consider your skin tone, current hair color, and consult with a professional stylist before making the change. If you do decide to go for platinum blonde hair, invest in quality hair products, protect your hair from heat damage, keep up with regular touch-ups, and follow our hair care tips to keep your hair looking healthy and vibrant.
