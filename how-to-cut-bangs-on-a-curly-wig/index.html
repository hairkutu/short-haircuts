How To Cut Bangs On A Curly Wig A Step By Step Guide

Written by Daniel Aug 05, 2023 Â· 7 min read

If you have a curly wig and you want to change up your look, cutting bangs is a great way to do it. However, cutting bangs on a curly wig can be tricky, especially if you've never done it before. In this article, we'll walk you through the process of cutting bangs on a curly wig, step-by-step. We'll also give you some tips and advice on how to care for your wig once you've cut the bangs.

Table of Contents

  • How to Cut Bangs on a Curly Wig

How to Cut Bangs on a Curly Wig

Cutting bangs on a curly wig is not much different than cutting bangs on straight hair. However, there are a few things you need to keep in mind when cutting curly bangs:

  • Curly hair tends to shrink up when it dries, so make sure to cut the bangs longer than you want them to be.
  • Curly hair also tends to bounce up, so cut the bangs straight across instead of at an angle.
  • Make sure to cut the bangs when the wig is dry, as wet hair will look longer than it actually is.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here's how to cut bangs on a curly wig:

  1. Put the wig on a wig stand or your own head.
  2. Using a rat tail comb, section off the hair you want to cut into bangs.
  3. Comb the hair straight down and hold it between your fingers at the length you want the bangs to be.
  4. Using sharp hair cutting scissors, cut the hair straight across, making sure to cut it longer than you want it to be.
  5. Repeat the process until you have cut all the hair you want to be bangs.
  6. Let the hair fall naturally and check to make sure the bangs are even.
  7. If they are not even, trim them little by little until they are.

Tips and Advice

Here are some tips and advice to keep in mind when cutting bangs on a curly wig:

  • Start with small cuts and work your way up. You can always cut more, but you can't put hair back once it's cut.
  • If you're not confident in your cutting skills, take the wig to a professional stylist to have the bangs cut.
  • Use a spray bottle filled with water to mist the hair as you cut. This will help you see the natural curl pattern and prevent you from cutting too much.
  • Be patient and take your time. Cutting bangs can be nerve-wracking, but rushing through it will only lead to mistakes.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about cutting bangs on a curly wig:

  • Can I cut bangs on a synthetic wig? Yes, you can cut bangs on a synthetic wig. Just be sure to use sharp scissors and take your time.
  • How do I care for my wig after cutting bangs? To care for your wig after cutting bangs, use a wig brush or wide-tooth comb to gently detangle the hair. You can also use a leave-in conditioner to keep the hair soft and manageable.
  • How often do I need to trim my bangs? This depends on how fast your hair grows and how often you wear the wig. Generally, you should trim your bangs every 4-6 weeks.

Hair Care After Cutting Bangs

After cutting bangs on your curly wig, it's important to take good care of the hair to keep it looking its best. Here are some tips:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to keep the hair soft and moisturized.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb or wig brush to detangle the hair gently.
  • Store the wig on a wig stand to keep its shape and prevent tangles.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools on the wig, as this can damage the hair.
  • Use a leave-in conditioner or hair oil to keep the hair shiny and healthy-looking.

Cutting bangs on a curly wig can be a fun and easy way to change up your look. By following these tips and advice, you can create beautiful, natural-looking bangs that flatter your face shape and enhance your overall style.
