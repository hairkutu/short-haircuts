Does Long Hair Make Guys Look Older A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Bardi Aug 27, 2023 · 7 min read

Long hair has been a popular trend for men over the years. It’s a style that can make a statement, and it’s versatile enough to fit any occasion. However, some people believe that long hair makes guys look older. So, does long hair really make guys look older? Let’s explore this topic in-depth.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to
  • Step by step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

Long hair can make guys look older or younger, depending on how they style it. Longer hair can create a more mature and sophisticated look, but it can also make guys look unkempt and unprofessional. The key is to find a hairstyle that complements your facial features and matches your personality.

How to

If you’re considering growing out your hair, there are a few things you should keep in mind:

  • Know your face shape: Different hairstyles suit different face shapes. Before you grow out your hair, determine your face shape and find a style that complements it.
  • Keep your hair healthy: Long hair requires more maintenance than short hair. Make sure you keep your hair healthy by washing it regularly, using conditioner, and getting regular trims.
  • Find the right products: Long hair requires different products than short hair. Look for products that are designed for longer hair, such as leave-in conditioner and hair oil.

Step by step

If you’re not sure how to style your long hair, here are some steps to follow:

  • Step 1: Start with clean, dry hair.
  • Step 2: Apply a small amount of hair oil or leave-in conditioner to the ends of your hair.
  • Step 3: Use a hairbrush or comb to detangle your hair.
  • Step 4: Style your hair as desired, using a hair tie or clips to keep it in place.
  • Step 5: Finish with a light hairspray to hold your style in place.

Tips

Here are some additional tips for styling long hair:

  • Experiment with different styles: Long hair is versatile, so don’t be afraid to try different styles and see what works best for you.
  • Get regular trims: Even if you’re growing out your hair, it’s important to get regular trims to prevent split ends and keep your hair healthy.
  • Accessorize: Add some personality to your hairstyle by using accessories such as headbands, hats, or scarves.

FAQ

Q: Does long hair make guys look older?

A: Long hair can make guys look older or younger, depending on how it’s styled. A well-groomed, styled look can create a sophisticated and mature appearance, while unkempt hair can make a person look older and less put together.

Q: What hairstyles suit longer hair?

A: Longer hair can be styled in many ways, including ponytails, braids, buns, and loose waves. The best hairstyle depends on the individual’s face shape, hair type, and personal style.

Hair Care

If you’re growing out your hair, it’s important to take good care of it. Here are some tips for maintaining healthy, long hair:

  • Wash your hair regularly: Long hair can get oily and dirty quickly, so it’s important to wash it regularly to keep it clean and fresh.
  • Use conditioner: Conditioner helps to hydrate and soften your hair, making it easier to manage and style.
  • Get regular trims: Even if you’re growing out your hair, it’s important to get regular trims to prevent split ends and keep your hair healthy.
  • Avoid heat styling: Heat styling tools such as flat irons and curling irons can damage your hair and cause split ends. Try to avoid using them as much as possible.
  • Protect your hair from the sun: The sun’s UV rays can damage your hair and cause it to become dry and brittle. Wear a hat or use a hair product with SPF to protect your hair from the sun.

In conclusion, long hair can make guys look older or younger, depending on how it’s styled. The key is to find a style that complements your facial features and matches your personality. With the right products, styling tools, and hair care routine, you can rock the long hair look at any age.
