Short Hair For Chubby Face 2021 A Complete Guide

Written by Frank Mar 16, 2023 Â· 8 min read

If you have a chubby face and are looking for a new hairstyle in 2021, short hair may be the perfect option for you. Not only can short hair help to elongate your face and make it appear slimmer, but it can also be easy to maintain and style. In this article, we'll guide you through everything you need to know about short hair for chubby faces in 2021, including how to choose the right style, tips for styling and maintaining your hair, and more.

Description

Short hair is a great option for those with a chubby face because it can help to create the illusion of length and slimness. The key is to choose a style that complements your face shape and features.

How to Choose the Right Style

When choosing a short hairstyle for your chubby face, there are a few things to keep in mind:

Consider Your Face Shape

The first thing to consider is your face shape. While short hair can look great on anyone, certain styles may be more flattering for your specific face shape. For example, if you have a round face, you may want to choose a style with volume on top to create the illusion of length. If you have a square face, a side-swept bang can help to soften your features.

Think About Your Hair Texture

Another factor to consider is your hair texture. If you have fine hair, you may want to choose a style with layers to add volume and dimension. If you have thick hair, a blunt cut may be more flattering.

Choose a Style That Fits Your Personality

Finally, it's important to choose a style that fits your personality and lifestyle. Are you low maintenance and prefer a wash-and-go style? Or do you enjoy spending time on your hair and want a more intricate style?

Step by Step Process

Once you've chosen the perfect short hairstyle for your chubby face, here's how to style it:

Wash and Condition Your Hair

The first step is to wash and condition your hair. Use a shampoo and conditioner that are appropriate for your hair type.

Towel Dry Your Hair

Gently towel dry your hair to remove excess water. Avoid rubbing your hair with the towel, as this can cause damage and frizz.

Apply Styling Products

Apply any styling products you like, such as mousse or gel, to your damp hair. This can help to add volume and hold.

Blow Dry Your Hair

Using a blow dryer, dry your hair while using a round brush to create volume and shape.

Style Your Hair

Once your hair is dry, use a styling tool such as a straightener or curling iron to create your desired style.

Tips for Styling and Maintaining Your Hair

Here are some tips for keeping your short hair looking great:

Get Regular Trims

Short hair can quickly lose its shape if not properly maintained. Schedule regular trims to keep your hair looking its best.

Use Heat Protectant

If you use heat styling tools, be sure to use a heat protectant spray to prevent damage to your hair.

Experiment with Products

Try different styling products to find what works best for your hair type and desired style.

FAQs

Q: Will short hair make my face look too round?

A: No, not if you choose the right style. A layered or angled cut can help to elongate your face and create the illusion of length.

Q: Can I still wear my hair up with a short cut?

A: Yes, there are plenty of ways to style short hair in an updo or half-up style. Talk to your stylist for suggestions.

Hair Care

Finally, it's important to take good care of your hair to keep it looking healthy and beautiful. Here are some tips:

Use a Gentle Shampoo

Choose a shampoo that is gentle and free of harsh chemicals that can strip your hair of its natural oils.

Deep Condition Regularly

Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair moisturized and healthy.

Avoid Over-Styling

While styling your hair can be fun, avoid over-styling or using too much heat, as this can cause damage and breakage.

By following these tips, you can keep your short hair looking great and feeling healthy. Whether you're looking for a drastic change or just a new way to style your hair, short hair for chubby faces is a great option for 2021.
