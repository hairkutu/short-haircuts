Will Short Hair Look Good On Me A Comprehensive Guide To Help You Decide

Written by Frank Jun 20, 2023 Â· 8 min read

Short hair has always been a popular choice for women, but not everyone can pull it off. If you're considering getting a short haircut, you might be wondering, "Will short hair look good on me?" This is a common question that many women ask themselves before making the big chop. The good news is, there are a few things you can do to help you make the right decision.

Description

In this article, we will discuss how to determine if short hair will look good on you. We'll cover everything from face shape to hair texture and provide you with tips and tricks to make the transition to short hair as smooth as possible. We'll also answer some frequently asked questions and provide you with some hair care tips to keep your short hair looking healthy and beautiful.

How to Determine if Short Hair Will Look Good on You

There are a few things you can do to determine if short hair will look good on you. Here are some steps you can take:

Step 1: Consider Your Face Shape

One of the most important things to consider when deciding on a short haircut is your face shape. Some short hairstyles look better on certain face shapes than others. Here are some general guidelines:

- Oval face: Almost any short hairstyle will look good on an oval-shaped face.

- Round face: A short hairstyle with height on top and volume on the sides can help elongate a round face.

- Square face: Soft, layered styles can help soften a square jawline.

- Heart-shaped face: A bob or pixie cut can help balance out a heart-shaped face.

- Long face: Short haircuts with volume on the sides can help widen a long face.


Step 2: Consider Your Hair Texture

Your hair texture can also play a role in determining if short hair will look good on you. Some short hairstyles work better with certain hair textures. Here are some general guidelines:

- Fine hair: Short haircuts with layers can help create the illusion of volume and thickness.

- Thick hair: Short haircuts with blunt edges can help thin out thick hair.

- Curly hair: Short haircuts with layers can help enhance natural curls.

- Straight hair: Short haircuts with texture and movement can help add dimension to straight hair.


Step 3: Consider Your Lifestyle

Your lifestyle can also play a role in determining if short hair will look good on you. Short hair can be low maintenance and easy to style, but it may not be suitable for everyone. Consider how much time you're willing to spend styling your hair each day and if a short hairstyle fits into your lifestyle.


Tips for Transitioning to Short Hair

If you've decided to go for a short haircut, here are some tips to make the transition as smooth as possible:

- Bring pictures of short hairstyles you like to your stylist to help them understand what you're looking for.

- Start with a longer length and gradually go shorter if you're unsure about going too short.

- Invest in quality styling products to help you achieve the look you want.

- Consider getting regular trims to maintain your short haircut.


FAQ

Q: Will short hair make my face look rounder?

A: It depends on the short hairstyle you choose. Short hairstyles with height on top and volume on the sides can help elongate a round face.

Q: Will short hair make me look older?

A: Short hair can actually make you look younger if it's styled correctly. A youthful, modern haircut can help take years off your appearance.

Q: Can I still style my short hair different ways?

A: Yes, short hair can be styled in many different ways. You can use a curling iron or flat iron to create different textures and styles.


Hair Care Tips for Short Hair

Here are some tips to keep your short hair looking healthy and beautiful:

- Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to prevent dryness.

- Avoid using hot tools too often to prevent damage.

- Use a hair mask once a week to nourish and hydrate your hair.

- Get regular trims to maintain your short haircut.


In Conclusion

Deciding if short hair will look good on you can be a difficult decision. By considering your face shape, hair texture, and lifestyle, you can determine if a short haircut is right for you. Remember, hair grows back, so don't be afraid to take a risk and try something new!
