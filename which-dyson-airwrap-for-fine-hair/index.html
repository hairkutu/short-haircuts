Which Dyson Airwrap Is Best For Fine Hair

Written by Christine Jul 28, 2023 Â· 9 min read

If you have fine hair, you know how difficult it can be to style. It can be even more challenging to find the right tools to get the look you want without causing damage. That's where the Dyson Airwrap comes in. This innovative styling tool uses air to dry and style your hair, giving you a sleek, polished look without the need for extreme heat. But which Dyson Airwrap is best for fine hair? In this article, we'll explore the different options and help you find the perfect one for your hair type.

Description

The Dyson Airwrap is a game-changer when it comes to hair styling. Unlike traditional heat-based tools, the Airwrap uses air to style your hair. This means less damage and a more natural look. It comes in different versions, each with a specific set of attachments and features.

How to Choose the Right Dyson Airwrap for Fine Hair

Choosing the right Dyson Airwrap for your fine hair requires a bit of research. Here are some factors to consider:

1. Barrel Size

The barrel size of the Airwrap determines the size of the curls or waves you can create. For fine hair, it's best to choose a smaller barrel size, as it will create tighter curls that will last longer. The 1.2-inch barrels are a good option for fine hair.

2. Heat Settings

The Dyson Airwrap has different heat settings that you can adjust according to your hair type. For fine hair, it's best to choose the lowest heat setting. This will help prevent damage and breakage.

3. Attachments

The Dyson Airwrap comes with multiple attachments that can be used to achieve different styles. When choosing an Airwrap for fine hair, look for attachments that are designed to add volume and body, such as the volumizing brush or the pre-styling dryer. These attachments will help you achieve a fuller look without damaging your hair.

Step by Step Guide to Using the Dyson Airwrap on Fine Hair

Using the Dyson Airwrap on fine hair requires a bit of practice, but it's easy once you get the hang of it. Here's how to use it:

1. Start with Clean, Dry Hair

Make sure your hair is clean and dry before using the Airwrap. If your hair is wet, use the pre-styling dryer attachment to dry it first.

2. Choose the Right Attachment

Choose the attachment that will help you achieve the style you want. For fine hair, we recommend using the 1.2-inch barrels or the volumizing brush attachment.

3. Section Your Hair

Section your hair into small sections, starting from the bottom. Use clips to keep the rest of your hair out of the way.

4. Use the Dyson Airwrap

Place the Airwrap close to the roots of your hair and let it do its job. Move the Airwrap down to the ends of your hair, making sure to wrap your hair around the barrel or brush as you go. Hold for a few seconds, then release.

5. Repeat

Repeat the process on all sections of your hair until you achieve the desired look.

Tips for Using the Dyson Airwrap on Fine Hair

Here are some tips to keep in mind when using the Dyson Airwrap on fine hair:

1. Use the Lowest Heat Setting

As mentioned earlier, it's best to use the lowest heat setting on the Airwrap when styling fine hair. This will help prevent damage and breakage.

2. Don't Overdo It

Don't use the Airwrap on your hair for too long. This can cause damage and breakage. Use it for a few seconds at a time, then release.

3. Use the Right Products

Use products that are designed for fine hair, such as volumizing sprays or mousses. This will help give your hair the body and fullness it needs.

FAQs

1. Can the Dyson Airwrap be Used on Wet Hair?

No, the Dyson Airwrap should not be used on wet hair. Make sure your hair is dry before using it.

2. Does the Dyson Airwrap Cause Damage to Fine Hair?

No, the Dyson Airwrap uses air to style your hair, which means less damage and breakage. However, it's important to use the lowest heat setting and not overdo it.

3. How Often Can I Use the Dyson Airwrap on Fine Hair?

You can use the Dyson Airwrap on your hair as often as you like. However, it's important to use it correctly and not overdo it.

Hair Care for Fine Hair

To keep your fine hair healthy and looking its best, here are some tips:

1. Use Gentle Shampoos and Conditioners

Choose shampoos and conditioners that are gentle and designed for fine hair. Avoid products that contain harsh chemicals or sulfates.

2. Don't Brush Your Hair When Wet

Wet hair is more prone to breakage, so avoid brushing it when it's wet. Instead, use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle it.

3. Avoid Heat Styling

Heat styling can cause damage and breakage, so try to avoid it as much as possible. If you must use heat, use the lowest setting and protect your hair with a heat protectant spray.

4. Get Regular Trims

Regular trims can help keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.

In conclusion, choosing the right Dyson Airwrap for fine hair requires a bit of research and practice. Look for a model with a smaller barrel size, low heat settings, and attachments that add volume and body. Follow our step-by-step guide and tips to achieve a polished, natural look without damaging your hair.
