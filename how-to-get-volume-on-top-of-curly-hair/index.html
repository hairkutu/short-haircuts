How To Get Volume On Top Of Curly Hair Tips And Tricks

Written by Eveline Sep 05, 2023 Â· 9 min read

Curly hair is beautiful, but it can be challenging to style. One of the biggest struggles for people with curly hair is getting volume on top. If you have curly hair, you know that it's easy for your hair to fall flat, leaving you with a lifeless, limp style. Fortunately, there are several ways to get volume on top of curly hair. In this article, we'll share some tips and tricks to help you achieve the voluminous curly hair of your dreams.

How to Get Volume on Top of Curly Hair

1. Use a Volumizing Shampoo and Conditioner

The first step in getting volume on top of curly hair is to use the right shampoo and conditioner. Look for products that are specifically designed to add volume to hair. These products will help to lift your hair at the roots, giving it more body and bounce.

2. Apply Mousse to Damp Hair

After washing your hair, apply a volumizing mousse to your damp hair. Use a golf ball-sized amount of mousse and work it through your hair, focusing on the roots. This will help to add volume and hold to your curls.

3. Use a Diffuser When Blow-Drying

When blow-drying your hair, use a diffuser attachment to help enhance your curls and add volume. Hold the diffuser close to your scalp and move it around to dry all areas of your hair. This will help to lift your hair at the roots and create more volume.

4. Tease Your Hair

If you're looking for even more volume, try teasing your hair. Take small sections of hair and backcomb them using a fine-toothed comb. This will create volume and texture, making your hair look fuller.

5. Use Hair Extensions

If you're looking for a more drastic change, you can use hair extensions to add volume to your hair. Clip-in extensions are easy to use and can be applied at home. You can also opt for more permanent extensions, such as tape-in or sew-in extensions.

Step-by-Step Guide to Getting Volume on Top of Curly Hair

Follow these steps to get volume on top of curly hair:

Step 1: Wash Your Hair

Start by washing your hair with a volumizing shampoo and conditioner.

Step 2: Apply Mousse to Damp Hair

Apply a golf ball-sized amount of volumizing mousse to your damp hair. Work the mousse through your hair, focusing on the roots.

Step 3: Blow-Dry Your Hair

Use a diffuser attachment when blow-drying your hair. Hold the diffuser close to your scalp and move it around to dry all areas of your hair.

Step 4: Tease Your Hair (Optional)

If you want even more volume, tease your hair using a fine-toothed comb. Take small sections of hair and backcomb them to create volume and texture.

Step 5: Finish with Hairspray

Finish your look with a light hairspray to hold your curls in place and add extra volume.

Tips for Getting Volume on Top of Curly Hair

Here are some additional tips for getting volume on top of curly hair:

Tip 1: Use a Wide-Toothed Comb

When detangling your curly hair, use a wide-toothed comb instead of a brush. This will help to prevent breakage and preserve your curls.

Tip 2: Sleep with Your Hair in a Pineapple

If you want to preserve your curls overnight, try sleeping with your hair in a pineapple. Gather your hair at the top of your head and secure it with a scrunchie or hair tie. This will help to prevent your curls from getting flattened while you sleep.

Tip 3: Avoid Over-Washing Your Hair

Over-washing your hair can strip it of its natural oils, leaving it dry and limp. Try to wash your hair only once or twice a week to help preserve its natural volume.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Can I use a curling iron to add volume to my hair?

A: Yes, you can use a curling iron to add volume to your hair. Use a large-barrel curling iron to create loose curls and add body to your hair.

Q: Can I use a volumizing powder instead of mousse?

A: Yes, you can use a volumizing powder instead of mousse. Apply the powder to your roots and work it through your hair to add volume and texture.

Q: Will hair extensions damage my hair?

A: Hair extensions can damage your hair if they're not applied or removed properly. Be sure to have your extensions applied by a professional to minimize the risk of damage.

Hair Care Tips for Curly Hair

Here are some additional hair care tips for curly hair:

Tip 1: Use a Deep Conditioning Treatment

Deep conditioning treatments can help to hydrate and nourish your curls, making them look and feel healthier.

Tip 2: Avoid Heat Styling Tools

Heat styling tools can damage your hair and cause your curls to fall flat. Try to avoid using them as much as possible.

Tip 3: Trim Your Hair Regularly

Regular trims can help to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends, which can make your hair look limp and lifeless.

By following these tips and tricks, you can achieve the voluminous curly hair of your dreams. Remember to use the right products, avoid over-washing your hair, and try different styling techniques to see what works best for you. With a little patience and experimentation, you'll be able to get volume on top of your curly hair in no time!
