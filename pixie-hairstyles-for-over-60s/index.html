Pixie Hairstyles For Over 60S A Guide

Written by Bardi May 10, 2023 Â· 6 min read

If you're over 60 and looking for a new hairstyle, a pixie cut might be perfect for you. Pixie cuts are short, low-maintenance, and can be incredibly stylish. In this guide, we'll go over everything you need to know about pixie hairstyles for over 60s, including how to get the look, styling tips, and hair care advice.

Description

Pixie haircuts are short hairstyles that are usually cut above the ears and around the back of the head. They can be styled with bangs or without, and can be tailored to suit any face shape. Pixie cuts are a great option for women over 60 who want a low-maintenance, youthful look.

How to Get the Look

The first step in getting a pixie cut is to find a stylist who specializes in short haircuts. Once you've found a stylist, bring in some photos of pixie cuts that you like to show them what you're looking for. They'll be able to advise you on what will work best for your face shape and hair type.

When you go in for your haircut, be sure to communicate with your stylist about what you want. Don't be afraid to speak up if you don't like something they're doing. It's important to get the cut right from the start, so that you can style it easily at home.

Step by Step

Getting a pixie cut is a simple process, but it's important to follow these steps to ensure that you get the best possible result:

  1. Find a stylist who specializes in short haircuts.
  2. Bring in photos of pixie cuts that you like.
  3. Communicate with your stylist about what you want.
  4. Sit back and relax while your stylist works their magic.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you get the most out of your pixie cut:

  • Invest in some good quality styling products, like a texturizing spray or a molding paste, to help you create different styles.
  • Consider adding some highlights or lowlights to your hair to add dimension and depth.
  • Experiment with different styling techniques, like finger-styling or blow-drying, to find what works best for you.

FAQ

Q: Will a pixie cut suit me if I have fine hair?

A: Yes! Pixie cuts can actually be great for women with fine hair, as they can add volume and texture.

Q: Can I still wear my glasses with a pixie cut?

A: Absolutely! Pixie cuts can actually be a great way to show off your glasses and draw attention to your eyes.

Q: Will I need to get my hair cut more often with a pixie cut?

A: Yes, you'll need to get your hair cut more often to maintain the shape of your pixie cut. Plan on getting your hair cut every 4-6 weeks.

Hair Care

Even though pixie cuts are low-maintenance, it's still important to take care of your hair to keep it looking healthy and stylish. Here are some tips:

  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner that's formulated for your hair type.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as this can cause damage.
  • Consider using a leave-in conditioner or hair oil to keep your hair hydrated.

With these tips and tricks, you'll be able to rock a stylish pixie cut well into your 60s and beyond!
