Description

As we age, our face shape can change, and it can be challenging to find the right hairstyle that suits our features. This is especially true for women over 60 with a round and chubby face. However, there are plenty of options available that can flatter your face shape and make you look fabulous.

How to

When choosing a hairstyle for a round and chubby face over 60, it's essential to consider the length, volume, and layers. A shorter haircut can make your face appear slimmer, while volume at the top of the head can elongate your face shape. Layers can also add dimension and movement to your hair.

Step by step

Here are some step-by-step instructions on how to achieve a flattering hairstyle for a round and chubby face over 60:

  1. Consult with your hairstylist to determine the best haircut that suits your face shape and features.
  2. Consider a short-to-medium length haircut, as it can make your face appear more slender.
  3. Add volume at the crown of your head to elongate your face shape.
  4. Incorporate layers to add dimension and movement to your hair.
  5. Style your hair with a side-swept bang or a deep side part to create asymmetry and balance your face shape.

Tips

Here are some additional tips to consider when choosing a hairstyle for a round and chubby face over 60:

  • Avoid hairstyles that are too short or too long, as they can make your face appear wider.
  • Opt for soft, wispy bangs or side-swept bangs instead of blunt bangs.
  • Avoid hairstyles that add too much volume to the sides of your face.
  • Consider incorporating highlights or lowlights to add depth and dimension to your hair.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about hairstyles for round and chubby faces over 60:

What is the best haircut for a round and chubby face over 60?

The best haircut for a round and chubby face over 60 is a shorter hairstyle with volume at the crown of the head and layers to add dimension and movement to the hair.

Should I avoid bangs if I have a round and chubby face over 60?

No, you can still incorporate bangs into your hairstyle. Soft, wispy bangs or side-swept bangs are more flattering for a round and chubby face over 60 than blunt bangs.

Hair Care

Proper hair care is essential to maintain healthy and beautiful hair. Here are some hair care tips to consider:

  • Wash your hair with a gentle shampoo and conditioner suitable for your hair type.
  • Avoid using hot tools, such as curling irons and straighteners, too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Apply a hair mask once a week to nourish and hydrate your hair.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.
