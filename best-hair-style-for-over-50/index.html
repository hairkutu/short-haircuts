Best Hair Style For Over 50 Tips How To And Faq

Written by Bardi Aug 12, 2023 · 9 min read

As we age, our hair goes through changes that may leave us wondering what the best hair style for over 50 is. Some of us may experience thinning, graying, or even balding. But fear not, because there are plenty of options to choose from that can flatter your features and make you feel confident. In this article, we'll go over the best hair style for over 50, how to achieve it, and some tips and FAQ to help you maintain your locks.

How to Find the Best Hair Style for Over 50

First and foremost, it's important to consider your face shape, hair texture, and personal style when choosing a hair style. Consult with a hairstylist to get their professional opinion on what would suit you best. In general, shorter hair styles tend to be more flattering for older women, as they can add volume and lift to the face. However, that doesn't mean you have to chop it all off. There are also plenty of mid-length and longer hair styles that can look great on mature women.

Short Hair Styles for Over 50

If you're looking for a short hair style, consider a pixie cut, bob, or layered cut. These styles can add volume and dimension to thinning hair, and can be customized to fit your face shape. A pixie cut, for example, can be tailored to be longer or shorter depending on your preference. A bob can be angled or blunt cut, and a layered cut can add texture and movement to your locks.

Mid-Length Hair Styles for Over 50

If you prefer a mid-length hair style, consider a lob, shag, or layered cut. These styles can be versatile and easy to maintain, while still giving you the option to style it in different ways. A lob, or long bob, can be straightened or curled for a more formal or casual look. A shag can add texture and volume to your locks, while a layered cut can give you a more natural and effortless style.

Long Hair Styles for Over 50

If you want to keep your long locks, consider a layered cut, side-swept bangs, or loose waves. These styles can add movement and body to your hair, while still keeping it long and flowing. A layered cut can add texture and dimension, while side-swept bangs can frame your face and give you a youthful look. Loose waves can also add volume and movement to your hair, without having to do too much styling.

Step-by-Step Guide to Achieving the Best Hair Style for Over 50

Once you've chosen the best hair style for over 50, it's important to know how to achieve it. Here's a step-by-step guide to help you get started:

  1. Wash your hair with a gentle shampoo and conditioner that suits your hair type.
  2. Towel dry your hair, then apply a heat protectant spray or serum.
  3. Blow dry your hair using a round brush to add volume and lift.
  4. Use a straightener or curling iron to style your hair, depending on the look you want to achieve.
  5. Finish with a hairspray or hair oil to add shine and hold.

Tips for Maintaining Your Hair Style

Once you've achieved your best hair style for over 50, it's important to maintain it. Here are some tips to help you keep your locks looking great:

  • Visit your hairstylist regularly for trims and touch-ups.
  • Use a hair mask or deep conditioner once a week to keep your hair hydrated.
  • Avoid using hot tools too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Choose hair products that are specifically formulated for your hair type and needs.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and harsh weather conditions by wearing a hat or scarf.

FAQ

What is the best hair color for over 50?

The best hair color for over 50 depends on your skin tone, natural hair color, and personal preference. Some popular hair colors for mature women include natural shades of blonde, brunette, and red, as well as highlights or balayage to add dimension.

Can I still wear my hair long over 50?

Yes, you can still wear your hair long over 50. However, it's important to choose a hair style that flatters your face shape and hair type, and to maintain it with regular trims and deep conditioning treatments.

What is the best hair style for thinning hair?

The best hair style for thinning hair is one that adds volume and body. Shorter hair styles like pixie cuts, bobs, and layered cuts can help achieve this, as well as using volumizing hair products and blow drying with a round brush.

Hair Care for Over 50

As we age, our hair may require more care and attention to keep it looking healthy and vibrant. Here are some hair care tips for over 50:

  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner that suits your hair type.
  • Avoid using hot tools too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Get regular trims to keep split ends at bay.
  • Use a hair mask or deep conditioner once a week to keep your hair hydrated.
  • Eat a balanced diet and drink plenty of water to promote hair health from the inside out.

With these tips and tricks, you can find the best hair style for over 50 that suits your features and personal style. Don't be afraid to experiment with different hair styles and colors, and remember to take good care of your locks to keep them looking their best.
