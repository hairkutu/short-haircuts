How To Get Side Swept Bangs Without Cutting Your Hair

Written by Daniel Apr 25, 2023 · 6 min read

Are you tired of your current hairstyle but not ready to commit to a major haircut? Side swept bangs are a great way to change up your look without sacrificing length. In this article, we’ll show you how to get side swept bangs without cutting your hair.

Description

Side swept bangs are a popular hairstyle that involves sweeping a section of hair across the forehead. They can help to frame the face and add dimension to a hairstyle. While many people opt to get their bangs cut by a professional stylist, it is possible to achieve side swept bangs without cutting your hair.

How to Get Side Swept Bangs Without Cutting Your Hair

Follow these steps to get side swept bangs without cutting your hair:

Step 1: Start with clean, dry hair

Wash and dry your hair before attempting to style your side swept bangs. This will ensure that your hair is free of any styling products or oils that could weigh it down.

Step 2: Section off your hair

Use a comb to section off a small portion of hair at the front of your head. This section should be about the width of your forehead and should extend from your hairline to the crown of your head.

Step 3: Twist the section of hair

Twist the section of hair away from your face. This will help to create a natural curve in the hair, which is key to achieving side swept bangs.

Step 4: Pin the section of hair

Pin the twisted section of hair to the side of your head with a bobby pin or hair clip. Make sure the pin is secure and hidden behind the rest of your hair.

Step 5: Style your hair

Style the rest of your hair as desired. You can wear your hair down or in a ponytail or bun.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you achieve the perfect side swept bangs:

  • Use a heat protectant spray if you plan to use heat styling tools on your hair.
  • If your hair is naturally curly or wavy, consider straightening the section of hair you plan to twist to help create a smoother look.
  • Experiment with the placement of your side swept bangs to find the most flattering look for your face shape.

FAQ

Can I achieve side swept bangs with short hair?

If your hair is too short to pull into a twist, you may not be able to achieve side swept bangs without cutting your hair. However, you can experiment with using hair accessories like headbands or barrettes to create the illusion of side swept bangs.

Will my side swept bangs stay in place all day?

Your side swept bangs should stay in place as long as you secure them with a bobby pin or hair clip. If you find that your bangs are slipping out of place, you may need to use more pins or consider using a stronger hold hairspray.

Hair Care

Make sure to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and looking its best. Here are some tips for maintaining healthy hair:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and other environmental factors by wearing a hat or using a leave-in conditioner with SPF.

With these tips and tricks, you can achieve the perfect side swept bangs without cutting your hair. Whether you’re looking for a new everyday style or a special occasion look, side swept bangs are a versatile and flattering hairstyle that anyone can pull off.
