How To Style Hair Growing Out Tips And Tricks

Written by Christine Jul 12, 2023 Â· 8 min read

Are you currently in the process of growing out your hair? It can be a frustrating and awkward stage, but fear not! With the right styling techniques, you can make the most out of your hair while it grows out. In this article, we will provide you with tips and tricks on how to style hair growing out, so you can look and feel your best during this transition period.

Description

When growing out your hair, it's important to have patience and let it grow naturally. However, this doesn't mean you have to sacrifice style. There are several ways you can style your hair as it grows out, whether you have short hair or long hair. From braids to messy buns, we've got you covered.

How to Style Hair Growing Out

1. Experiment with Braids

Braids are a great way to keep your hair out of your face while it grows out. Try different braid styles such as a French braid, Dutch braid, or fishtail braid. You can also add accessories such as hair clips or ribbons to give your braids a unique touch.

2. Embrace the Messy Bun

Messy buns are a quick and easy hairstyle that can be achieved with hair growing out. Pull your hair into a loose bun and leave some strands out to create a relaxed look. You can also add volume by teasing your hair before creating the bun.

3. Use Hair Accessories

Hair accessories can be a lifesaver when you're growing out your hair. Headbands, scarves, and hair clips can add a pop of color and style to any hairstyle. They can also keep your hair out of your face while it grows out.

4. Try a Half-Up, Half-Down Style

A half-up, half-down hairstyle is perfect for hair that's growing out. Pull the top half of your hair up and secure it with a clip or hair tie. Leave the rest of your hair down for a relaxed look.

Step by Step Guide

1. Start with Clean Hair

Before styling your hair, make sure it's clean and free of any tangles. This will make it easier to style and prevent damage to your hair.

2. Choose Your Style

Decide which hairstyle you want to try based on your hair length and personal preference. You can experiment with different styles until you find one that works for you.

3. Prep Your Hair

If you're using hair accessories or products, make sure to prep your hair accordingly. For example, if you're using a hair clip, tease your hair to add volume before securing the clip.

4. Style Your Hair

Start styling your hair according to the chosen hairstyle. Take your time and be gentle to avoid damaging your hair.

5. Finish with Hair Spray

Once you've styled your hair, finish with a light mist of hair spray to keep it in place. This will also help prevent flyaways and keep your hair looking polished.

Tips for Styling Hair Growing Out

Here are some additional tips to keep in mind when styling hair that's growing out:

  • Use heat protectant products to prevent damage to your hair
  • Don't be afraid to experiment with different styles
  • Avoid tight hairstyles that can pull on your hair and cause breakage
  • Invest in a good quality hairbrush to prevent tangles
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about styling hair growing out:

1. Can I still use heat styling tools on my hair while it grows out?

Yes, but make sure to use heat protectant products and avoid using them too frequently. Too much heat styling can damage your hair and slow down the growth process.

2. How often should I trim my hair while it grows out?

It's recommended to get a trim every 6-8 weeks to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.

3. Can I color my hair while it grows out?

Yes, but make sure to use hair dye that's gentle on your hair and avoid over-processing it. Coloring your hair can also slow down the growth process, so it's important to take extra care of your hair.

Hair Care Tips for Hair Growing Out

In addition to styling tips, it's important to take care of your hair while it grows out. Here are some hair care tips to keep in mind:

  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner that's free of harsh chemicals
  • Avoid using hot water when washing your hair, as it can strip your hair of its natural oils
  • Use a leave-in conditioner to moisturize your hair and prevent breakage
  • Eat a healthy diet that's rich in vitamins and minerals to promote hair growth
  • Get enough sleep and manage stress levels, as they can affect hair growth

With these tips and tricks, you can style your hair while it grows out and maintain healthy hair. Remember to be patient and enjoy the journey to longer, healthier hair.
