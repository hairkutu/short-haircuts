Best Haircuts To Suit Round Faces In 2023

Written by William Jul 11, 2023 Â· 7 min read

If you have a round face, finding the right haircut can be a challenge. But don't worry, we've got you covered! In this article, we will discuss the best haircuts to suit round faces in 2023. From short to long, curly to straight, we have gathered the most flattering hairstyles for your face shape. So, let's get started!

How to Choose the Right Haircut for Your Round Face

Before we dive into the haircuts, let's talk about how to determine your face shape. To determine if you have a round face, measure the width of your forehead, cheekbones, and jawline. If they are all about the same width, you have a round face.

Now that you know your face shape, it's time to choose the right haircut. The best haircuts for round faces are those that create the illusion of length and angles. Avoid haircuts that add volume to the sides of your face, as this will only make your face look rounder.

Short Haircuts for Round Faces

Short haircuts can be a great option for round faces. A pixie cut with long bangs can help elongate your face and create the illusion of more angles. A bob with layers can also be a great option, as it adds movement and texture to your hair. Avoid blunt bobs, as they can make your face look wider.

Medium Haircuts for Round Faces

If you prefer medium-length hair, a long bob with layers can be a great option. This haircut hits just below the chin and helps to elongate your face. You can also add side-swept bangs to create more angles. Another great option is a shoulder-length cut with layers. This haircut adds volume and movement to your hair without adding width to your face.

Long Haircuts for Round Faces

If you have long hair, you can still find a haircut that flatters your round face. Layers are key when it comes to long haircuts for round faces. Layers help to add volume and movement to your hair, which can help to elongate your face. You can also add face-framing layers to create more angles. Avoid straight, blunt cuts, as they can make your face look wider.

Tips for Styling Your Hair

Once you have chosen the right haircut for your round face, it's important to style it in a way that flatters your face shape. Here are some tips for styling your hair:

  • Add volume to the top of your head to create the illusion of length
  • Avoid center parts, as they can make your face look wider
  • Use a round brush when blow-drying your hair to add volume
  • Consider adding highlights or lowlights to create more depth and dimension

FAQ

Q: Can I wear bangs if I have a round face?

A: Yes, you can wear bangs if you have a round face. Side-swept bangs or long, face-framing bangs can help to create more angles and elongate your face. Avoid blunt bangs, as they can make your face look wider.

Q: Should I avoid updos if I have a round face?

A: Not necessarily. If you want to wear an updo, consider a high bun or topknot. This will create the illusion of more height and length, which can help to balance out your face shape.

Hair Care for Round Faces

In addition to choosing the right haircut and styling your hair properly, it's important to take care of your hair. Here are some tips for hair care for round faces:

  • Use a shampoo and conditioner that is suitable for your hair type
  • Use a heat protectant spray before using heat tools
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends
  • Consider using a hair mask once a week to nourish your hair

With these tips and haircuts, you can find a hairstyle that flatters your round face and makes you feel confident. Remember, the key is to create the illusion of length and angles, and to avoid adding volume to the sides of your face.
