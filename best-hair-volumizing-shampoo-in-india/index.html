Best Hair Volumizing Shampoo In India Get Bouncy Hair With These Top Picks

Written by Frank Jul 29, 2023 · 7 min read

Are you tired of having limp and lifeless hair? Do you want to add volume and bounce to your tresses? Look no further than a good hair volumizing shampoo! In this article, we will discuss the best hair volumizing shampoos available in India, how to use them, and tips for maintaining voluminous hair.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to Use Volumizing Shampoo
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Maintaining Voluminous Hair
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care and Volumizing Shampoo

Description

Volumizing shampoos work by adding thickness and body to your hair, making it appear fuller and more voluminous. They achieve this by removing excess oils and buildup from the hair and scalp, and by adding lightweight ingredients that create lift and volume.

When choosing a volumizing shampoo, look for formulas that are lightweight, sulfate-free, and contain ingredients like biotin, keratin, or collagen. These ingredients help to strengthen the hair and promote healthy growth, while also adding volume and thickness.

How to Use Volumizing Shampoo

Using a volumizing shampoo is simple and can be incorporated into your regular hair care routine. Follow these steps:

  1. Wet your hair thoroughly with warm water.
  2. Apply a small amount of volumizing shampoo to your scalp and massage gently with your fingertips.
  3. Rinse out the shampoo thoroughly with warm water.
  4. Repeat the shampooing process if desired.
  5. Follow with a volumizing conditioner, focusing on the ends of your hair.
  6. Rinse thoroughly with cool water to seal the hair cuticle and add shine.

It is important to not overuse volumizing shampoo, as it can strip your hair of its natural oils and cause dryness. Use it once or twice a week, and alternate with a gentle, moisturizing shampoo on other days.

Step-by-Step Guide

If you're unsure of which volumizing shampoo to choose, consider one of these top picks:

1. L'Oreal Paris Elvive Fibralogy Thickening Shampoo

This shampoo contains filloxane, which penetrates the hair shaft and expands it from within, creating thicker, fuller-looking hair. It also contains amino acids to strengthen the hair and prevent breakage.

2. Dove Oxygen Moisture Shampoo

This lightweight formula adds volume and moisture to the hair, without weighing it down. It contains oxygen-infused ingredients that plump up the hair and create lift.

3. TRESemmé Beauty-Full Volume Reverse System Shampoo

This unique formula works in reverse order, with the conditioner applied before the shampoo. It contains a special polymer that coats the hair and creates volume, without leaving any residue.

4. BBLUNT Full On Volume Shampoo

This shampoo contains rice protein that strengthens the hair and adds volume, while also protecting it from damage. It is also free of sulfates, parabens, and other harsh chemicals.

Tips for Maintaining Voluminous Hair

To maintain voluminous hair, follow these tips:

  • Avoid using heavy styling products that can weigh down your hair.
  • Blow-dry your hair upside down to create lift at the roots.
  • Avoid heat styling tools, like curling irons and straighteners, that can damage your hair and cause it to lose volume.
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends, which can make your hair look flat and lifeless.
  • Eat a healthy diet that is rich in vitamins and minerals, which can promote healthy hair growth.

FAQs

Q. Can volumizing shampoo be used on colored hair?

A. Yes, most volumizing shampoos are safe for use on colored hair. However, be sure to choose a formula that is gentle and free of sulfates, which can strip color from your hair.

Q. Can volumizing shampoo cause hair loss?

A. No, volumizing shampoo does not cause hair loss. In fact, it can help to strengthen the hair and promote healthy growth.

Q. Can volumizing shampoo be used on fine hair?

A. Yes, volumizing shampoo is ideal for fine hair, as it adds volume and thickness without weighing it down.

Hair Care and Volumizing Shampoo

Using a good volumizing shampoo can help you achieve the bouncy, voluminous hair of your dreams. By choosing a lightweight formula with nourishing ingredients, and following a proper hair care routine, you can maintain healthy, voluminous hair all year round.

So, go ahead and give one of these top picks a try, and enjoy the confidence that comes with having luscious, voluminous locks!
