How To Undercut Viking Haircut Tips And Tricks

Written by Frank Sep 04, 2023 · 6 min read

In recent years, the undercut viking haircut has become increasingly popular among men. This bold and edgy hairstyle is characterized by a short back and sides with longer hair on top. If you're thinking about trying out this style for yourself, then you've come to the right place. In this article, we'll be discussing how to undercut viking haircut and sharing some tips and tricks to help you achieve the perfect look.

Description

The undercut viking haircut is a modern take on the classic viking hairstyle. It features a shaved or closely cropped back and sides, with longer hair on top that can be styled in a variety of ways. This look is perfect for men who want a bold and edgy style that's easy to maintain and versatile enough for any occasion.

How to

Before you start cutting your hair, it's important to have a clear idea of what you want your final look to be. Look for inspiration online and bring pictures to your stylist to ensure they understand the style you're going for.

Step 1: Start by shaving or closely cropping the back and sides of your head. Use clippers to create a fade, starting with a longer guard and working your way down to a shorter one as you move towards the bottom of your head.

Step 2: Leave the hair on top of your head long enough to be styled, usually around 3-4 inches. Use scissors to trim any split ends or uneven hair.

Step 3: Style your hair on top using a pomade or wax. You can choose to slick it back, create a side part, or leave it messy for a more relaxed look.

Tips

- If you're not confident in your ability to cut your own hair, visit a professional stylist who can help you achieve the look you want.

- Use a good quality pomade or wax to keep your hair in place and add texture and shine.

- Experiment with different styles to find the one that works best for you. Don't be afraid to try something new!

FAQ

Q: How often should I trim my hair to maintain the undercut viking haircut?

A: It depends on how fast your hair grows, but typically every 4-6 weeks is recommended to maintain the shape and style of your haircut.

Q: Can I still have an undercut viking haircut if I have curly hair?

A: Of course! Just be sure to keep the back and sides short enough to create a contrast with the longer hair on top.

Hair Care

To keep your undercut viking haircut looking its best, it's important to take care of your hair. Use a good quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair clean and healthy. Avoid using too much heat when styling your hair, as this can damage it over time. And be sure to trim your hair regularly to maintain the shape and style of your haircut.

Now that you know how to undercut viking haircut, it's time to try it out for yourself. With a little bit of practice and some styling products, you'll be able to achieve the perfect look that's sure to turn heads.
