How To Cut A Mid Bald Fade A Step By Step Guide

Written by Luffy Jun 17, 2023 Â· 7 min read

If you're looking for a trendy and stylish haircut, a mid bald fade might be the perfect choice for you. This haircut is popular among men of all ages and can be easily styled to suit your personality and preferences. In this guide, we'll walk you through the process of cutting a mid bald fade, from start to finish.

How to Cut a Mid Bald Fade

Before we begin, it's important to gather all the tools and equipment you'll need. You'll need a pair of clippers, a comb, scissors, and a mirror. You may also want to use a hair cutting cape to keep the hair off your clothes.

Step 1: Start with the Top

To begin cutting a mid bald fade, start with the top of the hair. Use your scissors to trim the hair to the length you want. Be sure to cut the hair evenly and avoid cutting too much off.

Step 2: Create a Guideline

After you've trimmed the top of the hair, it's time to create a guideline for the fade. Use your clippers to create a line around the head, about an inch above the ears. This will be the starting point for your fade.

Step 3: Blend the Hair

Next, use your clippers to blend the hair from the guideline up to the top of the head. Use a higher guard setting for the bottom of the fade and gradually decrease the guard size as you move up.

Step 4: Blend the Sides

Once you've blended the hair on top, it's time to move on to the sides. Use your clippers to blend the hair from the guideline down to the skin. Make sure to use a smooth and steady motion to avoid any unevenness.

Step 5: Create a Taper

To give the mid bald fade a more natural look, it's important to create a taper. Use your clippers to blend the hair at the back of the head and the neckline. Gradually decrease the guard size as you move down to create a smooth and natural taper.

Step 6: Clean Up

After you've finished cutting the mid bald fade, it's time to clean up. Use your clippers to trim any stray hairs and make sure the hairline is straight and even.

Tips for Cutting a Mid Bald Fade

Cutting a mid bald fade can be a bit tricky, especially if you're new to hair cutting. Here are some tips to make the process easier:

  • Start with a longer guard size and gradually decrease it as you move up the head.
  • Use a comb to lift the hair and make sure you're cutting evenly.
  • Take your time and avoid rushing.
  • Use a mirror to check your progress from different angles.

FAQ

What is a mid bald fade?

A mid bald fade is a popular men's haircut where the hair gradually fades from longer on top to completely shaved around the sides and back of the head.

How long does a mid bald fade last?

A mid bald fade typically lasts around 2-3 weeks before the hair starts to grow back.

Hair Care

To keep your mid bald fade looking fresh and stylish, it's important to take good care of your hair. Use a high-quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair clean and moisturized. You may also want to use a styling product to add texture and volume to your hair.

Cutting a mid bald fade may seem intimidating, but with the right tools and techniques, it can be done easily and quickly. Follow these steps and tips to achieve a trendy and stylish look that will turn heads wherever you go.
