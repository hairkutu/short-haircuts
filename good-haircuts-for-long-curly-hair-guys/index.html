Good Haircuts For Long Curly Hair Guys

Written by Eveline Jun 28, 2023 · 8 min read

Long curly hair can be both a blessing and a curse. While it’s a unique and attractive feature, it can also be difficult to manage and style. But fear not, there are plenty of good haircuts for long curly hair guys that can help you tame your locks and look your best. In this article, we’ll be discussing some of the best hairstyles for men with long curly hair, along with tips for maintaining and caring for your hair.

Table of Contents

  • How to Choose the Right Haircut
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Maintaining Long Curly Hair
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care for Long Curly Hair

How to Choose the Right Haircut

When it comes to choosing a haircut for long curly hair, there are a few things to consider. First and foremost, you’ll want to choose a style that complements your face shape. Oval faces look good with almost any hairstyle, but if you have a round or square face, you’ll want to avoid styles that add volume to the sides of your head. You’ll also want to consider your hair texture and density, as some styles work better for thicker or thinner hair.

Best Haircuts for Long Curly Hair Guys

Here are some of the best haircuts for men with long curly hair:

  • Long Layers: This style works well for those with thick hair, as it helps to reduce bulk and add movement to the hair. It also works well for those with longer faces.
  • Shoulder-Length Cuts: This style works well for those with thinner hair, as it helps to add volume and texture. It also works well for those with square faces, as it helps to soften the jawline.
  • Undercuts: This style works well for those who want a more edgy look. It involves shaving the sides and back of the head, while leaving the top long and curly.
  • Classic Taper: This style involves cutting the hair short on the sides and back, while leaving the top longer and curly. It’s a classic and timeless look that works well for almost any face shape.

Step-by-Step Guide

If you’re looking to style your long curly hair at home, here’s a step-by-step guide:

  1. Start by washing your hair with a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner. This will help to keep your curls hydrated and reduce frizz.
  2. Towel dry your hair and apply a curl-enhancing product, such as a mousse or gel. This will help to define your curls and keep them in place.
  3. Use a diffuser attachment on your blow dryer to dry your hair. This will help to prevent frizz and preserve your curls.
  4. If you want to add more volume to your hair, use a pick or comb to lift your curls at the roots.
  5. Finish with a hairspray to keep your curls in place.

Tips for Maintaining Long Curly Hair

Here are some tips for maintaining healthy and beautiful long curly hair:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils.
  • Avoid heat styling tools, such as flat irons and curling irons, as they can damage your hair and cause frizz.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends and breakage.
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it’s dry, as this can cause frizz and damage your curls.
  • Sleep on a silk or satin pillowcase to prevent frizz and breakage.

FAQs

Q: Can I straighten my long curly hair?

A: Yes, you can straighten your long curly hair using a flat iron. However, it’s important to use a heat protectant spray and to avoid straightening your hair too often, as this can cause damage.

Q: How often should I wash my long curly hair?

A: It’s recommended to wash your long curly hair every 2-3 days, as this will help to keep your curls hydrated and reduce frizz. However, this can vary depending on your hair type and lifestyle.

Hair Care for Long Curly Hair

Here are some additional tips for caring for your long curly hair:

  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your curls hydrated and healthy.
  • Avoid using harsh chemicals, such as bleach and hair dye, as they can damage your hair and cause breakage.
  • Avoid pulling your hair back too tightly, as this can cause breakage and damage your curls.
  • Eat a healthy diet and drink plenty of water to promote healthy hair growth.

By following these tips and choosing the right haircut, you can rock your long curly hair with confidence and style. Whether you prefer a classic taper or an edgy undercut, there’s a perfect haircut out there for you.
