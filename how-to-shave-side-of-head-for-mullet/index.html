How To Shave Side Of Head For Mullet

Written by Luffy Jul 08, 2023 Â· 7 min read

If you're looking for a bold and edgy haircut, the mullet is a great choice. This hairstyle is characterized by short hair on the top and sides, with longer hair in the back. To achieve the full mullet effect, you'll need to shave the sides of your head. In this guide, we'll show you how to shave side of head for mullet in relaxed English language.

Description

The mullet is a hairstyle that's been around for decades, but it's recently made a comeback. This look is perfect for those who want to stand out and make a statement with their hair. To achieve the full mullet effect, you'll need to shave the sides of your head. This will create a stark contrast between the short hair on top and the longer hair in the back.

How to Shave Side of Head for Mullet

Before you start shaving, make sure you have the right tools. You'll need a pair of clippers, a razor, shaving cream, and a mirror. Here are the steps to follow:

Step 1: Trim the Hair

Start by using the clippers to trim the hair on the sides of your head. Use a #2 or #3 guard to leave some hair, or go shorter with a #1 guard. Make sure you're cutting in a straight line from your temple to the back of your head. You can also use scissors if you prefer.

Step 2: Apply Shaving Cream

Once you've trimmed the hair, apply shaving cream to the area you want to shave. This will help the razor glide smoothly over your skin and prevent irritation.

Step 3: Shave the Hair

Using a razor, shave the hair on the sides of your head. Make sure you're shaving in the direction of hair growth to avoid irritation. Take your time and be careful not to cut yourself. Rinse the razor frequently to remove hair and shaving cream.

Step 4: Rinse and Moisturize

Once you've finished shaving, rinse your head with cold water to close the pores. Then, apply a moisturizer to soothe any irritation and keep your skin hydrated.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when shaving side of head for mullet:

  • Use a sharp razor to avoid irritation and cuts.
  • Take your time and be careful not to rush.
  • Rinse the razor frequently to remove hair and shaving cream.
  • Apply a moisturizer after shaving to soothe any irritation and keep your skin hydrated.
  • Consider asking a friend for help if you're not comfortable shaving the back of your head yourself.

FAQ

Is shaving side of head for mullet difficult?

Shaving side of head for mullet can be difficult if you're not used to shaving your head. However, with the right tools and technique, anyone can do it.

How often should I shave side of head for mullet?

How often you shave side of head for mullet depends on how quickly your hair grows. You may need to shave every few weeks to maintain the look.

Can I shave the sides of my head with a trimmer?

You can use a trimmer to shave the sides of your head, but it may not give you as close of a shave as a razor. If you want a clean and smooth look, use a razor.

Hair Care

To keep your mullet looking its best, you'll need to take good care of your hair. Use a shampoo and conditioner that's designed for your hair type, and avoid using too much heat when styling. Trim the hair on top and the back regularly to maintain the shape of your mullet.

Now that you know how to shave side of head for mullet, you're ready to rock this bold and edgy hairstyle. Whether you're going for a classic or modern mullet, shaving the sides of your head is an essential step. Follow these steps and tips to achieve the perfect look.
