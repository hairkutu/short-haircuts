How To Cut Short Hair Yourself Woman A Step By Step Guide

Written by Frank Jul 29, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Are you tired of sitting in a salon for hours and spending a fortune on a simple haircut? Or maybe you just want to take matters into your own hands and try to cut your short hair yourself? Whatever your reason may be, cutting your own hair can be a bit intimidating, especially if you're not quite sure where to start. But don't worry, we've got you covered! In this article, we'll guide you through the process of how to cut short hair yourself woman in a relaxed English language, so you can achieve the perfect haircut without leaving your house.

Description

Before we dive into the step-by-step guide of how to cut short hair yourself woman, let's first talk about what you'll need. Here are the things you'll need:

  • Scissors (preferably hair cutting scissors)
  • Clippers (optional)
  • Comb
  • Hair ties
  • Water spray bottle
  • Mirror

How To

Now that you have all the necessary tools, it's time to start cutting your hair. Here's how:

Step 1: Wash and Dry Your Hair

Start by washing your hair and drying it completely. This will help you see your hair's natural texture and how it falls.

Step 2: Section Your Hair

Use a comb to section your hair into four parts: two in the front and two in the back. Tie each section with a hair tie to keep it out of the way.

Step 3: Cut the Back

Start with the back section of your hair. Use the comb to comb your hair straight down and cut it straight across. Go slow and make small cuts to avoid cutting too much at once.

Step 4: Cut the Sides

Move on to the side sections of your hair. Pull the hair straight out from your head and cut it to the desired length. Repeat on the other side.

Step 5: Cut the Front

Finally, move on to the front sections of your hair. Comb your hair forward and cut it to the desired length. Be careful not to cut too much at once, as this can result in an uneven cut.

Step 6: Check Your Work

Check your work in the mirror to ensure that your hair is even and looks the way you want it to. Make any necessary adjustments.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you achieve the perfect haircut:

  • Start with small cuts and work your way up.
  • Don't cut too much at once.
  • Use hair cutting scissors instead of regular scissors.
  • Keep your hair wet while cutting to make it easier to work with.
  • Use clippers to create a more precise cut.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about cutting short hair:

Can I cut my short hair myself?

Yes, you can. Just make sure you have the necessary tools and follow the steps carefully.

What kind of scissors should I use?

It's best to use hair cutting scissors, as they are sharper and will make a cleaner cut.

How often should I cut my short hair?

It depends on how fast your hair grows and how you want it to look. Generally, it's recommended to cut short hair every 4-6 weeks.

Hair Care

Now that you've cut your hair, it's important to take care of it properly. Here are some tips:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid heat styling tools as much as possible.
  • Use a heat protectant spray if you do use heat styling tools.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair healthy.

There you have it! A step-by-step guide on how to cut short hair yourself woman. With the right tools and a little patience, you can achieve the perfect haircut from the comfort of your own home.
