What Hairstyle Suits A Narrow Face

Written by William Sep 13, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Are you struggling to find the perfect hairstyle for your narrow face? Look no further! In this blog post, we will guide you through the best hairstyles that suit a narrow face shape. We'll cover everything from how to determine if you have a narrow face to the best hairstyles that flatter this facial shape.

What is a Narrow Face?

A narrow face is characterized by a longer length than width, with high cheekbones and a defined jawline. This facial shape is often referred to as a diamond or heart shape. If you have a narrow face, you may find that certain hairstyles can make your face appear even longer and narrower. But don't worry, we've got you covered!

How to Determine if You Have a Narrow Face

If you're not sure if you have a narrow face, here's a simple way to determine your face shape:

  1. Measure the distance across your forehead at the widest point
  2. Measure the distance between your cheekbones at the widest point
  3. Measure the distance between your jawline at the widest point
  4. Measure the length of your face from your hairline to your chin

If the distance between your cheekbones is the widest measurement and your face is longer than it is wide, you likely have a narrow face shape.

Hairstyles that Suit a Narrow Face

Now that you know your face shape, let's look at some hairstyles that suit a narrow face:

1. Bob Haircut

A bob haircut is perfect for a narrow face as it adds width and volume to the sides of the face. A chin-length bob with layers around the face can accentuate your cheekbones and jawline.

2. Side-Swept Bangs

Side-swept bangs are a great option for a narrow face as they create the illusion of width around the forehead. Make sure to keep the rest of your hair long and layered to balance out the narrowness of your face.

3. Soft Waves

Soft waves can add volume and dimension to a narrow face. Use a curling iron or rollers to create loose waves and avoid tight curls that can make your face appear even narrower.

4. Pixie Cut

A pixie cut can work well for a narrow face if it has layers and volume around the crown. This adds width and balance to the face. Avoid a straight, blunt cut with a pixie as it can make your face appear longer.

Tips for Styling Your Hair with a Narrow Face

Here are some additional tips to keep in mind when styling your hair with a narrow face:

  • Avoid styles that are too long and straight
  • Avoid center parts as they can elongate the face
  • Use volume and layers to add width and balance to your face
  • Consider highlights or lowlights to add dimension to your hair
  • Experiment with different styles to find what works best for you

Frequently Asked Questions

1. Can I wear my hair in a bun or ponytail with a narrow face?

Absolutely! Just make sure to add volume to the crown of your head and avoid pulling your hair back too tightly, as this can elongate your face even more.

2. Should I avoid short haircuts with a narrow face?

No, you can still wear short haircuts with a narrow face. Just make sure to add volume and layers to create balance and width.

3. Can I wear a middle part with a narrow face?

Avoid a middle part as it can elongate the face. Instead, try a side part or a deep side part to create the illusion of width around the forehead.

Hair Care for a Narrow Face

In addition to choosing the right hairstyle, it's important to take good care of your hair. Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and avoid damage. Regular trims can also help keep your hair looking its best.

Now that you know which hairstyles suit a narrow face, it's time to book that haircut and rock your new look with confidence!
