Can You Make Straight Hair Curly Without A Perm

Written by Oliver Sep 11, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Are you tired of your straight hair and want to add some curls, but don't want to damage your hair with a perm? You're not alone! Many people want to know if there's a way to make straight hair curly without a perm. The good news is, there are a few ways to achieve curls without the use of chemicals. In this article, we'll explore some of the methods for creating curls without a perm.

Description

Perming is the process of chemically treating hair to create curls or waves. While perms can give you the curls you desire, they can also damage your hair. The chemicals used in perms can be harsh and cause breakage, dryness, and frizz. That's why many people are looking for alternatives to perms to achieve curly hair.

How To Make Straight Hair Curly Without a Perm

Method 1: Braiding

One of the easiest ways to create curls without a perm is by braiding your hair. Here's how:

  1. Wash and condition your hair as usual.
  2. Towel dry your hair until it's damp, but not soaking wet.
  3. Divide your hair into sections and braid each section tightly.
  4. Leave the braids in overnight or until your hair is completely dry.
  5. Take out the braids and use your fingers to separate the curls.
  6. Apply a small amount of hair oil or serum to add shine and reduce frizz.

Method 2: Curling Iron

If you want more control over the size and shape of your curls, you can use a curling iron. Here's how:

  1. Wash and condition your hair as usual.
  2. Blow dry your hair until it's completely dry.
  3. Section your hair and spray each section with heat protectant spray.
  4. Take a small section of hair and wrap it around the curling iron.
  5. Hold for 10-15 seconds and release.
  6. Repeat until all sections of your hair are curled.
  7. Use your fingers to separate the curls and apply a small amount of hairspray to hold the curls in place.

Tips

  • Use a heat protectant spray to prevent damage to your hair when using a curling iron.
  • Apply a small amount of hair oil or serum to add shine and reduce frizz.
  • Don't braid your hair too tightly as this can cause breakage.
  • Don't use a curling iron on wet hair as this can damage your hair.

FAQ

Can you make straight hair curly permanently?

No, you cannot make straight hair curly permanently without the use of chemicals like a perm. However, you can create curls that last for several days using the methods mentioned above.

Will braiding my hair damage it?

No, braiding your hair will not damage it if done correctly. However, braiding your hair too tightly or leaving the braids in for too long can cause breakage.

How long will the curls last?

The longevity of the curls will depend on your hair type and the method you used to create the curls. Generally, braided curls can last for several days, while curls created with a curling iron may last for a day or two.

Hair Care

To keep your hair healthy and prevent damage, it's important to follow a proper hair care routine. This includes washing and conditioning your hair regularly, using a heat protectant spray when using heat tools, and avoiding harsh chemicals like perms. Be sure to also trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends and promote healthy hair growth.
