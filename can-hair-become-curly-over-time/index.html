Can Hair Become Curly Over Time

Written by Robby Jun 28, 2023 · 6 min read

It's a common belief that once your hair texture is set, it's set for life. However, some people have noticed changes in their hair texture over time. One of the most common changes is the development of curly hair. If you're wondering, "can hair become curly over time?" you're in the right place. In this article, we'll explore the causes of curly hair and how to care for it.

What Causes Curly Hair?

Curly hair is caused by the shape of the hair follicle. Straight hair follicles are round, while curly hair follicles are oval or asymmetrical. This asymmetry causes the hair to curve as it grows out of the scalp. You're born with a certain hair follicle shape, which is why some people have naturally curly or straight hair. However, there are a few factors that can cause your hair texture to change over time.

Hormones

Hormones can play a big role in your hair texture. For example, some women notice changes in their hair texture during pregnancy or menopause. This is because hormones can affect the shape of your hair follicles, causing your hair to become curlier or straighter.

Damaged Hair

If you've been using heat styling tools or chemical treatments on your hair for a long time, it's possible that your hair has become damaged. Damaged hair can become frizzy and curly, even if it was originally straight.

Aging

As you age, your hair texture can change. This is because your hair follicles shrink over time, which can cause your hair to become thinner and curlier.

How to Care for Curly Hair

If you've noticed that your hair has become curly over time, it's important to adjust your hair care routine accordingly. Here are some tips for caring for curly hair:

Use a Gentle Shampoo

Curly hair tends to be drier than straight hair, so it's important to use a gentle shampoo that won't strip your hair of its natural oils. Look for a sulfate-free shampoo that's designed for curly hair.

Avoid Heat Styling

Heat styling tools can damage curly hair, so it's best to avoid them whenever possible. If you must use a hair dryer or flat iron, be sure to use a heat protectant spray first.

Use a Wide-Tooth Comb

Curly hair is prone to tangles, so it's important to use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair. Start at the ends of your hair and work your way up to the roots, being careful not to pull or tug on your hair.

Apply a Leave-In Conditioner

Curly hair needs extra moisture, so it's important to use a leave-in conditioner to keep your hair hydrated. Apply the conditioner to damp hair and use your fingers to distribute it evenly.

FAQ

Can Straight Hair Become Curly Naturally?

While it's uncommon, it is possible for straight hair to become curly naturally. This can be caused by hormonal changes, damage to the hair, or simply aging.

Can You Make Straight Hair Curly?

Yes, you can make straight hair curly using heat styling tools or chemical treatments. However, these methods can be damaging to your hair, so it's important to use them sparingly and take good care of your hair.

Conclusion

If you're wondering, "can hair become curly over time?" the answer is yes. While it's not a common occurrence, it's possible for your hair texture to change over time due to hormonal changes, damage, or aging. If you've noticed that your hair has become curly, it's important to adjust your hair care routine accordingly to keep your curls looking their best.
