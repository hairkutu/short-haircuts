How To Make Grey Hair Less Wiry Tips And Tricks

Written by Aprilia Jun 11, 2023 · 9 min read

Grey hair can be beautiful, but it can also be wiry and hard to manage. If you're struggling with wiry grey hair, don't worry – there are plenty of tips and tricks you can use to soften and tame your locks. In this article, we'll share some of our best advice for making grey hair less wiry and more manageable, from hair care tips to styling tricks.

Description

As we age, our hair naturally loses its pigment and turns grey. This is a natural part of the aging process, but it can come with some challenges. Grey hair is often coarser and more wiry than pigmented hair, which can make it difficult to style and manage. However, with the right care and attention, you can make your grey hair softer, smoother, and more manageable.

How to Make Grey Hair Less Wiry: Tips and Tricks

1. Use a Moisturizing Shampoo and Conditioner

The first step in taming wiry grey hair is to use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner. Look for products that are specifically designed for dry or damaged hair, as these will help to hydrate your locks and make them softer and more manageable. Avoid using products that contain sulfates, as these can strip your hair of its natural oils and make it even more wiry.

2. Use a Deep Conditioning Treatment

In addition to using a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner, it's important to use a deep conditioning treatment once a week. This will help to nourish and hydrate your hair, making it softer and more manageable. Look for a treatment that contains nourishing ingredients like argan oil, coconut oil, or shea butter.

3. Use a Wide-Tooth Comb

When you're styling your hair, be sure to use a wide-tooth comb instead of a brush. This will help to prevent breakage and damage, and will also help to detangle your hair without causing it to become more wiry.

4. Avoid Heat Styling

If possible, avoid using heat styling tools like flat irons, curling irons, and blow dryers. These tools can dry out your hair and make it more wiry and difficult to manage. If you must use heat styling tools, be sure to use a heat protectant spray and keep the heat setting low.

5. Use a Smoothing Serum

To help tame wiry grey hair, use a smoothing serum or oil. Apply a small amount to your hair while it's still damp, focusing on the ends and any particularly wiry areas. This will help to smooth down the cuticle and make your hair softer and more manageable.

Step by Step Guide

Step 1: Wash Your Hair with a Moisturizing Shampoo and Conditioner

Start by washing your hair with a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner. Be sure to rinse your hair thoroughly to remove all product residue.

Step 2: Apply a Deep Conditioning Treatment

Once a week, apply a deep conditioning treatment to your hair. Leave it on for the recommended amount of time (usually 5-10 minutes), then rinse thoroughly.

Step 3: Detangle Your Hair with a Wide-Tooth Comb

After washing your hair, use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your locks. Start at the ends and work your way up to the roots, being careful not to tug or pull on your hair.

Step 4: Let Your Hair Air Dry

If possible, let your hair air dry instead of using heat styling tools. This will help to prevent damage and keep your hair soft and manageable.

Step 5: Apply a Smoothing Serum

Finally, apply a smoothing serum or oil to your hair to help tame any wiry areas. Use a small amount and focus on the ends and any particularly unruly sections.

Tips for Making Grey Hair Less Wiry

Here are a few additional tips to help make your grey hair more manageable:

  • Get regular trims to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.
  • Avoid using harsh chemicals and dyes, as these can dry out your hair and make it more wiry.
  • Eat a healthy diet rich in vitamins and minerals to promote hair health.
  • Consider using a silk pillowcase, as this can help to prevent frizz and breakage.

FAQs

Why is grey hair wiry?

Grey hair is often wiry because it contains less natural oils and moisture than pigmented hair. This can make it more prone to dryness and breakage, which can make it difficult to manage.

Can I dye my grey hair to make it softer?

Dyeing your grey hair can actually make it more wiry and difficult to manage. Instead, focus on using the tips and tricks outlined in this article to make your natural grey hair softer and more manageable.

How often should I deep condition my hair?

You should deep condition your hair once a week to keep it soft, hydrated, and healthy.

Hair Care for Grey Hair: Final Thoughts

Making grey hair less wiry and more manageable is all about taking care of your hair with the right products and techniques. Use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner, deep condition your hair once a week, and avoid heat styling tools whenever possible. With a little bit of TLC, you can enjoy soft, smooth, and manageable grey locks.
