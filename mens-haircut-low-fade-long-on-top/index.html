The Mens Haircut Low Fade Long On Top A Relaxed Guide For 2023

If you're looking for a stylish haircut that's easy to maintain, the mens haircut low fade long on top could be the perfect choice for you. This haircut is a classic that has been around for years, but it's still popular in 2023. In this guide, we'll take you through everything you need to know about this haircut, including how to get it, how to style it, and how to take care of it.

How to Get a Mens Haircut Low Fade Long on Top

The first step to getting a mens haircut low fade long on top is to find a barber or hairdresser who is experienced in this style. You'll want to bring in a picture of what you're looking for to help your stylist understand your vision. The haircut itself involves cutting the sides and back of your head short, while leaving the hair on top longer. The length of the hair on top can vary depending on your personal preference.

The key to this style is the low fade, which is a gradual taper from the longer hair on top to the shorter hair on the sides and back. This fade can be blended smoothly or have a more defined edge, depending on your preference. Overall, the haircut is versatile and can be customized to suit your face shape and hair type.

Step-by-Step Guide to Cutting a Low Fade Long on Top

If you're a hairdresser or barber looking to learn how to cut a low fade long on top, here's a step-by-step guide:

  1. Start by washing and drying the hair, then section it off into the top and sides/back.
  2. Using clippers or scissors, cut the sides and back to your desired length, starting with a longer guard and gradually decreasing to create the fade.
  3. Blend the fade using a comb and clippers, if necessary.
  4. Cut the hair on top to your desired length, taking care to leave it longer than the sides and back.
  5. Blend the top and sides/back using scissors or a razor, if necessary.
  6. Style the hair on top using product, such as pomade or wax.

Tips for Styling Your Low Fade Long on Top

To style your low fade long on top, follow these tips:

  • Use a small amount of product to avoid weighing down your hair.
  • Experiment with different styles, such as slicked back or messy.
  • Consider getting a trim every 4-6 weeks to maintain the shape of the haircut.

FAQs About the Mens Haircut Low Fade Long on Top

What face shapes does the low fade long on top work for?

The low fade long on top can work for most face shapes, but it's particularly flattering for those with square or oval faces.

What hair types does the low fade long on top work for?

This haircut can work for most hair types, but it's particularly well-suited for those with thick, straight hair.

What products should I use to style my low fade long on top?

You can use a variety of products to style your hair, such as pomade, wax, or clay. Experiment with different types to find what works best for you.

Hair Care Tips for a Low Fade Long on Top

To keep your low fade long on top looking its best, follow these hair care tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo.
  • Avoid using hot tools, such as hair dryers or straighteners, too often.
  • Use a leave-in conditioner to keep your hair hydrated and healthy.
  • Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a hair product with SPF.

With these tips and tricks, you can rock the mens haircut low fade long on top with confidence and style. Whether you're going for a sleek, professional look or a relaxed, casual vibe, this haircut is a classic that will never go out of style.
