How To Style A Lob With Wavy Hair Tips And Tricks

Written by Aprilia Jul 18, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Are you looking for a chic and versatile hairstyle that will complement your wavy hair? Look no further than the lob, a stylish and trendy haircut that has taken the fashion world by storm. Whether you have long, medium, or short wavy hair, the lob is an excellent choice that can be customized to suit your unique style and personality. In this article, we will share some tips and tricks on how to style a lob with wavy hair, so you can look effortlessly beautiful and confident every day.

How to Get the Perfect Lob for Your Wavy Hair

Before we dive into styling tips, let's talk about how to get the perfect lob for your wavy hair. Here are some things to keep in mind:

  • Choose the right length: The lob should be long enough to reach your collarbone or slightly below, depending on your preference.
  • Ask for layers: Layers can add volume and movement to your wavy hair, making it look more dynamic and interesting.
  • Consider face shape: The lob can be customized to suit different face shapes, so talk to your hairstylist about what will work best for you.

Step-by-Step Guide to Styling a Lob with Wavy Hair

Now that you have the perfect lob for your wavy hair, it's time to style it. Here's a step-by-step guide:

  1. Start with clean, damp hair. Apply a heat protectant spray to protect your hair from damage.
  2. Blow-dry your hair using a round brush. This will help smooth out any frizz and add volume to your roots.
  3. Once your hair is dry, use a curling iron or wand to create loose waves. Start at the mid-lengths of your hair and wrap small sections around the barrel, holding for a few seconds before releasing.
  4. After curling your entire head, flip your hair over and tousle it with your fingers to break up the curls and create a more natural, effortless look.
  5. Finish with a light-hold hairspray to keep your waves in place.

Tips for Styling a Lob with Wavy Hair

Here are some additional tips to help you style your lob with wavy hair:

  • Use a sea salt spray to enhance your natural waves and add texture to your hair.
  • Experiment with different partings to find the most flattering look for your face shape.
  • Accessorize your lob with hair clips, headbands, or scarves for a stylish and trendy touch.

FAQ: Frequently Asked Questions

Here are some common questions about styling a lob with wavy hair:

Q: How often should I wash my hair?

A: It depends on your hair type and lifestyle. If you have oily hair or work out frequently, you may need to wash your hair every day or every other day. If you have dry or curly hair, you can go longer between washes.

Q: Can I style my lob with wavy hair without heat?

A: Yes! You can create waves in your hair by braiding it overnight or twisting it into buns while it's damp. This will give you a more natural, beachy look without using heat.

Hair Care for Lobs with Wavy Hair

To keep your lob looking healthy and beautiful, here are some hair care tips:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping your hair of natural oils.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as this can cause damage and dryness.
  • Apply a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair moisturized and nourished.

With these tips and tricks, you can style your lob with wavy hair like a pro. Whether you're heading to work, a party, or the beach, your hair will look effortlessly beautiful and stylish.
