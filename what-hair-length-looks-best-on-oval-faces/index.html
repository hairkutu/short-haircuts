What Hair Length Looks Best On Oval Faces

Written by Christine Jun 11, 2023 Â· 7 min read

If you have an oval face shape, you're in luck! You have one of the most versatile face shapes when it comes to hairstyles. In this article, we'll explore the different hair lengths that look best on oval faces and provide tips on how to style and care for your hair.

Table of Contents

  • Short Hair
  • Medium Hair
  • Long Hair
  • How to Style Your Hair
  • Hair Care Tips
  • FAQ

Short Hair

For oval faces, short haircuts that hit at or above the chin are the most flattering. This length accentuates your cheekbones and jawline while softening your forehead. A pixie cut or a bob with choppy layers are great options for oval faces.

Short hair is also low maintenance and easy to style. You can add texture with a sea salt spray or create volume with a volumizing mousse. With short hair, you can also experiment with bold hair colors or highlights.


Medium Hair

Medium length hair that falls between the chin and shoulders is also a great option for oval faces. This length frames your face without overwhelming it. A lob or a shag haircut with choppy layers are perfect for oval faces.

With medium length hair, you have more styling options. You can create loose waves with a curling iron or straighten it for a sleek look. You can also experiment with different partings to change up your look.


Long Hair

If you have an oval face, you can also pull off long hair. However, it's important to avoid haircuts that add too much volume on top as this can elongate your face shape. Stick with long layers or a blunt cut to keep your hair looking balanced.

With long hair, you can create different styles such as braids, updos, and curls. You can also experiment with different hair accessories such as headbands, clips, and barrettes.


How to Style Your Hair

Styling your hair depends on your hair length and texture. Here are some styling tips for different hair lengths:

  • Short Hair: Use a sea salt spray for texture, or a volumizing mousse for volume. Style with your fingers for a messy, undone look.
  • Medium Hair: Use a curling iron for loose waves or straighten it for a sleek look. Experiment with different partings to change up your look.
  • Long Hair: Create different styles such as braids, updos, and curls. Use hair accessories such as headbands, clips, and barrettes to add some flair.

Hair Care Tips

Here are some hair care tips to keep your hair looking healthy and shiny:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils.
  • Avoid heat styling tools as much as possible to prevent damage to your hair.
  • Apply a hair mask once a week to nourish your hair.
  • Trim your hair every 6-8 weeks to prevent split ends.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about hair lengths for oval faces:

  • Can oval faces pull off bangs? Yes, oval faces can pull off bangs. However, it's important to choose the right type of bangs for your face shape. Side-swept bangs or curtain bangs are great options for oval faces.
  • What hair color looks best on oval faces? Oval faces can pull off any hair color. However, it's important to choose a hair color that complements your skin tone.
  • Can oval faces pull off a center part? Yes, oval faces can pull off a center part. However, it's important to choose the right hair length and texture for a center part.

In conclusion, oval faces are fortunate to be able to pull off a variety of hair lengths. Whether you prefer short, medium, or long hair, there's a style that will flatter your face shape. With the right styling and hair care, you can keep your hair looking healthy and shiny.
