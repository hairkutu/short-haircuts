How To Cut Men s Thick Coarse Hair A Complete Guide

If you're a barber or just looking to cut your own hair, dealing with thick coarse hair can be challenging. But with the right technique and tools, you can achieve a great haircut that looks great and is easy to maintain. In this article, we'll show you how to cut men's thick coarse hair step by step, answer common questions, and provide tips for hair care.

How to Cut Men's Thick Coarse Hair

Before you start cutting, it's important to have the right tools. You'll need a clipper, scissors, a comb, and a hair trimmer. Once you have your tools ready, follow these simple steps:

Step 1: Wash the Hair

Make sure the hair is clean and dry before you start cutting. This will make it easier to work with and help you achieve a more even cut. Use a good quality shampoo and conditioner to keep the hair healthy and hydrated.

Step 2: Section the Hair

Divide the hair into sections using your comb. Start at the back of the head and work your way forward. Use clips to hold each section in place while you work on the others. This will make it easier to achieve an even cut and prevent you from cutting too much at once.

Step 3: Cut the Sides

Using your clipper, start at the bottom of the hairline and work your way up. Use a comb to lift the hair as you go, and cut in a straight line. Repeat on the other side, making sure to keep the length even. Use a higher guard if you want to leave more length, or a lower guard if you want a shorter cut.

Step 4: Cut the Top

Using your scissors, cut the top of the hair in a straight line. Use a comb to lift the hair as you go, and make sure to keep the length even. If you want to add texture, you can use thinning shears to remove some of the bulk.

Step 5: Blend the Layers

Using your hair trimmer, blend the layers together to create a seamless look. Start at the sides and work your way up, using a comb to lift the hair as you go. Once you're done, check for any uneven spots and trim as needed.

Step by Step Guide

Here's a more detailed step by step guide on how to cut men's thick coarse hair:

Step 1: Prepare the Hair

Wash the hair with a good quality shampoo and conditioner to remove any dirt or oil. Towel dry the hair and comb it out to remove any tangles.

Step 2: Section the Hair

Divide the hair into sections using your comb. Start at the back of the head and work your way forward, using clips to hold each section in place while you work on the others.

Step 3: Cut the Sides

Using your clipper, start at the bottom of the hairline and work your way up. Use a comb to lift the hair as you go, and cut in a straight line. Repeat on the other side, making sure to keep the length even.

Step 4: Cut the Top

Using your scissors, cut the top of the hair in a straight line. Use a comb to lift the hair as you go, and make sure to keep the length even. If you want to add texture, you can use thinning shears to remove some of the bulk.

Step 5: Blend the Layers

Using your hair trimmer, blend the layers together to create a seamless look. Start at the sides and work your way up, using a comb to lift the hair as you go. Once you're done, check for any uneven spots and trim as needed.

Tips for Cutting Thick Coarse Hair

Here are some tips to help you achieve a great haircut:

  • Use a high-quality clipper and scissors that are sharp and well-maintained.
  • Start with a higher guard and work your way down if you want a shorter cut.
  • Take your time and don't rush the process.
  • Use a comb to lift the hair as you go, and make sure to keep the length even.
  • Blend the layers together to create a seamless look.

FAQ

What is the best tool for cutting thick coarse hair?

A good quality clipper and scissors are essential for cutting thick coarse hair. Make sure they are sharp and well-maintained for best results.

How often should I cut my thick coarse hair?

It depends on how fast your hair grows and how quickly it gets unruly. Generally, once every 4-6 weeks is a good rule of thumb.

Can I cut my own thick coarse hair?

Yes, but it can be challenging. Make sure you have the right tools and follow the steps carefully to achieve the best results.

Hair Care for Thick Coarse Hair

To keep your thick coarse hair looking great, it's important to take good care of it. Here are some tips:

  • Use a good quality shampoo and conditioner to keep the hair clean and hydrated.
  • Avoid using hot water, which can dry out the hair.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep the hair healthy and nourished.
  • Avoid using too many styling products, which can weigh down the hair.

By following these tips and the steps outlined in this article, you can achieve a great haircut that looks great and is easy to maintain. Good luck!
