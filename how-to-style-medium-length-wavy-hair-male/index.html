How To Style Medium Length Wavy Hair For Men

Written by Daniel Aug 10, 2023 Â· 9 min read

If you have medium length wavy hair, you are in luck as this hair type is one of the most versatile. It can be styled in various ways to achieve different looks. In this article, we will show you how to style medium length wavy hair for men in a relaxed and easy to understand language. We will cover the basics, step-by-step process, tips, and hair care to help you achieve the perfect look.

  2. How to Style Medium Length Wavy Hair for Men

The Basics

Before we dive into the styling process, it's important to understand the basics of medium length wavy hair. This hair type has natural waves that can be loose or tight. It's thicker than straight hair, which means it can hold styles longer. However, it can also be prone to frizz and tangles. To keep your hair healthy and manageable, it's important to use the right products and techniques.

How to Style Medium Length Wavy Hair for Men

There are several ways to style medium length wavy hair for men. Here are some popular options:

1. Messy Wavy Hair

For a casual and effortless look, try the messy wavy hair style. This look works best for those with longer hair. To achieve this look, apply a matte hair product to your damp hair and scrunch your hair with your fingers. Let your hair dry naturally, and use your fingers to shape your hair.

2. Side Part Wavy Hair

The side part wavy hair is a classic and timeless look that works well for any occasion. To achieve this look, apply a light hold hair product to your damp hair and comb it to the side. Use bobby pins or hair clips to keep your hair in place, and let it dry naturally.

3. Beachy Waves

The beachy waves style is perfect for those who want to add volume and texture to their hair. To achieve this look, apply a sea salt spray to your damp hair and scrunch your hair with your fingers. Use a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer to enhance the waves.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here is a step-by-step guide on how to style medium length wavy hair for men:

Step 1: Wash your hair

Start by washing your hair with a shampoo and conditioner designed for wavy hair. Make sure to rinse your hair thoroughly to avoid product buildup.

Step 2: Apply hair product

Apply a hair product that suits your desired style. For a messy look, use a matte hair product. For a sleek look, use a light hold hair product. For beachy waves, use a sea salt spray.

Step 3: Style your hair

Use your fingers or a comb to style your hair according to your desired look. For messy waves, scrunch your hair with your fingers. For a side part, comb your hair to the side. For beachy waves, use a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer.

Tips for Styling Medium Length Wavy Hair for Men

Here are some tips to help you achieve the perfect style:

1. Use the right hair products

Choose hair products that are designed for wavy hair. Look for products that provide hold, texture, and volume.

2. Don't overwash your hair

Washing your hair too often can strip your hair of its natural oils, which can lead to dryness and frizz. Try to wash your hair every other day or every two days.

3. Use a wide-tooth comb

Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair. This will help to prevent breakage and damage.

4. Avoid heat styling

Avoid using heat styling tools like flat irons and curling irons as much as possible. Heat can damage your hair and cause frizz.

FAQs

1. Can I use a hair dryer on my wavy hair?

Yes, you can use a hair dryer on your wavy hair. Use a diffuser attachment to enhance your natural waves.

2. How often should I trim my hair?

Trim your hair every six to eight weeks to prevent split ends and promote healthy growth.

3. How do I prevent frizz?

Prevent frizz by using a hair product that provides moisture and by avoiding heat styling tools. Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair, and avoid rubbing your hair with a towel.

Hair Care Tips for Medium Length Wavy Hair for Men

Here are some hair care tips to keep your medium length wavy hair healthy and manageable:

1. Use a shampoo and conditioner designed for wavy hair

Look for products that provide moisture and enhance your natural waves.

2. Avoid hot showers

Hot showers can strip your hair of its natural oils, which can lead to dryness and frizz. Use lukewarm water instead.

3. Use a hair mask once a week

Use a hair mask once a week to provide extra moisture to your hair.

4. Protect your hair from the sun

Wear a hat or use a hair product that contains SPF to protect your hair from the sun's harmful rays.

By following these tips and techniques, you can achieve the perfect style for your medium length wavy hair. Experiment with different looks and have fun with your hair.
