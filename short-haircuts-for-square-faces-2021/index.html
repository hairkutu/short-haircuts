Description

Short hairstyles have been a timeless trend that has been around for decades. It is a style that can be worn by people of all ages and is perfect for those who have busy lifestyles. If you have a square face, it is important to choose a haircut that will flatter your features. In this article, we will discuss the best short haircuts for square faces in 2021.

How to Choose Short Haircut for Square Faces 2021

Choosing the right short haircut for a square face can be a daunting task. It is important to consider the shape of your face, your hair texture, and your personal style. Here are some tips on how to choose the best short haircut for square faces in 2021:

  • Look for hairstyles that have soft layers around the face, which will help to soften the angles of your face shape.
  • Consider asymmetrical cuts, which can add more dimension to your face and draw attention away from your jawline.
  • Avoid blunt cuts, which can make your face appear more angular.
  • Consider pixie cuts, which are perfect for square faces and can be styled in a variety of ways.

Step by Step Guide in Getting Short Haircut for Square Faces 2021

Now that you know how to choose the best short haircut for square faces, it's time to get your hair cut. Here is a step-by-step guide on how to get the perfect short haircut:

  1. Consult with a professional hairstylist to determine the best haircut for your face shape.
  2. Wash and dry your hair before your appointment.
  3. Bring pictures of hairstyles you like to show your hairstylist.
  4. Be open to suggestions from your hairstylist.
  5. After your haircut, ask your hairstylist for tips on how to style your hair at home.

Tips in Maintaining Short Haircut for Square Faces 2021

Maintaining a short haircut for square faces can be easy with the right tips. Here are some tips on how to keep your short haircut looking great:

  • Use a good quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy.
  • Invest in styling products, such as mousse, gel, or hairspray, to help hold your style in place.
  • Get regular trims to maintain your haircut's shape.
  • Consider adding highlights or lowlights to add dimension to your hair.

FAQs About Short Haircuts for Square Faces 2021

Here are some frequently asked questions about short haircuts for square faces:

Q: Can people with square faces wear bangs?

A: Yes, people with square faces can wear bangs. However, it is important to choose bangs that are longer on the sides and shorter in the middle to help soften the angles of your face.

Q: What should I do if I don't like my new short haircut?

A: If you don't like your new short haircut, talk to your hairstylist about what you don't like and how it can be fixed. They may be able to adjust the cut or recommend a different style that will better suit your face shape.

Q: Can I wear a pixie cut if I have thick hair?

A: Yes, people with thick hair can wear a pixie cut. However, it is important to choose a style that will work with your hair texture and to use styling products to help hold your style in place.

Hair Care for Short Haircut for Square Faces 2021

Keeping your short haircut looking great requires proper hair care. Here are some tips on how to care for your short haircut:

  • Use a good quality shampoo and conditioner that is appropriate for your hair type.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your hair.
  • Avoid using hot tools, such as flat irons or curling irons, too often.
  • Get regular trims to maintain your haircut's shape.

With the right short haircut, hair care, and styling, people with square faces can look amazing in 2021. Follow these tips to find the best short haircut for your face shape and keep it looking great all year long.
