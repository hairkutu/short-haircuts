How To Style Your Hair Up Guys A Step By Step Guide

Written by Luffy Aug 03, 2023 · 6 min read

Are you tired of the same old hairstyle? Want to switch it up and try something new? Styling your hair up is a great way to add some variety to your look. Whether you have short or long hair, there are plenty of ways to style your hair up. In this article, we’ll go over some tips and tricks on how to style your hair up guys.

Table of Contents

  • How to Style Your Hair Up
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Styling Your Hair Up
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care Tips

How to Style Your Hair Up

Before we get into the step-by-step guide, let’s first go over some of the different ways you can style your hair up. Here are some popular options:

  • Slicked back
  • Pompadour
  • Quiff
  • Man bun
  • Top knot
  • Messy bun

Each of these styles has its own unique look and can be achieved with different hair lengths and textures. Now, let’s move on to the step-by-step guide.

Step-by-Step Guide

1. Start with clean, damp hair. It’s best to style your hair up when it’s slightly damp as it will be easier to manage.

2. Apply a styling product. Depending on your hair type and the style you’re going for, you may want to use a pomade, wax, or gel. Start with a small amount and gradually add more as needed.

3. Use a comb or your fingers to style your hair into the desired shape. For a slicked back look, comb your hair straight back. For a quiff or pompadour, comb your hair up and back.

4. Use a blow dryer to set your hair in place. This step is optional, but it can help your hair stay in place throughout the day.

5. Finish off with a hairspray. This will help hold your hair in place and prevent it from falling flat throughout the day.

Tips for Styling Your Hair Up

1. Experiment with different products. Not all styling products work for all hair types, so it’s important to find one that works well for you.

2. Don’t be afraid to use a little heat. If your hair is particularly stubborn, using a blow dryer or flat iron can help set it in place.

3. Use a high-quality hairspray. This will help keep your hair in place all day without making it feel too stiff or crunchy.

FAQs

Q: Can I style my hair up if it’s short?

A: Absolutely! There are plenty of ways to style short hair up, such as with a messy quiff or a textured crop.

Q: How often should I wash my hair?

A: This depends on your hair type and how oily it gets. Generally, it’s best to wash your hair every 2-3 days.

Hair Care Tips

1. Use a sulfate-free shampoo. This will help prevent your hair from drying out and becoming brittle.

2. Condition your hair regularly. This will help keep it soft and manageable.

3. Avoid using hot tools too often. Excessive heat can damage your hair and cause split ends.

4. Get regular haircuts. This will help keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.

Styling your hair up is a fun and easy way to switch up your look. With these tips and tricks, you’ll be able to achieve the perfect style in no time. Remember to take care of your hair by using high-quality products and getting regular haircuts. Happy styling!
