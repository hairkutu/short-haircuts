Easy Hairstyles For Super Thick Hair

Written by Robby May 23, 2023 Â· 10 min read

When you have super thick hair, it can be a blessing and a curse. On one hand, you have an abundance of hair to work with. On the other hand, it can be overwhelming and difficult to manage. That's why we've compiled a list of easy hairstyles for super thick hair that you can try out. From braids to buns, there's something for everyone!

How to Style Super Thick Hair

Styling super thick hair might seem like a daunting task, but it doesn't have to be. The key is to work with your hair's natural texture and volume. Here are some general tips to keep in mind:

  • Use hair products that are specifically designed for thick hair
  • Avoid using heavy products that can weigh your hair down
  • Blow dry your hair in sections to ensure that it's fully dry
  • Use a round brush to add volume and shape to your hair
  • Don't be afraid to experiment with different styles and techniques

Braids for Super Thick Hair

Braids are a great option for super thick hair because they can help keep your hair in place and prevent it from getting tangled. Here are some easy braids to try:

  • Fishtail braid - this braid looks intricate but is actually quite easy to do. Simply divide your hair into two sections, take a small piece from the outside of one section and cross it over to the other section. Repeat until you reach the end of your hair and secure with an elastic.
  • Dutch braid - this braid is similar to a French braid, but instead of crossing the strands over each other, you cross them under. This creates a braid that sits on top of your hair instead of being woven into it.
  • Side braid - this braid is perfect for a casual day out. Simply gather your hair to one side and braid it down to the end. Secure with an elastic and loosen the braid for a more relaxed look.

Buns for Super Thick Hair

Buns are a classic hairstyle that work well for super thick hair because they can help keep your hair off your face and neck. Here are some easy buns to try:

  • Messy bun - this bun is perfect for a lazy day. Simply gather your hair into a high ponytail, twist it around and secure with an elastic. Pull out a few strands to create a messy look.
  • Topknot - this bun is sleek and sophisticated. Gather your hair into a high ponytail, twist it around and secure with an elastic. Use bobby pins to tuck in any loose strands.
  • Braided bun - this bun is a combination of a braid and a bun. Start by braiding your hair and then wrap the braid around into a bun shape. Secure with bobby pins.

Ponytails for Super Thick Hair

Ponytails are a classic hairstyle that work well for super thick hair because they can help keep your hair off your face and neck. Here are some easy ponytails to try:

  • High ponytail - this ponytail is great for a workout or a hot day. Simply gather your hair into a high ponytail and secure with an elastic.
  • Braided ponytail - this ponytail is a combination of a braid and a ponytail. Start by braiding your hair and then gather it into a ponytail. Secure with an elastic.
  • Low ponytail - this ponytail is sleek and sophisticated. Gather your hair into a low ponytail and secure with an elastic. Use a small piece of hair to wrap around the elastic and secure with a bobby pin.

Tips for Styling Super Thick Hair

Styling super thick hair can be a challenge, but with a few tips and tricks, you can make it easier. Here are some tips to keep in mind:

  • Invest in a good quality hairbrush or comb to help detangle your hair
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy and moisturized
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends
  • Avoid using hot tools like flat irons and curling irons too often, as they can damage your hair
  • Experiment with different hair products to find what works best for your hair type

FAQs About Super Thick Hair

Q: How do I prevent my super thick hair from getting tangled?

A: Use a good quality hairbrush or comb to help detangle your hair. Start at the ends and work your way up to the roots, using gentle strokes to avoid pulling or breaking your hair.

Q: Can I still have long hair if I have super thick hair?

A: Absolutely! Long hair can look beautiful on super thick hair. Just be sure to keep it trimmed regularly to prevent split ends and use deep conditioning treatments to keep it healthy.

Q: How do I style my super thick hair for a special occasion?

A: Experiment with different styles to find what works best for you. Consider hiring a professional stylist or watching online tutorials for inspiration.

Hair Care for Super Thick Hair

Caring for super thick hair requires a little extra effort, but it's worth it to keep your hair healthy and looking its best. Here are some tips to keep in mind:

  • Use a shampoo and conditioner that are specifically designed for thick hair
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy and moisturized
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends
  • Avoid using hot tools like flat irons and curling irons too often, as they can damage your hair
  • Avoid using heavy products that can weigh your hair down

With these easy hairstyles for super thick hair and tips for styling and care, you'll be able to manage your hair with ease. Remember to experiment with different styles and techniques to find what works best for you, and don't be afraid to seek professional help if needed. With a little effort, you can have healthy, beautiful hair that you're proud of!
