Description

Having a square face shape means that your forehead, cheekbones, and jawline are all about the same width. While this face shape is often considered to be strong and angular, it can also appear harsh or boxy without the right hairstyle. If you're a female with a square face shape and you're considering a short haircut, there are a variety of styles that can soften your features and enhance your natural beauty.


How to Determine Your Face Shape

The first step in choosing the best short hairstyle for your square face shape is to determine your face shape. Here's how:

Step 1:

Pull your hair back away from your face and stand in front of a mirror.

Step 2:

Use a washable marker or lipstick to trace the outline of your face onto the mirror.

Step 3:

Step back and observe the shape you've drawn. Is it oval, round, square, heart-shaped, or diamond-shaped?


Step by Step Guide for Choosing the Best Short Hairstyle for Square Faces Female

Now that you've determined that you have a square face shape, it's time to choose the best short hairstyle to flatter your features. Here are some tips:

Step 1:

Consider a pixie cut. This short hairstyle can help to soften your features and draw attention to your eyes and cheekbones.

Step 2:

Opt for a side-swept bang. Bangs that are swept to the side can help to break up the width of your forehead and add softness to your face.

Step 3:

Try a layered bob. This hairstyle can create movement and texture around your face, and the layers can help to disguise any sharp angles.

Step 4:

Avoid blunt cuts. These can make your face appear even more angular.


Tips for Maintaining Your Short Hairstyle

Now that you've chosen your short hairstyle, it's important to take care of it to ensure it looks its best. Here are some tips:

Tip 1:

Visit your stylist regularly for trims. This will help to maintain the shape of your haircut.

Tip 2:

Use products designed for your hair type. If you have fine hair, for example, look for volumizing products that can help to add body and lift.

Tip 3:

Experiment with different styling techniques. Play around with a curling iron or flat iron to create different looks.

Tip 4:

Protect your hair from heat damage. Use a heat protectant spray before using hot tools on your hair.


FAQ About Short Hairstyles for Square Faces Female

Q: Can a square-faced female wear a bob?

A: Yes! A layered bob can be a great option for a square face shape.

Q: Should I avoid bangs if I have a square face shape?

A: No, but you may want to opt for side-swept bangs rather than blunt bangs that hit straight across your forehead.

Q: Can I add highlights to my short hair?

A: Absolutely! Highlights can add dimension and texture to your short hairstyle.


Hair Care Tips for Short Hairstyles

Tip 1:

Wash your hair every other day to avoid stripping it of its natural oils.

Tip 2:

Invest in a good dry shampoo to refresh your hair between washes.

Tip 3:

Avoid using too much heat on your hair. If you do use hot tools, use a heat protectant spray.

Tip 4:

Consider using a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy and hydrated.

With these tips and tricks, you can rock a fabulous short hairstyle that flatters your square face shape and shows off your unique sense of style!
