How To Cut Your Hair Short Guys A Step By Step Guide

Written by Petter May 23, 2023 Â· 7 min read

If you're looking to change up your hairstyle and go for a shorter cut, you may be wondering how to cut your hair short guys. While it may seem daunting, cutting your own hair can be a simple and rewarding experience. In this guide, we'll walk you through the steps to achieve a short haircut that suits your style and personality.

Description

Before we dive into the how-to, let's talk about why you may want to cut your hair short. Short hair can be low maintenance, easy to style, and can give you a fresh, new look. Whether you're tired of longer hair or simply want to try something different, a short haircut can be a great option.

How-to

Step 1: Gather Your Tools

Before you start cutting, make sure you have all the necessary tools. You'll need a sharp pair of scissors or clippers, a comb, and a mirror. You may also want to have a spray bottle of water on hand to dampen your hair as you cut.

Step 2: Decide on Your Style

There are many different styles of short haircuts, from a classic buzz cut to a more textured, layered look. Take some time to browse online and find a style that appeals to you. You may also want to consult with a hairstylist to get their opinion on what will work best for your hair type and face shape.

Step 3: Start Cutting

Begin at the sides of your head, using the clippers or scissors to trim the hair to your desired length. Work your way around the sides and back of your head, using the comb to guide your cutting. Take your time and make sure you're cutting evenly on both sides.

For the top of your head, you may want to use scissors to create a more textured look. Start at the front and work your way back, cutting small sections at a time. Use the comb to lift the hair and cut at an angle to create layers.

Step 4: Refine Your Cut

Once you've finished cutting, take a step back and evaluate your work. Use the mirror to check the back of your head and make any necessary adjustments. You may want to trim the hair around your ears or neckline for a clean, polished look.

Tips

  • Start with a longer length and gradually work your way shorter to avoid cutting too much off at once
  • Use caution when using clippers around the ears and neckline to avoid injury
  • Consider using a styling product, such as pomade or wax, to add texture and hold to your hair
  • If you're unsure about cutting your hair yourself, consider going to a professional stylist for a consultation or haircut

FAQ

Can I cut my hair with just scissors?

Yes, you can cut your hair with just scissors. However, using clippers can make the process quicker and more precise.

How often should I cut my short hair?

Short haircuts typically require more frequent maintenance than longer styles. You may need to trim your hair every 4-6 weeks to maintain your desired length and style.

Can I change my short haircut if I don't like it?

Yes, hair grows back! If you're not happy with your short haircut, you can always grow it out or try a different style.

Hair Care

After cutting your hair, it's important to take care of it to maintain its health and appearance. Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair clean and moisturized. Avoid using too much heat styling or harsh chemicals, which can damage the hair. And don't forget to schedule regular trims to keep your short haircut looking fresh.

Now that you know how to cut your hair short guys, it's time to give it a try! With a little practice and patience, you can achieve a stylish, low-maintenance haircut that suits your personality and lifestyle.
