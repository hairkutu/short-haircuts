How To Grow Out Thick Frizzy Hair Tips And Tricks

Written by Aprilia May 14, 2023 · 7 min read

Are you tired of dealing with frizzy hair every day? Do you wish you could grow out your thick hair without it turning into a wild mess? Well, you’re in luck! In this article, we’ll provide you with tips and tricks on how to grow out thick frizzy hair. From hair care to lifestyle changes, we’ve got you covered.

How to

Before we dive into the tips and tricks, let’s first talk about how to grow out thick frizzy hair. Growing out your hair requires patience and commitment. It won’t happen overnight, but with the right hair care routine and lifestyle changes, you can achieve the long and luscious locks you’ve always wanted.

Step 1: Proper Hair Care

The first step to growing out your thick frizzy hair is to establish a proper hair care routine. This means using the right shampoo and conditioner for your hair type and texture. Look for products that are specifically formulated for frizzy hair, such as those with smoothing or hydrating properties. Avoid using hot tools such as flat irons and curling irons, as these can cause further damage and frizz.

Step 2: Lifestyle Changes

In addition to proper hair care, lifestyle changes can also help promote hair growth. Eating a balanced diet that is rich in vitamins and minerals, such as biotin and iron, can help nourish your hair from the inside out. Drinking plenty of water and getting enough sleep can also aid in hair growth.

Step 3: Regular Trims

While it may seem counterintuitive, getting regular trims can actually help promote hair growth. Trimming off split ends can prevent further damage and breakage, allowing your hair to grow longer and healthier.

Tips

Now that you know how to grow out thick frizzy hair, here are some additional tips to help you along the way.

Tip 1: Use a Hair Mask

Using a hair mask once a week can help hydrate and nourish your hair, reducing frizz and promoting growth. Look for hair masks that are specifically formulated for frizzy hair, and leave them on for at least 20 minutes before rinsing out.

Tip 2: Avoid Overwashing

Washing your hair too frequently can strip it of its natural oils, causing it to become dry and frizzy. Aim to wash your hair every other day, or even every three days if possible.

Tip 3: Use a Wide-Tooth Comb

Using a wide-tooth comb can help prevent breakage and damage, reducing frizz and promoting growth. Start at the ends of your hair and work your way up, gently detangling any knots or tangles.

FAQ

Q: How long will it take to grow out my hair?

A: Hair growth varies from person to person, but on average, hair grows about half an inch per month. With proper hair care and lifestyle changes, you can expect to see results within a few months to a year.

Q: Can I still use hot tools on my hair?

A: While it’s best to avoid hot tools altogether, you can still use them sparingly. Just make sure to use a heat protectant spray beforehand and keep the heat at a low to medium setting.

Q: Will cutting my hair make it grow faster?

A: While cutting your hair won’t necessarily make it grow faster, it can help prevent further damage and breakage, promoting healthier and longer hair in the long run.

Hair Care

Lastly, taking care of your hair is crucial in growing out thick frizzy hair. Make sure to use products that are specifically formulated for your hair type and texture, and avoid using hot tools as much as possible. Eating a balanced diet, drinking plenty of water, and getting enough sleep can also aid in hair growth. With patience and commitment, you can achieve the long and luscious locks you’ve always wanted.
