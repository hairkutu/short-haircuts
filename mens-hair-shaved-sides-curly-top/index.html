Mens Hair Shaved Sides Curly Top

Description

The mens hair shaved sides curly top hairstyle has been trending for years now. It is a versatile hairstyle that can be worn in various ways. The hairstyle features shaved or faded sides and a curly or wavy top. This style gives a cool and edgy look to men of all ages. It is suitable for both formal and casual occasions.

How to

If you want to achieve the mens hair shaved sides curly top hairstyle, then follow the steps below:

Step by step

  1. Visit a professional barber or stylist who has experience in giving this hairstyle.
  2. Ask the stylist to shave or fade the sides of your head, leaving a significant length on the top.
  3. Ask the stylist to cut your hair on the top into curls or waves.
  4. Use a styling product to enhance the curls or waves on the top.
  5. You can also use a hairdryer to give volume to your curls or waves.

Tips

Here are some tips to maintain your mens hair shaved sides curly top hairstyle:

  • Apply a hair styling product to keep your curls or waves in place.
  • Use a hair conditioner to keep your hair smooth and hydrated.
  • Trim your hair regularly to maintain the length and shape of your hairstyle.
  • Use a hairbrush or a comb to style your hair in the desired way.

FAQ

Here are some commonly asked questions about mens hair shaved sides curly top hairstyle:

Q: Is this hairstyle suitable for all face shapes?

A: Yes, this hairstyle is suitable for all face shapes.

Q: Can I dye my hair in this hairstyle?

A: Yes, you can dye your hair in this hairstyle. However, it is recommended to do it with the help of a professional stylist.

Q: How long does it take to style this hairstyle?

A: It takes around 10 to 15 minutes to style this hairstyle.

Hair Care

Here are some hair care tips for your mens hair shaved sides curly top hairstyle:

  • Wash your hair with a mild shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using hot water to wash your hair as it can damage your curls or waves.
  • Use a hair serum to protect your hair from heat and environmental damage.
  • Avoid using a hairdryer on high heat as it can dry out your hair and damage your curls or waves.
