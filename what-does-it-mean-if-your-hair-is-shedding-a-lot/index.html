What Does It Mean If Your Hair Is Shedding A Lot?

It is normal for a person to shed 50 to 100 hair strands per day. However, if you notice that your hair is shedding a lot more than usual, it can be a cause for concern. Hair shedding can be caused by several factors such as stress, poor nutrition, hormonal changes, medication, illness, and genetics. In this article, we will discuss what it means if your hair is shedding a lot and what you can do about it.

How To Identify If You Are Shedding A Lot Of Hair?

If you are shedding more than 100 hair strands per day or if you notice bald spots or thinning, it is a sign that you are shedding a lot of hair. You can also do a simple test to check if you are shedding an excessive amount of hair. Run your fingers through your hair and gently tug. If more than 6 hairs come out, you may be experiencing excessive hair shedding.

Step By Step Guide To Reduce Hair Shedding:

  1. Identify the cause of your hair shedding. If it is due to stress, try to reduce stress levels through meditation or exercise.
  2. Improve your diet by including foods rich in vitamins and minerals such as iron, biotin, and zinc.
  3. Use a gentle shampoo and avoid harsh chemicals and heat styling tools.
  4. Avoid tight hairstyles that pull on your hair such as tight braids or ponytails.
  5. Take care of your scalp by massaging it regularly and using a scalp treatment.
  6. Consult a dermatologist if your hair shedding persists or if you notice any other symptoms.

Tips To Prevent Hair Shedding:

  • Avoid using hot water when washing your hair.
  • Brush your hair gently and avoid pulling or tugging.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair.
  • Avoid using hair products that contain sulfates and alcohol.
  • Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a UV protection spray.

FAQ:

Q: Can hair shedding be prevented?

A: Hair shedding can be prevented by maintaining a healthy diet, reducing stress levels, and taking care of your hair and scalp.

Q: Is hair shedding normal?

A: Yes, it is normal to shed 50 to 100 hair strands per day.

Hair Care Tips To Reduce Hair Shedding:

Here are some hair care tips to reduce hair shedding:

  • Wash your hair every other day instead of every day.
  • Avoid using hot water when washing your hair.
  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools and chemicals.
  • Massage your scalp regularly to promote blood circulation.
  • Eat a healthy diet rich in vitamins and minerals.

Remember that hair shedding can be caused by several factors and it is important to identify the cause before taking any measures to prevent it. If your hair shedding persists or if you notice any other symptoms, consult a dermatologist for proper diagnosis and treatment.
