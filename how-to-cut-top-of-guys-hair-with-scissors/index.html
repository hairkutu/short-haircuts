How To Cut Top Of Guys Hair With Scissors A Step By Step Guide

Written by Oliver Jun 23, 2023 Â· 8 min read

Cutting hair is an essential skill that everyone should learn. Whether you're trying to save money, prefer to do things yourself, or want to experiment with hairstyles, knowing how to cut your hair can come in handy. In this guide, we'll teach you how to cut the top of a guy's hair with scissors. We'll cover everything from preparation to finishing touches, so grab your scissors and let's get started!

  2. Sectioning the Hair
  3. Cutting the Hair
  4. Blending the Hair
  5. Finishing Touches

  8. Hair Care After Cutting
  9. Conclusion

Preparation

Before you start cutting, you'll need to gather all the necessary tools. Here's a list of things you'll need:

  • Sharp hairdressing scissors
  • Comb
  • Clippers (optional)
  • Hair clips (optional)
  • Spray bottle filled with water
  • Towel or cape to protect clothing

Once you have all the tools, you need to prepare the hair for cutting. Wet the hair with the spray bottle so that it's damp but not dripping. This makes it easier to cut and helps the hair to stay in place while you're cutting. Then, towel dry the hair to remove excess water.

Sectioning the Hair

The first step in cutting the top of a guy's hair is sectioning it. Here's how to do it:

  1. Start by parting the hair in the middle, from the forehead to the nape of the neck.
  2. Then, create a parting on each side of the head, from the forehead to the back of the ear.
  3. Clip the side sections of hair out of the way.

Now you should have a middle section of hair that you'll be cutting first.

Cutting the Hair

Now it's time to start cutting the hair. Here's how:

  1. Take a section of hair from the middle and comb it straight up.
  2. Hold the hair between your index and middle fingers, about an inch from the scalp.
  3. Using your scissors, cut the hair to the desired length, making sure to cut straight across.
  4. Repeat this process, working your way from the front to the back of the head.
  5. Unclip the side sections and repeat the process, cutting the hair to the same length as the middle section.

If you want a more textured look, you can use the point-cutting technique. Instead of cutting straight across, you cut into the hair at a slight angle. This creates a choppy, textured look.

Blending the Hair

After cutting the hair, it's important to blend the sections together so that there are no harsh lines. Here's how to do it:

  1. Comb the hair straight up, and use your scissors to cut into the hair in a zigzag motion. This helps to blend the sections together.
  2. Repeat this process on each section of hair, making sure to blend everything together.

Finishing Touches

After blending the hair, you can do some finishing touches to make it look neat and tidy. Here are some things you can do:

  • Trim any stray hairs that you missed.
  • Use your clippers to trim the hair around the edges and back of the neck.
  • Use your comb to create a parting, if desired.

Tips for Cutting Hair with Scissors

Here are some tips to keep in mind when cutting hair with scissors:

  • Make sure your scissors are sharp. Dull scissors can damage the hair and make it harder to cut.
  • Take your time. Rushing can lead to mistakes.
  • Use your comb to help guide you when cutting.
  • Keep the hair damp while cutting. This makes it easier to cut and helps the hair to stay in place.
  • Don't cut too much hair at once. It's easier to cut more hair than it is to fix a mistake.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about cutting hair with scissors:

Can you cut hair with regular scissors?

It's not recommended to cut hair with regular scissors. Hairdressing scissors are designed to cut hair cleanly and precisely, while regular scissors can damage the hair.

How often should you cut your hair?

It depends on how fast your hair grows and the style you're going for. Generally, it's recommended to trim your hair every 6-8 weeks to keep it healthy and neat.

Can you cut your own hair?

Yes, you can cut your own hair. However, it's important to be careful and take your time to avoid making mistakes.

Hair Care After Cutting

After cutting your hair, you should take care of it to keep it healthy and looking good. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly with shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as this can damage it.
  • Use a hair mask or deep conditioner once a week to keep your hair moisturized.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.

Conclusion

Cutting the top of a guy's hair with scissors is a simple process that anyone can learn. By following the steps in this guide and keeping the tips in mind, you can achieve a neat and tidy haircut. Remember to take your time and be careful, and you'll have a great haircut in no time!
