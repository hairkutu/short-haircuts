How Do I Get My Hair Curly Without A Perm

Written by Petter Aug 12, 2023 Â· 10 min read

If you have naturally straight hair or have recently relaxed your hair, you may be wondering how to achieve curly hair without getting a perm. Perms can be damaging to your hair and can also be expensive, so it's understandable that you would want to avoid them. Luckily, there are several ways you can get curly hair without a perm. In this article, we will discuss different methods to achieve curly hair without damaging your hair or breaking the bank.

How To

Method 1: Twist-Out

A twist-out is a popular method for achieving curly hair without using heat or chemicals. To do a twist-out, follow these steps:

  1. Wash and condition your hair
  2. Apply a leave-in conditioner or curl cream
  3. Section your hair into small sections
  4. Twist each section of hair, starting at the root and twisting all the way to the end
  5. Allow your hair to air-dry or use a hair dryer with a diffuser attachment
  6. Once your hair is completely dry, unravel the twists
  7. Use your fingers or a wide-tooth comb to separate the curls

Repeat this process as often as you like, depending on how tight you want your curls to be.

Method 2: Bantu Knots

Bantu knots are another popular method for achieving curly hair without using heat or chemicals. To do Bantu knots, follow these steps:

  1. Wash and condition your hair
  2. Apply a leave-in conditioner or curl cream
  3. Section your hair into small sections
  4. Twist each section of hair, starting at the root and twisting all the way to the end
  5. Wrap each twisted section around itself to create a small knot
  6. Secure each knot with a bobby pin
  7. Allow your hair to air-dry or use a hair dryer with a diffuser attachment
  8. Once your hair is completely dry, remove the bobby pins and unravel the knots
  9. Use your fingers or a wide-tooth comb to separate the curls

Repeat this process as often as you like, depending on how tight you want your curls to be.

Method 3: Flexi Rods

Flexi rods are a popular method for achieving curly hair without using heat or chemicals. To use flexi rods, follow these steps:

  1. Wash and condition your hair
  2. Apply a leave-in conditioner or curl cream
  3. Section your hair into small sections
  4. Wrap each section of hair around a flexi rod, starting at the root and wrapping all the way to the end
  5. Bend the ends of the flexi rod to secure your hair in place
  6. Allow your hair to air-dry or use a hair dryer with a diffuser attachment
  7. Once your hair is completely dry, remove the flexi rods
  8. Use your fingers or a wide-tooth comb to separate the curls

Repeat this process as often as you like, depending on how tight you want your curls to be.

Step by Step

Step 1: Wash and Condition Your Hair

The first step to achieving curly hair without a perm is to wash and condition your hair. Use a shampoo and conditioner that are formulated for your hair type to ensure that your hair is clean and healthy. Rinse your hair thoroughly to remove any product buildup.

Step 2: Apply a Leave-In Conditioner or Curl Cream

After washing and conditioning your hair, apply a leave-in conditioner or curl cream. These products will help to define your curls and keep your hair moisturized. Use a small amount of product, starting at the ends of your hair and working your way up to the roots.

Step 3: Section Your Hair

Section your hair into small sections using hair clips or hair ties. This will make it easier to work with your hair and ensure that all of your hair is evenly coated with product.

Step 4: Choose Your Method

Choose the method that you want to use to achieve curly hair. You can choose from twist-outs, Bantu knots, or flexi rods, depending on the type of curls you want to achieve.

Step 5: Follow the Method

Follow the steps for your chosen method, making sure to wrap your hair tightly around each twist, knot, or flexi rod. Allow your hair to air-dry or use a hair dryer with a diffuser attachment to speed up the drying process.

Step 6: Remove the Twists, Knots, or Flexi Rods

Once your hair is completely dry, remove the twists, knots, or flexi rods. Use your fingers or a wide-tooth comb to separate the curls and create the look that you want.

Tips

  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair before washing it
  • Apply a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy
  • Avoid using heat styling tools, as they can damage your hair
  • Use a satin or silk pillowcase to prevent frizz and breakage

FAQ

Q: Will my hair stay curly all day?

A: Yes, if you use the right products and techniques, your hair should stay curly all day.

Q: How often should I use these methods?

A: You can use these methods as often as you like, but it's important to listen to your hair and give it a break if it needs it.

Q: Can I use these methods on relaxed hair?

A: Yes, you can use these methods on relaxed hair, but it's important to be gentle and avoid using too much tension when twisting or knotting your hair.

Hair Care

It's important to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and looking its best. Here are some tips for maintaining healthy hair:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a shampoo and conditioner that are formulated for your hair type
  • Avoid using heat styling tools, as they can damage your hair
  • Trim your hair regularly to get rid of split ends
  • Eat a healthy diet that is rich in vitamins and minerals
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair moisturized

By following these tips and using the methods described in this article, you can achieve curly hair without a perm and keep your hair healthy and looking its best.
