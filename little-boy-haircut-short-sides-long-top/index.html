Little Boy Haircut Short Sides Long Top A Popular Trend In 2023

Written by Daniel Jul 04, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Are you looking for a new haircut for your little boy? Look no further than the short sides long top haircut. This popular trend in 2023 is perfect for boys who want a stylish and trendy haircut. In this article, we will provide you with a step-by-step guide on how to achieve this haircut, tips on how to style it, and answer some frequently asked questions.

What is a Little Boy Haircut Short Sides Long Top?

The little boy haircut short sides long top is a haircut where the sides of the head are cut short while the top is left longer. This creates a contrast between the two lengths and allows for versatility in styling. The short sides provide a clean and sharp look while the longer top can be styled in various ways, such as slicked back, messy, or even spiked.

How to Achieve the Little Boy Haircut Short Sides Long Top?

Here is a step-by-step guide on how to achieve the little boy haircut short sides long top:

Step 1: Prep the Hair

Wash your little boy's hair with shampoo and conditioner. Towel dry the hair and comb it to remove any tangles or knots.

Step 2: Cut the Sides

Use clippers with a short guard to cut the sides of the head. Start at the bottom of the head and work your way up. Make sure to blend the hair well so that there are no harsh lines or uneven spots.

Step 3: Cut the Top

Use scissors to cut the top of the head. Cut the hair to the desired length, leaving it longer than the sides. Use a comb to section the hair and cut it evenly.

Step 4: Style the Hair

Apply a small amount of hair styling product to the hair and style it as desired. Slick it back for a formal look, messy for a casual look, or spiked for a trendy look.

Tips on Styling the Little Boy Haircut Short Sides Long Top

Here are some tips on how to style the little boy haircut short sides long top:

  • Use a small amount of styling product to avoid a greasy or weighed-down look.
  • Experiment with different hairstyles and find the one that suits your little boy's personality and style.
  • Trim the hair regularly to maintain the shape and style of the haircut.

FAQs

1. Is the little boy haircut short sides long top suitable for all hair types?

Yes, this haircut can work for all hair types, including straight, wavy, curly, and thick hair.

2. Can I achieve this haircut at home?

While it is possible to achieve this haircut at home, it is recommended to go to a professional hairstylist, especially if you are not experienced in cutting hair.

3. How often should I trim my little boy's hair?

You should trim your little boy's hair every 4-6 weeks to maintain the shape and style of the haircut.

Hair Care for the Little Boy Haircut Short Sides Long Top

Here are some hair care tips for the little boy haircut short sides long top:

  • Wash the hair regularly with a gentle shampoo and conditioner to keep it clean and healthy.
  • Avoid using too much heat on the hair, such as blow dryers or straighteners, as this can damage the hair.
  • Apply a hair mask or treatment once a week to nourish and strengthen the hair.

Now that you have a better understanding of the little boy haircut short sides long top, it's time to try it out on your little one. With this trendy and versatile haircut, your little boy will be the talk of the playground!
