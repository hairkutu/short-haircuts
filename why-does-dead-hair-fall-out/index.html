Why Does Dead Hair Fall Out

Written by Daniel Apr 01, 2023 Â· 8 min read

Dead hair falling out is a common phenomenon for many people. Hair goes through various stages of growth, and one of those stages is the shedding of dead hair. In this blog post, we will discuss the reasons behind why does dead hair fall out, and how you can take care of your hair to prevent excessive hair fall.

What is Dead Hair?

Dead hair is the hair that has completed its growth cycle and has come to the end of its lifespan. It is the hair that is no longer attached to the hair follicle, and hence, it falls out. Dead hair shedding is a natural process, and it occurs to make way for new hair growth. Dead hair shedding is common in all hair types, whether curly, wavy, or straight.

Why Does Dead Hair Fall Out?

Dead hair falls out for several reasons. One of the primary reasons is the natural hair growth cycle. Hair goes through three stages of growth: anagen, catagen, and telogen. During the anagen phase, the hair grows, during the catagen phase, the hair stops growing, and during the telogen phase, the hair falls out. Dead hair falls out during the telogen phase to make way for new hair growth.

Another reason why does dead hair fall out is due to hair damage. Hair damage can cause hair to weaken, break, and fall out. Hair damage can be caused by several factors, including heat styling, chemical treatments, over-washing, and over-brushing.

How to Prevent Excessive Hair Fall?

There are several ways to prevent excessive hair fall. One of the primary ways is to take care of your hair. This involves avoiding harsh chemical treatments, limiting heat styling, and washing your hair only when necessary.

You can also prevent excessive hair fall by eating a healthy diet. Your hair needs essential nutrients to grow and remain healthy, including vitamins A, B, C, D, and E, iron, and protein.

Step-by-Step Guide to Prevent Excessive Hair Fall

Step 1: Avoid Harsh Chemical Treatments

Harsh chemical treatments, such as hair dyes and relaxers, can damage your hair, causing it to weaken and fall out. If you must use these treatments, opt for gentler products and avoid overusing them.

Step 2: Limit Heat Styling

Heat styling tools, such as flat irons and curling irons, can damage your hair, causing it to break and fall out. If you must use heat styling tools, use a heat protectant spray and limit the use to once a week.

Step 3: Wash Your Hair Only When Necessary

Washing your hair too often can strip it of its natural oils, causing it to dry out and fall out. Wash your hair only when necessary, and use a gentle shampoo and conditioner.

Step 4: Eat a Healthy Diet

Eating a healthy diet can help prevent excessive hair fall. Eat foods that are rich in vitamins A, B, C, D, and E, iron, and protein. Some foods that are good for your hair include spinach, eggs, salmon, and avocados.

Tips to Prevent Hair Fall

Here are some additional tips to prevent hair fall:

  • Avoid tight hairstyles, such as braids and ponytails, as they can cause hair breakage.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair gently.
  • Avoid brushing wet hair as it is more prone to breakage.
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends.
  • Avoid rubbing your hair with a towel as it can cause hair damage.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Q: Is hair fall normal?

A: Yes, hair fall is normal. It is a natural process that occurs as part of the hair growth cycle.

Q: How much hair fall is normal?

A: It is normal to lose up to 100 hairs a day. If you are losing more than this, it may be a sign of excessive hair fall.

Q: Can stress cause hair fall?

A: Yes, stress can cause hair fall. When you are stressed, your body releases hormones that can disrupt the hair growth cycle, causing hair to fall out.

Hair Care

Taking care of your hair is essential to prevent excessive hair fall. By following the steps outlined in this blog post, you can keep your hair healthy and prevent hair fall. Remember to eat a healthy diet, avoid harsh chemical treatments, limit heat styling, and wash your hair only when necessary.

By taking care of your hair, you can prevent hair fall and keep your hair looking healthy and beautiful.
