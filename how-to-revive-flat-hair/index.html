Revive Flat Hair Tips And Tricks

Written by Daniel Jun 06, 2023 · 7 min read

If you have naturally flat hair, you know how frustrating it can be to try and add volume. Even if you have thick hair, it can still fall flat and look dull. But don't worry, there are ways to revive your flat hair and add some life back into it. In this article, we will cover everything you need to know to give your hair the lift it needs.

How to Revive Flat Hair

Before we dive into the tips and tricks, let's talk about what causes flat hair. Most of the time, flat hair is caused by a lack of volume. This can be due to genetics, styling habits, or product buildup. So, the first step to reviving flat hair is to identify the cause and work from there.

Step by Step Guide

Here are some steps you can take to revive your flat hair:

  1. Start by washing your hair with a clarifying shampoo to remove any product buildup.
  2. Next, use a volumizing conditioner or a hair mask to add moisture and nourishment to your hair.
  3. Once you're out of the shower, towel dry your hair and use a volumizing mousse or root lift spray to add body and texture.
  4. Blow-dry your hair upside down to add even more volume.
  5. Use a round brush to add lift at the roots and create volume throughout the rest of your hair.
  6. Finish off with a light hairspray to hold everything in place.

Tips for Reviving Flat Hair

Here are some additional tips to help you revive your flat hair:

  • Switch up your part. If you always part your hair in the same place, it can create a flat look. Try switching it up to add some volume.
  • Use dry shampoo. Dry shampoo can help absorb excess oil and add texture to your hair, making it look fuller.
  • Avoid heavy products. Heavy products like oils and serums can weigh down your hair and make it look flat. Stick to lightweight products that add volume.
  • Don't over-wash your hair. Washing your hair too often can strip it of its natural oils, leaving it looking limp and lifeless.
  • Get regular haircuts. Regular haircuts can help remove split ends and keep your hair looking healthy and full.

FAQ About Reviving Flat Hair

Here are some frequently asked questions about reviving flat hair:

How often should I wash my hair?

It depends on your hair type and lifestyle. If you have oily hair, you may need to wash it more often. If you have dry hair, you can go longer between washes. Aim to wash your hair every 2-3 days.

Can I use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner?

Yes, using a volumizing shampoo and conditioner can help add body and texture to your hair.

What's the best way to blow-dry my hair?

The best way to blow-dry your hair for volume is to flip your head upside down and use a round brush to lift the roots. Once your hair is about 80% dry, flip your head back up and finish drying your hair while using the round brush to add volume throughout.

Hair Care for Flat Hair

Here are some hair care tips to keep your hair looking full and voluminous:

  • Avoid using hot tools every day. Heat can damage your hair and make it look flat and dull.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy and hydrated.
  • Eat a balanced diet and stay hydrated. Your hair needs nutrients to grow and stay healthy.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and wind. Wear a hat or use a scarf to protect your hair from the elements.

Reviving flat hair may seem like a daunting task, but with the right products and techniques, you can give your hair the lift it needs. Try out some of these tips and tricks and see what works best for you. Remember, healthy hair is happy hair!
