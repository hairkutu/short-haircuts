How To Loose Curl Short Hair With A Flat Iron A Step By Step Guide

Written by Robby Aug 18, 2023 · 7 min read

If you have short hair and want to add some loose curls, a flat iron is a great tool to use. It’s quick, easy, and doesn’t require any special skills. In this guide, we’ll show you how to loose curl short hair with a flat iron, and give you some tips and tricks to get the best results.

Table of Contents

  • How to
  • Step-by-Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

How to Loose Curl Short Hair with a Flat Iron

Before we get started, it’s important to note that you should always use a heat protectant spray on your hair before using any hot tools. This will help prevent damage and keep your hair healthy.

To loose curl short hair with a flat iron, follow these steps:

Step 1: Prep Your Hair

Start by washing your hair and letting it air dry or blow drying it. Next, apply a heat protectant spray to your hair to prevent damage from the heat of the flat iron. Finally, brush your hair to remove any tangles or knots.

Step 2: Section Your Hair

Section your hair into small sections, starting at the back of your head and working your way forward. Use hair clips or bands to hold each section in place.

Step 3: Heat Your Flat Iron

Turn on your flat iron and let it heat up to the desired temperature. For short hair, a temperature between 300-350 degrees Fahrenheit is usually sufficient.

Step 4: Curl Your Hair

Starting with the first section of hair, clamp the flat iron about an inch away from the roots. Twist the flat iron away from your face and slowly pull it down the length of your hair. The slower you go, the more defined the curl will be. Repeat this process with each section of hair, twisting the flat iron in different directions to create a natural-looking curl.

Step 5: Finish Your Look

Once you’ve curled all of your hair, use your fingers to gently loosen the curls and create a more natural look. Finish with a spritz of hairspray to hold the curls in place.

Tips for Loose Curling Short Hair with a Flat Iron

Here are some tips to help you get the best results when loose curling short hair with a flat iron:

  • Use a flat iron with rounded edges to create more natural-looking curls.
  • Don’t clamp the flat iron too tightly, as this can create a crease in the hair.
  • For a more voluminous look, curl your hair in different directions.
  • Use a heat protectant spray before using any hot tools to prevent damage to your hair.
  • Don’t use too much hairspray, as this can weigh your hair down and make it look greasy.

FAQs

Can I use a flat iron on wet hair?

No, you should never use a flat iron on wet hair. This can cause damage to your hair and even burn it. Make sure your hair is completely dry before using a flat iron.

How long will the curls last?

The length of time the curls will last depends on your hair type and the products you use. Generally, loose curls created with a flat iron will last for 1-2 days.

Hair Care for Loose Curled Short Hair

After you’ve curled your hair with a flat iron, it’s important to take care of it to keep it looking healthy and shiny. Here are some tips:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to prevent damage to your hair.
  • Avoid using hot tools on your hair every day, as this can cause damage and dryness.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy and hydrated.
  • Avoid using too many styling products, as this can weigh your hair down and make it look greasy.

Now that you know how to loose curl short hair with a flat iron, you can create a variety of different looks. Whether you want loose, beachy waves or more defined curls, a flat iron is a versatile tool that can help you achieve your desired look.
