Does Beard Make You Look Fat

Written by Eveline Jul 14, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Are you someone who loves growing a beard but often gets confused about whether it makes you look fat or not? If yes, then you have landed on the right page. A beard can indeed make you look fat if not maintained properly. But don't worry, we have got you covered. Keep reading to know more about how to maintain a beard that looks great on you.

Description

The beard trend has been around for a while now, and it doesn't seem to be going away anytime soon. While it is an excellent way to give your face a rugged and manly look, not everyone can pull it off. While some men can rock a thick and bushy beard, others may end up looking overweight with a beard. This is because the beard can make your face look rounder, giving the illusion of a double chin or chubby cheeks.

How to

If you are worried about your beard making you look fat, the good news is that there are steps you can take to avoid it.

Step 1: Choose the right beard style

Choosing the right beard style is essential to avoid looking overweight with a beard. For example, if you have a round face, a long beard will only make your face look rounder. Instead, opt for a shorter beard that complements your face shape.

Step 2: Keep it trimmed

Keeping your beard trimmed is crucial to avoid looking overweight. A well-groomed beard will make you look sharp and well put together. Regular grooming will also help prevent your beard from looking unkempt and messy.

Step 3: Keep the rest of your hair neat

The hair on your head can also contribute to how your beard looks. Keeping the rest of your hair neat and tidy can help balance out the beard and prevent it from making you look overweight.

Tips

Here are some additional tips to help you maintain a beard that looks great on you:

  • Use beard oil to keep your beard moisturized and soft.
  • Use a beard brush or comb to keep your beard looking neat.
  • Trim your beard regularly to avoid split ends and breakage.
  • Use a high-quality trimmer to get a precise and even trim.
  • Consider seeking advice from a professional barber to help you choose the right beard style for your face shape.

FAQ

Does a beard make you look older?

Yes, a beard can make you look older. This is because facial hair can cover up fine lines and wrinkles, making your face appear smoother and more youthful.

Does a beard make you look more attractive?

Studies have shown that women tend to find men with beards more attractive than those without. However, this is subjective, and not every woman may have the same preference.

Hair Care

Maintaining a beard requires a certain level of hair care. Here are some tips to help you take care of your beard:

  • Wash your beard regularly with a mild shampoo to remove dirt and oil.
  • Apply beard oil or balm to keep your beard moisturized and healthy.
  • Avoid using harsh chemicals or hot tools on your beard.
  • Avoid pulling or tugging on your beard, as this can cause breakage and split ends.
  • Visit a professional barber for regular trimming and grooming.

By following these tips and taking care of your beard, you can ensure that it looks great on you and doesn't make you look overweight. Remember, a well-groomed beard can be a great accessory to your overall look, so don't be afraid to experiment with different styles and find the one that works for you.
