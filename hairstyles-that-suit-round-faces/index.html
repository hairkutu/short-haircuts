Hairstyles That Suit Round Faces

Written by William Jul 20, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Are you tired of trying out different hairstyles that just don't seem to suit your round face? Don't worry, we've got you covered! In this article, we'll be discussing the best hairstyles that suit round faces. From short to long, we've got something for everyone. So, let's get started!

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to
  • Step by Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

Round faces are characterized by full cheeks and a rounded chin. Hairstyles that suit round faces are those that help to elongate the face and create more angles. This can be achieved through layered cuts, side-swept bangs, and hairstyles that add height to the crown of the head. It's important to avoid hairstyles that are too short or too round, as these can make the face appear even rounder.

How to

When choosing a hairstyle for a round face, it's important to consider your hair type and the amount of maintenance you're willing to do. Shorter hairstyles are great for low-maintenance individuals, while longer hairstyles require more upkeep. It's also important to consider your face shape, as some styles may not suit all round faces.

Step by Step

If you're looking for a short hairstyle that suits a round face, try a pixie cut with side-swept bangs. This style helps to elongate the face and draw attention to the eyes. To style, apply a small amount of hair wax to your fingertips and tousle the hair to create texture.

If you prefer longer hair, try a layered cut with face-framing layers. This style helps to add dimension to the face and create more angles. To style, blow dry your hair with a round brush and finish with a texturizing spray for added volume.

Tips

When getting a haircut for a round face, ask your stylist for a style that adds height to the crown of the head and creates angles around the face. Avoid styles that are too blunt or too round, as these can make the face appear even rounder.

If you have thin hair, try adding layers to create more volume and dimension. If you have thick hair, try a blunt cut with face-framing layers to create a more streamlined look.

FAQ

What hairstyles should I avoid if I have a round face?

Avoid hairstyles that are too short or too round, as these can make the face appear even rounder. Also, avoid center parts, as these can create a symmetrical look that accentuates the roundness of the face.

Can I still wear a bob if I have a round face?

Yes, but it's important to choose a longer bob with layers and angles that help to elongate the face. Avoid a blunt bob that ends at the chin, as this can accentuate the roundness of the face.

Hair Care

To keep your hair looking its best, it's important to use products that are suited to your hair type. If you have fine hair, use volumizing products that add texture and lift. If you have thick hair, use smoothing products that help to control frizz and add shine. It's also important to get regular trims to keep your hair looking healthy and to prevent split ends.

In conclusion, there are many hairstyles that suit round faces. From short to long, there's a style for everyone. Remember to consider your hair type, face shape, and maintenance level when choosing a style. With the right cut and styling, you'll have a hairstyle that flatters your face and makes you feel confident.
