How To Make Your Hair Go Up A Step By Step Guide

Written by Bardi Apr 12, 2023 Â· 6 min read

If you're looking for a new hairstyle that's fun and edgy, you might want to try making your hair go up. This bold look is perfect for a night out or a special occasion, but it can also be worn casually. Here's a step-by-step guide on how to achieve this look.

Description

Making your hair go up involves adding volume and height to your hair, which can be achieved through a variety of methods such as teasing, backcombing, and using styling products. The end result is a dramatic and eye-catching hairstyle that's perfect for those who want to stand out.

How To

To make your hair go up, you'll need:

  • Hairspray
  • Teasing comb or brush
  • Bobby pins
  • Styling gel or mousse

Here's how to do it:

Step 1: Start with clean, dry hair

It's best to start with clean, dry hair so that you can achieve maximum volume. If your hair is naturally oily, use a dry shampoo to absorb any excess oil before styling.

Step 2: Apply styling product

Apply a small amount of styling gel or mousse to your hair, focusing on the roots. This will give your hair extra hold and help it stay in place throughout the day.

Step 3: Tease your hair

Using a teasing comb or brush, gently tease your hair at the crown. Start at the back of your head and work your way forward, teasing small sections of hair at a time. Be careful not to over-tease or you'll end up with a tangled mess.

Step 4: Smooth the top layer

Once you've teased your hair to your desired height, use a comb to smooth the top layer. This will help to hide any teasing and give your hair a more polished look.

Step 5: Pin your hair in place

Using bobby pins, carefully pin your hair in place. Start at the back of your head and work your way forward, making sure to hide the pins as you go. You can also use a hair elastic to create a small ponytail at the back of your head, which will help to keep your hair in place.

Step 6: Finish with hairspray

Once your hair is pinned in place, finish with a spritz of hairspray. This will help to keep your hair in place all day long.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you achieve the perfect "hair up" look:

  • Start with a small amount of product and add more as needed.
  • Be gentle when teasing your hair to avoid damaging it.
  • Use bobby pins that match your hair color to make them less visible.
  • Experiment with different hairstyles, such as a messy bun or a half-up, half-down look.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about making your hair go up:

Q: Will teasing damage my hair?

A: Teasing can cause damage to your hair over time, so it's important to be gentle and use a good quality teasing comb or brush.

Q: How long will my hair stay up?

A: With the right products and techniques, your hair can stay up all day and night.

Q: Can I make my hair go up if I have short hair?

A: Yes, you can still achieve the look of "hair up" with short hair by using styling products and teasing your hair at the crown.

Hair Care

Making your hair go up can be a fun and exciting way to change up your look, but it's important to take care of your hair to prevent damage. Use a good quality shampoo and conditioner, and avoid using heat styling tools too often. You can also try using a hair mask once a week to keep your hair healthy and strong.
