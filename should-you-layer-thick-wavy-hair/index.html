Should You Layer Thick Wavy Hair A Complete Guide

Written by Bardi Mar 24, 2023 · 6 min read

If you have thick wavy hair, you might be wondering whether you should layer your hair or not. In this blog post, we’ll discuss the benefits of layering thick wavy hair, how to layer it, and how to take care of it afterwards. So, if you’re interested in learning more, keep reading!

Table of Contents

  • Benefits of Layering Thick Wavy Hair
  • How to Layer Thick Wavy Hair
  • Tips for Layering Thick Wavy Hair
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care Tips After Layering Thick Wavy Hair

Benefits of Layering Thick Wavy Hair

Layering thick wavy hair can offer numerous benefits, including:

  • Reducing bulk and weight
  • Adding movement and texture
  • Creating a more flattering shape for your face
  • Allowing for more styling options

Layering can also make your hair easier to manage and style, especially if you have long or unruly hair.

How to Layer Thick Wavy Hair

Layering thick wavy hair can be done in many ways, but the most common method is to use a razor or scissors to create choppy layers. Here’s how:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair
  2. Divide your hair into sections
  3. Starting at the bottom, use a razor or scissors to cut into the hair at an angle, creating choppy layers
  4. Work your way up the sections, creating layers as you go
  5. Blend the layers together by using a razor or scissors to lightly feather the ends

It’s important to note that layering is a skill that takes practice, so if you’re unsure about doing it yourself, it’s best to go to a professional stylist.

Tips for Layering Thick Wavy Hair

Here are some tips to keep in mind when layering thick wavy hair:

  • Don’t cut too much off at once – start with small sections and work your way up
  • Use a razor or scissors that are specifically designed for cutting hair
  • Be patient and take your time – rushing can lead to mistakes
  • Consult with a professional stylist if you’re unsure about layering your hair yourself

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about layering thick wavy hair:

Q: Will layering my hair make it look thinner?
A: Yes, layering can reduce bulk and weight, which can make your hair appear thinner.

Q: Is layering suitable for all hair types?
A: While layering can work for many hair types, it’s best to consult with a professional stylist to determine if it’s right for your hair type and texture.

Hair Care Tips After Layering Thick Wavy Hair

After layering thick wavy hair, it’s important to take care of it properly to maintain its health and appearance. Here are some tips:

  • Use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair hydrated
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as they can damage your hair
  • Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a UV-protectant spray
  • Get regular trims to maintain the shape and health of your layers

Overall, layering thick wavy hair can offer many benefits, including reducing bulk and weight, adding movement and texture, and creating a more flattering shape for your face. If you’re interested in layering your hair, be sure to follow the tips and techniques outlined in this post to achieve the best results.
