Short Curly Haircuts For Round Fat Faces The Ultimate Guide

Written by Christine May 04, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Are you tired of struggling to find the right haircut for your round, fat face? Look no further than short curly haircuts! Not only do they add volume and texture to your locks, but they also help to elongate your face and draw attention away from any problem areas. In this guide, we'll walk you through everything you need to know about short curly haircuts for round fat faces, from how to choose the right style to hair care tips to keep your curls looking their best.

How to Choose the Right Short Curly Haircut for a Round Fat Face

When it comes to choosing the right short curly haircut for a round fat face, there are a few key things to keep in mind:

  • Length: The length of your hair can have a big impact on how your face looks. Shorter haircuts tend to elongate the face, while longer haircuts can make it look wider. For round fat faces, we recommend going with a shorter haircut that falls between your chin and your shoulders.
  • Layers: Adding layers to your haircut can help to add volume and texture, which can be especially helpful for those with round fat faces. Layers can also help to frame your face and draw attention away from any problem areas.
  • Curls: If you have naturally curly hair, embrace it! Curly hair can help to add volume and texture to your locks, which can be especially helpful for those with round fat faces. If you don't have naturally curly hair, consider getting a perm or using curling products to add some texture.

Step-by-Step Guide to Getting a Short Curly Haircut for a Round Fat Face

Ready to take the plunge and get a short curly haircut? Here's what you need to know:

  1. Find a stylist who specializes in curly haircuts. Curly hair requires a different approach than straight hair, so it's important to find someone who knows how to work with your hair type.
  2. Bring pictures of styles you like. This will help your stylist get a sense of what you're looking for and what will work best for your face shape.
  3. Communicate with your stylist. Let them know what you're looking for and any concerns you have about your face shape. They can help you choose a style that will flatter your features.
  4. Trust your stylist. They're the expert, so let them do their thing!

Tips for Maintaining Short Curly Haircuts for Round Fat Faces

Once you've got your new haircut, it's important to take care of your curls to keep them looking their best. Here are some tips to help you maintain your short curly haircut:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to keep your curls hydrated.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools as much as possible, as they can damage your curls. If you do use heat styling tools, be sure to use a heat protectant spray.
  • Consider getting regular trims to keep your curls looking healthy and prevent split ends.
  • Use a diffuser when blow-drying your hair to help enhance your curls.

FAQs About Short Curly Haircuts for Round Fat Faces

Q: Will a short curly haircut make my face look even rounder?

A: No! In fact, a short curly haircut can actually help to elongate your face and draw attention away from any problem areas.

Q: What if I have straight hair?

A: If you have straight hair, you can still get a short curly haircut! Consider getting a perm or using curling products to add some texture to your hair.

Q: How often should I get my hair trimmed?

A: It's a good idea to get your hair trimmed every 6-8 weeks to keep your curls looking healthy and prevent split ends.

Final Thoughts

Short curly haircuts can be a great option for those with round fat faces. By choosing the right length, layers, and curls, you can create a style that flatters your features and makes you feel confident. Just be sure to take care of your curls to keep them looking their best!
