Short Hairstyles For Oval Face Girl Tips And Ideas

Written by Christine Jul 17, 2023 Â· 9 min read

If you have an oval-shaped face, consider yourself lucky! This face shape is considered the most versatile and can pull off different hairstyles, including short haircuts. However, with so many options available, it can be challenging to choose the right short hairstyle that complements your facial features. In this article, we'll provide you with tips and ideas on short hairstyles for oval face girls.

Table of Contents:

  • How to Choose the Right Short Hairstyle for Your Oval Face
  • Short Hairstyle Ideas for Oval Face Girl
  • Step-by-Step Guide on How to Style Short Hair for Oval Face Girl

  • Frequently Asked Questions
  • Hair Care Tips for Short Haircut for Oval Face Girl

How to Choose the Right Short Hairstyle for Your Oval Face

When choosing a short hairstyle for oval face girl, consider the following:

1. Face Shape

Although you have an oval-shaped face, you still need to consider your other facial features, such as your forehead, cheekbones, and jawline. If you have a high forehead, consider a short haircut with bangs to balance it out. If you have prominent cheekbones, opt for a layered haircut that frames your face.

2. Hair Texture

Your hair texture can also affect what short hairstyle you should choose. If you have fine hair, consider a pixie cut or a bob with layers to add volume. If you have thick hair, a blunt bob or a layered shag haircut can help thin it out.

3. Lifestyle

Consider your lifestyle when choosing a short hairstyle. If you're always on the go, choose a low-maintenance haircut that requires minimal styling. If you're more daring with your style, opt for a bold and edgy short haircut.

Short Hairstyle Ideas for Oval Face Girl

Here are some short hairstyle ideas that are perfect for oval face girls:

1. Pixie Cut

A pixie cut is a classic short haircut that works well with oval face girls. It's low maintenance, versatile, and can be styled in different ways.

2. Bob Haircut

A bob haircut is another popular short hairstyle that works well with oval face girls. It can be blunt or layered, and it's easy to style and maintain.

3. Shag Haircut

A shag haircut is a layered haircut that adds texture and volume to your hair. It's perfect for oval face girls with thick hair.

4. Short Bangs

Short bangs can help balance out a high forehead and add a touch of edginess to your look.

Step-by-Step Guide on How to Style Short Hair for Oval Face Girl

Here's a step-by-step guide on how to style short hair for oval face girl:

1. Wash Your Hair

Start by washing your hair with a shampoo and conditioner that's suitable for your hair type. Towel dry your hair and apply a styling product, such as mousse or hair gel.

2. Blow Dry Your Hair

Use a blow dryer and a round brush to dry your hair. Start at the roots and work your way down to the ends. Use the brush to add volume and texture to your hair.

3. Style Your Hair

Once your hair is dry, style it according to the haircut you have. Use a straightener, curling iron, or a hairdryer to create the desired look.

4. Finish with Hairspray

Finish off your look with a hairspray to keep your hair in place.

Tips to Maintain Short Haircut for Oval Face Girl

Here are some tips to maintain your short haircut:

1. Get Regular Trims

Short haircuts require regular trims to maintain their shape. Visit your hairstylist every 4-6 weeks for a trim.

2. Use the Right Hair Products

Use hair products that are suitable for your hair type and the style you want to achieve.

3. Protect Your Hair from Heat

Use a heat protectant spray before using hot styling tools to prevent damage to your hair.

Frequently Asked Questions

1. Can oval face girls pull off a pixie cut?

Yes, oval face girls can pull off a pixie cut. It's a classic short haircut that works well with this face shape.

2. Which short hairstyle is best for oval face girls with fine hair?

A pixie cut or a bob with layers is best for oval face girls with fine hair. It adds volume and texture to your hair.

3. How do I know if a short hairstyle will suit me?

Consult with your hairstylist to determine which short hairstyle suits your face shape and hair texture.

Hair Care Tips for Short Haircut for Oval Face Girl

Here are some hair care tips for short haircut for oval face girl:

1. Use a Gentle Shampoo

Use a gentle shampoo that's suitable for your hair type and avoid over-washing your hair.

2. Condition Your Hair

Use a conditioner after shampooing your hair to keep it soft and hydrated.

3. Protect Your Hair from the Sun

Use a hat or a scarf to protect your hair from the sun's harmful rays.

In conclusion, there are many short hairstyles for oval face girls to choose from. Consider your face shape, hair texture, and lifestyle when choosing the right short haircut. With the right hair care and styling products, you can maintain a beautiful short hairstyle that complements your facial features.
