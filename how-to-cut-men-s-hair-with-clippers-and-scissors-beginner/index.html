How To Cut Men s Hair With Clippers And Scissors Beginner

Written by Frank Apr 25, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Are you looking to save money on haircuts or just want to try your hand at cutting hair? Cutting men's hair with clippers and scissors is easier than you think. In this beginner's guide, we will show you step-by-step how to cut men's hair with clippers and scissors.

How to Cut Men's Hair with Clippers and Scissors Beginner

Cutting men's hair with clippers and scissors requires some basic tools and techniques. Here's what you'll need:

  • Clippers
  • Scissors
  • Comb
  • Hair cape or towel
  • Clipper guards
  • Water spray bottle

Step-by-Step Guide to Cutting Men's Hair with Clippers and Scissors

Follow these simple steps to cut men's hair with clippers and scissors:

Step 1: Wash and dry the hair

Wash the hair thoroughly and dry it with a towel. Wet hair tends to stick together, making it difficult to cut.

Step 2: Choose the right length

Decide on the length you want to cut the hair. Clippers come with different guard sizes that determine the length of the hair. Start with a longer guard size and work your way down to avoid cutting too much hair.

Step 3: Start with the clippers

Attach the guard to the clippers and start cutting the hair from the sides and back of the head. Move the clippers in an upward motion against the direction of hair growth.

Step 4: Blend with scissors

Remove the guard and use scissors to blend the hair on the top of the head. Hold the hair between your fingers and cut small sections at a time.

Step 5: Trim the sides and back

Use scissors to trim the hair on the sides and back of the head. Hold the hair between your fingers and cut in a straight line.

Step 6: Clean up the neckline

Use clippers without a guard to clean up the neckline. Start from the center and move outwards towards the ears.

Tips for Cutting Men's Hair with Clippers and Scissors

  • Start with a longer guard size and work your way down to avoid cutting too much hair.
  • Use scissors to blend the hair on the top of the head.
  • Hold the hair between your fingers to get an even cut.
  • Clean your tools after each use to prevent infections.

FAQs About Cutting Men's Hair with Clippers and Scissors

1. How often should I cut my hair?

It depends on the length of your hair and how fast it grows. On average, men should get a haircut every 4-6 weeks.

2. Can I cut my own hair?

Yes, you can cut your own hair with clippers and scissors, but it takes practice and patience to get it right.

3. How do I clean my clippers and scissors?

Wipe them down with a disinfectant after each use and oil the blades to keep them sharp.

Hair Care After Cutting Men's Hair with Clippers and Scissors

After cutting men's hair with clippers and scissors, wash the hair again to remove any loose hair. Apply a styling product if desired and style as usual.

Now that you know how to cut men's hair with clippers and scissors, you can save money on haircuts and try out new styles. Remember to take your time and practice to get the hang of it. Happy cutting!
