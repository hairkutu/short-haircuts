How To Reduce Square Face Tips And Tricks

Written by Eveline Apr 08, 2023 · 7 min read

Are you tired of your square face and looking for ways to reduce it? There are several ways to achieve a softer, more oval-shaped face. In this article, we’ll explore how to reduce square face using simple tips and tricks. From makeup to hairstyles, we’ve got you covered.

How to Reduce Square Face

1. Contouring

Contouring is a makeup technique that can help to create the illusion of a softer, more oval-shaped face. To contour your face, use a contouring powder or cream that is two shades darker than your skin tone. Apply it to the hollows of your cheeks, temples, and jawline, then blend it out with a brush or sponge. This will help to create shadows that make your face appear less angular.

2. Hairstyles

Hairstyles can also help to reduce the appearance of a square face. Soft, layered hairstyles that frame the face are ideal. Avoid blunt cuts or hairstyles that are too short, as they can make your face appear more angular. Side-swept bangs can also help to soften the face.

3. Facial Exercises

Facial exercises can help to tone and tighten the muscles in your face, which can help to create a more oval-shaped face. To do a facial exercise, try puckering your lips and holding for a few seconds, then releasing. Repeat this exercise several times a day.

4. Facial Massage

Facial massage can help to improve blood circulation and reduce puffiness, which can help to create a more sculpted appearance. Use upward strokes when massaging your face to help lift the skin.

Step by Step Guide

Step 1: Contouring

Choose a contouring powder or cream that is two shades darker than your skin tone. Apply it to the hollows of your cheeks, temples, and jawline using a brush or sponge.

Step 2: Hairstyles

Choose a soft, layered hairstyle that frames your face. Avoid blunt cuts or hairstyles that are too short. Side-swept bangs can also help to soften the face.

Step 3: Facial Exercises

Try puckering your lips and holding for a few seconds, then releasing. Repeat this exercise several times a day.

Step 4: Facial Massage

Use upward strokes when massaging your face to help lift the skin.

Tips

1. Use a highlighter to accentuate the high points of your face, such as your cheekbones and brow bone.

2. Drink plenty of water to keep your skin hydrated and healthy.

3. Avoid harsh, angular glasses frames that can make your face look more square.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Can I reduce my square face without makeup?

Yes, there are several ways to reduce the appearance of a square face without makeup. Hairstyles, facial exercises, and facial massage can all help to create a softer, more oval-shaped face.

Q: How long will it take to see results?

The time it takes to see results will vary depending on the method you choose. With makeup and hairstyles, you can see results immediately. With facial exercises and massage, it may take several weeks to see results.

Q: Can I reduce my square face permanently?

No, you cannot permanently change the shape of your face. However, with the right techniques, you can create the illusion of a softer, more oval-shaped face.

Hair Care

When choosing hairstyles to reduce square face, it’s important to take care of your hair. Use a shampoo and conditioner that is suitable for your hair type, and avoid using hot tools such as flat irons and curling irons too frequently.

By following these simple tips and tricks, you can reduce the appearance of your square face and achieve a softer, more oval-shaped face. Whether you choose makeup, hairstyles, or facial exercises, be patient and consistent, and you’ll see results in no time.
