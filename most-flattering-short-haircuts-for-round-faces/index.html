Most Flattering Short Haircuts For Round Faces

Written by Petter Aug 03, 2023 Â· 6 min read

If you have a round face, finding the right haircut can be a challenge. You want a style that will flatter your features and accentuate your best features. Luckily, there are plenty of short haircuts that are perfect for round faces. Whether you have fine or thick hair, there's a style that will work for you. In this article, we'll go over some of the most flattering short haircuts for round faces.

Description

When it comes to short haircuts for round faces, there are a few key things to keep in mind. First, you want to create length and height at the crown of your head to elongate your face. Second, you want to avoid styles that add width to your face, like blunt bangs or chin-length bobs. Instead, opt for styles that are shorter in the back and longer in the front, like a layered bob or a pixie cut.

How to

When you're ready to get a new haircut, it's important to do your research and find a stylist who has experience working with round faces. They will be able to recommend styles that will flatter your features and suit your hair type. Once you've found a stylist you trust, you can discuss your options and decide on a style that will work for you.

Step by Step

1. Start by washing and conditioning your hair with products that are designed for your hair type.

2. Blow dry your hair using a round brush to create volume at the crown of your head.

3. Use a flat iron to straighten your hair, or add waves with a curling iron for a more textured look.

4. Use a styling product like hairspray or pomade to add hold and definition to your hair.

5. Finish with a light mist of hairspray to keep your style in place all day.

Tips

- Avoid styles that add width to your face, like blunt bangs or chin-length bobs.

- Opt for styles that are shorter in the back and longer in the front, like a layered bob or a pixie cut.

- Use a round brush when blow drying your hair to create volume at the crown of your head.

- Use a flat iron or curling iron to add texture to your hair.

- Use a styling product like hairspray or pomade to add hold and definition to your hair.

FAQ

Q: What is the best haircut for a round face?

A: The best haircut for a round face is one that creates length and height at the crown of your head and avoids adding width to your face. Styles like a layered bob or a pixie cut are perfect for round faces.

Q: Can I pull off a short haircut with a round face?

A: Absolutely! There are plenty of short haircuts that are perfect for round faces. Just be sure to choose a style that flatters your features and suits your hair type.

Hair Care

When it comes to maintaining your short haircut, there are a few things you can do to keep your hair looking its best. First, make sure you're using products that are designed for your hair type. Second, schedule regular trims to keep your hair looking neat and tidy. Lastly, use a heat protectant when using styling tools like flat irons or curling irons to prevent damage to your hair.

Overall, there are plenty of short haircuts that are perfect for round faces. By following these tips and working with a stylist you trust, you can find a style that flatters your features and makes you feel confident and beautiful.
