How To Figure Out What Hair Color Looks Best On You

Written by Bardi Jun 11, 2023 Â· 7 min read

If you're thinking about changing your hair color, it can be overwhelming to decide what shade will look best on you. With so many options available, it's easy to get lost in a sea of colors. Fear not! In this article, we'll give you some tips on how to figure out what hair color looks best on you.

Table of Contents

  • How to
  • Step by Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

How to

The first step in figuring out what hair color looks best on you is to determine your skin tone. There are two main categories of skin tones - warm and cool. Warm skin tones have yellow, golden, or peach undertones, while cool skin tones have pink, blue, or red undertones. You can determine your skin tone by looking at the veins on the inside of your wrist. If they appear green, you likely have warm undertones. If they appear blue or purple, you likely have cool undertones.

Once you've determined your skin tone, you'll want to consider your natural hair color. If you have warm undertones, you'll want to stick with warm hair colors like golden blonde, copper, or auburn. If you have cool undertones, you'll want to stick with cool hair colors like ash blonde, platinum, or dark brown.

Another factor to consider is your eye color. Certain hair colors can make your eyes pop and enhance their natural color. For example, if you have blue eyes, a warm blonde or caramel hair color can bring out the blue in your eyes. If you have green eyes, a warm red or copper hair color can make your eyes pop.

Step by Step

Here are the steps to follow to figure out what hair color looks best on you:

  1. Determine your skin tone by looking at the veins on the inside of your wrist.
  2. Consider your natural hair color.
  3. Think about your eye color.
  4. Research hair colors that complement your skin tone, natural hair color, and eye color.
  5. Consult with a professional stylist to determine the best hair color for you.

Tips

  • Bring pictures of hair colors you like to your stylist.
  • Consider your lifestyle and how much maintenance your new hair color will require.
  • Invest in high-quality hair care products to maintain your new hair color.
  • Be open to trying something new - you may be surprised at how much you love a hair color you never would have considered before.

FAQ

What hair color looks best on warm skin tones?

Warm skin tones look best with warm hair colors like golden blonde, copper, or auburn.

What hair color looks best on cool skin tones?

Cool skin tones look best with cool hair colors like ash blonde, platinum, or dark brown.

How can I make my eye color pop with my hair color?

Certain hair colors can enhance your eye color. For example, if you have blue eyes, a warm blonde or caramel hair color can bring out the blue in your eyes. If you have green eyes, a warm red or copper hair color can make your eyes pop.

Hair Care

Once you've found the perfect hair color for you, it's important to take care of your hair to maintain the color and keep it looking healthy. Invest in high-quality hair care products that are specifically designed for color-treated hair. Use a color-safe shampoo and conditioner, and avoid washing your hair too frequently. You can also use a hair mask or treatment once a week to keep your hair looking and feeling its best.

Now that you know how to figure out what hair color looks best on you, it's time to book that appointment with your stylist and try something new. Remember, hair color should be fun and make you feel confident and beautiful.
