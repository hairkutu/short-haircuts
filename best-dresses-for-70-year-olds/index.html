Best Dresses For 70 Year Olds

Written by Luffy May 07, 2023 · 7 min read

Welcome to our guide on the best dresses for 70 year olds. If you’re looking for comfortable and flattering dresses that are perfect for any occasion, then you’ve come to the right place. We’ve researched and compiled a list of the best dresses for 70 year olds that are not only stylish but also comfortable and age-appropriate. In this article, we’ll discuss how to choose the right dress, tips on styling it, and answers to frequently asked questions. Let’s get started!

How to Choose the Right Dress

When choosing a dress, it’s important to consider the occasion, your body type, and personal style. Here are some tips on how to choose the right dress:

Consider the Occasion

Before you choose a dress, consider the occasion. Is it a formal event or a casual gathering? This will determine the style and length of the dress you choose. For formal events, opt for a longer dress or a cocktail dress. For casual events, choose a shorter dress or a sundress.

Consider Your Body Type

When choosing a dress, consider your body type. If you have an hourglass figure, opt for a dress that cinches at the waist. If you have a pear-shaped figure, choose a dress that flares out at the bottom. If you have a straight figure, choose a dress that accentuates your curves.

Consider Your Personal Style

When choosing a dress, consider your personal style. Do you prefer bold prints or solid colors? Do you like sleeveless or long-sleeved dresses? This will help you choose a dress that you’ll feel comfortable and confident in.

Tips on Styling Your Dress

Now that you’ve chosen the perfect dress, here are some tips on how to style it:

Accessorize

Add some flair to your dress by accessorizing with jewelry, a scarf, or a hat. This will add some personality to your look and make your dress stand out.

Choose the Right Shoes

Choose shoes that are comfortable and complement your dress. For formal events, opt for heels. For casual events, choose flats or sandals.

Add a Jacket or Cardigan

If you’re attending a formal event, add a jacket or cardigan to your dress. This will add some warmth and make your dress appropriate for the occasion.

FAQ

Q: What is the best dress length for a 70 year old?

A: The best dress length for a 70 year old is knee-length or longer. This is age-appropriate and flattering.

Q: What colors are best for a 70 year old?

A: Colors that are best for a 70 year old are muted and neutral colors such as navy, gray, and beige. These colors are age-appropriate and flattering.

Q: Should I wear shapewear under my dress?

A: It’s up to you. If you feel more comfortable wearing shapewear, then go for it. Just make sure that it’s comfortable and doesn’t show through your dress.

Hair Care

When it comes to hair care, it’s important to choose a hairstyle that complements your dress. Here are some tips:

Choose a Style That Complements Your Dress

Choose a hairstyle that complements your dress. If you’re wearing a formal dress, opt for an updo. If you’re wearing a casual dress, choose a hairstyle that’s more relaxed.

Use the Right Products

Use products that are appropriate for your hair type. If you have dry hair, use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner. If you have fine hair, use a volumizing product to add some body.

Avoid Heat Styling

Avoid using heat styling tools such as curling irons and straighteners. These can damage your hair and make it look dry and brittle.

With these tips, you’ll be able to choose the perfect dress and style it to perfection. Remember, the most important thing is to feel comfortable and confident in what you’re wearing. Happy shopping!
