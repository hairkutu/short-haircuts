How Does Face Shape Affect A Haircut

Written by Luffy Aug 22, 2023 · 8 min read

When it comes to getting a new haircut, many people focus on the latest trends or what looks good on their favorite celebrity. However, one of the most important factors to consider is your face shape. Your face shape can greatly affect how a haircut looks on you, so it’s important to choose a style that complements your unique features.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to Choose a Haircut Based on Your Face Shape
  • Step by Step Guide
  • Tips for Choosing the Right Haircut
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care for Different Face Shapes

Description

Face shape is determined by a combination of bone structure, facial features, and proportions. There are several different face shapes, including round, oval, square, heart, and diamond. Each face shape has unique characteristics that can be accentuated or minimized with the right haircut.

How to Choose a Haircut Based on Your Face Shape

The first step to choosing a haircut based on your face shape is to determine your face shape. You can do this by looking in the mirror and measuring the width of your forehead, cheekbones, and jawline. Once you know your face shape, you can choose a haircut that flatters your features.

Round Face Shape

If you have a round face shape, choose a haircut that creates angles and lengthens your face. A long bob or layered haircut that falls below the chin is a great option. Avoid blunt cuts or short hairstyles that emphasize the roundness of your face.

Oval Face Shape

If you have an oval face shape, you’re in luck! This face shape is considered the most versatile and can pull off a variety of haircuts. Choose a haircut that complements your best features, whether that’s a pixie cut, long layers, or a blunt bob.

Square Face Shape

If you have a square face shape, choose a haircut that softens your angular features. A layered haircut with soft, face-framing layers is a great option. Avoid blunt cuts or hairstyles that are too short, as they can accentuate the squareness of your jawline.

Heart Face Shape

If you have a heart-shaped face, choose a haircut that balances your features. A long bob or layered cut that falls at or below your chin is a great option. Avoid short, choppy hairstyles or heavy bangs that can make your forehead appear larger.

Diamond Face Shape

If you have a diamond face shape, choose a haircut that adds width to your forehead and jawline. A layered bob or long layers with side-swept bangs is a great option. Avoid heavy bangs or hairstyles that are too short, as they can emphasize the narrowness of your chin.

Step by Step Guide

Here’s a step-by-step guide for choosing a haircut based on your face shape:

  1. Determine your face shape by measuring the width of your forehead, cheekbones, and jawline.
  2. Research different haircuts that flatter your face shape.
  3. Consult with your hairstylist to determine the best haircut for you.
  4. Bring pictures of the haircut you want to your appointment.
  5. Maintain your haircut with regular trims and styling.

Tips for Choosing the Right Haircut

Here are some additional tips for choosing the right haircut:

  • Consider your lifestyle and how much time you have to style your hair.
  • Take into account your hair texture and thickness.
  • Don’t be afraid to try something new, but also be realistic about what will look good on you.
  • Bring pictures of haircuts you like to your hairstylist for inspiration.
  • Be open to your hairstylist’s suggestions and advice.

FAQs

Q: Can a haircut change the shape of my face?

A: While a haircut can’t change the shape of your face, it can create the illusion of a different shape. For example, a layered haircut can soften angular features, while a blunt bob can emphasize them.

Q: What haircut looks good on a round face?

A: A long bob or layered haircut that falls below the chin is a great option for a round face shape.

Q: What haircut looks good on a square face?

A: A layered haircut with soft, face-framing layers is a great option for a square face shape.

Hair Care for Different Face Shapes

Regardless of your face shape, it’s important to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and looking its best. Here are some hair care tips for different face shapes:

  • Round face shape: Avoid hairstyles that add volume to the sides of your head, as they can make your face look wider.
  • Oval face shape: Keep your hair healthy and shiny with regular deep conditioning treatments.
  • Square face shape: Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to add body to your hair.
  • Heart face shape: Use a smoothing serum or oil to tame frizz and flyaways.
  • Diamond face shape: Avoid hairstyles that add height to the top of your head, as they can make your face look longer.

In conclusion, your face shape can greatly affect how a haircut looks on you. By choosing a haircut that flatters your unique features, you can enhance your natural beauty and feel confident in your hair.
