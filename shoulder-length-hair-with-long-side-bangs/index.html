Shoulder Length Hair With Long Side Bangs A Trendy Look In 2023

Written by Luffy May 22, 2023 Â· 7 min read

If you're looking for a new hairstyle that's both trendy and versatile, you might want to consider shoulder length hair with long side bangs. This look has been gaining popularity in recent years, and it's easy to see why. Not only does it flatter a wide range of face shapes and hair types, but it can also be dressed up or down depending on the occasion.

Description

Shoulder length hair with long side bangs refers to a hairstyle where the hair falls just below the shoulders, and the front section is styled into long, sweeping bangs that frame the face. This look can be achieved with a variety of hair textures, from straight to wavy to curly. It's also a great option for those who want to add some dimension to their hair without committing to a drastic cut or color change.

How to Achieve Shoulder Length Hair with Long Side Bangs

If you're interested in trying out this hairstyle for yourself, here's how to achieve it:

Step 1: Decide on the Length

The first step is to decide on the length of your hair. Shoulder length is typically defined as hair that falls between the collarbone and the shoulders. However, you can adjust the length to suit your preferences and face shape. It's best to consult with a hairstylist to determine what length will work best for you.

Step 2: Cut the Bangs

Once you've decided on the length of your hair, it's time to cut the bangs. Long side bangs should start at the top of your head and fall diagonally towards your cheekbones. The length of the bangs can vary, but they should be long enough to frame your face without getting in your eyes.

Step 3: Style the Hair

Once your hair and bangs are cut, it's time to style them. Depending on your hair texture, you can either blow dry your hair straight or enhance your natural waves or curls with a curling iron or wand. To style the bangs, use a round brush and blow dryer to create volume and a slight wave.

Tips for Maintaining Shoulder Length Hair with Long Side Bangs

Now that you've achieved your desired look, here are some tips for maintaining it:

  • Regular trims will help keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.
  • Use a heat protectant spray before using hot tools to prevent damage to your hair.
  • Invest in a good dry shampoo to extend the life of your hairstyle between washes.
  • Experiment with different styling products, such as hair oils or texturizing sprays, to achieve different looks.

FAQ

Can shoulder length hair with long side bangs be styled for formal events?

Yes! This hairstyle is versatile enough to be dressed up or down. For formal events, consider styling your hair in loose waves or a sleek updo, and add some statement earrings for extra glamour.

Will shoulder length hair with long side bangs suit my face shape?

Shoulder length hair with long side bangs is a flattering look for a wide range of face shapes, including oval, square, and heart-shaped. However, it's best to consult with a hairstylist to determine what length and bangs will work best for your specific face shape.

Hair Care Tips

To keep your shoulder length hair with long side bangs looking its best, it's important to follow a regular hair care routine. This includes:

  • Washing your hair with a gentle shampoo and conditioner
  • Using a wide-toothed comb to detangle your hair when wet
  • Avoiding heat styling tools as much as possible
  • Protecting your hair from the sun with a hat or scarf
  • Using a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair hydrated and healthy

With these tips and a little bit of experimentation, you can rock the shoulder length hair with long side bangs look with confidence and style!
