Square Face Short Neck Hairstyles

Written by Bardi May 06, 2023 Â· 6 min read

If you have a square face and short neck, finding the right hairstyle can be a challenge. But don't worry, there are plenty of options available that will flatter your features and make you feel confident and beautiful. In this article, we'll explore some of the best square face short neck hairstyles and provide tips on how to achieve them.

Description

A square face is characterized by a strong, angular jawline and a broad forehead. Short necks can make it difficult to find hairstyles that don't emphasize this feature. The key is to find styles that create balance and draw attention away from the neck. This can be achieved through layers, volume, and length.

How To

When choosing a hairstyle for a square face and short neck, it's important to consider your hair type and texture. If you have thin or fine hair, adding layers and volume can create the illusion of thickness. If you have thick hair, adding layers can help prevent it from looking bulky.

One of the best hairstyles for square face short neck is a long bob. This style falls just below the chin and features layers that add volume and movement. Another great option is a side-swept pixie cut. This style is short at the back and longer at the front, with side-swept bangs that create a flattering frame around the face.

Step By Step

To achieve a long bob hairstyle, start by washing and conditioning your hair. Blow-dry your hair straight, then use a curling iron to add loose waves. Finish with a texturizing spray to add volume and hold. To create a side-swept pixie cut, start by cutting the back and sides short. Leave the front longer and add layers to create volume. Finish by sweeping the longer pieces to the side and styling with a texturizing spray.

Tips

When styling your hair, avoid styles that are too short or too tight to the scalp. These styles can emphasize the neck and make it appear shorter. Instead, opt for styles that are longer or have layers that add volume and movement. Use texturizing sprays and products to add texture and hold to your hair.

FAQ

Q: Can I wear my hair in a ponytail if I have a square face and short neck?

A: Yes, you can wear your hair in a ponytail. To make it more flattering, add volume to the crown of your head and leave some pieces around your face to soften the look.

Q: What haircuts should I avoid if I have a square face and short neck?

A: Avoid haircuts that are too short or too tight to the scalp. These styles can emphasize the neck and make it appear shorter. Also avoid blunt bangs and hairstyles that are too flat or straight.

Hair Care

It's important to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and looking its best. Use a shampoo and conditioner that are appropriate for your hair type and texture. Avoid using too much heat on your hair, and use a heat protectant when styling with hot tools. Get regular trims to keep split ends at bay, and consider using a hair mask or deep conditioner once a week to keep your hair moisturized.

With the right hairstyle and hair care routine, you can look and feel your best, no matter what your face shape or neck length.
