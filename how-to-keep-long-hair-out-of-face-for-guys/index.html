How To Keep Long Hair Out Of Face For Guys A Step By Step Guide

Written by Daniel Aug 06, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Long hair can be a great look for guys, but it can also be a hassle when it constantly falls into your face. If you're tired of constantly pushing your hair back, it's time to learn how to keep long hair out of your face. In this guide, we'll show you how to do just that, with easy-to-follow steps and helpful tips.

Table of Contents

  • How to Keep Long Hair Out of Your Face
  • Step-by-Step Instructions
  • Tips for Keeping Long Hair Out of Your Face
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care Tips for Long Hair

How to Keep Long Hair Out of Your Face

There are a few ways to keep long hair out of your face, depending on your personal style and the occasion.

Pull it back into a ponytail

The easiest way to keep your long hair out of your face is to pull it back into a ponytail. This works great for casual occasions, but it may not be appropriate for more formal events.

Use a headband

If you don't want to put your hair in a ponytail, you can use a headband to keep it back. This is a great option for guys with shorter hair, as it doesn't require as much length to pull off.

Try a man bun

A man bun can be a stylish way to keep your hair out of your face. This works best for guys with longer hair, as it requires more length to create the bun.

Step-by-Step Instructions

Here's how to keep long hair out of your face with each of the methods we mentioned.

Ponytail

  1. Comb your hair back towards the crown of your head.
  2. Gather your hair into a ponytail, making sure it's smooth and free of tangles.
  3. Secure the ponytail with a hair tie or elastic band.

Headband

  1. Choose a headband that fits comfortably and matches your style.
  2. Put the headband on, positioning it so that it's just behind your hairline.
  3. Push your hair back so that it's held in place by the headband.

Man Bun

  1. Gather your hair into a ponytail, just like you would for a regular ponytail.
  2. Twist the ponytail around itself to create a bun.
  3. Secure the bun with a hair tie or elastic band.

Tips for Keeping Long Hair Out of Your Face

Here are some additional tips to help keep your long hair out of your face:

  • Use a light hold styling product, like gel or mousse, to help keep your hair in place.
  • Trim your hair regularly to keep it healthy and prevent split ends.
  • Consider getting bangs or layers cut into your hair to help keep it out of your face.
  • Experiment with different hairstyles to find what works best for you.

FAQs

Can I use hairpins to keep my hair back?

Yes, hairpins can be a great way to keep your hair out of your face, especially if you're trying to create a specific hairstyle. Just make sure to use hairpins that match your hair color, and be careful not to poke yourself!

Will wearing a hat help keep my hair out of my face?

Wearing a hat can be a good temporary solution for keeping your hair out of your face, but it's not a long-term solution. Hats can also cause your hair to become oily or dirty, so it's best to use other methods whenever possible.

Hair Care Tips for Long Hair

Long hair requires special care to keep it healthy and looking its best. Here are some tips for taking care of your long hair:

  • Use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair hydrated.
  • Avoid using hot tools, like straighteners or curling irons, too often.
  • Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a UV protectant spray.
  • Brush your hair gently to prevent breakage.
  • Get regular trims to maintain healthy ends.

With these tips and tricks, you'll be able to keep your long hair out of your face and looking great all day long.
