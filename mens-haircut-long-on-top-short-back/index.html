Men s Haircut Long On Top Short Back The Ultimate Guide In 2023

Are you looking for a trendy and versatile hairstyle that's easy to maintain? Look no further than the men's haircut long on top short back. This style is a classic look that has been popular for decades, and it's not going anywhere anytime soon. In this guide, we'll cover everything you need to know about this popular hairstyle, including how to get it, styling tips, and hair care advice. Let's dive in!

Description

The men's haircut long on top short back, also known as the "short back and sides" or the "modern pompadour," is a versatile hairstyle that features longer hair on the top and shorter hair on the back and sides. This style has been popularized by celebrities like David Beckham and Brad Pitt, and it's a great option for men of all ages and hair types.

How to Get the Men's Haircut Long on Top Short Back

If you're interested in getting the men's haircut long on top short back, the first step is to find a qualified hair stylist who can help you achieve the look. Be sure to bring photos or examples of the style you're looking for, so your stylist can get a clear idea of what you want.

When it comes to the actual haircut, your stylist will typically use clippers to trim the back and sides to a short length, while leaving the top longer. The length of the top can vary based on your personal preference, but it's typically around 2-4 inches long.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide to getting the men's haircut long on top short back:

  1. Find a qualified hair stylist who can help you achieve the look.
  2. Bring photos or examples of the style you're looking for.
  3. Sit down with your stylist and discuss the length and style you want.
  4. Your stylist will use clippers to trim the back and sides to a short length.
  5. The top will be left longer, typically around 2-4 inches long.
  6. Your stylist will use scissors to trim and shape the top to your desired style.
  7. Finish with styling products to achieve your desired look.

Styling Tips

The men's haircut long on top short back is a versatile style that can be dressed up or down depending on the occasion. Here are some styling tips to help you achieve the look:

  • Use a high-quality pomade or wax to style the hair on top.
  • Experiment with different parting styles to find what works best for you.
  • Consider adding a fade or undercut to the sides for a more modern look.
  • Use a blow dryer to add volume and texture to the hair on top.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about the men's haircut long on top short back:

Is the men's haircut long on top short back suitable for all hair types?

Yes, this style can work for most hair types, including straight, wavy, and curly hair.

How often do I need to get this haircut?

This style requires regular maintenance, so plan on getting a trim every 4-6 weeks to keep it looking sharp.

Can I style this haircut in different ways?

Yes, the men's haircut long on top short back is a versatile style that can be styled in many different ways, from a slicked-back look to a messy, textured style.

Hair Care Advice

To keep your men's haircut long on top short back looking great, it's important to take good care of your hair. Here are some tips:

  • Use a high-quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and hydrated.
  • Avoid using too much heat styling, as this can damage your hair over time.
  • Consider using a leave-in conditioner or hair oil to keep your hair looking shiny and healthy.
  • If you have curly hair, use a diffuser attachment on your blow dryer to enhance your natural curls.

And there you have it - everything you need to know about the men's haircut long on top short back. Whether you're looking for a new style or just want to switch things up, this classic look is sure to turn heads. Find a qualified stylist, follow our styling tips, and enjoy your new look!
