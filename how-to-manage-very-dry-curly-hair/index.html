How To Manage Very Dry Curly Hair Tips And Tricks

Written by Petter Apr 23, 2023 · 7 min read

Curly hair can be a blessing and a curse. On one hand, it’s unique and beautiful, but on the other hand, it can be challenging to manage, especially when it’s very dry. If you have naturally curly hair, you know how important it is to find the right products and techniques to keep your locks looking healthy and hydrated. In this article, we’ll share some tips and tricks on how to manage very dry curly hair.

Table of Contents

  • How to Manage Very Dry Curly Hair
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips and Tricks
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care for Very Dry Curly Hair

How to Manage Very Dry Curly Hair

First things first, let’s talk about how to manage very dry curly hair. The key to keeping your curls looking their best is to keep them moisturized. When your hair is dry, it can become brittle and prone to breakage, which can lead to frizz and flyaways. Here are some steps you can take to manage very dry curly hair:

1. Use a Moisturizing Shampoo and Conditioner

Start by using a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner that are specifically designed for curly hair. Look for products that contain natural oils like coconut or argan oil, which can help moisturize your hair and make it more manageable.

2. Apply a Leave-In Conditioner

After washing your hair, apply a leave-in conditioner to help lock in moisture. This can help prevent your hair from drying out throughout the day, which can lead to frizz and flyaways.

3. Use a Deep Conditioning Treatment

Once a week, use a deep conditioning treatment to give your hair an extra boost of moisture. Apply the treatment to your hair and leave it on for at least 30 minutes before rinsing it out.

4. Avoid Using Heat Styling Tools

Heat styling tools like flat irons and curling irons can damage your hair and make it more prone to breakage. Instead, try air-drying your hair or using a diffuser attachment on your blow dryer to dry your hair without damaging it.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here’s a step-by-step guide on how to manage very dry curly hair:

Step 1: Wash Your Hair

Start by washing your hair with a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner that are specifically designed for curly hair.

Step 2: Apply a Leave-In Conditioner

After washing your hair, apply a leave-in conditioner to help lock in moisture.

Step 3: Use a Deep Conditioning Treatment

Once a week, use a deep conditioning treatment to give your hair an extra boost of moisture.

Step 4: Style Your Hair

Style your hair using a diffuser attachment on your blow dryer or by air-drying your hair. Avoid using heat styling tools like flat irons and curling irons.

Tips and Tricks

Here are some additional tips and tricks for managing very dry curly hair:

1. Use a Silk or Satin Pillowcase

Sleeping on a silk or satin pillowcase can help prevent your hair from drying out and getting frizzy while you sleep.

2. Don’t Overwash Your Hair

Washing your hair too frequently can strip it of its natural oils, which can lead to dryness and frizz. Try washing your hair every other day or every few days.

3. Use a Wide-Tooth Comb

When detangling your hair, use a wide-tooth comb to avoid breaking your curls.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about managing very dry curly hair:

1. Can I Use Oils on My Curly Hair?

Yes, you can use oils on your curly hair to help moisturize it. Look for natural oils like coconut or argan oil, which can help lock in moisture.

2. How Often Should I Deep Condition My Hair?

You should aim to deep condition your hair once a week to give it an extra boost of moisture.

Hair Care for Very Dry Curly Hair

Managing very dry curly hair can be a challenge, but with the right products and techniques, it’s possible to keep your curls looking their best. Remember to use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner, apply a leave-in conditioner, and use a deep conditioning treatment once a week. Avoid using heat styling tools and try sleeping on a silk or satin pillowcase to prevent your hair from drying out. With these tips and tricks, you’ll be able to manage very dry curly hair with ease.
