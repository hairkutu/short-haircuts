Boy Haircut Short Sides Longer Top The Ultimate Guide In 2023

Written by Oliver May 29, 2023 Â· 9 min read

If you're looking for a trendy and stylish haircut for your little boy, the short sides long top haircut is a great option. This hairstyle is popular among boys of all ages, and it's easy to see why. The short sides and longer top create a unique and edgy look that's perfect for any occasion. In this guide, we'll take a look at how to get the boy haircut short sides longer top, tips for maintaining the style, and answer some frequently asked questions. Let's get started!

How to Get the Boy Haircut Short Sides Longer Top

The boy haircut short sides longer top is a popular style that can be achieved with the help of a skilled stylist. When getting this haircut, it's important to communicate your desired look to your stylist so they can give you the best results. Here are the steps to follow:

Step 1: Gather Inspiration

Before you head to the salon, it's a good idea to have an idea of what you want your child's haircut to look like. You can find inspiration from magazines, social media, or by looking at pictures of celebrities or athletes with similar hairstyles.

Step 2: Find a Skilled Stylist

Once you have an idea of the haircut you want, it's time to find a stylist who can bring your vision to life. Look for a stylist who has experience with short sides longer top haircuts and has a portfolio of their work to showcase their skills.

Step 3: Communicate Your Desired Look

When you arrive at the salon, communicate your desired look to your stylist. Let them know how short you want the sides and how long you want the top to be. They may also ask if you want any texture or layers added to the style.

Step 4: Sit Back and Relax

Once you've communicated your desired look, sit back and relax while your stylist works their magic. They'll use clippers to trim the sides and scissors to trim the top, creating a seamless and stylish look that your child will love.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide to achieving the boy haircut short sides longer top:

Step 1: Wash and Dry Hair

Start by washing your child's hair with shampoo and conditioner. Once their hair is clean, towel dry it and comb it out to remove any tangles.

Step 2: Section the Hair

Using a comb, section off the hair on the top of your child's head. Clip it out of the way so you can focus on the sides.

Step 3: Trim the Sides

Using clippers, trim the hair on the sides of your child's head. Start with a longer guard and work your way down to a shorter guard until you achieve the desired length.

Step 4: Blend the Sides and Top

Use scissors to blend the hair on the sides with the hair on top. Make sure there are no harsh lines or transitions between the two lengths.

Step 5: Style the Hair

Finally, style the hair on top using a brush or comb. You can add some product, such as hair gel or wax, to create texture and hold the style in place.

Tips for Maintaining the Style

Here are some tips for maintaining the boy haircut short sides longer top:

  • Visit the salon every 4-6 weeks to maintain the length and shape of the haircut.
  • Use a high-quality shampoo and conditioner to keep the hair healthy and hydrated.
  • Use a small amount of product to style the hair, and avoid using too much, as this can weigh the hair down.
  • Brush or comb the hair regularly to prevent tangles and knots.

FAQs

What face shape is best for the boy haircut short sides longer top?

The boy haircut short sides longer top works well on most face shapes, but it's especially flattering on those with oval or square faces.

Can the boy haircut short sides longer top be worn with bangs?

Yes, the style can be worn with bangs. However, it's important to communicate this to your stylist so they can incorporate them into the haircut.

Is the boy haircut short sides longer top suitable for all hair types?

Yes, the style can be worn on all hair types, from straight to curly.

Hair Care for Short Sides Longer Top Haircut

Here are some tips for caring for the boy haircut short sides longer top:

  • Wash the hair regularly with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using hot tools, such as flat irons or curling irons, as they can damage the hair.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle the hair, and start at the ends and work your way up to the roots.
  • Apply a leave-in conditioner or hair oil to the ends of the hair to keep it hydrated and prevent split ends.

In conclusion, the boy haircut short sides longer top is a stylish and trendy hairstyle that's perfect for any occasion. With the help of a skilled stylist, you can achieve this look for your child and maintain it with proper care and styling. Follow the tips and steps in this guide to get the perfect short sides long top haircut for your little boy.
