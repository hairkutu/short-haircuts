How To Fix Back Combed Hair A Step By Step Guide

Written by Luffy Jul 29, 2023 · 7 min read

Back combing, or teasing, is a popular technique that can add volume and texture to your hair. However, it can be tricky to fix once you've gone too far. If you've found yourself with a tangled mess of hair, don't worry – there are several ways to fix back combed hair. In this guide, we'll walk you through the steps to restore your hair to its former glory.

How to Fix Back Combed Hair

Before we dive into the steps, here are a few things you'll need:

  • Wide-tooth comb
  • Detangling brush
  • Conditioner
  • Hair oil or serum

Step 1: Start with Dry Hair

The first step to fixing back combed hair is to start with dry hair. If your hair is wet or damp, it will be more difficult to detangle and style. Use a wide-tooth comb to gently remove any knots or tangles in your hair.

Step 2: Apply Conditioner

Once your hair is detangled, apply a generous amount of conditioner to your hair. This will help to soften your hair and make it more manageable. Use your detangling brush to distribute the conditioner evenly throughout your hair.

Step 3: Comb Through Your Hair

After applying conditioner, use your wide-tooth comb to comb through your hair. Start at the ends and work your way up to the roots. Be gentle and take your time to avoid damaging your hair.

Step 4: Use Hair Oil or Serum

Once you've detangled your hair, apply a small amount of hair oil or serum to the ends of your hair. This will help to smooth your hair and add shine. Be careful not to apply too much, as this can make your hair look greasy.

Step 5: Style Your Hair

Now that your hair is detangled and conditioned, you're ready to style it. Depending on your preference, you can wear your hair straight, curly, or wavy. Use a heat protectant spray if you're using heat styling tools, and be sure to let your hair cool before touching it.

Tips for Fixing Back Combed Hair

Here are a few tips to keep in mind when fixing back combed hair:

  • Start with dry hair to make detangling easier.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to avoid damaging your hair.
  • Apply conditioner to soften your hair.
  • Use hair oil or serum to add shine and smoothness.
  • Be gentle when combing through your hair to avoid breakage.
  • Choose a style that works for your hair type and texture.

FAQ

Can I fix back combed hair without washing it?

Yes, you can fix back combed hair without washing it. Start with dry hair and use a wide-tooth comb to gently detangle your hair. Apply conditioner and use a detangling brush to distribute it evenly throughout your hair. Comb through your hair and use hair oil or serum to smooth and add shine.

Can I use a regular comb to fix back combed hair?

We recommend using a wide-tooth comb to fix back combed hair. This will help to avoid damaging your hair and make detangling easier.

How often should I back comb my hair?

Back combing can be damaging to your hair if done too frequently. We recommend limiting back combing to once or twice a week, and always using a heat protectant spray if using heat styling tools.

Hair Care

Proper hair care is essential for maintaining healthy hair. Here are a few tips to keep your hair looking and feeling its best:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too frequently.
  • Use a heat protectant spray if using heat styling tools.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.
  • Eat a balanced diet rich in vitamins and minerals.
  • Stay hydrated by drinking plenty of water.

With these tips and techniques, you can fix back combed hair and keep your locks looking their best. Remember to be gentle and take your time, and always prioritize the health of your hair.
