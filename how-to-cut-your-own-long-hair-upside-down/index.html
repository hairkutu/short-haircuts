How To Cut Your Own Long Hair Upside Down

Written by Robby Aug 15, 2023 Â· 5 min read

Are you tired of paying for expensive haircuts at the salon? Do you want to try cutting your own hair but don't know where to start? Have no fear, cutting your own hair upside down is easier than you think! In this article, we will provide step-by-step instructions on how to cut your own long hair upside down in the comfort of your own home.

Description

Cutting your own hair upside down may sound daunting, but it's a great way to add layers and texture to your long locks. By cutting your hair upside down, you can see all of your hair and ensure that your layers are even. Plus, it's a fun and cost-effective way to switch up your look!

How to Cut Your Own Long Hair Upside Down

Before you begin, gather the following tools:

  • Sharp hair cutting scissors
  • Comb
  • Hair ties or clips
  • Mirror

Now, follow these steps:

Step 1: Wash and Dry Your Hair

Start by washing and drying your hair as you normally would. Make sure your hair is completely dry before you begin cutting.

Step 2: Flip Your Hair Upside Down

Stand in front of a mirror and flip your hair upside down. Use a comb to brush through your hair to ensure that there are no tangles.

Step 3: Section Your Hair

Use hair ties or clips to section off your hair. Start with the bottom layer and work your way up. This will help you see all of your hair and ensure that you cut each layer evenly.

Step 4: Cut Your Hair

Using sharp hair cutting scissors, start at the bottom of your hair and work your way up. Cut small sections of hair at a time and be sure to cut at an angle to create layers. Repeat this process for each layer of your hair.

Step 5: Check Your Hair

Once you have finished cutting each layer, flip your hair back up and check your work in the mirror. Make any necessary adjustments to ensure that your layers are even.

Tips

  • Start with small cuts, you can always cut more later!
  • Cut your hair in a well-lit area to ensure that you can see all of your hair.
  • Be patient and take your time.
  • Have a friend or family member help you if you're nervous.

FAQ

Can I cut my own hair upside down if it's curly?

Yes! Cutting your own hair upside down works for all hair types.

How often should I cut my own hair?

This depends on your hair type and how quickly it grows. Generally, it's recommended to get a trim every 6-8 weeks.

What if I make a mistake?

Don't panic! Hair grows back. If you make a mistake, you can always go to a salon to get it fixed.

Hair Care

After cutting your own hair upside down, be sure to take care of your locks with regular washing, conditioning, and styling. Use a heat protectant when using hot tools and avoid using too much heat on your hair. And don't forget to show off your new, cost-effective haircut!
