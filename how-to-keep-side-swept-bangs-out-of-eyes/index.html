How To Keep Side Swept Bangs Out Of Your Eyes Tips And Tricks

Written by Bardi Aug 18, 2023 Â· 8 min read

Side swept bangs are a popular hairstyle that can add a touch of glamor to your look. However, keeping them out of your eyes can be a challenge, especially on windy or humid days. In this article, we'll share some tips and tricks on how to keep side swept bangs out of your eyes, so you can enjoy your hairstyle without any hassle.

Table of Contents

  1. How to Keep Side Swept Bangs Out of Your Eyes
  2. Step-by-Step Guide
  3. Tips and Tricks
  4. FAQs
  5. Hair Care Tips

How to Keep Side Swept Bangs Out of Your Eyes

Here are some simple and effective ways to keep your side swept bangs out of your eyes:

1. Use a Bobby Pin

One of the easiest ways to keep your side swept bangs out of your eyes is by using a bobby pin. Simply slide the pin through the hair near your temple and secure it in place. Bobby pins are discreet and can hold your hair in place without causing any damage. You can also use a decorative pin to add some style to your look.

2. Twist and Pin

If you have longer side swept bangs, you can twist them away from your face and pin them at the back of your head. This will not only keep your hair out of your eyes but also create a chic and elegant look. To do this, divide your bangs into two sections, twist them away from your face, and pin them together at the back of your head. Use a bobby pin to secure the twist in place.

3. Use Hairspray

Hairspray can be a lifesaver when it comes to keeping your side swept bangs out of your eyes. Spray a light mist of hairspray on your bangs and use your fingers to smooth them back. This will help keep your hair in place and prevent it from falling into your face.

4. Wear a Headband

A headband is a stylish accessory that can also keep your side swept bangs out of your eyes. Choose a headband that complements your outfit and slide it over your hair. Position it so that it sits just above your eyebrows and holds your bangs back. You can also choose a headband with grips on the underside to prevent it from slipping.

Step-by-Step Guide

Follow these simple steps to keep your side swept bangs out of your eyes:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair.
  2. Part your hair on one side and sweep your bangs to the opposite side.
  3. Use a bobby pin to secure your hair near your temple, or twist and pin your bangs at the back of your head.
  4. Spray a light mist of hairspray on your bangs to keep them in place.
  5. Wear a headband to hold your hair back and add some style to your look.

Tips and Tricks

Here are some additional tips and tricks to help you keep your side swept bangs out of your eyes:

  • Use a hair tie to create a small bun at the back of your head and pin your bangs behind it.
  • Invest in a good quality hairspray that will hold your hair in place without making it stiff.
  • Trim your bangs regularly to prevent them from getting too long and falling into your eyes.
  • Experiment with different hairstyles, such as braids or updos, to keep your hair off your face.

FAQs

Q: How do I keep my side swept bangs out of my eyes without using bobby pins?

A: You can use a hair tie to create a small bun at the back of your head and pin your bangs behind it. You can also try twisting and pinning your bangs at the back of your head.

Q: What type of headband should I use to keep my side swept bangs out of my eyes?

A: Look for a headband that complements your outfit and sits comfortably on your head. You can choose a wide or narrow headband, depending on your preference. Make sure the headband has grips on the underside to prevent it from slipping.

Hair Care Tips

Keeping your hair healthy and well-maintained is essential for achieving the perfect side swept bangs. Here are some hair care tips to keep in mind:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a mild shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using hot styling tools too frequently, as they can damage your hair.
  • Use a heat protectant spray before using hot styling tools.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.

By following these tips and tricks, you can easily keep your side swept bangs out of your eyes and enjoy your hairstyle without any hassle. Whether you prefer bobby pins, hairspray, or headbands, there are many ways to keep your hair in place and create a chic and elegant look.
