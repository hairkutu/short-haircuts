What To Do With Thick Wavy Hair A Complete Guide

Written by Frank Aug 03, 2023 · 10 min read

Thick wavy hair can be both a blessing and a curse. On the one hand, it’s voluminous and full of life. On the other hand, it can be unruly and difficult to manage. If you’re struggling with what to do with your thick wavy hair, you’re not alone. In this guide, we’ll cover everything from how to style it to how to care for it.

Table of Contents

  • How to Style Thick Wavy Hair
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Managing Thick Wavy Hair
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care for Thick Wavy Hair

How to Style Thick Wavy Hair

When it comes to styling thick wavy hair, there are a few key things to keep in mind. First, you want to use products that will enhance your natural waves without weighing them down. Second, you want to choose a style that complements your face shape and hair texture. Here are some ideas to get you started:

Beachy Waves

Beachy waves are a popular style for thick wavy hair because they’re easy to achieve and look effortlessly chic. To create this look, start by washing your hair and applying a texturizing spray. Then, use a curling iron or wand to curl random sections of hair, alternating the direction of the curls for a more natural look. Finish by scrunching your hair with your fingers to break up the curls and create a messy, beachy texture.

Half Up, Half Down

If you’re looking for a style that’s both practical and stylish, consider a half up, half down look. This style works particularly well for thick wavy hair because it allows you to show off your natural texture while keeping your hair out of your face. To create this look, simply gather the top half of your hair and secure it with a clip or tie. Leave the rest of your hair loose and tousled.

Long Layers

Long layers are a great option for thick wavy hair because they help to distribute the weight of your hair more evenly, making it easier to manage. To achieve this look, ask your stylist for long layers that start around your chin or shoulders. Then, use a texturizing spray to enhance your natural waves and create a messy, undone look.

Step-by-Step Guide

If you’re new to styling thick wavy hair, it can be helpful to have a step-by-step guide to follow. Here’s a basic routine to get you started:

  1. Start by washing your hair with a sulfate-free shampoo to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils.
  2. Apply a lightweight conditioner to the ends of your hair, avoiding the roots.
  3. Towel-dry your hair gently, being careful not to rub your hair too hard and cause frizz.
  4. Apply a leave-in conditioner or detangler to help smooth out any knots or tangles.
  5. Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to gently comb through your hair, starting at the ends and working your way up.
  6. Apply a styling product that’s designed for wavy hair, such as a mousse or curl cream.
  7. Use a diffuser attachment on your blow dryer to dry your hair, using a low heat setting and scrunching your hair as you go.
  8. If you want to add more definition to your waves, use a curling iron or wand to curl random sections of hair.
  9. Finish by applying a light-hold hairspray to help set your style.

Tips for Managing Thick Wavy Hair

Managing thick wavy hair can be a challenge, but there are a few tips and tricks that can make it easier:

  • Invest in a good quality hairbrush or comb that’s gentle on your hair.
  • Avoid using hot tools too often, as they can damage your hair and make it more prone to frizz.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to help keep your hair hydrated and healthy.
  • Avoid using products that contain alcohol or sulfates, as they can dry out your hair and make it more prone to frizz.
  • Consider getting regular trims to help keep your hair looking healthy and prevent split ends.

FAQs

Q: How often should I wash my thick wavy hair?

A: It depends on your hair type and lifestyle, but in general, you should aim to wash your hair every 2-3 days. Washing your hair too often can strip it of its natural oils and make it more prone to frizz.

Q: What’s the best way to dry thick wavy hair?

A: The best way to dry thick wavy hair is to use a diffuser attachment on your blow dryer and scrunch your hair as you go. Avoid rubbing your hair too hard with a towel, as this can cause frizz.

Q: Can I straighten my thick wavy hair?

A: Yes, you can straighten your thick wavy hair using a flat iron or straightening brush. However, be sure to use a heat protectant spray and avoid using hot tools too often, as they can damage your hair.

Hair Care for Thick Wavy Hair

Caring for thick wavy hair requires a little extra effort, but it’s worth it to keep your hair looking healthy and beautiful. Here are some tips to help you care for your hair:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils.
  • Apply a deep conditioning treatment once a week to help keep your hair hydrated and healthy.
  • Avoid using hot tools too often, as they can damage your hair and make it more prone to frizz.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to gently comb through your hair, starting at the ends and working your way up.
  • Invest in a good quality hairbrush or comb that’s gentle on your hair.

With these tips and tricks, you can learn to love your thick wavy hair and make the most of its natural beauty. Whether you prefer beachy waves or long layers, there’s a style out there that’s perfect for you. So embrace your natural texture and have fun experimenting with different looks!
