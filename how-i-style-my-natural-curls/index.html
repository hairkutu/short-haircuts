How I Style My Natural Curls

Written by Frank Mar 21, 2023 Â· 6 min read

As someone with naturally curly hair, I know first-hand how challenging it can be to find the right styling products and techniques. That's why I've put together this guide to share my personal tips and tricks for styling my natural curls.

Table of Contents

  • How To Style Natural Curls
  • Step-By-Step Guide
  • Tips for Styling Natural Curls
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care Tips for Natural Curls

How To Style Natural Curls

The first step in styling natural curls is to find the right products for your hair type. For me, I've found that using a leave-in conditioner, curl defining cream, and a styling gel works best. I also avoid using products with sulfates and silicones, as they can dry out my hair and cause frizz.

Once you have your products, it's important to apply them correctly. I like to start with wet hair and divide it into sections. I apply the leave-in conditioner and curl defining cream to each section, making sure to saturate my curls from root to tip. Then, I use the styling gel to define my curls and hold them in place.

Step-By-Step Guide

Here's my step-by-step guide for styling natural curls:

  1. Start with wet hair
  2. Divide hair into sections
  3. Apply leave-in conditioner to each section, making sure to saturate curls from root to tip
  4. Apply curl defining cream to each section, making sure to saturate curls from root to tip
  5. Use a styling gel to define curls and hold them in place
  6. Scrunch curls upwards to encourage definition and reduce frizz
  7. Let hair air dry or use a diffuser attachment on a hair dryer to dry hair gently
  8. Once hair is dry, use a hair oil or serum to add shine and reduce frizz
  9. Fluff curls gently to add volume
  10. Enjoy your beautiful, natural curls!

Tips for Styling Natural Curls

Here are some additional tips for styling natural curls:

  • Use a microfiber towel or cotton t-shirt to dry hair instead of a regular towel, which can cause frizz
  • Avoid brushing or combing hair when it's dry, as this can cause frizz and breakage
  • Sleep on a silk or satin pillowcase to reduce frizz and breakage
  • Experiment with different products and techniques to find what works best for your hair

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about styling natural curls:

Q: How often should I wash my curly hair?

A: It depends on your hair type and how much product you use. Some people with curly hair can go several days without washing, while others need to wash every other day. Experiment to find what works best for your hair.

Q: How can I reduce frizz in my curly hair?

A: Use a microfiber towel or cotton t-shirt to dry hair, avoid brushing or combing hair when it's dry, and sleep on a silk or satin pillowcase. You can also use a hair oil or serum to add shine and reduce frizz.

Hair Care Tips for Natural Curls

Here are some additional hair care tips for natural curls:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner
  • Avoid using heat styling tools, or use a heat protectant before using them
  • Deep condition hair once a week to keep it healthy and hydrated
  • Trim hair regularly to prevent split ends and breakage
  • Avoid tight hairstyles that can cause pulling and breakage

With these tips and techniques, you can achieve beautiful, natural curls that are healthy and defined. Remember to be patient and experiment with different products and techniques to find what works best for your hair. Happy styling!
