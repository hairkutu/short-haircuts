How To Shape Up Around Ears Long Hair Tips And Tricks

Written by Frank Jun 26, 2023 Â· 6 min read

If you have long hair, you know how difficult it can be to manage. One of the biggest challenges is shaping up around your ears. It can be a frustrating task, but with the right techniques, it's possible to achieve a great looking hairstyle. In this guide, we'll share some tips and tricks on how to shape up around ears long hair.

Table of Contents

  • How to Shape Up Around Ears Long Hair
  • Step by Step Guide
  • Tips for Shaping Up Around Ears
  • Frequently Asked Questions
  • Hair Care for Long Hair

How to Shape Up Around Ears Long Hair

The first step in shaping up around ears long hair is to choose the right haircut. A layered haircut is the best option as it allows you to create volume and movement. Once you have the right haircut, you can start shaping up around your ears.

Tools You Will Need:

  • Clippers
  • Scissors
  • Comb
  • Hair Dryer

Step by Step Guide

Follow these steps to shape up around ears long hair:

  1. Start by washing and drying your hair. This will help you see the natural fall and texture of your hair.
  2. Use a comb to part your hair in the middle.
  3. Take the clippers and trim the hair around the ears. Make sure to start from the bottom and work your way up. This will help you create a clean and even cut.
  4. Use the scissors to blend the hair around the ears with the rest of your hair. This will help you create a natural look.
  5. Repeat this process on the other side.
  6. Once you have shaped up around your ears, use a hair dryer to style your hair. You can use a round brush to create volume and movement.
  7. Finish with a hair spray to keep your hair in place.

Tips for Shaping Up Around Ears

Here are some tips to help you shape up around ears long hair:

  • Choose the right haircut that suits your face shape and hair texture.
  • Use sharp and high-quality clippers and scissors.
  • Start trimming from the bottom and work your way up.
  • Blend the hair around the ears with the rest of your hair to create a natural look.
  • Use a hair dryer to style your hair and create volume and movement.
  • Don't cut too much hair at once. It's better to trim a little at a time.

Frequently Asked Questions

1. How often should I shape up around my ears?

It depends on how fast your hair grows. Generally, you should shape up around your ears every 4-6 weeks.

2. Can I shape up around my ears at home?

Yes, you can shape up around your ears at home. Just make sure to use the right tools and follow the steps carefully.

3. What if I make a mistake while shaping up around my ears?

If you make a mistake, don't panic. You can always go to a professional hairstylist to fix it.

Hair Care for Long Hair

Long hair requires special care to keep it healthy and shiny. Here are some tips for hair care:

  • Wash your hair with a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair after washing.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often.
  • Apply a hair mask once a week to nourish your hair.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.

Shaping up around ears long hair can be a challenging task, but with the right techniques, it's possible to achieve a great looking hairstyle. Follow these tips and tricks to shape up around your ears and keep your long hair looking healthy and shiny.
