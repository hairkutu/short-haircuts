What Color Dress Compliments My Skin Tone

Written by Eveline Jul 27, 2023 · 6 min read

Choosing the right color dress can make all the difference in how you look and feel. The right color can enhance your natural features and make you stand out in a crowd. But with so many color options available, it can be overwhelming to know which one is right for your skin tone. In this article, we’ll go over tips and tricks on what color dress compliments your skin tone, so you can feel confident and beautiful in any outfit.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How To
  • Step by Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

Before we dive into specific colors, let’s talk about skin tones. Skin tones generally fall into three categories: cool, warm, and neutral. Cool skin tones have pink or blue undertones, while warm skin tones have yellow or golden undertones. Neutral skin tones have a mix of both warm and cool undertones. Knowing your skin tone is the first step in choosing the right color dress.

How To

One way to determine your skin tone is to look at the veins on the underside of your wrist. If they appear blue, you likely have cool undertones. If they appear green, you likely have warm undertones. If you can’t tell if they are blue or green, you may have a neutral undertone.

Step by Step

Once you know your skin tone, you can start choosing colors that compliment it. Here are some general guidelines:

  • Cool skin tones look best in colors that have blue or purple undertones, such as navy, silver, and lavender.
  • Warm skin tones look best in colors that have yellow or golden undertones, such as mustard, coral, and olive.
  • Neutral skin tones can wear a wide range of colors, but look best in muted or earthy tones, such as beige, taupe, and gray.

Tips

While these guidelines can be helpful, it’s important to remember that everyone’s skin tone is unique, and what looks good on one person may not look good on another. Here are some additional tips to keep in mind:

  • Consider your hair color and eye color when choosing a dress color. Certain colors may enhance these features.
  • Don’t be afraid to experiment with different colors and shades. You may find that a color outside of your usual comfort zone looks great on you.
  • If you’re unsure about a color, try holding it up to your face in natural light. This can give you a better idea of how the color looks on your skin.

FAQ

What colors should I avoid if I have a cool skin tone?

Colors that have yellow or orange undertones should be avoided, as they can make cool skin tones look sallow or washed out. These colors include mustard, peach, and rust.

What colors should I avoid if I have a warm skin tone?

Colors that have blue or purple undertones should be avoided, as they can make warm skin tones look dull or ashy. These colors include navy, lavender, and silver.

Hair Care

Choosing the right dress color can also enhance your hair color. Here are some tips:

  • If you have blonde hair, pastel colors and shades of blue and green can complement your hair color.
  • If you have red hair, earthy tones such as olive and mustard can complement your hair color.
  • If you have dark hair, jewel tones such as emerald and ruby can complement your hair color.

Remember, the most important factor in choosing a dress color is how you feel in it. If you love a certain color, wear it with confidence!
