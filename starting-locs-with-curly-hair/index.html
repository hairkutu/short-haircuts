Starting Locs With Curly Hair A Step By Step Guide

Written by Daniel Mar 24, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Are you considering starting locs with your curly hair but not sure where to begin? Don't worry because you're not alone. Many people with curly hair struggle with starting locs because of their hair texture. However, with the right knowledge and tools, starting locs with curly hair can be a breeze. In this article, we'll provide you with a step-by-step guide on how to start your locs with curly hair, tips, and hair care advice to help you along the way.

Description

Locs are a popular hairstyle that involves matting and twisting sections of hair until they form ropes or cords. While locs can be created with any hair texture, curly hair requires a different approach. Curly hair has more texture and volume, making it prone to tangling and matting. Starting locs with curly hair requires patience and a gentle touch to avoid damaging your hair or causing breakage.

How to Start Locs with Curly Hair

Step 1: Prepare Your Hair

Before you start your locs, it's essential to prepare your hair by washing it with a clarifying shampoo to remove any buildup or product residue. You should also deep condition your hair to add moisture and nourishment.

Step 2: Section Your Hair

Section your hair into small, equal-sized parts, using a rat-tail comb. The size of your sections will depend on the thickness of your hair and the desired size of your locs.

Step 3: Twist Your Hair

Take each section of hair and twist it gently in one direction until it begins to coil. Once it starts to coil, continue twisting until it forms a small, tight spiral. Repeat this process for each section of hair.

Step 4: Secure Your Locs

Use a small rubber band or hair tie to secure the end of each loc. This will keep the coil from unraveling and help it form a more defined shape. Make sure that the rubber band or hair tie is not too tight, as this can cause breakage or damage to your hair.

Tips for Starting Locs with Curly Hair

Starting locs with curly hair requires patience and consistency. Here are a few tips to help you along the way:

  • Be gentle with your hair to avoid breakage or damage.
  • Avoid using harsh chemicals or products that can dry out your hair.
  • Moisturize your hair regularly to keep it healthy and nourished.
  • Protect your hair at night by sleeping with a satin or silk scarf or bonnet.
  • Be patient and let your locs form naturally, without forcing or manipulating them too much.

FAQs

Can I start locs with curly hair?

Yes, you can start locs with curly hair. However, it's essential to approach the process with care and patience to avoid damage or breakage.

How long does it take to start locs with curly hair?

The time it takes to start locs with curly hair varies depending on your hair texture, the size of your sections, and how well you care for your hair. It can take anywhere from a few months to a year or more for your locs to fully form.

Can I wash my hair with locs?

Yes, you can wash your hair with locs. However, it's important to use a gentle, sulfate-free shampoo and avoid over-washing or scrubbing your scalp too vigorously.

Hair Care for Locs with Curly Hair

Caring for your locs with curly hair is essential to keep them healthy and looking their best. Here are a few tips for hair care:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo.
  • Moisturize your hair with a light oil or leave-in conditioner.
  • Avoid using heavy products that can weigh down your hair or cause buildup.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and heat with a hat or scarf.
  • Trim your ends regularly to prevent split ends or breakage.

Starting locs with curly hair can be a rewarding and beautiful journey. With the right knowledge and tools, you can achieve the look you desire while keeping your hair healthy and strong. Remember to be patient, gentle, and consistent, and you'll have beautiful locs in no time.
