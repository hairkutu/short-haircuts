How To Cut Hair With Clippers Youtube A Step By Step Guide

When it comes to haircuts, many people prefer to do it on their own, especially during these times of social distancing. However, cutting hair can be a bit challenging, especially if you are not a professional hairdresser. But with the help of YouTube and clippers, you can achieve a great haircut without leaving your home. In this article, we will guide you on how to cut hair with clippers YouTube style.

How to Cut Hair with Clippers YouTube Style

YouTube is an excellent resource for learning how to cut hair, especially with clippers. There are many videos available that can guide you through the process. However, before you start, make sure you have the necessary tools such as clippers, scissors, comb, and hair clips.

In addition, it is essential to have a clear idea of the type of haircut you want. You can search for haircut styles and choose one that suits you. You can also watch videos of haircuts and follow the instructions.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here is a step-by-step guide on how to cut hair with clippers YouTube style:

  1. Wash the hair and dry it thoroughly.
  2. Section the hair and clip it away from the area you are going to cut first.
  3. Choose a guard size for the clippers depending on the length of hair you want.
  4. Start cutting the hair from the nape of the neck, moving upwards.
  5. When you reach the top of the head, switch to scissors to cut the hair on the crown area.
  6. Blend the hair using the clippers without a guard.
  7. Cut the hair on the sides and back using the same guard size.
  8. Blend the hair on the sides and back using the clippers without a guard.
  9. Trim the hairline and neckline using the clippers or scissors.
  10. Remove the hair clips and blend the hair using the clippers.

Tips for a Great Haircut

Here are some tips to help you achieve a great haircut:

  • Start with a longer guard size and gradually move to a shorter one.
  • Don't rush the process, take your time to ensure a great result.
  • Use a comb to guide the clippers and ensure an even cut.
  • Use scissors to add texture to the hair.
  • Keep the clippers clean and oiled for a smooth cut.

Frequently Asked Questions

Here are some frequently asked questions about cutting hair with clippers YouTube style:

Q: Can I cut my hair with regular scissors?
A: Regular scissors are not recommended for cutting hair as they can cause split ends and uneven cuts. It is best to use hair cutting scissors. Q: How often should I cut my hair?
A: It depends on the type of haircut you have and how fast your hair grows. On average, hair should be cut every six to eight weeks. Q: Can I use a hairdryer to dry my hair?
A: Yes, a hairdryer can be used to dry hair before cutting. However, make sure it is not too hot to avoid damaging the hair.

Hair Care after Haircut

After cutting your hair, it is essential to take care of it to maintain its health and style. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using too many hair styling products as they can cause buildup and damage.
  • Trim the hair regularly to avoid split ends.
  • Protect the hair from the sun and heat by wearing a hat or using a hair protectant spray.

Now that you know how to cut hair with clippers YouTube style, you can achieve a great haircut anytime, anywhere. With practice and patience, you can become a pro at cutting hair.
