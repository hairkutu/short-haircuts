Best Hair Accessories For Fine Hair Uk A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Bardi Aug 08, 2023 · 11 min read

Are you tired of your fine hair always falling flat or not holding a style? Well, fear not! The right hair accessories can make all the difference. In this article, we will be discussing the best hair accessories for fine hair in the UK. From headbands to hair clips, we’ve got you covered. Let’s get started!

Headbands

Headbands are a great option for adding volume and texture to fine hair. Look for headbands that are made from materials that grip the hair such as velvet or silicone. Thin headbands are also a great option for fine hair as they won’t weigh down your locks.

How to Use Headbands

Simply place the headband on top of your head, pushing it back towards your hairline. You can wear it with your hair down or in a ponytail for a sleeker look.

Tips

  • Choose headbands that match your hair colour for a natural look.
  • Wrap a small section of hair around the headband to hide it for a more sophisticated look.
  • Experiment with different placements of the headband to find your favourite look.

FAQ

Can headbands be worn with short hair?

Yes, headbands can be worn with short hair. Opt for thinner headbands or those with a flexible band for a comfortable fit.

Hair Clips

Hair clips are perfect for securing fine hair in place. Look for clips that are small and lightweight to avoid weighing down your hair.

How to Use Hair Clips

Section your hair and use the hair clip to hold it in place. You can also use hair clips to create a half-up, half-down hairstyle or to add volume to your roots.

Tips

  • Use hair clips to create a faux fringe or to pin back bangs.
  • Choose clips with a grip to hold fine hair in place.
  • Experiment with different sizes and shapes of clips to find your favourite look.

FAQ

Can hair clips be used on wet hair?

Yes, hair clips can be used on wet hair. However, make sure to choose clips that won’t rust or damage your hair.

Scrunchies

Scrunchies are back in style and are a great option for fine hair. Look for scrunchies that are made from silk or satin to avoid damaging your hair.

How to Use Scrunchies

Gather your hair into a ponytail and wrap the scrunchie around it. You can also use multiple scrunchies to create a fun and playful look.

Tips

  • Use a scrunchie to create a messy bun or topknot.
  • Choose scrunchies in bold colours or prints to add some personality to your hairstyle.
  • Opt for scrunchies with a strong elastic to hold fine hair in place.

FAQ

Can scrunchies be worn with straight hair?

Yes, scrunchies can be worn with straight hair. They are a great option for adding volume and texture to fine, straight hair.

How to Use Bobby Pins

Tips

  • Choose pins that match your hair colour for a natural look.
  • Experiment with different shapes and sizes of pins to find your favourite look.

FAQ

Can bobby pins be used to create an updo?

Yes, bobby pins are great for creating updos. They can be used to secure the hair in place and to add texture and volume to the style.

Hair Ties

Hair ties are an essential for any hair accessory collection. Look for hair ties that are gentle on your hair and won’t cause breakage or damage.

How to Use Hair Ties

Gather your hair into a ponytail or bun and wrap the hair tie around it. You can also use multiple hair ties to create a layered ponytail or to add volume to your roots.

Tips

  • Opt for hair ties made from silk or satin to avoid damage to your hair.
  • Use a hair tie to create a messy bun or a sleek ponytail.
  • Experiment with different colours and styles of hair ties to find your favourite look.

FAQ

Can hair ties be used on curly hair?

Yes, hair ties can be used on curly hair. However, make sure to choose hair ties that won’t cause breakage or damage to your curls.

Hair Combs

Hair combs are a great option for adding volume and texture to fine hair. Look for combs that are made from materials that grip the hair such as metal or wood.

How to Use Hair Combs

Section your hair and use the comb to tease the roots for added volume. You can also use a comb to create a sleek, polished look.

Tips

  • Use a comb to create a side part or to smooth down flyaways.
  • Choose combs with wide teeth for detangling and fine teeth for teasing.
  • Experiment with different styles and shapes of combs to find your favourite look.

FAQ

Can hair combs be used on wet hair?

Yes, hair combs can be used on wet hair. However, make sure to use a wide-toothed comb to avoid causing damage or breakage.
