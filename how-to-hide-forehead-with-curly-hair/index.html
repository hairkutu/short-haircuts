How To Hide Forehead With Curly Hair

Written by Robby Jun 27, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Are you tired of constantly trying to hide your forehead with your curly hair? Well, you're not alone. Many people with curly hair struggle with this issue. In this post, we'll discuss how to hide forehead with curly hair and provide you with some helpful tips and tricks.

Table of Contents

  1. Description
  2. How to
  3. Step by Step
  4. Tips
  5. FAQ
  6. Hair Care

Description

Curly hair can be both a blessing and a curse. While it looks gorgeous, it can also be difficult to manage. One common problem that many people with curly hair face is trying to hide their forehead. This can be especially frustrating if you have a high forehead or if you're simply self-conscious about it.

How to

There are a few different ways that you can hide your forehead with curly hair. Some of the most popular methods include:

  • Pinning your hair back
  • Wearing a headband
  • Using a scarf
  • Getting bangs

Each of these methods has its own pros and cons, so it's important to find the one that works best for you.

Step by Step

Step 1: Pin Your Hair Back

The first method you can try is pinning your hair back. Here's how:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair.
  2. Take a section of hair from the front of your head and twist it backwards.
  3. Use a bobby pin to secure the twisted section of hair to the back of your head.
  4. Repeat on the other side.
  5. You can also add additional bobby pins to secure any loose pieces of hair.

Step 2: Wear a Headband

The second method you can try is wearing a headband. Here's how:

  1. Select a headband that matches your hair color or complements your outfit.
  2. Place the headband on your head, positioning it just above your eyebrows.
  3. Tuck your hair behind your ears.
  4. You can also add additional bobby pins to secure any loose pieces of hair.

Step 3: Use a Scarf

The third method you can try is using a scarf. Here's how:

  1. Select a scarf that matches your outfit or complements your hair.
  2. Place the scarf on your head, positioning it just above your eyebrows.
  3. Tie the scarf at the back of your head, leaving the ends hanging down.
  4. You can also tuck the ends of the scarf behind your ears.

Step 4: Get Bangs

The fourth and final method you can try is getting bangs. Here's how:

  1. Select the type of bangs that will work best with your hair type and face shape.
  2. Part your hair to the side.
  3. Cut your bangs to the desired length.
  4. You can also use a flat iron to style your bangs.

Tips

Here are some additional tips to help you hide your forehead with curly hair:

  • Experiment with different hairstyles and accessories to find what works best for you.
  • Use a little bit of hairspray to keep your hair in place.
  • Don't be afraid to ask your hairstylist for advice.
  • Consider getting layers in your hair to add volume and texture.

FAQ

Can I hide my forehead without cutting my hair?

Yes, you can use accessories like headbands and scarves to hide your forehead without cutting your hair.

What type of bangs should I get?

The type of bangs that will work best for you will depend on your hair type and face shape. Consult with your hairstylist for advice.

Hair Care

To keep your curly hair looking its best, it's important to take good care of it. Here are some tips:

  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner specifically designed for curly hair.
  • Avoid using a brush on your hair as it can cause frizz.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your hair.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools as much as possible.
  • Deep condition your hair once a week to keep it hydrated and healthy.

With these tips and tricks, you'll be able to confidently hide your forehead with your curly hair. Remember to experiment with different styles and accessories to find what works best for you. And most importantly, embrace your curly hair and rock it with confidence!
