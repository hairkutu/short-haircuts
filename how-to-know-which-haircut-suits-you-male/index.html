How To Know Which Haircut Suits You Male

Written by Daniel Apr 09, 2023 · 7 min read

Choosing the right haircut can make a huge difference in your overall appearance. However, it can be overwhelming to know which style will suit you best. In this article, we will guide you on how to know which haircut suits you male, and provide tips on how to maintain your new look.

Table of Contents

  1. How to Determine Your Face Shape
  2. Step-by-Step Guide to Choosing the Right Haircut
  3. Tips for Maintaining Your New Haircut
  4. FAQs
  5. Hair Care Tips

How to Determine Your Face Shape

The first step in determining which haircut will suit you best is to determine your face shape. There are six common face shapes: oval, square, rectangle, round, heart, and diamond. To determine your face shape, follow these steps:

  1. Stand in front of a mirror with your hair pulled back.
  2. Use a measuring tape to measure across your forehead, cheekbones, and jawline.
  3. Take note of which measurement is the widest.
  4. Match your measurements to the corresponding face shape:
    • Oval: forehead, cheekbones, and jawline are roughly equal
    • Square: forehead, cheekbones, and jawline are roughly equal and angular
    • Rectangle: forehead, cheekbones, and jawline are roughly equal, but the face is longer
    • Round: forehead, cheekbones, and jawline are roughly equal and soft
    • Heart: forehead is wider than the jawline, and the chin is pointed
    • Diamond: cheekbones are the widest part of the face, and the forehead and jawline are narrower

Once you have determined your face shape, you can start looking for haircuts that will best complement your features.

Step-by-Step Guide to Choosing the Right Haircut

Now that you know your face shape, follow these steps to choose the right haircut:

  1. Research different haircuts that work well with your face shape. Look for inspiration online or in magazines.
  2. Consider your hair texture and thickness. Some haircuts may not work well with certain hair types.
  3. Think about your lifestyle and how much time you are willing to spend on styling your hair.
  4. Consult with a professional hairstylist. They can offer their expert opinion and suggest haircuts that will work well with your face shape and hair type.
  5. Try out different haircuts with virtual makeover tools. This can give you a better idea of how a certain haircut will look on you.
  6. Choose a haircut that you feel confident and comfortable with.

Tips for Maintaining Your New Haircut

Congratulations on your new haircut! Here are some tips to keep your hair looking its best:

  • Follow a regular haircare routine, including washing and conditioning your hair.
  • Use styling products that work well with your hair type.
  • Visit your hairstylist regularly for trims and touch-ups.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and chlorine by wearing a hat or using a swim cap.

FAQs

Q: Can I pull off a certain haircut even if it doesn't match my face shape?

A: It's possible, but it may not be the most flattering option. It's best to choose a haircut that complements your features.

Q: How often should I get my hair cut?

A: It depends on your hair type and how quickly it grows. Generally, it's recommended to get a trim every 6-8 weeks.

Q: Can I dye my hair after getting a new haircut?

A: Yes, but it's recommended to wait a few weeks after getting a haircut to allow your hair to recover.

Hair Care Tips

Here are some additional hair care tips to keep your hair healthy:

  • Avoid washing your hair too often, as it can strip your hair of its natural oils.
  • Use a wide-toothed comb to detangle your hair, starting from the ends and working your way up.
  • Avoid using hot water when washing your hair, as it can damage your hair and scalp.
  • Eat a balanced diet that includes foods rich in vitamins and minerals that promote hair health.
  • Stay hydrated by drinking plenty of water.

By following these tips, you can ensure that your new haircut looks great and your hair stays healthy.
