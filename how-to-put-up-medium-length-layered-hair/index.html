How To Put Up Medium Length Layered Hair Step By Step Guide

Medium length layered hair is a popular hairstyle for women who want to add volume and texture to their hair. However, putting up medium length layered hair can be a challenge. In this post, we will give you a step-by-step guide on how to put up medium length layered hair.

How to Put Up Medium Length Layered Hair

Putting up medium length layered hair can be done in various ways, depending on the occasion and your personal preference. Below are some of the most popular ways to put up medium length layered hair:

  • Ponytail
  • Half-up, half-down
  • Bun
  • Braids

Ponytail

A ponytail is a classic and easy way to put up medium length layered hair. Follow these steps:

  1. Brush your hair to remove any tangles.
  2. Gather your hair at the back of your head and secure it with a hair tie.
  3. If you want to add some volume to the ponytail, tease the crown of your hair with a comb.
  4. You can also pull out small sections of hair around your face for a more relaxed look.

Half-up, Half-down

A half-up, half-down hairstyle is a great option for a casual or formal event. Follow these steps:

  1. Brush your hair to remove any tangles.
  2. Take a section of hair from the top of your head and tie it into a small ponytail.
  3. If you want to add some volume to the half-up, half-down hairstyle, tease the crown of your hair with a comb.
  4. You can also curl or straighten the ends of your hair for a more polished look.

Bun

A bun is a chic way to put up medium length layered hair. Follow these steps:

  1. Brush your hair to remove any tangles.
  2. Gather your hair into a high or low ponytail and secure it with a hair tie.
  3. Twist the ponytail around the base to create a bun.
  4. Secure the bun with bobby pins.

Braids

Braids are a versatile way to put up medium length layered hair. Follow these steps:

  1. Brush your hair to remove any tangles.
  2. Divide your hair into three sections.
  3. Start braiding your hair by crossing the right section over the middle section, then the left section over the middle section.
  4. Continue braiding until you reach the end of your hair and secure it with a hair tie.

Step by Step Guide

Here is a step-by-step guide on how to put up medium length layered hair:

  1. Brush your hair to remove any tangles.
  2. Decide on the hairstyle you want to create (ponytail, half-up, half-down, bun, or braids).
  3. Follow the steps for the chosen hairstyle.
  4. Use bobby pins or a hair tie to secure the hairstyle.
  5. Finish with hairspray to hold the hairstyle in place.

Tips for Putting Up Medium Length Layered Hair

Here are some tips to help you put up medium length layered hair:

  • Use hair ties that match the color of your hair for a more natural look.
  • Tease the crown of your hair to add volume to your hairstyle.
  • Use bobby pins to secure any loose strands of hair.
  • Experiment with different hairstyles to find the one that suits you best.

FAQs

Can I put up medium length layered hair if I have thin hair?

Yes, you can. Teasing the crown of your hair and using volumizing products can help add volume to your hair.

How can I make my hairstyle last longer?

Use hairspray to hold your hairstyle in place and avoid touching your hair too much.

Can I put up medium length layered hair if I have curly hair?

Yes, you can. Just make sure to use products that will help define your curls and keep them in place.

Hair Care for Medium Length Layered Hair

To keep your medium length layered hair healthy and looking its best, follow these hair care tips:

  • Wash your hair every 2-3 days.
  • Use a conditioner to keep your hair moisturized.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.
  • Use a hair mask once a week to nourish your hair.

Putting up medium length layered hair can be easy and fun with these tips and techniques. Experiment with different hairstyles and find the one that makes you feel confident and beautiful.
