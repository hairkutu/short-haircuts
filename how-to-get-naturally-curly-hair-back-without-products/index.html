How To Get Naturally Curly Hair Back Without Products

Written by Aprilia Jul 30, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Having naturally curly hair is a blessing, but sometimes we need to straighten it for a change. However, if you've been straightening your hair for a long time and want to get your curls back, you might be wondering how to do it without using any products. In this article, we'll show you how to get naturally curly hair back without products using simple and natural methods.

Table of Contents

  1. How to Get Naturally Curly Hair Back Without Products
  2. Step by Step
  3. Tips
  4. FAQ

How to Get Naturally Curly Hair Back Without Products

The first step in getting your curls back is to stop straightening your hair. Constant heat exposure can damage your hair and make it difficult for your curls to bounce back. So, put down that flat iron and embrace your natural hair.

The second step is to start taking care of your hair. This means using a gentle shampoo and conditioner that won't strip your hair of its natural oils. You should also avoid using harsh chemicals on your hair, such as hair dye or bleach, as these can damage your hair and make it harder for your curls to form.

The third step is to use a diffuser when blow-drying your hair. This will help to enhance your curls and prevent frizz. Make sure to use a low heat setting on your hair dryer to avoid damaging your hair.

Step by Step

Here are the steps to get your naturally curly hair back:

  1. Stop straightening your hair
  2. Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner
  3. Avoid harsh chemicals
  4. Use a diffuser when blow-drying your hair

Tips

Here are some additional tips to help you get your curls back:

  • Avoid brushing your hair when it's dry
  • Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your hair
  • Avoid using a towel to dry your hair, as this can cause frizz
  • Use a silk or satin pillowcase to prevent breakage and frizz
  • Try using a hair mask once a week to nourish your hair

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about getting naturally curly hair back:

Q: How long does it take for my curls to come back?

A: It depends on how damaged your hair is and how often you've been straightening it. It could take anywhere from a few weeks to a few months for your curls to fully bounce back.

Q: Can I still use heat on my hair?

A: Yes, but it's important to use a heat protectant spray and avoid using heat too often. Try to limit heat styling to once a week or less.

Hair Care

Here are some additional tips for taking care of your naturally curly hair:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner
  • Avoid using hot water when washing your hair, as it can strip your hair of its natural oils
  • Use a leave-in conditioner to keep your hair moisturized
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends
  • Avoid using hair elastics with metal parts, as they can damage your hair

By following these tips and taking care of your hair, you can get your naturally curly hair back without using any products. Remember, it takes time and patience, but the end result will be worth it.
