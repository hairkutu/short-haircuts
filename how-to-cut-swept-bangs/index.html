How To Cut Swept Bangs Step By Step Guide

Written by Eveline Apr 14, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Are you tired of your plain old hairstyle and want to try something new? Why not give swept bangs a try? Swept bangs are a trendy and chic hairstyle that can instantly transform your look. In this step-by-step guide, we'll show you how to cut swept bangs at home, without spending a fortune at the salon.

Description

Swept bangs are a popular hairstyle that can be cut in various lengths and styles. They are commonly known as side-swept bangs, and they add a touch of elegance and sophistication to any hairstyle. Swept bangs can be cut to suit any face shape, and they can be styled in various ways, depending on the occasion.

How to Cut Swept Bangs

Cutting swept bangs is a simple process, and you can do it at home without the help of a professional hairstylist. To cut swept bangs, you will need a pair of sharp scissors, a comb, and a mirror.

Step-by-Step Guide

  1. Start by washing and drying your hair. Make sure your hair is completely dry before cutting your bangs.
  2. Using a comb, part your hair in the middle or on one side, depending on the style you want to achieve.
  3. Then, comb the section of hair that you want to cut into bangs, and pull it forward.
  4. Using your scissors, cut the hair in a straight line, about an inch below your eyebrows.
  5. Once you have cut the hair, comb it to the side and trim any uneven ends.
  6. Repeat the process on the other side if you want symmetrical bangs.
  7. Check your bangs in the mirror and trim any additional hair if needed.
  8. Style your new swept bangs by parting them to the side or sweeping them across your forehead.
  9. Voila! You now have gorgeous swept bangs.

Tips for Cutting Swept Bangs

To ensure you get the perfect swept bangs, here are some additional tips to keep in mind:

  • Start by cutting a small section of hair, as you can always cut more if needed.
  • Use sharp scissors to prevent split ends and uneven cuts.
  • Don't cut your bangs too short, as they will take a while to grow back.
  • Trim your bangs when they are dry, as wet hair tends to shrink when it dries.
  • When trimming your bangs, hold the scissors vertically and cut into the hair, rather than across it, for a softer look.

FAQs

How often should you trim your swept bangs?

You should trim your swept bangs every four to six weeks to keep them looking neat and tidy.

Can swept bangs be styled differently?

Yes, swept bangs can be styled in various ways, depending on your preference. You can part them in the middle or on one side, or sweep them to the side or across your forehead.

What face shape suits swept bangs?

Swept bangs are versatile and can suit any face shape, but they work particularly well for oval and heart-shaped faces.

Hair Care Tips for Swept Bangs

To keep your swept bangs looking fresh and healthy, here are some hair care tips to follow:

  • Wash your hair regularly to prevent oil build-up on your bangs.
  • Use a heat protectant spray before styling your hair with heat tools.
  • Use dry shampoo to refresh your bangs between washes.
  • Trim your bangs regularly to prevent split ends.
  • Avoid touching your bangs with dirty hands to prevent breakouts.

With these tips and tricks, you can master the art of cutting swept bangs and achieve a stylish and trendy look in no time.
