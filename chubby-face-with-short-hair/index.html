Chubby Face With Short Hair A Guide To Flattering Cuts And Styles

Written by Christine Jul 31, 2023 Â· 9 min read

If you have a round or chubby face, finding the right hairstyle can be tricky. But with the right cut and styling, you can accentuate your best features and look your best. Short hair can be a great option for chubby faces because it frames the face and draws attention to the eyes and cheekbones. In this article, we'll explore some tips and tricks for styling a chubby face with short hair.

Description

Chubby faces have a rounder and fuller appearance than other face shapes. The cheeks and jawline are usually more prominent, and the face lacks defined angles. Short hair, on the other hand, refers to any haircut that falls above the shoulders. It can include bob cuts, pixie cuts, and other styles that are cropped and layered.

How to Choose the Right Cut

When choosing a short haircut for a chubby face, it's important to consider the length, layers, and texture. Here are some tips to keep in mind:

Length

Short haircuts that fall above the chin can help to elongate the face and create a more oval shape. Avoid cuts that end at the widest part of your face, as this can make your face appear even rounder.

Layers

Layered cuts can help to add volume and movement to your hair, which can balance out a chubby face. Ask your hairstylist to add layers around your crown and face to create a more flattering shape.

Texture

Textured hair can also help to create a more flattering look for a chubby face. Consider adding curls or waves to your hair, as this can soften your features and add volume.

Step by Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide to styling short hair for a chubby face:

Step 1: Start with Clean Hair

Wash your hair with a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to add body and texture. Towel dry your hair, but avoid rubbing or pulling on it, as this can cause damage and frizz.

Step 2: Apply Styling Products

Apply a small amount of mousse or styling cream to your hair, focusing on the roots and crown. This will help to add volume and hold to your hair.

Step 3: Blow Dry Your Hair

Using a round brush, blow dry your hair in sections, starting from the back and working your way to the front. Lift the roots of your hair as you blow dry to add volume and create a more lifted look.

Step 4: Style Your Hair

Once your hair is dry, use a curling iron or flat iron to add texture and shape to your hair. You can also use a hair straightener to create sleek and smooth locks.

Tips for Styling Short Hair with a Chubby Face

Here are some additional tips to keep in mind when styling short hair for a chubby face:

Tip 1: Avoid Heavy Fringes

Heavy fringes or bangs can make your face appear even rounder. Instead, opt for side-swept bangs or a layered fringe that frames your face.

Tip 2: Add Highlights

Adding highlights to your hair can create depth and dimension, which can help to slim down your face.

Tip 3: Experiment with Accessories

Accessories can be a great way to add interest and style to your short hair. Try adding a headband, hair clip, or scarf to your look to create a more personalized style.

FAQs

What is the best short haircut for a chubby face?

The best short haircut for a chubby face is one that is layered, textured, and falls above the chin. Bob cuts, pixie cuts, and shaggy styles can all be flattering options.

Can I still have short hair if I have a chubby face?

Absolutely! Short hair can be a great option for a chubby face if you choose the right cut and style it properly. Just remember to keep the length above the chin and add layers and texture to balance out your features.

How can I make my face look slimmer with short hair?

Adding volume and texture to your hair can help to create a more lifted and elongated look for your face. You can also experiment with highlights and accessories to draw attention away from your cheeks and jawline.

Hair Care for Chubby Faces with Short Hair

Keeping your hair healthy and moisturized is key to maintaining a flattering style. Here are some tips for caring for your hair:

Tip 1: Use a Moisturizing Shampoo and Conditioner

Choose a shampoo and conditioner that is formulated for your hair type and texture. Look for products that contain moisturizing ingredients like shea butter, coconut oil, or argan oil.

Tip 2: Avoid Heat Styling Too Often

Heat styling can cause damage and breakage to your hair, so try to limit how often you use hot tools like curling irons and flat irons. If you do use heat styling, be sure to use a heat protectant spray to minimize damage.

Tip 3: Get Regular Haircuts

Regular haircuts can help to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends. Aim to get a trim every 6-8 weeks to keep your hair looking its best.

In conclusion, short hair can be a great option for chubby faces if you choose the right cut and style it properly. Remember to keep the length above the chin, add layers and texture, and experiment with accessories to create a personalized look. By following these tips and caring for your hair properly, you can achieve a flattering and stylish look that highlights your best features.
