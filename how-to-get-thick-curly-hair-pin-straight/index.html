How To Get Thick Curly Hair Pin Straight A Step By Step Guide

Written by Daniel May 11, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Do you have thick curly hair that you want to straighten? You're not alone. Many women with curly hair want to achieve a pin-straight look from time to time. However, it can be challenging to achieve this look without damaging your hair. In this guide, we'll walk you through how to get thick curly hair pin straight without causing damage to your hair.

How to Get Thick Curly Hair Pin Straight: Step by Step

Step 1: Wash Your Hair

The first step to getting pin-straight hair is to wash your hair. Use a clarifying shampoo to remove any product buildup or oil from your hair. Rinse your hair thoroughly with warm water and towel dry it.

Step 2: Apply Heat Protectant

Before you start straightening your hair, you need to apply a heat protectant. This will help prevent your hair from getting damaged. Apply the heat protectant evenly throughout your hair, focusing on the ends.

Step 3: Blow Dry Your Hair

Next, you need to blow dry your hair. Use a round brush to straighten your hair while you blow dry it. This will help to smooth out your hair and prepare it for the straightening process.

Step 4: Section Your Hair

Once your hair is dry, section it into small sections. This will make it easier to straighten your hair and ensure that all of your hair gets straightened.

Step 5: Straighten Your Hair

Using a high-quality flat iron, straighten each section of your hair. Be sure to start at the roots and work your way down to the ends. Use a comb to help guide the flat iron down each section of hair.

Step 6: Apply Finishing Products

Once you've straightened your hair, apply some finishing products to help keep it straight. You can use a serum or a shine spray to add some shine to your hair.

Tips for Getting Pin-Straight Hair

Here are some tips to keep in mind when straightening your hair:

  • Use a high-quality flat iron to prevent damage to your hair.
  • Apply a heat protectant to your hair before straightening it.
  • Blow dry your hair before straightening it to help smooth it out.
  • Section your hair into small sections to ensure that all of it gets straightened.
  • Apply finishing products to help keep your hair straight.

FAQs

Q: Can I straighten my hair if it's wet?

A: No, you should never straighten your hair when it's wet. This can cause damage to your hair and even burn it. Always make sure your hair is completely dry before straightening it.

Q: How often should I straighten my hair?

A: It's best to avoid straightening your hair too often, as this can cause damage to your hair. Try to limit straightening your hair to once a week or less.

Hair Care for Pin-Straight Hair

Once you've straightened your hair, it's important to take good care of it to keep it looking healthy. Here are some tips to help you care for your pin-straight hair:

  • Use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair hydrated.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.
  • Avoid using too much product on your hair, as this can weigh it down.
  • Consider using a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy.

By following these tips, you can keep your pin-straight hair looking healthy and beautiful. Now that you know how to get thick curly hair pin straight, you can achieve this look whenever you want!
