How To Cut Underarm Hair With Scissors A Step By Step Guide

Written by Luffy Apr 19, 2023 · 6 min read

If you're looking for a quick and easy way to get rid of your underarm hair, cutting it with scissors might be the solution you're looking for. Not only is it cost-effective, but it's also a great way to avoid irritation and ingrown hairs that may occur with other hair removal methods. In this article, we'll be discussing how to cut underarm hair with scissors in a few simple steps.

How to Cut Underarm Hair with Scissors

Cutting underarm hair with scissors may seem daunting at first, but with the right technique, it's actually quite simple. Here's how to do it:

Step 1: Gather Your Materials

Before you start, make sure you have the right tools. You'll need a pair of scissors, preferably with sharp blades, a comb, and a towel.

Step 2: Trim Your Hair

Start by combing your underarm hair downwards. This will help you see the length of your hair and make it easier to cut. Using your scissors, trim the hair to your desired length. Be sure to cut slowly and carefully to avoid any accidents.

Step 3: Check for Any Stray Hairs

After trimming, use your comb to check for any stray hairs. If you notice any, carefully trim them with your scissors.

Step 4: Clean Up

Once you're finished, clean up any loose hair with a towel or brush. You may also want to rinse your underarms with warm water to remove any hair clippings.

Tips for Cutting Underarm Hair with Scissors

While cutting your underarm hair with scissors is a simple process, there are a few things you can do to make it even easier:

  • Use sharp scissors to avoid any accidents or pulling
  • Trim slowly and carefully to avoid cutting too much hair
  • Comb your hair downwards before cutting to get an even trim
  • Check for stray hairs after trimming to ensure a smooth finish

FAQs

1. Is it safe to cut underarm hair with scissors?

Yes, it's safe to cut underarm hair with scissors as long as you're careful and use the right technique.

2. Can I use any type of scissors?

While you can technically use any type of scissors, it's best to use ones with sharp blades to avoid any accidents or pulling.

3. How often should I cut my underarm hair?

This depends on your personal preference and hair growth rate. Some people may need to cut their underarm hair every few weeks, while others may only need to do it once a month.

Hair Care for Cutting Underarm Hair with Scissors

While cutting your underarm hair with scissors is a great way to avoid irritation and ingrown hairs, it's still important to take good care of your skin. Here are a few tips:

  • Exfoliate regularly to avoid ingrown hairs
  • Moisturize your underarms to keep them smooth and hydrated
  • Avoid using harsh soaps or deodorants that may cause irritation
  • Consider using a soothing aloe vera gel after trimming to calm any irritation

Cutting underarm hair with scissors is a quick and easy way to get rid of unwanted hair. By following these simple steps and tips, you can achieve a smooth, irritation-free underarm area.
