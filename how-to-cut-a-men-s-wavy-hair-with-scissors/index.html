How To Cut A Men s Wavy Hair With Scissors

Written by Eveline Apr 20, 2023 · 7 min read

Wavy hair can be tricky to cut, but with the right tools and techniques, you can achieve a great look. In this article, we’ll show you how to cut a men's wavy hair with scissors. Whether you’re a professional barber or just trying to save some money by cutting your own hair, this guide will help you achieve a stylish and polished look.

Description

Wavy hair is a beautiful and unique hair type that requires a different approach when cutting. The texture of wavy hair can make it difficult to achieve a clean and even cut, but with the right knowledge and tools, it can be done. Cutting wavy hair with scissors requires a bit of patience and precision, but the end result is well worth the effort.

How to Cut a Men's Wavy Hair with Scissors

Step 1: Prepare your Tools

Before you start cutting, make sure you have the right tools. You will need a pair of sharp scissors, a comb, and a spray bottle filled with water. It’s important to use sharp scissors to ensure a clean and even cut. A comb will help you section off the hair and guide your scissors. The spray bottle will help you keep the hair moist and easier to work with.

Step 2: Wash the Hair

It’s important to start with clean and dry hair. Wash the hair and let it air dry or blow dry it. This will help you see the natural texture and shape of the hair.

Step 3: Section the Hair

Using the comb, section off the hair into manageable sections. Start at the back of the head and work your way forward. Use the comb to separate the hair into horizontal sections.

Step 4: Start Cutting

Starting at the back of the head, take a small section of hair and comb it out. Hold the hair between your fingers and start cutting with the scissors. Use a slight angle to create a layered effect. Continue cutting small sections of hair until you reach the top of the head.

Step 5: Cut the Top

For the top of the head, comb the hair forward and hold it between your fingers. Use the scissors to cut the hair at an angle, creating a layered effect. Be careful not to cut too much hair at once.

Step 6: Blend the Sides

Using the comb and scissors, blend the sides of the hair into the top. Start at the top and work your way down, cutting small sections of hair at an angle.

Step 7: Check for Evenness

Once you’ve finished cutting, check for evenness. Use the comb to comb the hair straight and check for any uneven spots. If you notice any unevenness, trim the hair until it’s even.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you achieve the best results when cutting wavy hair:

  • Start with clean and dry hair.
  • Use sharp scissors.
  • Section the hair into manageable sections.
  • Use a comb to guide your scissors.
  • Cut small sections of hair at a time.
  • Blend the sides into the top for a natural look.

FAQ

What type of scissors should I use to cut wavy hair?

It’s important to use sharp scissors to ensure a clean and even cut. Look for scissors that are specifically designed for cutting hair.

Do I need to use any products when cutting wavy hair?

No, you don’t need to use any products when cutting wavy hair. However, you can use a spray bottle filled with water to keep the hair moist and easier to work with.

Hair Care

To keep your wavy hair looking its best, it’s important to take good care of it. Use a shampoo and conditioner designed for wavy hair, and avoid using hot tools like blow dryers and straighteners too often. Instead, let your hair air dry and use a diffuser to enhance your natural waves.

Cutting a men's wavy hair with scissors can be challenging, but with the right tools and techniques, you can achieve a great look. Follow these steps and tips to achieve a stylish and polished cut. Remember to take good care of your hair to keep it looking its best.
