Best Short Haircut For Fat Round Face

Written by Oliver Jun 08, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Are you tired of trying new hairstyles that don't suit your round face? Do you want to find the best short haircut for your fat round face that will make you look stunning? Look no further, as we have compiled a list of the best short haircuts for a round face that will flatter your features and make you feel confident.

Description:

Having a round face can make it challenging to find the right hairstyle that complements your features. It's essential to choose a hairstyle that adds volume to your hair and creates an illusion of a slimmer face. Short haircuts are perfect for round faces as they add height and volume to the crown, making the face appear longer and slimmer. The best short haircut for a fat round face is one that adds height to the crown, has layers that frame the face, and has side-swept bangs that soften the features.

How to:

Here's how to get the best short haircut for your fat round face:

Step 1: Consult with a Hairstylist

Consult with a hairstylist to determine the best short haircut that will flatter your features. A hairstylist will be able to assess your face shape, hair texture, and personal style to recommend the perfect short haircut for you.

Step 2: Choose the Right Haircut

Choose a short haircut that adds height to the crown and has layers that frame the face. The layers will add volume to the hair, creating an illusion of a slimmer face. The side-swept bangs will soften the features and balance out the face shape.

Step 3: Style Your Hair

Style your hair by blow-drying it with a round brush to add volume to the crown. Use a hair straightener to create soft waves or curls, or a texturizing spray to add texture to the hair. Use a side part to create a flattering look that slims down the face.

Tips:

Here are some tips to keep in mind when choosing the best short haircut for your fat round face:

  • Choose a haircut that adds height to the crown
  • Add layers to the hair to create volume
  • Choose side-swept bangs to soften the features
  • Avoid blunt bangs or straight hairstyles that add width to the face

FAQ:

Here are some frequently asked questions about the best short haircut for a fat round face:

What is the best short haircut for a fat round face?

The best short haircut for a fat round face is one that adds height to the crown, has layers that frame the face, and has side-swept bangs that soften the features.

Should I avoid blunt bangs if I have a round face?

Yes, you should avoid blunt bangs if you have a round face as they add width to the face. Instead, choose side-swept bangs that soften the features and balance out the face shape.

How can I add volume to my hair?

You can add volume to your hair by blow-drying it with a round brush, using a texturizing spray, or getting layers cut into your hair.

Hair Care:

Here are some hair care tips to keep your hair healthy and looking its best:

  • Wash your hair with a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to prevent dryness
  • Avoid using hot tools on your hair too frequently to prevent damage
  • Use a hair mask once a week to moisturize and nourish your hair
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends and maintain the shape of your haircut

Now that you know the best short haircut for a fat round face, it's time to book an appointment with your hairstylist and rock a new look that flatters your features. Remember, confidence is key, so wear your new haircut with pride and strut your stuff!
