Hair Care for Growing Hairline on Forehead Female - Wash your hair regularly with a mild shampoo and conditioner. - Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair and avoid pulling on your hairline. - Avoid using hot water to wash your hair as it can damage your hair and scalp. - Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends and breakage. Conclusion Growing hairline on forehead female requires a combination of healthy habits, natural remedies, and patience.
