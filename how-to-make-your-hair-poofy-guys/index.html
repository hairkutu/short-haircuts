How To Make Your Hair Poofy Guys A Step By Step Guide

Written by Luffy Sep 13, 2023 Â· 6 min read

If you're looking to add some volume and texture to your hair, you've come to the right place. In this article, we'll be discussing how to make your hair poofy guys. We'll cover everything from the tools you'll need to the techniques you'll use, so let's get started.

Table of Contents

  • Tools You'll Need
  • Prepping Your Hair
  • Blow Drying Your Hair
  • Styling Your Hair
  • Tips and Tricks
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care Tips

Tools You'll Need

Before we get started, let's talk about the tools you'll need to achieve that poofy hair look. Here's what you'll need:

  • A blow dryer
  • A round brush
  • A heat protectant spray
  • A volumizing mousse or spray
  • Hairspray

Make sure you have all of these tools on hand before you begin.

Prepping Your Hair

Now that you have your tools, it's time to prep your hair. Start by washing your hair with a volumizing shampoo and conditioner. Towel dry your hair and apply a heat protectant spray to your hair to protect it from the heat of the blow dryer.

Blow Drying Your Hair

Next, it's time to blow dry your hair. Use a round brush to lift your hair at the roots and blow dry it in an upward motion. This will help create volume at the roots of your hair. Make sure to dry your hair completely before moving on to the next step.

Styling Your Hair

Now it's time to style your hair. Apply a volumizing mousse or spray to your hair and work it into your roots. Flip your hair upside down and blow dry it, using your hands to scrunch your hair as you go. This will help create texture and volume throughout your hair.

Once your hair is dry, use a round brush to smooth out any frizziness or flyaways. Finish off your look with a light mist of hairspray to hold your hair in place.

Tips and Tricks

Here are a few tips and tricks to help you achieve the perfect poofy hair:

  • Use a volumizing mousse or spray to add extra texture and volume to your hair.
  • Blow dry your hair in an upward motion to create volume at the roots.
  • Use a round brush to smooth out any frizziness.
  • Flip your hair upside down when blow drying to create extra volume.
  • Finish off your look with a light mist of hairspray to hold your hair in place.

FAQs

Q: Can I make my hair poofy without heat?

A: While heat is the most effective way to add volume and texture to your hair, there are other methods you can try. Try using a volumizing shampoo and conditioner, and use a volumizing mousse or spray to add texture to your hair.

Q: How long will my poofy hair last?

A: The length of time your poofy hair will last depends on a variety of factors, including the products you use and the weather conditions. Generally, poofy hair will last for a few hours to a day.

Hair Care Tips

Finally, it's important to take care of your hair to keep it looking healthy and voluminous. Here are a few hair care tips:

  • Avoid using heat on your hair too often, as this can damage your hair and make it look flat and lifeless.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy and moisturized.
  • Avoid using too many styling products, as this can weigh your hair down and make it look greasy.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair looking healthy and prevent split ends.

By following these tips and using the techniques we've outlined in this article, you can achieve the perfect poofy hair look. Have fun experimenting and finding the style that works best for you!
