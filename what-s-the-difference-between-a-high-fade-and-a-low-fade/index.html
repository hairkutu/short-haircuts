What s The Difference Between A High Fade And A Low Fade

Written by Aprilia Apr 01, 2023 Â· 9 min read

If you're someone who loves experimenting with new hairstyles, you've probably heard of fade haircuts. Fades are one of the most popular haircuts for men, and they come in different variations, including high fade and low fade. In this blog post, we'll discuss the difference between a high fade and a low fade haircut.

What is a Fade Haircut?

A fade haircut is a men's hairstyle that gradually fades from short to longer hair, creating a seamless blend. The fade can be high, low, or mid, depending on how high or low the taper starts. Fades are versatile haircuts that can be styled in different ways, including buzz cuts, crew cuts, and comb-overs. They're also suitable for all hair types and lengths.

What is a High Fade?

A high fade starts from the temple or the parietal ridge, which is the upper part of the head. The hair is cut short or shaved at the sides and back, gradually blending into longer hair on the top. The high fade creates a sharp and clean look, making it a popular choice for men who want a bold and edgy haircut. High fades are also suitable for men with round or oval face shapes.

What is a Low Fade?

A low fade starts from the nape of the neck or just above the ear. The hair is cut short or shaved at the sides and back, gradually blending into longer hair on the top. The low fade creates a subtle and natural look, making it a popular choice for men who want a classic and timeless haircut. Low fades are also suitable for men with square or rectangular face shapes.

What's the Difference Between a High Fade and a Low Fade?

The main difference between a high fade and a low fade is the starting point of the taper. High fades start from the temple or the parietal ridge, while low fades start from the nape of the neck or just above the ear. High fades create a more dramatic and bold look, while low fades create a more subtle and natural look. The choice between a high fade and a low fade depends on personal preference, face shape, and hair type.

How to Get a High Fade?

To get a high fade, you need to visit a professional barber or hairstylist. The barber will use clippers to cut the hair short or shave it at the sides and back, gradually blending it into longer hair on the top. The barber may use different clipper guards and scissors to create a seamless blend. After the haircut, the barber may style the hair using pomade, wax, or gel.

How to Get a Low Fade?

To get a low fade, you need to follow the same process as getting a high fade. The only difference is the starting point of the taper. The barber will start the taper from the nape of the neck or just above the ear, creating a subtle and natural look. The barber may also use different clipper guards and scissors to create a seamless blend. After the haircut, the barber may style the hair using pomade, wax, or gel.

Tips for Maintaining a Fade Haircut

Here are some tips for maintaining a fade haircut:

  • Visit a professional barber or hairstylist every 2-4 weeks for a touch-up.
  • Use a high-quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and moisturized.
  • Use a hairbrush or comb to style your hair and maintain the shape of the fade.
  • Avoid using too much product, as it can weigh down your hair and make it look greasy.
  • Protect your hair from the sun, wind, and other environmental factors that can damage it.

FAQs

1. Can I get a fade haircut if I have curly hair?

Yes, you can get a fade haircut if you have curly hair. However, you need to find a barber or hairstylist who is experienced in cutting curly hair. Curly hair requires different cutting techniques and products than straight hair, so it's essential to find someone who knows how to work with your hair type.

2. Can I style my fade haircut in different ways?

Yes, you can style your fade haircut in different ways. Fades are versatile haircuts that can be styled in different ways, depending on your personal preference and the occasion. You can style your fade in a slicked-back, messy, or textured look, using different hair products.

3. How do I know if a high fade or a low fade is suitable for my face shape?

If you have a round or oval face shape, a high fade may be more suitable for you, as it creates a sharper and more angular look. If you have a square or rectangular face shape, a low fade may be more suitable for you, as it creates a softer and more rounded look. However, the choice between a high fade and a low fade ultimately depends on personal preference and hair type.

Conclusion

Fade haircuts are trendy and versatile haircuts that come in different variations, including high fade and low fade. The main difference between a high fade and a low fade is the starting point of the taper, which creates a different look and style. Whether you choose a high fade or a low fade, it's essential to visit a professional barber or hairstylist and maintain your haircut regularly. With the right care and styling, your fade haircut can be a timeless and stylish look for any occasion.
