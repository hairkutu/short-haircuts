Does Short Hair Look Good On Square Faces

Written by Daniel Aug 01, 2023 Â· 9 min read

When it comes to hairstyles, one of the most common questions people ask is, "does short hair look good on square faces?" The answer is yes! Short hair can look great on square faces, but it's important to choose the right style. In this article, we'll discuss how to choose the perfect short hairstyle for your square face shape, as well as tips for maintaining and caring for your hair.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to Choose the Perfect Short Hairstyle for Square Faces
  • Step by Step Guide
  • Tips for Maintaining and Caring for Short Hair on Square Faces
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care for Short Hair on Square Faces

Description

Square faces are characterized by a strong, angular jawline and a forehead that is almost as wide as the cheeks. Short hair can help soften these features and create a more balanced look. However, not all short hairstyles are created equal. To flatter a square face, you'll want to choose a style that adds height and volume at the crown, while minimizing width at the jawline.

How to Choose the Perfect Short Hairstyle for Square Faces

When choosing a short hairstyle for a square face, there are a few key factors to consider:

  • Length: Ideally, the length should fall between your chin and your shoulders. This length helps elongate your face and draw attention away from your jawline.
  • Layers: Layers can help add volume and movement to your hair, which can balance out your strong jawline.
  • Bangs: Bangs can help soften your features and create a more feminine look. However, be sure to choose a style that is swept to the side or has an asymmetrical cut, as straight-across bangs can emphasize your square jawline.

Step by Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide to choosing the perfect short hairstyle for your square face:

  1. Start by determining your face shape. You can do this by measuring the width of your forehead, cheeks, and jawline.
  2. Choose a style that falls between your chin and shoulders.
  3. Look for styles with layers that add volume and movement.
  4. Consider adding bangs that are swept to the side or have an asymmetrical cut.
  5. Talk to your hairstylist about your face shape and the style you're considering.
  6. Be open to suggestions from your hairstylist. They may have ideas for styles that you haven't considered.
  7. Ask your hairstylist for tips on how to style and maintain your new haircut.

Tips for Maintaining and Caring for Short Hair on Square Faces

Once you've chosen the perfect short hairstyle for your square face, it's important to take care of your hair to keep it looking its best. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly: Short hair can get oily quickly, so it's important to wash it regularly to keep it looking clean and fresh.
  • Use a good conditioner: Conditioner helps keep your hair soft and manageable. Look for a conditioner that is designed for your hair type.
  • Use the right styling products: Choose styling products that are designed for short hair, and use them sparingly to avoid weighing down your hair.
  • Get regular trims: Short hair can lose its shape quickly, so it's important to get regular trims to keep it looking neat and tidy.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about short hair on square faces:

  • Can I wear my hair in a pixie cut if I have a square face? Yes! A pixie cut can look great on square faces, especially if it's styled with volume at the crown.
  • What's the best way to style short hair on a square face? The best way to style short hair on a square face is to add volume at the crown and minimize width at the jawline. Use a round brush to lift your hair at the roots, and avoid styles that are too flat or too spiky.
  • Can I wear my hair straight if I have a square face? Yes! Straight hair can look great on square faces, especially if it's cut with layers that add movement and volume.

Hair Care for Short Hair on Square Faces

Here are some additional tips for caring for short hair on square faces:

  • Avoid heat styling: Heat styling tools like flat irons and curling irons can damage your hair over time. If possible, let your hair air dry, or use a diffuser to dry it on a low heat setting.
  • Protect your hair from the sun: UV rays can damage your hair and cause it to become dry and brittle. Wear a hat or use a hair protectant spray when you're out in the sun.
  • Eat a healthy diet: A healthy diet can help keep your hair looking its best. Eat plenty of fruits and vegetables, and drink plenty of water to keep your hair hydrated.
  • Take care of your scalp: A healthy scalp is essential for healthy hair. Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner, and avoid scratching or picking at your scalp.

In conclusion, short hair can look great on square faces if you choose the right style. Look for styles that add volume at the crown and minimize width at the jawline, and be sure to take care of your hair to keep it looking its best. With the right care and styling, you can rock a short hairstyle that flatters your square face shape.
