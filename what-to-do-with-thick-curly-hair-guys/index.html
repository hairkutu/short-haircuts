What To Do With Thick Curly Hair Guys

Written by Robby Mar 23, 2023 · 7 min read

Thick curly hair can be both a blessing and a curse for guys. While it looks great when styled properly, it can be difficult to manage and maintain. In this article, we will provide you with tips and advice on how to style and care for your thick curly hair.

Description

Thick curly hair is characterized by its volume and texture. It can be difficult to style and manage, but with the right products and techniques, it can look amazing. Thick curly hair can be found in many different lengths and styles, from short and textured to long and flowing.

How To Style Thick Curly Hair

When it comes to styling thick curly hair, there are a few key things to keep in mind. First, it’s important to use products that are specifically designed for curly hair. Look for products that provide moisture, definition, and hold.

Second, it’s important to find a hairstyle that works for your hair type and face shape. Some popular hairstyles for thick curly hair include the undercut, the messy crop, and the curly fringe. Experiment with different styles to find the one that works best for you.

Step By Step Guide

Here’s a step-by-step guide to styling your thick curly hair:

  1. Start with freshly washed and conditioned hair.
  2. Apply a curl-defining product to damp hair, such as a curl cream or gel.
  3. Use a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer to dry your hair. This will help to enhance your natural curls and prevent frizz.
  4. Once your hair is dry, use your fingers to shape your curls and create your desired style.
  5. Finish with a hairspray or other finishing product to hold your style in place.

Tips

Here are some additional tips for styling your thick curly hair:

  • Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your hair, rather than a brush which can cause frizz.
  • Consider getting regular trims to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as this can cause damage and dryness.
  • Experiment with different products and techniques to find what works best for your hair type and style.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about styling thick curly hair:

Q: How often should I wash my thick curly hair?

A: It’s best to wash your hair every 2-3 days to prevent dryness and breakage. Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to help maintain moisture in your hair.

Q: Can I use a hair straightener on my thick curly hair?

A: While it is possible to straighten curly hair, it’s not recommended to do so too often as it can cause damage and dryness. If you do choose to straighten your hair, be sure to use a heat protectant and a low heat setting.

Q: How do I prevent frizz in my thick curly hair?

A: To prevent frizz, avoid touching your hair too much and use a microfiber towel or t-shirt to dry your hair rather than a regular towel. You can also apply a frizz-fighting serum or oil to your hair before styling.

Hair Care

Caring for your thick curly hair is important to keep it healthy and looking its best. Here are some tips for maintaining your hair:

  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to help moisturize your hair.
  • Avoid using hot tools too often, such as hair dryers and straighteners.
  • Wear a hat or use a UV protectant spray to protect your hair from the sun.
  • Avoid using harsh chemicals or dyes on your hair, as these can cause damage and dryness.

With these tips and techniques, you can learn to love and embrace your thick curly hair. Experiment with different styles and products to find what works best for you, and don’t be afraid to ask your stylist for advice.
