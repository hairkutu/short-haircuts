Short Bob Haircuts For Round Faces 2019 A Guide To Flattering Styles

Written by William Mar 25, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Are you tired of your round face shape making it difficult to find the perfect haircut? Look no further than the short bob haircut! In 2019, this classic style was updated with modern twists that make it perfect for round faces. Read on for our guide to short bob haircuts for round faces 2019.

Description

The short bob haircut is a classic style that has been around for decades. It is characterized by its length, which falls just above the shoulders, and its blunt cut. In 2019, the short bob was updated with modern twists such as textured layers, bold colors, and asymmetrical cuts to make it more flattering for round faces.

How to

If you're considering a short bob haircut for your round face, here are some steps to follow:

  1. Consult with your hairstylist to find the best length and style for your face shape.
  2. Choose a cut that adds volume and texture to the crown of your head to elongate your face.
  3. Consider adding layers to add movement and dimension to your hair.
  4. Opt for an asymmetrical cut to create angles that slim the face.
  5. Choose a bold color to draw attention away from the roundness of your face.

Step by Step

Here's a step-by-step guide to getting a short bob haircut for your round face:

  1. Visit your hairstylist and show them pictures of short bob haircuts you like.
  2. Consult with your stylist to determine the best length and style for your face shape.
  3. Wash and condition your hair before your appointment.
  4. Sit in the stylist's chair and let them cut your hair according to the agreed-upon style.
  5. Ask your stylist to add texture and layers to your hair to add volume and movement.
  6. Have your hair blow-dried and styled to your liking.
  7. Maintain your haircut by getting regular trims every 6-8 weeks.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when getting a short bob haircut for your round face:

  • Choose a cut that adds volume to the crown of your head to elongate your face.
  • Consider adding layers to add movement and dimension to your hair.
  • Opt for an asymmetrical cut to create angles that slim the face.
  • Choose a bold color to draw attention away from the roundness of your face.
  • Maintain your haircut by getting regular trims every 6-8 weeks.

FAQ

What is the best length for a short bob haircut for a round face?

The best length for a short bob haircut for a round face is just above the shoulders. This length elongates the face and draws attention away from the roundness of the face.

What is the best style for a short bob haircut for a round face?

The best style for a short bob haircut for a round face is one that adds volume and texture to the crown of the head. This creates the illusion of length and slims the face.

How often should I get a trim for my short bob haircut?

You should get a trim for your short bob haircut every 6-8 weeks. This will help maintain the shape and style of your haircut.

Hair Care

To keep your short bob haircut looking its best, follow these hair care tips:

  • Wash and condition your hair regularly.
  • Use a heat protectant spray before using heat styling tools.
  • Avoid using too much product, as this can weigh down your hair.
  • Get regular trims to maintain the shape and style of your haircut.

With these tips and tricks, you'll be able to rock a short bob haircut for your round face in 2019 and beyond!
