Best Length For Thick Wavy Hair Finding Your Ideal Look

Written by Aprilia Jul 26, 2023 Â· 7 min read

If you have thick wavy hair, you know how challenging it can be to find the perfect length that flatters your face shape and suits your lifestyle. In this article, we'll explore the best length for thick wavy hair and offer some tips on how to achieve your desired look.

Table of Contents

  • How to Determine Your Ideal Length
  • Step-by-Step Guide to Achieving Your Desired Length
  • Tips for Maintaining Your Thick Wavy Hair
  • FAQs about Best Length for Thick Wavy Hair
  • Hair Care Tips for Thick Wavy Hair

How to Determine Your Ideal Length

The best length for thick wavy hair depends on various factors such as your face shape, hair texture, and lifestyle. Here are some tips on how to determine your ideal length:

  • Consider your face shape. If you have an oval face shape, you can go for any length. If your face is round, go for longer lengths or layers that frame your face. For square faces, longer lengths with soft waves work well. Heart-shaped faces look great with chin-length bobs, and diamond-shaped faces can pull off short pixie cuts.
  • Think about your hair texture. If you have coarse, thick hair, longer lengths can weigh down your hair and make it look flat. Shorter layers can add more volume and movement. If you have fine hair, longer lengths can add more body and fullness.
  • Consider your lifestyle. If you're always on-the-go, shorter styles that require less maintenance may be more practical. If you have more time to style your hair, longer lengths can offer more versatility.

Step-by-Step Guide to Achieving Your Desired Length

Once you've determined your ideal length, here's a step-by-step guide to achieving your desired look:

  1. Consult with a hairstylist to discuss your desired length and style. They can offer suggestions based on your hair texture and face shape.
  2. Start with a trim to get rid of any split ends and create a fresh canvas.
  3. If you're going for shorter lengths, consider adding layers to add more volume and movement.
  4. If you're going for longer lengths, consider adding face-framing layers or soft waves to add more dimension.
  5. Use a high-quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and nourished.
  6. Consider using styling products such as mousse or texturizing spray to enhance your waves and add more volume.
  7. Experiment with different styling techniques such as braids or twists to create different looks.

Tips for Maintaining Your Thick Wavy Hair

Maintaining thick wavy hair can be challenging, but with a few tips, you can keep your hair healthy and looking great:

  • Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your hair instead of a brush to prevent breakage.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools such as blow dryers or straighteners too often to prevent damage.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair moisturized and nourished.
  • Avoid using harsh chemicals such as bleach or hair dye too often to prevent damage.

FAQs about Best Length for Thick Wavy Hair

Here are some frequently asked questions about finding the best length for thick wavy hair:

Q: Can I still have long hair if I have thick wavy hair?

A: Absolutely! Long hair can look great on thick wavy hair, but make sure to add layers or soft waves to prevent your hair from looking too heavy or flat.

Q: What styling products work best for thick wavy hair?

A: Mousse, texturizing spray, and sea salt spray work well for enhancing waves and adding volume.

Hair Care Tips for Thick Wavy Hair

Here are some hair care tips specifically for thick wavy hair:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo to prevent your hair from drying out.
  • Avoid using heavy conditioners that can weigh down your hair.
  • Use a microfiber towel or an old t-shirt to gently pat your hair dry instead of rubbing it vigorously with a towel.
  • Avoid using hot water when washing your hair, as it can strip your hair of its natural oils.
  • Consider getting regular trims to prevent split ends and keep your hair looking healthy.

With these tips and tricks, you can find the best length for your thick wavy hair and keep your locks looking healthy and fabulous.
