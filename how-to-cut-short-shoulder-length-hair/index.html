How To Cut Short Shoulder Length Hair A Step By Step Guide

Written by Robby Mar 24, 2023 Â· 6 min read

If you're thinking about cutting your hair short, shoulder length is a great length to start with. This length is versatile and suits most face shapes. Whether you're looking for a sleek bob or a messy pixie cut, cutting your hair short is a great way to change up your look. In this article, we'll guide you through how to cut short shoulder length hair.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to
  • Step by Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

Shoulder length hair is hair that falls just above or just below the shoulders. This length is popular because it's versatile and easy to maintain. Cutting your hair short can be a big change, so it's important to think about what style you want before you start cutting.

How to

The first step in cutting short shoulder length hair is to decide what style you want. Do you want a sleek bob, a messy pixie cut, or something in between? Once you've decided on your style, it's time to gather your tools. You'll need a comb, hair cutting scissors, and hair clips to section off your hair.

Step by Step

1. Start by washing and drying your hair. This will make it easier to cut and style.

2. Section off your hair into four parts: the top, left side, right side, and back. Clip each section out of the way.

3. Start with the back section. Comb through the hair and cut it straight across. This will be the shortest layer.

4. Move to the side sections. Comb through the hair and cut it at an angle, starting from the shortest layer in the back and gradually getting longer towards the front.

5. Finally, move to the top section. Comb through the hair and cut it at an angle, starting from the shortest layer in the back and gradually getting longer towards the front.

6. Once you've finished cutting, style your hair as desired.

Tips

- Start with small cuts and work your way up. You can always cut more hair, but you can't put it back once it's cut.

- Use hair clips to section off your hair. This will make it easier to cut and style.

- Cut your hair when it's dry. Wet hair can be tricky to cut because it looks longer than it actually is.

FAQ

Q: How often should I cut my short shoulder length hair?

A: It depends on how fast your hair grows and how quickly it loses its shape. As a general rule, you should aim to cut your hair every 6-8 weeks.

Q: Can I cut my short shoulder length hair myself?

A: It's possible, but it's always best to go to a professional stylist. They have the skills and experience to give you the best cut possible.

Hair Care

Once you've cut your short shoulder length hair, it's important to take care of it properly. Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and moisturized. Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as they can damage your hair. And finally, visit your stylist regularly to keep your hair looking its best.

Cutting your hair short can be a big change, but it's a great way to refresh your look. Whether you're going for a sleek bob or a messy pixie cut, following these steps will help you achieve the perfect short shoulder length haircut.
