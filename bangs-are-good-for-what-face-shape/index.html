Bangs Are Good For What Face Shape A Complete Guide

Bangs are a great way to switch up your hairstyle and add some personality to your look. However, not all bangs are created equal. The style that looks great on one person may not be as flattering on another. That's why it's important to determine your face shape and choose bangs that complement your features.

How to Determine Your Face Shape

The first step in choosing the right bangs for your face shape is determining what your face shape is. Here are the common face shapes:

  • Oval
  • Round
  • Square
  • Heart
  • Diamond

To determine your face shape, stand in front of a mirror and pull your hair back. Look at the outline of your face and compare it to the descriptions above. You can also use a tape measure to measure the width of your forehead, cheekbones, and jawline.

Here's how to measure:

  • Forehead: Measure across the widest part of your forehead, from one temple to the other.
  • Cheekbones: Measure across your cheekbones, starting and ending at the pointiest part below the outer corner of each eye.
  • Jawline: Measure from the tip of your chin to the point below your ear where your jaw angles upward. Multiply that number by two to get your jawline measurement.

Add these measurements together and compare them to the descriptions below:

  • Oval: Your face length is greater than the width of your cheekbones, and your forehead is wider than your jawline.
  • Round: Your face length and cheekbone width are similar, and your forehead and jawline are also similar in width.
  • Square: Your forehead, cheekbones, and jawline are all about the same width.
  • Heart: Your forehead is wider than your cheekbones, and your chin is pointed.
  • Diamond: Your cheekbones are the widest part of your face, and your forehead and jawline are narrower.

Step-by-Step Guide on Choosing the Right Bangs for Your Face Shape

Once you've determined your face shape, you can choose bangs that flatter your features. Here's a step-by-step guide:

1. Oval Face Shape

If you have an oval face shape, you're in luck! Almost any style of bangs will look great on you. Consider blunt, side-swept, or wispy bangs.

2. Round Face Shape

If you have a round face shape, you want to create the illusion of length. Consider long, side-swept bangs that angle down towards the jawline. Avoid blunt or straight-across bangs, as they can make your face look even rounder.

3. Square Face Shape

If you have a square face shape, you want to soften the angles of your face. Consider long, side-swept bangs that are tapered at the ends. Avoid straight-across or blunt bangs, as they can make your face look more angular.

4. Heart Face Shape

If you have a heart face shape, you want to balance out your forehead and chin. Consider side-swept or wispy bangs that are longer on the sides. Avoid blunt or heavy bangs, as they can make your forehead look even larger.

5. Diamond Face Shape

If you have a diamond face shape, you want to create the illusion of width at the forehead and jawline. Consider side-swept or wispy bangs that angle down towards the jawline. Avoid blunt or straight-across bangs, as they can make your face look even more narrow.

Tips on Styling Your Bangs

Now that you've chosen the right bangs for your face shape, here are some tips on styling them:

  • Blow-dry your bangs using a round brush to create volume and shape.
  • Use a flat iron to straighten your bangs for a sleek look.
  • Add texture to your bangs by using a small amount of styling wax or pomade.
  • Trim your bangs regularly to keep them looking fresh.
  • Sweep your bangs to the side for a more casual, effortless look.

FAQs

Q: Can I still wear bangs if I have curly hair?

A: Yes! Curly hair can look great with bangs. Consider getting longer, side-swept bangs that blend in with the rest of your curls.

Q: Can I wear bangs if I have a high forehead?

A: Yes! Bangs can actually help balance out a high forehead. Consider side-swept or wispy bangs that are longer on the sides.

Hair Care for Your Bangs

Finally, here are some tips on caring for your bangs:

  • Wash your bangs between regular hair washes to keep them looking fresh.
  • Blot your bangs dry with a towel instead of rubbing them to avoid frizz.
  • Avoid touching your bangs throughout the day to prevent them from getting oily.
  • Use dry shampoo to refresh your bangs between washes.
  • Trim your bangs regularly to avoid split ends and keep them looking polished.

Remember, the most important thing when it comes to bangs is choosing a style that makes you feel confident and comfortable. Whether you have an oval, round, square, heart, or diamond face shape, there's a bang style out there that will complement your features and help you express your unique personality.
