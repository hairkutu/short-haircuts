Casual Outfits

Guys appreciate simplicity when it comes to casual outfits. A simple pair of jeans, a t-shirt, and a pair of sneakers can do the trick. A leather jacket can also add some edge to the look. However, anything too revealing or attention-grabbing can come off as trying too hard. So, keep it simple and effortless.

Date Night Outfits

Workout Outfits

For workout outfits, guys appreciate a girl who can look cute and put together while breaking a sweat. A pair of leggings and a sports bra can do the trick. However, anything too revealing can be a turn-off, so be sure to keep it tasteful.

Lingerie
