Best Haircuts For Guys With Thick Curly Hair A Comprehensive Guide

Written by William May 20, 2023 · 9 min read

Are you a guy with thick curly hair? Do you struggle to find the perfect haircut that suits your hair type? Look no further! In this article, we will guide you through the best haircuts for guys with thick curly hair. Whether you have tight coils or loose waves, we have got you covered. Let's dive in!

Table of Contents

  1. How to Choose the Right Haircut
  2. Step-by-Step Guide to Getting the Perfect Haircut
  3. Tips for Maintaining Your Thick Curly Hair
  4. FAQs
  5. Hair Care for Thick Curly Hair

How to Choose the Right Haircut

Choosing the right haircut for your thick curly hair can be a daunting task. Your hair type determines the type of haircut that will suit you best. Here are some haircuts that work well with thick curly hair:

1. Short and Textured Haircut

A short textured haircut is perfect for guys with thick curly hair. It gives a natural look and is easy to maintain. The textured cut adds dimension and movement to your hair, making it look more voluminous.

2. Long and Layered Haircut

A long layered haircut is perfect for guys with long thick curly hair. It adds dimension and texture to your hair, making it look more voluminous. The layers add movement and give your hair a natural flow.

3. Taper Fade Haircut

A taper fade haircut is perfect for guys who want a clean-cut look. It involves cutting the hair short on the sides and back, and gradually blending it into the longer hair on top. This haircut works well with all hair types, including thick curly hair.

4. Undercut Haircut

An undercut haircut involves shaving the hair on the sides and back of your head, leaving the hair on top longer. This haircut works well with thick curly hair, as it adds contrast and volume to your hair.

Step-by-Step Guide to Getting the Perfect Haircut

Now that you know which haircut suits your hair type, it's time to get the perfect haircut. Here's a step-by-step guide:

Step 1: Choose a Hairstyle

Choose a hairstyle that suits your face shape and hair type. You can take inspiration from magazines or online sources.

Step 2: Find a Good Barber

Find a good barber who has experience with cutting thick curly hair. Look for reviews or ask for recommendations from friends or family.

Step 3: Communicate Clearly

Communicate clearly with your barber about the type of haircut you want. Show them pictures or describe the haircut in detail.

Step 4: Maintain Your Haircut

Maintain your haircut by getting regular trims and using hair care products that suit your hair type. Follow a proper hair care routine to keep your hair healthy and shiny.

Tips for Maintaining Your Thick Curly Hair

Maintaining thick curly hair can be tricky. Here are some tips to help you:

1. Use a Wide-Tooth Comb

Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair. It prevents breakage and damage to your hair.

2. Use Hair Care Products Specifically for Thick Curly Hair

Use hair care products that are specifically designed for thick curly hair. These products contain ingredients that nourish and moisturize your hair, keeping it healthy and shiny.

3. Avoid Heat Styling

Avoid using heat styling tools like straighteners or curling irons. Heat can damage your hair, causing breakage and split ends.

4. Get Regular Trims

Get regular trims to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends. It also helps your hair grow faster and look more voluminous.

FAQs

Q. How often should I wash my thick curly hair?

A. It's best to wash your hair once or twice a week. Over-washing can strip your hair of its natural oils, making it dry and frizzy.

Q. Can I use hair gel on my thick curly hair?

A. Yes, you can use hair gel on your thick curly hair. Look for gels that are specifically designed for curly hair and contain moisturizing ingredients.

Hair Care for Thick Curly Hair

Proper hair care is essential for maintaining healthy and shiny hair. Here are some tips for hair care:

1. Use a Moisturizing Shampoo and Conditioner

Use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner that are specifically designed for thick curly hair. These products contain ingredients that nourish and hydrate your hair, keeping it healthy and shiny.

2. Use a Deep Conditioner Once a Week

Use a deep conditioner once a week to nourish and moisturize your hair. Apply the conditioner to your hair and leave it on for 15-20 minutes before rinsing it off.

3. Use a Leave-In Conditioner

Use a leave-in conditioner to keep your hair hydrated and prevent frizz. Apply the conditioner to your hair after showering and leave it in.

4. Avoid Brushing Your Hair When Dry

Avoid brushing your hair when it's dry. It can cause breakage and damage to your hair. Instead, use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair when wet.

Now that you know the best haircuts for guys with thick curly hair, it's time to rock your curls with confidence. Follow these tips and maintain a proper hair care routine to keep your hair healthy and shiny. Remember, your hair is your crown, wear it with pride!
