How To Fix Hair Curling Outwards Tips And Tricks

Written by Bardi Apr 24, 2023 Â· 8 min read

If you're struggling with hair that curls outwards, you're not alone. This is a common issue among many people, and it can be frustrating to deal with. However, there are a few simple things you can do to fix this problem and achieve the smooth, sleek look you desire.

Description

Outward curling hair is a result of improper hair care and styling. This can happen due to using too much heat, using the wrong hair products, or not taking proper precautions to protect your hair. Outward curls can make your hair look frizzy, unkempt, and difficult to manage. However, with the right techniques and products, you can get your hair back to looking its best.

How to Fix Hair Curling Outwards

Fixing hair that curls outwards requires a few simple steps. Here are some tips to help you get started:

1. Use the Right Hair Products

Using the right hair products is crucial when it comes to managing outward curls. Look for products that are designed to smooth and tame frizz, such as anti-frizz serums, leave-in conditioners, and smoothing gels. These products will help to tame your hair and keep it looking sleek and smooth.

2. Blow Dry Your Hair Correctly

When blow-drying your hair, it's important to use the right techniques. Use a round brush to help smooth your hair and aim the dryer nozzle downwards to help prevent frizz. Also, be sure to use a heat protectant spray to protect your hair from heat damage.

3. Avoid Using High Heat

Using high heat can cause your hair to curl outwards. Try to avoid using high heat settings on your hair dryer, straightener or curling iron. Instead, use lower heat settings and take your time when styling your hair.

4. Use a Flat Iron

A flat iron can be a great tool for taming outward curls. Use it to straighten your hair and smooth out any frizz or flyaways. However, be sure to use a heat protectant spray and avoid using high heat settings.

Step by Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide to help you fix your outward curling hair:

Step 1: Wash Your Hair

Start by washing your hair with a smoothing shampoo and conditioner. This will help to smooth your hair and reduce frizz.

Step 2: Apply a Smoothing Product

Apply a smoothing product, such as an anti-frizz serum, to your damp hair. This will help to tame your hair and keep it looking sleek and smooth.

Step 3: Blow Dry Your Hair

Blow dry your hair using a round brush to help smooth your hair. Aim the dryer nozzle downwards to help prevent frizz.

Step 4: Use a Flat Iron

Use a flat iron to straighten your hair and smooth out any frizz or flyaways. Be sure to use a heat protectant spray and avoid using high heat settings.

Step 5: Finish with Hairspray

Finish with a light hold hairspray to keep your hair looking smooth and sleek throughout the day.

Tips

Here are some additional tips to help you fix your outward curling hair:

  • Avoid using heavy hair products that can weigh your hair down and cause it to curl outwards.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair instead of a brush, which can cause frizz.
  • Avoid touching your hair too much, as this can cause frizz and flyaways.
  • Consider getting a keratin treatment to help smooth your hair and reduce frizz.

FAQ

Q: Can I fix my outward curling hair without using heat?

A: Yes, you can fix your outward curling hair without using heat. Try using a smoothing product and blow drying your hair using a round brush to help smooth your hair.

Q: How often should I wash my hair?

A: It's best to wash your hair every 2-3 days to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils.

Q: Can I use a straightening brush to fix my outward curling hair?

A: Yes, a straightening brush can be a great tool for fixing outward curling hair. However, be sure to use a heat protectant spray and avoid using high heat settings.

Hair Care

Proper hair care is essential for maintaining healthy, beautiful hair. Be sure to use the right hair products and techniques to keep your hair looking its best. Avoid using too much heat and take precautions to protect your hair from damage.

By following these tips and tricks, you can fix your outward curling hair and achieve the smooth, sleek look you desire. Remember to be patient and take your time when styling your hair, and you'll be sure to see great results!
