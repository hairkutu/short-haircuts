How To Cut A Man s Hair With Clippers Only

Written by Eveline Aug 28, 2023 · 7 min read

If you're looking to save some money and time by cutting your man's hair at home, using clippers is a great option. Not only is it easy to use, but it also gives you the versatility to create different hairstyles. In this article, we'll go through step-by-step instructions on how to cut a man's hair with clippers only.

Description

Before we dive into the process, let's first understand what clippers are. Hair clippers are electric devices that are used to cut hair. They come with different sizes of blades that can be adjusted to achieve different lengths of hair. Clippers are a great tool for cutting men's hair as they are easy to use and can be used on different hair types and textures.

How to

Now, let's get into the process of cutting a man's hair with clippers only:

Step 1: Prepare the Hair

Start by washing and drying the hair. This will make it easier to cut and ensure that the clippers don't get clogged with dirt and oil. Comb the hair to make sure it's tangle-free and ready for cutting.

Step 2: Choose the Right Clipper Blade Size

Clippers come with different blade sizes that can be adjusted to achieve different lengths of hair. Choose the right blade size depending on the length of hair you want to achieve. Start with a longer blade size and gradually move to a shorter one if needed.

Step 3: Start Cutting

Start cutting from the back of the head and move towards the front. Use the clippers to trim the hair, moving against the direction of hair growth. Use a comb to guide the clippers and ensure that you're cutting evenly. Repeat this process on the sides and top of the head.

Step 4: Blend the Hair

Once you've cut the hair to the desired length, use a blending comb to blend the hair. This will ensure that there are no harsh lines and the hair looks natural. Use the clippers to blend the hair, moving in a circular motion at the point where you want to blend.

Step 5: Trim the Edges

Use the clippers to trim the edges around the ears and neckline. This will give the haircut a clean and polished look.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when cutting a man's hair with clippers:

  • Start with a longer blade size and gradually move to a shorter one if needed.
  • Use a comb to guide the clippers and ensure that you're cutting evenly.
  • Blend the hair to ensure that there are no harsh lines.
  • Trim the edges for a clean and polished look.
  • Use a cape or towel to keep the hair off the clothes.

FAQ

Can clippers be used on all hair types?

Yes, clippers can be used on all hair types and textures. However, you'll need to adjust the blade size depending on the hair type and texture.

What should I do if I make a mistake while cutting?

If you make a mistake while cutting, don't panic. You can always use a longer blade size to even out the hair or blend it in. If the mistake is significant, you may need to cut the hair shorter.

How often should I cut my hair with clippers?

This depends on the length of hair you want to achieve and how fast your hair grows. On average, men's hair grows about 0.5 inches per month. If you want to maintain a certain length, you'll need to cut your hair every 4-6 weeks.

Hair Care

After cutting the hair, make sure to clean the clippers thoroughly. This will ensure that they last longer and maintain their sharpness. Additionally, use a shampoo and conditioner that is suitable for your hair type to keep it healthy and looking great.

Cutting a man's hair with clippers only is easy and convenient. With the right tools and technique, you can achieve a great-looking haircut in the comfort of your own home.
