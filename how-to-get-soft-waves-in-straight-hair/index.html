How To Get Soft Waves In Straight Hair A Step By Step Guide

Written by Christine May 09, 2023 Â· 8 min read

Are you tired of having straight and lifeless hair? Do you want to add some texture and volume to your locks? Soft waves are the perfect solution! Not only do they add dimension to your hair, but they also look effortlessly chic. In this article, we will show you how to get soft waves in straight hair. Follow these simple steps to achieve a beachy and carefree look.

How to Get Soft Waves in Straight Hair

Soft waves are easy to achieve, even if you have straight hair. Here are the steps:

1. Start with Clean Hair

Wash your hair with a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to add body and texture. Make sure to rinse your hair thoroughly to avoid any residue.

2. Apply a Heat Protectant

Before using any heat styling tools, apply a heat protectant to prevent damage to your hair. Use a light spray or cream, and distribute it evenly throughout your hair.

3. Use a Curling Iron

Take small sections of your hair and wrap them around a curling iron. Hold the iron in place for a few seconds, then release the hair. Make sure to curl away from your face to create a natural-looking wave. Repeat this process until all of your hair is curled.

4. Finger-Comb Your Hair

After curling your hair, use your fingers to gently comb through the curls. This will create soft waves instead of tight curls. Don't worry if your hair looks a little messy at first - that's the look you're going for!

5. Finish with Hairspray

To keep your soft waves in place, finish with a light mist of hairspray. Hold the can about 12 inches away from your hair and spray evenly.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here is a step-by-step guide to help you get soft waves in straight hair:

Step 1: Wash Your Hair

Start with clean hair. Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to add body and texture.

Step 2: Apply a Heat Protectant

Before using any heat styling tools, apply a heat protectant to prevent damage to your hair. Use a light spray or cream, and distribute it evenly throughout your hair.

Step 3: Section Your Hair

Section your hair into four parts - two on top and two on the bottom. Use a hair clip to keep each section in place.

Step 4: Curl Your Hair

Take a small section of hair and wrap it around a curling iron. Hold the iron in place for a few seconds, then release the hair. Repeat this process until all of your hair is curled.

Step 5: Finger-Comb Your Hair

After curling your hair, use your fingers to gently comb through the curls. This will create soft waves instead of tight curls.

Step 6: Apply Hairspray

To keep your soft waves in place, finish with a light mist of hairspray.

Tips for Perfect Soft Waves

Here are some tips to help you achieve perfect soft waves:

  • Use a curling iron with a 1-inch barrel for natural-looking waves.
  • Don't curl your hair all the way to the ends - leave the last inch or two straight for a more relaxed look.
  • If your hair is thick, use smaller sections. If your hair is thin, use larger sections.
  • For a more beachy look, use a salt spray before curling your hair.
  • Don't brush your hair after curling - use your fingers to finger-comb through the curls.

FAQs

Q: Can I use a straightener instead of a curling iron?

A: Yes, you can use a straightener to create soft waves. Take a small section of hair and twist it away from your face. Hold the straightener at a 45-degree angle and clamp down on the twisted section. Slide the straightener down to the end of the hair, then release. Finger-comb the curl and repeat until all of your hair is curled.

Q: How do I get soft waves without heat?

A: You can create soft waves without heat by braiding your hair. Divide your hair into two sections and braid each section. Leave the braids in for a few hours or overnight, then take them out and finger-comb your hair.

Hair Care Tips for Soft Waves

Here are some hair care tips to keep your soft waves looking their best:

  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair hydrated and healthy.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as it can cause damage and breakage.
  • Limit the use of hair products, as they can weigh down your hair and make it look greasy.
  • Trim your hair regularly to get rid of split ends and keep your hair looking healthy.
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it's wet, as it can cause damage.

Now that you know how to get soft waves in straight hair, it's time to try it out for yourself! With these simple steps and tips, you can achieve a carefree and effortless look that's perfect for any occasion.
