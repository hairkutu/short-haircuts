How To Grow Slicked Back Hair A Step By Step Guide

Written by Eveline May 17, 2023 · 8 min read

Are you a fan of the classic slicked back hair look but struggling to grow your hair long enough to achieve it? Fear not! In this guide, we will provide you with tips and tricks on how to grow slicked back hair that will turn heads.

Description

Slicked back hair has been a popular hairstyle for decades, and it’s still in demand today. It’s a timeless look that exudes confidence, sophistication, and style. However, growing slicked back hair can be a challenge, especially if you’re not sure how to go about it. In this guide, we’ll walk you through the process of growing slicked back hair, from how to care for your hair to the best products to use.

How to Grow Slicked Back Hair

Step 1: Start with a Good Haircut

The first step to growing slicked back hair is to start with a good haircut. You want to trim your hair regularly to get rid of split ends and keep your hair healthy. You should also ask your hairstylist to cut your hair in a way that promotes growth. For slicked back hair, you’ll want to make sure that the back and sides of your hair are shorter than the top.

Step 2: Use the Right Products

Using the right products is essential when growing slicked back hair. You’ll want to use a shampoo and conditioner that are designed for your hair type. If you have thin hair, look for products that add volume. If you have thick hair, look for products that tame frizz. You should also use a styling product, such as a pomade or gel, to keep your hair in place.

Step 3: Be Patient

Growing slicked back hair takes time and patience. You won’t achieve the look overnight, so be prepared to wait a few months. In the meantime, you can experiment with different hairstyles that work well with shorter hair.

Step-by-Step Guide

Step 1: Wash Your Hair

The first step to growing slicked back hair is to wash your hair. Use a shampoo and conditioner that are designed for your hair type. Rinse your hair thoroughly and towel dry it gently.

Step 2: Apply Styling Product

Apply a small amount of styling product to your hair. Work it through your hair evenly, making sure to cover all areas. You can use a pomade or gel, depending on your hair type and the look you’re going for.

Step 3: Comb Your Hair Back

Use a comb to slick your hair back. Start at the front of your hairline and comb backward. You may need to use a bit of force to get your hair to stay in place. Make sure to comb your hair evenly.

Step 4: Allow Your Hair to Dry

Allow your hair to dry naturally. Avoid using a blow dryer, as it can damage your hair and ruin your slicked back look.

Tips for Growing Slicked Back Hair

Here are a few tips to help you grow slicked back hair:

  • Get regular haircuts to promote growth
  • Use products that are designed for your hair type
  • Be patient and don’t expect results overnight
  • Experiment with different hairstyles as your hair grows
  • Avoid using heat styling tools, such as blow dryers and straighteners

FAQ

How long does it take to grow slicked back hair?

It can take anywhere from a few months to a year to grow slicked back hair, depending on how fast your hair grows and how long you want it to be.

What products should I use to grow slicked back hair?

You should use a shampoo, conditioner, and styling product that are designed for your hair type. Look for products that promote growth and tame frizz.

Can I grow slicked back hair if I have thin hair?

Yes, you can grow slicked back hair if you have thin hair. Use products that add volume and avoid using heavy styling products that can weigh your hair down.

Hair Care for Slicked Back Hair

Here are a few tips to care for your hair when growing slicked back hair:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a shampoo and conditioner that are designed for your hair type
  • Avoid using heat styling tools, such as blow dryers and straighteners, as they can damage your hair
  • Use a hair mask once a week to keep your hair healthy and moisturized
  • Get regular haircuts to promote growth and keep your hair healthy

By following these tips and tricks, you’ll be able to grow slicked back hair that looks stylish and sophisticated. Remember to be patient and take good care of your hair, and you’ll be sure to turn heads with your new look.
