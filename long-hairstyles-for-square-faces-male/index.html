Long Hairstyles For Square Faces Male A Comprehensive Guide

Written by William Jun 13, 2023 Â· 8 min read

If you have a square face, you know that finding the right hairstyle can be a challenge. You want to find a style that complements your features and doesn't make your face appear too angular. One great option for square-faced men is a long hairstyle. In this article, we'll cover everything you need to know about long hairstyles for square faces male, including how to choose the right style, how to maintain it, and more.

Table of Contents

  1. How to Choose the Right Style
  2. How to Style Long Hair for Square Faces Male
  3. Step-by-Step Guide to Styling Long Hair for Square Faces Male
  4. Tips for Maintaining Long Hair for Square Faces Male
  5. FAQ
  6. Hair Care Tips for Square Faces Male

How to Choose the Right Style

When it comes to choosing the right long hairstyle for square faces male, there are a few key factors to consider. First, you want to choose a style that complements your face shape. This means avoiding styles that make your face appear too angular or boxy. Instead, opt for styles that add softness and movement to your face.

Another important factor to consider is your hair type. If you have thick, straight hair, you may want to opt for a longer style with layers to add movement and texture. If you have curly or wavy hair, a longer style with a side part can help balance out the angular lines of your face.

Some great long hairstyles for square faces male include:

  • Long and layered
  • Side-swept bangs
  • Messy waves
  • Low ponytail
  • Man bun

How to Style Long Hair for Square Faces Male

Styling long hair for square faces male doesn't have to be complicated. In fact, many of these styles are low-maintenance and easy to achieve. You can opt for a messy, tousled look or a sleek, polished style depending on the occasion.

One great way to style long hair for square faces male is to add texture and movement. You can achieve this by using a texturizing spray or styling cream on damp hair, then blow-drying with a diffuser attachment. This will help enhance your natural waves or curls and add volume and movement to your hair.

If you prefer a sleeker look, you can use a flat iron to straighten your hair and add shine. Just be sure to use a heat protectant spray to prevent damage to your hair.

Step-by-Step Guide to Styling Long Hair for Square Faces Male

Here's a step-by-step guide to styling long hair for square faces male:

  1. Start with clean, damp hair
  2. Apply a texturizing spray or styling cream
  3. Blow-dry hair with a diffuser attachment
  4. Use a flat iron to straighten hair (optional)
  5. Finish with a light-hold hairspray

Tips for Maintaining Long Hair for Square Faces Male

Maintaining long hair for square faces male requires a bit of effort, but it's worth it to achieve the perfect style. Here are some tips for keeping your long hair looking its best:

  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep hair healthy
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair
  • Invest in a good quality shampoo and conditioner
  • Consider using a leave-in conditioner or hair oil to keep hair moisturized

FAQ

What are the best long hairstyles for square faces male?

Some great long hairstyles for square faces male include long and layered, side-swept bangs, messy waves, low ponytail, and man bun.

How do I style long hair for square faces male?

You can style long hair for square faces male by adding texture and movement with a texturizing spray or styling cream, or by using a flat iron to straighten hair for a sleeker look.

How can I maintain long hair for square faces male?

You can maintain long hair for square faces male by getting regular trims, using a deep conditioning treatment once a week, avoiding too much heat, using a good quality shampoo and conditioner, and using a leave-in conditioner or hair oil to keep hair moisturized.

Hair Care Tips for Square Faces Male

When it comes to hair care for square faces male, there are a few key tips to keep in mind. First, be sure to choose a hairstyle that complements your face shape. Next, consider your hair type and choose a style that works best for your texture. Finally, be sure to maintain your hair with regular trims and a good hair care routine to keep it looking its best.

With these tips and some inspiration from the styles we've listed above, you're sure to find the perfect long hairstyle for square faces male that complements your features and makes you feel confident.
