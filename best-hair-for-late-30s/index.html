Best Hair For Late 30S A Guide To Looking Your Best

Written by Petter Jul 21, 2023 Â· 10 min read

As we age, our hair goes through changes just like the rest of our body. For women in their late 30s, finding the right hairstyle can be a challenge. You want something that's age-appropriate, but also stylish and easy to maintain. In this article, we'll explore the best hair for late 30s and give you tips on how to keep your hair looking its best.

Description: What is the Best Hair for Late 30s?

The best hair for late 30s is a style that is easy to maintain, looks youthful and fresh, and is appropriate for your lifestyle. As we age, our hair can become thinner, so you want to choose a style that adds volume and texture. Layers and bangs can help with this. You also want to choose a style that complements your face shape and skin tone.

How to Choose the Best Hair for Late 30s

Choosing the best hair for late 30s is a personal decision, but there are some things to consider when making your choice. Here are some tips:

1. Consider Your Face Shape

Your face shape can play a big role in what hairstyle will look best on you. If you have a round face, you may want to choose a hairstyle with layers that start at the chin. If you have a square face, a soft wave or loose curls can soften your features. If you have an oval face, you can pretty much pull off any hairstyle!

2. Think About Your Lifestyle

Your lifestyle can also play a role in what hairstyle you choose. If you're a busy mom, you may want something that's easy to maintain and doesn't require a lot of styling. If you work in a professional setting, you may want something more polished and sophisticated.

3. Consider Your Hair Texture

Your hair texture can also play a role in what hairstyle will look best on you. If you have fine hair, you may want to choose a style with layers to add volume. If you have thick hair, you may want to choose a style that thins out some of the bulk.

Step by Step: How to Get the Best Hair for Late 30s

Getting the best hair for late 30s is all about choosing the right style and taking care of your hair. Here are some steps you can take:

1. Choose the Right Style

Choose a style that complements your face shape, hair texture, and lifestyle. Bring pictures to your stylist to show them what you're looking for.

2. Take Care of Your Hair

Take care of your hair by using a good shampoo and conditioner, getting regular trims, and using heat protectant when styling with heat tools.

3. Experiment with Color

Experiment with color to add dimension and depth to your hair. Highlights and lowlights can add a youthful glow to your hair.

Tips for Maintaining Your Best Hair for Late 30s

Here are some tips for maintaining your best hair for late 30s:

1. Use Heat Protectant

Always use heat protectant when styling with heat tools. This will help prevent damage and keep your hair looking healthy.

2. Get Regular Trims

Get regular trims to keep your hair looking its best. This will help prevent split ends and breakage.

3. Use a Good Shampoo and Conditioner

Use a good shampoo and conditioner that is appropriate for your hair type. This will help keep your hair healthy and shiny.

4. Protect Your Hair from the Sun

Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a UV protectant spray. The sun can damage your hair, just like it can damage your skin.

FAQs About Best Hair for Late 30s

Q: What is the best haircut for a woman in her late 30s?

A: The best haircut for a woman in her late 30s is one that complements her face shape, hair texture, and lifestyle. Layers and bangs can add volume and texture to thinning hair.

Q: How can I make my hair look younger in my late 30s?

A: Experiment with color, add layers and bangs, and use products that add volume and texture to your hair.

Q: Can I still wear long hair in my late 30s?

A: Yes! You can still wear long hair in your late 30s. Just make sure it's healthy and well-maintained.

Hair Care: Taking Care of Your Best Hair for Late 30s

Taking care of your hair is important no matter what your age. Here are some hair care tips for women in their late 30s:

1. Use a Good Shampoo and Conditioner

Use a good shampoo and conditioner that is appropriate for your hair type. This will help keep your hair healthy and shiny.

2. Get Regular Trims

Get regular trims to keep your hair looking its best. This will help prevent split ends and breakage.

3. Use Heat Protectant

Always use heat protectant when styling with heat tools. This will help prevent damage and keep your hair looking healthy.

4. Protect Your Hair from the Sun

Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a UV protectant spray. The sun can damage your hair, just like it can damage your skin.

5. Eat a Healthy Diet

Eat a healthy diet that includes plenty of protein, vitamins, and minerals. This will help keep your hair healthy and strong.

6. Stay Hydrated

Drink plenty of water to keep your hair and scalp hydrated. This will help prevent dryness and breakage.

7. Use Products that Add Volume and Texture

Use products that add volume and texture to your hair, such as volumizing mousse or texturizing spray.

By following these tips, you can have the best hair for late 30s that looks youthful, fresh, and healthy. Remember to choose a style that complements your face shape and hair texture, and take care of your hair with regular trims and good hair care habits.
