How To Make Curly Hair Poofy A Step By Step Guide

Written by Christine Apr 25, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Having curly hair can be a blessing and a curse. While it's beautiful and unique, it can also be challenging to style. If you're looking to add some volume and bounce to your curls, you're in luck! In this article, we'll show you how to make curly hair poofy using simple techniques that anyone can do.

Table of Contents

  • How to Make Curly Hair Poofy
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Making Curly Hair Poofy

How to Make Curly Hair Poofy

Before we dive into the step-by-step guide, let's talk about some of the things that can help make your curly hair poofy. First, you'll need to use the right products. Look for products that are specifically designed for curly hair, such as mousses, foams, and serums. These products can help add volume and bounce to your curls.

Another thing to keep in mind is the way you style your hair. If you want to make your curly hair poofy, you'll need to avoid tight hairstyles that can flatten your curls. Instead, opt for loose hairstyles that allow your curls to breathe and bounce.

Step-by-Step Guide

Now that you know some of the basics, let's dive into the step-by-step guide for making curly hair poofy:

1. Start with clean, damp hair

Wash your hair with a curly hair shampoo and conditioner. Towel dry your hair to remove excess water, but don't rub your hair vigorously. This can cause frizz and damage your hair.

2. Apply a volumizing product

Apply a volumizing product, such as a mousse or foam, to your hair. Start at the roots and work your way down to the ends. Be sure to distribute the product evenly throughout your hair.

3. Use a diffuser

Attach a diffuser to your hair dryer and set it to low heat. Use the diffuser to dry your hair, scrunching your curls as you go. This will help add volume and bounce to your curls.

4. Flip your hair

Once your hair is dry, flip your head upside down and shake your curls out. This will help add volume to your roots and make your hair look poofy.

5. Finish with hairspray

Finish your look with a light hairspray to help hold your curls in place. Be sure to use a hairspray that won't weigh your hair down or make it look greasy.

Tips for Making Curly Hair Poofy

Here are some additional tips for making curly hair poofy:

  • Experiment with different products to find what works best for your hair.
  • Don't be afraid to use a little bit of product at a time. Too much product can weigh your hair down and make it look greasy.
  • Try using a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair instead of a brush. This can help prevent breakage and make your curls look more defined.
  • Avoid touching your hair too much. This can cause frizz and make your curls look flat.

Frequently Asked Questions

Here are some frequently asked questions about making curly hair poofy:

Can I make my curly hair poofy without heat?

Yes! You can use the same techniques listed above without heat. Instead of using a diffuser, you can air dry your hair or use a hair dryer on the cool setting.

What if my hair is naturally thin and flat?

If your hair is naturally thin and flat, you can still make it look poofy by using volumizing products and styling techniques. Just be sure to use products that are designed for your hair type.

Hair Care for Curly Hair

Here are some additional tips for taking care of your curly hair:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo to help prevent dryness and breakage.
  • Deep condition your hair once a week to help keep it moisturized and healthy.
  • Avoid using heat on your hair too often. This can cause damage and breakage.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends and breakage.

With these tips and techniques, you can make your curly hair poofy and beautiful. Just remember to use the right products, avoid tight hairstyles, and have fun experimenting with different styles!
