How To Find A Wedding Hair Stylist

Written by Eveline Apr 17, 2023 Â· 9 min read

Planning a wedding is an exciting time, but it can also be stressful. One of the most important things on your to-do list is finding the perfect hair stylist for your big day. Your hair is one of the most important parts of your overall look, so it's important to find a stylist who can create the perfect style for you. In this article, we'll go over how to find a wedding hair stylist, step by step, with tips and FAQ to help you along the way.

Table of Contents

  • How to Find a Wedding Hair Stylist
  • Step by Step Guide
  • Tips for Choosing a Wedding Hair Stylist
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care Tips for Your Wedding Day

How to Find a Wedding Hair Stylist

There are several ways to find a wedding hair stylist. You can ask for recommendations from friends or family members who have recently gotten married. You can also search online for wedding hair stylists in your area. Here are some steps to follow:

Step 1: Research and Gather Information

Start by doing some research online. Look for wedding hair stylists in your area and read reviews from previous clients. Check out their portfolio of work to get an idea of their style and skills. Take note of their pricing and availability.

Step 2: Schedule a Consultation

Once you have a list of potential stylists, schedule a consultation with each one. This will give you the opportunity to discuss your vision for your wedding day hair and get a sense of their personality and communication style. It's important to choose a stylist who you feel comfortable with and who understands your needs.

Step 3: Ask for Recommendations

During your consultation, ask the stylist for recommendations on the best styles for your hair type and face shape. They may also have suggestions for accessories or products that will enhance your overall look.

Step 4: Check Availability

Make sure to check the stylist's availability on your wedding day. They may book up quickly, especially during peak wedding season, so it's important to secure your appointment as soon as possible.

Step by Step Guide

Here's a step by step guide to finding the perfect wedding hair stylist:

  1. Research and gather information on potential stylists
  2. Schedule a consultation with each stylist
  3. Discuss your vision for your wedding day hair
  4. Ask for recommendations on styles and accessories
  5. Check availability for your wedding day
  6. Review pricing and payment options
  7. Book your appointment
  8. Confirm details with your stylist a few weeks before the wedding
  9. Have a trial run with your stylist to ensure you're happy with the final look
  10. Sit back, relax, and let your stylist work their magic on your wedding day!

Tips for Choosing a Wedding Hair Stylist

Here are some tips to keep in mind when choosing a wedding hair stylist:

  • Start your search early to ensure you have plenty of time to find the perfect stylist
  • Look for a stylist with experience in wedding hair and a portfolio of work to showcase their skills
  • Choose a stylist who listens to your needs and understands your vision for your wedding day hair
  • Take note of their communication style and make sure you feel comfortable working with them
  • Check their availability on your wedding day and book your appointment as soon as possible
  • Ask for recommendations on styles and accessories that will enhance your overall look
  • Review pricing and payment options to ensure they fit within your budget

FAQs

How far in advance should I book my wedding hair stylist?

You should start your search for a wedding hair stylist as soon as possible, ideally six to twelve months before your wedding day. This will give you plenty of time to research, schedule consultations, and secure your appointment.

How much should I expect to pay for wedding day hair styling?

Wedding day hair styling prices can vary depending on the stylist's experience and location, as well as the complexity of the style. On average, you can expect to pay between $100 and $300 for wedding day hair styling.

Should I have a trial run with my wedding hair stylist?

Yes, it's a good idea to have a trial run with your wedding hair stylist to ensure you're happy with the final look. This will give you the opportunity to make any necessary adjustments and ensure a stress-free wedding day.

Hair Care Tips for Your Wedding Day

Here are some tips to help you take care of your hair on your wedding day:

  • Wash your hair the day before your wedding, as freshly washed hair can be more difficult to style
  • Avoid using heavy styling products that can weigh your hair down or make it look greasy
  • Consider using a heat protectant product if your stylist will be using hot tools on your hair
  • Bring touch-up products with you, such as hairspray and bobby pins, in case of any mishaps
  • Relax and enjoy the experience of getting pampered on your special day!

By following these steps and tips, you'll be able to find the perfect wedding hair stylist for your big day. Remember to start your search early, schedule consultations, and choose a stylist who understands your needs and vision. With the right stylist by your side, you'll look and feel your best on your wedding day!
