What Blonde Suits Me Best A Guide To Finding The Perfect Shade

Written by Eveline Apr 18, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Are you considering going blonde but not sure which shade would suit you best? With so many options available, finding the right shade of blonde can be overwhelming. In this article, we'll guide you through the process of finding the perfect blonde shade for your skin tone, eye color, and personal style.

Description

Blonde hair is a classic look that never goes out of style. From bright platinum to honey blonde, there are countless shades of blonde to choose from. But not all shades will suit everyone. Finding the right shade of blonde is important to ensure you look confident and beautiful.

How to

So, how do you find the perfect blonde shade for you? Here are some tips to help you get started:

Step 1: Determine Your Skin Tone

The first step in finding the perfect blonde shade is to determine your skin tone. There are three main skin tones: cool, warm, and neutral.

Cool skin tones have pink, red, or blue undertones. If you have cool skin tone, you should consider shades like platinum, ash, or beige blonde.

Warm skin tones have yellow or golden undertones. If you have warm skin tone, you should consider shades like honey, caramel, or golden blonde.

Neutral skin tones have a mix of cool and warm undertones. If you have a neutral skin tone, you can pull off almost any shade of blonde.

Step 2: Consider Your Eye Color

Another factor to consider when choosing a blonde shade is your eye color. Certain shades of blonde can complement your eye color and make them stand out.

If you have blue eyes, consider shades like platinum, ash, or silver blonde. If you have green eyes, consider shades like honey or caramel blonde. If you have brown eyes, consider shades like golden or champagne blonde.

Step 3: Think About Your Personal Style

Your personal style should also be taken into consideration when choosing a blonde shade. If you have a bold and edgy style, you may want to consider a platinum or ash blonde. If you have a classic and elegant style, you may want to consider a honey or caramel blonde.

Tips

Here are some additional tips to help you find the perfect blonde shade:

  • Consider your natural hair color. If you have dark hair, you may need to bleach your hair before going blonde.
  • Consult with a professional hair stylist. They can help you determine the best shade of blonde for you and ensure that the color is applied correctly.
  • Use hair care products specifically designed for blonde hair to keep your color looking fresh and vibrant.
  • Consider getting regular touch-ups to maintain your blonde color.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about finding the perfect blonde shade:

What is the most popular shade of blonde?

The most popular shade of blonde is currently the "bronde" trend, which is a mix of blonde and brown.

Can anyone go blonde?

Technically, anyone can go blonde. However, some people may need to bleach their hair before going blonde, which can cause damage.

How do I know if blonde will suit me?

The best way to know if blonde will suit you is to consult with a professional hair stylist. They can help you determine the best shade of blonde for your skin tone, eye color, and personal style.

Hair Care

If you decide to go blonde, it's important to take care of your hair to keep it looking healthy and vibrant. Here are some hair care tips for blonde hair:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to prevent your color from fading.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as they can cause damage to your hair.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair hydrated.
  • Avoid chlorine, as it can cause your color to turn green.

By following these tips and finding the perfect blonde shade for you, you can rock a beautiful and confident blonde look!
