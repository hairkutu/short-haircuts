Easy Updo Hairstyles For Long Hair For Work

Written by Aprilia Aug 10, 2023 Â· 6 min read

If you have long hair, you know how difficult it can be to find a hairstyle that is both professional and stylish. Whether you work in a corporate office or a more casual environment, an updo can be a great way to keep your hair out of your face and look put-together at the same time. In this article, we will explore some easy updo hairstyles for long hair that are perfect for work.

Table of Content

  1. How to
  2. Step by Step
  3. Tips
  4. FAQ
  5. Hair Care

How to

Before we dive into the hairstyles themselves, it's important to understand some basic techniques for creating updos. Here are a few things to keep in mind:

  • Start with clean, dry hair. Updos work best on hair that isn't oily or greasy.
  • Use products like hairspray, mousse, or gel to help hold your hair in place.
  • Invest in some good hair accessories like bobby pins, hair clips, and hair ties.

Step by Step

Braided Updo

This elegant updo is perfect for a more formal work environment. Here's how to create it:

  1. Start by parting your hair down the middle and braiding each side into a French braid.
  2. Secure the ends of the braids with hair ties.
  3. Wrap the braids around each other at the back of your head and secure with bobby pins.
  4. Spray with hairspray to keep everything in place.

Messy Bun

This casual updo is great for a more relaxed work environment. Here's how to create it:

  1. Start by pulling your hair back into a low ponytail.
  2. Twist the ponytail around itself to create a bun and secure with a hair tie.
  3. Use bobby pins to pin any loose pieces in place.
  4. Pull a few pieces out around your face for a more relaxed look.

Tips

  • Experiment with different updo styles to find what works best for your hair type and face shape.
  • Use a dry shampoo to help add texture and volume to your hair before creating an updo.
  • Don't be afraid to ask a friend or coworker for help if you're having trouble creating an updo on your own.

FAQ

What if I have really thick hair?

Thick hair can be difficult to work with, but there are still plenty of updo options available. Try a braided updo or a messy bun and use plenty of hair accessories to keep everything in place.


What if I have really fine hair?

Fine hair can be more challenging to work with, but a sleek bun or a braided crown can be a great option. Use products like mousse or gel to help add volume and hold to your hair.


Hair Care

Remember, it's important to take care of your hair in order to keep it looking its best. Here are a few tips for maintaining healthy hair:

  • Wash your hair regularly, but don't overdo it. Too much shampooing can strip your hair of its natural oils.
  • Use a deep conditioner once a week to help nourish your hair.
  • Avoid using heat tools like flat irons and curling irons too often, as they can damage your hair.

With these easy updo hairstyles for long hair for work, you'll be able to look professional and put-together no matter what your work environment is like. Remember to experiment with different styles and have fun with your hair!
