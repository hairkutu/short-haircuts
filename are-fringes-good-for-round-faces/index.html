Are Fringes Good For Round Faces

Written by Luffy Jun 05, 2023 · 6 min read

If you have a round face, you may have wondered if fringes are a good idea or not. Fringes, also known as bangs, are a popular hairstyle choice for many women. However, not all fringes are created equal, and some may not be the best choice for those with a round face. In this article, we will explore whether fringes are good for round faces and what types of fringes work best for this face shape.

Description

Round faces are characterized by a circular shape with full cheeks and a rounded chin. The goal of any hairstyle for a round face is to create the illusion of length and angles. Fringes can be a great way to achieve this by drawing attention to the eyes and cheekbones, but not all fringes will work for everyone.

How to

If you're considering getting a fringe and you have a round face, there are a few things to keep in mind. First, consider the length of the fringe. Shorter fringes that fall above the eyebrows can make your face look rounder, while longer fringes that fall below the eyebrows can create the illusion of length.

Another factor to consider is the width of the fringe. A thick, heavy fringe can make your face look wider, while a wispy, layered fringe can create softness and movement.

Step by Step

Here are some steps to take when deciding if fringes are good for your round face:

  • Determine your face shape by pulling your hair back and looking at your face in the mirror.
  • Consider the length and width of the fringe you want.
  • Look at pictures of celebrities with round faces and fringes to see what styles work best.
  • Talk to your hairstylist about what type of fringe will work best for you.
  • Consider your lifestyle and how much time you want to spend styling your fringe each day.

Tips

Here are some tips for getting the perfect fringe for your round face:

  • Opt for a side-swept fringe that falls diagonally across the forehead.
  • Consider a layered fringe that adds movement and softness to your face.
  • Avoid blunt, straight-across fringes that can make your face look wider.
  • Use styling products like mousse or hairspray to add volume and texture to your fringe.
  • Consider getting highlights or lowlights to add dimension to your fringe.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about fringes and round faces:

Q: What type of fringe should I avoid if I have a round face?

A: Avoid heavy, blunt fringes that fall straight across the forehead. These can make your face look wider and more circular.

Q: Can I still wear a fringe if I have a round face?

A: Absolutely! Just make sure to choose a fringe that is the right length and width for your face shape.

Q: How do I style my fringe once I get it?

A: You can style your fringe with a round brush and a blow dryer, or use a straightener to create a sleek, straight look.

Hair Care

To keep your fringe looking its best, make sure to get regular trims to prevent it from getting too long or heavy. You can also use dry shampoo to absorb oil and keep your fringe looking fresh between washes.

In conclusion, fringes can be a great choice for those with round faces, as long as you choose the right type of fringe for your face shape. By considering the length and width of the fringe, as well as your lifestyle and styling preferences, you can find the perfect fringe to flatter your features and enhance your natural beauty.
