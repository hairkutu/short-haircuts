What Are The Most Popular Hairstyles For 2020

Written by Bardi Apr 20, 2023 Â· 8 min read

2020 has been a year of change and adaptation. With the pandemic affecting every aspect of our lives, hairstyles have become more practical and low-maintenance. However, that doesn't mean you have to sacrifice style for convenience. In this article, we'll explore the most popular hairstyles for 2020 that are both stylish and easy to maintain.

The Bob

The bob has been a popular hairstyle for decades, and it's not going anywhere in 2020. This classic hairstyle is versatile and can be customized to suit any face shape or hair texture. The most popular bob styles for 2020 are the blunt bob and the textured bob.

The blunt bob is a straight cut that falls just above the shoulders. It's a low-maintenance style that looks great on thick, straight hair. The textured bob, on the other hand, is a choppy cut that's perfect for those with fine hair. It's a great way to add volume and texture to your hair.

The Shag

The shag is a layered haircut that's perfect for those who want a low-maintenance style with lots of volume and texture. It's a great option for those with thick, curly hair, as it helps to frame the face and create a more defined look.

The shag is also a great option for those who want to add some edge to their look. It's a versatile style that can be customized to suit any face shape or hair texture.

The Pixie Cut

The pixie cut is a short, low-maintenance style that's perfect for those who want a bold, edgy look. It's a great option for those with fine hair, as it helps to create volume and texture.

The pixie cut is also a great option for those who want to highlight their facial features. It's a versatile style that can be customized to suit any face shape or hair texture.

Braids

Braids have been a popular hairstyle for centuries, and they're not going anywhere in 2020. Braids are a great option for those who want a low-maintenance style that looks great and keeps hair out of the face.

The most popular braid styles for 2020 are the Dutch braid, the French braid, and the fishtail braid. These styles are easy to do at home and can be customized to suit any face shape or hair texture.

The Top Knot

The top knot is a classic hairstyle that's perfect for those who want a low-maintenance style that looks great and keeps hair out of the face. It's a great option for those with long hair, as it helps to create a more defined look.

The top knot is also a great option for those who want to add some edge to their look. It's a versatile style that can be customized to suit any face shape or hair texture.

Natural Curls

Natural curls have always been in style, and they're not going anywhere in 2020. Natural curls can be styled in a variety of ways, from loose waves to tight coils.

The key to styling natural curls is to keep them moisturized and healthy. Use a leave-in conditioner and a curl-defining product to keep your curls looking their best.

Hair Accessories

Hair accessories are a great way to add some style and flair to any hairstyle. In 2020, hair accessories are all about making a statement.

The most popular hair accessories for 2020 are hair scarves, hair clips, and headbands. These accessories are easy to use and can be customized to suit any hairstyle.

FAQs

What is the most popular hairstyle for 2020?

The most popular hairstyles for 2020 are the bob, the shag, the pixie cut, braids, the top knot, natural curls, and hair accessories.

What is the easiest hairstyle to maintain?

The easiest hairstyle to maintain is the pixie cut. It's a short, low-maintenance style that requires little to no styling.

Hair Care Tips

To keep your hair looking its best in 2020, it's important to follow a good hair care routine. Here are some tips to help you keep your hair healthy and beautiful:

  • Wash your hair every other day to avoid stripping it of its natural oils.
  • Use a leave-in conditioner to keep your hair moisturized and healthy.
  • Avoid using hot tools like flat irons and curling irons too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair looking healthy and prevent split ends.

With these tips and the most popular hairstyles for 2020, you're sure to find a style that works for you. Whether you opt for a classic bob or a bold pixie cut, make sure to keep your hair healthy and well-maintained for the best results.
