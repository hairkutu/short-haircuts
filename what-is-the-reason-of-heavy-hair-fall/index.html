What Is The Reason Of Heavy Hair Fall

Written by Daniel Jul 28, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Are you experiencing heavy hair fall lately and wondering what could be the reason behind it? Hair fall is a common problem that affects many people, and there are several reasons why it happens. In this article, we will discuss the reasons for heavy hair fall and provide tips on how to prevent it.

Why Does Hair Fall Happen?

Hair fall can happen due to various reasons, including:

1. Genetics

If you have a family history of hair fall, then you are more likely to experience it too. Genetics plays a significant role in determining the thickness and health of your hair.

2. Hormonal Changes

Hormonal changes can cause hair fall, especially in women. Pregnancy, menopause, and thyroid problems can lead to hormonal changes that affect your hair growth cycle.

3. Nutritional Deficiencies

Your hair needs essential nutrients like vitamins, minerals, and proteins to grow and remain healthy. Lack of these nutrients in your diet can lead to hair fall and thinning.

4. Stress

Stress can cause hair fall by disrupting your hair growth cycle. It can also lead to conditions like telogen effluvium, where you experience sudden and excessive hair fall.

5. Medical Conditions

Medical conditions like alopecia, scalp infections, and autoimmune disorders can cause hair fall. If you are experiencing heavy hair fall, it is best to consult a doctor to rule out any underlying medical conditions.

How to Prevent Hair Fall?

Here are some tips that can help you prevent hair fall:

1. Eat a Balanced Diet

Eating a balanced diet that includes essential nutrients like vitamins, minerals, and proteins can help keep your hair healthy and prevent hair fall.

2. Manage Stress

Practice stress management techniques like meditation, yoga, and deep breathing to reduce stress levels and prevent hair fall.

3. Use Gentle Hair Care Products

Avoid using harsh hair care products that can damage your hair and cause hair fall. Use gentle products that are suitable for your hair type.

4. Avoid Heat Styling

Excessive heat styling can damage your hair and cause hair fall. Avoid using heat styling tools like straighteners and curling irons frequently.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

1. How much hair fall is normal?

It is normal to lose 50 to 100 hairs per day. If you are losing more hair than this, then you may be experiencing hair fall.

2. Can hair fall be reversed?

Yes, hair fall can be reversed by addressing the underlying cause. For example, if your hair fall is due to nutritional deficiencies, then taking supplements can help reverse it.

3. Can stress cause hair fall?

Yes, stress can cause hair fall by disrupting your hair growth cycle. It can also lead to conditions like telogen effluvium, where you experience sudden and excessive hair fall.

Hair Care Tips

Here are some additional tips that can help you take care of your hair:

1. Wash your hair regularly

Washing your hair regularly can help remove dirt and excess oil that can clog your hair follicles and cause hair fall.

2. Massage your scalp

Massaging your scalp can help improve blood circulation and stimulate hair growth.

3. Trim your hair regularly

Trimming your hair regularly can help prevent split ends and breakage, which can lead to hair fall.

4. Protect your hair from the sun

Exposure to the sun can damage your hair and cause hair fall. Wear a hat or use a hair sunscreen to protect your hair from the sun.

In conclusion, hair fall can happen due to various reasons, including genetics, hormonal changes, nutritional deficiencies, stress, and medical conditions. To prevent hair fall, you need to take care of your hair and address the underlying cause. By following the tips mentioned in this article, you can keep your hair healthy and prevent hair fall.
