Best Haircuts For Large Round Faces

Written by Petter Apr 17, 2023 Â· 9 min read

If you have a large round face, finding the perfect haircut can be a challenge. The wrong haircut can amplify your face shape and make your features appear unbalanced. However, there are plenty of hairstyles that can flatter and enhance your round face. In this article, we'll explore the best haircuts for large round faces, including How to, Step by step, Tips, FAQ and Hair Care.

How To Choose the Best Haircut for a Large Round Face

Before you choose a haircut, it's important to understand your face shape. A large round face typically has a wide forehead, full cheeks, and a round chin. To balance out your features, you want a haircut that elongates your face and creates the illusion of a more defined jawline. Here are some tips to keep in mind when choosing a haircut:

1. Consider the Length

The length of your hair can make a big difference in how your face appears. Longer hair can elongate your face and create a more balanced look, while shorter hair can emphasize the roundness of your face. If you prefer short hair, consider a pixie cut with longer layers on top to add height to your hair.

2. Opt for Layers

Layers can add depth and texture to your hair, which can help balance out your face shape. Long layers can elongate your face, while shorter layers can add volume to the top of your head. Be sure to avoid blunt cuts, which can make your face appear wider.

3. Try Bangs

Bangs can be a great way to balance out a round face. Side-swept bangs can create the illusion of a longer face, while blunt bangs can make your face appear wider. If you're unsure, start with longer bangs and gradually work your way up to a shorter cut.

Step by Step: Best Haircuts for Large Round Faces

Now that you know what to look for in a haircut, let's explore some of the best styles for large round faces:

1. The Long Bob

A long bob, or "lob," is a versatile style that can work for a variety of face shapes. For a round face, opt for a lob that hits just below the collarbone and features long layers. This will help elongate your face and create a more balanced look.

2. The Pixie Cut

A pixie cut can be a bold and stylish choice for a round face. To balance out your features, opt for a pixie cut with longer layers on top, which can add height and length to your hair. Avoid blunt cuts that can make your face appear wider.

3. The Side-Swept Bangs

Side-swept bangs can be a great way to balance out a round face. To make the most of this style, opt for longer bangs that sweep to one side, which can create the illusion of a longer face. Pair with long layers for a chic and stylish look.

Tips for Maintaining Your Haircut

Once you've found the perfect haircut for your round face, it's important to take care of your hair to maintain your new style. Here are a few tips:

1. Use a Quality Shampoo and Conditioner

Invest in a high-quality shampoo and conditioner that is designed for your hair type. If you have dry or damaged hair, look for products that are moisturizing and nourishing.

2. Avoid Heat Styling

Heat styling can damage your hair and make it more difficult to maintain your haircut. If you must use heat tools, be sure to use a heat protectant spray and avoid using the highest settings.

3. Get Regular Trims

To maintain your haircut, it's important to get regular trims every 6-8 weeks. This will help keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.

FAQ: Best Haircuts for Large Round Faces

Q: Can I wear a bob with a round face?

A: Yes! A long bob that hits just below your collarbone can be a great choice for a round face. Be sure to opt for long layers to elongate your face and create a more balanced look.

Q: Should I avoid bangs if I have a round face?

A: Not necessarily. Side-swept bangs can be a great way to balance out a round face. Longer bangs that sweep to one side can create the illusion of a longer face.

Q: Can I wear my hair straight if I have a round face?

A: Yes! Straight hair can be a great choice for a round face. Be sure to add volume to the top of your head with long layers or a side part to elongate your face.

Hair Care Tips for Large Round Faces

When it comes to hair care, there are a few things to keep in mind if you have a large round face:

1. Choose the Right Products

Invest in high-quality hair products that are designed for your hair type. If you have dry or damaged hair, look for moisturizing and nourishing products.

2. Protect Your Hair

Avoid heat styling tools and protect your hair from the sun and other environmental factors. Wear a hat or use a protective spray to shield your hair from damage.

3. Get Regular Trims

To maintain your haircut and keep your hair healthy, be sure to get regular trims every 6-8 weeks.

With these tips and best haircuts for large round faces, you can find a hairstyle that flatters your features and gives you confidence. Experiment with different styles and don't be afraid to try something new!
