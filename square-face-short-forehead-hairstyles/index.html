Square Face Short Forehead Hairstyles A Guide To Flattering Styles

Written by Eveline May 19, 2023 · 10 min read

When it comes to finding the perfect hairstyle for a square face shape with a short forehead, it can be a bit of a challenge. However, with the right style and cut, you can enhance your features and create a flattering look that suits you. In this article, we’ll be sharing tips and tricks on how to choose the perfect square face short forehead hairstyle, as well as how to care for your hair to keep it looking its best.

Table of Contents

  • How to Choose the Perfect Square Face Short Forehead Hairstyle
  • Step-by-Step Guide to Styling Your Hair
  • Tips for Maintaining Your Hairstyle
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care Tips for Square Face Short Forehead Hairstyles

How to Choose the Perfect Square Face Short Forehead Hairstyle

When choosing a hairstyle for a square face shape with a short forehead, it’s important to consider a few key factors:

Length

Shorter hairstyles tend to work best for square face shapes with short foreheads, as they help to balance out the proportions of the face. Consider a chin-length bob, a pixie cut, or a layered cut that falls just below the chin.

Layers

Layers can help to soften the angles of a square face shape and create a more feminine look. Opt for long layers that start at the cheekbones and frame the face, or shorter layers that add volume and texture to your hair.

Bangs

Bangs can be a great way to balance out a short forehead and add some dimension to a square face shape. Consider a side-swept fringe or choppy bangs that fall just above the eyebrows.

Texture

Adding texture to your hair can help to create movement and dimension, which can be especially flattering for square face shapes. Consider adding subtle waves or curls to your hair, or using a texturizing spray to create a tousled look.

Step-by-Step Guide to Styling Your Hair

Once you’ve chosen the perfect square face short forehead hairstyle, it’s important to know how to style it to achieve the desired look. Here’s a step-by-step guide to styling your hair:

Step 1: Wash and Condition

Start by washing your hair with a shampoo and conditioner that are suited to your hair type. Rinse thoroughly and gently towel-dry your hair.

Step 2: Apply Styling Products

Apply any styling products you plan to use, such as a texturizing spray or mousse, to damp hair. Work the product through your hair from root to tip, using your fingers or a comb to distribute it evenly.

Step 3: Blow-Dry Your Hair

Using a blow dryer and a round brush, blow-dry your hair in sections. Start at the roots and work your way down to the ends, using the brush to create volume and texture. Once your hair is dry, use a flat iron or curling iron to create any desired curls or waves.

Step 4: Add Finishing Touches

Finish your look by applying a small amount of styling product to your hair, such as a finishing spray or serum. Use your fingers to tousle your hair and create your desired look.

Tips for Maintaining Your Hairstyle

Once you’ve achieved the perfect square face short forehead hairstyle, it’s important to maintain it to keep it looking its best. Here are a few tips:

Get Regular Trims

To keep your hair looking healthy and prevent split ends, it’s important to get regular trims. Aim to get a trim every 6-8 weeks, or as needed depending on your hair type and the style of your cut.

Protect Your Hair

Protect your hair from heat styling and sun damage by using a heat protectant spray and wearing a hat or scarf when you’re outside for extended periods of time.

Use Quality Hair Products

Invest in quality hair products that are suited to your hair type and the style of your cut. This will help to keep your hair looking its best and prevent damage.

FAQs

Q: Can I still wear my hair up with a square face short forehead hairstyle?

A: Absolutely! There are plenty of ways to style your hair up, even with a shorter cut. Consider a messy bun, a half-up half-down style, or a braided updo.

Q: Are bangs a good option for a square face shape with a short forehead?

A: Yes, bangs can be a great way to balance out a short forehead and add some dimension to a square face shape. Just be sure to choose a style that complements your face shape and suits your personal style.

Q: Can I still color my hair with a square face short forehead hairstyle?

A: Yes, you can still color your hair with a square face short forehead hairstyle. Just be sure to choose a color that complements your skin tone and suits your personal style.

Hair Care Tips for Square Face Short Forehead Hairstyles

Here are a few additional hair care tips to keep in mind:

Avoid Over-Washing

Avoid washing your hair too often, as this can strip it of its natural oils and lead to dryness and damage. Aim to wash your hair every 2-3 days, or as needed depending on your hair type and the style of your cut.

Deep Condition Regularly

Deep condition your hair once a week to keep it hydrated and healthy. Use a hair mask or deep conditioning treatment that is suited to your hair type and the style of your cut.

Avoid Hot Tools

Avoid using hot tools on your hair too often, as this can lead to damage and breakage. If you do use hot tools, be sure to use a heat protectant spray and keep the temperature low.

With these tips and tricks, you’ll be able to find the perfect square face short forehead hairstyle and keep your hair looking its best. Remember to choose a style that complements your features and suits your personal style, and to take good care of your hair to keep it healthy and vibrant.
