Find The Perfect Haircut For Your Face Shape

Written by William Jul 26, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Are you looking for a new haircut but don't know where to start? Your face shape can be a helpful guide in finding the perfect haircut. In this article, we will discuss how to determine your face shape, which haircuts will complement your features, and some tips for maintaining your new hairstyle.

How to Determine Your Face Shape

The first step in finding the perfect haircut for your face shape is to determine what shape your face is. Here are the four main face shapes:

  • Round: Your face is widest at the cheeks and has soft, rounded features.
  • Oval: Your face is longer than it is wide and has a slightly rounded jawline.
  • Square: Your face is roughly the same width at the forehead, cheeks, and jawline.
  • Heart: Your face is widest at the forehead and narrows to a point at the chin.

To determine your face shape, measure the widest part of your face (forehead, cheeks, or jawline) and compare it to the length of your face. If your face is roughly the same length as it is wide, you have a square face shape. If it is longer than it is wide, you have an oval face shape. If it is widest at the cheeks, you have a round face shape. And if it is widest at the forehead, you have a heart-shaped face.

Haircuts for Your Face Shape

Now that you know your face shape, it's time to find a haircut that will complement your features. Here are some suggestions:

Round Face

If you have a round face, you want to elongate it and create the illusion of angles. Here are some haircuts that will achieve that:

  • A long bob that falls below the chin
  • Layers that start at the chin and angle downward
  • A side-swept fringe

Oval Face

If you have an oval face shape, you're in luck because almost any haircut will look good on you. Here are some options:

  • A pixie cut
  • A blunt bob
  • Long waves

Square Face

If you have a square face shape, you want to soften your features and create some curves. Here are some haircuts that will achieve that:

  • A long, layered cut
  • A side-swept fringe
  • Soft waves that start at the chin

Heart-Shaped Face

If you have a heart-shaped face, you want to balance out your features and draw attention away from your forehead. Here are some haircuts that will achieve that:

  • A long bob with layers
  • A side-swept fringe
  • Soft, face-framing layers

Tips for Maintaining Your New Hairstyle

Once you've found the perfect haircut for your face shape, you want to make sure it stays looking great. Here are some tips:

  • Invest in a good quality shampoo and conditioner that is tailored to your hair type.
  • Use a heat protectant spray before using any heat styling tools.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair looking healthy and to prevent split ends.
  • Experiment with different styling products to find what works best for your hair.

FAQ

Can I get a haircut that doesn't match my face shape?

Of course! These suggestions are just guidelines. Ultimately, you should choose a haircut that makes you feel confident and comfortable.

How often should I get a haircut?

It depends on how quickly your hair grows and how often you want to change up your look. On average, most people get a haircut every 6-8 weeks.

What if I don't know what my hair type is?

Consult with your stylist. They can help determine your hair type and recommend products that will work best for you.

Hair Care

Remember, finding the perfect haircut for your face shape is just the first step. Taking care of your hair is important too. Make sure to use products that are tailored to your hair type, and get regular trims to keep your hair looking healthy. With these tips and suggestions, you'll be on your way to a great new haircut!
