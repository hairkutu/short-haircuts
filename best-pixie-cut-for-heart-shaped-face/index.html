Best Pixie Cut For Heart Shaped Face A Guide To Flattering Hairstyles

Written by Robby Apr 05, 2023 Â· 9 min read

Are you one of the lucky ones with a heart-shaped face? This facial structure is characterized by a broad forehead, high cheekbones, and a narrow chin. Many women with this face shape often struggle to find the perfect hairstyle that flatters their features. A pixie cut can be a great option, but it's important to choose the right style that complements your face shape. In this guide, we'll share the best pixie cut for heart shaped face, along with tips and tricks for maintaining your new 'do.

How to Choose the Best Pixie Cut for Heart Shaped Face

When it comes to picking the right pixie cut, it's important to consider your face shape, hair texture, and personal style. Heart-shaped faces tend to look best with styles that balance out their features. Here are a few things to keep in mind:

1. Play Up Your Cheekbones

Your high cheekbones are one of your best assets, so choose a style that accentuates them. Opt for a pixie cut with volume on top or side-swept bangs that highlight your cheekbones.

2. Soften Your Jawline

If you have a sharp or pointy chin, look for a pixie cut that softens the jawline. A longer, layered pixie or a side-parted style can help balance out your features.

3. Consider Your Hair Texture

The best pixie cut for heart shaped face will also depend on your hair texture. If you have thick, coarse hair, a choppy or textured pixie can work well. If you have fine hair, a sleek, layered pixie can add volume and dimension.

Step-by-Step Guide: How to Get the Best Pixie Cut for Heart Shaped Face

Ready to take the plunge? Here's what you need to know before you head to the salon:

1. Do Your Research

Before you book your appointment, spend some time researching different pixie cut styles that flatter heart-shaped faces. Save some inspiration photos to show your stylist.

2. Consult with Your Stylist

When you arrive at the salon, talk to your stylist about your face shape, hair texture, and personal style. They can help recommend the best pixie cut for heart shaped face and make adjustments to fit your preferences.

3. Get a Precision Cut

A pixie cut requires precision cutting to create a balanced, flattering shape. Make sure your stylist has experience cutting short hair and uses sharp scissors to achieve a clean, polished look.

4. Style with Product

To maintain your pixie cut, invest in quality styling products that work for your hair texture. A volumizing mousse or texturizing spray can add body and movement, while a pomade can create a sleek, polished finish.

Tips for Maintaining Your Pixie Cut

Once you've got your new pixie cut, it's important to take care of your hair to keep it looking its best. Here are a few tips:

1. Get Regular Trims

To maintain the shape of your pixie cut, you'll need to get regular trims every 4-6 weeks. This will help prevent split ends and maintain the style.

2. Protect Your Hair

If you use heat styling tools, be sure to use a heat protectant spray to prevent damage. You can also protect your hair by wearing a hat or scarf in harsh weather conditions.

3. Experiment with Accessories

A pixie cut can be a great canvas for experimenting with different hair accessories. Try adding a headband, hair clips, or even a bold hair color to switch up your look.

FAQ: Frequently Asked Questions About Pixie Cuts for Heart Shaped Faces

1. Will a pixie cut make my face look too round?

No, the right pixie cut can actually balance out your features and create a more angular look. Just be sure to choose a style that complements your face shape and doesn't add too much volume to the sides.

2. Can I still style my pixie cut if I have fine hair?

Absolutely! With the right products and styling techniques, you can add volume and texture to your fine hair. Ask your stylist for recommendations.

3. How do I know if a pixie cut is right for me?

If you're considering a pixie cut, start by looking at your face shape and personal style. Consult with your stylist to see if a pixie cut is a good fit for you. You can also try a virtual haircut simulator to test out different styles before committing.

Haircare Routine for Pixie Cut

A pixie cut is a low-maintenance style, but it still requires some upkeep to keep it looking its best. Here's a simple hair care routine to follow:

1. Wash Your Hair

Wash your hair with a gentle shampoo and conditioner every 2-3 days. Avoid using hot water or harsh chemicals that can strip your hair of its natural oils.

2. Apply Product

Apply a small amount of styling product to damp hair, focusing on the roots and ends. Use your fingers to work the product through your hair and create your desired style.

3. Air Dry or Blow Dry

You can either let your hair air dry or use a blow dryer to speed up the process. If you're using a blow dryer, use a diffuser attachment to prevent frizz and damage.

4. Touch Up Your Style

Throughout the day, use your fingers to touch up your style and create more volume or texture. You can also use a small amount of product to refresh your hair.

With the right pixie cut for heart shaped face and a little bit of maintenance, you can rock a stylish, low-maintenance 'do that flatters your features. Follow these tips and tricks to keep your pixie cut looking polished and chic.
