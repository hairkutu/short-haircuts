Guys Braids Hairstyles 2019 The Ultimate Guide

Written by William Jun 21, 2023 Â· 8 min read

If you're looking for a hairstyle that's both versatile and stylish, then you might want to consider getting braids. Braids have been around for centuries and have been a popular hairstyle choice for men and women alike. In 2019, guys braids hairstyles have become even more popular, with a variety of styles and designs to choose from. In this article, we'll cover everything you need to know about guys braids hairstyles 2019, from how to get them to hair care tips.

Description

Guys braids hairstyles are a type of hairstyle where the hair is braided in different patterns and designs. Braids can be done on any hair type, but they work best on hair that's at least a few inches long. Braids can be done in different sizes, from small and tight to big and loose. There are a variety of different types of braids, including cornrows, box braids, and twists.

How to Get Guys Braids Hairstyles 2019

The first step to getting guys braids hairstyles is to decide on the type of braid you want. You can do some research and look at different styles online or ask your hairstylist for some suggestions. Once you've decided on a style, the next step is to find a professional hairstylist who specializes in braids. It's important to choose someone who has experience with braiding men's hair, as the process can be more complicated than braiding women's hair.

When you go to your hairstylist, they will start by washing and conditioning your hair. They may also trim your hair to get rid of any split ends or damaged hair. Then, they will start braiding your hair in the pattern and style that you've chosen. The process can take several hours, depending on the length and complexity of your chosen style.

Step by Step Guide

Here's a step by step guide on how to get guys braids hairstyles:

  1. Decide on the type of braid you want
  2. Find a professional hairstylist who specializes in braids
  3. Wash and condition your hair
  4. Trim your hair if necessary
  5. Start braiding your hair in the pattern and style that you've chosen
  6. Continue braiding until all of your hair is braided
  7. Tie off the ends of your hair with an elastic band or hair tie
  8. Apply some hair oil or conditioner to your braids to keep them moisturized
  9. Wrap your hair in a silk scarf or bonnet at night to prevent frizz and breakage
  10. Take care of your braids by washing them regularly and avoiding any harsh chemicals or heat styling tools

Tips for Guys Braids Hairstyles 2019

Here are some tips to keep in mind when getting guys braids hairstyles:

  • Choose a style that complements your face shape
  • Make sure to go to a professional hairstylist who specializes in braids
  • Use a hair oil or conditioner to keep your braids moisturized
  • Wrap your hair in a silk scarf or bonnet at night
  • Avoid any harsh chemicals or heat styling tools that can damage your braids
  • Wash your braids regularly to keep them clean and fresh

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about guys braids hairstyles:

Q: How long do braids last?

A: Braids can last anywhere from a few weeks to a few months, depending on how well you take care of them.

Q: Can I wash my hair with braids?

A: Yes, you can wash your hair with braids, but you need to be careful not to wash them too frequently or with harsh shampoos.

Q: Can I still style my hair with braids?

A: Yes, you can still style your hair with braids. You can try different updos or add accessories like beads or rings to your braids.

Hair Care for Guys Braids Hairstyles 2019

Here are some hair care tips for guys braids hairstyles:

  • Wash your braids regularly with a gentle shampoo
  • Use a hair oil or conditioner to keep your braids moisturized
  • Avoid any harsh chemicals or heat styling tools that can damage your braids
  • Wrap your hair in a silk scarf or bonnet at night to prevent frizz and breakage
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends

Guys braids hairstyles 2019 are a great hairstyle choice for men who want to add some style and flair to their look. Whether you choose a simple cornrow or a more intricate box braid, there's a style for everyone. Just make sure to go to a professional hairstylist and take good care of your braids to keep them looking their best.
