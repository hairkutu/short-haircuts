How To Cut Front Hair For Small Girl A Step By Step Guide

Written by Petter May 09, 2023 · 9 min read

Cutting a small girl’s hair can be a challenging task, especially when it comes to cutting the front hair. The front hair is what frames the face and gives the overall appearance of the haircut. In this article, we will provide you with a step-by-step guide on how to cut front hair for small girl.

Table of Contents:

  • How to Cut Front Hair for Small Girl
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips to Keep in Mind
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care After Cutting

How to Cut Front Hair for Small Girl

The first thing you need to do is to gather all the necessary tools to cut the hair. You will need a pair of scissors, a comb, and hair clips to hold the hair in place. Once you have all the tools, you can start cutting the hair.

Step 1: Wet the Hair

Before you start cutting the hair, it is important to wet it first. This will make it easier to cut and will also help you see the length of the hair more clearly. You can wet the hair by using a spray bottle filled with water or by washing the hair before cutting it.

Step 2: Section the Hair

Section the hair by dividing it into two parts, one on each side of the head. Use hair clips to hold the hair in place.

Step 3: Determine the Length of the Hair

Use the comb to comb the hair forward and determine the length of the hair you want to cut. It is important to be careful when cutting the hair and to cut it slowly to avoid any mistakes.

Step 4: Cut the Hair

Using the scissors, cut the hair slowly and carefully. Start at the center of the hair and work your way outwards. Be sure to cut the hair evenly on both sides of the head.

Step 5: Check the Hair Length

Once you have finished cutting the hair, check the length to make sure it is even on both sides of the head. If you notice any unevenness, you can trim the hair until you achieve the desired length.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here is a step-by-step guide on how to cut front hair for small girl:

  1. Gather all the necessary tools to cut the hair, including a pair of scissors, a comb, and hair clips.
  2. Wet the hair to make it easier to cut and see the length of the hair more clearly.
  3. Section the hair by dividing it into two parts, one on each side of the head.
  4. Use the comb to comb the hair forward and determine the length of the hair you want to cut.
  5. Using the scissors, cut the hair slowly and carefully, starting at the center of the hair and working your way outwards.
  6. Check the length of the hair to make sure it is even on both sides of the head.
  7. If you notice any unevenness, trim the hair until you achieve the desired length.
  8. Repeat the process until you have cut all the front hair.
  9. Style the hair as desired.
  10. Clean up the hair and tools.

Tips to Keep in Mind

Here are some tips to keep in mind when cutting front hair for small girl:

  • Be patient and take your time when cutting the hair.
  • Start cutting the hair slowly and carefully to avoid any mistakes.
  • Use a comb to help you see the length of the hair more clearly.
  • Wet the hair before cutting to make it easier to cut.
  • Check the length of the hair frequently to avoid any unevenness.
  • Trim the hair slowly and carefully until you achieve the desired length.
  • Style the hair as desired after cutting.

FAQs

Q: How often should I cut my small girl's front hair?

A: It is recommended to cut the front hair every 4-6 weeks to maintain the desired length and style.

Q: Should I cut the hair when it is wet or dry?

A: It is recommended to cut the hair when it is wet as it is easier to cut and see the length of the hair more clearly.

Q: Can I cut my small girl's front hair myself?

A: Yes, you can cut your small girl's front hair yourself as long as you have the necessary tools and follow the proper steps.

Hair Care After Cutting

After cutting the hair, it is important to take care of it to maintain its health and appearance. Here are some tips to keep in mind:

  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner to wash the hair.
  • Avoid using hot tools on the hair, such as curling irons or straighteners.
  • Trim the hair every 4-6 weeks to maintain the desired length and style.
  • Avoid using harsh chemicals on the hair, such as hair dye or bleach.
  • Eat a healthy diet to promote hair growth and overall hair health.

Cutting front hair for small girl can be a daunting task, but with the right tools and techniques, it can be done easily and effectively. Remember to take your time, be patient, and follow the proper steps to achieve the desired length and style. With these tips and tricks, you can cut your small girl's front hair like a pro!
