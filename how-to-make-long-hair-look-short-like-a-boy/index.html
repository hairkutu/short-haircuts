How To Make Long Hair Look Short Like A Boy

Written by Eveline Jun 24, 2023 · 8 min read

Do you have long hair but want to switch things up and try a shorter, more masculine haircut? There’s no need to make a drastic chop just yet! With a few simple tricks and styling techniques, you can make your long hair look short like a boy’s. In this article, we’ll show you how.

Table of Contents

  • How to Make Long Hair Look Short Like a Boy
  • Step by Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

How to Make Long Hair Look Short Like a Boy

There are a few different techniques you can use to make your long hair look shorter and more masculine:

1. Tie your hair up

One of the easiest ways to make your hair look short is to tie it up in a bun or ponytail. This will give the illusion of a shorter haircut, especially if you place the bun or ponytail towards the back of your head.

2. Cut your hair into layers

Another way to make your hair look shorter is to cut it into layers. This will add texture and volume to your hair, making it look less long and more structured. You can ask your hairdresser for a layered cut or try doing it yourself at home with a pair of scissors.

3. Style your hair with products

Using styling products like gel or wax can help you create a shorter, more masculine hairstyle. Try slicking your hair back or to the side to create a more defined look. You can also use a hair dryer to add volume and shape to your hair.

Step by Step

Here’s a step-by-step guide to making your long hair look short like a boy:

Step 1: Start with clean, dry hair

Make sure your hair is clean and dry before you start styling it. This will help your hair hold its shape and style better.

Step 2: Tie your hair up

Tie your hair up in a bun or ponytail towards the back of your head. Use a hair tie that matches your hair color to keep everything looking seamless.

Step 3: Add texture with layers

If you want to add more texture and volume to your hair, cut it into layers. You can do this yourself at home with a pair of scissors or ask your hairdresser for a layered cut.

Step 4: Apply styling products

Apply a small amount of styling product like gel or wax to your hair. Work the product through your hair with your fingers, focusing on the roots and tips.

Step 5: Style your hair

Using a comb, slick your hair back or to the side to create a more defined look. You can also use a hair dryer to add volume and shape to your hair.

Tips

Here are a few tips to help you make your long hair look short like a boy:

  • Experiment with different hairstyles to find one that works for you.
  • Invest in high-quality hair products to keep your hair looking healthy and styled.
  • Don’t be afraid to ask your hairdresser for advice or recommendations.

FAQ

Here are some common questions people have about making long hair look short like a boy:

Can I make my long hair look short without cutting it?

Yes, you can make your hair look shorter by tying it up or styling it with products. However, if you want a more dramatic change, you may need to consider cutting your hair into a shorter style.

What products should I use to style my hair?

It depends on your hair type and the style you’re trying to achieve. Generally, you can use gel or wax to add texture and volume to your hair, or hairspray to keep everything in place.

Hair Care

Whether you have long hair or short hair, it’s important to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and looking its best. Here are a few tips for maintaining healthy hair:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo.
  • Use conditioner to keep your hair soft and hydrated.
  • Avoid using hot tools like hair straighteners or curling irons too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Eat a healthy diet rich in vitamins and minerals to promote hair growth and strength.

With these tips and techniques, you can make your long hair look short like a boy’s in no time. Whether you’re looking for a new style or just want to switch things up, these tricks will help you achieve the look you want.
