How To Reduce Hair Fall Naturally At Home For Teenage Girls

Written by Robby Mar 20, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Hair fall is a common concern among teenage girls. Stress, unhealthy diet, and harsh hair treatments are some of the factors that contribute to hair fall. Fortunately, there are natural remedies that can help reduce hair fall at home. In this article, we will discuss how to reduce hair fall naturally at home for teenage girls.

Table of Contents

  1. How to Reduce Hair Fall Naturally
  2. Step by Step
  3. Tips
  4. FAQ
  5. Hair Care

How to Reduce Hair Fall Naturally

Here are some natural remedies that can help reduce hair fall:

1. Aloe vera

Aloe vera is a natural ingredient that can help reduce hair fall. Apply aloe vera gel to your scalp and hair and leave it on for 30 minutes before washing it off with a mild shampoo. Aloe vera helps to nourish the hair follicles and strengthen the hair.

2. Coconut oil

Coconut oil is another natural ingredient that can help reduce hair fall. Massage warm coconut oil into your scalp and hair and leave it on overnight. Wash it off with a mild shampoo in the morning. Coconut oil helps to nourish the hair and reduce hair fall.

3. Onion juice

Onion juice is a natural ingredient that can help reduce hair fall. Apply onion juice to your scalp and hair and leave it on for 30 minutes before washing it off with a mild shampoo. Onion juice helps to nourish the hair follicles and reduce hair fall.

4. Egg mask

Egg mask is a natural remedy that can help reduce hair fall. Beat an egg and apply it to your scalp and hair and leave it on for 30 minutes before washing it off with a mild shampoo. Egg helps to nourish the hair and reduce hair fall.

Step by Step

Here are the step by step instructions for using these natural remedies:

1. Aloe vera

  1. Take fresh aloe vera gel from the plant.
  2. Apply the gel to your scalp and hair.
  3. Leave it on for 30 minutes.
  4. Wash it off with a mild shampoo.

2. Coconut oil

  1. Warm up coconut oil.
  2. Massage the oil into your scalp and hair.
  3. Leave it on overnight.
  4. Wash it off with a mild shampoo in the morning.

3. Onion juice

  1. Grate an onion and squeeze out the juice.
  2. Apply the juice to your scalp and hair.
  3. Leave it on for 30 minutes.
  4. Wash it off with a mild shampoo.

4. Egg mask

  1. Beat an egg.
  2. Apply the egg to your scalp and hair.
  3. Leave it on for 30 minutes.
  4. Wash it off with a mild shampoo.

Tips

Here are some additional tips to help reduce hair fall:

  • Follow a healthy diet.
  • Reduce stress.
  • Avoid harsh hair treatments.
  • Use a mild shampoo.
  • Don't comb or brush your hair when it's wet.

FAQ

1. How often should I use these natural remedies?

You can use these remedies once a week.

2. Can these remedies be used on colored hair?

Yes, these remedies can be used on colored hair.

3. Can these remedies be used on oily hair?

Yes, these remedies can be used on oily hair.

Hair Care

Here are some additional tips to take care of your hair:

  • Wash your hair regularly.
  • Avoid using hot styling tools.
  • Don't tie your hair too tightly.
  • Trim your hair regularly.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and pollution.

Reducing hair fall naturally at home is possible with these natural remedies. However, if you are experiencing excessive hair fall, it's important to consult a doctor.
