How To See If You Would Look Good With Bangs

Written by Petter Jul 21, 2023 · 7 min read

Are you considering getting bangs but not sure if it would suit you? Bangs can be a great way to change up your look, but it's important to make sure they fit your face shape and style. In this article, we'll guide you on how to see if you would look good with bangs.

Table of Content

  • Try Virtual Bangs
  • Consider Your Hair Type
  • Ask for Opinions

How to Measure Your Face Shape

The first step to figuring out if bangs would suit you is to determine your face shape. Here's how:

  1. Measure the width of your forehead, cheekbones, and jawline.
  2. Determine which measurement is the widest.
  3. Compare your measurements to the following face shapes:
    • Oval: Forehead is slightly wider than the jawline, and the length of the face is about one and a half times the width.
    • Round: Face is almost as wide as it is long, with full cheeks and a rounded chin.
    • Square: Forehead, cheekbones, and jawline are all similar in width, with a strong jawline.
    • Heart: Forehead is wider than the jawline, with a pointed chin.
    • Diamond: Cheekbones are the widest part of the face, with a narrower forehead and jawline.
    • Rectangle: Face is longer than it is wide, with a high forehead and square jawline.

If you have an oval or heart-shaped face, bangs will likely look great on you. If you have a round or square face, you may want to avoid blunt bangs and opt for longer, side-swept bangs instead. If you have a diamond or rectangle face, you can experiment with both blunt and side-swept bangs.

Try Virtual Bangs

If you're still not sure if bangs are right for you, try out virtual bangs. Many websites and apps offer virtual try-ons, where you can upload a photo of yourself and see what you would look like with different styles of bangs. This can give you a good idea of whether or not you like the look of bangs on yourself.

Consider Your Hair Type

Another factor to consider is your hair type. Bangs require some maintenance, so if you have very curly, frizzy, or fine hair, you may want to avoid them. Straight or wavy hair is ideal for bangs, as they will lay flat and be easier to style.

Ask for Opinions

Lastly, ask for opinions from friends and family. They may be able to offer a different perspective and help you make a decision. However, ultimately, the decision should be based on what you feel comfortable and confident with.

FAQ

What type of bangs should I get?

The type of bangs you should get depends on your face shape and hair type. Side-swept bangs are versatile and suit most face shapes, while blunt bangs work well for oval and heart-shaped faces.

How do I style my bangs?

Styling your bangs will depend on your preferred look and hair type. You can blow-dry your bangs with a round brush for a sleek look, or air-dry them for a more natural look. Use a small amount of hair product to keep them in place.

How often do I need to trim my bangs?

You should trim your bangs every 4-6 weeks to keep them looking their best. However, if you're comfortable with scissors and have steady hands, you can trim them yourself at home.

Hair Care Tips for Bangs

Once you've decided to get bangs, it's important to take care of them. Here are some hair care tips:

  • Wash your bangs every 2-3 days to prevent them from becoming greasy.
  • Blow-dry your bangs immediately after washing to prevent them from becoming frizzy.
  • Avoid touching your bangs throughout the day to prevent them from becoming oily.
  • Use a small amount of hair product to keep them in place.
  • Trim your bangs regularly to prevent them from becoming too long and obstructing your vision.

By following these tips, you can keep your bangs looking great and enhance your overall hairstyle.

Conclusion

Determining whether or not you would look good with bangs can be a daunting task. But by considering your face shape, hair type, and trying virtual bangs, you can make an informed decision. Remember, the most important thing is that you feel comfortable and confident with your new hairstyle.
