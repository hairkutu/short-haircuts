Short Haircuts For Guys With Thick Curly Hair A Comprehensive Guide

If you're a guy with thick, curly hair, finding the right haircut can be a challenge. But don't worry, we're here to help! In this article, we'll go over everything you need to know about short haircuts for guys with thick curly hair, including how to choose the right cut, tips for styling your hair, and how to take care of your curls.

How to Choose the Right Haircut

The first step in choosing the right haircut for your thick curly hair is to consider your face shape. If you have a square or round face, a shorter cut with some texture on top can help balance out your features. If you have an oval or heart-shaped face, you can go for a shorter or longer cut with more volume.

Next, think about the texture of your hair. If your hair is very curly, you may want to consider a shorter cut that will allow your curls to spring up naturally. If your hair is more wavy than curly, a longer cut may work better for you.

Finally, consider your lifestyle and how much time you're willing to spend styling your hair each day. If you're always on the go, a shorter, low-maintenance cut may be the best option for you.

Popular Short Haircuts for Guys with Thick Curly Hair

Now that you know how to choose the right haircut, let's take a look at some popular short haircuts for guys with thick curly hair:

The Buzz Cut

The buzz cut is a classic short haircut that works well for guys with thick curly hair. This cut is low-maintenance and can be styled quickly and easily. Plus, it's a great option if you're looking for a low-key, no-fuss style.

The Crew Cut

The crew cut is another popular short haircut for guys with thick curly hair. This cut is slightly longer on top and shorter on the sides, with a tapered neckline. It's a versatile cut that can be styled in many different ways, depending on your preferences.

The High and Tight

The high and tight is a military-inspired haircut that works well for guys with thick curly hair. This cut is very short on the sides and back, with a longer, textured top. It's a great option if you're looking for a bold, masculine look.

How to Style Short Thick Curly Hair

Styling short thick curly hair can be a challenge, but with the right products and techniques, it's easy to achieve a great look. Here are some tips to help you style your hair:

  • Start with clean, damp hair.
  • Apply a curl-enhancing product, such as mousse or gel, to your hair.
  • Use a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer to dry your hair, using a low heat setting.
  • Once your hair is dry, use a small amount of pomade or wax to define your curls and add some texture.
  • Finish with a hairspray to hold your style in place.

Tips for Taking Care of Short Thick Curly Hair

Short thick curly hair requires some extra care to keep it looking healthy and well-maintained. Here are some tips to help you take care of your curls:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils.
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it's dry, as this can cause frizz.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your hair when it's wet.
  • Apply a leave-in conditioner or hair oil to your hair to help keep it moisturized.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair looking neat and healthy.

FAQ

What's the best haircut for guys with thick curly hair?

The best haircut for guys with thick curly hair depends on your face shape, hair texture, and lifestyle. Some popular options include the buzz cut, crew cut, and high and tight.

How do I style short thick curly hair?

To style short thick curly hair, start with clean, damp hair and apply a curl-enhancing product. Use a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer to dry your hair, then use a small amount of pomade or wax to define your curls and add texture.

How do I take care of short thick curly hair?

To take care of short thick curly hair, use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner, avoid brushing your hair when it's dry, and use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your hair when it's wet. Apply a leave-in conditioner or hair oil to your hair to keep it moisturized, and get regular trims to maintain your style.

With these tips and tricks, you'll be able to find the perfect short haircut for your thick curly hair, and keep it looking great every day.
