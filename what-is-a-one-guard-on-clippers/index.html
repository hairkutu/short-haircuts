What Is A One Guard On Clippers

Written by Daniel Aug 09, 2023 Â· 7 min read

When it comes to cutting hair, there are many different tools and techniques that a barber or hair stylist can use. One of the most important tools in their arsenal is the clipper. Clippers are electric tools that are designed to quickly and efficiently cut hair. They come with a variety of attachments, including guards. A one guard on clippers is a specific attachment that is used for a particular type of haircut.

Description

A one guard on clippers refers to a specific type of attachment that is used on a clipper to cut hair to a specific length. The guard is designed to fit over the blades of the clipper and help to control the length of the hair that is being cut. One guards are typically used for haircuts that require a shorter length, such as a buzz cut or a crew cut.

How to Use a One Guard on Clippers

If you are using a one guard on clippers, the first step is to make sure that the guard is securely attached to the clipper. Once the guard is in place, you can begin cutting the hair. Start at the bottom of the hairline and work your way up, using the guard to control the length of the hair that is being cut. Make sure to move the clipper in a smooth, even motion to ensure that the hair is cut evenly.

Step by Step

Here are the steps to follow when using a one guard on clippers:

  1. Attach the one guard securely to the clipper.
  2. Start at the bottom of the hairline and work your way up.
  3. Use the guard to control the length of the hair that is being cut.
  4. Move the clipper in a smooth, even motion to ensure that the hair is cut evenly.
  5. Repeat the process until all of the desired hair has been cut.

Tips for Using a One Guard on Clippers

When using a one guard on clippers, there are a few tips that can help you get the best results:

  • Make sure that the guard is securely attached to the clipper to prevent it from falling off during the haircut.
  • Use a comb to help guide the hair into the clipper.
  • Move the clipper in a smooth, even motion to prevent uneven cutting.
  • Start with a longer guard and work your way down to a shorter guard to achieve the desired length.
  • Clean the clipper and guard after each use to prevent hair build-up and keep the tool in good condition.

FAQ

What is the difference between a one guard and a two guard on clippers?

The main difference between a one guard and a two guard on clippers is the length of the hair that is cut. A one guard is shorter than a two guard, so it is typically used for haircuts that require a shorter length, such as a buzz cut or a crew cut.

Can a one guard be used on all types of hair?

A one guard can be used on most types of hair, but it may not be suitable for very thick or curly hair. In these cases, a longer guard may be needed to achieve the desired length.

Hair Care

It is important to take care of your hair after a haircut, regardless of whether a one guard was used or not. Here are a few tips to help keep your hair healthy:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo.
  • Use a conditioner to help keep your hair soft and manageable.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools on a regular basis.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.
  • Eat a healthy diet that is rich in vitamins and minerals to help promote healthy hair growth.

Overall, a one guard on clippers is a useful tool for achieving a variety of haircuts. By following the tips and techniques outlined in this article, you can use a one guard to create a professional-looking haircut at home or in the salon.
