Best Long Hairstyles For Fat Faces And Double Chins

Written by Bardi Apr 30, 2023 · 7 min read

Are you tired of hiding behind your hair because of your double chin and chubby cheeks? Well, we’ve got you covered! In this article, we’ll be discussing the best long hairstyles for fat faces and double chins. We’ll be giving you tips and tricks on how to style your hair to slim down your face and make you feel confident and beautiful. So, let’s get started!

Description

Having a round face and double chin can be challenging when it comes to finding the right hairstyle. But, with the right cut and style, you can accentuate your best features and hide your problem areas. In this article, we’ll be showcasing the best long hairstyles for fat faces and double chins. We’ll be discussing how to style each look and giving you tips on how to maintain your hair to keep it looking fresh and healthy.

How To

The key to finding the perfect long hairstyle for a fat face and double chin is to keep it simple. You want to avoid anything too complicated or fussy. A long layered cut is perfect for slimming down your face and creating a more balanced look. You can also add some soft waves or curls to add volume and dimension to your hair.

If you have a double chin, you can also try a side-swept bang or a deep side part to draw attention away from your chin. And, if you’re feeling bold, you can even try a blunt cut with some face-framing layers to create a more angular look.

Step by Step

Step 1: Start by washing your hair with a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to add body and texture to your hair.

Step 2: Blow-dry your hair using a round brush to add volume and create a smooth finish.

Step 3: Use a curling iron to add soft waves or curls to your hair. You can also use a straightener to create a sleek and straight look.

Step 4: Finish off with a hairspray or texturizing spray to keep your hair in place and add some extra volume.

Tips

When it comes to maintaining your long hairstyle, it’s important to keep your hair healthy and hydrated. Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair soft and shiny. And, don’t forget to trim your hair regularly to keep your ends healthy and prevent split ends.

You can also experiment with different hair accessories, such as headbands, clips, and scrunchies, to add some extra flair to your hairstyle.

FAQ

Q: Can I still wear my hair up if I have a fat face and double chin?

A: Absolutely! You can try a high ponytail, a messy bun, or a half-up/half-down style to create a more elongated look.

Q: What type of bangs should I try if I have a double chin?

A: Side-swept bangs or a deep side part are perfect for drawing attention away from your chin.

Hair Care

When it comes to hair care, it’s important to use products that are gentle on your hair and scalp. Avoid using harsh chemicals or hot tools too often, as this can damage your hair and make it more prone to breakage and split ends.

Invest in a good quality shampoo and conditioner that is specifically designed for your hair type. And, don’t forget to protect your hair from the sun and heat by wearing a hat or using a heat protectant spray.

And, last but not least, make sure to drink plenty of water and eat a balanced diet to keep your hair and skin healthy and glowing.

So, there you have it! The best long hairstyles for fat faces and double chins. With these tips and tricks, you can rock any hairstyle with confidence and style. Remember, the key is to keep it simple and embrace your unique features. Happy styling!
