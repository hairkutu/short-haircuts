What Are Women s Short Haircuts Called

Written by Petter Jun 03, 2023 Â· 10 min read

When it comes to women's hairstyles, there are a lot of options out there. From long and luscious locks to short and sassy cuts, the possibilities are endless. If you're considering making the chop and going for a shorter hairstyle, you may be wondering what the different types of short haircuts are called. In this article, we'll go over some of the most popular women's short haircuts and what they're called in relaxed English language.

  2. Bob Cut
  3. Lob Cut

Pixie Cut

The pixie cut is a short and layered haircut that's been popular for decades. It's characterized by its short length and asymmetrical layers, which can be styled in a variety of ways. Some popular variations include the classic pixie, the textured pixie, and the side-swept pixie. This cut is great for women who want a low-maintenance style that's still chic and stylish.

Bob Cut

The bob cut is another classic short haircut that's been popular for many years. It's characterized by its even length and straight lines, which can be styled in a variety of ways. Some popular variations include the blunt bob, the graduated bob, and the inverted bob. This cut is great for women who want a versatile and easy-to-style haircut.

Lob Cut

The lob cut, or long bob, is a slightly longer version of the classic bob cut. It's characterized by its length, which typically falls just above the shoulders. This cut is great for women who want a short haircut that's still long enough to style in a variety of ways.

Shag Cut

The shag cut is a layered haircut that's popular among women with short hair. It's characterized by its choppy layers and textured look, which can be styled in a variety of ways. Some popular variations include the messy shag, the layered shag, and the curly shag. This cut is great for women who want a low-maintenance style that's still trendy and fashionable.

Fade Cut

The fade cut is a short haircut that's typically worn by men, but has become popular among women in recent years. It's characterized by its short length and gradual fade, which can be styled in a variety of ways. This cut is great for women who want a bold and edgy style that's still feminine and chic.

Undercut

The undercut is a short haircut that's characterized by its shaved sides and longer top. It's a popular style among women who want a bold and edgy look, and can be styled in a variety of ways. Some popular variations include the disconnected undercut, the side-swept undercut, and the curly undercut. This cut is great for women who want a low-maintenance style that's still trendy and fashionable.

Mohawk Cut

The mohawk cut is a short and edgy haircut that's characterized by its shaved sides and longer top. It's a popular style among women who want a bold and daring look, and can be styled in a variety of ways. Some popular variations include the curly mohawk, the braided mohawk, and the messy mohawk. This cut is great for women who want a statement-making style that's still chic and fashionable.

Buzz Cut

The buzz cut is a very short haircut that's characterized by its even length all around. It's a popular style among women who want a low-maintenance and easy-to-style haircut. This cut is great for women who want a bold and edgy look that's still feminine and chic.

Faux Hawk

The faux hawk is a short haircut that's characterized by its longer top and shorter sides. It's a popular style among women who want a bold and edgy look, but don't want to commit to a full-on mohawk. This cut is great for women who want a statement-making style that's still versatile and easy to style.

Quiff Cut

The quiff cut is a short haircut that's characterized by its longer top and shorter sides. It's a popular style among women who want a bold and edgy look, but don't want to commit to a full-on mohawk. This cut is great for women who want a statement-making style that's still versatile and easy to style.

Hair Care Tips for Short Haircuts

Now that you know some of the most popular women's short haircuts and what they're called, it's important to know how to take care of your new style. Here are some tips:

  • Get regular trims to maintain the shape of your haircut.
  • Invest in quality styling products to keep your hair looking its best.
  • Experiment with different styles to find what works best for you.
  • Be gentle when brushing and styling your hair to avoid breakage.
  • Protect your hair from heat damage by using a heat protectant spray before styling.

FAQ

What is the best short haircut for older women?

The best short haircut for older women depends on individual preferences and face shape. Some popular options include the pixie cut, the bob cut, and the shag cut.

What is the difference between a bob and a lob?

The main difference between a bob and a lob is the length. A bob is typically shorter and falls above the shoulders, while a lob is slightly longer and falls just above or below the shoulders.

Can short haircuts be styled in different ways?

Yes, short haircuts can be styled in a variety of ways. Some popular styling options include adding texture, curling the hair, or adding volume with a styling product.

Now that you know what some of the most popular women's short haircuts are called, you can confidently choose a new style that fits your personality and lifestyle. Whether you want a bold and edgy look or a chic and sophisticated style, there's a short haircut out there for everyone.
