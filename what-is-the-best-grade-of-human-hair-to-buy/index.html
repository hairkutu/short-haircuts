What Is The Best Grade Of Human Hair To Buy

If you're looking to buy human hair, you may be wondering which grade is the best. With so many options available, it can be tough to know which one to choose. In this article, we'll go over what the different grades of human hair are, and which one is the best for your needs.

Grades of Human Hair

There are several grades of human hair available, ranging from the lowest quality to the highest. The grades are determined by the processing that the hair goes through, as well as the origin of the hair.

  • Non-Remy hair: This is the lowest quality of human hair. It is collected from the floor of hair salons, temples, and other locations, and the cuticles are often stripped. Non-Remy hair is chemically treated to make it look smoother and shinier.
  • Remy hair: This is a higher quality of human hair. It is collected from a single donor, and the cuticles are kept intact. Remy hair is not chemically treated, so it looks more natural and can be styled more easily.
  • Virgin hair: This is the highest quality of human hair. It is collected from a single donor, and the cuticles are kept intact. The hair is not chemically treated or processed in any way, so it looks and feels completely natural.

What is the Best Grade of Human Hair to Buy?

The best grade of human hair to buy depends on your needs and budget. If you're looking for the highest quality and most natural-looking hair, virgin hair is the way to go. However, it is also the most expensive grade of human hair. If you're on a budget, Remy hair is a good choice. It is still a high quality of hair, but it is more affordable than virgin hair.

How to Choose the Best Grade of Human Hair

When choosing the best grade of human hair for your needs, there are a few factors to consider.

  • Budget: Determine how much you are willing to spend on human hair extensions.
  • Style: Consider what style you want to achieve with your extensions.
  • Quality: Determine how important quality is to you.
  • Origin: Decide if you want hair from a specific region or country.

Hair Care for Your Human Hair Extensions

To keep your human hair extensions looking their best, it's important to take care of them properly.

  • Wash your extensions regularly with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using hot tools too often, as they can damage the hair.
  • Store your extensions properly, either in their original packaging or in a silk or satin bag.
  • Avoid sleeping with wet hair extensions, as this can cause tangles and matting.

Tips for Buying Human Hair

Here are some tips to keep in mind when buying human hair extensions:

  • Buy from a reputable seller, and read reviews before making a purchase.
  • Choose a length that works for your needs and lifestyle.
  • Consider the texture of the hair and how it will blend with your natural hair.
  • Choose a color that matches your natural hair or the color you want to achieve.

FAQs

Q: How can I tell if the human hair extensions I'm buying are high quality?

A: Look for hair that is soft, shiny, and free of tangles and matting.

Q: How long do human hair extensions last?

A: With proper care, human hair extensions can last up to a year or even longer.

Q: Can I dye human hair extensions?

A: Yes, you can dye human hair extensions, just like you would dye your natural hair. However, it's best to have a professional colorist do it for you.

In conclusion, the best grade of human hair to buy depends on your needs and budget. Consider your options carefully and choose a reputable seller to ensure you get high-quality hair that will last. With proper care, your human hair extensions will look great for months or even years to come.
