What Hairstyle Suits A Long Face Male

Written by Christine Apr 08, 2023 Â· 9 min read

A long face can be a blessing or a curse depending on how you carry yourself. Many people with a long face tend to feel self-conscious about it and look for ways to make it look shorter. Thankfully, there are plenty of hairstyles that can help you achieve that. In this article, we will discuss what hairstyles suit a long face male and give you tips on how to style your hair to make it look its best.

Description

Having a long face means that your face is longer than it is wide. This can make your face look a bit thin and narrow. The goal of choosing a hairstyle for a long face male is to make it look shorter and wider. The right hairstyle can also help balance out your facial features and give you a more proportionate look.

How To

When choosing a hairstyle for a long face male, it's important to consider the length and volume of your hair. Here are some tips that can help you choose the right hairstyle:

1. Short Haircuts

Short haircuts are a great option for men with long faces as they can make your face look wider. Short hairstyles like buzz cuts, crew cuts, and side parts can help balance out your facial features and give you a more proportionate look. However, make sure to avoid hairstyles that are too short as they can make your face look even longer.

2. Medium Length Haircuts

Medium length haircuts like textured crops, side-swept fringes, and messy hairstyles can also work well for men with long faces. These hairstyles can add volume to your hair and make your face look wider. However, make sure to avoid hairstyles that are too long as they can make your face look even longer.

3. Hairstyles with Bangs

Hairstyles with bangs can also work well for men with long faces. Bangs can help shorten your face and balance out your facial features. However, make sure to avoid blunt bangs as they can make your face look even longer. Instead, opt for side-swept or textured bangs.

Step by Step

Here are some step-by-step instructions on how to style your hair for a long face male:

1. Start with clean, dry hair.

Make sure to wash your hair with a shampoo and conditioner that is suitable for your hair type. Towel dry your hair and make sure it's completely dry before styling it.

2. Apply hair styling product

Apply a hair styling product like a hair gel, wax, or pomade to your hair. Make sure to choose a product that is suitable for your hair type and hair length.

3. Use a hair dryer and brush

Use a hair dryer and a brush to style your hair. Use the brush to direct your hair in the direction you want it to go, and use the hair dryer to set the style in place.

4. Finish with hair spray

Finish with a hair spray to hold your hairstyle in place. Choose a hair spray that is suitable for your hair type and hair length.

Tips

Here are some tips that can help you choose the right hairstyle for a long face male:

1. Avoid hairstyles that are too long or too short.

Long hairstyles can make your face look even longer, while short hairstyles can make your face look too narrow. Choose a hairstyle that is somewhere in the middle.

2. Add volume to your hair.

A hairstyle with volume can help balance out your facial features and make your face look wider. Use a hair styling product like a hair gel or wax to add volume to your hair.

3. Experiment with different hairstyles.

Don't be afraid to experiment with different hairstyles to find the one that suits you best. Try out different haircuts and styles until you find the one that makes you feel confident and comfortable.

FAQ

What is the best hairstyle for a long face male?

The best hairstyle for a long face male is one that adds volume to the hair and makes the face look wider. Short haircuts, medium length haircuts, and hairstyles with bangs can all work well for men with long faces.

What hairstyles should I avoid if I have a long face?

You should avoid hairstyles that are too long or too short as they can make your face look even longer or too narrow. Blunt bangs should also be avoided as they can make your face look longer.

Hair Care

Proper hair care is important to maintain healthy hair. Here are some tips that can help you take care of your hair:

1. Wash your hair regularly.

Washing your hair regularly can help keep it clean and healthy. Use a shampoo and conditioner that is suitable for your hair type.

2. Use hair styling products in moderation.

Using too much hair styling product can damage your hair and make it look greasy. Use hair styling products in moderation.

3. Get regular haircuts.

Getting regular haircuts can help keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends. Visit a hair salon or barbershop every 4-6 weeks for a haircut.

4. Protect your hair from heat damage.

Using hot tools like hair dryers, straighteners, and curling irons can damage your hair. Use heat protectant products and avoid using hot tools too often.

In conclusion, choosing the right hairstyle for a long face male can be tricky, but with the right tips and tricks, you can achieve a balanced and proportionate look that suits you best.
