How To Make Your Long Hair Boy A Step By Step Guide

Written by Daniel Apr 27, 2023 · 7 min read

If you have a long-haired boy, you may be wondering how to style his hair without resorting to the same old ponytail or messy bun. Luckily, there are plenty of ways to make your long-haired boy look stylish and put-together. In this article, we’ll provide you with a step-by-step guide on how to make your long hair boy, along with tips, FAQs, and hair care advice.

Table of Contents:

  • How to Make Your Long Hair Boy
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care

How to Make Your Long Hair Boy

There are several ways to make your long-haired boy look neat and stylish. Some popular options include:

  • Side part
  • Bun
  • Braid
  • Ponytail
  • Undercut

Each of these styles can be customized to suit your boy’s hair type, face shape, and personal preferences.

Step-by-Step Guide

Follow these steps to make your long-haired boy look great:

Side Part

  1. Wet your boy’s hair thoroughly.
  2. Use a comb to create a deep side part.
  3. Apply a small amount of styling gel or pomade to the hair and comb through.
  4. Optional: Use a hair dryer to set the style in place.

Bun

  1. Brush your boy’s hair back into a ponytail.
  2. Twist the hair and wrap it around the base of the ponytail.
  3. Secure the bun with bobby pins or a hair tie.
  4. Optional: Use a hair dryer to smooth out any flyaways.

Braid

  1. Brush your boy’s hair to remove any tangles.
  2. Divide the hair into three equal sections.
  3. Cross the right section over the middle section, then the left section over the new middle section.
  4. Continue braiding in this pattern until you reach the end of the hair.
  5. Secure the braid with a hair tie.

Ponytail

  1. Brush your boy’s hair back into a ponytail.
  2. Secure the ponytail with a hair tie.
  3. Optional: Use a comb to tease the hair at the crown of the head for added volume.

Undercut

  1. Shave the sides and back of your boy’s head with clippers.
  2. Leave the hair on top longer.
  3. Style the longer hair with gel, pomade, or wax.
  4. Optional: Use a hair dryer to set the style in place.

Tips

  • Use a hair product that suits your boy’s hair type (e.g. gel for thick hair, mousse for fine hair).
  • Don’t be afraid to experiment with different styles until you find one that suits your boy’s face shape and hair type.
  • Encourage your boy to brush his hair regularly to prevent tangles and breakage.
  • Use a detangling spray or conditioner to make brushing easier.
  • Trim your boy’s hair every 6-8 weeks to prevent split ends and maintain a healthy appearance.

FAQs

How often should I wash my boy’s hair?

It depends on his hair type and lifestyle. If he has oily hair or is very active, he may need to wash it every day or every other day. If his hair is dry or curly, he may only need to wash it once or twice a week.

Can I use the same products on my boy’s hair as I use on my own?

No. Children’s hair is more delicate and sensitive than adults’ hair, so it requires gentler products. Look for products that are specifically formulated for children or that are labeled “tear-free”.

Hair Care

In addition to styling your boy’s hair, it’s important to take care of it to keep it healthy and looking its best. Here are some tips for maintaining healthy hair:

  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner that are free of sulfates, parabens, and other harsh chemicals.
  • Massage the scalp to stimulate blood flow and promote hair growth.
  • Avoid using hot tools (e.g. hair straighteners, curling irons) on your boy’s hair.
  • Protect his hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a leave-in conditioner with SPF.
  • Encourage your boy to eat a healthy diet rich in vitamins and minerals that support hair growth (e.g. iron, zinc, biotin).

With these tips and our step-by-step guide, you’ll be able to make your long hair boy look great and keep his hair healthy and strong.
