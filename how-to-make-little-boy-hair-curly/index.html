How To Make Little Boy Hair Curly Tips And Tricks

Written by Christine Aug 01, 2023 · 7 min read

If you have a little boy with straight hair and want to add some curls to his hairstyle, there are a few ways you can do it. In this article, we will discuss how to make little boy hair curly using various techniques and products. Whether you want tight ringlets or loose waves, we have got you covered. So, let's get started!

How to Make Little Boy Hair Curly – Step by Step

Before we begin, it's essential to understand that not all hair types can hold curls. Some hair types are naturally straight and may require more effort to achieve curls. With that said, here are some steps to follow for making little boy hair curly:

Step 1: Wash and Condition the Hair

Start by washing and conditioning your little boy's hair. Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner suitable for his hair type. Avoid products that contain harsh chemicals that can damage his hair.

Step 2: Apply a Curl Activator

Next, apply a curl activator to your little boy's hair. A curl activator is a product that helps enhance the hair's natural curl pattern. Apply the product evenly throughout the hair, from roots to ends.

Step 3: Use a Diffuser

Use a diffuser attachment on your hairdryer to dry your little boy's hair. A diffuser helps distribute heat evenly and reduces frizz. Hold the diffuser close to the hair and scrunch the hair gently while drying. Be careful not to over-dry the hair, as this can cause frizz.

Step 4: Use a Curling Iron

If your little boy's hair is stubborn and won't hold curls, you can use a curling iron to add some definition. Use a small barrel curling iron and wrap small sections of hair around it. Hold the curl for a few seconds before releasing. Be careful not to burn the hair or scalp.

Tips for Making Little Boy Hair Curly

Here are some tips to keep in mind when making little boy hair curly:

  • Use products specifically designed for curly hair
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as this can damage the hair
  • Keep the hair moisturized to reduce frizz
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle the hair gently
  • Avoid brushing the hair when it's dry, as this can cause breakage
  • Trim the hair regularly to prevent split ends

Frequently Asked Questions

Here are some frequently asked questions about making little boy hair curly:

Can I use gel to make my little boy's hair curly?

Yes, you can use gel to make little boy hair curly. Apply a small amount of gel to the hair and scrunch gently. Be careful not to use too much, as this can cause the hair to become stiff and crunchy.

How often should I wash my little boy's hair?

It depends on your little boy's hair type and lifestyle. If he has oily hair, you may need to wash it more often. If his hair is dry, you can wash it once or twice a week. Avoid washing the hair too often, as this can strip the hair of its natural oils.

What is the best way to detangle curly hair?

Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle curly hair gently. Start at the ends of the hair and work your way up to the roots. Avoid using a brush, as this can cause breakage and frizz.

Hair Care for Little Boys with Curly Hair

Here are some hair care tips for little boys with curly hair:

  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner
  • Avoid using hot water, as this can dry out the hair
  • Use a leave-in conditioner to keep the hair moisturized
  • Avoid using hair products that contain alcohol, as this can dry out the hair
  • Trim the hair regularly to prevent split ends
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often
  • Protect the hair from the sun and chlorine

With these tips and tricks, you can make little boy hair curly and keep it looking great. Remember to choose products that are suitable for your little boy's hair type and lifestyle. With patience and practice, you can achieve the perfect curly hairstyle for your little boy.
