Men's Medium Length Hair 2020: How to Style and Care for It

Description

Medium length hair has been a popular trend for men in recent years, and it's still going strong in 2020. With many versatile styles to choose from, men can find a look that fits their personality and lifestyle. Whether you prefer a messy, textured look or a sleek, polished style, medium length hair can be styled in a variety of ways. It's also a great length for growing out from a shorter haircut or for those who don't want to commit to long hair.

How to Style Men's Medium Length Hair in 2020

Styling men's medium length hair in 2020 can be done in many ways, depending on personal preference and hair type. Some popular styles include:

  • The textured crop
  • The slick back
  • The messy fringe
  • The side part
  • The curly/wavy look

Each of these styles can be customized to fit your individual hair type and face shape. For example, those with thick hair may prefer a textured crop or messy fringe, while those with fine hair may opt for a sleek slick back or side part.

Step-by-Step Guide to Styling Men's Medium Length Hair in 2020

Here's a step-by-step guide to styling men's medium length hair in 2020:

  1. Wash and condition your hair with products that are appropriate for your hair type.
  2. Towel dry your hair until it's damp.
  3. Apply a styling product, such as pomade or wax, to your hair. Use a small amount and start at the back of your head, working your way forward.
  4. Use a comb or your fingers to style your hair in the desired look.
  5. Finish with a light hold hairspray, if desired.

Tips for Styling Men's Medium Length Hair in 2020

Here are some tips for styling men's medium length hair in 2020:

  • Experiment with different products to find the one that works best for your hair type.
  • Don't be afraid to try new styles and techniques.
  • Consider visiting a barber or stylist for a professional consultation on the best style for your face shape and hair type.
  • Use a hair dryer to add volume and texture to your hair.
  • Invest in a good quality hairbrush or comb for styling and maintenance.

FAQs about Men's Medium Length Hair in 2020

Here are some frequently asked questions about men's medium length hair in 2020:

Q: Is medium length hair suitable for all face shapes?

A: Yes, medium length hair can be styled to suit all face shapes. However, certain styles may be more flattering for certain face shapes.

Q: How often should I wash my medium length hair?

A: It depends on your hair type and lifestyle. Generally, washing your hair every other day or every two to three days is sufficient.

Q: What products should I use to style my medium length hair?

A: It depends on your hair type and desired style. Some popular products for styling medium length hair include pomade, wax, gel, and mousse.

Hair Care for Men's Medium Length Hair in 2020

Proper hair care is essential for maintaining healthy, stylish medium length hair. Here are some tips for hair care in 2020:

  • Use a shampoo and conditioner that are appropriate for your hair type.
  • Avoid using hot tools, such as straighteners or curling irons, too often as they can damage your hair.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends and maintain the desired length.
  • Avoid using too much product as it can weigh down your hair and make it look greasy.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and other environmental factors by wearing a hat or using a leave-in conditioner with SPF.

Overall, men's medium length hair in 2020 is all about finding a style that suits your personality and lifestyle. With the right products, tools, and techniques, you can achieve a stylish and versatile look that will turn heads.
