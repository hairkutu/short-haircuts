How To Straighten Short Curly Hair Naturally Tips And Steps

Written by Frank Apr 14, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Short curly hair can be a lot of fun, but sometimes you just want a sleek, straight look. However, using heat tools like flat irons and blow dryers can damage your hair over time. Luckily, there are natural ways to straighten short curly hair without causing harm. In this article, we'll share our tips and steps for achieving straight hair naturally.

How to Straighten Short Curly Hair Naturally

The key to straightening short curly hair naturally is moisture. When your hair is well-hydrated, it's more pliable and easier to straighten. Here's how to do it:

Step 1: Wash and Condition Your Hair

Start by washing your hair with a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner. Look for products that contain natural oils like coconut, argan, or jojoba. These oils will help to nourish and hydrate your hair.

Step 2: Apply a Leave-in Conditioner

After washing your hair, apply a leave-in conditioner to help lock in moisture. Use a wide-tooth comb to distribute the product evenly throughout your hair.

Step 3: Detangle Your Hair

Use a detangling brush or comb to gently remove any knots or tangles from your hair. Be gentle to avoid causing breakage or damage.

Step 4: Use a Natural Oil

Apply a natural oil like coconut or argan oil to your hair. This will add extra moisture and help to smooth your hair. Use a small amount, as too much can weigh your hair down.

Step 5: Stretch Your Hair

Divide your hair into sections and twist each section tightly. Use hair clips to hold each twist in place. Leave your hair twisted for several hours or overnight. When you untwist your hair, you should notice that it's much straighter than before.

Step 6: Use a Satin Scarf or Pillowcase

When you sleep, use a satin scarf or pillowcase to protect your hair. Satin is gentle on your hair and will help to prevent breakage and frizz.

Tips for Straightening Short Curly Hair Naturally

Here are some additional tips to help you achieve straight hair naturally:

  • Avoid using heat tools like flat irons and blow dryers, as they can damage your hair.
  • Be gentle when combing or brushing your hair, as curly hair is more prone to breakage.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair hydrated.
  • Trim your hair regularly to get rid of split ends and keep it healthy.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about straightening short curly hair naturally:

Is it possible to straighten curly hair without heat?

Yes, it's possible to straighten curly hair without heat. The key is to use natural methods like stretching your hair and using moisturizing products.

What products should I use to straighten my curly hair naturally?

Look for products that contain natural oils like coconut, argan, or jojoba. These oils will help to nourish and hydrate your hair.

How often should I straighten my curly hair?

It's best to avoid straightening your hair too often, as it can cause damage. Try to limit it to once a week or less.

Hair Care for Short Curly Hair

Here are some general hair care tips for short curly hair:

  • Use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner
  • Avoid using heat tools like flat irons and blow dryers
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week
  • Trim your hair regularly to get rid of split ends
  • Be gentle when combing or brushing your hair

By following these tips and steps, you can achieve straight hair naturally without causing damage to your hair. Remember to be patient and gentle, and your hair will thank you!
