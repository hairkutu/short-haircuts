Haircuts For Guys With Thinning Hair On Top A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Luffy Aug 18, 2023 · 8 min read

If you’re a guy with thinning hair on top, it’s important to find the right haircut that will make the most of your hair while also complementing your face shape and personal style. In this article, we’ll provide you with a comprehensive guide on haircuts for guys with thinning hair on top, including how to choose the right cut, step-by-step instructions, tips, FAQs, and hair care advice.

Table of Contents

  • How to Choose the Right Haircut
  • Step-by-Step Instructions
  • Tips for Styling Thin Hair
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care for Thinning Hair

How to Choose the Right Haircut

The first step in finding the perfect haircut for guys with thinning hair on top is to consider your face shape. Different face shapes will suit different haircuts, so it’s important to choose a cut that will complement your features. Generally speaking, a shorter cut is better for guys with thinning hair on top as it can help to give the illusion of more volume.

Another important factor to consider when choosing a haircut is your personal style. Some guys prefer a more classic look, while others like to experiment with edgy or trendy styles. Ultimately, the best haircut for you is one that you feel comfortable and confident with.

Best Haircuts for Guys with Thinning Hair on Top

Here are some of the best haircuts for guys with thinning hair on top:

  • The Buzz Cut – A classic short cut that can help to make hair look thicker and fuller.
  • The Crew Cut – Similar to the buzz cut, but with slightly longer hair on top.
  • The Side Part – A classic style that works well for guys with thinning hair on top.
  • The Textured Crop – A trendy cut that can help to add volume and texture to thin hair.
  • The Shag – A longer cut that can help to add volume and movement to thin hair.

Step-by-Step Instructions

Here are some step-by-step instructions for getting a haircut for guys with thinning hair on top:

  1. Choose a haircut that suits your face shape and personal style.
  2. Show your barber or stylist a picture of the cut you want.
  3. Ask your barber or stylist to use thinning shears to remove some of the bulk from your hair.
  4. Consider getting a shorter cut on top and leaving the sides slightly longer.
  5. Ask your barber or stylist to use a hairdryer and brush to add volume to your hair.
  6. Use a styling product, such as a pomade or wax, to add texture and hold to your hair.

Tips for Styling Thin Hair

Here are some tips for styling thin hair:

  • Avoid using heavy styling products, as these can weigh down hair and make it look flat.
  • Try blow-drying your hair upside down to add volume.
  • Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to help make hair look thicker.
  • Avoid over-washing your hair, as this can strip it of natural oils and make it look dull.
  • Consider getting a scalp massage to help stimulate hair growth.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about haircuts for guys with thinning hair on top:

Can I still have long hair if I have thinning hair on top?

While long hair can work for some guys with thinning hair on top, it’s generally better to opt for a shorter cut as this can help to make hair look thicker and fuller.

What styling products should I use for thinning hair?

Lightweight styling products, such as pomades and waxes, are best for guys with thinning hair as they won’t weigh hair down. Look for products that are specifically designed for thinning hair.

What should I do if I’m losing a lot of hair?

If you’re experiencing significant hair loss, it’s important to consult a doctor or dermatologist as there may be an underlying health issue that needs to be addressed.

Hair Care for Thinning Hair

Here are some tips for taking care of thinning hair:

  • Avoid using hot tools, such as straighteners and curling irons, as these can damage hair and make it look thinner.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb or brush to detangle hair, as this is less likely to cause breakage.
  • Avoid tight hairstyles, such as ponytails and buns, as these can pull on hair and cause damage.
  • Eat a healthy, balanced diet that includes plenty of vitamins and minerals that are important for hair health.
  • Consider taking a hair supplement that contains biotin, which can help to promote hair growth.

By following these tips and choosing the right haircut, you can make the most of your thinning hair and feel confident and stylish.
