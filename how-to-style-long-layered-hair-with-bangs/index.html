How To Style Long Layered Hair With Bangs A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Oliver Apr 17, 2023 · 9 min read

If you have long layered hair with bangs, you may find it challenging to style your hair in a way that looks flattering and sophisticated. But fear not – with a few simple tricks and techniques, you can create a variety of hairstyles that showcase your long layered locks and complement your face shape. In this article, we’ll walk you through how to style long layered hair with bangs, including tips, step-by-step instructions, and hair care advice. Let’s get started!

Table of Contents:

  1. Description
  2. How To Style Long Layered Hair with Bangs
  3. Step-by-Step Guide
  4. Tips for Styling Long Layered Hair with Bangs
  5. FAQs
  6. Hair Care for Long Layered Hair with Bangs

Description

Long layered hair with bangs is a versatile and trendy hairstyle that can be worn in many different ways. Whether you prefer a sleek and straight look or loose and tousled waves, there are plenty of ways to style your long layered locks to suit your personal style and face shape. With the right tools and techniques, you can transform your long layered hair with bangs from drab to fab in no time!

How To Style Long Layered Hair with Bangs

When it comes to styling long layered hair with bangs, the possibilities are endless. Here are a few ideas to get you started:

Sleek and Straight

If you want to create a sleek and straight look, start by blow-drying your hair with a round brush. Then, use a flat iron to straighten your hair in small sections, starting at the roots and working your way down to the ends. Finish with a spritz of hairspray to keep your locks in place.

Loose Waves

To create loose waves, apply a heat protectant spray to your hair and use a curling wand to curl your hair in small sections. Hold each curl for a few seconds before releasing it, then tousle your hair with your fingers to create loose waves. Finish with a spritz of texturizing spray to add volume and definition.

Braids and Twists

Braids and twists are a great way to add texture and interest to long layered hair with bangs. Try a French braid along the crown of your head, or twist small sections of hair and pin them back with bobby pins for a chic and effortless look.

Step-by-Step Guide

Now that you have some ideas for how to style your long layered hair with bangs, here’s a step-by-step guide to creating a sleek and straight look:

  1. Wash your hair with a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner, then towel dry.
  2. Apply a heat protectant spray to your hair to prevent damage from heat styling tools.
  3. Blow-dry your hair with a round brush, focusing on the roots to create volume.
  4. Section your hair into small sections and use a flat iron to straighten your hair.
  5. Finish with a spritz of hairspray to keep your locks in place.

And here’s a step-by-step guide to creating loose waves:

  1. Wash your hair with a volumizing shampoo and conditioner, then towel dry.
  2. Apply a heat protectant spray to your hair to prevent damage from heat styling tools.
  3. Section your hair into small sections and use a curling wand to curl your hair.
  4. Hold each curl for a few seconds before releasing it, then tousle your hair with your fingers to create loose waves.
  5. Finish with a spritz of texturizing spray to add volume and definition.

Tips for Styling Long Layered Hair with Bangs

Here are a few tips to keep in mind when styling your long layered hair with bangs:

  • Invest in high-quality heat styling tools to prevent damage to your hair.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your hair before styling it.
  • Experiment with different products, such as texturizing spray and volumizing mousse, to achieve the look you want.
  • Consider getting regular trims to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.

FAQs

How often should I wash my long layered hair with bangs?

It depends on your hair type and lifestyle. If you have oily hair, you may need to wash it every day or every other day. If you have dry hair, you may be able to wash it less frequently. Pay attention to how your hair looks and feels, and adjust your washing schedule accordingly.

What’s the best way to dry my long layered hair with bangs?

Blow-drying your hair with a round brush is a great way to add volume and create a sleek look. However, if you don’t have time to blow-dry your hair, you can let it air dry and use a texturizing spray to add definition.

Hair Care for Long Layered Hair with Bangs

Long layered hair with bangs requires regular care to keep it healthy and looking its best. Here are a few tips:

  • Use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner to prevent dryness and split ends.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Consider getting regular trims to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.
  • Treat your hair to a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep it soft and shiny.

With these tips and techniques, you can create a variety of stylish and sophisticated looks for your long layered hair with bangs. Experiment with different styles and products to find what works best for you, and don’t be afraid to try something new!
