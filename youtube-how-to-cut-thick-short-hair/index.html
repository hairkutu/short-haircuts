Youtube How To Cut Thick Short Hair A Step By Step Guide

Written by Bardi May 31, 2023 · 6 min read

If you have thick short hair, finding the perfect haircut can be a daunting task. Fortunately, YouTube is home to many hair experts who can guide you through the process. In this article, we’ll show you how to cut thick short hair with the help of YouTube videos. From the basics to the latest trends, we’ve got you covered.

Description

Thick short hair is a challenge to style, but it can look beautiful with the right cut. Many people prefer to cut their hair at home to save money and time. YouTube is the perfect platform to learn how to do it yourself. You can find a wide range of tutorials from professional hairstylists and beauty influencers.

How to Cut Thick Short Hair

Cutting thick short hair may seem intimidating, but it’s not as difficult as it seems. Here are the basic steps:

Step 1: Gather the Right Tools

Before you start cutting your hair, make sure you have the right tools. You’ll need a pair of sharp scissors, a comb, hair clips, and a mirror. It’s also a good idea to have a spray bottle filled with water to dampen your hair as you cut it.

Step 2: Wash and Dry Your Hair

Wash your hair with a shampoo and conditioner that suits your hair type. After washing, dry your hair completely. Make sure your hair is tangle-free and smooth before you start cutting.

Step 3: Section Your Hair

Using your comb, section your hair into four parts: the crown, the sides, and the back. Secure each section with a hair clip.

Step 4: Start Cutting

Start with the back section and work your way up. Use your comb to hold the hair in place and cut the ends with your scissors. Gradually work your way to the sides and crown, cutting small sections at a time.

Step 5: Check Your Work

After you finish cutting, check your work in the mirror. Make any adjustments if necessary.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you achieve the perfect cut:

  • Use sharp scissors to prevent split ends.
  • Cut small sections at a time to avoid mistakes.
  • Don’t cut too much at once. You can always cut more later.
  • Take breaks if you feel tired or overwhelmed.
  • Watch YouTube tutorials before you start cutting.

FAQs

Can I cut my hair at home?

Yes, you can cut your hair at home if you have the right tools and knowledge. YouTube is a great resource for learning how to cut your hair.

How often should I cut my hair?

You should cut your hair every 4-6 weeks to maintain its shape and health.

Can I cut my hair when it’s wet?

It’s better to cut your hair when it’s dry so you can see the exact length and shape.

Hair Care

After you cut your hair, it’s important to take care of it properly. Use a shampoo and conditioner that suit your hair type. You can also use hair masks and oils to keep your hair healthy and shiny. Avoid using heat styling tools too often, and protect your hair from the sun and pollution.

Conclusion

Cutting thick short hair can be a fun and rewarding experience. With the help of YouTube tutorials, you can learn how to do it yourself and save money on salon visits. Remember to gather the right tools, section your hair, and cut small sections at a time. Don’t forget to take breaks and watch tutorials before you start cutting. With these tips, you’ll be able to achieve the perfect cut in no time.
