What Hairstyle Suits An Oblong Face Shape

Written by William Jul 17, 2023 · 7 min read

Are you someone with an oblong or rectangular face shape? Do you often wonder what hairstyle would suit your face shape? Well, you’ve come to the right place. In this article, we’ll be discussing what hairstyles would complement and flatter an oblong face shape.

Table of Contents

  • What is an Oblong Face Shape?
  • How to Determine Your Face Shape
  • Hairstyles for Oblong Face Shape
  • Tips for Oblong Face Shape
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

What is an Oblong Face Shape?

An oblong or rectangular face shape is characterized by a longer face with a narrow width. The forehead, cheekbones, and jawline are all relatively the same width, with the face being longer than it is wide.

How to Determine Your Face Shape

There are a few simple steps you can take to determine your face shape:

  1. Take a selfie or have someone take a picture of your face.
  2. Trace the outline of your face shape onto the photo using an editing tool or by drawing on the photo.
  3. Compare the shape you drew to the common face shapes – oval, round, square, heart, and oblong.

If you’re still unsure, there are online quizzes and tools that can help you determine your face shape.

Hairstyles for Oblong Face Shape

When it comes to hairstyles for oblong face shapes, the goal is to create width and volume to balance out the longer face shape.

Short Hair

Short haircuts that add volume on the sides and crown are great for oblong face shapes. This includes pixie cuts, layered bobs, and angled bobs. Avoid straight, blunt cuts that can elongate the face even more.

Medium-Length Hair

Mid-length haircuts that add volume and width to the sides of the face are perfect for oblong face shapes. This includes layered cuts, wavy styles, and shoulder-length bobs. Avoid center parts and straight, blunt cuts.

Long Hair

If you have long hair and an oblong face shape, adding layers and waves can create volume and width to balance out the face shape. Long, side-swept bangs can also help to shorten the face. Avoid straight, blunt cuts and center parts.

Tips for Oblong Face Shape

  • Avoid hairstyles that add height to the top of the head, as this can elongate the face even more.
  • Avoid center parts, as they can make the face appear longer.
  • Add volume to the sides of the face to balance out the longer face shape.
  • Consider adding bangs to shorten the face.

FAQ

What hairstyles should I avoid with an oblong face shape?

Avoid hairstyles that add height to the top of the head or center parts, as they can elongate the face even more.

What hairstyles are best for oblong face shapes?

Hairstyles that add volume and width to the sides of the face are best for oblong face shapes. This includes layered cuts, wavy styles, and side-swept bangs.

Can I wear my hair straight with an oblong face shape?

Avoid straight, blunt cuts and center parts, as they can make the face appear longer. If you have straight hair, consider adding layers or waves to create volume and width.

Hair Care

Regardless of your face shape, it’s important to take care of your hair. Regular trims and deep conditioning treatments can keep your hair healthy and looking its best. Additionally, using heat protectant sprays and avoiding excessive heat styling can help prevent damage.

In conclusion, if you have an oblong face shape, focus on creating volume and width to balance out the longer face shape. Short, medium-length, and long hairstyles can all work, as long as they add volume and width to the sides of the face. Avoid center parts and straight, blunt cuts, and consider adding bangs to shorten the face. With the right hairstyle, you can enhance your natural beauty and feel confident in your look.
