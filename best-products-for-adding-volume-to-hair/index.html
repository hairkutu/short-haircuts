Best Products For Adding Volume To Hair

Written by Robby May 02, 2023 Â· 9 min read

If you have thin or flat hair, you may be looking for ways to add volume and body to your locks. Fortunately, there are many products available that can help you achieve the look you want. In this article, we will discuss some of the best products for adding volume to hair.

Dry Shampoo

Dry shampoo is a great product for adding volume to hair. It works by absorbing excess oil and dirt from the scalp, which can weigh down hair and make it look flat. Simply spray the dry shampoo onto your roots, let it sit for a few minutes, and then brush it out. Your hair will look and feel refreshed, and you'll have added volume without having to wash your hair.

Volumizing Spray

Volumizing spray is another great product for adding volume to hair. It works by coating the hair shaft, making it appear thicker and fuller. To use a volumizing spray, simply spray it onto damp hair and blow dry your hair upside down. This will help lift the hair at the roots and create volume.

Mousse

Mousse is a classic product for adding volume to hair. It works by adding texture and hold to the hair, which can help create volume and body. To use mousse, apply a small amount to damp hair and then blow dry your hair with a round brush. This will help create volume and shape.

Root Lifter

Root lifter is a product that is specifically designed to add volume to the roots of the hair. It works by lifting the hair at the roots, creating a fuller, more voluminous look. To use a root lifter, simply spray it onto your roots and then blow dry your hair upside down. This will help lift the hair at the roots and create volume.

Texturizing Powder

Texturizing powder is a great product for adding volume to hair. It works by adding grip and texture to the hair, which can help create volume and body. To use a texturizing powder, simply sprinkle it onto your roots and then massage it in. This will help add volume and create a tousled, textured look.

Hair Powder

Hair powder is another great product for adding volume to hair. It works by absorbing excess oil and adding grip to the hair, which can help create volume and body. To use hair powder, simply sprinkle it onto your roots and then massage it in. This will help add volume and create a textured, tousled look.

Thickening Serum

Thickening serum is a product that is specifically designed to add thickness and volume to the hair. It works by coating the hair shaft and adding thickness, which can help create volume and body. To use thickening serum, apply a small amount to damp hair and then blow dry your hair with a round brush. This will help create volume and shape.

Hair Extensions

Hair extensions are a great way to add volume to hair. They come in a variety of lengths and colors, and can be clipped in or sewn in for a more permanent look. To use hair extensions, simply clip them into your hair and then style your hair as desired. This will help create volume and length.

Blow Dryer

A blow dryer is an essential tool for adding volume to hair. By blow drying your hair upside down, you can lift the hair at the roots and create volume. To use a blow dryer, simply flip your hair upside down and blow dry it while using a round brush to create volume.

Hair Care Tips

There are also some hair care tips that can help you add volume to your hair. For example, washing your hair less often can help prevent excess oil buildup, which can weigh down the hair and make it look flat. Additionally, using a wide-tooth comb to detangle wet hair can help prevent breakage and damage, which can also contribute to flat, lifeless hair.

FAQs

Q: Can I use multiple products to add volume to my hair?

A: Yes, you can use multiple products to add volume to your hair. For example, you can use a root lifter and a volumizing spray to create maximum volume and body.

Q: How often should I use dry shampoo?

A: You can use dry shampoo as often as you like, but it's generally best to use it no more than once or twice a week. Using it too often can cause buildup on the scalp, which can lead to irritation and breakouts.

Q: Can I use hair extensions if I have thin hair?

A: Yes, hair extensions can be a great option for adding volume to thin hair. However, it's important to choose extensions that are lightweight and won't weigh down your hair.

Adding volume to hair can be a great way to create a more full, voluminous look. By using the right products and techniques, you can achieve the look you want and feel confident and beautiful.
