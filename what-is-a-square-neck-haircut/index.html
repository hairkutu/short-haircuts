What Is A Square Neck Haircut

Written by Frank Aug 15, 2023 Â· 6 min read

If you're looking for a new haircut that's both stylish and sophisticated, a square neck haircut might be just what you need. This classic haircut is characterized by a straight, blunt cut across the back of the neck, creating a sharp, clean look that's perfect for any occasion. In this article, we'll take a closer look at what a square neck haircut is, how to get one, and how to take care of your new 'do.

Description

A square neck haircut is a timeless style that's been around for decades. It's a classic look that's perfect for anyone who wants a low-maintenance, easy-to-style haircut. The square neck is created by cutting the hair straight across the back of the neck, creating a sharp, clean line that's perfect for framing the face. This look is often paired with long layers and side-swept bangs, creating a soft, feminine look that's perfect for any occasion.

How To

If you're interested in getting a square neck haircut, the first step is to find a salon or stylist that specializes in this type of cut. Once you've found a stylist, you'll need to schedule a consultation to discuss your hair type, face shape, and personal style. Your stylist will then create a customized cut that's tailored to your needs, using a combination of scissors and razor blades to create a sharp, clean line across the back of the neck.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide to getting a square neck haircut:

  1. Find a salon or stylist that specializes in square neck haircuts.
  2. Schedule a consultation with your stylist.
  3. Discuss your hair type, face shape, and personal style with your stylist.
  4. Take a seat in the stylist's chair and let them work their magic.
  5. After the haircut is complete, style your hair as desired.

Tips

Here are a few tips to keep in mind when getting a square neck haircut:

  • Choose a stylist who has experience with this type of cut.
  • Bring in pictures of the style you're looking for to help your stylist understand your vision.
  • Be honest about your hair type and any concerns you have about the cut.
  • Ask your stylist for tips on how to style your hair at home.

FAQ

Here are a few frequently asked questions about square neck haircuts:

How long does a square neck haircut last?

A square neck haircut can last anywhere from 4 to 8 weeks, depending on how fast your hair grows and how well you take care of it.

What face shape is best suited for a square neck haircut?

A square neck haircut is well-suited for oval and heart-shaped faces, as it helps to accentuate the cheekbones and jawline.

What kind of hair works best for a square neck haircut?

A square neck haircut works best for straight, thick hair, but can be adapted to suit other hair types as well.

Hair Care

After getting a square neck haircut, it's important to take care of your hair to keep it looking healthy and vibrant. Here are a few tips to keep in mind:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as this can damage your hair.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair moisturized.
  • Trim your hair regularly to maintain the shape of your square neck haircut.

With these tips and tricks, you'll be able to rock your new square neck haircut with confidence and style.
