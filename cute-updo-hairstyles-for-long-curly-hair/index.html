Cute Updo Hairstyles for Long Curly Hair - How to, Step by Step, Tips, and Hair Care

In this post, we will show you how to create cute updo hairstyles for long curly hair, step by step. We'll also provide you with some tips and tricks to help you maintain your beautiful curls. So, let's get started!

How to Create Cute Updo Hairstyles for Long Curly Hair

If you have long curly hair, you have a lot of options when it comes to cute updo hairstyles. The key is to find a style that works with your natural curls and complements your face shape. Here are some popular updo hairstyles for long curly hair:

  • Braided Bun
  • Curly Ponytail
  • Half-Up Half-Down
  • Top Knot
  • Messy Bun

Braided Bun

The braided bun is a classic updo hairstyle that looks great on long curly hair. To create this style, start by braiding a section of hair from the front of your head. Pull the rest of your hair back into a ponytail and twist it into a bun. Secure the bun with bobby pins and tuck the end of the braid into the bun. Voila! You have a beautiful braided bun.

Curly Ponytail

A curly ponytail is another great updo hairstyle for long curly hair. To create this style, simply pull your hair back into a high ponytail and secure it with an elastic. Use a curling iron to curl the ends of your ponytail for a more polished look.

Half-Up Half-Down

The half-up half-down style is perfect for those who want to show off their beautiful curls while still keeping their hair out of their face. To create this style, take a small section of hair from the top of your head and pull it back. Secure it with a clip or bobby pins. Leave the rest of your hair down and curly.

Top Knot

The top knot is a trendy updo hairstyle that looks great on long curly hair. To create this style, pull your hair back into a high ponytail and twist it into a knot. Secure the knot with bobby pins and use a curling iron to curl the ends of your hair for a more polished look.

Messy Bun

If you want a more casual updo hairstyle, the messy bun is a great option. To create this style, pull your hair back into a low ponytail and twist it into a messy bun. Secure the bun with bobby pins and pull out a few strands of hair to frame your face.

Step by Step Guide

Now that you know some popular updo hairstyles for long curly hair, let's go over the steps to create them:

Braided Bun

  1. Start by braiding a section of hair from the front of your head.
  2. Pull the rest of your hair back into a ponytail.
  3. Twist the ponytail into a bun and secure it with bobby pins.
  4. Tuck the end of the braid into the bun and secure it with bobby pins.

Curly Ponytail

  1. Pull your hair back into a high ponytail.
  2. Secure the ponytail with an elastic.
  3. Use a curling iron to curl the ends of your ponytail.

Half-Up Half-Down

  1. Take a small section of hair from the top of your head and pull it back.
  2. Secure the section with a clip or bobby pins.
  3. Leave the rest of your hair down and curly.

Top Knot

  1. Pull your hair back into a high ponytail.
  2. Twist the ponytail into a knot and secure it with bobby pins.
  3. Use a curling iron to curl the ends of your hair.

Messy Bun

  1. Pull your hair back into a low ponytail.
  2. Twist the ponytail into a messy bun and secure it with bobby pins.
  3. Pull out a few strands of hair to frame your face.

Tips and Tricks

Here are some tips and tricks to help you create the perfect updo hairstyle for your long curly hair:

  • Use bobby pins that match the color of your hair to keep your updo hairstyle in place.
  • Apply a small amount of hair gel or mousse to your curls before styling to help them hold their shape.
  • Use a curling iron to touch up any curls that have fallen flat.
  • Experiment with different updo hairstyles to find the one that works best for you.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about cute updo hairstyles for long curly hair:

Q: Can I create an updo hairstyle with naturally curly hair?

A: Yes, you can! In fact, updo hairstyles often look better on naturally curly hair because the curls add volume and texture.

Q: Do I need to use heat tools to create an updo hairstyle?

A: No, you don't. While a curling iron can help you touch up your curls, you can create beautiful updo hairstyles with just bobby pins and elastics.

Q: How can I make my updo hairstyle last all day?

A: Use bobby pins that match the color of your hair to keep your updo hairstyle in place. You can also apply a small amount of hairspray to help it hold its shape.

Hair Care

To keep your long curly hair healthy and beautiful, follow these tips:

  • Wash your hair with a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using heat tools on your hair too often.
  • Apply a deep conditioning treatment to your hair once a week.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair when it's wet.
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it's dry, as it can cause frizz.

Now that you know how to create cute updo hairstyles for long curly hair, it's time to try them out for yourself! Remember to have fun and experiment with different styles until you find the one that works best for you.
