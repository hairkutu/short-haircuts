Men s Hairstyles To Suit Round Faces

Written by Robby May 19, 2023 Â· 7 min read

If you have a round face, finding the right hairstyle can be a challenge. A round face typically has fuller cheeks and a softer jawline, so it's important to choose a hairstyle that elongates the face and balances out the features. In this article, we'll discuss the best men's hairstyles to suit round faces and provide tips on how to style and care for your hair.

Table of Contents

  • How to Choose the Right Hairstyle
  • Step-by-Step Guide to Styling
  • Tips for Maintaining Your Hairstyle
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care Tips for Men

How to Choose the Right Hairstyle

When choosing a hairstyle for a round face, the goal is to create the illusion of a more elongated face shape. Here are some things to keep in mind when selecting a hairstyle:

Length

Short hairstyles work best for round faces. Longer hair can emphasize the roundness of the face, while shorter hair can make the face appear longer and slimmer.

Texture

Adding texture to your hair can help create the illusion of a more angular face shape. Consider adding layers or waves to your hair.

Volume

Avoid hairstyles that add volume to the sides of the head. This can make the face appear wider. Instead, opt for hairstyles that add volume to the top of the head, like a pompadour or quiff.

Step-by-Step Guide to Styling

Once you've chosen the right hairstyle, here are some tips for styling:

1. Choose the Right Products

Choose hair products that work well with your hair type and the style you want to achieve. A lightweight pomade or wax can help add volume and texture to your hair.

2. Blow Dry Your Hair

Blow drying your hair can help add volume and create the desired shape. Use a round brush to lift the hair at the roots and blow dry in an upward motion.

3. Style Your Hair

Use your fingers or a comb to style your hair into the desired shape. Be sure to distribute the product evenly throughout your hair.

Tips for Maintaining Your Hairstyle

Here are some tips for maintaining your hairstyle:

1. Schedule Regular Haircuts

Regular haircuts are important for maintaining the shape of your hairstyle. Aim to get a haircut every 4-6 weeks.

2. Use the Right Products

Using the right hair products can help maintain the shape and texture of your hairstyle. Avoid using too much product, as this can weigh down your hair and make it appear greasy.

3. Protect Your Hair from Heat

Excessive heat can damage your hair and cause it to become dry and brittle. Use a heat protectant spray before using hot styling tools.

FAQs

Q: What is the best hairstyle for a round face?

A: Short hairstyles that add volume to the top of the head, like a pompadour or quiff, work well for round faces.

Q: Can I still have long hair if I have a round face?

A: Yes, but it's best to keep the length below the chin and add layers to create volume and texture.

Hair Care Tips for Men

Here are some general hair care tips for men:

1. Wash Your Hair Regularly

Washing your hair regularly can help remove dirt and excess oil. Aim to wash your hair every 2-3 days.

2. Use Conditioner

Conditioner helps moisturize your hair and keep it soft and shiny. Use a conditioner that works well with your hair type.

3. Protect Your Hair from the Sun

Exposure to the sun can damage your hair and cause it to become dry and brittle. Wear a hat or use a hair product with SPF to protect your hair from the sun.

In conclusion, choosing the right hairstyle for a round face can be a challenge, but with the right tips and techniques, you can achieve a stylish and flattering look. Remember to choose a hairstyle that adds length to your face, use the right products, and maintain your hairstyle with regular haircuts and proper hair care.
