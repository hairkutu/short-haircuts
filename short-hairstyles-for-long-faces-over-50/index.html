Short Hairstyles For Long Faces Over 50 How To Choose The Perfect Style

Written by Robby Sep 04, 2023 · 7 min read

Are you over 50 and have a long face shape? If yes, then you’re in the right place. Choosing the right hairstyle can be quite tricky, especially if you’re not sure what suits your face shape. But don’t worry, we’ve got you covered. In this article, we’ll discuss the best short hairstyles for long faces over 50, how to choose the perfect style, and some tips and tricks to maintain your hair.

Table of Contents

  • How to Choose the Perfect Short Hairstyle for Long Faces Over 50
  • Step-by-Step Guide to Styling Short Hair for Long Faces Over 50
  • Tips and Tricks to Maintain Your Short Hair
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care for Short Hair

How to Choose the Perfect Short Hairstyle for Long Faces Over 50

When choosing a short hairstyle for long faces over 50, it’s essential to keep in mind your face shape and hair texture. It’s best to opt for hairstyles that add volume to your hair and make your face look wider. Here are some tips to help you choose the perfect short hairstyle:

  • Choose a hairstyle that adds width to your face, such as a bob or a pixie cut.
  • Avoid hairstyles that are too long or too short, as they can make your face look even longer.
  • Opt for layered hairstyles that add volume and texture to your hair.
  • Consider adding bangs to your hairstyle, as they can make your face look shorter and wider.
  • If you have curly hair, opt for a short curly hairstyle that adds volume to your hair and makes your face look wider.

Step-by-Step Guide to Styling Short Hair for Long Faces Over 50

Styling short hair can be quite easy and convenient. Here’s a step-by-step guide to help you style your short hair:

  1. Wash your hair with a shampoo and conditioner that suits your hair type.
  2. Towel dry your hair and apply a styling product, such as mousse or gel, to add volume to your hair.
  3. Blow-dry your hair using a round brush to add volume and texture.
  4. If you have bangs, style them using a straightening iron or a round brush.
  5. Finish off by applying a hairspray to hold your hairstyle in place.

Tips and Tricks to Maintain Your Short Hair

Maintaining short hair can be quite easy if you follow these tips and tricks:

  • Get a trim every six to eight weeks to maintain the shape of your hairstyle.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools frequently, as they can damage your hair.
  • Use a hair mask once a week to keep your hair moisturized and healthy.
  • Avoid using too many hair products, as they can weigh your hair down.
  • Use a wide-toothed comb to detangle your hair, as it’s less damaging than a brush.

FAQs

Q. What is the best short hairstyle for long faces over 50?

A. The best short hairstyle for long faces over 50 is a bob or a pixie cut, as they add width to your face and make your face look shorter.

Q. Can I add highlights to my short hair?

A. Yes, you can add highlights to your short hair to add depth and texture to your hairstyle.

Q. How often should I wash my short hair?

A. It’s best to wash your short hair every two to three days, depending on your hair type and texture.

Hair Care for Short Hair

Short hair requires minimal care, but it’s essential to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and beautiful. Here are some tips to help you take care of your short hair:

  • Use a shampoo and conditioner that suits your hair type and texture.
  • Avoid using hot water to wash your hair, as it can strip your hair of its natural oils.
  • Use a hair mask once a week to keep your hair moisturized and healthy.
  • Avoid using too many hair products, as they can weigh your hair down.
  • Get a trim every six to eight weeks to maintain the shape of your hairstyle.

Choosing the perfect short hairstyle for long faces over 50 can be quite challenging, but with these tips and tricks, you’ll be able to find the perfect style that suits your face shape and hair texture. Remember to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and beautiful.
