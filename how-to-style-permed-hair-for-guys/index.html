How To Style Permed Hair For Guys A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Eveline May 19, 2023 · 7 min read

Perms are no longer exclusive to women. Men have also jumped into the trend, and it’s not hard to see why. Permed hair can add volume and texture that straight hair cannot achieve. However, styling permed hair can be quite intimidating, especially for guys who are new to the game.

  2. How to Style Permed Hair for Guys
  3. Step-by-Step Guide
  4. Tips for Styling Permed Hair
  5. FAQs
  6. Hair Care for Permed Hair

Description

A perm is a chemical treatment that alters the structure of the hair, making it curly or wavy. It involves applying a solution to the hair that breaks down the hair bonds and then reforming them into a new shape. The result is bouncy, voluminous curls or waves that can last for months, depending on how well you take care of them.

Permed hair requires more maintenance than straight hair. It needs to be styled regularly to maintain its shape and prevent frizz. Styling permed hair for guys can be challenging, but with the right products and techniques, it can look amazing.

How to Style Permed Hair for Guys

Before you style your permed hair, you need to make sure that it is clean and dry. Wash your hair with a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to keep your curls moisturized. Once your hair is dry, you can start styling it.

There are different ways to style permed hair for guys, but the most popular ones include:

  • Curly fringe
  • Short curly hair with fade
  • Long curly hair
  • Curly hair with man bun

Step-by-Step Guide

Here’s a step-by-step guide on how to style permed hair for guys:

  1. Apply a curl-enhancing product to damp hair. This will help define your curls and prevent frizz.
  2. Use a diffuser attachment on your blow dryer to dry your hair. This will help enhance your curls and prevent them from becoming flat.
  3. If you want to create a curly fringe, use a small curling iron to curl the front section of your hair. Use a comb to separate the curls and create volume.
  4. If you have short curly hair, use a hair clipper to create a fade. This will help emphasize your curls and make them more defined.
  5. If you have long curly hair, you can wear it down or tie it up in a man bun. Use a hair tie to secure your hair in place.
  6. Finish with a hairspray to hold your curls in place.

Tips for Styling Permed Hair

If you want to achieve the perfect permed hair for guys, here are some tips to keep in mind:

  • Invest in a good curl-enhancing product. This will help define your curls and prevent frizz.
  • Avoid using a brush on your permed hair. This can cause your curls to become frizzy.
  • Use a diffuser attachment on your blow dryer to enhance your curls.
  • Don’t wash your hair too often. Permed hair needs natural oils to keep it moisturized.
  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to keep your curls moisturized.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about styling permed hair for guys:

1. How long does a perm last?

A perm can last for several months, depending on how well you take care of it. If you use the right products and techniques, your permed hair can look great for up to six months.

2. Can I color my permed hair?

Yes, you can color your permed hair. However, it is recommended that you wait at least two weeks after getting a perm before coloring your hair.

3. Can I straighten my permed hair?

Yes, you can straighten your permed hair, but it is not recommended. Straightening your permed hair can damage your curls and make them less defined.

Hair Care for Permed Hair

Permed hair requires special care to keep it healthy and moisturized. Here are some tips for taking care of your permed hair:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to keep your curls moisturized.
  • Avoid using hot styling tools on your permed hair. This can damage your curls and make them less defined.
  • Apply a leave-in conditioner to your hair to keep it moisturized.
  • Avoid washing your hair too often. Permed hair needs natural oils to keep it moisturized.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.

Styling permed hair for guys can be challenging, but with the right products and techniques, it can look amazing. Follow the tips and guidelines in this article to achieve the perfect permed hair for guys.
