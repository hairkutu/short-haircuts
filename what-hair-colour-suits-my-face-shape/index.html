What Hair Colour Suits My Face Shape

Written by Robby Mar 20, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Are you looking to change up your hair colour, but not sure what will suit your face shape? This comprehensive guide will help you find the perfect hair colour that complements your unique features. Keep reading to learn more!

Description

The right hair colour can make a world of difference when it comes to enhancing your natural beauty. Whether you have a round, square, heart, oval, or diamond-shaped face, there's a hair colour that will flatter your features and make you feel confident and beautiful.

How To

Before deciding on a hair colour, it's important to determine your face shape. You can do this by measuring the width of your forehead, cheekbones, and jawline. Once you have identified your face shape, you can then choose a hair colour that will balance and accentuate your features.

Round Face Shape

If you have a round face shape, you'll want to choose a hair colour that adds length and definition to your face. Opt for highlights or lowlights that are placed around your face to create depth and dimension. Darker shades of brown or blonde can also help elongate your face and make it appear slimmer.

Square Face Shape

For those with a square face shape, a hair colour that softens your angles and adds dimension is ideal. Choose a colour that has warm tones like honey or caramel, and consider adding soft layers to frame your face.

Heart Face Shape

If you have a heart-shaped face, you'll want to avoid hair colours that are too light or too dark. Opt for a colour that is somewhere in between, like a medium brown with subtle highlights. This will help balance your features and draw attention away from a prominent forehead.

Oval Face Shape

Lucky for those with an oval face shape, almost any hair colour will suit them! Play around with different shades and styles to find what you like best. Just be sure to avoid hairstyles that are too long, as they can overpower your features.

Diamond Face Shape

For those with a diamond-shaped face, a hair colour that adds width to your forehead and jawline is ideal. Consider a colour that has warm undertones like auburn or copper, and avoid hairstyles that are too short or too long.

Step by Step

Here's a step by step guide to help you choose the perfect hair colour for your face shape:

  1. Determine your face shape
  2. Research hair colours that complement your face shape
  3. Consider your skin tone and eye colour
  4. Consult with a hairstylist for professional advice
  5. Decide on a hair colour and make an appointment with your stylist
  6. Care for your new hair colour with appropriate hair care products
  7. Enjoy your new look!

Tips

Here are some additional tips to keep in mind when choosing a hair colour:

  • Consider your lifestyle and how much time you're willing to spend on maintenance
  • Don't be afraid to experiment with different shades and styles
  • Be realistic about what will suit your skin tone and eye colour
  • Invest in quality hair care products to keep your new colour looking fresh and vibrant

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about choosing the right hair colour for your face shape:

Q: Can I dye my hair a completely different colour?

A: Yes, you can! Just be sure to choose a colour that complements your skin tone and eye colour.

Q: Will a drastic hair colour change suit me?

A: It depends on your face shape and features. Consult with a hairstylist for professional advice on what will suit you best.

Q: How often should I touch up my roots?

A: It depends on the type of hair colour you have and how quickly your hair grows. Consult with your hairstylist for their recommendation.

Hair Care

Once you've chosen your new hair colour, it's important to care for it properly to keep it looking fresh and vibrant. Use high-quality hair care products that are specifically designed for coloured hair, and avoid using hot tools on a daily basis. Additionally, consider getting regular trims to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.

Now that you know what hair colour suits your face shape, it's time to head to the salon and transform your look! With the right hair colour, you'll feel confident and beautiful every day.
