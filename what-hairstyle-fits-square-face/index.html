What Hairstyle Fits Square Face A Guide For 2023

Are you wondering what hairstyle fits square face in 2023? Look no further! In this article, we will discuss the best hairstyles for square faces, how to achieve them, and tips for hair care.

Description

A square face shape has a strong jawline, a broad forehead, and a straight hairline. The length and width of the face are almost the same. The goal of choosing a hairstyle for square face is to soften the strong features and create balance. The best hairstyles for square faces are those that add height and volume on top while keeping the sides and bottom minimal. In this way, the face appears longer and more proportional.

How to Choose a Hairstyle for Square Face

The first step in choosing a hairstyle for square face is to determine your face shape. This can be done by measuring the length and width of your face with a tape measure. If the measurements are similar, you have a square face shape. The next step is to consider your hair texture, thickness, and personal style. The best hairstyles for square faces are those that complement your natural hair texture, enhance your facial features, and reflect your personality.

Hairstyles for Square Face with Thick Hair

If you have thick hair, the best hairstyles for square faces are those that add movement and dimension. Layered haircuts, long bobs, and shaggy hairstyles are great options. Avoid blunt cuts or short hairstyles that add width to the face.

Hairstyles for Square Face with Fine Hair

If you have fine hair, the best hairstyles for square faces are those that add volume and texture. Pixie cuts, chin-length bobs, and asymmetrical hairstyles are great options. Avoid long hairstyles that weigh down the hair and make the face appear boxy.

Hairstyles for Square Face with Curly Hair

If you have curly hair, the best hairstyles for square faces are those that embrace the natural texture. Curly bobs, shoulder-length curls, and layered hairstyles are great options. Avoid straightening the hair or pulling it back tightly, as this can accentuate the squareness of the face.

Step by Step Guide for Hairstyles for Square Faces

Here is a step by step guide for achieving the best hairstyles for square faces:

Step 1: Consult with a Hair Stylist

A professional hair stylist can help you choose the best hairstyle for square face based on your face shape, hair texture, and personal style. They can also recommend hair care products and treatments to maintain healthy hair.

Step 2: Choose the Right Haircut

The best haircuts for square faces are those that add height and volume on top while keeping the sides and bottom minimal. A layered haircut, long bob, or shaggy hairstyle can achieve this effect.

Step 3: Choose the Right Hair Color

The right hair color can enhance the hairstyle and complement the facial features. A warm hair color such as caramel or honey can soften the strong features of a square face.

Step 4: Style the Hair

Styling the hair can add dimension and texture to the hairstyle. Use a volumizing mousse or spray to add height and hold. Use a curling iron or flat iron to create waves or curls. Finish with a hairspray or hair oil to add shine and hold.

Tips for Hair Care

Here are some tips for hair care to maintain healthy hair:

Tip 1: Use the Right Hair Products

Choose hair products that are suitable for your hair type and texture. Use a shampoo and conditioner that nourish and hydrate the hair. Use a hair mask or treatment once a week to deep condition the hair.

Tip 2: Protect the Hair from Heat

Use a heat protectant spray or serum before using heat styling tools. Use a lower heat setting to avoid damaging the hair. Limit the use of heat styling tools to once or twice a week.

Tip 3: Trim the Hair Regularly

Trim the hair every 6-8 weeks to maintain the shape of the haircut and prevent split ends. This will also promote healthy hair growth.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about what hairstyle fits square face:

Q: Can I wear bangs with a square face?

A: Yes, you can wear bangs with a square face. However, choose wispy or side-swept bangs that soften the strong features of the face.

Q: Can I wear a bob with a square face?

A: Yes, you can wear a bob with a square face. However, choose a long bob or layered bob that adds height on top and softness on the sides.

Q: Can I wear my hair straight with a square face?

A: Yes, you can wear your hair straight with a square face. However, add volume on top with a volumizing product or backcombing to balance the strong jawline.

Choosing the right hairstyle for square face can enhance your natural beauty and boost your confidence. By following these guidelines and tips for hair care, you can achieve a hairstyle that flatters your face shape and reflects your personal style. Happy hairstyling!
