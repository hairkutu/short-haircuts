Why Do I Look Bad With Facial Hair

Written by Oliver Mar 27, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Do you ever look in the mirror after a few weeks of growing out your facial hair and think to yourself, "Why do I look bad with facial hair?" You're not alone. Many men struggle to find the right facial hair style that suits their face shape and personal style. In this article, we'll explore some of the reasons why you might not look your best with facial hair and what you can do to improve your look.

Description

Facial hair can be a great way to change up your look and add some personality to your appearance. However, not all facial hair styles work for everyone. Depending on your face shape, hair type, and personal style, certain facial hair styles might not be the best fit for you.

How To

The first step to finding the right facial hair style for you is to consider your face shape. Different face shapes will look better with different styles of facial hair. For example, if you have a round face shape, a beard that is longer on the chin and shorter on the cheeks can help to elongate your face and make it appear more angular.

Next, think about your hair type. If you have fine or thin hair, a full beard might not be the best choice for you as it can make your face appear smaller. Instead, consider a shorter beard or a mustache. If you have coarse or curly hair, a fuller beard might work well for you as it can help to add some weight and definition to your face.

Finally, consider your personal style. If you prefer a more polished or professional look, a clean-shaven face or a short beard might be the best choice for you. If you're more of a free spirit and like to experiment with your look, a longer beard or a bold mustache could be a great option.

Step by Step

1. Consider your face shape and choose a facial hair style that complements it.

2. Think about your hair type and choose a style that works well with it.

3. Consider your personal style and choose a style that reflects it.

4. Experiment with different styles until you find one that you love.

Tips

- Keep your facial hair clean and well-groomed to avoid looking unkempt.

- Use a beard oil or balm to keep your facial hair soft and manageable.

- Don't be afraid to ask for help from a barber or stylist to find the right style for you.

FAQ

Q: Can anyone pull off a beard?

A: While many men look great with a beard, not everyone can pull off every style. It's important to consider your face shape, hair type, and personal style when choosing a facial hair style.

Q: How long does it take to grow a beard?

A: The amount of time it takes to grow a beard depends on your genetics, age, and other factors. On average, it takes about 2-4 months to grow a full beard.

Hair Care

When growing facial hair, it's important to take care of it just like you would the hair on your head. This means washing it regularly, using a beard oil or balm to keep it soft and manageable, and trimming it regularly to avoid split ends and breakage.

By taking these steps, you can ensure that your facial hair looks its best and complements your overall look.
