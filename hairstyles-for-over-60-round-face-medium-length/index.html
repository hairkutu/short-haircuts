Description

As we age, our hair texture and face shape change. Many women over 60 have round face shapes, which can be challenging when choosing a hairstyle. The right hairstyle can enhance your features and make you look younger and more vibrant. In this blog post, we will discuss hairstyles for over 60 round face medium length that are easy to maintain and stylish.

How to

The first step in choosing a hairstyle is to determine your face shape. If you have a round face shape, you want to choose a hairstyle that elongates your face and adds height to your crown. Medium length hairstyles are ideal because they can be styled in various ways and are easy to maintain. Here are some tips on how to choose the right hairstyle:

  • Choose a hairstyle that adds height to your crown
  • Avoid hairstyles that add width to your face
  • Choose hairstyles with layers and texture
  • Consider a side-parted hairstyle to create an illusion of length

Step-by-Step

Here are some step-by-step instructions on how to style medium length hairstyles for over 60 round face:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair
  2. Apply a heat protectant and a volumizing mousse
  3. Blow-dry your hair with a round brush to add volume
  4. Use a curling iron to add loose waves
  5. Tease the crown of your hair to add height
  6. Finish with a hairspray to hold the style

Tips

Here are some additional tips to keep in mind when choosing and styling medium length hairstyles for over 60 round face:

  • Choose a hairstyle that complements your personality and lifestyle
  • Consider adding highlights or lowlights for dimension
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair to prevent damage
  • Trim your hair regularly to maintain the shape

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about hairstyles for over 60 round face medium length:

Q: What are the best hairstyles for round faces?

A: The best hairstyles for round faces are those that add height to the crown and elongate the face. Medium length hairstyles with layers and texture are ideal.

Q: How often should I trim my hair?

A: It is recommended to trim your hair every 6-8 weeks to maintain the shape and prevent split ends.

Q: How do I choose the right hair color?

A: Choose a hair color that complements your skin tone and eye color. Consult with a professional hair colorist to determine the best shade for you.

Hair Care

Proper hair care is essential to maintain healthy and beautiful hair. Here are some tips on how to take care of your hair:

  • Use a shampoo and conditioner that is suitable for your hair type
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair
  • Protect your hair from the sun and harsh weather conditions
  • Eat a balanced diet that includes protein and vitamins for healthy hair
  • Drink plenty of water to hydrate your hair and scalp

With these tips and hairstyles for over 60 round face medium length, you can look and feel your best at any age. Remember to choose a hairstyle that complements your features and lifestyle, and take care of your hair to maintain its health and beauty.
