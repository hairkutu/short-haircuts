Updo Hairstyles For 50 Year Old Woman With Long Hair A Complete Guide

Written by Luffy Apr 09, 2023 · 9 min read

If you're a 50 year old woman with long hair, you might be wondering what kind of hairstyles are suitable for you. While you can always wear your hair down, updo hairstyles are a great way to add some elegance and sophistication to your look. In this article, we'll be discussing some of the best updo hairstyles for 50 year old women with long hair. We'll cover everything from how to create the look to the best products to use for styling. So, let's get started!

Description

Updo hairstyles are a classic way to style long hair. They're perfect for special occasions and can make you look elegant and sophisticated. For 50 year old women, updo hairstyles can also be a great way to add some youthfulness to your look. With the right styling products and techniques, you can create a beautiful updo that flatters your face and enhances your natural beauty.

How to

Creating an updo hairstyle might seem intimidating at first, but it's actually easier than you might think. Here are the basic steps to creating a simple updo:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair. If your hair is very long, you might want to consider trimming it or getting some layers to make it more manageable.
  2. Brush your hair to remove any tangles.
  3. Gather your hair into a ponytail at the base of your neck.
  4. Twist the ponytail into a bun and secure it with bobby pins.
  5. Use hairspray to set the style and smooth any flyaways.

Of course, there are many variations on this basic updo. You can braid your hair before twisting it into a bun, or add accessories like flowers or hairpins for a more glamorous look. The key is to experiment and find a style that works for you.

Step by Step

Here's a more detailed step-by-step guide to creating an updo hairstyle:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair. If your hair is very long, you might want to consider trimming it or getting some layers to make it more manageable.
  2. Brush your hair to remove any tangles.
  3. Take a section of hair from the crown of your head and tease it with a comb to add volume.
  4. Gently smooth the teased hair with a brush so it looks more natural.
  5. Gather the teased section of hair and pin it at the back of your head with bobby pins.
  6. Take another section of hair from the side of your head and twist it loosely.
  7. Wrap the twisted section around the pinned section at the back of your head and secure it with bobby pins.
  8. Repeat on the other side of your head, twisting and pinning the hair around the first section.
  9. Twist the remaining hair into a bun and secure it with bobby pins.
  10. Use hairspray to set the style and smooth any flyaways.

This is just one example of an updo hairstyle. There are many other styles you can create, depending on your hair type and personal preferences. You can experiment with different braids, twists, and accessories to create a look that's uniquely yours.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when creating an updo hairstyle:

  • Use high-quality styling products to ensure your hair stays in place.
  • Don't be afraid to experiment with different styles until you find one that works for you.
  • If you have trouble creating an updo on your own, consider asking a friend or hairstylist for help.
  • Practice makes perfect! The more you try different updo hairstyles, the easier it will become.

FAQ

What kind of products should I use to create an updo hairstyle?

You'll need hairspray, bobby pins, and possibly some hair elastics or hairpins, depending on the style you're creating. Look for products that are designed for your hair type, such as volumizing spray for fine hair or smoothing serum for frizzy hair.

How long does an updo hairstyle typically last?

An updo hairstyle can last anywhere from a few hours to a full day, depending on how well you've secured it and the products you've used. You can help your style last longer by using plenty of hairspray and avoiding touching your hair too much.

Can I create an updo hairstyle if my hair is thin or fine?

Absolutely! In fact, an updo can be a great way to add volume and fullness to thin or fine hair. Just be sure to use the right products to add texture and hold to your hair.

Hair Care

Creating an updo hairstyle requires healthy, well-maintained hair. Here are some tips for keeping your long hair in great shape:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using hot styling tools too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair moisturized and soft.

By following these hair care tips and experimenting with different updo hairstyles, you can create a look that's both elegant and age-appropriate. So go ahead and try out some of these styles – you might just discover a new favorite!
