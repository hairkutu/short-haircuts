What Hair Shape Suits My Face A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Aprilia Apr 14, 2023 Â· 8 min read

If you're wondering what hair shape suits your face, you're not alone. Your hair can be your crowning glory, but only if it complements your face shape. Choosing the wrong hairstyle can make your face look rounder, longer, or wider than it actually is. In this article, we'll be discussing the different face shapes and the hairstyles that suit them best.

Face Shapes

Before we dive into the different hairstyles, it's important to know your face shape. There are six main face shapes: oval, round, square, heart, diamond, and long.

Oval Face

An oval face is longer than it is wide, with a rounded hairline and jawline. This face shape is considered the most versatile, as it suits almost any hairstyle. Opt for a layered cut that frames your face, or a side-swept fringe to add depth. Avoid hairstyles that add too much height on top, as this can make your face look longer.

Round Face

A round face is as wide as it is long, with a rounded hairline and jawline. To elongate your face, choose a hairstyle that adds height on top, such as a high ponytail or a voluminous updo. Avoid hairstyles that add width to your face, such as blunt bangs or a chin-length bob. Instead, opt for a layered cut that falls below your chin.

Square Face

A square face has a strong, angular jawline and a square hairline. To soften your features, choose a hairstyle that adds volume on top and softens your jawline. Opt for a long, layered cut that falls below your shoulders, or a side-swept fringe that covers your forehead. Avoid hairstyles that are too blunt or straight, as they can accentuate your square features.

Heart Face

A heart face has a wide forehead and a narrow chin, with a pointed hairline. To balance your features, choose a hairstyle that adds volume to your chin area. Opt for a layered cut that falls below your shoulders, or a side-swept fringe that covers your forehead. Avoid hairstyles that add too much volume on top, as this can make your forehead look wider.

Diamond Face

A diamond face has a narrow forehead and jawline, with wide cheekbones. To balance your features, choose a hairstyle that adds volume to your forehead and chin area. Opt for a long, layered cut that falls below your shoulders, or a side-swept fringe that covers your forehead. Avoid hairstyles that add too much volume to your cheekbones, as this can make your face look wider.

Tips for Choosing the Right Hairstyle

Now that you know your face shape and the hairstyles that suit it best, here are some tips to keep in mind:

  • Consider your hair texture and thickness when choosing a hairstyle.
  • Don't be afraid to experiment with different lengths and styles.
  • Choose a hairstyle that complements your lifestyle and personality.
  • Consult with a hairstylist for personalized recommendations.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about choosing the right hairstyle:

Should I choose a hairstyle based on my age?

No, age is just a number. Choose a hairstyle that makes you feel confident and comfortable, regardless of your age.

Can I still wear bangs if I have a round face?

Yes, but opt for a side-swept fringe that covers your forehead and softens your features.

What hairstyles should I avoid if I have a square face?

Avoid hairstyles that are too blunt or straight, as they can accentuate your square features.

Hair Care for Different Hair Shapes

Once you've chosen the right hairstyle for your face shape, it's important to take care of your hair to maintain its shape and health. Here are some tips for hair care based on your hair shape:

  • For straight hair, use a heat protectant when styling with heat tools and avoid over-washing to prevent dryness.
  • For curly hair, use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to prevent frizz and maintain moisture.
  • For thick hair, use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair hydrated and healthy.
  • For thin hair, avoid using heavy products that can weigh your hair down and make it look flat.

Choosing the right hairstyle for your face shape can make a big difference in your overall appearance. With these tips and recommendations, you can find a hairstyle that complements your face shape and makes you feel confident and beautiful.
