How To Keep Your Hair Up Without Gel For Guys

Written by Eveline Apr 18, 2023 Â· 7 min read

If you're a guy who likes to keep his hair up, you may have noticed that gel can be sticky and leave your hair feeling stiff. Luckily, there are plenty of other ways to keep your hair up without using gel. In this post, we'll go over some tips and tricks for keeping your hair up without gel, so you can achieve the style you want without sacrificing comfort or flexibility.

  • Step-By-Step Guide

How To Keep Your Hair Up Without Gel For Guys

The first step to keeping your hair up without gel is to find the right styling product for your hair type. Here are some options:

Hair Wax

Hair wax is a great alternative to gel for guys who want a natural, flexible hold. It's easy to apply and can be used to create a variety of styles, from messy to slicked back.

Pomade

Pomade is a classic styling product that can give your hair a high-shine finish. It's great for creating slicked back styles or for keeping your hair in place without a lot of stiffness.

Hair Spray

Hair spray is a quick and easy way to keep your hair in place, without the sticky feeling of gel. It's perfect for guys who want a natural look with a little bit of hold.

Step-By-Step Guide

Here's how to keep your hair up without gel:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair.
  2. Apply a small amount of your chosen styling product to your fingertips.
  3. Distribute the product evenly through your hair, focusing on the roots and tips.
  4. Use a comb or your fingers to style your hair into the desired shape.
  5. Let your hair air dry, or use a blow dryer on a low heat setting to speed up the process.
  6. If necessary, apply a little bit more product to touch up any areas that need it.

Tips and Tricks

Here are some tips and tricks for keeping your hair up without gel:

  • Experiment with different styling products to find the one that works best for your hair type and desired style.
  • If you have thick or coarse hair, use a stronger hold product, like hair wax or pomade.
  • If you have fine or thin hair, use a lighter hold product, like hair spray or a light hold pomade.
  • Don't be afraid to use your fingers to style your hair, instead of a comb or brush.
  • Avoid using too much product, as this can weigh down your hair and make it look greasy.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about keeping your hair up without gel:

Can I keep my hair up without using any product?

Yes, you can keep your hair up without using any product, but it may be difficult to achieve the desired style and hold without some kind of styling product.

How do I choose the right styling product for my hair type?

You can choose the right styling product for your hair type by considering the texture, thickness, and desired style of your hair. Experiment with different products to find the one that works best for you.

How do I apply styling product to my hair?

Start with a small amount of product and distribute it evenly through your hair, focusing on the roots and tips. Use a comb or your fingers to style your hair into the desired shape.

Hair Care

It's important to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and looking its best. Here are some tips for maintaining healthy hair:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using hot tools, like blow dryers and straighteners, too often.
  • Eat a balanced diet and drink plenty of water to promote healthy hair growth.
  • Get regular haircuts to keep your hair looking neat and tidy.

By following these tips and tricks, you can keep your hair up without gel and achieve the style you want without sacrificing comfort or flexibility. Experiment with different products and techniques to find the ones that work best for you, and don't forget to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and looking its best.
