Description

Short hair with bangs is a trendy and chic hairstyle that is perfect for anyone who wants to change up their look. It's easy to maintain, and there are countless ways to style it to suit your personality and individual style. In this post, we'll be showing you how to style your short hair with bangs and sharing some tips and tricks to make the most of your new haircut.

How to Style Your Short Hair with Bangs

Styling short hair with bangs is simple, and the key is to experiment with different styles until you find the one that works for you. Here are some popular ways to style short hair with bangs:

1. Straight and Sleek

For a classic look, straighten your hair with a flat iron and create a center part. This style works well with blunt bangs that hit just above your eyebrows.

2. Wavy Bob

For a more relaxed and casual look, create loose waves with a curling iron or wand. This style works well with side-swept bangs that are longer on one side.

3. Pixie Cut

If you have a super short pixie cut, you can still add bangs to your hairstyle. Keep them short and choppy for a edgy look or longer and side-swept for a more feminine vibe.

Step by Step Guide

Here's a step by step guide on how to style your short hair with bangs:

1. Start with clean, dry hair.

Wash your hair with a shampoo and conditioner that is suited for your hair type. Blow dry your hair until it is completely dry.

2. Apply heat protectant.

Apply a heat protectant spray or serum to your hair to protect it from damage caused by heat styling tools.

3. Style your bangs.

Blow dry your bangs using a round brush to give them volume and shape. If you want a more relaxed look, use your fingers to tousle your bangs.

4. Style the rest of your hair.

Use a flat iron, curling iron, or wand to style the rest of your hair. Experiment with different styles until you find the one that works for you.

5. Finish with hairspray.

Spray a light mist of hairspray over your hair to hold the style in place.

Tips for Styling Your Short Hair with Bangs

Here are some tips to help you style your short hair with bangs:

  • Invest in a high-quality flat iron or curling iron to achieve the best results.
  • Use a volumizing mousse or spray to give your hair more body and texture.
  • If you have fine hair, avoid using heavy products that can weigh it down.
  • Experiment with different bang lengths and styles to find the one that suits your face shape.
  • Don't be afraid to try out different hair accessories, such as headbands or clips, to add some extra flair to your hairstyle.

FAQ

Can I still wear my hair up with bangs?

Absolutely! You can still wear your hair up with bangs. Experiment with different updos, such as a messy bun or a sleek ponytail, to find the style that works for you.

How often should I trim my bangs?

You should trim your bangs every 4-6 weeks to keep them looking fresh and prevent them from getting too long.

Can I style my bangs with a side part?

Yes, side-swept bangs are a popular style and can be easily styled with a side part.

Hair Care for Short Hair with Bangs

Here are some tips for maintaining healthy hair when you have short hair with bangs:

  • Wash your hair every 2-3 days to prevent it from becoming too oily.
  • Avoid using hot tools on your hair every day to prevent damage.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair soft and nourished.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair looking healthy and prevent split ends.

With these tips and tricks, you'll be able to rock your short hair with bangs in style. Whether you go for a sleek and straight look or opt for loose waves, this hairstyle is versatile and perfect for any occasion. So go ahead and try out some new styles and have fun with your new haircut!
