How To See What Hair Color Will Look Good On You

Written by Petter Apr 27, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Choosing a new hair color can be a daunting task, but it doesn't have to be. By considering your skin tone, eye color, and personal style, you can find the perfect hair color that will make you look and feel your best. In this article, we'll walk you through how to see what hair color will look good on you.

Table of Contents

  • Consider Your Skin Tone
  • Think About Your Eye Color
  • Take Your Personal Style into Account
  • Hair Care Tips
  • FAQs

Consider Your Skin Tone

Your skin tone is one of the most important factors to consider when choosing a new hair color. There are three main categories of skin tones: warm, cool, and neutral. If you have a warm skin tone, you will want to choose hair colors that have golden or honey undertones. If you have a cool skin tone, you will want to choose hair colors that have more ash or cool tones. Neutral skin tones can pull off both warm and cool hair colors.

One way to determine your skin tone is to look at the veins on the inside of your wrist. If your veins appear green, you likely have warm undertones. If they appear blue, you likely have cool undertones. If they appear both blue and green, you likely have a neutral undertone.

Think About Your Eye Color

Your eye color can also play a role in determining which hair color will look best on you. If you have blue or green eyes, you may want to consider warmer hair colors like golden blonde or copper. If you have brown eyes, you can pull off almost any hair color, but shades of brown or red can really make your eyes pop. If you have hazel eyes, you can choose hair colors that complement the flecks of color in your eyes.

Take Your Personal Style into Account

Your personal style should also play a role in your hair color choice. If you prefer a more natural look, you may want to choose a hair color that is close to your natural shade. If you like to stand out, you can choose a bold hair color like pink or blue. If you want to make a subtle change, you can choose a hair color that is just a few shades lighter or darker than your current shade.

Hair Care Tips

Once you have chosen your new hair color, it's important to take care of your hair to keep it looking its best. Here are some hair care tips:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo to prevent your hair color from fading.
  • Avoid washing your hair every day to prevent your hair color from fading.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy and shiny.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often to prevent damage to your hair.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair looking healthy and to prevent split ends.

FAQs

How do I know if a hair color will look good on me?

You can determine which hair color will look good on you by considering your skin tone, eye color, and personal style.

What is the best hair color for warm skin tones?

The best hair colors for warm skin tones have golden or honey undertones.

What is the best hair color for cool skin tones?

The best hair colors for cool skin tones have more ash or cool tones.

How often should I wash my hair?

You should avoid washing your hair every day to prevent your hair color from fading. Try washing your hair every other day or every two days instead.

How often should I get a trim?

You should get a trim every six to eight weeks to keep your hair looking healthy and to prevent split ends.

By considering your skin tone, eye color, and personal style, you can find the perfect hair color that will make you feel confident and beautiful. Remember to take care of your hair to keep it looking its best. Happy coloring!
