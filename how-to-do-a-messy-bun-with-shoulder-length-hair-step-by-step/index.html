How To Do A Messy Bun With Shoulder Length Hair Step By Step

Written by Eveline Jul 30, 2023 Â· 6 min read

If you have shoulder-length hair, a messy bun is a great hairstyle to try. It's effortless, chic, and perfect for any occasion. In this article, we will guide you through the step-by-step process of creating a messy bun with shoulder length hair. We will also provide some tips and answer some frequently asked questions to help you achieve the perfect messy bun every time.

Table of Contents

  1. Description
  2. How to
  3. Step by Step
  4. Tips
  5. FAQ
  6. Hair Care

Description

A messy bun is a hairstyle that looks like you just threw your hair up quickly, but in reality, there is a method to the madness. It's a casual look that is perfect for running errands, going to the gym, or even a night out. The messy bun is a versatile style that can be dressed up or down, depending on the occasion.

How to

To create a messy bun with shoulder-length hair, you will need a few tools:

  • Bobby pins
  • Clear elastic bands
  • Hairbrush
  • Hairspray (optional)

Step by Step

Follow these simple steps to create a messy bun with shoulder length hair:

  1. Start by brushing your hair to remove any tangles or knots.
  2. Gather your hair into a high ponytail and secure it with a clear elastic band.
  3. Twist your ponytail and wrap it around the base to create a bun shape.
  4. Secure the bun with bobby pins, making sure to hide the ends of your hair.
  5. Pull out a few strands of hair around your face to create a messy look.
  6. Use hairspray to set your hairstyle in place (optional).

Tips

Here are some tips to help you achieve the perfect messy bun with shoulder length hair:

  • Use bobby pins that match your hair color to make them less visible.
  • Add texture to your hair by using a volumizing spray or texturizing powder before styling.
  • For a more polished look, use a comb to smooth down any flyaways.
  • If your hair is too short to create a high ponytail, try creating a low messy bun instead.

FAQ

Can I create a messy bun with shoulder length hair?

Yes, you can create a messy bun with shoulder length hair. Follow the steps outlined above to achieve the perfect look.

What kind of hair is best for a messy bun?

A messy bun works best on hair that has some texture and volume. If your hair is too straight, try adding some waves or curls before styling.

What should I do if my bun keeps falling out?

If your bun keeps falling out, try using more bobby pins to secure it in place. You can also try using a stronger elastic band to hold your ponytail.

Hair Care

To keep your hair healthy and looking great, make sure to follow a good hair care routine. This includes washing your hair regularly, using a conditioner, and avoiding heat styling tools as much as possible. You can also use a hair mask once a week to nourish your hair and keep it shiny and soft.

With these tips and tricks, you can create a messy bun with shoulder length hair that looks effortless and chic. Whether you're running errands or going out for a night on the town, this hairstyle is sure to turn heads.
