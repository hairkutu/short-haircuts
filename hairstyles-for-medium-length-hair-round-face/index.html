Hairstyles For Medium Length Hair Round Face A Complete Guide

If you have a round face, you know the struggle of finding the right hairstyle to flatter your features. But fear not, as we have compiled a complete guide on hairstyles for medium length hair round face. From how to determine your face shape to step-by-step guides on different hairstyles, we have got you covered.

Table of Contents

  • Hairstyles for Medium Length Hair Round Face
  • Tips for Styling Medium Length Hair on a Round Face

Determining Your Face Shape

The first step to finding the right hairstyle for your round face is determining your face shape. The easiest way to do this is by measuring your face with a tape measure or ruler. Measure the distance between your hairline and chin, and then measure the distance between the widest part of your face (usually the cheekbones). If the two measurements are roughly the same, then you have a round face shape.

Other features of a round face include a soft jawline, full cheeks, and a wider forehead. To balance out these features, you want to choose hairstyles that add height and length to your face.

Hairstyles for Medium Length Hair Round Face

Long Bob

The long bob, or "lob", is a great hairstyle for medium length hair on a round face. The length of the hair helps elongate the face, while the layers add texture and volume. You can also add side-swept bangs to frame your face and draw attention away from the roundness.

Layered Shag

A layered shag is another great hairstyle for a round face. The layers add texture and volume, while the length helps elongate the face. You can also add side-swept bangs or a middle part to create the illusion of length.

Half-Up Half-Down

A half-up half-down hairstyle is a great way to add height to your face. You can create a voluminous top knot or braid to add texture and draw attention upward. Leave some hair down to frame your face and create a softer look.

Side Part

A side part is a great way to create the illusion of length on a round face. You can add waves or curls to create volume and texture. You can also tuck one side behind your ear to create a more polished look.

Tips for Styling Medium Length Hair on a Round Face

  • Choose hairstyles that add height and length to your face.
  • Avoid hairstyles that add volume to the sides of your face.
  • Experiment with different partings to create the illusion of length.
  • Avoid blunt cuts and straight lines, as they can accentuate the roundness of your face.

FAQs

What hairstyles should I avoid if I have a round face?

Avoid hairstyles that add volume to the sides of your face, such as blunt cuts or straight lines. These can accentuate the roundness of your face.

What hairstyles should I choose if I have a round face?

Choose hairstyles that add height and length to your face, such as a long bob or layered shag. You can also experiment with different partings to create the illusion of length.

Should I add bangs to my hairstyle?

Side-swept bangs can be a great addition to a hairstyle for a round face. They can help frame your face and draw attention away from the roundness. However, avoid blunt bangs, as they can accentuate the roundness of your face.

Hair Care Tips for Medium Length Hair

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo.
  • Use a conditioner to keep your hair moisturized and prevent breakage.
  • Avoid using hot tools on your hair too often, as this can cause damage.
  • Use a heat protectant spray before using hot tools.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.

With these tips and hairstyle ideas, you can find the perfect hairstyle for your medium length hair and round face. Remember to experiment and have fun with your hair, as it is a great way to express your personality and style.
