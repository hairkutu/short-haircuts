How To Do High Messy Ponytail A Step By Step Guide

Written by Oliver Sep 05, 2023 · 6 min read

Are you tired of the same old boring ponytail? Why not switch things up and try a high messy ponytail? This hairstyle is perfect for those days when you want to look effortlessly chic. In this article, we’ll give you a step-by-step guide on how to do a high messy ponytail, as well as tips and tricks to make it look even better.

Table of Contents:

  • How to Do High Messy Ponytail
  • Tips for a Perfect High Messy Ponytail
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care for High Messy Ponytail

How to Do High Messy Ponytail

Before we start, make sure that you have all the necessary tools. You will need a hair tie, bobby pins, and a comb.

Step 1: Brush Your Hair

Brush your hair to remove any tangles and knots. This will make it easier to create the ponytail.

Step 2: Gather Your Hair

Gather all your hair at the crown of your head. Use a comb to smooth out any bumps or flyaways.

Step 3: Tie Your Hair

Tie your hair with a hair tie. Make sure that it’s tight enough to hold your hair in place, but not too tight that it causes a headache.

Step 4: Create the Messy Look

Use your fingers to loosen the hair on the crown of your head. This will create the messy look that we want. Pull out a few strands to frame your face.

Step 5: Secure with Bobby Pins

Use bobby pins to secure any loose hair. You can also use bobby pins to create more volume by pinning the hair underneath the ponytail.

Step 6: Finish with Hairspray

Finish the look with a light spritz of hairspray to hold the messy ponytail in place.

Tips for a Perfect High Messy Ponytail

Here are some tips to make your high messy ponytail look even better:

  • Tease the crown of your head to add more volume.
  • Use a dry shampoo to add texture and make your hair easier to style.
  • Don’t worry if it’s not perfect. The beauty of a messy ponytail is that it’s supposed to look effortless.
  • Add accessories like a scarf or a headband to make the look more interesting.

FAQs

Can I do a high messy ponytail with short hair?

Yes, you can still do a high messy ponytail with short hair. Just use bobby pins to secure any loose strands.

How do I make the messy ponytail look more polished?

If you want a more polished look, use a curling iron to add some waves to your hair before creating the ponytail. You can also use a smoothing serum or cream to tame any flyaways.

Can I wear a high messy ponytail to a formal event?

It depends on the event. A high messy ponytail is perfect for a casual or semi-formal event, but may not be appropriate for a very formal event like a wedding or a gala.

Hair Care for High Messy Ponytail

To keep your hair healthy and looking great, make sure to follow a hair care routine. This includes washing your hair regularly, using a conditioner, and avoiding heat styling tools as much as possible.

When you do use heat styling tools, make sure to use a heat protectant spray to prevent damage. You can also use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair moisturized.

With these tips and tricks, you can create a high messy ponytail that looks effortless and chic. Give it a try and see how it works for you!
