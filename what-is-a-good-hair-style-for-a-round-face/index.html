What Is A Good Hair Style For A Round Face

Written by Aprilia May 19, 2023 · 7 min read

Do you have a round face and want to know what hair style will suit you best? Look no further! In this article, we will discuss everything you need to know about choosing the right hair style for a round face. From how to determine if you have a round face to the best haircuts and styles, we’ve got you covered!

Determining if You Have a Round Face

The first step in choosing the right hair style for a round face is to determine if you actually have a round face. A round face is characterized by equal width and height, with soft, rounded features. To determine if you have a round face, measure the width of your forehead, cheekbones, and jawline. If they are all roughly the same width, then you have a round face.

Choosing the Best Haircuts for a Round Face

The best haircuts for a round face are those that create the illusion of length and definition. Avoid haircuts that add volume to the sides of your face, as this will make your face appear rounder. Instead, opt for haircuts that add volume to the top of your head or create length around your face.

Short Haircuts

Short haircuts can be a great option for those with round faces. However, it’s important to choose a style that adds height to the top of your head. A pixie cut with long, side-swept bangs or a short bob with volume at the crown are both great options.

Medium Haircuts

For those who prefer medium-length hair, a layered cut that falls just below the chin can be very flattering. Layers add movement and dimension to your hair, while the longer length creates the illusion of length in your face.

Long Haircuts

If you have long hair and want to keep it that way, try adding layers that start at your chin or longer. This will create length and definition in your face, while still allowing you to keep your long locks.

Styling Tips for a Round Face

Once you’ve chosen the right haircut, it’s important to style your hair in a way that flatters your face shape. Here are some tips:

  • Use a round brush when blow-drying your hair to add volume at the crown.
  • Style your hair with a side part to create the illusion of length in your face.
  • Avoid hairstyles that are too tight or pulled back, as this will make your face appear rounder.
  • Experiment with different styles, such as braids and updos, to find what works best for you.

Hair Care for a Round Face

Finally, it’s important to take care of your hair to keep it looking its best. Here are some tips:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and shiny.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as this can damage it and make it look dull.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair looking healthy and to prevent split ends.
  • Use a hair mask once a week to add moisture and nourishment to your hair.

FAQ

What is the most flattering haircut for a round face?

The most flattering haircut for a round face is one that adds length and definition. Short haircuts with volume at the crown or medium-length haircuts with layers are both great options.

Can I still have long hair if I have a round face?

Yes, you can still have long hair if you have a round face. Just be sure to add layers that start at your chin or longer to create length and definition.

What styling products should I use for a round face?

Use a round brush when blow-drying your hair to add volume at the crown. You can also use a texturizing spray or mousse to add texture and movement to your hair.

Conclusion

Choosing the right hair style for a round face can be tricky, but with the right tips and tricks, it’s easy to find a style that flatters your face shape. Remember to choose a haircut that adds length and definition, and to experiment with different styles and products to find what works best for you. With a little bit of effort, you can have a hair style that makes you look and feel your best!
