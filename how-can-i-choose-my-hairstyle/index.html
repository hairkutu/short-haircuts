How Can I Choose My Hairstyle A Step By Step Guide

Written by Robby Sep 07, 2023 Â· 8 min read

Choosing the right hairstyle can be a daunting task, especially with so many options available. However, with a few simple steps, you can find the perfect hairstyle that suits your face shape, personality, and lifestyle. In this article, we will guide you on how to choose your hairstyle, provide some tips, answer frequently asked questions, and share some hair care tips to keep your locks healthy and shiny.

Determining Your Face Shape

The first step in choosing your hairstyle is to determine your face shape. There are six basic face shapes, including oval, round, square, heart, diamond, and oblong. By identifying your face shape, you can narrow down your hairstyle options and find the most flattering look.

If you have an oval face shape, you're lucky as you can pull off most hairstyles. For round faces, go for hairstyles that add height and lengthen the face. For square faces, choose styles that soften the angles and add curves. Heart-shaped faces look great with side-swept bangs and long layers. Diamond-shaped faces can try short hairstyles with volume on the top, while oblong faces can benefit from adding width to the sides of the face.

Considering Your Lifestyle and Personality

Your lifestyle and personality also play a significant role in selecting your hairstyle. If you have an active lifestyle, you may want a low-maintenance hairstyle that requires minimal styling. On the other hand, if you enjoy experimenting with different looks, you can opt for more intricate styles.

Your personality also influences your hairstyle choice. If you're outgoing and fun-loving, you may want a hairstyle that reflects your vibrant personality. If you're more reserved and prefer a classic look, you may opt for a more conservative hairstyle.

Choosing the Right Length

The length of your hair is another important factor to consider. Long hair offers more styling options and can be versatile, while short hair is easy to maintain and perfect for busy individuals. Medium-length hair is also a great option as it offers the best of both worlds.

If you're unsure about the length, you can start with a mid-length cut and gradually work your way up or down. You can also try clip-in extensions to experiment with different lengths without committing to a particular style.

Selecting the Right Color

Choosing the right hair color can enhance your natural features and complement your skin tone. If you're unsure about the right color for you, consult with a professional stylist who can recommend the perfect shade.

You can also experiment with different colors by using temporary hair dyes or highlights. This allows you to try different shades without committing to a permanent change.

Experimenting with Different Styles

Once you've determined your face shape, lifestyle, personality, length, and color, you can start experimenting with different styles. You can browse through magazines or online galleries for inspiration or consult with a stylist for recommendations.

When trying out a new style, bring a photo to your stylist to ensure they understand the look you want. You can also try styling your hair differently by using different products, tools, or accessories.

Hairstyling Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when styling your hair:

  • Invest in quality hair care products that suit your hair type.
  • Use heat protectant spray before using hot tools.
  • Avoid using too much product, as it can weigh down your hair.
  • Brush your hair gently to avoid breakage.
  • Get regular trims to maintain the health of your hair.

Frequently Asked Questions

Here are some frequently asked questions about choosing your hairstyle:

Q: How often should I change my hairstyle?

A: There's no set rule for how often you should change your hairstyle. It depends on your personal preference, but you can try changing it up every few months to keep things fresh.

Q: Can I pull off short hair?

A: Short hair can be flattering on many people, but it depends on your face shape, hair texture, and personal style. Consult with a stylist to determine if short hair is right for you.

Q: How can I make my hair look fuller?

A: You can add volume to your hair by using volumizing products, blow-drying your hair upside down, or getting a layered haircut.

Hair Care Tips

Here are some tips to keep your hair healthy and shiny:

  • Wash your hair with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using hot water, as it can strip your hair of natural oils.
  • Don't over-wash your hair, as it can dry out your scalp.
  • Eat a healthy diet that includes vitamins and minerals that promote hair growth.
  • Avoid using harsh chemicals or heat tools too often.

Choosing your hairstyle can be a fun and exciting process. By following these tips and considering your face shape, lifestyle, personality, length, and color, you can find the perfect hairstyle that enhances your natural beauty and makes you feel confident and stylish.
