Best Haircuts For Over 50S

Written by Robby Mar 27, 2023 Â· 11 min read

As we age, our hair changes along with our skin, body, and lifestyle. What used to be a perfect haircut in our 20s may not look as flattering in our 50s. However, there are plenty of hairstyles that suit women over 50 and bring out their best features. In this article, we will explore the best haircuts for over 50s, how to style them, and how to take care of your hair in your golden years.

Table of Content

  • Description
  • How to
  • Step by step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

Women over 50 have different hair types, face shapes, and personal styles. Therefore, there is no one-size-fits-all haircut for this age group. However, some haircuts are more popular and flattering than others. Some of the best haircuts for over 50s include:

1. Bob Haircut

The bob haircut is a classic hairstyle that never goes out of style. It is perfect for women who want a low-maintenance and chic look. The bob can be styled in different ways, such as straight, wavy, or curly, depending on your hair texture and face shape.

2. Pixie Haircut

The pixie haircut is a bold and edgy hairstyle that can make you look younger and more stylish. It is ideal for women with fine hair and sharp facial features. The pixie can be styled in different ways, such as messy, spiky, or slicked back.

3. Layered Haircut

The layered haircut is a versatile hairstyle that adds volume and movement to your hair. It is perfect for women with thin or fine hair who want to add texture and depth to their locks. The layered haircut can be styled in different ways, such as tousled, straight, or curly.

4. Shag Haircut

The shag haircut is a retro hairstyle that has made a comeback in recent years. It is perfect for women who want a messy and carefree look. The shag can be styled in different ways, such as choppy, layered, or feathered.

How to

Before choosing a haircut, it is important to consider your hair type, face shape, and personal style. You can consult with a hairstylist who specializes in haircuts for over 50s and ask for their advice. Once you have chosen a haircut, you can follow these steps to style it:

1. Wash and condition your hair

Use a shampoo and conditioner that are suitable for your hair type and condition. Rinse your hair thoroughly and towel dry it gently.

2. Apply a styling product

You can apply a mousse, gel, or serum to your damp hair to add volume, texture, or shine. Use a small amount and distribute it evenly throughout your hair.

3. Blow-dry your hair

Use a round brush and a hairdryer to style your hair. Start at the roots and work your way to the ends. Use a cool setting to set your hair in place.

4. Use a styling tool

You can use a straightener, curling iron, or hot rollers to add more definition and style to your hair. Use a heat protectant spray to avoid damaging your hair.

Step by step

Here are some step-by-step instructions on how to style some of the best haircuts for over 50s:

1. Bob Haircut

Step 1: Part your hair in the middle or on one side.

Step 2: Use a round brush and a hairdryer to blow-dry your hair.

Step 3: Use a straightener or a curling iron to add more definition to your hair.

Step 4: Apply a hairspray to set your hair in place.

2. Pixie Haircut

Step 1: Apply a styling product to your damp hair.

Step 2: Use your fingers to tousle your hair and create a messy look.

Step 3: Use a hairspray to set your hair in place.

3. Layered Haircut

Step 1: Blow-dry your hair using a round brush.

Step 2: Use a straightener or a curling iron to add more texture to your hair.

Step 3: Use a hairspray to set your hair in place.

4. Shag Haircut

Step 1: Apply a volumizing mousse to your damp hair.

Step 2: Use your fingers to tousle your hair and create a messy look.

Step 3: Use a hairspray to set your hair in place.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you get the most out of your haircut:

1. Choose a haircut that suits your face shape and hair type.

Not all haircuts look good on everyone. Make sure you choose a haircut that flatters your face shape and hair type.

2. Experiment with different styles.

Don't be afraid to try different styles and techniques to see what works best for you.

3. Use high-quality hair products.

Invest in high-quality shampoos, conditioners, and styling products to keep your hair healthy and shiny.

4. Get regular trims.

Even if you have a short haircut, you still need to get regular trims to maintain its shape and style.

FAQ

1. How often should I wash my hair?

It depends on your hair type and condition. If you have oily hair, you may need to wash it every day. If you have dry hair, you may only need to wash it once or twice a week.

2. How do I choose a haircut that suits me?

You can consult with a hairstylist who specializes in haircuts for over 50s and ask for their advice. You can also look for inspiration online or in magazines.

3. Can I still color my hair?

Yes, you can still color your hair. However, you may want to choose a hair color that flatters your skin tone and complements your haircut.

Hair Care

As we age, our hair becomes thinner, drier, and more brittle. Therefore, it is important to take good care of your hair to keep it healthy and beautiful. Here are some tips to help you care for your hair:

1. Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner.

Choose a shampoo and conditioner that are designed for your hair type and condition. Avoid products that contain harsh chemicals or sulfates.

2. Use a deep conditioning treatment.

Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to nourish and hydrate your hair. You can use a store-bought product or make your own at home.

3. Protect your hair from heat.

Avoid using hot styling tools too often, as they can damage your hair. Use a heat protectant spray when you do use them.

4. Get regular trims.

Get your hair trimmed every 6-8 weeks to get rid of split ends and maintain your haircut's shape and style.

5. Eat a healthy diet.

Eat a balanced diet that is rich in vitamins and minerals, such as protein, iron, and biotin. These nutrients can help promote healthy hair growth and prevent hair loss.

Now that you know the best haircuts for over 50s, how to style them, and how to take care of your hair, you can feel confident and beautiful no matter your age.
