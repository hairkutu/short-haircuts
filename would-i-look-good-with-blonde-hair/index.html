Would I Look Good With Blonde Hair

Written by Aprilia May 03, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Are you considering going blonde but not sure if it will suit you? Many people have this question in their minds before making a drastic hair change. This post will provide you with valuable information to help you decide whether blonde hair will suit you or not.

Description

Blonde hair is a popular hair color that many people desire. Whether it's platinum blonde, honey blonde, or ash blonde, it's a versatile color that can suit many skin tones. However, not everyone can pull off blonde hair. It's essential to consider your skin tone, eye color, and natural hair color before making the change.

How to

The first step in determining if you would look good with blonde hair is to consult with a professional hairstylist. They can provide you with valuable advice on whether blonde hair will suit you or not.

Secondly, you can use virtual hair color apps to see how you would look with blonde hair. These apps allow you to upload your photo and try different blonde shades without making a permanent change.

Step-by-Step

Step 1: Determine Your Skin Tone

Your skin tone is one of the most crucial factors in determining whether blonde hair will suit you. If you have warm undertones, honey blonde or golden blonde shades will look best on you. If you have cool undertones, platinum blonde or ash blonde shades will suit you.

Step 2: Consider Your Eye Color

Your eye color is another factor to consider when deciding if blonde hair will suit you. If you have blue or green eyes, blonde hair will complement your eyes and make them pop. If you have brown eyes, honey blonde or caramel blonde shades will suit you.

Step 3: Try Virtual Hair Color Apps

Virtual hair color apps are an excellent way to see how you would look with blonde hair without making a permanent change. Some popular apps include ModiFace, YouCam Makeup, and Style My Hair.

Tips

If you're going blonde for the first time, it's essential to take care of your hair. Bleaching can damage your hair, so it's crucial to use hair care products that nourish and protect your hair. Also, make sure to visit your hairstylist regularly for touch-ups and trims.

FAQ

Q: Will blonde hair suit me if I have a warm skin tone?

A: Yes, honey blonde or golden blonde shades will suit you if you have a warm skin tone.

Q: Can I go blonde if I have dark hair?

A: Yes, you can go blonde if you have dark hair. However, it may take several sessions to achieve your desired blonde shade.

Q: Will platinum blonde suit me if I have a warm skin tone?

A: No, platinum blonde is better suited for cool skin tones.

Hair Care

If you decide to go blonde, it's crucial to take care of your hair. Use hair care products specifically designed for blonde hair, such as purple shampoo to prevent brassy tones. Also, use a heat protectant before using hot styling tools to prevent damage.

In conclusion, whether blonde hair will suit you or not depends on several factors such as your skin tone, eye color, and natural hair color. It's essential to consult with a professional hairstylist and use virtual hair color apps to help you make the decision. If you do decide to go blonde, make sure to take care of your hair and visit your hairstylist regularly.
