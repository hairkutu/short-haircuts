Hairstyles For Medium Length Hair Straight Tips How To And Care

If you have medium-length hair that's straight, you're in luck! There are tons of great hairstyles that you can try out to switch up your look. Whether you're looking for a low-maintenance style or something more elaborate, there's a hairstyle out there that will work for you. In this article, we'll take a look at some popular hairstyles for medium length hair straight, along with tips for how to achieve them and how to take care of your hair.

How to Style Medium Length Hair Straight

Before we get into specific hairstyles, let's talk about how to style medium length hair that's straight. Here are some steps to follow:

  1. Wash and condition your hair as usual.
  2. Blow dry your hair straight using a round brush to smooth out any kinks or bumps.
  3. Use a flat iron to straighten any areas that need it.
  4. Apply a heat protectant spray to your hair to prevent damage.
  5. Finish with a light hairspray to hold your style in place.

With these steps in mind, you're ready to try out some popular hairstyles for medium length hair straight.

Popular Hairstyles for Medium Length Hair Straight

1. The Lob

The lob (short for "long bob") is a popular hairstyle that's perfect for medium length hair. It's a simple yet stylish cut that looks great straight or wavy. To achieve the lob, have your hair cut to just above your shoulders, with some long layers added for movement. You can style it with a center or side part, and add some texture with a sea salt spray.

2. The Blunt Cut

The blunt cut is a classic hairstyle that's been around for decades. It's a simple, straight cut that's perfect for medium length hair. To achieve the blunt cut, have your hair cut to all one length, with no layers. You can style it with a center or side part, and add some shine with a smoothing serum.

3. The Half-Up, Half-Down

The half-up, half-down hairstyle is a great option if you want to keep your hair out of your face but still show off your length. To achieve this style, simply pull the top half of your hair back into a ponytail or bun, leaving the bottom half down. You can add some volume to the top half by teasing it with a comb, and finish with a light hairspray.

4. The Sleek Ponytail

The sleek ponytail is a simple yet elegant hairstyle that's perfect for any occasion. To achieve this style, simply gather your hair into a low ponytail at the nape of your neck, and secure with an elastic band. You can add some shine with a smoothing serum, and finish with a light hairspray to hold your style in place.

Tips for Maintaining Medium Length Hair Straight

Now that you've got some great hairstyles to try out, here are some tips for maintaining your medium length hair straight:

  • Wash your hair every 2-3 days to prevent it from becoming too oily or greasy.
  • Use a heat protectant spray before using any heat styling tools.
  • Invest in a good quality flat iron to ensure that your hair stays straight and smooth.
  • Get regular haircuts to prevent split ends and damage.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy and shiny.

Frequently Asked Questions

How often should I wash my medium length hair straight?

You should aim to wash your hair every 2-3 days to prevent it from becoming too oily or greasy. If you have particularly fine hair, you may need to wash it more frequently.

What's the best way to straighten medium length hair?

The best way to straighten medium length hair is to use a flat iron. Make sure to apply a heat protectant spray before using any heat styling tools, and work in small sections to ensure that your hair stays straight and smooth.

How can I add volume to my medium length hair straight?

You can add volume to your medium length hair straight by teasing it with a comb. Simply backcomb the roots of your hair to create volume, and finish with a light hairspray to hold your style in place.

Hair Care for Medium Length Hair Straight

To keep your medium length hair straight looking its best, it's important to take good care of it. Here are some tips:

  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner that's designed for straight hair.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as it can cause damage and breakage.
  • Use a wide-toothed comb to detangle your hair, as it's less damaging than a brush.
  • Invest in a good quality hair mask or deep conditioning treatment to keep your hair healthy and shiny.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and wind by wearing a hat or scarf.

With these tips and hairstyles in mind, you're ready to rock your medium length hair straight with confidence and style!
