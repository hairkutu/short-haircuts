How To Make Your Hair Look Professionally Styled

Written by Frank Apr 02, 2023 · 10 min read
How to Make Your Hair Look Professionally Styled | Tips & Tricks

Do you ever wonder how celebrities and models always seem to have perfectly styled hair? It's not just genetics or expensive hair products – there are tricks of the trade that you can use to achieve a professional look from the comfort of your own home. In this article, we'll share our top tips and step-by-step instructions for making your hair look like it was styled by a pro.

  • Bonus Tips and Tricks
  • FAQ

Understanding Your Hair Type

The first step to achieving a professional-looking hairstyle is understanding your hair type. Different hair types require different styling techniques, so it's important to know what you're working with. Here are some common hair types and how to style them:

Straight Hair:

Straight hair is typically easy to style and can be worn in a variety of ways. To add some volume and texture, try using a texturizing spray or dry shampoo. You can also add some waves or curls using a curling iron or hot rollers.

Wavy Hair:

Wavy hair can be styled to enhance its natural texture. Use a diffuser attachment on your blow dryer to enhance your waves and add some volume. You can also use a curling iron or wand to create loose waves or curls.

Curly Hair:

Curly hair can be tricky to style, but with the right products and techniques, you can achieve a gorgeous look. Use a leave-in conditioner or curl enhancer to define your curls and reduce frizz. You can also use a diffuser attachment on your blow dryer or a curling iron to enhance your curls.

Gathering the Right Tools

To achieve a professional-looking hairstyle, you'll need the right tools. Here are some must-have tools for styling your hair:

  • Blow dryer
  • Flat iron or curling iron
  • Hairbrush and/or comb
  • Hair clips or elastics
  • Texturizing spray or dry shampoo
  • Hairspray

Investing in high-quality tools can make a big difference in the look and feel of your hair. Look for tools with ceramic or tourmaline plates, as these materials distribute heat more evenly and cause less damage to your hair.

Washing and Prepping Your Hair

The key to achieving a professional-looking hairstyle is starting with clean, healthy hair. Here's how to wash and prep your hair:

  1. Wash your hair with a high-quality shampoo and conditioner. Look for products that are formulated for your hair type.
  2. Towel-dry your hair gently, being careful not to rub it too hard or damage your hair.
  3. Apply a heat protectant spray or serum to your hair to protect it from heat damage.
  4. Apply a styling product, such as mousse or texturizing spray, to add volume and texture to your hair.

Blow-Drying Your Hair

Blow-drying your hair is an important step in achieving a professional-looking hairstyle. Here's how to do it:

  1. Divide your hair into sections using clips or elastics.
  2. Starting at the roots, use a round brush to lift your hair and blow-dry it in sections.
  3. Use the cool setting on your blow dryer to set your style and add shine to your hair.

Curling Your Hair

Curling your hair can add volume and texture to your style. Here's how to curl your hair:

  1. Divide your hair into sections using clips or elastics.
  2. Starting at the bottom layer, use a curling iron or wand to curl sections of your hair.
  3. Hold the curling iron in place for a few seconds, then release the curl.
  4. Repeat the process on each section of your hair until all of your hair is curled.

Straightening Your Hair

Straightening your hair can give you a sleek, polished look. Here's how to straighten your hair:

  1. Divide your hair into sections using clips or elastics.
  2. Starting at the bottom layer, use a flat iron to straighten sections of your hair.
  3. Hold the flat iron in place for a few seconds, then release the section of hair.
  4. Repeat the process on each section of your hair until all of your hair is straightened.

Creating Updos

Updos are a great way to create a polished, professional look. Here's how to create an updo:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair.
  2. Gather your hair into a ponytail or bun, securing it with an elastic.
  3. Use bobby pins to secure any loose strands or flyaways.
  4. Spray your hair with hairspray to hold the style in place.

Finishing Touches

Finishing touches can take your hairstyle to the next level. Here are some finishing touches to try:

  • Add a hair accessory, such as a headband or barrette, to your style.
  • Use a shine serum or spray to add shine and gloss to your hair.
  • Spritz your hair with hairspray to lock in your style and reduce frizz.

Bonus Tips and Tricks

Here are some bonus tips and tricks for achieving a professional-looking hairstyle:

  • Use a high-quality hairbrush or comb to detangle your hair and reduce breakage.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as this can cause damage and breakage.
  • Try using a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy and hydrated.

FAQ

Q: How often should I wash my hair?

A: It depends on your hair type and personal preferences. Some people can go several days without washing their hair, while others prefer to wash their hair every day. Experiment to find what works best for you.

Q: How can I prevent frizz?

A: Use a smoothing serum or spray to reduce frizz. You can also try using a diffuser attachment on your blow dryer, as this can help to distribute heat more evenly and reduce frizz.

Q: What should I do if my hair is damaged?

A: Try using a deep conditioning treatment to help repair and nourish your hair. You can also try cutting back on heat styling and using gentle, sulfate-free shampoos and conditioners.

With these tips and tricks, you can achieve a professional-looking hairstyle from the comfort of your own home. Experiment with different styles and techniques to find what works best for you, and don't be afraid to try something new!
