What Shades Suit Round Faces A Guide To Flattering Sunglasses

Do you have a round face and struggle to find sunglasses that suit you? Look no further! In this post, we'll give you a guide to the best shades for your face shape.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How To Find Sunglasses For Round Faces
  • Step By Step Guide
  • Tips for Choosing the Right Sunglasses
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care Tips for Wearing Sunglasses

Description

Round faces have softer features and a circular shape, with the width and length of the face being almost equal. When choosing sunglasses, it's important to choose a style that will balance out the roundness of your face and create more definition.

How To Find Sunglasses For Round Faces

When choosing sunglasses for a round face, you want to look for styles that will elongate your face and draw attention to your cheekbones. Angular and geometric shapes will help to create contrast and add definition to your face.

Some styles to consider include:

  • Rectangular frames
  • Square frames
  • Cat-eye frames
  • Wayfarer frames

Step By Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide to help you find the perfect sunglasses for your round face:

  1. Measure your face to determine your face shape.
  2. Look for sunglasses with angular or geometric shapes.
  3. Choose frames that are wider than your face.
  4. Avoid round frames and small frames that will accentuate the roundness of your face.
  5. Try on different styles to see what looks best on you.

Tips for Choosing the Right Sunglasses

Here are some tips to keep in mind when choosing sunglasses for a round face:

  • Choose frames that are wider than your face to create balance.
  • Look for frames with angles and geometric shapes to add definition to your face.
  • Avoid round frames and small frames that will accentuate the roundness of your face.
  • Consider the color of the frames and choose a color that complements your skin tone and hair color.
  • Make sure the sunglasses fit properly and don't slide down your nose.

FAQs

What color sunglasses are best for a round face?

When choosing the color of your sunglasses, consider your skin tone and hair color. Black and tortoise are classic colors that look good on everyone, but don't be afraid to try other colors that complement your features.

Can I wear oversized sunglasses with a round face?

Yes, oversized sunglasses can look great on a round face. Just make sure the frames are wider than your face and have angles or geometric shapes to add definition.

What should I avoid when choosing sunglasses for a round face?

Avoid round frames and small frames that will accentuate the roundness of your face. Also, avoid frames that are too narrow or too wide for your face.

Hair Care Tips for Wearing Sunglasses

When wearing sunglasses, it's important to consider your hair style and how it will look with the frames. Here are some tips to keep in mind:

  • Choose frames that complement your hair color.
  • If you have long hair, consider wearing it up or back to show off the frames.
  • For short hair, choose frames that don't overwhelm your face.
  • Avoid frames that clash with your hairstyle or compete for attention.

Now that you know what shades suit round faces, it's time to go out and find the perfect sunglasses for you. Remember to choose frames that complement your face shape and style, and have fun trying on different styles until you find the perfect pair.
