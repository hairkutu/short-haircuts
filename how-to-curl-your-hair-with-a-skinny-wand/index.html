How To Curl Your Hair With A Skinny Wand A Step By Step Guide

Written by Oliver May 25, 2023 · 7 min read

Are you looking to add some curls to your hairstyle? Using a skinny wand is a great way to create tight, bouncy curls that will last all day. In this guide, we’ll walk you through the steps to create the perfect curls using a skinny wand.

Table of Contents

  2. Step-by-Step Guide to Curl Your Hair with a Skinny Wand
  3. Tips for Using a Skinny Wand to Curl Your Hair
  4. FAQs About Using a Skinny Wand to Curl Your Hair
  5. Hair Care Tips for Maintaining Your Curls

How to Use a Skinny Wand to Curl Your Hair

A skinny wand is a curling iron with a narrow barrel, usually around 1/2 inch in diameter. This makes it perfect for creating tight, bouncy curls. Here are the basics of using a skinny wand:

  • Start with clean, dry hair
  • Apply heat protectant spray to your hair
  • Divide your hair into small sections
  • Wrap each section around the wand, holding for a few seconds
  • Release the curl and let it cool
  • Repeat until you've curled your entire head of hair

Step-by-Step Guide to Curl Your Hair with a Skinny Wand

Now that you know the basics of using a skinny wand, let's dive into a step-by-step guide:

Step 1: Prepare Your Hair

Before you start curling, make sure your hair is clean and dry. Apply a heat protectant spray to your hair to protect it from the heat of the curling iron.

Step 2: Divide Your Hair

Divide your hair into small, manageable sections. You may want to use clips to keep the sections separated.

Step 3: Start Curling

Starting with the bottom section of your hair, take a small section and wrap it around the skinny wand. Hold the curl for a few seconds, then release.

Step 4: Repeat

Continue curling your hair, working your way up to the top of your head. Remember to take small sections and hold each curl for a few seconds before releasing it.

Step 5: Let Your Hair Cool

After you've curled your entire head of hair, let the curls cool for a few minutes. This will help them set and last longer.

Tips for Using a Skinny Wand to Curl Your Hair

Here are some tips to help you get the most out of your skinny wand:

  • Use a heat protectant spray to protect your hair from heat damage
  • Start with small sections of hair to create tight, bouncy curls
  • Hold each curl for a few seconds to ensure it sets
  • Let your curls cool before styling them
  • Use hairspray to help your curls last longer

FAQs About Using a Skinny Wand to Curl Your Hair

Q: What temperature should I use on my skinny wand?

A: It depends on your hair type and the thickness of your hair. For fine hair, use a lower temperature setting. For thicker hair, you may need to use a higher temperature setting.

Q: How long should I hold the curl on the skinny wand?

A: Hold each curl for a few seconds, then release. The exact time will depend on your hair type and the thickness of your hair.

Q: Can I use a skinny wand on wet hair?

A: No, you should never use a curling iron on wet hair. Make sure your hair is completely dry before using a skinny wand.

Hair Care Tips for Maintaining Your Curls

To keep your curls looking great, follow these hair care tips:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo to prevent your curls from drying out
  • Use a deep conditioner once a week to keep your hair moisturized
  • Avoid using hot tools on your hair every day
  • Use a silk or satin pillowcase to help prevent frizz
  • Avoid brushing your curls, as this can cause them to frizz

Now that you know how to curl your hair with a skinny wand, it's time to give it a try! Follow these steps and tips to create beautiful, bouncy curls that will last all day.
