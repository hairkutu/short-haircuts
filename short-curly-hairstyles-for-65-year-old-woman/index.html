Short Curly Hairstyles For 65 Year Old Woman

Written by Bardi Jul 02, 2023 Â· 8 min read

In today's world, age is just a number. Women over 65 can rock any hairstyle they want, including short curly hairstyles. Short curly hairstyles are perfect for women who want a low-maintenance hairstyle that is easy to style and manage. In this article, we will discuss some of the best short curly hairstyles for 65 year-old women.

How to Choose the Right Short Curly Hairstyle

Choosing the right short curly hairstyle can be a daunting task, especially if you have never had short hair before. Here are some tips to help you choose the right short curly hairstyle:

Consider Your Face Shape

The first thing you need to consider is your face shape. Different hairstyles suit different face shapes, so it's important to choose a style that complements your face. For example, if you have a round face, you should opt for a short curly hairstyle with volume on top to create the illusion of length.

Consider Your Hair Type

You also need to consider your hair type when choosing a short curly hairstyle. If you have fine hair, you should opt for a style with layers to add volume and texture. If you have thick hair, you should opt for a style with less volume to prevent your hair from looking too bulky.

Consider Your Lifestyle

You should also consider your lifestyle when choosing a short curly hairstyle. If you lead an active lifestyle, you should opt for a low-maintenance style that is easy to style and manage. If you have a more relaxed lifestyle, you can opt for a more elaborate style that requires more maintenance.

Step by Step Guide to Creating Short Curly Hairstyles

Here is a step-by-step guide to creating short curly hairstyles:

Step 1: Wash Your Hair

Start by washing your hair with a shampoo and conditioner that is suitable for your hair type. Use a towel to dry your hair gently, but do not rub it vigorously.

Step 2: Apply Styling Products

Apply a styling product to your damp hair, such as a mousse or curl enhancer. This will help to define your curls and add volume to your hair.

Step 3: Blow Dry Your Hair

Use a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer to blow dry your hair. This will help to enhance your curls and add volume to your hair.

Step 4: Style Your Hair

Use a curling iron or hot rollers to style your hair. This will add volume and definition to your curls. You can also use a hair straightener to create loose curls or waves.

Tips for Maintaining Short Curly Hairstyles

Here are some tips for maintaining short curly hairstyles:

Use a Wide-Tooth Comb

Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair gently. This will help to prevent breakage and damage to your hair.

Use a Deep Conditioning Treatment

Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair hydrated and moisturized. This will help to prevent frizz and breakage.

Trim Your Hair Regularly

Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends and breakage. This will help to keep your hair healthy and looking great.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the Best Short Curly Hairstyle for 65 Year-Old Women?

The best short curly hairstyle for 65 year-old women depends on their face shape, hair type, and lifestyle. Some popular options include the pixie cut, bob, and shaggy hairstyle.

Can 65 Year-Old Women Rock Short Curly Hairstyles?

Yes, 65 year-old women can rock short curly hairstyles. Short curly hairstyles are perfect for women who want a low-maintenance hairstyle that is easy to style and manage.

How Do I Style Short Curly Hair?

To style short curly hair, start by washing your hair with a shampoo and conditioner that is suitable for your hair type. Apply a styling product, such as a mousse or curl enhancer, to your damp hair. Blow dry your hair using a diffuser attachment, then style your hair using a curling iron or hot rollers.

Hair Care for Short Curly Hairstyles

Here are some hair care tips for short curly hairstyles:

Avoid Over-Washing Your Hair

Avoid over-washing your hair, as this can strip your hair of its natural oils and make it dry and brittle.

Avoid Using Heat Styling Tools Too Often

Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as this can damage your hair and make it dry and frizzy.

Protect Your Hair from the Sun

Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a hair product that contains SPF.

In conclusion, short curly hairstyles are perfect for 65 year-old women who want a low-maintenance hairstyle that is easy to style and manage. By following the tips and advice in this article, you can choose the right short curly hairstyle and maintain healthy, beautiful hair.
