How To Wear A Thick Headband With Hair Down A Step By Step Guide

Written by William Aug 16, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Are you looking for a way to add some personality to your hairstyle? A thick headband is an easy and stylish solution. Not only does it keep your hair out of your face, but it also adds a pop of color or texture to your look. In this guide, we'll show you how to wear a thick headband with hair down.

How to Wear a Thick Headband with Hair Down

Follow these simple steps to rock a thick headband with your hair down:

Step 1: Choose Your Headband

The first step is to choose the right headband for your hair type and style. A thick headband is perfect for those with medium to long hair. You can opt for a patterned, textured, or solid headband depending on your outfit and personal style.

Step 2: Style Your Hair

Before you put on your headband, style your hair as desired. You can leave it straight, wavy, or curly. Make sure your hair is dry and detangled before putting on the headband.

Step 3: Put on the Headband

Place the headband over your head and position it where you want it to sit. You can wear it closer to your hairline for a more retro look, or further back for a modern touch.

Step 4: Adjust Your Hair

After you've put on the headband, adjust your hair around it. You can pull some strands forward for a messy look, or tuck them behind your ears for a sleeker style.

Step 5: Finish with Hairspray

Once you're happy with your hair and headband placement, finish with a light mist of hairspray. This will keep your hair in place and prevent any flyaways.

Tips for Wearing a Thick Headband with Hair Down

Here are some additional tips to help you rock a thick headband with your hair down:

  • Choose a headband that complements your outfit and personal style.
  • Experiment with different headband placements to find your favorite look.
  • Use bobby pins to secure any loose strands or the headband itself.
  • Avoid wearing a headband that is too tight, as it can cause headaches.
  • Have fun and embrace your unique style!

FAQs

Can I wear a thick headband with short hair?

While a thick headband is typically best for medium to long hair, you can still wear one with short hair. Opt for a thinner headband that won't overpower your hair length.

How do I choose the right headband for my hair type?

Consider the thickness and texture of your hair when choosing a headband. Thicker hair may require a wider headband, while finer hair may benefit from a narrower one.

Can I wear a thick headband to work or formal events?

Yes! A thick headband can add a touch of personality to any outfit, including work or formal attire. Just make sure to choose a headband that complements your outfit and isn't too flashy.

Hair Care

When wearing a headband, it's important to take care of your hair to prevent damage or breakage. Here are some tips to keep your hair healthy and strong:

  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair hydrated.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Use a heat protectant spray if you do use heat styling tools.
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends.
  • Use a hair mask once a week to nourish and strengthen your hair.

With these tips, you can wear a thick headband with your hair down and keep your hair healthy and strong. Have fun experimenting with different headband styles and find the perfect one for your unique look.
