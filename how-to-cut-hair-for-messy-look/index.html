How To Cut Hair For A Messy Look

Written by Christine Aug 14, 2023 Â· 7 min read

If you're looking for a hairstyle that's easy to maintain and gives off a casual, carefree vibe, a messy look might be the perfect choice for you! But how do you achieve that perfectly imperfect hairstyle? In this blog post, we'll go over how to cut hair for a messy look, step by step. We'll also provide some tips and tricks to help you maintain your hairstyle and answer some frequently asked questions. Let's get started!

Table of Contents

How to Cut Hair for a Messy Look

The first step to achieving a messy hairstyle is to start with the right cut. A layered cut is ideal for creating a tousled, textured look. You'll want to avoid blunt cuts or one-length styles, as these won't allow for much movement or volume.

When working with your stylist or barber, be sure to communicate that you want a messy style. This will help them understand how to cut your hair to achieve the look you're going for. You can also bring in pictures of hairstyles you like to give them a better idea of what you're looking for.

Step by Step

Here's a step-by-step guide to cutting hair for a messy look:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair.
  2. Divide your hair into sections, starting at the back of your head.
  3. Using a pair of scissors, cut each section to the desired length. Make sure to cut at an angle to create layers.
  4. Once you've cut all sections, use thinning shears to add texture and remove bulk.
  5. If desired, use a razor to create even more texture.
  6. Style your hair using your fingers, rather than a brush or comb, to create a messy, tousled look.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you achieve the perfect messy hairstyle:

  • Don't overdo it with product. A little bit of pomade or texturizing spray can help enhance your hairstyle, but too much can weigh it down and make it look greasy.
  • If you have straight hair, consider using a curling iron or wand to add some waves or curls. This can help create more texture and movement in your hairstyle.
  • Keep up with regular trims to maintain your messy style. As your hair grows, it can start to look unkempt rather than intentionally messy.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about cutting hair for a messy look:

How long does hair need to be to achieve a messy style?

There's no specific length that works best for a messy hairstyle. However, if your hair is too short, it may not have enough length to create the desired texture and movement.

Can I achieve a messy look without cutting my hair?

Yes! You can create a messy hairstyle with the right styling products and techniques. However, if you want a truly effortless, low-maintenance look, cutting your hair in layers can help.

Is a messy hairstyle appropriate for all occasions?

A messy hairstyle can be a great choice for casual occasions, but may not be appropriate for more formal events. Use your best judgment and consider the dress code before opting for a messy style.

Hair Care

To maintain your messy hairstyle, it's important to take good care of your hair. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your hair every 2-3 days, using a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using hot tools like flat irons or curling irons too frequently, as these can damage your hair.
  • Use a leave-in conditioner or hair oil to keep your hair hydrated and healthy.
  • Consider using a dry shampoo between washes to keep your hair looking fresh and voluminous.

With these tips and techniques, you'll be able to achieve a perfectly undone, effortlessly cool hairstyle that's perfect for any occasion. Have fun experimenting with different cuts, styles, and products to find the perfect messy look for you!
