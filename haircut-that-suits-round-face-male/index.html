The Best Haircut For Round Face Males

Written by Christine Jun 07, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Are you a male with a round face who's struggling to find the perfect haircut? Look no further! In this blog post, we will discuss the best haircut that suits round face males, as well as some tips and tricks to keep your hair looking fresh and stylish.

Description

Round faces are characterized by a soft jawline and cheekbones that are wider than the forehead. This shape can make it challenging to find a hairstyle that complements your features. The goal is to create the illusion of a more angular face shape by adding volume and height to the top of the head while keeping the sides short.

How to

The best haircut for round face males is the textured crop. This cut is short on the sides and back, while the top is left longer and styled with texture and volume. Here's how to achieve the look:

Step 1: Consult with Your Barber

Before getting a haircut, it's essential to consult with your barber. They can recommend the best style for your face shape and hair type. Bring some photos of the textured crop to show your barber what you're looking for.

Step 2: Choose the Right Length

The sides and back of the hair should be cut short, while the top should be left longer. The length of the top will depend on your hair type and personal preferences. Ask your barber to leave enough length to create texture and volume.

Step 3: Add Texture

To achieve the textured crop, the hair on top needs to have texture. This can be done with a texturizing spray, sea salt spray, or pomade. Apply the product to damp hair and style with your fingers to create a messy, textured look.

Tips

Here are some additional tips to keep in mind when getting a haircut that suits round face males:

  • Avoid hairstyles that add volume to the sides of the head, such as a pompadour or slicked-back style.
  • Avoid hairstyles that are too short on the sides, as they can make the face look rounder.
  • Consider getting a beard or stubble to add definition to the jawline.
  • Use a matte hair product to avoid adding shine to the hair, which can make the face look rounder.

FAQ

  • Q: Can I still get a textured crop if I have curly hair?
  • A: Yes! The textured crop can work with curly hair. However, it's essential to work with a barber who has experience cutting curly hair.
  • Q: How often should I get my hair cut?
  • A: It depends on how fast your hair grows. Typically, getting a haircut every 4-6 weeks is recommended to maintain the shape of the cut.

Hair Care

Maintaining healthy hair is essential for any haircut to look its best. Here are some tips for keeping your hair healthy:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo to avoid stripping the hair of its natural oils.
  • Use a conditioner to keep the hair moisturized.
  • Avoid using hot tools on the hair, such as straighteners or curling irons, as they can damage the hair.
  • Eat a balanced diet and drink plenty of water to promote healthy hair growth.

With the textured crop, round face males can achieve a stylish and modern haircut that complements their features. Remember to consult with your barber, choose the right length, add texture, and maintain healthy hair to keep your cut looking its best.
