Best Hair Length For Diamond Face Shape

Written by Daniel Jun 23, 2023 · 8 min read

Are you confused about which hair length suits your diamond face shape? Don't worry, we've got you covered! In this article, we will discuss the best hair length for diamond face shape, along with some tips and tricks to help you style your hair. So, let's get started!

Description

Diamond face shape is characterized by high cheekbones and a narrow jawline. It is one of the most versatile face shapes, as it can pull off a variety of hairstyles. However, choosing the right hair length can make a huge difference in enhancing your facial features.

How To

The first step in determining the best hair length for diamond face shape is to identify the widest part of your face. This is usually the cheekbones. Once you have determined the widest part of your face, you can choose a hair length that complements it.

Short Hair

If you have a diamond face shape, short hair can be a great option for you. A pixie cut or a bob that hits just above your shoulders can accentuate your cheekbones and make your face look more symmetrical. Short haircuts also draw attention to your eyes and lips, making them look more prominent.

Medium Hair

Medium-length hair is another great option for diamond face shape. A shoulder-length cut with layers can soften your strong jawline and balance out your facial proportions. You can also try a lob (long bob) with side-swept bangs to add more volume to your hair and create an illusion of a wider forehead.

Long Hair

If you prefer long hair, you can still rock it with a diamond face shape. However, it is essential to keep it layered and voluminous. A layered haircut that hits just below your shoulders can make your face look more elongated and draw attention away from your narrow jawline. You can also try loose waves or curls to add more dimension to your hair.

Step By Step

Here are some steps you can follow to choose the best hair length for diamond face shape:

  1. Identify the widest part of your face (usually the cheekbones).
  2. Choose a hair length that complements your facial features.
  3. Consider adding layers to your haircut to balance out your facial proportions.
  4. Experiment with different hairstyles to find the one that suits you the best.

Tips

Here are some additional tips to help you style your hair with diamond face shape:

  • Avoid hairstyles that add volume to the top of your head, as they can make your face look wider.
  • Opt for side-swept bangs instead of straight across bangs to soften your forehead.
  • Try a messy bun or a high ponytail to elongate your face.
  • Use hair accessories such as headbands or hair clips to add more dimension to your hair.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about the best hair length for diamond face shape:

Q. Can diamond face shape pull off a short haircut?

A. Yes, diamond face shape can pull off a short haircut, such as a pixie cut or a bob.

Q. What is the best way to style long hair with diamond face shape?

A. The best way to style long hair with diamond face shape is to keep it layered and voluminous. You can also try loose waves or curls to add more dimension to your hair.

Q. Should I avoid bangs with diamond face shape?

A. No, you can still try bangs with diamond face shape. However, side-swept bangs are more suitable than straight across bangs.

Hair Care

Finally, it is essential to take care of your hair to maintain its health and shine. Here are some tips for hair care:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a shampoo and conditioner suitable for your hair type.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Trim your hair regularly to get rid of split ends.
  • Eat a balanced diet rich in vitamins and minerals to promote hair growth and strength.

So, there you have it – the best hair length for diamond face shape and some tips and tricks to help you style your hair. Remember to experiment with different hairstyles to find the one that suits you the best. With the right hair length and style, you can enhance your facial features and feel confident in your own skin.
