Short Haircuts For Round Face Wavy Hair A Complete Guide

If you have a round face with wavy hair, you might think that short haircuts are not for you. However, that's not the case. There are plenty of short haircuts that can flatter your round face and enhance your beautiful waves. In this article, we'll show you how to choose the best short haircut for your round face and wavy hair. We'll also give you some tips on how to take care of your hair after the cut. Let's get started!

How to Choose the Right Short Haircut

Choosing the right short haircut for your round face and wavy hair can be a daunting task. Here are some tips to help you make the right choice:

Consider your face shape

When choosing a short haircut, it's important to consider your face shape. If you have a round face, you should opt for hairstyles that add height and length to your face. Avoid hairstyles that add width to your face, as this will only make your face look rounder.

Consider your hair texture

Your hair texture also plays a role in choosing the right short haircut. If you have wavy hair, you should opt for haircuts that enhance your waves. Avoid haircuts that require a lot of styling, as this can damage your hair and make it look frizzy.

Consult a hairstylist

If you're not sure which short haircut is right for you, consult a hairstylist. A professional hairstylist can help you choose a haircut that flatters your face shape and enhances your wavy hair.

Best Short Haircuts for Round Face Wavy Hair

Now that you know how to choose the right short haircut, here are some of the best short haircuts for round face wavy hair:

Pixie Cut

The pixie cut is a classic short haircut that works well for round face wavy hair. The short layers in the back add height to your face, while the longer layers in the front frame your face and enhance your waves. This haircut is low-maintenance and perfect for those who don't have a lot of time to style their hair.

Bob Cut

The bob cut is another great short haircut for round face wavy hair. The length of the bob can vary, but it should always be longer in the front than in the back. This haircut adds height to your face and enhances your waves. It's also versatile and can be styled in different ways.

Layered Cut

The layered cut is a great option for those who want to add more volume to their hair. This haircut adds height to your face and enhances your waves. It's also versatile and can be styled in different ways. You can add more layers to make your hair look fuller.

Step-by-Step Guide to Getting a Short Haircut for Round Face Wavy Hair

Here's a step-by-step guide to getting a short haircut for round face wavy hair:

Step 1: Choose a haircut

Choose a short haircut that flatters your face shape and enhances your waves. You can choose from the pixie cut, bob cut, or layered cut.

Step 2: Find a hairstylist

Find a hairstylist who specializes in short haircuts for round face wavy hair. Make sure to check their portfolio before booking an appointment.

Step 3: Consult with your hairstylist

Consult with your hairstylist and show them pictures of the haircut you want. They can give you advice on whether the haircut will suit your face shape and hair texture.

Step 4: Get the haircut

Get the haircut and enjoy your new look!

Tips for Styling Short Haircuts for Round Face Wavy Hair

Here are some tips for styling short haircuts for round face wavy hair:

Use a diffuser

Use a diffuser when blow-drying your hair to enhance your waves and avoid frizz.

Use a curling iron

Use a curling iron to enhance your waves and add volume to your hair.

Use a texturizing product

Use a texturizing product to add more texture and volume to your hair.

FAQ

Can I get bangs with a short haircut for round face wavy hair?

Yes, you can get bangs with a short haircut for round face wavy hair. However, make sure to choose the right type of bangs that flatter your face shape.

Do I need to use heat protectant when styling my hair?

Yes, it's important to use heat protectant when styling your hair to avoid damage and breakage.

How often should I get a haircut?

You should get a haircut every six to eight weeks to maintain the shape of your haircut and keep your hair healthy.

Hair Care for Round Face Wavy Hair

Here are some tips for taking care of your round face wavy hair:

Use a sulfate-free shampoo

Use a sulfate-free shampoo to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils.

Condition your hair regularly

Condition your hair regularly to keep it moisturized and healthy.

Avoid heat styling

Avoid heat styling your hair too often to avoid damage and breakage.

Now that you know everything about short haircuts for round face wavy hair, it's time to book an appointment with your hairstylist and try out a new look. Remember to take care of your hair and style it in a way that enhances your natural waves. Happy hairstyling!
