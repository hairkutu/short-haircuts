How To Get The Back Of Your Hair To Lay Flat Tips And Tricks

Written by Daniel Jul 30, 2023 Â· 8 min read

If you're like many people, you may struggle with getting the back of your hair to lay flat. Whether you have curly, wavy, or straight hair, it can be frustrating to deal with hair that sticks out or won't stay in place. Fortunately, there are several techniques you can use to tame the back of your hair and achieve a smooth, polished look. In this article, we'll share some tips and tricks for getting the back of your hair to lay flat.

How to Get the Back of Your Hair to Lay Flat: Step by Step

Before we get into the specifics, let's go over the basic steps for getting the back of your hair to lay flat:

  1. Wash and condition your hair
  2. Towel-dry your hair
  3. Apply a styling product
  4. Blow-dry your hair
  5. Style your hair as desired

Now let's take a closer look at each step.

Step 1: Wash and Condition Your Hair

The first step in getting the back of your hair to lay flat is to start with clean, moisturized hair. Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner that are appropriate for your hair type. If you have curly or wavy hair, you may want to consider using a conditioner with extra moisture to help smooth your hair.

Step 2: Towel-Dry Your Hair

Once you've washed and conditioned your hair, gently squeeze out any excess water with a towel. Avoid rubbing your hair vigorously, as this can create frizz and damage your hair.

Step 3: Apply a Styling Product

Next, apply a styling product to your hair. Choose a product that is appropriate for your hair type and desired style. For example, if you have straight hair and want a sleek look, you may want to use a smoothing serum or cream. If you have curly hair and want to enhance your curls, you may want to use a curl-defining product.

Step 4: Blow-Dry Your Hair

Using a blow dryer, dry your hair in sections. Start at the back of your head and work your way forward. Use a round brush to help smooth your hair as you dry it. When you get to the back of your head, use the brush to pull your hair straight down and create tension. This will help prevent your hair from sticking out or flipping up.

Step 5: Style Your Hair as Desired

Once your hair is dry, you can style it as desired. Use a flat iron or curling iron to create the look you want. If you're going for a sleek, straight look, be sure to use a flat iron to smooth the back of your hair.

Tips for Getting the Back of Your Hair to Lay Flat

Here are some additional tips to help you get the back of your hair to lay flat:

  • Use a high-quality blow dryer with a concentrator nozzle to help direct the airflow and create a smoother finish.
  • Consider using a paddle brush instead of a round brush to help smooth your hair.
  • Avoid using too much product, as this can weigh down your hair and make it look greasy.
  • If you have very thick or curly hair, you may want to consider using a straightening treatment or getting a professional blowout to help achieve a smooth finish.

FAQs About Getting the Back of Your Hair to Lay Flat

Q: Why does the back of my hair always stick out?

A: There are several reasons why the back of your hair may stick out, including the natural shape of your hair, the way you sleep, and the way you style your hair. By following the steps outlined in this article, you can help tame the back of your hair and achieve a smoother finish.

Q: Can I use a hair straightener to get the back of my hair to lay flat?

A: Yes, a hair straightener can be an effective tool for smoothing the back of your hair. Just be sure to use a heat protectant spray to prevent damage to your hair.

Hair Care Tips for Healthy, Smooth Hair

In addition to the techniques outlined in this article, there are several things you can do to keep your hair healthy and smooth:

  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner that are appropriate for your hair type.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too frequently, as this can damage your hair.
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends and promote healthy hair growth.
  • Eat a balanced diet that includes plenty of vitamins and nutrients that are good for your hair, such as protein, iron, and omega-3 fatty acids.

By following these tips and tricks, you can get the back of your hair to lay flat and achieve a smooth, polished look. Whether you have straight, curly, or wavy hair, these techniques can help you tame the back of your hair and create a style that you love.
