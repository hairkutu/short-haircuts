How To Get Rid Of Excessive Hair Fall A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Robby Aug 23, 2023 Â· 9 min read

If you're struggling with excessive hair fall, you're not alone. Hair fall is a common problem that affects millions of people around the world. It can be caused by a variety of factors, including stress, genetics, and hormonal imbalances. Fortunately, there are many ways to combat hair fall and promote healthy hair growth. In this article, we'll explore some of the most effective methods for getting rid of excessive hair fall.

How to Get Rid of Excessive Hair Fall

There are many ways to tackle hair fall, but the most effective methods involve treating the root cause of the problem. Here are some of the most effective ways to get rid of excessive hair fall:

1. Address Nutritional Deficiencies

One of the most common causes of hair fall is a lack of essential nutrients. Iron, zinc, biotin, and vitamin D are all crucial for healthy hair growth. If you're not getting enough of these nutrients in your diet, consider taking supplements or eating more nutrient-dense foods like leafy greens, nuts, and eggs.

2. Manage Stress

Stress is a major contributor to hair fall. When you're stressed, your body produces hormones that can disrupt the hair growth cycle. To manage stress, try relaxation techniques like meditation, yoga, or deep breathing exercises. You can also try getting more exercise, spending time outdoors, or taking breaks throughout the day to do something you enjoy.

3. Use Gentle Hair Products

Harsh chemicals in hair products can damage your hair and lead to hair fall. Look for gentle, natural products that are free from sulfates, parabens, and other harmful ingredients. You should also avoid using heat styling tools too often, as they can weaken your hair and cause breakage.

4. Avoid Tight Hairstyles

Tight hairstyles like braids and ponytails can put tension on your hair and lead to hair fall. If you must wear your hair up, try using a looser style or using hair accessories that don't put too much pressure on your hair.

5. Consult a Doctor

If you've tried these methods and still can't get rid of excessive hair fall, it may be time to consult a doctor. They can help you identify any underlying medical conditions that may be causing your hair fall and recommend appropriate treatment.

Step-by-Step Guide

Follow these steps to get rid of excessive hair fall:

Step 1: Identify the Cause

Before you can effectively treat hair fall, you need to identify the underlying cause. Consider factors like your diet, stress levels, and hair care routine.

Step 2: Address Nutritional Deficiencies

If your hair fall is caused by a lack of essential nutrients, consider taking supplements or adjusting your diet to include more nutrient-dense foods.

Step 3: Manage Stress

Use relaxation techniques like meditation or yoga to manage stress and prevent hair fall.

Step 4: Use Gentle Hair Products

Switch to gentle, natural hair products that are free from harsh chemicals.

Step 5: Avoid Tight Hairstyles

Avoid tight hairstyles that put tension on your hair and can lead to hair fall.

Step 6: Consult a Doctor

If your hair fall persists despite these measures, consult a doctor to identify any underlying medical conditions.

Tips for Preventing Hair Fall

Here are some additional tips for preventing hair fall:

  • Avoid using hot water to wash your hair, as it can damage your hair and cause hair fall.
  • Massage your scalp regularly to increase blood flow and promote healthy hair growth.
  • Use a wide-toothed comb instead of a brush to avoid pulling on your hair.
  • Avoid using hair styling products that contain alcohol, as it can dry out your hair and lead to breakage.
  • Protect your hair from sun damage by wearing a hat or using a leave-in conditioner with SPF.

FAQs About Hair Fall

Q: How much hair fall is normal?

A: It's normal to lose 50-100 hairs per day, but if you're losing more than that, it may be a sign of excessive hair fall.

Q: Can hair fall be reversed?

A: Yes, in many cases hair fall can be reversed through lifestyle changes and proper hair care.

Q: How long does it take to see results from hair fall treatment?

A: It can take several months to see results from hair fall treatment, so be patient and consistent with your efforts.

Q: Can hair fall be caused by hormonal imbalances?

A: Yes, hormonal imbalances can contribute to hair fall. Consult a doctor if you suspect this may be the cause.

Hair Care Routine for Healthy Hair

Follow these tips for a healthy hair care routine:

  • Wash your hair every 2-3 days with a gentle, sulfate-free shampoo.
  • Use a conditioner after shampooing to keep your hair moisturized.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often.
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends and breakage.
  • Eat a healthy diet rich in essential nutrients for healthy hair growth.

By following these tips and methods, you can get rid of excessive hair fall and promote healthy hair growth. Remember, consistency is key, so be patient and persistent in your efforts to achieve healthy hair.
