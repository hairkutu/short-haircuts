How To Use Hair Clippers For Beginners A Step By Step Guide

Written by Daniel Jul 27, 2023 · 7 min read

Are you tired of spending money on haircuts? Do you want to learn how to use hair clippers for yourself or your loved ones? If so, you’ve come to the right place! In this guide, we will walk you through the process of using hair clippers for beginners. We’ll cover everything from choosing the right clippers to cleaning them after use. Let’s get started!

How to Choose the Right Clippers

Before you start using hair clippers, it’s important to choose the right ones for your needs. Here are a few things to consider:

The Type of Clippers

There are two main types of hair clippers: corded and cordless. Corded clippers are more powerful and don’t require charging, but they can be less convenient to use. Cordless clippers are portable and easy to use, but they may require frequent charging. Decide which type of clipper would work best for you.

The Blade Size

The blade size determines how short or long your hair will be cut. Clippers usually come with different blade sizes, so choose the one that fits your desired hair length.

The Brand

Choose a reputable brand that produces quality clippers. Some top brands of hair clippers include Wahl, Andis, and Oster.

How to Use Hair Clippers for Beginners

Now that you’ve chosen the right clippers, it’s time to learn how to use them. Follow these steps:

Step 1: Prepare Your Hair

Before you start cutting your hair, make sure it’s clean and dry. Comb your hair to remove any tangles or knots.

Step 2: Choose the Right Blade Size

Attach the blade size that you want to use. If you’re not sure which blade size to use, start with a longer one and work your way down to a shorter one.

Step 3: Turn on the Clippers

Plug in the corded clippers or charge the cordless ones. Make sure the blades are lubricated before turning them on.

Step 4: Start Cutting

Hold the clippers at a 45-degree angle and start cutting your hair from the bottom up. Use smooth, even strokes and go against the direction of hair growth.

Step 5: Clean Up

Once you’re done, use a brush or cloth to remove any hair clippings. Clean the blades with a special clipper oil or spray.

Tips for Using Hair Clippers

Here are a few tips to help you use hair clippers more effectively:

  • Start with a longer blade size and work your way down to a shorter one
  • Don’t press too hard on the clippers, as this can cause skin irritation or cuts
  • Use a mirror to check the back of your head or ask someone to help you
  • Use a comb to guide the clippers and create a more even cut

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about using hair clippers:

Can I use hair clippers on wet hair?

No, it’s not recommended to use hair clippers on wet hair, as this can damage the blades and cause uneven cuts. Make sure your hair is dry before using clippers.

How often should I clean my hair clippers?

You should clean your hair clippers after each use to prevent bacteria buildup and ensure they last longer.

Can I use hair clippers for facial hair?

Yes, you can use hair clippers for facial hair, but make sure to use a smaller blade size and be careful around sensitive areas like the lips and nose.

Hair Care

After using hair clippers, it’s important to take care of your hair. Here are a few tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly to remove any hair clippings or debris
  • Condition your hair to keep it soft and healthy
  • Use a moisturizer to prevent dryness or irritation

With these tips and tricks, you’re ready to start using hair clippers like a pro! Happy cutting!
