How To Get Dreads With Soft Curly Hair A Step By Step Guide

If you have soft curly hair and you want to get dreads, you may be wondering how to achieve the look without damaging your hair. The good news is that it is possible to get dreads with soft curly hair, and it doesn't have to be difficult. In this guide, we'll take you through the steps to achieve the look you want, as well as some tips and tricks to make the process easier.

How to Get Dreads with Soft Curly Hair

Dreads, or locs, are a popular hairstyle that involves twisting or braiding the hair to create long, rope-like strands. If you have soft curly hair, there are a few things you should keep in mind when trying to achieve this look:

  • Your hair may take longer to lock than someone with straight hair
  • You may need to use different methods to create your dreads
  • You may need to be more gentle with your hair to prevent damage

That being said, it is still possible to get dreads with soft curly hair. Keep reading to learn how.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide to getting dreads with soft curly hair:

1. Decide on your method

There are a few different methods you can use to create dreads, including backcombing, twisting, and braiding. Do some research and decide which method will work best for your hair type.

2. Wash your hair

Before you start, make sure your hair is clean and free of any product buildup. Use a clarifying shampoo to remove any residue from your hair.

3. Section your hair

Divide your hair into small sections using clips or hair ties. The smaller the sections, the tighter your dreads will be.

4. Begin your method

Start your method of choice on each section of hair. For example, if you are using the backcombing method, backcomb each section of hair until it starts to lock.

5. Maintain your dreads

Once your dreads have started to lock, you will need to maintain them to keep them looking neat. This involves palm rolling, separating any sections that have merged together, and using a crochet hook to tighten up any loose hairs.

Tips and Tricks

Here are some tips and tricks to make the process of getting dreads with soft curly hair easier:

  • Use a leave-in conditioner to help your hair lock
  • Be patient - it may take longer for your hair to lock than someone with straight hair
  • Don't over-wash your hair - this can cause your dreads to unravel
  • Avoid using heavy products or oils that can weigh down your hair

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about getting dreads with soft curly hair:

Can I get dreads with short hair?

Yes, you can get dreads with short hair. However, you may need to use a different method than someone with longer hair.

How long does it take for dreads to form?

It can take anywhere from a few weeks to several months for dreads to form, depending on your hair type and the method you use.

Can I wash my hair with dreads?

Yes, you can wash your hair with dreads. However, you should avoid over-washing and use a residue-free shampoo to avoid buildup.

Hair Care for Dreads

Once you have your dreads, it's important to take care of them properly to keep them looking their best. Here are some tips:

  • Use a residue-free shampoo to avoid buildup
  • Avoid using heavy products that can weigh down your hair
  • Keep your scalp moisturized with a light oil or moisturizer
  • Get regular maintenance to keep your dreads looking neat

With these tips and tricks, you can get dreads with soft curly hair and keep them looking great.
