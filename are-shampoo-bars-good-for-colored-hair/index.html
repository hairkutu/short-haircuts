Are Shampoo Bars Good For Colored Hair

Written by Aprilia Jun 18, 2023 · 8 min read

Shampoo bars have become increasingly popular in recent years as a more eco-friendly and sustainable alternative to traditional liquid shampoos. But what about for those with colored hair? Are shampoo bars good for colored hair? The answer is not so simple, but in this blog post, we’ll explore the pros and cons of shampoo bars for colored hair and provide some tips for using them effectively.

Table of Contents

  • Pros of Shampoo Bars for Colored Hair
  • Cons of Shampoo Bars for Colored Hair
  • Tip #1: Choose the Right Shampoo Bar
  • Tip #2: Use a Conditioner
  • Tip #3: Use Lukewarm Water
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care for Colored Hair

Pros of Shampoo Bars for Colored Hair

One of the biggest advantages of shampoo bars is that they are typically made with natural ingredients and are free from harsh chemicals that can strip hair of its color. This means that shampoo bars can be gentler on colored hair and help to preserve the vibrancy and longevity of your color.

Additionally, shampoo bars are often more concentrated than liquid shampoos, meaning you can use less product and make it last longer. This can be especially beneficial for those with colored hair, as over-washing can lead to fading and dullness.

Cons of Shampoo Bars for Colored Hair

While shampoo bars can be good for colored hair, there are some potential downsides to consider. One of the biggest concerns is that some shampoo bars can be too harsh for colored hair and strip away the color. This is why it’s important to choose a shampoo bar that is specifically formulated for colored hair.

Another potential issue is that shampoo bars can be more difficult to rinse out than traditional liquid shampoos. This can lead to product buildup and dullness over time. To avoid this, make sure to thoroughly rinse your hair after using a shampoo bar and consider using a clarifying shampoo occasionally to remove any buildup.

Tip #1: Choose the Right Shampoo Bar

When it comes to using shampoo bars on colored hair, it’s important to choose a product that is specifically formulated for colored hair. Look for a shampoo bar that is gentle, moisturizing, and free from harsh chemicals like sulfates and parabens.

Some good options include the Ethique Heali Kiwi Shampoo Bar, the Lush Honey I Washed My Hair Shampoo Bar, and the HiBAR Maintain Shampoo Bar. These products are all designed to be gentle on colored hair and help to preserve color.

Tip #2: Use a Conditioner

One potential downside of shampoo bars is that they can be drying on hair. To combat this, it’s important to use a conditioner after shampooing to help hydrate and nourish your hair. Look for a conditioner that is also formulated for colored hair and free from harsh chemicals.

Some good options include the Ethique Guardian Conditioner Bar, the Lush American Cream Conditioner Bar, and the HiBAR Maintain Conditioner Bar. These products are all designed to work well with shampoo bars and help to keep colored hair looking healthy and hydrated.

Tip #3: Use Lukewarm Water

Using hot water on colored hair can cause the color to fade more quickly. To help preserve your color, it’s important to use lukewarm water when washing and rinsing your hair. This will help to minimize damage and keep your color looking vibrant for longer.

FAQs

Can I use any shampoo bar on colored hair?

No, it’s important to choose a shampoo bar that is specifically formulated for colored hair. This will help to ensure that the product is gentle and won’t strip away your color.

Will using a shampoo bar make my hair more dry?

Some shampoo bars can be drying on hair, which is why it’s important to use a conditioner after shampooing. Look for a conditioner that is formulated for colored hair and free from harsh chemicals.

How often should I wash my colored hair with a shampoo bar?

It’s generally recommended to wash colored hair no more than 2-3 times per week. Over-washing can lead to fading and dullness, so using a shampoo bar can be a good way to extend the time between washes.

Hair Care for Colored Hair

Using a shampoo bar can be a great way to care for colored hair, but it’s important to also take other steps to keep your hair healthy and vibrant. This includes using a heat protectant when styling with hot tools, avoiding over-washing, and using a deep conditioning treatment regularly to keep your hair hydrated and nourished.

By following these tips and using a high-quality shampoo bar designed for colored hair, you can help to extend the life of your color and keep your hair looking healthy and vibrant.
