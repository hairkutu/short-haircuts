Hairstyle That Suits Round Chubby Face

Written by Christine Aug 22, 2023 Â· 8 min read

Are you tired of trying different hairstyles that don't complement your round chubby face? Don't worry; we've got you covered. In this article, we will discuss the best hairstyles that suit round chubby faces. From short to long, curly to straight, we have all the options that you can choose from to enhance your look. So, let's dive in.

Description

A round chubby face has a circular shape and fuller cheeks. The goal of a hairstyle for this face shape is to create the illusion of length and slim down the face. The right hairstyle can make a significant difference in how your face looks. A good haircut can help accentuate your best features and balance out your face shape.

How to Choose the Right Hairstyle?

Choosing the right hairstyle for a round chubby face can be a tricky task. However, some factors can help you determine which hairstyle will suit you best. Here are some tips to keep in mind when choosing a hairstyle:

  • Consider the length of your hair. Short hair can make your face look rounder, while long hair can make it appear slimmer.
  • Look for styles that create height and volume at the crown of your head.
  • Avoid hairstyles that add width to the sides of your face.
  • Choose a style that complements your facial features.

Step by Step Guide for Hairstyles

Short Hairstyles

Short hairstyles are perfect for round chubby faces as they can make your face appear slimmer. Here are some short hairstyle options:

  • Pixie cut: This is a short and layered cut that adds volume to the crown of the head, making your face appear slimmer.
  • Bob cut: A bob cut that ends just above the chin can create the illusion of length and slim down your face.
  • Asymmetrical cut: An asymmetrical cut that is longer on one side can create a slimming effect.

Medium Hairstyles

Medium hairstyles can also work well for round chubby faces. Here are some options:

  • Layered cut: A layered cut can add volume and texture to your hair, creating a slimming effect.
  • Shoulder-length cut: A shoulder-length cut can elongate your face and make it appear slimmer.
  • Side-swept bangs: Side-swept bangs can create a diagonal line across your face, making it appear longer and slimmer.

Long Hairstyles

Long hairstyles can be tricky for round chubby faces, but there are still some options available:

  • Layered cut: A layered cut can add volume and texture to your hair, creating a slimming effect.
  • Long bob: A long bob that ends just below the chin can create the illusion of length and slim down your face.
  • Soft curls: Soft curls that frame your face can create a slimming effect.

Tips for Maintaining Your Hairstyle

Once you have found the perfect hairstyle for your round chubby face, it's essential to maintain it to keep it looking its best. Here are some tips:

  • Get regular trims to keep your hair looking healthy and prevent split ends.
  • Use a good quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair hydrated and shiny.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often as they can damage your hair.
  • Use a heat protectant spray when using heat styling tools to protect your hair from damage.
  • Avoid tight hairstyles that can pull on your hair and cause breakage.

FAQs

What Hairstyles Should I Avoid If I Have a Round Chubby Face?

Avoid hairstyles that add width to the sides of your face, such as blunt bangs, one-length cuts, and blunt bobs. These hairstyles can make your face appear rounder and wider.

Can I Wear a Middle Parting If I Have a Round Chubby Face?

A middle parting can make your face appear wider, so it's best to avoid it if you have a round chubby face. Instead, try a side parting or a diagonal parting.

Will a Pixie Cut Suit Me If I Have a Round Chubby Face?

Yes, a pixie cut can be a great option for a round chubby face as it can make your face appear slimmer. However, make sure to choose a layered cut that adds volume to the crown of your head.

Conclusion

Choosing the right hairstyle for a round chubby face can be challenging, but with these tips and options, you can find the perfect one that complements your facial features and enhances your look. Remember to maintain your hairstyle to keep it looking its best, and avoid hairstyles that add width to your face. With the right hairstyle, you can feel confident and beautiful every day.
