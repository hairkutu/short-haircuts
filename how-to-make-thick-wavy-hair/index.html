How To Make Thick Wavy Hair A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Oliver Aug 21, 2023 · 7 min read
Are you tired of your straight, limp hair? Do you want to add more texture and volume to your hair? Look no further because we have the perfect solution for you - thick wavy hair! With just a few simple steps, you can achieve the voluminous, beachy waves that you’ve always wanted. In this article, we’ll show you how to make thick wavy hair in easy-to-understand language.

Table of Contents:

  • Description
  • How To
  • Step-by-step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

Thick wavy hair is a hairstyle that is characterized by loose curls or waves and lots of volume. This hairstyle is perfect for those who want to add more texture and movement to their hair. It’s a low-maintenance style that can be achieved with just a few products and tools.

How To

To make thick wavy hair, you’ll need the following products and tools:
  • Shampoo and conditioner
  • Heat protectant spray
  • Curling iron or wand
  • Hairspray

Step-by-Step

Follow these steps to make thick wavy hair:
  1. Start by washing your hair with a volumizing shampoo and conditioner. This will help add more body and texture to your hair.

  2. Apply a heat protectant spray to your hair to prevent any damage from the curling iron or wand.

  3. Using a curling iron or wand, curl small sections of your hair away from your face. Hold the curling iron for 10-15 seconds and release.

  4. After curling all of your hair, use your fingers to gently comb through your curls to loosen them up and create more volume.

  5. Finish off with a light hairspray to hold your curls in place.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you make thick wavy hair:
  • Use a curling iron or wand with a larger barrel size to create bigger, looser waves.
  • Don’t curl your hair all the way to the ends to create a more natural look.
  • Use a texturizing spray or dry shampoo to add more volume and texture to your hair.
  • If you have naturally curly or wavy hair, use a diffuser attachment on your blow dryer to enhance your natural waves.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about making thick wavy hair:

Can I make thick wavy hair without a curling iron or wand?

Yes, you can use braids or twists to create waves in your hair. Simply braid or twist small sections of your hair and leave them overnight. In the morning, unravel the braids or twists and you’ll have natural-looking waves.

How often should I wash my hair to make thick wavy hair?

It’s best to wash your hair every 2-3 days to prevent it from drying out. Over-washing can strip your hair of its natural oils, which can lead to frizz and breakage.

What’s the best way to maintain thick wavy hair?

To maintain your thick wavy hair, use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair hydrated. It’s also important to trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends and breakage.

Hair Care

Here are some hair care tips to keep your hair healthy and strong:
  • Avoid using hot tools on a daily basis to prevent damage to your hair.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb or brush to detangle your hair to prevent breakage.
  • Eat a healthy diet that is rich in vitamins and minerals to promote healthy hair growth.
  • Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a UV protectant spray.

Conclusion

Making thick wavy hair is easy and achievable with just a few products and tools. Follow our step-by-step guide and tips to achieve the voluminous, beachy waves that you’ve always wanted. Remember to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and strong. With a little bit of effort, you can have the perfect hairstyle that will turn heads wherever you go.
