How To Do Updo For Long Hair Yourself

Written by Christine Jun 20, 2023 Â· 8 min read

If you have long hair, you know how versatile it can be. From loose waves to sleek ponytails, there are countless ways to style your locks. But if you're looking for an elegant and sophisticated look, nothing beats an updo. And the best part? You don't need to go to a salon to get one. With a bit of patience and practice, you can easily do an updo for long hair yourself. In this article, we'll show you how.

How to

The first step in doing an updo for long hair is to choose the right style. There are many different types of updos, from messy buns to sleek chignons. To get started, choose a style that matches your personal taste and the occasion you're dressing up for. For example, a messy bun might be perfect for a casual day out, while a sleek chignon might be more appropriate for a formal event.

Once you've chosen your desired style, gather all the tools you'll need. This might include a brush, hair ties, bobby pins, and hairspray. It's also a good idea to have a mirror nearby so you can see what you're doing.

Next, brush your hair to remove any tangles or knots. Then, gather your hair into a high ponytail and secure it with a hair tie. This will serve as the base for your updo.

Now it's time to start creating your updo. There are many different techniques you can use, depending on the style you've chosen. For a messy bun, for example, simply twist your ponytail into a bun and secure it with bobby pins. For a sleek chignon, twist your ponytail into a knot and tuck the ends under, securing with bobby pins as you go.

Step by Step

Here's a step-by-step guide to creating a simple updo for long hair:

Step 1: Brush your hair

Start by brushing your hair to remove any tangles or knots. This will make it easier to create your updo.

Step 2: Create a high ponytail

Gather your hair into a high ponytail and secure it with a hair tie. This will serve as the base for your updo.

Step 3: Twist your ponytail

Twist your ponytail into a bun or knot, depending on the style you've chosen. For a messy bun, twist your hair loosely and secure it with bobby pins. For a sleek chignon, twist your hair tightly and tuck the ends under, securing with bobby pins as you go.

Step 4: Secure with bobby pins

Use bobby pins to secure your updo in place. Make sure to hide the pins as best you can.

Step 5: Finish with hairspray

Spray your updo with hairspray to set it in place and add some shine.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you create the perfect updo:

  • Experiment with different styles to find the one that works best for you.
  • Use bobby pins that match your hair color to help them blend in.
  • Don't be afraid to use hairspray to keep your updo in place.
  • Add some accessories, like a headband or hairpins, to dress up your updo.
  • Practice makes perfect! Don't get discouraged if your first attempt doesn't turn out how you want it to.

FAQ

Here are some common questions about doing an updo for long hair:

Can I do an updo if I have short hair?

While it's more difficult to do an updo with short hair, it's not impossible. Try using hair extensions or a hairpiece to add length and volume.

What's the best way to hide bobby pins?

One trick is to criss-cross two bobby pins to create an X-shape. This will help them stay in place and hide them from view.

How long does an updo typically last?

An updo can last anywhere from a few hours to a whole day, depending on how well it's secured and the thickness of your hair.

Hair Care

Remember to take care of your hair before and after creating an updo. Use a good conditioner to keep your locks healthy and hydrated, and avoid using too much heat on your hair to prevent damage. It's also a good idea to wash your hair the day before you plan to do an updo, as freshly-washed hair can be harder to work with.

Now that you know how to do an updo for long hair yourself, it's time to give it a try. With a bit of practice and some patience, you'll be able to create a beautiful and elegant updo that's perfect for any occasion.
