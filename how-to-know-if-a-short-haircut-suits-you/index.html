How To Know If A Short Haircut Suits You

Written by Bardi Jul 06, 2023 Â· 7 min read

If you're considering a short haircut, you might be wondering if it will suit your face shape, hair texture, and personal style. Short haircuts can be fun and stylish, but they're not for everyone. In this article, we'll share some tips on how to know if a short haircut suits you, so you can make an informed decision.

How to Determine Your Face Shape

The first step in deciding if a short haircut will suit you is to determine your face shape. There are six main face shapes:

  • Round
  • Oval
  • Square
  • Heart
  • Diamond
  • Triangle

To determine your face shape, stand in front of a mirror and trace the outline of your face with lipstick or a washable marker. Then compare your face shape to the six options above. You can also ask your stylist for help determining your face shape.

Consider Your Hair Texture

Your hair texture is another important factor to consider when choosing a short haircut. If you have curly or thick hair, you might want to opt for a shorter cut that can be easily styled. If you have fine or thin hair, a short haircut can add volume and texture.

Think About Your Personal Style

Your personal style is also important when choosing a short haircut. If you prefer a classic or conservative look, a short bob or pixie cut might be a good choice. If you're more edgy and bold, you might prefer a short undercut or shaved side.

Try On Different Short Haircuts

The best way to know if a short haircut suits you is to try on different styles. You can use virtual makeover tools or try on wigs to see how you look with different cuts. You can also experiment with different partings, lengths, and textures.

Ask Your Stylist for Advice

Your stylist can also provide valuable advice on which short haircuts will suit you best. They can take into account your face shape, hair texture, and personal style to recommend a cut that will enhance your features and flatter your face shape.

Consider Maintenance and Styling

Short haircuts require regular maintenance and styling to keep them looking their best. If you're low-maintenance or don't have much time to style your hair, a short haircut might not be the best option for you. Talk to your stylist about the upkeep required for different short haircuts.

Trust Your Instincts and Be Confident

Ultimately, the most important factor in deciding if a short haircut suits you is how you feel when you look in the mirror. Trust your instincts and choose a cut that makes you feel confident and beautiful.

FAQs

Q: Will a short haircut suit my face shape?

A: It depends on your face shape, hair texture, and personal style. Use the tips in this article to determine if a short haircut will suit you.

Q: How do I know if I'll like a short haircut?

A: Try on different styles using virtual makeover tools or wigs. You can also ask your stylist for advice.

Q: How do I maintain a short haircut?

A: Short haircuts require regular maintenance and styling. Talk to your stylist about the upkeep required for different cuts.

Hair Care Tips for Short Haircuts

Short haircuts require different hair care than long hair. Here are some tips to keep your short haircut looking its best:

  • Wash your hair every other day to avoid drying it out
  • Use a lightweight conditioner to prevent buildup
  • Avoid using heavy styling products that can weigh down your hair
  • Use a heat protectant when styling with hot tools
  • Trim your hair every 4-6 weeks to maintain the shape

Conclusion

Choosing a short haircut can be a fun and exciting change, but it's important to consider your face shape, hair texture, and personal style before making the leap. Use the tips in this article to determine if a short haircut will suit you, and don't be afraid to experiment with different styles to find your perfect look.
