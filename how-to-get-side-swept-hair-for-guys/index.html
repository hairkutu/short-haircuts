How To Get Side Swept Hair For Guys

Written by Christine Jul 04, 2023 Â· 8 min read

Side swept hair is a classic hairstyle that has been popular for decades. It's a versatile look that can be dressed up or down, making it perfect for any occasion. If you're a guy who wants to try this style, you've come to the right place. In this article, we'll show you how to get side swept hair for guys in a relaxed, easy-to-follow language. Let's get started!

Description

Side swept hair is a hairstyle where the hair is brushed to one side of the head, typically the front. It's a popular look that can be achieved with different hair lengths and textures. Side swept hair is versatile and can be styled in different ways, from a slick, formal look to a more casual, messy look.

How to Get Side Swept Hair for Guys

Getting side swept hair for guys is easy if you follow these steps:

Step 1: Choose the Right Haircut

The first step to getting side swept hair is to have the right haircut. The length of your hair will determine how easy it is to style and maintain the side swept look. For shorter hair, a buzz cut or a crew cut will work well. For longer hair, a layered cut or a textured cut will give you more options for styling.

Step 2: Prep Your Hair

Before you start styling your hair, it's important to prep it properly. Start by washing your hair with a good-quality shampoo and conditioner. Towel-dry your hair until it's damp, but not dripping wet. Apply a styling product, such as a pomade or a wax, to your hair, focusing on the roots.

Step 3: Blow Dry Your Hair

Using a blow dryer, dry your hair while using a brush to direct the hair to one side. This will help train your hair to stay in place. Use a low heat setting and a diffuser attachment to avoid damaging your hair or causing frizz.

Step 4: Style Your Hair

Once your hair is dry, use a comb or your fingers to style your hair to one side. You can choose to go for a slick, formal look or a more casual, messy look. Experiment with different styles until you find the one that suits you best.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you get the best side swept hair for guys:

  • Use a good-quality styling product that suits your hair type and texture.
  • Don't use too much product as it can weigh your hair down and make it look greasy.
  • Try different styling techniques, such as using a brush or your fingers, to achieve different looks.
  • Trim your hair regularly to maintain the shape and style.
  • Ask your stylist for advice on the best haircut and styling products for your hair type and texture.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about getting side swept hair for guys:

Q: Can I get side swept hair if I have curly hair?

A: Yes, you can. Use a good-quality curl-defining product and a diffuser attachment on your blow dryer to enhance your curls and create the side swept look.

Q: How often should I wash my hair?

A: It depends on your hair type and texture. If you have oily hair, you may need to wash it every day. If you have dry hair, you can wash it every other day or every few days.

Q: Can I get side swept hair if I have a receding hairline?

A: Yes, you can. Choose a shorter haircut that suits your face shape and use a styling product to create the side swept look.

Hair Care

To keep your side swept hair looking its best, it's important to take good care of your hair. Here are some hair care tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a good-quality shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using hot tools, such as straighteners and curling irons, too often as they can damage your hair.
  • Eat a healthy, balanced diet and drink plenty of water to promote healthy hair growth.
  • Avoid using harsh chemicals on your hair, such as bleach or hair dyes, as they can damage your hair.
  • Get regular haircuts to maintain the shape and style of your hair.

That's it! Now you know how to get side swept hair for guys. With the right haircut, styling products, and techniques, you can create a versatile, stylish look that suits any occasion. Experiment with different styles and find the one that works best for you. Remember to take good care of your hair to keep it looking healthy and shiny. Good luck!
