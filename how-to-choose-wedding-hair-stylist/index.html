How To Choose The Perfect Wedding Hair Stylist

Written by Luffy Jun 07, 2023 · 9 min read

Are you planning your wedding day and looking for the perfect hair stylist to create your dream bridal look? Choosing the right hair stylist is a crucial decision for your big day, as your hairstyle will be one of the most important aspects of your bridal look. In this guide, we will provide you with helpful tips on how to choose the perfect wedding hair stylist.

Step 1: Research and Recommendations

The first step in choosing the perfect wedding hair stylist is to do your research. Start by asking friends and family members for recommendations. If you have a wedding planner, they can also provide you with a list of recommended hair stylists in your area. You can also search online for wedding hair stylists and read reviews from previous clients.

Make a list of potential hair stylists that you are interested in and check their availability on your wedding day. Once you have a list of potential hair stylists, it’s time to move on to the next step.

Step 2: Portfolio and Style

Before booking a hair stylist, it’s important to check their portfolio and style. Look for a hair stylist who has experience in creating the type of hairstyle that you want for your wedding day. Check their portfolio for pictures of previous bridal hairstyles that they have done.

If you have a specific style in mind, bring pictures to your hair stylist to ensure that they understand what you want. You can also ask the hair stylist for their opinion on what hairstyle would suit you best based on your face shape and wedding dress.

Step 3: Trial Run

Once you have found a hair stylist that you are interested in, it’s important to schedule a trial run. This will give you the opportunity to see how the hair stylist works and to ensure that you are happy with the final result.

During the trial run, bring pictures of the hairstyle that you want to the hair stylist. This will help them to understand what you want and to make any necessary adjustments. The trial run should take place several weeks before your wedding day to ensure that you have enough time to make any changes if necessary.

Step 4: Communication and Availability

Communication is key when it comes to choosing the perfect wedding hair stylist. Make sure that the hair stylist is easy to communicate with and is responsive to your needs. You should also ensure that the hair stylist is available on your wedding day and that they are willing to travel to your wedding venue if necessary.

It’s also important to discuss any special requirements or requests that you may have, such as using specific hair products or incorporating hair accessories into your hairstyle.

Step 5: Budget

Before booking a wedding hair stylist, it’s important to consider your budget. Wedding hair stylists can vary in price depending on their level of experience and the services that they offer. Make sure that you discuss pricing with the hair stylist before booking to ensure that you are comfortable with the cost.

Remember that your hair is an investment in your overall bridal look, so it’s important to choose a hair stylist that you trust and feel comfortable with.

Tips for Choosing the Perfect Wedding Hair Stylist

  • Start your search early to ensure that you have enough time to find the perfect hair stylist.
  • Ask for recommendations from friends and family members.
  • Check the hair stylist’s portfolio and style to ensure that they have experience in creating the type of hairstyle that you want.
  • Schedule a trial run to ensure that you are happy with the final result.
  • Ensure that the hair stylist is easy to communicate with and is responsive to your needs.
  • Discuss pricing with the hair stylist before booking to ensure that you are comfortable with the cost.

Frequently Asked Questions

How much does a wedding hair stylist cost?

Wedding hair stylist prices can vary depending on their level of experience and the services that they offer. It’s important to discuss pricing with the hair stylist before booking to ensure that you are comfortable with the cost.

When should I schedule a trial run?

The trial run should take place several weeks before your wedding day to ensure that you have enough time to make any changes if necessary.

What should I bring to the trial run?

Bring pictures of the hairstyle that you want to the hair stylist. This will help them to understand what you want and to make any necessary adjustments.

Hair Care Tips for Your Wedding Day

On your wedding day, it’s important to take care of your hair to ensure that it looks its best. Here are a few tips:

  • Wash your hair the night before your wedding day, as freshly washed hair can be more difficult to style.
  • Avoid using too much product, as this can weigh down your hair and make it difficult to style.
  • Use a heat protectant spray before using any hot tools to protect your hair from damage.
  • Consider using hair extensions to add length or volume to your hair.
  • Bring a touch-up kit with you on your wedding day, including hair spray, bobby pins, and a comb.

Choosing the perfect wedding hair stylist is an important decision that can make a big difference in your overall bridal look. By following these tips, you can find the perfect hair stylist to create your dream bridal hairstyle.
