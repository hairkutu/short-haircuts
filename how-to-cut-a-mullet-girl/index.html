How To Cut A Mullet Girl A Step By Step Guide

Written by Aprilia Sep 07, 2023 Â· 6 min read

If you're looking for a bold and edgy haircut, the mullet is back in style! This 80s-inspired look features a shorter cut on the top and sides with longer hair in the back. In this article, we'll show you how to cut a mullet girl step-by-step. Read on for tips, tricks, and hair care advice.

Table of Contents

  • How to Cut a Mullet Girl
  • Tips for Cutting a Mullet Girl
  • FAQ About Cutting a Mullet Girl
  • Hair Care for a Mullet Girl

How to Cut a Mullet Girl

To begin, make sure your client's hair is clean and dry. Section off the top and sides of the hair, leaving the back section loose. Use hair clips to keep the sections separated.

Starting with the back section, use a comb to create a center part down the middle of the head. Then, using hair cutting scissors, cut the hair straight across at the desired length. Be sure to cut the hair longer than you want it to be, since it will spring up when it's released.

Next, work on the top and side sections. Use the same technique to cut the hair straight across at the desired length. Keep in mind that the top and side sections should be shorter than the back section to create the mullet shape.

Once you've finished cutting, release the clips and use a comb to blend the different sections together. You may need to do some additional trimming to create a smooth transition between the different lengths.

Step-by-Step Guide:

  1. Section off the top and sides of the hair, leaving the back section loose.
  2. Create a center part down the middle of the head.
  3. Cut the back section straight across at the desired length.
  4. Cut the top and side sections straight across at the desired length, keeping them shorter than the back section.
  5. Release the clips and blend the different sections together.

Tips for Cutting a Mullet Girl

Here are some tips to keep in mind when cutting a mullet:

  • Start with longer hair and work your way down to the desired length.
  • Use hair cutting scissors, not regular household scissors.
  • Work slowly and carefully, making small cuts as you go.
  • Check your work frequently in a mirror to make sure the haircut is even.
  • Consider using a razor to create texture and movement in the hair.

FAQ About Cutting a Mullet Girl

Q: Is a mullet suitable for all hair types?

A: A mullet can work for most hair types, but it may be more difficult to achieve on very fine or very curly hair.

Q: How often should a mullet be trimmed?

A: It's generally recommended to trim a mullet every 6-8 weeks to maintain the shape.

Q: Can I cut a mullet myself?

A: While it's possible to cut your own hair, it's best to have a professional stylist do it for you to ensure a balanced and even cut.

Hair Care for a Mullet Girl

Once you've got your new mullet haircut, it's important to take good care of it to keep it looking its best. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using too much heat when styling your hair, as this can damage the ends.
  • Use a leave-in conditioner or hair oil to keep the hair hydrated and prevent split ends.
  • Consider using a hair mask once a week to nourish and strengthen the hair.

With these tips and tricks, you'll be able to cut a mullet girl like a pro. Whether you're looking for a bold new look or just want to try something different, the mullet is definitely worth considering. Happy cutting!
