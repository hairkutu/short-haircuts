What Hairstyle Was Popular In The 80 S

Written by Daniel Aug 28, 2023 Â· 6 min read

The 80's was an era of big hair, neon colors, and funky fashion. One of the most memorable aspects of the 80's was the hairstyles. From the iconic mullet to the perm, there were a variety of popular hairstyles that defined the decade. In this article, we will take a look at some of the most popular hairstyles of the 80's.

Description

The 80's was the era when hairstyles were all about making a statement. The bigger, the better! The hairstyles were influenced by popular culture, music, and fashion. The 80's hairstyles were characterized by voluminous curls, big bangs, and bold colors. The hairstyles were often accessorized with scrunchies, headbands, and hair clips.

How to

If you want to recreate the 80's hairstyles, here are some steps to follow:

Step 1: Get the Right Cut

The first step to achieving an 80's hairstyle is to get the right cut. The most popular hairstyles of the 80's were the mullet, the shag, and the perm. The mullet was characterized by short hair on the top and long hair at the back. The shag was a layered haircut with long bangs. The perm involved curling your hair using chemicals to create tight curls.

Step 2: Add Volume

The 80's hairstyles were all about volume. To add volume to your hair, you can use a volumizing mousse or hairspray. Apply the product to your hair and blow-dry it using a round brush to create volume.

Step 3: Style Your Hair

Once you have added volume to your hair, you can style it in different ways. The most popular hairstyles of the 80's were the side ponytail, the high ponytail, and the half-up half-down hairstyle. You can also experiment with different hair accessories like headbands, scrunchies, and hair clips.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you achieve the perfect 80's hairstyle:

  • Use a volumizing mousse to add volume to your hair.
  • Blow-dry your hair using a round brush to create volume.
  • Experiment with different hairstyles and accessories.
  • Use hairspray to hold your hairstyle in place.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about 80's hairstyles:

What was the most popular hairstyle of the 80's?

The most popular hairstyles of the 80's were the mullet, the shag, and the perm.

What hair accessories were popular in the 80's?

The most popular hair accessories of the 80's were scrunchies, headbands, and hair clips.

How can I recreate an 80's hairstyle?

You can recreate an 80's hairstyle by getting the right cut, adding volume to your hair, and styling it using different techniques and accessories.

Hair Care

To maintain your 80's hairstyle, it is important to take care of your hair. Here are some tips:

  • Use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair hydrated.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair as it can cause damage.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.
  • Avoid using too many chemicals on your hair as it can cause damage.

In conclusion, the 80's hairstyles were all about making a statement. The hairstyles were big, bold, and colorful. If you want to recreate an 80's hairstyle, follow the steps and tips mentioned above. Remember to take care of your hair to maintain your hairstyle and keep it healthy.
