Short Hair Makes You Look Taller The Ultimate Guide

Written by Luffy Apr 09, 2023 · 8 min read

Are you tired of feeling short and petite? Do you wish you could magically grow a few inches taller? Well, we have some good news for you! Short hair can actually make you look taller! Yes, you read that right. Short hair can give you the illusion of added height and we’re here to tell you how. In this guide, we’ll cover everything from how to style short hair to hair care tips to FAQs. So, let’s get started!

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How To
  • Step By Step
  • Tips
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care

Description

Short hair has been a popular trend for decades, and for good reason! Not only is it easy to maintain and style, but it can also give you the illusion of added height. When your hair is short, your neck appears longer which can make you look taller. Additionally, short hair doesn’t weigh down your head, which can also add to the illusion of height.

How To

Now that you know short hair can make you look taller, you might be wondering how to achieve this look. Here are some tips:

Choose the Right Length

The length of your hair can have a big impact on how tall you appear. Generally, hair that falls above your shoulders is the most flattering for adding height. This length helps elongate your neck and makes your overall silhouette look taller.

Add Layers

Adding layers to your short hair can also help create the illusion of added height. Layers add volume and texture to your hair, which can make it look fuller and longer.

Try a Pixie Cut

If you’re really looking to maximize the height-adding benefits of short hair, consider a pixie cut. This style keeps your hair close to your head, which can make your neck look longer and your overall frame look taller.

Step By Step

Here’s a step-by-step guide on how to style short hair to make you look taller:

Step 1: Wash and Condition

Start with clean, damp hair. Wash and condition your hair as normal, making sure to use products that are appropriate for your hair type.

Step 2: Apply a Volumizing Product

Apply a volumizing product, such as mousse or texturizing spray, to your roots. This will help add volume and texture to your hair, making it look fuller and longer.

Step 3: Blow Dry

Blow dry your hair using a round brush, pulling your hair upwards as you dry. This will help create volume at the roots and add height to your hairstyle.

Step 4: Add Texture

Use a curling iron or flat iron to add texture to your hair. This will give your hair more body and create the illusion of added height.

Step 5: Finish with Hairspray

Finish your look with a light hold hairspray to keep your style in place.

Tips

Here are some additional tips for making short hair make you look taller:

  • Avoid heavy, blunt cuts that can weigh down your hair and make you look shorter.
  • Consider adding highlights or lowlights to create depth and dimension in your hair.
  • Experiment with different partings to find the most flattering look for your face shape.
  • Don’t be afraid to try new styles and techniques to find what works best for you!

FAQs

Does short hair always make you look taller?

While short hair can often make you look taller, it’s not a guarantee. The length and style of your hair will vary from person to person, so it’s important to find what works best for you.

Is there a certain face shape that looks best with short hair?

Short hair can be flattering on all face shapes, but some styles may be more flattering for certain face shapes. It’s best to consult with a stylist to find the most flattering look for you.

How often should I get my short hair trimmed?

It’s recommended to get your hair trimmed every 6-8 weeks to keep your style looking fresh and healthy.

Hair Care

Maintaining healthy hair is important for any style, but especially for short hair. Here are some hair care tips for keeping your short hair looking its best:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and other environmental factors by wearing a hat or using a UV protectant spray.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair looking healthy and prevent split ends.

With these tips and tricks, you’ll be able to rock short hair and look taller than ever!
