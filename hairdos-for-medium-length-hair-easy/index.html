Easy And Stylish Hairdos For Medium Length Hair

Written by Eveline Jun 02, 2023 · 8 min read

Medium length hair is the perfect length for experimenting with different hairstyles. It’s not too long that it becomes unmanageable, and not too short that it limits your styling options. In this blog post, we’ll share some easy and stylish hairdos for medium length hair that you can try at home. Whether you’re looking for a quick and easy hairstyle for a busy day, or a chic hairstyle for a special occasion, we’ve got you covered.

Table of Contents

  • How to Style Medium Length Hair
  • Step-by-Step Guide to Easy Hairdos for Medium Length Hair
  • Tips for Maintaining Medium Length Hair
  • Frequently Asked Questions
  • Hair Care Tips for Medium Length Hair

How to Style Medium Length Hair

Before we dive into the different hairdos, let’s talk about some basic tips for styling medium length hair:

  • Invest in good quality hair products that are suitable for your hair type.
  • Blow-dry your hair using a round brush to add volume.
  • Use a heat protectant spray before using any hot styling tools.
  • Experiment with different parting styles to change up your look.
  • Accessorize with hair clips, headbands, and scarves to add some flair to your hairstyle.

Step-by-Step Guide to Easy Hairdos for Medium Length Hair

1. Messy Bun

To create a messy bun, follow these simple steps:

  1. Gather your hair into a high ponytail.
  2. Twist the ponytail and wrap it around the base to create a bun.
  3. Secure with bobby pins.
  4. Pull out a few strands of hair to create a messy, undone look.

2. Half-Up Half-Down

The half-up half-down hairstyle is a classic and easy option. Here’s how to do it:

  1. Take a section of hair from the crown of your head and tie it with an elastic band.
  2. Fluff up the ponytail to add volume.
  3. Optional: Curl the ends for a softer look.

3. Side Braid

The side braid is a cute and easy hairstyle that is perfect for a casual day out. Follow these steps to create it:

  1. Part your hair to one side.
  2. Take a section of hair from the front and divide it into three sections.
  3. Create a French braid by crossing the side sections over the middle section, adding hair from the sides as you go.
  4. Continue braiding until you reach the nape of your neck, then finish with a regular braid.
  5. Secure with an elastic band.

4. Low Ponytail

A low ponytail is a chic and effortless hairstyle that you can wear to any occasion. Here’s how to create it:

  1. Gather your hair into a low ponytail at the nape of your neck.
  2. Secure with an elastic band.
  3. Take a small section of hair from the ponytail and wrap it around the elastic band to conceal it.
  4. Secure with bobby pins.

Tips for Maintaining Medium Length Hair

Now that you know how to style your medium length hair, it’s important to maintain it properly. Here are some tips to keep your hair healthy and shiny:

  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to nourish your hair.
  • Avoid using hot styling tools too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and harsh weather conditions by wearing a hat or using a UV protection spray.

Frequently Asked Questions

What are some other easy hairstyles for medium length hair?

Other easy hairstyles for medium length hair include the messy side braid, the high ponytail, and the sleek straight look.

Can I curl my medium length hair?

Absolutely! Use a curling iron or a curling wand to create loose waves or tight curls.

How often should I wash my medium length hair?

It depends on your hair type and lifestyle. If you have oily hair, you may need to wash it every other day. If you have dry hair, you can wash it once or twice a week.

Hair Care Tips for Medium Length Hair

Here are some additional hair care tips to keep your medium length hair looking its best:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to prevent dryness and breakage.
  • Avoid using hot water when washing your hair, as it can strip away natural oils.
  • Use a wide-toothed comb to detangle your hair, starting from the ends and working your way up.
  • Apply a leave-in conditioner or hair serum to add shine and reduce frizz.

With these easy and stylish hairdos for medium length hair, you’ll never run out of ideas for your next hairstyle. Remember to take care of your hair by using good quality products and following a regular hair care routine. Happy styling!
