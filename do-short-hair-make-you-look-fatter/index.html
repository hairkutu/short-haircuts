Do Short Hair Make You Look Fatter

Written by Robby Jul 16, 2023 · 7 min read

Short hair has been a popular choice for women for many years. It’s easy to maintain, and it can be styled in many different ways. However, one question that often comes up is whether short hair makes you look fatter. In this article, we’ll explore this topic and provide you with some tips on how to choose the right hairstyle for your body type.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to
  • Step by step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

Short hair can make you look fatter or thinner depending on how you style it. It’s all about creating balance and proportion. The length of your hair can affect the appearance of your face shape and your body shape. Short hair can add volume to your head, which can make your face look rounder. However, if you style it correctly, short hair can also elongate your neck and make you appear slimmer.

How to

If you want to make your short hair work for your body type, here are some tips:

Choose the right length

The length of your hair can affect the appearance of your face shape and your body shape. If you have a round face, avoid going too short as this can make your face look even rounder. Instead, opt for a longer pixie cut or a bob that hits just below your chin. If you have a long face, a shorter cut can help balance out your features.

Add volume in the right places

Adding volume to the crown of your head can make your face look rounder. Instead, add volume at the sides of your head or at the back of your head to create balance. You can achieve this by using a volumizing mousse or by blow-drying your hair upside down.

Create angles

Creating angles in your hair can help elongate your neck and make you appear slimmer. Ask your hairdresser to add layers or to cut your hair at an angle to create this effect.

Step by step

Here’s how to style your short hair to make you look thinner:

  1. Start by washing your hair with a volumizing shampoo and conditioner.
  2. Apply a volumizing mousse to your hair and blow-dry your hair upside down to add volume at the back of your head.
  3. Create angles by adding layers to your hair or by cutting your hair at an angle.
  4. Finish off your look with a texturizing spray to add texture and definition to your hair.

Tips

  • Avoid adding volume to the crown of your head as this can make your face look rounder.
  • Use a round brush when blow-drying your hair to create volume at the sides of your head.
  • Don’t be afraid to experiment with different hairstyles to find the one that works best for your body type.

FAQ

Does short hair make you look older?

Short hair can make you look older if it’s not styled correctly. However, if you choose the right length and style it correctly, short hair can make you look younger and more modern.

Will a bob make me look thinner?

A bob can make you look thinner if it’s styled correctly. A bob that hits just below your chin can help elongate your neck and make you appear slimmer.

Hair Care

Proper hair care is essential for maintaining healthy hair. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your hair with a sulfate-free shampoo to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair hydrated.
  • Avoid using hot tools on your hair too often as this can cause damage.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair healthy and to prevent split ends.

In conclusion, short hair can make you look fatter or thinner depending on how you style it. By choosing the right length, adding volume in the right places, and creating angles, you can make your short hair work for your body type. Don’t be afraid to experiment with different hairstyles to find the one that works best for you.
