How To Hold A Wig On A Bald Head A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Luffy Apr 17, 2023 · 7 min read

Wearing a wig can give you a new sense of confidence, especially if you’re experiencing hair loss. But if you have a bald head, you may be wondering how to keep your wig in place. In this article, we’ll give you a step-by-step guide on how to hold a wig on a bald head, along with some tips and hair care advice.

Table of Contents

  • How to Hold a Wig on a Bald Head
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Holding a Wig on a Bald Head
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care for Wigs and Bald Heads

How to Hold a Wig on a Bald Head

There are several ways to hold a wig on a bald head, depending on your preferences and the type of wig you have. Here are some common methods:

Hair Clips or Bobby Pins

You can use hair clips or bobby pins to secure your wig to your head. This is a popular method because it’s easy and doesn’t require any special tools. Simply clip the wig onto your scalp in several places, making sure to avoid the rim of the wig cap. Make sure the clips are tight enough to hold the wig in place, but not so tight that they cause discomfort.

Wig Grip Band

A wig grip band is a specially designed headband that helps keep wigs in place. It’s made of a soft, stretchy fabric that fits snugly around your head. You can wear it under your wig to help keep it in place, even if you have a bald head. Wig grip bands are available in different colors and sizes, so you can choose one that matches your skin tone and head size.

Adhesive Tapes or Glue

Adhesive tapes or glue can be used to hold a wig in place, but this method requires more preparation and care. You’ll need to apply the tape or glue to both your scalp and the wig cap, making sure to avoid your hairline. This method provides a more secure hold, but it can be difficult to remove the tape or glue without damaging your wig or scalp.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here’s a step-by-step guide on how to hold a wig on a bald head using hair clips or bobby pins:

  1. Put on a wig cap to protect your scalp and hair from the wig.
  2. Put the wig on your head and adjust it to fit properly.
  3. Insert hair clips or bobby pins around the perimeter of the wig cap, avoiding the rim.
  4. Insert additional clips or pins at the crown of the wig for extra security.
  5. Adjust the wig as needed and style it as desired.

Tips for Holding a Wig on a Bald Head

Here are some tips to help you hold your wig in place:

  • Choose a wig cap that matches your skin tone and head size.
  • Use hair clips or bobby pins that are the same color as your wig.
  • Apply adhesive tapes or glue sparingly to avoid damaging your wig or scalp.
  • Always remove your wig before going to bed to prevent damage to your hair and scalp.

FAQs

Can I use regular hair clips or bobby pins to hold my wig in place?

Yes, you can use regular hair clips or bobby pins to hold your wig in place. Just make sure they’re tight enough to hold the wig, but not so tight that they cause discomfort.

How do I remove adhesive tape or glue from my scalp?

You can remove adhesive tape or glue by gently rubbing a cotton ball soaked in rubbing alcohol or wig adhesive solvent on the affected area. Do not pull or tug on the tape or glue, as this can cause damage to your scalp or wig.

Hair Care for Wigs and Bald Heads

Proper hair care is important for both wigs and bald heads. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your wig regularly with a mild shampoo and conditioner.
  • Store your wig on a wig stand or mannequin head to maintain its shape.
  • Keep your scalp moisturized and protected from the sun and harsh weather.
  • Avoid using hot styling tools on your wig, as this can damage the fibers.

By following these tips and using the methods we’ve outlined, you can hold your wig securely on your bald head and enjoy a new sense of confidence and style.
