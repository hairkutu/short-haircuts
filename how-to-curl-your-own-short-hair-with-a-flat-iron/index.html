How To Curl Your Own Short Hair With A Flat Iron

Written by Christine Aug 16, 2023 Â· 7 min read

If you have short hair, you may think that curling it with a flat iron is impossible. However, with the right technique and tools, you can achieve gorgeous curls with just a few easy steps. In this article, we will show you how to curl your own short hair with a flat iron using relaxed English language to make it easy to understand. We will cover everything from how to prep your hair to FAQs and hair care tips. Let's get started!

Table of Contents

  • Sectioning Your Hair
  • Curling Your Hair

Prepping Your Hair

Before you start curling your short hair with a flat iron, it's important to prep your hair properly. Here's how:

  1. Wash your hair and blow dry it until it's completely dry.
  2. Apply a heat protectant spray or serum to your hair to protect it from heat damage.
  3. Brush your hair to remove any tangles or knots.


Now that your hair is prepped, it's time to section it.

Sectioning Your Hair

The key to curling short hair with a flat iron is to section it properly. Here's how:

  1. Divide your hair into three sections: the bottom, the middle, and the top.
  2. Clip the top section up and out of the way.
  3. Take a small section of hair from the bottom section and clip the rest of the hair up and out of the way.


Now that your hair is sectioned, it's time to start curling.

Curling Your Hair

Here's how to curl your short hair with a flat iron:

  1. Take a small section of hair and clamp the flat iron near the root.
  2. Twist the flat iron away from your face and pull it down to the end of the hair.
  3. Release the flat iron and let the curl cool for a few seconds.
  4. Repeat this process on all sections of hair, alternating the direction of the twist for a more natural look.


Once you've curled all the sections of your hair, it's time to add some finishing touches.

Tips for Curling Short Hair with a Flat Iron

Here are some tips to help you achieve the best results when curling your short hair with a flat iron:

  • Use a high-quality flat iron with adjustable temperature settings.
  • Start with a lower temperature and gradually increase it as needed.
  • Use a comb to help guide your hair through the flat iron.
  • Be careful not to burn your scalp or ears with the flat iron.
  • For a looser curl, use a larger section of hair.


Now that you know how to curl your short hair with a flat iron, let's answer some FAQs.

FAQs

Can I curl my short hair with a flat iron?

Yes, you can curl your short hair with a flat iron. Just be sure to section your hair properly and use the right technique.

What temperature should I use on my flat iron?

Start with a lower temperature and gradually increase it as needed. A temperature of 350-400 degrees Fahrenheit is usually sufficient for short hair.

How do I prevent my curls from falling flat?

Use a hairspray or styling product to hold your curls in place. You can also clip your curls up and let them cool completely before letting them down.

Hair Care Tips

Here are some hair care tips to keep your short hair healthy and beautiful:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to prevent dryness and damage.
  • Avoid using heat tools too often, as they can cause damage to your hair.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.
  • Eat a healthy diet and drink plenty of water to promote hair growth and health.


Now that you know how to curl your own short hair with a flat iron, you can achieve gorgeous, bouncy curls in just a few easy steps. Remember to prep your hair properly, section it correctly, and use the right technique for the best results. Happy curling!
