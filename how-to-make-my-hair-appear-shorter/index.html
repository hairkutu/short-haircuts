How To Make My Hair Appear Shorter Tips And Tricks

Written by Daniel Mar 20, 2023 · 6 min read

Do you want to experiment with shorter hair but don't want to commit to a haircut? Luckily, there are plenty of ways to make your hair appear shorter without actually cutting it! In this article, we'll go over some tips and tricks to help you achieve the look you want.

How To

1. Try a bob

A bob haircut is a classic way to make your hair appear shorter. However, if you're not ready to commit to a haircut, you can fake a bob by tucking your hair under and securing it with bobby pins. This works best if you have shoulder-length hair or longer.

2. Use a hairband

Another way to create the illusion of shorter hair is to use a hairband. Pull your hair back into a low ponytail and then slide the hairband up towards your earlobes. This will create a faux-bob look that's perfect for a night out.

3. Create a messy bun

A messy bun is a great hairstyle for making your hair appear shorter. Gather your hair into a high ponytail and then twist it into a bun. Pull out a few strands to create a messy look.

Step by Step

1. Tucked Bob

- Start with clean, dry hair
- Divide hair into two sections
- Tuck the ends of your hair under and pin in place
- Use hairspray or texturizing spray to hold the style in place

2. Hairband Bob

- Start with clean, dry hair
- Pull hair back into a low ponytail
- Slide the hairband up towards your earlobes
- Adjust the hair to create a bob-like shape
- Use hairspray to hold the style in place

3. Messy Bun

- Start with clean, dry hair
- Gather hair into a high ponytail
- Twist hair into a bun
- Pull out a few strands to create a messy look
- Use hairspray or texturizing spray to hold the style in place

Tips

- Use texturizing spray or dry shampoo to add volume and texture to your hair
- Experiment with different hairstyles to find what works best for you
- Don't be afraid to use bobby pins and hairbands to create the look you want
- Use hairspray to hold your style in place

FAQ

Q: Will my hair look noticeably shorter?

A: It depends on the style you choose and the length of your hair. However, most of these styles will create the illusion of shorter hair.

Q: Can I use these styles on any hair type?

A: Yes! These styles work on all hair types, including straight, wavy, curly, and coily hair.

Q: How long will these styles last?

A: These styles can last all day with the help of hairspray or texturizing spray.

Hair Care

Remember to take care of your hair by using heat protectant sprays and deep conditioning treatments. If you're using bobby pins or hairbands, be gentle when removing them to avoid damaging your hair. And always remember to use a wide-tooth comb or brush when detangling your hair to prevent breakage.

With these tips and tricks, you can make your hair appear shorter without actually cutting it. So go ahead and experiment with new styles and have fun with your hair!
