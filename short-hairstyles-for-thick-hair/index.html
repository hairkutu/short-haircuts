Short Hairstyles For Thick Hair The Ultimate Guide

Written by Petter Jun 06, 2023 Â· 9 min read
If you have thick hair, you know that it can be a blessing and a curse. On one hand, you have a lot of hair to work with, which means you can create a variety of different styles. On the other hand, it can be difficult to manage and style your hair, especially if you have a busy lifestyle. Luckily, there are plenty of short hairstyles for thick hair that are both stylish and low-maintenance. In this article, we'll provide you with everything you need to know about short hairstyles for thick hair, including how to style them, tips for maintaining them, and more.

Table of Contents

In this article, we'll cover the following topics:

  • How to Choose the Right Short Hairstyle for Thick Hair
  • Step-by-Step Guide to Styling Short Hairstyles for Thick Hair
  • Tips for Maintaining Short Hairstyles for Thick Hair
  • FAQs About Short Hairstyles for Thick Hair
  • Hair Care for Short Hairstyles for Thick Hair

How to Choose the Right Short Hairstyle for Thick Hair

When it comes to choosing a short hairstyle for thick hair, there are a few things you should keep in mind. First, consider your face shape. Some short hairstyles may look better on certain face shapes than others. Second, think about your hair texture. While thick hair can be versatile, some hairstyles may require more maintenance than others. Finally, consider your lifestyle. If you're constantly on the go, you may want a low-maintenance style that requires minimal styling.

Short Bob

One of the most popular short hairstyles for thick hair is the short bob. This classic style is versatile and can be customized to fit your face shape and hair texture. For those with a round face, a longer bob may be more flattering, while those with a square face may want to opt for a shorter bob. To style, simply blow dry your hair and use a flat iron or curling iron to add some texture.

Pixie Cut

For those who want a more low-maintenance style, the pixie cut is a great option. This short hairstyle is perfect for those with thick hair, as it can help to thin out your hair and make it more manageable. To style, simply use a bit of styling gel or mousse to add some texture and volume.

Shaggy Layers

If you're looking for a more edgy and textured style, shaggy layers may be the perfect option. This short hairstyle is great for those with thick hair, as it can help to add some dimension and movement to your locks. To style, simply blow dry your hair and use a texturizing spray or pomade to add some texture.

Step-by-Step Guide to Styling Short Hairstyles for Thick Hair

Now that you've chosen your short hairstyle for thick hair, it's time to learn how to style it. Follow these steps to create the perfect look: 1. Start with clean, dry hair. 2. Apply a heat protectant spray to your hair. 3. Use a round brush to blow dry your hair, focusing on the roots to add volume. 4. Once your hair is dry, use a flat iron or curling iron to add some texture. 5. Finish with a texturizing spray or pomade to add some hold and definition.

Tips for Maintaining Short Hairstyles for Thick Hair

To keep your short hairstyle looking its best, follow these tips: - Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy and hydrated. - Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as this can cause damage. - Use a dry shampoo to extend the life of your style between washes. - Regularly trim your hair to maintain its shape and prevent split ends.

FAQs About Short Hairstyles for Thick Hair

Q: Can I still wear a ponytail with a short hairstyle?

A: Yes! While it may take some creativity, you can still wear a ponytail with a short hairstyle. Try using hair accessories like headbands or hair clips to keep your hair out of your face.

Q: Will a short hairstyle make my hair look thinner?

A: It depends on the style you choose. Some short hairstyles, like the pixie cut, can help to thin out your hair and make it more manageable. However, other styles may make your hair look thicker and more voluminous.

Hair Care for Short Hairstyles for Thick Hair

To keep your short hairstyle looking its best, it's important to take care of your hair. Follow these tips to keep your locks healthy and hydrated: - Use a shampoo and conditioner that's designed for your hair type. - Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as this can cause damage. - Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair hydrated. - Regularly trim your hair to maintain its shape and prevent split ends. In conclusion, there are plenty of short hairstyles for thick hair that are both stylish and low-maintenance. Whether you opt for a classic bob or an edgy shaggy style, make sure to choose a style that fits your face shape, hair texture, and lifestyle. With the right styling tools and hair care routine, you can keep your short hairstyle looking its best all year round.
