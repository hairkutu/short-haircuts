What Is Deva Cut Curly Hair

Written by Aprilia Jun 07, 2023 · 8 min read

Are you looking for a new haircut that will enhance your curly hair? Deva Cut Curly Hair might be the right choice for you. In this post, we will explore what Deva Cut Curly Hair is, how to get it, and how to take care of it.

Description

Deva Cut Curly Hair is a technique designed specifically for curly hair. It was created by Lorraine Massey, the founder of Devachan Salons and DevaCurl products. The technique involves cutting curly hair while it is dry, rather than wet, to create a shape that suits the natural curl pattern of the hair.

Deva Cut Curly Hair is not just a regular haircut. It is a specialized technique that takes into account the unique needs of curly hair. The technique involves cutting the hair in a way that enhances its natural curls, minimizes frizz, and creates a shape that suits the individual’s face and hair type.

How to Get Deva Cut Curly Hair

If you are interested in getting Deva Cut Curly Hair, the first step is to find a stylist who is trained in the technique. You can visit the DevaCurl website to find a stylist near you who is certified in Deva Cut Curly Hair.

When you go in for your appointment, your stylist will examine your hair and discuss your hair goals with you. They will then wash and condition your hair but will not use any styling products. They will cut your hair while it is dry, using a variety of techniques to enhance your natural curl pattern.

After your hair is cut, your stylist will style your hair using DevaCurl products. They will show you how to maintain your curls at home and recommend products that will work best for your hair type.

Step by Step

Here is a step-by-step guide to getting Deva Cut Curly Hair:

Step 1: Find a Stylist

Visit the DevaCurl website to find a stylist near you who is certified in Deva Cut Curly Hair.

Step 2: Consultation

Your stylist will examine your hair and discuss your hair goals with you.

Step 3: Wash and Condition

Your stylist will wash and condition your hair but will not use any styling products.

Step 4: Dry Cut

Your stylist will cut your hair while it is dry, using a variety of techniques to enhance your natural curl pattern.

Step 5: Styling

After your hair is cut, your stylist will style your hair using DevaCurl products. They will show you how to maintain your curls at home and recommend products that will work best for your hair type.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind if you have Deva Cut Curly Hair:

  • Do not brush your hair when it is dry. Use your fingers or a wide-tooth comb to detangle your curls.
  • Use a microfiber towel or an old t-shirt to dry your hair. Do not use a regular towel, as it can create frizz.
  • Avoid using products that contain sulfates, as they can dry out your hair.
  • Deep condition your hair once a week to keep it hydrated and healthy.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about Deva Cut Curly Hair:

What is the difference between a Deva Cut and a regular haircut?

A Deva Cut is a specialized technique designed specifically for curly hair. It involves cutting the hair while it is dry to enhance its natural curl pattern. A regular haircut is done on wet hair and does not take into account the unique needs of curly hair.

How often should I get a Deva Cut?

It is recommended to get a Deva Cut every 3-4 months to maintain the shape of your curls.

Can I get a Deva Cut if I have straight hair?

No, a Deva Cut is designed specifically for curly hair. If you have straight hair, a regular haircut would be more appropriate.

Hair Care

Here are some tips for taking care of your Deva Cut Curly Hair:

  • Wash your hair with a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner.
  • Deep condition your hair once a week to keep it hydrated and healthy.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools, as they can damage your hair.
  • Use a diffuser when blow-drying your hair to enhance your natural curl pattern.

In conclusion, Deva Cut Curly Hair is a specialized technique designed specifically for curly hair. It involves cutting the hair while it is dry to enhance its natural curl pattern. If you are interested in getting Deva Cut Curly Hair, make sure to find a stylist who is certified in the technique and follow the tips for taking care of your curls.
