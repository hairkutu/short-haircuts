How To Find The Best Sunglasses For Your Face Shape

Are you someone who loves to wear sunglasses? Are you looking for the perfect pair of shades that will not only protect your eyes but also look great on you? If the answer is yes, then you have come to the right place. In this article, we will guide you on how to find the best sunglasses for your face shape.

Description

Sunglasses are not just a fashion accessory, but they are also important for protecting your eyes from the harmful UV rays of the sun. However, not all sunglasses are made equal, and not all of them will suit your face shape. Finding the perfect pair of sunglasses can be challenging, but with the right guidance, you can find the perfect pair that will not only look great but will also protect your eyes.

How to Find the Best Sunglasses for Your Face Shape

The first step in finding the perfect pair of sunglasses is to determine your face shape. There are different face shapes such as round, square, oval, heart-shaped, and diamond-shaped. Once you have determined your face shape, you can then choose the sunglasses that will complement your face shape.

Round Face Shape

People with a round face shape have a circular face with soft angles. To balance out the roundness of your face, choose sunglasses with angular frames such as square or rectangular frames. Avoid round and small frames, as they will make your face look even rounder.

Square Face Shape

People with a square face shape have a strong jawline and angular features. To soften the angles of your face, choose sunglasses with round or oval frames. Avoid square and rectangular frames, as they will make your face look even more angular.

Oval Face Shape

People with an oval face shape have balanced features and a slightly curved jawline. People with an oval face shape are lucky, as they can wear almost any shape of sunglasses. However, it is recommended to choose sunglasses that are as wide or wider than the broadest part of your face.

Heart-Shaped Face

People with a heart-shaped face have a broad forehead and a narrow chin. To balance out your face, choose sunglasses with bottom-heavy frames such as aviators or cat-eye frames. Avoid top-heavy frames, as they will make your forehead look even broader.

Diamond-Shaped Face

People with a diamond-shaped face have wide cheekbones and a narrow forehead and chin. To balance out your face, choose sunglasses with oval or cat-eye frames. Avoid narrow frames, as they will make your cheekbones look even wider.

Step by Step Guide

Here is a step-by-step guide on how to find the perfect pair of sunglasses:

  1. Determine your face shape.
  2. Choose sunglasses that complement your face shape.
  3. Consider the size of the sunglasses.
  4. Choose a color that complements your skin tone.
  5. Consider the purpose of the sunglasses - are they for fashion or for protection?
  6. Try on different styles and shapes of sunglasses.
  7. Choose a pair that feels comfortable and fits well.

Tips to Keep in Mind

Here are some tips to keep in mind when choosing sunglasses:

  • Choose sunglasses that provide 100% UV protection.
  • Consider polarized lenses if you spend a lot of time outdoors.
  • Choose a shape that complements your style and personality.
  • Consider the occasion when choosing sunglasses.
  • Choose a pair of sunglasses that you can wear with different outfits.
  • Invest in a high-quality pair of sunglasses that will last a long time.

Frequently Asked Questions

What if I have a combination of face shapes?

If you have a combination of face shapes, choose sunglasses that complement the dominant shape of your face.

What if I have a small face?

Choose smaller frames that fit your face well. Avoid oversized frames, as they will overwhelm your face.

What if I have a wide face?

Choose wider frames that balance out the width of your face. Avoid narrow frames, as they will make your face look even wider.

Hair Care Tips

Here are some hair care tips to keep in mind when wearing sunglasses:

  • Avoid wearing sunglasses on your head, as it can damage your hair.
  • Choose sunglasses that do not get tangled in your hair.
  • Consider wearing a hat or a headband to protect your hair from the sun.
  • Use hair products that protect your hair from the sun and heat.
  • Wear a hair tie or a clip to keep your hair away from your face when wearing sunglasses.

Now that you know how to find the perfect pair of sunglasses, you can confidently choose a pair that complements your face shape, style, and personality. Remember to choose sunglasses that not only look great but also provide the necessary protection for your eyes.
