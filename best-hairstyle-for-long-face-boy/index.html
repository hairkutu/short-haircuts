Best Hairstyle For Long Face Boy Tips And Tricks

Written by Daniel Jul 16, 2023 · 6 min read

If you have a long face, finding the right hairstyle can be a challenge. You want something that flatters your face shape, but also suits your personal style. In this article, we’ll explore the best hairstyle for long face boy and provide tips and tricks for achieving the perfect look.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to
  • Step by step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

A long face is characterized by a high forehead, long cheekbones, and a narrow chin. The best hairstyle for long face boy is one that balances the face and adds width to the sides. This can be achieved through a variety of haircuts and styling techniques.

How to

The first step in finding the best hairstyle for long face boy is to determine your face shape. This can be done by measuring the length of your face from your hairline to your chin and comparing it to the width of your face at the cheekbones.

Once you know your face shape, you can start exploring different haircuts that flatter your features. Some popular options for long face boys include:

  • The undercut
  • The side part
  • The textured crop
  • The buzz cut

Step by step

Here are some steps to follow when getting the best hairstyle for long face boy:

  1. Consult with a hairstylist who specializes in men’s haircuts.
  2. Show them pictures of hairstyles you like and ask for their opinion on what would work best for your face shape.
  3. Consider the amount of maintenance and styling required for each haircut.
  4. Choose a haircut that suits your personal style and complements your features.
  5. Ask your hairstylist for tips on styling your hair at home.

Tips

Here are some additional tips for getting the best hairstyle for long face boy:

  • Avoid hairstyles that add height to the top of your head, as this can make your face appear even longer.
  • Consider adding bangs or a fringe to your haircut to balance your forehead.
  • Experiment with different styling products to find the right one for your hair type.
  • Use a hair dryer and brush to add volume and texture to your hair.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about the best hairstyle for long face boy:

  • Q: Can I wear my hair long if I have a long face?
  • A: Yes, but it’s important to add volume to the sides of your hair to balance your face shape.
  • Q: What styling products are best for long face boys?
  • A: Texturizing products like pomade and wax are great for adding volume and texture to your hair.
  • Q: How often should I get my hair cut?
  • A: This depends on your hair type and the length of your haircut. Generally, it’s recommended to get a trim every 4-6 weeks to maintain your style.

Hair Care

Proper hair care is essential for maintaining a healthy and stylish look. Here are some tips for taking care of your hair:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair after washing.
  • Avoid using hot tools like straighteners and curling irons too often, as this can damage your hair.
  • Apply a hair mask or deep conditioner once a week to keep your hair hydrated and nourished.
  • Stay hydrated and eat a balanced diet to promote healthy hair growth.

With these tips and tricks, you can find the best hairstyle for long face boy and rock a stylish look that flatters your features. Remember to consult with a hairstylist and experiment with different haircuts and styling techniques to find the perfect look for you.
