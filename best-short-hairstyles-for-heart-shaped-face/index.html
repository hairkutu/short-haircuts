Best Short Hairstyles For Heart Shaped Face

Written by Christine Apr 26, 2023 Â· 6 min read

If you have a heart-shaped face, you have a wide forehead and a narrow chin, which gives you a unique look. Finding the right hairstyle can help accentuate your features and balance your face shape. In this article, we'll share the best short hairstyles for heart-shaped faces to help you find a flattering look.

Description

Heart-shaped faces are often referred to as the "inverted triangle" shape. This means that your forehead is wider than your jawline, and you have a pointed chin that is narrow. When it comes to finding the right haircut, you want to choose a style that will balance out your features and create a harmonious look.

How to

When choosing a short hairstyle for a heart-shaped face, there are a few things to keep in mind. First, you want to create balance between your forehead and jawline. This can be achieved with a haircut that adds volume to the top of your head, while keeping the sides and back of your hair close to your head.

You may also want to consider adding some layers to your hair, which can help soften your features and create movement. Short bangs can also be flattering, as long as they are swept to the side and not cut straight across.

Step by step

1. Determine your face shape: You want to make sure you have a heart-shaped face before choosing a haircut that flatters your features. Look in the mirror and note the width of your forehead compared to your jawline.

2. Browse hairstyles: Look for hairstyles that add volume to the top of your head, while keeping the sides and back close to your head. Consider adding layers or side-swept bangs to soften your features.

3. Consult with a stylist: Your stylist can help you choose a haircut that flatters your face shape and works with your hair texture and lifestyle.

Tips

- Avoid hairstyles that add volume to the sides of your head, as this can make your forehead look wider.

- Opt for side-swept bangs instead of straight across bangs.

- Consider adding some texture to your hair with layers or a choppy cut.

FAQ

What short hairstyles are best for heart-shaped faces?

The best short hairstyles for heart-shaped faces include pixie cuts, bob haircuts, and layered cuts that add volume to the top of your head.

Can heart-shaped faces pull off bangs?

Yes, heart-shaped faces can pull off bangs, as long as they are swept to the side and not cut straight across.

How often should I get my hair cut?

It depends on your hair texture and how quickly it grows, but most people benefit from getting a trim every 6-8 weeks.

Hair Care

To keep your short hairstyle looking its best, it's important to take good care of your hair. This includes washing and conditioning your hair regularly, using heat-protectant products before styling, and avoiding excessive heat styling or chemical treatments.

You may also want to consider using a hair mask once a week to help nourish and hydrate your hair. And don't forget to schedule regular trims to keep your haircut looking fresh and healthy.

Overall, there are many short hairstyles that can flatter a heart-shaped face. By keeping your face shape in mind and choosing a haircut that creates balance and harmony, you can find a look that makes you feel confident and beautiful.
