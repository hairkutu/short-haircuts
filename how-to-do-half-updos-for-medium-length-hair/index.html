How To Do Half Updos For Medium Length Hair

Written by Bardi May 22, 2023 Â· 7 min read

If you have medium length hair, you know just how versatile it can be. You can wear it down for a casual look, or you can pull it up into a stylish updo for a more formal occasion. One of the best hairstyles for medium length hair is the half updo. It's easy to do, looks great on everyone, and can be dressed up or down depending on your mood. In this article, we'll show you how to do half updos for medium length hair, step by step.

Description

Half updos for medium length hair are a great way to add some style and sophistication to your everyday look. They're perfect for everything from a day at the office to a night out with friends. The best part is that they're easy to do, so you don't have to spend hours in front of the mirror getting ready.

How to

Here's how to do half updos for medium length hair:

Step 1: Start with clean, dry hair

The first step to creating a half updo is to start with clean, dry hair. This will make it easier to style and will help your hair stay in place throughout the day. If you have naturally curly or wavy hair, you may want to straighten it first to make it easier to work with.

Step 2: Section your hair

Next, use a comb to section your hair. Start by dividing your hair into two sections, one on top and one on the bottom. Use a clip to secure the top section out of the way.

Step 3: Create your half updo

Now it's time to create your half updo. Take a small section of hair from the front of your head and pull it back towards the crown of your head. Use a clip or bobby pins to secure it in place. You can leave some hair out around your face for a softer look, or you can pull it all back for a more polished look.

Step 4: Finish your half updo

Finally, finish your half updo by adding some hairspray or other styling products to hold it in place. You can also add some accessories, like a headband or hairpin, to dress it up even more.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you create the perfect half updo for your medium length hair:

  • Use bobby pins that match your hair color to help them blend in.
  • Try adding some volume to your hair by teasing it before you create your half updo.
  • Experiment with different styles, like a messy bun or a braided half updo.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about half updos for medium length hair:

Q: Can I do a half updo with short hair?

A: Yes, you can do a half updo with short hair. Just adjust the size of the sections to fit your hair length.

Q: How long does it take to do a half updo?

A: It usually takes about 5-10 minutes to create a half updo, depending on your hair length and the style you choose.

Q: Can I wear a half updo to a formal event?

A: Yes, you can definitely wear a half updo to a formal event. Just make sure to choose a style that is appropriate for the occasion.

Hair Care

To keep your medium length hair healthy and looking great, it's important to take good care of it. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo.
  • Use a conditioner to keep your hair moisturized.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair looking healthy and prevent split ends.

With these tips and tricks, you'll be able to create beautiful half updos for your medium length hair in no time. Whether you're going to work or out on the town, this versatile hairstyle is sure to turn heads.
