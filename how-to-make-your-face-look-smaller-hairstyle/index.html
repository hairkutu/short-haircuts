How To Make Your Face Look Smaller Hairstyle

Written by Aprilia Jul 18, 2023 · 7 min read

If you’re looking to create a new hairstyle that will make your face look smaller, there are many options available to you. In this article, we will be discussing how to make your face look smaller hairstyle using some simple techniques and hairstyles that will suit your face shape.

Description

Having a rounder face can sometimes be a challenge when it comes to finding a hairstyle that suits your features. It can be frustrating to feel like your face is the main focus of your look rather than your hair. However, with the right hairstyle, you can create a flattering look that will make your face appear smaller and more proportionate.

How To

One of the best ways to make your face look smaller is by creating more volume on the top of your head. This can be achieved by adding layers and texture to your hair. You can also try adding bangs to your hairstyle, as this can help to create a more balanced look.

Another technique that can help to make your face look smaller is by creating a side part in your hair. This will help to create more volume on one side of your head, which will help to balance out your face. You can also try adding waves or curls to your hair, as this will create more movement and texture.

Step By Step

Here are some simple steps you can follow to create a hairstyle that will make your face look smaller:

  1. Start by washing and conditioning your hair using products that are suitable for your hair type.
  2. Towel dry your hair and apply a heat protectant spray.
  3. Blow dry your hair using a round brush to create volume.
  4. If you have long hair, consider adding layers to create more texture.
  5. Create a side part in your hair to help balance out your face.
  6. If you have bangs, style them to create a flattering look.
  7. Add waves or curls to your hair using a curling iron or hair straightener.
  8. Finish off your style with some hairspray to help hold the look in place.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you create a hairstyle that will make your face look smaller:

  • Consider getting a haircut that is suitable for your face shape.
  • Avoid hairstyles that are too tight or pulled back, as this can accentuate the size of your face.
  • Use a hair mask once a week to keep your hair healthy and hydrated.
  • Try using a volumizing mousse or spray to create more volume in your hair.
  • Experiment with different hairstyles to find what works best for you.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about how to make your face look smaller hairstyle:

Q: What is the best hairstyle for a round face?

A: The best hairstyle for a round face is one that adds volume to the top of the head and creates a more angular look. Consider adding layers, texture, and a side part to your hair.

Q: Can bangs make your face look smaller?

A: Yes, bangs can help to create a more balanced look and make your face appear smaller. Consider getting side-swept or curtain bangs for a flattering look.

Hair Care

It’s important to take care of your hair to keep it looking healthy and beautiful. Here are some tips to help you maintain healthy hair:

  • Wash your hair every 2-3 days to avoid stripping it of its natural oils.
  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid damaging your hair.
  • Avoid using heat tools on your hair every day, as this can cause damage.
  • Use a hair mask once a week to keep your hair hydrated and healthy.
  • Trim your hair regularly to avoid split ends.

By following these tips and techniques, you can create a hairstyle that will make your face appear smaller and more proportionate. Experiment with different hairstyles and techniques to find what works best for you.
