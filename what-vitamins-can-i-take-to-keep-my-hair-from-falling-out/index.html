What Vitamins Can I Take To Keep My Hair From Falling Out

Written by Luffy Aug 15, 2023 Â· 7 min read

As we age, our hair tends to get thinner and fall out. This can be caused by a variety of factors, including genetics, stress, hormonal changes, and poor nutrition. Fortunately, there are several vitamins that can help keep your hair healthy and prevent it from falling out. In this article, we will discuss the best vitamins for hair growth and how to incorporate them into your daily routine.

Table of Contents

  • Vitamin A
  • Vitamin B
  • Vitamin C
  • Vitamin D
  • Vitamin E
  • Vitamin H (Biotin)
  • Tips for Healthy Hair
  • FAQs

Vitamin A

Vitamin A is essential for healthy hair growth. It helps produce sebum, which is an oily substance that keeps your scalp moisturized and prevents hair from drying out. Vitamin A also aids in the production of keratin, which is a protein that makes up the structure of your hair. You can find vitamin A in foods such as sweet potatoes, carrots, spinach, kale, and liver.

Vitamin B

Vitamin B is a group of vitamins that are essential for healthy hair growth. Biotin, in particular, is a B vitamin that is crucial for hair health. It helps produce keratin, which is necessary for strong and healthy hair. B vitamins also help improve blood circulation to the scalp, which can promote hair growth. Foods rich in vitamin B include eggs, nuts, whole grains, and leafy greens.

Vitamin H (Biotin)

Biotin is a B vitamin that is essential for healthy hair growth. It helps produce keratin, which is necessary for strong and healthy hair. Biotin also improves the elasticity of your hair, which can prevent breakage and split ends. You can find biotin in foods such as eggs, nuts, whole grains, and leafy greens. You can also take biotin supplements to ensure that you are getting enough of this essential vitamin.

Vitamin C

Vitamin C is an antioxidant that helps protect your hair from damage caused by free radicals. It also helps your body absorb iron, which is necessary for healthy hair growth. Vitamin C can be found in foods such as citrus fruits, strawberries, kiwi, bell peppers, and broccoli.

Vitamin D

Vitamin D is essential for healthy hair growth. It helps regulate the growth and cycling of your hair follicles. Vitamin D also helps your scalp produce sebum, which keeps your hair moisturized and prevents it from drying out. You can get vitamin D from sunlight, fatty fish, and fortified foods such as milk and cereal.

Vitamin E

Vitamin E is an antioxidant that helps protect your hair from damage caused by free radicals. It also improves blood circulation to the scalp, which can promote hair growth. Vitamin E can be found in foods such as almonds, sunflower seeds, spinach, and avocado.

Tips for Healthy Hair

  • Avoid using hot tools on your hair, such as flat irons and curling irons.
  • Avoid using harsh chemicals on your hair, such as hair dyes and relaxers.
  • Eat a balanced diet rich in vitamins and minerals.
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends.
  • Use a wide-toothed comb to detangle your hair.
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it is wet, as this can cause breakage.

FAQs

How much biotin should I take for hair growth?

The recommended daily dose of biotin for hair growth is 2.5mg. However, it is important to talk to your doctor before taking any supplements.

Can vitamin supplements help prevent hair loss?

Vitamin supplements can help prevent hair loss if the hair loss is caused by a vitamin deficiency. However, it is important to talk to your doctor before taking any supplements.

Can stress cause hair loss?

Yes, stress can cause hair loss. When you are under stress, your body produces hormones that can cause hair follicles to enter the resting phase, which can lead to hair loss.

In conclusion, incorporating vitamins into your daily routine can help keep your hair healthy and prevent it from falling out. Vitamins A, B, C, D, and E are essential for healthy hair growth, and biotin is particularly crucial for hair health. In addition to taking vitamins, it is important to follow a healthy hair care routine and avoid using hot tools and harsh chemicals on your hair.
