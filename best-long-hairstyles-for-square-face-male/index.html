Best Long Hairstyles For Square Face Male

Written by Luffy Aug 31, 2023 · 8 min read

Are you a man with a square face shape and looking for the best long hairstyle that suits your face? Look no further! This article will provide you with a comprehensive guide to the best long hairstyles for square face males. We’ll give you tips on how to style your hair, the steps to achieve the perfect look, and hair care tips to maintain your new look.

Description

A square face shape is characterized by a strong jawline, a wide forehead, and a square chin. The best long hairstyles for square face men are those that soften the angles of the face and create an oval shape. This is achieved by keeping the hair off the forehead and adding volume to the top of the head. The ideal long hairstyle for a square face male is one that creates balance and proportion.

How to

The best way to achieve the perfect long hairstyle for a square face male is to follow these simple steps:

  1. Choose a hairstyle that suits your face shape. The best long hairstyles for square face men are those that soften the angles of the face and create an oval shape.
  2. Visit a professional hairstylist to help you achieve the desired look.
  3. Use the right hair products to style your hair. Use a hair gel, mousse or wax to add volume to the top of the head.
  4. Use a hair dryer to dry your hair and create the desired volume.
  5. Finish off the look with a hair spray to hold the style in place.

Step by Step

Here are the step-by-step instructions to achieve the best long hairstyle for a square face male:

  1. Choose a hairstyle that suits your face shape. The best long hairstyles for square face men are those that soften the angles of the face and create an oval shape.
  2. Visit a professional hairstylist to help you achieve the desired look.
  3. Wash your hair with a shampoo and conditioner that suits your hair type.
  4. Blow-dry your hair using a hair dryer and a round brush to add volume to the top of the head.
  5. Apply a hair gel, mousse or wax to the top of the head and style it according to your preference.
  6. Finish off the look with a hair spray to hold the style in place.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you maintain your long hairstyle:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a shampoo and conditioner that suits your hair type.
  • Use a hair mask once a week to nourish and hydrate your hair.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair to prevent damage.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair instead of a brush.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about the best long hairstyles for square face males:

What is the best long hairstyle for a square face male?

The best long hairstyle for a square face male is one that creates balance and proportion by softening the angles of the face and creating an oval shape. This can be achieved by keeping the hair off the forehead and adding volume to the top of the head.

How do I style my long hair if I have a square face?

To style your long hair if you have a square face, use a hair gel, mousse, or wax to add volume to the top of the head. Blow-dry your hair using a round brush to create the desired volume. Finish off the look with a hair spray to hold the style in place.

How do I maintain my long hairstyle?

To maintain your long hairstyle, wash your hair regularly with a shampoo and conditioner that suits your hair type. Use a hair mask once a week to nourish and hydrate your hair. Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends. Avoid using too much heat on your hair to prevent damage. Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair instead of a brush.

Hair Care

Here are some hair care tips to maintain your long hairstyle:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a shampoo and conditioner that suits your hair type.
  • Use a hair mask once a week to nourish and hydrate your hair.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair to prevent damage.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair instead of a brush.

By following these tips, you can maintain your long hairstyle and keep it looking great for a long time. Remember, a good hairstyle not only enhances your appearance, but it also boosts your confidence and self-esteem.
