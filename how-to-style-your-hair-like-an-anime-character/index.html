How To Style Your Hair Like An Anime Character A Step By Step Guide

Written by Petter Jun 09, 2023 Â· 7 min read

If you're a fan of anime, then you know that one of the most iconic things about anime characters is their hair. From spiky and colorful to long and flowing, anime hairstyles are often a work of art. If you've ever wondered how to style your hair like an anime character, look no further! In this article, we'll go over some tips and tricks for achieving that perfect anime hairstyle.

Table of Contents:

  • How to Style Your Hair Like an Anime Character
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Achieving the Perfect Anime Hairstyle
  • FAQs

How to Style Your Hair Like an Anime Character

Before you start styling your hair, it's important to know what kind of anime character you want to emulate. Do you want to go for a spiky, punk rock look like Goku from Dragon Ball Z? Or maybe you prefer a more elegant, flowing style like Sailor Moon? Once you've identified the character you want to emulate, it's time to start styling!

Step-by-Step Guide

Step 1: Start with clean hair. Wash and condition your hair using products that are appropriate for your hair type. For example, if you have oily hair, use a clarifying shampoo to help remove excess oil.

Step 2: Apply a heat protectant. If you're going to be using hot tools like a curling iron or straightener, it's important to protect your hair from heat damage. Apply a heat protectant spray or serum to your hair before styling.

Step 3: Blow dry your hair. Use a blow dryer to dry your hair completely. If you have curly or wavy hair, use a diffuser attachment to help define your curls or waves.

Step 4: Use hot tools to create your desired style. Depending on the anime character you're emulating, you may need to use hot tools like a curling iron or straightener to create your desired style. For example, if you're going for a spiky look like Goku, you'll need to use a straightener to create those signature spikes.

Step 5: Use styling products to hold your style in place. Once you've created your desired style, use styling products like hair spray or pomade to hold it in place.

Tips for Achieving the Perfect Anime Hairstyle

Here are some additional tips for achieving the perfect anime hairstyle:

  • Invest in high-quality hot tools. Cheap hot tools can damage your hair and won't give you the desired results.
  • Experiment with different hair products to find what works best for your hair type.
  • Don't be afraid to use hair extensions or wigs to achieve your desired look.
  • Practice makes perfect! It may take some time to perfect your anime-inspired hairstyle, so be patient and keep trying.

FAQs

Q: Can I achieve an anime-inspired hairstyle with short hair?

A: Yes! While some anime characters have long hair, there are plenty of characters with short hair as well. Just make sure to choose a character with a hairstyle that will work with your hair length.

Q: Do I need to have naturally straight hair to achieve an anime-inspired hairstyle?

A: No! While some anime characters have straight hair, there are plenty of characters with curly or wavy hair as well. You can use hot tools to straighten or curl your hair as needed.

Hair Care for Anime-Inspired Hair

Once you've achieved your perfect anime-inspired hairstyle, it's important to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and looking great. Here are some tips for hair care:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping your hair of natural oils.
  • Avoid using hot tools every day to prevent heat damage.
  • Deep condition your hair regularly to keep it hydrated and healthy.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.

With these tips and tricks, you'll be able to achieve an anime-inspired hairstyle that's sure to turn heads. Whether you're cosplaying at a convention or just want to add some anime flair to your everyday look, these styling tips will help you achieve the perfect look.
