How To Take Care Of Thick Wavy Hair Male A Complete Guide

Written by Robby Apr 25, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Thick wavy hair is a blessing and a curse. On one hand, it looks great and can add a lot of volume to your style. On the other hand, it can be difficult to manage and prone to frizz. If you're a male with thick wavy hair, you might be wondering how to take care of it to keep it looking its best. In this guide, we'll give you some tips and tricks for taking care of your thick wavy hair.

Table of Contents

  2. Step-by-Step Guide
  3. Tips for Managing Thick Wavy Hair
  4. Frequently Asked Questions
  5. Hair Care Products for Thick Wavy Hair

How to Take Care of Thick Wavy Hair Male

The first step in taking care of your thick wavy hair is to find the right haircut. You want to avoid styles that are too short or too long, as they can make your hair look unruly. Instead, opt for a mid-length cut that can help control your waves.

Next, you'll want to invest in some high-quality hair care products. Look for shampoos and conditioners that are specifically formulated for thick, wavy hair. These products will help to moisturize and tame your hair, making it easier to manage.

When it comes to styling your hair, avoid using heat tools like blow dryers and straighteners. These tools can damage your hair and cause frizz. Instead, opt for air-drying your hair or using a diffuser attachment on your blow dryer.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide to taking care of your thick wavy hair:

  1. Find the right haircut
  2. Invest in high-quality hair care products
  3. Avoid heat tools
  4. Air-dry your hair or use a diffuser attachment

Tips for Managing Thick Wavy Hair

Here are some additional tips for managing your thick wavy hair:

  • Brush your hair before showering to prevent tangles
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair after showering
  • Apply a leave-in conditioner to damp hair to help control frizz
  • Avoid washing your hair every day, as this can strip it of its natural oils

Frequently Asked Questions

Here are some frequently asked questions about taking care of thick wavy hair male:

How often should I wash my hair?

You should aim to wash your hair every 2-3 days, as washing it too often can strip it of its natural oils.

What kind of shampoo and conditioner should I use?

You should look for shampoos and conditioners that are specifically formulated for thick, wavy hair. These products will help to moisturize and tame your hair, making it easier to manage.

Can I use heat tools on my hair?

You should avoid using heat tools like blow dryers and straighteners, as they can damage your hair and cause frizz.

Hair Care Products for Thick Wavy Hair

Here are some hair care products that are great for taking care of thick wavy hair:

  • Redken Curvaceous High Foam Shampoo
  • Oribe Conditioner for Moisture & Control
  • Bumble and Bumble Curl Defining Cream
  • Moroccanoil Treatment

By following these tips and using the right hair care products, you can keep your thick wavy hair looking its best. Remember to be patient and experiment with different products and styles until you find what works best for you.
