Which Way To Curl Your Hair With Straightener

Written by Christine Jun 13, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Are you tired of using the same old curling iron to style your hair? Why not switch things up and use a straightener instead? It may seem counterintuitive, but a straightener can actually give you some gorgeous curls. In this article, we'll go over which way to curl your hair with straightener, step by step, and give you some tips and FAQ. Let's get started!

Table of Contents

  2. Step by Step Guide
  3. Tips for Perfect Curls

How to Curl Your Hair with Straightener

Before we dive into the step by step guide, let's talk about the basics of curling your hair with a straightener. The key to getting curls with a straightener is all in the technique. You'll need to twist your wrist as you glide the straightener down your hair, creating a curl in the process.

Another important thing to keep in mind is the temperature of your straightener. If it's too hot, you risk damaging your hair. If it's not hot enough, your curls may not hold. We recommend setting your straightener to a temperature between 300-350 degrees Fahrenheit.

Step by Step Guide

Now that you know the basics, let's go over the step by step guide for curling your hair with a straightener.

  1. Start by washing and drying your hair. If you have naturally curly or wavy hair, you may want to straighten it first so that your curls come out more defined.
  2. Apply a heat protectant spray to your hair to prevent damage from the straightener.
  3. Section your hair into 1-2 inch sections, depending on how thick your hair is.
  4. Take the first section of hair and clamp the straightener down at the root, then twist your wrist so that the hair wraps around the straightener.
  5. Slowly glide the straightener down the hair shaft, keeping the twist in place.
  6. Repeat this process with each section of hair, twisting the straightener in the opposite direction each time to create a more natural-looking curl.
  7. Once you've curled all of your hair, use your fingers to gently separate the curls and create a more voluminous look.
  8. Finish off with a hairspray to hold the curls in place.

Tips for Perfect Curls

Here are some additional tips to help you get perfect curls with a straightener:

  • Use a straightener with rounded edges to prevent kinks in your hair.
  • Don't hold the straightener in one spot for too long, as this can cause damage to your hair.
  • For tighter curls, use smaller sections of hair. For looser curls, use larger sections.
  • Experiment with different twisting techniques to find what works best for your hair.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about curling your hair with a straightener:

  • Can I use any type of straightener to curl my hair? Yes, you can use any straightener to curl your hair, but we recommend using one with rounded edges for the best results.
  • Do I need to use a heat protectant spray? Yes, it's important to use a heat protectant spray to prevent damage to your hair from the straightener.
  • How long will my curls last? This depends on your hair type and how well you maintain your curls. On average, curls from a straightener can last up to 2-3 days.

Hair Care Tips

Lastly, here are some hair care tips to keep your curls looking their best:

  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy and moisturized.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as this can cause damage and breakage.
  • Limit how often you wash your hair to prevent stripping it of its natural oils.
  • Invest in a good quality hairbrush to prevent tangles and breakage.

Now that you know which way to curl your hair with straightener, it's time to give it a try! Remember to be patient and practice your technique, and you'll be rocking gorgeous curls in no time.
