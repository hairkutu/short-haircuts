How To Get Sixties Hair A Step By Step Guide

Written by Daniel May 15, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Are you looking for a fun and retro hairstyle? Look no further than sixties hair! This iconic look is all about big volume, sleek shine, and bold accessories. In this guide, we'll show you how to get sixties hair from start to finish.

Table of Contents

  • How to Prep Your Hair
  • Creating Volume and Texture
  • Styling Your Hair
  • Adding Accessories
  • Tips for Maintaining Your Sixties Hair
  • Frequently Asked Questions
  • Hair Care for Sixties Hair

How to Prep Your Hair

Before you begin styling your hair, you need to prep it properly. Start by washing your hair with a volumizing shampoo and conditioner. Then, towel-dry your hair and apply a heat protectant spray. This will protect your hair from damage caused by hot styling tools.

Next, blow-dry your hair until it's about 80% dry. Use a round brush to add volume at the roots and smooth out any tangles. For extra volume, flip your head upside down while blow-drying.

Creating Volume and Texture

The key to sixties hair is big volume and texture. To achieve this, you'll need some styling products. Start by applying a volumizing mousse or spray to your roots. Then, use a texturizing spray or powder to add grip and definition to your hair.

If you have fine hair, you can also use hot rollers or a curling iron to create waves and curls. For a more natural look, wrap your hair around the barrel and hold for a few seconds before releasing.

Styling Your Hair

Now it's time to style your hair into a sixties look. Start by parting your hair down the middle or to the side. Then, use a fine-tooth comb to tease the crown of your hair. This will create a bouffant effect and add volume.

Smooth down the top layer of your hair with a brush or comb. Then, use bobby pins to secure the bouffant in place. You can also use hairspray to hold it in place.

Adding Accessories

No sixties hairstyle is complete without some fun accessories! Try adding a headband, scarf, or hair clip to your hair. You can also use a ribbon or bow to tie your hair back.

Don't be afraid to experiment with different colors and patterns. Sixties hair is all about bold and playful style!

Tips for Maintaining Your Sixties Hair

To keep your sixties hair looking great, follow these tips:

  • Use a dry shampoo to refresh your hair between washes
  • Avoid washing your hair too often, as this can strip it of natural oils
  • Protect your hair from heat damage by using a heat protectant spray
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I get sixties hair if I have short hair?

If you have short hair, you can still achieve a sixties look. Use a texturizing spray or powder to add volume and grip to your hair. Then, tease the crown of your hair to create a bouffant effect. You can also use a headband or scarf to add some retro flair.

Can I use hot rollers or a curling iron on my hair every day?

It's best to avoid using hot styling tools on your hair every day, as this can cause damage and breakage. Try to limit your use to a few times a week, and always use a heat protectant spray.

Hair Care for Sixties Hair

To keep your hair healthy and strong, follow these tips:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week

With these tips and tricks, you'll be rocking sixties hair in no time!
