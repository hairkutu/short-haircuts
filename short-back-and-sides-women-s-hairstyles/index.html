Short Back And Sides Women s Hairstyles Rocking The Bold Look

Written by Bardi Sep 03, 2023 Â· 6 min read

When it comes to hairstyles, there is an endless list of options to choose from. From long and flowing locks to short and sassy cuts, the world of hair is vast and varied. However, one style that has been gaining popularity in recent years is the short back and sides women's hairstyle.

Description

Short back and sides women's hairstyles typically involve cutting the hair short on the sides and back, while leaving the top longer. This creates a bold and edgy look that is perfect for those who want to stand out from the crowd.

How to Achieve the Look

If you are interested in getting a short back and sides women's hairstyle, there are a few things to keep in mind. First, it is important to find a stylist who is experienced in this type of cut. They will be able to give you the best advice on what will work for your hair type and face shape.

Once you have found a stylist, it is time to decide on the length and style of your cut. You may want to bring in pictures of hairstyles that you like to give your stylist an idea of what you are looking for.

When it comes to actually cutting your hair, your stylist will typically start with the sides and back, using clippers or scissors to create a short, tapered look. They will then move on to the top, leaving it longer and more textured.

Step by Step Guide

If you are interested in trying out a short back and sides women's hairstyle at home, here is a step by step guide to help you achieve the look:

  1. Start by washing and drying your hair.
  2. Part your hair where you normally would.
  3. Using clippers or scissors, cut the sides and back of your hair short, leaving the top longer.
  4. Blend the top with the sides and back using scissors or clippers.
  5. Use texturizing scissors to add texture to the top of your hair.
  6. Style your hair as desired.

Tips for Maintaining the Look

Once you have achieved your short back and sides women's hairstyle, there are a few things you can do to keep it looking great:

  • Use a texturizing spray or wax to add texture to your hair.
  • Visit your stylist regularly for touch ups and trims.
  • Consider using a deep conditioning treatment to keep your hair healthy and strong.

FAQ

Is a short back and sides women's hairstyle suitable for all hair types?

While this hairstyle can work for a variety of hair types, it is important to consult with your stylist to see if it will work for your specific hair type and face shape.

What face shapes does a short back and sides women's hairstyle work well with?

This hairstyle can work well with a variety of face shapes, but is particularly flattering on those with oval or heart-shaped faces.

How often should I visit my stylist for touch ups and trims?

It is recommended that you visit your stylist every 4-6 weeks for touch ups and trims to keep your hairstyle looking great.

Hair Care

Keeping your hair healthy and strong is important for maintaining any hairstyle, including a short back and sides women's hairstyle. Here are a few tips to keep in mind:

  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner that is appropriate for your hair type.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as this can cause damage.
  • Eat a healthy diet that is rich in vitamins and minerals to promote healthy hair growth.

With these tips and tricks, you can rock a short back and sides women's hairstyle like a pro.
