How To Know What Hair Colour Suits You Best A Complete Guide

Are you tired of your current hair colour and looking for a change? Or maybe you've never coloured your hair before and want to try something new? No matter the reason, choosing the right hair colour can be a daunting task. With so many options available, it's easy to get overwhelmed and end up with a colour that doesn't suit you. But don't worry, we've got you covered. In this article, we'll guide you on how to know what hair colour suits you best.

How to Know What Hair Colour Suits You Best

Before we dive into the details, it's important to understand that there's no one-size-fits-all solution when it comes to hair colour. What suits one person may not necessarily suit another. However, there are a few factors that can help you determine what hair colour will complement your skin tone, eye colour, and overall look.

Here are some things to consider:

Skin Tone

Your skin tone plays a crucial role in determining the right hair colour for you. There are three main skin undertones: cool, warm, and neutral. If you have cool undertones, your skin will have a pink, red, or blue hue. Warm undertones, on the other hand, have a yellow, peach, or golden hue. And if you have neutral undertones, your skin will have a mix of cool and warm tones.

If you have cool undertones, colours like platinum blonde, ash blonde, and cool brown shades will look great on you. Warm undertones, on the other hand, will look better with warm colours like golden blonde, honey brown, and copper red. Neutral undertones can pull off both warm and cool colours.

Eye Colour

Your eye colour can also play a role in determining the right hair colour for you. If you have blue or green eyes, warm hair colours like golden blonde and copper red will make your eyes pop. If you have brown or hazel eyes, cool hair colours like ash blonde and cool brown shades will complement your eye colour.

Current Hair Colour

If you're planning on going from a dark to a light hair colour or vice versa, it's important to consider the maintenance that comes with it. Going from dark to light requires bleaching, which can be damaging to your hair. On the other hand, going from light to dark requires regular touch-ups to maintain the colour.

Personality

Your personality and style can also play a role in determining the right hair colour for you. If you have a bold and edgy personality, bright colours like pink or blue might suit you. If you prefer a more classic and elegant look, natural colours like brown or blonde might be a better fit.

Step-by-Step Guide

Now that you have an idea of what to consider, here's a step-by-step guide on how to know what hair colour suits you best:

  1. Identify your skin undertone (cool, warm, or neutral).
  2. Determine your eye colour.
  3. Consider your current hair colour and the maintenance required for the new colour.
  4. Think about your personality and style.
  5. Research different hair colours and shades that complement your skin tone and eye colour.
  6. Consult with a professional hairstylist for their opinion and recommendations.
  7. Try on wigs or use virtual makeover tools to see how the colour will look on you.
  8. Take the plunge and book an appointment with a trusted hairstylist.
  9. Take care of your new hair colour with the right products and maintenance routine.

Tips for Choosing the Right Hair Colour

Here are a few additional tips to keep in mind when choosing the right hair colour:

  • Consider the season. Warm colours like red and copper are great for fall and winter, while cool colours like platinum blonde and ash brown are perfect for spring and summer.
  • Don't forget about your eyebrows. Your eyebrows should complement your hair colour, so consider getting them tinted or filling them in with a matching shade.
  • Start with subtle changes. If you're not ready for a drastic change, try highlights or lowlights to add dimension to your current hair colour.
  • Take inspiration from celebrities and influencers, but remember that what looks good on them may not necessarily look good on you.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about choosing the right hair colour:

Can I dye my hair if I have sensitive skin?

Yes, but it's important to do a patch test first to make sure you're not allergic to the hair dye. You can also look for hair dyes that are specifically formulated for sensitive skin.

How often should I touch up my roots?

It depends on the rate of your hair growth and the contrast between your natural hair colour and the dyed colour. Generally, it's recommended to touch up your roots every 4-6 weeks.

Will bleaching damage my hair?

Bleaching can be damaging to your hair, but a professional hairstylist can minimize the damage by using the right products and techniques. It's important to take care of your bleached hair with regular deep conditioning treatments and avoiding heat styling as much as possible.

Hair Care Tips for Coloured Hair

Now that you've chosen the right hair colour, it's important to take care of it to maintain its vibrancy and health. Here are some tips for caring for coloured hair:

  • Use a colour-safe shampoo and conditioner to prevent fading.
  • Avoid washing your hair too often, as this can strip the colour.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair moisturized.
  • Avoid heat styling as much as possible, as this can damage your hair and cause the colour to fade.
  • Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a UV-protective hair spray.

Choosing the right hair colour can be a fun and exciting experience. By considering your skin tone, eye colour, current hair colour, personality, and style, you can find a colour that suits you best. Remember to consult with a professional hairstylist and take care of your coloured hair with the right products and maintenance routine. Happy colouring!
