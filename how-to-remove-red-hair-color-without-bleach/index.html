How To Remove Red Hair Color Without Bleach

Written by Oliver Jun 27, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Are you tired of your red hair color and want to get rid of it without using bleach? Bleach can be harsh on your hair and scalp, so it's understandable that you want to find a gentler alternative. In this article, we'll discuss how to remove red hair color without bleach.

Description

Red hair color can be difficult to remove, especially if it's a permanent dye. However, there are a few methods that you can try to help fade the color and lighten your hair. It's important to note that these methods may not completely remove the red color, but they can help to make it less noticeable.

How to Remove Red Hair Color Without Bleach

Method 1: Vitamin C

Vitamin C is a natural and gentle way to help fade hair color. Here's how to use it:

  1. Crush 8-9 vitamin C tablets into a fine powder.
  2. Mix the powder with enough shampoo to cover your hair.
  3. Apply the mixture to your hair and let it sit for 30-60 minutes.
  4. Rinse your hair thoroughly with warm water.

You can repeat this process once a week until you see the desired results.

Method 2: Baking Soda

Baking soda is another natural ingredient that can help remove hair color. Here's how to use it:

  1. Mix equal parts baking soda and shampoo to form a paste.
  2. Apply the paste to your hair and let it sit for 5-10 minutes.
  3. Rinse your hair thoroughly with warm water.

You can repeat this process once a week until you see the desired results.

Method 3: Dish Soap

Dish soap can help to strip hair color due to its high pH level. Here's how to use it:

  1. Mix equal parts dish soap and shampoo.
  2. Apply the mixture to your hair and let it sit for 5-10 minutes.
  3. Rinse your hair thoroughly with warm water.

You can repeat this process once a week until you see the desired results.

Step by Step

Here's a step-by-step guide to removing red hair color without bleach:

  1. Choose a method that you feel comfortable with and have all the necessary ingredients.
  2. Wet your hair thoroughly with warm water.
  3. Apply the mixture to your hair, making sure to cover all areas.
  4. Let the mixture sit for the recommended amount of time.
  5. Rinse your hair thoroughly with warm water.
  6. Condition your hair to replenish moisture.

Tips

Here are a few tips to keep in mind when trying to remove red hair color without bleach:

  • Be patient - it may take a few tries before you see results.
  • Avoid using hot water as it can open up the hair cuticle and cause color to fade faster.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment to help nourish your hair after the process.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools as they can further damage your hair.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about removing red hair color without bleach:

Can I use these methods on all hair types?

Yes, these methods are safe to use on all hair types.

Will these methods completely remove the red color?

It depends on the individual and the type of hair dye used. These methods can help to fade the color and make it less noticeable, but they may not completely remove the color.

Can I use these methods on colored hair?

Yes, these methods can be used on colored hair, but they may also fade the existing color.

Hair Care

After using any of these methods, it's important to take care of your hair to prevent further damage. Here are some hair care tips:

  • Avoid using harsh shampoos and conditioners.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools, or use them on a low setting.
  • Wear a hat or use a UV protectant spray to protect your hair from the sun.

Removing red hair color without bleach can be challenging, but with patience and the right methods, you can achieve the desired results. Remember to take care of your hair after the process to keep it healthy and strong.
