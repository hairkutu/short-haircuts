Long On Top Short On Sides Men s Cut The Ultimate Guide

Written by Aprilia Aug 02, 2023 Â· 9 min read

Are you looking for a stylish and trendy haircut that is both modern and timeless? Look no further than the long on top short on sides men's cut! This versatile hairstyle has been popular for years and is still going strong in 2023. In this guide, we'll cover everything you need to know about this cut, including how to get it, how to style it, and how to take care of it.

Description

The long on top short on sides men's cut is a classic hairstyle that features longer hair on the top of the head and shorter hair on the sides and back. The length on top can vary, but it is typically at least 2-3 inches long. The sides and back are usually faded or tapered, creating a clean and polished look. This cut is known for its versatility and can be styled in a variety of ways, from slicked back to messy and textured.

How to Get the Long on Top Short on Sides Men's Cut

The long on top short on sides men's cut is a popular style that can be achieved by visiting your local barber or stylist. Here are the steps involved in getting this cut:

Step 1: Choose Your Length

The first step in getting the long on top short on sides men's cut is to decide how long you want the hair on top to be. This will depend on your personal preference and the shape of your face. If you have a round face, you may want to keep the length shorter to create a more balanced look. If you have a longer face, you can go for a longer length on top.

Step 2: Determine Your Fade

The next step is to decide on the type of fade you want on the sides and back. A fade is a gradual transition from longer to shorter hair, and there are several types to choose from, including a high fade, low fade, and mid fade. Your barber or stylist can help you choose the right fade for your face shape and hair type.

Step 3: Style Your Hair

Once you have your cut, it's time to style your hair. You can use a variety of products to achieve different looks, from pomade for a slicked-back style to texturizing spray for a more tousled look. Experiment with different products and techniques to find the style that works best for you.

Tips for Maintaining the Long on Top Short on Sides Men's Cut

Now that you have your long on top short on sides men's cut, it's important to take care of it to keep it looking its best. Here are some tips for maintaining your new hairstyle:

- Get regular trims to keep the sides and back looking neat and tidy. - Use a good quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and hydrated. - Experiment with different styling products to find the ones that work best for your hair type and style. - Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as this can damage it over time. - Consider getting a touch-up on your fade every few weeks to keep it looking fresh and clean.

FAQ

What face shapes work best with the long on top short on sides men's cut?

The long on top short on sides men's cut can work well with a variety of face shapes, but it tends to look best on those with oval or square faces. If you have a round face, you may want to keep the length on top shorter to create a more balanced look.

Can I style the long on top short on sides men's cut in different ways?

Yes! The long on top short on sides men's cut is a versatile hairstyle that can be styled in a variety of ways, from slicked back to messy and textured. Experiment with different products and techniques to find the style that works best for you.

How often do I need to get a touch-up on my fade?

This will depend on how quickly your hair grows and how well you maintain your cut. As a general rule, you should aim to get a touch-up on your fade every 2-4 weeks to keep it looking fresh and clean.

Hair Care for the Long on Top Short on Sides Men's Cut

Proper hair care is key to maintaining the long on top short on sides men's cut. Here are some tips for keeping your hair healthy and looking its best:

- Use a good quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair clean and hydrated. - Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as this can damage it over time. - Experiment with different styling products to find the ones that work best for your hair type and style. - Get regular trims to keep the sides and back looking neat and tidy. - Consider using a deep conditioning treatment once a week to nourish and hydrate your hair.

Final Thoughts

The long on top short on sides men's cut is a classic hairstyle that is both versatile and easy to maintain. With the right cut, styling products, and hair care routine, you can keep your hair looking stylish and trendy for years to come.
