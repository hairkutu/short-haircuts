How To Fake Curly Hair A Step By Step Guide

Written by Luffy Aug 18, 2023 · 7 min read

Are you tired of your straight hair and want to try something new? Curly hair can be a great way to switch up your look, but not everyone is blessed with natural curls. Fortunately, faking curly hair is easier than ever before. In this guide, we’ll show you how to fake curly hair in just a few simple steps.

Table of Contents

  • How to Fake Curly Hair
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Faking Curly Hair
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care for Faux Curls

How to Fake Curly Hair

Faking curly hair is all about creating the illusion of curls using various tools and techniques. Here are some of the most popular ways to fake curly hair:

  • Using a curling iron or wand
  • Using foam rollers or flexi rods
  • Braiding damp hair
  • Twisting damp hair
  • Using a perm kit or texturizer

It’s important to note that some of these methods may cause damage to your hair if not done correctly or if done too frequently. Be sure to read and follow all instructions carefully, and avoid overusing heat tools.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here’s a step-by-step guide to faking curly hair using a curling iron:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair. If your hair is naturally oily, you may want to use a dry shampoo to absorb excess oil before styling.
  2. Apply a heat protectant spray to your hair to minimize damage from the curling iron.
  3. Section your hair into small sections, starting at the bottom and working your way up.
  4. Take a small section of hair and wrap it around the curling iron, starting at the base and twisting as you go.
  5. Hold the curling iron in place for 10-15 seconds, then release the hair.
  6. Repeat steps 4-5 for each section of hair until your entire head is curled.
  7. Use your fingers to gently separate and loosen the curls to create a more natural look.
  8. Finish with a light hairspray to hold the curls in place.

Remember, the key to faking curly hair is to make it look as natural as possible. Don't be afraid to experiment with different techniques and tools to find what works best for you.

Tips for Faking Curly Hair

Here are some additional tips to help you achieve the perfect faux curls:

  • Use a smaller curling iron for tighter curls, and a larger curling iron for looser curls.
  • Don’t curl your hair all the way up to the roots – leave a little bit of straight hair at the top to create a more natural look.
  • For a more natural look, vary the direction of your curls – curl some sections toward your face, and others away from your face.
  • Experiment with different products, such as mousse or curl-enhancing cream, to help hold your curls in place.
  • Consider using a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer to help enhance your natural curl pattern.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about faking curly hair:

Can I fake curly hair if my hair is naturally straight?

Yes! All of the methods we’ve mentioned can be used on straight hair.

Will faking curly hair damage my hair?

Using heat tools too frequently can cause damage to your hair over time. Be sure to use a heat protectant spray and avoid overusing heat tools.

How long will my faux curls last?

This depends on the method you use and how well you maintain your curls. Using a hairspray and avoiding excessive touching or brushing can help your curls last longer.

Hair Care for Faux Curls

Whether you’re using heat tools or other methods to fake curly hair, it’s important to take good care of your hair to minimize damage and maintain healthy locks. Here are some tips:

  • Use a deep conditioner once a week to keep your hair moisturized.
  • Avoid excessive heat styling and use a heat protectant spray when using heat tools.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your hair, especially when it’s wet.
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it’s dry, as this can cause frizz and damage.
  • Consider using a silk or satin pillowcase to minimize friction and prevent frizz.

By following these tips and using the right techniques and tools, you can fake curly hair that looks natural and beautiful. So go ahead and give it a try – you might just fall in love with your new look!
