Can Anyone Wear A Pixie Cut

Written by Eveline Aug 23, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Are you thinking about getting a pixie cut but unsure if it will suit you? Well, the good news is that anyone can wear a pixie cut! It's all about finding the right style that complements your face shape and personal style. In this article, we'll guide you through the process of getting a pixie cut.

Description

A pixie cut is a short hairstyle that's typically cut above the ears and nape of the neck. It's a popular choice for people who want a low-maintenance hairstyle that still looks chic and stylish. Pixie cuts can be customized to suit different face shapes and hair types, making them a versatile option for anyone looking to switch up their look.

How to Get a Pixie Cut

If you're ready to take the plunge and get a pixie cut, here's what you need to know:

Step 1: Do Your Research

Before you head to the salon, do some research on different pixie cut styles that you like. Look for inspiration on social media and in magazines, and save some photos to show to your stylist.

Step 2: Find a Stylist

Not all stylists are created equal when it comes to pixie cuts, so it's important to find someone who has experience cutting short hair. Ask for recommendations from friends and family, or search for stylists in your area who specialize in short haircuts.

Step 3: Consult with Your Stylist

Before your stylist starts cutting, have a consultation to discuss the best pixie cut style for your face shape and hair type. They may recommend a specific cut, or work with you to customize a style that suits your preferences.

Step 4: Get the Cut

Once you've decided on a style, it's time to get the cut! Your stylist will use scissors and/or clippers to create the desired shape, and may use texturizing techniques to add volume and movement to your hair.

Tips for Choosing the Right Pixie Cut

Here are some tips to keep in mind when choosing a pixie cut:

- Consider your face shape: Different pixie cuts suit different face shapes. For example, a longer pixie cut can help balance out a round face, while a shorter cut can elongate a square face. - Think about your lifestyle: Pixie cuts are low-maintenance, but they still require some styling. Consider how much time you're willing to spend on your hair each day, and choose a style that suits your lifestyle. - Experiment with color: Pixie cuts can be a great opportunity to experiment with hair color. Talk to your stylist about adding highlights or trying a bold new shade.

FAQ

What face shape suits a pixie cut?

Pixie cuts can suit a variety of face shapes, but some cuts may be more flattering than others. For example, a longer pixie cut can help balance out a round face, while a shorter cut can elongate a square face.

Can I still style a pixie cut?

Yes! While pixie cuts are low-maintenance, they still require some styling to keep them looking their best. Talk to your stylist about the best styling products and techniques for your hair type.

Will a pixie cut suit my hair type?

Pixie cuts can be customized to suit different hair types, including fine, thick, curly, and straight hair. Talk to your stylist about the best pixie cut style for your hair type.

Hair Care for Pixie Cuts

While pixie cuts are low-maintenance, it's still important to take care of your hair to keep it looking healthy and stylish. Here are some tips:

- Wash your hair regularly: Pixie cuts can show oil and grease more easily than longer hairstyles, so make sure to wash your hair regularly. - Use the right products: Talk to your stylist about the best products for your hair type and cut. You may need a different shampoo, conditioner, and styling products than you did when you had longer hair. - Get regular trims: Pixie cuts require regular trims to maintain their shape and style. Make sure to schedule appointments with your stylist every 4-6 weeks.

With the right style and care, anyone can rock a pixie cut! So why not take the plunge and try something new?
