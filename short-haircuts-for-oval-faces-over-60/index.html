Short Haircuts For Oval Faces Over 60

Written by Petter May 09, 2023 Â· 7 min read

As we age, it is important to choose a hairstyle that flatters our face shape and enhances our natural beauty. For those with an oval face shape over the age of 60, short haircuts can be a great option. In this article, we will discuss how to choose the perfect short haircut for oval faces over 60, step by step guide, tips, frequently asked questions, and hair care.

Table of Contents

  • Step by Step Guide for Getting a Short Haircut for Oval Faces over 60

  • Hair Care for Short Haircuts for Oval Faces over 60

How to Choose the Perfect Short Haircut for Oval Faces over 60

When choosing a short haircut for an oval face over 60, it is important to consider the texture and thickness of your hair, as well as your personal style and preferences. Here are some tips to help you choose the perfect short haircut for your oval face:

  • Consider a layered bob or pixie cut to add volume and dimension to your hair.
  • Avoid hairstyles that are too blunt or one-length, as they can make your face appear longer.
  • Try a side-swept bang or a layered fringe to soften your features and add interest to your haircut.
  • Choose a style that complements your natural hair color and skin tone.

Short Haircut Ideas for Oval Faces over 60

Here are some short haircut ideas for those with oval faces over 60:

  • Textured bob
  • Pixie cut with long layers
  • Layered crop
  • Asymmetrical bob
  • Side-swept bangs with a layered bob

Step by Step Guide for Getting a Short Haircut for Oval Faces over 60

Here is a step by step guide for getting a short haircut for oval faces over 60:

  1. Choose a short haircut that flatters your face shape and suits your personal style.
  2. Consult with your hairstylist to determine the best length and style for your hair.
  3. Wash and dry your hair before your appointment.
  4. Bring pictures of the haircut you want to your appointment.
  5. Communicate with your hairstylist throughout the haircut to ensure that you are happy with the length and style.
  6. Use styling products to enhance your haircut and keep it looking great.

Tips for Maintaining Short Haircuts for Oval Faces over 60

Here are some tips for maintaining short haircuts for oval faces over 60:

  • Visit your hairstylist regularly for trims and touch-ups.
  • Use a high-quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and shiny.
  • Experiment with different styling products to find the ones that work best for your hair type and texture.
  • Consider using a hair mask or deep conditioner once a week to nourish your hair.
  • Avoid using heat tools on your hair too often, as they can cause damage and breakage.

Frequently Asked Questions about Short Haircuts for Oval Faces over 60

What short haircut is best for an oval face over 60?

A layered bob or pixie cut with long layers is a great option for those with oval faces over 60.

What styling products should I use for my short haircut?

Experiment with different styling products to find the ones that work best for your hair type and texture. Some good options include mousse, styling cream, and hairspray.

How often should I visit my hairstylist for trims?

It is recommended that you visit your hairstylist every 6-8 weeks for trims and touch-ups.

Hair Care for Short Haircuts for Oval Faces over 60

Here are some tips for caring for your short haircut:

  • Wash your hair every other day to prevent it from becoming dry and brittle.
  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and moisturized.
  • Apply a leave-in conditioner or hair serum to your hair after washing to help protect it from heat and environmental damage.
  • Avoid using hot tools on your hair too often, as they can cause damage and breakage.
  • Consider using a hair mask or deep conditioner once a week to nourish your hair.

With these tips and ideas, you can choose the perfect short haircut for your oval face over 60 and keep it looking great for years to come.
