How To Get Beach Waves With Curly Hair Without Heat

Written by Aprilia May 25, 2023 Â· 9 min read

Welcome to our step-by-step guide on how to get beach waves with curly hair without heat. This article will provide you with all the tips and tricks you need to achieve effortless, beachy waves without damaging your curly locks. So, let's dive into it!

Table of Contents

  • How to
  • Step by Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

How to

The first step to getting beach waves with curly hair without heat is to start by understanding your hair type. Curly hair tends to be more fragile and prone to breakage, so it's essential to take extra care when styling it. Here are some tips to help you achieve those beautiful beach waves:

1. Use the Right Products

Start by using a shampoo and conditioner that are specifically designed for curly hair. Look for products that contain natural ingredients like shea butter, coconut oil, and aloe vera. These ingredients help to moisturize and define your curls, making them easier to style.

2. Apply a Styling Cream

After washing your hair, apply a styling cream to damp hair. This will help to define your curls and provide hold for your beach waves. Make sure to distribute the product evenly throughout your hair, focusing on the ends.

3. Braid Your Hair

Divide your hair into two sections and braid each section tightly. Make sure to start the braid as close to your scalp as possible, and keep braiding until you reach the ends of your hair. Secure each braid with a hair tie or clip.

4. Sleep on It

Once your hair is braided, it's time to go to bed. Sleeping on your braids will help to set your beach waves naturally. In the morning, carefully undo the braids and use your fingers to gently separate the waves.

Step by Step

Now that you know the basics of how to get beach waves with curly hair without heat let's break it down step-by-step:

Step 1: Wash Your Hair

Start by washing your hair with a shampoo and conditioner that are specifically designed for curly hair. This will help to define your curls and make them easier to style.

Step 2: Apply a Styling Cream

Apply a styling cream to your damp hair, focusing on the ends. Make sure to distribute the product evenly throughout your hair.

Step 3: Divide Your Hair

Divide your hair into two sections, using a comb or your fingers. Make sure to separate your hair in a way that feels natural to you.

Step 4: Braid Your Hair

Braid each section of your hair tightly, starting as close to your scalp as possible. Keep braiding until you reach the ends of your hair. Secure each braid with a hair tie or clip.

Step 5: Sleep on It

Go to bed and sleep on your braids. This will help to set your beach waves naturally.

Step 6: Undo the Braids

In the morning, carefully undo the braids and use your fingers to gently separate the waves.

Tips

Here are a few tips to help you get the most out of your beach waves:

1. Use a Silk Pillowcase

Silk pillowcases are gentle on curly hair and help to prevent breakage. They also reduce frizz and help to maintain your beach waves.

2. Don't Overdo It

Remember, less is more when it comes to styling curly hair. Over-styling can lead to breakage and damage. Stick to simple, low-maintenance styles like beach waves.

3. Protect Your Hair

Always protect your hair from the sun, wind, and other environmental factors that can damage your hair. Wear a hat or use a leave-in conditioner with SPF.

FAQ

Can I use a diffuser to get beach waves with curly hair?

Yes, you can use a diffuser to get beach waves with curly hair. However, using heat can damage your hair over time. If you want to avoid heat damage, try the braiding method described in this article.

How long will my beach waves last?

Your beach waves should last for a few days, depending on your hair type and the products you use. To maintain your waves, avoid brushing your hair too much and use a leave-in conditioner to keep your curls hydrated.

Can I use this method on short hair?

Yes, you can use this method on short hair. However, you may need to use smaller braids to achieve the desired effect.

Hair Care

It's essential to take care of your hair to maintain healthy, beautiful curls. Here are a few tips:

1. Avoid Heat

Avoid using heat styling tools like flat irons and curling irons. Heat can damage your hair and cause breakage. If you want to style your hair without heat, try the braiding method described in this article.

2. Use the Right Products

Use products that are specifically designed for curly hair. Look for natural ingredients like shea butter, coconut oil, and aloe vera. These ingredients help to moisturize and define your curls.

3. Protect Your Hair

Protect your hair from the sun, wind, and other environmental factors that can damage your hair. Wear a hat or use a leave-in conditioner with SPF.

We hope this article has been helpful in teaching you how to get beach waves with curly hair without heat. Remember, it's essential to take care of your hair to maintain healthy, beautiful curls. So, follow our tips and tricks, and enjoy your new beachy waves!
