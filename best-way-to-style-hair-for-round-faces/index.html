Best Way To Style Hair For Round Faces

Written by Aprilia Jul 18, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Are you struggling to find the perfect hairstyle for your round face shape? Look no further! In this article, we will cover the best way to style hair for round faces, including how to, step-by-step instructions, tips, frequently asked questions, and hair care advice. Read on to discover the perfect hairstyle for your face shape!

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How To
  • Step-By-Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ

Description

A round face shape is characterized by full cheeks and a curved jawline. The best way to style hair for round faces is to create volume at the crown and elongate the face with layers and angles. Avoid hairstyles that add width to the face, like blunt bangs or straight bobs. Instead, opt for hairstyles that add height and length, like long layers, side-swept bangs, and angled bobs.

How To

The best way to style hair for round faces is to create volume at the crown and elongate the face with layers and angles. Here are some tips to achieve the perfect hairstyle for your round face shape:

1. Add Volume

The first step in styling hair for a round face is to add volume at the crown. This can be achieved by blow-drying hair upside down or using a volumizing mousse or spray. Avoid adding volume to the sides of the face, as this will only make the face appear wider.

2. Create Angles

Angles help to elongate the face, so be sure to add layers and angles to your hairstyle. This can be done with a layered haircut, an angled bob, or face-framing layers. Avoid blunt cuts that add width to the face.

3. Side-Swept Bangs

Side-swept bangs are perfect for round faces, as they help to create angles and elongate the face. Avoid blunt bangs, as these will only make the face appear rounder.

Step-By-Step

Here is a step-by-step guide to styling hair for a round face:

1. Wash and Condition

Start by washing and conditioning your hair with products that are suitable for your hair type. Rinse thoroughly and towel dry.

2. Apply Products

Apply a volumizing mousse or spray to the roots of your hair and work through to the ends. Blow-dry hair upside down to add volume at the crown.

3. Add Layers

Add layers to your hair with a layered haircut or an angled bob. Face-framing layers are also a great option for round faces.

4. Side-Swept Bangs

Add side-swept bangs to your hairstyle to create angles and elongate the face. Avoid blunt bangs, as these will only make the face appear rounder.

Tips

Here are some additional tips for styling hair for a round face:

  • Use a round brush when blow-drying hair to add volume at the crown.
  • Avoid hairstyles that add width to the face, like blunt bangs or straight bobs.
  • Use a heat protectant spray to protect hair from heat damage when using styling tools.
  • Consider adding highlights or lowlights to your hair to create dimension and add depth.

FAQ

What hairstyles should I avoid if I have a round face?

Avoid hairstyles that add width to the face, like blunt bangs or straight bobs. Instead, opt for hairstyles that add height and length, like long layers, side-swept bangs, and angled bobs.

What is the best way to add volume to hair for a round face?

The best way to add volume to hair for a round face is to blow-dry hair upside down or use a volumizing mousse or spray. Avoid adding volume to the sides of the face, as this will only make the face appear wider.

Hair Care

Proper hair care is essential for maintaining healthy hair and achieving the perfect hairstyle for your face shape. Here are some hair care tips:

  • Use products that are suitable for your hair type.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as this can cause heat damage.
  • Protect hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a UV protectant spray.
  • Trim hair regularly to prevent split ends and breakage.

Now that you know the best way to style hair for round faces, it's time to book your next hair appointment and try out a new hairstyle! Remember to add volume at the crown, create angles with layers and angles, and avoid hairstyles that add width to the face. With these tips and tricks, you'll be sure to find the perfect hairstyle for your face shape.
