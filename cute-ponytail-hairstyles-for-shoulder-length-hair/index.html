Cute Ponytail Hairstyles For Shoulder Length Hair

Written by Luffy Jul 25, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Are you tired of the same old boring hairstyle for your shoulder length hair? Why not try a cute ponytail hairstyle? Ponytails are not just for the gym or a casual day out, they can also be a chic and stylish option for any occasion. Here are some easy and cute ponytail hairstyles for shoulder length hair that you can try out.

Table of Content

  • Description
  • How to
  • Step by step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

Ponytails are a versatile hairstyle that can be dressed up or down depending on the occasion. They are also a great option for those with shoulder length hair who want to keep their hair out of their face. There are many different cute ponytail hairstyles to choose from, including the sleek and chic low ponytail, the messy high ponytail, and the braided ponytail.

How to

To achieve a cute ponytail hairstyle for shoulder length hair, you will need a few basic hair tools such as a brush, hair ties, and bobby pins. You may also want to use some hair styling products such as hairspray or texturizing spray to help hold your ponytail in place.

Step by step

Here is a step by step guide on how to achieve some of the most popular cute ponytail hairstyles for shoulder length hair:

Low Ponytail

  1. Brush your hair to remove any tangles.
  2. Gather your hair at the nape of your neck and secure with a hair tie.
  3. If desired, you can wrap a small section of hair around the hair tie to hide it.
  4. Finish with hairspray to hold your ponytail in place.

Messy High Ponytail

  1. Brush your hair to remove any tangles.
  2. Gather your hair at the crown of your head and secure with a hair tie.
  3. Using your fingers, gently tease the hair at the crown of your head to create volume.
  4. If desired, you can pull out a few strands of hair around your face to create a more relaxed look.
  5. Finish with hairspray to hold your ponytail in place.

Braided Ponytail

  1. Brush your hair to remove any tangles.
  2. Gather your hair at the nape of your neck and secure with a hair tie.
  3. Braid your ponytail and secure the end with another hair tie.
  4. If desired, you can gently pull on the strands of the braid to create a more relaxed look.
  5. Finish with hairspray to hold your ponytail in place.

Tips

Here are some additional tips to help you achieve the perfect cute ponytail hairstyle for your shoulder length hair:

  • Experiment with different hair ties to find the one that works best for you. Some people prefer elastic hair ties, while others prefer scrunchies or ribbon ties.
  • Use bobby pins to secure any flyaway hairs or to create additional volume.
  • Try adding accessories such as hair clips or bows to dress up your ponytail.

FAQ

What if my hair is too thick for a ponytail?

If your hair is too thick for a regular ponytail, try dividing your hair into two sections and creating two ponytails instead.

Can I wear a ponytail to a formal event?

Yes, you can dress up a ponytail by adding accessories or by creating a sleek and chic low ponytail.

How do I prevent my hair from getting tangled in a ponytail?

Be sure to brush your hair thoroughly before putting it up in a ponytail. You can also apply a leave-in conditioner or detangling spray to help prevent tangles.

Hair Care

To keep your hair healthy and looking its best, be sure to follow a regular hair care routine. This may include washing and conditioning your hair regularly, using a heat protectant when styling your hair with heat tools, and getting regular trims to prevent split ends.

Now that you know how to achieve cute ponytail hairstyles for shoulder length hair, it's time to start experimenting and finding the perfect ponytail style for you. Whether you're headed to the office or a night out on the town, a ponytail is a great option for any occasion.
