Is Blonde Hair Unprofessional Debunking The Myth

Written by Aprilia Jul 19, 2023 Â· 8 min read

As we enter the year 2023, the debate about whether blonde hair is unprofessional still lingers. This topic has been a hot button issue for years, with people on both sides of the argument. In this blog post, we will explore the question of whether blonde hair is unprofessional and provide answers to some of the most frequently asked questions about the topic. We will also provide tips and hair care advice for those who choose to dye their hair blonde.

Table of Contents

  1. Description
  2. How to Dye Your Hair Blonde
  3. Step-by-Step Guide to Dyeing Your Hair Blonde
  4. Tips for Maintaining Blonde Hair
  5. FAQ About Blonde Hair in the Workplace
  6. Hair Care Advice for Blonde Hair

Description

Blonde hair has long been associated with youth, beauty, and femininity. However, some people believe that blonde hair is unprofessional in the workplace. This belief is based on the idea that blonde hair is attention-grabbing and can be distracting in professional settings. It is also believed that blonde hair is not taken seriously and can lead to people being viewed as less competent.

Despite these beliefs, there is no evidence to support the idea that blonde hair is unprofessional. Hair color is a personal choice and should not be a factor in how someone is perceived in the workplace. It is important to remember that professionalism is about how a person conducts themselves, not their appearance.

How to Dye Your Hair Blonde

If you are considering dyeing your hair blonde, there are a few things you should consider before taking the plunge. First, it is important to choose a shade of blonde that complements your skin tone. If you have warm undertones, a honey or golden blonde might be a good choice. If you have cool undertones, a platinum or ash blonde might be a better fit.

Before dyeing your hair, it is important to do a strand test to ensure that you are not allergic to the product. You should also make sure that your hair is in good condition and free from any damage. If your hair is damaged, it is recommended that you wait until it is healthy before dyeing it.

Step-by-Step Guide to Dyeing Your Hair Blonde

1. Choose a shade of blonde that complements your skin tone.
2. Do a strand test to ensure that you are not allergic to the product.
3. Make sure that your hair is in good condition and free from any damage.
4. Apply the dye according to the instructions on the package.
5. Rinse the dye out thoroughly and apply a conditioner.
6. Style your hair as desired.

Tips for Maintaining Blonde Hair

Once you have dyed your hair blonde, it is important to take care of it to ensure that it stays healthy and vibrant. Here are some tips for maintaining blonde hair:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to prevent fading.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Use a hair mask once a week to nourish your hair.
  • Avoid swimming in chlorinated water, as it can cause your hair to turn green.

FAQ About Blonde Hair in the Workplace

Q: Is blonde hair unprofessional?
A: No, hair color is a personal choice and should not be a factor in how someone is perceived in the workplace.

Q: Will blonde hair make me look less competent?
A: No, competence is not based on appearance.

Q: Should I dye my hair blonde for a job interview?
A: It is not necessary to dye your hair for a job interview. You should focus on your qualifications and experience.

Hair Care Advice for Blonde Hair

If you choose to dye your hair blonde, it is important to take care of it to ensure that it stays healthy and vibrant. Here are some hair care tips for blonde hair:

  • Use a purple shampoo to prevent your hair from turning brassy.
  • Avoid using hot water to wash your hair, as it can cause your hair to become dry and brittle.
  • Use a leave-in conditioner to keep your hair hydrated.
  • Avoid over-washing your hair, as it can strip it of its natural oils.

In conclusion, blonde hair is not unprofessional in the workplace. Hair color is a personal choice and should not be a factor in how someone is perceived. If you choose to dye your hair blonde, it is important to take care of it to ensure that it stays healthy and vibrant. By following the tips and hair care advice outlined in this blog post, you can maintain beautiful blonde hair that looks and feels great.
