Can You Train Your Hair To Lay Back

Written by Frank Jun 12, 2023 Â· 6 min read

If you're tired of your hair always sticking up or standing on end, you may be wondering if it's possible to train your hair to lay back. The good news is that with a little effort and the right techniques, you can train your hair to lay back and achieve the sleek, polished look you're after.

Description

Training your hair to lay back involves using specific styling techniques and products to encourage your hair to grow and lay in a certain direction. This can be particularly helpful for people with thick or unruly hair, as it can help to keep your hair under control and looking its best.

How to Train Your Hair to Lay Back

Step 1: Start with a Fresh Cut

If you're serious about training your hair to lay back, the first step is to start with a fresh cut. This will ensure that your hair is all the same length and is in the best possible condition for styling.

Step 2: Use the Right Products

To train your hair to lay back, you'll need to invest in some high-quality styling products. Look for products that can help to smooth and control your hair, such as a good quality pomade or hair gel.

Step 3: Blow Dry Your Hair

Once you've applied your styling products, use a blow dryer to help encourage your hair to lay back. Use a brush to smooth your hair as you blow dry, and direct the air flow towards the back of your head.

Step 4: Train Your Hair with a Comb

As your hair begins to grow, use a comb to help train it to lay back. Start by combing your hair straight back from your forehead, and then use your fingers to push it back even further. Repeat this process every day, gradually training your hair to lay back further and further.

Tips for Training Your Hair to Lay Back

  • Be patient. It can take several weeks or even months to train your hair to lay back.
  • Use a good quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and strong.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as this can damage it and make it harder to style.
  • Consider getting a professional haircut or styling advice to help you achieve the look you're after.

FAQ

Can you train any hair type to lay back?

Yes, with the right techniques and products, you can train any hair type to lay back.

How long does it take to train your hair to lay back?

It can take several weeks or even months to train your hair to lay back, depending on your hair type and how often you practice the techniques.

Do I need to use specific products to train my hair to lay back?

Yes, using the right styling products can make a big difference when it comes to training your hair to lay back. Look for products that can help to smooth and control your hair, such as a good quality pomade or hair gel.

Hair Care Tips

  • Wash your hair regularly to keep it clean and healthy.
  • Avoid using hot tools on your hair too often, as this can cause damage and breakage.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair moisturized.
  • Eat a healthy diet rich in vitamins and minerals to promote hair growth and strength.

With a little patience and the right techniques, you can train your hair to lay back and achieve the sleek, polished look you're after. Remember to take good care of your hair by using quality products and practicing good hair care habits, and you'll be sure to see results in no time.
