Men s Disconnected Undercut Slicked Back A Timeless Style

Written by Oliver Jun 13, 2023 Â· 6 min read

If you're a man who wants to look stylish and sophisticated, there's no hairstyle quite like the disconnected undercut slicked back. This classic style has been a favorite among men for decades, and it shows no signs of going out of fashion anytime soon.

Description

The disconnected undercut slicked back is a hairstyle that features buzzed or shaved sides with longer hair on top. The hair on top is typically slicked back with a pomade or gel to create a sleek and polished look. The disconnected aspect comes from the contrast between the short sides and the longer top, which creates a dramatic and eye-catching effect.

How to Achieve the Look

If you're interested in trying out this style for yourself, here's how to achieve the perfect disconnected undercut slicked back:

Step 1: Choose the Right Cut

The first step is to get the right cut. You'll need to have the sides of your hair buzzed or shaved, while leaving the hair on top longer. The length of the hair on top is up to you, but it should be long enough to slick back.

Step 2: Apply Product

Once you have the right cut, it's time to apply product. Pomade or gel works best for this style. Apply a small amount of product to your hands and rub them together to distribute it evenly.

Step 3: Slick Back the Hair

Next, use your hands to slick back the hair on top. Start at the front and work your way back, smoothing the hair as you go. You may need to use a comb to help with this step.

Step 4: Finish with Hairspray

Once you've slicked back your hair, finish with a light mist of hairspray to hold the style in place.

Tips for Maintaining the Look

Now that you've achieved the perfect disconnected undercut slicked back, here are some tips for maintaining the look:

  • Visit your barber regularly to keep the sides short and the top trimmed.
  • Wash your hair regularly, but avoid using too much shampoo, as it can strip the hair of its natural oils.
  • Use a high-quality pomade or gel to keep your hair in place.
  • Avoid touching your hair too much throughout the day, as this can cause the style to loosen.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about the disconnected undercut slicked back:

What face shape is best suited for this style?

The disconnected undercut slicked back is a versatile style that can work for many face shapes, but it tends to look best on men with square or oval faces.

What hair type is best suited for this style?

Men with thick, straight hair tend to have the easiest time achieving this style, but it can work for men with other hair types as well.

How long does it take to achieve this style?

The time it takes to achieve this style will depend on the length and texture of your hair, as well as your skill level. With practice, you should be able to achieve the look in just a few minutes.

Hair Care

To keep your hair looking healthy and shiny, it's important to take good care of it. Here are some tips for maintaining healthy hair:

  • Wash your hair regularly, but not too often.
  • Avoid using hot tools like straighteners or curling irons too frequently, as they can damage your hair.
  • Eat a balanced diet that includes plenty of protein, which is essential for healthy hair.
  • Drink plenty of water to keep your hair hydrated.
  • Avoid using harsh chemicals on your hair, like bleach or hair dye.

With these tips and tricks, you'll be able to achieve and maintain the perfect disconnected undercut slicked back. So why not give it a try and see how you look?
