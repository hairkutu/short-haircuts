Short Hairstyles For Thin Hair And Round Face 2019 A Complete Guide

Written by Petter Sep 03, 2023 Â· 7 min read

If you have thin hair and a round face, finding the right hairstyle that flatters your features can be a bit of a challenge. However, with the right haircut, you can create an illusion of volume and add definition to your face. In this article, we will discuss some of the best short hairstyles for thin hair and round face in 2019.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to Choose the Right Hairstyle
  • Step by Step Guide to Styling
  • Tips for Maintaining Your Hairstyle
  • Frequently Asked Questions
  • Hair Care Tips for Thin Hair

Description

Short hairstyles are versatile and can be customized to suit any face shape. For round faces, it's important to choose a hairstyle that creates the illusion of length and definition. A short haircut can help to elongate your face and draw attention to your features. Additionally, a short hairstyle is easy to maintain and style, making it perfect for those with thin hair.

How to Choose the Right Hairstyle

When choosing a hairstyle for a round face and thin hair, it's important to consider the following factors:

  • The length of the hairstyle
  • The texture of your hair
  • The shape of your face
  • Your personal style and preferences

Short hairstyles that are layered and textured can add volume and dimension to thin hair. Additionally, hairstyles that are chin-length or shorter can help to elongate a round face. Some of the best short hairstyles for thin hair and round face in 2019 include:

Pixie Cut

A pixie cut is a short and cropped hairstyle that is perfect for those with thin hair. This hairstyle is low maintenance and can be customized to suit any face shape. Additionally, a pixie cut can add volume and texture to thin hair, making it look fuller and more voluminous.

Bob Cut

A bob cut is a classic and timeless hairstyle that works well for those with thin hair and a round face. This hairstyle is versatile and can be customized to suit any face shape. Additionally, a bob cut can add volume and texture to thin hair, making it look fuller and more voluminous.

Asymmetrical Cut

An asymmetrical cut is a modern and edgy hairstyle that works well for those with thin hair and a round face. This hairstyle features a longer length on one side of the head, which can help to elongate a round face. Additionally, an asymmetrical cut can add volume and texture to thin hair, making it look fuller and more voluminous.

Step by Step Guide to Styling

Styling short hair can be quick and easy. Follow these simple steps to achieve a polished and put-together look:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair
  2. Apply a volumizing mousse or spray to your roots
  3. Blow dry your hair using a round brush, lifting at the roots to create volume
  4. If desired, use a flat iron to straighten any unruly pieces
  5. Finish with a light hold hairspray

Tips for Maintaining Your Hairstyle

To keep your short hairstyle looking its best, follow these tips:

  • Get regular trims to maintain your haircut
  • Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to add texture and fullness to your hair
  • Avoid using heavy styling products, as they can weigh down your hair
  • Experiment with different styling techniques to find what works best for you

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the best short hairstyle for a round face and thin hair?

Some of the best short hairstyles for thin hair and round face in 2019 include the pixie cut, bob cut, and asymmetrical cut.

Can I add highlights to my short hair?

Yes, adding highlights to your short hair can add dimension and texture.

How often should I get my hair trimmed?

It's recommended to get your hair trimmed every 6-8 weeks to maintain your haircut.

Hair Care Tips for Thin Hair

To keep your thin hair looking healthy and voluminous, follow these tips:

  • Avoid using heavy styling products, as they can weigh down your hair
  • Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to add texture and fullness to your hair
  • Avoid using hot tools on a daily basis, as they can damage your hair
  • Eat a healthy diet and stay hydrated to promote hair growth and health

With the right haircut and styling techniques, you can create a flattering and stylish look that enhances your features. Experiment with different hairstyles and have fun with your hair!
