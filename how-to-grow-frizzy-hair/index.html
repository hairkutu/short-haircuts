How To Grow Frizzy Hair Tips And Tricks In 2023

Written by Aprilia Jun 22, 2023 · 8 min read

Frizzy hair can be a major frustration for many people, but it doesn't have to be a permanent problem. If you're looking to grow your frizzy hair, there are a few tips and tricks you can try to encourage healthy growth and minimize frizz. In this article, we'll explore how to grow frizzy hair, from caring for your scalp to choosing the right hair products.

How to Grow Frizzy Hair: A Step-by-Step Guide

Step 1: Start with a Healthy Scalp

A healthy scalp is essential for healthy hair growth, so start by taking care of your scalp. This means keeping it clean and free of buildup, which can clog hair follicles and slow down growth. Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner that is designed for your hair type, and avoid over-washing or using harsh chemicals that can strip your hair of its natural oils.

Step 2: Nourish Your Hair

To encourage hair growth and minimize frizz, it's important to nourish your hair from the inside out. This means eating a healthy diet that is rich in vitamins and minerals that support healthy hair growth, such as biotin, vitamin C, and iron. You can also try supplements or hair growth products that are designed to support healthy hair growth.

Step 3: Protect Your Hair

Frizzy hair is often prone to breakage and damage, so it's important to protect your hair from heat, sun, and other environmental factors. Use a heat protectant spray before using hot tools, and avoid over-styling or pulling your hair too tight. Consider wearing a hat or using a scarf to protect your hair from the sun, and avoid using harsh chemicals like bleach or relaxers that can damage your hair.

Step 4: Choose the Right Products

To minimize frizz and encourage healthy hair growth, it's important to choose the right hair products. Look for products that are designed for your hair type and texture, and avoid products that contain harsh chemicals or alcohol that can dry out your hair. Consider using a leave-in conditioner or hair oil to nourish your hair and minimize frizz, and avoid using too much product, which can weigh your hair down and make it look greasy.

Tips for Growing Frizzy Hair

In addition to following the steps above, there are a few tips and tricks you can try to encourage healthy hair growth and minimize frizz.

Tip 1: Get Regular Trims

Regular trims can help to prevent split ends and breakage, which can slow down hair growth and make your hair look frizzy. Aim to get a trim every 6-8 weeks, or as needed to keep your hair healthy and strong.

Tip 2: Use a Silk Pillowcase

A silk pillowcase can help to minimize frizz and prevent breakage while you sleep. Silk is a smooth and gentle material that won't pull or snag your hair, which can help to keep it healthy and strong.

Tip 3: Experiment with Hairstyles

Experimenting with different hairstyles can help to minimize frizz and give your hair a healthy boost. Try braiding your hair before bed to prevent tangles and breakage, or wearing your hair up in a loose bun or ponytail to protect it from damage.

Frequently Asked Questions

How long does it take to grow frizzy hair?

The length of time it takes to grow frizzy hair can vary depending on many factors, including your hair type, genetics, and lifestyle. In general, hair grows about half an inch per month, so it may take several months or even years to grow longer, healthier hair.

Can I use hair extensions to grow my frizzy hair?

Hair extensions can be a great way to add length and volume to your hair, but they won't necessarily help your hair grow. In fact, using extensions too often or too tightly can cause damage and breakage, which can slow down hair growth and make your frizz worse.

How often should I wash my frizzy hair?

The frequency of washing your frizzy hair depends on your hair type and lifestyle. In general, it's best to wash your hair every 2-3 days to keep it clean and healthy. If you have an oily scalp or sweat a lot, you may need to wash your hair more often.

Hair Care for Frizzy Hair

Caring for frizzy hair can be a challenge, but with the right tools and techniques, you can keep your hair healthy and strong. Follow the steps above to encourage healthy hair growth and minimize frizz, and experiment with different hairstyles and products to find what works best for you. With a little patience and care, you can grow beautiful, healthy hair that you'll be proud to show off.
