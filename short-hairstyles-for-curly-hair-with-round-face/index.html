Short Hairstyles For Curly Hair With Round Face A Guide

Written by Daniel Aug 16, 2023 Â· 7 min read

If you have curly hair and a round face, finding the perfect hairstyle can be a challenge. You want to find a style that accentuates your best features while also complementing your natural curls. In this guide, we will walk you through some of the best short hairstyles for curly hair with a round face. Whether you're looking for a bold new look or just want to switch things up, we've got you covered.

Description

Curly hair is a beautiful and versatile hair type that can be styled in a variety of ways. However, when it comes to finding the perfect hairstyle for a round face shape, it can be challenging. A round face shape is characterized by a circular shape with equal length and width. The goal is to find a hairstyle that elongates the face and creates the illusion of a more oval shape. In this guide, we will explore some of the best short hairstyles for curly hair with a round face.

How to

When choosing a short hairstyle for curly hair with a round face, keep in mind that you want to create height and volume at the crown of your head. This will elongate your face and create a more balanced look. Here are some steps to follow:

Step 1: Determine your face shape

Before you start choosing a hairstyle, determine your face shape. A round face shape is characterized by a circular shape with equal length and width.

Step 2: Choose a short hairstyle

Choose a short hairstyle that creates height and volume at the crown of your head. This will elongate your face and create a more balanced look. Some great options include a pixie cut, a layered bob, or a curly shag.

Step 3: Consider bangs

Bangs can also help elongate a round face shape. Consider a side-swept bang or a wispy fringe to add some dimension to your hairstyle.

Tips

Here are some additional tips to keep in mind when choosing a short hairstyle for curly hair with a round face:

  • Avoid blunt cuts that end at the chin, as this can make a round face shape appear wider.
  • Consider highlights or lowlights to add dimension to your hair.
  • Avoid hairstyles that are too short, as this can make a round face shape appear even rounder.
  • Experiment with different styles and find what works best for you.

FAQ

What is the best short hairstyle for curly hair with a round face?

Some great options include a pixie cut, a layered bob, or a curly shag. These styles create height and volume at the crown of your head, which elongates a round face shape.

Should I get bangs if I have a round face shape?

Bangs can help elongate a round face shape, but it's important to choose the right type of bangs. Consider a side-swept bang or a wispy fringe to add some dimension to your hairstyle.

How do I style my short curly hair?

To style short curly hair, start by applying a curl-defining product to damp hair. Use your fingers to scrunch your curls and let them air dry or diffuse with a blow dryer. Once your hair is dry, use a curling iron to touch up any areas that need extra definition.

Hair Care

When you have curly hair, it's important to take good care of it to keep it healthy and looking its best. Here are some tips:

  • Avoid over-washing your hair, as this can strip it of its natural oils.
  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to keep your curls moisturized.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as this can damage your hair.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.

With these tips and hairstyles, you can find the perfect short hairstyle for curly hair with a round face. Don't be afraid to experiment and try new looks until you find the one that works best for you.
