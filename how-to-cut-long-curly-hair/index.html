How To Cut Long Curly Hair A Step By Step Guide

Written by Petter May 18, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Long curly hair can be a beautiful feature, but it can also be difficult to manage. One way to make your long curly hair more manageable is to cut it. Cutting curly hair can be tricky, but with the right tools and techniques, you can achieve a beautiful and manageable hairstyle. In this article, we will provide you with a step-by-step guide on how to cut long curly hair.

Table of Contents

  • Tools You'll Need
  • Preparation
  • Sectioning Your Hair
  • Cutting Your Hair
  • Finishing Touches
  • Tips for Cutting Long Curly Hair
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care After Cutting Long Curly Hair

Tools You'll Need

Before you start cutting your long curly hair, you'll need to make sure you have the right tools. Here are the tools you'll need:

  • Sharp hairdressing scissors
  • Sectioning clips
  • Comb
  • Spray bottle filled with water

Preparation

Before you start cutting your long curly hair, you should make sure it's clean and dry. You should also have an idea of what type of cut you want. You can find inspiration online or in magazines. If you're not sure what cut would suit you, you can ask your hairdresser for advice.

Sectioning Your Hair

Once your hair is dry and clean, you should section it. This will make it easier to cut and ensure that you don't miss any sections. You should start by parting your hair down the middle and then dividing it into four sections. You can do this by creating a part down the middle of your head and then creating a part from your ears to the middle part.

Cutting Your Hair

Now that your hair is sectioned, you can start cutting it. It's best to start with the back sections and work your way forward. You should take small sections of hair and cut them at an angle. This will give your hair a softer and more natural look. You should also make sure that you're cutting your hair evenly on both sides.

Finishing Touches

Once you've finished cutting your hair, you can add some finishing touches. You can use your scissors to add texture to your hair by cutting into the ends. You can also use a razor to create a softer look. It's important to be careful when using a razor, as it can damage your hair if used incorrectly.

Tips for Cutting Long Curly Hair

Cutting long curly hair can be challenging, but these tips will make it easier:

  • Use sharp scissors
  • Take small sections of hair
  • Cut at an angle
  • Start with the back sections
  • Be careful when using a razor

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about cutting long curly hair:

  • How often should I cut my long curly hair?
  • How can I prevent my long curly hair from getting tangled?
  • What should I do if I make a mistake while cutting my long curly hair?

Hair Care After Cutting Long Curly Hair

After cutting your long curly hair, it's important to take care of it. You should use a shampoo and conditioner that's designed for curly hair, as this will help to keep your hair moisturized and prevent frizz. You should also avoid using heat styling tools, as these can damage your hair. Instead, you can use a diffuser to dry your hair or let it air dry. You can also use a leave-in conditioner or hair oil to keep your hair hydrated.

Now that you know how to cut long curly hair, you can achieve a beautiful and manageable hairstyle. With the right tools and techniques, cutting long curly hair can be easy and fun. Just remember to take your time and be patient, and you'll have gorgeous curls in no time!
