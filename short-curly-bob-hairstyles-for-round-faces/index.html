Short Curly Bob Hairstyles For Round Faces

Written by Christine Apr 09, 2023 Â· 7 min read

If you have a round face and curly hair, you may be hesitant to try a short bob hairstyle. However, there are many beautiful and flattering options that can enhance your features and show off your curls. In this article, we will discuss some of the best short curly bob hairstyles for round faces, as well as tips for styling and maintaining them.

Description

A short curly bob is a hairstyle that is cut above the shoulders and has natural curls or waves. This style can be customizable to fit any face shape, including round faces. By adding layers or length, you can create a look that complements your features and highlights your best qualities. The short curly bob is perfect for those who want a low-maintenance yet stylish hairstyle.

How to Style

Styling a short curly bob is easy, and it requires minimal effort. After washing your hair, apply a styling cream or mousse to damp hair. This will help to define your curls and keep them in place. You can then either air-dry your hair or use a diffuser attachment on your blow dryer. Once your hair is dry, you can use a curling iron to touch up any areas that need more definition.

Step by Step

Step 1: Wash your hair

Start by washing your hair with a hydrating shampoo and conditioner. This will help to keep your curls moisturized and prevent frizz.

Step 2: Apply styling product

Apply a styling cream or mousse to damp hair. This will help to define your curls and keep them in place.

Step 3: Air-dry or use a diffuser

Either air-dry your hair or use a diffuser attachment on your blow dryer. This will help to enhance your natural curls and prevent frizz.

Step 4: Touch up with a curling iron

Once your hair is dry, use a curling iron to touch up any areas that need more definition. Be sure to use a heat protectant spray before using the curling iron.

Tips

  • Use a hydrating shampoo and conditioner to keep your curls moisturized.
  • Apply a styling cream or mousse to damp hair to define your curls.
  • Air-dry your hair or use a diffuser attachment on your blow dryer to enhance your natural curls and prevent frizz.
  • Use a curling iron to touch up any areas that need more definition.
  • Be sure to use a heat protectant spray before using any heat styling tools.

FAQ

What is the best length for a short curly bob?

The best length for a short curly bob is above the shoulders. This length will help to enhance your features and show off your curls.

Can a short curly bob be customized for a round face?

Yes, a short curly bob can be customized for a round face. By adding layers or length, you can create a look that complements your features and highlights your best qualities.

How can I maintain my short curly bob?

To maintain your short curly bob, use a hydrating shampoo and conditioner, apply a styling cream or mousse to damp hair, air-dry your hair or use a diffuser attachment on your blow dryer, and use a curling iron to touch up any areas that need more definition.

Hair Care

To keep your short curly bob looking its best, it is important to take care of your hair. Use a hydrating shampoo and conditioner to keep your curls moisturized and prevent frizz. Apply a styling cream or mousse to damp hair to define your curls. Air-dry your hair or use a diffuser attachment on your blow dryer to enhance your natural curls and prevent frizz. Use a curling iron to touch up any areas that need more definition, but be sure to use a heat protectant spray before using any heat styling tools. Finally, be sure to get regular trims to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.
