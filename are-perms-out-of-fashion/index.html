Are Perms Out Of Fashion A Comprehensive Guide To The Trend

Written by Petter Jul 24, 2023 · 7 min read

Perms have been a popular hairstyle for decades, but in recent years, the trend seems to have lost its appeal. Many people are left wondering, "Are perms out of fashion?" If you're one of those people, keep reading to get a comprehensive guide to the trend.

Description

Perms are a chemical treatment that changes the structure of your hair. The process involves wrapping your hair around small rods and then applying a chemical solution that breaks down the bonds in your hair. Once the bonds are broken, the hair is reshaped into a new form and set with a neutralizer.

Perms were popular in the 80s and 90s, but in recent years, the trend has lost its appeal. Many people are moving towards more natural hairstyles and avoiding chemical treatments that can damage their hair.

How to Get a Perm

If you're interested in getting a perm, you'll need to visit a salon that offers the service. Here are the steps involved in getting a perm:

  1. Your hair will be washed and wrapped around small rods.
  2. A chemical solution will be applied to your hair to break down the bonds.
  3. The hair will be rinsed and set with a neutralizer to re-form the bonds.
  4. Your hair will be rinsed again and styled.

It's important to note that getting a perm can be damaging to your hair. The chemicals used in the process can cause dryness, breakage, and hair loss. It's important to talk to your stylist about the best way to care for your hair after getting a perm.

Step by Step Guide

If you're considering getting a perm, here is a step-by-step guide to help you through the process:

  1. Research salons in your area that offer perms.
  2. Book a consultation with a stylist to discuss the process and any concerns you may have.
  3. Prepare your hair by washing it a day or two before your appointment and avoiding any chemical treatments or heat styling.
  4. Bring pictures of the type of perm you want to show your stylist.
  5. During the appointment, your stylist will wrap your hair around small rods and apply the chemical solution.
  6. After rinsing and neutralizing, your stylist will style your hair as desired.
  7. After the appointment, follow your stylist's advice on how to care for your hair to avoid damage.

Tips for Getting a Perm

If you're considering getting a perm, here are some tips to help you through the process:

  • Choose a stylist who has experience with perms.
  • Bring pictures of the type of perm you want to show your stylist.
  • Be prepared for the process to take a few hours.
  • Avoid washing your hair for at least 24 hours after getting a perm.
  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner to avoid damage.
  • Avoid heat styling as much as possible.

FAQ

Q: Are perms out of fashion?

A: Perms have lost their popularity in recent years, but they are still worn by some people.

Q: How long does a perm last?

A: A perm can last anywhere from 3-6 months, depending on how fast your hair grows and how well you take care of it.

Q: Can a perm damage your hair?

A: Yes, a perm can be damaging to your hair. The chemicals used in the process can cause dryness, breakage, and hair loss.

Q: Can you color your hair after getting a perm?

A: It's best to wait at least two weeks after getting a perm before coloring your hair. The chemicals in the perm can affect the way your hair takes color.

Hair Care After Getting a Perm

After getting a perm, it's important to take care of your hair to avoid damage. Here are some tips:

  • Avoid washing your hair for at least 24 hours after getting a perm.
  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner formulated for chemically treated hair.
  • Avoid heat styling as much as possible.
  • Get regular trims to avoid split ends.
  • Deep condition your hair once a week to keep it moisturized.

Conclusion

So, are perms out of fashion? While they may not be as popular as they once were, perms are still worn by some people. If you're considering getting a perm, it's important to do your research and talk to your stylist about the best way to care for your hair.
