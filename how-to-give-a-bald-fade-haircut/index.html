How To Give A Bald Fade Haircut Step By Step Guide

Written by Oliver May 29, 2023 · 8 min read

Are you looking for a trendy and stylish haircut? If yes, then a bald fade haircut can be a perfect choice. A bald fade haircut is a popular men’s hairstyle that involves trimming the hair from the sides and back of the head while leaving the hair on top longer. In this article, we will guide you on how to give a bald fade haircut step by step.

Description of Bald Fade Haircut

A bald fade haircut is a popular men’s hairstyle that involves gradually trimming the hair from the sides and back of the head until it blends into the skin. This haircut is also known as a skin fade or a bald taper. The hair on top of the head is left longer than the hair on the sides and back, giving a stylish and trendy look.

How to Give a Bald Fade Haircut

Giving a bald fade haircut is not difficult if you have the right tools and a step-by-step guide. Here are the tools that you will need:

  • Clippers
  • Scissors
  • Comb
  • Hair spray or gel
  • Cape or towel

Step-by-Step Guide

Follow these simple steps to give a bald fade haircut:

Step 1: Prepare the hair

Wash the hair and dry it with a towel. Comb the hair to remove any tangles or knots. Apply hair spray or gel to make it easier to cut.

Step 2: Choose the clipper guard size

Choose the clipper guard size depending on how short you want the hair on the sides and back of the head. Start with a longer guard size and make it shorter gradually.

Step 3: Cut the hair on the sides and back

Start cutting the hair on the sides and back of the head with the clipper. Start at the bottom and move the clipper upwards while keeping it parallel to the head. Use a comb to make sure the hair is even. Repeat this step until you reach the desired length.

Step 4: Create a fade

Switch to a shorter clipper guard size and cut the hair above the previous line. Use a comb to blend the hair and create a fade. Repeat this step with even shorter clipper guard sizes until you reach the skin.

Step 5: Cut the hair on top

Use scissors to trim the hair on top of the head. Use a comb to hold the hair and cut it to the desired length.

Step 6: Blend the hair

Use the scissors to blend the hair on top with the hair on the sides and back of the head. Use a comb to make sure the hair is even.

Step 7: Apply finishing touches

Use the clipper to trim any stray hairs and create sharp lines. Use a comb to check if the hair is even.

Tips for Giving a Bald Fade Haircut

Here are some tips that will help you give a perfect bald fade haircut:

  • Start with a longer clipper guard size and gradually make it shorter.
  • Use a comb to make sure the hair is even.
  • Blend the hair on top with the hair on the sides and back of the head.
  • Trim the hair gradually and check if it is even.
  • Use scissors to trim the hair on top of the head.

FAQ

1. How long does a bald fade haircut last?

A bald fade haircut can last for 2-4 weeks depending on how fast your hair grows.

2. Can I give myself a bald fade haircut?

It is recommended to get a bald fade haircut from a professional hairdresser. However, if you have the right tools and a step-by-step guide, you can give yourself a bald fade haircut.

3. Can I use hair gel on a bald fade haircut?

Yes, you can use hair gel or spray on a bald fade haircut to keep it in place.

Hair Care Tips for Bald Fade Haircut

Here are some hair care tips that will help you maintain your bald fade haircut:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a good quality shampoo.
  • Use a conditioner to keep your hair soft and shiny.
  • Avoid using hot tools like hair straighteners and curling irons.
  • Apply hair oil or serum to keep your hair healthy and nourished.
  • Visit a professional hairdresser regularly to maintain your bald fade haircut.

Now that you know how to give a bald fade haircut, you can try it on yourself or your friends and family. Follow these simple steps and tips to give a perfect bald fade haircut.
