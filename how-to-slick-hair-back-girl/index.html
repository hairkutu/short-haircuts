If you're on the hunt for a chic and sophisticated hairstyle, look no further than slicked back hair. This sleek and polished look is perfect for any occasion, from a night out with friends to a professional business meeting. In this article, we'll show you how to slick hair back girl and achieve this modern and stylish look.

How to Slick Hair Back Girl

Slicking hair back may seem like a daunting task, but it's actually quite simple. Here are the basic steps:

Step 1: Start with Clean Hair

Begin by washing your hair with a clarifying shampoo and conditioner to remove any product buildup. This will help your hair lay flat and smooth, making it easier to slick back.

Step 2: Apply Styling Product

Apply a styling product, such as a pomade or gel, to your hair. Start at the roots and work your way down to the ends, making sure to evenly distribute the product throughout your hair. This will help your hair stay in place and maintain its sleekness.

Step 3: Brush Hair Back

Using a fine-tooth comb or brush, gently brush your hair back. Make sure to smooth out any bumps or lumps in your hair to create a polished look. For a more dramatic look, you can use a comb to create a deep side part.

Step 4: Secure Hair in Place

Once your hair is slicked back, use bobby pins or a hair elastic to secure it in place. Make sure to tuck any loose hairs behind your ears for a clean and polished look.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here's a more detailed step-by-step guide to help you achieve the perfect slicked back hairstyle:

Step 1: Wash Your Hair

Start by washing your hair with a clarifying shampoo and conditioner to remove any product buildup. This will make your hair easier to style and help it stay in place.

Step 2: Towel Dry Your Hair

Use a towel to gently dry your hair until it's slightly damp. Avoid rubbing your hair vigorously with the towel, as this can cause frizz and damage.

Step 3: Apply Styling Product

Apply a styling product, such as a pomade or gel, to your hair. Start at the roots and work your way down to the ends, making sure to evenly distribute the product throughout your hair. Use a comb to help distribute the product evenly.

Step 4: Brush Your Hair Back

Using a fine-tooth comb or brush, gently brush your hair back. Make sure to smooth out any bumps or lumps in your hair to create a polished look. For a more dramatic look, you can use a comb to create a deep side part.

Step 5: Secure Your Hair in Place

Once your hair is slicked back, use bobby pins or a hair elastic to secure it in place. Make sure to tuck any loose hairs behind your ears for a clean and polished look.

Tips for Slicking Hair Back

Here are some tips to help you achieve the perfect slicked back hairstyle:

  • Use a clarifying shampoo and conditioner to remove any product buildup from your hair.
  • Apply a styling product evenly throughout your hair to help it stay in place.
  • Use a fine-tooth comb or brush to smooth out any bumps or lumps in your hair.
  • Secure your hair in place with bobby pins or a hair elastic.
  • Use hairspray to keep your hair in place throughout the day.

FAQs

Can I slick back short hair?

Yes, you can slick back short hair. Use a styling product that provides a strong hold and brush your hair back using a fine-tooth comb.

Can I slick back curly hair?

Yes, you can slick back curly hair. Use a styling product that provides a strong hold and brush your hair back using a wide-tooth comb or your fingers.

How long will my slicked back hair last?

Your slicked back hair should last for the entire day if you use a strong-hold styling product and secure your hair in place with bobby pins or a hair elastic.

Hair Care for Slicked Back Hair

To maintain your slicked back hairstyle, it's important to take good care of your hair. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a clarifying shampoo and conditioner to remove any product buildup.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as this can damage it and make it harder to style.
  • Use a hair mask or deep conditioner once a week to keep your hair healthy and moisturized.
  • Avoid touching your hair throughout the day, as this can cause it to become oily or frizzy.

With these tips and tricks, you can achieve the perfect slicked back hairstyle and rock this chic and sophisticated look with confidence.
