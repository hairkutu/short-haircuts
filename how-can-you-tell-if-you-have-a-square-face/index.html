How Can You Tell If You Have A Square Face

Written by Luffy Mar 25, 2023 Â· 6 min read

If you are wondering whether you have a square face or not, there are certain features that can help you identify it. In this article, we will discuss how to identify a square face and provide tips on how to style your hair to complement your features.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to Identify a Square Face
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Styling a Square Face
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care for Square Faces

Description

A square face is characterized by a strong jawline, forehead, and chin, giving it a boxy appearance. If you have a square face, your cheekbones will be straight and your forehead and jawline will be of similar width. Square faces are often seen as strong and powerful, and there are many hairstyles that can complement this shape.

How to Identify a Square Face

The easiest way to identify if you have a square face is to observe the shape of your face in the mirror. Here are some features that are common in square faces:

  • A broad forehead
  • Straight cheekbones
  • A strong jawline
  • A wide chin

If you have these features, chances are you have a square face. However, if you are still unsure, you can also use a measuring tape to measure the length and width of your face.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here is a step-by-step guide to help you identify if you have a square face:

  1. Stand in front of a mirror in a well-lit room.
  2. Pull your hair back so that your face is fully visible.
  3. Observe the shape of your face. Is it square or boxy?
  4. Check your forehead. Is it broad and flat?
  5. Check your cheekbones. Are they straight and prominent?
  6. Check your jawline. Is it wide and strong?
  7. Check your chin. Is it square or flat?
  8. Compare the length and width of your face. Are they similar?

If most of your answers are yes, then you likely have a square face.

Tips for Styling a Square Face

If you have a square face, there are many hairstyles that can complement your features. Here are some tips:

  • Avoid hairstyles that add volume to the sides of your face, as this can make your face look wider.
  • Choose hairstyles that add volume to the top of your head, as this can balance out your strong jawline.
  • Consider a side-swept fringe, as this can soften your features.
  • Avoid blunt cuts, as this can make your face look boxy.
  • Consider layers, as this can add movement and dimension to your hair.

FAQs

Q: Can makeup help soften a square face?

A: Yes, makeup can help soften a square face. You can use contouring to create the illusion of a softer jawline or use blush to add a pop of color to your cheeks.

Q: Can facial hair make a square face look more masculine?

A: Yes, facial hair can make a square face look more masculine. If you want to soften your features, consider trimming your facial hair or opting for a clean-shaven look.

Hair Care for Square Faces

Regardless of your face shape, it is important to take care of your hair. Here are some tips:

  • Use a shampoo and conditioner that is suited for your hair type.
  • Avoid heat styling tools as much as possible to minimize damage.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.
  • Avoid tight hairstyles that can pull on your hairline and cause damage.
  • Consider using a hair mask once a week to nourish your hair.

Now that you know how to identify a square face and how to style your hair to complement your features, you can confidently choose a hairstyle that flatters your face shape. Remember to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and beautiful.
