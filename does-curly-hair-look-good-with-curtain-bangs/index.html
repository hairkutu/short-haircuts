Does Curly Hair Look Good With Curtain Bangs

Written by Robby Mar 27, 2023 · 8 min read

Curly hair is a beautiful texture that can be styled in many ways. One of the latest trends is curtain bangs, a style that has been seen on many celebrities and influencers. But, does curly hair look good with curtain bangs? In this blog post, we will explore this question and give you tips on how to make it work.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to
  • Step by Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

Curtain bangs are a style of bangs that are parted in the middle and frame the face on both sides. They are named after the curtains that hang on either side of a window. They are a versatile style that can be worn with many hair textures, including curly hair. However, curly hair can be challenging to style, and it's important to know how to make it work with curtain bangs.

How to

The first step in making curtain bangs work with curly hair is to find the right stylist. Look for someone who has experience working with curly hair and can give you suggestions based on your hair type and face shape. Once you have found the right stylist, it's essential to communicate your needs and the look you want to achieve.

Step by Step

1. Start by washing your hair with a shampoo and conditioner designed for curly hair. This will help to enhance your natural curls and make them more defined.

2. Towel dry your hair and apply a curl-enhancing product, such as a mousse or gel.

3. Use a diffuser attachment on your hairdryer to dry your hair gently. This will help to prevent frizz and enhance your curls.

4. Once your hair is dry, use a curling wand to curl your curtain bangs. Curl them away from your face to create a natural-looking frame.

5. Finish off with a hairspray to hold the style in place.

Tips

Here are some tips to make curtain bangs work with curly hair:

  • Choose a length that works for your hair type and face shape. Long curtain bangs work well with long hair, while short curtain bangs look great with short hair.
  • Use a diffuser attachment on your hairdryer to dry your hair gently. This will help to prevent frizz and enhance your curls.
  • Invest in a good curl-enhancing product, such as a mousse or gel, to enhance your natural curls.
  • Be patient. It may take a few tries to get the perfect curtain bangs, but with practice, you can achieve the look you want.

FAQ

Can I wear curtain bangs with curly hair?

Yes, absolutely! Curtain bangs can be worn with many hair textures, including curly hair. The key is to find the right stylist and communicate your needs.

What length should my curtain bangs be?

The length of your curtain bangs will depend on your hair type and face shape. Long curtain bangs work well with long hair, while short curtain bangs look great with short hair.

How do I style my curtain bangs with curly hair?

To style your curtain bangs with curly hair, start by washing your hair with a shampoo and conditioner designed for curly hair. Towel dry your hair and apply a curl-enhancing product, such as a mousse or gel. Use a diffuser attachment on your hairdryer to dry your hair gently. Once your hair is dry, use a curling wand to curl your curtain bangs. Curl them away from your face to create a natural-looking frame. Finish off with a hairspray to hold the style in place.

Hair Care

Curly hair requires special care to maintain its natural texture and prevent frizz. Here are some tips for taking care of your curly hair:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner designed for curly hair.
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it's dry. Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your hair when it's wet.
  • Apply a leave-in conditioner or curl-enhancing product to your hair to keep it moisturized and defined.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as they can damage your hair and cause frizz.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.

In conclusion, curly hair looks great with curtain bangs, and with the right stylist and products, you can achieve the look you want. Follow our tips and hair care advice to keep your curly hair healthy and beautiful.
