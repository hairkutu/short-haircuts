How To Prevent Wind Blown Hair

Written by Luffy Mar 20, 2023 Â· 8 min read

If you are someone who loves to spend time outdoors, you know how frustrating it can be to have your hair ruined by the wind. Wind blown hair is a common problem for people with all types of hair, but it can be especially frustrating for those with relaxed hair. Fortunately, there are some simple steps you can take to prevent wind blown hair and keep your hair looking great, no matter what the weather is like.

Table of Contents

  • How to Prevent Wind Blown Hair
  • Step by Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ

How to Prevent Wind Blown Hair

Preventing wind blown hair is all about taking the right precautions before you head outside. Here are some simple steps you can take:

1. Use a Hair Tie or Headband

One of the easiest ways to prevent wind blown hair is to use a hair tie or headband. This will keep your hair in place and prevent it from flying around in the wind. If you have shorter hair, you might want to use bobby pins or hair clips instead.

2. Apply Hair Oil or Serum

Before you head outside, apply a small amount of hair oil or serum to your hair. This will help to smooth down any flyaways and prevent your hair from getting tangled in the wind.

3. Use a Hairspray or Hair Gel

If you really want to keep your hair in place, you can use a hairspray or hair gel. Just be sure to use a product that won't leave your hair feeling stiff or crunchy.

4. Choose a Protective Hairstyle

Another option is to choose a protective hairstyle that will keep your hair out of the wind. This could be a bun, braids, or a low ponytail. Just be sure to use a hair tie or headband to secure your hair in place.

Step by Step

Here are some step by step instructions for preventing wind blown hair:

Step 1: Choose a Protective Hairstyle

Decide on a hairstyle that will keep your hair out of the wind. This could be a bun, braids, or a low ponytail.

Step 2: Secure Your Hair in Place

Use a hair tie or headband to secure your hair in place. If you have shorter hair, use bobby pins or hair clips.

Step 3: Apply Hair Oil or Serum

Apply a small amount of hair oil or serum to your hair to help smooth down any flyaways.

Step 4: Use a Hairspray or Hair Gel

If you want to keep your hair in place, use a hairspray or hair gel. Just be sure to use a product that won't leave your hair feeling stiff or crunchy.

Step 5: Enjoy the Outdoors

Now that your hair is secure, you can head outside and enjoy the great outdoors without worrying about wind blown hair.

Tips

Here are some additional tips for preventing wind blown hair:

1. Choose the Right Hair Products

Be sure to choose hair products that are designed to keep your hair in place without leaving it feeling stiff or crunchy.

2. Use a Silk or Satin Hair Tie

Using a silk or satin hair tie can help to prevent breakage and frizz.

3. Apply Hair Oil or Serum to Wet Hair

If you apply hair oil or serum to wet hair, it will be easier to distribute evenly and will help to prevent flyaways.

FAQ

Q: Can wind blown hair cause damage?

A: Yes, wind blown hair can cause damage by causing tangles and breakage.

Q: How often should I apply hair oil or serum?

A: You can apply hair oil or serum as often as needed, but be careful not to apply too much or your hair may become greasy.

Q: What is the best protective hairstyle for preventing wind blown hair?

A: A bun, braids, or a low ponytail are all good options for preventing wind blown hair.

Hair Care

Preventing wind blown hair is just one aspect of hair care. Here are some additional tips for keeping your hair healthy and looking great:

1. Use a Gentle Shampoo

Use a gentle shampoo that won't strip your hair of its natural oils.

2. Use a Deep Conditioning Treatment

Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair hydrated and healthy.

3. Protect Your Hair from Heat

Use a heat protectant spray before using any heat styling tools to prevent damage to your hair.

4. Get Regular Trims

Get regular trims to keep your hair looking healthy and prevent split ends.

By following these tips, you can prevent wind blown hair and keep your hair looking great, no matter what the weather is like.
