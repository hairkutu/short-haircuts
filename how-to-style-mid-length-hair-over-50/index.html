How To Style Mid Length Hair Over 50

Written by Daniel Jul 16, 2023 Â· 6 min read

As we age, our hair may lose some of its thickness and luster. However, that doesn't mean we can't still rock a stylish cut and look fabulous. Mid length hair is perfect for women over 50 as it's versatile and easy to maintain. In this article, we'll show you how to style your mid-length hair and look your best at any age.

Description

Mid-length hair is hair that falls between the chin and the shoulders. It's a popular length for women over 50 because it's easy to style and looks great on most face shapes. Mid-length hair can be styled in many ways, from sleek straight locks to loose waves or curls.

How to Style Mid Length Hair Over 50

Step 1: Choose the Right Cut

The first step in styling your mid-length hair is to choose the right cut. It's essential to choose a cut that complements your face shape and hair texture. Consult with your hairstylist to find the perfect cut for you.

Step 2: Add Layers

Adding layers to your mid-length hair can add volume and movement. Layers can also help to frame your face and highlight your best features. Ask your stylist to add some layers to your mid-length cut.

Step 3: Blow Dry Your Hair

Blow-drying your hair is an essential step in styling your mid-length hair. Use a round brush to create volume and movement. Start at the roots and work your way down to the ends.

Step 4: Style Your Hair

When it comes to styling your mid-length hair, you have many options. You can create loose waves or curls using a curling iron or flat iron. You can also wear your hair straight and sleek or add some texture with a texturizing spray.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when styling your mid-length hair:

  • Use a heat protectant spray to protect your hair from damage
  • Invest in a good quality hairbrush and comb
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment at least once a week to keep your hair healthy and shiny
  • Don't be afraid to experiment with different styles and products

FAQ

Q: How often should I get my hair cut?

A: It's recommended to get your hair cut every 6-8 weeks to maintain your style and keep your hair healthy.

Q: What products should I use on my mid-length hair?

A: It depends on your hair type and the style you're going for. Consult with your hairstylist to find the best products for your hair.

Hair Care

Taking care of your hair is essential to keep it looking healthy and shiny. Here are some tips to keep your mid-length hair looking its best:

  • Wash your hair with a gentle shampoo and conditioner
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often
  • Protect your hair from the sun and wind
  • Eat a healthy diet and drink plenty of water to keep your hair healthy from the inside out

By following these tips and steps, you can style your mid-length hair over 50 and look your best at any age. Remember, your hair is an extension of your personality, so have fun experimenting with different styles and products.
