What Is The Best Gel For Curly Hair In 2023

Written by Frank May 01, 2023 · 10 min read

Curly hair can be both a blessing and a curse, especially when it comes to finding the right hair products. One essential product for curly hair is gel, which helps define curls, reduce frizz, and add hold. But with so many gels on the market, it can be overwhelming to choose the right one. In this article, we’ll explore what is the best gel for curly hair in 2023, how to use it, and tips for maintaining healthy curls.

Table of Contents

  1. Description
  2. How to Choose the Best Gel for Curly Hair
  3. Step-by-Step Guide to Using Gel on Curly Hair
  4. Tips for Maintaining Healthy Curls
  5. FAQs
  6. Curly Hair Care Tips

Description

Before we dive into the best gels for curly hair, let’s first understand what to look for in a good gel. The best gel for curly hair should have a strong hold, but not be too heavy or sticky. It should also contain moisturizing ingredients to prevent the hair from becoming dry or crunchy. Lastly, it should be easy to apply and distribute evenly throughout the hair.

How to Choose the Best Gel for Curly Hair

When looking for the best gel for curly hair, there are a few key factors to consider:

Hold

The hold of a gel determines how well it will define curls and keep them in place. For curly hair, a strong hold gel is necessary to prevent frizz and maintain shape. Look for gels that specifically state they have a strong hold.

Ingredients

Curly hair tends to be dry, so it’s important to choose a gel that contains moisturizing ingredients such as glycerin, aloe vera, or honey. Avoid gels that contain alcohol, as they can further dry out the hair.

Consistency

The consistency of a gel affects how easy it is to apply and distribute throughout the hair. For curly hair, a lightweight gel that spreads easily is best. Avoid gels that are too thick or sticky, as they can weigh down the hair and cause buildup.

Step-by-Step Guide to Using Gel on Curly Hair

Now that you’ve chosen the best gel for your curly hair, it’s time to learn how to use it. Follow these steps for best results:

Step 1: Wash and Condition Hair

Start with clean, conditioned hair. Use a sulfate-free shampoo and a moisturizing conditioner to keep your curls hydrated.

Step 2: Apply Leave-In Conditioner

Apply a leave-in conditioner to damp hair to help detangle and moisturize curls. This step is crucial for preventing frizz and keeping curls defined.

Step 3: Apply Gel

Apply the gel to your hair in sections, smoothing it through from root to tip. Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to distribute the gel evenly. Be sure to apply enough gel to each section to ensure a strong hold.

Step 4: Scrunch Hair

Scrunch your hair with a microfiber towel or t-shirt to encourage curl formation and remove excess water. Avoid using a regular towel, as it can cause frizz.

Step 5: Air Dry or Diffuse

Allow your hair to air dry or use a diffuser on low heat to speed up the process. Avoid touching your hair while it’s drying to prevent frizz.

Tips for Maintaining Healthy Curls

Here are some additional tips for maintaining healthy, defined curls:

1. Don’t Overwash

Curly hair tends to be dry, so avoid washing it every day. Instead, wash your hair once or twice a week with a sulfate-free shampoo.

2. Use a Satin Pillowcase

Sleeping on a cotton pillowcase can cause friction and frizz. Use a satin pillowcase instead to keep your curls smooth.

3. Avoid Heat Styling

Heat styling tools can damage curly hair and cause it to lose its natural shape. Avoid using flat irons and curling wands, and opt for air-drying or diffusing instead.

4. Get Regular Trims

Split ends can cause hair to look frizzy and undefined. Get regular trims every 8-12 weeks to keep your curls looking healthy.

FAQs

What is the difference between gel and mousse?

Gel and mousse are both styling products for curly hair, but they have different consistencies and hold levels. Gel is a thicker, more concentrated formula that provides a stronger hold, while mousse is lighter and provides a softer hold.

How do I prevent my curls from getting frizzy?

Frizz is a common issue for curly hair. To prevent frizz, use a leave-in conditioner, avoid touching your hair while it’s drying, and sleep on a satin pillowcase.

Can I use gel on dry hair?

While it’s best to apply gel to damp hair, you can also use it on dry hair to refresh curls. Simply apply a small amount of gel to your hands and scrunch it into your hair.

Curly Hair Care Tips

Here are some additional tips for caring for your curly hair:

1. Use a Wide-Tooth Comb

A wide-tooth comb is gentler on curly hair and helps prevent breakage. Avoid using a brush, which can cause frizz.

2. Deep Condition Regularly

Deep conditioning treatments help moisturize and strengthen curly hair. Use a deep conditioner once a week for best results.

3. Protect Hair from the Sun

UV rays can damage hair and cause it to become dry and brittle. Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a UV-protectant spray.

4. Experiment with Different Products

Every curly head is different, so don’t be afraid to experiment with different products to find what works best for you. Try different gels, mousses, and styling creams to see what gives you the best results.

In conclusion, the best gel for curly hair in 2023 should have a strong hold, be moisturizing, and have a lightweight consistency. Follow our step-by-step guide to using gel on curly hair, and use our tips for maintaining healthy, defined curls. With the right products and care, you can have beautiful, bouncy curls all year long.
