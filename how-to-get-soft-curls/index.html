How To Get Soft Curls A Step By Step Guide

Written by William May 01, 2023 · 6 min read

Are you tired of straight hair and want to add some bounce to your locks? Soft curls are a great way to add some texture and volume to your hair. In this step-by-step guide, we will show you how to get soft curls in relaxed English language. From how to prep your hair to the best styling tools and techniques, we’ve got you covered. Let’s get started!

Table of Contents

  • Prepping Your Hair
  • Choosing the Right Tools
  • The Technique
  • Tips for Perfect Soft Curls
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care for Soft Curls

Prepping Your Hair

Before you start curling your hair, it's important to prep it properly. Start by washing your hair with a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner. This will help to keep your hair hydrated and prevent frizz.

Next, apply a heat protectant spray or serum to your hair. This will help to protect your hair from the heat of the curling iron and prevent damage.

Finally, blow dry your hair using a round brush to add some volume and texture. You can also use a diffuser attachment on your blow dryer to enhance your natural curls.

Choosing the Right Tools

When it comes to getting soft curls, the right tools are essential. Here are some of the best tools to use:

  • A curling iron with a 1-inch barrel
  • Hot rollers
  • A curling wand
  • Bobby pins
  • Hairspray

The Technique

Now that you have prepped your hair and chosen the right tools, it's time to start curling. Here is a step-by-step guide:

  1. Divide your hair into small sections.
  2. Take a section and wrap it around the curling iron, holding for 10-15 seconds.
  3. Release the curl and pin it in place with a bobby pin.
  4. Repeat until all of your hair is curled and pinned.
  5. Let your hair cool for 10-15 minutes.
  6. Remove the bobby pins and gently comb through your curls with your fingers.
  7. Spray with hairspray to hold the curls in place.

Tips for Perfect Soft Curls

Here are some tips to help you get the perfect soft curls:

  • Use a curling iron or wand with a 1-inch barrel for more natural-looking curls.
  • Don't curl your hair too tightly. Looser curls will give you a more relaxed, natural look.
  • Use hot rollers for a softer, more voluminous curl.
  • Don't forget to use a heat protectant spray or serum to prevent damage to your hair.
  • Let your curls cool for at least 10-15 minutes before removing the pins.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about getting soft curls:

Q: Can I curl my hair without heat?

A: Yes, you can use overnight hair rollers or braids to get soft curls without heat.

Q: How long will my soft curls last?

A: Soft curls can last up to 2-3 days with the right hair care and styling products.

Q: Can I use hairspray to hold my curls in place?

A: Yes, using hairspray is a great way to hold your curls in place and prevent frizz. Just be sure to use a light hold hairspray to avoid a crunchy, stiff look.

Hair Care for Soft Curls

To maintain your soft curls, it's important to take care of your hair. Here are some tips:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair hydrated and healthy.
  • Avoid using hot tools on a daily basis to prevent damage to your hair.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair moisturized.
  • Avoid brushing your hair too much, as this can cause frizz and breakage.

With these tips and techniques, you can get soft curls that will make your hair look full and voluminous. Remember to take care of your hair to maintain your curls and keep them looking their best!
