How To Cut Short Hair Woman At Home A Step By Step Guide

Written by Bardi Apr 13, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Are you looking to cut your short hair at home? Perhaps you can't make it to the salon or you're trying to save some money. Whatever your reason may be, cutting short hair at home is possible with the right tools, techniques, and guidance. In this guide, we will take you through the process of cutting short hair at home, step-by-step. We'll also provide tips, answer frequently asked questions, and provide some hair care advice.

Table of Contents

  • Tools Needed
  • Preparation
  • Sectioning Your Hair
  • Cutting Your Hair
  • Finishing Touches
  • Tips for Cutting Short Hair at Home
  • Frequently Asked Questions
  • Hair Care Tips

Tools Needed

Before you start cutting your hair, there are a few tools you will need:

  • Hair scissors
  • Comb
  • Hair clips or elastics
  • Spray bottle filled with water
  • Mirror
  • Towel or cape to protect your clothes

Preparation

Before you start cutting your hair, it's important to prepare your work area. Choose a well-lit space, and lay a towel or cape over your shoulders to protect your clothes from any hair clippings. Make sure you have all the tools you need within reach.

Sectioning Your Hair

Start by sectioning your hair. Divide your hair into four sections: two in the front and two in the back. Use hair clips or elastics to secure each section.

Take one of the front sections, and comb it straight down over your forehead. Use your fingers to measure how much hair you want to cut. For a subtle trim, take about a quarter-inch of hair. For a more dramatic change, take more hair.

Cutting Your Hair

Using your hair scissors, cut the hair straight across. Be careful not to cut too much hair at once. It's better to make small cuts and check your progress as you go. Repeat this process on the other front section.

Now move onto the back sections. Take one section, and comb it straight down. Use your fingers to measure how much hair you want to cut, and cut the hair straight across. Repeat this process on the other back section.

Finishing Touches

After you've cut all four sections, it's time to add some finishing touches. Comb through your hair, and look for any uneven spots or longer pieces. Make small cuts to even out your hair, if necessary.

After you're happy with your haircut, style your hair as desired.

Tips for Cutting Short Hair at Home

  • Start with small cuts and check your progress as you go.
  • Use hair clips or elastics to secure each section of hair.
  • Comb through your hair before cutting to ensure evenness.
  • Don't cut too much hair at once.
  • When in doubt, leave it to the professionals.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I cut my own hair at home?

Yes, you can cut your own hair at home. However, it's important to have the right tools, techniques, and guidance.

How often should I cut my hair?

It depends on your hair type and desired length. On average, hair should be trimmed every 6-8 weeks to prevent split ends and maintain healthy hair.

Can I cut my hair wet or dry?

It's best to cut hair when it's dry, as it allows you to see the actual length of your hair.

Hair Care Tips

  • Use a high-quality shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often.
  • Eat a balanced diet to promote healthy hair growth.
  • Use a hair mask once a week to nourish your hair.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and harsh weather conditions.

With these tips and techniques, you can confidently cut your own short hair at home. Just remember to start small, check your progress as you go, and have fun with it!
