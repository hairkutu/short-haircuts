How To Cut The Back Of A Short Bob Yourself Step By Step Guide

Written by Eveline Sep 09, 2023 Â· 6 min read

If you're rocking a short bob, you know how important it is to keep it trimmed and well-maintained. However, heading to the salon every few weeks can be costly and time-consuming. Luckily, with a little know-how and the right tools, you can easily cut the back of your short bob yourself. Read on for a step-by-step guide on how to do it.

How to Cut the Back of a Short Bob Yourself

Step 1: Gather Your Tools

Before you begin, make sure you have all the necessary tools on hand. You'll need a pair of sharp scissors, a comb, a mirror, and hair clips to help section your hair.

Step 2: Prep Your Hair

Start by washing and drying your hair thoroughly. Comb out any tangles and make sure your hair is completely dry. If you have particularly thick or curly hair, you may want to straighten it before you begin cutting.

Step 3: Section Your Hair

Using your comb, divide your hair into two sections: the top and the bottom. Clip the top section of your hair up and out of the way.

Step 4: Trim the Bottom Layer

Starting with the bottom layer of your hair, take a small section at the back and comb it straight down. Using your scissors, cut the hair to your desired length, making sure to keep the cut straight and even. Continue cutting small sections of hair until you've trimmed the entire bottom layer.

Step 5: Blend the Top Layer

Release the top layer of your hair and use your comb to blend the cut you just made with the longer hair on top. Take small sections of hair and trim them to match the length of the bottom layer. Continue blending and trimming until the top layer is the same length as the bottom layer.

Step 6: Check Your Work

Once you've finished cutting, use your mirror to check the back of your hair. Make any final adjustments as needed to ensure your cut is even and straight.

Tips for Cutting the Back of a Short Bob Yourself

Here are a few tips to keep in mind as you cut the back of your short bob:

  • Take your time and work slowly, cutting small sections of hair at a time.
  • Make sure your scissors are sharp to ensure a clean cut.
  • Don't be afraid to ask for help from a friend or family member if you need it.

FAQs About Cutting the Back of a Short Bob Yourself

Can I cut the back of my short bob myself?

Yes, you can! With the right tools and a little know-how, cutting the back of your short bob is easy and cost-effective.

What if I mess up?

If you make a mistake while cutting your hair, don't panic. You can always visit a professional stylist to fix any errors or uneven spots.

How often should I cut the back of my short bob?

It's recommended to trim the back of your short bob every 4-6 weeks to maintain its shape and length.

Hair Care After Cutting the Back of a Short Bob Yourself

After you've finished cutting the back of your short bob, be sure to take good care of your hair to keep it healthy and looking great. This includes washing and conditioning regularly, using a heat protectant when styling with heat tools, and avoiding harsh chemicals or dyes.

Now that you know how to cut the back of your short bob yourself, you can save time and money while still looking fabulous. Follow these simple steps and tips for a perfect cut every time!
