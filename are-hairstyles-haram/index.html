Are Hairstyles Haram Exploring The Islamic Perspective On Hair

Written by Oliver May 08, 2023 Â· 6 min read

As Muslims, it is important for us to understand what is permissible or haram in our faith. When it comes to hairstyles, there are several opinions and beliefs. In this article, we will discuss the Islamic perspective on hairstyles and whether or not they are haram.

What Does Islam Say About Hair?

In Islam, hair is considered a blessing from Allah. It is important to take care of it and keep it clean. Men and women are both encouraged to keep their hair clean and well-groomed. However, there are certain guidelines that Muslims must follow when it comes to hairstyles.

For Men

For men, it is recommended to keep their hair short and tidy. The Prophet Muhammad (peace be upon him) would often trim his hair and beard, and he encouraged his followers to do the same. However, it is not haram for men to have longer hair, as long as it is clean and well-maintained.

For Women

For women, there are more guidelines to follow when it comes to hairstyles. It is recommended for women to cover their hair with a hijab or headscarf. When in the presence of men who are not their mahram (close relatives), women should cover their hair and wear modest clothing. However, there is no specific hairstyle that is haram for women, as long as it is not provocative or immodest.

Are Certain Hairstyles Haram?

There is no specific hairstyle that is considered haram in Islam. However, there are certain hairstyles that are discouraged or disliked. For example, some scholars believe that hairstyles that imitate non-Muslims or are considered too flashy or trendy are not recommended. It is important to remember that moderation is key when it comes to hairstyles.

How to Choose a Halal Hairstyle

Choosing a halal hairstyle can be a personal decision. It is important to consider your own cultural and personal preferences, as well as what is appropriate for your gender and age. It is also important to consider the practicality and ease of maintenance of your hairstyle.

Some tips for choosing a halal hairstyle include:

  • Consulting with a trusted imam or scholar
  • Avoiding hairstyles that imitate non-Muslims or are considered too flashy or trendy
  • Keeping hair clean and well-maintained
  • Choosing a hairstyle that is modest and appropriate for your gender and age

FAQ

Can men have long hair?

Yes, men can have long hair as long as it is clean and well-maintained.

Do women have to cover their hair all the time?

No, women only have to cover their hair in the presence of men who are not their mahram (close relatives).

Are certain hairstyles haram?

There is no specific hairstyle that is haram in Islam, but some hairstyles are discouraged or disliked.

Hair Care Tips

Regardless of your hairstyle, it is important to take care of your hair. Some tips for hair care include:

  • Washing hair regularly with a gentle shampoo
  • Using a conditioner to keep hair soft and manageable
  • Avoiding harsh chemicals and heat styling tools
  • Trimming hair regularly to prevent split ends
  • Eating a healthy diet to promote hair growth and strength

Conclusion

Overall, there is no specific hairstyle that is haram in Islam. It is important to keep hair clean and well-maintained, and to choose a hairstyle that is modest and appropriate for your gender and age. Consulting with a trusted imam or scholar can also provide guidance and support in choosing a halal hairstyle.
