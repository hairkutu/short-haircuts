Are Layers Good For Thick Curly Hair

Written by Christine May 29, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Thick curly hair is beautiful, but it can also be challenging to manage. One way to make it more manageable is by getting a haircut with layers. In this blog post, we'll explore whether layers are good for thick curly hair and provide tips on how to care for your hair after getting a layered cut.

Table of Contents

  1. Description
  2. How To
  3. Step By Step
  4. Tips
  5. FAQ
  6. Hair Care

Description

Layers can be great for adding volume, texture, and movement to thick curly hair. They can help distribute the weight of your hair more evenly, making it easier to style and preventing your curls from looking weighed down. Layers can also help your hair look more polished and healthy by removing any damaged or split ends.

How To

If you're considering getting a layered haircut for your thick curly hair, here are some tips to keep in mind:

Find a Stylist Who Specializes in Curly Hair

Not all hairstylists are created equal, especially when it comes to curly hair. Look for a stylist who has experience working with curly hair and can recommend the best layers for your hair type and face shape.

Consider the Length of Your Hair

The length of your hair will determine how many layers you can get. If you have long hair, you can get more layers without sacrificing the length. If you have shorter hair, you may want to stick with fewer layers.

Decide on the Type of Layers You Want

There are a few types of layers you can get, including long layers, short layers, and choppy layers. Long layers are great for adding volume and movement to your hair, while short layers can help define your curls. Choppy layers can create a trendy, edgy look.

Step By Step

Here are the steps you can expect when getting a layered haircut for your thick curly hair:

  1. Consult with your stylist to determine the best type of layers for your hair type and face shape.
  2. Wash and condition your hair before the haircut.
  3. Sit in the stylist's chair and communicate any concerns or questions you may have.
  4. Watch as your stylist cuts your hair into layers.
  5. Style your hair and enjoy your new look!

Tips

Here are some tips for caring for your layered, thick curly hair:

  • Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your hair.
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it's dry, as it can cause frizz and breakage.
  • Use a diffuser when blow-drying your hair to enhance your curls.
  • Apply a leave-in conditioner or curl-enhancing product to your hair to keep it moisturized and defined.
  • Get regular trims to maintain the health of your hair and prevent split ends.

FAQ

What face shapes are best for layered haircuts?

Layered haircuts can work for any face shape, but the type of layers you get may vary depending on your face shape. Your stylist can recommend the best layers for your face shape.

Can I still straighten my hair after getting layers?

Yes, you can still straighten your hair after getting layers. However, keep in mind that layers may add more volume and texture to your hair, which may affect how your hair looks when straightened.

Hair Care

Caring for your thick curly hair is essential to maintaining its health and beauty. Here are some tips to keep in mind:

  • Wash your hair with a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as they can cause damage to your hair.
  • Deep condition your hair once a week to keep it moisturized and healthy.
  • Sleep on a silk or satin pillowcase to prevent frizz and breakage.
  • Avoid using hair elastics with metal parts, as they can damage your hair.

In conclusion, layers can be great for adding volume, texture, and movement to thick curly hair. Just make sure to find a stylist who specializes in curly hair, consider the length and type of layers you want, and follow the proper hair care routine to maintain the health and beauty of your hair.
