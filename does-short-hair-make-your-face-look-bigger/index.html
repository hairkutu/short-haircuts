Does Short Hair Make Your Face Look Bigger

Written by Bardi Mar 22, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Short hair has been a trend for a long time, and many people wonder if it can make their face look bigger. There are many different factors that can affect the perception of your face size, and hair length is one of them. In this article, we will discuss whether short hair makes your face look bigger, and provide some tips on how to style short hair to flatter your face shape.

Table of Content

  • Description
  • How to
  • Step by step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

Many people believe that short hair can make your face look bigger, but this is not always the case. The length of your hair can affect the perception of your face size, but it is not the only factor that influences it. Other factors include your face shape, hair texture, and the way you style your hair.

There are many different short hairstyles that can flatter different face shapes, so it is important to choose a hairstyle that complements your features. A good hairstylist can help you find a style that works for you, based on your face shape and hair texture.

How to

If you are considering a short hairstyle, there are a few things you can do to ensure that it flatters your face shape:

  • Choose a style that complements your face shape. For example, if you have a round face, a pixie cut with longer layers on top can help elongate your face.
  • Consider your hair texture. If you have thick, curly hair, a shorter hairstyle may require more maintenance to keep it looking neat.
  • Experiment with different styles. Don't be afraid to try different looks to see what works best for you.

Step by step

Here are some steps you can follow to style short hair to flatter your face shape:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair.
  2. Apply a styling product, such as mousse or pomade, to add volume and hold.
  3. Use a round brush to blow-dry your hair, lifting the roots to create volume.
  4. Once your hair is dry, use a flat iron or curling iron to add texture and shape.
  5. Finish with a hairspray or other finishing product to hold your style in place.

Tips

Here are some tips for styling short hair to flatter your face shape:

  • If you have a round face, avoid blunt bangs and instead opt for longer layers to elongate your face.
  • If you have a square face, try a short bob with soft, rounded edges to soften your features.
  • If you have an oval face, you can pull off almost any short hairstyle, but a pixie cut or a bob with bangs can be particularly flattering.
  • If you have a heart-shaped face, a short bob with side-swept bangs can balance out your features.

FAQ

Q: Does short hair make your face look bigger?

A: Short hair can make your face look bigger, but it depends on your face shape and the way you style your hair. A good hairstylist can help you find a style that flatters your features.

Q: What short hairstyles are best for round faces?

A: Longer layers on top of a pixie cut can help elongate a round face. A short bob with side-swept bangs can also be flattering.

Q: How can I add volume to my short hair?

A: Use a volumizing product, such as mousse or pomade, and blow-dry your hair with a round brush, lifting the roots to create volume.

Hair Care

Short hair requires regular maintenance to keep it looking neat and tidy. It is important to get regular haircuts to keep your style looking fresh, and to use styling products that protect your hair from heat damage. Regular conditioning treatments can also help keep your hair healthy and shiny.

So, does short hair make your face look bigger? The answer is not a simple yes or no. It depends on many different factors, including your face shape and hair texture. The key to finding a flattering short hairstyle is to work with a stylist who can help you find a style that complements your features. With the right cut and styling techniques, short hair can be a stylish and flattering choice for any face shape.
