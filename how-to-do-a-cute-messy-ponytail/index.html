How To Do A Cute Messy Ponytail

Written by Bardi Jun 12, 2023 · 7 min read

If you're looking for a cute and effortless hairstyle, a messy ponytail is the perfect solution. Not only is it easy to do, but it's also versatile and can be worn for any occasion. In this article, we'll show you how to do a cute messy ponytail step by step, along with some tips, FAQs, and hair care advice.

Table of Contents

  • How to Do a Cute Messy Ponytail
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for a Perfect Messy Ponytail
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care Tips

How to Do a Cute Messy Ponytail

To do a cute messy ponytail, you'll need a few essentials, including:

  • Hair ties
  • Bobby pins
  • Texture spray or dry shampoo
  • Comb or brush

Step 1: Prep Your Hair

Start by prepping your hair with texture spray or dry shampoo to add some volume and texture. This will help your ponytail look fuller and more messy.

Step 2: Gather Your Hair

Gather your hair at the crown of your head and secure it with a hair tie. You can make the ponytail as high or low as you like, depending on your preference.

Step 3: Tease Your Ponytail

Tease your ponytail with a comb or brush to create more volume and texture. This will give your ponytail a messy and undone look.

Step 4: Secure with Bobby Pins

Use bobby pins to secure any loose strands and keep your ponytail in place. You can also pull out some pieces around your face to frame it and make the look more casual.

Step 5: Finish with Texture Spray

Finish the look with a spritz of texture spray or hairspray to hold your ponytail in place and add some extra texture.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide to help you do a cute messy ponytail:

  1. Prep your hair with texture spray or dry shampoo.
  2. Gather your hair at the crown of your head and secure it with a hair tie.
  3. Tease your ponytail with a comb or brush to create more volume and texture.
  4. Use bobby pins to secure any loose strands and keep your ponytail in place.
  5. Pull out some pieces around your face to frame it and make the look more casual.
  6. Finish the look with a spritz of texture spray or hairspray to hold your ponytail in place and add some extra texture.

Tips for a Perfect Messy Ponytail

Here are some tips to help you achieve the perfect messy ponytail:

  • Use a hair tie that matches your hair color to make it less noticeable.
  • Tease your hair gently to avoid damaging it.
  • Add some hair accessories, such as a scrunchie or hair clip, to make your ponytail more stylish.
  • Don't worry about making it perfect – the beauty of a messy ponytail is in its imperfections.

FAQs

Is a messy ponytail appropriate for formal occasions?

Yes, a messy ponytail can be dressed up or down depending on the occasion. For formal events, you can add some accessories or style it with a sleeker finish.

How do I keep my ponytail from falling out?

Use bobby pins or hair ties to keep your ponytail in place. You can also use hairspray or texture spray to hold it in place.

Can I do a messy ponytail on short hair?

Yes, you can still do a messy ponytail on short hair. Use bobby pins to secure any loose strands and add some texture with a texture spray.

Hair Care Tips

To keep your hair healthy and looking its best, here are some hair care tips:

  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to nourish your hair.
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends and promote healthy hair growth.
  • Avoid using harsh chemicals or hair dyes that can damage your hair.

With these tips and techniques, you can easily do a cute messy ponytail that looks effortless and stylish.
