Hats That Go With Short Hair

Written by Christine Mar 23, 2023 Â· 10 min read

If you have short hair, you might be wondering what type of hats you can wear without looking like you have a mushroom on your head. Luckily, there are plenty of hats that go with short hair, and this article will help you find the perfect one for you. In this guide, we'll go over the different types of hats that work well with short hair, how to style them, and some tips for keeping your hair looking great while wearing a hat.

Types of Hats That Go With Short Hair

When it comes to finding the perfect hat for short hair, there are a few types that work particularly well. Here are some of the best options:

Berets

Berets are a great choice for short hair because they sit close to the head and don't add too much volume. They're also very versatile and can be dressed up or down, depending on the occasion. Try pairing a black beret with a leather jacket and skinny jeans for a chic, edgy look.

Beanies

Beanies are another great option for short hair, especially during the colder months. They come in a variety of styles, from slouchy to fitted, and can be worn with almost any outfit. If you're looking for a casual, laid-back look, try pairing a slouchy beanie with a denim jacket and leggings.

Caps

Caps, such as baseball caps or snapbacks, are a great choice for short hair because they sit low on the head and don't add too much height. They're also very versatile and can be worn with almost any outfit. If you're looking for a sporty, casual look, try pairing a baseball cap with a hoodie and joggers.

Fedora Hats

Fedora hats can be a bit trickier to pull off with short hair, but they can look great if styled correctly. Make sure to choose a fedora with a smaller brim and pair it with a tailored blazer and slim-fit trousers for a sophisticated, polished look.

How to Style Hats With Short Hair

Styling hats with short hair can be a bit different than styling them with longer hair. Here are some tips to keep in mind:

- Choose hats that sit close to the head and don't add too much volume.

- Make sure your hair is styled in a way that complements the hat. For example, if you're wearing a beanie, try leaving some pieces of hair out around your face for a more relaxed, effortless look.

- If you're wearing a hat with a brim, make sure that it's not covering your eyes or casting shadows on your face.

- Use hair accessories, such as headbands or hair clips, to keep your hair in place and prevent it from getting too messy underneath the hat.

Step-by-Step Guide to Wearing Hats With Short Hair

Here's a step-by-step guide to wearing hats with short hair:

1. Choose a hat that works well with short hair, such as a beanie or beret.

2. Style your hair in a way that complements the hat.

3. Put on the hat, making sure that it fits well and doesn't feel too tight or too loose.

4. Adjust the hat as needed to make sure it's sitting comfortably on your head.

5. Use hair accessories, such as bobby pins or clips, to keep your hair in place and prevent it from getting too messy underneath the hat.

Tips for Keeping Your Hair Looking Great While Wearing a Hat

Wearing a hat can be great for adding some style to your outfit, but it can also cause some hair problems. Here are some tips for keeping your hair looking great while wearing a hat:

- Use a volumizing spray before putting on the hat to prevent flat, lifeless hair.

- Avoid wearing hats for too long, as they can cause your hair to become oily and greasy.

- Use dry shampoo to freshen up your hair if it's feeling oily or greasy after wearing a hat.

- Take breaks from wearing hats to allow your hair to breathe and recover.

FAQ

1. Can I wear a fedora hat with short hair?

Yes, you can wear a fedora hat with short hair. Just make sure to choose a fedora with a smaller brim and pair it with a tailored blazer and slim-fit trousers for a sophisticated, polished look.

2. Can I wear a baseball cap with short hair?

Yes, baseball caps are a great choice for short hair. Just make sure to choose a cap that sits low on the head and doesn't add too much height.

3. How do I prevent my hair from getting flat while wearing a hat?

Use a volumizing spray before putting on the hat to prevent flat, lifeless hair.

Hair Care for Short Hair

Keeping your short hair looking great is important, especially if you're wearing hats often. Here are some tips for maintaining healthy, beautiful short hair:

- Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to prevent stripping your hair of its natural oils.

- Use a heat protectant spray before using any heat styling tools.

- Get regular haircuts to keep your hair looking fresh and healthy.

- Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair moisturized and nourished.

Conclusion

Now that you know which hats go best with short hair, how to style them, and how to care for your hair while wearing them, you can confidently rock any hat you want. Whether you're going for a casual, laid-back look or a sophisticated, polished look, there's a hat out there that will work perfectly for you.
