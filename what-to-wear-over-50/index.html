What To Wear Over 50 Tips And Ideas For Women

Written by Frank May 14, 2023 · 7 min read

As women age, their fashion and style preferences may evolve. Whether you’re nearing the age of 50 or have already crossed that milestone, you may be wondering what to wear over 50. In this article, we’ll give you some tips, ideas, and FAQs to help you dress confidently and comfortably.

Table of Contents

  • How to Dress Over 50
  • Step by Step Guide to Dressing Over 50
  • Tips for Dressing Over 50
  • FAQs About Dressing Over 50
  • Hair Care Tips for Women Over 50

How to Dress Over 50

First and foremost, dressing over 50 should be about comfort and confidence. You should wear clothes that make you feel good and reflect your personal style. However, there are some general guidelines you may want to keep in mind:

  • Choose high-quality fabrics that feel good against your skin and drape nicely.
  • Opt for classic styles that never go out of fashion, such as tailored blazers, A-line skirts, and straight-leg pants.
  • Avoid overly-trendy clothes that may look outdated in a few seasons.
  • Find pieces that fit you well and flatter your body shape.
  • Accessorize with jewelry, scarves, and other accessories to add interest to your outfit.

Of course, these are just guidelines, and you should feel free to experiment with your style as you see fit.

Step by Step Guide to Dressing Over 50

If you’re feeling overwhelmed by the prospect of revamping your wardrobe, follow these steps:

  1. Assess your current wardrobe. What items do you love? What items do you never wear?
  2. Identify your personal style. Do you prefer classic, trendy, bohemian, or another style?
  3. Determine your body shape. Are you pear-shaped, apple-shaped, hourglass-shaped, or another shape?
  4. Make a list of items you need to add to your wardrobe. Do you need more blouses, pants, or dresses?
  5. Shop for high-quality, well-fitting clothes that reflect your personal style.
  6. Accessorize with jewelry, scarves, and other accessories.
  7. Try on your outfits and make sure you feel comfortable and confident in them.
  8. Donate or sell any items that no longer fit or reflect your personal style.

Tips for Dressing Over 50

Here are some additional tips to help you dress over 50:

  • Invest in a few high-quality, versatile pieces that you can mix and match.
  • Choose colors that flatter your skin tone and make you feel good.
  • Don’t be afraid to show some skin, but do it tastefully. For example, a v-neck sweater can be sexy without being too revealing.
  • Avoid baggy or ill-fitting clothes, which can make you look frumpy.
  • Consider layering to add interest and texture to your outfit.
  • Accessorize with statement jewelry or scarves to add color and personality to your outfit.

FAQs About Dressing Over 50

Q: Can I still wear jeans over 50?

A: Yes! Jeans are a timeless classic and can be worn at any age. Just make sure you choose a style that flatters your body shape and fits well.

Q: Should I wear heels or flats over 50?

A: It depends on your personal style and comfort level. Heels can add height and elegance to an outfit, but flats can be more comfortable for everyday wear. Choose the style that works best for you.

Q: Can I wear prints over 50?

A: Yes! Prints can add interest and personality to your outfit. Just make sure you choose a print that flatters your body shape and isn’t too overwhelming.

Hair Care Tips for Women Over 50

Your hair is an important part of your overall look, so it’s important to take care of it. Here are some hair care tips for women over 50:

  • Choose a hairstyle that flatters your face shape and hair texture.
  • Consider adding highlights or lowlights to add dimension to your hair.
  • Use a high-quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and shiny.
  • Avoid over-styling your hair with heat tools, which can damage your hair.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair looking healthy and prevent split ends.

With these tips and ideas, you can dress confidently and stylishly over 50. Remember, the most important thing is to wear what makes you feel good and reflects your personal style.
