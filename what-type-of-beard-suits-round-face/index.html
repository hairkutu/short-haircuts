What Type Of Beard Suits Round Face The Ultimate Guide In 2023

Written by Eveline Apr 17, 2023 · 8 min read

Are you tired of trying different beard styles and none of them seem to suit your round face? Don't worry, we've got you covered. In this ultimate guide, we will discuss what type of beard suits round face and give you tips on how to maintain it. So, let's dive in!

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to Choose a Beard Style for a Round Face
  • Step by Step Guide on How to Grow and Maintain a Beard for a Round Face
  • Tips on How to Style and Maintain Your Beard
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care Tips for Your Beard

Description

A round face is characterized by soft, curved lines and a width that is almost equal to its length. If you have a round face, you need to choose a beard style that will add some angles and definition to your face. The right beard style can make your face look longer and slimmer, while the wrong one can make it look even rounder.

How to Choose a Beard Style for a Round Face

The best beard style for a round face is one that adds length and definition to your face. Here are some tips to help you choose the right beard style:

1. Go for Angular Beard Styles

Angular beard styles like the goatee, French fork, and anchor beard can add some angles and definition to your round face. These beard styles create a V-shape that makes your face look longer and slimmer.

2. Avoid Full Beards

Full beards can make your face look even rounder. If you want to grow a full beard, make sure to keep it trimmed and well-groomed.

3. Keep the Sides Shorter

Keeping the sides of your beard shorter can also make your face look longer. This creates an illusion of a longer jawline, which can make your face look less round.

Step by Step Guide on How to Grow and Maintain a Beard for a Round Face

Here is a step by step guide on how to grow and maintain a beard for a round face:

Step 1: Start with a Clean Shave

Start by shaving your face clean. This will give you a fresh start and help you see the shape of your face clearly.

Step 2: Choose the Right Beard Style

Choose a beard style that adds definition and angles to your face. Angular beard styles like the goatee, French fork, and anchor beard are great options for a round face.

Step 3: Let Your Beard Grow

Let your beard grow for a few weeks until it reaches the desired length. Make sure to keep the sides shorter to create an illusion of a longer jawline.

Step 4: Trim and Shape Your Beard

Trim and shape your beard regularly to maintain its shape. Use a beard trimmer to trim the sides and a pair of scissors to trim the top.

Tips on How to Style and Maintain Your Beard

Here are some tips on how to style and maintain your beard:

1. Use Beard Oil

Beard oil can help keep your beard soft and moisturized. Apply a few drops of beard oil to your beard daily to keep it healthy and shiny.

2. Brush Your Beard

Brushing your beard regularly can help distribute the natural oils and prevent tangles. Use a beard brush to brush your beard daily.

3. Trim Regularly

Trim your beard regularly to maintain its shape. Use a beard trimmer to trim the sides and a pair of scissors to trim the top.

FAQs

What type of beard suits a round face?

The best beard style for a round face is one that adds length and definition to your face. Angular beard styles like the goatee, French fork, and anchor beard are great options for a round face.

How do I maintain my beard?

To maintain your beard, you need to trim it regularly, use beard oil to keep it moisturized, and brush it daily to prevent tangles.

Hair Care Tips for Your Beard

Here are some hair care tips for your beard:

1. Wash Your Beard Regularly

Wash your beard regularly with a beard shampoo to keep it clean and prevent dandruff.

2. Use Conditioner

Use a beard conditioner to keep your beard soft and moisturized.

3. Avoid Hot Water

Avoid washing your beard with hot water as it can strip the natural oils and make it dry and brittle.

In conclusion, choosing the right beard style for a round face can be challenging. However, with the tips and tricks we've given you in this article, you should be able to find the perfect beard style that suits your face shape. Remember to maintain your beard regularly to keep it healthy and shiny. Good luck!
