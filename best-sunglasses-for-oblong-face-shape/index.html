How to Choose Sunglasses for Oblong Face Shape

When it comes to choosing sunglasses for an oblong face shape, there are a few things to keep in mind:

  • Frame shape: Look for frames that are wider than your face and have rounded edges. This will help balance out the length of your face.
  • Frame size: Make sure the frames are not too big or too small for your face. You want them to fit snugly without being too tight.
  • Lens shape: Oval, round, and square lenses work well with oblong face shapes. Avoid narrow and rectangular lenses.
  • Lens color: Darker lenses are great for bright days, but if you plan on wearing your sunglasses indoors or in low light, you may want to consider lighter lenses.

Best Sunglasses for Oblong Face Shape

Now that you know what to look for, here are some of the best sunglasses for oblong face shape:

1. Ray-Ban Aviator Sunglasses

The iconic Ray-Ban aviator sunglasses are a great choice for oblong face shapes. The teardrop shape of the lenses and the curved edges of the frames help balance out the length of the face.

2. Oakley Holbrook Sunglasses

The Oakley Holbrook sunglasses have a classic square shape that works well with oblong face shapes. The frames are also wider than the face, which helps create a more balanced look.

3. Maui Jim Breakwall Sunglasses

The Maui Jim Breakwall sunglasses have a round shape that works well with oblong face shapes. The frames are also wider than the face, which helps create a more balanced look.

4. Tom Ford Miranda Sunglasses

The Tom Ford Miranda sunglasses have an oversized, round shape that works well with oblong face shapes. The frames are also wider than the face, which helps create a more balanced look.

Hair Care Tips for Oblong Face Shape

When it comes to hair care for oblong face shapes, there are a few things to keep in mind:

  • Side-swept bangs: Side-swept bangs can help create the illusion of a shorter face.
  • Layers: Adding layers to your hair can help create volume and add width to your face.
  • Bobs: A chin-length bob can help balance out the length of your face.
  • Avoid long, straight hair: Long, straight hair can make an oblong face shape look even longer.

FAQs

What sunglasses should I avoid if I have an oblong face shape?

Avoid narrow and rectangular lenses, as they can make your face look even longer.

Can I wear oversized sunglasses if I have an oblong face shape?

Yes, but make sure the frames are wider than your face to help create a more balanced look.

What is the best frame shape for oblong face shapes?

Look for frames that are wider than your face and have rounded edges.
