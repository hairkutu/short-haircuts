How To Cut Long Men s Hair At Home A Step By Step Guide

Written by Robby Apr 09, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Long hair has become a trend among men in recent years, but going to the salon or barbershop to get a trim can be expensive. Luckily, cutting long men's hair at home is a simple and cost-effective solution. In this article, we will provide you with a step-by-step guide on how to cut long men's hair at home.

Table of Contents:

  • How to Cut Long Men's Hair at Home
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Cutting Long Men's Hair at Home
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care After Cutting Long Men's Hair at Home

How to Cut Long Men's Hair at Home

Cutting long men's hair at home may seem daunting, but with the right tools and techniques, it can be done easily. Before getting started, ensure that you have the following:

  • Scissors
  • Clipper
  • Comb
  • Hair ties
  • Water spray bottle
  • Cape or towel to cover shoulders

Once you have your tools ready, follow these steps to cut long men's hair at home.

Step-by-Step Guide

Step 1: Start by washing and drying the hair. Comb it to remove any knots or tangles.

Step 2: Use the clipper to trim the hair on the sides and back of the head. Begin by attaching the longest guard to the clipper and trimming the hair in an upward motion. Gradually decrease the guard length and continue trimming until the desired length is achieved. Use the comb to ensure that the hair is evenly trimmed.

Step 3: Use the scissors to trim the top of the hair. Start at the front and work towards the back, using the comb to pull the hair upward and trim the ends evenly.

Step 4: Use the scissors to trim the hair around the ears and neck. Use the comb to pull the hair outwards and trim the ends evenly.

Step 5: Blend the hair by going over the sections with both the clipper and scissors. This will ensure that the hair looks even and natural.

Step 6: Use the comb to check for any uneven sections and trim them accordingly.

Step 7: Style the hair as desired using products such as hair gel or wax.

Tips for Cutting Long Men's Hair at Home

Here are some tips to help you cut long men's hair at home like a pro:

  • Start by trimming a little bit of hair at a time to avoid cutting too much.
  • Use the scissors to blend the hair to create a natural look.
  • Make sure that the hair is wet before trimming to prevent any uneven cutting.
  • Take breaks in between to avoid fatigue and ensure that the hair is being cut evenly.
  • Invest in good quality tools to make the process easier and more efficient.

FAQs

Q1: How often should I cut my long hair?

A1: It is recommended to trim your hair every 6-8 weeks to maintain its length and health.

Q2: Can I cut my hair with regular scissors?

A2: While it is possible to cut hair with regular scissors, it is recommended to use hair-cutting scissors for a more precise cut.

Hair Care After Cutting Long Men's Hair at Home

After cutting long men's hair at home, it is important to take care of it to maintain its health and length. Here are some tips:

  • Use a good quality shampoo and conditioner to keep the hair clean and moisturized.
  • Avoid using hot tools such as blow dryers or straighteners too often, as they can damage the hair.
  • Trim the hair regularly to avoid split ends and maintain its length.
  • Avoid washing the hair too often, as it can strip the hair of its natural oils.

By following this guide, you can easily cut long men's hair at home and save yourself a trip to the salon or barbershop. Remember to take your time and use the right tools to achieve the desired result. Happy cutting!
