How To Fade Haircut Yourself A Step By Step Guide

Written by Frank Aug 14, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Are you tired of spending money and time at the barbershop? Do you want to learn how to fade haircut yourself? If so, you've come to the right place. In this article, we'll guide you through the process of fading your hair at home with ease. From how to prepare your hair to the final touches, we've got you covered. So, let's get started.

How to Prepare Your Hair for Fading

Before you start fading your hair, you need to prepare it properly. Here's what you need to do:

Step 1: Wash Your Hair

Start by washing your hair with shampoo and conditioner. This will remove any dirt and oil buildup and make it easier to cut.

Step 2: Dry Your Hair

Use a towel to dry your hair thoroughly. Wet hair can be challenging to cut, so ensure your hair is completely dry before you start.

Step 3: Comb Your Hair

Use a comb to detangle your hair and make it easier to cut.

How to Fade Haircut Yourself: Step-by-Step Guide

Now that you've prepared your hair, it's time to start fading. Here's how:

Step 1: Choose the Right Clippers

The first thing you need to do is choose the right clippers. We recommend using a professional-grade clipper with adjustable blades.

Step 2: Choose the Right Guard

Next, choose the right guard for your desired length. Start with a higher guard and work your way down for a gradual fade.

Step 3: Start Fading

Start by using the highest guard and clip your hair from the bottom up. Make sure to blend the hair as you go along.

Step 4: Change the Guard

As you move up your head, change to a lower guard to create a gradual fade.

Step 5: Blend the Sides

Use a comb to blend the sides and the top of the hair.

Step 6: Taper the Neckline

Lastly, taper the neckline to give your haircut a clean finish.

Tips for Fading Your Hair at Home

Here are some tips to help you fade your hair like a pro:

  • Take your time and be patient. Fading can be time-consuming, but it's worth it in the end.
  • Use a mirror to check your progress.
  • Start with a higher guard and work your way down.
  • Blend the hair as you go along.
  • Don't forget to taper the neckline for a clean finish.

Frequently Asked Questions

Here are some common questions about fading your hair at home:

Is it easy to fade your hair at home?

With the right tools and techniques, fading your hair at home can be easy and cost-effective.

What clippers should I use?

We recommend using a professional-grade clipper with adjustable blades for the best results.

Can I fade my hair without a guard?

It's not recommended to fade your hair without a guard, as this can result in uneven and choppy hair.

Hair Care Tips

After fading your hair, it's important to take care of it properly. Here are some tips:

  • Use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy.
  • Avoid using heat-styling tools too often.
  • Trim your hair regularly to maintain your haircut.
  • Protect your hair from the sun with a hat or scarf.

Now that you know how to fade haircut yourself, you can save time and money by doing it at home. With the right tools and techniques, you can achieve a professional-looking fade that will make you look and feel great. So, go ahead and give it a try!
