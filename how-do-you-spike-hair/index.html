How Do You Spike Hair: A Complete Guide | Hair Care Tips 2023

Do you want to add volume and texture to your hair? Spiking your hair is a great way to achieve this look. In this article, we will show you step-by-step how to spike your hair, along with some tips and hair care advice.

Table of Contents

  • How to Spike Hair
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Spiking Hair
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care for Spiked Hair

How to Spike Hair

Spiking your hair involves using a product to create height and texture. There are several products that you can use to spike your hair, including hair gel, pomade, wax, and hairspray. The type of product you use will depend on your hair type and the level of hold you want.

Hair Gel

Hair gel is the most common product used for spiking hair. It provides a strong hold and works well for most hair types. Gel can also be used to create different textures, from a wet look to a matte finish.

Pomade

Pomade is a wax-like substance that provides a medium hold. It works well for shorter hair and can be used to create a shiny finish.

Hair Wax

Hair wax is a thicker product that provides a strong hold. It works well for thicker hair and can be used to create a messy, textured look.

Hairspray

Hairspray can be used on its own or in combination with other products. It provides a strong hold and works well for longer hair.

Step-by-Step Guide

Follow these steps to spike your hair:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair.
  2. Apply your chosen product to your hair, starting at the roots and working your way to the tips. Use a small amount of product to avoid weighing down your hair.
  3. Use your fingers or a comb to create the desired shape and texture. You can create spikes all over your head or just in certain areas.
  4. Finish with a hairspray to set the style and provide extra hold.

It's important to note that spiking your hair can be damaging to your hair if done frequently. Make sure to give your hair a break in between spiking to avoid damage.

Tips for Spiking Hair

Here are some tips to help you achieve the perfect spiked hair look:

  • Choose the right product for your hair type and the level of hold you want.
  • Start with a small amount of product and add more as needed.
  • Use a comb or your fingers to create the desired shape and texture.
  • Experiment with different products and techniques to find what works best for you.
  • Use a hairspray to set the style and provide extra hold.

FAQs

Can I spike my hair if it's thin or fine?

Yes, you can still spike your hair if it's thin or fine. Use a lightweight gel or pomade to avoid weighing down your hair.

How long will my spiked hair last?

The length of time your spiked hair will last depends on the product you use and how well you set the style. Hairspray can help to prolong the hold.

Can I create spikes with curly hair?

Yes, you can create spikes with curly hair. Use a curl-defining gel and a diffuser to create volume and texture.

Hair Care for Spiked Hair

Spiking your hair can be damaging to your hair if done frequently. Make sure to give your hair a break in between spiking to avoid damage. Here are some tips to help care for your spiked hair:

  • Use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair hydrated.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools, such as flat irons and curling irons, as they can further damage your hair.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.
  • Consider using a hair mask or deep conditioner once a week to help nourish and repair your hair.

With these tips and techniques, you'll be able to create the perfect spiked hair look. Remember to take care of your hair to avoid damage and keep it looking healthy and shiny.
