How To Cut Bangs Yourself Twist A Step By Step Guide

Written by Robby May 19, 2023 · 5 min read

Cutting your own bangs can be a daunting task, but with a little bit of patience and practice, you can achieve a salon-worthy look right at home. In this post, we’ll guide you through the process of cutting bangs yourself twist style. Whether you’re looking for side-swept bangs or blunt ones, we’ve got you covered. So, grab your scissors and let’s get started!

How to Cut Bangs Yourself

The first thing you’ll need to do is section your hair. Take a comb and create a triangle section of hair at the front of your head. This will be the area where you’ll be cutting your bangs. Make sure the triangle is centered and not too wide or narrow.

Next, comb the hair in the triangle downwards, and twist the hair so that it’s in a tight bundle. This will make it easier for you to cut the hair evenly. If you’re going for side-swept bangs, twist the hair to the side you want your bangs to fall.

Step-by-Step Guide

1. Start by combing your hair and creating a triangle section at the front.

2. Comb the hair in the triangle downwards and twist it into a tight bundle.

3. Cut the hair at an angle, following the shape of the triangle. For side-swept bangs, cut diagonally.

4. Check the length of your bangs and trim any uneven pieces. Be careful not to cut too much.

5. Release the twist and style your new bangs.

Tips

- Use sharp scissors to avoid split ends.

- Cut a little bit at a time, as you can always cut more if needed.

- Don’t cut your bangs when your hair is wet, as it will shrink when it dries.

FAQ

Q: Can I cut my bangs with regular scissors?

A: While you can use regular scissors, it’s best to use hair-cutting scissors as they are sharper and will give you a cleaner cut.

Q: How often should I trim my bangs?

A: It depends on how fast your hair grows and how long you want your bangs to be. On average, every 4-6 weeks is a good time frame for a trim.

Hair Care

Now that you’ve cut your bangs, it’s important to take care of them to maintain their shape and style. Here are a few tips:

- Use a dry shampoo to keep your bangs from getting oily.

- Don’t use too much heat on your bangs, as it can damage the hair.

- If you have curly hair, use a diffuser when blow-drying your bangs to avoid frizz.

With these tips and tricks, you’ll be able to cut your own bangs twist style and maintain them like a pro. Good luck!
