Can You Fade Your Own Hair A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Aprilia May 21, 2023 Â· 6 min read

If you're looking to switch up your hair game, fading your hair is a popular trend that can give you a fresh, edgy look. But can you fade your own hair at home? The answer is yes! In this article, we'll cover everything you need to know about fading your own hair, including step-by-step instructions, helpful tips, and hair care advice.

How to Fade Your Own Hair: Step by Step

Fading your own hair can seem daunting, but with the right tools and techniques, you can achieve a professional-looking fade from the comfort of your own home. Here's how to do it:

Step 1: Gather Your Supplies

Before you begin, you'll need to gather the following supplies:

  • Clippers
  • Guard combs
  • A mirror
  • Scissors
  • A comb
  • A cape or towel to protect your clothes

Step 2: Start with a High Guard

Start with a high guard comb and work your way down to a lower guard as you progress. Begin by cutting the hair on the sides and back of your head with the highest guard. Use the clippers in an upward motion to create a blended look.

Step 3: Use a Lower Guard

Once you've blended the hair with the highest guard, switch to a lower guard and repeat the process. Start at the bottom and work your way up, using an upward motion to blend the hair as you go.

Step 4: Use Scissors to Blend

Once you've used the clippers, use scissors to blend the hair even further. Comb the hair upward and use the scissors to trim any uneven pieces. Use the comb to blend the hair by cutting in a downward motion.

Step 5: Final Touches

Once you've blended the hair to your liking, use the clippers to clean up any stray hairs around the ears and neckline. Use the scissors to trim the hair at the top of your head to your desired length.

Tips for Fading Your Own Hair

Fading your own hair can be challenging, but these tips can help you achieve a professional-looking fade:

  • Take your time and be patient. Fading your own hair requires precision and attention to detail.
  • Start with a high guard and work your way down to a lower guard to achieve a blended look.
  • Use scissors to blend the hair even further.
  • Use a mirror to check the back of your head periodically throughout the process.
  • Don't be afraid to ask a friend or family member for help if you're struggling.

FAQs About Fading Your Own Hair

Can You Fade Your Hair Without Clippers?

No, clippers are essential for fading your own hair. Without them, it's nearly impossible to achieve a blended look.

What Guard Should You Use to Start Fading Your Hair?

Start with a high guard, such as a #4, and work your way down to a lower guard as you progress.

How Long Does It Take to Fade Your Own Hair?

The amount of time it takes to fade your own hair depends on the length of your hair and your skill level. It can take anywhere from 30 minutes to an hour or more.

Hair Care After Fading Your Hair

After fading your own hair, it's important to take care of it properly to maintain the look. Here are some tips:

  • Use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair hydrated.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools, as they can damage your hair.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair looking neat and tidy.
  • Avoid over-washing your hair, as it can strip it of its natural oils.

With these tips and techniques, you can successfully fade your own hair at home. Remember to take your time, be patient, and practice regularly to perfect your skills. Happy fading!
