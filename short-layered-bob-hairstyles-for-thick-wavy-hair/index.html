Short Layered Bob Hairstyles For Thick Wavy Hair

Written by Frank Jul 02, 2023 Â· 6 min read

If you have thick and wavy hair, you might find it challenging to find the perfect hairstyle that suits your hair texture. Fortunately, a short layered bob is one of the best hairstyles for thick wavy hair. In this article, we will discuss how to achieve a short layered bob hairstyle for thick wavy hair, including tips, FAQs, and hair care.

Description

A short layered bob is a haircut that features a shorter length at the back and longer strands at the front. The layers are cut at different lengths to create dimension and movement. This hairstyle is perfect for women with thick and wavy hair as it can help reduce the bulkiness of the hair while still maintaining its natural texture.

How to Achieve Short Layered Bob Hairstyles for Thick Wavy Hair

Here are the steps to achieve a short layered bob hairstyle for thick wavy hair:

Step 1: Consult with a Hairstylist

Before getting a short layered bob haircut, it is essential to consult with a hairstylist who has experience cutting thick and wavy hair. They will be able to recommend the best haircut based on your hair texture and face shape.

Step 2: Wash and Condition Your Hair

Wash and condition your hair before going to the salon. It will help your hairstylist to cut your hair more accurately and precisely.

Step 3: Section Your Hair

Section your hair into four parts (two at the front and two at the back) to make it easier for your hairstylist to cut your hair.

Step 4: Cut Your Hair

Your hairstylist will cut your hair at different lengths to create layers. They may also use thinning shears to reduce the bulkiness of your hair.

Step 5: Style Your Hair

After cutting your hair, your hairstylist will blow dry and style your hair. You can add some texture by using a curling iron or a texturizing spray.

Tips

Here are some tips to maintain your short layered bob hairstyle for thick wavy hair:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to prevent your hair from becoming dry and frizzy.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often as it can damage your hair.
  • Apply a hair mask once a week to keep your hair moisturized and healthy.
  • Trim your hair every six to eight weeks to maintain the shape of your haircut.

FAQ

Q: Will a short layered bob work for all face shapes?

A: A short layered bob can work for all face shapes, but the length and layers may vary based on your face shape.

Q: Can I still wear my hair straight with a short layered bob?

A: Yes, you can still wear your hair straight with a short layered bob. However, it may require more styling time and effort.

Q: How often should I wash my hair with a short layered bob?

A: It depends on your hair type and lifestyle. If you have oily hair, you may need to wash it every other day. If you have dry hair, you can wash it twice a week.

Hair Care

Here are some hair care tips to keep your short layered bob hairstyle for thick wavy hair healthy:

  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair gently.
  • Avoid using hot water to wash your hair as it can strip away natural oils and cause dryness.
  • Use a heat protectant spray before using heat styling tools.
  • Avoid using hair products that contain alcohol as it can dry out your hair.

A short layered bob is a versatile and trendy hairstyle that can suit any occasion. With the right hair care routine, you can maintain your short layered bob hairstyle for thick wavy hair and keep it healthy and beautiful.
