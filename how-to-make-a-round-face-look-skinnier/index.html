How To Make A Round Face Look Skinnier Tips Tricks And Hair Care

Written by Oliver May 08, 2023 · 7 min read

If you have a round face, you may be wondering how to make it look skinnier. Luckily, there are several tips and tricks you can use to create the illusion of a slimmer face. In this article, we’ll go over some of the best techniques for making a round face look skinnier, including hair care tips that can help accentuate your features.

Table of Contents

  • How To Make A Round Face Look Skinnier
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Looking Skinnier
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care Tips

How To Make A Round Face Look Skinnier

There are several techniques you can use to make a round face look skinnier. Here are a few of the best:

Contouring

Contouring is a makeup technique that involves using darker shades to create the illusion of shadows and depth. By contouring your cheeks, jawline, and forehead, you can make your face look slimmer and more defined.

Hairstyles

The right hairstyle can make a huge difference when it comes to making your face look slimmer. Long, straight hair can elongate your face, while layers can create the illusion of volume and texture.

Clothing

Wearing clothes that fit well and flatter your body shape can also help make your face look skinnier. Avoid baggy or loose clothing, which can make your face look rounder, and opt for more structured pieces that accentuate your curves.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here is a step-by-step guide on how to make a round face look skinnier:

Step 1: Contour Your Cheeks

Start by applying a darker shade of foundation or contour powder to the hollows of your cheeks. Use a brush to blend the product upwards towards your ears, creating a natural-looking shadow.

Step 2: Contour Your Jawline

Next, apply the same darker shade to your jawline, blending downwards towards your neck. This will create the illusion of a more defined jawline, making your face look slimmer.

Step 3: Highlight Your Forehead

To further accentuate your features, apply a lighter shade of foundation or concealer to the center of your forehead. This will help create the illusion of a taller forehead, making your face look longer and slimmer.

Tips for Looking Skinnier

Here are a few additional tips for making your face look skinnier:

Drink Plenty of Water

Staying hydrated can help reduce bloating and puffiness in your face, making it look slimmer. Aim to drink at least 8 glasses of water a day for best results.

Avoid Salt and Sugar

Salt and sugar can both contribute to bloating, so it’s best to avoid them as much as possible. Opt for fresh, whole foods instead, and try seasoning your meals with herbs and spices instead of salt.

Get Plenty of Sleep

Lack of sleep can cause fluid retention and puffiness in the face, making it look rounder. Aim for 7-8 hours of sleep a night to help reduce bloating and puffiness.

FAQs

Q: Can I make my face look skinnier without makeup?

A: Yes! By choosing the right hairstyles and clothing, you can create the illusion of a slimmer face without any makeup.

Q: How often should I wash my hair?

A: It depends on your hair type and lifestyle, but most people can get away with washing their hair every 2-3 days.

Hair Care Tips

Your hair can play a big role in how your face looks, so it’s important to take good care of it. Here are a few hair care tips that can help make your face look skinnier:

Avoid Heavy Products

Heavy hair products, like waxes and gels, can weigh down your hair and make your face look rounder. Opt for lighter products, like mousses and sprays, that add volume without weighing your hair down.

Try a Side Part

A side part can help create the illusion of a longer face, making your face look slimmer. Experiment with different parting styles to see what works best for you.

Get Regular Trims

Regular trims can help keep your hair looking healthy and prevent split ends, which can make your hair look thin and flat. Aim to get a trim every 6-8 weeks to keep your hair looking its best.

By following these tips and tricks, you can create the illusion of a slimmer face and feel confident and beautiful in your own skin.
