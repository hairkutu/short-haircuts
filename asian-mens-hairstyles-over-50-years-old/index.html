Asian Men s Hairstyles Over 50 Years Old A Guide

As men age, their hair also changes with them. It may become thinner, gray, or even fall out. However, it doesn't mean that you can't look stylish and fashionable anymore. If you're an Asian man over 50 years old, we've got you covered. In this article, we will share some Asian men's hairstyles that are perfect for mature men. Let's dive in!

Description

As an older Asian man, you need a hairstyle that complements your age and personality. You may want to choose a hairstyle that's easy to maintain, looks professional, and suits your face shape. Some of the popular hairstyles for Asian men over 50 are the crew cut, buzz cut, slick back, side part, and textured crop.

How to Choose the Right Hairstyle

Choosing the right hairstyle can be overwhelming, but it doesn't have to be. Here are some factors to consider when selecting a hairstyle:

  • Face shape: Your hairstyle should complement your face shape. For example, if you have a round face, a buzz cut or a crew cut with short sides and volume on top can make your face appear longer.
  • Hair texture: Your hair's texture can also affect how a hairstyle looks on you. If you have thinning hair, a textured crop or a crew cut can add volume to your hair. On the other hand, if you have thick hair, a slick back or a side part can give you a polished look.
  • Lifestyle: Your lifestyle can also influence your hairstyle choice. If you're an active person, you may want a low-maintenance hairstyle that doesn't require a lot of styling. If you have a formal job, you may want a hairstyle that looks professional.

Step by Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide on how to get some of the popular Asian men's hairstyles:

Crew Cut

To get a crew cut:

  1. Ask your barber to cut your hair short on the sides and back, leaving about an inch of length on top.
  2. Use a styling product, such as pomade or wax, to add texture and volume to your hair.

Buzz Cut

To get a buzz cut:

  1. Ask your barber to use clippers to cut your hair short all over.
  2. It's a low-maintenance hairstyle that doesn't require any styling.

Slick Back

To get a slick back:

  1. Ask your barber to cut your hair short on the sides and back, leaving about 2-3 inches of length on top.
  2. Apply a generous amount of hair gel or pomade to your hair.
  3. Comb your hair back, starting from the forehead, and work your way towards the back of your head.

Side Part

To get a side part:

  1. Ask your barber to cut your hair short on the sides and back, leaving about 3-4 inches of length on top.
  2. Apply a small amount of hair product to your hair.
  3. Part your hair on one side, using a comb or your fingers.

Textured Crop

To get a textured crop:

  1. Ask your barber to cut your hair short on the sides and back, leaving about 2-3 inches of length on top.
  2. Ask your barber to use scissors to create choppy layers on top of your hair.
  3. Apply a hair styling product, such as clay or paste, to your hair.

Tips for Styling

Here are some tips for styling your hair:

  • Use a hair styling product that works for your hair type and hairstyle.
  • Don't use too much product as it can make your hair look greasy.
  • Comb your hair in the direction that you want it to go.
  • Invest in a good quality comb or brush.
  • Get regular haircuts to maintain your hairstyle.

FAQs

What hairstyles are popular for Asian men over 50?

Some of the popular hairstyles for Asian men over 50 are the crew cut, buzz cut, slick back, side part, and textured crop.

What factors should I consider when choosing a hairstyle?

You should consider your face shape, hair texture, and lifestyle when choosing a hairstyle.

Hair Care Tips

Here are some hair care tips for mature men:

  • Wash your hair with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using hot water as it can dry out your hair.
  • Use a hair mask once a week to nourish your hair.
  • Avoid using harsh chemicals or heat styling tools that can damage your hair.
  • Eat a balanced diet that's rich in vitamins and minerals to promote healthy hair growth.

In conclusion, as an Asian man over 50, you have plenty of hairstyle options to choose from. Consider your face shape, hair texture, and lifestyle when selecting a hairstyle. Don't forget to take care of your hair by washing it regularly and using hair care products that work for you. With the right hairstyle and hair care routine, you can look stylish and confident at any age.
