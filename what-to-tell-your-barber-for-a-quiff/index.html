What To Tell Your Barber For A Quiff

Written by Luffy Aug 08, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Are you looking to spice up your hairstyle with a quiff? A quiff is a classic and versatile hairstyle that can be worn by men of all ages. However, getting the perfect quiff requires more than just a good haircut. You need to communicate with your barber and tell them exactly what you want. In this article, we will guide you on what to tell your barber for a quiff.

Table of Content

  1. Description
  2. How to
  3. Step by step
  4. Tips
  5. FAQ
  6. Hair Care

Description

A quiff is a hairstyle where the hair at the front is styled upwards and back, while the hair at the sides and back is kept short. The quiff has been around since the 1950s and is still popular today. The hairstyle can be worn in different variations, such as a classic quiff, a messy quiff, a slicked-back quiff, or a textured quiff. The key to getting the perfect quiff is to communicate with your barber and tell them exactly what you want.

How to

The first step is to find a barber who is skilled in cutting quiffs. Look for a barber who has experience in cutting men's hair and has a portfolio of quiff hairstyles. Once you find a good barber, schedule an appointment and explain to them that you want a quiff. Be specific about the type of quiff you want, whether it's a classic or modern quiff, and show them pictures for reference.

Step by step

Here are the steps to follow to get a quiff:

  1. Show your barber a picture of the quiff you want
  2. Ask your barber to leave at least 2-3 inches of hair at the front
  3. Ask your barber to taper the sides and back with clippers or scissors
  4. Ask your barber to add texture to the hair at the top
  5. Ask your barber to use a hair dryer and a round brush to style the hair upwards and back
  6. Ask your barber to use a hair product, such as pomade, clay, or wax, to hold the hair in place

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when getting a quiff:

  • Make sure your hair is clean and dry before getting a quiff
  • Use a good quality shampoo and conditioner to maintain healthy hair
  • Choose a hair product that suits your hair type and the type of quiff you want
  • Experiment with different styles and variations of the quiff to find the one that suits you best

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about getting a quiff:

How long does it take to get a quiff?

Getting a quiff can take anywhere from 30 minutes to an hour, depending on the complexity of the style and the skill of your barber.

What hair products should I use for a quiff?

The best hair products for a quiff are pomade, clay, or wax. These products provide a strong hold and a matte or shiny finish, depending on your preference.

How often should I get a quiff?

You should get a quiff trimmed every 4-6 weeks to maintain the shape and length of the hairstyle.

Hair Care

Here are some tips to maintain healthy hair:

  • Wash your hair with a good quality shampoo and conditioner
  • Avoid using hot water and harsh chemicals on your hair
  • Use a hair mask or oil treatment once a week to nourish your hair
  • Avoid over-styling your hair with heat tools and hair products

Now that you know what to tell your barber for a quiff, it's time to book an appointment and get a new look. Remember to communicate clearly with your barber and be specific about the type of quiff you want. With the right haircut and styling, you can rock a quiff like a pro.
