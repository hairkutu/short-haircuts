Will I Look Good With Strawberry Blonde Hair

Written by Robby May 26, 2023 Â· 8 min read

Are you considering a new hair color and wondering if strawberry blonde would suit you? This shade is trendy and can give you a fresh, new look. In this article, we will discuss how to determine if you will look good with strawberry blonde hair, how to achieve the color, and tips on maintaining it.

Description

Strawberry blonde hair is a warm, golden blonde with hints of red. This color is perfect for those who want a subtle change from their natural hair color. It can also complement fair skin tones and bring out the green or blue in your eyes.

How to Determine if Strawberry Blonde will Suit You

There are a few factors to consider when determining if strawberry blonde hair will suit you:

1. Skin Tone

Fair skin with warm undertones looks best with strawberry blonde hair. If your skin has cool undertones, you may want to try a cooler blonde shade.

2. Eye Color

Strawberry blonde hair can bring out the green or blue in your eyes. If you have brown eyes, this color may not be as flattering.

3. Natural Hair Color

If your natural hair color is blonde or light brown, strawberry blonde will be easier to achieve. If you have darker hair, you may need to lighten it first before applying the color.

How to Achieve Strawberry Blonde Hair

If you have light hair, you can achieve strawberry blonde hair by using a box dye from a drugstore or salon. If you have dark hair, you may need to bleach it first before applying the color. It is recommended to seek the help of a professional stylist to avoid damaging your hair.

Step by Step Guide

Here is a step by step guide on how to achieve strawberry blonde hair:

1. Consult a Professional

If you have dark hair, it is best to consult a professional stylist to avoid damaging your hair. They can also recommend the best shade of strawberry blonde for your skin tone and natural hair color.

2. Bleach Your Hair (If Necessary)

If you have dark hair, your stylist will need to bleach it first before applying the color. This will lighten your hair and allow the strawberry blonde color to show.

3. Apply the Color

The stylist will apply the strawberry blonde color to your hair and let it process according to the instructions on the box.

4. Rinse and Style

Once the color has processed, your stylist will rinse it out and style your hair. They may recommend a color-safe shampoo and conditioner to maintain the color.

Tips for Maintaining Strawberry Blonde Hair

Here are some tips for maintaining your strawberry blonde hair:

1. Use Color-Safe Products

Use a color-safe shampoo and conditioner to avoid fading the color.

2. Avoid Heat Styling

Avoid using heat styling tools such as flat irons and curling irons as they can damage your hair and fade the color.

3. Protect Your Hair from the Sun

Wear a hat or use a UV protectant spray to protect your hair from the sun's harmful rays, which can fade the color.

FAQ

1. Can I Achieve Strawberry Blonde Hair at Home?

If you have light hair, you can achieve strawberry blonde hair at home using a box dye from a drugstore or salon. If you have dark hair, it is recommended to seek the help of a professional stylist to avoid damaging your hair.

2. Will Strawberry Blonde Hair Suit Me?

Strawberry blonde hair suits those with fair skin tones and warm undertones. It can also bring out the green or blue in your eyes.

3. How Do I Maintain Strawberry Blonde Hair?

Use color-safe products, avoid heat styling, and protect your hair from the sun to maintain your strawberry blonde hair.

Hair Care

It is essential to take care of your hair after dyeing it to maintain its health and vibrancy. Here are some tips for hair care:

1. Use a Deep Conditioning Treatment

Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair nourished and healthy.

2. Trim Your Hair Regularly

Trim your hair every six to eight weeks to prevent split ends and keep your hair looking healthy.

3. Avoid Over-Washing Your Hair

Avoid washing your hair every day as it can strip your hair of its natural oils. Instead, wash your hair every two to three days.

In conclusion, strawberry blonde hair can be a great option for those looking for a subtle change from their natural hair color. Determine if it will suit you based on your skin tone, eye color, and natural hair color. Achieve the color by either using a box dye at home or seeking the help of a professional stylist. Maintain the color by using color-safe products, avoiding heat styling, and protecting your hair from the sun.
