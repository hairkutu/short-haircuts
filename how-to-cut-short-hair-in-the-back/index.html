How To Cut Short Hair In The Back A Step By Step Guide

Written by Frank Aug 03, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Are you tired of paying a fortune to get your hair cut? Do you want to learn how to cut your own hair? If you have short hair, you may be wondering how to cut the back. In this article, we will provide you with a step-by-step guide on how to cut short hair in the back.

How to Cut Short Hair in the Back

Cutting the back of your short hair can be a challenging task, but with the right tools and techniques, you can do it yourself. Here are the steps you should follow:

Step 1: Gather the Tools

To cut your hair in the back, you will need a few tools. You will need a pair of sharp scissors, a comb, and a mirror. Make sure that your scissors are sharp, as dull scissors can damage your hair.

Step 2: Wash and Dry Your Hair

Before you start cutting your hair, you should wash and dry it. This will make it easier to work with and will help you see the shape of your hair.

Step 3: Divide Your Hair

To cut the back of your hair, you need to divide it into sections. Start by dividing your hair in half, from ear to ear.

Step 4: Cut the Bottom Section

Take the bottom section of your hair and comb it straight down. Hold the hair between your fingers and cut it straight across. Make sure that you cut the hair evenly, as this will affect the overall shape of your haircut.

Step 5: Cut the Top Section

Take the top section of your hair and comb it straight down. Hold the hair between your fingers and cut it straight across. Make sure that you cut the hair evenly, as this will affect the overall shape of your haircut.

Step 6: Check for Evenness

After you have cut both sections, check to make sure that they are even. Take a small section of hair from each side and compare them. If they are not even, trim the longer side until they match.

Step 7: Blend the Layers

If you want to add layers to your hair, you can do so by blending the layers. Take small sections of hair and cut them at an angle. This will create a layered effect.

Step 8: Clean Up the Edges

To clean up the edges of your haircut, take small sections of hair and trim them. Make sure that you are cutting the hair at the right angle, as this will affect the overall shape of your haircut.

Step 9: Style Your Hair

After you have finished cutting your hair, you can style it however you like. Use a blow dryer, curling iron, or straightener to create the look you want.

Tips for Cutting Short Hair in the Back

If you are new to cutting hair, here are some tips that can help you:

  • Start with small cuts and work your way up.
  • Use sharp scissors to avoid damaging your hair.
  • Take your time and be patient.
  • Use a mirror to check your progress.
  • Don't be afraid to ask for help.

FAQ

Q: Can I cut my hair in the back without a mirror?

A: It is not recommended to cut your hair in the back without a mirror, as it can be difficult to see what you are doing.

Q: How often should I cut my hair?

A: It is recommended to cut your hair every 6 to 8 weeks, depending on how fast your hair grows.

Hair Care

To keep your hair healthy, make sure that you are using the right products. Use a shampoo and conditioner that is appropriate for your hair type. Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as this can damage it.

With these tips and techniques, you can cut your own short hair in the back. Remember to be patient and take your time, and you will have a great haircut in no time.
