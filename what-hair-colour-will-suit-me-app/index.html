What Hair Colour Will Suit Me App A Guide To Finding Your Perfect Hair Colour

Written by Oliver Jun 18, 2023 Â· 6 min read

If you're thinking about changing your hair colour, you're not alone. Many people are interested in experimenting with different hair colours, but it can be hard to know which shade will suit you best. Luckily, there's a solution: what hair colour will suit me app. In this guide, we'll walk you through how to use this app to find your perfect hair colour.

Description

The what hair colour will suit me app is a mobile application that helps you determine which hair colour will suit you best. The app uses your skin tone, eye colour, and natural hair colour to suggest shades that will complement your features. It's a great tool to use if you're considering a new hair colour but don't know where to start.

How to Use the App

Using the what hair colour will suit me app is easy. Here's how to get started:

Step 1: Download the App

The first step is to download the app. You can find it in the App Store or Google Play Store. Once you've downloaded it, open the app.

Step 2: Take a Selfie

The app will prompt you to take a selfie. Make sure you're in good lighting and that your face is fully visible. The app will use this photo to analyze your features and suggest hair colours.

Step 3: Answer a Few Questions

The app will ask you a few questions about your natural hair colour, eye colour, and skin tone. Make sure to answer these questions honestly, as the app's suggestions will be based on your answers.

Step 4: Get Your Recommendations

Once you've answered the questions, the app will generate a list of hair colours that will suit you. You can browse through the list and see which shades you like best.

Tips for Using the App

Here are a few tips to keep in mind when using the what hair colour will suit me app:

  • Make sure your selfie is taken in good lighting.
  • Answer the questions honestly to get the best recommendations.
  • Take your time browsing through the suggested shades.
  • Consider consulting with a professional hairstylist before making a final decision.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about the what hair colour will suit me app:

What if I don't like any of the suggested shades?

If you don't like any of the suggested shades, you can retake the selfie and answer the questions again. The app will generate a new list of recommendations based on your new answers.

Is the app free?

Yes, the what hair colour will suit me app is free to download and use.

Can the app be used on all devices?

The app is available on both iOS and Android devices.

Hair Care

Once you've found your perfect hair colour, it's important to take care of your hair to maintain its vibrancy. Here are a few tips:

  • Use a shampoo and conditioner designed for coloured hair.
  • Avoid washing your hair too frequently.
  • Use a heat protectant when styling with heat tools.
  • Avoid over-processing your hair with too many chemical treatments.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair healthy.

Overall, the what hair colour will suit me app is a great tool to use when considering a new hair colour. By following the steps outlined in this guide and keeping our tips in mind, you'll be on your way to finding your perfect shade.
