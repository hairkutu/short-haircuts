Description

Mens hairstyles with long hair on top have become increasingly popular in recent years. This style is versatile and can be adapted to suit a variety of face shapes, hair types, and personal styles. Whether you're looking for a casual, laid-back look or a more polished and professional style, there's a long-hair-on-top hairstyle that's perfect for you.

How to Achieve Mens Hairstyles with Long Hair on Top

The first step to achieving mens hairstyles with long hair on top is to grow out your hair. This can take some time, but it's worth the wait. Once your hair is long enough, you can start experimenting with different styles. Some popular long-hair-on-top styles include the undercut, the man bun, and the messy top.

Undercut

The undercut is a popular mens hairstyle that features short hair on the sides and back and long hair on top. To achieve this look, ask your barber to buzz the sides and back of your head with clippers and leave the hair on top long. You can wear the hair on top slicked back, brushed to the side, or styled in a messy, textured way.

Man Bun

The man bun is another popular mens hairstyle with long hair on top. To achieve this look, gather your hair into a ponytail at the top of your head and wrap the hair around the base to create a bun. You can leave the bun messy and textured or slick it back for a more polished look.

Messy Top

The messy top is a great mens hairstyle for those who prefer a more casual, laid-back look. To achieve this style, leave your hair long on top and use a texturizing product to add volume and texture. You can wear the hair messy and tousled or slick it back for a more polished look.

Step by Step Guide

To achieve mens hairstyles with long hair on top, follow these simple steps:

  1. Grow out your hair until it's long enough to style on top.
  2. Choose a long-hair-on-top style that suits your face shape, hair type, and personal style.
  3. Visit a barber or stylist who is experienced in cutting long hair.
  4. Show them a picture of the style you want and ask for their advice on how to achieve it.
  5. Maintain your hairstyle by getting regular trims and using high-quality hair care products.

Tips for Maintaining Mens Hairstyles with Long Hair on Top

To keep your mens hairstyle with long hair on top looking great, follow these tips:

  • Use high-quality hair care products specifically designed for your hair type.
  • Avoid washing your hair every day, as this can strip it of its natural oils.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair looking neat and tidy.
  • Experiment with different styling products to find the one that works best for your hair.
  • Consider investing in a good quality hair dryer and styling tools to help you achieve the perfect look.

Frequently Asked Questions

What face shapes suit mens hairstyles with long hair on top?

Mens hairstyles with long hair on top can be adapted to suit a variety of face shapes, including oval, square, and heart-shaped faces. It's important to choose a style that complements your face shape and enhances your best features.

Can I achieve mens hairstyles with long hair on top if I have thinning hair?

Yes, you can still achieve mens hairstyles with long hair on top if you have thinning hair. You may need to use volumizing products or consider getting hair extensions to add volume and thickness to your hair.

Do I need to use special hair care products for mens hairstyles with long hair on top?

Yes, it's important to use high-quality hair care products specifically designed for your hair type to keep your mens hairstyle with long hair on top looking great. Look for products that are free from harsh chemicals and sulfates and contain nourishing ingredients like vitamins and minerals.

Hair Care for Mens Hairstyles with Long Hair on Top

To keep your mens hairstyle with long hair on top looking great, follow these hair care tips:

  • Use a gentle, sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils.
  • Apply a leave-in conditioner or hair mask once a week to nourish and hydrate your hair.
  • Avoid using hot styling tools too often, as this can damage your hair and make it brittle.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and wind by wearing a hat or using a hair product with built-in UV protection.
  • Eat a healthy, balanced diet rich in vitamins and minerals to promote healthy hair growth.

With these tips and tricks, you can achieve mens hairstyles with long hair on top that look great and are easy to maintain. Whether you prefer a polished, professional look or a more casual, laid-back style, there's a long-hair-on-top hairstyle that's perfect for you.
