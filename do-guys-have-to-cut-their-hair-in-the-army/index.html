Do Guys Have To Cut Their Hair In The Army

Written by Bardi May 26, 2023 Â· 6 min read

When it comes to joining the army, there are many questions that come to mind. One of the most common questions is "do guys have to cut their hair in the army?" The answer is yes, but there is much more to it than just a simple yes or no answer. In this article, we will dive into the details of the army's hair regulations for men.

Description

Joining the army is a big decision, and it comes with a lot of rules and regulations. One of the most well-known regulations is the hair policy. Men are required to maintain a certain length and style of hair while serving in the army. This policy is in place for a number of reasons, including hygiene, uniformity, and safety.

How to

If you are considering joining the army, it is important to know how to maintain your hair to meet the regulations. Here are some steps to follow:

Step 1: Understand the Regulations

The first step is to understand the regulations. Men are required to have their hair cut to a certain length and style. The hair on the top of the head must be kept neatly groomed and trimmed. The sides and back of the head must be tapered or faded to the skin. The hair cannot touch the collar, ears, or eyebrows.

Step 2: Find a Good Barber

It is important to find a barber who is familiar with military hair regulations. They will know how to cut your hair to meet the standards. It is also a good idea to establish a regular schedule for haircuts to ensure that your hair always meets the regulations.

Step 3: Maintain Your Hair

Maintaining your hair is essential to meeting the regulations. You should wash your hair regularly and keep it clean and neat. You should also use hair products that are approved by the army.

Tips

Here are some additional tips for maintaining your hair in the army:

  • Keep your hair short and clean
  • Avoid extreme hairstyles and colors
  • Use approved hair products
  • Establish a regular schedule for haircuts
  • Follow the regulations at all times

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about the army's hair regulations for men:

Q: Do I have to cut my hair before joining the army?

A: It is recommended that you have your hair cut to meet the regulations before joining the army.

Q: Can I have facial hair in the army?

A: Yes, but it must be kept neatly groomed and trimmed.

Q: What happens if my hair doesn't meet the regulations?

A: You may be subject to disciplinary action if your hair does not meet the regulations.

Hair Care

Proper hair care is essential to maintaining healthy hair while serving in the army. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly
  • Use a good quality shampoo and conditioner
  • Avoid using hot tools on your hair
  • Avoid using hair products that contain alcohol
  • Eat a healthy diet to promote hair growth and health

In conclusion, men are required to cut their hair to meet the army's regulations. It is important to understand and follow these regulations to ensure uniformity, hygiene, and safety. By following the steps and tips outlined in this article, you can maintain your hair to meet the regulations and keep it healthy while serving in the army.
