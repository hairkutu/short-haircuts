Best Wigs For 50 Year Old Woman Tips How To And Hair Care

Written by Bardi Apr 21, 2023 · 7 min read

Are you 50 years old and looking for the best wigs to enhance your look and boost your confidence? Look no further because we have the best wigs for you! In this article, we’ll provide you with tips on how to choose the perfect wig, a step-by-step guide on how to wear it, and hair care tips to maintain its quality.

Why Choose Wigs?

Wigs are a great way to change your look without committing to a permanent change. They are also perfect for women who have thinning hair or hair loss due to age, hormonal changes, or medical conditions. Wigs come in various styles, colors, and lengths that can give you an instant makeover with minimal effort.

How to Choose the Perfect Wig

Choosing the perfect wig can be overwhelming, but these tips can help you narrow down your choices:

1. Determine your face shape

Identify your face shape (round, oval, square, heart-shaped, or diamond-shaped) to find the wig style that will best suit you.

2. Consider the wig cap

Choose a wig cap that fits your head comfortably and securely. Wig caps come in different materials such as lace, monofilament, and machine-made.

3. Select the hair fiber

Choose the hair fiber that suits your needs and budget. Synthetic wigs are affordable and easy to maintain, while human hair wigs look more natural but require more care and maintenance.

4. Pick the wig style

Choose a wig style that complements your face shape and personal style. You can choose from short, medium, or long wigs with straight, wavy, or curly hair.

Step-by-Step Guide on How to Wear a Wig

Follow these simple steps to wear your wig properly:

1. Prepare your hair and scalp

Put your hair in a wig cap and secure it with bobby pins. Cleanse and dry your scalp to avoid irritation and infection.

2. Adjust the wig cap

Place the wig cap over your head and adjust it to fit your head snugly.

3. Put on the wig

Hold the wig by the ear tabs and place it on your head. Adjust the wig until it fits comfortably and securely.

4. Style the wig

Style the wig as desired using a comb, brush, or your fingers. You can use heat-styling tools on human hair wigs but not on synthetic wigs.

Tips to Maintain Your Wig’s Quality

To keep your wig looking its best, follow these hair care tips:

1. Store the wig properly

Store your wig on a wig stand or mannequin head to maintain its shape and prevent tangles.

2. Wash the wig regularly

Wash your wig every 6-8 wears using a wig shampoo and conditioner. Rinse it thoroughly and let it air dry.

3. Avoid heat and direct sunlight

Avoid exposing your wig to heat and direct sunlight as it can damage the hair fibers.

4. Use specialized products

Use specialized products for wigs such as wig spray, wig oil, and wig detangler to maintain its quality.

FAQs

1. How long can I wear my wig?

You can wear your wig for up to 12 hours a day, but it’s best to remove it before sleeping to avoid tangling and matting.

2. How do I know if the wig is the right size?

The wig should fit comfortably and securely without slipping or sliding off your head. You can measure your head circumference to ensure a proper fit.

3. Can I swim with my wig on?

It’s not recommended to swim with your wig on as chlorine and saltwater can damage the hair fibers.

Conclusion

Choosing the best wigs for 50 year old woman can be a daunting task, but with the right tips, you can find the perfect one that suits your needs and preferences. Remember to choose a wig that complements your face shape, fits your head comfortably, and suits your lifestyle. Follow our step-by-step guide on how to wear a wig and hair care tips to maintain its quality. With the right wig, you can boost your confidence, enhance your look, and feel fabulous at any age!
