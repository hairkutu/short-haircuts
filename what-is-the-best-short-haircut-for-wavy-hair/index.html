What Is The Best Short Haircut For Wavy Hair

Written by Bardi May 20, 2023 · 6 min read

Wavy hair can be a blessing and a curse. While it’s beautiful and unique, it can also be difficult to manage, especially when it comes to choosing the right haircut. But fear not, because we’ve got you covered. In this article, we’ll be discussing the best short haircut for wavy hair, along with tips and tricks to help you care for your hair and keep it looking its best.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to
  • Step by Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

Short haircuts are a great option for wavy hair. They’re easy to manage and style, and they can help bring out the natural texture and wave of your hair. The best short haircut for wavy hair is one that works with your hair’s natural texture and wave pattern, rather than against it. This means avoiding haircuts that are too short or too layered, as these can make your hair look frizzy and unmanageable.

How to

The first step in choosing the best short haircut for wavy hair is to determine your hair’s natural wave pattern. This can be done by washing and air-drying your hair, and then examining the way it falls and moves. Once you’ve determined your hair’s wave pattern, you can start looking for haircuts that will work with it.

Step by Step

Step 1: Determine your hair’s natural wave pattern

Step 2: Research short haircuts that work with your wave pattern

Step 3: Consult with a hairstylist to determine the best haircut for your face shape and hair type

Step 4: Maintain your haircut with regular trims and styling

Tips

When choosing a short haircut for wavy hair, it’s important to consider your face shape and hair type. For example, if you have a round face, you may want to avoid haircuts that are too short or too voluminous. Similarly, if you have fine hair, you may want to opt for a shorter haircut with fewer layers to avoid weighing down your hair.

FAQ

Q: Can I still straighten my hair if I get a short haircut for wavy hair?

A: Yes, you can still straighten your hair if you get a short haircut for wavy hair. However, it’s important to use heat protectant and avoid using high heat to prevent damage to your hair.

Q: How often should I get my short haircut trimmed?

A: It’s recommended to get your short haircut trimmed every 4-6 weeks to maintain its shape and prevent split ends.

Hair Care

To keep your wavy hair looking its best, it’s important to use the right hair care products and techniques. This includes using a shampoo and conditioner designed for wavy hair, using a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair, and using a diffuser when blow-drying your hair to help enhance your hair’s natural wave pattern.

Overall, the best short haircut for wavy hair is one that works with your hair’s natural texture and wave pattern. By taking the time to determine your hair’s wave pattern and consulting with a hairstylist, you can find the perfect short haircut to suit your style and personality.
