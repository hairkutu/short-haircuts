Why Does My Hair Get Frizzy When I Flat Iron It

Written by Eveline Sep 04, 2023 Â· 6 min read

If you have ever tried to flat iron your hair and ended up with a frizzy mess, you are not alone. Frizzy hair after flat ironing is a common problem that many women face. In this article, we will explore the reasons why your hair gets frizzy when you flat iron it and provide tips on how to prevent it.

Description

Frizz occurs when the hair cuticle is not lying flat, causing it to become rough and uneven. When you flat iron your hair, you are applying heat to the hair shaft, which can cause the cuticle to open up, leading to frizzy hair. Other factors that can contribute to frizzy hair after flat ironing include humidity, using the wrong products, and using the wrong flat iron temperature.

How to Flat Iron Your Hair

Flat ironing your hair involves using a heated flat iron to straighten out your hair. Before you flat iron your hair, you should wash and condition it with a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner. This will help to keep your hair hydrated and reduce the chances of frizz. You should also use a heat protectant spray to protect your hair from the heat of the flat iron.

Step-by-Step Guide to Flat Ironing Your Hair

1. Start by sectioning your hair into small sections using clips.
2. Take one section of hair and comb it out, making sure there are no tangles.
3. Apply a heat protectant spray to the section of hair.
4. Clamp the flat iron onto the section of hair, starting at the roots and slowly gliding it down to the ends.
5. Repeat this process until all the sections of your hair have been flat ironed.
6. Once you have finished flat ironing your hair, apply a serum or oil to keep it smooth and shiny.

Tips for Preventing Frizzy Hair After Flat Ironing

1. Use a heat protectant spray before flat ironing your hair.
2. Use the lowest heat setting possible that still straightens your hair.
3. Don't flat iron your hair when it is wet or damp.
4. Use a serum or oil to keep your hair smooth and shiny.
5. Don't flat iron your hair too frequently.
6. Use a high-quality flat iron with ceramic plates.
7. Don't use too much product on your hair, as this can weigh it down and cause frizz.
8. Avoid using a flat iron on damaged or over-processed hair.

FAQs

Q: Can I flat iron my hair every day?
A: No, it is not recommended to flat iron your hair every day as this can cause damage and lead to frizz and breakage.

Q: Can I use a flat iron on wet hair?
A: No, you should never use a flat iron on wet hair as this can cause damage and lead to frizz.

Hair Care Tips for Healthy Hair

1. Use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner.
2. Use a hair mask once a week to keep your hair hydrated.
3. Avoid using hot tools on your hair too frequently.
4. Avoid using harsh chemicals on your hair.
5. Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.
6. Eat a healthy diet and drink plenty of water to keep your hair healthy from the inside out.

By following these tips, you can prevent frizzy hair after flat ironing and maintain healthy, beautiful hair.
