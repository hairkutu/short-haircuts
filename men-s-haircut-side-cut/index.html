Men s Haircut Side Cut A Trendy Hairstyle For Men In 2023

Written by Petter Jul 14, 2023 Â· 8 min read

If you're looking for a trendy and stylish haircut that will turn heads, then a men's haircut side cut might be just what you need. This hairstyle has become increasingly popular among men in recent years and is a great way to add some edge to your look. In this article, we'll go over everything you need to know about the men's haircut side cut, including how to get it, tips for styling, and how to take care of your hair.

What is a Men's Haircut Side Cut?

A men's haircut side cut is a hairstyle where the hair on the sides of the head is shaved or cut very short, while the hair on top is left longer. This creates a striking contrast between the two lengths and can be styled in many different ways. The side cut can be done with clippers or scissors, depending on the desired look. This hairstyle is also known as an undercut or disconnected undercut.

How to Get a Men's Haircut Side Cut

Getting a men's haircut side cut is relatively easy, but it's important to find a skilled barber or stylist who can give you the look you want. Here's how to get a men's haircut side cut:

1. Choose your length

Decide how short you want the sides of your hair to be. This will depend on your personal style and the shape of your face. Some men prefer a very short cut, while others prefer a more gradual fade.

2. Find a skilled barber

Look for a barber or stylist who has experience with men's haircuts and can give you the look you want. It's important to communicate your preferences clearly and ask for their advice on what will look best on you.

3. Get the cut

Once you've found a skilled barber, it's time to get the cut. They will use clippers or scissors to cut the sides of your hair to the desired length, and leave the top longer. You can also ask for a specific style, such as a slicked-back look, a messy fringe, or a pompadour.

Tips for Styling a Men's Haircut Side Cut

Styling a men's haircut side cut is easy and versatile. Here are some tips for getting the most out of your new hairstyle:

1. Use the right products

Use a good quality hair product, such as wax or pomade, to style your hair. This will help keep it in place and give it a polished look.

2. Experiment with different styles

The great thing about a men's haircut side cut is that it can be styled in many different ways. Try a slicked-back look for a formal occasion, or a messy fringe for a more casual look.

3. Get regular trims

To keep your men's haircut side cut looking sharp, it's important to get regular trims. This will help maintain the length and shape of your hair, and prevent split ends.

FAQ

Q: How often should I get a men's haircut side cut?

A: It's recommended to get a men's haircut side cut every 4-6 weeks to maintain the length and shape of your hair.

Q: Can I style a men's haircut side cut with curly hair?

A: Yes, a men's haircut side cut can be styled with curly hair. However, it's important to use the right products and techniques to avoid frizz and maintain the shape of your curls.

Hair Care Tips for Men's Haircut Side Cut

Taking care of your hair is important to keep it healthy and looking its best. Here are some hair care tips for men's haircut side cut:

1. Use a shampoo and conditioner

Use a good quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair clean and nourished. Look for products that are designed for your hair type, such as oily or dry hair.

2. Avoid heat styling

Heat styling can damage your hair and cause split ends. If you must use heat styling tools, use a heat protectant spray and keep the temperature low.

3. Protect your hair from the sun

The sun can damage your hair and cause it to become dry and brittle. Wear a hat or use a hair product with SPF to protect your hair from the sun's harmful rays.

Conclusion

A men's haircut side cut is a trendy and stylish hairstyle that can be customized to suit your personal style. Whether you prefer a short or long cut, a slicked-back look or a messy fringe, a men's haircut side cut is a versatile choice that's sure to turn heads. By following these tips for getting the cut, styling, and hair care, you'll be able to keep your men's haircut side cut looking its best.
