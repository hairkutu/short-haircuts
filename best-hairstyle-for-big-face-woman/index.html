Best Hairstyle for Big Face Woman - Tips, FAQs and Hair Care

Are you struggling to find the perfect hairstyle that flatters your big face? Look no further! In this article, we will discuss the best hairstyles for big face women, along with tips, FAQs, and hair care advice.

Table of Contents

  • How to Determine Your Face Shape
  • Best Hairstyles for Big Face Women
  • Tips for Big Face Women

How to Determine Your Face Shape

Before we dive into the best hairstyles for big face women, it's important to determine your face shape. Here's how:

  1. Start by pulling your hair back and away from your face.
  2. Take a good look at your face in the mirror.
  3. Compare the width of your forehead, cheekbones, and jawline.
  4. Is your face longest at the forehead and gradually narrows down to the chin? You have an oval face shape.
  5. Is your face widest at the cheekbones and gradually narrows down to the chin and forehead? You have a heart-shaped face.
  6. Is your face widest at the jawline and gradually narrows up towards the forehead? You have a square face.
  7. Is your face widest at the forehead and jawline, with a narrower middle section? You have a round face.

Now that you know your face shape, let's dive into the best hairstyles for big face women.

Best Hairstyles for Big Face Women

1. Long Layers

Long layers are perfect for big face women as they help elongate the face and draw attention away from the width. To achieve this style, ask your stylist for long layers starting at the chin or collarbone.

2. Side-Swept Bangs

Side-swept bangs are another great option for big face women as they create an angle across the face, making it appear narrower. Make sure to keep the bangs long and swept to the side.

3. Bob with Volume

A bob with volume is a great way to add height to the crown of the head, creating the illusion of a longer, narrower face. Ask your stylist for a chin-length bob with layers and volume at the crown.

4. Pixie Cut

If you're feeling bold, a pixie cut can be a great option for big face women. This style adds height to the crown of the head, making the face appear longer.

Tips for Big Face Women

1. Avoid Heavy Bangs

Heavy bangs can overpower a big face, making it appear even wider. Opt for side-swept bangs instead.

2. Embrace Layers

Layers are your friend! They help add dimension and movement to the hair, drawing attention away from the width of the face.

3. Experiment with Updos

Updos can be a great way to elongate the face. Try a high bun or top knot to add height to the crown of the head.

FAQs

1. Can big face women wear short hair?

Absolutely! Short hair can be a great option for big face women, just make sure to add volume to the crown of the head to create the illusion of a longer face.

2. What hair colors are best for big face women?

Any hair color can work for big face women, but highlights and lowlights can help add dimension to the hair, creating the illusion of a more narrow face.

Hair Care for Big Face Women

1. Use a Volumizing Shampoo

A volumizing shampoo can help add height to the crown of the head, making the face appear longer.

2. Invest in a Good Blow Dryer

A good blow dryer can help add volume and movement to the hair, creating the illusion of a more narrow face.

3. Regular Trims

Regular trims are important to maintain the shape of the hairstyle and prevent split ends.

Now that you know the best hairstyles for big face women, along with tips and hair care advice, it's time to book your appointment with your stylist and rock your new look with confidence!
