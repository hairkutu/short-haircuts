Long Hairstyles With Short Layers On Top

Written by Aprilia Aug 25, 2023 Â· 5 min read

Are you looking for a trendy and stylish hairstyle that will give you a new look? Long hairstyles with short layers on top might be the perfect choice for you. This hairstyle is perfect for people with long hair who want to add some dimension and style to their look.

Description

Long hairstyles with short layers on top are versatile and can be styled in many different ways. The top layers are cut shorter than the bottom layers, creating a layered effect that adds volume and movement to your hair. This style is perfect for people with straight or wavy hair, as it helps to add texture and dimension to your locks.

How to

If you want to achieve long hairstyles with short layers on top, you should visit a professional hairstylist. They can help you choose the perfect layering technique that suits your face shape and hair type. Here are some tips to help you achieve this hairstyle:

Step by Step

  • Step 1: Wash your hair and towel-dry it.
  • Step 2: Comb your hair and detangle any knots.
  • Step 3: Part your hair in the center or on the side.
  • Step 4: Visit a professional hairstylist and ask them to cut your hair in short layers on top.
  • Step 5: Style your hair as desired.

Tips

  • Use a heat protectant spray before using any hot tools on your hair.
  • Apply a hair serum to add shine and smoothness to your locks.
  • Avoid using too much hairspray as it can make your hair look stiff and unnatural.

FAQ

1. Will long hairstyles with short layers on top suit my face shape?

Yes, this hairstyle suits most face shapes. You can consult with your hairstylist to choose the best layering technique that complements your face shape.

2. Can I achieve this hairstyle at home?

We recommend visiting a professional hairstylist to achieve this hairstyle. They can help you get the perfect layering technique that suits your hair type and face shape.

3. How often should I trim my hair to maintain this hairstyle?

You should trim your hair every six to eight weeks to maintain this hairstyle and prevent split ends.

Hair Care

Long hairstyles with short layers on top require regular maintenance and care to keep your locks healthy and shiny. Here are some tips:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to prevent dryness and damage to your hair.
  • Avoid using hot tools on your hair every day as it can lead to breakage and damage.
  • Eat a healthy diet rich in vitamins and minerals to promote hair growth and strength.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to nourish your hair and prevent dryness.

Long hairstyles with short layers on top are a trendy and stylish hairstyle that can transform your look. With the right layering technique and care, you can achieve a beautiful and healthy mane that will turn heads wherever you go.
