How To Do 60S Hair Bun A Step By Step Guide

Written by Frank May 11, 2023 · 7 min read

Are you looking for a retro hairstyle that can make you stand out from the crowd? Look no further than the 60s hair bun! This iconic hairstyle was popularized by famous celebrities like Audrey Hepburn and Brigitte Bardot, and it continues to be a popular choice for those who want to add a vintage touch to their look.

In this article, we’ll guide you through the process of creating a 60s hair bun, from start to finish. We’ll also provide you with some tips and tricks to make the process easier and answer some frequently asked questions about this hairstyle.

How to Create a 60s Hair Bun: Step-by-Step Guide

Here’s how you can create a 60s hair bun in just a few simple steps:

Step 1: Prep Your Hair

Start by washing your hair and blow-drying it until it’s completely dry. Apply some hair mousse to your hair to give it some texture and hold. You can also use a curling iron to create loose waves if you want to add some extra volume to your hair.

Step 2: Section Your Hair

Next, part your hair in the middle and divide it into two equal sections. Brush each section to make sure it’s smooth and free of tangles.

Step 3: Create a Ponytail

Tie each section into a low ponytail at the nape of your neck. Make sure the ponytails are even in length and position.

Step 4: Twist and Pin

Take one of the ponytails and twist it until it forms a tight rope-like shape. Wrap the twisted ponytail around the base of the ponytail to create a bun. Use bobby pins to secure the bun in place. Repeat the process with the other ponytail.

Step 5: Finishing Touches

Once you’ve created both buns, use some hairspray to hold them in place. You can also use a hair accessory like a ribbon or a headband to add some extra flair to your look.

Tips for Creating a 60s Hair Bun

Here are some tips and tricks to help you create the perfect 60s hair bun:

  • Use bobby pins that match the color of your hair to hide them better.
  • Apply hairspray to your hair before you start to give it some extra hold.
  • Use a hair donut to create a bigger bun.
  • Tease the crown of your hair to add some extra volume to your look.
  • Don’t worry if your bun is not perfectly symmetrical – the slightly messy look is part of the charm of this hairstyle.

FAQ: 60s Hair Bun

Here are some frequently asked questions about the 60s hair bun:

What hair type works best for a 60s hair bun?

A 60s hair bun works best for medium to long hair that’s not too thick or too thin. If you have very fine hair, you can use hair extensions to add some extra volume to your bun.

How long does it take to create a 60s hair bun?

Creating a 60s hair bun should take anywhere from 10 to 20 minutes, depending on your hair length and thickness.

Can I wear a 60s hair bun for formal occasions?

Yes, a 60s hair bun can be dressed up or down depending on the occasion. You can add some extra glamor to your look by using a sparkly hair accessory or a fancy headband.

Hair Care Tips for 60s Hair Bun

Here are some hair care tips to keep in mind when creating a 60s hair bun:

  • Don’t use too much heat on your hair when blow-drying or curling it, as this can damage your hair over time.
  • Avoid using hair products that contain harsh chemicals, as these can also damage your hair.
  • Wash your hair regularly to keep it clean and healthy.
  • Use a heat protectant spray before using any heat styling tools on your hair.

With these tips and tricks, you’ll be able to create the perfect 60s hair bun in no time. Whether you’re going for a retro look or just want to switch up your hairstyle, the 60s hair bun is a timeless choice that’s sure to turn heads.
