How To Reduce Hair Volume Haircut A Step By Step Guide

Written by William Apr 08, 2023 Â· 8 min read

If you have thick hair, you are probably familiar with the struggle of trying to tame your hair. It can be frustrating to deal with hair that seems to have a life of its own, and it can be especially challenging when it comes to haircuts. But don't worry, reducing hair volume is easier than you think. In this article, we will discuss how to reduce hair volume haircut with step-by-step instructions, tips, and FAQs.

Table of Contents

  • How to Reduce Hair Volume Haircut
  • Step-by-Step Guide

How to Reduce Hair Volume Haircut

Reducing hair volume can be achieved through a haircut that removes some of the bulk from your hair. The key is to find a haircut that is suitable for your face shape and hair type. Here are some haircuts that can help you reduce hair volume:

1. Layers

Layers are a great way to add movement to your hair and reduce bulk. Ask your stylist for long layers that start at the chin or lower. This will help to thin out your hair and create a more manageable style.

2. Bob

A bob is a classic haircut that can work wonders for reducing hair volume. The length of the bob will depend on your face shape and hair type, but a chin-length or shoulder-length bob can be a great option.

3. Pixie cut

A pixie cut is a short, cropped haircut that can help to reduce hair volume. This is a great option if you are looking for a low-maintenance style that is easy to style and maintain.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here are some steps to follow when getting a haircut to reduce hair volume:

Step 1: Consult with your stylist

Before getting a haircut, it is important to talk to your stylist about your hair concerns. They can recommend a haircut that will work best for your hair type and face shape.

Step 2: Wash your hair

Make sure to wash your hair before your appointment. This will ensure that your stylist can see your natural hair texture and work with it accordingly.

Step 3: Bring a photo

If you have a specific haircut in mind, bring a photo to show your stylist. This will help them to understand exactly what you are looking for.

Step 4: Sit back and relax

Once you have discussed your haircut with your stylist, sit back and relax while they work their magic.

Tips for Reducing Hair Volume

Here are some tips to help you reduce hair volume:

1. Use the right products

Using the right products can make a big difference in reducing hair volume. Look for products that are designed for voluminous hair, such as volumizing shampoo and conditioner.

2. Blow-dry your hair

Blow-drying your hair can help to reduce volume and create a more sleek look. Use a round brush to help smooth out your hair and create volume at the roots.

3. Avoid heavy styling products

Heavy styling products, such as gel and mousse, can add to the volume of your hair. Instead, opt for lightweight products that will help to tame your hair without adding bulk.

FAQs

1. Can I reduce hair volume without getting a haircut?

Yes, you can reduce hair volume without getting a haircut. Using the right products and styling techniques can help to tame your hair and reduce bulk.

2. How often should I get a haircut to reduce hair volume?

The frequency of haircuts will depend on your hair type and how quickly your hair grows. It is generally recommended to get a haircut every six to eight weeks to maintain your style.

Hair Care for Thick Hair

Thick hair can be a blessing and a curse. While it can be challenging to style, thick hair is generally healthy and resilient. Here are some tips for caring for thick hair:

1. Use a wide-tooth comb

Using a wide-tooth comb can help to prevent breakage and damage to your hair.

2. Avoid over-washing

Washing your hair too often can strip it of its natural oils, causing it to become dry and brittle. Try to wash your hair every other day or every two to three days.

3. Deep-condition regularly

Deep-conditioning treatments can help to keep your hair healthy and moisturized. Consider using a deep-conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair in top condition.

Reducing hair volume can be achieved through a haircut that removes some of the bulk from your hair. By following our step-by-step guide, using the right products, and taking care of your hair, you can achieve a sleek, manageable style that looks great.
