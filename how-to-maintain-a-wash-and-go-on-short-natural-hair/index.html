How To Maintain A Wash And Go On Short Natural Hair

Written by Oliver Mar 20, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Are you looking for a quick and easy hairstyle for your short natural hair? A wash and go is a great option that can give you a stylish and effortless look. However, maintaining a wash and go can be a challenge, especially if you're new to natural hair care. In this article, we'll show you how to maintain a wash and go on short natural hair with some simple steps and tips.

Table of Content:

  • How to Maintain a Wash and Go on Short Natural Hair
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Maintaining a Wash and Go
  • Frequently Asked Questions
  • Hair Care Tips for Natural Hair

Step-by-Step Guide

Here's how to maintain a wash and go on short natural hair:

Step 1: Start with Clean Hair

Wash your hair with a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to remove any buildup and detangle your hair. You can also use a leave-in conditioner or curl cream to add moisture and definition to your curls.

Step 2: Apply a Styling Product

Apply a styling product, such as a gel or curl defining cream, to your hair while it's still damp. Use your fingers or a wide-tooth comb to distribute the product evenly throughout your hair.

Step 3: Define Your Curls

Use a Denman brush, a comb or your fingers to define your curls. Make sure to separate each curl and smooth out any frizz or flyaways. You can also use a diffuser to dry your hair, but make sure to use a low heat setting to prevent damage to your hair.

Step 4: Let Your Hair Dry

Let your hair air dry or use a diffuser to dry your hair. Avoid touching your hair while it's drying to prevent frizz.

Step 5: Refresh Your Curls

Refresh your curls on the second or third day by spritzing your hair with water or a leave-in conditioner and scrunching your hair. You can also use a curl refreshing spray to revive your curls.

Tips for Maintaining a Wash and Go

Here are some tips to help you maintain your wash and go:

  • Use a satin or silk pillowcase or bonnet to protect your hair while you sleep.
  • Don't use too much product, as it can weigh down your curls and cause buildup.
  • Avoid touching your hair too much, as it can cause frizz.
  • Use a clarifying shampoo once a month to remove any buildup.
  • Deep condition your hair once a week to keep it hydrated and healthy.

Frequently Asked Questions

Here are some frequently asked questions about maintaining a wash and go:

How often should I wash my hair?

You should wash your hair once or twice a week, depending on your hair type and how active you are. If you sweat a lot or have an oily scalp, you may need to wash your hair more frequently.

Can I use a diffuser on high heat?

No, you should avoid using a diffuser on high heat, as it can damage your hair. Use a low or medium heat setting and move the diffuser around your hair to dry it evenly.

What products should I use for my wash and go?

You can use a gel, curl defining cream, leave-in conditioner, or curl refreshing spray for your wash and go. Experiment with different products to find what works best for your hair.

Hair Care Tips for Natural Hair

Here are some general hair care tips for natural hair:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils.
  • Deep condition your hair regularly to keep it hydrated and healthy.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and harsh weather conditions by wearing a hat or scarf.
  • Eat a healthy diet rich in vitamins and minerals to promote hair growth and strength.

By following these tips and steps, you can maintain a beautiful wash and go on your short natural hair. Remember to be patient and experiment with different products and techniques to find what works best for your hair.
