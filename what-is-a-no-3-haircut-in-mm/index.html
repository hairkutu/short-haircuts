What Is A No 3 Haircut In Mm

Written by Robby Sep 04, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Are you looking for a new haircut but not sure what the numbers mean? A no 3 haircut in mm is a popular buzz cut style that measures 9mm in length. This haircut is achieved by using a clipper with a guard number 3 attached to it.

How to Get a No 3 Haircut in MM

If you want to get a no 3 haircut in mm, here are the steps you need to follow:

Step 1: Wash Your Hair

It's important to wash your hair before getting a haircut. This will remove any dirt or oil that may be on your scalp or hair, making it easier for the barber to cut your hair evenly.

Step 2: Discuss Your Haircut with Your Barber

Before your barber starts cutting your hair, it's important to discuss the style you want. Ask for a no 3 haircut in mm and explain any other details you want, such as how short you want your sides or how long you want your fringe.

Step 3: Use a Guard Number 3

To achieve a no 3 haircut in mm, your barber will use a clipper with a guard number 3 attached to it. This will ensure that your hair is cut evenly to the desired length.

Step 4: Trim the Sides and Back

After using the guard number 3, your barber will trim the sides and back of your head. This will create a neat and tidy look that's perfect for any occasion.

Step 5: Style Your Hair

Once your haircut is complete, you can style your hair however you like. A no 3 haircut in mm is versatile and can be styled in many ways, from a messy bedhead look to a slicked-back style.

Tips for Getting a No 3 Haircut in MM

Here are some tips to keep in mind when getting a no 3 haircut in mm:

  • Choose a reputable barber who has experience with buzz cuts.
  • Bring a photo of the style you want to ensure that your barber understands your desired look.
  • Consider using a pre-shave oil or lotion to soften your hair before getting it cut.
  • Use a good quality hair clipper with a guard number 3 to achieve the perfect length.
  • Experiment with different styling products to find the one that works best for your hair type and desired look.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the difference between a no 2 and no 3 haircut?

A no 2 haircut measures 6mm in length, while a no 3 haircut measures 9mm in length. The difference between the two is quite subtle, but a no 3 haircut will give you slightly longer hair than a no 2 haircut.

Can I get a no 3 haircut with scissors instead of clippers?

Technically, yes, you can get a no 3 haircut with scissors. However, it's much easier and faster to achieve the desired length using clippers with a guard number 3 attached to them.

How often should I get a no 3 haircut?

This depends on how quickly your hair grows and how often you want to maintain your look. On average, most people get a haircut every 4-6 weeks to keep their hair looking neat and tidy.

Hair Care for a No 3 Haircut in MM

To keep your no 3 haircut looking fresh and stylish, it's important to take good care of your hair. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a good quality shampoo and conditioner.
  • Use a hair dryer on a low heat setting to dry your hair and style it as desired.
  • Avoid using too much styling products, as this can weigh down your hair and make it look greasy.
  • Get regular trims to maintain your haircut and prevent split ends.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and harsh weather by wearing a hat or using a hair serum with SPF protection.

Now that you know what a no 3 haircut in mm is, you can confidently ask your barber for this popular style. With the right care and styling, your no 3 haircut will look great and keep you looking stylish and confident.
