Is Shoulder Length Hair In For 2021

Written by Bardi Jul 18, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Shoulder length hair has been a popular hairstyle for many years, but is it still in for 2021? The answer is yes! Shoulder length hair is still a trendy and versatile hairstyle that can be worn in many different ways. In this article, we will discuss why shoulder length hair is in for 2021, how to style it, hair care tips, and more. Let's dive in!

Description

Shoulder length hair is a hairstyle that falls between the chin and shoulders. It's a versatile length that can be styled in many different ways, such as straight, wavy, curly, or braided. Shoulder length hair can also be layered to add volume and movement to the hair.

How to Style Shoulder Length Hair

There are many different ways to style shoulder length hair. Here are a few ideas:

1. Straight and Sleek

To achieve a straight and sleek look, use a flat iron to straighten your hair. Apply a heat protectant spray before using the flat iron to avoid damaging your hair. Finish off with a shine serum to add a glossy finish.

2. Beach Waves

To achieve beach waves, use a curling iron or wand to curl your hair. Once you've curled your entire head, use a texturizing spray to add volume and texture to your waves.

3. Half-Up Half-Down

For a half-up half-down look, start by curling your hair. Then, take the top half of your hair and secure it with a hair tie or clip. You can leave your curls loose or twist them into a bun for a more polished look.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide on how to style shoulder length hair:

1. Wash and condition your hair.

Start by washing and conditioning your hair with products that are suited for your hair type. Towel dry your hair and apply a heat protectant spray.

2. Blow dry your hair.

Blow dry your hair using a round brush to add volume and movement to your hair.

3. Style your hair.

Choose a styling method that works best for you, such as straightening, curling, or braiding. Finish off with a hair spray to hold your style in place.

Tips for Maintaining Shoulder Length Hair

Here are a few tips for maintaining shoulder length hair:

1. Regular trims

Regular trims are essential to maintaining the health and shape of your hair. Schedule a trim every 6-8 weeks to keep your hair looking its best.

2. Use a heat protectant spray

Using a heat protectant spray before using hot tools can help prevent damage to your hair.

3. Deep condition regularly

Deep conditioning treatments can help keep your hair moisturized and healthy. Aim to deep condition your hair once a week.

FAQs

Q: Is shoulder length hair suitable for all face shapes?

A: Yes, shoulder length hair is suitable for all face shapes. It's a versatile length that can be customized to flatter your face shape.

Q: Can I still wear my hair in a ponytail with shoulder length hair?

A: Yes, you can still wear your hair in a ponytail with shoulder length hair. You may need to use hair ties or clips to keep your hair in place.

Q: How can I add volume to my shoulder length hair?

A: You can add volume to your shoulder length hair by using a volumizing mousse or spray, or by blow drying your hair with a round brush.

Hair Care

When it comes to hair care, it's important to use products that are suited for your hair type. Regular trims, deep conditioning treatments, and using a heat protectant spray can also help keep your hair healthy and looking its best.

In conclusion, shoulder length hair is still in for 2021 and is a versatile length that can be styled in many different ways. Whether you prefer a straight and sleek look or beachy waves, shoulder length hair is a trendy and chic option. Use the tips and tricks in this article to help you style and care for your shoulder length hair.
