Do Long Faces Look Better With Long Or Short Hair

Written by Bardi Jun 08, 2023 Â· 8 min read

If you have a long face, you may have wondered about the best hairstyle to flatter your features. Should you go for long locks or a shorter cut? In this article, we'll explore the pros and cons of each option and give you some tips on how to make the most of your hair.

  2. How to Determine Your Face Shape
  3. Step by Step Guide

Description

Long faces are characterized by a high forehead, long chin, and narrow width. The right hairstyle can help balance out these features and create a more proportional look. Some people believe that long hair is the best option for long faces, while others prefer shorter styles. There's no right or wrong answer - it all depends on your personal preferences and the shape of your face.

How to Determine Your Face Shape

Before you decide on a hairstyle, it's important to determine your face shape. You can do this by standing in front of a mirror and tracing the outline of your face with a lipstick or a washable marker. Then, compare your shape to the following descriptions:

  • Oval: forehead is wider than the jawline, and the length of the face is longer than the width
  • Round: face is almost as wide as it is long, with soft curves and no angles
  • Square: face is as wide as it is long, with a strong jawline and angular features
  • Heart: forehead is wider than the jawline, with a pointed chin
  • Diamond: face is widest at the cheekbones, with a narrow forehead and jawline
  • Long: face is longer than it is wide, with a high forehead and long chin

Step by Step Guide

Once you've determined that you have a long face, you can start thinking about the best hairstyles to flatter your features. Here's a step-by-step guide:

  1. Consider your hair texture and thickness. If you have thick, straight hair, a longer style can create a sleek, polished look. If your hair is fine or curly, a shorter cut can add volume and bounce.
  2. Think about your lifestyle. If you're always on the go, a shorter cut can be more manageable and easier to style. If you have more time to devote to your hair, longer styles can be more versatile and offer more styling options.
  3. Take your face shape into account. Long faces can benefit from hairstyles that add width to the sides of the face, such as layers, waves, and curls. Avoid styles that are too long and straight, as they can elongate the face even further.
  4. Consult with a hairstylist. A professional can help you choose the best cut and style for your face shape and hair type.

Tips for Choosing a Hairstyle

Here are some additional tips to keep in mind when choosing a hairstyle for a long face:

  • A side part can help balance out a long forehead.
  • Layers can add volume and movement to the hair, creating a more dynamic look.
  • Soft, loose waves can add width to the sides of the face.
  • A bob or lob can create a more angular look, balancing out the length of the face.

FAQs

Is long hair or short hair better for a long face?

There's no definitive answer to this question - it all depends on your personal preferences and the shape of your face. Some people believe that long hair can elongate the face even further, while others think that short hair can create a more proportional look.

What hairstyles should I avoid if I have a long face?

Avoid hairstyles that are too long and straight, as they can elongate the face even further. Also, stay away from center parts, as they can make the face appear longer and narrower.

Can I still wear bangs if I have a long face?

Absolutely! Bangs can be a great way to balance out a long face. Consider going for side-swept bangs or a curtain fringe, which can add width to the forehead and create a more proportional look.

Hair Care Tips

No matter what hairstyle you choose, it's important to take good care of your hair. Here are some tips to keep your locks healthy and shiny:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using hot tools (like hair straighteners and curling irons) too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair looking healthy and prevent split ends.
  • Eat a healthy diet, rich in vitamins and nutrients that support hair growth and health.

Ultimately, the best hairstyle for a long face is the one that makes you feel confident and beautiful. Experiment with different cuts and styles until you find the perfect look for you!
