How To Style A Pixie Bob A Step By Step Guide

Written by Robby Apr 08, 2023 · 6 min read

Pixie bobs are a trendy and chic hairstyle that can be worn by anyone. It’s a short haircut that can be styled in many different ways, making it a versatile and popular choice. However, styling a pixie bob can be a bit intimidating, especially if you’re new to this haircut. In this guide, we’ll show you how to style a pixie bob step-by-step, so you can rock this look with confidence!

Description

A pixie bob is a short haircut that’s longer than a traditional pixie, but shorter than a bob. It’s cut in layers to create texture and movement, and can be styled in a variety of ways. It’s a great option for those who want a low-maintenance haircut that’s still stylish and trendy.

How to Style a Pixie Bob

Here’s a step-by-step guide on how to style a pixie bob:

Step 1: Start with clean, dry hair

Before you start styling your pixie bob, make sure your hair is clean and dry. You can blow dry your hair or let it air dry, depending on your preference.

Step 2: Apply styling product

Apply a small amount of styling product to your hair, such as a mousse or pomade. This will help add texture and hold to your hair.

Step 3: Use a round brush to add volume

Using a round brush, lift your hair at the roots to add volume. Brush your hair in an upward motion, away from your scalp. This will create a lifted, voluminous look.

Step 4: Use a flat iron to add texture

Using a flat iron, add texture to your hair by twisting and turning the iron as you run it through your hair. This will create a tousled, beachy look.

Step 5: Finish with hairspray

Finish your look with a light mist of hairspray to hold your style in place.

Tips

Here are some additional tips to help you style your pixie bob:

  • Experiment with different styling products to find the one that works best for your hair type.
  • Use a diffuser attachment on your blow dryer to add volume and texture to your hair.
  • Try using a curling iron to create soft, loose waves in your hair.
  • Consider getting regular trims to maintain the shape of your pixie bob.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about styling a pixie bob:

1. How often should I wash my hair?

It’s recommended to wash your hair every 2-3 days to keep it healthy and hydrated.

2. Can I style my pixie bob without heat?

Yes, you can style your pixie bob without heat by using a texturizing spray or dry shampoo.

3. How can I add volume to my pixie bob?

You can add volume to your pixie bob by using a round brush to lift your hair at the roots, or by using a volumizing spray or mousse.

Hair Care

It’s important to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and looking its best. Here are some tips for maintaining a healthy pixie bob:

  • Use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair hydrated.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Get regular trims to maintain the shape of your pixie bob.
  • Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a leave-in conditioner with SPF.

With these tips and tricks, you’ll be able to style your pixie bob with confidence and ease. Experiment with different products and techniques to find the style that works best for you, and don’t be afraid to have fun with your hair!
