How To Make A Bun With Short Hair Man A Step By Step Guide

Written by William May 20, 2023 Â· 6 min read

If you have short hair and you want to try out the trendy man bun, you might think it's impossible. But with a few simple steps, you can create a perfect bun that will make you look stylish and trendy. In this article, we'll show you how to make a bun with short hair man, including tips and tricks to make the process easier.

Description

The man bun has become a popular hairstyle among men in recent years. It's a versatile style that can be worn in a variety of ways, from messy and relaxed to sleek and polished. However, many men with short hair struggle to achieve the look. The good news is that it's possible to create a man bun with short hair, and it's easier than you might think.

How to Make a Bun with Short Hair Man

Follow these steps to create a perfect man bun with short hair:

Step 1: Gather Your Supplies

You'll need a few things to create a man bun with short hair, including:

  • Elastic hair tie
  • Bobby pins
  • Hairbrush or comb

Step 2: Brush Your Hair

Brush your hair to remove any tangles or knots. Make sure your hair is clean and dry as well.

Step 3: Pull Your Hair Back

Gather your hair at the back of your head, using your fingers or a comb to smooth it out. Position the hair tie over the hair, and pull it tight to secure the hair in place.

Step 4: Twist and Pin

Twist the hair around the hair tie, creating a bun shape. Use bobby pins to secure the hair in place, tucking in any loose ends.

Step 5: Adjust and Style

Adjust the bun as needed, using bobby pins to keep it in place. You can also add hairspray or gel to keep the style in place and add shine.

Tips

Here are a few tips to make the process of making a bun with short hair man easier:

  • Use a hair tie that matches your hair color for a more natural look.
  • Try using a sock bun or donut bun to create a fuller, more voluminous bun.
  • Experiment with different styles, such as a messy bun or a half-up, half-down bun.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about making a bun with short hair man:

Can I make a man bun with very short hair?

Yes, you can make a man bun with very short hair. Use a small hair tie and bobby pins to secure the hair in place.

How long does my hair need to be to make a man bun?

Your hair should be at least 3-4 inches long to make a man bun.

Is it okay to use gel or hairspray on my hair when making a man bun?

Yes, you can use gel or hairspray on your hair when making a man bun. This will help keep the style in place and add shine.

Hair Care

Creating a man bun with short hair man can be a great way to change up your look and stay on-trend. However, it's important to take care of your hair to ensure it stays healthy and strong. Here are a few hair care tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo
  • Avoid using hot tools, such as a straightener or curling iron, too often
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair moisturized

With these tips and tricks, you'll be able to make a perfect man bun with short hair man in no time. Whether you're going for a casual or polished look, this hairstyle is sure to turn heads and make you stand out.
