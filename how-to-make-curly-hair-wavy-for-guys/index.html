How To Make Curly Hair Wavy For Guys

Written by Oliver Apr 14, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Curly hair can be a blessing and a curse. While it can look great when styled properly, it can also be difficult to manage. If you're looking for a way to change up your curly hair without losing your natural texture, creating waves might be the way to go. In this article, we'll go over how to make curly hair wavy for guys, including tips, tricks, and hair care advice.

Table of Contents

  • How to Make Curly Hair Wavy for Guys
  • Step-by-Step Instructions
  • Tips for Creating Wavy Hair
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care for Wavy Hair

How to Make Curly Hair Wavy for Guys

The first step in making curly hair wavy is to start with clean, damp hair. You can either wash your hair or wet it down in the shower or with a spray bottle. Once your hair is damp, you'll need to apply a styling product. A good option for creating waves is a sea salt spray. This type of product can help add texture and definition to your hair.

Next, you'll want to use a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer. This attachment can help to distribute heat more evenly and prevent frizz. Start by scrunching your hair with your hands, then use the diffuser to dry your hair on low heat. As you dry, continue to scrunch your hair to encourage the wave pattern.

Once your hair is dry, you can use a curling iron or wand to further define your waves. However, you'll want to use a larger barrel size than you would for creating curls. A 1-1.5 inch barrel can help create loose, natural-looking waves.

Step-by-Step Instructions

Here's a breakdown of the steps to make curly hair wavy:

  1. Start with clean, damp hair.
  2. Apply a sea salt spray or other styling product.
  3. Use a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer to dry your hair on low heat, scrunching as you go.
  4. Use a curling iron or wand with a larger barrel size to define your waves.

Tips for Creating Wavy Hair

Here are some additional tips to help you create wavy hair:

  • Don't over-dry your hair with the diffuser. Leaving a little moisture in your hair can help create a more natural-looking wave.
  • Experiment with different products to find what works best for your hair. Sea salt spray is a popular option, but you can also try mousse, gel, or wax.
  • Try using a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to gently detangle your hair after applying product.
  • Don't be afraid to play around with the direction of your waves. You can create a more tousled, messy look by scrunching your hair in different directions.

FAQ

Here are some common questions about making curly hair wavy:

Can I make my hair wavy without using heat?

Yes, you can try braiding your hair while it's wet and leaving it to air dry. Once the braids are dry, take them out and use your fingers to gently separate the waves.

How long will my waves last?

This can vary depending on your hair type and the products you use. However, you can help your waves last longer by sleeping on a silk pillowcase and avoiding touching your hair too much throughout the day.

Can I make my waves more defined?

Yes, you can try using a curl-enhancing product or a gel to help define your waves. You can also use a smaller curling iron or wand to create tighter waves.

Hair Care for Wavy Hair

Once you've created waves in your hair, it's important to take care of them to maintain their shape and texture. Here are some tips for hair care:

  • Avoid washing your hair too often, as this can strip natural oils and make your hair frizzy.
  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to help preserve your hair's natural oils.
  • Avoid using hot tools too often, as this can damage your hair and cause split ends.
  • Consider using a leave-in conditioner or hair oil to help moisturize your hair and prevent frizz.

By following these tips and tricks, you can easily make curly hair wavy for guys. Whether you're looking for a new hairstyle or just want to switch up your look, creating waves can be a great option.
