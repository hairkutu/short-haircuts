Best Hair Length For Round Faces Guide 2023

Description

Do you have a round face and are struggling to find the perfect hairstyle? Look no further because we have compiled a list of the best hair lengths for round faces. Whether you have short hair or long hair, we have options for you. In this guide, we will provide you with tips, how-to's, and hair care advice to help you achieve your desired look.


How To Determine Your Face Shape

Before we begin discussing the best hair lengths for round faces, let's first determine if you have a round face. To do this, follow these steps:

Step 1:

Tie your hair back so that your face is completely visible.

Step 2:

Stand in front of a mirror and take a close look at the shape of your face.

Step 3:

Compare your face to the following shapes: oval, square, heart, and round.

If your face is widest at the cheeks and has a rounded chin, then you have a round face.


Best Hair Lengths for Round Faces – Step By Step

Now that you have determined your face shape, let's move on to the best hair lengths for round faces.

Step 1: Short Hair

If you have a round face, a short haircut can be a great option. A pixie cut or a bob that is chin-length or shorter can help to elongate your face and make it appear slimmer.

Step 2: Medium Hair

For those with round faces, a medium-length haircut that falls just below the shoulders is a great option. This length will help to create the illusion of a longer face.

Step 3: Long Hair

If you prefer a longer hairstyle, opt for hair that is shoulder-length or longer. Layers can help to frame your face and create a slimming effect.


Tips for Choosing the Right Hair Length

Here are some additional tips to keep in mind when choosing the right hair length for your round face:

  • Stay away from hairstyles that add volume to the sides of your face.
  • Consider getting bangs to help elongate your face.
  • Opt for hairstyles that have layers and texture to add dimension to your hair.
  • Avoid hairstyles that are too blunt or one-length.

FAQ

Q: Can I still have long hair if I have a round face?

A: Yes, you can still have long hair if you have a round face. Just make sure to add layers to help frame your face and create a slimming effect.

Q: What hairstyles should I avoid if I have a round face?

A: You should avoid hairstyles that add volume to the sides of your face, such as blunt bobs or hairstyles with heavy layers around the cheeks.


Hair Care Tips

Now that you have found the perfect hair length for your round face, it's important to keep your hair healthy and looking great. Here are some hair care tips to keep in mind:

  • Wash your hair regularly, but not too often.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week.
  • Avoid using hot tools too often, and always use a heat protectant.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.
