What Type Of Haircut Do Guys Like

Written by Daniel Aug 02, 2023 · 8 min read

Getting a new haircut can be daunting, especially if you’re not quite sure what style to go for. If you’re a guy wondering what type of haircut to get, you’ve come to the right place. In this article, we’ll cover everything you need to know about the types of haircuts that guys like, how to get them, and how to take care of your hair afterwards.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to
  • Step by Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

Before we dive into the different types of haircuts that guys like, it’s important to understand what factors can influence a guy’s decision. Generally, guys prefer haircuts that are low maintenance, stylish, and easy to maintain. They also tend to prefer haircuts that suit their face shape and personal style.

With that said, here are some of the most popular types of haircuts that guys like:

Short Haircuts

Short haircuts are a classic option for guys who want a low maintenance, stylish look. There are many different types of short haircuts to choose from, including buzz cuts, crew cuts, and fades. These haircuts are perfect for guys who want a no-fuss look that’s easy to maintain.

Medium Length Haircuts

Medium length haircuts are a popular option for guys who want a little more length without having to deal with long hair. The most popular medium length haircuts include the textured crop, side part, and the messy quiff. These haircuts are perfect for guys who want a stylish, versatile look.

Long Haircuts

Long haircuts are a more daring option for guys who want to make a statement. The most popular long haircuts include the man bun, messy top knot, and the shoulder-length cut. These haircuts require a bit more maintenance, but they can be a great way to show off your personal style.

How to

If you’re getting a new haircut, there are a few things you can do to ensure that you get the style you want. Here are some tips:

Do Your Research

Before you go to the barber, do some research to find the type of haircut you want. Look at pictures online or in magazines to get an idea of the style you’re after.

Communicate with Your Barber

When you’re at the barber, be sure to communicate clearly about the type of haircut you want. Use pictures or descriptive language to explain the style you’re after.

Be Open to Suggestions

Your barber may have some suggestions for the type of haircut that would suit you best. Be open to their suggestions and consider their advice.

Step by Step

Here’s a step-by-step guide to getting a new haircut:

  1. Do your research and find the type of haircut you want.
  2. Book an appointment with a barber.
  3. Communicate clearly with your barber about the type of haircut you want.
  4. Relax and let the barber do their work.
  5. After your haircut, be sure to ask your barber about how to maintain your new style.

Tips

Here are some tips for maintaining your new haircut:

  • Use a good quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair clean and healthy.
  • Invest in some styling products to help you achieve your desired look.
  • Get regular trims to keep your haircut looking sharp.
  • Consider your face shape and personal style when choosing a haircut.

FAQ

What’s the most popular type of haircut for guys?

The most popular type of haircut for guys varies depending on personal preference and current trends. Some of the most popular haircuts for guys include the buzz cut, crew cut, side part, and textured crop.

How often should I get a haircut?

Most guys should get a haircut every 4-6 weeks to keep their hair looking sharp and healthy.

Hair Care

Proper hair care is essential for maintaining a healthy, stylish haircut. Here are some tips for taking care of your hair:

  • Use a good quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair clean and healthy.
  • Avoid using hot tools like blow dryers and straighteners too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Eat a healthy, balanced diet to promote hair growth and health.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair looking neat and healthy.

Conclusion

Choosing the right haircut can be a challenge, but with the right research and communication, you can find a style that suits your face shape and personal style. Whether you prefer short, medium, or long haircuts, there’s a style out there that’s perfect for you. With the right care and maintenance, your new haircut will look great for weeks to come.
