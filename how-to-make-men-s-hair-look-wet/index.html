How To Make Men s Hair Look Wet A Step By Step Guide

Written by Aprilia May 30, 2023 Â· 7 min read

If you're looking for a sleek and modern hairstyle, making your hair look wet can be a great option. This style can work for both short and long hair and is perfect for a variety of occasions, from casual to formal. In this article, we'll take you through the steps to achieve the perfect wet look for men's hair.

Table of Contents

  • How to Make Men's Hair Look Wet
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Achieving the Wet Look
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care for the Wet Look

How to Make Men's Hair Look Wet

There are a few different methods for making men's hair look wet:

  • Using a hair gel or pomade
  • Applying a hair oil or serum
  • Washing your hair and leaving it damp

Depending on your hair type and the look you're going for, you may prefer one method over the others. In general, using a hair gel or pomade will give you the strongest hold, while applying a hair oil or serum will give you a more natural, flexible look.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here's how to make men's hair look wet using a hair gel or pomade:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair.
  2. Apply a dime-sized amount of hair gel or pomade to your fingertips.
  3. Work the product evenly through your hair, focusing on the top and sides.
  4. Use a comb or your fingers to style your hair into the desired shape.
  5. Finish with a light mist of hairspray to hold the style in place.

If you prefer to use a hair oil or serum, here's what to do:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair.
  2. Apply a small amount of hair oil or serum to your palms.
  3. Work the product evenly through your hair, focusing on the ends.
  4. Use your fingers or a comb to style your hair into the desired shape.
  5. Finish with a light mist of hairspray if desired.

Tips for Achieving the Wet Look

Here are some additional tips for making men's hair look wet:

  • Start with a small amount of product and add more as needed.
  • Use a comb or your fingers to create texture and definition in your hair.
  • Experiment with different products to find the one that works best for your hair type and desired look.
  • Consider using a matte hairspray to give your hair a natural-looking shine without making it look greasy.

FAQs

Can I achieve the wet look with curly hair?

Absolutely! The wet look can work well with curly hair, but you may need to use a stronger hold product to keep your curls in place.

Will the wet look damage my hair?

As long as you're using products that are designed for your hair type and not applying them too frequently or in excessive amounts, the wet look shouldn't damage your hair.

Can I achieve the wet look without using product?

If you have naturally oily hair, you may be able to achieve a wet look without using any product. However, most people will need to use some sort of hair gel, pomade, oil, or serum to achieve the look.

Hair Care for the Wet Look

To keep your hair looking its best when you're going for the wet look, it's important to take good care of your hair. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly to keep it clean and healthy.
  • Use a conditioner to keep your hair moisturized.
  • Avoid using too much product, which can make your hair look greasy and weighed down.
  • Try not to style your hair with heat too often, as this can damage your hair and make it look dry and brittle.

With these tips and techniques, you'll be able to achieve the perfect wet look for men's hair. Whether you're going for a sleek and modern style or a more casual look, the wet look is a great option for a variety of occasions.
