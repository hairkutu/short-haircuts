Mid Length Hairstyles For Long Faces A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Oliver Jul 25, 2023 Â· 7 min read

If you have a long face, you may have struggled with finding the right hairstyle that flatters your features. Many hairstyles can make your face appear even longer, so it's important to find the right length and style that complements your face shape. In this guide, we will explore mid length hairstyles for long faces and offer tips on how to style them.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How To
  • Step by Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

Mid length hairstyles are a great option for those with long faces. This length falls between the chin and shoulders and can help balance out a longer face shape. There are many different mid length hairstyles to choose from, including layered cuts, blunt bobs, and shaggy styles.

When choosing a mid length hairstyle, it's important to consider your hair type and texture. A layered cut can add volume to fine hair, while a blunt cut can work well for thick hair. It's also important to consider your face shape and features when choosing a mid length hairstyle.

How To

To achieve a mid length hairstyle for a long face, start by consulting with a hairstylist. They can help you choose a cut and style that flatters your face shape and works with your hair type and texture.

Once you have your mid length cut, there are many ways to style it. You can add waves or curls for a more relaxed look, or straighten it for a sleeker style. You can also experiment with different partings and accessories to change up your look.

Step by Step

Here is a step by step guide to achieving a mid length hairstyle for a long face:

  1. Consult with a hairstylist to choose a cut and style that works for you
  2. Wash and condition your hair
  3. Towel dry your hair until it is damp
  4. Apply a heat protectant to your hair
  5. Blow dry your hair using a round brush to add volume
  6. Use a curling iron or straightener to style your hair as desired
  7. Finish with a hairspray or styling product to hold your style

Tips

Here are some tips for achieving the perfect mid length hairstyle for a long face:

  • Consider adding layers to add volume and movement to your hair
  • Experiment with different partings to change up your look
  • Use a heat protectant to prevent damage from styling tools
  • Don't be afraid to try new styles and accessories
  • Use a hairspray or styling product to hold your style in place

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about mid length hairstyles for long faces:

  • Q: Will a mid length hairstyle work for all face shapes?
  • A: While mid length hairstyles can work well for long faces, they may not be the best option for other face shapes. It's important to consult with a hairstylist to find the right cut and style for your face shape.
  • Q: How often should I get my mid length hairstyle trimmed?
  • A: It's recommended to get a trim every 6-8 weeks to maintain the shape and style of your mid length cut.
  • Q: Can I style my mid length hair without heat tools?
  • A: Yes, you can achieve a variety of styles without using heat tools. You can try braids, twists, or updos to change up your mid length hairstyle.

Hair Care

To keep your mid length hairstyle looking healthy and vibrant, it's important to care for your hair properly. Here are some tips for maintaining your mid length hair:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo and conditioner
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair hydrated
  • Limit your use of heat styling tools to prevent damage
  • Protect your hair from the sun with a hat or scarf
  • Get regular trims to maintain the shape and style of your mid length cut

With these tips and tricks, you can achieve a beautiful mid length hairstyle that flatters your long face. Remember to consult with a hairstylist to find the right cut and style for you, and to care for your hair properly to keep it looking its best.
