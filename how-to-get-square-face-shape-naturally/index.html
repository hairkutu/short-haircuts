How To Get Square Face Shape Naturally

Written by Frank Aug 17, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Are you tired of your round or oval face shape? Do you want to achieve a more defined and angular look? If so, you may be interested in learning how to get square face shape naturally. In this article, we will provide you with step-by-step tips and tricks to achieve a more chiseled jawline and defined cheekbones.

Table of Content

  1. Description
  2. How to
  3. Step by step
  4. Tips
  5. FAQ
  6. Hair Care

Description

A square face shape is characterized by a strong, angular jawline and well-defined cheekbones. This shape is often considered the most attractive and desirable, as it gives the face a more masculine or angular appearance. While some people are born with this shape naturally, others may need to take certain steps to achieve it.

How to

There are several ways to get square face shape naturally, including:

Step by step

1. Facial Exercises

Facial exercises can help to tone and define the muscles in your face, including your jawline and cheekbones. Some effective exercises include:

  • Jaw Clenches: Clench your jaw for 10 seconds, then relax.
  • Cheek Puffs: Puff your cheeks out with air and hold for 10 seconds, then release.
  • Kiss the Ceiling: Tilt your head back and pucker your lips up towards the ceiling.

2. Diet and Exercise

Eating a healthy diet and getting regular exercise can also help to achieve a square face shape naturally. A diet rich in protein and low in carbohydrates can help to reduce the amount of fat in your face, while exercise can help to tone and define your muscles.

3. Hairstyles

Choosing the right hairstyle can also help to enhance your facial features and achieve a square face shape naturally. Avoid hairstyles that add volume to the sides of your face, and instead opt for styles that are shorter on the sides and longer on top.

Tips

Here are some additional tips to help you get square face shape naturally:

  • Avoid smoking and excessive alcohol consumption, as these can contribute to a puffy or rounded face shape.
  • Use contouring makeup techniques to enhance your jawline and cheekbones.
  • Consider cosmetic procedures such as chin implants or cheek fillers to enhance your facial structure.

FAQ

Can I get a square face shape naturally?

Yes, it is possible to achieve a square face shape naturally through facial exercises, diet and exercise, and choosing the right hairstyles.

Are there any risks associated with facial exercises?

No, facial exercises are generally considered safe and do not pose any significant risks. However, it is important to avoid overexerting your facial muscles, as this can cause pain or discomfort.

Hair Care

When trying to achieve a square face shape naturally, it is important to choose the right hairstyles that complement your facial features. Here are some tips:

  • Choose a hairstyle that is shorter on the sides and longer on top.
  • Avoid hairstyles that add volume to the sides of your face.
  • Consider getting a haircut that features layers or angles to enhance your jawline and cheekbones.

By following these tips and techniques, you can achieve a more defined and angular square face shape naturally. Remember to be patient and consistent with your efforts, as it may take some time to see results.
