How To Shave Your Own Head Back And Sides

Written by Christine Aug 23, 2023 · 6 min read

If you're looking to shave your own head back and sides, you've come to the right place. Shaving your head can be a liberating experience, but it's important to do it safely and correctly. In this article, we'll go over everything you need to know about shaving your own head back and sides, including how to do it, step by step instructions, tips, FAQs, and hair care advice.

Table of Contents

  • How To Shave Your Own Head Back And Sides
  • Step by Step Instructions
  • Tips for Shaving Your Own Head Back And Sides
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care Advice

How To Shave Your Own Head Back And Sides

Before you start shaving your head, make sure you have all the necessary tools. You'll need a razor or electric shaver, shaving cream or gel, a mirror, and a towel. Once you have everything you need, follow these steps:

Step 1: Trim Your Hair

Before you start shaving, it's a good idea to trim your hair first. This will make it easier to shave and reduce the risk of clogging your razor. Use a pair of clippers or scissors to trim your hair to about 1/4 inch.

Step 2: Apply Shaving Cream or Gel

Apply shaving cream or gel to the back and sides of your head. Massage it into your scalp to create a lather. This will help lubricate your skin and reduce the risk of razor burn.

Step 3: Start Shaving

Start shaving the back of your head first. Use long, smooth strokes and be careful not to press too hard. Rinse your razor frequently to prevent clogging. Once you've finished the back of your head, move on to the sides. Use your mirror to help guide you.

Step 4: Rinse and Repeat

Once you've finished shaving, rinse your head with warm water. Apply more shaving cream or gel and repeat the process until you've achieved your desired level of smoothness.

Tips for Shaving Your Own Head Back And Sides

Here are some tips to make shaving your own head back and sides easier:

  • Use a sharp razor or electric shaver.
  • Go slowly and use light pressure.
  • Rinse your razor frequently to prevent clogging.
  • Shave in the direction of hair growth to reduce the risk of ingrown hairs.
  • Take your time and be patient.

FAQs

Is it safe to shave your own head back and sides?

Yes, it's safe to shave your own head back and sides as long as you do it correctly. Make sure you use a sharp razor or electric shaver and go slowly to avoid cuts and nicks.

How often should I shave my head?

How often you shave your head is up to personal preference. Some people shave every day, while others do it once a week or less frequently.

What should I do if I get razor burn?

If you get razor burn, apply a soothing lotion or cream to the affected area. You can also try using a cold compress or taking an over-the-counter pain reliever.

Hair Care Advice

After you've shaved your head back and sides, it's important to take care of your hair and scalp. Here are some tips:

  • Apply a moisturizer or oil to your scalp to keep it hydrated.
  • Wear a hat or sunscreen when you're outside to protect your scalp from the sun.
  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo.
  • Use a conditioner to keep your hair soft and manageable.

Now that you know how to shave your own head back and sides, you can enjoy the freedom and confidence that comes with a freshly shaved head. Remember to take your time, be patient, and follow these tips for the best results.
