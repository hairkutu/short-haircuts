Short Pixie With Long Bangs A Trending Hairstyle For 2023

Written by Eveline Jul 14, 2023 Â· 9 min read
If you're looking for a bold and edgy hairstyle that's easy to maintain, a short pixie with long bangs might be the perfect choice for you. This hairstyle has been gaining popularity in recent years, and it's not hard to see why. With its short length and long, sweeping bangs, it's a chic and versatile look that can be customized to suit any face shape and hair type. In this article, we'll dive into everything you need to know about the short pixie with long bangs hairstyle, including how to achieve it, tips for styling and maintaining it, and answers to some frequently asked questions.

Description: What is a Short Pixie with Long Bangs?

A short pixie with long bangs is a hairstyle that features short, cropped hair on the sides and back of the head, with longer, sweeping bangs that fall across the forehead. The length of the bangs can vary, but they typically extend several inches past the eyebrows. This hairstyle is often associated with a bold and edgy aesthetic, but it can also be customized to suit a more subtle and sophisticated look. It's a versatile style that can be dressed up or down, making it a popular choice for many women.

How to Achieve a Short Pixie with Long Bangs

Achieving a short pixie with long bangs hairstyle will depend on your current hair length and texture. If you already have short hair, you can simply trim the sides and back of your hair to create a pixie cut, and then leave the front section longer to form the bangs. If you have longer hair, you'll need to have it cut to a shorter length first. A stylist can help you determine the best length for your face shape and hair texture. Once your hair is cut to the desired length, your stylist can then create the bangs by cutting a section of hair in the front and leaving it longer than the rest.

Step-by-Step Guide:

1. Wash your hair and towel dry it. 2. Apply a heat protectant to your hair to prevent heat damage from the styling tools. 3. Using a round brush, blow-dry your hair in sections, starting with the bangs. 4. Once your hair is dry, use a flat iron to smooth out any kinks or frizz. 5. Apply a small amount of hair wax or pomade to the tips of your bangs to add definition and texture.

Tips for Styling and Maintaining a Short Pixie with Long Bangs

Styling and maintaining a short pixie with long bangs is relatively easy, but there are a few tips to keep in mind: - Use a good quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and strong. - Trim your hair regularly to maintain the shape and length of your pixie cut. - Use a heat protectant when styling your hair with heat tools to prevent damage. - Experiment with different hair products to find what works best for your hair type and texture.

FAQ: Common Questions About Short Pixie with Long Bangs

Q: Will a short pixie with long bangs suit my face shape?

A: Short pixie with long bangs can suit a variety of face shapes, but it's important to consult with a stylist to determine the best length and shape for your features.

Q: Can I style my short pixie with long bangs in different ways?

A: Yes! While the basic shape of the haircut will remain the same, you can experiment with different styling techniques to create different looks.

Q: Will a short pixie with long bangs require a lot of maintenance?

A: Maintaining a short pixie with long bangs is relatively easy, but you'll need to trim your hair regularly to keep the shape and length looking fresh.

Hair Care for Short Pixie with Long Bangs

To keep your short pixie with long bangs looking its best, it's important to take good care of your hair. Here are a few tips to keep in mind: - Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner that's formulated for your hair type. - Use a heat protectant when using heat tools to style your hair. - Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy and hydrated. - Trim your hair regularly to maintain the shape and length of your pixie cut. - Avoid using too much hair product, as this can weigh down your hair and make it look greasy. In conclusion, a short pixie with long bangs is a trendy and versatile hairstyle that's perfect for a bold and edgy look. With the right styling tools and hair care routine, you can easily maintain this look and experiment with different styling techniques to create a variety of looks. So, if you're looking for a chic and low-maintenance hairstyle, a short pixie with long bangs might be the perfect choice for you!
