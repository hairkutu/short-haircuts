Description

Mens short spiked hairstyles have been popular for decades, and they are still a trendy choice in 2023. This style is perfect for those who want to achieve a bold and edgy look without spending too much time on their hair. It is also versatile and can be adapted to different hair types and face shapes.

Spiked hairstyles are created by cutting the sides and back short while leaving the top longer. The longer hair is then styled upwards or combed to the side to create the signature spikes. This style can be achieved with various hair products, such as pomade, wax, or gel, depending on the desired hold and finish.

How to Style Mens Short Spiked Hairstyles

The key to styling mens short spiked hairstyles is to have the right tools and products at hand. You will need a good quality comb, a hair dryer, and your preferred hair product. Here are the basic steps to achieve this style:

Step 1: Wash your hair

Start with clean and dry hair. Wash your hair with a clarifying shampoo to remove any buildup and residue. Apply a conditioner to keep your hair moisturized and manageable.

Step 2: Dry your hair

Use a towel to pat your hair dry, then use a hair dryer to dry it completely. Make sure to dry your hair in the direction you want it to lay.

Step 3: Apply hair product

Take a small amount of your preferred hair product, such as pomade or wax, and rub it between your hands. Apply it evenly to your hair, making sure to focus on the top and front sections.

Step 4: Style your hair

Use a comb to style your hair in the desired direction. To create spikes, use your fingers to lift and twist sections of hair upwards. Use the comb to shape and define the spikes. You can also use a hair dryer on low heat to set your style.

Step by Step Guide

Here is a step by step guide to achieve mens short spiked hairstyles:

Step 1: Choose the right haircut

The first step to achieving this style is to get the right haircut. The sides and back should be cut short, while the top should be left longer. The length of the top will depend on your personal preference and face shape.

Step 2: Wash your hair

Start with clean and dry hair. Wash your hair with a clarifying shampoo to remove any buildup and residue. Apply a conditioner to keep your hair moisturized and manageable.

Step 3: Dry your hair

Use a towel to pat your hair dry, then use a hair dryer to dry it completely. Make sure to dry your hair in the direction you want it to lay.

Step 4: Apply hair product

Take a small amount of your preferred hair product, such as pomade or wax, and rub it between your hands. Apply it evenly to your hair, making sure to focus on the top and front sections.

Step 5: Style your hair

Use a comb to style your hair in the desired direction. To create spikes, use your fingers to lift and twist sections of hair upwards. Use the comb to shape and define the spikes. You can also use a hair dryer on low heat to set your style.

Tips and Tricks

Here are some tips and tricks to help you achieve the perfect mens short spiked hairstyle:

  • Experiment with different hair products to find the one that works best for your hair type and desired hold.
  • Use a hair dryer on low heat to set your style and make it last longer.
  • Trim your hair regularly to maintain the shape and length of your style.
  • Ask your stylist for advice on the best haircut and style for your face shape and hair type.

Frequently Asked Questions

What hair products should I use for mens short spiked hairstyles?

You can use a variety of hair products to achieve this style, such as pomade, wax, or gel. Choose a product that provides the hold and finish you want, and that works well with your hair type.

How often should I trim my hair for this style?

You should trim your hair every 4-6 weeks to maintain the shape and length of your style.

Can I achieve this style with curly or wavy hair?

Yes, you can achieve this style with curly or wavy hair. However, you may need to use more hair product and spend more time styling your hair to create the desired spikes.

Hair Care for Spiked Hairstyles

Maintaining healthy hair is essential to achieving and maintaining mens short spiked hairstyles. Here are some hair care tips to keep your hair looking its best:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a clarifying shampoo to remove buildup and residue.
  • Use a conditioner to keep your hair moisturized and manageable.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, such as blow dryers and straighteners, as this can damage your hair.
  • Trim your hair regularly to maintain its shape and health.

With the right tools, products, and techniques, you can achieve the perfect mens short spiked hairstyle. Experiment with different styles and products to find the one that works best for you, and don't be afraid to ask your stylist for advice and guidance.
