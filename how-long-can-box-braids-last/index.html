How Long Can Box Braids Last Tips And Hair Care

Written by Aprilia Mar 28, 2023 · 7 min read

If you’re looking for a low-maintenance hairstyle that will last for weeks, box braids are a great option. But how long can box braids last? In this article, we’ll cover everything you need to know about box braids, including how to care for them and how long they can last.

Table of Contents

  • What are Box Braids?
  • How Long Do Box Braids Last?
  • Tips for Keeping Box Braids Looking Great
  • Frequently Asked Questions
  • Hair Care Tips for Box Braids

What are Box Braids?

Box braids are a popular protective style for natural hair. They are created by sectioning the hair into small boxes and braiding each section. Box braids can be worn in a variety of styles, including updos, ponytails, and half-up half-down styles.

How Long Do Box Braids Last?

The answer to this question depends on a few factors, including the size of the braids and how well you take care of them. On average, box braids can last anywhere from 4-6 weeks. However, some people are able to keep their box braids in for up to 8 weeks.

If you want your box braids to last as long as possible, there are a few things you can do. First, make sure that your braids are not too tight. Tight braids can cause damage to your hair and scalp, and can also cause your braids to come out sooner.

Second, avoid getting your braids wet as much as possible. Water can cause your braids to loosen and frizz, which can shorten their lifespan. If you do get your braids wet, be sure to dry them thoroughly with a towel or blow dryer.

Finally, be gentle when styling your braids. Avoid pulling or tugging on them, as this can cause them to come out sooner. Instead, use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to style your braids.

Tips for Keeping Box Braids Looking Great

Here are a few tips for keeping your box braids looking great:

  • Use a satin or silk scarf or pillowcase to protect your braids while you sleep
  • Avoid using heavy hair products, as they can cause buildup on your braids
  • Keep your scalp moisturized with a lightweight oil or spray
  • Wash your braids once a week with a gentle shampoo
  • Avoid using heat styling tools on your braids, as this can cause damage

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I swim with box braids?

Yes, you can swim with box braids. However, it’s important to protect your braids from chlorine and saltwater, as these can cause damage and shorten the lifespan of your braids. Before swimming, apply a leave-in conditioner to your braids and wear a swim cap.

How long should I wait before getting box braids again?

It’s best to wait at least 2-4 weeks before getting box braids again. This will give your hair and scalp time to rest and recover.

Can box braids cause hair breakage?

If box braids are installed too tightly or if they are left in for too long, they can cause hair breakage. It’s important to make sure that your braids are not too tight and that you take them out after 4-6 weeks.

Hair Care Tips for Box Braids

Here are a few more hair care tips for box braids:

  • Avoid using heavy hair products, as they can cause buildup and make your braids look dull
  • Keep your scalp moisturized with a lightweight oil or spray
  • Avoid pulling or tugging on your braids, as this can cause them to come out sooner
  • Wash your braids once a week with a gentle shampoo
  • Avoid using heat styling tools on your braids

With these tips, you can keep your box braids looking great for up to 6 weeks or longer. Just remember to take good care of your braids and be gentle when styling them.
