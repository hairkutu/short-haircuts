How To Control Frizzy Hair On Top Of Head Tips And Tricks

Written by William Apr 30, 2023 Â· 6 min read

If you have curly or wavy hair, you know how frustrating it can be to deal with frizz. While frizz can occur throughout the hair, it is particularly frustrating when it happens on the top of the head. In this blog post, we will discuss how to control frizzy hair on top of the head.

Table of Contents

  1. How to Control Frizzy Hair on Top of Head
  2. Step-by-Step Guide
  3. Tips for Managing Frizz
  4. FAQs
  5. Hair Care for Frizzy Hair

How to Control Frizzy Hair on Top of Head

The first step in controlling frizzy hair on top of the head is to identify the cause of the frizz. Frizz can be caused by a variety of factors, including humidity, dryness, heat, and chemical treatments. Once you have identified the cause, you can take steps to address it.

If the cause of the frizz is humidity, you may want to consider using a product that contains silicone to help seal the hair cuticle and prevent moisture from entering the hair shaft. If the cause is dryness, you may want to use a deep conditioning treatment to add moisture to the hair. If the cause is heat, you may want to avoid using hot styling tools or use them on a lower heat setting. If the cause is chemical treatments, you may want to consider using a gentle shampoo and conditioner that are designed for chemically treated hair.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here is a step-by-step guide on how to control frizzy hair on top of the head:

  1. Identify the cause of the frizz
  2. Select a product or treatment that addresses the cause
  3. Use the product or treatment as directed
  4. Avoid using hot styling tools or use them on a lower heat setting

Tips for Managing Frizz

Here are some additional tips for managing frizz:

  • Avoid over-washing your hair, as this can strip the hair of its natural oils
  • Use a wide-toothed comb or a paddle brush to detangle your hair
  • Avoid rubbing your hair with a towel after washing, as this can cause friction and lead to frizz
  • Consider using a silk or satin pillowcase, as these materials are gentler on the hair and can help prevent frizz

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about controlling frizzy hair on top of the head:

Q: Can I use hair oil to control frizz?

A: Yes, hair oil can be a great way to control frizz. Look for oils that are lightweight and non-greasy, such as argan oil or jojoba oil.

Q: How often should I deep condition my hair?

A: This depends on your hair type and the level of damage. As a general rule, it is recommended to deep condition your hair once a week.

Q: Can I use hairspray to control frizz?

A: Yes, hairspray can be a great way to control frizz. Look for hairsprays that are designed to provide hold without leaving a sticky or crunchy residue.

Hair Care for Frizzy Hair

If you have frizzy hair, it is important to take care of it properly. Here are some tips for caring for frizzy hair:

  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner that are designed for frizzy hair
  • Avoid using hot styling tools or use them on a lower heat setting
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week
  • Avoid over-washing your hair, as this can strip the hair of its natural oils
  • Consider getting a regular trim to help prevent split ends and breakage

By following these tips and tricks, you can control frizzy hair on top of the head and enjoy beautiful, healthy-looking hair.
