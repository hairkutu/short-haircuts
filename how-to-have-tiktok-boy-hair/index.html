How To Have Tiktok Boy Hair A Step By Step Guide

If you're looking to rock the popular TikTok boy hair trend, you're in the right place. This hairstyle has taken social media by storm, with its effortless and stylish look. In this guide, we'll go through step-by-step on how to achieve the perfect TikTok boy hair, along with tips, FAQs, and hair care advice.

Table of Contents:

  • How to Have TikTok Boy Hair

How to Have TikTok Boy Hair

The TikTok boy hair trend is all about a messy and textured look with longer hair on top and shorter sides. Here's how you can achieve this look:

Step 1: Grow Out Your Hair

The first step in achieving TikTok boy hair is to let your hair grow out. You'll need at least 4-6 inches of hair on top to get the right length and texture.

Step 2: Get the Right Haircut

Once your hair has grown out, it's time to get a haircut. Make sure to ask your hairstylist for a layered cut with longer hair on top and shorter sides. The layers will help create texture, and the shorter sides will add contrast to the longer top.

Step 3: Style Your Hair

Now that you have the right haircut, it's time to style your hair. Start by applying a small amount of hair product, such as a texturizing spray or pomade, to your hair. Use your fingers to work the product through your hair, creating texture and volume. You can also use a hairdryer to add more volume and shape to your hair.

Step 4: Finish with Hairspray

Once you've styled your hair, finish it off with a light mist of hairspray to hold the look in place. You don't want too much hairspray, or it will weigh down your hair and make it look greasy.

Tips for Styling and Maintaining TikTok Boy Hair

Here are some tips for styling and maintaining your TikTok boy hair:

Tip 1: Don't Wash Your Hair Every Day

Washing your hair every day can strip it of its natural oils and make it look dry and dull. Try washing your hair every 2-3 days instead and use dry shampoo in between washes to keep your hair looking fresh.

Tip 2: Use the Right Hair Products

When it comes to hair products, less is more. Use a small amount of texturizing spray or pomade to create texture and volume in your hair. Avoid using too much product, or it will weigh down your hair and make it look greasy.

Tip 3: Get Regular Haircuts

To maintain your TikTok boy hair, you'll need to get regular haircuts. Aim to get a trim every 4-6 weeks to keep your hair looking its best.

FAQs About TikTok Boy Hair

Q: Can I have TikTok boy hair if I have curly hair?

A: Absolutely! TikTok boy hair works with all hair types, including curly hair. Just make sure to get a layered cut that works with your natural curl pattern.

Q: How do I keep my TikTok boy hair looking good all day?

A: Use a light mist of hairspray to hold your hair in place and avoid touching it too much throughout the day. You can also use a little bit of dry shampoo to refresh your hair if it starts to look oily.

Q: Can I still have TikTok boy hair if I have a receding hairline?

A: Absolutely! Talk to your hairstylist about getting a haircut that works with your receding hairline, such as a side-swept fringe or a textured crop.

Hair Care Tips for Healthy TikTok Boy Hair

Here are some hair care tips to keep your TikTok boy hair looking healthy and shiny:

Tip 1: Use a Gentle Shampoo and Conditioner

Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils. Look for products that are sulfate-free and contain natural ingredients.

Tip 2: Protect Your Hair from Heat

Use a heat protectant spray before using hot tools like a hairdryer or straightener. This will help protect your hair from heat damage and keep it looking healthy.

Tip 3: Avoid Tight Hairstyles

Avoid tight hairstyles that pull on your hair, such as tight braids or ponytails. These styles can cause breakage and damage to your hair.

With these tips and tricks, you'll be able to achieve the perfect TikTok boy hair. Remember to take care of your hair with regular haircuts and gentle hair care products to keep it looking healthy and shiny. Happy styling!
