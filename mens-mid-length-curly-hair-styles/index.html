Mens Mid Length Curly Hair Styles A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Aprilia Sep 02, 2023 Â· 9 min read

Welcome to our guide on mens mid length curly hair styles. If you're a man with curly hair, you already know that it's not always easy to find the right hairstyle that suits your hair type. But don't worry, we've got you covered. In this article, we will cover everything from how to style your hair, tips on maintenance, and frequently asked questions. So, sit back, relax and let's get started!

Description

Mens mid length curly hair styles are versatile and offer numerous options for men with curly hair. It can be challenging to style curly hair, but mid-length hair offers you the perfect balance between maintaining length and managing your curls.

How to Style Mens Mid Length Curly Hair

The key to styling mid length curly hair is to find a style that works best for your hair type. Here are some styling options:

1. The Messy Bun

The messy bun is a popular style among men with curly hair. It's effortless to achieve and only takes a few minutes to style. To achieve this look, gather your hair into a ponytail, twist it and wrap it around the base of your ponytail to create a bun. Use a hair tie to secure it in place and let some strands of hair fall around your face.

2. The Textured Quiff

The textured quiff is a modern hairstyle that works great for men with mid-length curly hair. To achieve this look, apply some styling product to your hair and use a hairdryer to create volume. Use your fingers to create a messy texture and style it into a quiff.

3. The Afro

The afro is a classic hairstyle that works great for men with curly hair. To achieve this look, let your hair grow out to shoulder length and use a pick to create volume. Use some hair oil to keep your curls moisturized and well-defined.

Step by Step Guide to Style Mens Mid Length Curly Hair

Here is a step-by-step guide to help you style your mid-length curly hair:

Step 1: Wash Your Hair

Wash your hair with a sulfate-free shampoo and a conditioner that's specifically designed for curly hair. Rinse your hair thoroughly and avoid using hot water as it can damage your curls.

Step 2: Apply Styling Product

Apply a styling product that's suitable for your hair type, such as a curl defining cream. Apply it to your damp hair and distribute it evenly using your fingers.

Step 3: Dry Your Hair

Use a diffuser attachment on your hairdryer and set it on a low heat setting. Use it to dry your hair while scrunching it with your fingers. This will help create volume and definition.

Step 4: Style Your Hair

Once your hair is dry, use your fingers to style it into your desired look. Use a hair spray to set it in place.

Tips for Maintaining Mens Mid Length Curly Hair

Maintaining mid-length curly hair requires some effort, but it's worth it. Here are some tips to help you maintain your curly locks:

1. Use a Wide-Tooth Comb

Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair, starting from the ends and working your way up. This will prevent breakage and damage to your curls.

2. Moisturize Your Hair

Curly hair tends to be dry, so it's essential to keep it moisturized. Use a hair oil or a leave-in conditioner to keep your curls hydrated and well-defined.

3. Trim Your Hair Regularly

Regular trims will help prevent split ends and keep your hair healthy. Trim your hair every 6-8 weeks to maintain your style.

FAQs

1. Can I straighten my curly hair?

Yes, you can straighten your curly hair, but it's not recommended as it can damage your hair. If you want to change your hairstyle, try a different curly style or consult with a hairstylist.

2. How often should I wash my curly hair?

It's recommended to wash your curly hair once or twice a week, depending on your hair type. Over-washing can strip your hair of its natural oils and cause dryness.

3. Can I use regular shampoo on my curly hair?

No, regular shampoo contains sulfates that can dry out your curly hair. Use a sulfate-free shampoo that's specifically designed for curly hair.

Hair Care

Taking care of your mid length curly hair requires some effort, but it's worth it. Here are some hair care tips:

1. Use a Satin Pillowcase

Using a satin pillowcase will help prevent frizz and breakage while you sleep.

2. Protect Your Hair from Heat

Avoid using heat styling tools as they can damage your hair. If you must use them, use a heat protectant spray to protect your curls.

3. Protect Your Hair from the Sun

Exposure to the sun can dry out your hair and cause damage. Wear a hat or use a hair product that contains SPF to protect your hair from the sun.

That's it for our comprehensive guide on mens mid length curly hair styles. We hope you found it helpful and informative. Remember to experiment with different styles and products until you find what works best for your hair. Good luck!
