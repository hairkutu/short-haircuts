How To Make Your Hair Look Like An Old Man

Written by Eveline Aug 09, 2023 · 7 min read

Are you tired of the same old hairstyle and looking for something unique? Why not try out a hairstyle that will make you look like an old man? This might sound crazy, but it’s a trend that’s gaining popularity. Whether you’re going for a Halloween costume or just want a change in your hairdo, we’ve got you covered. In this post, we’ll show you how to make your hair look like an old man in simple steps.

  1. How to Make Your Hair Look Like an Old Man
  2. Step by Step Guide
  3. Tips to Keep in Mind
  4. FAQs
  5. Hair Care Tips

How to Make Your Hair Look Like an Old Man

Before we dive into the steps, here are some keywords that you should keep in mind for better SEO optimization: old man hairstyle, grey hair, hair wax, hair gel, hair spray, blow dryer, hair comb, barber scissors, hair clippers.

Step 1: Get the Right Haircut

The first step in making your hair look like an old man is getting the right haircut. You need to go for a short, tapered cut that has a lot of layers. This will give your hair a textured, messy look. Ask your barber to leave some length on top and around the ears, so you can style it later.

Step 2: Color Your Hair

If your hair isn't already grey, you’ll need to color it. You can use a temporary hair color or go for a permanent one. Make sure to choose a shade that matches your skin tone and natural hair color. Apply the dye evenly, and leave it on for the recommended time.

Step 3: Use Hair Styling Products

To achieve the old man look, you’ll need to use some hair styling products. Apply a small amount of hair wax, gel or spray to your hair, and use a blow dryer to add volume. Use a hair comb to style your hair in the desired direction. Once you’re happy with the style, use some hair spray to keep it in place.

Step by Step Guide

Here’s a step by step guide to help you make your hair look like an old man:

  1. Get the right haircut.
  2. Color your hair.
  3. Use hair styling products.
  4. Blow dry your hair.
  5. Use a hair comb to style your hair.
  6. Add some hair spray to keep the style in place.
  7. Trim your eyebrows to make them look thinner.
  8. Grow a beard and mustache.
  9. Wear glasses with thick frames.
  10. Dress like an old man.

Tips to Keep in Mind

Here are some tips to help you make your hair look like an old man:

  • Use a hair dryer to add volume to your hair.
  • Apply hair spray to keep the style in place.
  • Trim your eyebrows to make them look thinner.
  • Grow a beard and mustache to complete the look.
  • Wear glasses with thick frames to add to the overall look.
  • Dress like an old man for a complete transformation.

FAQs

Here are some commonly asked questions about making your hair look like an old man:

Can I make my hair look like an old man without coloring it?

Yes, you can. You can use hair wax, gel or spray to style your hair in a way that resembles an old man's hair. However, coloring your hair will give it an authentic look.

What is the best hair color for an old man look?

The best hair color for an old man look is a shade of grey that matches your skin tone and natural hair color.

What is the best hairstyle for an old man look?

The best hairstyle for an old man look is a short, tapered cut that has a lot of layers. This will give your hair a textured, messy look.

Hair Care Tips

Here are some hair care tips to keep in mind:

  • Use a good quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as it can damage it.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.
  • Use hair styling products sparingly to avoid buildup.
  • Visit your barber regularly to maintain your hairstyle.

Now that you know how to make your hair look like an old man, it's time to get started. Follow these simple steps, and you'll be rocking the old man look in no time. Don’t forget to complete the look with a beard, glasses and the right clothes. Happy styling!
