Pixie Short Sides Long Top Female A Trendy Hairstyle In 2023

If you're looking for a bold and trendy hairstyle that is easy to maintain, then the pixie short sides long top female is the perfect look for you. This hairstyle has been gaining popularity over the years and is a favorite among women who want to make a statement with their hair. In this article, we'll be discussing everything you need to know about the pixie short sides long top female hairstyle.

Description

The pixie short sides long top female hairstyle is a short haircut that features shorter hair on the sides and a longer top. This hairstyle is versatile and can be styled in many different ways, such as slicked back or textured. The pixie short sides long top female hairstyle is perfect for women who want a low maintenance haircut that still makes a statement.

How to Style Pixie Short Sides Long Top Female

Styling the pixie short sides long top female hairstyle is easy and can be done in just a few minutes. Here's how:

Step 1: Wash Your Hair

Before styling your hair, you need to make sure it is clean. Wash your hair with a gentle shampoo and conditioner to remove any dirt, oil, or product buildup.

Step 2: Apply Styling Product

Apply a styling product to your hair, such as a mousse or gel. This will help to add volume and texture to your hair.

Step 3: Blow Dry Your Hair

Blow dry your hair with a round brush to add volume and shape to your hair. You can use a diffuser attachment to add more texture to your hair.

Step 4: Style Your Hair

Use your fingers or a comb to style your hair into the desired shape. You can slick your hair back for a sleek look or add texture for a more casual look.

Step by Step Guide

Here's a step by step guide on how to achieve the pixie short sides long top female hairstyle:

Step 1: Cut Your Hair

The first step to achieving the pixie short sides long top female hairstyle is to cut your hair. Make sure to tell your hairstylist that you want shorter hair on the sides and a longer top. You can also ask for layers or texture to add more dimension to your hair.

Step 2: Choose Your Styling Product

Choose a styling product that works best for your hair type and the look you want to achieve. You can use a mousse or gel for volume and texture or a wax or pomade for a more defined look.

Step 3: Apply Styling Product

Apply the styling product to your hair, starting with a small amount and adding more as needed. Make sure to evenly distribute the product throughout your hair.

Step 4: Blow Dry Your Hair

Use a blow dryer and a round brush to blow dry your hair. Start by drying the roots and then work your way down to the ends. Use the round brush to add volume and shape to your hair.

Step 5: Style Your Hair

Use your fingers or a comb to style your hair into the desired shape. You can slick your hair back for a sleek look or add texture for a more casual look. Finish with a hairspray to hold your style in place.

Tips for Maintaining Pixie Short Sides Long Top Female

Here are some tips to help you maintain your pixie short sides long top female hairstyle:

  • Get regular trims to keep your hair looking fresh and healthy.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair moisturized.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Use a dry shampoo in between washes to keep your hair looking fresh.

FAQ

Q: Will the pixie short sides long top female hairstyle suit me?

A: The pixie short sides long top female hairstyle is a versatile look that can suit many different face shapes and hair types. Consult with your hairstylist to find the best version of this hairstyle for you.

Q: Is the pixie short sides long top female hairstyle easy to maintain?

A: Yes, the pixie short sides long top female hairstyle is easy to maintain and requires minimal styling. However, you will need to get regular trims to keep your hair looking fresh.

Hair Care Tips for Pixie Short Sides Long Top Female

Here are some hair care tips to keep your pixie short sides long top female hairstyle looking its best:

  • Wash your hair with a gentle shampoo and conditioner to prevent damage.
  • Avoid over-washing your hair, as it can strip your hair of its natural oils.
  • Use a heat protectant before using any heat styling tools.
  • Avoid using harsh chemicals or hair dyes on your hair, as they can damage your hair.
  • Eat a healthy diet and drink plenty of water to promote healthy hair growth.

In conclusion, the pixie short sides long top female hairstyle is a trendy and low maintenance look that is perfect for women who want to make a statement with their hair. With the right styling products and techniques, you can easily achieve this bold and stylish look.
