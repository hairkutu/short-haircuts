How To Get Red Tone Out Of Blonde Hair A Complete Guide

Written by William Apr 17, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Are you tired of having red tones in your blonde hair? Don't worry, you're not alone. Many people experience this problem, but fortunately, there are effective ways to get rid of it. In this guide, we'll take you through the step-by-step process of removing the red tone from your blonde hair.

Description

Red tones in blonde hair can occur due to a variety of reasons, including exposure to the sun, chemical treatments, or using the wrong hair products. Whatever the cause, it can be frustrating and make you feel less confident about your hair. But there are solutions to this problem, and we'll show you how to achieve that perfect blonde shade you've always wanted.

How to Get Red Tone Out of Blonde Hair

Step 1: Choose the Right Toning Shampoo

The first step to getting rid of red tones in your blonde hair is to use a toning shampoo. Look for a purple or blue-toned shampoo, as these colors neutralize the red tones in your hair. Use the shampoo once or twice a week, depending on how often you wash your hair, and leave it on for a few minutes before rinsing it off.

Step 2: Use a Hair Toner

If the toning shampoo doesn't work, try using a hair toner. A toner is a semi-permanent hair dye that neutralizes unwanted tones. Choose a toner with a blue or purple base, and mix it with a developer according to the instructions on the package. Apply the toner to your hair, making sure to cover all areas, and leave it on for the recommended time. Rinse it off and style your hair as usual.

Step 3: Visit a Professional

If you're still struggling to remove the red tones from your hair, it's time to visit a professional. A hairstylist can assess your hair and recommend the best course of action. They may suggest a color correction treatment or a deep conditioning treatment to restore your hair's natural shine and color.

Tips

- Avoid using hot tools on your hair, as they can cause further damage and make the red tones more prominent.

- Use a hair mask once a week to keep your hair hydrated and healthy.

- If you're going to be in the sun for an extended period, wear a hat or use a UV protectant spray to prevent further damage to your hair.

FAQ

Q: Can I use regular shampoo to remove red tones from my blonde hair?

A: No, regular shampoo won't be effective in removing red tones from your hair. You need a toning shampoo or a hair toner to neutralize the unwanted tones.

Q: How long does it take to remove red tones from blonde hair?

A: It depends on the severity of the red tones and the method you're using to remove them. It can take anywhere from one to four weeks to see a significant difference.

Q: Can I dye my hair after using a toner?

A: Yes, you can dye your hair after using a toner. Just make sure to wait at least two weeks before applying any new color to your hair.

Hair Care

To keep your hair healthy and prevent red tones from coming back, it's essential to take care of your hair properly. Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner, and avoid using hot tools too often. If you're going to color your hair, make sure to use a hair dye that's specifically formulated for blonde hair, and follow the instructions carefully.

Red tones in blonde hair can be frustrating, but with the right products and techniques, you can achieve the perfect shade of blonde. Follow the steps in this guide and take care of your hair, and you'll have beautiful, healthy, and red-tone-free hair in no time.
