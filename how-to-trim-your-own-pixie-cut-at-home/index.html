How To Trim Your Own Pixie Cut At Home In 2023

Written by Bardi May 18, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Having a pixie cut is a great way to create a bold and stylish look. However, it can be quite expensive to maintain the cut as it requires frequent trips to the hair salon. But did you know that you can easily trim your own pixie cut at home? Not only will this save you money, but it will also give you more control over your hair. In this article, we will provide you with a step-by-step guide on how to trim your own pixie cut at home.

How to Trim Your Own Pixie Cut at Home

Before we get started, it's important to note that trimming your own pixie cut can be tricky, especially if you're not used to cutting your own hair. It's always a good idea to start with small trims and work your way up to bigger cuts. Here are the steps you should follow:

Step 1: Gather Your Tools

Before you start trimming your hair, you'll need to gather the necessary tools. You'll need a pair of sharp scissors, a comb, and a mirror. It's also a good idea to have hair clips on hand to keep your hair in place as you cut.

Step 2: Section Your Hair

Next, section your hair into manageable sections. Start by parting your hair down the middle and then divide it into four equal sections - two in the front and two in the back. Use hair clips to secure each section.

Step 3: Start Trimming

Now it's time to start trimming your hair. Begin with small cuts and work your way up to bigger cuts. Start with the sides and work your way towards the top of your head. Use the comb to lift your hair and then trim the ends with the scissors. Be sure to cut small sections at a time to avoid cutting too much hair.

Step 4: Trim the Back

Trimming the back of your head can be a bit tricky, so take your time. Use the mirror to see the back of your head and start trimming the ends with the scissors. Again, it's important to start with small cuts and work your way up to bigger cuts.

Step 5: Check Your Work

Once you've finished trimming your hair, use the mirror to check your work. Make any necessary adjustments and trim any stray hairs that you may have missed.

Tips for Trimming Your Own Pixie Cut at Home

Here are some tips to keep in mind when trimming your own pixie cut at home:

  • Start with small cuts and work your way up to bigger cuts
  • Use sharp scissors to avoid damaging your hair
  • Keep your hair well-moisturized to make it easier to cut
  • Don't rush the process - take your time and be patient
  • If you're unsure about cutting your hair, consider asking a friend for help

FAQs About Trimming Your Own Pixie Cut at Home

Can you trim your own pixie cut?

Yes, you can trim your own pixie cut at home. However, it can be tricky, so it's important to start with small cuts and work your way up to bigger cuts.

What tools do you need to trim your own pixie cut?

You'll need a pair of sharp scissors, a comb, and a mirror. It's also a good idea to have hair clips on hand to keep your hair in place as you cut.

How often should you trim your pixie cut?

You should trim your pixie cut every 4-6 weeks to keep it looking fresh and stylish.

Hair Care Tips for Your Pixie Cut

Once you've trimmed your own pixie cut at home, it's important to take care of it properly. Here are some hair care tips:

  • Wash your hair with a gentle shampoo and conditioner
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair
  • Use a leave-in conditioner to keep your hair moisturized
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair looking fresh

Trimming your own pixie cut at home can be a great way to save money and have more control over your hair. Follow these steps and tips to achieve a stylish and beautiful pixie cut in the comfort of your own home.
