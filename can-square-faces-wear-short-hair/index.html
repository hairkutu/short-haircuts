Can Square Faces Wear Short Hair Tips How To And Hair Care

Written by Daniel Aug 02, 2023 Â· 9 min read

A square face shape is characterized by a strong jawline, forehead, and cheekbones that are all equally wide. Women with square faces often wonder if they can wear short hair without looking too masculine or angular. The answer is yes, absolutely! In fact, there are plenty of short hairstyles that can flatter your square face shape and make you look stylish and feminine. In this blog post, we'll show you how to choose the best short haircut for your face shape, provide tips for styling it, and share some hair care tips to keep your locks healthy and shiny.

How to Choose the Best Short Haircut for Square Faces

The key to finding the perfect short haircut for square faces is to look for styles that soften the angles of your face and add some curves. Here are some tips to keep in mind:

1. Go for Layers

Layers can add movement and texture to your hair, which can soften your face shape. Ask your hairstylist to add layers to your short haircut to create some volume and shape. Avoid blunt cuts that can make your face appear boxy.

2. Side-Swept Bangs

Side-swept bangs can help to break up the width of your forehead and add some softness to your face. Opt for long, wispy bangs that graze your eyebrows rather than blunt or heavy bangs that can make your face look even more angular.

3. Pixie Cut

A pixie cut can be a great option for square faces, especially if you have fine hair. This short haircut can make your face appear longer and more delicate, and it can also highlight your cheekbones. Ask your hairstylist to add some texture and movement to your pixie cut to avoid a severe look.

4. Bob Haircut

A bob haircut can also be a great choice for square faces, especially if you opt for an asymmetrical or angled bob. These styles can add some curves to your face and draw attention away from your strong jawline. Avoid chin-length bobs that can make your face appear wider.

How to Style Short Hair for Square Faces

Styling short hair can be quick and easy, but it's important to choose the right products and techniques to avoid making your face look too angular. Here are some tips for styling short hair for square faces:

1. Use a Volumizing Mousse

A volumizing mousse can add some lift and volume to your hair, which can help to soften your face shape. Apply a small amount of mousse to your damp hair and blow-dry it with a round brush to create some volume and movement.

2. Avoid Center Parts

A center part can make your face appear even more boxy, so opt for a side part instead. A deep side part can help to create some asymmetry and draw attention to your eyes and cheekbones.

3. Embrace Waves

Soft waves can add some femininity and texture to your short haircut. Use a curling iron to create loose waves or apply some texturizing spray to your hair and scrunch it with your fingers to create some natural-looking waves.

FAQ About Short Haircuts for Square Faces

1. Can Square Faces Wear Short Bob Haircuts?

Yes, square faces can wear short bob haircuts. However, it's important to choose an angled or asymmetrical bob that can soften your face shape and add some curves. Avoid blunt bobs that can make your face appear wider.

2. Can Square Faces Wear Pixie Haircuts?

Yes, square faces can wear pixie haircuts. A pixie cut can make your face appear longer and more delicate, and it can also highlight your cheekbones. However, it's important to choose a textured and layered pixie cut that can add some movement and soften your face shape.

3. Can Square Faces Wear Bangs?

Yes, square faces can wear bangs. However, it's important to choose side-swept bangs that can break up the width of your forehead and add some softness to your face. Avoid blunt or heavy bangs that can make your face appear even more angular.

Hair Care Tips for Short Hair

Keeping your short hair healthy and shiny requires some extra care and attention. Here are some hair care tips to keep in mind:

1. Use a Gentle Shampoo

Choose a gentle shampoo that's free of sulfates and harsh chemicals to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils. Look for a shampoo that's formulated for your hair type and texture.

2. Avoid Over-Styling

Over-styling your short hair with heat tools can cause damage and breakage. Try to limit your use of flat irons, curling irons, and blow dryers, and use heat protectant products when you do style your hair with heat tools.

3. Get Regular Trims

Even though short hair doesn't require as much maintenance as long hair, it's still important to get regular trims to keep your hair looking neat and healthy. Aim to get a trim every 6-8 weeks to prevent split ends and breakage.

With these tips and tricks, you can confidently rock a short haircut with a square face shape. Remember to choose a style that suits your personality and lifestyle, and don't be afraid to experiment with different looks until you find the perfect one for you.
