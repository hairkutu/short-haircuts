How To Make Your Curtain Bangs Look Good Tips And Tricks

Written by Eveline Jun 24, 2023 Â· 9 min read

Curtain bangs are a popular hairstyle trend that has been around for several years now. They're a great way to add some style and personality to your hair without committing to a full haircut. However, styling and maintaining curtain bangs can be tricky, especially if you're new to the trend. In this article, we'll share some tips and tricks on how to make your curtain bangs look good, so you can rock this trendy hairstyle with confidence.

Description

Curtain bangs are a type of fringe that are parted down the middle and frame the face on either side. They're called "curtain" bangs because they're designed to mimic the look of curtains, with the hair parted in the middle like a drawn curtain. Curtain bangs can work with any hair type, length, or texture, and they're a great way to switch up your look without committing to a full haircut. However, styling curtain bangs can be a bit tricky, as they require some finesse to get just right.

How to Make Your Curtain Bangs Look Good: Step by Step

If you're new to curtain bangs, styling them can seem overwhelming at first. However, with a bit of practice and some helpful tips, you can master this trendy hairstyle in no time. Here's a step-by-step guide on how to make your curtain bangs look good:

Step 1: Start with clean, dry hair

Curtain bangs look best when they're styled on clean, dry hair. Start by washing and drying your hair as you normally would, using a shampoo and conditioner that works well for your hair type. Once your hair is dry, use a comb to part your hair down the middle, creating a clear divide for your curtain bangs.

Step 2: Prep your bangs

Before you start styling your curtain bangs, it's important to prep them properly. Use a heat protectant spray to protect your hair from heat damage, then use a round brush to blow-dry your bangs straight. This will help to smooth out any kinks or waves in your hair and create a sleek, polished look.

Step 3: Style your bangs

Once your bangs are prepped, it's time to style them. You can either use a straightener or a curling iron to create the perfect look for your curtain bangs. If you want a sleek, straight look, use a straightener to smooth out your bangs and create a polished finish. If you want a more tousled, textured look, use a curling iron to create loose waves in your bangs.

Step 4: Finish with hairspray

Once your curtain bangs are styled to perfection, finish off the look with a spritz of hairspray. This will help to keep your bangs in place and prevent them from falling flat throughout the day. Use a light-hold hairspray to avoid weighing down your hair or making it look greasy.

Tips for Making Your Curtain Bangs Look Good

Now that you know how to style your curtain bangs, here are some additional tips to help you make them look their best:

  • Trim your bangs regularly to keep them looking fresh and prevent split ends
  • Use a dry shampoo to help absorb excess oil and add volume to your hair
  • Experiment with different styling products, such as mousse or texturizing spray, to find what works best for your hair type
  • Consider adding some highlights or lowlights to your curtain bangs to add dimension and depth to your hair

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about curtain bangs:

How long do curtain bangs take to grow out?

Curtain bangs typically take around 3-4 months to grow out completely. However, you can still style them in different ways as they grow out to keep them looking fresh.

Can I style my curtain bangs without heat tools?

Absolutely! You can style your curtain bangs using a variety of different techniques, such as braiding them or pinning them back with bobby pins. Experiment with different styles to find what works best for you.

What face shape looks best with curtain bangs?

Curtain bangs can work with any face shape, but they're particularly flattering on oval or heart-shaped faces. If you have a round face, you may want to opt for a longer, more textured fringe to help balance out your features.

Hair Care for Curtain Bangs

Styling your curtain bangs is just one part of keeping them looking good. Here are some hair care tips to help you maintain healthy, shiny hair:

  • Wash your hair regularly to keep it clean and free of buildup
  • Avoid using hot tools on your hair every day to prevent damage
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair soft and hydrated
  • Avoid using harsh chemicals or dyes on your hair, as they can strip your hair of its natural oils and cause damage

With these tips and tricks, you'll be able to make your curtain bangs look good and keep them looking great for weeks to come. Have fun experimenting with different styles and techniques, and don't be afraid to try something new!
