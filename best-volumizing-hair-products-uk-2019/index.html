How to Achieve Voluminous Hair

Before we dive into the best volumizing hair products, you need to know how to achieve voluminous hair. Here are some tips:

  • Blow dry your hair upside down to create volume at the roots
  • Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner
  • Add volume with hair mousse or a volumizing spray
  • Tease your hair gently at the roots to create volume
  • Finish off with a volumizing hairspray to hold the style in place

Best Volumizing Hair Products UK 2019

Now that you know how to achieve voluminous hair, let's take a look at the best volumizing hair products in the UK for 2019:

1. Bumble and Bumble Thickening Shampoo

This shampoo is perfect for those with fine or thin hair as it adds volume and thickness to each strand. It contains panthenol, which helps to strengthen and protect hair while adding volume.

2. Oribe Maximista Thickening Spray

This thickening spray gives hair an instant boost of volume and thickness. It contains keratin amino acids, which help to strengthen hair and add shine.

3. Living Proof Full Dry Volume Blast

This dry volume spray adds instant volume and texture to hair. It contains a blend of flexible and rigid polymers that create a voluminous, textured look that lasts all day.

4. Redken Guts 10 Volume Spray Foam

This volumizing spray foam adds volume and fullness to hair. It contains a unique blend of filloxane and polymers that create a full-bodied look that lasts all day.

5. L'Oreal Elvive Fibrology Air Volumizing Dry Shampoo

This dry shampoo not only refreshes hair, but it also adds volume and fullness. It contains a blend of polymers and mineral powders that add texture and body to hair.

Tips for Voluminous Hair

Here are some additional tips for achieving voluminous hair:

  • Avoid using heavy hair products that weigh hair down
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle hair instead of a brush
  • Avoid washing hair every day as it can strip hair of natural oils
  • Use a clarifying shampoo once a week to remove product buildup
  • Get regular trims to keep hair healthy and prevent split ends

Frequently Asked Questions

What causes flat, lifeless hair?

Flat, lifeless hair can be caused by a variety of factors including genetics, hair type, and hair care habits. Using heavy hair products, over-washing hair, and using hot styling tools can also weigh hair down and make it look flat.

Can I use volumizing hair products on curly hair?

Absolutely! Volumizing hair products work on all hair types, including curly hair. Just be sure to use a product that is specifically designed for curly hair to avoid any unwanted frizz.

Can I achieve voluminous hair without using heat?

Yes, you can achieve voluminous hair without using heat. Try using a volumizing mousse or spray on damp hair and let it air dry for a natural, voluminous look.

Hair Care for Voluminous Hair

Here are some tips for taking care of voluminous hair:

  • Avoid using hot styling tools every day
  • Use a heat protectant spray before using hot tools
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep hair healthy and moisturized
  • Protect hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a UV protectant spray
  • Avoid using hair ties with metal clasps as they can damage hair

With the help of these best volumizing hair products in the UK for 2019 and these tips, you can achieve the voluminous hair of your dreams. Remember to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and looking its best.
