Does Short Haircut Suit Round Face: Your Ultimate Guide

If you have a round face, you might be wondering if a short haircut will suit you or not. Well, the answer is yes! Short haircuts can look great on women with round faces, but you have to choose the right style. In this article, we will discuss everything you need to know about short haircuts for round faces.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to Choose the Right Short Haircut for Round Face
  • Step-by-Step Guide to Get Short Haircut for Round Face
  • Tips to Maintain Short Haircut for Round Face
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care for Short Haircut for Round Face

Description

A round face has a circular shape with full cheeks and a rounded chin. If you have a round face, you can create an illusion of length and slimness by choosing the right haircut. Short haircuts can be a great option for round faces as they can help to elongate the face and draw attention to the eyes and cheekbones.

How to Choose the Right Short Haircut for Round Face

When choosing a short haircut for a round face, it's essential to consider your face shape and hair texture. Here are some tips to help you choose the right short haircut for a round face:

  • Choose a haircut that adds height to the crown of the head to elongate the face.
  • Go for a layered cut to add texture and volume to your hair.
  • Opt for a side-swept or deep side part to create an asymmetrical look.
  • Avoid blunt cuts that end at the chin level as they can make your face look wider.
  • Opt for short haircuts that are above the chin level, such as pixie cuts and bobs.

Step-by-Step Guide to Get Short Haircut for Round Face

If you have decided to get a short haircut, here is a step-by-step guide to help you:

  • Research different short haircuts that suit round faces.
  • Choose a hairstyle that matches your hair texture and face shape.
  • Show your hairstylist a picture of the hairstyle you want.
  • Discuss the length and layers that will suit your face shape.
  • Ask your hairstylist to create an asymmetrical look by adding a side-swept or deep side part.
  • Maintain your short haircut by trimming it every 4-6 weeks.

Tips to Maintain Short Haircut for Round Face

Here are some tips to help you maintain your short haircut:

  • Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to add texture and volume to your hair.
  • Blow-dry your hair using a round brush to add height to the crown of the head.
  • Use a texturizing spray or pomade to add definition and hold to your hair.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair as it can damage your hair.
  • Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or applying a leave-in conditioner with SPF.

FAQs

1. Can I get a short haircut if I have a round face?

Yes, you can get a short haircut if you have a round face. Just make sure to choose a hairstyle that elongates your face and adds height to the crown of the head.

2. Which short haircuts are suitable for round faces?

Short haircuts that add height to the crown of the head, such as pixie cuts and bobs, are suitable for round faces. Layered cuts and asymmetrical looks can also be great options.

3. How often should I trim my short haircut?

You should trim your short haircut every 4-6 weeks to maintain its shape and style.

Hair Care for Short Haircut for Round Face

Short haircuts require less maintenance compared to long hair, but you still need to take care of your hair. Here are some hair care tips:

  • Wash your hair every 2-3 days to avoid stripping natural oils from your hair.
  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and moisturized.
  • Avoid using too many styling products as they can weigh down your hair.
  • Massage your scalp regularly to improve blood circulation and promote hair growth.
  • Eat a balanced diet rich in protein, vitamins, and minerals to keep your hair healthy from within.

Now that you know everything about short haircuts for round faces, you can choose a hairstyle that suits you the best!
