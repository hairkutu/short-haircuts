Growing Out Short Thick Curly Hair A Step By Step Guide

Written by Oliver Apr 12, 2023 · 6 min read

Are you tired of your short, thick curly hair and looking to grow it out? Growing out short thick curly hair can be a challenging task, but with the right approach, it can be achieved. In this guide, we will provide you with step-by-step instructions, tips, and hair care advice to help you grow out your short thick curly hair.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to Grow Out Short Thick Curly Hair
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Growing Out Short Thick Curly Hair
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care for Short Thick Curly Hair

Description

Growing out short thick curly hair requires patience and the right approach. You need to be prepared for a lengthy transition period, during which your hair will go through different stages. The process can take several months or even years depending on your hair type and growth rate.

How to Grow Out Short Thick Curly Hair

Before you start growing out your hair, it’s essential to have a plan in place. Here are the steps you need to follow to grow out your short thick curly hair:

Step-by-Step Guide

  1. Get regular trims: Regular trims will help keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.
  2. Use the right products: Use products that are specifically designed for curly hair. Avoid products that contain sulfates and other harsh chemicals.
  3. Avoid heat styling: Heat styling can damage your hair and slow down the growth process. Avoid using heat styling tools as much as possible.
  4. Protect your hair at night: Use a silk or satin pillowcase to prevent your hair from getting tangled or frizzy while you sleep.
  5. Be patient: Growing out your hair takes time, so be patient and don’t give up.

Tips for Growing Out Short Thick Curly Hair

Here are some additional tips that will help you grow out your short thick curly hair:

  • Experiment with different hairstyles: Try out different hairstyles that work with your hair type and length.
  • Get a professional haircut: A professional haircut can help you transition from short to long hair more smoothly.
  • Eat a healthy diet: Eating a healthy diet rich in vitamins and minerals can help promote hair growth.
  • Stay hydrated: Drinking plenty of water will keep your hair and scalp healthy.
  • Avoid chemical treatments: Chemical treatments, such as perms or relaxers, can damage your hair and slow down the growth process.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about growing out short thick curly hair:

How long does it take to grow out short thick curly hair?

The process can take several months or even years depending on your hair type and growth rate.

Should I cut my hair while growing it out?

Yes, getting regular trims will help keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.

Can I use heat styling tools while growing out my hair?

Avoid using heat styling tools as much as possible as they can damage your hair and slow down the growth process.

Hair Care for Short Thick Curly Hair

Here are some tips for caring for your short thick curly hair:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it’s dry. Use a wide-toothed comb or your fingers to detangle your hair when it’s wet.
  • Avoid towel-drying your hair. Instead, use a microfiber towel or an old t-shirt to gently squeeze out the excess water.
  • Use a leave-in conditioner or a hair oil to keep your hair moisturized.
  • Avoid using too many products as they can weigh down your hair and make it look greasy.

Growing out short thick curly hair can be challenging, but with the right approach and patience, it can be achieved. Follow the steps and tips outlined in this guide, and you’ll be on your way to achieving long, healthy, and beautiful curly hair.
