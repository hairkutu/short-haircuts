Mens Short Haircut Styles A Guide For 2023

Written by Eveline Mar 22, 2023 Â· 7 min read

If you're looking for a fresh new look in 2023, a short haircut might be just the thing for you. Mens short haircut styles can range from classic to modern, and there are plenty of options to choose from. In this guide, we'll explore the different types of short haircuts for men, how to get them, and how to take care of your new style.

Types of Mens Short Haircut Styles

There are many different types of mens short haircut styles to choose from. Here are a few popular options:

Crew Cut

The crew cut is a classic short haircut that's been popular for decades. It's a simple, low-maintenance cut that works well for men of all ages.

Buzz Cut

The buzz cut is an even shorter haircut that's achieved with clippers. It's a great option for men who want a clean, minimalist look.

Short Textured Cut

The short textured cut is a modern style that features layers and texture. It's a great option for men with thick hair who want a stylish, low-maintenance look.

Side Part

The side part is a classic short haircut that's been popular for decades. It features a part on one side of the head and can be styled in a variety of ways.

Undercut

The undercut is a popular mens short haircut style that features short hair on the sides and longer hair on top. It's a great option for men who want a trendy, edgy look.

How to Get a Short Haircut

If you're ready to get a short haircut, here's what you need to do:

  1. Find a reputable barber or hairstylist who specializes in short haircuts.
  2. Bring a photo of the style you want, or be prepared to describe it in detail.
  3. Discuss any concerns or questions you have with your barber or hairstylist.
  4. Sit back and relax while your hair is cut.
  5. Pay attention to any styling tips your barber or hairstylist gives you.

Step-by-Step Guide to Cutting Short Hair

If you're interested in cutting your own short hair, here's a step-by-step guide:

  1. Gather your tools, including a pair of sharp scissors and clippers with guards.
  2. Start by trimming the sides and back of your head with the clippers.
  3. Use the scissors to trim the top of your hair to your desired length.
  4. Blend the top and sides of your hair together with the scissors.
  5. Use the clippers to create a clean line around your ears and at the nape of your neck.
  6. Style your hair as desired.

Tips for Maintaining Your Short Haircut

Here are a few tips for keeping your mens short haircut style looking its best:

  • Visit your barber or hairstylist for regular trims.
  • Use the right styling products for your hair type and style.
  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo.
  • Avoid using too much heat when styling your hair.
  • Consider using a scalp exfoliating treatment to keep your scalp healthy.

FAQ About Mens Short Haircut Styles

What is the most popular mens short haircut style?

The most popular mens short haircut style varies depending on current trends, but the crew cut and side part are perennial favorites.

Will a short haircut suit me?

A short haircut can work for men of all ages and face shapes, but it's important to choose a style that flatters your features.

How often should I get my short haircut trimmed?

You should visit your barber or hairstylist for a trim every 4-6 weeks to keep your short haircut looking its best.

Hair Care Tips for Short Hair

Here are a few tips for taking care of your short hair:

  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner to prevent dryness and breakage.
  • Avoid over-washing your hair, as this can strip it of its natural oils.
  • Use a leave-in conditioner or hair oil to keep your hair moisturized.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and chlorine by wearing a hat or using a swim cap.
  • Avoid using too much product, as this can weigh down your hair and make it look greasy.

With the right mens short haircut style and proper hair care, you can look and feel your best in 2023. Whether you prefer a classic crew cut or a trendy undercut, there's a short haircut that's perfect for you.
