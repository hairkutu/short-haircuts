How Do I Revive My Synthetic Wig

Written by Daniel Jun 18, 2023 Â· 7 min read

If you are a wig wearer, you may have encountered the issue of your synthetic wig losing its shine and looking dull. The good news is that there are ways to revive your synthetic wig and bring it back to life. In this blog post, we will go over how to revive your synthetic wig, step by step, to help you achieve the best results.

Description

Synthetic wigs are a popular choice for many people due to their affordability and low maintenance. However, over time, synthetic wigs can lose their shine and become frizzy, making them look dull and lifeless. Reviving your synthetic wig can help restore its shine and bring it back to life.

How to Revive Your Synthetic Wig

Step 1: Detangle Your Wig

The first step to reviving your synthetic wig is to detangle it. Use a wide-tooth comb or a wig brush to gently comb through your wig, starting from the ends and working your way up to the roots. Be gentle and avoid pulling or tugging on the hair, as this can cause damage to the fibers.

Step 2: Wash Your Wig

After detangling your wig, it's time to wash it. Fill a sink or basin with cool water and add a small amount of wig shampoo. Gently swish the wig around in the water, being careful not to rub or agitate the hair. Rinse the wig thoroughly with cool water to remove all of the shampoo.

Step 3: Condition Your Wig

Once your wig is clean, it's time to condition it. Apply a small amount of wig conditioner to the hair, avoiding the roots. Leave the conditioner in for a few minutes, then rinse it out with cool water.

Step 4: Dry Your Wig

To dry your wig, gently blot it with a towel to remove excess water. Avoid rubbing or twisting the hair, as this can cause damage. Place the wig on a wig stand or mannequin head to air dry. Avoid using heat tools to dry your wig, as this can cause damage to the fibers.

Tips for Reviving Your Synthetic Wig

Here are some tips to help you achieve the best results when reviving your synthetic wig:

  • Use a wig brush or wide-tooth comb to detangle your wig
  • Wash your wig in cool water to avoid damaging the fibers
  • Avoid using hot tools on your wig, as this can cause damage to the fibers
  • Store your wig on a wig stand or mannequin head to help it maintain its shape

FAQ

How often should I revive my synthetic wig?

It's recommended to revive your synthetic wig every 4-6 weeks, or as needed. This will help keep your wig looking its best and ensure that it lasts as long as possible.

Can I revive my synthetic wig if it's already damaged?

If your synthetic wig is already damaged, it may not be possible to fully revive it. However, following the steps outlined in this blog post can help improve the overall appearance of your wig and extend its lifespan.

Hair Care for Synthetic Wigs

Proper hair care is essential to keeping your synthetic wig looking its best. Here are some tips to help you care for your wig:

  • Use a wig brush or wide-tooth comb to detangle your wig
  • Wash your wig in cool water using wig shampoo and conditioner
  • Avoid using hot tools on your wig, as this can cause damage to the fibers
  • Store your wig on a wig stand or mannequin head to help it maintain its shape
  • Avoid exposing your wig to excessive heat or sunlight

By following these tips and properly caring for your synthetic wig, you can help ensure that it looks its best and lasts as long as possible.
