How To Identify Diamond Face Shape A Complete Guide

Written by William Jun 24, 2023 · 5 min read

If you’re looking to change up your hairstyle or trying to find the perfect pair of sunglasses, it’s important to know your face shape. One of the less common face shapes is the diamond face shape. In this blog post, we’ll show you how to identify diamond face shape and give you tips on how to flatter your unique features.

How to Identify Diamond Face Shape

It’s easy to confuse diamond face shape with other face shapes like oval or heart. Here’s how to tell if you have a diamond face shape:

Step 1: Measure Your Face

Take a measuring tape and measure the width of your forehead, cheekbones, and jawline. Write down each measurement.

Step 2: Calculate Ratios

Take the measurement of your cheekbones and divide it by the measurement of your forehead. Then, take the measurement of your jawline and divide it by the measurement of your cheekbones. If both ratios are greater than 1.5, you have a diamond face shape.

Tips for Flattering Diamond Face Shape

Now that you know how to identify diamond face shape, let’s talk about how to flatter it:

Tip 1: Play up Your Cheekbones

Diamond face shapes have high cheekbones, so play them up with a blush or bronzer. Apply the product to the apples of your cheeks and blend it towards your temples.

Tip 2: Soften Your Jawline

If you have a sharp jawline, try softening it with a wavy or curly hairstyle. Soft layers can also help to create a more feminine look.

Tip 3: Experiment with Bangs

Side-swept bangs can help to balance out a diamond face shape by creating the illusion of a wider forehead.

Tip 4: Try Cat-Eye Sunglasses

Cat-eye sunglasses are a great choice for diamond face shapes because they mimic the shape of your cheekbones.

FAQs

What hairstyles should diamond face shapes avoid?

Diamond face shapes should avoid hairstyles that add volume at the crown or jawline, as this can make the face look wider.

Can diamond face shapes wear short hair?

Absolutely! Diamond face shapes can rock a pixie cut or bob, as long as the hair is styled to add volume on top and soften the jawline.

What celebrities have diamond face shapes?

Celebrities with diamond face shapes include Rihanna, Keira Knightley, and Jennifer Lopez.

Hair Care for Diamond Face Shapes

When it comes to hair care, diamond face shapes should focus on keeping their hair healthy and shiny. Use a nourishing shampoo and conditioner, and avoid products that weigh down the hair. Regular trims can also help to maintain the shape of your hairstyle.

Now that you know how to identify diamond face shape, it’s time to experiment with new hairstyles and accessories. Remember, the key is to play up your unique features and have fun!
