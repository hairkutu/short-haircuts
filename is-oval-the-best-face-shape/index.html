Is Oval The Best Face Shape

Description

There are seven basic face shapes: oval, round, square, heart, diamond, oblong, and triangle. Among these, oval is considered to be the most ideal face shape. The reason for this is that an oval face shape is proportionate and versatile, making it easy to style and flatter with different hairstyles and makeup looks.

How to Determine Your Face Shape

To determine your face shape, you need to have a clear view of your face without any hair or accessories that may obscure your features. You can do this by pulling your hair back and standing in front of a mirror in a well-lit room.

Step 1:

Measure the width of your forehead, cheekbones, and jawline using a tape measure or ruler.

Step 2:

Compare the measurements to determine the widest part of your face.

Step 3:

Look at the shape of your face from the front.

Step by Step Guide to Determine Your Face Shape

Here is a step-by-step guide to help you determine your face shape:

Oval Face Shape:

If the width of your forehead, cheekbones, and jawline are approximately equal and the shape of your face is slightly longer than it is wide, you have an oval face shape.

Round Face Shape:

If the width of your forehead, cheekbones, and jawline are approximately equal and the shape of your face is as wide as it is long, you have a round face shape.

Square Face Shape:

If the width of your forehead, cheekbones, and jawline are approximately equal and the shape of your face is angular with a prominent jawline, you have a square face shape.

Heart Face Shape:

If the width of your forehead is wider than your cheekbones and jawline, and your chin is pointed, you have a heart face shape.

Diamond Face Shape:

If the width of your cheekbones is wider than your forehead and jawline, and your chin is pointed, you have a diamond face shape.

Oblong Face Shape:

If the width of your forehead, cheekbones, and jawline are approximately equal, but your face is longer than it is wide, you have an oblong face shape.

Triangle Face Shape:

If the width of your jawline is wider than your forehead and cheekbones, you have a triangle face shape.

Tips for Choosing the Right Hairstyle for Oval Face Shape

Here are some tips for choosing the right hairstyle for oval face shape:

Tip 1:

Choose a hairstyle that flatters your face shape.

Tip 2:

Consider your hair texture and thickness when choosing a hairstyle.

Tip 3:

Experiment with different lengths and styles, such as layers, bangs, and updos.

Tip 4:

Avoid hairstyles that add volume to the sides of your face, as this can make your face appear wider.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Is oval the best face shape?

A: Yes, oval is considered the best face shape because it is proportionate and versatile.

Q: Can oval face shape wear any hairstyle?

A: Yes, oval face shape can wear almost any hairstyle.

Q: What are some hairstyles that flatter oval face shape?

A: Some hairstyles that flatter oval face shape include long layers, blunt bob, side-swept bangs, and messy bun.

Hair Care Tips for Oval Face Shape

Here are some hair care tips for oval face shape:

Tip 1:

Maintain a healthy hair care routine, including regular washing, conditioning, and trimming.

Tip 2:

Protect your hair from heat damage by using heat protectant products and avoiding excessive heat styling.

Tip 3:

Eat a balanced diet and stay hydrated to promote healthy hair growth.

Tip 4:

Use hair accessories, such as headbands and clips, to add variety to your hairstyles.

Tip 5:

Consult with a professional hairstylist for personalized hair care and styling advice.
