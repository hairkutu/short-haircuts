How To Cut The Back Of Your Short Hair Straight A Step By Step Guide

Written by Daniel Jun 16, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Short haircuts have been in trend for quite some time now, and they are here to stay. While it is easy to style short hair, cutting the back of your short hair straight can be a bit challenging. However, with the right tools and techniques, you can achieve a flawless cut at home. In this blog post, we will guide you on how to cut the back of your short hair straight in easy steps.

Description

When it comes to cutting the back of your short hair, it is essential to have a clear understanding of what you want to achieve. Cutting short hair straight requires precision and patience. You also need to have the right tools, such as a sharp pair of scissors and a comb, to ensure a perfect cut.

How To

Here is a step-by-step guide on how to cut the back of your short hair straight:

Step 1: Wash your hair

Before you start cutting your hair, ensure that it is clean and tangle-free. Wash your hair and dry it thoroughly with a towel or a hairdryer.

Step 2: Section your hair

Using a comb, section your hair from the crown of your head to the nape of your neck. Clip the top half of your hair to the front to avoid cutting it accidentally.

Step 3: Cut the back of your hair

Using your comb, comb your hair downwards, starting from the center of your head. Hold your hair with your fingers and cut it straight across. Be careful not to cut too much at once. Repeat this process on each section of your hair until you have cut the back of your hair straight.

Step 4: Check for any unevenness

Once you have finished cutting the back of your hair, check for any unevenness. Use your comb to ensure that the hair is straight and even.

Step 5: Trim any stray hairs

If you notice any stray hairs, trim them with your scissors. Be careful not to cut too much hair in the process.

Step 6: Style your hair

Once you have finished cutting your hair, style it as desired. You can use a hair straightener or a curling iron to create your desired look.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you achieve a perfect cut:

  • Use sharp scissors to avoid split ends and uneven cuts.
  • Ensure that your hair is completely dry before you start cutting it.
  • Start cutting small sections of your hair to avoid cutting too much at once.
  • Invest in a good quality comb to ensure that your hair is evenly combed.
  • Take your time and be patient when cutting your hair to avoid any mistakes.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about cutting the back of short hair straight:

Can I cut the back of my short hair straight at home?

Yes, you can cut the back of your short hair straight at home with the right tools and techniques.

How often should I cut the back of my short hair?

You should cut the back of your short hair every 4-6 weeks to maintain its shape and style.

What should I do if I make a mistake while cutting my hair?

If you make a mistake while cutting your hair, don't panic. You can always trim the hair to even it out. If the mistake is significant, you may need to seek the help of a professional hairstylist.

Hair Care

To keep your hair healthy and strong, it is essential to take good care of it. Here are some tips to keep your hair in good condition:

  • Use a good quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair clean and nourished.
  • Avoid using too many styling products as they can damage your hair.
  • Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a UV protection spray.
  • Avoid using hot tools on your hair too often as they can cause damage and split ends.
  • Keep your hair hydrated by drinking plenty of water and eating a healthy diet.

Now that you know how to cut the back of your short hair straight, you can achieve a flawless cut at home. Remember to take your time and follow the steps carefully to avoid any mistakes. With a little patience and practice, you can become an expert at cutting your hair.
