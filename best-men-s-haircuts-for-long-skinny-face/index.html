Best Men s Haircuts For Long Skinny Face

Written by Oliver Mar 17, 2023 Â· 7 min read

If you have a long and skinny face, you might find it challenging to find the right haircut that suits your face shape. However, with the right knowledge and guidance, you can easily find the best men's haircut for your long and skinny face. In this article, we will discuss the best haircuts for men with a long and skinny face, along with some tips and hair care advice.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to
  • Step by step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

A long and skinny face has a narrow and elongated shape, and the goal of a good haircut for this face shape is to create volume and width to balance out the face. The right haircut can make a significant difference in how your face looks, and it can also enhance your overall appearance.

How to

The first step in finding the best men's haircut for a long and skinny face is to determine your face shape. You can do this by measuring the length and width of your face and comparing them to the ideal face shape, which is an oval shape. Once you have determined your face shape, you can start looking for haircuts that suit your face shape.

Step by step

Here are some of the best men's haircuts for a long and skinny face:

1. Side-parted pompadour

This haircut features short sides and a longer top that is styled into a pompadour. The side-parting adds volume and width to the face, making it look more balanced. This haircut is perfect for men with a long and skinny face.

2. Buzz cut

The buzz cut is a popular haircut for men with a long and skinny face. It is a low maintenance haircut that looks great on most face shapes. The buzz cut can also make your face look more proportionate by creating a uniform length all over the head.

3. Textured crop

The textured crop is a versatile haircut that works well for men with a long and skinny face. It features short sides and a textured top that is styled with a messy look. This haircut adds volume and texture to the hair, making it look thicker and fuller.

4. Faux hawk

The faux hawk is another great haircut for men with a long and skinny face. It features short sides and a longer top that is styled into a faux hawk. This haircut creates volume and height, making your face look more balanced.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when choosing the best men's haircut for a long and skinny face:

  • Avoid haircuts with sharp angles and straight lines, as they can make your face look even longer.
  • Opt for haircuts with volume and width, as they can balance out your face shape.
  • Consider getting a beard or stubble, as it can add width to your face.
  • Consult with your hairstylist to find the best haircut for your face shape.

FAQ

What is the ideal face shape for men?

The ideal face shape for men is an oval shape.

How can I determine my face shape?

You can determine your face shape by measuring the length and width of your face and comparing them to the ideal face shape.

Hair Care

Here are some tips to keep your hair looking its best:

  • Wash your hair regularly to keep it clean and healthy.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as it can damage it.
  • Use a conditioner to keep your hair moisturized.
  • Get regular haircuts to keep your hair looking neat and tidy.

In conclusion, finding the best men's haircut for a long and skinny face is all about creating volume and width to balance out the face. With the right haircut and hair care, you can enhance your overall appearance and feel more confident about your look.
