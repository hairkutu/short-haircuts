Best Haircut for Narrow Face Male - The Ultimate Guide

If you have a narrow face, it can be a bit challenging to find the right haircut. You want something that will make your face look fuller and more proportional. In this guide, we'll take a look at the best hairstyles for narrow face male, how to achieve them, and some tips on hair care.

Table of Contents

  • How to Determine if You Have a Narrow Face
  • Best Haircut for Narrow Face Male
  • Step by Step Guide to Achieving the Best Haircut
  • Tips for Maintaining Your Haircut
  • FAQs

How to Determine if You Have a Narrow Face

Before we dive into the best haircuts for narrow face male, let's first determine if you have a narrow face. A narrow face is typically characterized by a forehead, cheekbones, and jawline that are all roughly the same width. People with narrow faces also tend to have a high forehead and a pointed chin.

If you're still not sure if you have a narrow face, try standing in front of a mirror and measuring your face. Use a tape measure to measure the width of your forehead, cheekbones, and jawline. If they're all roughly the same width, then you have a narrow face.

Best Haircut for Narrow Face Male

The best haircut for narrow face male is one that adds volume and width to the face. Here are some of the best haircuts to consider:

1. Side Part Hairstyle

The side part hairstyle is a classic look that works well for narrow face male. This style involves parting your hair on one side and combing it over to the other side. This adds volume to one side of the face, making it appear wider.

2. Quiff Hairstyle

The quiff hairstyle is another great option for narrow face male. This style involves sweeping the hair up and back, creating height and volume on top of the head. This helps to balance out the narrowness of the face.

3. Textured Crop Hairstyle

The textured crop hairstyle is a modern and trendy look that works well for narrow face male. This style involves cutting the hair short on the sides and back, while leaving it longer on top. The hair on top is then styled with a textured, messy look, which adds volume and width to the face.

Step by Step Guide to Achieving the Best Haircut

Now that you know which haircuts work best for narrow face male, let's take a look at how to achieve them.

1. Choose the Right Haircut

Start by choosing the right haircut for your face shape. Consult with your hair stylist to find a style that works well for your hair type and face shape.

2. Wash Your Hair

Wash your hair thoroughly before getting a haircut. This will remove any product buildup and ensure that your hair is clean and healthy.

3. Get the Haircut

Once you've chosen your haircut, it's time to get it. Make sure to communicate clearly with your hair stylist to ensure that they achieve the look you want.

4. Style Your Hair

Once you have your new haircut, it's time to style it. Follow the instructions of your hair stylist for the best way to style your hair at home.

Tips for Maintaining Your Haircut

Here are some tips to help you maintain your new haircut:

1. Use the Right Hair Products

Use hair products that are designed for your hair type and the style you're trying to achieve. This will help keep your hair looking its best.

2. Don't Overwash Your Hair

Don't wash your hair too often, as this can strip it of its natural oils and cause damage. Instead, aim to wash your hair every other day or every few days.

3. Get Regular Haircuts

Get regular haircuts to maintain the shape and style of your haircut. This will also help keep your hair healthy.

FAQs

What if I'm not sure which haircut is right for me?

If you're not sure which haircut is right for you, consult with your hair stylist. They'll be able to recommend a style that works well for your face shape and hair type.

How often should I get a haircut?

You should get a haircut every 4-6 weeks to maintain the shape and style of your haircut.

Can I still style my hair if it's thin?

Yes, you can still style your hair if it's thin. Just make sure to use hair products that add volume and texture to your hair.

In conclusion, the best haircut for narrow face male is one that adds volume and width to the face. Choose a style that works well for your hair type and face shape, and make sure to maintain it with regular haircuts and proper hair care.
