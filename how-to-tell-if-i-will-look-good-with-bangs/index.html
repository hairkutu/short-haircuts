How To Tell If I Will Look Good With Bangs

Written by Frank May 07, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Are you thinking about getting bangs but not sure if they will look good on you? Bangs can be a great way to change up your look but it's important to make sure they are the right fit for your face shape and hair type. In this article, we will discuss how to tell if you will look good with bangs and provide some tips on how to care for them.

Table of Contents:

  • Determining Your Face Shape
  • Considering Your Hair Type
  • Trying on Different Styles
  • Asking Your Stylist
  • Tips for Maintaining Bangs
  • Frequently Asked Questions

Determining Your Face Shape

The first step in determining if you will look good with bangs is to determine your face shape. The most common face shapes are round, oval, square, heart, and diamond. Each face shape has different features that can be accentuated with the right type of bangs. For example, if you have a round face, you may want to consider side-swept bangs to elongate your face. If you have a square face, you may want to consider soft, wispy bangs to soften your features.

Considering Your Hair Type

Another important factor to consider when getting bangs is your hair type. Different hair types require different types of bangs. For example, if you have thin hair, you may want to avoid thick, heavy bangs as they can weigh down your hair. If you have curly hair, you may want to consider longer, side-swept bangs that blend in with your curls.

Trying on Different Styles

Before committing to bangs, it's a good idea to try on different styles to see what looks best on you. You can try on different styles using virtual makeover tools or by simply pinning your hair back to create the illusion of bangs. This will give you a better idea of what bangs will look like on you and help you make an informed decision.

Asking Your Stylist

Your stylist is an expert when it comes to hair and can provide valuable advice on whether or not bangs are right for you. They can also help you determine the best type of bangs for your face shape and hair type. Be sure to bring in pictures of styles you like to help guide the conversation.

Tips for Maintaining Bangs

Once you've decided to get bangs, it's important to maintain them properly to keep them looking their best. Some tips for maintaining bangs include:

  • Wash and blow-dry your bangs regularly to keep them looking fresh.
  • Use a round brush to blow-dry your bangs to create volume and shape.
  • Trim your bangs regularly to prevent them from getting too long.
  • Use a dry shampoo to absorb excess oil and keep your bangs looking clean.

Frequently Asked Questions

1. Will bangs work with my curly hair?

Yes, bangs can work with curly hair. It's important to choose the right type of bangs that will blend in with your curls and not create too much volume.

2. Can I still wear my hair in a ponytail with bangs?

Yes, you can still wear your hair in a ponytail with bangs. Simply pull your bangs back with bobby pins or a headband.

3. How often should I trim my bangs?

It's recommended to trim your bangs every 4-6 weeks to keep them looking their best.

4. Can bangs be styled different ways?

Yes, bangs can be styled in different ways. You can wear them straight down, swept to the side, or even pinned back.

In conclusion, getting bangs can be a great way to change up your look but it's important to make sure they are the right fit for your face shape and hair type. By considering these factors, trying on different styles, and consulting with your stylist, you can determine if bangs are right for you. With proper maintenance, you can keep your bangs looking their best and enjoy your new look!
