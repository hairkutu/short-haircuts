How Do You Know If Short Hair Looks Good On You

Written by Petter Apr 08, 2023 · 9 min read

Short hair is a bold and daring move, and it can be difficult to know if it will suit you. Before you take the plunge, there are a few things you should consider to ensure that short hair will look good on you. In this article, we’ll explore how to know if short hair looks good on you, and provide some tips for maintaining your new shorter locks.

Table of Contents

  • How to Know If Short Hair Looks Good on You
  • Step-by-Step Guide to Deciding on Short Hair
  • Tips for Maintaining Short Hair
  • Frequently Asked Questions
  • Hair Care for Short Hair

How to Know If Short Hair Looks Good on You

Short hair can be a great way to change up your look and try something new. However, it’s important to consider your face shape, hair texture, and personal style before making the chop. Here are a few things to keep in mind when deciding if short hair will look good on you:

Face Shape

Your face shape is one of the most important factors to consider when deciding on a short hairstyle. Some short haircuts may flatter certain face shapes better than others. For example, a pixie cut can look great on someone with a heart-shaped face, while a bob may be more flattering on someone with a round face.

Hair Texture

Another important factor to consider is your hair texture. Short hair can be a great option for those with fine or thin hair, as it can make hair appear fuller and thicker. However, if you have thick or curly hair, you may need to spend more time styling and maintaining your shorter locks.

Personal Style

Your personal style is also an important factor to consider when deciding on a short hairstyle. Short hair can be edgy, sophisticated, or playful, depending on the cut and styling. Consider your personal style and choose a short hairstyle that complements it.

Step-by-Step Guide to Deciding on Short Hair

If you’re still not sure if short hair will look good on you, follow these steps to help you make a decision:

Step 1: Research Short Hairstyles

Start by browsing magazines, social media, and hair salons to get an idea of the different short hairstyles that are out there. Take note of any styles that catch your eye, and consider how they would look on you.

Step 2: Consider Your Face Shape

Next, consider your face shape and which short hairstyles will flatter it. Heart-shaped faces may look great with a pixie cut, while oval faces may be better suited for a bob.

Step 3: Think About Your Hair Texture

Consider your hair texture and how it will work with a shorter cut. Fine or thin hair may benefit from a short cut, while thick or curly hair may require more maintenance and styling.

Step 4: Consult with a Stylist

If you’re still not sure, consult with a stylist. They can offer advice on which short hairstyles will suit you best, and help you choose a cut that complements your face shape and hair texture.

Tips for Maintaining Short Hair

Once you’ve decided to go for a short haircut, it’s important to maintain it properly. Here are a few tips for keeping your short locks looking great:

Tip 1: Invest in High-Quality Products

Short hair requires regular maintenance, so it’s important to invest in high-quality products that will keep your hair healthy and looking great. Look for products that are specifically designed for short hair, and avoid using too much product, as this can weigh hair down.

Tip 2: Visit the Salon Regularly

Short hair requires more frequent visits to the salon, as it grows out more quickly than longer hair. Schedule regular appointments with your stylist to keep your short haircut looking fresh.

Tip 3: Experiment with Styling

Short hair is versatile, and there are many different ways to style it. Experiment with different products and techniques to find a style that works for you.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Will short hair look good on me if I have a round face?

A: Yes, short hair can look great on someone with a round face. Consider a bob or a lob that falls just below the chin to create the illusion of length.

Q: How do I know if my hair texture is suitable for a short haircut?

A: Short hair can work for any hair texture, but it’s important to consider the maintenance required. If you have thick or curly hair, you may need to spend more time styling and maintaining your shorter locks.

Hair Care for Short Hair

Short hair requires regular maintenance to keep it looking great. Here are a few tips for caring for your shorter locks:

Tip 1: Wash Your Hair Regularly

Short hair can become greasy more quickly than longer hair, so it’s important to wash it regularly to prevent buildup.

Tip 2: Use a Lightweight Conditioner

Short hair can benefit from a lightweight conditioner that won’t weigh hair down. Look for a conditioner that is specifically designed for short hair.

Tip 3: Protect Your Hair from Heat Damage

Short hair is more susceptible to heat damage, so it’s important to protect it from heat styling tools. Use a heat protectant spray before using hot tools, and avoid using them too often.

In conclusion, short hair can be a great way to change up your look and try something new. Consider your face shape, hair texture, and personal style before deciding on a short haircut, and maintain it properly to keep it looking great.
