How To Cut Top Of Men s Hair With Scissors At Home A Step By Step Guide

Written by Eveline Mar 22, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Are you tired of your shaggy hair during this pandemic? Why not cut your own hair at home? Cutting your hair at home can save you a lot of time and money. In this blog post, we will guide you on how to cut top of men's hair with scissors at home. We will also provide you with some tips and hair care advice.

How to

Before we start, make sure you have the right tools. You will need a pair of sharp scissors, a comb, and a mirror.

Step 1: Wash Your Hair

It's important to wash your hair before cutting it. This will help remove any dirt or oil that may interfere with your haircut.

Step 2: Comb Your Hair

Next, comb your hair to remove any tangles or knots. This will help you achieve a more even cut.

Step 3: Section Your Hair

Section your hair by using a comb to create a center part. Then, divide your hair into two sections, one on each side. Use hair clips to secure each section.

Step 4: Cut the Top of Your Hair

Starting from the front, cut the top of your hair using scissors. Use the comb as a guide and cut the hair to the desired length. Be careful not to cut too much at once.

Step 5: Blend the Sides

Next, blend the sides of your hair with the top. Use scissors to cut the hair in a downward motion, blending the sides into the top.

Step 6: Check Your Work

After cutting your hair, check your work in the mirror. Make any necessary adjustments to achieve the desired look.

Step 7: Style Your Hair

Finally, style your hair as desired. Use hair gel or wax to give your hair a finished look.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you achieve the perfect haircut:

  • Start with small cuts and work your way up to avoid cutting too much at once.
  • Use a comb as a guide to ensure even cuts.
  • Take breaks to avoid fatigue and to check your progress.
  • Invest in good quality scissors to ensure a clean cut.
  • Ask a friend or family member to help if you're not comfortable cutting your hair alone.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about cutting men's hair at home:

Can I cut my own hair with kitchen scissors?

It's not recommended to use kitchen scissors to cut your hair. They may not be sharp enough and can cause uneven cuts. Invest in a pair of good quality scissors for best results.

How often should I cut my hair?

This depends on how fast your hair grows and how often you want to maintain your desired haircut. On average, men should cut their hair every 4-6 weeks.

Is it easy to cut your own hair?

It may take some practice to get the hang of cutting your own hair, but with some patience and the right tools, it can be done.

Hair Care

Proper hair care is important to maintain healthy hair. Here are some tips to keep your hair looking its best:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a mild shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using hot water as it can dry out your scalp and hair.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.
  • Avoid using too much heat when styling your hair as it can damage your hair.
  • Eat a balanced diet and stay hydrated to promote healthy hair growth.

Now that you know how to cut top of men's hair with scissors at home, you can save time and money by doing it yourself. Remember to take your time and follow the steps carefully. Happy cutting!
