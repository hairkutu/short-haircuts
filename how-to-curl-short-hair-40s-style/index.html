How To Curl Short Hair 40S Style

Written by Bardi Jul 04, 2023 Â· 6 min read

If you're looking for a vintage-inspired hairstyle, why not try curling your short hair 40s style? This look was popular during the 1940s and can add a touch of glamour to your everyday look. In this article, we'll show you how to curl short hair 40s style, step-by-step.

Description

The 40s style is characterized by soft, voluminous curls that frame the face. This style was often worn by Hollywood starlets and was a popular choice for special occasions.

How to Curl Short Hair 40s Style

Now, let's get started with the step-by-step process of curling short hair 40s style:

Step 1: Prep Your Hair

Start by washing and conditioning your hair. Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to add some bounce to your hair. Towel dry your hair and apply a heat protectant spray to prevent damage from the curling iron.

Step 2: Section Your Hair

Divide your hair into small sections using hair clips. This will make it easier to curl each section and ensure that your curls are uniform in size.

Step 3: Curl Your Hair

Take a small section of hair and wrap it around a curling iron. Hold the curling iron in place for a few seconds and then release the curl. Repeat this process for each section of hair until your entire head is curled.

Step 4: Comb Out Your Curls

Use a wide-tooth comb to gently comb out your curls. This will create soft, voluminous waves that are typical of the 40s style.

Step 5: Set Your Curls

Spray your hair with a light-hold hairspray to set your curls in place. You can also use bobby pins to hold any loose curls in place.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you achieve the perfect 40s style curls:

  • Use a curling iron with a small barrel to create tight curls.
  • Don't curl your hair too tightly, as this can create ringlets instead of soft waves.
  • Use a heat protectant spray to prevent damage to your hair.
  • Don't forget to comb out your curls to create soft waves.
  • Use a light-hold hairspray to set your curls without making them stiff or crunchy.

FAQ

Q: Can I curl my short hair without a curling iron?

A: Yes, you can curl your short hair using hot rollers or flexi rods. These tools can create soft, voluminous curls that are perfect for the 40s style.

Q: How do I maintain my 40s-style curls?

A: To maintain your curls, sleep with your hair in a loose bun or braid. In the morning, use a wide-tooth comb to gently comb out any tangles or knots.

Hair Care

To keep your hair healthy and strong, use a moisturizing hair mask once a week. This will help to nourish your hair and prevent breakage. Also, avoid using hot tools on your hair every day, as this can cause damage and breakage.

Now that you know how to curl short hair 40s style, you can add a touch of vintage glamour to your look. Follow these steps, and you'll be ready to step out in style!
