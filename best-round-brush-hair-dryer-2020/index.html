Description

A round brush hair dryer is a two-in-one styling tool that combines a round brush and a hair dryer in one device. It is designed to give you a salon-quality blowout at home while minimizing hair damage and reducing drying time. The best round brush hair dryers in 2020 are equipped with advanced features such as ceramic or tourmaline technology, multiple heat and speed settings, and ionic technology that helps reduce frizz and static.

How to Use a Round Brush Hair Dryer

Using a round brush hair dryer is easier than you might think. Here are the basic steps:

Step 1: Wash Your Hair

Start with clean, damp hair. Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner to avoid damaging your hair.

Step 2: Apply Heat Protectant

Apply a heat protectant spray or cream to your hair to minimize heat damage.

Step 3: Section Your Hair

Divide your hair into sections using clips. This will make it easier to dry and style your hair.

Step 4: Start Drying Your Hair

Start with the bottom section of your hair and work your way up. Use the round brush to lift your hair at the roots and direct the airflow from the hair dryer downwards. Roll the brush downwards while drying your hair to create curls or waves.

Step 5: Repeat

Repeat this process for each section of your hair until it is completely dry.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here is a step-by-step guide to using a round brush hair dryer:

Step 1: Choose the Right Brush Size

Choose a brush size that is appropriate for your hair length and desired style.

Step 2: Adjust the Heat and Speed Settings

Adjust the heat and speed settings on your hair dryer to suit your hair type and desired style.

Step 3: Apply Heat Protectant

Apply a heat protectant spray or cream to your hair to minimize heat damage.

Step 4: Section Your Hair

Divide your hair into sections using clips. This will make it easier to dry and style your hair.

Step 5: Start Drying Your Hair

Start with the bottom section of your hair and work your way up. Use the round brush to lift your hair at the roots and direct the airflow from the hair dryer downwards. Roll the brush downwards while drying your hair to create curls or waves.

Step 6: Repeat

Repeat this process for each section of your hair until it is completely dry.

Tips for Using a Round Brush Hair Dryer

Here are some tips to help you get the most out of your round brush hair dryer:

  • Use a heat protectant spray or cream to minimize heat damage.
  • Divide your hair into sections to make it easier to dry and style.
  • Use a brush size that is appropriate for your hair length and desired style.
  • Adjust the heat and speed settings on your hair dryer to suit your hair type and desired style.
  • Roll the brush downwards while drying your hair to create curls or waves.

FAQs

Q: Can I use a round brush hair dryer on wet hair?

A: No, it is not recommended to use a round brush hair dryer on wet hair. Make sure your hair is damp before using the dryer.

Q: Can a round brush hair dryer damage my hair?

A: Yes, if used improperly, a round brush hair dryer can damage your hair. Use a heat protectant spray or cream and avoid using the highest heat setting.

Q: How often should I clean my round brush hair dryer?

A: You should clean your round brush hair dryer after every use to prevent buildup of hair and product residue.

Hair Care Tips

Here are some hair care tips to keep your hair healthy and beautiful:

  • Avoid using hot tools on a daily basis to minimize heat damage.
  • Use a deep-conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair moisturized.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.
  • Eat a healthy diet that includes plenty of vitamins and minerals to promote hair growth and health.
  • Avoid using harsh chemicals on your hair, such as bleach or relaxers.
