Short Haircut For Oval Face Wavy Hair A Complete Guide

Are you an oval-faced beauty with wavy hair? Do you want to switch up your look with a short haircut? Look no further! In this article, we will guide you through the process of choosing the right short haircut for your face shape and hair type, as well as provide tips for hair care and maintenance.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How To Choose the Right Short Haircut
  • Step-by-Step Guide to Cutting Your Hair
  • Tips for Hair Care and Maintenance
  • FAQ

Description

Short haircuts are a great way to switch up your look and make a bold statement. However, choosing the right short haircut can be tricky, especially if you have an oval face shape and wavy hair. An oval face shape is versatile and can pull off almost any haircut, but you want to make sure that the haircut complements your face shape and enhances your natural features.

Wavy hair adds volume and texture to your hair, making it a great choice for short haircuts. However, wavy hair can be difficult to manage and style, especially if you have a busy lifestyle. With the right short haircut and hair care routine, you can showcase your wavy locks and look fabulous every day.

How To Choose the Right Short Haircut

When choosing a short haircut for oval face wavy hair, you want to consider the following factors:

Face Shape

As an oval-faced beauty, you have a versatile face shape that can pull off almost any short haircut. However, you want to choose a haircut that complements your natural features and enhances your face shape. A short haircut that adds volume and texture to your hair can accentuate your cheekbones and jawline, making your face appear more defined.

Hair Texture

Wavy hair adds volume and texture to your hair, making it a great choice for short haircuts. However, you want to choose a haircut that works well with your natural hair texture. A layered haircut can help enhance your natural waves and add movement to your hair, while a blunt cut can make your hair appear thicker and more voluminous.

Lifestyle

When choosing a short haircut, you want to consider your lifestyle and how much time you have to style your hair. If you have a busy lifestyle, you may want to opt for a low-maintenance haircut that requires little styling. A pixie cut or a bob with layers can be a great choice for busy women who want to look fabulous without spending too much time on their hair.

Step-by-Step Guide to Cutting Your Hair

If you want to cut your hair at home, follow these simple steps:

Step 1: Wash Your Hair

Start by washing your hair with a clarifying shampoo to remove any buildup or residue. Towel-dry your hair and comb it out to remove any tangles.

Step 2: Section Your Hair

Section your hair into four parts: two sections in the front and two sections in the back. Use hair clips to secure each section.

Step 3: Cut Your Hair

Starting with the back sections, use a pair of hair cutting scissors to cut your hair to the desired length. Work your way up to the top sections, using the back sections as a guide.

Step 4: Style Your Hair

Once you have cut your hair, style it according to your preference. You can use a hair dryer and a round brush to add volume and texture to your hair, or you can let your natural waves do the talking.

Tips for Hair Care and Maintenance

Here are some tips for maintaining your short haircut for oval face wavy hair:

Use a Moisturizing Shampoo

Wavy hair can be prone to dryness, so it's important to use a moisturizing shampoo that can help hydrate your hair. Look for shampoos that contain natural ingredients like shea butter, coconut oil, and argan oil.

Use a Wide-Tooth Comb

Wavy hair can be prone to tangles, so it's important to use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair. Start from the ends of your hair and work your way up to the roots to prevent breakage.

Use a Heat Protectant

If you use heat styling tools like a hair dryer or a flat iron, it's important to use a heat protectant to prevent damage to your hair. Look for heat protectants that contain natural ingredients like keratin and argan oil.

FAQ

What short haircut is best for oval face wavy hair?

There are many short haircuts that can work well for oval face wavy hair, including a pixie cut, a bob with layers, and a lob. The key is to choose a haircut that complements your natural features and enhances your face shape.

How often should I cut my hair?

It's recommended to cut your hair every 6-8 weeks to keep it looking healthy and vibrant. However, this may vary depending on your hair type and how fast your hair grows.

Can I color my hair if I have wavy hair?

Yes, you can color your hair if you have wavy hair. However, it's important to use a color-safe shampoo and conditioner to prevent color fading and damage to your hair.

With these tips and tricks, you can find the perfect short haircut for your oval face wavy hair and showcase your natural beauty.
