How To Cut Side Bangs Step By Step

Are you tired of your plain old hairstyle and want to add some flare to it? Look no further than cutting some side bangs! Side bangs can add a trendy and chic touch to any hairstyle. In this article, we will go over everything you need to know about how to cut side bangs step by step.

Table of Contents

  • How to Cut Side Bangs
  • Step by Step Guide
  • Tips for Cutting Side Bangs
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care after Cutting Side Bangs

How to Cut Side Bangs

Before we jump into the step by step guide, it's important to know what you'll need to get started. Here's a list of things you'll need:

  • Sharp scissors
  • Comb or brush
  • Water spray bottle
  • Hair clips

Step by Step Guide

Follow these steps to cut side bangs:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair.
  2. Separate the section of hair you want to cut into side bangs.
  3. Use your comb to create a straight part down the middle of your head to the outside edge of your eyebrow on the side you want to cut your bangs.
  4. Comb the hair forward, using your fingers to hold the hair in place.
  5. Use your scissors to cut the hair at an angle, starting from the middle of the forehead and angling down towards the side of the head.
  6. Check your progress by looking in the mirror, and adjust as needed.
  7. Repeat steps 3-6 on the other side of your head.
  8. Blend the bangs into the rest of your hair by combing and cutting any uneven pieces.
  9. Style your new side bangs as desired.
  10. Finish with hairspray or other styling products as needed.

Tips for Cutting Side Bangs

Here are some additional tips to keep in mind while cutting your side bangs:

  • Start with a longer length than you think you want. You can always go shorter, but you can't make hair grow back instantly.
  • Use hair clips to keep the rest of your hair out of the way while you cut your bangs.
  • Don't rush the process. Take your time and make small cuts at a time.
  • If you're unsure about cutting your own side bangs, consider going to a professional hairstylist for help.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about cutting side bangs:

  • Can I cut my side bangs while they're wet? Yes, but keep in mind that hair tends to look shorter when it's wet, so cut less than you think you need to.
  • How often should I trim my side bangs? It depends on how fast your hair grows and how quickly your bangs grow out. A good rule of thumb is to trim them every 4-6 weeks.
  • What if I make a mistake while cutting my side bangs? Don't panic! It's just hair and it will grow back. If it's a small mistake, you can try to fix it yourself or go to a hairstylist for help.

Hair Care after Cutting Side Bangs

Now that you've cut your side bangs, it's important to take care of them properly. Here are some tips:

  • Wash and condition your hair regularly to keep it healthy.
  • Blow dry your hair using a round brush to style your bangs.
  • Use a flat iron or curling iron to add some extra style to your bangs.
  • Trim your bangs regularly to keep them looking fresh.
  • Use hair products, such as hairspray or hair wax, to keep your bangs in place.

Now that you know how to cut side bangs step by step, it's time to give it a try! Remember to take your time, use the right tools, and don't be afraid to ask for help if you need it. Happy cutting!
