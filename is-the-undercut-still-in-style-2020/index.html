Is The Undercut Still In Style 2020

Description

The undercut is a popular hairstyle that has been in style for several years, but is it still in style in 2020? The answer is yes! The undercut is still a trendy and stylish haircut that can be customized to fit your personal style. This hairstyle is characterized by short sides and back with longer hair on top, creating a sharp contrast between the two lengths.

The undercut is a versatile haircut that can be worn in a variety of ways, from slicked-back to messy and textured. It is a great choice for those who want a low-maintenance hairstyle that still looks stylish and fashionable. Whether you have straight, wavy, or curly hair, the undercut can be tailored to suit your hair texture and face shape.

How to Get the Undercut

The undercut is a relatively easy hairstyle to achieve, but it is best to go to a professional hairstylist to get the best results. A hairstylist can help you choose the right length and style to suit your face shape and hair texture.

Step by Step Guide

1. Start by washing your hair with a good shampoo and conditioner. This will help to remove any excess oil and dirt from your hair and make it easier to style.

2. Towel dry your hair and apply a small amount of styling product, such as a pomade or wax. This will help to hold your hair in place and add texture.

3. Use a comb to part your hair on one side, and then use clippers to cut the sides and back of your hair short. You can choose how short you want the sides and back to be, but a good rule of thumb is to keep it shorter than the top.

4. Use scissors to trim the top of your hair to the desired length. You can leave it longer for a more dramatic look or shorter for a more subtle look.

5. Style your hair as desired using your fingers or a comb.

Tips for Maintaining the Undercut

To keep your undercut looking stylish and trendy, there are a few things you can do:

  • Visit your hairstylist regularly to keep the sides and back of your hair trimmed.
  • Use a good quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and hydrated.
  • Apply styling product to your hair when it is slightly damp to help hold the style in place.
  • Experiment with different styling techniques to find the look that works best for you.

Frequently Asked Questions

What face shape suits an undercut?

An undercut can suit a variety of face shapes, but it is best for those with oval or square faces. If you have a round face, you may want to avoid an undercut as it can make your face appear even rounder.

Can I still wear an undercut if I have curly hair?

Absolutely! The undercut can be tailored to suit any hair texture, including curly hair. Your hairstylist can help you choose the right length and style to suit your hair type and face shape.

Will my hair look messy if I don't style it every day?

The undercut is a low-maintenance hairstyle that can look stylish even if you don't style it every day. However, if you want a more polished look, you may want to spend a few minutes styling your hair each day.

Hair Care for the Undercut

To keep your undercut looking its best, it is important to take good care of your hair. This means using a good quality shampoo and conditioner, and avoiding harsh chemicals and heat styling tools that can damage your hair.

You may also want to consider using a hair mask or deep conditioner once a week to keep your hair hydrated and healthy. And don't forget to visit your hairstylist regularly to keep your undercut looking sharp and stylish!
