Best Hair Style For Oval Face

Written by Robby Aug 21, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Oval faces are considered the ideal face shape as they are versatile and can pull off almost any hairstyle. However, it can be overwhelming to choose the perfect hairstyle that complements your face shape. In this blog post, we will discuss the best hair style for oval face and provide tips on how to maintain healthy hair.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to Choose the Best Hair Style for Oval Face
  • Step by Step Guide to Achieving the Best Hair Style for Oval Face
  • Tips for Maintaining Healthy Hair
  • FAQs

Description

An oval face shape is characterized by balanced proportions with a slightly narrow chin and forehead. The length of the face is longer than the width, and the cheekbones are the widest part of the face. The best hair style for oval face should accentuate the balanced features and enhance the natural beauty of the face. Oval faces have the freedom to experiment with different haircuts, lengths, and styles. However, some hairstyles may be more flattering than others.

How to Choose the Best Hair Style for Oval Face

When choosing the best hair style for oval face, consider the following factors:

  • Face shape: Oval faces can pull off almost any hairstyle, but some styles may be more flattering than others.
  • Hair texture: The texture of your hair can affect the way a hairstyle looks. Some styles may work better with thick, wavy hair while others may work better with fine, straight hair.
  • Lifestyle: Consider your lifestyle when choosing a hairstyle. If you have a busy lifestyle, choose a low-maintenance hairstyle that requires minimal styling.
  • Personality: Your hairstyle should reflect your personality and personal style.

Step by Step Guide to Achieving the Best Hair Style for Oval Face

Here are some steps to achieve the best hair style for oval face:

  1. Start by determining your face shape. Take a photo of yourself or look in the mirror and trace the outline of your face with a washable marker or lipstick.
  2. Research different hairstyles that complement oval faces. Look for inspiration in magazines, social media, and celebrity hairstyles.
  3. Consult with a hairstylist to determine which hairstyle will work best for your face shape and hair texture.
  4. Choose a hairstyle that accentuates your best features and enhances your natural beauty. Some popular hairstyles for oval faces include long layers, blunt bobs, and beachy waves.
  5. Opt for a hairstyle that is low-maintenance and easy to style. Consider your lifestyle and daily routine when choosing a hairstyle.
  6. Invest in quality hair products that will enhance and maintain your hairstyle. Use a shampoo and conditioner that is suitable for your hair type and texture.
  7. Style your hair according to your chosen hairstyle. Use styling tools such as a curling iron or straightener to achieve the desired look.
  8. Maintain regular haircuts to keep your hair healthy and maintain the shape of your hairstyle.

Tips for Maintaining Healthy Hair

To maintain healthy hair, follow these tips:

  • Use a shampoo and conditioner that is suitable for your hair type and texture.
  • Avoid using hot tools on a daily basis as it can damage your hair.
  • Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a protective spray.
  • Eat a balanced diet that includes protein, vitamins, and minerals to promote healthy hair growth.
  • Drink plenty of water to keep your hair hydrated.

FAQs

What is the best haircut for oval faces?

The best haircut for oval faces depends on the individual's hair texture, personality, and lifestyle. Some popular hairstyles for oval faces include long layers, blunt bobs, and beachy waves.

Can oval faces pull off bangs?

Oval faces can pull off almost any hairstyle, including bangs. However, it is important to choose a bang style that complements your face shape and hair texture.

How often should I get a haircut?

It is recommended to get a haircut every 6-8 weeks to maintain healthy hair and the shape of your hairstyle.

In conclusion, the best hair style for oval face is one that accentuates the balanced features and enhances the natural beauty of the face. Consider your face shape, hair texture, lifestyle, and personality when choosing a hairstyle. Maintain healthy hair by using quality hair products, protecting your hair from damage, and eating a balanced diet.
