How To Make Your Hair Look Short With A Beanie

Written by Christine Sep 07, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Are you tired of having long hair but don't want to cut it? Or maybe you just want to switch up your look for the day? Whatever your reason may be, there's an easy solution - wear a beanie! Not only will it keep you warm, but it can also make your hair look shorter. Keep reading for some tips and tricks on how to achieve this look.

Description

A beanie is a type of hat that is often made of wool or acrylic material. It is designed to fit snugly on the head, covering the ears and forehead. Beanies come in a variety of colors and styles, making them a versatile accessory for any outfit.

How to

Step 1: Start with clean, dry hair. It's easier to style your hair when it's not wet or damp.

Step 2: Brush your hair to remove any tangles or knots. This will make it easier to style your hair under the beanie.

Step 3: Put on the beanie, making sure it covers your entire head. You can adjust the beanie to sit higher or lower on your forehead depending on how much hair you want to show.

Step 4: Tuck your hair behind your ears. This will create the illusion of shorter hair and give your face a more defined look.

Step 5: If you have bangs, you can leave them out or tuck them under the beanie. If you choose to leave them out, make sure they are styled to your liking.

Step by Step

Step 1: Start with clean, dry hair.

Step 2: Brush your hair to remove any tangles or knots.

Step 3: Put on the beanie, making sure it covers your entire head.

Step 4: Tuck your hair behind your ears.

Step 5: If you have bangs, you can leave them out or tuck them under the beanie.

Tips

  • Experiment with different styles of beanies to find what works best for you.
  • Use a hair tie to create a low ponytail before putting on the beanie for a different look.
  • Accessorize with earrings or a statement necklace to draw attention to your face.

FAQ

Q: Can I wear a beanie with any hair length?

A: Yes, a beanie can be worn with any hair length. However, it will be more effective in making your hair look shorter if you have longer hair.

Q: Will a beanie damage my hair?

A: No, wearing a beanie will not damage your hair. Just make sure to wash the beanie regularly to prevent any buildup of oils or sweat.

Hair Care

It's important to take care of your hair, even when wearing a beanie. Make sure to wash your hair regularly and use a conditioner to keep it hydrated. Avoid using heat styling tools too often and use a heat protectant spray when you do. Lastly, get regular trims to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.

In conclusion, wearing a beanie is a simple and stylish way to make your hair look shorter. Follow these steps and tips to achieve the perfect look for any occasion.
