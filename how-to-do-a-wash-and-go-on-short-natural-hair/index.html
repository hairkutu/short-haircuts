How to Do a Wash and Go on Short Natural Hair - Step by Step Guide

If you're looking for an easy and low maintenance hairstyle for your short natural hair, a wash and go is a perfect option. In this step by step guide, we will show you how to achieve a wash and go on short natural hair, including tips and FAQs to help you get the best results.

How to Do a Wash and Go on Short Natural Hair

A wash and go is a simple styling technique that involves washing your hair and allowing it to air dry without manipulating it too much. It works best on hair types that have defined curls or coils, such as type 3 and 4 hair. Here are the steps to achieve a wash and go on short natural hair:

Step by Step Guide

  1. Start with freshly washed hair. Use a sulfate-free shampoo and a moisturizing conditioner to cleanse and condition your hair.
  2. Apply a leave-in conditioner or curl defining cream to your damp hair. Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to distribute the product evenly throughout your hair.
  3. Flip your hair upside down and gently scrunch it to encourage your curls to form. You can also use a diffuser attachment on your blow dryer to speed up the drying process.
  4. Once your hair is dry, use a hair pick or your fingers to fluff your curls and create more volume.
  5. Optional: apply a hair oil or serum to add shine and reduce frizz.

Tips for a Perfect Wash and Go

Here are some tips to help you get the best results from your wash and go:

  • Use a microfiber towel or a cotton t-shirt to dry your hair instead of a regular towel to avoid frizz.
  • Apply products in small sections to ensure even distribution.
  • Experiment with different products to find what works best for your hair type.
  • Don't touch your hair too much while it's drying to avoid disrupting your curls.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about wash and go hairstyles:

  • How long does a wash and go last? A wash and go can last 3-5 days depending on your hair type and how you maintain it.
  • Can you do a wash and go on transitioning hair? Yes, you can do a wash and go on transitioning hair, but you may need to use different products to accommodate your two textures.
  • Can you do a wash and go on short hair? Yes, a wash and go is a great option for short hair as it requires little maintenance.

Hair Care for Short Natural Hair

Here are some tips for taking care of your short natural hair:

  • Wash your hair once a week or every other week to avoid dryness.
  • Moisturize your hair daily with a water-based leave-in conditioner or moisturizer.
  • Protect your hair at night by wearing a satin bonnet or using a satin pillowcase.
  • Trim your ends regularly to prevent split ends and breakage.

By following these tips and steps, you can achieve a beautiful and low maintenance wash and go on your short natural hair. Experiment with different products and techniques to find what works best for your hair type and enjoy your new hairstyle!
