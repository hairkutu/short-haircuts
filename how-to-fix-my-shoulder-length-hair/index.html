How To Fix My Shoulder Length Hair A Step By Step Guide

Written by Eveline Jun 13, 2023 Â· 8 min read

Are you struggling to style your shoulder length hair? Do you find yourself in a hair rut, wearing the same style day after day? It's time to switch things up and learn how to fix your shoulder length hair. In this guide, we'll provide you with simple steps and tips to help you achieve a fresh and stylish look.

Description

Shoulder length hair is a versatile length that can be styled in numerous ways. However, it can be challenging to figure out how to style it in a way that suits your face shape and hair texture. Whether you have straight, wavy, or curly hair, this guide will help you find a style that flatters you.

How to

Step 1: Determine Your Face Shape

The first step in figuring out how to fix your shoulder length hair is to determine your face shape. Face shape plays a significant role in determining which hairstyles will look best on you. If you have an oval face shape, you can wear almost any style. If you have a round face shape, you'll want to avoid styles that add volume to the sides of your face.

Step 2: Consider Your Hair Texture

Your hair texture also plays a role in determining which styles will work for you. If you have straight hair, you may want to add some volume with layers or waves. If you have wavy hair, you can enhance your natural texture with products and diffusers. If you have curly hair, you may want to define your curls with a curling cream or gel.

Step 3: Choose a Style

Once you've determined your face shape and hair texture, it's time to choose a style. If you have an oval or heart-shaped face, you can wear almost any style, from a blunt bob to long layers. If you have a round face shape, you'll want to avoid styles that add volume to the sides of your face. Instead, opt for a style that elongates your face, such as a long bob or a side-swept fringe.

Step 4: Prep Your Hair

Before styling your hair, it's essential to prep it properly. Start by washing and conditioning your hair with products that suit your hair type. If you have fine hair, you may want to use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner. If you have curly hair, you may want to use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner. Towel dry your hair gently and apply a heat protectant spray.

Step 5: Style Your Hair

Now it's time to style your hair. If you want to add volume, use a round brush while blow-drying your hair. For a sleek and straight look, use a flat iron. If you want to enhance your natural waves or curls, use a diffuser and a curling cream or gel. Finish with a hairspray or a shine spray.

Tips

Here are some additional tips to help you fix your shoulder length hair:

  • Invest in quality hair tools, such as a good blow dryer, flat iron, and curling iron.
  • Use heat protectant spray before using any hot tools.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.
  • Experiment with different styles to find what suits you best.
  • Don't be afraid to try new products.

FAQ

What are some popular shoulder length hairstyles?

Some popular shoulder length hairstyles include the lob (long bob), layered haircuts, and blunt cuts. You can also add bangs or a side-swept fringe to change up your look.

How often should I wash my shoulder length hair?

It depends on your hair type and lifestyle. If you have oily hair, you may want to wash it every day or every other day. If you have dry hair, you can wash it every few days. If you work out regularly or live in a humid climate, you may need to wash your hair more frequently.

Can I still style my shoulder length hair if it's thin?

Absolutely! There are many styles that work well for thin hair, such as a blunt bob or a layered cut. You can also add volume with products and tools, such as a volumizing mousse or a round brush.

Hair Care

It's essential to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and strong. Here are some hair care tips:

  • Use a shampoo and conditioner that suits your hair type.
  • Avoid using hot tools every day, as they can damage your hair.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week.
  • Eat a healthy diet and drink plenty of water to nourish your hair from the inside out.
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends.

Now that you know how to fix your shoulder length hair, it's time to try some new styles and experiment with different products. With a little effort and creativity, you can achieve a fresh and stylish look that suits you.
