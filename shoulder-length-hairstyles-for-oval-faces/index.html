Shoulder Length Hairstyles For Oval Faces The Perfect Look For You

Written by Luffy Sep 03, 2023 · 10 min read

Are you looking for the perfect hairstyle that will complement your oval face shape? Look no further than shoulder length hairstyles! This versatile length is perfect for those with oval faces, as it highlights your features and complements your face shape. In this article, we’ll go over some of the best shoulder length hairstyles for oval faces, as well as tips on how to care for your hair to keep it looking its best.

Table of Contents

  • How to Choose the Right Shoulder Length Hairstyle for Your Oval Face
  • Step-by-Step Guide to Styling Your Shoulder Length Hair
  • Tips for Maintaining Your Shoulder Length Hairstyle
  • FAQs About Shoulder Length Hairstyles for Oval Faces
  • Hair Care for Shoulder Length Hairstyles

How to Choose the Right Shoulder Length Hairstyle for Your Oval Face

When it comes to choosing the right shoulder length hairstyle for your oval face, there are a few things to keep in mind. First, you want to choose a hairstyle that complements your face shape, highlighting your natural features. Second, you want to choose a style that works with your hair type and texture, so that it’s easy to maintain and style.

Some great shoulder length hairstyles for oval faces include:

1. Long Layers

Long layers are a great choice for those with oval faces, as they help to frame your face and highlight your cheekbones. This style works well with straight or wavy hair textures, and can be easily styled with a curling iron or straightener.

2. Side-Swept Bangs

Side-swept bangs are another great choice for oval faces, as they help to balance out your features and add some dimension to your hair. This style works well with straight or wavy hair textures, and can be easily styled with a round brush and blow dryer.

3. Bob Haircut

A classic bob haircut is always a great choice for those with oval faces, as it helps to frame your face and highlight your cheekbones. This style works well with straight or wavy hair textures, and can be easily styled with a straightener or curling iron.

Step-by-Step Guide to Styling Your Shoulder Length Hair

Once you’ve chosen the perfect shoulder length hairstyle for your oval face, it’s time to start styling! Here’s a step-by-step guide to help you get the perfect look:

1. Start with clean, dry hair

Before you begin styling, make sure your hair is clean and dry. Use a shampoo and conditioner that’s appropriate for your hair type and texture, and towel dry your hair gently.

2. Apply a heat protectant

If you plan on using heat styling tools like a curling iron or straightener, make sure to apply a heat protectant spray or serum to your hair to prevent damage.

3. Style your hair

Using a curling iron or straightener, style your hair according to the chosen shoulder length hairstyle. Be sure to use a comb or brush to help evenly distribute heat and create your desired look.

4. Finish with hairspray

Once you’ve achieved your desired style, finish with a light mist of hairspray to help hold the look in place.

Tips for Maintaining Your Shoulder Length Hairstyle

Now that you’ve got the perfect shoulder length hairstyle for your oval face, it’s important to keep it looking its best. Here are some tips for maintaining your shoulder length hairstyle:

1. Wash your hair regularly

Washing your hair regularly helps to keep it clean and healthy, and prevents buildup of oils and products. Aim to wash your hair every 2-3 days, depending on your hair type and texture.

2. Use a deep conditioning treatment

Using a deep conditioning treatment once a week can help to keep your hair soft, smooth, and healthy-looking. Look for a product that’s appropriate for your hair type and texture, and follow the instructions carefully.

3. Avoid heat styling too often

While heat styling tools can help you achieve the perfect look, using them too often can damage your hair and make it more difficult to style. Try to limit heat styling to a few times a week, and always use a heat protectant.

FAQs About Shoulder Length Hairstyles for Oval Faces

1. Can I wear bangs with a shoulder length hairstyle?

Yes, side-swept bangs are a great choice for those with oval faces and shoulder length hair. They help to balance out your features and add some dimension to your hair.

2. What’s the best way to style my shoulder length hair?

The best way to style your shoulder length hair will depend on your hair type and texture, as well as the chosen hairstyle. Experiment with different styling products and tools to find the perfect look for you.

Hair Care for Shoulder Length Hairstyles

Keeping your hair healthy and looking its best is key to maintaining a great shoulder length hairstyle. Here are some tips for caring for your hair:

1. Use a shampoo and conditioner that’s appropriate for your hair type and texture

Choosing the right shampoo and conditioner is important for keeping your hair clean, healthy, and hydrated. Look for products that are specifically designed for your hair type and texture.

2. Avoid using too many styling products

While styling products can help you achieve the perfect look, using too many can weigh down your hair and make it more difficult to style. Aim to use just a few key products that work well for your hair.

3. Protect your hair from the sun

Exposure to the sun can cause damage to your hair, so it’s important to protect it by wearing a hat or using a UV-protectant spray or serum.

With these tips and tricks, you’re sure to find the perfect shoulder length hairstyle for your oval face shape, and keep it looking its best. Happy styling!
