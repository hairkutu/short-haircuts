How To Get Big Curls For Natural Hair

Written by Christine May 15, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Are you tired of your straight hair and looking for a way to transform your look? The good news is that you can achieve big curls for natural hair without using any heat. In this article, we will guide you step-by-step on how to achieve big curls for natural hair.

Table of Contents

  • How to Get Big Curls for Natural Hair
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Maintaining Your Curls
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care Tips for Natural Hair

Step-by-Step Guide

Here are the steps to follow:

1. Wash Your Hair

Start by washing your hair with a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner. This will help to remove any buildup from your hair and make it easier to manage. After washing, towel-dry your hair gently.

2. Apply a Leave-In Conditioner

Next, apply a leave-in conditioner to your hair. This will help to moisturize your hair and make it easier to detangle.

3. Detangle Your Hair

Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair. This will help to prevent any breakage and make it easier to style your hair.

4. Apply a Styling Cream

Apply a styling cream to your hair. This will help to define your curls and hold them in place. Use your fingers to evenly distribute the product throughout your hair.

5. Divide Your Hair into Sections

Divide your hair into sections. This will make it easier to style your hair and ensure that each section is evenly curled.

6. Curl Your Hair

Take a small section of hair and twist it around your finger. Release the curl and clip it in place using a hair clip. Repeat this process until all of your hair is curled.

7. Allow Your Hair to Dry

Allow your hair to dry naturally. Do not use a hair dryer as this can cause frizz and damage your hair.

8. Remove the Hair Clips

Once your hair is completely dry, remove the hair clips. Use your fingers to gently separate the curls and create volume.

9. Apply a Hair Oil

Apply a hair oil to your hair to add shine and lock in moisture.

10. Fluff Your Curls

Finally, fluff your curls using your fingers. This will help to create volume and give your hair a natural look.

Tips for Maintaining Your Curls

Here are some tips to help you maintain your curls:

  • Avoid brushing your hair as this can cause frizz
  • Sleep on a silk pillowcase to prevent your curls from becoming flattened
  • Use a diffuser when blow-drying your hair to help maintain your curls
  • Avoid using heat styling tools as they can damage your hair
  • Avoid using hair products that contain alcohol as they can dry out your hair

FAQs

How long will my curls last?

Your curls should last for a few days if you follow the tips above.

Can I use a hair dryer to dry my hair?

Avoid using a hair dryer as this can cause frizz and damage your hair. Instead, allow your hair to dry naturally.

Do I need to use a styling cream?

Using a styling cream will help to define your curls and hold them in place. However, if you prefer a more natural look, you can skip this step.

Hair Care Tips for Natural Hair

Here are some general hair care tips for natural hair:

  • Wash your hair once a week with a sulfate-free shampoo
  • Deep condition your hair once a month to keep it moisturized
  • Avoid using heat styling tools as they can damage your hair
  • Avoid using hair products that contain alcohol as they can dry out your hair
  • Sleep on a silk pillowcase to prevent your hair from becoming tangled

Now that you know how to get big curls for natural hair, it's time to try it out for yourself. Follow the steps above and enjoy your new look!
