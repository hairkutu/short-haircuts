How To Gel Guys Hair The Ultimate Guide

Written by Oliver Aug 04, 2023 · 7 min read

Are you tired of your hair falling flat or being unmanageable? Well, fear not, because we have the ultimate guide on how to gel guys hair. In this article, we will cover everything from how to apply gel to tips for maintaining your hair. So, let's get started!

Table of Contents:

  • How to Gel Guys Hair
  • Step by Step Guide
  • Tips for Maintaining Your Hair
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care

How to Gel Guys Hair

Gel is a great way to style your hair and keep it in place. Here are the steps to properly gel your hair:

  1. Start with clean, towel-dried hair.
  2. Take a small amount of gel (about a dime-sized amount) and rub it between your palms.
  3. Apply the gel evenly throughout your hair, starting from the roots and working your way to the tips.
  4. Use a comb or your fingers to style your hair as desired.
  5. Allow the gel to dry completely before touching your hair.

It's important to note that using too much gel can make your hair look greasy and weighed down. Start with a small amount and add more if needed.

Step by Step Guide

Here is a more detailed step by step guide on how to gel guys hair:

  1. Wash your hair with a shampoo and conditioner that suits your hair type. Avoid using too much conditioner as it can make your hair too soft and difficult to manage.
  2. Towel dry your hair by gently patting it with a towel. Avoid rubbing your hair vigorously as it can cause damage and frizz.
  3. Take a dime-sized amount of gel and rub it between your palms. Make sure to evenly distribute the gel on your palms.
  4. Apply the gel to your hair, starting from the roots and working your way to the tips. Use your fingers to evenly distribute the gel throughout your hair.
  5. Use a comb or your fingers to style your hair as desired. You can create spikes, a messy look, or a slicked-back style.
  6. Allow the gel to dry completely before touching your hair. This will ensure that the gel sets properly and your hair stays in place.

Tips for Maintaining Your Hair

Here are some tips to keep your hair looking great:

  • Choose the right gel for your hair type. If you have thick hair, opt for a stronger hold gel. If you have fine hair, choose a lighter hold gel.
  • Avoid using too much gel as it can make your hair look greasy and weighed down.
  • Wash your hair regularly to keep it clean and healthy.
  • Avoid using hot water to wash your hair as it can damage your hair and make it dry.
  • Use a hair conditioner regularly to keep your hair soft and manageable.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about gelling guys hair:

Q: Can I use gel on wet hair?

A: It's not recommended to use gel on wet hair as it can dilute the gel and make it less effective. Towel dry your hair before applying gel.

Q: How much gel should I use?

A: Start with a dime-sized amount of gel and add more if needed.

Q: How do I remove gel from my hair?

A: You can remove gel from your hair by washing it with shampoo and warm water.

Hair Care

Gelling your hair is a great way to style it and keep it in place. However, it's important to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and strong. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a shampoo that suits your hair type.
  • Avoid using hot water to wash your hair as it can damage your hair and make it dry.
  • Use a hair conditioner regularly to keep your hair soft and manageable.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair as it can damage it. Use a heat protectant spray before using a hair dryer or flat iron.
  • Eat a healthy, balanced diet to promote hair growth and strength.

By following these tips and using gel to style your hair, you can have great looking hair that's healthy and strong. So, go ahead and give it a try!
