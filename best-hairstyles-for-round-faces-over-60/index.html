Best Hairstyles For Round Faces Over 60 A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Bardi May 05, 2023 Â· 9 min read

As we age, our hair changes, and so do our face shapes. It's important to find a hairstyle that flatters our features and makes us feel confident. Round faces are known for their soft curves and full cheeks, but finding the perfect hairstyle for this face shape can be challenging. In this article, we'll explore the best hairstyles for round faces over 60 and provide tips on how to care for your hair.

Table of Contents

  • How to Choose the Right Hairstyle
  • Short Hairstyles for Round Faces Over 60
  • Medium Hairstyles for Round Faces Over 60
  • Long Hairstyles for Round Faces Over 60
  • Expert Tips for Maintaining Your Hair
  • Frequently Asked Questions

How to Choose the Right Hairstyle

Before we dive into specific hairstyles, it's important to consider a few factors when choosing a hairstyle for a round face:

  • Length: Shorter hairstyles tend to elongate the face, while longer hairstyles can make the face appear wider.
  • Layers: Layers can add dimension and texture to the hair, but too many layers can make the face appear rounder.
  • Bangs: Bangs can help balance out a round face, but they should be swept to the side or cut diagonally.
  • Volume: Avoid styles that add too much volume to the top of the head, as this can make the face appear wider.

Short Hairstyles for Round Faces Over 60

Short hairstyles are a great option for women over 60 with round faces. A pixie cut or a bob with layers can add volume and texture to the hair, while also elongating the face. Here are a few examples:

  • Classic Bob: A chin-length bob with subtle layers can frame the face and add texture to the hair.
  • Pixie Cut: A short, layered pixie cut can add volume and texture to the hair, while also elongating the face.
  • Asymmetrical Bob: An asymmetrical bob with a longer side can create the illusion of a longer face.

Medium Hairstyles for Round Faces Over 60

Medium hairstyles are a great option for women who want a bit more length without sacrificing style. Here are a few examples of medium hairstyles for round faces over 60:

  • Shoulder-Length Bob: A shoulder-length bob with subtle layers can add volume and texture to the hair, while also elongating the face.
  • Long Layers: Long layers can add dimension and texture to the hair, while also creating the illusion of a longer face.
  • Side-Swept Bangs: Side-swept bangs can help balance out a round face, while also adding a touch of glamour.

Long Hairstyles for Round Faces Over 60

Long hairstyles can be tricky for women with round faces, as they can make the face appear wider. However, there are a few styles that can work:

  • Face-Framing Layers: Long layers that frame the face can create the illusion of a longer face.
  • Long Bob: A long bob with layers can add volume and texture to the hair, while also elongating the face.
  • Loose Waves: Loose waves can add texture and movement to the hair, while also creating the illusion of a longer face.

Expert Tips for Maintaining Your Hair

Now that you've found the perfect hairstyle for your round face, it's important to take care of your hair to keep it looking healthy and vibrant. Here are a few tips:

  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner that is formulated for your hair type.
  • Avoid using hot tools on a regular basis, as this can damage the hair.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair looking healthy and free of split ends.
  • Consider using a hair mask once a week to add moisture and nourishment to your hair.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Can women over 60 wear bangs?

A: Yes, women over 60 can wear bangs, but they should be swept to the side or cut diagonally to balance out a round face.

Q: What is the best hairstyle for a round face over 60?

A: The best hairstyle for a round face over 60 depends on several factors, including length, layers, and volume. Short hairstyles like a pixie cut or a bob with layers can elongate the face, while medium hairstyles like shoulder-length bobs or long layers can add volume and texture. Long hairstyles can work as well, but they should be styled with face-framing layers or loose waves to create the illusion of a longer face.

Q: How often should I get my hair trimmed?

A: It's recommended to get your hair trimmed every 6-8 weeks to keep it looking healthy and free of split ends.

Q: What is the best way to care for my hair?

A: To care for your hair, use a gentle shampoo and conditioner that is formulated for your hair type, avoid using hot tools on a regular basis, get regular trims, and consider using a hair mask once a week to add moisture and nourishment to your hair.

In conclusion, finding the perfect hairstyle for a round face over 60 can be challenging, but with these tips and examples, you can find a style that flatters your features and makes you feel confident. Remember to take care of your hair to keep it looking healthy and vibrant. Happy styling!
