How To Make Dreads With Short Hair A Step By Step Guide

Written by Eveline Apr 25, 2023 · 6 min read

Are you tired of having short hair and want to try something new? Look no further than dreadlocks! Contrary to popular belief, you can still make dreads with short hair. In this article, we’ll provide you with a step-by-step guide on how to make dreads with short hair, along with tips, FAQs, and hair care advice.

Table of Contents

  • How to Make Dreads with Short Hair
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Making Dreads with Short Hair
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care for Dreadlocks

How to Make Dreads with Short Hair

Before we dive into the step-by-step guide, it’s important to note that making dreads with short hair may take longer than if you had longer hair. However, it’s still possible to achieve the look you want. Here’s what you’ll need:

  • Dread wax or gel
  • A comb or brush
  • Small hair elastics or rubber bands
  • A hair dryer (optional)

Step-by-Step Guide

Follow these steps to make dreads with short hair:

  1. Wash your hair thoroughly and let it dry completely.
  2. Divide your hair into small sections using a comb or brush.
  3. Apply a small amount of dread wax or gel to the first section of hair.
  4. Twist the section of hair tightly until it starts to coil into a dread.
  5. Secure the dread with a small hair elastic or rubber band.
  6. Repeat steps 3-5 for each section of hair until your entire head is covered in dreads.
  7. If your hair is not staying in place, use a hair dryer on low heat to dry the wax or gel and help the dreads set.

Remember to be patient when making dreads with short hair. It may take several weeks or even months for your dreads to fully form and tighten.

Tips for Making Dreads with Short Hair

Here are some additional tips to make the process of making dreads with short hair easier:

  • Use a small amount of wax or gel to avoid weighing down your hair.
  • Don’t make your sections of hair too big, as this can make it difficult for the dreads to form properly.
  • Be gentle when twisting your hair to avoid breakage.
  • Use a hair dryer on low heat to help set the dreads if they’re not staying in place.
  • Consider using a crochet hook to help tighten your dreads.

FAQs

Can you make dreads with really short hair?

Yes, you can make dreads with really short hair. However, it may take longer for your dreads to form and tighten.

Do you need long hair for dreads?

No, you don’t need long hair for dreads. You can make dreads with short hair, although it may take longer for them to form and tighten.

How long does it take to make dreads with short hair?

It can take several weeks or even months for dreads to fully form and tighten, especially if you have short hair.

Hair Care for Dreadlocks

Once you’ve made your dreads, it’s important to take care of them properly. Here are some tips for hair care:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a residue-free shampoo.
  • Avoid using too much wax or gel, as this can cause buildup and make your dreads heavy.
  • Use a light oil or conditioning spray to keep your dreads moisturized.
  • Use a crochet hook to tighten your dreads as needed.
  • Avoid using a hair dryer on high heat, as this can damage your dreads.

With these tips and our step-by-step guide, you’ll be able to make dreads with short hair in no time. Happy dreadlocking!
