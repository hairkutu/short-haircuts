What Type Of Hair Style Suits Me A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Eveline Mar 26, 2023 Â· 8 min read

Choosing the right hair style can be a daunting task. With so many options available, it can be overwhelming to figure out what will suit you best. In this guide, we will help you determine what type of hair style suits you based on your face shape, hair texture, and personal style.

Table of Contents:

  1. Determining Your Face Shape
  2. Understanding Your Hair Texture
  3. Embracing Your Personal Style
  4. Hair Styles for Different Face Shapes and Hair Textures
  5. Tips for Choosing the Right Hair Style
  6. Frequently Asked Questions
  7. Hair Care Tips

Determining Your Face Shape

Before choosing a hair style, it's important to determine your face shape. Your face shape will help you determine which hair styles will flatter you the most. There are six basic face shapes:

  • Oval
  • Round
  • Square
  • Heart
  • Diamond
  • Pear

To determine your face shape, look in the mirror and trace the outline of your face with a lipstick or eyeliner pencil. Compare the shape to the six options above, and you'll be able to determine your face shape.

Understanding Your Hair Texture

Aside from face shape, hair texture is also an important factor to consider when choosing a hair style. Hair texture refers to the thickness, density, and natural movement of your hair. Understanding your hair texture will help you determine which hair styles will work best for you. There are three basic hair textures:

  • Straight
  • Wavy
  • Curly

If you're not sure about your hair texture, ask your stylist for their opinion.

Embracing Your Personal Style

When choosing a hair style, it's important to consider your personal style. Your hair style should reflect your personality and lifestyle. If you're someone who prefers a low-maintenance look, a short, easy-to-style cut may be the best option. If you're someone who likes to experiment with different looks, a longer style with layers may be more suitable.

Hair Styles for Different Face Shapes and Hair Textures

Now that you've determined your face shape and hair texture, let's take a look at some hair styles that will suit you best:

Oval Face Shape:

Those with an oval face shape have the most versatility in terms of hair styles. Long layers, bangs, and bobs all work well with this face shape.

Round Face Shape:

Avoid styles that add volume to the sides of the face. Instead, opt for a long, layered cut or a pixie cut with volume on top.

Square Face Shape:

Choose a style that softens the jawline, such as a shoulder-length cut with layers or a side-swept bang.

Heart Face Shape:

Choose a style that adds volume to the bottom half of the face, such as a chin-length bob or a side-swept bang.

Diamond Face Shape:

Choose a style that adds width to the forehead and chin, such as a bob with volume on top or a side-swept bang.

Pear Face Shape:

Choose a style that adds volume to the top half of the face, such as a pixie cut with volume on top or a side-swept bang.

When it comes to hair texture, the following styles work best:

Straight Hair:

Straight hair looks best in sleek, simple styles such as a blunt bob or long layers.

Wavy Hair:

Wavy hair looks best in styles that embrace the natural texture, such as long layers or a beachy wave.

Curly Hair:

Curly hair looks best in styles that embrace the curls, such as a layered cut or a curly bob.

Tips for Choosing the Right Hair Style

  • Consider your lifestyle and how much time you're willing to spend on your hair each day.
  • Bring pictures of hair styles you like to your stylist for inspiration.
  • Don't be afraid to experiment with different styles until you find the one that works best for you.
  • Take into consideration any hair concerns you may have, such as thinning hair or a receding hairline.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Can I still wear a certain hair style even if it doesn't suit my face shape?

A: Of course! At the end of the day, it's important to choose a hair style that makes you feel confident and happy.

Q: How often should I get my hair cut?

A: It's recommended to get your hair cut every 6-8 weeks to maintain the health of your hair.

Hair Care Tips

  • Use a shampoo and conditioner that's suitable for your hair type.
  • Avoid using hot tools on your hair every day.
  • Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a UV protectant spray.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair looking healthy.

Choosing the right hair style can be a fun and exciting process. By taking into consideration your face shape, hair texture, and personal style, you'll be able to find a hair style that suits you best. Remember to embrace your individuality and have fun with your hair!
