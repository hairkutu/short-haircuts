How To Cut Your Own Hair Into A Pixie Bob A Step By Step Guide

Written by Bardi Jul 23, 2023 · 6 min read

Are you tired of your long locks and want to try something new? Why not go for a pixie bob? This trendy haircut is perfect for those who want to achieve a fresh and youthful look. The best part? You can do it on your own! In this article, we’ll guide you on how to cut your own hair into a pixie bob.

How to Cut Your Own Hair into a Pixie Bob

The pixie bob is a short haircut that’s usually longer at the front and shorter at the back. It’s a versatile haircut that flatters most face shapes and hair types. Here’s how you can cut your own hair into a pixie bob:

Step-by-Step Guide:

Step 1: Wash your hair and towel-dry it. Comb it thoroughly to remove any tangles.

Step 2: Divide your hair into sections. Start by parting your hair in the middle and then from ear to ear.

Step 3: Clip away the top section of your hair so that it doesn’t get in the way.

Step 4: Take a small section of hair at the back and cut it to your desired length. This will be your guide for the rest of your hair.

Step 5: Take small sections of hair and cut them to the length of your guide. Work your way up to the top section.

Step 6: Unclip the top section and cut it to the same length as the rest of your hair.

Step 7: Cut the front section of your hair to your desired length. You can opt for a longer or shorter fringe depending on your preference.

Step 8: Use a razor to add texture to your hair. This will give your hair a softer and more natural look.

Step 9: Check for any uneven sections and trim them accordingly.

Step 10: Style your hair as desired. You can use a curling iron, straightener, or leave it as it is.

Tips for Cutting Your Hair at Home:

Tip 1: Invest in a good pair of scissors. The sharper the scissors, the easier it is to cut your hair.

Tip 2: Cut your hair when it’s dry. Wet hair can stretch and look longer than it actually is.

Tip 3: Take your time. Rushing can lead to mistakes, which can be difficult to fix.

Tip 4: Start with small sections of hair. You can always cut more, but you can’t put it back once it’s cut.

FAQs:

Q: How do I decide which length to cut my hair?

A: It’s best to start with a longer length and gradually cut it shorter. This way, you can see how it looks and make adjustments accordingly.

Q: What do I do if I make a mistake?

A: Don’t panic! Mistakes happen. If you cut your hair too short, you can always style it differently or wear a hat until it grows out.

Hair Care After Cutting:

Now that you’ve cut your hair into a pixie bob, it’s important to take care of it. Here are some tips:

Tip 1: Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy.

Tip 2: Use a hair mask once a week to nourish your hair.

Tip 3: Trim your hair every 6-8 weeks to maintain your pixie bob.

Tip 4: Protect your hair from heat damage by using a heat protectant spray before using hot tools.

With these tips, you can rock your new pixie bob with confidence!
