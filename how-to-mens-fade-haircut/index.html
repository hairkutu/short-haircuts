How To Mens Fade Haircut A Step By Step Guide

Written by Luffy Mar 26, 2023 · 9 min read

If you're looking for a stylish and trendy haircut, a mens fade haircut may be just what you need. This popular hairstyle features short hair on the sides and back of the head that gradually fades into longer hair on the top. In this guide, we'll walk you through the steps to achieving a perfect mens fade haircut.

Table of Contents

  • How to Mens Fade Haircut
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for a Perfect Mens Fade Haircut
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care Tips for a Mens Fade Haircut

How to Mens Fade Haircut

A mens fade haircut is a short haircut that gradually fades from the sides and back of the head into longer hair on the top. It's a popular choice for men of all ages and can be customized to suit your individual style. Here's how to achieve a perfect mens fade haircut:

Step 1: Choose Your Fade Style

Before you start cutting your hair, it's important to decide which type of fade you want. There are several different styles of mens fade haircuts, including:

  • Low fade: The hair gradually tapers down to the skin around the bottom of the hairline.
  • High fade: The hair is clipped very short starting at the temples and gradually tapers up to the crown.
  • Mid fade: The hair is clipped short around the temples and gradually tapers down to the neckline.
  • Bald fade: The hair is clipped to the skin, creating a bald look.

Choose the style that best suits your face shape and personal style.

Step 2: Gather Your Tools

You'll need a few basic tools to achieve a mens fade haircut:

  • Clippers
  • Scissors
  • Comb
  • Hair spray or gel
  • Mirror

Make sure your clippers are fully charged or plugged in before you start cutting.

Step 3: Start Cutting

Start by cutting the sides and back of your hair with a clipper guard that's about one size shorter than you want. Use the longest guard first and gradually work your way down to a shorter guard until you achieve the desired length.

Next, use scissors to blend the hair at the top of the fade into the longer hair on the top of your head. Use a comb to lift the hair and cut it at an angle to create a natural-looking blend.

Step 4: Refine Your Look

Once you've achieved your desired length and blended your hair, it's time to refine your look. Use scissors to trim any stray hairs or uneven spots, and use hair spray or gel to style your hair into the desired shape.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide to achieving a perfect mens fade haircut:

  1. Decide on the type of fade you want.
  2. Gather your tools.
  3. Cut the sides and back of your hair with a clipper guard one size shorter than your desired length.
  4. Blend the hair at the top of the fade into the longer hair on top of your head using scissors.
  5. Refine your look by trimming any stray hairs or uneven spots with scissors.
  6. Style your hair using hair spray or gel.

Tips for a Perfect Mens Fade Haircut

Here are some tips to help you achieve a perfect mens fade haircut:

  • Start with a longer guard on your clippers and gradually work your way down to avoid cutting off too much hair.
  • Use a mirror to check your progress from different angles.
  • Don't be afraid to ask for help from a friend or professional barber.
  • Take your time and be patient – a great haircut takes time and effort.
  • Experiment with different styles to find the one that suits you best.

FAQs

Q: How often should I get a mens fade haircut?

A: It depends on how fast your hair grows and how quickly the fade grows out. Generally, you should get a mens fade haircut every 2-4 weeks to maintain the look.

Q: Can I achieve a mens fade haircut at home?

A: Yes, with the right tools and some practice, you can achieve a great mens fade haircut at home. However, if you're unsure or want a more professional look, it's best to visit a barber or stylist.

Q: Will a mens fade haircut work with any hair type?

A: Yes, a mens fade haircut can work with any hair type, from straight to curly to wavy. However, the length and style of the fade may need to be adjusted based on your hair type and texture.

Hair Care Tips for a Mens Fade Haircut

Once you've achieved a perfect mens fade haircut, it's important to take care of your hair to maintain the look. Here are some hair care tips for a mens fade haircut:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo and conditioner to keep it clean and healthy.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, such as blow dryers or flat irons, as this can damage your hair and make it harder to style.
  • Use a hair oil or serum to keep your hair moisturized and prevent breakage.
  • Visit a barber or stylist regularly to maintain the length and style of your haircut.

Follow these tips and you'll be able to maintain a perfect mens fade haircut for weeks to come.
