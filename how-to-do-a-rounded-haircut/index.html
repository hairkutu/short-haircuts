How To Do A Rounded Haircut Step By Step Guide

Description

A rounded haircut is a classic style that can suit anyone. It's a versatile look that can be adapted to different hair types and face shapes. This style is achieved by cutting the hair in rounded layers, creating a soft, full shape. If you're looking for a haircut that's easy to style and maintain, a rounded haircut might be the perfect choice for you.

How to Do a Rounded Haircut

Step-by-Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide on how to do a rounded haircut:

  1. Start by washing and blow-drying the hair. This will make it easier to cut and style.
  2. Section the hair into four parts: two at the front and two at the back. Use hair clips to secure each section.
  3. Start at the back of the head and take a small section of hair. Hold it between your fingers and cut it to the desired length. Repeat this process until you've cut all of the hair at the back.
  4. Move to the front sections and repeat the same process of taking small sections of hair and cutting them to the desired length.
  5. Once you've cut all of the hair, check for any unevenness and trim as necessary.
  6. Blend the layers by using thinning scissors to remove any excess bulk in the hair.
  7. Create the rounded shape by using scissors to cut the hair in a curved motion, following the shape of the head.
  8. Finish by styling the hair using your preferred method. You can use a hair dryer, straightener or curling iron to achieve the desired look.

Tips for a Perfect Rounded Haircut

Here are some tips to help you achieve a perfect rounded haircut:

  • Use sharp scissors to ensure clean and precise cuts.
  • Take your time and cut small sections of hair at a time.
  • Work on dry hair to get an accurate length and shape.
  • Use a comb to help guide your scissors and create a smooth, rounded shape.
  • Don't be afraid to ask for help from a friend or professional stylist.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about rounded haircuts:

  • What face shape is best suited for a rounded haircut? A rounded haircut can suit any face shape, but it's particularly flattering for those with round or square faces.
  • How often should I get a rounded haircut? It's recommended to get a haircut every 6-8 weeks to maintain the shape and prevent split ends.
  • Can I style a rounded haircut in different ways? Yes, a rounded haircut is versatile and can be styled in different ways. You can straighten it, curl it or leave it natural.

Hair Care after a Rounded Haircut

After getting a rounded haircut, it's important to take care of your hair to maintain its shape and health. Here are some tips:

  • Use a shampoo and conditioner that's suitable for your hair type.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too frequently as they can damage your hair.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.
  • Use a hair mask or oil treatment once a week to nourish your hair.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and chlorine by wearing a hat or using a protective spray.

By following these tips and getting regular haircuts, you can maintain a healthy and stylish rounded haircut.
