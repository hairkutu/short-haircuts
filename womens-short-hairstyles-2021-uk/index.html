Womens Short Hairstyles 2021 Uk The Ultimate Guide

Written by Bardi May 26, 2023 Â· 8 min read

Are you thinking of chopping off your locks and embracing a short hairstyle? You're in luck because this year's trend for womens short hairstyles in the UK is all about versatility and ease. From pixie cuts to bobs, there's a perfect haircut for every face shape and hair type. In this guide, we'll walk you through how to choose the right short hairstyle for you, tips for styling and hair care, and common questions about short hair.

How to Choose the Right Short Hairstyle for You

Before you book an appointment with your hairdresser, it's essential to consider your face shape and hair type. Short haircuts can accentuate your best features and create balance, but the wrong cut can also highlight your flaws. Here are some tips to help you choose the right short hairstyle:

Face Shape

The first step in choosing the right short hairstyle is to determine your face shape. There are six basic face shapes: round, oval, square, heart, diamond, and long. Each face shape has its ideal haircut that can balance your features and flatter your face. For example, a bob or a layered pixie cut is perfect for a heart-shaped face, while a chin-length bob or a short shag is ideal for a square-shaped face.

Hair Type

The second factor to consider when choosing a short hairstyle is your hair type. Short haircuts require less maintenance, but you still need to consider your hair texture and thickness. If you have fine hair, for example, a layered bob or a pixie cut can add volume and texture. If you have thick hair, a blunt bob or a short shag can help to remove bulk and make your hair more manageable.

Step by Step Guide to Styling Short Hair

Styling short hair can be quick and easy with the right tools and products. Here's a step-by-step guide to styling short hair:

Step 1: Wash Your Hair

Start with clean, freshly washed hair. Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils.

Step 2: Apply Styling Products

Apply a styling product to your damp hair to add texture and hold. For example, a mousse or a styling cream can add volume and texture to fine hair, while a pomade or a wax can create definition and hold for thicker hair.

Step 3: Blow Dry Your Hair

Blow-dry your hair using a round brush to add volume and shape. Use a diffuser attachment to enhance your natural curls or waves.

Step 4: Style Your Hair

Use a flat iron or a curling iron to create waves or curls. You can also use a texturizing spray to add more texture and hold.

Tips for Maintaining Short Hair

Short haircuts require less maintenance than long hair, but you still need to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and shiny. Here are some tips for maintaining short hair:

Trim Your Hair Regularly

Short haircuts need regular trims to maintain their shape and prevent split ends. Visit your hairdresser every 4-6 weeks for a trim.

Use Heat Protectant

Short hair is more vulnerable to heat damage, so use a heat protectant before using any hot tools.

Avoid Over-Washing Your Hair

Washing your hair every day can strip it of its natural oils, making it dry and brittle. Aim to wash your hair every other day or every three days.

FAQ About Short Hair

Here are some common questions about short hair:

Can I still style my short hair if I have curly or wavy hair?

Absolutely! Short haircuts can enhance your natural curls or waves. Use a diffuser attachment when blow-drying your hair to enhance your curls or waves.

Will a short haircut make my face look rounder?

No. The right short haircut can actually balance your features and make your face look slimmer. Consult with your hairdresser to find the best short haircut for your face shape.

Can I still wear hair accessories with short hair?

Yes! Headbands, hair clips, and barrettes can add a pop of color or texture to your short hair. Opt for smaller accessories that won't overwhelm your hair.

Hair Care for Short Hair

Short hair needs minimal maintenance, but you still need to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and shiny. Here are some hair care tips for short hair:

Use a Gentle Shampoo and Conditioner

Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils.

Avoid Over-Brushing Your Hair

Brush your hair gently to avoid pulling or tugging on your hair. Use a wide-tooth comb or a soft-bristled brush.

Avoid Using Too Much Product

Using too much product can weigh down your hair and make it look greasy. Use only a small amount of product and apply it evenly throughout your hair.

With these tips and tricks, you're ready to embrace a short hairstyle that suits your face shape and hair type. Whether you're going for a pixie cut or a bob, remember to maintain your hair's health with regular trims and gentle hair care.
