Best Hair Cut For Round Face Girls How To Get The Perfect Look

Written by Eveline Aug 22, 2023 · 8 min read

Do you have a round face and find it difficult to choose the right hair cut that flatters your features? Worry not, as we have got you covered with this comprehensive guide on the best hair cut for round face girls. From how to choose the right cut to styling tips and hair care, we have got all the information you need to achieve the perfect look.

Table of Contents

  • How to Choose the Right Hair Cut for Round Face Girls
  • Step-by-Step Guide to Getting the Perfect Hair Cut
  • Tips for Styling Round Face Girl Hair
  • Frequently Asked Questions
  • Hair Care Tips for Round Face Girls

How to Choose the Right Hair Cut for Round Face Girls

The key to choosing the right hair cut for round face girls is to create the illusion of length and angles. A good hair cut will elongate your face and draw attention to your best features. Here are some tips to help you choose the right cut:

1. Avoid Cuts that Add Volume at the Sides

Round faces tend to be wider at the cheeks, so avoid any hair cut that adds volume at the sides. This includes blunt bobs, pixie cuts, and layers that start at the cheeks.

2. Opt for Cuts that Create Height

Cuts that add height to the top of your head create the illusion of length and angles. This includes long layers, side-swept bangs, and hairstyles with volume at the crown.

3. Consider a Long Bob with Layers

A long bob with layers is a great option for round face girls. The length elongates your face, while the layers create movement and texture. You can also add side-swept bangs to frame your face.

Step-by-Step Guide to Getting the Perfect Hair Cut

Now that you know what hair cut to choose, it's time to get the perfect cut. Here's a step-by-step guide:

1. Find a Hair Stylist

Look for a hair stylist who specializes in cutting hair for round face girls. They will be able to recommend the best cut for your face shape and give you personalized advice.

2. Bring Pictures for Inspiration

Bring pictures of hair cuts that you like to your appointment. This will give your stylist an idea of the look you want to achieve.

3. Consult with Your Stylist

Discuss your face shape, hair type, and lifestyle with your stylist. They will recommend the best cut for you and give you tips on how to style your hair.

4. Get the Cut

Sit back and relax while your stylist gives you the perfect hair cut. They will use their expertise to create a style that flatters your features.

Tips for Styling Round Face Girl Hair

Styling your hair is just as important as getting the right cut. Here are some tips to help you style your hair:

1. Use a Round Brush

Use a round brush when blow-drying your hair to create volume and texture. Roll the brush away from your face to create height at the crown.

2. Avoid Heavy Products

Avoid heavy products that weigh down your hair, such as oils and serums. Opt for lightweight products that add volume and texture, such as mousse and sea salt spray.

3. Experiment with Accessories

Experiment with accessories, such as headbands and hair clips, to add interest to your hairstyle. Avoid accessories that add volume at the sides.

Frequently Asked Questions

1. Can round face girls have short hair?

Yes, round face girls can have short hair. However, it's important to choose the right cut that creates height and angles.

2. What is the best hair color for round face girls?

The best hair color for round face girls is one that creates depth and contrast. This includes highlights and lowlights, as well as ombre and balayage.

Hair Care Tips for Round Face Girls

Keeping your hair healthy is important for maintaining your style. Here are some hair care tips for round face girls:

1. Use a Heat Protectant

Use a heat protectant spray when using hot tools, such as a curling iron or flat iron. This will protect your hair from damage and keep it healthy.

2. Wash Your Hair Regularly

Wash your hair regularly to keep it clean and healthy. Use a shampoo and conditioner that is suitable for your hair type.

3. Trim Your Hair Regularly

Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends and maintain your style. Aim to get a trim every 6-8 weeks.

By following these tips and getting the right hair cut, you can achieve the perfect look for your round face. Experiment with different styles and have fun with your hair!
