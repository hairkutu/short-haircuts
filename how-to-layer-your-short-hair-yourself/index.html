How To Layer Your Short Hair Yourself A Step By Step Guide

Written by Luffy May 27, 2023 · 5 min read

Short hair can be a challenge to style, but layering can add volume and texture to your locks. If you’re looking to layer your short hair at home, you’ve come to the right place. In this article, we’ll take you through how to layer your short hair yourself, step by step.

Table of Contents

  • How to Layer Your Short Hair Yourself
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Layering Short Hair
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care for Layered Short Hair

How to Layer Your Short Hair Yourself

Layering your short hair yourself is easier than you might think. All you need is a good pair of scissors, a comb, and some patience. Here’s what you’ll need to do:

Step-by-Step Guide

Step 1: Start with clean, dry hair. Comb through it to remove any tangles.

Step 2: Separate the top section of your hair from the bottom section. Clip the top section up and out of the way.

Step 3: Take a small section of hair from the bottom layer and comb it straight down. Hold the hair between your fingers and determine how much length you want to take off.

Step 4: Cut the hair at an angle, starting from the bottom and working your way up. This will create a layered effect.

Step 5: Continue cutting small sections of hair in this way until you’ve layered the entire bottom section.

Step 6: Release the top section of your hair and repeat the process, layering the top section in the same way as the bottom.

Step 7: Blend the two sections together by combing through your hair and cutting any stray hairs that stick out.

Step 8: Style your hair as desired.

Tips for Layering Short Hair

Layering short hair can be tricky, so here are a few tips to keep in mind:

  • Start with small sections of hair to make sure you don’t cut too much off.
  • Use sharp scissors to ensure a clean cut.
  • Don’t cut your hair when it’s wet, as it will look different when it dries.

FAQs

Q: How often should I layer my short hair?

A: It depends on how fast your hair grows and how quickly it loses its shape. As a general rule, you should layer your short hair every 6-8 weeks.

Q: Can I layer my short hair with a razor?

A: Yes, you can. However, using a razor can be more difficult and can lead to split ends if not done correctly. It’s best to stick to scissors if you’re new to layering your short hair.

Hair Care for Layered Short Hair

Layering your short hair can make it easier to style, but it also requires a bit of maintenance. Here are some tips for keeping your layered short hair looking its best:

  • Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to add body to your hair.
  • Blow dry your hair upside down to add volume at the roots.
  • Avoid using heavy products, as they can weigh down your hair and make it look flat.

With these tips and our step-by-step guide, you’ll be able to layer your short hair yourself in no time. Happy styling!
