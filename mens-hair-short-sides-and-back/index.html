Are you looking for a stylish and easy-to-maintain haircut? Then the mens hair short sides and back style might be just what you need. This classic look has been popular for decades and is still going strong in 2023. In this guide, we'll show you how to get the perfect mens hair short sides and back cut, care for your hair, and answer some frequently asked questions.

How to Get the Mens Hair Short Sides and Back Cut

The mens hair short sides and back cut is a versatile and low-maintenance hairstyle that works for most face shapes and hair types. It involves trimming the sides and back of the head short while leaving the top longer. This creates a contrast in length that can be tailored to your preference. To get this cut, you can either go to a professional hairstylist or do it yourself at home.

Professional Stylist

If you're not confident in cutting your own hair, it's best to visit a professional stylist. They can give you a precise and tailored cut that suits your facial features and hair type. You can show them a picture of the mens hair short sides and back style you want, and they'll be able to replicate it for you.

DIY

If you prefer to save money and cut your hair at home, you can follow our step-by-step guide below. However, keep in mind that cutting your hair yourself can be tricky and may not turn out as you expect. It's also best to invest in quality hair clippers and scissors to ensure a clean cut.

Step-by-Step Guide

Follow these steps to get the perfect mens hair short sides and back cut:

  1. Wash your hair and towel-dry it.
  2. Comb your hair to remove any tangles or knots.
  3. Use hair clippers to trim the sides and back of your head. Start with a longer guard size and work your way down until you reach the desired length. Be sure to blend the hair at the back of your head into the hair on top.
  4. Use scissors to trim the hair on top of your head. Use a comb to lift the hair and cut it to your preferred length. It's best to cut a little bit of hair at a time to avoid cutting too much off.
  5. Blend the hair on top with the hair on the sides and back using scissors or clippers.
  6. Check for any uneven spots and trim them as needed.
  7. Style your hair using a hair product of your choice.

Tips for Maintaining Your Haircut

To keep your mens hair short sides and back looking sharp and stylish, follow these tips:

  • Get regular trims every 4-6 weeks to maintain the length and shape of your haircut.
  • Use a quality hair product to style your hair and keep it in place. Avoid using too much product, as it can weigh down your hair and make it look greasy.
  • Wash your hair every 2-3 days to keep it clean and healthy. Use a mild shampoo and conditioner that's suitable for your hair type.
  • Avoid using hot tools like hair dryers or straighteners too often, as they can damage your hair and make it look dull.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and wind by wearing a hat or using a hair product with SPF.

FAQ

What face shapes suit the mens hair short sides and back cut?

The mens hair short sides and back cut works well for most face shapes, especially round and oval-shaped faces. It can help to create a more defined jawline and cheekbones.

What hair types suit the mens hair short sides and back cut?

The mens hair short sides and back cut works well for most hair types, including straight, wavy, and curly hair. However, if you have very thin or fine hair, it may not be the best style for you as it can make your hair look flat.

Can I style my mens hair short sides and back cut in different ways?

Yes, you can style your mens hair short sides and back cut in different ways depending on your preference. You can slick it back, comb it to the side, or add volume to the top using a hair product.

Hair Care Tips

To keep your hair healthy and strong, follow these hair care tips:

  • Eat a healthy and balanced diet rich in vitamins and minerals that promote hair growth.
  • Drink plenty of water to keep your hair hydrated and moisturized.
  • Avoid smoking and excessive alcohol consumption, as they can damage your hair and scalp.
  • Use a hair mask or oil treatment once a week to nourish your hair and prevent split ends.
  • Massage your scalp regularly to stimulate blood flow and promote hair growth.

With these tips and tricks, you can achieve the perfect mens hair short sides and back cut and keep your hair healthy and strong. Remember to take care of your hair and enjoy your new stylish look!
