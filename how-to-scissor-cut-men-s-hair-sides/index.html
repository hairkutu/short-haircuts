How To Scissor Cut Men s Hair Sides A Step By Step Guide

Written by Oliver Jun 05, 2023 Â· 7 min read

If you're looking to learn how to scissor cut men's hair sides, you've come to the right place. In this guide, we'll cover everything you need to know about creating a clean and stylish look for your male clients. From the tools you'll need to the techniques you'll use, we'll walk you through the process step by step. So let's get started!

Table of Contents

  • Tools You'll Need
  • Prepping Your Client's Hair
  • Sectioning the Hair
  • Cutting the Hair
  • Blending the Hair
  • Finishing Touches
  • Tips for Success
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care Tips for Your Clients

Tools You'll Need

Before you start cutting, make sure you have the right tools on hand. Here's what you'll need:

  • Scissors
  • Comb
  • Clippers (optional)
  • Clip or hair tie
  • Cape or towel
  • Hair product (optional)

Once you have all your tools, you're ready to prep your client's hair.

Prepping Your Client's Hair

Before you start cutting, you'll need to prep your client's hair. Here's how:

  1. Wash and dry your client's hair
  2. Section off the top of the hair and clip it out of the way
  3. Comb the sides of the hair straight down
  4. Mist the hair with water to keep it damp

Once you've prepped the hair, you're ready to start sectioning.

Sectioning the Hair

Sectioning the hair is an important step in scissor cutting men's hair sides. Here's how to do it:

  1. Start at the temple and create a diagonal parting that goes back toward the crown of the head
  2. Clip the top section out of the way
  3. Continue creating diagonal partings all the way around the head, clipping each section out of the way as you go

Now that the hair is sectioned, you're ready to start cutting.

Cutting the Hair

When cutting men's hair sides, it's important to use the right technique. Here's how to do it:

  1. Take a small section of hair and hold it between your fingers
  2. Cut the hair using a scissor-over-comb technique or point-cutting technique
  3. Work your way around the head, cutting each section of hair to the desired length

Once you've cut all the hair, it's time to blend the sides with the top.

Blending the Hair

Blending the hair is an important step in creating a seamless look. Here's how to do it:

  1. Take down the top section of hair
  2. Comb the hair straight down
  3. Use a scissor-over-comb technique or point-cutting technique to blend the sides with the top

Once you've blended the sides with the top, it's time for the finishing touches.

Finishing Touches

Now that the hair is cut and blended, it's time to add some finishing touches. Here's how:

  1. Use clippers to clean up the hairline and sideburns (optional)
  2. Apply hair product to create the desired style (optional)

Now you're done! Your client should have a clean and stylish look that they'll love.

Tips for Success

Here are some tips to keep in mind as you scissor cut men's hair sides:

  • Keep the hair damp to make it easier to cut
  • Use a light touch when cutting to avoid mistakes
  • Take breaks to avoid fatigue
  • Ask your client for feedback throughout the process

FAQs

Here are some common questions about scissor cutting men's hair sides:

  • What if I make a mistake?

    • If you make a mistake, don't panic. You can always use clippers to fix the mistake or start over if necessary.

  • What if my client doesn't like the cut?

    • If your client doesn't like the cut, ask them what they don't like and try to fix it. Remember, communication is key!

Hair Care Tips for Your Clients

After you've scissor cut your client's hair sides, it's important to give them some hair care tips. Here are some tips to share:

  • Wash and condition the hair regularly
  • Avoid using too much heat on the hair
  • Use a high-quality hair product to maintain the style
  • Get regular trims to keep the hair looking neat

By following these tips, your clients can keep their hair looking great between cuts.

Scissor cutting men's hair sides can seem intimidating at first, but with the right tools and techniques, it's easy to do. By following the steps in this guide, you'll be able to create a clean and stylish look for your male clients. So grab your scissors and get cutting!
