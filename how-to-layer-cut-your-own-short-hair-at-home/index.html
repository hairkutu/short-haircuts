How To Layer Cut Your Own Short Hair At Home

Written by Daniel Jul 11, 2023 · 7 min read

Are you tired of going to the salon every few weeks to get a trim? Or maybe you just want to switch up your look without breaking the bank. Whatever your reason, layer cutting your own short hair at home is easier than you might think. In this article, we’ll go over the steps to create layers in your short hair, as well as some tips and FAQ to ensure your haircut turns out great.

How to Layer Cut Your Own Short Hair at Home

Before we get started, make sure you have the right tools: sharp hair cutting scissors, a comb, and a mirror. Here’s how to layer cut your own short hair at home:

Step 1: Wash Your Hair

Start with clean, dry hair. This will help you see any split ends or uneven areas that need to be trimmed.

Step 2: Section Your Hair

Using your comb, divide your hair into four sections: two on the top and two on the bottom. Clip each section to keep them separate.

Step 3: Cut the Bottom Layer

Start with the bottom layer of your hair. Unclip one of the bottom sections and comb it straight down. Hold the hair between your fingers and cut straight across. Start with a small amount and gradually cut more if needed.

Step 4: Cut the Middle Layer

Unclip one of the top sections and comb it straight down. Hold the hair between your fingers and cut at an angle, starting from the bottom layer. This will create a gradual layer effect.

Step 5: Cut the Top Layer

Repeat step 4 for the other top section. Make sure both sides are even.

Step 6: Check for Evenness

Once you’ve cut all the layers, check to make sure they’re even. If not, trim any areas that need it.

Step 7: Blend the Layers

Using your scissors, blend the layers together by cutting at an angle where they meet. This will create a more natural look.

Step 8: Style Your Hair

Once you’re happy with your layers, style your hair as desired. You can blow dry it straight or use a curling iron to create waves.

Tips for Layer Cutting Your Own Short Hair

Here are some tips to help you achieve the perfect layer cut:

  • Start with small cuts and gradually add more if needed.
  • Use a mirror to see the back of your head.
  • If you have curly hair, cut it while it’s dry to avoid any unexpected shrinkage.
  • Don’t cut too much off at once – you can always cut more later.

FAQ About Layer Cutting Your Own Short Hair

Here are some frequently asked questions about layer cutting your own short hair:

Is it easy to layer cut your own short hair?

With the right tools and some practice, layer cutting your own short hair can be easy.

What length hair is best for layer cutting?

Short hair is best for layer cutting, but it can be done on longer hair as well.

How often should I layer cut my short hair?

It depends on how fast your hair grows and how often you want to change up your look. As a general rule, every 4-6 weeks is a good time frame.

Hair Care After Layer Cutting Your Own Short Hair

After layer cutting your own short hair, it’s important to take care of it properly. Here are some tips:

  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, such as blow dryers and straighteners.
  • Trim any split ends as soon as you notice them to prevent further damage.

Now that you know how to layer cut your own short hair at home, you can save time and money by doing it yourself. Follow these steps and tips for a beautiful, layered haircut.
