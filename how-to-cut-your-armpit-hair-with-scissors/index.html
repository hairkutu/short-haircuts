How To Cut Your Armpit Hair With Scissors

Written by Frank Aug 01, 2023 Â· 7 min read

If you're looking for a way to get rid of your armpit hair without using a razor, scissors may be a good option for you. Not only is it a safer method, but it also allows for more precision when it comes to shaping and trimming your hair. In this article, we'll go over the steps on how to cut your armpit hair with scissors.

How to Cut Your Armpit Hair with Scissors

Cutting your armpit hair with scissors is a simple process, but it does require some patience and care to avoid any accidents. Here are the steps you should follow:

Step 1: Gather Your Materials

First, you'll need to gather a few materials. These include:

  • A pair of small, sharp scissors
  • A comb
  • A mirror

Step 2: Clean and Dry Your Armpits

Before you begin, make sure your armpits are clean and dry. This will make it easier to see the hair you need to trim.

Step 3: Comb Your Armpit Hair

Using a comb, gently comb through your armpit hair to remove any tangles. This will make it easier to see the length of your hair and where you need to trim.

Step 4: Start Trimming

Using your scissors, carefully trim the hair in small sections. Start by trimming a little bit at a time and work your way up to the desired length. Be sure to keep your scissors parallel to your skin to avoid any accidental cuts.

Step 5: Check Your Progress

Periodically check your progress by using a mirror to see the back of your armpit. Continue trimming until you're satisfied with the length.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide to help you cut your armpit hair with scissors:

  1. Gather your materials: small, sharp scissors, comb, and mirror.
  2. Clean and dry your armpits.
  3. Comb your armpit hair.
  4. Start trimming, being careful not to cut your skin.
  5. Check your progress periodically using a mirror.
  6. Continue trimming until you're satisfied with the length.
  7. Clean up any loose hair with a towel or brush.
  8. Rinse your armpits with cold water to soothe any irritation.
  9. Apply a moisturizer or oil to keep your skin hydrated.
  10. Dispose of your scissors and wash your hands.

Tips for Cutting Your Armpit Hair with Scissors

Here are a few tips to keep in mind when cutting your armpit hair with scissors:

  • Use small, sharp scissors for better precision.
  • Trim in small sections to avoid cutting too much at once.
  • Be patient and take your time.
  • Use a mirror to check your progress.
  • Don't cut your hair too short, as it may cause irritation.

FAQs

Can I use regular scissors to cut my armpit hair?

While you can use regular scissors, it's best to use small, sharp scissors for better precision.

How often should I cut my armpit hair?

This depends on how quickly your hair grows. Generally, you should trim your armpit hair every 2-4 weeks.

Will cutting my armpit hair make it grow back thicker?

No, this is a common myth. Cutting your hair does not affect its texture or thickness.

Hair Care for Your Armpit Hair

After cutting your armpit hair, it's important to take care of it to prevent irritation and ingrown hairs. Here are a few tips:

  • Use a gentle cleanser to clean your armpits.
  • Exfoliate once a week to remove dead skin cells.
  • Apply a moisturizer or oil to keep your skin hydrated.
  • Avoid using deodorants with alcohol or fragrances, as they can irritate your skin.
  • Wear loose-fitting clothing to allow your skin to breathe.

Now that you know how to cut your armpit hair with scissors, you can confidently groom yourself without worrying about any accidents. Remember to take your time and be patient, and always prioritize your skin's health and comfort.
