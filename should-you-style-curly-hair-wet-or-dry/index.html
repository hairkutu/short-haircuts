Should You Style Curly Hair Wet Or Dry

Written by Aprilia May 07, 2023 · 9 min read

Curly hair can be a challenge to style, and one of the most common questions is whether to style it wet or dry. In this article, we’ll explore the pros and cons of both methods, and provide you with tips and tricks to achieve the best results.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to Style Curly Hair Wet or Dry
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Styling Curly Hair
  • FAQ
  • Curly Hair Care

Description

Styling curly hair can be a daunting task, especially if you’re not sure whether to do it wet or dry. There are pros and cons to both methods, and the decision ultimately comes down to personal preference and hair type. Styling curly hair wet can help to define curls and reduce frizz, while styling it dry can provide more volume and texture. In this article, we’ll explore both methods and provide you with tips and tricks to achieve the best results.

How to Style Curly Hair Wet or Dry

The method you choose to style your curly hair depends on various factors such as hair type, texture, and desired style. Here are some of the ways you can style curly hair wet or dry:

Wet Styling

Wet styling involves applying styling products to wet hair before styling. This method can help to define curls and reduce frizz. Here are some steps to follow when styling your curly hair wet:

  1. Wash your hair with a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner.
  2. Apply a leave-in conditioner to your wet hair.
  3. Apply a curl-defining cream or gel to your hair, starting from the roots and working your way down to the ends.
  4. Use a diffuser attachment on your blow dryer to dry your hair, using a low heat setting.
  5. Once your hair is dry, use a hair oil or serum to add shine and reduce frizz.

Dry Styling

Dry styling involves applying styling products to dry hair before styling. This method can provide more volume and texture. Here are some steps to follow when styling your curly hair dry:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair.
  2. Apply a volumizing mousse or spray to your hair, focusing on the roots.
  3. Use a diffuser attachment on your blow dryer to dry your hair, using a low heat setting.
  4. Once your hair is dry, use a curl-defining cream or gel to define curls and reduce frizz.
  5. Finish with a hair oil or serum to add shine and reduce frizz.

Step-by-Step Guide

Whether you choose to style your curly hair wet or dry, here’s a step-by-step guide to help you achieve the best results:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair.
  2. Apply a leave-in conditioner to your hair if styling wet, or a volumizing mousse or spray if styling dry.
  3. If styling wet, apply a curl-defining cream or gel to your hair, starting from the roots and working your way down to the ends.
  4. If styling dry, use a diffuser attachment on your blow dryer to dry your hair, using a low heat setting.
  5. Use a curl-defining cream or gel to define curls and reduce frizz.
  6. Finish with a hair oil or serum to add shine and reduce frizz.

Tips for Styling Curly Hair

Styling curly hair can be tricky, but with the right tips and tricks, you can achieve beautiful curls every time. Here are some tips to keep in mind:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid drying out your hair.
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it’s wet, as this can cause breakage and damage.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your hair when it’s wet.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as this can damage your hair and cause frizz.
  • Try different styling products to find what works best for your hair type and texture.
  • Don’t be afraid to experiment with different hairstyles and techniques.

FAQ

Q: Should I style my curly hair wet or dry?

A: The decision to style your curly hair wet or dry ultimately comes down to personal preference and hair type. Styling wet can help to define curls and reduce frizz, while styling dry can provide more volume and texture.

Q: What products should I use to style my curly hair?

A: The products you use to style your curly hair depend on your hair type and texture. Some popular options include curl-defining creams, gels, mousses, and sprays. Look for products that are specifically designed for curly hair, and avoid those that contain sulfates or alcohol.

Q: How often should I wash my curly hair?

A: Curly hair tends to be drier than other hair types, so it’s important to avoid over-washing. Aim to wash your hair no more than once or twice a week, and use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid drying out your hair.

Curly Hair Care

In addition to styling your curly hair, it’s important to take care of it to keep it healthy and looking its best. Here are some tips for curly hair care:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid drying out your hair.
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it’s wet, as this can cause breakage and damage.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your hair when it’s wet.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as this can damage your hair and cause frizz.
  • Wrap your hair in a silk scarf or use a silk pillowcase to reduce frizz and breakage while you sleep.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.

Conclusion

Whether you choose to style your curly hair wet or dry, there are pros and cons to both methods. The decision ultimately comes down to personal preference and hair type. By following the tips and tricks outlined in this article, you can achieve beautiful, healthy curls every time.
