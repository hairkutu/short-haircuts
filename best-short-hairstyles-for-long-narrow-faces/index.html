Best Short Hairstyles For Long Narrow Faces

Written by Bardi Jul 29, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Are you someone with a long narrow face and looking for a perfect short hairstyle that compliments your facial features? Look no further, we have compiled a list of the best short hairstyles for long narrow faces that will leave you looking stunning and confident. In this article, we will cover the how-tos, tips, FAQs, and hair care for these hairstyles.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to style
  • Step by step tutorial
  • Tips
  • FAQs
  • Hair care

Description

Long narrow faces are best suited for short hairstyles that add width and volume to the face. These hairstyles create an illusion of having a wider face and make the features look more proportionate.

The best short hairstyles for long narrow faces include pixie cuts, bob hairstyles, layered hairstyles, asymmetrical cuts, and shaggy hairstyles.

How to Style

The key to styling short hairstyles for long narrow faces is to add volume and texture to the hair. Blow-drying the hair with a round brush adds volume to the roots, while using a texturizing spray or mousse adds texture to the hair.

For a sleek and polished look, use a flat iron to straighten the hair. For a more edgy look, use a curling iron to create loose waves or use a choppy razor cut to add texture to the hair.

Step by Step Tutorial

Here is a step-by-step tutorial on how to style a bob haircut for long narrow faces:

  1. Wash and condition your hair with a volumizing shampoo and conditioner.
  2. Towel dry your hair and apply a volumizing mousse to the roots.
  3. Blow-dry your hair with a round brush, focusing on the roots to add volume.
  4. Use a flat iron to straighten your hair.
  5. Spray a texturizing spray to the ends of your hair to add texture.
  6. Apply a small amount of hair serum to the ends to add shine.

Tips

When choosing a short hairstyle for a long narrow face, avoid hairstyles that are too long or too short. Opt for hairstyles that fall between the chin and the shoulders.

Adding layers to a short hairstyle creates movement and texture, which makes the hair look thicker and adds volume to the face.

When styling a short hairstyle, use a heat protectant spray to prevent damage to the hair.

FAQs

Q: Can I still wear bangs with a short hairstyle for a long narrow face?

A: Yes, you can still wear bangs with a short hairstyle for a long narrow face. Opt for wispy or side-swept bangs that create an illusion of width to the face.

Q: Will a pixie cut suit a long narrow face?

A: Yes, a pixie cut will suit a long narrow face. It creates an illusion of width and adds volume to the face.

Hair Care

Short hairstyles for long narrow faces require regular trims to maintain the shape of the hairstyle. Use a deep conditioning mask once a week to keep the hair moisturized and healthy.

Avoid using hot tools on the hair every day, as it can cause damage and breakage. Use a heat protectant spray before using hot tools on the hair.

In conclusion, short hairstyles for long narrow faces are a great way to enhance your facial features and create an illusion of width to the face. With the right styling techniques and hair care, you can rock any of these hairstyles with confidence.
