How To Cut Short Hair In Back Step By Step Guide And Tips

Written by Frank Apr 18, 2023 Â· 7 min read

If you have short hair, you know that it can be a challenge to maintain the perfect cut. One of the most challenging parts of cutting short hair is getting the back just right. In this post, we'll walk you through how to cut short hair in the back, step-by-step. We'll also offer some tips to help you get the perfect cut every time.

Description

When cutting short hair in the back, it's important to have a plan. Start by deciding how short you want your hair to be. Then, consider the shape of your head and the natural curve of your neck. Finally, choose the right tools for the job, including a good pair of scissors and a comb.

How to Cut Short Hair in Back

Now, let's get started with the step-by-step guide to cutting short hair in the back:

Step 1: Wet Your Hair

Before you start cutting, wet your hair thoroughly. This will help you see the natural shape of your hair and make it easier to cut. Use warm water to wet your hair, and then towel dry it so that it's damp but not dripping.

Step 2: Comb Your Hair

Next, use a comb to part your hair where you want to cut it. This will help you see the line of the cut and ensure that you cut your hair evenly. Make sure to comb your hair straight down so that it's flat against your head.

Step 3: Start Cutting

Now it's time to start cutting. Using your scissors, cut in a straight line across the back of your head. Make sure to cut slowly and carefully, checking your progress as you go.

Step 4: Check Your Progress

As you cut, use a hand mirror to check your progress. This will help you see if you're cutting your hair evenly and if you're following the line you created with your comb. Make adjustments as needed to ensure that your hair is cut evenly.

Step 5: Blend the Sides

Once you've cut the back of your hair, it's time to blend the sides. Use your scissors to cut the sides of your hair at an angle, blending them into the back. Take your time and cut slowly, checking your progress as you go.

Tips for Cutting Short Hair in Back

Now that you know the basics of cutting short hair in the back, here are some tips to help you get the perfect cut every time:

Tip 1: Use Sharp Scissors

Using sharp scissors will make it easier to cut your hair evenly and will help you avoid split ends.

Tip 2: Take Your Time

Don't rush through the cutting process. Take your time and make sure that you're cutting your hair evenly.

Tip 3: Check Your Progress

Use a hand mirror to check your progress as you cut. This will help you see if you're cutting your hair evenly and if you're following the line you created with your comb.

FAQ

Q: Should I cut my hair when it's wet or dry?

A: It's best to cut your hair when it's wet. This will help you see the natural shape of your hair and make it easier to cut.

Q: How often should I cut my short hair?

A: You should cut your short hair every 4-6 weeks to maintain the perfect cut.

Hair Care

After you've cut your hair, it's important to take care of it properly. Use a good shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and moisturized. Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as this can damage your hair. Finally, consider getting regular trims to maintain the perfect cut.

Now that you know how to cut short hair in the back, you can maintain the perfect cut at home. Remember to take your time, use the right tools, and check your progress as you go. With a little practice, you'll be a pro at cutting short hair in no time!
