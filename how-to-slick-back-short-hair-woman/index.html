How To Slick Back Short Hair Woman A Step By Step Guide

If you're looking for a chic and stylish hairstyle that's easy to maintain, slicked back short hair might be the perfect option for you. Whether you're going for a casual or formal look, this hairstyle can be versatile and flattering on any face shape. In this article, we'll go through the steps on how to slick back short hair woman and provide you with some tips and tricks to make the process easier.

How to Slick Back Short Hair Woman

Slick back short hair may seem daunting at first, but with the right tools and techniques, you can easily achieve this sleek and polished look. Here are the basic steps to follow:

Step 1: Wash and Condition Your Hair

Before styling your hair, make sure it's clean and conditioned. Use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and soft. Rinse thoroughly with cool water to avoid frizz and towel dry your hair.

Step 2: Apply a Styling Product

To make your hair more manageable and easier to slick back, apply a styling product such as a mousse, gel or pomade. A dime-sized amount is usually enough for short hair. Rub it between your palms and distribute it evenly throughout your hair, focusing on the roots.

Step 3: Comb Your Hair Back

Using a fine-tooth comb, comb your hair back from the hairline towards the crown of your head. Make sure to comb in the direction that you want your hair to slick back. For a more polished look, use a boar bristle brush to smooth out any bumps or flyaways.

Step 4: Secure Your Hair

Once you've combed your hair back, use bobby pins or hair clips to secure the sides and back of your hair. You can also use a hair elastic to create a low ponytail if you prefer.

Step 5: Finish with Hairspray

To ensure your hair stays in place, finish with a light mist of hairspray. Hold the can about 10 inches away from your head and spray evenly.

Tips for Slick Back Short Hair

While slick back short hair is a relatively simple hairstyle, there are some tips and tricks that can help make the process easier:

Tip 1: Use the Right Products

Choosing the right styling products is key to achieving the perfect slick back short hair. Experiment with different products to find the one that works best for your hair type and texture.

Tip 2: Don't Overdo It

Applying too much product can make your hair look greasy and weighed down. Start with a small amount and add more if needed.

Tip 3: Use a Comb or Brush

Using a comb or brush can help you achieve a smooth and polished look. Make sure to comb or brush in the direction that you want your hair to slick back.

Tip 4: Experiment with Accessories

Adding accessories such as hair clips or headbands can add some extra flair to your slick back short hair. Play around with different styles to find what works best for you.

FAQ

What kind of products should I use for slick back short hair?

The best products for slick back short hair are mousse, gel, and pomade. Choose a product that works best for your hair type and texture.

Can I slick back short hair if it's curly?

Yes, you can slick back short hair even if it's curly. Use a styling product that's designed for curly hair and use a brush or comb to smooth out any bumps.

How often should I wash my hair if I have slick back short hair?

It's recommended to wash your hair every other day if you have slick back short hair. Over-washing can strip your hair of its natural oils and cause it to become dry and brittle.

Hair Care Tips

To keep your slick back short hair looking healthy and shiny, follow these hair care tips:

Tip 1: Use a Moisturizing Shampoo and Conditioner

Moisturizing shampoo and conditioner can help keep your hair soft and healthy. Look for products that are free of sulfates and parabens to avoid drying out your hair.

Tip 2: Avoid Heat Styling

Excessive heat styling can damage your hair and cause it to become dry and brittle. If you must use heat styling tools, make sure to use a heat protectant spray and limit your use to once a week.

Tip 3: Protect Your Hair from Sun Damage

UV rays can cause your hair to become dry and brittle. Protect your hair by wearing a hat or using a leave-in conditioner with SPF.

With these tips and tricks, you can easily achieve a sleek and polished look with slick back short hair. Experiment with different styles and products to find what works best for you. Remember to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and shiny.
