Half Up Hairstyles For Long Hair Wedding A Complete Guide

Written by Daniel Mar 23, 2023 Â· 8 min read

Are you a bride-to-be looking for inspiration for your wedding day hairstyle? If you have long hair, half up hairstyles can be a perfect choice. In this post, we will provide you with a complete guide on half up hairstyles for long hair wedding, including how to create them, step-by-step instructions, tips, FAQ, and hair care.

Table of Content

  1. Description
  2. How to
  3. Step by step
  4. Tips
  5. FAQ
  6. Hair Care

Description

Half up hairstyles are a perfect combination of elegance and practicality. They allow you to show off your long hair while keeping it out of your face. Half up hairstyles can be customized to suit any wedding style, whether it's boho, classic, or modern. They can be adorned with accessories such as flowers, hairpins, and veils to make you look even more stunning on your special day.

How to

To create a half up hairstyle for your wedding day, you will need the following tools: a comb, bobby pins, hair ties, and hairspray.

Step 1: Start with clean, dry hair

Wash and blow-dry your hair, and make sure it's completely dry before you start styling.

Step 2: Create a section

Use a comb to create a section of hair from the top of your head to the back of your ears.

Step 3: Tie the section

Gather the section of hair and tie it into a ponytail using a hair tie.

Step 4: Create volume

Use a comb to tease the hair at the crown of your head to create volume.

Step 5: Pin the ponytail

Take the ponytail and twist it into a bun or braid, then pin it to the back of your head using bobby pins.

Step 6: Add accessories

Add accessories such as flowers or hairpins to enhance the look of your half up hairstyle.

Step by step

Here is a more detailed step-by-step guide on how to create a half up hairstyle for your wedding day:

Step 1: Choose your hairstyle

There are several half up hairstyles to choose from, such as braids, twists, and buns. Choose a style that complements your wedding dress and fits your personal style.

Step 2: Prep your hair

Wash your hair the day before your wedding day to give it time to settle. Apply a leave-in conditioner to keep it moisturized and healthy. Blow-dry your hair and use a curling iron or straightener to create the desired texture.

Step 3: Section your hair

Use a comb to create a section of hair from the top of your head to the back of your ears. Clip the top section out of the way.

Step 4: Create volume

Tease the hair at the crown of your head to create volume. Use a comb to gently backcomb the hair, starting at the roots and working your way down.

Step 5: Tie the section

Gather the section of hair from the back of your ears and tie it into a ponytail using a hair tie.

Step 6: Pin the ponytail

Twist the ponytail into a bun or braid, and pin it to the back of your head using bobby pins.

Step 7: Add accessories

Add accessories such as flowers or hairpins to enhance the look of your half up hairstyle.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you create the perfect half up hairstyle for your wedding day:

  • Experiment with different styles until you find one that works best for you.
  • Use hairspray to keep your hair in place throughout the day.
  • Don't be afraid to add accessories to enhance the look of your hairstyle.
  • Consult with your hairstylist to ensure that your hairstyle complements your wedding dress.

FAQ

Q: Can I create a half up hairstyle if I have short hair?

A: Half up hairstyles work best for long hair. If you have short hair, consider adding extensions to create the desired length.

Q: How long does it take to create a half up hairstyle?

A: The time it takes to create a half up hairstyle depends on the complexity of the style. It can take anywhere from 30 minutes to an hour.

Hair Care

To keep your hair healthy and shiny on your wedding day, follow these hair care tips:

  • Wash your hair with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often.
  • Apply a leave-in conditioner to keep your hair moisturized.
  • Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a UV protection spray.

In conclusion, half up hairstyles are a versatile and elegant choice for long hair wedding. With the right tools and techniques, you can create a stunning hairstyle that complements your wedding dress and showcases your beautiful long hair.
