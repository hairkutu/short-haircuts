How To Know My Face Shape Quiz

Written by Oliver May 11, 2023 Â· 5 min read

Are you confused about what your face shape is? Knowing your face shape is essential when choosing hairstyles, makeup, and even glasses. In this article, we will provide you with a step-by-step guide on how to know my face shape quiz.

Table of Contents

  1. Description
  2. How To
  3. Step by Step
  4. Tips
  5. FAQ
  6. Hair Care

Description

Before we dive into the quiz, let's first describe the five main face shapes: oval, round, square, heart, and diamond. An oval face shape has a lengthened shape with a slightly curved jawline. A round face shape is circular and has a soft jawline. A square face shape has a strong jawline and a square forehead. A heart-shaped face has a wide forehead and a pointed chin. Lastly, a diamond face shape has a narrow forehead and a pointed chin.

How To

The easiest way to determine your face shape is through a quiz. A face shape quiz involves answering a series of questions that will give you a clear idea of the shape of your face.

Step by Step

Here are the steps on how to know my face shape quiz:

  1. Take a selfie of your face with your hair pulled back and no makeup on.
  2. Look straight ahead and position the camera at eye level.
  3. Answer the following questions:
    • What is the widest part of your face? (forehead, cheeks, jawline)
    • What is the shape of your jawline? (rounded, square, pointed)
    • What is the length of your face? (short, medium, long)
    • What is the shape of your forehead? (round, square, pointed)
    • What is the shape of your chin? (round, square, pointed)
  4. After answering the questions, tally up your answers to determine your face shape.
  5. Refer to the description above to identify your face shape.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you determine your face shape:

  • Ask a friend or family member to help you take the selfie.
  • Make sure the lighting is even and not too harsh.
  • Use a ruler or measuring tape to determine the length of your face.
  • Take the quiz multiple times to ensure accuracy.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about face shape:

What is the most common face shape?

The most common face shape is oval.

Can face shape change over time?

No, your face shape remains the same throughout your life.

Can I have a combination of face shapes?

Yes, it is possible to have a combination of face shapes.

Hair Care

Knowing your face shape is crucial when choosing a hairstyle that flatters your features. Here are some hair care tips based on your face shape:

  • Oval face shape: almost any hairstyle will suit you.
  • Round face shape: try a hairstyle that adds height to your crown.
  • Square face shape: opt for a soft, layered hairstyle.
  • Heart face shape: a side-swept hairstyle or bangs will complement your features.
  • Diamond face shape: try a short, layered hairstyle.

Now that you know how to know my face shape quiz, take the quiz and start experimenting with hairstyles and makeup that flatter your features.
