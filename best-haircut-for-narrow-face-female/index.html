The Best Haircut For Narrow Face Female Tips And Ideas

Written by Robby Apr 17, 2023 · 10 min read

If you have a narrow face, you need to choose a haircut that complements your features and enhances your natural beauty. With the right haircut, you can create the illusion of a wider face and add volume and texture to your hair. In this post, we’ve put together some tips and ideas for the best haircut for narrow face female.

How to Choose the Best Haircut for Narrow Face Female

When choosing a haircut, it’s important to consider the shape of your face, as well as your hair type and lifestyle. If you have a narrow face, you want to choose a haircut that adds width and volume to your hair, without overwhelming your features. Here are some things to keep in mind:

Consider Your Face Shape

When choosing a haircut for a narrow face, you want to create the illusion of width and balance. Look for haircuts that add volume to the sides of your face, such as layered cuts, textured bobs, or side-swept bangs. Avoid hairstyles that are too long or too short, as they can elongate your face and make it appear even narrower.

Choose the Right Length

The length of your hair can also make a big difference in how your face looks. If you have a narrow face, you may want to avoid haircuts that are too short, as they can make your face look longer and more narrow. Instead, look for haircuts that fall just below your chin or shoulder-length, as these lengths can help add volume and balance to your face.

Consider Your Hair Type

The texture and thickness of your hair can also play a role in choosing the best haircut for your face shape. If you have thin or fine hair, you may want to choose a haircut that adds volume and texture, such as a layered bob or a shaggy cut. If you have thick or curly hair, you may want to choose a haircut that helps control and define your curls, such as a long layered cut or a curly bob.

Step by Step: Best Haircut for Narrow Face Female

Now that you know what to look for when choosing a haircut for a narrow face, here are some step-by-step instructions for getting the perfect cut:

Step 1: Consult with Your Stylist

Before getting a haircut, it’s important to consult with your stylist about what you want and what will work best for your face shape and hair type. Your stylist can help you choose the right haircut and give you tips for maintaining your new look.

Step 2: Choose the Right Length

Once you’ve consulted with your stylist, it’s time to choose the right length for your haircut. As we mentioned earlier, you want to choose a length that adds volume and balance to your face. This may mean going for a shoulder-length cut or a layered bob.

Step 3: Add Layers and Texture

To add volume and texture to your hair, you may want to consider adding layers and texture to your haircut. This can help create the illusion of width and add movement to your hair.

Step 4: Consider Bangs

If you want to add even more volume and balance to your face, you may want to consider getting bangs. Side-swept bangs or layered bangs can help frame your face and add texture to your hair.

Tips for Maintaining Your Haircut

Once you’ve gotten the perfect haircut for your narrow face, it’s important to maintain it to keep it looking fresh and healthy. Here are some tips for maintaining your new look:

Use the Right Products

Using the right hair products can help keep your hair healthy and looking great. Look for products that are designed for your hair type and use them regularly to keep your hair looking its best.

Get Regular Trims

To keep your haircut looking its best, it’s important to get regular trims. This can help keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends and breakage.

Style Your Hair

To keep your hair looking fresh and stylish, take the time to style it regularly. This can be as simple as using a blow dryer or flat iron to add volume and texture, or as complex as creating a new hairstyle every day.

FAQ: Best Haircut for Narrow Face Female

What is the best haircut for a narrow face?

The best haircut for a narrow face is one that adds volume and balance to your features. This may mean choosing a shoulder-length cut, a layered bob, or side-swept bangs.

What length should I choose for my haircut?

For a narrow face, you want to choose a length that adds volume and balance. This may mean going for a shoulder-length cut or a layered bob.

Should I get bangs?

If you want to add even more volume and balance to your face, you may want to consider getting bangs. Side-swept or layered bangs can help frame your face and add texture to your hair.

Hair Care Tips for Narrow Face Female

Here are some hair care tips to keep your hair looking healthy and beautiful:

Wash your hair regularly

Wash your hair regularly to keep it clean and healthy. Use a mild shampoo and conditioner that are designed for your hair type.

Avoid heat styling

Heat styling can damage your hair and make it more prone to breakage. Try to avoid using heat styling tools as much as possible, or use them sparingly and with a heat protectant product.

Protect your hair from the sun

UV rays from the sun can damage your hair and make it more brittle. Wear a hat or use a hair product that contains SPF to protect your hair from the sun.

Choosing the best haircut for narrow face female may seem daunting, but with the right tips and ideas, you can find a look that complements your features and enhances your natural beauty. Remember to consult with your stylist, consider your face shape and hair type, and maintain your new look with regular trims and styling. With these tips, you’ll have a haircut that looks great and makes you feel confident and beautiful!
