How To Cut Hair In Layers Step By Step At Home

Written by Eveline Jun 02, 2023 · 6 min read

Are you tired of your long, boring hair and looking for a change? Layers are a great way to add depth and texture to your hair, giving it a fresh new look. But going to a salon can be expensive and time-consuming. Why not try cutting your hair in layers at home? It's easier than you think! In this article, we'll guide you through the process step by step so that you can achieve the perfect layered look.

How to Cut Hair in Layers: Step by Step

Step 1: Gather Your Supplies

Before you start cutting your hair, make sure you have all the necessary supplies. You'll need a pair of sharp hair cutting scissors, a comb, and hair ties.

Step 2: Wash and Dry Your Hair

Wash and dry your hair thoroughly before you start cutting. This will make it easier to work with and ensure a more even cut.

Step 3: Divide Your Hair into Sections

Using your comb, divide your hair into sections. Start by parting your hair down the middle and then create two more sections on either side of your head.

Step 4: Start Cutting

Start by cutting the bottom layer of your hair. Use your comb to comb out a small section of hair from the bottom layer and hold it between your fingers. Use your scissors to cut the hair at an angle, moving upwards towards your fingers.

Repeat this process for each section of your hair, making sure to cut at an angle to create layers.

Step 5: Blend the Layers

Once you've cut all the sections, it's time to blend the layers. This will ensure that your hair looks natural and not choppy. Use your scissors to cut into the ends of the hair, blending the layers together.

Step 6: Check the Length

Once you've finished cutting your hair, check the length to make sure it's even. If you notice any uneven spots, go back and trim them.

Step 7: Style Your Hair

Now that you've cut your hair into layers, it's time to style it. You can curl or straighten your hair to show off the layers or leave it natural.

Tips for Cutting Your Hair in Layers

  • Start with small sections of hair to ensure an even cut.
  • Use sharp scissors to prevent split ends.
  • Be patient and take your time.
  • Consult online tutorials or videos for additional guidance.

FAQ

Can I cut my hair in layers if it's curly?

Yes, you can cut your hair in layers even if it's curly. However, it's important to cut curly hair when it's dry to get the best results.

How often should I cut my hair in layers?

It depends on how fast your hair grows and how quickly you want to maintain the layered look. On average, cutting your hair in layers every 3-6 months is recommended.

Hair Care

After cutting your hair in layers, it's important to take care of it to maintain its health and texture. Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner, avoid excessive heat styling, and get regular trims to prevent split ends.

Cutting your hair in layers at home may seem intimidating, but with the right tools and technique, it's a simple process. Follow these steps and tips to achieve a fresh, new look without breaking the bank.
