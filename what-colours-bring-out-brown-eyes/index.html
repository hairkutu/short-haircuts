What Colours Bring Out Brown Eyes A Guide

Written by Oliver Jul 07, 2023 Â· 7 min read

If you have brown eyes, you're in luck! Brown eyes are one of the most versatile eye colors when it comes to makeup and fashion. However, if you're looking to enhance your brown eyes, you might be wondering what colors bring them out the best. In this guide, we'll go over the best colors to make your brown eyes pop.

How to Enhance Your Brown Eyes

First, it's important to understand what makes brown eyes unique. Brown eyes have a warm, earthy tone that can vary from light hazel to deep chocolate. The key to enhancing brown eyes is to choose colors that complement this warm tone.

One way to enhance your brown eyes is to use makeup. Choosing the right eyeshadow colors can make a big difference in how your eyes look. Another way to bring out your brown eyes is to choose clothing colors that complement your eye color.

Makeup Tips for Brown Eyes

When it comes to eyeshadow, there are a variety of colors that can enhance brown eyes. Some of the best eyeshadow colors for brown eyes include:

  • Warm, earthy tones like gold, bronze, and copper
  • Deep purples and plums
  • Rich greens
  • Dark blues

These colors complement the warm undertones in brown eyes and make them stand out. When applying eyeshadow, be sure to blend well to create a seamless look.

Clothing Colors for Brown Eyes

Another way to enhance your brown eyes is to choose clothing colors that complement your eye color. Some of the best clothing colors for brown eyes include:

  • Warm neutrals like beige, tan, and camel
  • Rich jewel tones like emerald, sapphire, and ruby
  • Earthy greens and browns
  • Deep purples and plums
  • Bold oranges and yellows

These colors bring out the warmth in brown eyes and make them pop. When choosing clothing colors, consider the occasion and your personal style.

Hair Colors for Brown Eyes

Hair color can also play a role in enhancing brown eyes. Some of the best hair colors for brown eyes include:

  • Warm, golden blondes
  • Rich, chocolate browns
  • Deep reds

These hair colors complement the warmth in brown eyes and create a cohesive look. When choosing a hair color, consider your skin tone as well for the most flattering look.

Frequently Asked Questions

What colors should I avoid if I have brown eyes?

While there are many colors that can enhance brown eyes, there are some colors to avoid. Cool, icy colors like silver and pastels can make brown eyes look dull.

Can I wear black if I have brown eyes?

Yes! Black is a versatile color that looks great with brown eyes. However, if you're looking to enhance your brown eyes, try pairing black with warm, earthy tones like gold or bronze.

Do I need to match my clothing and makeup to my eye color?

No, you don't need to match your clothing and makeup to your eye color. However, choosing colors that complement your eye color can create a cohesive look.

Hair Care Tips for Brown Eyes

If you're looking to enhance your brown eyes, taking care of your hair is important. Here are some hair care tips for brown eyes:

  • Use a shampoo and conditioner that's designed for your hair type
  • Avoid using hot tools like flat irons and curling irons too often
  • Protect your hair from the sun with a hat or scarf
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair healthy

By taking care of your hair, you can create a healthy, shiny look that complements your brown eyes.

Conclusion

Brown eyes are a beautiful and versatile eye color that can be enhanced with the right colors. By choosing warm, earthy tones for makeup and clothing, and complementing your eye color with the right hair color, you can make your brown eyes pop. Remember to have fun with your look and experiment with different colors to find what works best for you.
