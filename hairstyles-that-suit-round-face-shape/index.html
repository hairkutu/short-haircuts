Hairstyles That Suit Round Face Shape

Written by Aprilia Aug 06, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Are you tired of trying out different hairstyles that don't seem to suit your round face shape? Worry no more as we've got you covered. In this article, we'll be discussing hairstyles that are perfect for those with round faces. Read on to discover the best hairstyles that will enhance your features and make you look fabulous!

Description

Round faces tend to have fuller cheeks and a softer jawline. When choosing a hairstyle, it's important to create the illusion of an oval face shape. The key is to add height and length to your face to balance out the roundness. Hairstyles that are layered, have volume on top, and are longer in length tend to work best for those with round faces.

How to

When selecting a hairstyle, consider the length of your hair, your lifestyle, and the shape of your face. Look for hairstyles that have layers, side-swept bangs, or a long side part. These will create the illusion of length and balance out your round face shape. Avoid hairstyles that are too short or too blunt, as they can make your face appear wider.

Step by Step

Step 1: Determine Your Face Shape

Before selecting a hairstyle, it's important to determine your face shape. This will help you choose a hairstyle that flatters your features. To determine your face shape, measure the width of your forehead, cheekbones, and jawline. If all three are roughly the same, you have a round face shape.

Step 2: Select a Hairstyle

Once you've determined your face shape, it's time to select a hairstyle. Look for hairstyles that add height and length to your face. This can be achieved through layers, side-swept bangs, or a long side part. Avoid hairstyles that are too blunt or too short.

Step 3: Consult with a Hairstylist

If you're unsure about which hairstyle to choose, consult with a hairstylist. They'll be able to recommend a hairstyle that flatters your features and suits your lifestyle. They may also suggest adding highlights or lowlights to create dimension and depth.

Tips

  • Choose hairstyles that add height and length to your face
  • Consider adding layers, side-swept bangs, or a long side part
  • Avoid hairstyles that are too blunt or too short
  • Consult with a hairstylist if you're unsure about which hairstyle to choose
  • Consider adding highlights or lowlights to create dimension and depth

FAQ

What hairstyles should I avoid if I have a round face shape?

Avoid hairstyles that are too short or too blunt. These can make your face appear wider. Instead, opt for hairstyles that add height and length to your face.

What hairstyles should I consider if I have a round face shape?

Consider hairstyles that have layers, side-swept bangs, or a long side part. These will create the illusion of length and balance out your round face shape.

Should I consult with a hairstylist before choosing a hairstyle?

If you're unsure about which hairstyle to choose, it's always a good idea to consult with a hairstylist. They'll be able to recommend a hairstyle that flatters your features and suits your lifestyle.

Hair Care

When choosing a hairstyle, it's important to consider the health of your hair. Keep your hair in good condition by using a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner. Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as this can cause damage. Additionally, trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends and breakage.

Now that you know the best hairstyles for round face shapes, it's time to try them out! Remember to choose a hairstyle that flatters your features and suits your lifestyle. Consult with a hairstylist if you're unsure about which hairstyle to choose. With the right hairstyle, you'll look and feel fabulous!
