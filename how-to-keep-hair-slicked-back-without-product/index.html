How To Keep Hair Slicked Back Without Product

Written by Petter Jun 02, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Are you tired of using hair products to keep your hair slicked back? Not only can they be expensive, but they can also damage your hair over time. Luckily, there are natural ways to achieve a sleek, slicked-back hairstyle without using any product. In this article, we will show you how to keep hair slicked back without product in a few easy steps.

Description

Keeping your hair slicked back can be a great way to keep your look polished and professional. However, many people find that using hair products to achieve this style can be time-consuming and damaging to their hair. In this article, we will show you how to keep your hair slicked back without product, using simple techniques that you can do at home.

How to Keep Hair Slicked Back Without Product: Step by Step

Step 1: Start with Clean Hair

The first step in achieving a sleek, slicked-back hairstyle without product is to start with clean hair. This will help to prevent any buildup that can make your hair look greasy or weighed down. Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner to clean your hair, and rinse thoroughly to ensure that all products are removed.

Step 2: Brush Your Hair Back

Once your hair is clean, use a brush to comb it straight back from your forehead. Make sure to brush all of your hair back, including any flyaways or baby hairs around your hairline. Use a fine-toothed comb to smooth out any bumps or tangles.

Step 3: Use a Headband

If you have long hair, use a headband to keep it in place. Choose a headband that matches your hair color, and place it around your head, just behind your hairline. This will help to keep your hair in place and prevent it from falling forward.

Step 4: Secure Your Hair with Bobby Pins

If you have shorter hair, use bobby pins to hold it in place. Take small sections of hair and twist them tightly, then secure them to your scalp with bobby pins. Continue doing this until all of your hair is pinned back.

Step 5: Finish with Hairspray

Once your hair is in place, use a light mist of hairspray to hold it in place. Choose a hairspray that is lightweight and won't make your hair feel crunchy or stiff. Hold the hairspray about six inches away from your head, and spray lightly all over your hair.

Tips for Keeping Hair Slicked Back Without Product

Here are a few tips to help you keep your hair slicked back without using any product:

  • Use a silk or satin pillowcase to prevent frizz and breakage while you sleep.
  • Avoid touching your hair too much, as this can cause it to become oily and greasy.
  • Use dry shampoo between washes to help absorb any excess oil and keep your hair looking fresh.
  • Try using a boar bristle brush to help distribute your hair's natural oils and keep it looking shiny and healthy.

FAQs

Can I use gel or pomade to keep my hair slicked back?

Yes, you can use gel or pomade to keep your hair slicked back, but these products can be heavy and can weigh your hair down over time. If you prefer to use products, choose a lightweight option and use it sparingly.

How often should I wash my hair?

It's best to wash your hair every two to three days, depending on your hair type and how oily it gets. Over-washing can strip your hair of its natural oils, which can cause it to become dry and brittle.

Hair Care: Keeping Your Hair Healthy

Whether you're using products or not, it's important to take good care of your hair to keep it looking healthy and shiny. Here are a few tips to help you keep your hair in top condition:

  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as this can cause damage and breakage.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to help nourish and repair your hair.
  • Eat a healthy diet that is rich in vitamins and minerals to help promote healthy hair growth.
  • Avoid using harsh chemicals or dyes on your hair, as these can cause damage and breakage.

By following these tips and techniques, you can keep your hair slicked back without using any product, while still keeping your hair healthy and strong.
