How To Style Men s Short Hair With Gel A Complete Guide

Written by Robby Aug 26, 2023 · 6 min read

If you have short hair and want to add some texture and hold, then styling with gel is a great option. However, knowing how to use gel correctly can be tricky. In this guide, we’ll go through the steps on how to style men’s short hair with gel for a polished and put-together look.

Table of Contents

  • How to Style Men's Short Hair with Gel
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Styling Men's Short Hair with Gel
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care Tips for Men's Short Hair

How to Style Men's Short Hair with Gel

The first step in styling men's short hair with gel is to choose the right gel for your hair type. Look for a gel that is specifically designed for short hair and provides a strong hold.

Once you have your gel, start with clean, towel-dried hair. Apply a small amount of gel to your fingertips and work it evenly through your hair, starting from the roots and working your way to the tips.

Next, use a comb or your fingers to style your hair as desired. Remember to use small, controlled motions to avoid clumps or excess gel.

Finally, allow your hair to air dry or use a blow dryer on a low heat setting to speed up the process.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here is a more detailed step-by-step guide on how to style men’s short hair with gel:

  1. Start with clean, towel-dried hair.
  2. Take a small amount of gel and apply it to your fingertips.
  3. Work the gel evenly through your hair, starting from the roots and working your way to the tips.
  4. Use a comb or your fingers to style your hair as desired.
  5. Use small, controlled motions to avoid clumps or excess gel.
  6. Allow your hair to air dry or use a blow dryer on a low heat setting to speed up the process.

Tips for Styling Men's Short Hair with Gel

Here are some additional tips to keep in mind when styling men’s short hair with gel:

  • Use a small amount of gel to start and add more as needed.
  • Avoid using too much gel, as it can make your hair look greasy or weighed down.
  • Experiment with different hair styles to find the one that works best for you.
  • Use a high-quality gel that is specifically designed for short hair.

FAQs

Can I style my hair with gel every day?

Yes, you can style your hair with gel every day, but it’s important to use a high-quality gel and avoid using too much product.

How do I remove gel from my hair?

To remove gel from your hair, simply wash your hair with shampoo and warm water.

Hair Care Tips for Men's Short Hair

Keeping your hair healthy and well-maintained is important for achieving a great style. Here are some hair care tips for men's short hair:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using hot tools on your hair, such as blow dryers or straighteners, as they can damage your hair.
  • Use a hair mask once a week to nourish and hydrate your hair.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends and maintain a neat appearance.

By following these tips and using the right gel, you can achieve a polished and put-together look with your short hair.
