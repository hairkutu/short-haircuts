Best Haircut For Big Forehead And Round Face Flattering Hairstyles For You

Written by William Jul 17, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Are you struggling to find the perfect haircut that flatters your big forehead and round face? Look no further, as we have compiled a list of the best haircuts that will accentuate your features and make you feel confident! In this article, we will discuss how to choose the right haircut, step-by-step instructions, tips, frequently asked questions, and hair care tips to maintain your new look.

Description

A big forehead and round face can be a challenge when it comes to finding the right hairstyle. It's important to choose a cut that balances your proportions and draws attention away from your forehead. A great haircut can enhance your features, boost your self-esteem, and make you feel beautiful.

How to Choose the Right Haircut

When choosing a haircut for a big forehead and round face, there are a few things to consider:

  • Length: Shorter haircuts that are above the chin will make your forehead appear larger, while longer haircuts that fall below the chin can balance out your features.
  • Bangs: Bangs can be a great way to cover a big forehead and add dimension to your face, but be careful not to go too short or too heavy.
  • Layers: Layered haircuts can add volume and texture to your hair, but avoid layers that start too high on your head as they can accentuate your forehead.

Step-by-Step Instructions

Now that you know what to consider when choosing a haircut, let's go through some step-by-step instructions:

  1. Start by washing and drying your hair
  2. Part your hair in the middle and section it off
  3. Choose the desired length and style
  4. Cut your hair in small sections, starting from the back and working your way forward
  5. Blend the layers and check for any unevenness
  6. Style your hair as desired

Tips

Here are some additional tips to keep in mind:

  • Consider face-framing layers or wispy bangs to soften your features
  • Avoid blunt cuts or heavy bangs that can make your forehead appear larger
  • Experiment with different styles and lengths to find what works best for you
  • Consult with a hairstylist for professional advice
  • Invest in quality hair care products to maintain your new haircut

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions:

Q: What is the best haircut for a big forehead and round face?

A: A long layered haircut with side-swept bangs is a great option.

Q: Should I avoid short haircuts?

A: It's not necessary to avoid short haircuts, but keep in mind that they can make your forehead appear larger.

Q: Can I still wear my hair up?

A: Yes, you can still wear your hair up in a ponytail or bun, but consider leaving some pieces in the front to frame your face.

Hair Care

After getting your new haircut, it's important to maintain it with proper hair care. Here are some tips:

  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often
  • Invest in a quality hairbrush to prevent breakage
  • Use a hair mask or deep conditioner once a week
  • Trim your hair every 6-8 weeks to maintain the shape

Now that you know the best haircut for big forehead and round face, it's time to schedule that appointment with your hairstylist! Remember to choose a cut that makes you feel confident and beautiful. With the right haircut and proper hair care, you'll be turning heads in no time!
