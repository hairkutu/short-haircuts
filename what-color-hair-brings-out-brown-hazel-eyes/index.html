What Color Hair Brings Out Brown Hazel Eyes

Written by Petter Mar 26, 2023 Â· 6 min read

If you have brown hazel eyes, you may be wondering what color hair will bring out your natural eye color. The good news is that there are several hair colors that can complement and enhance the beauty of your brown hazel eyes.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to
  • Step by Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

Brown hazel eyes are a beautiful combination of brown and green. They are often described as being warm, rich, and inviting. To bring out the natural beauty of your brown hazel eyes, it's important to choose a hair color that will complement your eye color and skin tone.

How to

Choosing the right hair color to bring out your brown hazel eyes is easier than you might think. Here are some tips to help you choose the perfect shade:

Consider Your Skin Tone

Your skin tone plays a big role in determining which hair color will look best with your brown hazel eyes. If you have warm undertones, consider hair colors with golden or honey tones, such as caramel, chestnut, or auburn. If you have cool undertones, consider hair colors with ashy or cool tones, such as ash brown, platinum blonde, or silver.

Think About Your Natural Hair Color

Your natural hair color can also play a role in determining which hair color will bring out your brown hazel eyes. If you have naturally dark hair, consider adding some subtle highlights or lowlights to create depth and dimension. If you have lighter hair, consider going a few shades darker to create contrast.

Experiment with Different Shades

The best way to find the perfect hair color for your brown hazel eyes is to experiment with different shades. Try on wigs or use virtual try-on tools to see how different hair colors will look with your skin tone and eye color.

Step by Step

Here's a step-by-step guide to choosing the perfect hair color for your brown hazel eyes:

  1. Determine your skin tone.
  2. Consider your natural hair color.
  3. Experiment with different shades.
  4. Consult with a professional stylist.

By following these steps, you can find the perfect hair color to bring out the natural beauty of your brown hazel eyes.

Tips

Here are some additional tips to help you choose the perfect hair color for your brown hazel eyes:

  • Consider your lifestyle and how much maintenance you're willing to do.
  • Ask for recommendations from friends or a professional stylist.
  • Try on wigs or use virtual try-on tools to see how different hair colors will look.
  • Consider adding highlights or lowlights to create depth and dimension.
  • Don't be afraid to try something new!

FAQ

What hair color brings out brown hazel eyes?

Hair colors with golden or honey tones, such as caramel, chestnut, or auburn, can bring out the natural beauty of brown hazel eyes.

What hair color makes hazel eyes look more green?

Hair colors with cool tones, such as ash brown or platinum blonde, can make hazel eyes look more green.

Can I dye my hair at home?

While you can dye your hair at home, it's recommended that you consult with a professional stylist to ensure that you achieve the desired results.

Hair Care

After you've chosen the perfect hair color for your brown hazel eyes, it's important to take care of your hair to keep it looking healthy and vibrant. Here are some tips for maintaining your new hair color:

  • Use a color-safe shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid washing your hair too frequently.
  • Avoid using hot tools too often.
  • Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a UV protectant spray.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair looking healthy and prevent split ends.

By following these tips, you can keep your hair looking beautiful and vibrant, and bring out the natural beauty of your brown hazel eyes.
