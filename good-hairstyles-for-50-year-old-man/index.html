Good Hairstyles For 50 Year Old Man Tips And Tricks

Written by Christine Jul 05, 2023 Â· 6 min read

As men get older, their hair tends to thin out and become grayer. However, this doesn't mean that they have to settle for boring or outdated hairstyles. There are plenty of good hairstyles for 50 year old men that can make them look stylish, confident, and suave. In this article, we will discuss some of the best hairstyles for 50 year old men and provide tips on how to achieve them.

How to Choose the Right Hairstyle

Before we dive into specific hairstyles, it's important to note that not all hairstyles are suitable for every man. When choosing a hairstyle, you should consider your face shape, hair texture, and lifestyle. For example, if you have a round face, you may want to avoid hairstyles that make your face appear even rounder. If you have thinning hair, you may want to opt for a shorter hairstyle that adds volume.

Step-by-Step Guide to Achieving Good Hairstyles for 50 Year Old Man

1. Slicked Back Hair

This hairstyle is perfect for men who have thick hair and a square or oval face shape. To achieve this look, apply a pomade or gel to damp hair and comb it back. Use a hair dryer to dry your hair while combing it back to create a sleek and polished look.

2. Short and Textured Hair

If you have thinning hair or a receding hairline, a short and textured hairstyle can help to create the illusion of volume. To achieve this look, ask your barber for a short haircut with textured layers. Use a styling product like a clay or wax to add definition and texture to your hair.

3. Buzz Cut

The buzz cut is a classic hairstyle that never goes out of style. It's also a great option for men who are experiencing hair loss or want a low maintenance hairstyle. To achieve this look, ask your barber to use clippers to cut your hair short all over.

Tips for Maintaining Good Hairstyles for 50 Year Old Man

1. Use a good quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and shiny.

2. Avoid using too much product, as this can weigh down your hair and make it look greasy.

3. Visit your barber regularly to maintain your hairstyle and trim any split ends.

4. Experiment with different hairstyles to find the one that suits you best.

FAQs

Q: Can I dye my hair if I'm over 50?

A: Yes, you can dye your hair at any age. However, it's important to choose a color that complements your skin tone and looks natural.

Q: How often should I wash my hair?

A: It depends on your hair type and lifestyle. If you have oily hair, you may need to wash it every day. If you have dry hair, you may only need to wash it once or twice a week.

Hair Care Tips for Good Hairstyles for 50 Year Old Man

1. Use a wide-toothed comb to detangle your hair when it's wet.

2. Avoid using hot tools like blow dryers and straighteners too often, as this can damage your hair.

3. Eat a healthy diet that's rich in vitamins and minerals to promote healthy hair growth.

4. Protect your hair from the sun and harsh weather conditions by wearing a hat or using a leave-in conditioner.

With these tips and tricks, you can achieve a good hairstyle that makes you look and feel your best. Remember to choose a hairstyle that suits your face shape and hair texture, and don't be afraid to experiment with different styles.
