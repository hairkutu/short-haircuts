What Hairstyle Suits Oval Face A Guide To Flattering Haircuts

Written by Frank Jul 24, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Are you wondering what hairstyle suits oval face? Look no further! This guide will help you choose the perfect haircut to flatter your features and highlight your natural beauty. Whether you have long or short hair, straight or curly locks, there is a hairstyle that will suit your oval face shape.

Description: What is an Oval Face Shape?

An oval face shape is characterized by balanced proportions and a gently rounded hairline. This face shape is considered to be the most versatile and can pull off a wide range of hairstyles. If you have an oval face shape, you have the freedom to experiment with different haircuts and styles without worrying about unflattering results.

How to Determine Your Face Shape

If you are unsure whether you have an oval face shape, you can determine it by measuring the width of your forehead, cheeks, and jawline. If all three measurements are roughly the same, you have an oval face shape. Another way to determine your face shape is to take a selfie and trace the outline of your face on the photo. This will give you a clear idea of your face shape.

Step by Step Guide to Choosing the Perfect Hairstyle for Oval Face Shape

Step 1: Consider Your Hair Texture

The first step in choosing the perfect hairstyle for oval face shape is to consider your hair texture. If you have fine hair, you may want to opt for a layered haircut to add volume and movement. If you have thick hair, you may want to go for a blunt haircut to add structure and definition.

Step 2: Think About Your Personality

Your hairstyle should reflect your personality and personal style. If you are a free-spirited individual, you may want to go for a messy, bohemian hairstyle. If you are more conservative, you may want to opt for a classic, polished haircut.

Step 3: Consider Your Lifestyle

Your hairstyle should also be practical for your lifestyle. If you are always on the go, you may want to opt for a low-maintenance haircut. If you have a more relaxed lifestyle, you may want to go for a more elaborate hairstyle.

Step 4: Consult with Your Hairstylist

Your hairstylist is an expert in haircuts and can help you choose the perfect hairstyle for your oval face shape. They can also provide you with tips on how to maintain your hairstyle and keep it looking its best.

Tips for Flattering Hairstyles for Oval Face Shape

Here are some tips for choosing a flattering hairstyle for oval face shape:

  • Opt for a haircut that highlights your cheekbones and jawline.
  • Avoid hairstyles that add too much volume on top of your head.
  • Consider adding layers to your hair to add movement and texture.
  • Choose a hairstyle that complements your natural hair texture.
  • Try different hairstyles and experiment with different looks to find your perfect match.

FAQs

What is the most flattering hairstyle for oval face shape?

The most flattering hairstyle for oval face shape is one that highlights your cheekbones and jawline. A layered haircut or a bob with side-swept bangs are great options.

Can oval face shape pull off any hairstyle?

Yes, oval face shape is considered to be the most versatile and can pull off a wide range of hairstyles.

Hair Care for Oval Face Shape

To keep your hairstyle looking its best, it is important to take good care of your hair. Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner that is suitable for your hair type. Avoid using too much heat on your hair and use a heat protectant spray before styling. Trim your hair regularly to maintain its shape and prevent split ends.

Now that you know what hairstyle suits oval face shape, you can confidently choose the perfect haircut to flatter your features and highlight your natural beauty. Remember to consult with your hairstylist and experiment with different looks to find the perfect match.
