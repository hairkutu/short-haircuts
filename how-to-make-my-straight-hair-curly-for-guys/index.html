How To Make My Straight Hair Curly For Guys A Complete Guide

Written by Frank May 16, 2023 · 12 min read

If you're a guy with straight hair and you're looking for a change, curly hair might be just what you need. But how do you make your hair curly? In this article, we'll cover everything you need to know about how to make your straight hair curly for guys.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to Make My Straight Hair Curly for Guys
  • Step by Step Guide
  • Tips to Consider
  • Frequently Asked Questions
  • Hair Care for Curly Hair

Description

There are many ways to make straight hair curly, but the most popular methods are using heat styling tools, chemical treatments, or natural methods. Each method has its pros and cons, and the best one for you will depend on your hair type, lifestyle, and preferences.

Heat styling tools, such as curling irons and hot rollers, can create beautiful curls quickly and easily. However, they can also damage your hair if not used properly, and the curls may not last long. Chemical treatments, such as perming or relaxing, can permanently change the texture of your hair, but they can also be harsh on your scalp and hair. Natural methods, such as braiding, twisting, or using foam rollers, can create soft and bouncy curls without damaging your hair, but they may take longer to achieve and may not work for everyone.

How to Make My Straight Hair Curly for Guys

Here are some of the most effective ways to make your straight hair curly for guys:

1. Use a Curling Iron

A curling iron is a classic tool for creating curls. To use a curling iron, follow these steps:

  1. Divide your hair into small sections
  2. Clamp the curling iron at the base of your hair
  3. Twist the curling iron away from your face
  4. Hold for a few seconds
  5. Release the curling iron and let the curl cool
  6. Repeat on all sections of your hair
  7. Finish with a hairspray or a curl-defining product

Curling irons come in different sizes, from small to large, so choose the one that suits your desired curl size. For a natural look, vary the direction and size of the curls.

2. Try Hot Rollers

Hot rollers are another heat styling tool that can create curls. To use hot rollers, follow these steps:

  1. Heat up the rollers
  2. Divide your hair into small sections
  3. Roll each section onto a hot roller
  4. Let the rollers cool
  5. Remove the rollers
  6. Finish with a hairspray or a curl-defining product

Hot rollers come in different sizes and materials, such as ceramic or foam, so choose the one that suits your hair type and desired curl size. Hot rollers can be left in your hair for a longer time than curling irons, so you can do other things while waiting for the curls to set.

3. Get a Perm

A perm is a chemical treatment that can permanently curl your hair. To get a perm, follow these steps:

  1. Choose a salon and a stylist who specializes in perms
  2. Consult with the stylist about the perm style, size, and maintenance
  3. Prepare your hair by washing and conditioning it
  4. Apply the perm solution to your hair
  5. Wait for the perm to set
  6. Rinse the perm solution out
  7. Apply the neutralizer to your hair
  8. Rinse the neutralizer out
  9. Style your hair as desired

Perms can be costly and time-consuming, and they can damage your hair if not done properly. However, they can also give you long-lasting and low-maintenance curls, especially if you have thick and coarse hair.

Step by Step Guide

Here is a step-by-step guide on how to make your straight hair curly for guys using natural methods:

1. Wash and Condition Your Hair

Start with clean and moisturized hair. Use a shampoo and conditioner that are suitable for curly hair, and avoid using products that contain sulfates, silicones, or alcohol, as they can dry out your hair and scalp.

2. Towel Dry Your Hair

Gently squeeze out the excess water from your hair using a soft towel. Do not rub your hair, as it can cause frizz and breakage.

3. Apply a Curl-Enhancing Product

Apply a curl-enhancing product, such as a mousse, gel, or cream, to your hair. Distribute the product evenly from roots to ends, and scrunch your hair to enhance the curls.

4. Braid or Twist Your Hair

Divide your hair into small sections and braid or twist each section. Use small elastics or bobby pins to secure the ends of the braids or twists. The tighter the braids or twists, the tighter the curls will be.

5. Let Your Hair Dry

Let your hair air dry or use a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer to dry your hair. Do not touch your hair while it's drying, as it can disturb the curls and cause frizz.

6. Remove the Braids or Twists

Once your hair is completely dry, remove the elastics or bobby pins and gently unravel the braids or twists. Use your fingers to separate the curls and create volume.

7. Finish with a Hairspray

Finish with a light-hold hairspray or a curl-defining product to set the curls and prevent frizz.

Tips to Consider

Here are some tips to consider when making your straight hair curly for guys:

  • Use a heat protectant spray or serum before using heat styling tools to prevent damage to your hair.
  • Do not use heat styling tools or chemical treatments too frequently, as they can weaken your hair and cause breakage.
  • Experiment with different curl sizes and styles to find the one that suits your face shape and personality.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your hair, and avoid using a brush, as it can disrupt the curls.
  • Protect your hair from the sun, wind, and humidity by wearing a hat or using a scarf.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I make my straight hair curly without heat or chemicals?

Yes, you can make your straight hair curly using natural methods, such as braiding, twisting, or using foam rollers. These methods can create soft and bouncy curls without damaging your hair, but they may take longer to achieve and may not work for everyone.

How long do the curls last?

The duration of the curls depends on the method you used, your hair type, and the weather conditions. Heat styling tools and chemical treatments can create longer-lasting curls, but they can also damage your hair. Natural methods may create shorter-lasting curls, but they can be gentler on your hair.

How do I maintain my curly hair?

To maintain your curly hair, use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner that are suitable for curly hair. Avoid washing your hair too frequently, as it can dry out your hair and scalp. Use a deep conditioner or hair mask once a week to moisturize your hair. Use a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer to dry your hair, and avoid rubbing your hair with a towel. Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your hair, and avoid using a brush.

Hair Care for Curly Hair

Curly hair requires special care to stay healthy and beautiful. Here are some tips for hair care for curly hair:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner that are suitable for curly hair.
