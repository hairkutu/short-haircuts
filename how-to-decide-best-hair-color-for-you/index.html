How To Decide Best Hair Color For You

Written by Petter Aug 13, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Choosing the perfect hair color can be a daunting task, especially if you are doing it for the first time. With so many shades and hues available, it can be challenging to decide which one will suit you the best. In this article, we will guide you on how to decide the best hair color for you.

Table of Content

  • How to
  • Step by step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

How to

The first step in deciding the best hair color for you is to determine your skin tone. Skin tone is the underlying pigmentation of your skin, and it can be cool, warm or neutral. If you have cool undertones, you have pink, red or blue undertones in your skin. If you have warm undertones, you have yellow, peach or golden undertones in your skin. If you have neutral undertones, you have a balance of both cool and warm undertones.

Once you have determined your skin tone, you can choose a hair color that complements it. If you have cool undertones, choose a hair color with cool undertones like ash blonde, platinum blonde or cool brown. If you have warm undertones, choose a hair color with warm undertones like golden blonde, honey blonde or warm brown. If you have neutral undertones, you can choose a hair color with either cool or warm undertones.

Step by step

Here are the steps to help you decide the best hair color for you:

  1. Determine your skin tone.
  2. Choose a hair color that complements your skin tone.
  3. Consider your natural hair color.
  4. Think about the maintenance required.
  5. Consider your lifestyle.
  6. Try on wigs or use virtual hair color apps.
  7. Consult a professional hairstylist.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you choose the best hair color for you:

  • Consider your eye color as well.
  • Take inspiration from celebrities with similar skin tones.
  • Choose a hair color that complements your wardrobe.
  • Choose a hair color that matches your personality.
  • Don't be afraid to try something new.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about choosing the best hair color:

1. Can I dye my hair a lighter color?

Yes, you can dye your hair a lighter color, but you may need to bleach it first, especially if you have dark hair. Bleaching can damage your hair, so it's best to consult a professional hairstylist.

2. Can I dye my hair a darker color?

Yes, you can dye your hair a darker color, and it's easier than dyeing it a lighter color. However, if you want to go from light to dark, you may need to use a color remover first, especially if you have dyed your hair before.

3. Can I dye my hair a different color every month?

Yes, you can dye your hair a different color every month, but it's not recommended as it can damage your hair. It's best to wait at least 6-8 weeks between dyeing sessions.

Hair Care

After dyeing your hair, it's essential to take care of it to maintain its color and health. Here are some hair care tips for color-treated hair:

  • Use a shampoo and conditioner specifically designed for color-treated hair.
  • Avoid washing your hair too often as it can fade the color.
  • Avoid using hot tools like blow dryers, curling irons and straighteners too often.
  • Avoid exposing your hair to direct sunlight for too long as it can fade the color.
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends.

By following these tips, you can choose the best hair color for you and maintain its color and health.
