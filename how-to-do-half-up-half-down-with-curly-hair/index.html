How To Do Half Up Half Down With Curly Hair A Step By Step Guide

Written by Aprilia May 24, 2023 · 8 min read

Description

If you have curly hair, you know that styling it can be a challenge. One hairstyle that can work well with curly hair is the half up half down look. This style is perfect for both formal and casual occasions, and it’s relatively easy to do. In this article, we’ll give you a step-by-step guide on how to do half up half down with curly hair, as well as some tips and tricks to help you perfect the look.

How To

To achieve the half up half down look with curly hair, you’ll need a few things: - A brush or comb - Hair ties - Bobby pins - Hair spray (optional) Follow these steps to create the hairstyle: 1. Brush your hair to remove any tangles and knots. 2. Take a section of hair from the front of your head, just above your ears. 3. Twist the section of hair and pull it back towards the crown of your head. 4. Secure the twisted section of hair with a hair tie. 5. Gently pull on the twisted section of hair to create volume. 6. Take another section of hair from just behind your ears. 7. Twist this section of hair and pull it back towards the first twisted section. 8. Secure the second twisted section of hair with a hair tie, just below the first one. 9. Again, gently pull on the twisted section of hair to create volume. 10. Use bobby pins to secure any loose strands of hair and to hold the twisted sections in place. 11. If desired, you can use hair spray to hold the style in place.

Step by Step

1. Brush your hair to remove any tangles and knots. 2. Take a section of hair from the front of your head, just above your ears. 3. Twist the section of hair and pull it back towards the crown of your head. 4. Secure the twisted section of hair with a hair tie. 5. Gently pull on the twisted section of hair to create volume. 6. Take another section of hair from just behind your ears. 7. Twist this section of hair and pull it back towards the first twisted section. 8. Secure the second twisted section of hair with a hair tie, just below the first one. 9. Again, gently pull on the twisted section of hair to create volume. 10. Use bobby pins to secure any loose strands of hair and to hold the twisted sections in place. 11. If desired, you can use hair spray to hold the style in place.

Tips

- Use a wide-toothed comb or your fingers to detangle your curly hair. This will prevent breakage and damage. - If you have naturally curly hair, you can enhance your curls by using a curl-enhancing product. - For a more polished look, you can use a straightening iron to smooth out the sections of hair that you’re pulling back. - If you have shorter hair, you can still achieve the half up half down look by using smaller sections of hair and securing them with bobby pins.

FAQ

Q: Can I do the half up half down look with curly hair if my hair is very long? A: Yes! The half up half down look can work with any length of hair, as long as you can pull back a section of hair from the front of your head. Q: Will the half up half down style work with very tight curls? A: Absolutely! This style can work with any type of curl, from loose waves to tight coils.

Hair Care

To keep your curly hair healthy and looking its best, it’s important to take good care of it. Here are a few tips: - Use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair hydrated. - Avoid using heat tools on your hair too often, as this can cause damage and breakage. - Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to help nourish your hair. - Avoid brushing your hair when it’s dry, as this can cause frizz and breakage. Instead, use a wide-toothed comb or your fingers to detangle your hair when it’s wet. - Sleep on a silk or satin pillowcase to help prevent frizz and breakage. With these tips and our step-by-step guide, you’ll be able to create the half up half down look with curly hair in no time. Experiment with different twists and sections to find the style that works best for you, and don’t be afraid to add your own personal touch. Have fun with it!
