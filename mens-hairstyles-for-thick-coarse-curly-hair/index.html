Mens Hairstyles For Thick Coarse Curly Hair How To Tips And Hair Care

Thick coarse curly hair can be a challenge to style, but with the right techniques and products, you can rock a dapper look that suits your personality. In this article, we will explore mens hairstyles for thick coarse curly hair, how to style them, tips to maintain your style, and hair care tips to keep your hair healthy and strong.

How to Style Mens Hairstyles for Thick Coarse Curly Hair

When it comes to styling thick coarse curly hair, you have a variety of options. Here are some of the most popular mens hairstyles for thick coarse curly hair:

Curls with Fade

This hairstyle features a short fade on the sides and back with longer curls on top. To style, apply a curl-defining cream on damp hair and scrunch your curls with your fingers. Use a blow dryer with a diffuser attachment to dry your hair and enhance your curls. Finish off with a hairspray to keep your curls in place.

Curly Quiff

This hairstyle features a voluminous quiff with short sides and back. To style, apply a volumizing mousse on damp hair and blow dry your hair using a round brush. Use a curling iron to create loose curls on the top and front of your hair. Finish off with a hairspray to hold your hair in place.

Short Curls

This hairstyle features short curls all over the head. To style, apply a curl-defining cream on damp hair and scrunch your curls with your fingers. Use a blow dryer with a diffuser attachment to dry your hair and enhance your curls. Finish off with a hairspray to keep your curls in place.

Long Curls with Undercut

This hairstyle features long curls on top with an undercut on the sides and back. To style, apply a curl-defining cream on damp hair and scrunch your curls with your fingers. Use a blow dryer with a diffuser attachment to dry your hair and enhance your curls. Finish off with a hairspray to keep your curls in place.

Tips for Maintaining Your Mens Hairstyle for Thick Coarse Curly Hair

Here are some tips to maintain your mens hairstyle for thick coarse curly hair:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to prevent your hair from drying out.
  • Apply a leave-in conditioner on damp hair to keep your curls hydrated and frizz-free.
  • Avoid using a brush on your curls as it can cause breakage and frizz.
  • Trim your hair every 6-8 weeks to prevent split ends and maintain the shape of your hairstyle.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools frequently as they can cause damage to your hair.

Frequently Asked Questions

Here are some frequently asked questions about mens hairstyles for thick coarse curly hair:

What products should I use for my thick coarse curly hair?

You should use a curl-defining cream, a leave-in conditioner, and a hairspray to style and maintain your thick coarse curly hair.

How often should I wash my thick coarse curly hair?

You should wash your thick coarse curly hair every 2-3 days to prevent it from drying out.

Can I straighten my thick coarse curly hair?

Yes, you can straighten your thick coarse curly hair using a flat iron or a blow dryer with a round brush. However, frequent heat styling can cause damage to your hair.

Hair Care Tips for Thick Coarse Curly Hair

Here are some hair care tips for thick coarse curly hair:

  • Avoid using hot water to wash your hair as it can strip your hair of its natural oils.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your hair to prevent breakage.
  • Avoid using alcohol-based products as they can dry out your hair.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and chlorine by wearing a hat or using a leave-in conditioner with SPF.
  • Eat a balanced diet and stay hydrated to promote healthy hair growth.

With these mens hairstyles for thick coarse curly hair, you can rock a stylish look that suits your personality. Remember to use the right products and techniques to maintain your hair's health and strength. If you have any other questions or concerns about your hair, consult with a professional hairstylist.
