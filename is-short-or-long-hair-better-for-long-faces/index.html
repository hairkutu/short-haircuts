Is Short Or Long Hair Better For Long Faces

Written by Frank Jul 17, 2023 · 8 min read

If you have a long face, you may have been wondering whether short or long hair would suit you better. The truth is, both can work well for long faces, but it really depends on your personal preferences and the specific features of your face. In this article, we’ll take a closer look at the pros and cons of short and long hair for long faces, and provide some helpful tips for choosing the right style for you.

Table of Contents

  • Short Hair Pros
  • Short Hair Cons
  • Long Hair Pros
  • Long Hair Cons
  • How to Choose the Right Style
  • Tips for Styling Your Hair
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care for Long Faces

Short Hair Pros

Short hair can be a great option for long faces because it can help balance out the length of your face. By keeping your hair shorter, you can create the illusion of a wider face, which can be more flattering for some people. Short hair can also be easier to manage and style than long hair, which is great if you have a busy lifestyle.

Short Hair Styles for Long Faces

If you decide to go with short hair, there are several styles that can work well for long faces. Some popular options include:

  • Pixie cut
  • Bob
  • Crop
  • Asymmetrical cut

Short Hair Cons

While short hair can be a great option for some people, it’s not for everyone. If you have a long face and you’re considering a short hair style, here are some potential downsides to keep in mind:

  • Short hair can be less versatile than long hair, which means you may have fewer styling options.
  • Some short hair styles may emphasize the length of your face, rather than balance it out.
  • Short hair can require more frequent trips to the salon for maintenance.

Long Hair Pros

Long hair can also be a great option for long faces, as it can help create the illusion of a shorter face. By adding volume and texture to your hair, you can balance out the length of your face and create a more proportionate look. Long hair also provides more styling options, which can be fun to experiment with.

Long Hair Styles for Long Faces

If you decide to go with long hair, there are several styles that can work well for long faces. Some popular options include:

  • Layers
  • Beachy waves
  • Straight and sleek
  • Updos

Long Hair Cons

While long hair can be a great option for some people, there are also some potential downsides to consider. These may include:

  • Long hair can be more difficult to manage and style than short hair, which may not be ideal if you have a busy lifestyle.
  • Long hair can be heavy and may weigh down your face, which can emphasize its length.
  • Long hair can require more maintenance and care than short hair, which may not be practical for everyone.

How to Choose the Right Style

When choosing a hair style for a long face, there are several factors to consider. These may include:

  • Your personal preferences
  • The shape of your face
  • Your hair texture and thickness
  • Your lifestyle and how much time you’re willing to spend on styling and maintenance

To help you choose the right style, it’s a good idea to consult with a professional stylist. They can provide expert advice on what styles will work best for your face shape and hair type.

Tips for Styling Your Hair

No matter what hair style you choose, there are some styling tips that can help make your hair look its best. These may include:

  • Using the right products for your hair type
  • Blow drying your hair in the right direction to create volume and texture
  • Using a curling iron or straightener to add waves or straighten your hair
  • Experimenting with different parting styles to find what works best for you

FAQs

Q: Can I have both short and long hair?

A: Yes! Many people choose to have a mix of short and long hair, which can create a unique and interesting look.

Q: How often should I get my hair cut?

A: This will depend on your hair type and the style you choose. Your stylist can provide recommendations on how often you should come in for maintenance cuts.

Q: Should I avoid certain colors or highlights?

A: Not necessarily. However, it’s a good idea to consult with a stylist to find colors and highlights that will complement your skin tone and hair type.

Hair Care for Long Faces

Regardless of whether you choose short or long hair, it’s important to take good care of your hair to keep it looking healthy and vibrant. This may include:

  • Using a high-quality shampoo and conditioner
  • Avoiding excessive heat styling
  • Eating a healthy diet to promote hair growth and strength
  • Protecting your hair from the sun and other environmental factors

By following these tips and taking good care of your hair, you can create a style that complements your long face and makes you feel confident and beautiful.
