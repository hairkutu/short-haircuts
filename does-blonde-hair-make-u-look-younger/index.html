Does Blonde Hair Make You Look Younger

Written by Robby Aug 09, 2023 Â· 8 min read

Blonde hair has always been associated with youthfulness and beauty. From Marilyn Monroe to Taylor Swift, we've seen countless celebrities rock the blonde look. But does blonde hair really make you look younger? In this article, we'll explore this question and provide tips on how to rock blonde hair at any age.

Description

Blonde hair has been a popular hair color for decades. It's a versatile color that can range from icy platinum to warm honey. Many people believe that blonde hair makes them look younger and more vibrant. But is there any truth to this belief?

How to Rock Blonde Hair

If you're considering going blonde, there are a few things you should keep in mind. First, consider your skin tone. Cool-toned skin looks best with ashy or platinum blonde hair, while warm-toned skin looks best with golden or honey blonde. Second, consider your natural hair color. If you have dark hair, you may need to lighten it gradually to avoid damaging your hair. Third, consider your lifestyle. If you're low-maintenance, you may want to opt for a more natural-looking blonde rather than a high-maintenance platinum shade.

Step by Step Guide to Going Blonde

If you're ready to go blonde, here's a step-by-step guide:

Step 1: Consult with a Professional

Before you take the plunge, consult with a professional hairstylist. They can advise you on the best shade of blonde for your skin tone and natural hair color. They can also help you avoid damaging your hair during the lightening process.

Step 2: Lighten Gradually

If you have dark hair, your stylist may recommend lightening your hair gradually over several sessions. This will help minimize damage to your hair and ensure that you achieve the desired shade of blonde.

Step 3: Use a Toner

After lightening your hair, your stylist may apply a toner to remove any brassy tones and help your hair look more natural.

Step 4: Maintain Your Blonde Hair

Blonde hair requires maintenance to keep it looking its best. Use a purple shampoo to prevent brassiness and a deep conditioner to keep your hair healthy and shiny.

Tips for Rocking Blonde Hair at Any Age

Whether you're in your 20s or your 60s, you can rock blonde hair. Here are some tips:

Tip 1: Consider Your Skin Tone

As mentioned earlier, your skin tone plays a big role in which shade of blonde looks best on you. If you have cool-toned skin, opt for ashy or platinum blonde. If you have warm-toned skin, opt for honey or golden blonde.

Tip 2: Keep Your Hair Healthy

Blonde hair requires maintenance to keep it healthy and shiny. Use a deep conditioner regularly and avoid over-styling your hair with heat tools.

Tip 3: Consider Your Lifestyle

If you're low-maintenance, opt for a more natural-looking blonde rather than a high-maintenance platinum shade. This will save you time and money in the long run.

FAQ

Q: Does blonde hair really make you look younger?

A: While there's no definitive answer to this question, many people believe that blonde hair makes them look younger and more vibrant.

Q: What shade of blonde is best for cool-toned skin?

A: Ashy or platinum blonde looks best on cool-toned skin.

Q: What shade of blonde is best for warm-toned skin?

A: Honey or golden blonde looks best on warm-toned skin.

Q: Can I go blonde if I have dark hair?

A: Yes, but it's best to lighten your hair gradually over several sessions to avoid damaging your hair.

Hair Care Tips for Blonde Hair

If you've gone blonde, here are some hair care tips:

Tip 1: Use a Purple Shampoo

Purple shampoo helps prevent brassiness and keep your blonde hair looking vibrant.

Tip 2: Avoid Over-styling Your Hair

Blonde hair is more prone to damage from heat styling tools, so avoid over-styling your hair with these tools.

Tip 3: Use a Deep Conditioner

Blonde hair requires extra care to keep it healthy and shiny. Use a deep conditioner at least once a week to keep your hair moisturized.

In conclusion, blonde hair can make you look younger and more vibrant, but it's important to choose the right shade of blonde for your skin tone and natural hair color. If you're ready to go blonde, consult with a professional hairstylist and follow their advice to avoid damaging your hair. With proper maintenance, you can rock blonde hair at any age!
