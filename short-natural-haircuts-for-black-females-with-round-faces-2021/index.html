Short Natural Haircuts For Black Females With Round Faces 2021

Written by Oliver Apr 20, 2023 Â· 6 min read

If you're a black female with a round face, you know how difficult it can be to find a short natural haircut that flatters your features. But fear not, as we've put together a guide to help you find the perfect haircut for your face shape in 2021. From the best cuts to styling tips and hair care advice, we've got you covered.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to
  • Step by step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

Short natural haircuts for black females with round faces should ideally elongate the face and create the illusion of a more defined jawline. This can be achieved with haircuts that have more height on top and volume on the sides. Additionally, haircuts that have layers and texture can help to create movement and dimension, which can also flatter a round face shape.

How to

The first step in finding the perfect short natural haircut for your round face is to consider your face shape and features. You may want to consult with a hairstylist who has experience working with black hair and round faces, as they can offer advice and recommendations based on your specific needs.

Face Shape

When it comes to round faces, the goal is to create the illusion of length and definition. This can be achieved with haircuts that have more height on top and volume on the sides. Haircuts that are too short or too long can accentuate the roundness of the face, so it's important to find a length that's just right.

Texture and Layers

Texture and layers can also help to create the illusion of length and definition. Curls and waves can add volume and movement, while layers can add dimension and shape. It's important to find a haircut that works with your natural texture and can be easily styled at home.

Step by step

Here are some steps to follow when getting a short natural haircut:

  1. Consult with a hairstylist to find the best haircut for your face shape and features.
  2. Bring pictures of haircuts you like to show your hairstylist.
  3. Discuss your natural texture and how the haircut will work with it.
  4. Ask your hairstylist for tips on styling your new haircut at home.

Tips

Here are some tips for maintaining your short natural haircut:

  • Wash and condition your hair regularly with products that are gentle and moisturizing.
  • Avoid heat styling as much as possible to prevent damage to your hair.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your hair when wet.
  • Consider using a leave-in conditioner or hair oil to add moisture and shine.

FAQ

Q: What are some good short natural haircuts for black females with round faces?

A: Some good haircuts to consider are pixie cuts, tapered cuts, and curly bobs. These haircuts can create height and volume on top, while also adding texture and movement.

Q: How often should I get my short natural haircut trimmed?

A: It's generally recommended to get your hair trimmed every 4-6 weeks to maintain the shape and style of your haircut.

Hair Care

Here are some hair care tips for maintaining healthy natural hair:

  • Wash and condition your hair regularly with products that are gentle and moisturizing.
  • Avoid over-manipulating and pulling on your hair, as this can lead to breakage and damage.
  • Use a satin or silk pillowcase to prevent friction and keep your hair hydrated.
  • Consider using a deep conditioning treatment once a week to add moisture and nourishment to your hair.

With these tips and recommendations, you're sure to find the perfect short natural haircut for your round face in 2021. Remember to consult with a hairstylist and maintain a healthy hair care routine to keep your hair looking and feeling its best.
