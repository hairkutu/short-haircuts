Best Bangs For Round Fat Faces A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Eveline Aug 27, 2023 · 8 min read

Are you tired of struggling to find the perfect hairstyle for your round face? Look no further than the power of bangs. Bangs can add dimension and structure to your face, creating a flattering look that enhances your natural beauty. But not all bangs are created equal, especially when it comes to round, fat faces. Here, we’ll explore the best bangs for round fat faces and give you tips on how to style them to perfection. Let’s dive in!

How to Determine Your Face Shape

Before we get into the best bangs for round fat faces, it’s important to determine your face shape. This will help you understand the best hairstyles to complement your features. To determine your face shape, follow these simple steps:

Step 1: Measure Your Face

Start by measuring your face from your hairline to your chin and from your cheekbone to your other cheekbone.

Step 2: Compare Your Measurements

Once you have your measurements, compare them to determine your face shape. If your face is about as wide as it is long, with a rounded chin and full cheeks, you likely have a round face shape.

The Best Bangs for Round Fat Faces

Now that you’ve determined your face shape, let’s dive into the best bangs for round fat faces. These bangs will help create the illusion of length in your face, balancing out your features and creating a more defined look.

1. Side-Swept Bangs

Side-swept bangs are a great option for round fat faces. These bangs are longer on one side, creating a diagonal line that draws the eye down and elongates your face. Plus, they’re versatile and can be styled in a variety of ways.

2. Curtain Bangs

Curtain bangs are another great option for round fat faces. These bangs are parted down the middle and frame your face on either side. They create a soft, romantic look and are great for those who want to add a touch of femininity to their style.

3. Wispy Bangs

Wispy bangs are a great option for those who want a low-maintenance style. These bangs are light and airy, creating a soft, effortless look. Plus, they’re great for hiding forehead wrinkles and creating a more youthful appearance.

4. Textured Bangs

Textured bangs are a great option for those with thicker hair. These bangs are cut with choppy, textured layers that create a messy, edgy look. They’re perfect for those who want to add some volume and dimension to their hair.

Tips for Styling Your Bangs

Now that you’ve chosen the perfect bangs for your round fat face, it’s important to know how to style them. Here are some tips to help you achieve a flawless look:

1. Blow Dry Your Bangs

Blow drying your bangs will help set them in place and create a smooth, polished look. Use a round brush to shape your bangs as you blow dry them.

2. Use a Flat Iron

If you have naturally curly or wavy hair, use a flat iron to smooth out your bangs and create a sleek, straight look.

3. Add Texture

If you have textured bangs, use a texturizing spray or pomade to enhance the choppy, messy look.

FAQ: Frequently Asked Questions

Here are some frequently asked questions about the best bangs for round fat faces:

Q: Can I wear blunt bangs with a round fat face?

A: Blunt bangs can be too harsh on round fat faces, creating a boxy look. It’s best to opt for side-swept or wispy bangs instead.

Q: What is the best haircut for a round fat face?

A: A layered haircut with face-framing layers is a great option for round fat faces. This will create the illusion of length and add dimension to your face.

Hair Care for Round Fat Faces

Proper hair care is essential for maintaining healthy, beautiful hair. Here are some tips for hair care for round fat faces:

1. Keep Your Hair Clean

Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo and conditioner to keep it clean and healthy.

2. Protect Your Hair

Use heat protectant products when using hot styling tools to prevent damage to your hair.

3. Trim Your Hair Regularly

Regular trims will help keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.

Now that you know the best bangs for round fat faces and how to style them, it’s time to book your next salon appointment. With these tips, you’ll be sure to find the perfect hairstyle that enhances your natural beauty and boosts your confidence.
