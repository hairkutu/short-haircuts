How To Tell If A Hairstyle Suits You

Written by Robby Mar 28, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Choosing a new hairstyle can be exciting, but it can also be overwhelming. With so many options available, it's hard to know what will look best on you. That's why we've put together this guide on how to tell if a hairstyle suits you. Whether you're looking to switch up your look or just want to make sure you're making the right choice, these tips will help you determine which hairstyle is right for you.

Table of Contents

  • How to Tell if a Hairstyle Suits You
  • Step by Step
  • Tips

How to Tell if a Hairstyle Suits You

There are a few things to consider when choosing a new hairstyle:

Face Shape

Your face shape plays a big role in determining which hairstyles will look best on you. There are generally six different face shapes: oval, round, square, heart, diamond, and oblong. Each face shape has different characteristics and features that can be accentuated by certain hairstyles.

Hair Texture

Your hair texture is another important factor to consider. Do you have fine, thin hair or thick, curly hair? Certain hairstyles work better with different hair textures.

Lifestyle

Your lifestyle should also be taken into account when choosing a hairstyle. Do you have a job that requires a more professional look? Or are you more laid back and prefer a low-maintenance style?

Step by Step

Here's a step-by-step guide to help you determine if a hairstyle suits you:

Step 1: Determine Your Face Shape

Take a look at yourself in the mirror and determine your face shape. This will help you narrow down which hairstyles will look best on you.

Step 2: Consider Your Hair Texture

Think about your hair texture and what styles work best with it. If you have fine, thin hair, you may want to avoid heavy, layered styles that can make your hair look even thinner. If you have thick, curly hair, you may want to consider a shorter style that's easier to manage.

Step 3: Think About Your Lifestyle

Consider your lifestyle and what type of hairstyle will work best for you. If you have a job that requires a more professional look, you may want to opt for a more conservative style. If you're more laid back, you may want to go for a low-maintenance look.

Step 4: Try It Out

Once you've narrowed down your options, try out a few different hairstyles. You can do this by trying on wigs or using virtual try-on tools online. This will give you a better idea of what the style will look like on you.

Tips

Here are a few additional tips to keep in mind when choosing a new hairstyle:

  • Don't be afraid to try something new, but also don't feel pressured to follow every trend.
  • Consider your hair color when choosing a new style. Some styles may work better with certain hair colors.
  • Ask your hairstylist for their opinion. They can give you valuable advice on what will work best for your face shape and hair texture.

FAQ

Q: How do I know if a hairstyle is in style?

A: You can check out the latest hair trends online or in magazines. However, keep in mind that just because a style is trendy doesn't mean it will look good on you.

Q: Can I pull off a drastic change, like going from long hair to a pixie cut?

A: It depends on your face shape and hair texture. A pixie cut can look great on some people, but not everyone can pull it off.

Hair Care

Once you've chosen your new hairstyle, it's important to take care of your hair to keep it looking its best. This includes regular shampooing and conditioning, using heat protectant when styling with hot tools, and getting regular trims to keep your ends healthy.

Now that you know how to tell if a hairstyle suits you, you can confidently choose your next look. Remember to take your face shape, hair texture, and lifestyle into account, and don't be afraid to try something new!
