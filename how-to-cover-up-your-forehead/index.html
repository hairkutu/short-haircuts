How To Cover Up Your Forehead Tips And Tricks In 2023

Written by Luffy May 24, 2023 · 8 min read

Do you have a high forehead that you’re self-conscious about? Don’t worry, you’re not alone. Many people feel insecure about their forehead, but there are plenty of ways to cover it up and feel confident. In this article, we’ll share tips and tricks for how to cover up your forehead in 2023.

Table of Contents

  • How to Cover Up Your Forehead
  • Step by Step Guide
  • Tips and Tricks
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care for High Forehead

How to Cover Up Your Forehead

The easiest way to cover up your forehead is by using hairstyles that can provide the illusion of a smaller forehead. Here are some hairstyles that can help:

Bangs

Bangs are a classic way to cover up a high forehead. You can choose from different types of bangs, such as side-swept, blunt, long, or short. Make sure to choose a style that suits your face shape and hair type. For instance, side-swept bangs can flatter round and heart-shaped faces, while blunt bangs can work well for oval and square faces.

Layers

Layers can create volume and movement that can draw attention away from your forehead. You can opt for long layers, choppy layers, or textured layers. Just avoid adding too much volume on top of your head, as it can make your forehead look even more prominent.

Ponytail

A ponytail can also help to cover up your forehead. You can style it high or low, and add some volume on top of your head to create a more balanced look. You can also add some accessories, such as headbands, scarves, or clips, to add interest to your hairstyle.

Step by Step Guide

Here’s a step-by-step guide on how to cover up your forehead using bangs:

  1. Choose the type of bangs that suits your face shape and hair type.
  2. Section off the hair that you want to cut for your bangs.
  3. Use sharp scissors to cut your bangs in a straight line.
  4. Start from the center and work your way outwards.
  5. Blow-dry your bangs using a round brush to create volume.
  6. Style your hair as desired.

Here’s a step-by-step guide on how to cover up your forehead using a ponytail:

  1. Gather your hair in a high or low ponytail, depending on your preference.
  2. Add some volume on top of your head by teasing your hair or using a volumizing spray.
  3. Smooth out the sides and back of your hair using a brush or comb.
  4. Add some accessories, such as headbands, scarves, or clips, to add interest to your hairstyle.
  5. Style your hair as desired.

Tips and Tricks

Here are some tips and tricks for how to cover up your forehead:

  • Choose hairstyles that suit your face shape and hair type.
  • Avoid adding too much volume on top of your head, as it can make your forehead look even more prominent.
  • Experiment with different types of bangs, such as side-swept, blunt, long, or short.
  • Add some accessories, such as headbands, scarves, or clips, to add interest to your hairstyle.
  • Consider getting hair extensions or a wig if you want to try a different hairstyle.

FAQs

Q: Can I cover up my forehead without cutting my hair?

A: Yes, you can use accessories, such as headbands, scarves, or clips, to cover up your forehead. You can also experiment with different hairstyles, such as braids, buns, or twists.

Q: What type of bangs should I choose?

A: The type of bangs you should choose depends on your face shape and hair type. Side-swept bangs can flatter round and heart-shaped faces, while blunt bangs can work well for oval and square faces.

Q: Can I use hair products to cover up my forehead?

A: Yes, you can use hair products, such as volumizing spray, hairspray, or dry shampoo, to add volume and texture to your hair. However, be careful not to add too much product, as it can weigh your hair down and make it look greasy.

Hair Care for High Forehead

Here are some hair care tips for high forehead:

  • Avoid using heavy hair products, such as oil-based serums or pomades, as they can weigh your hair down and make it look greasy.
  • Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to add volume and texture to your hair.
  • Use a heat protectant spray before using heat styling tools, such as curling irons or straighteners.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.
  • Eat a balanced diet and drink plenty of water to promote healthy hair growth.

With these tips and tricks, you can confidently cover up your forehead and rock any hairstyle you want. Whether you choose to use bangs, layers, or ponytails, remember to choose a style that suits your face shape and hair type. With a little bit of experimentation and practice, you can find the perfect hairstyle that makes you feel confident and beautiful.
