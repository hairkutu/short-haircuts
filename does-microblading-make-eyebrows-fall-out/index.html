Does Microblading Make Eyebrows Fall Out

Written by Luffy Apr 26, 2023 Â· 5 min read

If you're considering microblading to give your eyebrows a fuller and more defined look, you may have heard some rumors about the procedure causing eyebrows to fall out. This can be a scary thought, especially if you're already self-conscious about your sparse brows. However, the truth is that microblading does not cause eyebrows to fall out when done by a skilled and experienced technician.

What is Microblading?

Microblading is a semi-permanent makeup technique that involves using a small handheld tool to create tiny strokes that mimic the look of natural eyebrow hairs. The pigment is deposited into the skin, creating a fuller and more defined brow. The procedure typically takes around two hours and can last up to two years.

How Does Microblading Work?

The microblading process involves numbing the area with a topical anesthetic and then using a small handheld tool with tiny needles to create the hair-like strokes. The pigment is then deposited into the skin, creating a natural-looking brow. The healing process takes around two weeks, during which time the pigment may appear darker than desired. However, as the skin heals, the color will fade to a more natural-looking shade.

Does Microblading Cause Eyebrows to Fall Out?

No, microblading does not cause eyebrows to fall out when done correctly. However, there are some factors that can cause the pigment to fade or the brows to appear patchy, such as:

  • Poor aftercare
  • Exposure to sunlight
  • Excessive sweating or swimming
  • Using harsh skincare products on the brow area

To prevent these issues, it's important to follow the aftercare instructions provided by your technician and avoid exposing your brows to sunlight or excessive moisture for at least two weeks after the procedure.

Microblading Aftercare Tips

Proper aftercare is essential for ensuring the best results from your microblading procedure. Here are some tips to follow:

  • Avoid getting your brows wet for at least two weeks after the procedure
  • Avoid exposure to sunlight or tanning beds
  • Avoid using harsh skincare products on the brow area
  • Avoid touching or picking at your brows
  • Apply the prescribed aftercare ointment as directed by your technician

FAQ

How long does microblading last?

Microblading typically lasts up to two years, depending on factors such as skin type and aftercare.

Is microblading painful?

The procedure is typically not painful, as a topical anesthetic is used to numb the area.

Can I wear makeup after microblading?

You should avoid wearing makeup on or around the brow area for at least two weeks after the procedure to allow the skin to heal.

Hair Care for Healthy Brows

In addition to proper aftercare, it's important to take care of your brows to keep them healthy and full. Some tips for healthy brows include:

  • Eating a healthy diet rich in vitamins and minerals
  • Avoiding over-plucking or waxing
  • Using a brow serum or oil to promote growth

Overall, microblading is a safe and effective way to achieve fuller and more defined brows. As long as you follow proper aftercare and take care of your brows, you should not experience any issues with the procedure.
