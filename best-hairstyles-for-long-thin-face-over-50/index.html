Best Hairstyles For Long Thin Face Over 50 Tips And Tricks

Written by Christine Apr 08, 2023 · 8 min read

As we age, our hair changes in texture and density, and our face shape also changes. For women over 50 with long and thin faces, choosing the right hairstyle can be a challenge. But worry not, we’ve got you covered! In this article, we’ll discuss the best hairstyles for long thin face over 50, and provide you with tips and tricks to achieve the perfect look.

  2. Best Hairstyles for Long Thin Face Over 50
  3. Step-by-Step Guide to Styling Your Hair
  4. Tips for Maintaining Your Hairstyle
  5. Frequently Asked Questions
  6. Hair Care for Long Thin Hair

How to Choose the Best Hairstyle for Long Thin Face Over 50

Before we dive into the best hairstyles for long thin face over 50, it’s important to understand what works best for your face shape. A long thin face typically has a high forehead, long nose, and narrow chin. The goal is to create volume and width to balance out the face shape.

When choosing a hairstyle, consider these factors:

  • Length: Shorter hairstyles can make your face appear wider, while longer hairstyles can elongate the face. Choose a length that works best for your face shape.
  • Layers: Layers can add volume and movement to your hair, creating the illusion of width.
  • Bangs: Bangs can help shorten the appearance of a long forehead and balance out the face shape.
  • Hair Color: Adding highlights or lowlights can create dimension and texture to your hair.

Best Hairstyles for Long Thin Face Over 50

Here are some of the best hairstyles for long thin face over 50:

1. Bob Haircut

The classic bob haircut is a timeless look that works well for women of all ages. It’s a low-maintenance style that can be customized to fit your face shape. A bob that hits right at the chin can create width and balance out a long face.

2. Layered Haircut

Layered haircuts can add volume and movement to your hair, creating the illusion of width. Layers that start at the cheekbones can also help add definition to your face.

3. Pixie Cut

A pixie cut is a short and sassy look that can add volume and dimension to your hair. It’s also easy to maintain and can work well for women with thin hair.

4. Side-Swept Bangs

Side-swept bangs can help shorten the appearance of a long forehead and balance out a long face shape. They can also add volume and texture to your hair.

Step-by-Step Guide to Styling Your Hair

Here’s a step-by-step guide to styling your hair for a long thin face:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair.
  2. Apply a volumizing mousse to your roots to add volume and texture.
  3. Blow-dry your hair using a round brush to add volume and curl to your hair.
  4. If you have bangs, use a flat iron to smooth them out.
  5. Finish with a light hairspray to hold your style in place.

Tips for Maintaining Your Hairstyle

Here are some tips for maintaining your hairstyle:

  • Get regular trims to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.
  • Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to add volume and texture to your hair.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as it can cause damage and breakage.
  • Use a heat protectant spray before using any hot tools on your hair.

Frequently Asked Questions

Here are some frequently asked questions about hairstyles for long thin face over 50:

What is the best length for a long thin face?

The best length for a long thin face is one that creates width and balance. Shorter hairstyles can make your face appear wider, while longer hairstyles can elongate the face. Choose a length that works best for your face shape.

What type of bangs work best for a long thin face?

Side-swept bangs can help shorten the appearance of a long forehead and balance out a long face shape. They can also add volume and texture to your hair.

Hair Care for Long Thin Hair

Here are some tips for taking care of long thin hair:

  • Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to add volume and texture to your hair.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as it can cause damage and breakage.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.
  • Consider using a hair mask once a week to add moisture and nourishment to your hair.

Now that you know the best hairstyles for long thin face over 50, it’s time to try them out! Remember to choose a style that works best for your face shape and hair texture, and don’t be afraid to experiment with different lengths and colors.
