How To Cut Men s Hair With Clippers Beginners A Step By Step Guide

Written by Frank Aug 29, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Are you looking to learn how to cut men's hair with clippers? Whether you're a beginner or just looking to improve your skills, this guide will provide you with all the information you need to get started. From the tools you'll need to the step-by-step process, we've got you covered.

Tools You'll Need

The first step in cutting men's hair with clippers is to gather all the necessary tools. Here's what you'll need:

  • Clippers with guards
  • Comb
  • Hair scissors
  • Hair cape or towel
  • Clipper oil
  • Brush

How to Cut Men's Hair with Clippers Beginners: Step-by-Step Guide

Step 1: Wash and Dry Hair

Before you start cutting, it's important to wash and dry the hair. This will help remove any dirt, oil, or product buildup that can interfere with the clippers. Once the hair is dry, comb it in the direction you want it to lay.

Step 2: Choose a Guard Size

Next, choose the guard size you want to use. The larger the guard, the longer the hair will be. Start with a larger guard size and work your way down to achieve the desired length.

Step 3: Start Cutting

Start by using the clippers to remove the bulk of the hair. Begin at the nape of the neck and work your way up to the crown of the head. Use a sweeping motion and make sure to keep the clippers flat against the head.

Step 4: Blend the Sides and Back

Once you've removed the bulk of the hair, use a smaller guard size to blend the sides and back. Use an upward motion to blend the hair and make sure to check for any uneven areas.

Step 5: Trim the Top

Use hair scissors to trim the top of the hair to the desired length. Start at the front and work your way to the back, cutting small sections at a time.

Step 6: Blend the Top and Sides

Use the clippers to blend the top and sides of the hair. Use a larger guard size and work your way down to a smaller guard size to achieve a seamless blend.

Step 7: Clean Up the Neckline

Use the clippers to clean up the neckline by trimming any stray hairs. Use an upward motion and make sure to check for any uneven areas.

Step 8: Clean Up the Ears

Use the clippers to clean up the hair around the ears. Use a smaller guard size and work your way around the ear, trimming any stray hairs.

Step 9: Brush and Style

Once you've finished cutting, use a brush to remove any loose hairs. Style the hair as desired using pomade, gel, or hairspray.

Tips for Cutting Men's Hair with Clippers Beginners

  • Choose a well-lit area to ensure you can see what you're doing
  • Start with a larger guard size and work your way down to achieve the desired length
  • Use a sweeping motion when cutting with the clippers
  • Take your time and check for any uneven areas
  • Clean and oil your clippers regularly to keep them in good condition

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I cut men's hair with scissors?

While it's possible to cut men's hair with scissors, using clippers is typically faster and easier. Clippers allow you to remove the bulk of the hair quickly and efficiently.

What guard size should I use?

The guard size you use will depend on the length of the hair you want to achieve. Start with a larger guard size and work your way down to achieve the desired length.

Can I cut my own hair with clippers?

Yes, it's possible to cut your own hair with clippers, but it can be difficult to achieve a professional-looking cut. It's best to have someone else do it for you if possible.

Hair Care Tips

To keep your hair looking its best after a haircut, follow these tips:

  • Wash and condition your hair regularly
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair
  • Use a hair serum or oil to keep your hair healthy and shiny
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair looking neat and tidy

By following these tips, you can ensure that your hair looks its best after a haircut.
