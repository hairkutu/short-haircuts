Men s Hair Styling Products For Short Length Hair

Written by Petter May 18, 2023 · 7 min read

Short hair is a popular style among men, but it still requires some maintenance and styling to look its best. In this article, we will discuss the best men's hair styling products for short length hair.

Table of Content

  • Description
  • How to Style Short Hair
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Styling Short Hair
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care Tips for Short Hair

Description

Short hair is versatile and easy to style, but it can still benefit from the use of styling products. The right product can help you achieve your desired look, whether that's a messy, textured style or a slick, polished look. Men's hair styling products for short length hair come in a variety of forms, including pomades, waxes, gels, and sprays.

How to Style Short Hair

The first step to styling short hair is to determine the type of look you want to achieve. This will help you choose the right product and technique to use. Some popular short hair styles include:

  • Crew cut
  • Buzz cut
  • Side part
  • Textured crop
  • Fade

Once you know the style you want, you can choose the right product and follow the steps below to style your hair.

Step-by-Step Guide

1. Wash your hair

Start by washing your hair with a shampoo and conditioner that's appropriate for your hair type. This will help remove any dirt or buildup and leave your hair clean and manageable.

2. Towel dry your hair

Gently towel dry your hair to remove excess water. Avoid rubbing your hair vigorously with the towel, as this can cause damage and frizz.

3. Apply the product

Take a small amount of product and rub it between your palms to warm it up. Apply it evenly to your hair, starting at the roots and working your way to the tips.

4. Style your hair

Use your fingers or a comb to style your hair as desired. For a messy, textured look, use your fingers to create volume and texture. For a slick, polished look, use a comb to smooth your hair into place.

5. Finish with hairspray

If desired, finish your style with a light spritz of hairspray to help hold it in place.

Tips for Styling Short Hair

Here are some additional tips to help you style short hair:

  • Start with a small amount of product and add more as needed. You can always add more, but it's harder to remove excess product.
  • Choose a product that's appropriate for your hair type. If you have fine hair, choose a lightweight product that won't weigh your hair down. If you have thick, coarse hair, choose a heavier product that will provide more hold.
  • Experiment with different products and techniques to find the one that works best for you.

FAQs

What's the best men's hair styling product for short length hair?

The best product depends on the type of look you want to achieve and your hair type. Pomades are great for creating a slick, polished look, while waxes and clays are better for creating texture and volume.

Can I use hair gel on short hair?

Yes, hair gel can be used on short hair to create a variety of styles. However, it's important to choose a gel that's appropriate for your hair type and to use it sparingly to avoid a crunchy, stiff look.

How often should I wash my short hair?

How often you should wash your hair depends on your hair type and lifestyle. If you have oily hair, you may need to wash it more frequently than if you have dry hair. Generally, washing your hair every other day is a good rule of thumb.

Hair Care Tips for Short Hair

To keep your short hair looking its best, follow these hair care tips:

  • Use a shampoo and conditioner that's appropriate for your hair type.
  • Avoid using hot tools like blow dryers and straighteners too often, as this can cause damage and breakage.
  • Get regular haircuts to maintain your style and prevent split ends.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and harsh weather conditions by wearing a hat or using a leave-in conditioner with SPF.

With these tips and the right men's hair styling products for short length hair, you can achieve a variety of stylish looks and keep your hair looking healthy and vibrant.
