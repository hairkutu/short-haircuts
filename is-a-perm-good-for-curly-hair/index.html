Is A Perm Good For Curly Hair

Written by Bardi May 23, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Curly hair can be both a blessing and a curse. While it looks gorgeous and unique, it can also be challenging to manage and maintain. One popular solution is to get a perm. However, before you jump into it, you may wonder, is a perm good for curly hair? In this article, we will answer that question and provide you with everything you need to know about perming curly hair.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to Get a Perm for Curly Hair
  • Step-by-Step Guide for Perming Curly Hair
  • Tips for Maintaining Permed Curly Hair
  • FAQs about Perming Curly Hair
  • Hair Care for Permed Curly Hair

Description

Perming curly hair is a process that involves using chemicals to change the shape of your hair. The perm solution breaks down the hair's structure, and then the hair is wrapped around rods to create curls or waves. The result is permanent, and you will have curly hair until it grows out.

Perming has been around for decades, and it's a popular method for adding volume and texture to hair. However, perming curly hair is not without its risks. The chemicals used in perming can damage the hair, and if not done correctly, it can lead to breakage and hair loss.

How to Get a Perm for Curly Hair

If you're considering perming your curly hair, the first step is to find a reputable stylist who specializes in perming. It's important to choose someone who has experience with perming curly hair, as it requires a different technique than perming straight hair.

Before the perm, your stylist will assess your hair's condition and determine if it's healthy enough for the process. They may also recommend a conditioning treatment to prepare your hair for the chemicals.

Step-by-Step Guide for Perming Curly Hair

Here is a step-by-step guide for perming curly hair:

  1. Wash your hair with a clarifying shampoo to remove any buildup.
  2. Section your hair and wrap it around rods of different sizes to create the desired curl pattern.
  3. Apply the perm solution to your hair and leave it on for the recommended time.
  4. Rinse your hair thoroughly and apply a neutralizing solution to stop the perming process.
  5. Remove the rods and rinse your hair again.
  6. Apply a deep conditioning treatment to restore moisture to your hair.

Tips for Maintaining Permed Curly Hair

Here are some tips for maintaining permed curly hair:

  • Avoid heat styling tools, as they can damage your hair.
  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy.
  • Apply a leave-in conditioner to keep your curls moisturized.
  • Sleep with a silk or satin pillowcase to prevent frizz.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.

FAQs about Perming Curly Hair

Here are some frequently asked questions about perming curly hair:

Is a perm good for curly hair?

Yes, a perm can be good for curly hair if done correctly. It can add volume and texture to your curls and make them more manageable.

How long does a perm last on curly hair?

A perm can last up to six months on curly hair, depending on how fast your hair grows.

Can you color permed curly hair?

Yes, you can color permed curly hair, but it's best to wait at least two weeks after perming to color your hair.

Hair Care for Permed Curly Hair

To keep your permed curly hair healthy, you need to follow a proper hair care routine. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your hair with a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner.
  • Apply a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your curls moisturized.
  • Avoid heat styling tools, as they can damage your hair.
  • Sleep with a silk or satin pillowcase to prevent frizz.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.

In conclusion, perming curly hair can be a good option if you want to add volume and texture to your curls. However, it's essential to choose a reputable stylist and follow a proper hair care routine to keep your hair healthy and prevent damage.
