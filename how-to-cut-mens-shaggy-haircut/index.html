How To Cut Men s Shaggy Haircut A Step By Step Guide

Written by William Mar 23, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Are you looking to switch up your hairstyle and go for a shaggy look? Cutting a shaggy haircut is not as complicated as it may seem. This relaxed style is perfect for those who want to add some volume and texture to their hair. In this article, we will provide you with a step-by-step guide on how to cut men's shaggy haircut.

Description

A shaggy haircut is a layered haircut that creates a messy and textured look. This hairstyle is perfect for those who want to achieve a relaxed and effortless look. Shaggy haircuts are versatile and can be customized to suit your face shape and hair type.

How to Cut Men's Shaggy Haircut

Step 1: Prepare Your Tools
Before you start cutting your hair, make sure you have all the necessary tools. You will need a pair of scissors, a comb, and hair clippers (if you plan to trim your hair).

Step 2: Decide on the Length
Decide on the length of your hair. Shaggy haircuts usually have longer layers on top and shorter layers on the sides and back.

Step 3: Cut the Top Layers
Start by cutting the top layers of your hair. Use your comb to section off the top layers of your hair and cut it to your desired length. Make sure to cut the hair at an angle to create a layered effect.

Step 4: Cut the Side Layers
Next, move on to cutting the side layers. Use your comb to section off the sides of your hair and cut it to your desired length. Make sure to blend the top layers with the side layers to create a seamless look.

Step 5: Cut the Back Layers
Lastly, cut the back layers of your hair. Use your comb to section off the back of your hair and cut it to your desired length. Make sure to blend the top and side layers with the back layers to create a cohesive look.

Tips

  • Start with longer layers and gradually work your way to shorter layers
  • Use hair clippers to trim the hair around your ears and neck
  • Take your time and be patient when cutting your hair
  • Don't cut too much hair at once to avoid mistakes

FAQ

Q: Can you cut a shaggy haircut on any hair type?
A: Yes, shaggy haircuts can be customized to suit any hair type.

Q: How often should you get a shaggy haircut?
A: It depends on how fast your hair grows. Generally, it is recommended to get a shaggy haircut every 6-8 weeks.

Hair Care

After cutting your hair, it is important to take care of it to maintain the shaggy look. Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to add texture and volume to your hair. Use a hair dryer to style your hair and add some volume. Apply a small amount of hair wax or pomade to add some texture and hold to your hair.

Cutting a shaggy haircut is a simple process that can be done at home. Follow our step-by-step guide and tips to achieve a relaxed and effortless look. Remember to take care of your hair to maintain the shaggy look.
