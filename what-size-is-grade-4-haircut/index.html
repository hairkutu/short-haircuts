What Size Is A Grade 4 Haircut

Written by Aprilia Jun 19, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Are you looking to get a grade 4 haircut but unsure of what size it actually is? You're not alone! Many people are confused by the grading system used by barbers and hairdressers. In this article, we'll explain what size is a grade 4 haircut, how to achieve it, and some tips and tricks to keep your hair looking great.

What is a Grade 4 Haircut?

A grade 4 haircut is a style in which the hair is cut to a length of 1/2 inch or 12mm. This is the fourth shortest haircut in the standard clipper guard sizes, with the higher numbers indicating longer hair. The grade 4 is a popular choice for those who want to keep their hair short but not too short.

How to Achieve a Grade 4 Haircut

To achieve a grade 4 haircut, you will need to use a clipper with a #4 guard attached. Start by washing and drying your hair, then section it into manageable pieces. Using the clipper, run it over your head in a back and forth motion, starting at the nape of your neck and moving upwards. Be sure to go over each section a few times to ensure an even cut.

Once you've gone over your entire head with the #4 guard, you can use scissors to trim any longer hairs and create a more polished look. If you want a more tapered or layered look, you can ask your barber or hairdresser to use different guard sizes on different parts of your head.

Tips and Tricks

To keep your grade 4 haircut looking great, there are a few tips and tricks you can follow. Firstly, make sure to use a good quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and hydrated. Secondly, use a styling product such as hair wax or gel to add texture and hold to your hair. Finally, schedule regular trims every 4-6 weeks to maintain the length and shape of your haircut.

FAQ

Q: Is a grade 4 haircut suitable for all hair types?

A: Yes, a grade 4 haircut can work for all hair types, including straight, wavy, and curly hair.

Q: Can I achieve a grade 4 haircut at home?

A: Yes, you can achieve a grade 4 haircut at home using a clipper with a #4 guard attached. However, for best results, it's recommended to see a professional barber or hairdresser.

Q: How long does a grade 4 haircut last?

A: A grade 4 haircut can last anywhere from 4-6 weeks depending on how quickly your hair grows. It's recommended to schedule regular trims to maintain the length and shape of your haircut.

Hair Care

To keep your hair healthy and looking its best, it's important to take care of it both inside and out. This includes eating a balanced diet rich in vitamins and minerals, staying hydrated, and avoiding harsh chemicals and heat styling tools. Additionally, using a good quality shampoo and conditioner, and regularly trimming your hair can help keep it in optimal condition.

Conclusion

In summary, a grade 4 haircut is a popular style in which the hair is cut to a length of 1/2 inch or 12mm. To achieve this style, you will need to use a clipper with a #4 guard attached and follow the steps outlined in this article. By following the tips and tricks, and taking care of your hair, you can maintain a great-looking grade 4 haircut for weeks to come.
