How To Wave Natural Hair Step By Step Guide

Written by Robby Mar 25, 2023 · 6 min read

Welcome to our complete guide on how to wave natural hair! If you're looking to add some texture and volume to your hair, this is the perfect hairstyle for you. Whether you have straight, curly or kinky hair, you can achieve beautiful waves that will last for days. In this article, we'll take you through the steps to get the perfect waves every time.

What are Waves?

Waves are a type of hairstyle that involves creating soft, loose curls in your hair. This style is perfect for adding volume and texture to your hair, and it's a great way to switch up your look without going for a complete cut or colour change. Waves are a popular style for both short and long hair, and they're perfect for any occasion.

How to Wave Natural Hair: Step by Step

Step 1: Wash and Condition Your Hair

The first step in creating waves is to start with freshly washed and conditioned hair. Use a shampoo and conditioner that are suitable for your hair type.

Step 2: Apply Heat Protectant

Before using any heat styling tools on your hair, it's important to apply a heat protectant. This will help to prevent damage to your hair and keep it looking healthy.

Step 3: Divide Your Hair into Sections

To make it easier to create waves, divide your hair into sections. Start by creating a part down the middle of your head, and then divide your hair into two sections.

Step 4: Use a Curling Wand

Take a small section of hair and wrap it around a curling wand. Hold this for a few seconds and then release the hair. Repeat this process for each section of hair until you've created waves throughout your hair.

Step 5: Comb Through Your Waves

Once you've finished waving your hair, use a wide-tooth comb to gently comb through your waves. This will help to create a softer, more natural look.

Step 6: Apply Hairspray

To help your waves last all day, apply a light hairspray to your hair. This will help to hold the waves in place and prevent frizz.

Tips for Waving Natural Hair

Here are some additional tips to help you achieve the perfect waves:

  • Use a curling wand with a larger barrel for looser waves
  • Use a curling wand with a smaller barrel for tighter waves
  • Don't hold the curling wand on your hair for too long, as this can cause damage
  • Comb through your waves gently to avoid creating frizz
  • Use a light hairspray to hold your waves in place

FAQ

Is it possible to wave natural hair without using heat styling tools?

Yes, it is possible to create waves in natural hair without using heat styling tools. You can use techniques such as braiding or twisting your hair to create waves.

How long will my waves last?

Your waves should last for several days if you use a light hairspray to hold them in place.

Hair Care

To keep your hair looking healthy and to prevent damage, it's important to take good care of it. Here are some tips for maintaining healthy hair:

  • Wash your hair regularly
  • Use a conditioner after washing your hair
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often
  • Use a heat protectant before using any heat styling tools
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends

With these tips and our step-by-step guide, you should be able to create beautiful waves in your natural hair. Remember to take good care of your hair to keep it healthy and looking its best.
