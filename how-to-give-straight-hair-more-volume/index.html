How To Give Straight Hair More Volume

Written by Oliver Jun 17, 2023 Â· 6 min read

In this year 2023, having voluminous hair is all the rage. While curly-haired individuals have a natural advantage, those with straight hair may feel like they're missing out. Fear not, as there are plenty of ways to give straight hair more volume! In this blog post, we'll go over a step-by-step process to achieve the look you desire.

How to:

Step 1: Wash Your Hair

The first step to giving your straight hair more volume is washing it correctly. Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to add volume from the roots. Avoid using heavy, oily products that can weigh down your hair.

Step 2: Blow Dry Your Hair Upside Down

Flip your head upside down and blow-dry your hair. This will help to lift your roots and create volume. Use a round brush to guide your hair in the direction you want it to go.

Step 3: Use a Volumizing Mousse

Apply a volumizing mousse to your hair when it's still damp. Focus on the roots, where you want the most volume. Work the mousse through your hair, making sure to distribute it evenly.

Step 4: Blow Dry Your Hair Again

Blow dry your hair again, this time using a round brush to lift your roots even further. Focus on the crown of your head, where you want the most volume.

Step 5: Tease Your Hair

Take small sections of your hair and backcomb them using a teasing comb. This will create more volume and texture. Be careful not to overdo it, as too much teasing can damage your hair.

Step 6: Use Dry Shampoo

Dry shampoo is a great way to add volume to your hair without washing it. Spray dry shampoo at the roots and massage it into your scalp. This will absorb any excess oil and give your hair a lift.

Tips:

  • Don't use too much product, as this can weigh down your hair
  • Avoid using heavy conditioners, as they can make your hair greasy
  • Use a heat protectant spray to protect your hair from damage
  • Avoid brushing your hair too much, as this can cause frizz
  • Don't use a straightener, as this can flatten your hair

FAQ:

Q: Can I use a volumizing spray instead of a mousse?

A: Yes, you can use a volumizing spray instead of a mousse. Just make sure to follow the instructions on the bottle and apply it evenly.

Q: How often should I wash my hair?

A: It depends on your hair type and lifestyle. If you have oily hair, you may need to wash it more frequently. If you have dry hair, you can wash it less often.

Q: Will teasing my hair damage it?

A: Teasing your hair can cause damage over time, so it's important to be gentle. Use a teasing comb instead of a brush, and don't tease the same section of hair too much.

Hair Care:

It's important to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and voluminous. Use a clarifying shampoo once a week to remove any product buildup. Deep condition your hair once a week to keep it moisturized. Avoid using hot tools too often, and always use a heat protectant spray.

With these tips and tricks, you can give your straight hair more volume and achieve the voluminous look you desire.
