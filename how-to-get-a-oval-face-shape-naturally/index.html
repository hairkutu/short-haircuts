How To Get An Oval Face Shape Naturally

Written by Daniel May 16, 2023 Â· 8 min read

If you are wondering how to get an oval face shape naturally, you are not alone. Many people are looking for ways to enhance their facial features and achieve a more symmetrical appearance. While there are surgical procedures that can help you achieve this, there are also natural ways to do it.

Description

An oval face shape is considered the most desirable shape because it is symmetrical and balanced. People with an oval face shape have a forehead that is slightly wider than their jaw, and their cheekbones are the widest part of their face. If you have a round or square face, you can still achieve an oval face shape by following these tips.

How to

There are several things you can do to get an oval face shape naturally:

1. Lose weight

If you have excess facial fat, losing weight can help you achieve a more defined jawline and cheekbones. You can do this by following a healthy diet and exercising regularly.

2. Facial exercises

Facial exercises can help tone and tighten the muscles in your face, giving you a more sculpted appearance. Some effective facial exercises include cheek lifts, jawline exercises, and neck stretches.

3. Hairstyles

Your hairstyle can also help create the illusion of an oval face shape. A layered haircut or a side-swept bang can help elongate your face and give you a more balanced appearance.

4. Makeup

Makeup can also help you achieve an oval face shape. Contouring can help create shadows and highlights that can give you a more defined jawline and cheekbones. Using a lighter shade of foundation in the center of your face can also help create an illusion of an oval shape.

Step by Step

Here are the step-by-step instructions on how to get an oval face shape:

Step 1: Determine your face shape

Before you start working on getting an oval face shape, you need to determine your current face shape. You can do this by standing in front of a mirror and analyzing your facial features. If you have a round or square face, you can still achieve an oval shape by following these tips.

Step 2: Lose weight

If you have excess facial fat, losing weight can help you achieve a more defined jawline and cheekbones. You can do this by following a healthy diet and exercising regularly.

Step 3: Facial exercises

Facial exercises can help tone and tighten the muscles in your face, giving you a more sculpted appearance. Some effective facial exercises include cheek lifts, jawline exercises, and neck stretches.

Step 4: Hairstyles

Your hairstyle can also help create the illusion of an oval face shape. A layered haircut or a side-swept bang can help elongate your face and give you a more balanced appearance.

Step 5: Makeup

Makeup can also help you achieve an oval face shape. Contouring can help create shadows and highlights that can give you a more defined jawline and cheekbones. Using a lighter shade of foundation in the center of your face can also help create an illusion of an oval shape.

Tips

Here are some additional tips to help you get an oval face shape:

  • Drink plenty of water to keep your skin hydrated and healthy
  • Avoid smoking and excessive alcohol consumption, as they can damage your skin and cause premature aging
  • Get enough sleep to allow your body to repair and regenerate
  • Avoid stress, as it can cause wrinkles and other signs of aging

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about getting an oval face shape:

Q: Can I get an oval face shape if I have a round face?

A: Yes, you can still achieve an oval face shape if you have a round face by following these tips.

Q: How long will it take to get an oval face shape?

A: It depends on your current face shape and how much work you need to do to achieve an oval shape. It could take a few weeks to several months to see results.

Q: Do I need surgery to get an oval face shape?

A: No, you can achieve an oval face shape naturally by following these tips.

Hair Care

When it comes to hair care, it's important to keep your hair healthy and well-maintained. Here are some tips to help you achieve healthy hair:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo
  • Avoid using hot tools on your hair, such as hair dryers and curling irons
  • Use a wide-toothed comb to detangle your hair, and avoid brushing it when it's wet
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends and breakage
  • Use a deep-conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair moisturized and healthy

By following these tips, you can achieve an oval face shape naturally and enhance your facial features. Remember to be patient and consistent, as it may take some time to see results. With a little effort and dedication, you can achieve the look you desire.
