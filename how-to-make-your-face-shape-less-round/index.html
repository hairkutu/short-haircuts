How To Make Your Face Shape Less Round

Written by Petter Sep 04, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Are you tired of your face looking round and chubby? Do you feel self-conscious about your appearance? If so, you're not alone. Many people struggle with a round face shape, but there are ways to make it look less round. In this article, we'll discuss how to make your face shape less round and provide tips for achieving a more sculpted appearance.

Description

A round face shape is characterized by full cheeks, a rounded chin, and a lack of definition in the jawline. This shape can make you look younger and more approachable, but it can also make your face appear larger than it actually is. If you're looking to make your face shape less round, there are several techniques you can try.

How To

The first step in making your face shape less round is to lose weight. Excess fat on the face can contribute to a round appearance, so shedding some pounds can help to slim it down. However, losing weight isn't always easy, and it may not be enough to achieve the look you want.

A more effective way to make your face shape less round is to focus on building muscle in your jawline and cheeks. This can be done through facial exercises, such as chewing gum, blowing up balloons, and making exaggerated facial expressions. These exercises can help to tone the muscles in your face and give it a more sculpted appearance.

Step by Step

Here are some steps you can follow to make your face shape less round:

  1. Start by losing weight if you need to.
  2. Focus on building muscle in your jawline and cheeks through facial exercises.
  3. Consider getting a haircut that flatters your face shape.
  4. Experiment with makeup to create the illusion of a slimmer face.

Tips

Here are some additional tips to help you achieve a more sculpted face shape:

  • Avoid hairstyles that add volume to your face, such as bangs and layers.
  • Use contouring makeup to create the illusion of a more defined jawline.
  • Wear earrings that elongate your face, such as drop earrings.
  • Avoid wearing hats that sit low on your forehead, as they can make your face appear wider.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about making your face shape less round:

  • Can facial exercises really make a difference? Yes, facial exercises can help to tone the muscles in your face and give it a more sculpted appearance.
  • Do I need to lose weight to make my face shape less round? Losing weight can help to reduce excess fat on your face, but it's not always necessary to achieve a more sculpted appearance.
  • What kind of makeup should I use to create the illusion of a slimmer face? Contouring makeup can be used to create the illusion of a more defined jawline and cheekbones.
  • What kind of earrings should I wear to elongate my face? Drop earrings can help to elongate your face and make it appear more slender.

Hair Care

Your hair can also play a role in making your face shape look less round. Consider getting a haircut that flatters your face shape, such as a long bob or a layered cut. Avoid hairstyles that add volume to your face, such as bangs and layers. If you have a round face shape, a side part can help to create the illusion of a more angular jawline.

Overall, making your face shape less round is possible with the right techniques and tips. By incorporating facial exercises, makeup, and hair care into your routine, you can achieve a more sculpted appearance and feel confident in your own skin.
