How To Spike Short Hair Male A Step By Step Guide

Written by Bardi Mar 18, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Short hair can be versatile, especially when it comes to spiking it up. Whether you want a spiky hairstyle for a night out or for your daily look, spiking short hair is a quick and easy way to give your hair some edge. In this article, we'll show you how to spike short hair male with our step-by-step guide.

How to Spike Short Hair Male

Before we get started, let's gather the necessary tools:

  • Hair gel or wax
  • Comb
  • Hair dryer (optional)

Step 1: Wash your hair

Before styling your hair, make sure it is clean and dry. Use a shampoo and conditioner that suits your hair type to ensure a healthy scalp and soft hair.

Step 2: Apply hair gel or wax

Take a small amount of hair gel or wax and apply it to your hair. Start at the roots and work your way up to the tips. Use your fingers to spread the product evenly throughout your hair.

Step 3: Comb your hair

Use a comb to comb your hair in the direction you want it to spike. If you want a messy look, comb your hair in different directions to create texture.

Step 4: Blow-dry your hair (optional)

If you want your hair to set faster, use a hair dryer to blow-dry your hair. Use a low heat setting to prevent damage to your hair.

Step 5: Spike your hair

Use your fingers to spike your hair in the direction you want it to go. If you want a more structured look, use a comb to shape your hair.

Step 6: Add finishing touches

Once you're satisfied with the shape and height of your spikes, use a little more product to hold them in place. You can also use hairspray to set your hairstyle and add shine.

Tips for Spiking Short Hair Male

Here are some tips to help you achieve the perfect spiky hairstyle:

  • Start with a small amount of product and add more as needed to prevent your hair from looking greasy.
  • Use a hair dryer to set your spikes faster.
  • Experiment with different hairstyles to find the one that suits you best.
  • Use a comb to add texture to your hair for a more natural look.
  • Don't forget to wash your hair regularly to keep it healthy and clean.

FAQs

Can I spike my hair if it's too short?

Yes, you can still spike your hair if it's very short. Use a small amount of product and a comb to create small spikes.

What's the best product to use for spiking short hair?

Hair gel and wax are both great products for spiking short hair. Choose the one that suits your hair type and the look you want to achieve.

Hair Care for Spiked Hair

Spiking your hair can put stress on your hair strands, so it's important to take care of your hair to keep it healthy. Here are some tips:

  • Use a shampoo and conditioner that suits your hair type.
  • Avoid using too much product, as it can weigh down your hair and make it look greasy.
  • Use a hair mask once a week to nourish and hydrate your hair.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.

Now that you know how to spike short hair male, you can experiment with different hairstyles to find the one that suits you best. Remember to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and looking great.
