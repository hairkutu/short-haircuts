How To Get Cornrows For Guys A Step By Step Guide

Cornrows are a popular hairstyle for both men and women, but getting them can be a bit of a challenge if you're not familiar with the process. In this article, we'll provide a step-by-step guide on how to get cornrows for guys, including tips on hair care and frequently asked questions.

Table of Contents

  • How to Get Cornrows for Guys
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Maintaining Cornrows

How to Get Cornrows for Guys

Cornrows involve braiding the hair into a series of small, tight braids that lay flat against the scalp. This hairstyle is often associated with African American culture, but it can be worn by anyone regardless of ethnicity. Here's what you'll need to get started:

  • A comb
  • A brush
  • A pair of scissors
  • Hair gel or pomade
  • Braiding hair (optional)

Step-by-Step Guide

Follow these steps to get cornrows:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair. If your hair is too oily, it may be difficult to braid.
  2. Using a comb, part your hair into sections. The size of the sections will depend on how thick you want your cornrows to be. The smaller the section, the tighter the braid will be.
  3. Apply hair gel or pomade to each section. This will help to keep the hair in place and make it easier to braid.
  4. Take a small section of hair at the front of your head and divide it into three sections.
  5. Begin braiding the hair by crossing the right section over the middle section, then the left section over the middle section.
  6. Continue braiding, adding more hair to each section as you go. This will create a small braid that lays flat against the scalp.
  7. Continue braiding until you reach the end of the section, then tie off the braid with a small elastic band or piece of thread. Repeat this process with each section of hair, working your way back towards the nape of your neck.
  8. Once you've braided all of the sections, you can leave the braids as is or style them into different patterns.
  9. Trim any loose hairs or flyaways with a pair of scissors.

Tips for Maintaining Cornrows

Here are a few tips for maintaining cornrows:

  • Avoid getting your hair wet for the first few days after getting cornrows. This will help the braids to set and prevent frizz.
  • Use a silk or satin pillowcase to prevent the braids from getting tangled while you sleep.
  • Avoid using heavy products on your hair, as they can weigh down the braids and cause them to come loose.
  • Moisturize your scalp regularly to prevent dryness and itching.

FAQs

How long does it take to get cornrows?

The length of time it takes to get cornrows will depend on the size and complexity of the braids. On average, it can take anywhere from 2-6 hours.

Are cornrows painful?

Cornrows can be uncomfortable at first, especially if the braids are tight. However, the discomfort should subside after a day or two.

How long do cornrows last?

Cornrows can last anywhere from 2-8 weeks, depending on how well they are maintained and how quickly your hair grows.

Hair Care for Cornrows

Caring for your hair while it's in cornrows is important for maintaining the style and preventing damage. Here are some tips:

  • Moisturize your scalp regularly with a light oil or leave-in conditioner.
  • Avoid using heavy products on your hair, as they can weigh down the braids and cause them to come loose.
  • Wash your hair once a week with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using hot styling tools on your hair, as they can damage the braids and cause breakage.

With these tips and a bit of practice, you can achieve the perfect cornrow hairstyle for guys. Whether you're going for a classic look or something more modern, cornrows are a versatile and stylish choice that can be worn by anyone.
