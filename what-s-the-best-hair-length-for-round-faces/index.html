What s The Best Hair Length For Round Faces

Written by Christine Jun 19, 2023 · 6 min read

If you have a round face, you may be wondering what the best hair length is for your face shape. The good news is that there is no one-size-fits-all answer – it all depends on your individual features and personal style. In this article, we'll explore some of the best hair lengths for round faces, as well as some tips for maintaining healthy and beautiful hair.

How to Determine Your Face Shape

Before we dive into the best hair lengths for round faces, it's important to determine if you actually have a round face shape. Here's how to do it:

  1. Stand in front of a mirror with your hair pulled back.
  2. Use a measuring tape to measure the width of your forehead, cheekbones, and jawline.
  3. If all three measurements are roughly equal, you have a round face shape.

Once you've determined your face shape, you can start considering different hair lengths that will flatter your features.

Best Hair Lengths for Round Faces

Short Hair

Short hair can be a great option for round faces, as it can help to elongate the face and create the illusion of height. A pixie cut or a bob that falls just below the chin are two great options to consider. Adding layers can also help to create movement and texture.

Medium-Length Hair

For those who prefer a bit more length, a medium-length cut can also work well for round faces. A collarbone-length lob (long bob) or a shaggy layered cut can help to create the appearance of a more angular jawline.

Long Hair

Contrary to popular belief, long hair can actually work well for round faces – as long as it's cut and styled correctly. A long layered cut with face-framing pieces can help to balance out a round face shape. If you prefer straight hair, consider adding some volume at the crown to create the illusion of a more oval-shaped face.

Tips for Maintaining Healthy Hair

No matter what hair length you choose, it's important to take good care of your locks. Here are some tips for maintaining healthy hair:

  • Wash your hair regularly, but not too often – over-washing can strip your hair of natural oils.
  • Use a nourishing conditioner to keep your hair hydrated and prevent breakage.
  • Avoid using heat tools (like blow dryers and straighteners) too often, as they can damage your hair and cause split ends.
  • Eat a healthy diet rich in vitamins and minerals to promote hair growth and strength.
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends and keep your hair looking healthy.

FAQ

What hairstyles should I avoid if I have a round face?

Avoid hairstyles that add too much volume to the sides of your face, as this can make your face look wider. Also, avoid blunt bangs or straight, one-length cuts that end at your chin.

Can I still wear my hair in a ponytail if I have a round face?

Absolutely – just make sure to position your ponytail higher up on your head to create the illusion of more height. You can also add some face-framing layers to soften the look.

Hair Care

Remember, the best hair length for your round face is one that makes you feel confident and beautiful. Whether you prefer short, medium, or long hair, there are plenty of options to choose from. And with the right hair care routine, you can keep your locks looking healthy and gorgeous all year long.
