How To Make Bangs Flatter A Step By Step Guide

Written by Oliver Jul 13, 2023 Â· 6 min read

If you have bangs, you know how frustrating it can be when they start to stick up or look too poofy. But don't worry, there are easy ways to make your bangs flatter and more sleek. In this article, we'll go over some tips and tricks to help you achieve the perfect flat bangs look.

Table of Contents

  1. How to Make Bangs Flatter
  2. Step-by-Step Guide
  3. Tips for Flatter Bangs
  4. FAQ
  5. Hair Care for Flatter Bangs

How to Make Bangs Flatter

Before we get started, it's important to note that there are a few different ways to make your bangs flatter. Depending on your hair type and the look you're going for, some methods may work better than others. Here are a few options:

  • Blow-dry your bangs with a round brush
  • Use a flat iron
  • Apply heat protectant and blow-dry with a paddle brush
  • Use hair products like mousse or hairspray

Step-by-Step Guide

Now that you know your options, let's go over a step-by-step guide to making your bangs flatter:

  1. Wash your hair and towel dry your bangs
  2. Apply a heat protectant to your bangs
  3. Blow-dry your bangs with a round brush, pulling them down and to the side
  4. Once they're dry, use a flat iron to smooth out any remaining bumps or waves
  5. Apply a small amount of hair oil to your bangs to add shine and smoothness
  6. Finish with a light-hold hairspray to keep your bangs in place

Remember to use heat protectant before applying any heat to your hair, and avoid using the flat iron on wet hair.

Tips for Flatter Bangs

Here are some additional tips for achieving flatter bangs:

  • Blow-dry your bangs in the opposite direction of where you want them to lay to create volume, then use the flat iron to smooth them down
  • Use a paddle brush instead of a round brush if you have thick, coarse hair
  • Apply a volumizing mousse to your roots before blow-drying to add lift
  • Trim your bangs regularly to keep them at the right length and prevent them from looking too heavy

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about making bangs flatter:

Can I use a hair straightener to make my bangs flatter?

Yes, a hair straightener can be a great tool for making your bangs flatter. Just be sure to use a heat protectant and avoid using it on wet hair.

Should I blow-dry my bangs before using a flat iron?

Yes, it's best to blow-dry your bangs first to remove any excess moisture and create a smooth base. Then, use the flat iron to further smooth out any bumps or waves.

Hair Care for Flatter Bangs

Maintaining flatter bangs requires some extra care and attention. Here are a few tips for keeping your bangs looking their best:

  • Avoid touching your bangs throughout the day to prevent them from becoming oily or misshapen
  • Use dry shampoo to absorb any excess oil or sweat
  • Trim your bangs regularly to keep them at the right length and prevent them from looking too heavy
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy and nourished

By following these tips and techniques, you can easily make your bangs flatter and more polished. Experiment with different methods to find the one that works best for you, and don't forget to take good care of your hair to keep it looking its best.
