How To Cut Your Own Hair Choppy Bob A Step By Step Guide

Written by Christine Aug 12, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Are you tired of spending a fortune at the salon to get a choppy bob haircut? Well, the good news is you can achieve the same look by cutting your own hair at home. In this article, we will guide you through the process of cutting your own hair choppy bob in relaxed English language.

Description

A choppy bob is a trendy and versatile haircut that looks great on anyone. It involves cutting the hair into layers of different lengths to create a messy, textured look. This hairstyle is perfect for those who want to add volume and dimension to their hair.

How to Cut Your Own Hair Choppy Bob

Before you start cutting your hair, make sure you have the necessary tools such as sharp scissors, a comb, and hair clips. Here's how to cut your own hair choppy bob:

Step 1: Wash and Dry Your Hair

Start by washing your hair and drying it thoroughly. This will make it easier to cut and ensure an even cut.

Step 2: Section Your Hair

Using a comb, section your hair into four parts: the top, left, right, and back. Clip each section to keep it out of the way.

Step 3: Cut the Back Section

Start by cutting the back section of your hair. Take a small section of hair, hold it between your fingers, and cut the hair at an angle. Repeat this process for the entire back section.

Step 4: Cut the Side Sections

Once the back section is complete, move on to the side sections. Take small sections of hair, hold them at an angle, and cut them to match the length of the back section.

Step 5: Cut the Top Section

Finally, move on to the top section of your hair. Take small sections of hair and cut them at an angle to create layers. Remember to keep the length of the top section longer than the back and side sections.

Step 6: Check for Evenness

Once you've finished cutting your hair, check for evenness by pulling the hair forward and checking for any uneven sections. If you find any, trim them until you're satisfied with the result.

Tips

Cutting your own hair can be challenging, but with these tips, you can achieve a professional-looking choppy bob:

  • Start with small sections of hair and work your way up to larger sections.
  • Use sharp scissors to ensure a clean cut.
  • Don't cut too much hair at once. It's better to take small sections and cut them gradually.
  • Keep the length of the top section longer than the back and side sections.

FAQ

Can I cut my hair choppy bob with any hair type?

Yes, you can cut your hair choppy bob with any hair type. However, the length and thickness of your hair will affect the final result. If you have thin hair, you may want to keep the layers longer to add volume. If you have thick hair, you can cut the layers shorter for a more textured look.

How often should I cut my hair choppy bob?

It's recommended to cut your hair choppy bob every 6-8 weeks to maintain the shape and texture.

Hair Care

After cutting your hair, it's important to take care of it to maintain its health and texture. Here are some hair care tips:

  • Use a shampoo and conditioner that's suitable for your hair type.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often.
  • Apply a hair mask once a week to nourish and hydrate your hair.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.

Now that you know how to cut your own hair choppy bob, it's time to give it a try. Remember to take your time, follow the steps carefully, and have fun experimenting with different lengths and angles. With practice, you'll be able to achieve a salon-worthy choppy bob haircut at home.
