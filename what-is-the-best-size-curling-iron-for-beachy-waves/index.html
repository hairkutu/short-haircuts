What Is The Best Size Curling Iron For Beachy Waves

Written by Christine Aug 14, 2023 · 7 min read

If you’re looking to achieve those gorgeous, effortless beachy waves, you’ve probably wondered what the best size curling iron is for the job. With so many options available, it can be tough to know which one to choose. In this article, we’ll break down everything you need to know about selecting the perfect size curling iron for your beachy waves.

Description: Understanding the Basics

Before we dive into the specifics of curling iron size, let’s start with the basics of beachy waves. This style is all about loose, relaxed curls that mimic the look of a day spent at the beach. The waves should be undone and tousled, with a natural, carefree vibe. Achieving this look requires the right tools, including the perfect curling iron.

How to: Choosing the Right Size Curling Iron

So, what size curling iron should you be using for beachy waves? The answer depends on several factors, including your hair length and thickness, as well as the specific look you’re going for. In general, a barrel size between 1 and 1.5 inches is ideal for creating beachy waves.

Step-by-Step Guide:

1. Start by selecting a curling iron with a barrel size between 1 and 1.5 inches. This size will allow you to create loose, relaxed waves without making them too tight or structured.

2. Begin by sectioning your hair into manageable pieces. This will make it easier to curl and ensure that you don’t miss any sections.

3. Take a small section of hair and wrap it around the curling iron, starting at the base of the section and working your way to the ends.

4. Hold the hair on the curling iron for a few seconds, then release. Repeat this process on all sections of your hair until you’ve achieved your desired look.

Tips: Getting the Best Results

While selecting the right size curling iron is important, there are a few other tips and tricks you should keep in mind to achieve the best results:

  • Use a heat protectant spray to prevent damage to your hair
  • Don’t curl your hair too tightly – loose, relaxed waves are key
  • Alternate the direction of your curls to create a more natural look
  • Finish with a texturizing spray or sea salt spray to add volume and hold to your waves

FAQ: Common Questions About Beachy Waves

Q: Can I achieve beachy waves with a straightener?

A: Yes! While a curling iron is the traditional tool for creating beachy waves, you can also achieve this look with a straightener. Simply twist your hair before running the straightener over it to create loose, undone waves.

Q: How do I prevent my beachy waves from falling out?

A: To ensure your waves stay in place, be sure to use a hairspray or other styling product to hold them in place. You can also try curling your hair in smaller sections or using a smaller curling iron barrel for tighter curls.

Hair Care: Keeping Your Beachy Waves Looking Great

Now that you’ve achieved those gorgeous beachy waves, it’s important to take care of your hair to ensure they last as long as possible. Here are a few tips to keep in mind:

  • Avoid washing your hair too frequently, as this can dry it out and make your waves fall flat
  • Use a hydrating conditioner to keep your hair nourished and healthy
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair – let it air dry whenever possible
  • When styling, use a heat protectant spray to prevent damage to your hair

By following these tips and choosing the right size curling iron, you can achieve those beautiful, effortless beachy waves that everyone loves. So go ahead – rock that tousled, carefree look and enjoy your gorgeous new style!
