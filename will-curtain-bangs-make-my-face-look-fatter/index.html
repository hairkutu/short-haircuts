Will Curtain Bangs Make My Face Look Fatter Everything You Need To Know

Written by William Aug 24, 2023 Â· 7 min read

If you're considering getting curtain bangs, you might be wondering if they will make your face look fatter. Curtain bangs are a trendy and versatile hairstyle that can work for many face shapes and hair types. However, it's essential to know if they will flatter your face or not before getting them.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to Get Curtain Bangs
  • Step-by-Step Guide to Getting Curtain Bangs
  • Tips to Avoid Making Your Face Look Fatter
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care Tips for Curtain Bangs

Description

Curtain bangs are a type of bangs that are parted in the middle and swept to the sides, framing the face. They are called curtain bangs because they resemble curtains that are parted in the middle. They are a popular hairstyle because they can work for many face shapes and hair types. However, if you have a round or chubby face, you might be wondering if curtain bangs will make your face look fatter.

How to Get Curtain Bangs

If you're interested in getting curtain bangs, you should visit a professional hairstylist who can help you determine if they will suit your face shape and hair type. Your hairstylist will also be able to give you advice on how to style and maintain your curtain bangs.

Step-by-Step Guide to Getting Curtain Bangs

If you're ready to get curtain bangs, here's a step-by-step guide to help you:

  1. Research different types of curtain bangs to find the one that will suit you best.
  2. Visit a professional hairstylist who can help you determine if curtain bangs are right for you.
  3. Discuss the length and thickness of your curtain bangs with your hairstylist.
  4. Decide if you want your curtain bangs to be wispy or blunt.
  5. Get your curtain bangs cut by a professional hairstylist.
  6. Style your curtain bangs using a blow dryer and a round brush.

Tips to Avoid Making Your Face Look Fatter

If you're worried about curtain bangs making your face look fatter, here are some tips to help you:

  • Choose curtain bangs that are longer and more voluminous to help elongate your face.
  • Avoid curtain bangs that are blunt and straight across, as they can make your face appear wider.
  • Style your curtain bangs by parting them in the middle and sweeping them to the sides to create a slimming effect.
  • Avoid adding too much volume to your curtain bangs, as this can make your face look wider.

FAQs

Will curtain bangs make my face look fatter?

Curtain bangs can make your face look fatter if they are too short or too blunt. However, if you choose the right length and style for your face shape, they can be very flattering and slimming.

What face shapes are best for curtain bangs?

Curtain bangs can work for many face shapes, including oval, square, heart, and diamond-shaped faces. However, they might not be the best option for round or chubby faces.

How do I style curtain bangs?

You can style your curtain bangs by parting them in the middle and sweeping them to the sides using a blow dryer and a round brush. You can also add some texture and volume by using a texturizing spray or a volumizing mousse.

Hair Care Tips for Curtain Bangs

If you want to keep your curtain bangs looking their best, here are some hair care tips to follow:

  • Wash your curtain bangs regularly to keep them clean and free of oil and dirt.
  • Use a conditioner to keep your curtain bangs soft and manageable.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your curtain bangs, as this can cause damage and breakage.
  • Trim your curtain bangs regularly to keep them looking neat and tidy.
  • Avoid using heavy styling products on your curtain bangs, as this can weigh them down and make them look greasy.

In conclusion, curtain bangs can be a flattering and trendy hairstyle for many face shapes and hair types. However, if you have a round or chubby face, you should be careful when choosing the length and style of your curtain bangs to avoid making your face look fatter. If you follow the tips and hair care advice in this article, you can enjoy beautifully styled curtain bangs that flatter your face and make you feel confident and beautiful.
