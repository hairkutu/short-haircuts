How To Make Really Straight Hair Curly A Guide To Perfect Curls

Written by Aprilia Aug 11, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Do you have naturally straight hair and wish to switch things up? Wavy or curly hair can add volume and texture to your hair, but it can be challenging to achieve those perfect curls without damaging your hair. In this article, we will guide you on how to make really straight hair curly without using any heat or chemicals.

How to Make Really Straight Hair Curly

There are several ways to make straight hair curly without using heat or chemicals. Here are some easy and effective methods to get those perfect curls.

Method 1: Braid Your Hair

Braiding your hair is one of the simplest and most effective ways to get curly hair without using any heat or chemicals. Follow these steps for perfect curls:

  1. Wash your hair and let it air dry until it's about 80% dry.
  2. Divide your hair into sections and braid each section tightly.
  3. Secure the ends of each braid with a hair tie.
  4. Leave the braids overnight or for at least 6-8 hours.
  5. Undo the braids and gently comb your hair with your fingers to separate the curls.

You can also use different types of braids, such as French braids or Dutch braids, for different types of curls.

Method 2: Twist Your Hair

Twisting your hair is another easy and effective way to get curly hair without using any heat or chemicals. Follow these steps for perfect curls:

  1. Wash your hair and let it air dry until it's about 80% dry.
  2. Divide your hair into sections and twist each section tightly.
  3. Secure the ends of each twist with a hair tie.
  4. Leave the twists overnight or for at least 6-8 hours.
  5. Undo the twists and gently comb your hair with your fingers to separate the curls.

You can also use different types of twists, such as two-strand twists or three-strand twists, for different types of curls.

Method 3: Use Rollers

Using rollers is another great way to get curly hair without using any heat or chemicals. Follow these steps for perfect curls:

  1. Wash your hair and let it air dry until it's about 80% dry.
  2. Divide your hair into sections and roll each section tightly with a roller.
  3. Secure the rollers with hair clips.
  4. Leave the rollers in for at least 6-8 hours or overnight.
  5. Remove the rollers and gently comb your hair with your fingers to separate the curls.

You can use different types of rollers, such as foam rollers or Velcro rollers, for different types of curls.

Tips for Perfect Curls

Here are some tips to help you achieve perfect curls:

  • Use a leave-in conditioner or curl-enhancing product to help define your curls.
  • Don't brush your curls too much, as it can cause frizz.
  • Use a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer to dry your curls without causing frizz.
  • Sleep on a silk or satin pillowcase to prevent frizz and breakage.
  • Avoid using hot tools, such as flat irons or curling irons, as they can damage your hair.

FAQs

Can I use heat to make my straight hair curly?

Yes, you can use heat to make your hair curly, but it can damage your hair if not done properly. We recommend using heatless methods, such as braiding or twisting your hair, to get curly hair.

How long will my curls last?

Your curls can last for several days, depending on how well you maintain them. You can refresh your curls by spraying them with water or a curl-enhancing product.

Hair Care

It's important to take care of your hair to prevent damage and maintain healthy curls. Here are some tips for hair care:

  • Wash your hair with a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to prevent dryness.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to moisturize your hair.
  • Avoid using harsh chemicals or dyes on your hair.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and heat by wearing a hat or using a leave-in conditioner with SPF.

With these tips and methods, you can achieve perfect curls without damaging your hair. Experiment with different styles and find the perfect curls that suit you.
