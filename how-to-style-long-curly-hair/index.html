How To Style Long Curly Hair A Step By Step Guide

Written by Petter Jun 28, 2023 · 7 min read

Long curly hair can be both beautiful and challenging to style. Whether you have natural curls or use curling tools to achieve your look, there are many ways to style your hair to perfection. In this guide, we’ll show you step-by-step how to style long curly hair and provide tips on how to care for your hair.

Table of Contents

  • How to Style Long Curly Hair
  • Tips for Styling Long Curly Hair
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care for Long Curly Hair

How to Style Long Curly Hair

Step 1: Start with Clean Hair

Before you style your long curly hair, make sure it’s clean and free of any product buildup. Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner that’s designed for curly hair, and avoid using hot water, which can dry out your hair and cause frizz.

Step 2: Apply a Leave-In Conditioner

After washing your hair, apply a leave-in conditioner to help detangle your curls and keep them hydrated. This will also make it easier to style your hair later on.

Step 3: Define Your Curls

If you have natural curls, use a curl-defining cream or gel to enhance your curls and reduce frizz. Apply the product evenly throughout your hair, then use your fingers or a wide-tooth comb to define your curls.

If you don’t have natural curls, you can use a curling iron or hot rollers to create loose curls. Start by dividing your hair into sections, then wrap each section around the curling iron or hot roller and hold for a few seconds. Repeat this process until all of your hair is curled.

Step 4: Choose Your Style

There are many ways to style long curly hair, depending on your personal preference and the occasion. Here are a few ideas:

  • Half-Up, Half-Down: Divide your hair into two sections, then gather the top section and secure it with a hair tie or clip. Leave the bottom section down.
  • Braids: Create a braided crown by braiding two sections of hair on either side of your head, then pinning them together at the back.
  • Ponytail: Pull your hair back into a high ponytail, leaving a few curly tendrils loose around your face.

Step 5: Lock in Your Style

Once you’ve styled your hair, use a hairspray or hair oil to lock in your look and reduce frizz. Avoid touching your hair too much, as this can cause your curls to lose their shape.

Tips for Styling Long Curly Hair

Styling long curly hair can be a bit of a challenge, but with these tips, you can achieve the perfect look:

  • Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your curls, as brushes can cause frizz.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as this can damage your hair and cause split ends.
  • Consider getting regular trims to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.
  • Try sleeping on a silk pillowcase, which can help reduce frizz and prevent hair breakage.

FAQs

How often should I wash my long curly hair?

This depends on your hair type and personal preference. Some people with curly hair prefer to wash their hair every few days, while others wash their hair once a week. Experiment with different washing schedules to find what works best for you.

What’s the best way to prevent frizz in long curly hair?

There are a few ways to prevent frizz in long curly hair. First, avoid using hot water when washing your hair, as this can dry out your hair and cause frizz. Second, use a leave-in conditioner to keep your hair hydrated. Finally, avoid touching your hair too much, as this can cause your curls to lose their shape.

Hair Care for Long Curly Hair

Long curly hair requires a bit of extra care to keep it healthy and looking its best. Here are a few tips:

  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner that’s designed for curly hair.
  • Avoid using hot water when washing your hair.
  • Use a leave-in conditioner to keep your hair hydrated.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often.
  • Consider getting regular trims to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.

With these tips and techniques, you can style long curly hair with ease and confidence. Whether you prefer a casual, everyday look or something more formal, there’s a style that’s perfect for you.
