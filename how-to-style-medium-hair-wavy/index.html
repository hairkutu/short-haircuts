Introduction

Medium hair with waves can be a versatile and beautiful hairstyle. However, it can be challenging to style, especially if you don't know where to start. Fortunately, with the right techniques, you can achieve effortless, beachy waves or defined curls that last all day.

In this step-by-step guide, we'll show you how to style medium hair wavy, along with tips for maintaining your waves and caring for your hair.

How to Style Medium Hair Wavy

Follow these steps to style your medium hair wavy:

Step 1: Prep Your Hair

Start by washing your hair with a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner. Towel dry your hair and apply a heat protectant spray or serum to protect your hair from heat damage.

Step 2: Add Texture

Apply a texturizing spray or mousse to your hair to add volume and hold. Use your fingers to scrunch your hair while blow drying on a low heat setting.

Step 3: Use a Curling Iron or Wand

Section your hair and use a curling iron or wand to create loose waves or tight curls, depending on your preference. Start at the roots and wrap the hair around the barrel, holding for a few seconds before releasing. Continue until all sections are complete.

Step 4: Shake and Set

Use your fingers to shake out your hair and loosen the curls. Finish with a light hold hairspray to set your waves in place.

Tips for Styling Wavy Hair

Here are some tips for styling medium hair wavy:

  • Apply products to damp hair to enhance waves and prevent frizz.
  • Use a diffuser attachment on your blow dryer to enhance natural waves and curls.
  • Experiment with different curling techniques, such as twisting or braiding sections of hair before curling.
  • Consider getting regular trims to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.

FAQs

Q: Can I style wavy hair without heat?

A: Yes, you can create waves without heat by braiding your hair or twisting sections and letting them air dry. You can also use rollers or flexi rods to set your hair overnight.

Q: How often should I wash my wavy hair?

A: It depends on your hair type and lifestyle. If you have oily hair, you may need to wash more frequently, while dry hair can go longer between washes. Aim to wash your hair every 2-3 days, or as needed.

Hair Care for Wavy Hair

Here are some tips for caring for your wavy hair:

  • Use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner to prevent dryness and frizz.
  • Avoid using hot tools too often, as this can damage your hair.
  • Apply a deep conditioning treatment once a week to nourish and hydrate your hair.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and chlorine by wearing a hat or using a leave-in conditioner.

By following these tips and techniques, you can easily style your medium hair wavy and keep it looking healthy and beautiful.
