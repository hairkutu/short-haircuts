How To Make A Fat Face Look Thinner With Hair Tips And Tricks

Written by Luffy Apr 14, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Having a round or chubby face can be adorable, but if you're looking for ways to achieve a slimmer look, there are some hair styling tricks you can try. In this article, we'll show you how to make a fat face look thinner with hair.

Table of Contents

  • Haircut
  • Hairstyle
  • Color
  • Bangs
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Haircut

The first step to making a fat face look thinner with hair is to get the right haircut. A layered haircut that falls below the chin can help elongate the face and create a slimmer appearance. Avoid blunt cuts or bobs that end at the chin, as they can make the face look wider.

Step by Step

  • Consult with your hairstylist to find the best haircut for your face shape.
  • Opt for a layered haircut that falls below the chin.
  • Avoid blunt cuts or bobs that end at the chin.

Hairstyle

The way you style your hair can also make a big difference in how slim your face looks. Avoid hairstyles that add volume to the sides of your face, such as tight curls or big waves. Instead, opt for sleek hairstyles that pull your hair away from your face.

Step by Step

  • Use a straightening iron to create sleek, straight hair.
  • Pull your hair back into a low ponytail or bun to elongate the face.
  • Avoid hairstyles that add volume to the sides of your face, such as tight curls or big waves.

Color

The right hair color can also help make a fat face look thinner. Darker hair colors create a shadow effect that can help slim the face. Avoid lighter hair colors that can make the face look wider.

Step by Step

  • Consult with your hairstylist to find the best hair color for your skin tone.
  • Opt for darker hair colors that create a shadow effect.
  • Avoid lighter hair colors that can make the face look wider.

Bangs

Bangs can be a great way to slim down your face, but it's important to get the right type of bangs. Avoid heavy, blunt bangs that can shorten the face and make it look wider. Instead, opt for side-swept bangs that create a diagonal line across the face.

Step by Step

  • Consult with your hairstylist to find the best type of bangs for your face shape.
  • Avoid heavy, blunt bangs that can shorten the face and make it look wider.
  • Opt for side-swept bangs that create a diagonal line across the face.

Tips

  • Avoid hairstyles that add volume to the sides of your face.
  • Try a layered haircut that falls below the chin.
  • Pull your hair back into a low ponytail or bun to elongate the face.
  • Opt for darker hair colors that create a shadow effect.
  • Avoid heavy, blunt bangs that can shorten the face and make it look wider.

FAQ

Q: Can I still have bangs if I have a chubby face?

A: Yes, you can still have bangs, but it's important to choose the right type of bangs. Side-swept bangs that create a diagonal line across the face can help elongate the face and make it look slimmer.

Q: Can I still have curly hair if I have a chubby face?

A: Yes, you can still have curly hair, but it's important to avoid tight curls that add volume to the sides of your face. Opt for loose curls or waves that create a more elongated look.

Hair Care

It's important to take good care of your hair to maintain a healthy, shiny appearance. Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner that's appropriate for your hair type, and avoid using hot styling tools too often.

Step by Step

  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner that's appropriate for your hair type.
  • Avoid using hot styling tools too often.
  • Use a heat protectant spray before using hot styling tools.

By following these tips and tricks, you can make a fat face look thinner with hair. Remember to choose the right haircut, hairstyle, and hair color that flatters your face shape. And don't forget to take good care of your hair to maintain a healthy, shiny appearance.
