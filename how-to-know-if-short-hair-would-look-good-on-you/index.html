How To Know If Short Hair Would Look Good On You The Ultimate Guide

Written by Frank Apr 03, 2023 Â· 9 min read

Are you considering chopping off your locks but not sure if short hair would look good on you? Short hair can be a bold and daring move, but it's not for everyone. It's essential to know your face shape, hair texture, and lifestyle before making a drastic change. In this article, we'll walk you through how to know if short hair would look good on you, step by step.

Table of Contents

  1. Step 1: Determine Your Face Shape
  2. Step 2: Consider Your Hair Texture
  3. Step 3: Evaluate Your Lifestyle
  4. Tips for Pulling Off Short Hair
  5. FAQs
  6. Hair Care Tips for Short Hair

Step 1: Determine Your Face Shape

The first step in figuring out if short hair would look good on you is to determine your face shape. Your face shape can help determine which hairstyles will flatter you the most. The most common face shapes are round, square, oval, and heart-shaped.

If you're not sure what your face shape is, pull your hair back and stand in front of a mirror. Use a washable marker to trace the outline of your face onto the mirror. Compare your traced shape to the common face shapes and see which one matches the best.

Round Face Shape:

If you have a round face shape, short hair can be a great option to elongate your face. Consider a pixie cut that is longer on top and shorter on the sides to add height to your face. You can also add layers to create volume and movement.

Square Face Shape:

If you have a square face shape, a short bob can soften your features. A chin-length bob with layered ends can help balance your jawline. Avoid blunt cuts that can make your face appear boxier.

Oval Face Shape:

If you have an oval face shape, you're in luck! Almost any short hairstyle will look great on you. Consider a blunt bob or a layered pixie cut to show off your features.

Heart-Shaped Face:

If you have a heart-shaped face, a short bob with side-swept bangs can balance your features. A layered pixie cut can also work well to draw attention to your eyes and cheekbones.

Step 2: Consider Your Hair Texture

Another factor to consider when deciding if short hair would look good on you is your hair texture. Short hair can work well for all hair types, but some styles may require more maintenance than others.

Straight Hair:

If you have straight hair, a blunt bob or a short pixie cut can work well. Consider adding layers or texture to create movement and volume.

Curly Hair:

If you have curly hair, a short bob or a pixie cut with longer layers can work well. Avoid a blunt cut that can make your hair appear triangular. Consider using a curl-defining product to enhance your natural texture.

Thick Hair:

If you have thick hair, a layered bob or a pixie cut can work well. Consider adding choppy layers to thin out your hair and make it more manageable.

Thin Hair:

If you have thin hair, a short bob or a pixie cut can add volume and thickness. Consider adding layers or texture to create the illusion of fuller hair.

Step 3: Evaluate Your Lifestyle

The last step in determining if short hair would look good on you is to evaluate your lifestyle. Short hair can be a low-maintenance option, but some styles may require more upkeep than others.

Consider your daily routine, job, and activity level when deciding on a short hairstyle. If you're always on the go, a wash-and-go style may work best. If you have a more formal job, a sleek bob or a pixie cut with longer layers can be a great option.

Tips for Pulling Off Short Hair

  • Invest in a good stylist who specializes in short haircuts.
  • Experiment with different styles and lengths to find what works best for you.
  • Consider adding highlights or lowlights to add dimension to your hair.
  • Use a texturizing product to create volume and movement.
  • Accessorize with statement earrings or bold makeup to draw attention to your face.

FAQs

Can short hair work for any age?

Yes! Short hair can work for any age, as long as you choose a style that flatters your face shape, hair texture, and lifestyle.

Will short hair make my face look rounder?

Short hair can elongate your face, especially if you choose a style with longer layers on top. Avoid blunt cuts that can make your face appear boxier.

Can I still style short hair?

Absolutely! Short hair can be styled in many different ways, from sleek and straight to tousled and textured.

Hair Care Tips for Short Hair

  • Wash your hair every 2-3 days to avoid over-drying.
  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to maintain your hair's natural oils.
  • Invest in a good texturizing product to add volume and movement.
  • Trim your hair every 4-6 weeks to maintain your style.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair to prevent damage.

Now that you know how to determine if short hair would look good on you, it's time to take the plunge! Remember to consult with a stylist and consider your face shape, hair texture, and lifestyle before making a drastic change. With the right style, you'll be rocking your short hair in no time!
