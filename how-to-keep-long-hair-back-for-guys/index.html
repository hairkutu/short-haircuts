How To Keep Long Hair Back For Guys A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Frank May 22, 2023 · 8 min read

Long hair has been a popular trend for guys in recent years, but keeping it out of the face can be a challenge. Whether you're growing your hair out or have been sporting long locks for a while, it's important to know how to keep it back and under control. In this article, we'll provide you with the best tips and tricks on how to keep long hair back for guys.

Table of Contents

  • How to Keep Long Hair Back for Guys
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips and Tricks
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care for Long Hair

How to Keep Long Hair Back for Guys

There are several ways to keep long hair back for guys, including using hair products, tying it back, and styling it in a certain way. Here are some popular methods:

Tie it Back with a Hair Tie

One of the easiest and most popular ways to keep long hair back for guys is to tie it back with a hair tie. This works well for those with longer hair that can be pulled back into a ponytail or bun. You can experiment with different types of hair ties, such as elastic bands or scrunchies, to find one that works best for you.

Use Hair Products

Hair products can also help keep long hair back for guys. This includes products like gel, wax, and hairspray. Apply the product to your hair and style it in a way that keeps it away from your face. Be sure to choose a product that works well with your hair type and texture.

Style it with a Headband

If you're not a fan of tying your hair back or using hair products, a headband can be a great alternative. This works well for those with shorter hair or bangs that tend to fall into the face. Choose a headband that fits comfortably and keeps your hair back securely.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide on how to keep long hair back for guys:

  1. Start with clean hair. Wash and condition your hair to remove any dirt or oil that can weigh it down.
  2. Apply your preferred hair product. This can be gel, wax, or hairspray. Apply it to your hair and style it in a way that keeps it away from your face.
  3. Tie your hair back. If your hair is long enough, tie it back into a ponytail or bun. Use a hair tie that fits securely but isn't too tight.
  4. Style it with a headband. If you prefer not to tie your hair back, try using a headband to keep it back and out of your face.
  5. Use bobby pins. For shorter hair or bangs, bobby pins can be a great way to keep your hair back. Simply pin back any sections that tend to fall into your face.

Tips and Tricks

Here are some additional tips and tricks on how to keep long hair back for guys:

  • Experiment with different hair products to find one that works best for your hair type and texture.
  • Invest in a good quality hair tie that won't break or damage your hair.
  • Keep a few bobby pins on hand for quick touch-ups throughout the day.
  • If you're growing out your hair, consider getting regular trims to keep it healthy and avoid split ends.

FAQs

Q: Can I still style my long hair if I keep it back?

A: Absolutely! Keeping your hair back doesn't mean you can't still style it. Use your preferred hair product to create different looks that still keep your hair out of your face.

Q: How often should I wash my long hair?

A: It depends on your hair type and how oily it gets. Generally, it's recommended to wash long hair every 2-3 days to keep it clean and healthy.

Hair Care for Long Hair

Keeping long hair back isn't just about styling it in a certain way. It's also important to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and looking its best. Here are some hair care tips for long hair:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using hot tools like blow dryers and straighteners too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair, starting from the bottom and working your way up.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair healthy and avoid split ends.
  • Avoid pulling your hair back too tightly, as this can cause breakage and damage.

With these tips and tricks, you'll be able to keep your long hair back and looking great. Whether you prefer tying it back, using hair products, or styling it with a headband, there's a method that will work for you. Remember to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and looking its best.
