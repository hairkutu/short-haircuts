How To Shorten Your Hair At Home A Step By Step Guide

Written by Bardi Jul 01, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Are you tired of your long locks and ready for a change? With the right tools and technique, you can easily shorten your hair at home. Not only is it convenient and cost-effective, but it also allows you to experiment with different styles and lengths without having to visit a salon. In this article, we'll walk you through how to shorten your hair at home, including tips, FAQ, and hair care advice.

How to Shorten Your Hair at Home

Before you start cutting, make sure you have the right tools on hand. Here's what you'll need:

  • Sharp hair cutting scissors
  • Comb
  • Hair clips
  • Mirror

Once you have your tools ready, follow these steps:

Step 1: Wash and Dry Your Hair

Start by washing and drying your hair. This will ensure that your hair is clean and free of any tangles that may interfere with the cutting process. It's best to use a gentle shampoo and conditioner to avoid damaging your hair.

Step 2: Section Your Hair

Using your comb, divide your hair into sections. Start by parting your hair down the middle, then divide it into four equal sections (two in the front and two in the back). Use hair clips to keep each section in place.

Step 3: Start Cutting

Starting with one of the back sections, use your comb to pull the hair straight up and away from your head. Hold it between your fingers and use your scissors to cut the desired length. Repeat this process with the other sections, making sure to cut evenly on both sides.

Step 4: Check for Evenness

Once you've finished cutting, use your mirror to check for evenness. If you notice any uneven areas, go back and trim them until you achieve your desired length.

Step 5: Style Your Hair

Now that you've shortened your hair, it's time to style it. You can use a blow dryer, flat iron, or curling iron to create your desired look. Don't forget to use a heat protectant to prevent damage to your hair.

Tips for Shortening Your Hair at Home

Shortening your hair at home can be a bit intimidating, but with these tips, you'll be a pro in no time:

  • Start with small cuts and work your way up to the desired length to avoid cutting too much off at once.
  • Use hair clips to keep your sections in place and avoid cutting the wrong section of hair.
  • Be patient and take your time. Rushing can lead to uneven cuts and mistakes.
  • When in doubt, leave it to the professionals. If you're unsure about cutting your hair, it's best to visit a salon.

FAQ

Is it difficult to shorten your hair at home?

Shortening your hair at home can be challenging, especially if you're not familiar with the process. However, with the right tools and technique, it's definitely doable.

How much hair should I cut off?

The amount of hair you cut off depends on your desired length. It's best to start with small cuts and work your way up to avoid cutting off too much at once.

Do I need to use special scissors?

Yes, it's important to use sharp hair cutting scissors. Using regular scissors can lead to split ends and damage to your hair.

Hair Care

After shortening your hair, it's important to take care of it to keep it healthy and looking its best. Here are some tips:

  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils.
  • Avoid using hot tools too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Use a heat protectant when using hot tools to prevent damage.
  • Get regular trims to maintain your desired length and keep your hair healthy.

Shortening your hair at home can be a fun and rewarding experience. With the right tools and technique, you can achieve your desired look without having to leave your house. Remember to be patient, take your time, and use the tips and advice in this article to ensure a successful cut.
