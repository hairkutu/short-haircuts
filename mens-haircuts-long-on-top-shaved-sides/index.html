Mens Haircuts Long On Top Shaved Sides A Stylish And Trendy Look

Written by Daniel Jun 14, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Are you looking for a stylish and trendy haircut? A mens haircut long on top shaved sides can be the perfect choice for you. This haircut is perfect for those who want to maintain a clean and sharp look while also keeping their hair long and stylish. In this article, we will go through everything you need to know about mens haircuts long on top shaved sides, including how to get the perfect cut, hair care tips, FAQ, and more.

Description

Mens haircuts long on top shaved sides are a popular and stylish choice for men who want to keep their hair long but also maintain a clean and sharp look. The haircut features long hair on the top of the head, which is usually styled into a pompadour, quiff, or slick back. The sides and back of the head are shaved or buzzed, creating a sharp contrast between the long hair on top and the short hair on the sides.

How to Get the Perfect Cut

If you want to get a mens haircut long on top shaved sides, it's important to find a good hairstylist who has experience with this style. You should also bring in pictures of the style you want to achieve so that your stylist can understand your vision. Here are the steps to get the perfect cut:

Step 1: Decide on the Length

The first step is to decide on the length of the hair on top. You can either go for a medium length or a longer length depending on your personal preference.

Step 2: Shave the Sides

The sides and back of the head need to be shaved or buzzed using clippers. The length of the sides can vary depending on your preference, but a number 1 or 2 clipper guard is usually used.

Step 3: Style the Top

The top of the hair can be styled into a pompadour, quiff, slick back, or any other style that you prefer. Use a hair product such as pomade, clay, or wax to style your hair in the desired shape.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind if you want to get a mens haircut long on top shaved sides:

- Make sure you find a good hairstylist who has experience with this style. - Bring in pictures of the style you want to achieve. - Use a high-quality hair product to style your hair. - Visit your hairstylist regularly to maintain the shape and length of your haircut.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about mens haircuts long on top shaved sides:

Q: How often do I need to get this haircut?

A: You should visit your hairstylist every 4 to 6 weeks to maintain the shape and length of your haircut.

Q: Can I style my hair differently?

A: Yes, you can style your hair in any way you like. However, the long hair on top and shaved sides are the key features of this haircut.

Q: Is this haircut suitable for all hair types?

A: Yes, this haircut can work for most hair types, including straight, wavy, or curly hair.

Hair Care

If you want to keep your mens haircut long on top shaved sides looking its best, it's important to take good care of your hair. Here are some tips:

- Use a high-quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair clean and healthy. - Use a hair product that suits your hair type and the style you want to achieve. - Avoid using hot tools such as straighteners or curling irons too often, as they can damage your hair. - Visit your hairstylist regularly to maintain the shape and length of your haircut.

In conclusion, mens haircuts long on top shaved sides are a stylish and trendy choice for men who want to keep their hair long while also maintaining a clean and sharp look. With the right hairstylist, hair care, and styling products, you can achieve the perfect look and maintain it for a long time.
