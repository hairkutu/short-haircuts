How To Fade Easy A Complete Guide

Written by Eveline Mar 30, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Fading your hair can add a touch of style and sophistication to your overall appearance. Fading is a popular hairstyle trend, but it can be challenging to get it right. In this article, we will guide you through the process of how to fade easy. We will cover everything from the tools you need to the step-by-step process and tips for maintaining your faded hair. Let's get started!

Table of Contents

  • Tools Needed
  • How to Fade Easy
  • Step-by-Step Process
  • Tips for Maintaining Your Fade
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Tools Needed

Before you start fading your hair, you need to have the right tools. Here are the essential tools you need:

  • Clippers
  • Guard sizes (1-4)
  • Barber comb
  • Scissors
  • Hair clip
  • Mirror
  • Towel
  • Hair product (optional)

How to Fade Easy

The first step to a successful fade is to start with clean, dry hair. You can wash your hair the day before or on the same day, but make sure it is completely dry before you start. Next, you need to determine the length of your fade. The fade can be short, medium, or long, depending on your preference. Once you have decided on the length, you need to choose the appropriate guard size.

Step-by-Step Process

Here is a step-by-step process to achieve an easy fade:

  1. Attach the appropriate guard size to your clippers.
  2. Start from the bottom of your hairline and work your way up, using an upward motion.
  3. Once you have finished the bottom, move up to the next section and repeat the process.
  4. Blend the sides and the top of your hair with scissors and a comb until you achieve your desired length.
  5. Use the hair clip to separate the top section from the sides and back.
  6. Attach a smaller guard size to your clippers and start from the bottom of the sides and back, working your way up. Use a flicking motion to blend the hair.
  7. Move to the top section and blend it with the sides and back until you achieve a seamless fade.
  8. Finish the look with your favorite hair product, if desired.

Tips for Maintaining Your Fade

Now that you have achieved an easy fade, it's essential to maintain it. Here are some tips:

  • Visit a barber every 2-4 weeks to maintain the fade.
  • Use a comb or brush to style your hair every day.
  • Apply a small amount of hair product to keep your hair in place.
  • Avoid washing your hair every day to prevent your fade from fading too quickly.
  • Cover your hair with a towel or hat when exposed to the sun for extended periods.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about fading hair:

Is fading hair damaging?

No, fading hair is not damaging. However, improper use of clippers can lead to cuts and bruises.

Can I fade my hair at home?

Yes, you can fade your hair at home. But it is recommended to seek professional help if you are not confident in your skills.

How long does a fade last?

A fade can last up to 2-4 weeks before needing maintenance.

Hair Care

Faded hair requires proper hair care to maintain its look. Here are some tips:

  • Use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using hot tools like straighteners and curling irons.
  • Apply a leave-in conditioner to protect your hair from heat damage.
  • Avoid using too much hair product to prevent buildup.
  • Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a leave-in conditioner with SPF.

Now that you know how to fade easy, you can achieve a stylish and sophisticated look with ease. Remember to use the right tools, follow the step-by-step process, and maintain your fade to keep it looking sharp. Happy fading!
