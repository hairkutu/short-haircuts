What Are Some Cute Hairstyles For Short Hair

Written by Petter Apr 17, 2023 Â· 6 min read

If you have short hair, you may think that your hairstyling options are limited. However, there are many cute hairstyles that you can try! In this blog post, we will discuss some of the best hairstyles for short hair that are easy to do and look great.

Description

Short hair can be a challenge to style, but with the right techniques and products, you can create a variety of cute hairstyles. Whether you have a pixie cut, a bob, or a lob, there are many options to choose from.

How to

Here are some step-by-step instructions for creating cute hairstyles for short hair:

1. Wavy Bob

To create a wavy bob, start by applying a texturizing spray to damp hair. Then, use a curling iron to create loose waves. Use your fingers to separate the waves and create a tousled look.

2. Faux Hawk

To create a faux hawk, start by applying a styling cream to your hair. Then, use a comb to create a small section of hair at the crown of your head. Twist the section of hair and secure it with bobby pins. Use a hair spray to keep the style in place.

3. Braided Crown

To create a braided crown, start by parting your hair down the middle. Then, braid each side of your hair and secure the braids with bobby pins. Use a hair spray to keep the style in place.

Step by step

Wavy Bob

  1. Apply texturizing spray to damp hair
  2. Use a curling iron to create loose waves
  3. Use your fingers to separate the waves and create a tousled look

Faux Hawk

  1. Apply styling cream to your hair
  2. Use a comb to create a small section of hair at the crown of your head
  3. Twist the section of hair and secure it with bobby pins
  4. Use a hair spray to keep the style in place

Braided Crown

  1. Part your hair down the middle
  2. Braid each side of your hair and secure the braids with bobby pins
  3. Use a hair spray to keep the style in place

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when styling short hair:

  • Use a texturizing spray to add volume and texture to your hair
  • Experiment with different hair accessories, such as headbands and hair clips, to add interest to your style
  • Invest in a high-quality hair dryer and styling tools to make the process easier and more efficient

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about cute hairstyles for short hair:

1. Can I still wear a ponytail with short hair?

Yes, you can wear a ponytail with short hair. Simply gather your hair at the nape of your neck and secure it with a hair tie.

2. What products should I use to style my short hair?

You should use a texturizing spray, a styling cream, and a hair spray to style your short hair. These products will help add volume, texture, and hold to your hair.

3. Can I still curl my short hair?

Yes, you can curl your short hair. Use a curling iron with a small barrel to create tight curls, or a larger barrel to create loose waves.

Hair Care

It's important to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and looking its best. Here are some tips for maintaining healthy short hair:

  • Wash your hair with a gentle shampoo and conditioner
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair looking neat and healthy
  • Protect your hair from the sun and harsh weather by wearing a hat or using a protective spray

Conclusion

There are many cute hairstyles for short hair that you can try. From wavy bobs to braided crowns, there are options for every occasion. Use these tips and techniques to create a look that's perfect for you!
