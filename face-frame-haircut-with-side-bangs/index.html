Face Frame Haircut With Side Bangs A Complete Guide In 2023

Written by Aprilia Jul 08, 2023 Â· 8 min read

Are you looking for a new hairstyle that will give you a fresh and updated look? If so, you may want to consider getting a face frame haircut with side bangs. This trendy hairstyle is perfect for anyone who wants to add some dimension and texture to their hair while still keeping it manageable and easy to style. In this article, we will provide you with a complete guide on how to get the perfect face frame haircut with side bangs, including tips, FAQs, and hair care advice.

Description: What is a Face Frame Haircut with Side Bangs?

A face frame haircut with side bangs is a versatile hairstyle that is designed to frame your face and enhance your natural features. It involves cutting the hair around the face into layers that gradually get longer towards the back. This creates a soft and feminine look that is perfect for any occasion. The side bangs are cut to frame the face and add some volume and texture to the hair. This haircut is suitable for any hair type, length, and texture, and can be customized to suit your individual preferences.

How to: Steps to Get a Face Frame Haircut with Side Bangs

Step 1: Consultation

The first step in getting a face frame haircut with side bangs is to schedule a consultation with your hairstylist. During this consultation, you should discuss your hair type, length, and texture, as well as your face shape and personal preferences. Your hairstylist will then recommend the best haircut and style that will suit your individual needs.

Step 2: Preparation

Once you have agreed on the haircut and style, your hairstylist will prepare your hair by washing and conditioning it. They will then comb through your hair and section it into manageable parts.

Step 3: Cutting

Your hairstylist will start cutting your hair using sharp scissors, starting at the front and working towards the back. They will create layers that gradually get longer towards the back, framing your face and enhancing your natural features. They will also cut the side bangs to frame your face and add some volume and texture to your hair.

Step 4: Styling

Once your haircut is complete, your hairstylist will style your hair using a blow dryer, brushes, and styling products. They may also use a curling iron or flat iron to add some waves or curls to your hair. They will then finish off the look with some hairspray or other finishing products to keep your hair in place.

Tips: How to Maintain a Face Frame Haircut with Side Bangs

To maintain your face frame haircut with side bangs, you should follow these tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly using a gentle shampoo and conditioner
  • Use a wide-toothed comb to detangle your hair
  • Blow-dry your hair using a round brush to add volume and texture
  • Use a curling iron or flat iron to add waves or curls to your hair
  • Trim your hair regularly to maintain its shape and texture
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as this can damage it
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy and shiny.

FAQ: Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Is a face frame haircut with side bangs suitable for all face shapes?

A: Yes, a face frame haircut with side bangs can be customized to suit any face shape. Your hairstylist will adjust the length and angle of the layers and bangs to complement your features.

Q: How often should I trim my hair to maintain a face frame haircut with side bangs?

A: You should trim your hair every 4-6 weeks to maintain its shape and texture.

Q: Can I style my face frame haircut with side bangs in different ways?

A: Yes, a face frame haircut with side bangs is versatile and can be styled in many different ways. You can wear it straight, wavy, curly, or in a ponytail or updo.

Hair Care: Products to Use for a Face Frame Haircut with Side Bangs

To keep your face frame haircut with side bangs looking its best, you should use the following hair care products:

  • Gentle shampoo and conditioner
  • Leave-in conditioner or hair serum
  • Hairspray or finishing spray
  • Heat protectant spray (if using heat styling tools)
  • Deep conditioning treatment
  • Styling products (such as mousse, gel, or wax)

By following these tips and using the right hair care products, you can maintain a beautiful and stylish face frame haircut with side bangs that will enhance your natural features and give you a fresh and updated look. So go ahead and book that appointment with your hairstylist today!
