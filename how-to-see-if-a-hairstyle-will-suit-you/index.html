How To See If A Hairstyle Will Suit You

Written by Bardi Apr 10, 2023 Â· 9 min read

Are you tired of getting a haircut and feeling disappointed with the results? Or have you ever seen a hairstyle you love but weren't sure if it would suit you? Well, worry no more! In this article, we'll show you how to see if a hairstyle will suit you and provide you with some tips to help you choose the perfect hairstyle for you.

Step 1: Determine Your Face Shape

The first step to determining if a hairstyle will suit you is to determine your face shape. Your face shape plays a significant role in choosing the right hairstyle because certain hairstyles look better on certain face shapes. There are six basic face shapes: round, square, oval, heart, diamond, and oblong.

If you're not sure what your face shape is, stand in front of a mirror and pull your hair back. Trace the outline of your face with a washable marker or lipstick. Then, look at the shape you've drawn and compare it to the six basic face shapes.

Once you know your face shape, you can start looking for hairstyles that will complement your features. For example, if you have a round face, you might want to avoid hairstyles that add volume to the sides of your face, as they can make your face appear wider. Instead, opt for hairstyles that add height and length, such as a high ponytail or a layered haircut.

Step 2: Consider Your Hair Texture and Type

Another important factor to consider when choosing a hairstyle is your hair texture and type. Your hair texture refers to the thickness and coarseness of your hair, while your hair type refers to whether your hair is straight, wavy, curly, or kinky.

For example, if you have fine hair, you might want to avoid hairstyles that are too heavy or voluminous, as they can make your hair look flat and lifeless. Instead, opt for hairstyles that add texture and movement, such as a layered bob or a shag haircut.

On the other hand, if you have thick, curly hair, you might want to avoid hairstyles that require a lot of maintenance, such as a blunt bob or a pixie cut. Instead, opt for hairstyles that embrace your natural texture, such as a long, layered haircut or a curly updo.

Step 3: Think About Your Lifestyle

When choosing a hairstyle, it's important to consider your lifestyle. Do you have a busy schedule and need a low-maintenance hairstyle? Or do you have a lot of free time to experiment with different hairstyles?

If you have a busy schedule, you might want to opt for a hairstyle that requires minimal styling, such as a messy bun or a low ponytail. On the other hand, if you have a lot of free time, you might want to experiment with different hairstyles, such as braids or a sleek, straight look.

Step 4: Try Virtual Hairstyle Apps

If you're still not sure if a hairstyle will suit you, try using virtual hairstyle apps. These apps allow you to upload a photo of yourself and try on different hairstyles virtually. Some popular virtual hairstyle apps include Hairstyle Try On and YouCam Makeup.

Using virtual hairstyle apps can be a fun and easy way to experiment with different hairstyles without committing to a haircut.

Tips for Choosing the Perfect Hairstyle

Here are some additional tips to help you choose the perfect hairstyle:

  • Consider your hair color and skin tone when choosing a hairstyle.
  • Look for inspiration from celebrities or friends with similar features.
  • Don't be afraid to try something new, but also be realistic about what will work for you.
  • Bring a photo of the hairstyle you want to your stylist to ensure they understand your vision.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about choosing the perfect hairstyle:

What is the best hairstyle for a round face?

The best hairstyle for a round face is one that adds height and length, such as a high ponytail or a layered haircut.

What is the best hairstyle for fine hair?

The best hairstyle for fine hair is one that adds texture and movement, such as a layered bob or a shag haircut.

What is the best hairstyle for thick, curly hair?

The best hairstyle for thick, curly hair is one that embraces your natural texture, such as a long, layered haircut or a curly updo.

Hair Care Tips

Once you've chosen the perfect hairstyle, it's important to take care of your hair to ensure it looks its best. Here are some hair care tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair looking healthy and prevent split ends.
  • Use hair products that are suited for your hair type and texture.

In conclusion, choosing the perfect hairstyle can be a daunting task, but by following these steps and tips, you'll be able to find a hairstyle that suits you and your lifestyle. Remember to consider your face shape, hair texture and type, and lifestyle when choosing a hairstyle, and don't be afraid to try something new!
