Can Short Hair Make Your Face Look Skinnier

Written by Robby Jun 09, 2023 Â· 9 min read

Short hair has always been a popular hairstyle choice for women. Not only does it look chic and trendy, but it can also give the appearance of a slimmer face. If you are looking to achieve a more contoured look, then short hair may be the perfect solution for you. In this blog post, we will explore whether short hair can really make your face look skinnier and provide some tips on how to achieve the best results.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How To
  • Step By Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

Short hair can definitely make your face look skinnier. When you have long hair, it can add volume and width to your face, making it appear larger. Short hair, on the other hand, can help to elongate your face and create a more contoured look.

The key to achieving a skinnier face with short hair is in the cut. A good haircut can create angles and lines that flatter your face shape and make it appear more slender.

How To

To achieve a skinnier face with short hair, you need to find the right cut for your face shape. Here are some tips to help you get started:

1. Determine your face shape

The first step is to determine your face shape. This will help you choose a haircut that flatters your features and complements your natural beauty.

There are six basic face shapes: round, oval, square, heart, diamond, and long. You can determine your face shape by measuring the width of your forehead, cheekbones, and jawline.

2. Choose the right haircut

Once you have determined your face shape, you can choose a haircut that flatters your features. Here are some general guidelines:

  • Round faces: opt for a cut that adds height and length to your face, such as a pixie or a bob with layers.
  • Oval faces: almost any short hairstyle will flatter your face shape, so feel free to experiment with different cuts and styles.
  • Square faces: choose a cut that softens your features, such as a layered bob or a side-swept pixie.
  • Heart-shaped faces: go for a cut that adds volume to the top of your head, such as a textured pixie or a layered bob.
  • Diamond faces: opt for a cut that adds width to your forehead and chin, such as a side-swept pixie or a bob with bangs.
  • Long faces: choose a cut that adds width to your face, such as a bob with layers or a textured pixie.

Step By Step

Here are some general steps to follow when getting a short haircut to make your face look skinnier:

1. Research hairstyles

Before going to the salon, do some research on hairstyles that would flatter your face shape. Look for pictures of celebrities or models with similar face shapes and hair textures to get an idea of what you like.

2. Consult with your stylist

When you arrive at the salon, consult with your stylist about your desired look. They can give you advice on what would work best for your face shape and hair texture.

3. Cut your hair

Once you have decided on a style, your stylist will cut your hair accordingly. They may also add layers or texture to create the illusion of a skinnier face.

4. Style your hair

After your haircut, your stylist will style your hair to complement your new look. They may use products such as hairspray or mousse to add volume and texture to your hair.

Tips

Here are some additional tips to keep in mind when trying to make your face look skinnier with short hair:

  • Add volume to the top of your head to create the illusion of a longer face.
  • Use a side part to balance out your features.
  • Avoid blunt cuts, which can make your face appear wider.
  • Experiment with different styles and textures to find what works best for you.

FAQ

Can short hair make my face look thinner?

Yes, short hair can create the illusion of a slimmer face by elongating your features and creating angles and lines that flatter your face shape.

What is the best short haircut for a round face?

A pixie cut or a bob with layers can add height and length to a round face, creating the illusion of a slimmer face.

What is the best short haircut for an oval face?

Almost any short haircut will flatter an oval face, so feel free to experiment with different styles and textures.

Hair Care

To keep your short hair looking its best, it is important to take good care of it. Here are some tips:

  • Use a shampoo and conditioner that is appropriate for your hair type.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as this can cause damage.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.
  • Use a hair mask or oil treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy and shiny.

In conclusion, short hair can definitely make your face look skinnier. By choosing the right haircut and styling your hair to complement your features, you can achieve a more contoured look that flatters your natural beauty.
