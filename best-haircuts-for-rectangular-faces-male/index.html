Best Haircuts For Rectangular Faces Male A Comprehensive Guide In 2023

Description

Rectangular faces are characterized by having a longer face shape with sharp, angular features. If you have a rectangular face, you may feel that certain haircuts don't flatter your features. However, there are plenty of haircuts that can complement your face shape and enhance your natural features. In this guide, we'll explore the best haircuts for rectangular faces male, how to determine your face shape, tips for styling your hair, and hair care tips for men with rectangular faces.

How to Determine Your Face Shape

Before we dive into the best haircuts for rectangular faces male, it's important to determine if you actually have a rectangular face shape. Here are the steps to follow:

  1. Stand in front of a mirror in a well-lit room.
  2. Using a washable marker or lipstick, trace the outline of your face on the mirror.
  3. Step back and examine the shape you've drawn.
  4. If your face is longer than it is wide and your forehead, cheeks, and jawline are all about the same width, you likely have a rectangular face shape.

Best Haircuts for Rectangular Faces Male

Now that you've determined your face shape, let's explore the best haircuts for rectangular faces male:

The Buzz Cut

The buzz cut is a classic and low-maintenance haircut that works well for rectangular faces. It emphasizes the sharp angles of your face and draws attention to your eyes.

The Crew Cut

The crew cut is another short haircut that complements rectangular faces. It's similar to the buzz cut, but with slightly longer hair on top that can be styled in different ways.

The Side Part

The side part is a versatile haircut that works well for rectangular faces. It adds volume to the top of your head, which balances out the length of your face.

The Quiff

The quiff is a stylish and trendy haircut that looks great on rectangular faces. It's similar to the side part, but with more volume on top that's styled upwards and back.

The Textured Crop

The textured crop is a short haircut with textured layers on top that add volume and dimension to your hair. It's a great option for rectangular faces because it adds width to your forehead and temples.

Tips for Styling Your Hair

Once you've chosen a haircut that complements your rectangular face, here are some tips for styling your hair:

  • Use a hair product that works well with your hair type and the style you're trying to achieve.
  • If you have a longer haircut, consider using a blow dryer to add volume and texture to your hair.
  • If you're going for a more polished look, consider using a pomade or wax to create definition and hold.
  • If you're going for a more casual look, consider using a sea salt spray to create texture and a messy, beachy look.

Frequently Asked Questions

What if I don't like short haircuts?

While short haircuts tend to work best for rectangular faces, there are still plenty of longer hairstyles that can complement your features. Consider a layered cut with textured ends or a medium-length haircut with a side part.

Can I still wear my hair in a ponytail?

A ponytail can be a great option for men with rectangular faces, especially if you have longer hair. Just make sure to add volume to the top of your head to balance out the length of your face.

Hair Care Tips for Men with Rectangular Faces

Here are some hair care tips to keep in mind if you have a rectangular face:

  • Regularly trim your hair to maintain your chosen haircut and keep your hair healthy.
  • Use a shampoo and conditioner that works well with your hair type and scalp.
  • Consider using a hair mask or deep conditioner once a week to keep your hair hydrated and healthy.
  • Avoid using hot tools like hair straighteners or curling irons too often, as they can damage your hair and make it look unhealthy.

With these tips and haircut options, you can find a hairstyle that complements your rectangular face and enhances your natural features. Whether you prefer a short, low-maintenance cut or a longer, layered style, there's a haircut out there for you.
