Is Straight Hair Back In Style 2020 A Guide To Achieving Sleek And Straight Locks

Written by Luffy Aug 10, 2023 ¬∑ 6 min read

Are you wondering if straight hair is back in style for 2020? The answer is yes! Straight hair is a classic look that has remained popular throughout the years. In this guide, we will provide you with tips, steps, and hair care advice so that you can achieve sleek and straight locks that are in style for 2020.

Description

Straight hair has been a popular look for decades, and it doesn't seem to be going anywhere anytime soon. Many celebrities and influencers have been spotted with straight, shiny locks in recent years, and it has become a staple hairstyle for many people. With the right tools and techniques, anyone can achieve straight hair that looks healthy and stylish.

How to Achieve Straight Hair

If you want to achieve straight hair, there are a few steps you should follow:

Step 1: Wash Your Hair

Start by washing your hair with a shampoo and conditioner that is designed for straight hair. This will help to smooth your hair and prepare it for straightening.

Step 2: Apply Heat Protectant

Before you begin straightening your hair, it is essential to apply a heat protectant. This will help to prevent damage from the straightening tool.

Step 3: Use a Straightening Tool

There are many different tools you can use to straighten your hair, including a flat iron, straightening brush, or a blow dryer with a straightening attachment. Experiment with different tools to find the one that works best for your hair.

Step 4: Finish with Hairspray

Once you have straightened your hair, finish with a light mist of hairspray to help hold the style in place.

Tips for Achieving Sleek and Straight Locks

Here are some additional tips to help you achieve sleek and straight locks:

  • Invest in a high-quality straightening tool.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair.
  • Don't forget to use a heat protectant.
  • Consider getting regular trims to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.
  • Experiment with different products, such as serums or oils, to help smooth your hair.

FAQ

Is straight hair back in style for 2020?

Yes, straight hair is back in style for 2020.

What is the best tool for straightening hair?

The best tool for straightening hair will vary depending on your hair type and texture. Some popular options include flat irons, straightening brushes, and blow dryers with straightening attachments.

How often can I straighten my hair?

You can straighten your hair as often as you'd like, but it is important to use a heat protectant and avoid excessive heat to prevent damage.

Hair Care Tips

To keep your hair looking healthy and sleek, it is essential to take good care of it. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a shampoo and conditioner that is designed for your hair type.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair.
  • Consider getting regular trims to prevent split ends.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair hydrated.
  • Avoid using too many styling products, as they can weigh down your hair and make it look greasy.

Conclusion

Straight hair is back in style for 2020, and with the right tools and techniques, anyone can achieve this sleek and stylish look. Follow our tips and steps, and take good care of your hair to keep it healthy and beautiful.
