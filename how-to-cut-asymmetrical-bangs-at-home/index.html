How To Cut Asymmetrical Bangs At Home A Step By Step Guide

Written by Eveline Sep 03, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Asymmetrical bangs are a great way to add some excitement and edge to your hairstyle. But going to a salon to get them cut can be expensive, not to mention time-consuming. So why not try cutting them at home? In this guide, we will walk you through the steps of how to cut asymmetrical bangs at home, plus tips and haircare advice to ensure you get the look you want.

Table of Contents

  • How to Cut Asymmetrical Bangs at Home
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Cutting Asymmetrical Bangs
  • FAQ

How to Cut Asymmetrical Bangs at Home

Before you start cutting, it's important to decide on the length and angle of your asymmetrical bangs. Use a photo for inspiration, or experiment with different lengths and angles using hair clips or bobby pins.

Once you have a clear idea of the length and angle you want, follow these steps:

Step-by-Step Guide

  1. Start with clean, dry hair. Comb your hair straight down and divide your bangs into two sections.
  2. Using a comb or your fingers, comb one section of bangs to the side. Hold the hair between two fingers and point cut it at the desired angle. Repeat on the other side.
  3. Use a mirror to check for symmetry. If needed, make slight adjustments by trimming a little more from one side.
  4. Blend the two sides together by point cutting any uneven pieces. Be careful not to cut too much or you may end up with uneven bangs.
  5. If you want your bangs to be shorter in the middle, use scissors to cut straight across the middle of your bangs. Be sure to cut small sections at a time to avoid cutting too much.
  6. Once you are happy with the length and angle, use thinning shears to soften any harsh lines and create a more natural look.
  7. Style your hair as desired and enjoy your new asymmetrical bangs!

Tips for Cutting Asymmetrical Bangs

  • Use sharp scissors to avoid split ends and jagged edges.
  • Start with a longer length than you want, as you can always trim more later.
  • Take your time and make small cuts to avoid making mistakes.
  • If you're not confident in cutting your own hair, enlist the help of a friend or family member.

FAQ

Q: How do I decide on the length and angle of my asymmetrical bangs?

A: Look for inspiration photos online or in magazines, or experiment with different lengths and angles using hair clips or bobby pins.

Q: What if I make a mistake while cutting my bangs?

A: Don't panic! It's just hair and it will grow back. If the mistake is minor, try to fix it yourself or enlist the help of a friend. If it's a major mistake, visit a salon to have it fixed.

Haircare Tips for Asymmetrical Bangs

  • Wash your hair every other day to prevent your bangs from getting too oily.
  • Blow dry your bangs in the direction you want them to lie, using a round brush to smooth them out.
  • Apply a small amount of hair oil or serum to your bangs to keep them shiny and healthy-looking.
  • Visit a salon every 4-6 weeks to have your bangs trimmed and maintained.

Now that you know how to cut asymmetrical bangs at home, you can experiment with different lengths and angles to find the perfect look for you. Remember to take your time, use sharp scissors, and don't be afraid to make small adjustments until you get the desired result. With a little patience and practice, you'll have salon-worthy bangs in no time!
