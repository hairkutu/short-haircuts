What Does A 1 Guard Haircut Look Like A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Oliver Apr 12, 2023 Â· 8 min read

If you're looking to get a new haircut, you might have come across the term "1 guard haircut." But what does it mean, and how does it look like? In this article, we will explore everything you need to know about a 1 guard haircut, including its description, how-to, step-by-step guide, tips, frequently asked questions, and hair care tips.

Description: What is a 1 Guard Haircut?

A 1 guard haircut is a type of haircut that uses a clipper guard with the number 1 attachment. The clipper guard is a plastic attachment that fits over the clipper blades, which determines the length of the hair that will be cut. The number 1 guard is equivalent to 1/8 of an inch or 3mm, which means that the haircut will leave hair that is about 1/8 of an inch long.

The 1 guard haircut is also known as a buzz cut or an induction cut. It is a popular haircut for men who want a low-maintenance and easy-to-maintain hairstyle. The 1 guard haircut is also a popular choice for military personnel, athletes, and people who work in professions that require short haircuts.

How to Get a 1 Guard Haircut: Step-by-Step Guide

Getting a 1 guard haircut is relatively easy and can be done at home or by a professional barber. Here's a step-by-step guide on how to get a 1 guard haircut:

Step 1: Choose the Right Clipper and Guard

To get a 1 guard haircut, you will need a clipper with a number 1 guard attachment. You can buy a clipper and guard at a local beauty supply store or online.

Step 2: Prepare Your Hair

Before you start cutting your hair, make sure that your hair is clean and dry. You can wash your hair with shampoo and conditioner and then dry it with a towel.

Step 3: Attach the Guard to the Clipper

Attach the number 1 guard to the clipper by snapping it onto the clipper blades. Make sure that the guard is securely attached to the clipper.

Step 4: Start Cutting Your Hair

Start cutting your hair by running the clipper with the guard over your head. Start at the back of your head and work your way to the front. Make sure that you move the clipper in the opposite direction of the hair growth for a more even cut.

Step 5: Clean Up the Edges

After you have finished cutting your hair, use a trimmer or a razor to clean up the edges around your hairline and neck.

Tips for Maintaining a 1 Guard Haircut

A 1 guard haircut is easy to maintain, and there are a few tips that you can follow to keep your haircut looking fresh:

  • Trim your hair regularly to keep it at the same length.
  • Clean up the edges around your hairline and neck every few days.
  • Use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy.
  • Avoid using hair products that can weigh your hair down.

FAQs: Frequently Asked Questions About 1 Guard Haircut

What does a 1 guard haircut look like?

A 1 guard haircut leaves hair that is about 1/8 of an inch long. It is a very short and low-maintenance haircut that is ideal for men who want a clean and easy-to-maintain hairstyle.

Can I get a 1 guard haircut if I have curly hair?

Yes, you can get a 1 guard haircut if you have curly hair. However, you may need to use a smaller clipper guard to ensure that your curls are not cut too short.

How often should I get a 1 guard haircut?

You can get a 1 guard haircut every few weeks to keep your hair looking fresh and maintained. However, the frequency of your haircuts will depend on how fast your hair grows and how short you want to keep your hair.

Hair Care Tips for a 1 Guard Haircut

Here are a few hair care tips that you can follow to keep your 1 guard haircut looking great:

  • Use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy.
  • Avoid using hair products that can weigh your hair down.
  • Use a leave-in conditioner or hair oil to keep your hair soft and shiny.
  • Wear a hat or use sunscreen to protect your scalp from the sun.

A 1 guard haircut is a simple and low-maintenance hairstyle that is perfect for men who want a clean and easy-to-maintain look. With the right tools and techniques, you can easily achieve this classic haircut at home or with a professional barber. Follow our step-by-step guide, tips, and hair care advice to keep your 1 guard haircut looking great all year round.
