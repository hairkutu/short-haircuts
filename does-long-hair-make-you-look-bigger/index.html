Does Long Hair Make You Look Bigger

Written by Luffy Apr 09, 2023 Â· 8 min read

Long hair has always been considered a symbol of femininity and beauty. But, there is a myth that long hair can make you look bigger. Is it true? Let's find out in this article. Here's a table of content:

Table of Content

  • Description
  • How To
  • Step By Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

There is no scientific evidence that long hair makes you look bigger. However, long hair can add volume to your overall appearance, which may make you appear bigger. Also, if you have a round face or a fuller body, long hair can make your face and body look fuller. But, this doesn't mean that long hair is not suitable for you if you have a fuller body. You can still look amazing with long hair if you choose the right hairstyle that suits your face and body shape.

How To

If you are worried about whether long hair is suitable for your body type, here are some tips to help you:

1. Choose the right hairstyle

Choose a hairstyle that flatters your face and body shape. If you have a fuller body, avoid hairstyles that add volume to the sides of your face. Instead, opt for hairstyles that add volume to the top of your head.

2. Add layers

Adding layers to your hair can make it look more voluminous and add movement to your hair, which can make you look slimmer. However, avoid adding layers to the sides of your face.

3. Avoid heavy bangs

Heavy bangs can make your face look rounder, which can make you look bigger. Instead, opt for side-swept bangs that can make your face look slimmer.

Step By Step

Here's how to choose the right hairstyle:

1. Determine your face shape

Before choosing a hairstyle, determine your face shape. You can do this by pulling your hair back and looking at your face in the mirror. The four common face shapes are oval, round, square, and heart-shaped.

2. Choose a hairstyle that flatters your face shape

If you have an oval face, you can choose any hairstyle. If you have a round face, choose a hairstyle that adds height to the top of your head. If you have a square face, choose a hairstyle with soft layers. If you have a heart-shaped face, choose a hairstyle that adds volume to the sides of your face.

3. Consider your body shape

If you have a fuller body, avoid hairstyles that add volume to the sides of your face. Instead, opt for hairstyles that add volume to the top of your head.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you look amazing with long hair:

1. Use hair accessories

Use hair accessories, such as headbands, to add interest to your hair. This can make your hair look more stylish and distract attention from your body.

2. Keep your hair healthy

Long hair requires regular maintenance to keep it healthy. Use a good quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair clean and moisturized. Also, trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.

FAQ

Q. Does long hair make you look taller?

A. No, long hair doesn't make you look taller. However, long hair can add volume to your overall appearance, which may make you appear taller.

Q. Does long hair make you look younger?

A. Long hair can make you look younger, but it depends on the hairstyle. Avoid hairstyles that make you look too serious or mature.

Q. Can I have long hair if I have a fuller body?

A. Yes, you can have long hair if you have a fuller body. Choose a hairstyle that flatters your face and body shape.

Hair Care

Long hair requires regular maintenance to keep it healthy. Here are some tips to help you take care of your long hair:

1. Wash your hair regularly

Wash your hair regularly to keep it clean and moisturized. Use a good quality shampoo and conditioner that suits your hair type.

2. Use a hair mask

Use a hair mask once a week to keep your hair moisturized and nourished. You can make a hair mask at home using natural ingredients, such as avocado, honey, and coconut oil.

3. Use a heat protectant

If you use heat styling tools, such as a flat iron or a curling iron, use a heat protectant to prevent damage to your hair.

In conclusion, long hair doesn't make you look bigger. However, long hair can add volume to your overall appearance, which may make you appear bigger. If you have a fuller body, choose a hairstyle that flatters your face and body shape. Use hair accessories to add interest to your hair and keep your hair healthy with regular maintenance.
