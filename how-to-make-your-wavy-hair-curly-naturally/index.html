How To Make Your Wavy Hair Curly Naturally

Written by Daniel May 11, 2023 Â· 10 min read

Are you tired of your wavy hair and want to try something new? Do you want to know how to make your hair curly naturally without using any chemicals or heat? Look no further, because we've got you covered. In this article, we will share with you some tips and tricks on how to make your wavy hair curly naturally.

Description

Wavy hair can be a blessing and a curse. On one hand, it looks effortlessly cool and beachy. On the other hand, it can be hard to style and can sometimes look messy. If you want to switch up your look and make your wavy hair curly, there are some natural methods you can try. These methods will not only give you the desired curly look, but they will also help to keep your hair healthy and strong.

How to Make Your Wavy Hair Curly Naturally

1. Braid Your Hair

One of the easiest and most natural ways to make your wavy hair curly is by braiding it. This is a great option if you want to achieve a loose, beachy wave. Simply braid your hair when it's damp and leave it overnight. When you wake up in the morning, undo the braids and gently finger-comb your hair. You'll be left with beautiful, natural-looking curls.

2. Use a Diffuser

If you have a hair dryer with a diffuser attachment, you can use it to make your wavy hair curly. Start by applying some curl-enhancing cream to your damp hair. Then, use the diffuser to dry your hair on a low heat setting. The diffuser will help to enhance your natural waves and create soft, bouncy curls.

3. Try a Twist Out

A twist out is another natural method for making your wavy hair curly. To do a twist out, start by dividing your damp hair into sections. Then, twist each section tightly and secure it with a hair tie. Leave the twists in overnight and undo them in the morning. You'll be left with defined, bouncy curls.

4. Use Rollers

If you want to achieve tighter curls, you can use rollers to set your hair. Start by applying some curl-enhancing cream to your damp hair. Then, divide your hair into small sections and wrap each section around a roller. Leave the rollers in for a few hours or overnight, depending on how tight you want your curls to be. When you remove the rollers, you'll have beautiful, bouncy curls.

Step by Step Guide

Step 1: Wash Your Hair

Start by washing your hair with a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner. This will help to nourish your hair and prepare it for styling.

Step 2: Apply Curl-Enhancing Cream

Apply a curl-enhancing cream to your damp hair. This will help to define your natural waves and curls and make them more prominent.

Step 3: Choose Your Method

Choose one of the natural methods we've outlined above. Whether you decide to braid your hair, use a diffuser, try a twist out, or use rollers, make sure you follow the instructions carefully.

Step 4: Let Your Hair Set

Once you've styled your hair, let it set for a few hours or overnight. This will give your curls time to form and set in place.

Step 5: Undo Your Curls

When you're ready to undo your curls, be gentle. Use your fingers or a wide-tooth comb to gently separate your curls and create more volume.

Tips for Making Your Wavy Hair Curly Naturally

- Use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and strong.
- Apply a leave-in conditioner or oil to your hair to keep it moisturized.
- Use a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer to enhance your natural waves and curls.
- Avoid using heat styling tools, as they can damage your hair over time.
- Be gentle when styling your hair to avoid breakage and damage.
- Experiment with different natural methods to find what works best for your hair.

FAQ

Can I make my wavy hair curly without using any products?

Yes, you can make your wavy hair curly naturally without using any products. Try braiding your hair or doing a twist out to create natural-looking curls.

How can I keep my curly hair looking healthy?

To keep your curly hair looking healthy, make sure you use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner, and apply a leave-in conditioner or oil to your hair regularly. Avoid using heat styling tools and be gentle when styling your hair to avoid breakage.

How often should I wash my curly hair?

You should wash your curly hair every 2-3 days to keep it clean and healthy. Over-washing can strip your hair of its natural oils and cause it to become dry and brittle.

Hair Care for Curly Hair

If you have curly hair, it's important to take good care of it to keep it healthy and strong. Here are some tips for caring for your curly hair:

- Use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair hydrated.
- Apply a leave-in conditioner or oil to your hair to keep it moisturized.
- Avoid using heat styling tools, as they can damage your hair over time.
- Be gentle when styling your hair to avoid breakage and damage.
- Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your hair, rather than a brush.
- Sleep on a silk or satin pillowcase to reduce friction and prevent frizz.
- Get regular trims to keep your hair looking healthy and prevent split ends.

By following these tips and tricks, you can make your wavy hair curly naturally and keep it healthy and strong. Experiment with different natural methods to find what works best for your hair, and enjoy your new, curly look!
