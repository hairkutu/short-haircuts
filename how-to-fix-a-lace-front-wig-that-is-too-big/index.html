How To Fix A Lace Front Wig That Is Too Big

Written by Daniel Sep 05, 2023 Â· 8 min read

If you've recently purchased a lace front wig but realized that it is too big for your head, don't worry! There are a few easy steps you can take to fix your wig and ensure that it fits comfortably and securely. In this article, we'll go over how to fix a lace front wig that is too big, step by step, and share some tips for maintaining and caring for your wig.

Table of Contents

  • How to Fix a Lace Front Wig That is Too Big
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Maintaining Your Wig
  • Frequently Asked Questions
  • Hair Care for Wigs

Step-by-Step Guide

Here are the steps you can take to fix a lace front wig that is too big:

Step 1: Determine How Much Smaller Your Wig Needs to Be

First, you'll need to figure out how much smaller your wig needs to be. Try on the wig and use a measuring tape to measure the circumference of your head. Then, measure the wig to see how much excess material you need to remove.

Step 2: Pin the Wig to Your Head

Once you've determined how much smaller your wig needs to be, pin the wig to your head using bobby pins. Make sure the wig is snug and secure.

Step 3: Mark the Excess Material

Using a fabric marker or chalk, mark the excess material that needs to be removed. Be sure to mark both the lace and the cap of the wig.

Step 4: Remove the Wig and Cut the Excess Material

Remove the wig from your head and lay it flat on a table. Using sharp scissors, carefully cut along the marked lines to remove the excess material. Be sure to cut through both the lace and the cap of the wig.

Step 5: Reattach the Lace Front

After cutting the excess material, you'll need to reattach the lace front of the wig. Use a small amount of wig glue or tape to secure the lace back in place.

Step 6: Style and Wear Your Wig

Once the lace front is reattached, you can style and wear your wig as usual. You should notice that it fits much more comfortably and securely now!

Tips for Maintaining Your Wig

Here are some tips for maintaining your lace front wig:

  • Store your wig on a wig stand or mannequin head to help maintain its shape.
  • Wash your wig regularly using a wig shampoo and conditioner.
  • Be gentle when brushing or combing your wig to avoid tangling or damaging the hair fibers.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools on your wig, as this can damage the fibers.
  • Consider investing in a wig cap to help protect your natural hair and keep your wig in place.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I know if my wig is too big?

If your wig slides around or feels loose on your head, it may be too big. You may also notice excess material around the perimeter of the wig.

Can I alter a synthetic lace front wig?

It is possible to alter a synthetic lace front wig, but it can be more difficult than altering a human hair wig. Synthetic wigs cannot be heat styled, so you'll need to use other methods to remove excess material.

How often should I wash my wig?

You should wash your wig every 6-8 wears, or as needed if it becomes dirty or oily.

Hair Care for Wigs

Whether you're wearing a lace front wig or any other type of wig, it's important to care for it properly to ensure it lasts as long as possible. Here are some tips for caring for your wig:

  • Use a wig brush or wide-tooth comb to gently detangle your wig before washing.
  • Fill a sink or basin with cool water and add a small amount of wig shampoo.
  • Submerge the wig in the water and gently swirl it around for a few minutes.
  • Rinse the wig thoroughly with cool water and gently squeeze out any excess water.
  • Apply a small amount of wig conditioner and leave it on for a few minutes before rinsing.
  • Gently squeeze out any excess water and lay the wig flat on a towel to air dry.

By following these tips and steps, you should be able to fix a lace front wig that is too big and enjoy a comfortable and secure fit. Remember to take good care of your wig to keep it looking and feeling great!
