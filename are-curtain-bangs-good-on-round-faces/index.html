Are Curtain Bangs Good On Round Faces

Written by Luffy Apr 10, 2023 Â· 6 min read

When it comes to trying out new hairstyles, many people worry about whether it will suit their face shape or not. One such hairstyle that has gained a lot of popularity in recent years is curtain bangs. However, the question remains - are curtain bangs good on round faces?

Table of Contents

  1. Description
  2. How to Style Curtain Bangs
  3. Step-by-Step Guide to Cutting Curtain Bangs
  4. Tips for Maintaining Curtain Bangs
  5. FAQs
  6. Hair Care Tips for Curtain Bangs

Description

Curtain bangs are a type of fringe that are parted in the middle, framing the face on either side. They are versatile and can be styled in different ways, depending on your preference. They are also low-maintenance and can be easily grown out if you decide that they are not for you.

When it comes to round faces, many people are hesitant to try curtain bangs, thinking that it will make their face look even rounder. However, this is not necessarily the case. With the right cut and styling techniques, curtain bangs can actually complement round faces quite well.

How to Style Curtain Bangs

Styling curtain bangs is relatively easy. Here are a few steps to follow:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair
  2. Apply some heat protectant spray to your hair
  3. Use a round brush and a hairdryer to blow-dry your bangs
  4. Part your bangs in the middle using a comb
  5. If you want a wavy look, use a curling iron to curl your bangs
  6. Apply some hairspray to keep your bangs in place

Step-by-Step Guide to Cutting Curtain Bangs

If you want to cut your own curtain bangs at home, here is a step-by-step guide:

  1. Start with dry hair
  2. Part your hair down the middle
  3. Take a section of hair from the crown of your head and comb it forward
  4. Decide on the length you want your bangs to be and cut them straight across
  5. Gradually angle the scissors towards the ends of your bangs to create a softer, more natural look
  6. Repeat on the other side

Tips for Maintaining Curtain Bangs

Here are a few tips for maintaining your curtain bangs:

  • Trim your bangs every 4-6 weeks to keep them looking neat
  • Use a dry shampoo to keep your bangs from getting oily
  • Avoid touching your bangs too much, as this can make them greasy
  • Don't use too much heat on your bangs, as this can cause damage

FAQs

1. Will curtain bangs make my face look rounder?

Not necessarily. With the right cut and styling techniques, curtain bangs can actually complement round faces quite well.

2. Can I cut my own curtain bangs at home?

Yes, you can cut your own curtain bangs at home. However, if you are not confident in your skills, it's best to leave it to a professional.

3. How often should I trim my curtain bangs?

You should trim your curtain bangs every 4-6 weeks to keep them looking neat.

Hair Care Tips for Curtain Bangs

Here are a few hair care tips for keeping your curtain bangs looking their best:

  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair
  • Apply a hair mask once a week to keep your hair healthy
  • Avoid using too much product on your hair, as this can weigh it down

In conclusion, curtain bangs can look great on round faces, as long as they are cut and styled correctly. If you're thinking of trying out this trendy hairstyle, go for it!
