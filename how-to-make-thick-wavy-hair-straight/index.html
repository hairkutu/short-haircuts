How To Make Thick Wavy Hair Straight A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Eveline Jul 06, 2023 · 6 min read

If you have thick wavy hair, you know how challenging it can be to tame it. While those luscious waves look incredible, they can be a hassle to maintain. Luckily, there are ways to make your hair straight without damaging it. In this article, we’ll show you how to make thick wavy hair straight in a relaxed and easy-to-follow language.

How to Make Thick Wavy Hair Straight

Making thick wavy hair straight isn’t rocket science, but it requires some effort and patience. Here are the steps to follow:

Step 1: Wash Your Hair

Start by washing your hair with a clarifying shampoo to remove any buildup. Rinse your hair thoroughly and towel dry it.

Step 2: Apply a Serum or Heat Protectant

Apply a serum or heat protectant to your hair to protect it from heat damage. Make sure to distribute the product evenly, focusing on the ends of your hair.

Step 3: Blow-Dry Your Hair

Blow-dry your hair using a round brush. Start at the roots and work your way down, pulling the brush through your hair as you go. Use a concentrator nozzle to focus the heat and make it easier to straighten your hair.

Step 4: Straighten Your Hair

Once your hair is dry, divide it into sections and use a flat iron to straighten it. Start at the roots and work your way down, using slow, steady strokes. Make sure to use a heat protectant spray before you straighten your hair.

Step 5: Finish with Hairspray

Finish by spraying your hair with a light hairspray to keep it in place. You can also apply a shine serum to add some extra luster to your hair.

Tips for Making Thick Wavy Hair Straight

Making thick wavy hair straight can be a bit of a challenge, but these tips will make the process easier:

  • Use a high-quality flat iron with ceramic plates to prevent heat damage.
  • Apply a heat protectant spray before you straighten your hair.
  • Blow-dry your hair using a round brush to create tension and straighten your hair.
  • Divide your hair into sections to make it easier to straighten.
  • Use a concentrator nozzle on your blow dryer to focus the heat and make it easier to straighten your hair.
  • Apply a light hairspray to keep your hair in place.

FAQs

Q: Can I straighten my hair without using heat?

A: Yes, you can straighten your hair without using heat by using a straightening brush or a hair straightening cream.

Q: Can I straighten my hair every day?

A: It’s not recommended to straighten your hair every day as it can cause heat damage and breakage. Try to limit straightening to once or twice a week.

Q: How long will my straight hair last?

A: Your straight hair can last up to three days, depending on your hair type and the products you use.

Hair Care for Thick Wavy Hair

Proper hair care is essential for keeping your thick wavy hair healthy and manageable. Here are some tips:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo to prevent dryness and frizz.
  • Condition your hair regularly to keep it hydrated and soft.
  • Avoid using hot tools every day to prevent heat damage.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to nourish your hair.

Making thick wavy hair straight is easy if you follow these steps and tips. With a little patience and practice, you can achieve sleek, straight locks without damaging your hair. Remember to take good care of your hair to keep it healthy and beautiful.
