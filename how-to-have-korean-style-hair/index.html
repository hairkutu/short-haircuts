How To Have Korean Style Hair A Guide For Everyone

Are you a fan of K-pop and K-dramas? Do you love the way Korean celebrities style their hair? If your answer is yes, then you might want to try having Korean style hair. In this article, we will show you how to achieve that look and give you some tips on how to maintain it.

Table of Contents

  • How to Have Korean Style Hair
  • Step by Step Guide

How to Have Korean Style Hair

Korean style hair is all about soft waves or curls, a middle part, and a natural-looking color. If you want to achieve this look, you need to start with the right haircut. You can ask your hairstylist for a shoulder-length cut with layers. This will give you the volume and movement that you need for the soft waves.

Once you have the right cut, you can start styling your hair. You can use a curling iron or a straightener to create the waves. Make sure that you curl the ends of your hair inward to achieve that natural look. You can also use a hair serum to add shine and reduce frizz.

Step by Step Guide

Here is a step-by-step guide on how to achieve Korean style hair:

  1. Wash your hair with a mild shampoo and conditioner.
  2. Blow-dry your hair until it is about 80% dry.
  3. Apply a heat protectant spray to your hair to prevent heat damage.
  4. Divide your hair into sections and start curling with a curling iron or a straightener.
  5. Curl the ends of your hair inward to create soft waves.
  6. Finish with a hair serum to add shine and reduce frizz.

Tips for Maintaining Korean Style Hair

Here are some tips on how to maintain your Korean style hair:

  • Use a mild shampoo and conditioner to prevent damage to your hair.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair. Use a heat protectant spray before curling or straightening.
  • Trim your hair regularly to keep it healthy and prevent split ends.
  • Avoid using too many styling products on your hair as this can weigh it down and make it look greasy.

FAQ

Q: Can I have Korean style hair if I have short hair?

A: Yes, you can still achieve the Korean style look even if you have short hair. You can ask your hairstylist for a bob cut with layers to create volume and movement.

Q: What hair color should I choose for Korean style hair?

A: Korean style hair is all about natural-looking hair color. You can go for shades of brown, black, or dark blonde.

Hair Care Tips

Here are some hair care tips to keep your Korean style hair healthy:

  • Use a hair mask once a week to nourish your hair and prevent damage.
  • Avoid using hot tools on your hair too often. Let your hair air-dry whenever possible.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair instead of a brush.
  • Avoid using too much hairspray or styling products as this can cause buildup on your scalp.

With these tips, you can achieve the Korean style hair that you have been dreaming of. Remember to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and always look your best!
