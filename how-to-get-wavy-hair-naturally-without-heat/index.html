How To Get Wavy Hair Naturally Without Heat

Written by Oliver Jul 17, 2023 · 6 min read

If you’re tired of using heat styling tools on your hair and want to achieve natural-looking waves, you’ve come to the right place! In this blog post, we’ll be sharing some tips and tricks on how to get wavy hair naturally without heat. From hair care to styling techniques, we’ve got you covered.

Table of Contents

  1. Hair Care
  2. How to Get Wavy Hair Naturally Without Heat
  3. Step-by-Step Guide
  4. Tips and Tricks
  5. FAQ

Hair Care

Before we dive into how to get wavy hair naturally, it’s important to take care of your hair. Healthy hair is key to achieving any hairstyle, including natural waves. Here are a few tips on how to take care of your hair:

  • Wash your hair every 2-3 days
  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner
  • Avoid using hot water when washing your hair
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair
  • Avoid using heat styling tools
  • Deep condition your hair once a week

By following these tips, you’ll be able to maintain healthy hair, which is essential for natural-looking waves.

How to Get Wavy Hair Naturally Without Heat

Now that you’ve taken care of your hair, it’s time to achieve those natural-looking waves. Here are a few ways to get wavy hair naturally without heat:

  • Braid your hair before bed
  • Twist your hair into buns
  • Use a salt spray
  • Apply hair mousse to damp hair

These techniques will help you get natural-looking waves without using heat styling tools.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here’s a step-by-step guide on how to get wavy hair naturally without heat:

  1. Wash your hair and let it air dry until it’s damp
  2. Apply a hair mousse to your damp hair
  3. Section your hair into two parts
  4. Twist each section of hair into a bun and secure it with a hair tie
  5. Let your hair dry in the buns for a few hours or overnight
  6. Take out the hair ties and gently shake out your hair
  7. You should now have natural-looking waves!

Tips and Tricks

Here are a few tips and tricks to help you achieve the perfect natural-looking waves:

  • Use a salt spray to add texture to your hair
  • Apply hair mousse to damp hair for extra hold
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair
  • Don’t overdo it with the hair products
  • Experiment with different techniques to find what works best for you

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about how to get wavy hair naturally without heat:

Q: Can I use a hair dryer on a low heat setting?

A: While it’s best to avoid heat styling tools altogether, you can use a hair dryer on a low heat setting if you absolutely need to.

Q: How long will the waves last?

A: The duration of the waves will depend on your hair type and the technique you use. Generally, the waves should last a few days.

Q: Can I achieve natural-looking waves on short hair?

A: Yes, you can achieve natural-looking waves on short hair using the techniques mentioned above.

Hair Care is Key to Beautiful Waves

Getting wavy hair naturally without heat is easy if you follow these tips and tricks. Remember, healthy hair is key to achieving any hairstyle, including natural waves. By taking care of your hair and using the right techniques, you’ll be able to achieve natural-looking waves in no time!
