How Much Does A Bald Fade Cost A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Aprilia Jun 18, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Are you looking for a stylish and trendy haircut? If yes, then a bald fade haircut can be the perfect choice for you. A bald fade is a haircut in which the hair is gradually tapered on the sides and back, creating a bald look at the bottom. It's a popular hairstyle among men of all ages because of its versatility and low maintenance.

Description

A bald fade haircut is a popular hairstyle among men because it offers a trendy look with low maintenance. It is a haircut in which the hair is gradually tapered on the sides and back, creating a bald look at the bottom. The bald fade haircut can be customized according to your preferences, and it can be combined with different hairstyles like the crew cut, pompadour, or quiff.

How To Get a Bald Fade Haircut

If you want to get a bald fade haircut, you need to visit a professional hairstylist. You can bring a picture of the style you want, but keep in mind that your hairstylist may suggest some changes according to your hair type and face shape. A bald fade haircut requires a skilled hairstylist because it involves precise cutting and blending techniques.

Step by Step Guide

The hairstylist will start by cutting your hair on the top of your head according to the desired length. Then he will use clippers to create a tapered effect on the sides and back of your head. The hairstylist will blend the hair on the top of your head with the tapered sides using scissors or clippers. Finally, he will shave the hair on the bottom of your head to create a bald look.

Tips for Getting the Perfect Bald Fade

To get the perfect bald fade haircut, you should follow these tips:

  • Choose a professional hairstylist who has experience in creating bald fades.
  • Bring a picture of the style you want, but be open to suggestions from your hairstylist.
  • Communicate clearly with your hairstylist about the length and style you want.
  • Take care of your hair by washing it regularly and using hair products recommended by your hairstylist.

Frequently Asked Questions

1. How often do I need to get a bald fade haircut?

A bald fade haircut can last for 2-4 weeks, depending on your hair growth rate. You should get a haircut every 3-4 weeks to maintain the style.

2. Can I style my hair differently after getting a bald fade?

Yes, you can style your hair differently after getting a bald fade. The bald fade haircut can be combined with different hairstyles like the crew cut, pompadour, or quiff.

Hair Care Tips for Bald Fade Haircut

To take care of your bald fade haircut, you should follow these tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a shampoo recommended by your hairstylist.
  • Use a conditioner to keep your hair soft and hydrated.
  • Apply hair products like pomade or wax to style your hair.
  • Avoid exposing your hair to extreme heat or cold.

How Much Does a Bald Fade Cost?

The cost of a bald fade haircut depends on various factors like the location, the popularity of the hairstylist, and the complexity of the style. On average, a bald fade haircut can cost between $25 to $50. However, it can cost more in a high-end salon or a big city. Make sure to check the prices before booking an appointment with a hairstylist.

In conclusion, a bald fade haircut is a versatile and trendy hairstyle that can suit men of all ages. It requires a skilled hairstylist and regular maintenance to keep the style looking fresh. The cost of a bald fade haircut can vary depending on various factors, but it's worth the investment for a stylish and low maintenance look.
