Short Medium Hairstyles For Over 60 A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Aprilia Aug 20, 2023 Â· 7 min read

As we age, our hair tends to lose its thickness and volume, making it more challenging to find the right hairstyle that suits our preference and appearance. However, that doesn't mean you can't look fabulous with short/medium hairstyles for over 60. In this guide, we'll explore how to choose the right hairstyle, the steps to achieve it, tips, frequently asked questions, and hair care tips to maintain healthy hair.

Table of Contents

  • How to Choose the Right Hairstyle
  • Hairstyle Ideas for Short/Medium Hair
  • Step-by-Step Guide to Achieve the Hairstyle
  • Tips to Maintain the Hairstyle
  • Frequently Asked Questions
  • Hair Care Tips for Over 60

How to Choose the Right Hairstyle

Choosing the right hairstyle is crucial to look and feel confident. Before you decide on a haircut, consider the following factors:

  • Your face shape
  • Your hair texture and thickness
  • Your lifestyle and preference
  • Your hair care routine

Once you've identified your face shape, hair texture, and thickness, you can choose from a variety of short/medium hairstyles for over 60.

Hairstyle Ideas for Short/Medium Hair

If you're looking for inspiration for short/medium hairstyles for over 60, here are some ideas to consider:

Short Bob

A short bob is a classic hairstyle that never goes out of style. It's perfect for those with thin hair as it adds volume and texture. You can also add layers to create more fullness and movement.

Pixie Cut

A pixie cut is a low-maintenance hairstyle that's perfect for those who want a bold and edgy look. It's also an excellent option for those with fine hair as it adds volume at the crown. You can style it messy or sleek, depending on your preference.

Layered Lob

A layered lob is a versatile hairstyle that flatters most face shapes. It's ideal for those with medium hair as it adds texture and movement. You can keep it straight or add some waves for a more relaxed look.

Step-by-Step Guide to Achieve the Hairstyle

Once you've chosen the right hairstyle, here are the steps to achieve it:

  • Wash your hair with a shampoo and conditioner that suits your hair type.
  • Towel dry your hair and apply a heat protectant spray.
  • Blow-dry your hair using a round brush to create volume.
  • Style your hair using a curling iron or straightener, depending on the hairstyle you want to achieve.
  • Apply a hairspray to set the style.

Remember to use heat protectant spray to prevent hair damage and hairspray to hold the style in place.

Tips to Maintain the Hairstyle

To maintain your short/medium hairstyle for over 60, consider the following tips:

  • Visit your hairstylist regularly for a trim and touch-up.
  • Use a dry shampoo to refresh your hair between washes.
  • Avoid using hair products that contain alcohol as it can dry out your hair.
  • Use a deep conditioner once a week to keep your hair healthy and moisturized.

Frequently Asked Questions

Here are some frequently asked questions about short/medium hairstyles for over 60:

1. Can I dye my hair with a short/medium hairstyle?

Yes, you can dye your hair with a short/medium hairstyle. However, it's essential to choose a hair color that complements your skin tone and face shape.

2. Can I wear bangs with a short/medium hairstyle?

Yes, you can wear bangs with a short/medium hairstyle. It's a great way to add a youthful and trendy touch to your look.

3. Can I style my short/medium hair differently for different occasions?

Yes, you can style your short/medium hair differently for different occasions. For example, you can style it sleek and straight for a formal event or add some waves for a casual outing.

Hair Care Tips for Over 60

Here are some hair care tips for over 60 to keep your hair healthy and vibrant:

  • Eat a balanced diet that includes protein, vitamins, and minerals.
  • Drink plenty of water to keep your hair hydrated.
  • Avoid using hot tools excessively as it can damage your hair.
  • Get enough sleep to reduce stress and promote hair growth.

With these tips and short/medium hairstyles for over 60, you can look and feel confident at any age. Remember to choose a hairstyle that suits your face shape, hair texture, and thickness, and follow a hair care routine to maintain healthy and vibrant hair.
