Best Hairstyles For Chubby Face Males

Written by William Aug 21, 2023 · 7 min read

If you have a round face or a chubby face, you might feel that it’s challenging to find a hairstyle that suits you. However, there are plenty of hairstyles that can help you to look your best. In this article, we will show you some of the best hairstyles for chubby face males that you can try out.

Description

A chubby face can make you look younger and more approachable, but it can also make you look heavier than you actually are. The right hairstyle can help to balance out your face and create a more symmetrical look. The key is to choose a hairstyle that adds height and volume to the top of your head while keeping the sides and back short.

How To Choose a Hairstyle for Chubby Face Males

When it comes to choosing a hairstyle for a chubby face male, there are a few things to keep in mind. First, you want to avoid hairstyles that make your face look wider. This means avoiding hairstyles that are too short on the sides or that have too much volume on top.

Instead, opt for hairstyles that add height and volume to the top of your head while keeping the sides and back short. This will help to create a more balanced look and make your face appear slimmer.

Step by Step Guide

1. Start by choosing a haircut that adds height and volume to the top of your head. This could be a quiff, a pompadour, or a messy fringe.

2. Ask your barber or stylist to keep the sides and back short. The length will depend on your personal preference, but you want to avoid anything that is too short or too long.

3. Style your hair using a hair product that adds volume and texture. This could be a pomade, wax, or clay.

4. Use a hairdryer to blow-dry your hair while using a brush or your fingers to create volume.

Tips for Maintaining the Hairstyle

1. Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to help add volume and texture to your hair.

2. Style your hair using a hair product that is suitable for your hair type. If you have thin hair, opt for a lightweight product. If you have thick hair, opt for a heavier product.

3. Avoid using too much product, as this can make your hair look greasy and weighed down.

4. Visit your barber or stylist every 4-6 weeks to maintain the shape of your haircut.

FAQs

What are the best hairstyles for chubby face males?

The best hairstyles for chubby face males are those that add height and volume to the top of your head while keeping the sides and back short. This could be a quiff, a pompadour, or a messy fringe.

How do I style my hair to make my face look slimmer?

You can style your hair to make your face look slimmer by adding height and volume to the top of your head while keeping the sides and back short. Use a hair product that adds volume and texture, and blow-dry your hair while using a brush or your fingers to create volume.

Hair Care

Proper hair care is essential for maintaining healthy hair and a healthy scalp. Here are some tips for taking care of your hair:

1. Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.

2. Avoid using hot water, as this can strip your hair of its natural oils.

3. Use a wide-tooth comb or brush to detangle your hair when it’s wet.

4. Avoid using heat styling tools, such as flat irons and curling irons, as these can damage your hair.

5. Use a hair mask or deep conditioner once a week to help nourish and moisturize your hair.

In conclusion, there are plenty of hairstyles for chubby face males that can help you to look your best. The key is to choose a hairstyle that adds height and volume to the top of your head while keeping the sides and back short. With the right haircut and styling products, you can create a look that is both stylish and flattering.
