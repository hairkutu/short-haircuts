How To Get Thick And Wavy Hair A Complete Guide

Written by Petter May 21, 2023 Â· 8 min read

Are you tired of lifeless and limp hair? Do you dream of having thick and wavy locks? Thankfully, you can achieve your dream hair with a little effort and the right techniques. In this article, we'll show you how to get thick and wavy hair step by step.

Table of Contents

  1. Hair Care
  2. How to Get Thick and Wavy Hair
  3. Step by Step Guide
  4. Tips for Maintaining Thick and Wavy Hair
  5. FAQs

Hair Care

Before we dive into the steps for getting thick and wavy hair, it's important to understand the importance of hair care. Good hair care is essential for maintaining healthy hair, which is the foundation for achieving your dream hair.

Here are some tips for maintaining healthy hair:

  • Wash your hair regularly, but not too frequently. Over-washing can strip your hair of its natural oils.
  • Use a conditioner after shampooing to help detangle and moisturize your hair.
  • Avoid using hot water when washing your hair, as it can damage your hair and dry out your scalp.
  • Use a wide-toothed comb to detangle your hair, starting from the ends and working your way up to the roots.
  • Avoid using heat-styling tools too frequently, as they can damage your hair and cause split ends.

How to Get Thick and Wavy Hair

Now that you know the importance of hair care, let's move on to the steps for getting thick and wavy hair:

  1. Start by washing your hair with a volumizing shampoo and conditioner. Volumizing products help add body and fullness to your hair.
  2. Apply a root-lifting spray or mousse to your hair before blow-drying. These products help add volume to your roots.
  3. Blow-dry your hair using a round brush. This technique helps create volume and waves in your hair.
  4. Once your hair is dry, use a curling iron to create loose waves. Start at the mid-section of your hair and wrap sections of hair around the barrel of the curling iron, holding for a few seconds before releasing.
  5. Brush out your waves with a wide-toothed comb to create softer, more natural-looking waves.
  6. Finish off with a hairspray to hold your waves in place.

Step by Step Guide

Here's a more detailed step-by-step guide for getting thick and wavy hair:

  1. Wash your hair with a volumizing shampoo and conditioner.
  2. Towel-dry your hair to remove excess water.
  3. Apply a root-lifting spray or mousse to your roots. Use your fingers to massage the product into your scalp.
  4. Divide your hair into sections using clips.
  5. Using a round brush, blow-dry each section of your hair, starting from the roots and working your way down to the ends.
  6. Once your hair is dry, apply a heat protectant spray to your hair.
  7. Using a curling iron, take sections of your hair and wrap them around the barrel of the curling iron, holding for a few seconds before releasing.
  8. Continue curling sections of your hair until you've curled all of your hair.
  9. Brush out your curls with a wide-toothed comb to create soft, natural-looking waves.
  10. Finish off with a hairspray to hold your waves in place.

Tips for Maintaining Thick and Wavy Hair

Once you've achieved your dream hair, it's important to take steps to maintain it. Here are some tips for maintaining thick and wavy hair:

  • Avoid washing your hair too frequently, as it can strip your hair of its natural oils.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to help nourish and moisturize your hair.
  • Avoid using hot water when washing your hair, as it can cause dryness and damage.
  • Avoid using heat-styling tools too frequently, as they can cause split ends and damage your hair.
  • Use a wide-toothed comb to detangle your hair, starting from the ends and working your way up to the roots.

FAQs

Q: How long will it take to get thick and wavy hair?

A: The time it takes to achieve thick and wavy hair depends on your hair type and the techniques you use. With consistent effort and the right techniques, you can achieve thick and wavy hair in a few weeks to a few months.

Q: Can I achieve thick and wavy hair without using heat-styling tools?

A: Yes, you can achieve thick and wavy hair without using heat-styling tools. You can try using rollers or braiding your hair overnight to create waves.

Q: Can I use a straightening iron to create waves in my hair?

A: While you can use a straightening iron to create waves, it's not the most effective method. It's better to use a curling iron or try other heatless methods to create waves.

With these tips and techniques, you can achieve the thick and wavy hair of your dreams. Remember to take good care of your hair, and be patient as you work towards your hair goals. Good luck!
