How To Keep Side Swept Bangs Out Of Your Eyes

Written by Aprilia Aug 30, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Do you have side swept bangs that constantly fall into your eyes? Are you tired of constantly pushing them back? Don't worry, we've got you covered! In this article, we'll show you how to keep your side swept bangs out of your eyes. Read on for helpful tips and tricks!

Table of Contents

  • How to Keep Side Swept Bangs Out of Your Eyes
  • Step by Step Guide
  • Tips and Tricks
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care for Side Swept Bangs

How to Keep Side Swept Bangs Out of Your Eyes

The first step in keeping your side swept bangs out of your eyes is to use bobby pins. Bobby pins are an easy and affordable way to keep your bangs in place. Simply take a few bobby pins and pin your bangs back towards your crown. This will keep them out of your eyes and create a chic look.

If you're looking for a more stylish way to keep your bangs out of your eyes, try a hair accessory. Headbands, hair clips, and hair ties are all great options. Experiment with different colors and styles to find the perfect accessory for you. You can even match your accessory to your outfit!

Another way to keep your side swept bangs out of your eyes is to trim them regularly. If your bangs are too long, they will constantly fall into your eyes. Schedule regular trims with your stylist to keep your bangs at the desired length.

Step by Step Guide

Here's a step by step guide on how to keep your side swept bangs out of your eyes:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair.
  2. Take a small section of your bangs and twist it away from your face.
  3. Secure the twist with a bobby pin or hair clip.
  4. Repeat on the other side.
  5. If you prefer a more polished look, use a flat iron to straighten your bangs.
  6. Finish with hairspray to hold your style in place.

Tips and Tricks

Here are some additional tips and tricks for keeping your side swept bangs out of your eyes:

  • Use dry shampoo to add volume to your bangs and keep them from falling flat.
  • If you have curly hair, use a diffuser to dry your bangs and prevent frizz.
  • Experiment with different hairstyles, such as braids or ponytails, to keep your bangs out of your face.
  • Invest in a good quality hairspray that will hold your style in place without making your hair feel stiff or crunchy.

FAQs

Q: How do I keep my bangs from getting oily?

A: If you have oily hair, you may find that your bangs get greasy quickly. To prevent this, use a dry shampoo to absorb excess oil and keep your hair looking fresh.

Q: Can I wear a hat with side swept bangs?

A: Yes, you can definitely wear a hat with side swept bangs. To keep your bangs from getting flattened, wear your hat slightly back on your head and use bobby pins to secure your bangs in place.

Hair Care for Side Swept Bangs

Finally, it's important to take good care of your hair to ensure that your side swept bangs stay healthy and looking their best. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using hot tools, such as curling irons and straighteners, too often as they can damage your hair.
  • Use a heat protectant spray if you do use hot tools.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair and bangs healthy and prevent split ends.
  • Eat a healthy diet rich in vitamins and nutrients to promote healthy hair growth.

With these tips and tricks, you can keep your side swept bangs out of your eyes and looking fabulous all day long. Experiment with different styles and accessories to find the perfect look for you!
