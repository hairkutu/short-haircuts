How Do You Revive A Wig Tips For Bringing Your Favorite Hairpiece Back To Life

Written by Luffy Apr 23, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Wigs are a great way to change up your look without committing to a drastic haircut or color. However, just like natural hair, wigs can become dull, tangled, and lifeless over time. If you're wondering how to revive a wig and restore its luster, you've come to the right place. In this post, we'll explore tips and tricks for bringing your favorite hairpiece back to life.

Why Do Wigs Lose Their Shine?

Before we dive into the how-tos of wig revival, let's talk about why wigs can lose their shine in the first place. There are a few reasons why this might happen:

  • Exposure to heat, sunlight, or humidity
  • Excessive styling or brushing
  • Products buildup
  • Age and wear

How to Revive a Wig

If your wig has lost its shine and looks lifeless, don't worry â there are a few things you can do to bring it back to life. Here's how:

1. Wash Your Wig

The first step in wig revival is to wash your wig. Use a wig shampoo and conditioner to gently clean and condition your wig. Avoid using regular shampoo or conditioner, as it can be too harsh and strip the wig of its natural oils. Rinse your wig thoroughly and let it air dry.

2. Detangle Your Wig

After washing, your wig may be tangled and difficult to manage. Use a wide-tooth comb or wig brush to gently detangle the hair, starting at the ends and working your way up. Be patient and gentle â pulling or tugging on the hair can cause damage.

3. Use a Wig Revitalizer Spray

Wig revitalizer sprays are designed to add shine and luster back to your wig. Simply spray the revitalizer onto your wig and comb through. This will help to soften the hair and give it a healthy sheen.

4. Style Your Wig

Once your wig is clean, detangled, and revitalized, you can style it to your liking. Use a heat protectant spray if you plan on using heat styling tools, and be gentle when brushing or combing the hair.

Tips for Wig Care

If you want to keep your wig looking its best, there are a few things you can do to care for it properly:

  • Store your wig on a wig stand or mannequin head to help preserve its shape
  • Avoid exposing your wig to heat, sunlight, or humidity
  • Clean your wig regularly to prevent product buildup and keep it looking fresh
  • Be gentle when brushing or combing your wig to avoid damage and tangling

FAQ

How often should I wash my wig?

It depends on how often you wear your wig and how much product you use. As a general rule, you should wash your wig every 6-8 wears or when it starts to look dull or dirty.

Can I use regular shampoo and conditioner on my wig?

No â regular shampoo and conditioner can be too harsh and strip the wig of its natural oils. Use a wig shampoo and conditioner instead.

Can I use heat styling tools on my wig?

Yes, but be sure to use a heat protectant spray and keep the heat setting low to avoid damaging the hair.

Conclusion

Reviving a wig is a simple process that can make a big difference in how your hairpiece looks and feels. By following these tips and caring for your wig properly, you can keep it looking beautiful for years to come.
