How To Cut My Curly Hair In Layers At Home Tips And Tricks

Written by Bardi Jun 26, 2023 · 6 min read

If you have curly hair, you know that getting a haircut can be a challenge. Many hairdressers don't know how to properly cut curly hair, and you may end up with a style that doesn't suit your curls. That's why cutting your own hair in layers at home can be a great option. In this article, we'll give you tips and tricks for how to cut curly hair in layers at home.

Table of Contents

  • How to Cut Curly Hair in Layers at Home
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Cutting Curly Hair in Layers
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care after Cutting Curly Hair

How to Cut Curly Hair in Layers at Home

Cutting curly hair in layers at home may seem daunting, but it's actually quite easy with the right tools and techniques. Here's what you'll need:

  • Sharp scissors
  • Wide-tooth comb
  • Regular comb
  • Hair clips
  • Spray bottle filled with water

Before you start cutting, make sure your hair is clean and dry. This will make it easier to see the shape of your curls and ensure an even cut.

Step-by-Step Guide

Now that you have your tools, here's how to cut curly hair in layers at home:

  1. Start by parting your hair down the middle.
  2. Take a section of hair from the front and comb it straight down.
  3. Hold the section of hair between your fingers and cut the ends, starting at the bottom and working your way up.
  4. Release the section of hair and move on to the next section, working your way around your head.
  5. When you're finished with the first layer, release the top layer of hair and repeat the process.
  6. Once you've finished cutting, use a wide-tooth comb to gently comb through your curls.
  7. Check for any uneven areas and make any necessary adjustments.

Tips for Cutting Curly Hair in Layers

Cutting curly hair in layers can be tricky, so here are some tips to help you get the best results:

  • Start with a small section of hair to ensure an even cut.
  • Don't cut too much at once - it's better to take small amounts off gradually.
  • Make sure you're cutting at the same angle for each section of hair.
  • When cutting the top layer, make sure to blend it with the bottom layer for a seamless look.

FAQs

How often should I cut my curly hair?

It's recommended to cut curly hair every 3-4 months to maintain its shape and prevent split ends.

What's the best way to cut curly hair?

While everyone's hair is different, cutting curly hair in layers is generally a good option. It helps to define curls and add volume.

Do I need to cut my hair when it's wet or dry?

It's best to cut curly hair when it's dry so you can see the shape of your curls and ensure an even cut.

Hair Care after Cutting Curly Hair

After cutting your curly hair in layers at home, it's important to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and looking great. Here are some tips:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner specifically designed for curly hair.
  • Avoid using heat tools as much as possible to prevent damage.
  • Apply a leave-in conditioner or curl cream to help define your curls and reduce frizz.
  • Try not to touch your hair too much throughout the day, as this can cause frizz and disrupt your curls' shape.

With these tips and tricks, you'll be able to cut your curly hair in layers at home with confidence. Just remember to take your time and be patient - the results will be worth it!
