How To Cut Guys Hair At Home With Clippers

Written by Luffy May 20, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Are you tired of paying a lot of money to get your hair cut at a salon or barbershop? With the right tools and techniques, you can cut your own hair at home with clippers. This article will guide you on how to cut guys hair at home with clippers. Follow the steps below to get a great haircut without leaving your house!

Description

Clippers are an essential tool for men who want to keep their hair neat and tidy. They are easy to use, affordable, and can help you achieve a professional-looking haircut. Cutting your hair with clippers at home can save you time and money, and you can do it at your convenience.

How To

Before you start cutting your hair, make sure you have the right tools. You will need a pair of clippers with different guard sizes, a comb, scissors, and a mirror. Here are the steps to follow:

Step 1: Prepare Your Hair

Start by washing and drying your hair. This will make it easier to cut and ensure that your hair is clean. Comb your hair to remove any tangles and knots.

Step 2: Choose the Right Guard Size

Clippers come with different guard sizes that determine the length of your hair. Choose the right guard size based on how short you want your hair to be. Start with a longer guard size and work your way down until you get the desired length.

Step 3: Start Cutting

Turn on the clippers and start cutting your hair from the sides and back. Use the comb to lift the hair and cut against the grain of your hair. Be careful not to cut too much hair at once.

Step 4: Cut the Top

Once you are done with the sides and back, move to the top of your hair. Use the scissors to cut the hair on the top to the desired length.

Step 5: Trim the Edges

Use the clippers without a guard to trim the edges around your ears and neck. This will give you a clean and neat look.

Tips

Here are some tips to ensure that you get the best results when cutting your hair with clippers:

  • Start with a longer guard size and work your way down until you get the desired length.
  • Use the comb to lift the hair and cut against the grain of your hair.
  • Be careful not to cut too much hair at once.
  • Use scissors to cut the top of your hair to the desired length.
  • Trim the edges around your ears and neck with clippers without a guard.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about cutting guys hair at home with clippers:

Is it hard to cut your own hair with clippers?

No, it is not hard to cut your own hair with clippers. With the right tools and techniques, you can achieve a great haircut without leaving your house.

Can I use clippers to cut long hair?

Yes, you can use clippers to cut long hair. However, you will need to use a longer guard size and work your way down until you get the desired length.

How often should I cut my hair with clippers?

The frequency of cutting your hair with clippers depends on how fast your hair grows and how short you want it to be. Generally, it is recommended to cut your hair every 4-6 weeks.

Hair Care

After cutting your hair with clippers, it is important to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and neat. Wash your hair regularly with a mild shampoo and conditioner. Use hair products that suit your hair type and style. Avoid using hot tools such as hair dryers and straighteners too often as they can damage your hair.

Cutting guys hair at home with clippers is an easy and affordable way to get a great haircut. With the right tools and techniques, you can achieve a professional-looking hairstyle without leaving your house. Follow the steps and tips in this article to get the best results!
