How To Style Dirty Curly Hair A Complete Guide

Written by Bardi Apr 09, 2023 Â· 8 min read

Having curly hair can be a blessing and a curse. On one hand, curly hair is beautiful and unique, but on the other hand, it can be difficult to manage, especially when it's dirty. Styling dirty curly hair can be a challenge, but with the right techniques and products, you can achieve a gorgeous and effortless look. In this article, we will provide you with a step-by-step guide on how to style dirty curly hair.

Description

Dirty curly hair can be difficult to style because it tends to be dry, frizzy, and tangled. However, there are several techniques and products you can use to style your hair and make it look fabulous. Styling dirty curly hair can actually be easier than styling clean hair because the oils in your hair can help hold the style better. In this article, we will provide you with a step-by-step guide on how to style dirty curly hair, as well as tips and FAQs.

How to Style Dirty Curly Hair

The first step to styling dirty curly hair is to determine what kind of style you want to achieve. Do you want to wear your hair up or down? Do you want to create loose curls or tight ringlets? Once you have determined the style you want, you can start preparing your hair for styling.

Step-by-Step Guide

Follow these steps to style your dirty curly hair:

  1. Start by brushing your hair to remove any tangles. Use a wide-tooth comb or a brush specifically designed for curly hair.
  2. Apply a leave-in conditioner or a hair oil to your hair to help detangle and moisturize your curls. This will also help reduce frizz.
  3. Apply a styling product to your hair, such as a curl cream or a mousse. This will help define your curls and hold your style.
  4. Scrunch your hair to encourage your curls to form. You can also twist your curls around your fingers to create more defined curls.
  5. If you want to create loose curls, use a diffuser attachment on your blow dryer to dry your hair. If you want to create tighter curls, let your hair air dry or use a diffuser on a low heat setting.
  6. Once your hair is dry, use a curling iron or a wand to touch up any curls that need more definition.
  7. Finish your style with a hairspray or a hair oil to add shine and hold your curls in place.

Tips for Styling Dirty Curly Hair

Here are some tips to help you style your dirty curly hair:

  • Use a microfiber towel or an old t-shirt to dry your hair instead of a regular towel. Regular towels can create frizz and damage your curls.
  • Avoid using a brush on your hair when it's dry, as this can create frizz and break your curls.
  • Use a satin or silk pillowcase to reduce frizz and prevent your hair from tangling while you sleep.
  • Experiment with different styling products to find the ones that work best for your hair.

Frequently Asked Questions

Here are some frequently asked questions about styling dirty curly hair:

  • Can I style my dirty curly hair without washing it? Yes, you can style your dirty curly hair without washing it. In fact, styling dirty hair can be easier and hold styles better than clean hair.
  • What kind of styling products should I use on my curly hair? It depends on your hair type and the style you want to achieve. Some good styling products for curly hair include curl creams, mousses, and gels.
  • How often should I wash my curly hair? It depends on your hair type and personal preference. Some people with curly hair can go several days without washing their hair, while others prefer to wash their hair every other day.

Hair Care for Curly Hair

Styling your hair is just one part of taking care of your curly hair. Here are some tips for keeping your curls healthy and beautiful:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils.
  • Deep condition your hair once a week to keep it moisturized and healthy.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools on your hair too often, as this can damage your curls.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and chlorine by wearing a hat or using a hair protectant spray.
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends and keep your hair looking healthy.

By following these tips and techniques, you can style your dirty curly hair and achieve a gorgeous and effortless look. Remember to experiment with different products and techniques to find the ones that work best for your hair type and style.
