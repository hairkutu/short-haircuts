Best Oblog Face Short Hairstyles For Females

Written by Eveline Aug 03, 2023 Â· 6 min read

If you have an oblong face shape and short hair, you might be wondering what hairstyles will flatter your features. The good news is that there are a variety of short hairstyles that can enhance an oblong face shape. In this article, we will discuss the best oblong face short hairstyles for females.

Description

An oblong face shape is characterized by a long and narrow shape, with a high forehead, long cheekbones, and a pointed jawline. The goal of a hairstyle for an oblong face shape is to add width and volume to the face, while balancing out the length. Short hairstyles can be a great option for oblong face shapes, as they can create the illusion of width and volume.

How to Choose the Right Short Hairstyle for Your Oblong Face Shape

When choosing a short hairstyle for an oblong face shape, the key is to look for styles that add volume and width to the face. This can be achieved through layers, curls, and texture. Avoid hairstyles that are too straight and flat, as they can make the face appear even longer.

Step by Step Guide

1. Start by identifying your face shape.

2. Browse through different short hairstyles for oblong face shapes.

3. Consider the texture and volume of the hairstyle.

4. Choose a style that adds width and volume to the face.

5. Consult with a hairstylist to ensure the style will work with your hair type and face shape.

Tips for Styling Short Hair for Oblong Face Shapes

1. Use volumizing products, such as mousse or root-boosting spray, to add volume to the hair.

2. Consider adding layers to the hair to create texture and movement.

3. Embrace curls or waves to add width to the face.

4. Avoid hairstyles that are too straight and flat, as they can elongate the face.

FAQs

What are the best short hairstyles for oblong face shapes?

The best short hairstyles for oblong face shapes are those that add volume and width to the face. This can be achieved through layers, curls, and texture. Some examples include a layered bob, a wavy pixie cut, or a side-swept fringe.

Can I still have a short hairstyle if I have an oblong face shape?

Absolutely! There are plenty of short hairstyles that can flatter an oblong face shape. The key is to choose a style that adds volume and width to the face, while balancing out the length. Consult with a hairstylist to find the perfect style for you.

Hair Care for Short Hairstyles for Oblong Face Shapes

1. Use a shampoo and conditioner that is formulated for your hair type.

2. Avoid over-washing the hair, as this can strip it of natural oils.

3. Use a heat protectant spray before using hot tools, such as a curling iron or straightener.

4. Schedule regular trims to keep the hair healthy and prevent split ends.

In conclusion, there are plenty of short hairstyles that can flatter an oblong face shape. By choosing a style that adds volume and width to the face, you can enhance your features and feel confident in your look. Consult with a hairstylist to find the perfect style for you, and don't forget to take good care of your hair to keep it healthy and looking great.
