Short Haircuts For Oval Face 2021 A Guide To The Best Styles

If you have an oval face shape and you're looking for a new haircut, you're in luck! There are plenty of trendy and stylish short haircuts for oval face shapes that you can try in 2021. From pixie cuts to bobs, there are many options to choose from. In this guide, we'll discuss how to choose the best short haircut for an oval face, provide a step-by-step guide to getting a short haircut, and give you tips for maintaining your new look.

How to Choose the Best Short Haircut for an Oval Face

When choosing a short haircut for an oval face, you want to keep the overall balance of your face in mind. With an oval face shape, you have a balanced and symmetrical face, so you can pull off a variety of haircuts. However, you want to avoid styles that make your face look too long or too round.

One of the best short haircuts for an oval face is the classic bob. A bob that hits just below the chin can help to accentuate your jawline and balance out your face. Another great option is a pixie cut. This short and edgy style can help to highlight your cheekbones and add some dimension to your face.

Other Short Haircuts to Consider:

  • Asymmetrical bob
  • Choppy layers
  • Side-swept bangs
  • Pixie cut with long bangs
  • Mohawk

Step by Step Guide to Getting a Short Haircut for an Oval Face

Once you've decided on the best short haircut for your oval face, it's time to visit your stylist. Here's a step-by-step guide to getting a short haircut:

  1. Consult with your stylist to determine the best short haircut for your face shape
  2. Wash and dry your hair
  3. Section your hair into manageable sections
  4. Use hair clips to secure each section of hair
  5. Start cutting your hair, starting at the back and working your way to the front
  6. Use a razor or scissors to create texture and layers
  7. Style your hair using a blow dryer, straightener, or curling iron

Tips for Maintaining Your Short Haircut

Once you've got your new short haircut, it's important to take care of it properly. Here are some tips for maintaining your new look:

  • Use a high-quality shampoo and conditioner
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often
  • Get regular trims to keep your haircut looking fresh
  • Use hair products that are specifically designed for your hair type
  • Consider using a hair mask or treatment to keep your hair healthy and moisturized

FAQs

Q: Will a short haircut make my face look rounder?

A: It depends on the style of the haircut. A bob that hits just below the chin or a pixie cut with long bangs can help to elongate your face and balance out your features.

Q: Can I still wear my hair up with a short haircut?

A: Yes! You can still wear your hair up with a short haircut. Try a half-up, half-down style or a messy bun.

Q: Is it okay to wash my hair every day with a short haircut?

A: It's generally not necessary to wash your hair every day, even with a short haircut. However, if you have an oily scalp, you may need to wash your hair more frequently.

Hair Care for Short Haircuts

Short haircuts require a different type of hair care than longer hairstyles. Here are some tips for taking care of your short haircut:

  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner
  • Don't use too much product, as it can weigh down your hair
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair
  • Consider using a dry shampoo to extend the life of your hairstyle

With these tips and a great short haircut for oval face 2021, you'll be ready to rock a new look in no time!
