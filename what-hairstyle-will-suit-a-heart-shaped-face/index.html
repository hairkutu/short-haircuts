What Hairstyle Will Suit A Heart Shaped Face

Written by Oliver Jun 27, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Are you looking for the perfect hairstyle to flatter your heart shaped face? Look no further! In this article, we will discuss the best hairstyles for heart shaped faces, as well as provide tips and tricks for maintaining your new look. Read on to find out more!

Description: Heart Shaped Faces

Heart shaped faces have a wider forehead and cheekbones, with a narrow chin and jawline. This face shape is often compared to an inverted triangle, and can be quite striking. When choosing a hairstyle for a heart shaped face, it is important to balance out the proportions and enhance your natural features.

How To: Choosing the Perfect Hairstyle

The key to choosing the perfect hairstyle for a heart shaped face is to create balance and symmetry. Here are some tips to keep in mind:

Step 1: Determine Your Hair Texture and Length

Before choosing a hairstyle, take into consideration your hair texture and length. Fine hair may need more volume and layers, while thick hair may benefit from blunt cuts and sleek styles. Short hair can accentuate your cheekbones, while longer hair can elongate your face.

Step 2: Consider Your Face Shape

While heart shaped faces have a distinct shape, they can still vary in proportions. Take into consideration the length of your forehead and the width of your cheekbones when choosing a hairstyle. Longer bangs or side-swept fringe can balance out a wider forehead, while layers or waves can soften a narrow chin.

Step 3: Play Up Your Best Features

Heart shaped faces often have a defined jawline and cheekbones. Enhance these features by choosing a hairstyle that draws attention to them. A sleek ponytail or updo can showcase your cheekbones, while a side-parted bob can highlight your jawline.

Tips for Maintaining Your New Hairstyle

Once you've chosen the perfect hairstyle for your heart shaped face, it's important to maintain it properly. Here are some tips:

Tip 1: Use the Right Products

Using the right products for your hair type can make all the difference. Fine hair may benefit from volumizing mousse, while curly hair may need a defining cream. Look for products that cater to your specific needs.

Tip 2: Schedule Regular Haircuts

To keep your hairstyle looking fresh and polished, it's important to schedule regular haircuts. This will prevent split ends and maintain the shape of your cut.

Tip 3: Protect Your Hair

Protect your hair from heat damage by using a heat protectant spray before using hot tools. Also, avoid using too much heat too often, as this can cause damage and breakage.

FAQ: Frequently Asked Questions

Here are some frequently asked questions about hairstyles for heart shaped faces:

What hairstyles should I avoid with a heart shaped face?

Avoid hairstyles that are too top-heavy, such as a blunt bob or heavy bangs. These styles can accentuate your narrow chin and make your face appear unbalanced.

Can I pull off a pixie cut with a heart shaped face?

A pixie cut can be a great option for heart shaped faces, as long as it is balanced out with volume on top or longer layers around the face.

What is the best way to style a lob with a heart shaped face?

A lob, or long bob, can be a great option for heart shaped faces. To style, add loose waves or curls to soften your jawline and add volume. Side-swept fringe can also balance out a wider forehead.

Hair Care for Heart Shaped Faces

Overall, hair care for heart shaped faces is similar to that for any other face shape. Regular trims, proper product use, and protecting your hair from damage are all important factors in maintaining healthy, beautiful hair.

By following the tips and tricks outlined in this article, you can choose the perfect hairstyle for your heart shaped face and maintain it with ease. Experiment with different styles and have fun with your new look!
