5 On Sides Long On Top The Trendy Hairstyle Of 2023

Written by Petter Apr 15, 2023 · 8 min read

If you're looking for a stylish and low-maintenance haircut, then the 5 on sides long on top is perfect for you. This hairstyle has been gaining popularity in recent years and is still going strong in 2023. In this blog post, we'll cover everything you need to know about this trendy hairstyle, including how to achieve it, tips for maintenance, and more.

Table of Contents

  • Step-by-Step Guide to Cutting Your Hair in This Style
  • Tips for Maintaining Your 5 on Sides Long on Top Haircut

  • Hair Care Products to Use for This Hairstyle

How to Achieve the 5 on Sides Long on Top Haircut

The 5 on sides long on top haircut is a versatile hairstyle that can be customized to suit your face shape and hair type. The key to achieving this look is to keep the sides and back of your hair short, while leaving the top longer. This creates a contrast that makes the hairstyle stand out.

To achieve this haircut, you'll need to use clippers to cut the sides and back of your hair to a length of 5. This is typically done using a number 5 guard attachment. Then, you'll need to use scissors to cut the top of your hair to your desired length. This can range from a few inches to several inches, depending on your preference.

Step-by-Step Guide to Cutting Your Hair in This Style

If you're planning to cut your own hair in this style, here's a step-by-step guide to follow:

  1. Start by washing your hair and towel-drying it until it's slightly damp.
  2. Use clippers with a number 5 guard attachment to cut the sides and back of your hair. Start at the bottom and work your way up, making sure to keep the clippers level.
  3. Once you've finished cutting the sides and back, switch to scissors and cut the top of your hair to your desired length. Use a comb to separate the hair and cut it in sections.
  4. Blend the top and sides of your hair together using scissors or clippers with a shorter guard attachment.
  5. Use a comb to style your hair and apply a styling product to hold it in place.

Tips for Maintaining Your 5 on Sides Long on Top Haircut

One of the benefits of the 5 on sides long on top haircut is that it's low-maintenance. However, there are still some things you can do to keep your hair looking its best:

  • Visit a barber every 4-6 weeks to keep your hair looking neat and tidy.
  • Use a styling product to keep your hair in place and prevent frizz.
  • Wash your hair every other day to prevent it from becoming too oily.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as this can damage your hair.

FAQs About the 5 on Sides Long on Top Haircut

Is the 5 on sides long on top haircut suitable for all hair types?

Yes, this haircut can be customized to suit any hair type. However, if you have very curly or thick hair, you may need to spend more time styling it to keep it in place.

How long does it take to style this haircut?

The 5 on sides long on top haircut is a low-maintenance style that can be styled in just a few minutes. However, if you want a more polished look, you may need to spend more time styling your hair.

Can women wear the 5 on sides long on top haircut?

Yes, this haircut is suitable for both men and women. However, women may want to customize the style slightly to suit their face shape and hair type.

Hair Care Products to Use for This Hairstyle

To keep your 5 on sides long on top haircut looking its best, you'll need to use the right hair care products. Here are some recommendations:

  • A styling product, such as pomade or wax, to hold your hair in place.
  • A shampoo and conditioner that's suitable for your hair type.
  • A hairbrush or comb to style your hair.

Overall, the 5 on sides long on top haircut is a versatile and trendy hairstyle that's perfect for anyone looking for a low-maintenance haircut. Whether you're a man or a woman, you can customize this style to suit your face shape and hair type. By following the tips in this blog post, you can keep your hair looking its best and stay on-trend in 2023.
