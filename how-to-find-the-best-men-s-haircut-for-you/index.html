How To Find The Best Men s Haircut For You

Written by Luffy Jun 18, 2023 Â· 9 min read

Are you tired of always getting the same haircut? Do you want to try something new but don't know where to start? Don't worry, we've got you covered. In this article, we'll give you tips on how to find the best men's haircut for you. From choosing the right length to finding the perfect style, we'll guide you through the process. So, let's get started.

Table of Contents

  • Choosing the Right Length
  • Matching Your Haircut with Your Face Shape
  • Finding the Perfect Style
  • Considering Maintenance
  • Expert Tips
  • FAQ

Choosing the Right Length

The first step to finding the perfect haircut is to choose the right length. This depends on your hair type, face shape, and personal preference. If you have thick hair, you may want to go for a shorter haircut to make it more manageable. If you have thin hair, a longer haircut may make it look fuller.

When it comes to face shape, there are a few general guidelines to follow. If you have a round face, you may want to go for a haircut that adds height to your hair, such as a pompadour. If you have a square face, a haircut with softer edges, such as a layered cut, may complement your features. If you have an oval face, you're lucky as most haircuts will suit you.

Lastly, consider your personal preference. Do you prefer a low-maintenance haircut that doesn't require much styling, or are you willing to spend time styling your hair every day? This will determine the length that works best for you.

Matching Your Haircut with Your Face Shape

As we mentioned earlier, your face shape plays a big role in finding the right haircut. Here are some tips to help you match your haircut with your face shape:

  • If you have a round face, go for a haircut that adds height to your hair, such as a pompadour or quiff.
  • If you have a square face, a haircut with softer edges, such as a layered cut, may complement your features.
  • If you have an oval face, you're lucky as most haircuts will suit you. However, avoid haircuts that cover your forehead as they may make your face look shorter.
  • If you have a long face, go for a haircut that adds width to your face, such as a side-swept fringe or a textured crop.

Finding the Perfect Style

Once you've chosen the right length and matched your haircut with your face shape, it's time to find the perfect style. This depends on your hair type, the occasion, and your personal style. Here are some popular men's hairstyles to consider:

  • The classic side part: This is a timeless hairstyle that suits most face shapes.
  • The textured crop: This is a low-maintenance haircut that works well for men with thick hair.
  • The buzz cut: This is a short, low-maintenance haircut that's perfect for summer.
  • The undercut: This is a versatile haircut that can be styled in many ways, from a slick back to a messy fringe.
  • The curly top: This is a great haircut for men with curly hair who want to embrace their natural texture.

Considering Maintenance

Before you finalize your haircut, consider the maintenance required to keep it looking good. Some haircuts require more upkeep than others, so make sure you're willing to commit to the upkeep before you get the haircut. If you're not sure how much maintenance a certain haircut requires, ask your barber or stylist for advice.

Expert Tips

Here are some expert tips to help you find the best men's haircut for you:

  • Bring a photo: If you're not sure how to describe the haircut you want, bring a photo to your barber or stylist. This will give them a clear idea of what you're looking for.
  • Consider your lifestyle: If you're always on the go, choose a low-maintenance haircut that doesn't require much styling.
  • Ask for advice: Your barber or stylist is the expert, so don't be afraid to ask for their opinion on what would suit you best.
  • Experiment: Don't be afraid to try something new. You may be surprised at how much you like a completely different haircut.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about finding the best men's haircut:

  • How often should I get a haircut?
  • It depends on your hair type and how fast it grows. As a general rule, most men get a haircut every 4-6 weeks.
  • What's the best haircut for thinning hair?
  • A short, textured haircut can make thinning hair look fuller. Avoid long hairstyles as they may emphasize the thinning areas.
  • What's the best haircut for curly hair?
  • A layered cut can help control curly hair and prevent it from looking too bulky.

Hair Care

Once you've found the perfect haircut, it's important to take care of your hair to keep it looking good. Here are some hair care tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a shampoo that suits your hair type.
  • Condition your hair to keep it moisturized.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, such as blow dryers and straighteners.
  • Use a styling product that suits your hair type and the style you want to achieve.
  • Visit your barber or stylist regularly to maintain your haircut.

Now that you know how to find the best men's haircut for you, it's time to book an appointment with your barber or stylist. Remember, finding the perfect haircut takes time and experimentation, so don't be afraid to try something new. Good luck!
