Men s Hairstyles Longer On Top Shorter On Sides A Guide For 2023

Written by Luffy Jun 17, 2023 Â· 8 min read

If you're a man looking to switch up your hairstyle, you may want to consider the popular trend of longer hair on top and shorter sides. This versatile look can be styled in various ways and suits different face shapes and hair types. In this guide, we will discuss how to achieve this style, tips for maintenance and care, and answer some frequently asked questions.

Table of Contents

  • How to Achieve the Look
  • Tips for Maintenance and Care
  • FAQ

How to Achieve the Look

The longer on top shorter on the sides look can be achieved with different haircuts, such as the classic undercut, the modern fade, or the textured crop. Regardless of the specific style, the general idea is to have longer hair on the top of your head that gradually gets shorter towards the sides and back.

To get this style, you should visit a professional hairstylist or barber, who can assess your hair texture and face shape and recommend the best cut for you. They will likely use a combination of scissors and clippers to create the desired length and shape. You can also bring pictures of hairstyles you like to show your stylist.

Once you have your new haircut, you can experiment with different styling products and techniques to create different looks. For example, you can slick back your hair for a formal occasion, or tousle it for a more casual or messy look. Some popular products for longer on top shorter on sides styles include pomade, wax, clay, and sea salt spray.

Tips for Maintenance and Care

To keep your longer on top shorter on sides hairstyle looking fresh and stylish, here are some tips:

  • Get regular trims every 4-6 weeks to maintain the shape and prevent split ends.
  • Wash your hair with a gentle shampoo and conditioner every other day or as needed, depending on your hair type and lifestyle.
  • Avoid using hot tools like blow dryers and flat irons too frequently, as they can damage your hair and cause frizz.
  • If you have curly or wavy hair, use a diffuser attachment on your blow dryer and scrunch your hair with your hands to enhance the texture.
  • If you have straight hair, use a round brush and blow dry your hair away from your face for more volume.
  • Apply styling products sparingly and evenly, starting from the back of your head and working towards the front, to avoid overloading the hair.
  • Experiment with different styles and products, but don't be afraid to ask for advice from your stylist or barber.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about longer on top shorter on sides hairstyles:

What face shapes suit this style?

This style can suit different face shapes, but generally works best for oval, square, and diamond face shapes. If you have a round face, you may want to keep some length on the sides to balance out the proportions.

What hair types work best with this style?

This style can work for different hair types, from straight to wavy to curly. However, the texture and thickness of your hair may affect the way the style looks and holds. If you have fine or thin hair, you may want to use volumizing products to create more fullness and texture.

How do I maintain this style?

To maintain this style, you should get regular trims, wash and condition your hair regularly, and use styling products that suit your hair type and desired look. You can also ask your stylist for tips on how to style your hair at home.

Can I wear this style for formal occasions?

Yes, you can wear this style for formal occasions, such as weddings or job interviews. To make it look more polished, you can slick back your hair with a pomade or gel and use a comb to create a side part.

Hair Care

Lastly, it's essential to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and strong. This includes eating a balanced diet, staying hydrated, and avoiding excessive heat, chemicals, and stress. You can also use hair masks and oils to nourish and moisturize your hair, and protect it from environmental damage.

With these tips and advice, you can rock a longer on top shorter on sides hairstyle with confidence and style. Remember to choose a cut and style that suits your personality and lifestyle, and have fun experimenting with different looks.
