How To Do A Low Curly Ponytail A Step By Step Guide

Written by Frank Aug 09, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Are you looking for a quick and easy hairstyle that will make you look effortlessly chic? Look no further than the low curly ponytail! This classic hairstyle is perfect for any occasion, whether it's a day at the office or a night out with friends. In this blog post, we'll show you how to achieve this look in just a few easy steps.

Table of Contents

  1. How to Do a Low Curly Ponytail
  2. Tips for a Perfect Low Curly Ponytail
  3. Frequently Asked Questions
  4. Hair Care Tips for a Long-Lasting Ponytail

How to Do a Low Curly Ponytail

Follow these simple steps to achieve a gorgeous low curly ponytail:

  1. Start by washing and conditioning your hair. This will help your curls to stay defined and prevent frizz.
  2. Apply a leave-in conditioner or curl-defining cream to your damp hair. Use a wide-toothed comb to distribute the product evenly throughout your hair.
  3. Flip your hair over and dry it with a diffuser attachment on your blow dryer. This will help to enhance your natural curls and add volume.
  4. Once your hair is dry, use a curling iron to add more definition to your curls. Take small sections of hair and wrap them around the curling iron, holding for a few seconds before releasing. Continue until all of your hair is curled.
  5. Brush your hair back into a low ponytail, securing it with a hair tie. Make sure to leave a few strands of hair out at the front to frame your face.
  6. Take a small section of hair from the ponytail and wrap it around the hair tie, securing it with a bobby pin.
  7. Use a serum or hairspray to tame any flyaways and add shine.
  8. And voila! You now have a beautiful low curly ponytail.

Pro tip: If you want more volume at the crown of your head, backcomb your hair before starting the ponytail.

Tips for a Perfect Low Curly Ponytail

Here are some additional tips to help you achieve the perfect low curly ponytail:

  • Use a heat protectant spray before using any hot tools to prevent damage to your hair.
  • For a more natural look, use a curling wand instead of a curling iron.
  • If you have thin or fine hair, use a volumizing mousse before blow drying your hair.
  • Don't use too much product, as this can weigh your hair down and make it look greasy.

Frequently Asked Questions

How long will this hairstyle last?

If you take care of your hair and use the right products, this hairstyle can last for several days.

Can I wear this hairstyle to a formal event?

Absolutely! Just add some hair accessories, such as a sparkly hairpin or headband, to dress up the look.

Can I do this hairstyle on short hair?

Yes, but you may need to use hair extensions or a hairpiece to achieve the ponytail.

Hair Care Tips for a Long-Lasting Ponytail

Here are some tips to help your low curly ponytail last as long as possible:

  • Avoid touching your hair too much, as this can cause frizz and make your curls fall out.
  • Sleep with your hair in a loose bun or braid to prevent tangling and frizz.
  • Use a silk or satin pillowcase to prevent friction and breakage.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as this can cause damage and breakage.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.

Now that you know how to do a low curly ponytail, you can rock this chic and effortless hairstyle anytime, anywhere. Whether you're heading to work or out for a night on the town, this look is guaranteed to turn heads. So grab your curling iron and hair tie and get ready to slay!
