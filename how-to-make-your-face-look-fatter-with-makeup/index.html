How To Make Your Face Look Fatter With Makeup Tips And Tricks

Written by William Jun 19, 2023 Â· 7 min read
If you're looking to add a little more plumpness to your face, there are several makeup techniques that can help you achieve this look. From highlighting certain areas to contouring your cheeks, there are many ways to create the illusion of a fuller face. In this article, we'll go over some tips and tricks on how to make your face look fatter with makeup.

Table of Contents

  • How to Make Your Face Look Fatter with Makeup
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips and Tricks
  • FAQs

How to Make Your Face Look Fatter with Makeup

One of the easiest ways to create the illusion of a fuller face is by using a highlighter. By applying highlighter to certain areas of your face, you can make those areas appear more prominent and full. Here are some areas to focus on:

Cheekbones

Using a highlighter, apply the product to the highest point of your cheekbones. This will make your cheekbones appear more pronounced and give your face a lifted look.

Brow Bone

Apply highlighter to your brow bone to create the illusion of a more defined brow. This will also add some dimension to your face.

Cupid's Bow

Highlighting your cupid's bow can make your lips appear fuller and more plump. This is a great technique to use if you want to draw attention to your lips.

Nose

Applying highlighter down the bridge of your nose can make your nose appear more prominent and defined. This can also help balance out your face if you have a wider forehead.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here is a step-by-step guide on how to make your face look fatter with makeup: 1. Start by applying foundation to your face. Use a shade that matches your skin tone. 2. Apply a darker shade of foundation or contour powder to the hollows of your cheeks. Use a brush to blend the product in. 3. Apply a highlighter to the highest point of your cheekbones, brow bone, cupid's bow, and down the bridge of your nose. 4. Use a blush to add some color to your cheeks. Smile and apply the blush to the apples of your cheeks. 5. Finish off the look with some mascara and a lip gloss.

Tips and Tricks

- Use a light hand when applying the contour powder. You don't want to overdo it and end up with a muddy look. - Make sure to blend your makeup properly. Harsh lines can make your face appear more angular and less full. - Experiment with different shades of blush to find one that complements your skin tone.

FAQs

Q: Can I use bronzer instead of contour powder? A: Yes, you can use bronzer instead of contour powder. Just make sure to choose a shade that is a few shades darker than your skin tone. Q: Will this technique work on all face shapes? A: Yes, this technique can work on all face shapes. Just make sure to focus on the areas that you want to appear more prominent.

Hair Care Tips to Complement Your New Look

Now that you know how to make your face look fatter with makeup, why not complement your new look with a new hairstyle? Here are some hair care tips to help you achieve a fuller look: - Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to add some bounce to your hair. - Blow dry your hair upside down to create more volume at the roots. - Use a round brush to add some curl and volume to your hair. By following these tips and tricks, you can create the illusion of a fuller face and achieve a more youthful, refreshed look. Experiment with different makeup techniques and hairstyles to find a look that works best for you.
