How To Style A Pixie Cut With Bobby Pins A Complete Guide

If you have a pixie cut, you know that styling it can be a challenge. However, using bobby pins can be a great way to add flair and personality to your short hair. In this article, we'll show you how to style a pixie cut with bobby pins, step-by-step.

How to Style a Pixie Cut with Bobby Pins

Styling a pixie cut with bobby pins is actually pretty easy, and it can be done in a variety of ways. Here are a few options:

Option 1: Use Bobby Pins to Pin Back Your Bangs

If you have bangs, you can use bobby pins to pin them back in a variety of ways. For example, you can:

  • Twist your bangs back and pin them to the side
  • Sweep your bangs to one side and pin them in place
  • Create a mini pompadour by pinning your bangs up and back

Whichever option you choose, be sure to use bobby pins that match your hair color.

Option 2: Use Bobby Pins to Create a Fauxhawk

A fauxhawk is a fun and edgy style that can be achieved with bobby pins. To create a fauxhawk, follow these steps:

  1. Use a comb to create a center part in your hair
  2. Use bobby pins to pin back the sides of your hair, leaving the center section loose
  3. Tease the center section of your hair to add volume
  4. Use bobby pins to pin the center section up and back, creating a fauxhawk

This style works best if you have longer hair on top of your head than on the sides.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide for styling your pixie cut with bobby pins:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair.
  2. Decide which style you want to create (see options above).
  3. Use a comb to separate the section of hair you want to pin back.
  4. Twist or tease the hair (depending on the style you're going for).
  5. Use bobby pins to secure the hair in place.
  6. Repeat on the other side (if necessary).
  7. Finish with hairspray to hold the style in place.

Tips for Styling a Pixie Cut with Bobby Pins

Here are a few tips to keep in mind when styling your pixie cut with bobby pins:

  • Use bobby pins that match your hair color for a seamless look.
  • Don't be afraid to experiment with different styles and techniques.
  • Use hairspray to help hold your style in place.
  • Be gentle when removing the bobby pins to avoid damaging your hair.

FAQs

Can I use bobby pins on wet hair?

It's best to use bobby pins on dry hair, as wet hair is more fragile and prone to breakage.

How many bobby pins should I use?

It depends on the style you're trying to create. Start with a few, and add more as needed.

How long can I leave bobby pins in my hair?

It's best to remove bobby pins before going to bed to avoid damaging your hair.

Hair Care Tips for Pixie Cuts

Maintaining a pixie cut requires regular trims and proper hair care. Here are a few tips to keep your hair healthy:

  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner that's formulated for your hair type.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools (like flat irons and curling irons) too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a UV protectant spray.
  • Get regular trims (every 4-6 weeks) to maintain your style and prevent split ends.

With these tips and techniques, you'll be able to create a variety of stylish looks with your pixie cut and bobby pins. Have fun experimenting, and don't be afraid to try something new!
