Wedding Hairstyles For Medium Length Hair With Fringe

Written by Frank Aug 24, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Are you a bride-to-be with medium length hair and a fringe? Choosing the perfect hairstyle for your wedding day can be a daunting task, but don't worry, we've got you covered! In this article, we'll be sharing some of the most beautiful and elegant wedding hairstyles for medium length hair with fringe. Follow our tips and step-by-step guide to achieve your dream wedding hairstyle.

Description

Medium length hair with a fringe is a versatile and trendy hairstyle choice. It allows you to experiment with different styles and textures while maintaining your hair's length. Wedding hairstyles for medium length hair with fringe can range from simple and elegant to intricate and glamorous. The key to achieving the perfect wedding hairstyle is to choose a style that complements your face shape, dress, and overall wedding theme.

How to

Before you start styling your hair, make sure it's clean and dry. Apply a heat protectant to prevent damage from heat styling tools. You can also add some texture to your hair by using a curling iron or a flat iron. Once your hair is prepped, you can start creating your wedding hairstyle.

Step-by-Step

1. Sleek Low Bun: Start by parting your hair in the middle and create a low ponytail at the nape of your neck. Twist the ponytail into a bun and secure it with bobby pins. Use a flat iron to straighten your fringe and tuck it behind your ears.

2. Loose Waves: Use a curling iron to create loose waves in your hair. Sweep your fringe to one side and secure it with bobby pins. Add a floral hair accessory to complete the look.

3. Half-Up Half-Down: Part your hair in the middle and create two sections. Take the top section and create a loose bun at the back of your head. Leave the bottom section down and add some loose waves. Sweep your fringe to one side and secure it with bobby pins.

Tips

- Choose a wedding hairstyle that complements your face shape.

- Consider the neckline of your dress when choosing a hairstyle.

- Add some texture to your hair by using a curling iron or a flat iron.

- Use hair accessories such as flowers, pins, or a headband to add some extra glam to your hairstyle.

FAQ

Q: Can I wear my fringe down for my wedding?

A: Yes, you can definitely wear your fringe down for your wedding. Just make sure to use a flat iron to straighten it and secure it with bobby pins to prevent it from falling into your face.

Q: How do I choose the perfect wedding hairstyle?

A: Consider your face shape, dress, and wedding theme. Browse through wedding magazines or Pinterest for inspiration, and schedule a hair trial with your hairstylist to try out different styles.

Hair Care

To keep your hair healthy and shiny, make sure to use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner. Avoid using heat styling tools too often, and use a heat protectant when you do. Get regular trims to prevent split ends and breakage, and consider using a deep conditioning treatment once a week.

With these tips and ideas, you're sure to find the perfect wedding hairstyle for your medium length hair with fringe. Remember to choose a style that makes you feel confident and beautiful on your special day.
