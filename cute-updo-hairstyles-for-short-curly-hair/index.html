Cute Updo Hairstyles For Short Curly Hair

Written by Luffy Aug 21, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Curly hair can be a challenge to style, but with the right techniques and products, you can create some amazing updo hairstyles. Whether you have natural curls or you have permed your hair, there are many cute updo hairstyles for short curly hair that you can try. In this article, we will share some tips, tricks, and step-by-step instructions for creating beautiful updo hairstyles that will make you look and feel fabulous.

Description

Short curly hair can be styled in many different ways, from braids and twists to buns and top knots. These hairstyles can be casual or formal, depending on the occasion. Cute updo hairstyles for short curly hair can be achieved with the help of some hair accessories, styling products, and a little bit of creativity.

How to

The first step in creating a cute updo hairstyle for short curly hair is to wash and condition your hair. Use a shampoo and conditioner that are designed for curly hair, as these products will help to define your curls and reduce frizz. Once your hair is clean and conditioned, towel-dry it gently, and apply a leave-in conditioner or styling cream to help keep your curls in place.

Next, section your hair and start styling it. You can create a braided updo, a twisted updo, or a bun updo. Each of these styles requires a different technique, but they all start with the same basic steps:

Braided Updo

Step 1: Start by creating a braid at the crown of your head. Use a small elastic to secure the end of the braid.

Step 2: Take the rest of your hair and twist it into a bun, securing it with bobby pins.

Step 3: Wrap the braid around the bun and secure it with bobby pins.

Twisted Updo

Step 1: Twist small sections of hair around your finger to create curls.

Step 2: Take two or three curls and twist them together. Continue this process until you have twisted all of your curls.

Step 3: Twist the twisted curls into a bun and secure it with bobby pins.

Bun Updo

Step 1: Gather your hair into a high ponytail and secure it with an elastic.

Step 2: Twist the ponytail around the elastic to create a bun.

Step 3: Secure the bun with bobby pins.

Step by Step

Step 1: Start by washing and conditioning your hair

Step 2: Towel-dry your hair and apply leave-in conditioner or styling cream

Step 3: Section your hair

Step 4: Choose the updo hairstyle you want to create (braided updo, twisted updo, or bun updo)

Step 5: Follow the instructions above for the updo hairstyle you have chosen

Step 6: Add hair accessories if desired

Tips

- Use hair accessories such as hairpins, hairbands, or hair ties to enhance your updo hairstyle.

- Apply hair serum or oil to reduce frizz and add shine to your hair.

- Experiment with different updo hairstyles until you find the one that suits you best.

FAQ

Q: Can I create updo hairstyles with natural curls?

A: Yes, you can. Natural curls can be styled in many different ways, from braids and twists to buns and top knots.

Q: Can I create updo hairstyles with permed hair?

A: Yes, you can. Permed hair can be styled in the same way as natural curls.

Hair Care

To keep your short curly hair healthy and beautiful, it is important to follow a regular hair care routine. This routine should include washing and conditioning your hair with products that are designed for curly hair, using a leave-in conditioner or styling cream to help keep your curls in place, and using hair accessories to enhance your updo hairstyles. Additionally, you should avoid using heat styling tools such as flat irons and curling irons, as these can damage your hair and cause frizz.

Cute updo hairstyles for short curly hair can be achieved with the right techniques and products. Whether you have natural curls or permed hair, you can create beautiful updo hairstyles that will make you look and feel fabulous. So, give these hairstyles a try and see which one works best for you.
