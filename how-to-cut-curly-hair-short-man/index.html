How To Cut Curly Hair Short Man A Step By Step Guide In 2023

Written by Eveline Jun 04, 2023 · 7 min read

Are you a man with curly hair who wants to rock a short hairstyle? Cutting curly hair can be challenging, but with the right techniques and tools, you can achieve a sharp and stylish look. In this article, we’ll walk you through the process of how to cut curly hair short man. We’ll cover everything from how to prepare your hair to how to maintain your new look. Let’s get started!

Table of Contents:

  • Preparation
  • Tools You’ll Need
  • Cutting Techniques
  • Tips for Cutting Curly Hair
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care After Cutting

Preparation

Before you start cutting your curly hair, you need to prepare it properly. Make sure your hair is clean and dry. Wash your hair with a good quality shampoo and conditioner. Dry your hair with a towel or blow dryer until it’s completely dry. Curly hair tends to shrink when it dries, so make sure you stretch it out before cutting.

Tools You’ll Need

To cut curly hair short man, you’ll need the following tools:

  • Scissors
  • Clippers
  • Comb
  • Hair clips
  • Water spray bottle
  • Barber cape or towel

Cutting Techniques

Now that you’ve prepared your hair and gathered your tools, it’s time to start cutting. Here’s a step-by-step guide:

Step 1: Determine the Length

Decide how short you want your hair to be. Use clippers to trim the sides and back of your head. Start with the longest guard and work your way down. Use a mirror to see the back of your head.

Step 2: Cut the Top

Use scissors to trim the top of your hair. Start at the front and work your way to the back. Keep in mind that curly hair bounces up when it dries, so cut it longer than you want it to be. Use hair clips to section off the hair you’re not cutting.

Step 3: Blend the Sides and Top

Use scissors to blend the sides and top of your hair. Cut the hair at a slight angle to create a natural-looking fade. Use a comb to guide the scissors as you cut.

Step 4: Define the Edges

Use clippers to define the edges of your hairline. Start at the sideburns and work your way around the ears and the back of your neck. Use a mirror to check your work.

Tips for Cutting Curly Hair

Here are some additional tips to help you cut curly hair short man:

  • Don’t cut your hair too short. Curly hair tends to spring up when it dries, so it will look shorter than it actually is.
  • Use a light touch when cutting curly hair. Avoid using too much tension or pulling the hair too tightly.
  • Take your time and be patient. Cutting curly hair requires a bit more time and effort than cutting straight hair.

FAQs

Q: How often should I cut my curly hair?

A: It depends on how fast your hair grows and how quickly it loses its shape. Generally, it’s a good idea to cut curly hair every 6-8 weeks to maintain the shape and style.

Q: Can I cut my own curly hair?

A: Yes, you can cut your own curly hair, but it’s recommended that you get it done by a professional. Cutting curly hair requires special techniques and tools that may not be readily available to the average person.

Hair Care After Cutting

Now that you’ve cut your curly hair short man, it’s important to take care of it properly. Here are some tips:

  • Use a good quality shampoo and conditioner designed for curly hair.
  • Avoid using hot styling tools, as they can damage your hair.
  • Use a leave-in conditioner to keep your hair moisturized.
  • Get regular trims to maintain the shape and style of your hair.

Cutting curly hair short man can be a bit challenging, but with the right techniques and tools, you can achieve a stylish and sharp look. Remember to take your time and be patient, and always take good care of your hair. With these tips and tricks, you’ll be rocking a new short hairstyle in no time!
