Does Straight Or Curly Hair Look Better On Round Faces

Written by Christine Jul 01, 2023 Â· 8 min read

When it comes to hair, there's no one-size-fits-all solution. What looks great on one person may not look as good on another. In this post, we'll be discussing whether straight or curly hair looks better on round faces. It's a common question among people with round faces, and we're here to help you find the answer. Here's a quick table of content to help you navigate through this post:

  • Description
  • How to Determine Your Face Shape
  • Straight Hair on Round Faces
  • Curly Hair on Round Faces
  • Tips for Styling Hair on Round Faces
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care for Round Faces

Description

Round faces are characterized by soft, curved lines and fullness around the cheeks and jawline. People with round faces often have a shorter forehead and a wider chin. If you're unsure whether you have a round face or not, take a look at the mirror and try to identify these features. Knowing your face shape is important when choosing a hairstyle that flatters your features.

How to Determine Your Face Shape

Determining your face shape is easy. Here's how:

  1. Pull your hair back and away from your face.
  2. Take a picture of your face head-on.
  3. Print out the picture and draw lines to outline the shape of your face.
  4. Compare your face shape to the following shapes: oval, square, round, heart, and diamond.

Once you've determined your face shape, you can start exploring different hairstyles that flatter your features.

Straight Hair on Round Faces

Straight hair can look great on round faces, especially if it's long and layered. The layers add texture and movement to the hair, creating the illusion of length. However, straight hair can also make a round face look wider, especially if it's cut bluntly. If you have straight hair and a round face, consider adding layers to your hair or opting for a side-swept fringe to create the illusion of a longer face.

Curly Hair on Round Faces

Curly hair can also look great on round faces, especially if it's styled in loose waves or curls. The volume and texture of curly hair can balance out the roundness of the face, creating a more angular look. However, curly hair can also add width to the face, so it's important to choose the right length and style. If you have curly hair and a round face, consider a shoulder-length cut with layers to add volume and movement.

Tips for Styling Hair on Round Faces

Here are some tips to help you style your hair if you have a round face:

  • Opt for hairstyles that add volume or height to the crown of your head.
  • Avoid hairstyles that add width to the sides of your face.
  • Consider a side-swept fringe or asymmetrical cut to create the illusion of a longer face.
  • Experiment with different parting styles to find the one that flatters your face shape the most.
  • Avoid blunt cuts and instead opt for layers or texture to add dimension to your hair.

FAQs

Can short hair look good on round faces?

Yes, short hair can look great on round faces as long as it's cut to flatter your features. Consider a pixie cut or a bob that's longer in the front and shorter in the back to create the illusion of length.

Should I avoid bangs if I have a round face?

Not necessarily. Bangs can look great on round faces, especially if they're side-swept or asymmetrical. However, avoid blunt bangs that cut straight across your forehead as they can make your face look wider.

Hair Care for Round Faces

Regardless of your face shape, it's important to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and looking its best. Here are some tips for hair care for round faces:

  • Invest in a good shampoo and conditioner that's suited to your hair type.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often as they can damage your hair.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and harsh weather conditions by wearing a hat or using a leave-in conditioner with SPF.
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends and keep your hair looking healthy.
  • Eat a balanced diet and stay hydrated to promote healthy hair growth.

In conclusion, whether straight or curly hair looks better on round faces depends on the individual and their personal style. By following these tips and experimenting with different styles, you'll be able to find the perfect hairstyle that flatters your features and makes you feel confident.
