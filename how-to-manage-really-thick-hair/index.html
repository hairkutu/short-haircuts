How To Manage Really Thick Hair Tips And Tricks

Written by Bardi Jun 04, 2023 Â· 9 min read

Thick hair can be both a blessing and a curse. While it may look beautiful and luxurious, it can also be a challenge to manage. If you have thick hair, you know that it can take hours to style, and it can be prone to frizz and tangles. But don't worry! With the right tips and tricks, you can easily manage your thick hair and keep it looking healthy and beautiful.

How to

1. Use the Right Products

One of the most important things you can do to manage your thick hair is to use the right products. Look for shampoos and conditioners that are specifically designed for thick hair. These products will help to moisturize your hair and prevent tangles.

You should also look for styling products that are designed for thick hair. These products will help to tame your hair and keep it looking sleek and shiny.

2. Use a Wide-Tooth Comb

When you're brushing your hair, always use a wide-tooth comb. This will help to prevent tangles and breakage. Start at the ends of your hair and work your way up, gently detangling as you go.

3. Don't Overwash Your Hair

If you have thick hair, you don't need to wash it every day. In fact, washing your hair too often can strip it of its natural oils and make it more prone to frizz and tangles.

Instead, try washing your hair every other day or every three days. This will help to keep your hair healthy and moisturized.

4. Use a Heat Protectant

If you're using heat styling tools, always use a heat protectant. This will help to prevent damage to your hair and keep it looking healthy and shiny.

5. Get Regular Trims

Regular trims are key to managing thick hair. They help to prevent split ends and keep your hair looking healthy and vibrant.

Try to get a trim every six to eight weeks to keep your hair looking its best.

Step by Step

Step 1: Wash Your Hair

Start by washing your hair with a shampoo and conditioner that are specifically designed for thick hair. Massage the shampoo into your scalp and work it through your hair.

Rinse your hair thoroughly and follow up with the conditioner. Leave the conditioner on for a few minutes before rinsing it out.

Step 2: Towel Dry Your Hair

After you've washed your hair, gently towel dry it. Don't rub your hair with the towel, as this can cause frizz and tangles.

Instead, gently squeeze the excess water out of your hair with the towel.

Step 3: Apply Styling Products

Apply a styling product that is designed for thick hair. This will help to tame your hair and keep it looking sleek and shiny.

You can use a mousse, gel, or cream, depending on your hair type and the style you're trying to achieve.

Step 4: Blow Dry Your Hair

Use a blow dryer to dry your hair. Start by flipping your head upside down and blow drying your hair from the roots to the tips.

Once your hair is mostly dry, flip your head back up and continue blow drying your hair, using a round brush to smooth out any tangles or frizz.

Step 5: Style Your Hair

Once your hair is dry, you can style it however you like. You can use a flat iron or curling iron to create waves or curls, or you can leave it straight.

Finish off your style with a hairspray or other finishing product to keep it in place.

Tips

  • Use a satin pillowcase to prevent tangles and frizz while you sleep.
  • Avoid using hot water to wash your hair, as this can strip it of its natural oils.
  • Try using a hair mask once a week to keep your hair healthy and moisturized.
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it's wet, as this can cause breakage.

FAQ

Hair Care

Managing thick hair can be a challenge, but with the right tips and tricks, you can keep your hair looking healthy and beautiful. Remember to use the right products, avoid overwashing your hair, and get regular trims to keep split ends at bay. With a little bit of care, you can make your thick hair your best feature.
