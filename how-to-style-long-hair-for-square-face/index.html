Description

If you have a square face, you know that it can be a challenge to find hairstyles that flatter your features. Luckily, there are plenty of ways to style long hair that can soften your angles and enhance your natural beauty. In this post, we'll show you how to style long hair for square face shapes, including step-by-step instructions, tips, and FAQs. Whether you're looking for a casual look for everyday wear, or an elegant updo for a special occasion, we've got you covered.

How to Style Long Hair for Square Face

If you have a square face, you want to choose hairstyles that will soften your angles and create a more oval-like shape. Long hair is a great option for square faces, as it can add length and balance to your features. Here are some popular ways to style long hair for square face shapes:

1. Beachy Waves

Beachy waves are a popular and low-maintenance hairstyle that work well with square face shapes. To get the look, start by applying a heat protectant spray to your hair. Then, use a curling iron or wand to create loose waves. Finish with a texturizing spray to add volume and hold.

2. Side-Swept Bangs

Side-swept bangs are a great way to soften your angles and draw attention to your eyes. To get the look, part your hair to the side and sweep your bangs to the opposite side. Use a round brush to blow dry your bangs for a smooth finish. You can also add some texture with a styling cream or mousse.

3. Half-Up Half-Down

A half-up half-down hairstyle is a versatile and flattering option for square faces. To get the look, start by parting your hair down the middle. Take the top section of your hair and pull it back, securing it with a hair tie or clip. You can leave the bottom section of your hair straight or add some waves for a more textured look.

4. Low Ponytail

A low ponytail is a chic and sophisticated option for square faces. To get the look, start by brushing your hair back and securing it with a hair tie at the nape of your neck. You can also wrap a small section of hair around the hair tie to conceal it. Add some volume and texture by teasing the crown of your hair.

Step by Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide for styling long hair for square face shapes:

Step 1: Wash and Condition Your Hair

Start by washing and conditioning your hair with products that are suited to your hair type. This will help to keep your hair healthy and nourished.

Step 2: Apply Styling Products

Apply any styling products that you like to use, such as heat protectant spray, mousse, or texturizing spray. These will help to protect your hair and add volume and texture.

Step 3: Choose Your Hairstyle

Choose the hairstyle that you want to create, based on your personal style and the occasion. Refer to the previous section for some popular options.

Step 4: Style Your Hair

Follow the instructions for your chosen hairstyle, using the appropriate tools and techniques. Take your time and be gentle with your hair to avoid causing damage.

Step 5: Finish with Hairspray

Once you're happy with your hairstyle, finish with a light mist of hairspray to hold it in place. Choose a hairspray that is suited to your hair type and desired level of hold.

Tips for Styling Long Hair for Square Face

Here are some tips to keep in mind when styling long hair for square face shapes:

  • Choose hairstyles that add length and softness to your features
  • Consider adding layers to your hair to create movement and texture
  • Use styling products that are suited to your hair type and desired look
  • Be gentle with your hair and avoid using excessive heat or styling tools
  • Experiment with different hairstyles to find what works best for you

FAQs

1. Can I wear my hair in a center part if I have a square face?

Yes, you can wear your hair in a center part if you have a square face. However, you may want to consider adding some layers or side-swept bangs to soften your features.

2. Are updos a good option for square faces?

Yes, updos can be a great option for square faces, especially if they incorporate soft curls or waves. Consider a low bun, chignon, or braided updo for an elegant and flattering look.

3. How often should I wash my long hair?

It depends on your hair type and personal preference. Generally, you should aim to wash your hair every 2-3 days to avoid stripping it of its natural oils. However, if you have oily hair, you may need to wash it more frequently.

Hair Care for Long Hair

Long hair requires some extra care to keep it healthy and looking its best. Here are some tips:

  • Use a wide-toothed comb to detangle your hair, starting from the ends and working your way up
  • Avoid using excessive heat or styling tools, and always use a heat protectant spray if you do
  • Apply a hair mask or deep conditioner once a week to nourish and hydrate your hair
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends and maintain its shape
  • Avoid tight hairstyles that can pull on your hair and cause breakage

By following these tips and using the styling techniques outlined in this post, you can create beautiful and flattering hairstyles for your square face shape.
