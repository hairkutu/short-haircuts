How To Do Space Buns With Short Thin Hair

Written by Christine Apr 04, 2023 Â· 5 min read

If you have short and thin hair, you might think that space buns are out of the question. But fear not, because we have some tips and tricks to help you achieve this fun and trendy hairstyle!

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How To
  • Step by Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ

Description

Space buns are a popular hairstyle that involves creating two buns on the top of your head. This style is often associated with festivals, but it can also be a fun everyday look. However, if you have short and thin hair, you might think that this hairstyle is not possible for you. But don't worry, because we have some tips and tricks to help you achieve space buns with short thin hair!

How To

To achieve space buns with short thin hair, you will need some hair ties and bobby pins. You can also use some texturizing spray or dry shampoo to add some volume to your hair.

Step by Step

Here are the steps to achieve space buns with short thin hair:

  1. Start by parting your hair down the middle.
  2. Take a small section of hair from one side and twist it into a bun.
  3. Secure the bun with a hair tie or bobby pins.
  4. Repeat on the other side.
  5. Use bobby pins to secure any loose hair or flyaways.
  6. Optional: add some texturizing spray or dry shampoo to add volume to your hair.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you achieve the perfect space buns with short thin hair:

  • Use bobby pins to secure any loose hair or flyaways.
  • Add some texturizing spray or dry shampoo to add volume to your hair.
  • If you have longer bangs, you can leave them out for a more relaxed look.
  • Experiment with different bun sizes to find the perfect look for you.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about space buns with short thin hair:

Can I do space buns with short thin hair?

Yes, you can! Follow our step-by-step guide and use some texturizing spray or dry shampoo to add volume to your hair.

How do I secure my space buns?

Use hair ties and bobby pins to secure your buns.

Can I leave my bangs out?

Yes, if you have longer bangs, you can leave them out for a more relaxed look.

Hair Care

To keep your short thin hair healthy, make sure to use a gentle shampoo and conditioner. Avoid using hot tools too often and use a heat protectant when you do. Regular trims can also help to keep your hair looking healthy and full.

And there you have it, a simple and easy way to achieve space buns with short thin hair! With our tips and tricks, you can rock this fun and trendy hairstyle with confidence.
