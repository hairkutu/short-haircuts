The Best Product To Slick Back Long Hair A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Frank May 07, 2023 Â· 8 min read
If you have long hair and want to achieve a sleek, polished look, you might be wondering what the best product is to achieve that. Well, look no further! In this article, we'll be discussing the best product to slick back long hair, how to use it, step-by-step instructions, tips for achieving the perfect slick back, and some frequently asked questions.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to Slick Back Long Hair
  • Step-by-Step Instructions
  • Tips for Achieving the Perfect Slick Back
  • Frequently Asked Questions
  • Hair Care Tips

Description

The best product to slick back long hair is pomade. Pomade is a type of hair styling product that provides a high-shine, firm hold, and is perfect for creating a slick back hairstyle. It's also great for adding texture and definition to your hair, making it a versatile product for a variety of hairstyles.

How to Slick Back Long Hair

Before we dive into how to use pomade to slick back long hair, it's important to note that this hairstyle works best on medium to long hair lengths. If you have shorter hair, you may want to consider a different hairstyle.

Step-by-Step Instructions

Now, let's get into the step-by-step instructions for slicking back long hair with pomade. 1. Start with clean, damp hair. Towel dry your hair until it's slightly damp, but not soaking wet.
2. Apply a small amount of pomade to your fingertips. Start with a dime-sized amount and add more as needed.
3. Rub the pomade between your fingertips to warm it up. This will make it easier to apply to your hair.
4. Starting at the crown of your head, work the pomade through your hair. Use your fingers to evenly distribute the product, making sure to cover all of your hair.
5. Comb your hair back, starting at the front and working your way to the back. Use a comb to create a neat, even part down the middle of your head.
6. Smooth your hair down with your hands to create a sleek, polished look.
7. Use a little more pomade if needed to tame any flyaways or frizz.
8. Finish with a light hairspray to hold the style in place.

Tips for Achieving the Perfect Slick Back

- Use a high-quality pomade for the best results. Look for a product that provides a strong hold and high shine.
- Start with a small amount of pomade and add more as needed. Using too much product can make your hair look greasy and weighed down.
- Comb your hair back in one direction for a polished, streamlined look.
- If you have thick or curly hair, blow dry your hair before applying pomade to make it easier to style.
- Experiment with different parting styles to find the one that works best for your face shape.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Can I slick back my hair if I have a receding hairline?
A: Yes! Slicking back your hair can actually help to conceal a receding hairline. Just make sure to use a high-quality pomade that provides a strong hold. Q: Can I slick back my hair if it's thin?
A: Yes, you can! However, you may want to use a lighter pomade to prevent your hair from looking weighed down. Q: How often should I wash my hair if I'm using pomade?
A: It's recommended to wash your hair every other day if you're using pomade regularly. This will prevent product buildup and keep your hair healthy.

Hair Care Tips

- Use a high-quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and strong.
- Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as this can cause damage and breakage.
- Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends and promote healthy growth.
- Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to nourish and moisturize your hair.
- Protect your hair from the sun and heat by wearing a hat or using a leave-in conditioner with SPF. In conclusion, pomade is the best product to slick back long hair. By following the step-by-step instructions in this article and using the tips provided, you'll be able to achieve the perfect slick back hairstyle in no time. Don't forget to take care of your hair by using high-quality hair care products and following a healthy hair care routine.
