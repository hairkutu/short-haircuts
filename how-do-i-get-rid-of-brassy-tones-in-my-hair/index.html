How Do I Get Rid Of Brassy Tones In My Hair A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Christine Aug 19, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Are you tired of dealing with brassy tones in your hair? Don't worry, you're not alone. Many people with blonde, gray, or light-colored hair struggle with this issue. But the good news is, there are several ways to get rid of brassy tones and achieve the beautiful, cool-toned hair you desire. In this article, we'll cover everything you need to know about how to get rid of brassy tones in your hair.

Table of Contents

  1. Description
  2. How To Get Rid of Brassy Tones in Hair
  3. Step by Step Guide
  4. Tips to Prevent Brassy Hair
  5. FAQ
  6. Hair Care Routine for Brassy Hair

Description

Brassy tones in hair are caused by the buildup of warm tones, which can make your hair look dull, yellow, or orange. This can happen to anyone, but it's more common in people with blonde, gray, or light-colored hair. The main causes of brassy hair are exposure to sunlight, hard water, or mineral buildup from hair products. But don't worry, there are several ways to remove brassy tones and prevent them from coming back.

How To Get Rid of Brassy Tones in Hair

The best way to get rid of brassy tones in hair is by using a toner. A toner is a hair product that helps to neutralize warm tones and restore cool tones. You can use a toner at home or go to a salon to get it done professionally. Another option is to use a purple shampoo, which is specifically designed to neutralize yellow tones in blonde or gray hair. Here are some steps to follow:

Step by Step Guide

  1. Wash your hair with a clarifying shampoo to remove any buildup or residue.
  2. Apply a toner or purple shampoo to your hair, following the instructions on the product.
  3. Leave the toner or shampoo on for the recommended time, usually 5-15 minutes.
  4. Rinse your hair thoroughly with cool water.
  5. Apply a moisturizing conditioner to your hair to prevent dryness.
  6. Style your hair as usual.

Tips to Prevent Brassy Hair

Preventing brassy hair is all about taking care of your hair and using the right products. Here are some tips to keep your hair looking cool-toned:

  • Avoid exposure to sunlight by wearing a hat or using a UV protectant spray.
  • Use a water softener or filter to remove minerals from your water.
  • Use a purple shampoo or toner regularly to maintain cool tones in your hair.
  • Avoid using hair products with sulfates or alcohol, which can strip your hair of natural oils.
  • Deep condition your hair once a week to keep it moisturized and healthy.

FAQ

Q: How often should I use a toner or purple shampoo?

A: It depends on your hair type and the level of brassy tones. For mild brassiness, you can use a toner or shampoo once a week. For more severe brassiness, you may need to use it more often, up to 2-3 times a week. But be careful not to overdo it, as too much toner or shampoo can dry out your hair.

Q: Can I use a toner or purple shampoo on colored hair?

A: Yes, you can use a toner or purple shampoo on colored hair, but be careful not to use too much, as it can affect the color. It's best to consult with your hairstylist before using any new products on colored hair.

Hair Care Routine for Brassy Hair

If you have brassy hair, it's important to take care of it properly to prevent further damage. Here's a simple hair care routine to follow:

  1. Wash your hair with a clarifying shampoo once a week to remove buildup.
  2. Use a purple shampoo or toner once a week to maintain cool tones.
  3. Deep condition your hair once a week to keep it moisturized.
  4. Avoid using hot tools or styling products that can damage your hair.
  5. Get regular trims to prevent split ends and breakage.

By following these tips and using the right products, you can get rid of brassy tones in your hair and keep it looking healthy and beautiful. Don't be afraid to experiment with different toners and shampoos to find the one that works best for you.
