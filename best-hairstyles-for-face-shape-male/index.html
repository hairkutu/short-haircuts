Best Hairstyles For Face Shape Male A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Luffy Jun 14, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Choosing the right hairstyle can make a huge difference in a man's appearance. It can enhance his facial features, make him look more attractive, and even boost his confidence. However, not all hairstyles are created equal, as some may not suit a particular face shape.

In this article, we'll provide you with a comprehensive guide on the best hairstyles for face shape male. Whether you have a round, square, oval, or heart-shaped face, we've got you covered. Read on to discover the perfect hairstyle that suits you best.

How to Determine Your Face Shape

The first step in finding the right hairstyle for your face shape is to determine your face shape. Here's how:

  1. Stand in front of a mirror and pull your hair back.
  2. Use a washable marker or lipstick to trace the outline of your face on the mirror.
  3. Step back and examine the shape of your face.

There are four main face shapes: round, square, oval, and heart-shaped. Each has its own distinct characteristics that determine which hairstyle would look best.

Best Hairstyles for Round Face Shape Male

A round face has soft, curved lines with no sharp angles. The best hairstyles for round face shape male are those that create the illusion of length and angles, making the face appear slimmer. Here are some of the best hairstyles for round face shape male:

  • Short back and sides with longer hair on top
  • Side-parted quiff
  • Pompadour
  • Side-swept fringe

Best Hairstyles for Square Face Shape Male

A square face has a strong jawline and a broad forehead. The best hairstyles for square face shape male are those that soften the angular features and create a more balanced look. Here are some of the best hairstyles for square face shape male:

  • Textured crop
  • Side-parted slick back
  • Long fringe
  • Short curly hair

Best Hairstyles for Oval Face Shape Male

An oval face has balanced proportions with a gently curved jawline. The best hairstyles for oval face shape male are those that accentuate the oval shape and balance out the features. Here are some of the best hairstyles for oval face shape male:

  • Classic side part
  • Short messy hair
  • Textured quiff
  • Long slicked back hair

Best Hairstyles for Heart-Shaped Face Male

A heart-shaped face has a wider forehead and a narrower chin. The best hairstyles for heart-shaped face male are those that balance out the features and add width to the jawline. Here are some of the best hairstyles for heart-shaped face male:

  • Side-swept fringe
  • Short buzz cut
  • Classic side part
  • Long wavy hair

Tips for Choosing the Right Hairstyle

Here are some tips to keep in mind when choosing the right hairstyle for your face shape:

  • Consider your hair type and texture
  • Think about your lifestyle and how much time you're willing to spend on styling your hair
  • Consult with a professional hairstylist for advice
  • Experiment with different hairstyles to find the one that suits you best

FAQs

Q: Can I still wear a certain hairstyle even if it doesn't suit my face shape?

A: Yes, you can. Ultimately, the decision is up to you. However, keep in mind that certain hairstyles may not enhance your features as much as others.

Q: How often should I get a haircut?

A: It depends on your hair type and how fast it grows. On average, most men get a haircut every 4-6 weeks.

Q: How can I maintain my hairstyle?

A: Use the right hair products for your hair type and style, and follow a regular hair care routine that includes washing, conditioning, and styling.

Hair Care Tips

Here are some hair care tips to keep your hair healthy and looking its best:

  • Use a shampoo and conditioner that's suited for your hair type
  • Avoid using hot tools like hair dryers and straighteners too often, as they can damage your hair
  • Get regular haircuts to keep your hair looking neat and healthy
  • Eat a healthy diet that's rich in vitamins and minerals to promote hair growth

Now that you know the best hairstyles for face shape male, you can choose the one that suits you best and rock it with confidence. Remember to take care of your hair by following a regular hair care routine, and consult with a professional hairstylist if you're unsure about which hairstyle to choose. Happy styling!
