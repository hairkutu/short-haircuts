How Do I Know If I ll Look Good With Blonde Hair

Written by Frank Apr 16, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Are you considering dyeing your hair blonde but not sure if it will suit you? Blonde hair can be a fun and exciting change, but it's important to make sure it will complement your skin tone and features before taking the plunge. Here's everything you need to know to determine if blonde hair is right for you.

Table of Contents

  • Determining Your Skin Tone
  • Matching Your Face Shape
  • Matching Your Personality
  • Hair Care Tips for Blonde Hair
  • Frequently Asked Questions

Determining Your Skin Tone

The first step in determining if blonde hair will suit you is to figure out your skin tone. This can be done by examining the veins on your wrist. If they appear blue, you have cool undertones, and if they appear green, you have warm undertones. If you can't tell if they're blue or green, you likely have neutral undertones.

Cool skin tones generally look best with platinum or ashy blonde shades, while warm skin tones pair well with honey or golden blonde hues. Neutral skin tones can pull off a wide range of blonde shades.

Matching Your Face Shape

Another important factor to consider is your face shape. Certain blonde hairstyles can complement different face shapes. For example, if you have a round face, a long bob with side-swept bangs can elongate your features. If you have a square face, a layered cut with soft waves can soften your angles.

Additionally, consider your hair texture and thickness. Fine hair may not hold up as well to platinum blonde, while thicker hair can handle more drastic changes.

Matching Your Personality

Your personality can also play a role in determining if blonde hair is right for you. Blonde hair can convey a fun and youthful vibe, but it's important to make sure it matches your personal style and image. If you're naturally reserved or have a more serious demeanor, a drastic blonde change may not feel authentic.

Hair Care Tips for Blonde Hair

If you do decide to go blonde, it's important to take proper care of your hair to maintain its health and color. Here are some tips:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner specifically designed for color-treated hair.
  • Protect your hair from heat styling tools with a heat protectant spray.
  • Avoid over-washing your hair, which can strip it of natural oils and cause color fading.
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends and maintain hair health.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I dye my hair blonde at home?

While it is possible to dye your hair blonde at home, it's recommended to seek the help of a professional hair colorist. Blonde hair requires more precision and expertise to achieve the desired color and avoid damage to the hair.

What if I don't like how I look with blonde hair?

If you've already gone blonde and don't like how it looks, don't panic. Hair color can always be changed or adjusted. Speak with your stylist about options for changing the color or altering the style to better suit your preferences.

How often do I need to touch up my blonde hair?

This will depend on the individual and the specific shade of blonde. Some shades will require more frequent touch-ups than others. Most blonde hair will need to be touched up every 4-6 weeks to maintain the color.

In conclusion, determining if blonde hair is right for you requires considering your skin tone, face shape, and personal style. If you do decide to make the change, proper hair care is essential to maintain the health and vibrancy of your blonde locks. And remember, if you don't like how it looks, it can always be changed. Good luck on your blonde hair journey!
