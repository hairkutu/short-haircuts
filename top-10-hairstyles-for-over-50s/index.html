Top 10 Hairstyles For Over 50S

Written by Luffy Jul 27, 2023 · 12 min read

As we age, our hair changes in texture, thickness, and color. It's important to find the right hairstyle that complements our face shape, hair texture, and lifestyle. In this article, we'll be discussing the top 10 hairstyles for over 50s that are effortless, chic, and easy to maintain. From short bobs to long layers, we've got you covered!

1. The Bob

The bob is a classic hairstyle that never goes out of fashion. It's a perfect hairstyle for over 50s who want to look chic and stylish. The bob can be customized to suit any face shape, hair texture, and lifestyle. It's a low maintenance hairstyle that requires minimal styling.

To achieve the perfect bob, ask your hairstylist to cut your hair to chin length. You can choose to have a blunt cut or add layers for more texture. The bob can be styled straight, wavy, or curly. It's a versatile hairstyle that can be dressed up or down.

2. The Pixie

The pixie is a short hairstyle that's perfect for over 50s who want to embrace their natural hair texture. It's a low maintenance hairstyle that requires minimal styling. The pixie can be customized to suit any face shape, hair texture, and lifestyle.

To achieve the perfect pixie, ask your hairstylist to cut your hair short and choppy. You can choose to have a blunt cut or add layers for more texture. The pixie can be styled straight, wavy, or curly. It's a versatile hairstyle that can be dressed up or down.

3. The Shag

The shag is a layered hairstyle that's perfect for over 50s who want to add more volume and texture to their hair. It's a low maintenance hairstyle that requires minimal styling. The shag can be customized to suit any face shape, hair texture, and lifestyle.

To achieve the perfect shag, ask your hairstylist to cut your hair in layers. You can choose to have a blunt cut or add more layers for more volume. The shag can be styled straight, wavy, or curly. It's a versatile hairstyle that can be dressed up or down.

4. The Lob

The lob is a longer version of the bob that's perfect for over 50s who want to keep their hair long but still maintain a chic and stylish look. It's a low maintenance hairstyle that requires minimal styling. The lob can be customized to suit any face shape, hair texture, and lifestyle.

To achieve the perfect lob, ask your hairstylist to cut your hair to shoulder length. You can choose to have a blunt cut or add layers for more texture. The lob can be styled straight, wavy, or curly. It's a versatile hairstyle that can be dressed up or down.

5. Layered Hair

Layered hair is a hairstyle that's perfect for over 50s who want to add more volume and texture to their hair. It's a low maintenance hairstyle that requires minimal styling. Layered hair can be customized to suit any face shape, hair texture, and lifestyle.

To achieve the perfect layered hair, ask your hairstylist to cut your hair in layers. You can choose to have a blunt cut or add more layers for more volume. Layered hair can be styled straight, wavy, or curly. It's a versatile hairstyle that can be dressed up or down.

6. Bangs

Bangs are a hairstyle that's perfect for over 50s who want to add more dimension to their hair. It's a low maintenance hairstyle that requires minimal styling. Bangs can be customized to suit any face shape, hair texture, and lifestyle.

To achieve the perfect bangs, ask your hairstylist to cut your hair in a fringe. You can choose to have blunt bangs or side-swept bangs. Bangs can be styled straight, wavy, or curly. It's a versatile hairstyle that can be dressed up or down.

7. Updo

An updo is a hairstyle that's perfect for over 50s who want to keep their hair off their face and neck. It's a low maintenance hairstyle that requires minimal styling. An updo can be customized to suit any face shape, hair texture, and lifestyle.

To achieve the perfect updo, ask your hairstylist to create a bun or a twist. You can choose to have a messy updo or a sleek updo. An updo can be styled with accessories such as hairpins and headbands. It's a versatile hairstyle that can be dressed up or down.

8. Curls

Curls are a hairstyle that's perfect for over 50s who want to embrace their natural hair texture. It's a low maintenance hairstyle that requires minimal styling. Curls can be customized to suit any face shape, hair texture, and lifestyle.

To achieve the perfect curls, ask your hairstylist to use a curling iron or hot rollers. You can choose to have loose curls or tight curls. Curls can be styled with accessories such as hairpins and headbands. It's a versatile hairstyle that can be dressed up or down.

9. Waves

Waves are a hairstyle that's perfect for over 50s who want to add more texture to their hair. It's a low maintenance hairstyle that requires minimal styling. Waves can be customized to suit any face shape, hair texture, and lifestyle.

To achieve the perfect waves, ask your hairstylist to use a curling iron or hot rollers. You can choose to have loose waves or beachy waves. Waves can be styled with accessories such as hairpins and headbands. It's a versatile hairstyle that can be dressed up or down.

10. Braids

Braids are a hairstyle that's perfect for over 50s who want to add more dimension to their hair. It's a low maintenance hairstyle that requires minimal styling. Braids can be customized to suit any face shape, hair texture, and lifestyle.

To achieve the perfect braids, ask your hairstylist to braid your hair in different styles. You can choose to have a French braid or a Dutch braid. Braids can be styled with accessories such as hairpins and headbands. It's a versatile hairstyle that can be dressed up or down.

Hair Care Tips for Over 50s

As we age, our hair becomes more fragile and prone to breakage. It's important to take care of our hair to keep it healthy and strong. Here are some hair care tips for over 50s:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to prevent dryness and breakage.
  • Avoid using hot tools such as curling irons and straighteners too often.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to nourish and moisturize your hair.
  • Avoid using harsh chemicals such as hair dyes and relaxers that can damage your hair.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends and breakage.
