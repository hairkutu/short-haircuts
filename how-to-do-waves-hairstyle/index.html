How To Do Waves Hairstyle A Step By Step Guide

Written by Frank Apr 29, 2023 Â· 5 min read

If you're looking to add some texture and movement to your hair, waves are a great option. This style is perfect for those who want a relaxed, beachy vibe. In this article, we'll show you how to do waves hairstyle in easy steps. From the tools you need to the techniques to master, we've got you covered. So, let's get started!

Tools You'll Need

Before we jump into the steps, let's first discuss the tools you'll need:

  • Heat protectant spray
  • Curling iron (preferably one with a larger barrel size)
  • Clips
  • Hairspray

Step-by-Step Guide

Step 1: Prepare Your Hair

Start by washing your hair and applying a heat protectant spray. This will help protect your hair from any heat damage caused by the curling iron. Blow dry your hair until it's completely dry.

Step 2: Divide Your Hair

Next, divide your hair into sections using clips. Start by separating the top section of your hair and clipping it away. Then divide the remaining hair into two sections, left and right.

Step 3: Curl Your Hair

Starting with the left section, take a small section of hair and wrap it around the barrel of the curling iron. Hold for a few seconds and release. Repeat this process until you've curled all the hair in this section. Repeat on the right section.

Step 4: Clip Your Hair

Once you've curled all the hair, clip it up and away from your face. Let it cool for a few minutes.

Step 5: Brush Your Hair

After a few minutes, remove the clips and brush out your hair. This will help create a more natural wave look.

Step 6: Finish with Hairspray

Finally, finish with hairspray to hold the waves in place. Use a light misting of hairspray to avoid creating a stiff, unnatural look.

Tips for Perfect Waves

Here are some tips to help you achieve perfect waves:

  • Use a larger barrel curling iron for looser waves.
  • Alternate curling the hair towards and away from your face for a more natural look.
  • Leave the ends of your hair out of the curling iron for a more relaxed look.
  • Don't forget to use heat protectant spray to avoid damaging your hair.

FAQs

Q: How long will the waves last?

A: The waves should last all day if you use hairspray to hold them in place.

Q: Can I create waves without a curling iron?

A: Yes, you can create waves using a flat iron or even braids. However, a curling iron will provide the most defined waves.

Hair Care Tips

To keep your hair healthy, make sure to use a deep conditioning treatment once a week. Avoid using too much heat on your hair, and always use a heat protectant spray before using any hot tools. Finally, try to avoid using too many hair products, as they can weigh down your hair and make it look greasy.

Now that you know how to do waves hairstyle, it's time to give it a try! With a little practice, you'll be able to master this look and add some texture and movement to your hair.
