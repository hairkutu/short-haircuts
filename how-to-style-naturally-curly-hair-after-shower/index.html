How To Style Naturally Curly Hair After Shower Tips And Tricks

Written by Daniel Mar 15, 2023 · 8 min read

Are you tired of dealing with frizzy, unmanageable curls after every shower? Styling naturally curly hair can be tricky, but with the right techniques and products, you can achieve the perfect look every time. In this article, we’ll share some tips and tricks on how to style naturally curly hair after a shower.

Table of Contents

  1. How to Prep Your Hair
  2. The Best Products for Styling Curly Hair
  3. How to Style Your Hair
  4. Tips for Maintaining Your Curly Hair
  5. FAQs
  6. Hair Care Tips for Naturally Curly Hair

How to Prep Your Hair

The first step to styling your curly hair after a shower is to prep it properly. Start by gently towel-drying your hair, being careful not to rub it vigorously. This can cause frizz and damage to your hair.

Next, apply a leave-in conditioner to your hair while it’s still damp. This will help to detangle your curls and provide extra moisture to prevent frizz. You can also use a wide-tooth comb to gently detangle your hair, starting from the ends and working your way up.

The Best Products for Styling Curly Hair

Choosing the right products for your curly hair can make all the difference in achieving the perfect look. Look for products that are specifically designed for curly hair, such as curl-enhancing creams, gels, and mousses. These products will help to define your curls and provide hold without weighing them down.

One of the best products for styling curly hair after a shower is a diffuser attachment for your hair dryer. This will help to distribute heat evenly and prevent frizz while enhancing your natural curls.

How to Style Your Hair

Now that you’ve prepped your hair and have the right products, it’s time to style your curls. Start by applying a small amount of curl-enhancing cream or mousse to your hair, focusing on the mid-lengths to ends. Avoid applying too much product, as this can weigh down your curls and create a greasy look.

Next, use your diffuser attachment to dry your hair on low heat. Start at the roots and work your way down, scrunching your hair with your hands as you go. This will help to enhance your curls and create volume.

Once your hair is dry, you can use a curling iron to touch up any areas that need more definition. Just be sure to use a heat protectant spray before using any hot tools on your hair.

Tips for Maintaining Your Curly Hair

To keep your curly hair looking its best, it’s important to follow a few simple tips. First, avoid washing your hair too often, as this can strip your hair of its natural oils and cause dryness. Instead, try washing your hair every two to three days.

Secondly, be gentle with your curls. Avoid brushing or combing your hair when it’s dry, as this can cause frizz and breakage. Instead, use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your hair when it’s damp.

Lastly, protect your curls while you sleep. Use a silk or satin pillowcase to prevent friction and minimize frizz. You can also tie your hair up in a loose bun or pineapple on top of your head to preserve your curls overnight.

FAQs

Q: How do I prevent frizz in my curly hair?

A: To prevent frizz in your curly hair, be sure to use a leave-in conditioner, avoid rubbing your hair with a towel, and use a diffuser attachment when drying your hair.

Q: Can I straighten my curly hair?

A: Yes, you can straighten your curly hair using a flat iron. Just be sure to use a heat protectant spray and avoid straightening your hair too often, as this can cause damage.

Hair Care Tips for Naturally Curly Hair

To keep your curly hair healthy and looking its best, it’s important to follow a few simple hair care tips. First, avoid using harsh shampoos that can strip your hair of its natural oils. Instead, use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner that’s specifically designed for curly hair.

Secondly, deep condition your hair once a week to provide extra moisture and prevent breakage. You can also use a hair mask or oil treatment to nourish your curls and boost their shine.

Lastly, protect your hair from the sun and other environmental stressors. Wear a hat or use a UV-protectant spray when you’re outside for extended periods, and avoid using hot tools on your hair too often.

With these tips and tricks, you can style your naturally curly hair after a shower with confidence and achieve the perfect look every time.
