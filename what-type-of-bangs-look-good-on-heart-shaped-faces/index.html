What Type Of Bangs Look Good On Heart Shaped Faces

Written by Christine Mar 25, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Welcome to our hair care blog! Today we will discuss what type of bangs look good on heart shaped faces. If you have a heart shaped face, it means your face is wider at the forehead and temples and narrows down to a point at the chin. Bangs can be a great way to enhance your features and balance out your face shape. Let's dive into the details!

Table of Contents

  1. Description
  2. How to Choose the Right Bangs
  3. Step by Step Guide to Cut Bangs
  4. Tips to Maintain Your Bangs
  5. FAQs
  6. Hair Care for Your Bangs

Description

Heart shaped faces are versatile and can look good with various types of bangs. The goal is to choose bangs that add width to the forehead and temples while softening the pointy chin.

Consider the length and thickness of your hair before deciding on bangs. If you have thick hair, you can go for full bangs that cover your forehead. If you have thin hair, wispy bangs or side-swept bangs can add volume and texture.

How to Choose the Right Bangs

When choosing bangs for a heart shaped face, consider the following:

  • Length: Bangs that hit at or above the eyebrows work well to balance out the face shape. Avoid bangs that are too short or too long.
  • Width: Look for bangs that add width to the forehead and temples. Full bangs or side-swept bangs that start from the crown of the head can achieve this.
  • Texture: Wispy bangs or choppy bangs can soften the pointy chin and add texture to thin hair.

Step by Step Guide to Cut Bangs

If you decide to cut your own bangs, follow these steps:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair.
  2. Section off the hair you want to cut into a triangle shape.
  3. Use sharp scissors to cut the hair straight across. Cut less than you think you need, as you can always go back and trim more.
  4. For side-swept bangs, angle the scissors slightly while cutting to create a diagonal line.
  5. Check the length and trim any stray hairs.
  6. Style your bangs as desired.

Tips to Maintain Your Bangs

Here are some tips to keep your bangs looking fresh:

  • Trim your bangs every 4-6 weeks to avoid split ends and maintain the shape.
  • Blow dry your bangs with a round brush to add volume and shape.
  • Use dry shampoo to avoid oily bangs.
  • Don't touch your bangs too much to avoid greasiness.

FAQs

1. Can heart shaped faces pull off blunt bangs?

Yes, heart shaped faces can pull off blunt bangs. However, it's important to make sure they hit at or above the eyebrows to balance out the face shape.

2. Are side-swept bangs a good option for heart shaped faces?

Yes, side-swept bangs that start from the crown of the head can add width to the forehead and temples, balancing out the face shape.

Hair Care for Your Bangs

Here are some hair care tips to keep your bangs looking healthy and shiny:

  • Wash your bangs every 2-3 days with a gentle shampoo.
  • Condition your bangs with a lightweight conditioner to avoid weighing them down.
  • Avoid using hot tools like flat irons or curling irons on your bangs too often to avoid heat damage.
  • Apply a hair serum or oil to your bangs to add shine and moisture.

That's it for our guide on what type of bangs look good on heart shaped faces! We hope this helped you choose the perfect bangs for your face shape. Remember to maintain your bangs regularly to keep them looking fresh and healthy.
