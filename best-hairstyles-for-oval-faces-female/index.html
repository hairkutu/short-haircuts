Best Hairstyles For Oval Faces Female

Best Hairstyles for Oval Faces Female | Hair Care Tips | 2023

Oval faces are considered one of the most versatile face shapes, as almost all hairstyles look great on them. However, choosing the right hairstyle that flatters your face shape can make a huge difference in your overall appearance. In this article, we will discuss the best hairstyles for oval faces female in 2023, along with some hair care tips to maintain healthy and beautiful hair.

How to Determine Your Face Shape?

The first step to finding the perfect hairstyle is to determine your face shape. If you have an oval face, your face will be longer than it is wide, and your forehead, cheekbones, and jawline will be well-balanced. You can also measure your face to confirm your face shape.

To measure your face, use a tape measure and measure the width of your forehead at the widest point, the distance between your cheekbones, and the length of your face from the hairline to the chin. If your forehead, cheekbones, and jawline are roughly the same width, and your face length is about one and a half times the width, you have an oval face.

Best Hairstyles for Oval Faces

As mentioned earlier, oval faces are versatile, and almost all hairstyles look great on them. However, some hairstyles can enhance your face shape and bring out your best features. Here are some of the best hairstyles for oval faces female:

1. Long Layers

Long layers are perfect for oval faces as they add volume and movement to the hair without overwhelming the face. You can style your hair with loose waves or straighten it for a sleek look.

2. Bob Haircut

A classic bob that falls just below the chin is a perfect choice for oval faces. This haircut accentuates your jawline and gives your face a more defined look. You can also add some layers to the haircut for a more textured look.

3. Side-Swept Bangs

Side-swept bangs are a great way to frame your face and draw attention to your eyes. They look great on oval faces as they soften the forehead and add dimension to the hair.

4. Pixie Cut

If you want to go for a bold and edgy look, a pixie cut is a perfect choice for oval faces. This haircut accentuates your cheekbones and gives your face a more defined look. You can also add some texture to the hairstyle with some layers or choppy ends.

Step-by-Step Guide to Style Your Hair

Now that you know the best hairstyles for oval faces, let's learn how to style them. Here's a step-by-step guide to style your hair:

1. Long Layers

  1. Apply a heat protectant spray to your hair to prevent damage from heat styling tools.
  2. Section your hair and curl it with a curling iron or straighten it with a flat iron.
  3. Run your fingers through your hair to create loose waves.
  4. Finish the look with a hairspray to hold the style in place.

2. Bob Haircut

  1. Apply a styling cream to your hair to add shine and texture.
  2. Blow dry your hair with a round brush to create volume and movement.
  3. Style your hair with a flat iron to create a sleek look.
  4. Finish the look with a hairspray to hold the style in place.

3. Side-Swept Bangs

  1. Section your hair and blow dry it with a round brush to add volume.
  2. Use a flat iron to straighten your hair.
  3. Part your hair to one side and sweep the bangs to the opposite side.
  4. Finish the look with a hairspray to hold the style in place.

4. Pixie Cut

  1. Apply a styling cream to your hair to add texture.
  2. Blow dry your hair with a round brush to add volume.
  3. Style your hair with a flat iron to create a sleek look.
  4. Finish the look with a hairspray to hold the style in place.

Tips to Maintain Healthy Hair

Now that you know how to style your hair, let's learn some tips to maintain healthy hair:

1. Use a Sulfate-Free Shampoo

Sulfate-based shampoos can strip your hair of its natural oils and cause dryness and breakage. Use a sulfate-free shampoo to keep your hair healthy and hydrated.

2. Avoid Heat Styling Tools

Heat styling tools can damage your hair and cause split ends and breakage. Limit your use of heat styling tools and use a heat protectant spray when you do.

3. Trim Your Hair Regularly

Regular trims can help prevent split ends and breakage and keep your hair healthy and strong. Aim to trim your hair every 6-8 weeks.

4. Eat a Healthy Diet

A healthy diet can promote healthy hair growth and prevent hair loss. Eat a balanced diet that includes protein, vitamins, and minerals.

FAQs

1. Can oval faces pull off short hair?

Yes, oval faces can pull off short hair, such as a pixie cut or a bob. These haircuts accentuate the cheekbones and jawline, giving your face a more defined look.

2. What hairstyles should oval faces avoid?

Oval faces can pull off almost all hairstyles, but they should avoid hairstyles that add too much volume to the top of the head, such as a high bun or a top knot. These hairstyles can make your face appear longer.

3. Can oval faces wear bangs?

Yes, oval faces can wear bangs, especially side-swept bangs. Bangs can frame your face and draw attention to your eyes.

Now that you know the best hairstyles for oval faces female and some hair care tips, you can choose the perfect hairstyle that flatters your face shape and maintain healthy and beautiful hair.
