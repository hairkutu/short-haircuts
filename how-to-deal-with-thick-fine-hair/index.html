How To Deal With Thick Fine Hair Tips Tricks And Hair Care

Written by Aprilia Aug 02, 2023 · 9 min read

Are you struggling with thick fine hair? You are not alone. Many people with this hair type often find it challenging to style and maintain their hair. Thick fine hair is characterized by having a lot of hair strands that are thin in texture. The hair strands may be delicate and fragile, making it prone to breakage and damage. However, with the right hair care routine and styling techniques, you can manage your thick fine hair and achieve the perfect look. Here are some tips, tricks, and hair care advice to help you deal with your thick fine hair.

How to Deal with Thick Fine Hair

Thick fine hair requires a different approach than other hair types. Here are some tips and tricks to help you deal with your hair:

1. Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner

Thick fine hair can easily become flat and lifeless, so it's essential to use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner. These products are formulated to add volume to your hair without weighing it down. Look for products that contain natural ingredients like tea tree oil, eucalyptus, and peppermint.

2. Avoid heavy products

Thick fine hair doesn't need heavy products like oils and serums. These products can weigh down your hair and make it look greasy. Instead, opt for lightweight products like mousses, gels, and sprays.

3. Use a wide-tooth comb

Thick fine hair can be fragile and prone to breakage. Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair gently. Start at the ends and work your way up to prevent pulling and breakage.

4. Blow-dry your hair upside down

Blow-drying your hair upside down can add volume and create a natural-looking lift. Use a diffuser attachment on your blow dryer to help distribute the heat evenly throughout your hair.

5. Avoid heat styling

Heat styling tools like flat irons and curling irons can damage your hair and make it look dull and lifeless. If you must use heat styling tools, use a heat protectant spray to minimize damage. Try to limit your use of these tools to once or twice a week.

Hair Care for Thick Fine Hair

Proper hair care is essential for maintaining healthy, beautiful hair. Here are some tips for caring for your thick fine hair:

1. Wash your hair every other day

Washing your hair every other day can help prevent your hair from becoming greasy and flat. Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner that is designed for your hair type.

2. Deep condition once a week

Deep conditioning treatments can help nourish your hair and prevent damage. Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to help keep your hair healthy and shiny.

3. Avoid tight hairstyles

Tight hairstyles like braids and ponytails can pull on your hair and cause breakage. Try to avoid these styles or wear them loosely to prevent damage.

4. Get regular trims

Getting regular trims can help keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends. Aim to get a trim every six to eight weeks.

Tips for Styling Thick Fine Hair

Styling thick fine hair can be a challenge, but there are ways to make it easier. Here are some tips for styling your hair:

1. Use a volumizing mousse

Apply a volumizing mousse to your roots before blow-drying to add volume and lift.

2. Tease your hair

Teasing your hair can add volume and create a fuller look. Use a teasing comb to tease the roots of your hair gently.

3. Use a dry shampoo

Dry shampoo can help absorb excess oil and add volume to your hair. Apply dry shampoo to your roots and massage it into your scalp before brushing it out.

4. Experiment with different styles

Don't be afraid to experiment with different styles to find what works best for your hair type. Try different braids, updos, and ponytails to see what looks best on you.

FAQs about Thick Fine Hair

1. Can thick fine hair be thinning?

Yes, thick fine hair can be prone to thinning due to its fragile nature. It's important to take good care of your hair and avoid damaging it to prevent thinning.

2. How often should I wash my hair if I have thick fine hair?

You should wash your hair every other day if you have thick fine hair. Washing your hair too often can strip it of its natural oils and cause it to become dry and brittle.

3. How can I add volume to my thick fine hair?

You can add volume to your hair by using volumizing products like mousses and sprays, blow-drying your hair upside down, and teasing your hair at the roots.

In conclusion, dealing with thick fine hair requires a different approach than other hair types. By following the tips, tricks, and hair care advice in this article, you can manage your thick fine hair and achieve the perfect look. Remember to take good care of your hair, avoid damaging it, and experiment with different styles to find what works best for you.
