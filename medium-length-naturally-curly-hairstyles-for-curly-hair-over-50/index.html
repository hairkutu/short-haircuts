Medium Length Naturally Curly Hairstyles For Curly Hair Over 50

Written by Petter Jul 17, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Are you over 50 and wondering what hairstyles work best for your curly hair? Look no further than medium length naturally curly hairstyles. Not only are they versatile and easy to maintain, but they also enhance your natural texture and add volume to your locks. In this article, we will provide you with how-to's, step-by-steps, tips, FAQs, and hair care advice for medium length naturally curly hairstyles for curly hair over 50.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How To
  • Step By Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

Medium length naturally curly hairstyles are perfect for women over 50 as they are easy to maintain and flatter your natural texture. These hairstyles provide volume and movement to your hair, making them look youthful and vibrant. With the right cut and styling, you can achieve a variety of looks, from sleek and sophisticated to wild and carefree.

How To

The first step to achieving medium length naturally curly hairstyles is to get the right haircut. Consult with your stylist to find a cut that works best for your hair type and face shape. Ask for layers to add volume and movement to your hair. The length of your hair should fall between your shoulders and collarbone to achieve the medium length look.

Styling Products

Use products that enhance your natural texture and define your curls. Avoid products that contain alcohol, sulfates, and silicones as they can dry out your hair and cause frizz. Opt for products that contain natural ingredients such as aloe vera, coconut oil, and shea butter.

Blow Drying

Blow dry your hair using a diffuser attachment. This will help distribute the heat evenly and reduce frizz. Start by using a low heat setting and gradually increase the heat if needed. Scrunch your hair while blow-drying to enhance your curls.

Straightening

If you want to straighten your hair, use a flat iron with ceramic plates. This will help distribute the heat evenly and reduce damage to your hair. Use a heat protectant spray before straightening your hair to prevent damage.

Step By Step

Here are some step-by-step instructions to achieve medium length naturally curly hairstyles:

  1. Wash your hair with a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner.
  2. Towel dry your hair and apply a leave-in conditioner or curl-defining cream.
  3. Use a diffuser attachment on your blow dryer to dry your hair. Scrunch your hair while blow-drying to enhance your curls.
  4. Once your hair is dry, use a curling iron to define any loose curls.
  5. Apply a small amount of hair oil to add shine and tame any frizz.

Tips

  • Avoid brushing your hair when it's dry as it can cause frizz.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your hair when it's wet.
  • Sleep on a satin pillowcase to prevent frizz and breakage.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.

FAQ

Q: Can I color my hair if I have naturally curly hair?

A: Yes, you can color your hair if you have naturally curly hair. However, it's important to use a dye that is gentle on your hair and doesn't contain harsh chemicals.

Q: How often should I wash my hair?

A: It depends on your hair type and lifestyle. If you have dry hair, you can wash your hair once a week. If you have oily hair, you can wash your hair every other day. If you exercise regularly, you may need to wash your hair more often.

Hair Care

Proper hair care is essential to maintaining healthy, beautiful curls. Here are some hair care tips:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils.
  • Avoid using hot tools on your hair too often as it can cause damage and breakage.
  • Deep condition your hair once a week to keep it hydrated and nourished.
  • Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a UV protectant spray.
  • Stay hydrated and eat a balanced diet to promote healthy hair growth.

With these tips and techniques, you can achieve a variety of medium length naturally curly hairstyles that flatter your natural texture and enhance your features. Experiment with different products and styles to find the look that works best for you. Embrace your curls and rock your natural beauty!
