How To Ask For A Simple Haircut In Relaxed English Language

Written by Bardi Jul 24, 2023 Â· 5 min read

Are you tired of going to the hair salon and not getting the haircut you wanted? Do you feel intimidated by the language used by hairstylists? Fear not, because we have got you covered. In this article, we will teach you how to ask for a simple haircut in relaxed English language.

Table of Content:

  • How to Ask for a Simple Haircut
  • Step by Step Guide
  • Tips for a Successful Haircut
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care After Your Haircut

How to Ask for a Simple Haircut:

Asking for a simple haircut is not as complicated as it may seem. Here are some phrases that you can use to communicate with your hairstylist:

  • "I would like a trim."
  • "Can you cut off an inch or two?"
  • "I want to keep the length but get rid of split ends."
  • "Please just give me a simple straight cut."

Remember to be specific about what you want. If you have a picture or a particular style in mind, bring it along with you to the salon.

Step by Step Guide:

Follow these simple steps to ensure that you get the haircut you want:

  1. Research: Look up the type of haircut that you want and make sure to find a picture to show your hairstylist.
  2. Communicate: Use the phrases mentioned above to communicate with your hairstylist.
  3. Confirm: Double-check with your hairstylist before they start cutting to make sure that they understood what you want.
  4. Be patient: Allow your hairstylist to work their magic and don't be afraid to speak up if you are not satisfied.

Tips for a Successful Haircut:

Here are some tips that can help you have a successful haircut:

  • Choose a reputable salon with experienced hairstylists.
  • Be open to suggestions from your hairstylist.
  • Be honest about your hair history, including any previous color or chemical treatments.
  • Provide clear communication about what you want.
  • Trust your hairstylist and their expertise.

FAQs:

How much should I tip my hairstylist?

Tipping your hairstylist is a personal choice, but it is customary to tip between 15%-20% of the total cost of the service.

What should I do if I am not satisfied with my haircut?

Speak up and communicate with your hairstylist. Most salons offer complimentary fixes within a certain timeframe.

How often should I get a haircut?

It is recommended to get a haircut every 6-8 weeks to maintain healthy hair.

Hair Care After Your Haircut:

After your haircut, it's important to take care of your hair to maintain its health and style. Here are some tips:

  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and harsh weather conditions.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.

Now that you know how to ask for a simple haircut, go out and confidently communicate with your hairstylist. Remember, clear communication and trust are key to a successful haircut.
