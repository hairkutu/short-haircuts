Which Hair Style Will Suit Me A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Frank May 28, 2023 · 10 min read

Are you tired of your current hairstyle and looking to switch things up? Choosing a new hairstyle can be daunting, but don't worry, we are here to help. In this article, we will provide you with a comprehensive guide on which hair style will suit you. We will cover everything from how to determine your face shape, to different haircuts and styles that will flatter your features. Let's dive in!

How to Determine Your Face Shape

Before we get into different hairstyles, it's essential to determine your face shape. Knowing your face shape will help you select a hairstyle that flatters your features and enhances your natural beauty. Here are the four main face shapes:

1. Round Face Shape

If you have a round face shape, your face is widest at the cheeks, and you have a circular shape. To flatter this face shape, opt for hairstyles that create height and elongate the face. Avoid hairstyles that add width to the face, such as blunt bangs or chin-length bobs.

2. Square Face Shape

If you have a square face shape, your face is equal in length and width, and your jawline is angular. To soften this face shape, go for hairstyles that add volume and texture to the crown of the head. Avoid hairstyles that accentuate the jawline, such as blunt bobs or center parts.

3. Heart Face Shape

If you have a heart-shaped face, your forehead is wider than your chin, and you have a sharp chin. To balance this face shape, go for hairstyles that add volume to the chin area and create width at the bottom of the head. Avoid hairstyles that add volume to the crown of the head, such as high ponytails or updos.

4. Oval Face Shape

If you have an oval face shape, your face is longer than it is wide, and your forehead is slightly wider than your chin. Lucky for you, oval face shapes can pull off almost any hairstyle. Play around with different lengths and textures to find the hairstyle that suits you best.

Different Haircuts and Styles to Consider

Now that you know your face shape, it's time to explore different haircuts and styles that will flatter your features. Here are a few options to consider:

1. Bob Haircut

The bob haircut is a timeless classic that's perfect for almost any face shape. It's versatile, low-maintenance, and can be customized to suit your preferences. If you have a round face shape, go for a longer bob that hits just below the chin. If you have a square face shape, go for a textured bob with layers. If you have a heart-shaped face, go for a bob with volume at the bottom. And if you have an oval face shape, you can pretty much pull off any bob style!

2. Pixie Cut

The pixie cut is a bold and daring choice that's perfect for those who want to make a statement. It's great for oval and heart-shaped faces, as it accentuates the cheekbones and jawline. If you have a round or square face shape, opt for a longer pixie cut with side-swept bangs to create a more elongated look.

3. Layered Haircut

A layered haircut is perfect for adding volume and texture to the hair. It works well for all face shapes, but particularly well for square and oval faces. If you have a round face shape, make sure the layers start below the chin to create a slimming effect. If you have a heart-shaped face, go for layers that start at the chin to balance out the forehead.

Tips for Choosing the Right Hairstyle

Choosing the right hairstyle can be overwhelming, but here are a few tips to help you make the right decision:

  • Consider your hair type and texture. Some hairstyles work better for certain hair types, so make sure you choose a style that complements your natural hair.
  • Think about your lifestyle. If you're always on the go, a low-maintenance hairstyle might be a better fit for you.
  • Consult with a hairstylist. A professional hairstylist can help you determine the best hairstyle for your face shape and provide expert advice on how to maintain it.

Frequently Asked Questions

Here are a few frequently asked questions about choosing the right hairstyle:

1. Can I pull off a short haircut?

Short haircuts can be flattering on almost anyone, but it's essential to consider your face shape and hair type before making the chop. Consult with a hairstylist to determine if a short haircut is right for you.

2. How do I know if a certain hairstyle will suit me?

Consider your face shape, hair type, and lifestyle when choosing a hairstyle. Look for inspiration in magazines and online, and consult with a hairstylist for expert advice.

3. How often should I get a haircut?

It depends on your hair type and the style you're going for. Short haircuts require more frequent trims, while longer styles can go longer between cuts. Consult with a hairstylist for a personalized recommendation.

Hair Care Tips

No matter what hairstyle you choose, it's essential to take care of your hair. Here are a few tips to keep your locks looking healthy and shiny:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using hot tools too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Use a heat protectant spray when using hot tools.
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to nourish your hair.

Choosing the right hairstyle can be a game-changer, but it can also be intimidating. We hope this guide has helped you determine which hair style will suit you best. Remember, the most important thing is to choose a style that makes you feel confident and beautiful!
