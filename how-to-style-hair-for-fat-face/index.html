How To Style Hair For Fat Face A Comprehensive Guide

Written by William Aug 05, 2023 Â· 7 min read

If you have a round or chubby face, finding the right hairstyle can be a challenge. However, with the right tips and tricks, you can choose a hairstyle that flatters your face shape and enhances your features. In this guide, we'll show you how to style hair for fat face and provide you with the best haircuts and styles for round faces.

Description

A fat face can be a result of genetics, weight gain, or simply the shape of your face. Whatever the cause, it can be challenging to find a hairstyle that complements your features. The key to styling hair for a fat face is to create the illusion of a slimmer face shape. This can be achieved by choosing the right haircut, parting your hair in the right place, and styling your hair in a way that adds volume to the top of your head.

How to Style Hair for Fat Face

Step 1: Choose the Right Haircut

The right haircut can make all the difference when it comes to styling hair for a fat face. The most flattering haircuts for round faces are those that add height to the top of your head and elongate your face. Some of the best haircuts for round faces include:

  • Long layers
  • Angled bobs
  • Shoulder-length haircuts
  • Pixie cuts with volume on top

When choosing a haircut, avoid blunt cuts that end at your chin or jawline, as these can make your face appear wider. Instead, opt for haircuts that frame your face and add volume to the top of your head.

Step 2: Part Your Hair in the Right Place

The way you part your hair can also make a difference in how your face appears. For a fat face, the best place to part your hair is off-center. This creates the illusion of a slimmer face shape by drawing attention away from the center of your face.

Step 3: Add Volume to the Top of Your Head

Adding volume to the top of your head can also make your face appear slimmer. You can achieve this by blow-drying your hair upside down or using a volumizing mousse or spray. Avoid adding volume to the sides of your hair, as this can make your face appear wider.

Tips for Styling Hair for Fat Face

  • Choose hairstyles that frame your face
  • Avoid blunt cuts that end at your chin or jawline
  • Part your hair off-center
  • Add volume to the top of your head
  • Avoid hairstyles that add volume to the sides of your hair

FAQ

What is the best haircut for a fat face?

The most flattering haircuts for a fat face are those that add height to the top of your head and elongate your face. Long layers, angled bobs, shoulder-length haircuts, and pixie cuts with volume on top are all great options.

What is the best way to part your hair for a fat face?

The best way to part your hair for a fat face is off-center. This creates the illusion of a slimmer face shape by drawing attention away from the center of your face.

Should I avoid adding volume to the sides of my hair?

Yes, adding volume to the sides of your hair can make your face appear wider. Instead, focus on adding volume to the top of your head.

Hair Care for Fat Face

Keeping your hair healthy is essential when it comes to styling hair for a fat face. Regular trims, deep conditioning treatments, and using heat protectant products can help keep your hair looking and feeling its best. Additionally, be sure to use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to prevent damage to your hair.

Now that you know how to style hair for fat face, it's time to try out some new hairstyles and find the perfect look for you. Remember to choose haircuts that frame your face, part your hair off-center, and add volume to the top of your head. With these tips and tricks, you'll be able to create a hairstyle that flatters your features and enhances your natural beauty.
