How To Wear A Short Dress With Leggings

Written by Aprilia Mar 22, 2023 · 6 min read

Are you wondering how to wear a short dress with leggings? Look no further! In this article, we will guide you step by step on how to rock this look with ease. Whether you’re going for a casual or dressy look, leggings can be a great addition to your outfit. So let’s get started!

Description

Wearing a short dress with leggings is a popular trend in fashion. It’s a versatile look that can be worn in different ways. Leggings can add warmth, comfort and style to your outfit. They can also help you transition your summer dresses into fall or winter. You can wear this look for a casual day out or dress it up for a night out with your friends.

How to

Step 1: Choose the right dress

When choosing a dress to wear with leggings, it’s important to consider the length and style of the dress. Short dresses that fall above the knee are perfect for this look. If you’re going for a flowy dress, make sure it’s not too loose or too long. A fitted dress will work best for this look.

Step 2: Pick the right leggings

When it comes to leggings, there are many different styles and materials to choose from. For a casual look, you can opt for cotton leggings. For a dressier look, choose leggings with a leather finish or a shiny texture. Make sure the leggings are not see-through and fit comfortably.

Step 3: Choose the right shoes

The right shoes can make or break this look. For a casual look, you can wear sneakers or flats. For a dressier look, you can wear heels or ankle boots. Make sure the shoes complement the dress and leggings.

Step 4: Accessorize

You can accessorize this look with a belt, scarf, or jewelry. A belt can help define your waist and add structure to the outfit. A scarf can add warmth and color to the outfit. Jewelry can add a touch of elegance to the outfit.

Tips

  • Choose the right color combination. Make sure the colors of the dress, leggings and shoes complement each other.
  • Keep it simple. Don’t overload the outfit with too many accessories.
  • Experiment with different styles. Try different types of dresses and leggings to find the perfect combination for you.
  • Choose the right underwear. Make sure your underwear is not visible through the leggings.

FAQ

Can I wear shorts instead of a dress?

Yes, you can wear shorts with leggings. Follow the same steps as you would with a short dress.

What kind of leggings should I wear?

You can wear leggings made of cotton, leather or other materials. Just make sure they are comfortable and not see-through.

Can I wear this look to work?

It depends on your workplace dress code. If your workplace allows casual dress, then you can wear this look to work.

Hair Care

When wearing a short dress with leggings, your hair can complete the look. You can wear your hair up or down, depending on the occasion. If you’re going for a casual look, you can wear your hair in a messy bun or braid. For a dressier look, you can wear your hair in curls or a sleek ponytail. Use hair products to keep your hair in place and add shine.

Now that you know how to wear a short dress with leggings, it’s time to try it out for yourself. Remember to choose the right dress, leggings, shoes, and accessories. Keep it simple and experiment with different styles. Have fun with it and make it your own!
