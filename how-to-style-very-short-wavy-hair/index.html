How To Style Very Short Wavy Hair A Complete Guide

Are you struggling to style your very short wavy hair? Do you often find yourself resorting to the same old hairstyle every day? Fret not, we've got you covered! Here's a complete guide on how to style very short wavy hair, along with tips, FAQs, and hair care advice.

How to Style Very Short Wavy Hair

The first step to styling very short wavy hair is to embrace your natural texture. Short haircuts can be tricky to style, but waves can add volume and movement to your hair. Here are some popular hairstyles for very short wavy hair:

  • Pixie cut: This is a classic hairstyle that works well for wavy hair. Keep the length short and add layers to add texture.
  • Buzz cut: This is a bold and daring hairstyle that can look great on wavy hair. Keep the sides and back short and leave more length on top.
  • Cropped bob: This is a great hairstyle for wavy hair that is easy to maintain. Keep the length just above the chin and add layers for texture.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide on how to style very short wavy hair:

  1. Start by washing your hair with a shampoo and conditioner that is designed for wavy hair. This will help to enhance your natural texture.
  2. Blow dry your hair using a diffuser attachment. This will help to define your waves and add volume to your hair.
  3. Apply a small amount of styling product to your hair, such as a hair wax or pomade. This will help to hold your style in place.
  4. Use your fingers to style your hair into your desired shape. You can create a messy, textured look or a more polished and sleek style.
  5. Finish with a light hairspray to hold your style in place.

Tips for Styling Very Short Wavy Hair

Here are some tips to help you style your very short wavy hair:

  • Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your hair when it's wet. This will help to prevent breakage and damage.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as this can damage your natural waves.
  • Experiment with different styling products to find the one that works best for your hair. Some people prefer a matte finish, while others prefer a shiny finish.
  • Consider adding highlights or lowlights to your hair to add dimension and depth.

FAQs

Here are some commonly asked questions about styling very short wavy hair:

Q: Can I straighten my very short wavy hair?

A: Yes, you can straighten your hair using a flat iron or hair straightener. However, be sure to use a heat protectant spray to prevent damage.

Q: How often should I wash my very short wavy hair?

A: It depends on your hair type and lifestyle. Some people may need to wash their hair every day, while others can go a few days between washes. Use your judgement and listen to your hair.

Q: Can I dye my very short wavy hair?

A: Yes, you can dye your hair. However, be sure to use a gentle, ammonia-free hair dye to prevent damage to your hair.

Hair Care Tips for Very Short Wavy Hair

Here are some hair care tips to keep your very short wavy hair healthy and strong:

  • Use a shampoo and conditioner that is designed for wavy hair.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and chlorine by wearing a hat or swim cap.

With these tips and tricks, you can style your very short wavy hair with confidence and ease. Embrace your natural texture and have fun experimenting with different hairstyles!
