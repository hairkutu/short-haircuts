How To Make Easy Hairstyles For Short Hair In 2023

Written by Petter Aug 12, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Short hair can be a blessing and a curse. It's easy to maintain, but sometimes styling it can be a challenge. However, there are plenty of easy hairstyles for short hair that you can do at home. In this post, we'll go over some simple styles and tips to make your short hair look fabulous.

Table of Contents

  • How to
  • Step by Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

How to

The first step to styling your short hair is to make sure it's clean and dry. Use a good quality shampoo and conditioner that suits your hair type, and towel-dry it before you start styling. If you have curly or wavy hair, you can use a diffuser attachment on your blow dryer to enhance your natural texture.

Next, apply a styling product that suits your hair type and the style you want to achieve. For example, if you want to add volume, use a volumizing mousse. If you want to define your curls, use a curl-enhancing cream. If you want to create a sleek look, use a smoothing serum.

Step by Step

Here are some easy hairstyles for short hair that you can try:

1. Messy Pixie

Apply a texturizing spray to your hair and use your fingers to create a messy, tousled look. You can also use a flat iron to add some waves or curls.

2. Half-Up Twist

Take a small section of hair from each side of your head and twist them towards the back. Pin them in place with bobby pins or a small hair clip.

3. Side-Swept Bangs

If you have bangs, sweep them to one side and secure them with a bobby pin. This will give you a chic, asymmetrical look.

4. Braided Crown

Part your hair down the middle and braid each side. Cross the braids over the top of your head and pin them in place to create a crown-like effect.

Tips

Here are some tips to make your short hair look its best:

  • Use a heat protectant spray before using hot tools like flat irons or curling irons.
  • Experiment with different styling products to find what works best for your hair.
  • Don't be afraid to try new hairstyles! You might be surprised at how good you look with a different style.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about styling short hair:

Q: Can I still use hair accessories with short hair?

A: Yes! You can use bobby pins, hair clips, headbands, and other accessories to add some flair to your short hair.

Q: Can I still get a haircut if I have short hair?

A: Absolutely! Short hair needs to be trimmed regularly to maintain its shape and style.

Hair Care

Proper hair care is essential for healthy-looking hair. Here are some tips to keep your short hair looking great:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a good quality shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using hot tools like flat irons or curling irons too often.
  • Use a leave-in conditioner or hair mask once a week to keep your hair hydrated.
  • Avoid using harsh chemicals or dyes on your hair, as they can damage your hair and scalp.

With these easy hairstyles for short hair and some basic hair care tips, you can keep your short hair looking fabulous all year round.
