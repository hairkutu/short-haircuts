How To Cut My Own Hair With Electric Clippers

Written by Oliver Mar 24, 2023 Â· 7 min read

If you're looking for a way to save money and time, cutting your own hair with electric clippers is a great option. With the right tools and techniques, you can achieve a professional-looking haircut from the comfort of your own home. In this article, we'll go over everything you need to know about how to cut your own hair with electric clippers.

Description

Cutting your own hair can be intimidating, but with a little practice and some guidance, you can become a pro. Electric clippers are a great tool to use because they are easy to handle and can cut hair quickly and efficiently. Whether you're looking to trim your ends or give yourself a full haircut, electric clippers can help you achieve the look you want.

How To

Before you begin, make sure you have all the necessary tools. You will need electric clippers, a comb, scissors, and a mirror. You may also want to have a towel or cape to protect your clothes from hair clippings.

Step 1: Choose the Right Clipper

When selecting an electric clipper, look for one with adjustable blades and a variety of guards. This will allow you to customize your haircut and achieve the desired length. Make sure the clipper is charged and oiled before use.

Step 2: Wash and Dry Your Hair

Before you begin cutting, wash and dry your hair. This will make it easier to cut and ensure a more even result.

Step 3: Choose Your Style

Decide on the style you want before you begin cutting. Look up pictures online for inspiration or choose a style you've had before.

Step 4: Start with the Guards

Begin by attaching the appropriate guard to the clipper. Start with the longest guard and work your way down to achieve the desired length.

Step 5: Cut Your Hair

Start cutting your hair, moving the clipper against the direction of hair growth. Use the comb to lift the hair so you can cut it evenly.

Step 6: Blend the Edges

Once you've cut most of your hair, remove the guard and use the clipper without a guard to clean up any stray hairs around the edges. Use a comb to blend the edges for a natural look.

Step 7: Finish with Scissors

Use scissors to trim any longer hairs and blend any uneven areas.

Tips

  • Start with a longer guard and work your way down to achieve the desired length.
  • Use a comb to lift the hair so you can cut it evenly.
  • Cut in small sections to avoid mistakes.
  • Use scissors to blend any uneven areas.
  • Watch tutorials online for additional guidance.

FAQ

Can I cut my hair with regular scissors?

While regular scissors can be used to cut hair, they may not give you the desired result. It is recommended to use electric clippers with guards to achieve an even and professional-looking cut.

Can I cut my own hair if I have curly hair?

Cutting curly hair can be more challenging, but it is possible. Make sure you use a guard that is appropriate for your hair length and cut in small sections to avoid mistakes.

Hair Care

After cutting your hair, it's important to take care of it. Make sure to wash and condition your hair regularly and use products that are appropriate for your hair type. Avoid using hot tools too often and protect your hair from the sun and harsh weather conditions.

Cutting your own hair can be a great way to save money and time. With the right tools and techniques, you can achieve a professional-looking cut from the comfort of your own home. Remember to take your time, start with a longer guard, and use scissors to blend any uneven areas. Happy cutting!
