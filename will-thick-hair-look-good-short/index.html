Will Thick Hair Look Good Short The Ultimate Guide To Short Hair For Thick Hair

Written by Eveline Jun 07, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Are you considering chopping off your luscious locks but wondering if it will look good with your thick hair? Rest assured, thick hair can look amazing in a short hairstyle! In this article, we will explore the various ways you can style your thick hair to look great in a short haircut.

Description

Thick hair can be a blessing and a curse. While it offers volume and thickness, it can also be challenging to manage and style. However, with the right cut and styling, thick hair can look stunning in a short haircut. Short haircuts are versatile and can suit any face shape and hair texture, including thick hair.

How to Cut Thick Hair Short

When cutting thick hair short, it is essential to consider the hair's texture and density. A layered cut is perfect for thick hair as it provides movement and volume without adding weight. A bob or a lob is also a great choice for thick hair as it adds structure and shape without making the hair look bulky.

Step by Step Guide to Styling Short Hair for Thick Hair

Step 1: Wash and Condition Your Hair

Start by washing and conditioning your hair with products that are suitable for thick hair. Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to add body and volume to your hair.

Step 2: Towel Dry Your Hair

Gently towel dry your hair to remove excess water. Avoid rubbing your hair vigorously as it can cause damage and frizz.

Step 3: Apply a Heat Protectant

Apply a heat protectant to your hair to protect it from heat damage. Thick hair can take longer to dry, so using a heat protectant is crucial to prevent damage.

Step 4: Blow Dry Your Hair

Blow-dry your hair using a round brush to add volume and shape. Start at the roots and work your way to the ends, lifting the hair as you go.

Step 5: Style Your Hair

Once your hair is dry, style it using a flat iron or curling iron. You can create loose waves or curls to add texture and dimension to your hair.

Tips for Maintaining Short Hair for Thick Hair

Short hair for thick hair requires regular maintenance to keep it looking healthy and stylish. Here are some tips for maintaining short hair for thick hair:

  • Trim your hair every six to eight weeks to keep the ends from splitting.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair moisturized.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair as it can cause damage and frizz.
  • Invest in a good quality hairbrush to detangle your hair without causing breakage.
  • Use a hair serum to add shine and smoothness to your hair.

FAQ

What short haircuts are best for thick hair?

The best short haircuts for thick hair are layered cuts, bobs, and lobs. These cuts add structure and shape to thick hair without making it look bulky.

Can I color my short hair if I have thick hair?

Yes, you can color your short hair if you have thick hair. However, it is essential to use products that are gentle on your hair to prevent damage and breakage.

How often should I wash my short hair if I have thick hair?

You should wash your short hair every two to three days if you have thick hair. Washing your hair too often can strip it of its natural oils, making it dry and brittle.

Hair Care for Thick Hair

Thick hair requires special care to keep it healthy and strong. Here are some tips for caring for thick hair:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to prevent dryness and breakage.
  • Avoid using hot water on your hair as it can strip it of its natural oils.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb or brush to detangle your hair without causing breakage.
  • Avoid using too many styling products on your hair as it can weigh it down and make it look greasy.
  • Eat a healthy diet rich in protein, vitamins, and minerals to promote healthy hair growth.

In conclusion, thick hair can look amazing in a short haircut. With the right cut and styling, you can transform your thick hair into a stylish and chic hairstyle. Follow our tips and tricks for maintaining short hair for thick hair, and you'll have a head-turning hairstyle that will make heads turn!
