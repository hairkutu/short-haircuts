How to Get Thicker Curly Hair

If you have naturally curly hair, you may have noticed that it can sometimes look limp and lifeless. Fortunately, there are several things you can do to help give your curls more volume and thickness. In this article, we'll go through some tips and tricks for getting thicker and more voluminous curly hair.

Table of Contents

  1. How to Get Thicker Curly Hair
  2. Step-by-Step Guide
  3. Tips for Thicker Curly Hair
  4. FAQ
  5. Hair Care for Thick Curly Hair

How to Get Thicker Curly Hair

The first step to getting thicker curly hair is to make sure you're using the right products for your hair type. Look for shampoos, conditioners, and styling products that are specifically formulated for curly hair. These products will help to enhance your natural curl pattern and give your hair more volume.

1. Use a Clarifying Shampoo

Over time, hair products and natural oils can build up on your hair, weighing it down and making it look flat. To combat this, use a clarifying shampoo once a week to remove any buildup and give your hair a fresh start.

2. Condition Your Hair Properly

When conditioning your hair, focus on the ends and mid-lengths rather than the scalp. This will help to keep your hair hydrated without weighing it down. Also, try using a deep conditioning treatment once a week to give your hair an extra boost of moisture.

3. Avoid Using Heat Styling Tools

Heat styling tools like flat irons and curling irons can damage your hair and cause it to look flat and lifeless. Instead, embrace your natural curls and try air-drying your hair or using a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer to enhance your curls.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide to getting thicker curly hair:

Step 1: Shampoo your hair.

Use a clarifying shampoo once a week to remove buildup from your hair.

Step 2: Condition your hair.

Apply conditioner to the mid-lengths and ends of your hair and leave it on for a few minutes before rinsing it out.

Step 3: Apply a styling product.

Apply a styling product like mousse or gel to your hair while it's still wet to enhance your natural curl pattern and give your hair more volume.

Step 4: Air-dry your hair or use a diffuser attachment.

Instead of using heat styling tools, try air-drying your hair or using a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer to enhance your curls.

Tips for Thicker Curly Hair

Here are some additional tips for getting thicker curly hair:

1. Get Regular Haircuts

Regular haircuts can help to remove any split ends and keep your hair looking healthy and full.

2. Use a Wide-Tooth Comb

When detangling your hair, use a wide-tooth comb to avoid pulling and damaging your curls.

3. Sleep on a Silk Pillowcase

Silk pillowcases can help to prevent frizz and keep your curls looking smooth and defined.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about getting thicker curly hair:

Q: Can I still use heat styling tools?

A: While it's best to avoid heat styling tools as much as possible, you can still use them occasionally. Just be sure to use a heat protectant spray and keep the heat setting low.

Q: How often should I wash my hair?

A: This varies depending on your hair type and personal preference. Some people with curly hair prefer to wash their hair once a week, while others may need to wash it more often.

Hair Care for Thick Curly Hair

Here are some additional tips for taking care of your thick curly hair:

1. Avoid Brushing Your Hair

Brushing your hair can cause it to become frizzy and lose its curl pattern. Instead, use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair.

2. Use a Leave-In Conditioner

A leave-in conditioner can help to keep your hair hydrated and prevent frizz.

3. Protect Your Hair from the Sun

UV rays from the sun can damage your hair and cause it to become dry and brittle. Wear a hat or use a hair product with SPF to protect your hair from the sun.

By following these tips and tricks, you can help to give your curly hair more volume and thickness. Remember to be patient and consistent with your hair care routine, and you'll be rewarded with beautiful, healthy curls!
