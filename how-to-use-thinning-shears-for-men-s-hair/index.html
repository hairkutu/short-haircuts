How To Use Thinning Shears For Men s Hair

Written by Petter May 26, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Thinning shears are a popular tool for men who want to achieve a textured, layered haircut without removing too much bulk. In this article, we will cover the basics of how to use thinning shears for men's hair, including tips and tricks for achieving the perfect style.

Table of Contents

  2. How to Use Thinning Shears for Men's Hair
  3. Step by Step Guide
  4. Tips and Tricks
  5. FAQs
  6. Hair Care

Description

Thinning shears are a type of scissors that have teeth on one blade and a smooth edge on the other. They are used to remove bulk from the hair without changing the overall length. Thinning shears are great for creating texture and movement in men's hair, and they are especially useful for those with thick, heavy hair.

How to Use Thinning Shears for Men's Hair

Before using thinning shears, it's important to have a clear idea of the style you want to achieve. Thinning shears are not recommended for cutting hair from scratch, so it's best to have a base haircut before using them.

To use thinning shears, follow these steps:

Step 1: Section the Hair

Start by sectioning the hair you want to thin. Use clips to separate the hair into manageable sections, then begin with the bottom layer.

Step 2: Hold the Shears at an Angle

Hold the thinning shears at a 45-degree angle to the hair. This will help to remove bulk from the hair without creating choppy, uneven layers.

Step 3: Make Small Cuts

Take small sections of hair and make small, gentle cuts with the thinning shears. Start at the ends of the hair and work your way up, taking care not to remove too much hair at once.

Step 4: Blend the Hair

After thinning each section, use a comb to blend the hair and check for any unevenness. Continue to thin the hair in small sections until you achieve your desired style.

Tips and Tricks

Here are some tips and tricks to help you achieve the perfect style with thinning shears:

  • Use thinning shears sparingly - it's easy to remove too much hair and create an uneven look.
  • Always start with a small amount of hair and work your way up.
  • Use a comb to blend the hair after each section to ensure evenness.
  • Work slowly and carefully to avoid mistakes.

FAQs

Here are some common questions about using thinning shears for men's hair:

Q: Can thinning shears be used on all hair types?

A: Thinning shears are best suited for thick, heavy hair. If you have fine or thinning hair, it's best to avoid using thinning shears as they can create an uneven look.

Q: How often should I use thinning shears?

A: Thinning shears should be used sparingly - no more than once every 6-8 weeks. Overuse of thinning shears can result in damaged, frizzy hair.

Hair Care

After using thinning shears, it's important to take care of your hair to keep it looking healthy and vibrant. Here are some tips for men's hair care:

  • Wash your hair with a gentle shampoo and conditioner every 2-3 days.
  • Avoid using hot tools like hairdryers and straighteners as they can damage the hair.
  • Use a leave-in conditioner or hair oil to keep the hair moisturized and healthy.
  • Trim your hair every 6-8 weeks to keep it looking neat and tidy.

By following these tips and tricks, you can achieve the perfect style with thinning shears and keep your hair looking healthy and vibrant.
