Wedding Hairstyles For Shoulder Length Hair A Complete Guide

Written by Frank Apr 09, 2023 · 7 min read

Are you a bride-to-be with shoulder length hair wondering how to style it for your big day? Look no further! In this article, we will cover everything you need to know about wedding hairstyles for shoulder length hair. From updos to half-up half-down styles, we've got you covered. Let's dive in!

Description

Shoulder length hair is the perfect length for a wedding hairstyle. It's long enough to create intricate styles, but short enough to keep you cool and comfortable throughout the day. However, with so many options to choose from, it can be overwhelming to decide which hairstyle to go for. That's where we come in. In this article, we will give you inspiration and tips for wedding hairstyles for shoulder length hair.

How to

Before we dive into specific hairstyles, let's go over some general tips for styling shoulder length hair for a wedding:

Tip 1: Consider Your Dress

When choosing a hairstyle, consider your wedding dress. If your dress has intricate details on the back, you may want to opt for an updo to showcase the details. Alternatively, if your dress has a high neckline or intricate details on the front, you may want to opt for a half-up half-down style to showcase those details.

Tip 2: Think About the Weather

The weather on your wedding day can affect your hairstyle. If it's going to be hot and humid, you may want to opt for an updo to keep your hair off your neck and prevent frizz. Alternatively, if it's going to be cold and dry, you may want to opt for a style that keeps your hair down to keep you warm.

Step by Step

Updo

Step 1: Start by curling your hair with a curling iron or wand. This will give your hair texture and make it easier to style.

Step 2: Pull your hair back into a low ponytail and secure it with a hair tie.

Step 3: Twist the ponytail around itself to create a bun and secure it with bobby pins.

Step 4: Pull out a few strands of hair around your face to frame it.

Half-Up Half-Down

Step 1: Start by curling your hair with a curling iron or wand. This will give your hair texture and make it easier to style.

Step 2: Take a small section of hair from each side of your head and pull it back, securing it with a clear elastic band.

Step 3: Tease the crown of your head for extra volume.

Step 4: Pull out a few strands of hair around your face to frame it.

Tips

Tip 1: Use Hair Accessories

Hair accessories can add an extra touch of elegance to your wedding hairstyle. Consider adding a flower crown, hairpins, or a veil to your hairstyle.

Tip 2: Don't Overdo It

While you want your hair to look stunning on your wedding day, you don't want to overdo it. Keep your hairstyle simple and elegant to avoid looking too busy.

FAQ

Q: Can I do my own wedding hairstyle?

A: Yes, you can do your own wedding hairstyle. However, we recommend practicing beforehand to ensure you get the desired look.

Q: How long before my wedding should I get my hair done?

A: We recommend getting your hair done the day of your wedding to ensure it looks fresh and holds throughout the day.

Hair Care

Leading up to your wedding day, make sure to take care of your hair. This includes using a deep conditioning treatment once a week, avoiding heat styling as much as possible, and getting regular trims to prevent split ends.

There you have it – a complete guide to wedding hairstyles for shoulder length hair. Whether you opt for an updo or a half-up half-down style, make sure to choose a hairstyle that makes you feel confident and beautiful on your big day. Good luck!
