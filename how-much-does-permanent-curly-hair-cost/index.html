How Much Does Permanent Curly Hair Cost

Written by Daniel Jul 09, 2023 · 6 min read

If you’re considering getting permanent curly hair, one of the first things on your mind is probably the cost. In this guide, we will break down everything you need to know about how much permanent curly hair costs, including the different factors that can influence the price.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to
  • Step by step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

Permanent curly hair, also known as a perm, is a chemical treatment that changes the structure of your hair. This treatment is designed to create permanent curls or waves, which can last for several months. The cost of a perm can vary depending on several factors, such as the length and thickness of your hair, the type of perm you want, and the salon you go to.

How to

The process of getting a perm typically involves several steps. First, your stylist will apply a solution to your hair that breaks down the bonds that give your hair its shape. They will then wrap your hair around perm rods or rollers and apply another solution that reforms the bonds in a new shape. After this, your hair will be rinsed, and a neutralizer will be applied to set the new shape. The entire process can take several hours.

Step by step

Here are the typical steps involved in getting a perm:

  1. Consultation with your stylist to discuss your desired outcome and the health of your hair
  2. Cleanse your hair to remove any dirt or oil
  3. Apply the first solution to break down hair bonds
  4. Wrap hair around perm rods or rollers
  5. Apply the second solution to reform bonds in new shape
  6. Rinse hair thoroughly
  7. Apply neutralizer to set new shape
  8. Rinse again and apply conditioner
  9. Style your new curls

Tips

Here are a few tips to keep in mind when getting a perm:

  • Choose a stylist who has experience with perms
  • Be honest with your stylist about your hair history and any previous chemical treatments
  • Consider getting a trim before your perm to remove any split ends
  • Be prepared to spend several hours at the salon
  • Follow your stylist's aftercare instructions to maintain your new curls

FAQ

How much does a perm cost?

The cost of a perm can vary depending on several factors, such as the length and thickness of your hair, the type of perm you want, and the salon you go to. On average, a perm can cost anywhere from $75 to $250.

How long does a perm last?

A perm can last for several months, but the exact length of time can vary depending on how well you take care of your hair and the type of perm you get.

Can you color your hair after getting a perm?

It is generally recommended that you wait at least two weeks after getting a perm before coloring your hair. This will give your hair time to recover from the chemical treatment and reduce the risk of damage.

Hair Care

Proper hair care is essential for maintaining your new curls after getting a perm. Here are a few tips:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner designed for curly hair
  • Avoid using heat styling tools, such as flat irons and curling irons, as much as possible
  • Use a diffuser when blow drying your hair to enhance your curls
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it is dry, as this can cause frizz
  • Consider using a leave-in conditioner or styling product to enhance your curls

In conclusion, getting a perm can be a great way to add some texture and volume to your hair. While the cost can vary, it is important to choose a skilled stylist and follow proper aftercare to maintain your new curls.
