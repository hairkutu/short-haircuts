How To Cut Hair In Long Layers Step By Step

Written by Bardi Jul 06, 2023 · 7 min read

In this year 2023, long layers are still a popular hairstyle. Layers give your hair movement, texture, and volume. Cutting long layers can be intimidating, but with the right tools and techniques, it can be done at home. In this blog post, we will guide you on how to cut hair in long layers step by step while giving you some tips and tricks to make it easier.

Table of Contents

  1. Tools You Will Need
  2. Preparation
  3. Sectioning Your Hair
  4. Cutting Your Hair
  5. Finishing Touches
  6. Tips and Tricks
  7. FAQ
  8. Hair Care

Tools You Will Need

Before we start, let's gather the tools you will need:

  • Sharp hair cutting scissors
  • Comb
  • Hair clips
  • Spray bottle filled with water
  • Flat iron or curling iron (optional)

Preparation

Start with clean dry hair. Brush your hair and remove any tangles before you start. It's best to cut your hair when it's dry, so you can see the length and shape of the layers better. If you want to cut your hair wet, make sure to cut it longer than what you want since hair shrinks when it dries.

Sectioning Your Hair

Divide your hair into four sections. First, create a part down the middle of your head, dividing it into two sections. Then, create another part from the top of your left ear to the top of your right ear, dividing your hair into four sections. Use hair clips to secure each section.

Cutting Your Hair

Start with the back section of your hair. Take a small section of hair, about 1 inch wide, from the bottom of the section. Hold the section between your fingers and point cut the hair, which means cutting the hair vertically instead of horizontally. This creates a softer edge and blends the layers.

Continue with the rest of the back section, taking small sections and point cutting until you reach the top of the section. Make sure to cut the hair evenly and at the same angle. Repeat with the other three sections of your hair.

Finishing Touches

Once you have cut all the sections, remove the hair clips and shake your hair to see the layers. If you want to add more texture, you can use a flat iron or curling iron to create waves or curls. You can also trim any uneven ends or long pieces that you missed.

Tips and Tricks

  • Always cut less than you think you need. You can always cut more, but you can't add hair back.
  • Use a mirror or ask a friend to help you see the back of your head.
  • Don't cut your hair too short. Aim for a length that falls between your collarbone and chest.
  • If you have thick hair, consider thinning it out with scissors or a razor before cutting long layers.

FAQ

Q: Can I cut my hair in long layers if I have curly hair?

A: Yes, you can. Just remember that curly hair shrinks when it dries, so cut your hair longer than what you want.

Q: How often should I cut my hair in long layers?

A: It depends on how fast your hair grows and how much you want to maintain the length and shape of the layers. You can cut your hair in long layers every 3-6 months.

Hair Care

To keep your long layers looking healthy and shiny, make sure to take care of your hair. Use a shampoo and conditioner that suits your hair type and avoid using too much heat on your hair. Use a heat protectant spray before using a flat iron or curling iron. Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends and breakage.

Now that you know how to cut hair in long layers step by step, you can save money and time by doing it at home. Remember to be patient and take your time. With practice, you will become a pro at cutting long layers.
