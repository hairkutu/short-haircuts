Guys Hair Short Sides Long Top The Ultimate Guide

Written by Eveline Aug 16, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Are you looking for a new hairstyle? Look no further than the popular "guys hair short sides long top" look. This style is easy to maintain and can be customized to fit your personal taste. In this guide, we will cover everything you need to know about this hairstyle, including how to achieve it, tips for maintaining it, and answers to frequently asked questions.

Description

The guys hair short sides long top look is exactly what it sounds like - short hair on the sides and longer hair on the top. This style can be worn in many different ways, including slicked back, messy, or with a side part. It is a versatile look that can be dressed up or down, making it perfect for any occasion.

How to Achieve the Look

1. Start by washing your hair with a high-quality shampoo and conditioner. This will help to remove any buildup and ensure that your hair is clean and healthy.

2. Towel dry your hair until it is damp but not dripping wet.

3. Use a comb to create a part on one side of your head, if desired.

4. Use clippers or scissors to trim the hair on the sides of your head. The length will depend on your personal preference, but generally, the hair should be no longer than an inch on the sides.

5. Use scissors to cut the hair on the top of your head to your desired length. This can be anywhere from two to six inches, depending on your preference.

6. Style your hair using your preferred method. This can be done with a comb, your fingers, or a hairdryer.

Step by Step

1. Wash your hair with shampoo and conditioner.

2. Towel dry your hair until it is damp.

3. Use a comb to create a part, if desired.

4. Use clippers or scissors to trim the hair on the sides of your head.

5. Use scissors to cut the hair on the top of your head to your desired length.

6. Style your hair using your preferred method.

Tips for Maintaining the Look

1. Use a high-quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy.

2. Trim the hair on the sides of your head regularly to maintain the style.

3. Consider using a hair product, such as gel or mousse, to help hold the style in place.

4. Experiment with different styling methods to find the one that works best for you.

FAQ

Q: Is the guys hair short sides long top look easy to maintain?

A: Yes, this style is easy to maintain with regular trims and the use of high-quality hair products.

Q: Can this style be worn by all hair types?

A: Yes, this style can be worn by all hair types, including straight, wavy, and curly hair.

Q: How often should I trim the hair on the sides of my head?

A: It is recommended that you trim the hair on the sides of your head every 2-4 weeks to maintain the style.

Hair Care

In addition to regular trims and the use of high-quality hair products, it is important to take care of your hair to maintain the health and appearance of your style. This includes:

- Avoiding excessive heat styling

- Using a heat protectant spray before using hot tools

- Avoiding tight hairstyles that can pull on the hair

- Eating a healthy diet that includes plenty of vitamins and minerals

By following these tips and taking care of your hair, you can enjoy the guys hair short sides long top look for years to come.
