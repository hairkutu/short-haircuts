What Hair Color Compliments My Skin Tone

Written by Aprilia Jun 05, 2023 · 8 min read

Choosing the right hair color can be a daunting task, especially if you’re not sure what color will complement your skin tone. The right hair color can enhance your natural beauty and make you look and feel your best. In this article, we’ll discuss how to determine your skin tone and which hair colors will work best for you.

Table of Contents

  • Determining Your Skin Tone
  • Hair Colors for Warm Skin Tones
  • Hair Colors for Cool Skin Tones
  • Hair Colors for Neutral Skin Tones
  • Tips for Choosing the Right Hair Color
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care Tips for Colored Hair

Determining Your Skin Tone

The first step in choosing the right hair color is to determine your skin tone. There are three main categories of skin tones: warm, cool, and neutral. Here’s how to tell which category you fall into:

  • Warm skin tones: Your skin has a yellow, peachy, or golden undertone. You may also have freckles or a warm, golden tan.
  • Cool skin tones: Your skin has a pink, red, or bluish undertone. You may also have blue or green eyes.
  • Neutral skin tones: Your skin has a mix of warm and cool undertones. Your veins may appear blue-green or a neutral color.

Once you’ve determined your skin tone, you can start looking at hair colors that will complement it.

Hair Colors for Warm Skin Tones

If you have a warm skin tone, you’ll want to look for hair colors that have warm undertones, such as golden blonde, honey blonde, copper, or auburn. Avoid cool-toned colors, such as platinum blonde or ash brown, as they can make your skin look washed out.

Hair Colors for Cool Skin Tones

If you have a cool skin tone, you’ll want to look for hair colors with cool undertones, such as ash blonde, platinum blonde, or dark brown. Avoid warm-toned colors, such as golden blonde or copper, as they can clash with your skin tone.

Hair Colors for Neutral Skin Tones

If you have a neutral skin tone, you’re lucky – you can pull off a wide range of hair colors! You can experiment with warm and cool-toned colors, such as caramel highlights or auburn ombre.

Tips for Choosing the Right Hair Color

Here are some additional tips to keep in mind when choosing a hair color:

  • Consider your eye color: Your eye color can also play a role in which hair color will look best on you. For example, if you have blue eyes, you might want to try a cool-toned hair color to enhance your eye color.
  • Go gradual: If you’re not ready to make a drastic change, try a gradual transition, such as adding highlights or lowlights to your natural hair color.
  • Consult with a professional: If you’re not sure which hair color will look best on you, consult with a professional stylist. They can help you choose a color that will complement your skin tone and enhance your natural beauty.

FAQs

Q: Can I dye my hair a color that doesn’t complement my skin tone?

A: Of course! At the end of the day, you should choose a hair color that makes you feel confident and beautiful. However, keep in mind that certain hair colors may clash with your skin tone and make you look washed out or unbalanced.

Q: How often should I touch up my hair color?

A: It depends on the type of color and how fast your hair grows. Generally, you should touch up your roots every 4-6 weeks to keep your color looking fresh and vibrant.

Q: Can I dye my hair at home?

A: Yes, but keep in mind that at-home hair dye kits can be tricky to use and may not give you the results you’re looking for. If you’re not confident in your ability to dye your hair at home, it’s best to consult with a professional stylist.

Hair Care Tips for Colored Hair

Once you’ve found the perfect hair color, it’s important to take care of your hair to keep it looking healthy and vibrant. Here are some tips:

  • Use a color-safe shampoo and conditioner to protect your hair color from fading.
  • Avoid washing your hair every day, as this can strip your hair of its natural oils and cause your color to fade faster.
  • Avoid using hot tools, such as curling irons or straighteners, as these can damage your hair and cause your color to fade.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair looking healthy and prevent split ends.

With these tips in mind, you can find the perfect hair color to complement your skin tone and enhance your natural beauty. Remember to take care of your colored hair to keep it looking healthy and vibrant!
