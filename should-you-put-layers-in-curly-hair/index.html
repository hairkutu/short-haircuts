Should You Put Layers In Curly Hair Everything You Need To Know

Written by Aprilia May 07, 2023 Â· 7 min read

If you have curly hair, you may be wondering whether to get layers or not. Layers can add volume, texture, and movement to your curls, but they can also make your hair look frizzy and uneven if not done properly. In this article, we will discuss everything you need to know about whether you should put layers in curly hair.

Table of Contents:

  • Description
  • How to
  • Step by Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

Layers are a popular haircut for curly hair because they can enhance the natural texture and shape of the curls. Layers can be cut at different lengths and angles to create a variety of looks, from subtle to dramatic. However, not all curly hair types are suitable for layers. Fine, thin, or damaged hair may not benefit from layers, as they can make the hair look even thinner and more fragile.

How to

The first step in deciding whether to get layers in curly hair is to determine your hair type and texture. Thick, coarse, and bouncy curls can handle more layers, while fine or delicate curls may need fewer layers or none at all. You should also consider your face shape and personal style, as layers can accentuate certain features and complement different fashion choices.

Step by Step

If you decide to get layers in curly hair, you should communicate your desired look and length with your hairstylist. They will then section your hair and cut the layers at the appropriate angles and thickness. They may also use different cutting techniques, such as point cutting or slide cutting, to create more texture and movement in the curls. After the layers are cut, they may be styled with a diffuser, curling iron, or other tools to enhance the shape and volume of the curls.

Tips

When getting layers in curly hair, it's important to go to a hairstylist who specializes in curly hair. They will know how to cut the layers to avoid frizz, breakage, or unevenness in the curls. You should also use a shampoo and conditioner that are specifically formulated for curly hair, as they can help to hydrate, define, and protect the curls. Lastly, you can use styling products like mousse, gel, or cream to enhance the curl pattern and hold the layers in place.

FAQ

How many layers should I get in curly hair?

The number of layers you get in curly hair depends on your hair type, length, and desired look. You can start with a few layers and gradually add more if you want more volume and texture. It's best to consult with a hairstylist who can recommend the best layers for your hair.

Do layers make curly hair frizzy?

Layers can make curly hair frizzy if not cut properly or if the hair is already dry or damaged. To avoid frizz, you should use a deep conditioner, avoid brushing or combing the hair too much, and use a diffuser when blow-drying.

Can I put layers in short curly hair?

Yes, you can put layers in short curly hair to create more volume and movement. However, you should be aware that shorter layers can make the hair look more textured and may require more maintenance to keep the curls defined.

Hair Care

To maintain healthy and vibrant curly hair with layers, you should follow a consistent hair care routine that includes regular washing, conditioning, and styling. You should also avoid heat styling or chemical treatments that can damage the hair or alter the curl pattern. Instead, you can use natural remedies like coconut oil, aloe vera, or apple cider vinegar to nourish and strengthen the hair from the inside out.

Overall, whether to put layers in curly hair depends on your personal preferences and hair type. Layers can add dimension and movement to curly hair, but they can also make it look frizzy or uneven if not done correctly. By consulting with a hairstylist who specializes in curly hair and following a proper hair care routine, you can achieve beautiful and healthy curls with layers.
