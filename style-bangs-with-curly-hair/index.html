Style Bangs With Curly Hair A Guide To Looking Fabulous

Written by Aprilia Jul 10, 2023 Â· 9 min read

If you have curly hair, you may have thought that bangs are not for you. But with the right technique and style, you can rock bangs with your curly locks. In this article, we will guide you on how to style bangs with curly hair, including tips, step-by-step instructions, and hair care advice to help you achieve a fabulous look.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to Style Bangs with Curly Hair
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Styling Bangs with Curly Hair
  • Frequently Asked Questions
  • Hair Care for Curly Hair with Bangs

Description

Bangs are a great way to change up your look and add some flair to your style. However, for those with curly hair, styling bangs can be a challenge. Curly hair tends to be more voluminous and textured, so it's important to choose a bang style that complements your curls.

There are various types of bangs that suit curly hair, such as side-swept bangs, curtain bangs, and baby bangs. Side-swept bangs are the most popular style for curly hair since they blend in well with the curls and frame your face. Curtain bangs are also a great option for those who want a more modern and trendy look. Baby bangs are shorter and cut straight across, giving a bold and edgy vibe.

How to Style Bangs with Curly Hair

Before you start styling your bangs, make sure your hair is clean and conditioned. Apply a leave-in conditioner or hair oil to keep your curls hydrated and bouncy. Then, follow these tips to style your bangs:

1. Choose the Right Bangs

As mentioned earlier, there are various types of bangs that suit curly hair. Choose one that complements your face shape and hair texture. Consult with your hairstylist to find the right cut for you.

2. Use a Diffuser

A diffuser is a must-have tool for curly hair. It helps to enhance your curls and reduce frizz. Use a diffuser attachment on your blow dryer to dry your bangs. Hold the diffuser close to your hair and scrunch your curls gently to add volume.

3. Finger-Comb Your Bangs

After drying your bangs, use your fingers to comb through them gently. Avoid using a brush or a fine-tooth comb as it can ruin your curls and create frizz. Finger-combing helps to separate your curls and add texture to your bangs.

4. Apply a Styling Product

Apply a styling product to your bangs to hold them in place. Use a lightweight product such as mousse or gel to avoid weighing down your curls. Apply the product to your palms and scrunch your bangs gently with your hands.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here is a step-by-step guide to styling bangs with curly hair:

Step 1: Choose the Right Bangs

Consult with your hairstylist to choose the right bangs that suit your face shape and hair texture.

Step 2: Wash and Condition Your Hair

Wash your hair with a sulfate-free shampoo and condition it with a hydrating conditioner. Apply a leave-in conditioner or hair oil to keep your curls moisturized.

Step 3: Dry Your Hair

Use a microfiber towel or an old t-shirt to dry your hair gently. Avoid rubbing your hair vigorously as it can create frizz. Use a diffuser attachment on your blow dryer to dry your bangs.

Step 4: Finger-Comb Your Bangs

Use your fingers to comb through your bangs gently. Avoid using a brush or a fine-tooth comb as it can ruin your curls.

Step 5: Apply a Styling Product

Apply a lightweight styling product such as mousse or gel to your palms. Scrunch your bangs gently with your hands to distribute the product evenly.

Tips for Styling Bangs with Curly Hair

Here are some tips to help you style bangs with curly hair:

  • Trim your bangs regularly to keep them in shape.
  • Avoid using heat tools such as straighteners or curling irons on your bangs as it can damage your curls.
  • Use a silk or satin pillowcase to prevent frizz and breakage.
  • Apply a hair mask once a week to nourish your curls.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I have bangs with curly hair?

Yes, you can have bangs with curly hair. Consult with your hairstylist to choose the right cut that suits your face shape and hair texture.

What type of bangs suit curly hair?

Side-swept bangs, curtain bangs, and baby bangs are popular styles that suit curly hair. Choose one that complements your curls and face shape.

How do I style my bangs without damaging my curls?

Avoid using heat tools such as straighteners or curling irons on your bangs. Use a diffuser attachment on your blow dryer and finger-comb your bangs gently to avoid ruining your curls.

Hair Care for Curly Hair with Bangs

Curly hair requires special care to keep it healthy and bouncy. Here are some hair care tips for curly hair with bangs:

  • Wash your hair with a sulfate-free shampoo and condition it with a hydrating conditioner.
  • Apply a leave-in conditioner or hair oil to keep your curls moisturized.
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it's dry as it can create frizz.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a month to nourish your curls.

Styling bangs with curly hair can be tricky, but with the right technique and style, you can achieve a fabulous look. Follow our tips and step-by-step guide to style your bangs like a pro. Remember to take care of your curls by using the right hair care products and techniques to keep them healthy and bouncy. With a little effort, you can rock bangs with your curly locks and look fabulous.
