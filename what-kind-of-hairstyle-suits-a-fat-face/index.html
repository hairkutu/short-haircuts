What Kind Of Hairstyle Suits A Fat Face

Written by William Aug 14, 2023 Â· 8 min read

Having a chubby or round face can present its own unique challenges when it comes to choosing a hairstyle that flatters your features. You want a hairstyle that can create the illusion of a slimmer face and enhance your best features. In this blog post, we'll explore different hairstyles that suit a fat face and provide some tips on how to maintain your hair's health.

Table of Contents

  • How to Determine Your Face Shape
  • Haircuts for a Fat Face
  • Hairstyles for a Fat Face
  • Tips for Maintaining Healthy Hair
  • FAQ

How to Determine Your Face Shape

Before we dive into specific hairstyles, it's essential to determine your face shape correctly. To do this, you need to stand in front of a mirror and take a good look at yourself. Look at the width and length of your face, as well as the shape of your jawline and forehead.

The most common face shapes are oval, round, square, heart, and diamond. If your face is round like a circle, then you have a chubby or fat face. You want to choose a hairstyle that can elongate your face and make it appear slimmer.

Haircuts for a Fat Face

When it comes to haircuts for a fat face, you want to choose a hairstyle that can create the illusion of a slimmer face. The best haircuts for a chubby face are those that are shoulder-length or longer. Long hair can elongate your face and make it appear slimmer.

If you prefer short hair, you can opt for a layered bob or a short pixie cut. Layers can create the illusion of a longer face, while a pixie cut can accentuate your best features. However, be sure to avoid haircuts that are too short or too blunt, as they can emphasize the roundness of your face.

Hairstyles for a Fat Face

The right hairstyle can make all the difference when it comes to enhancing your best features. Here are some hairstyles that can flatter a fat face:

Side Swept Bangs

Side swept bangs can create the illusion of a slimmer face by drawing attention away from the roundness of your face. They can also highlight your eyes, making them appear more prominent.

Long Layers

Long layers can elongate your face and make it appear slimmer. They can also add texture and movement to your hair, making it look more voluminous.

Beachy Waves

Beachy waves can add volume and texture to your hair, making it look more voluminous. They can also create the illusion of a longer face by adding dimension and movement to your hair.

High Ponytail

A high ponytail can create the illusion of a slimmer face by elongating your neck and drawing attention away from the roundness of your face. It can also highlight your cheekbones, making them appear more prominent.

Tips for Maintaining Healthy Hair

Regardless of your face shape, it's essential to maintain healthy hair. Here are some tips for keeping your hair healthy:

Regular Trims

Regular trims can help prevent split ends and breakage, keeping your hair healthy and strong. You should aim to trim your hair every 6-8 weeks to maintain its health.

Deep Conditioning

Deep conditioning can help nourish and moisturize your hair, preventing breakage and damage. You should aim to deep condition your hair at least once a week to maintain its health.

Avoid Heat Styling

Heat styling can damage your hair, causing breakage and split ends. You should avoid using heat styling tools like flat irons and curling irons as much as possible. If you must use them, be sure to use a heat protectant spray to minimize damage.

FAQ

Can short hair flatter a fat face?

Yes, short hair can flatter a fat face. However, you want to choose a short haircut that is layered or textured to create the illusion of a slimmer face. Avoid blunt cuts that can emphasize the roundness of your face.

What hairstyle should I avoid if I have a fat face?

If you have a chubby or round face, you should avoid hairstyles that are too short or too blunt. These haircuts can emphasize the roundness of your face, making it appear wider.

How often should I wash my hair?

You should aim to wash your hair every 2-3 days to maintain its health. Overwashing can strip your hair of its natural oils, causing it to become dry and brittle.

In conclusion, choosing the right hairstyle for a fat face can be challenging, but it's not impossible. By following these tips and exploring different hairstyles, you can find a look that flatters your features and enhances your natural beauty.
