How To Make Men s Hair Lay Back A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Christine Aug 15, 2023 Â· 8 min read

Men's hair styling has come a long way in recent years. From the slicked-back looks of the 1950s to the more relaxed hairstyles of today, there are a variety of ways to make your hair look great. If you're looking for tips on how to make men's hair lay back, you've come to the right place. In this guide, we'll go over everything you need to know to achieve the perfect laid-back hairstyle.

Table of Contents

  • How to Make Men's Hair Lay Back
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Laying Your Hair Back
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care Tips

How to Make Men's Hair Lay Back

The first step in making your hair lay back is to choose the right product. You'll want to look for a hair product that provides hold without making your hair look stiff or greasy. Some good options include pomade, wax, and clay. Once you've chosen your product, you'll need to apply it to your hair in the right way.

Choose the Right Product

As mentioned, there are a variety of products to choose from when it comes to laying your hair back. Here are a few of the most popular options:

  • Pomade: Pomade is a great choice if you want a shiny, slick look. It provides a medium hold and is easy to work with.
  • Wax: Wax is perfect if you want a more matte finish. It provides a strong hold and can be used to create a variety of styles.
  • Clay: Clay is ideal if you want a natural, textured look. It provides a medium hold and is great for adding volume to your hair.

Apply the Product Correctly

Once you've chosen your product, it's time to apply it to your hair. Here's how to do it:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair. If your hair is wet, the product won't hold as well.
  2. Scoop a small amount of product onto your fingers. You can always add more later if you need it.
  3. Rub the product between your palms to warm it up. This will make it easier to apply.
  4. Starting at the back of your head, work the product through your hair. Use your fingers to comb your hair back, starting at your hairline and working your way back.
  5. Once you've applied the product, use a comb or your fingers to shape your hair into the desired style.
  6. Finally, use a hair dryer on a low setting to set the style in place.

Step-by-Step Guide

Now that you know the basics, here's a step-by-step guide to laying your hair back:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair.
  2. Choose the right product for your hair type and desired style.
  3. Scoop a small amount of product onto your fingers and rub it between your palms.
  4. Starting at the back of your head, work the product through your hair, using your fingers to comb your hair back.
  5. Use a comb or your fingers to shape your hair into the desired style.
  6. Use a hair dryer on a low setting to set the style in place.

Tips for Laying Your Hair Back

Here are a few tips for getting the perfect laid-back hairstyle:

  • Use a small amount of product to start, and add more as needed.
  • If you have thick or curly hair, consider using a hair straightener to help lay your hair back.
  • When applying the product, focus on the roots of your hair to add volume and lift.
  • Experiment with different products and application techniques to find what works best for you.

FAQs

What if I have thinning hair?

If you have thinning hair, it's important to be gentle when applying product. Use a lightweight product, and avoid pulling or tugging on your hair.

What if I have a receding hairline?

If you have a receding hairline, you can still lay your hair back. Start the product at the point where your hairline begins to recede, and comb your hair back from there.

What if I have long hair?

If you have long hair, you can still lay your hair back. Use a hair tie to pull your hair back into a ponytail, and apply the product to the hair that's left loose.

Hair Care Tips

In addition to using the right products and styling techniques, it's important to take care of your hair. Here are a few tips for keeping your hair healthy:

  • Wash your hair regularly, but not too often. Too much washing can strip your hair of its natural oils.
  • Avoid using hot tools like hair straighteners and curling irons too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Eat a healthy diet that's rich in vitamins and minerals, as this can help promote healthy hair growth.
  • Consider using a hair mask once a week to nourish your hair and scalp.

With these tips and techniques, you can achieve the perfect laid-back hairstyle. Whether you're going for a slicked-back look or something more natural, the right product and application method can make all the difference.
