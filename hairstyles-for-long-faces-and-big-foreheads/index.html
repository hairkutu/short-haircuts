Hairstyles for Long Faces and Big Foreheads

If you have a long face and a big forehead, it can be challenging to find the right hairstyle that flatters your features. Fortunately, there are plenty of hairstyles that can help balance out your face shape and enhance your natural beauty. In this article, we will be sharing some of the best hairstyles for long faces and big foreheads, along with tips and tricks to help you achieve the perfect look.

Table of Contents

  • How to Determine Your Face Shape
  • Hairstyles for Long Faces and Big Foreheads
  • Tips and Tricks
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

How to Determine Your Face Shape

Before choosing a hairstyle, it's important to determine your face shape. The most common face shapes are oval, round, square, heart, and diamond. To determine your face shape, take a look at your forehead, cheekbones, and jawline.

If you have a long face, your forehead, cheekbones, and jawline are all roughly the same width. Your face may also have a more vertical appearance, with a high forehead and long chin.

Hairstyles for Long Faces and Big Foreheads

Now that you know your face shape, it's time to choose a hairstyle that flatters your features. Here are some of the best hairstyles for long faces and big foreheads:

1. Side-Swept Bangs

Side-swept bangs are a great way to balance out a long face and draw attention away from a big forehead. Opt for long, sweeping bangs that start at the side of your forehead and gradually blend into the rest of your hair.

2. Layers

Layers can add volume and texture to your hair, which can help balance out a long face. Ask your stylist for long layers that start at your cheekbones and blend into the rest of your hair.

3. Shoulder-Length Hair

Shoulder-length hair is a great option for those with long faces and big foreheads. This length can help create the illusion of width and balance out your face shape. Try adding layers or waves for added texture.

4. Bob

A bob is a timeless hairstyle that can flatter any face shape. Opt for a chin-length bob that hits at your jawline or slightly above. This length can help balance out your face shape and draw attention away from your forehead.

Tips and Tricks

Here are some additional tips and tricks to help you achieve the perfect hairstyle for your long face and big forehead:

  • Stay away from blunt bangs, as they can make your forehead appear even larger.
  • Consider getting highlights or lowlights to add dimension to your hair.
  • Avoid pulling your hair back tightly, as this can accentuate your forehead.
  • Try adding volume to the crown of your head to balance out your face shape.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about hairstyles for long faces and big foreheads:

What is the best haircut for a long face and big forehead?

Side-swept bangs, layers, shoulder-length hair, and bobs are all great options for those with long faces and big foreheads.

Should I avoid bangs if I have a big forehead?

Blunt bangs can make a big forehead appear even larger. Instead, opt for side-swept bangs that start at the side of your forehead and gradually blend into the rest of your hair.

Hair Care

Proper hair care is essential for maintaining healthy, beautiful hair. Here are some tips to keep your hair looking its best:

  • Wash your hair every 2-3 days to prevent over-drying.
  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as this can damage your hair.
  • Use a heat protectant spray before using any heat styling tools.
  • Regularly trim your hair to get rid of split ends and keep your hair looking healthy.

With these tips and tricks, you can find the perfect hairstyle for your long face and big forehead. Remember to take your face shape into consideration when choosing a hairstyle, and don't be afraid to try something new!
