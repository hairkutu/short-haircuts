Guys Haircut Long On Top Shaved On Sides The Ultimate Guide

Written by Eveline Jun 08, 2023 Â· 7 min read

If you're looking for a trendy and stylish haircut for men, then the guys haircut long on top shaved on sides is the perfect choice for you. This haircut has become increasingly popular in recent years, and it's easy to see why. The long hair on top gives you a versatile look, while the shaved sides add a touch of edginess. In this article, we'll guide you through the process of getting this haircut and provide you with tips on how to style and care for it.

Table of Contents

  1. Description
  2. How to Get the Haircut
  3. Step-by-Step Guide
  4. Tips for Styling
  5. FAQs
  6. Hair Care Tips

Description

The guys haircut long on top shaved on sides is a versatile and stylish haircut that combines long hair on top with shaved or closely trimmed sides. The long hair on top can be styled in a variety of ways, such as slicked back, messy, or textured, while the shaved sides add a touch of edginess and modernity.

How to Get the Haircut

To get the guys haircut long on top shaved on sides, you'll need to visit a barber or hair stylist who is experienced in this type of haircut. It's important to have a clear idea of what you want before you go in for your appointment, so bring photos or examples of the haircut you're looking for.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide to getting the guys haircut long on top shaved on sides:

  1. Choose the length of the hair on top. You can go for a short, medium, or long length depending on your personal preference.
  2. Decide on the type of fade you want for the sides. A fade can be high, mid, or low, depending on how closely you want the sides to be shaved.
  3. Communicate your preferences clearly to your barber or hair stylist.
  4. Sit back and relax while your hair is cut and styled.

Tips for Styling

Once you've got the guys haircut long on top shaved on sides, there are a few tips you can use to style it:

  • Use a good quality pomade, wax, or gel to style your hair on top. Apply a small amount to your fingertips and work it through your hair.
  • Experiment with different styles, such as slicked back, messy, or textured, to find the look that suits you best.
  • Use a comb or your fingers to create texture and volume in your hair on top.
  • Trim the sides regularly to maintain the clean and edgy look of the haircut.

FAQs

How often do I need to get my hair cut?

You'll need to get your hair cut every 4-6 weeks to maintain the length and shape of the haircut.

Can I still wear a hat with this haircut?

Yes, you can still wear a hat with this haircut. Just make sure that the hat fits well and doesn't flatten your hair on top.

Is this haircut suitable for all hair types?

Yes, the guys haircut long on top shaved on sides can be adapted to suit all hair types, including straight, wavy, curly, and thick hair.

Hair Care Tips

To keep your guys haircut long on top shaved on sides looking its best, follow these hair care tips:

  • Use a good quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair clean and healthy.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as this can damage it. Use a heat protectant spray if you need to use a hair dryer or straightener.
  • Eat a healthy diet and drink plenty of water to nourish your hair from within.
  • Get regular trims to keep the haircut looking sharp and fresh.

Now that you know everything there is to know about the guys haircut long on top shaved on sides, it's time to book your appointment and try it out for yourself. With a little bit of styling and care, you'll be rocking this trendy and modern haircut in no time.
