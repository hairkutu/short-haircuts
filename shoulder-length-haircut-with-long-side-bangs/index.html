Shoulder Length Haircut With Long Side Bangs A Relaxed Guide To This Trendy Hairstyle

Written by Christine Mar 27, 2023 Â· 8 min read

If you're looking for a new hairstyle that's both trendy and easy to maintain, a shoulder length haircut with long side bangs might be just what you need. This hairstyle has been popular for years and continues to be a favorite among women of all ages. In this guide, we'll go over how to achieve this look, share some tips on how to style it, and answer some frequently asked questions about shoulder length haircuts with long side bangs.

Table of Contents

  1. Description
  2. How to Achieve the Look
  3. Step-by-Step Guide
  4. Tips for Styling
  5. FAQ
  6. Hair Care Tips

Description

A shoulder length haircut with long side bangs is a versatile hairstyle that can be worn in many different ways. The hair is cut to shoulder length and the bangs are cut to a longer length, typically falling just below the chin. This style can be straight, wavy, or curly and can be styled in various ways to suit your face shape and personal style.

One of the great things about this hairstyle is that it's easy to maintain. It requires minimal styling and can be worn as a wash-and-go look or styled with a few simple tools. It's also a great choice for those who want to try a new look without committing to a drastic change.

How to Achieve the Look

The first step in achieving a shoulder length haircut with long side bangs is to find a good hairstylist who can help you achieve the look you want. Be sure to bring in pictures of the style you're interested in so that your stylist can get a clear idea of what you're looking for.

Once you've found the right stylist, it's time to decide on the length of your hair and bangs. Shoulder length hair is typically cut to just below the shoulders, but you can adjust the length to suit your face shape and personal style. The bangs should be cut to a longer length, falling just below the chin. This length will help to frame your face and bring attention to your eyes.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide to achieving a shoulder length haircut with long side bangs:

  1. Wash and dry your hair.
  2. Section your hair into four parts: two at the front and two at the back.
  3. Cut your hair to the desired length, starting at the back and working your way forward.
  4. Cut your bangs to the desired length, starting at the center and working your way outwards.
  5. Style your hair as desired.

Tips for Styling

There are many ways to style a shoulder length haircut with long side bangs. Here are some tips to help you achieve the perfect look:

  • Blow dry your hair with a round brush to create volume and bounce.
  • Straighten your hair with a flat iron for a sleek and polished look.
  • Add waves or curls to your hair with a curling iron or wand for a more casual look.
  • Experiment with different partings to see which one suits you best.
  • Use a texturizing spray or mousse to add texture and definition to your hair.

FAQ

Q: Will a shoulder length haircut with long side bangs suit my face shape?

A: This hairstyle suits most face shapes, but it's always a good idea to consult with your stylist to determine the best length and style for your face.

Q: How often will I need to get my hair cut to maintain this style?

A: It's recommended that you get your hair cut every six to eight weeks to maintain the length and shape of the style.

Q: Can I wear my hair up with this style?

A: Yes, you can wear your hair up with this style. Try pulling your hair back into a low ponytail or bun to show off your side bangs.

Hair Care Tips

To keep your shoulder length haircut with long side bangs looking great, follow these hair care tips:

  • Wash your hair every two to three days to avoid stripping it of its natural oils.
  • Use a shampoo and conditioner that are formulated for your hair type.
  • Avoid using hot tools on your hair every day to prevent damage.
  • Use a heat protectant spray before using hot tools.
  • Trim your hair regularly to maintain the length and shape of the style.

With these tips and tricks, you'll be able to rock a shoulder length haircut with long side bangs with ease. Whether you're looking for a new look for work or play, this hairstyle is sure to turn heads and make you feel confident and beautiful.
