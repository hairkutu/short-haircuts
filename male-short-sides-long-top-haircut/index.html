Male Short Sides Long Top Haircut A Complete Guide

Written by Robby Apr 28, 2023 Â· 7 min read

If you're looking for a versatile and stylish haircut, then a male short sides long top haircut might just be the perfect option for you. This haircut has gained immense popularity in recent years and has become a go-to style for many men. In this article, we'll discuss everything you need to know about the male short sides long top haircut, including how to get it, styling tips, and hair care.

Table of Contents

  1. Description
  2. How to Get a Male Short Sides Long Top Haircut
  3. Step-by-Step Guide
  4. Tips for Styling a Male Short Sides Long Top Haircut
  5. Frequently Asked Questions
  6. Hair Care Tips

Description

The male short sides long top haircut is a trendy and modern hairstyle that involves keeping the sides of your hair short while the top is kept long. This haircut is perfect for men who want to experiment with their look and want a style that is easy to maintain but still looks great. It's a versatile haircut that can be styled in many different ways, making it perfect for any occasion.

How to Get a Male Short Sides Long Top Haircut

If you're interested in getting a male short sides long top haircut, the first step is to find a good barber who can give you the cut you want. Make sure to do your research and find a barber who has experience with this type of haircut.

Once you've found a barber, you'll need to decide on the length of the top and the sides. You can choose to keep the sides very short, such as a buzz cut or a fade, or you can keep them slightly longer. The length of the top is up to you, but it's usually kept at least 2-3 inches long.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here is a step-by-step guide on how to get a male short sides long top haircut:

  1. Find a good barber who has experience with this type of haircut.
  2. Decide on the length of the top and the sides.
  3. Sit in the barber's chair and let them know what you want.
  4. The barber will start by cutting the sides of your hair.
  5. They will then move on to the top of your hair, cutting it to the desired length.
  6. Once the haircut is complete, the barber will style your hair according to your preferences.

Tips for Styling a Male Short Sides Long Top Haircut

Here are some tips for styling a male short sides long top haircut:

  • Use a small amount of pomade or hair wax to style the top of your hair.
  • Experiment with different styles to find the one that suits you best.
  • Keep your hair clean and well-maintained to ensure it looks great at all times.

Frequently Asked Questions

Here are some frequently asked questions about the male short sides long top haircut:

Q: Is the male short sides long top haircut suitable for all hair types?

A: Yes, this haircut is suitable for all hair types.

Q: How often do I need to get this haircut?

A: It depends on how fast your hair grows, but you'll generally need to get this haircut every 4-6 weeks.

Q: What face shapes does this haircut suit?

A: This haircut suits a variety of face shapes, including oval, square, and heart-shaped faces.

Hair Care Tips

Here are some hair care tips for maintaining your male short sides long top haircut:

  • Use a good quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair clean and healthy.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, such as blow-drying or straightening.
  • Use a hair mask once a week to keep your hair moisturized.
  • Trim the ends of your hair every 6-8 weeks to prevent split ends.

In conclusion, the male short sides long top haircut is a stylish and versatile haircut that's perfect for men who want to experiment with their look. By following the tips and advice in this article, you can ensure that your haircut looks great and is easy to maintain. So go ahead and try out this popular haircut today!
