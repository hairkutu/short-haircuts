Hairstyles For Oval Faces Over 50 The Best Looks For 2023

If you're over 50 and have an oval face shape, you're in luck! This face shape is versatile and can pull off a variety of hairstyles. However, with so many options out there, it can be overwhelming to choose the right one. In this article, we'll go over the best hairstyles for oval faces over 50, how to style them, and tips for maintaining healthy hair.

How to Choose the Right Hairstyle

Before we dive into specific hairstyles, it's important to understand the general guidelines for choosing a hairstyle that flatters your face shape. If you have an oval face, you want to choose a style that accentuates your features and complements your face shape.

General Guidelines for Oval Faces

Oval faces have a balanced appearance with a slightly wider forehead, high cheekbones, and a narrow chin. When choosing a hairstyle, keep these tips in mind:

  • Avoid styles with heavy bangs or volume at the crown, as they can make your face appear rounder.
  • Opt for styles that frame your face, such as layered cuts, side-swept bangs, or angled bobs.
  • Consider your hair texture and thickness when choosing a hairstyle. Fine hair looks best with shorter styles, while thick hair can handle longer lengths.

Short Hairstyles for Oval Faces Over 50

Short hairstyles can be a great option for oval faces over 50, as they can emphasize your features and give you a youthful appearance. Here are some of the best short hairstyles to consider:

Pixie Cut

The pixie cut is a classic short hairstyle that is perfect for oval faces. This cut features short, choppy layers that flatter your face shape and highlight your cheekbones. To style, simply apply a small amount of styling cream to damp hair and use your fingers to tousle and shape your hair.

Bob with Bangs

A bob with bangs is another great option for oval faces over 50. This cut features a chin-length bob with side-swept bangs that frame your face and accentuate your features. To style, blow dry your hair with a round brush and finish with a light hold hairspray.

Medium Hairstyles for Oval Faces Over 50

Medium hairstyles are a great choice for oval faces over 50, as they can be versatile and easy to style. Here are some of the best medium hairstyles to consider:

Shoulder-Length Layers

Shoulder-length layers are a great option for oval faces over 50, as they can add volume and texture to your hair. To style, apply a volumizing mousse to damp hair and blow dry with a round brush. Finish with a light hold hairspray to keep your hair in place.

Long Bob

A long bob, or "lob," is a trendy medium-length hairstyle that can flatter oval faces over 50. This cut features a shoulder-length bob with subtle layers and a side part. To style, blow dry your hair with a round brush and finish with a texturizing spray.

Long Hairstyles for Oval Faces Over 50

Long hairstyles can be a great option for oval faces over 50, as they can create a soft, feminine look. Here are some of the best long hairstyles to consider:

Beachy Waves

Beachy waves are a great option for long hair, as they can create a relaxed, carefree look. To style, apply a sea salt spray to damp hair and scrunch with your fingers. You can also use a curling iron to create loose waves.

Sleek and Straight

A sleek and straight hairstyle can be a great option for oval faces over 50, as it can create a polished, sophisticated look. To style, blow dry your hair with a round brush and finish with a straightening iron. Use a smoothing serum to add shine and tame any flyaways.

Hair Care Tips for Healthy Hair

No matter what hairstyle you choose, it's important to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and looking its best. Here are some tips for maintaining healthy hair:

  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using hot tools too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Apply a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair moisturized.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and heat by wearing a hat or using a heat protectant spray.

FAQ

What hairstyles should I avoid if I have an oval face?

Avoid hairstyles with heavy bangs or volume at the crown, as they can make your face appear rounder. You should also avoid hairstyles that are too long or too short, as they can throw off the balance of your face shape.

Can I still wear my hair in a ponytail if I have an oval face?

Yes, you can still wear your hair in a ponytail if you have an oval face. However, it's best to avoid tight, high ponytails that can pull back your features and make your face appear longer. Instead, opt for a low, loose ponytail or a messy bun.

How often should I get my hair trimmed?

It's recommended to get your hair trimmed every 6-8 weeks to keep split ends at bay and maintain a healthy hair length.

Can I dye my hair if I have an oval face?

Yes, you can dye your hair if you have an oval face. However, it's important to choose a hair color that complements your skin tone and doesn't clash with your natural features. It's also important to use a high-quality hair dye and follow the instructions carefully to avoid damaging your hair.

There you have it - the best hairstyles for oval faces over 50! Whether you prefer short, medium, or long hair, there's a style out there that can flatter your face shape and make you look and feel your best. Remember to take care of your hair and experiment with different styles to find the one that works best for you.
