Best Styling Products For Curly Hair At Target

If you have curly hair, you know that it can be a challenge to find the right styling products. Fortunately, Target has a variety of options to choose from. In this article, we'll be discussing the best styling products for curly hair at Target, how to use them, and some tips for keeping your curls looking their best.

  • How to Use
  • Step by Step Guide

Description

Target offers a wide variety of styling products for curly hair, including mousses, gels, creams, and oils. Some of the best products for curly hair at Target include:

  • SheaMoisture Coconut & Hibiscus Curl Enhancing Smoothie
  • Cantu Shea Butter for Natural Hair Coconut Curling Cream
  • OGX Quenching + Coconut Curls Frizz-Defying Curl Styling Milk
  • Aussie Instant Freeze Sculpting Maximum Hold Hair Gel
  • Herbal Essences Totally Twisted Curl Scrunching Gel

How to Use

The way you use a styling product will depend on the specific product you're using. However, there are some general tips to keep in mind:

  • Apply the product to damp hair
  • Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to distribute the product evenly
  • Avoid using too much product, as this can weigh down your curls
  • Scrunch your hair to encourage curl formation
  • Allow your hair to air dry or use a diffuser on your blow dryer

Step by Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide for using a styling product:

  1. Wash and condition your hair
  2. Towel dry your hair, but don't rub it vigorously
  3. Apply the styling product to your hair, starting at the ends and working your way up
  4. Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to distribute the product evenly
  5. Scrunch your hair to encourage curl formation
  6. Allow your hair to air dry or use a diffuser on your blow dryer

Tips for Curly Hair

Here are some tips for keeping your curly hair looking its best:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo to avoid stripping your hair of natural oils
  • Deep condition your hair once a week
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it's dry, as this can cause frizz
  • Sleep on a satin pillowcase to prevent tangles and breakage
  • Avoid using heat styling tools on a daily basis

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about styling curly hair:

Q: How often should I wash my curly hair?

A: It's best to wash your hair every 2-3 days, as washing too frequently can dry out your hair and cause frizz.

Q: Can I use a styling product on dry hair?

A: While it's best to apply styling products to damp hair, you can use a small amount of product on dry hair to tame frizz.

Q: What's the best way to dry curly hair?

A: The best way to dry curly hair is to let it air dry or use a diffuser on your blow dryer. Avoid using a regular hair dryer, as this can cause frizz.

Hair Care for Curly Hair

In addition to using the right styling products, it's important to take good care of your curly hair. Here are some tips:

  • Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your hair
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends
  • Avoid using hot water when washing your hair, as this can dry it out
  • Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a hair product with SPF

In conclusion, Target offers a variety of great styling products for curly hair. By using the right products and following a good hair care routine, you can keep your curls looking their best.
