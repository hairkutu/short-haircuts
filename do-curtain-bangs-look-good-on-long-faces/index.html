Do Curtain Bangs Look Good On Long Faces

Written by Oliver Apr 08, 2023 Â· 9 min read

Are you looking for a new hairstyle that will flatter your long face shape? If so, you might have heard about curtain bangs. These bangs have been a popular trend for a few years now and for a good reason. They are versatile, low-maintenance, and can suit many face shapes. However, you might be wondering: do curtain bangs look good on long faces? In this article, we will explore this question and provide you with helpful tips and tricks.

  4. Tips and Tricks

Description

First, let's define what curtain bangs are. These bangs are a type of layered bangs that are parted in the middle to create a curtain-like effect. They are longer on the sides and can frame the face beautifully. Curtain bangs can be worn with any hair length, texture, and color. They are also low-maintenance and can be styled easily with a blow-dryer, round brush, or curling iron.

Now, let's talk about long face shapes. If you have a long face, your features are elongated, and your forehead, cheekbones, and jawline are roughly the same width. People with long faces often look for hairstyles that can balance their features and create an illusion of width. That's where curtain bangs can come in handy.

How to Style Curtain Bangs

Before we dive into whether curtain bangs look good on long faces, let's talk about how to style them. Here are some simple steps to achieve a chic and effortless curtain bangs look:

Step 1: Start with clean, dry hair

Wash your hair with a clarifying shampoo and conditioner to remove any buildup or oils. Towel-dry your hair and let it air-dry until it's about 80% dry.

Step 2: Apply heat protectant

Spray a heat protectant all over your hair, including your bangs. This will prevent heat damage and keep your hair healthy.

Step 3: Blow-dry your bangs

Using a round brush, blow-dry your curtain bangs in the direction you want them to fall. For more volume, flip your hair upside down and blow-dry it that way.

Step 4: Style your hair

Once your bangs are dry, you can style the rest of your hair as desired. You can leave it straight, add some waves or curls, or put it up in a ponytail or bun.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here's a detailed step-by-step guide on how to cut curtain bangs:

Step 1: Section your hair

Start by sectioning your hair into two parts, one for the bangs and one for the rest of your hair.

Step 2: Determine the length

Decide on the length of your bangs. Curtain bangs are usually longer on the sides and shorter in the middle.

Step 3: Cut the middle section

Using sharp scissors, cut the middle section of your bangs to the desired length. Make sure to cut in a straight line.

Step 4: Cut the sides

Starting from the middle section, cut the sides of your bangs diagonally, following the natural shape of your face.

Step 5: Blend the bangs

Blend the bangs into the rest of your hair by cutting some face-framing layers. This will make the transition from the bangs to the rest of your hair seamless.

Tips and Tricks

Here are some tips and tricks to make your curtain bangs look great on your long face:

Tip 1: Add some texture

If you have fine or thin hair, add some texture to your curtain bangs by curling or waving them. This will make them look fuller and more voluminous.

Tip 2: Avoid blunt cuts

Avoid getting blunt bangs if you have a long face. Blunt bangs can make your face look longer and narrower. Instead, opt for layered and textured bangs.

Tip 3: Part them in the middle

Part your curtain bangs in the middle to create a symmetrical and balanced look. This will also make your face look wider.

FAQ

Q: Do curtain bangs look good on square faces?

Yes, curtain bangs can look great on square faces. They can soften the angles of the face and create a more feminine look.

Q: How often do I need to trim my curtain bangs?

You should trim your curtain bangs every 4-6 weeks to keep them looking fresh and healthy.

Q: Can I wear my curtain bangs straight or do I need to style them?

You can wear your curtain bangs straight or style them as desired. They are versatile and can suit any occasion.

Hair Care Tips

Here are some hair care tips to keep your curtain bangs and long hair healthy:

Tip 1: Use sulfate-free shampoo

Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair and scalp healthy. Sulfates can strip your hair of natural oils and cause dryness and breakage.

Tip 2: Avoid heat styling

Avoid using heat styling tools too often to prevent heat damage. If you must use them, apply a heat protectant before styling.

Tip 3: Get regular trims

Get regular trims to prevent split ends and breakage. This will also keep your hair looking healthy and shiny.

In conclusion, curtain bangs can look great on long faces if styled and cut properly. They can balance your features and create a flattering frame for your face. Use the tips and tricks provided in this article to achieve a chic and effortless look.
