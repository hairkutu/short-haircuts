What Hairstyle Suits Big Face A Complete Guide

Written by Daniel Aug 23, 2023 · 9 min read

If you have a big face, you might be wondering what hairstyle will suit you best. With so many options out there, it can be overwhelming to choose the right one that will flatter your features. In this guide, we’ll go over everything you need to know about what hairstyle suits big face - from how to determine your face shape to different haircuts and styles that will complement your face. So let’s get started!

Table of Contents

  • Haircuts for Big Face
  • Hairstyles for Big Face
  • Tips for Styling Big Face
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care for Big Face

Determining Your Face Shape

The first step in finding a hairstyle that suits your big face is to determine your face shape. There are six main face shapes: oval, round, square, heart, diamond, and long. To determine your face shape, stand in front of a mirror and use a measuring tape to measure the widest part of your forehead, cheekbones, and jawline.

Once you have your measurements, compare them to the following guidelines:

  • Oval: Forehead is slightly wider than chin, and the length of the face is about one and a half times the width.
  • Round: Cheekbones and face length have a similar measurement. The width and length of the face are almost equal.
  • Square: All measurements are fairly similar. The forehead, cheekbones, and jawline are all about the same width.
  • Heart: Forehead is wider than the cheeks and the chin is pointed.
  • Diamond: Face is widest at the cheekbones, and the forehead and jawline are narrow.
  • Long: Forehead, cheekbones, and jawline are all about the same width, with a face length that is longer than the other measurements.

Knowing your face shape will help you choose a hairstyle that will flatter your features and create balance in your overall appearance.

Haircuts for Big Face

When choosing a haircut for a big face, you want to choose a style that will elongate your face and create the illusion of a more oval shape. Here are some haircuts that will suit big face:

Long Layers

Long layers are a great option for big face. They create movement and volume, which can help to elongate the face. You can also add side-swept bangs to create angles and balance out your features.

Side-Swept Bangs

Side-swept bangs are a great way to add angles to your face and create balance. They can be paired with any haircut, from long layers to a bob. Just make sure the bangs are long enough to sweep to the side and not too short.

Bob

A bob is another great option for big face. It can create the illusion of a more oval shape, especially if it hits right at the jawline. You can add volume and movement with layers or opt for a sleek and straight look.

Hairstyles for Big Face

Now that we’ve gone over some haircuts that suit big face, let’s talk about hairstyles. Here are some hairstyles that will complement your big face:

High Ponytail

A high ponytail can elongate your face and create a slimming effect. To create this look, gather your hair at the crown of your head and secure it with a hair tie. Use a comb to smooth out any bumps or flyaways.

Loose Waves

Loose waves can create movement and volume, which can help to elongate the face. You can achieve this look with a curling iron or by braiding your hair overnight and undoing the braids in the morning.

Half-Up, Half-Down

A half-up, half-down style can create balance and angles. You can add volume with a teasing comb or keep it sleek and straight with a flat iron.

Tips for Styling Big Face

Here are some tips to keep in mind when styling your hair if you have a big face:

  • Avoid styles that add width to your face, such as blunt bangs or straight-across bangs.
  • Opt for styles that add length to your face, such as layers or volume at the crown of your head.
  • Keep your hair off your face to create the illusion of a longer face.
  • Use a round brush when blow-drying your hair to create volume and movement.
  • Experiment with different hairstyles to find what works best for you.

FAQs

Q: Can I still wear bangs if I have a big face?

A: Yes, you can still wear bangs if you have a big face. Just make sure to opt for side-swept bangs rather than straight-across bangs, which can make your face appear wider.

Q: Will a short haircut suit my big face?

A: It depends on your face shape and personal style. Some short haircuts can elongate the face and create balance, while others can add width. It’s best to consult with a hairstylist to determine what will suit you best.

Hair Care for Big Face

Regardless of your face shape, it’s important to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and looking its best. Here are some tips for hair care:

  • Wash your hair with a sulfate-free shampoo to avoid stripping it of natural oils.
  • Use a conditioner to keep your hair moisturized and prevent breakage.
  • Limit the use of heat styling tools, such as flat irons and curling irons, to prevent damage.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.
  • Use a hair mask once a week to nourish your hair and keep it healthy.

By following these tips and choosing the right hairstyle, you can enhance your features and feel confident in your appearance. Remember, the most important thing is to choose a style that makes you feel comfortable and beautiful.
