How To Tell If You Would Suit A Hair Colour

Written by Eveline Aug 25, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Choosing a new hair color can be a daunting task, but it doesn't have to be. With a little bit of knowledge, you can determine which hair color will suit you best. In this article, we will explore how to tell if you would suit a hair color and provide some tips to help you choose the perfect shade for your skin tone and personality.

Table of Contents

  • How to
  • Step by Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

How to

The first step in determining which hair color will suit you is to identify your skin tone. There are three main skin tones: cool, warm, and neutral. You can determine your skin tone by looking at the veins on the underside of your wrist. If your veins appear blue, you have a cool skin tone. If your veins appear green, you have a warm skin tone. If your veins appear both blue and green, you have a neutral skin tone.

Once you have identified your skin tone, you can then choose a hair color that will complement it. If you have a cool skin tone, you should choose hair colors that have cool undertones, such as ash blonde, platinum blonde, or cool brown. If you have a warm skin tone, you should choose hair colors that have warm undertones, such as golden blonde, honey blonde, or warm brown. If you have a neutral skin tone, you can experiment with both cool and warm hair colors.

Step by Step

1. Identify your skin tone by looking at the veins on the underside of your wrist.

2. If your veins appear blue, you have a cool skin tone. If your veins appear green, you have a warm skin tone. If your veins appear both blue and green, you have a neutral skin tone.

3. Choose a hair color that will complement your skin tone. If you have a cool skin tone, choose cool-toned hair colors. If you have a warm skin tone, choose warm-toned hair colors. If you have a neutral skin tone, you can experiment with both cool and warm hair colors.

Tips

  • Consider your natural hair color when choosing a new shade.
  • Consult with a professional hair stylist to ensure you choose a hair color that suits you.
  • Take into account your personal style and personality when choosing a hair color.
  • Don't be afraid to experiment with different shades and tones to find the perfect hair color for you.

FAQ

Q: Can I dye my hair if I have sensitive skin?

A: Yes, you can dye your hair if you have sensitive skin. However, you should use a hair dye that is specifically designed for sensitive skin and perform a patch test before applying the dye to your hair.

Q: How often should I touch up my hair color?

A: It depends on the type of hair color you use and how quickly your hair grows. Generally, permanent hair color should be touched up every 6-8 weeks, while semi-permanent hair color should be touched up every 4-6 weeks.

Hair Care

After coloring your hair, it is important to take care of it to ensure the color lasts as long as possible. Use a color-safe shampoo and conditioner to prevent the color from fading. Avoid washing your hair too frequently, as this can cause the color to fade faster. Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy and moisturized.

By following these tips and taking proper care of your hair, you can ensure that your new hair color will suit you and look beautiful for as long as possible.
