Shoulder Length Hair With Bangs And Layers 2021 A Complete Guide

Written by Petter Sep 13, 2023 Â· 6 min read

If you're looking for a hairstyle that's both versatile and trendy, you can't go wrong with shoulder length hair with bangs and layers 2021. This hairstyle has been a popular choice for quite some time now, and it's easy to see why. Not only is it easy to maintain, but it also looks great on just about anyone. In this article, we'll be going over how to achieve this hairstyle, some tips and tricks, and how to care for your hair once you've got it.

Description

Shoulder length hair with bangs and layers is a hairstyle that is exactly what it sounds like. It's hair that falls just above or below the shoulders and has layers cut into it to add texture and movement. The bangs can be styled in many different ways, such as side-swept or straight across, depending on your preference.

How to Achieve Shoulder Length Hair with Bangs and Layers 2021

The first step in achieving this hairstyle is to start with clean, dry hair. From there, you'll want to section off your hair into manageable sections, starting from the back and working your way forward. Using hair cutting shears, cut the hair in a straight line, making sure to follow the natural curve of your head. Once you've cut the first section, you can use it as a guide for the rest of your hair.

After you've cut the length, it's time to add layers. Take small sections of hair and hold them up at a 45-degree angle. Using the shears, cut into the hair at a slight angle, creating a layered effect. Continue this process until you've added layers throughout your hair.

Finally, it's time to add the bangs. Depending on the style you want, you can either cut them straight across or at an angle. It's important to remember to cut them longer than you think you want them, as they will shrink up once they're dry.

Tips for Maintaining Your Shoulder Length Hair with Bangs and Layers 2021

Once you've achieved your desired hairstyle, it's important to take care of it properly. Here are some tips to help you maintain your shoulder length hair with bangs and layers:

  • Use a wide-toothed comb to detangle your hair, starting at the ends and working your way up.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools as much as possible, as they can damage your hair.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair hydrated.
  • Trim your hair every six to eight weeks to prevent split ends.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I still wear my hair up with this hairstyle?

Absolutely! Shoulder length hair with bangs and layers can be styled in many different ways, including updos and ponytails.

Will this hairstyle work for all hair types?

Yes, this hairstyle is versatile and can work for all hair types, from fine to thick.

How often do I need to cut my hair to maintain this style?

It's recommended to trim your hair every six to eight weeks to keep the style looking fresh.

Conclusion

Shoulder length hair with bangs and layers 2021 is a trendy and versatile hairstyle that's easy to maintain. With the right tools and techniques, you can achieve this look at home. Just remember to take care of your hair properly to keep it looking its best.
