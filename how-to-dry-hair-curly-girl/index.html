How To Dry Hair Curly Girl Tips Tricks And Techniques

Written by Christine Aug 22, 2023 · 8 min read

If you have curly hair, you know how important it is to dry it properly. Drying curly hair can be a challenge, but with the right techniques and products, you can achieve beautiful, defined curls. In this blog post, we'll go over some tips, tricks, and techniques for drying hair curly girl.

Table of Contents

  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips and Tricks
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care for Curly Hair

How to Dry Hair Curly Girl

First things first, start by washing your hair with a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner. Sulfates can dry out your hair and cause frizz, so it's best to avoid them. Once you've washed your hair, use a microfiber towel or a cotton t-shirt to gently squeeze out excess water. Avoid rubbing your hair with a regular towel, as this can cause frizz.

Next, apply your favorite styling products. This can be a leave-in conditioner, a curl cream, a gel, or a combination of these. Apply the products to wet hair, starting from the ends and working your way up to the roots. Be sure to distribute the products evenly throughout your hair.

Now it's time to dry your hair. There are a few different methods you can use depending on your hair type and the look you're going for. Here are a few techniques:

Diffusing

If you have a diffuser attachment for your hair dryer, this is a great way to dry your curls without causing frizz. Start by flipping your head over and placing sections of your hair into the diffuser. Use the low or medium heat setting and gently scrunch your hair as you dry it. This will help define your curls and add volume.

Plopping

Plopping is a technique where you use a t-shirt or a special hair plopping towel to wrap your hair up and let it dry. To plop your hair, lay a t-shirt or towel flat on a surface and flip your hair over onto it. Use the edges of the fabric to wrap your hair up into a turban-like shape. Leave your hair wrapped up for at least 20-30 minutes, then gently unwrap it and let it finish drying.

Air Drying

If you have time and don't mind waiting for your hair to air dry, this can be a great option. Simply apply your styling products and let your hair air dry naturally. This will help prevent frizz and allow your curls to form naturally.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide for drying hair curly girl:

  1. Wash your hair with a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner.
  2. Squeeze out excess water using a microfiber towel or cotton t-shirt.
  3. Apply your favorite styling products to wet hair.
  4. Choose your drying method (diffusing, plopping, or air drying).
  5. Dry your hair using your chosen method.
  6. Once your hair is dry, gently scrunch it to release any crunchiness from the styling products.

Tips and Tricks

Here are a few additional tips and tricks to help you dry your hair curly girl:

  • Use a wide-toothed comb or a detangling brush to help distribute your products.
  • Don't touch your hair too much while it's drying, as this can cause frizz.
  • If you're diffusing your hair, don't hold the dryer too close to your head.
  • Try using a silk or satin pillowcase to help prevent frizz while you sleep.

FAQs

How often should I wash my curly hair?

It depends on your hair type and how much oil your scalp produces. Some people with curly hair can go several days between washes, while others prefer to wash their hair daily. Experiment to find what works best for you.

Can I use a regular towel to dry my curly hair?

No, it's best to avoid using a regular towel to dry your hair. Regular towels can cause frizz and breakage. Instead, use a microfiber towel or a cotton t-shirt to gently squeeze out excess water.

Hair Care for Curly Hair

Drying your hair curly girl is just one part of a healthy hair care routine. Here are a few additional tips for caring for your curly hair:

  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as this can damage your hair.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your curls hydrated.
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it's dry, as this can cause frizz. Instead, use a wide-toothed comb or detangling brush on wet hair.
  • Protect your curls from the sun by wearing a hat or using a UV-protective spray.

Drying hair curly girl can be a challenge, but with the right products and techniques, you can achieve beautiful, defined curls. Experiment with different methods to find what works best for your hair type and lifestyle. And remember, a healthy hair care routine is key to keeping your curls looking their best.
