How To Use The Revlon One Step Hair Dryer And Volumizer

Written by Bardi Aug 07, 2023 Â· 7 min read

If you're tired of using a separate hair dryer and brush to style your hair, the Revlon One Step Hair Dryer and Volumizer may be the perfect tool for you. This versatile tool combines a hair dryer and brush in one, making it easy to achieve a salon-worthy blowout at home. In this article, we'll walk you through how to use the Revlon One Step Hair Dryer and Volumizer, as well as some tips and tricks to help you get the most out of this game-changing hair tool.

Description

The Revlon One Step Hair Dryer and Volumizer is a hair tool that combines a hair dryer and brush in one. It has a ceramic coating to help reduce heat damage and frizz, and can be used on all hair types. The tool has two heat settings and a cool setting, as well as a swivel cord to make it easier to use.

How to Use the Revlon One Step Hair Dryer and Volumizer

Step 1: Wash your hair

Start by washing your hair and towel-drying it until it is damp but not dripping wet. You can use a heat protectant spray or serum if you like.

Step 2: Section your hair

Section your hair into manageable sections using clips or hair ties. Start with the bottom section and work your way up.

Step 3: Turn on the tool

Plug in the Revlon One Step Hair Dryer and Volumizer and turn it on. Select the heat setting that is appropriate for your hair type.

Step 4: Start styling

Take a section of hair and place the tool at the roots. Use the brush to pull the tool through your hair, following it with your other hand to guide it. Continue working through each section until your hair is dry and styled.

Tips for Using the Revlon One Step Hair Dryer and Volumizer

Here are some tips to help you get the most out of your Revlon One Step Hair Dryer and Volumizer:

  • Use a heat protectant spray or serum to help prevent heat damage to your hair.
  • Section your hair into manageable sections to make it easier to style.
  • Start with the bottom sections and work your way up to the top.
  • Use the cool setting to set your style and add shine.
  • Experiment with different brush techniques to achieve different styles.

FAQ

Can the Revlon One Step Hair Dryer and Volumizer be used on all hair types?

Yes, the Revlon One Step Hair Dryer and Volumizer can be used on all hair types. However, if you have very thick or curly hair, you may need to use smaller sections to achieve the desired result.

Does the Revlon One Step Hair Dryer and Volumizer have a warranty?

Yes, the Revlon One Step Hair Dryer and Volumizer comes with a 4-year limited warranty.

Can the Revlon One Step Hair Dryer and Volumizer be used on wet hair?

The Revlon One Step Hair Dryer and Volumizer is designed to be used on damp hair, not wet hair. It is recommended that you towel-dry your hair before using the tool.

Hair Care Tips

Using the Revlon One Step Hair Dryer and Volumizer can help you achieve a salon-worthy blowout at home, but it's important to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and looking its best. Here are some tips to help you take care of your hair:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too frequently, as they can cause damage to your hair.
  • Use a heat protectant spray or serum when using heat styling tools.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends and breakage.
  • Eat a healthy diet and drink plenty of water to keep your hair healthy from the inside out.

With these tips and the Revlon One Step Hair Dryer and Volumizer, you'll be able to achieve a salon-worthy blowout at home in no time.
