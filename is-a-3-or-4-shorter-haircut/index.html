Is A 3 Or 4 Shorter Haircut Everything You Need To Know

Written by Christine Apr 21, 2023 Â· 6 min read

If you're considering getting a new haircut, you may have heard about the 3 or 4 shorter haircut. This style has become increasingly popular in recent years, and for good reason. In this article, we'll cover everything you need to know about this trendy haircut, including what it is, how to get it, and how to take care of it.

Description

The 3 or 4 shorter haircut is a style where the hair is cut to a length of 3 or 4 inches all around the head. This style is popular among men as it is low maintenance and easy to style. However, it can also be worn by women who want a shorter, edgier look.

How to Get the Haircut

If you're interested in getting a 3 or 4 shorter haircut, the first step is to find a reputable hair salon or barber. Once you've found a stylist you trust, you can discuss the details of the haircut and how short you want your hair to be.

During the haircut, your stylist will use clippers to trim your hair to the desired length. They may also use scissors to blend the shorter hair with the longer hair on top of your head. The process typically takes around 30 minutes to an hour, depending on the thickness and length of your hair.

Step-by-Step Guide

If you're interested in cutting your hair at home, here's a step-by-step guide to getting the 3 or 4 shorter haircut:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair.
  2. Use clippers to trim the hair on the sides and back of the head to a length of 3 or 4 inches.
  3. Blend the shorter hair with the longer hair on top of the head using scissors.
  4. Style your hair as desired.

Tips for Maintaining the Haircut

Once you have your new haircut, you'll want to keep it looking great. Here are some tips for maintaining the 3 or 4 shorter haircut:

  • Visit your stylist every 4-6 weeks for a trim.
  • Use a good quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy.
  • Style your hair with a lightweight styling product to add texture and hold.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as this can damage your hair.

FAQ

Is the 3 or 4 shorter haircut suitable for all hair types?

Yes, this haircut can work for all hair types, including straight, wavy, and curly hair.

Can women wear the 3 or 4 shorter haircut?

Absolutely! This haircut can look great on women who want a shorter, edgier look.

What face shape is best suited for the 3 or 4 shorter haircut?

This haircut can work for all face shapes, but it is particularly flattering for those with round or oval faces.

Hair Care

Keeping your hair healthy is key to maintaining any haircut. Here are some tips for keeping your hair in good condition:

  • Wash your hair every 2-3 days to avoid stripping it of its natural oils.
  • Avoid using hot water when washing your hair, as this can damage the hair follicles.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair hydrated.
  • Avoid using harsh chemicals or dyes on your hair, as this can damage the hair and cause breakage.

Now that you know everything there is to know about the 3 or 4 shorter haircut, why not give it a try? With its low maintenance style and versatility, this haircut is sure to become your new go-to look.
