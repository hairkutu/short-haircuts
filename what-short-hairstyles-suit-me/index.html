What Short Hairstyles Suit Me A Guide For 2023

Written by Luffy Apr 09, 2023 · 8 min read

Looking for a new short hairstyle but not sure which one will suit you? Whether you have a round, oval, square, or heart-shaped face, there is a hairstyle that can flatter your features. In this guide, we'll share tips on how to determine your face shape and which short hairstyles will suit you best.

How to Determine Your Face Shape

Before we dive into the different short hairstyles, it's important to determine your face shape. Here's how:

  1. Take a photo of yourself facing the camera directly.
  2. Trace the outline of your face on the photo using a pen or pencil.
  3. Compare the shape to the following:
  • Round: Your face is widest at the cheeks and has soft, curved lines.
  • Oval: Your face is longer than it is wide and has a slightly curved jawline.
  • Square: Your face has a strong, angular jawline and forehead with similar width.
  • Heart: Your face is widest at the forehead and tapers down to a pointed chin.

Short Hairstyles for Round Faces

If you have a round face, you want to create the illusion of length and height. Here are some short hairstyles that can achieve this:

  • Long pixie cut with side-swept bangs
  • Asymmetrical bob
  • Short shag
  • Textured crop

These hairstyles add volume to the crown and give the appearance of a longer face. Avoid blunt bobs or cuts that end at the chin, as they can make the face look rounder.

Short Hairstyles for Oval Faces

Oval faces are considered the most versatile because they can pull off almost any hairstyle. Here are some short hairstyles to consider:

  • Pixie cut
  • Bob with layers
  • Side-swept bangs with a bob
  • Long fringe with a textured crop

These hairstyles enhance the natural symmetry of the face and can emphasize the cheekbones. Avoid heavy bangs or hair that falls in front of the face, as it can hide the natural shape of the face.

Short Hairstyles for Square Faces

Square faces have strong, angular features that can be softened with the right hairstyle. Here are some short hairstyles to consider:

  • Soft layered bob
  • Wavy pixie cut
  • Side-swept bangs with a lob
  • Textured crop with a side part

These hairstyles add volume to the top of the head and soften the angles of the face. Avoid blunt cuts or hairstyles that end at the jawline, as they can accentuate the squareness of the face.

Short Hairstyles for Heart Faces

Heart-shaped faces have a wide forehead and narrow chin, which can be balanced out with the right hairstyle. Here are some short hairstyles to consider:

  • Pixie cut with side-swept bangs
  • Layered bob
  • Textured crop with a side part
  • Long fringe with a lob

These hairstyles draw attention to the eyes and cheekbones while softening the chin. Avoid heavy bangs or hair that falls in front of the face, as it can make the forehead look wider.

Tips for Styling Short Hair

Once you've found the perfect short hairstyle, here are some tips for styling it:

  • Invest in quality styling tools, such as a blow dryer, straightener, and curling iron.
  • Use products that are appropriate for your hair type and texture.
  • Experiment with different partings and styles to find what works best for you.
  • Visit a professional stylist for regular trims to maintain the shape of your hairstyle.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about short hairstyles:

Q: Will a short hairstyle suit me if I have thick hair?
A: Yes! A short hairstyle can be a great way to manage thick hair and make it more manageable. Q: Can I still wear a ponytail with a short hairstyle?
A: Yes! Depending on the length of your hair, you may be able to tie it back into a small ponytail or use bobby pins to create a half-up, half-down style. Q: How often do I need to get my short hair cut?
A: It depends on the style and how fast your hair grows. Generally, short hair should be trimmed every 4-6 weeks to maintain the shape of the haircut.

Hair Care

Lastly, it's important to take care of your hair to maintain its health and appearance. Here are some tips for keeping your short hair looking its best:

  • Wash your hair with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as they can damage the hair.
  • Apply a leave-in conditioner or hair oil to add moisture and shine.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and harsh weather conditions by wearing a hat or using a protective spray.

With these tips and hairstyles, you'll be sure to find a short hairstyle that suits you and your face shape. Remember to experiment and have fun with your hair!
