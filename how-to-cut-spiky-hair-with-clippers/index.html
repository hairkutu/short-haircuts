How To Cut Spiky Hair With Clippers A Complete Guide

Written by William Aug 10, 2023 Â· 7 min read

If you're looking for a stylish and low-maintenance haircut, a spiky hairstyle might be the right choice for you. Spiky haircuts are easy to maintain, versatile, and can be customized to suit any face shape or hair type. And the best part? You can achieve this edgy look at home with the help of clippers.

Description

In this article, we'll show you how to cut spiky hair with clippers step-by-step. We'll also provide you with tips on how to maintain your spiky haircut and answer some frequently asked questions about hair care.

How to Cut Spiky Hair with Clippers

If you're ready to give yourself a spiky haircut, here's what you'll need:

  • Clippers with various guard sizes
  • Scissors
  • Comb
  • Clip or hair tie
  • Mirror

Once you have all your tools handy, follow these steps:

Step 1: Wash and Dry Your Hair

Before you start cutting, make sure your hair is clean and dry. This will help you see the natural texture and shape of your hair and prevent any tugging or pulling during the cutting process.

Step 2: Decide on the Length of Your Spikes

Using your clippers, decide on the length of your spikes. Start with a longer guard size and work your way down until you find the right length for your hair. Remember, you can always go shorter, but you can't go longer once you've cut your hair.

Step 3: Cut the Sides and Back of Your Hair

Using the clippers, cut the sides and back of your hair to the desired length. Start from the bottom and work your way up, using a higher guard size for the sides and a lower guard size for the back. Use your comb to guide the clippers and create a uniform length.

Step 4: Cut the Top of Your Hair

Now it's time to tackle the top of your hair. Use your scissors to cut the hair on top to the desired length. Remember to leave some length at the front to create the spikes. You can also create layers on top to add volume and texture to your hair.

Step 5: Style Your Hair

Once you've cut your hair, it's time to style it. Use a hair gel or wax to create the spikes. Apply the product to your fingertips and work it into your hair, starting from the front and working your way back. Use your fingertips to create the spikes and add texture to your hair. You can also use a blow dryer to set the style.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you achieve the perfect spiky haircut:

  • Start with a longer guard size and work your way down to avoid cutting your hair too short.
  • Use your comb to guide the clippers and create a uniform length.
  • Leave some length at the front to create the spikes.
  • Use a hair gel or wax to create the spikes and add texture to your hair.
  • Trim your hair every 2-3 weeks to maintain the shape and length of your spiky haircut.

FAQ

1. Can I cut my hair with clippers if I have curly hair?

Yes, you can cut your hair with clippers even if you have curly hair. However, curly hair requires a different cutting technique than straight hair. It's best to consult a professional hairstylist if you're unsure how to cut curly hair.

2. How do I maintain my spiky haircut?

To maintain your spiky haircut, trim your hair every 2-3 weeks. Use a hair gel or wax to create the spikes and add texture to your hair. Avoid using too much product, as this can weigh down your hair and make it look greasy.

3. Can I create spiky hair without using clippers?

Yes, you can create spiky hair without using clippers. You can use scissors to cut your hair to the desired length and then style it using a hair gel or wax.

Hair Care

Remember to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and shiny. Use a shampoo and conditioner that's suitable for your hair type. Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as this can damage your hair and make it look dull. And don't forget to visit your hairstylist regularly for a trim and to keep your hair in good condition.
