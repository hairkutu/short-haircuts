Medium Length Hairstyles For Round Faces With Glasses

Written by Christine Jun 30, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Are you a glasses-wearing round-faced individual looking for the perfect hairstyle? Look no further than medium-length hairstyles! With the right cut and styling, medium-length hair can flatter your face shape and complement your glasses. In this article, we'll provide some tips and tricks for achieving the perfect medium-length hairstyle for your round face and glasses.

Description

Round faces typically have wider cheekbones and a round chin. The goal of a hairstyle for a round face is to elongate the face and add some angles. Glasses can also play a role in the hairstyle decision since they can add width to the face. Medium-length hair, which falls between the shoulders and the collarbone, is a great option for round faces with glasses.

How to Choose the Right Cut

When choosing a medium-length hairstyle for a round face with glasses, it's important to consider both the length and the cut. A layered cut with long layers can add movement and texture to the hair, while a blunt cut can create a sleek and sophisticated look. Avoid chin-length cuts, as they can accentuate the roundness of your face.

Step by Step Styling

Once you've chosen the right cut for your face shape and glasses, it's time to start styling! Here's a step-by-step guide:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair.
  2. Apply a heat protectant to your hair to prevent damage from styling tools.
  3. Use a large barrel curling iron to create loose waves throughout your hair.
  4. Flip your head upside down and spray a texturizing spray throughout your hair.
  5. Flip your head back up and use your fingers to separate the waves and create texture.
  6. Finish with a light hold hairspray.

Tips for Maintaining Your Hairstyle

To keep your medium-length hairstyle looking its best, follow these tips:

  • Get regular trims to maintain the shape of your cut.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy and shiny.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as it can cause damage.
  • Invest in a good dry shampoo to extend the life of your hairstyle between washes.

FAQ

Can I wear bangs with glasses and a round face?

Avoid blunt bangs, as they can make your face look rounder. Instead, opt for side-swept bangs or long, wispy bangs that can help elongate your face.

What hair color is best for a round face with glasses?

There's no one-size-fits-all answer to this question, as the best hair color for you will depend on your skin tone and personal style. However, generally, lighter hair colors can help brighten up your face, while darker colors can create a slimming effect.

Hair Care

To keep your hair healthy and looking its best, it's important to take good care of it. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your hair with a sulfate-free shampoo to prevent stripping your hair of its natural oils.
  • Use a wide-toothed comb to detangle your hair while it's wet to prevent breakage.
  • Avoid washing your hair every day, as it can dry out your hair and scalp.
  • Use a hair mask once a week to moisturize your hair.

With the right medium-length hairstyle, you can flatter your round face and complement your glasses. Follow these tips and tricks to achieve your perfect look!
