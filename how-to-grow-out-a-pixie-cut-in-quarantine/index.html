How To Grow Out A Pixie Cut In Quarantine A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Bardi Aug 29, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Are you tired of your pixie cut and ready to grow it out while in quarantine? Look no further than our guide for tips, tricks, and advice on how to grow out your pixie cut during this time.

How to

Step 1: Be patient. Growing out a pixie cut takes time, and it may not be easy, but it's important to remember that it's a process.

Step 2: Keep your hair healthy. While you're growing out your hair, it's important to keep it healthy. This means regular trims, deep conditioning treatments, and avoiding heat styling as much as possible.

Step 3: Experiment with different styles. As your hair grows out, you may find that it's not always easy to style. Experiment with different hair accessories, braids, and updos to find a style that works for you.

Step 4: Embrace the awkward stage. There will be a point during the growing out process where your hair will be in an awkward stage. Embrace it and remember that it's just a phase.

Step by Step

Step 1: Trim Your Hair

Start by trimming your hair every 6-8 weeks. This will help keep the ends healthy and prevent split ends.

Step 2: Use Hair Masks

Use a hair mask once a week to keep your hair healthy and strong. This will help prevent breakage and make your hair easier to manage.

Step 3: Avoid Heat Styling

Avoid heat styling your hair as much as possible. This will help prevent damage and breakage. If you must use heat, use a heat protectant spray.

Step 4: Experiment with Accessories

Experiment with different hair accessories, such as headbands, scarves, and hats. This will help you find a style that works for you during the awkward growing out phase.

Tips

Tip 1: Be patient. Growing out a pixie cut takes time, and it may not be easy, but it's important to remember that it's a process.

Tip 2: Stay hydrated. Drinking plenty of water will help keep your hair healthy and strong.

Tip 3: Eat a healthy diet. Eating a diet rich in vitamins and minerals will help keep your hair healthy and strong.

FAQ

Q: How long does it take to grow out a pixie cut?

It can take anywhere from 6 months to a year to grow out a pixie cut, depending on how fast your hair grows.

Q: How often should I trim my hair while growing out a pixie cut?

You should trim your hair every 6-8 weeks to keep the ends healthy and prevent split ends.

Q: Can I dye my hair while growing out a pixie cut?

Yes, you can dye your hair while growing out a pixie cut. Just be sure to use a gentle dye and avoid heat styling as much as possible.

Hair Care

While growing out a pixie cut, it's important to take care of your hair. This means regular trims, deep conditioning treatments, and avoiding heat styling as much as possible. Remember to be patient during the growing out process, and experiment with different styles and accessories to find a look that works for you.
