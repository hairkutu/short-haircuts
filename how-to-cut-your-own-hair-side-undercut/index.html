How To Cut Your Own Hair Side Undercut

Written by Oliver Aug 28, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Are you looking to switch up your hairstyle with a trendy side undercut? Rather than spending time and money at the salon, why not try cutting your own hair at home? With a few simple steps and some helpful tips, you can achieve the perfect side undercut without leaving your house. Read on to learn more!

Description

A side undercut is a trendy hairstyle where one side of the hair is shaved or cut extremely short, while the rest of the hair is left longer. It's a bold and edgy look that's perfect for those who want to stand out.

How to

Before you begin, make sure you have the necessary tools on hand. You'll need a pair of clippers, scissors, a comb, and a mirror.

Step 1: Wash and Dry Your Hair

Start by washing your hair thoroughly and letting it air dry or blow drying it. You want your hair to be clean and dry before you start cutting.

Step 2: Section Your Hair

Use a comb to section off the hair you want to cut. For a side undercut, this will typically be one side of your head. Use clips to keep the other hair out of the way.

Step 3: Decide on Your Length

Decide on the length you want for the undercut. Use the clippers to shave the hair to your desired length. You can start with a longer guard and work your way shorter, or go straight to the length you want.

Step 4: Use Scissors to Blend

Once you have the desired length, use scissors to blend the hair between the undercut and the longer hair on top. This will create a seamless transition and prevent any harsh lines.

Step 5: Check for Evenness

Use a mirror to check for evenness and make any necessary adjustments. You want the undercut to be symmetrical and even on both sides.

Step 6: Final Touches

Once you're satisfied with the length and blending, use the clippers to clean up any stray hairs and give the undercut a clean finish.

Tips

  • Start with a longer guard and work your way shorter to avoid cutting too much hair.
  • Use scissors to blend the hair for a seamless transition.
  • Check for evenness in the mirror and make adjustments as needed.
  • Be patient and take your time. Rushing can lead to mistakes.

FAQ

Can I cut my own hair if I've never done it before?

Yes, you can! Just be patient and take your time. If you're nervous, start with a longer guard and work your way shorter. You can also practice on a small section of hair before committing to the whole undercut.

Can I use regular scissors to cut my hair?

No, it's best to use hair cutting scissors. Regular scissors can cause split ends and damage to the hair.

How often do I need to touch up my undercut?

It depends on how quickly your hair grows and how long you want to keep the undercut. You may need to touch it up every 4-6 weeks.

Hair Care

After cutting your hair, it's important to take care of it to keep it healthy and looking great. Use a high-quality shampoo and conditioner, and avoid using too much heat on your hair. Regular trims can also help maintain the shape of your undercut.

With these simple steps and tips, you can achieve the perfect side undercut at home. Remember to be patient and take your time, and don't be afraid to ask for help if you need it. Good luck!
