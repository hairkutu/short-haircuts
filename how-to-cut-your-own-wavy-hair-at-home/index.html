How To Cut Your Own Wavy Hair At Home A Step By Step Guide

Written by Oliver May 02, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Are you tired of spending a fortune on haircuts? Do you have wavy hair that needs a little extra attention? If so, you might be interested in learning how to cut your own hair at home. Not only is it more affordable, but it can also be a fun and empowering experience. In this article, we'll provide you with a step-by-step guide on how to cut your own wavy hair at home.

Table of Contents

  • How to Cut Your Own Wavy Hair at Home
  • Step-by-Step Guide

How to Cut Your Own Wavy Hair at Home

Before we dive into the step-by-step guide, let's first go over what you'll need:

  • Sharp scissors
  • Comb
  • Clips or hair ties
  • Large mirror
  • Towel or cape to catch hair

Now that you have your tools ready, it's time to get started.

Step-by-Step Guide

Step 1: Start with clean, dry hair. It's important to cut your hair when it's dry, as wet hair can appear longer and thinner than it actually is.

Step 2: Comb your hair and part it down the middle. Divide your hair into two sections, one on each side of your head.

Step 3: Take one section of hair and clip or tie the rest out of the way. Comb through the section of hair to ensure there are no tangles.

Step 4: Hold the section of hair between your index and middle fingers, with your fingers pointing downwards. This will create a guide for your cut.

Step 5: Using your scissors, cut the hair below your fingers in a straight line. Don't worry about it being perfect, as we'll be adding layers in the next steps.

Step 6: Repeat steps 3-5 on the other side of your head.

Step 7: Take a small section of hair from one side of your head, close to your face. Hold it between your fingers, pointing downwards.

Step 8: Angle your scissors towards the hair and cut at a slight angle. This will create a layer and add texture to your hair.

Step 9: Repeat steps 7-8 on the other side of your head, and continue throughout your hair until you've achieved your desired length and layers.

Step 10: Use your comb to check for any uneven sections. Trim as necessary until you're happy with your cut.

Tips for a Successful DIY Haircut

Now that you know how to cut your own wavy hair at home, here are some tips to keep in mind:

  • Don't cut too much off at once. It's always easier to cut more hair later than to try to fix a mistake.
  • Invest in quality scissors. Dull scissors can lead to uneven cuts and damage your hair.
  • Take breaks if you need them. Don't rush through your haircut and risk making mistakes.
  • Have a friend or family member help you if you're nervous about cutting your own hair.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Can I cut my wavy hair when it's wet?

A: It's generally recommended to cut your hair when it's dry, as wet hair can appear longer and thinner than it actually is.

Q: How often should I cut my hair?

A: It depends on your individual hair growth and style. On average, it's recommended to get a trim every 6-8 weeks.

Q: Is it easy to cut your own hair?

A: It can be a bit nerve-wracking at first, but with practice and the right tools, it can be a fun and empowering experience.

Hair Care After Your DIY Haircut

After you've finished cutting your own wavy hair at home, it's important to take care of your hair properly. Here are some tips:

  • Wash and condition your hair as usual.
  • Use a wide-toothed comb to avoid tangles.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Consider using a leave-in conditioner or hair oil to add moisture and shine to your hair.

Now that you know how to cut your own wavy hair at home, you can save money and have fun experimenting with new styles. Just remember to take your time, use the right tools, and don't be afraid to ask for help if you need it.
