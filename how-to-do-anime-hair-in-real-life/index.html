How To Do Anime Hair In Real Life A Step By Step Guide

Are you a fan of anime and want to try out some of the hairstyles yourself? Look no further! In this article, we'll go over how to do anime hair in real life. From choosing the right style to hair care tips, we've got you covered.

Table of Contents

  • Choosing the Right Anime Hairstyle
  • Preparation
  • Cutting the Hair
  • Coloring the Hair
  • Styling the Hair

Choosing the Right Anime Hairstyle

The first step to achieving anime hair in real life is choosing the right hairstyle. There are a lot of different anime hairstyles out there, so you'll want to find one that suits your face shape and personality. Some popular anime hairstyles include:

  • Spiky hair
  • Long, flowing hair
  • Bangs
  • Twin tails
  • Ponytail
  • Short bob

Once you've chosen your hairstyle, it's time to start preparing.

Preparation

Before you start cutting or coloring your hair, it's important to prepare it properly. This means washing and drying it thoroughly, and brushing out any tangles or knots. You may also want to use a heat protectant if you plan on using heat styling tools.

It's also a good idea to do a strand test before you start coloring your hair. This will help you determine how the color will look and whether or not you need to adjust the formula.

Cutting the Hair

Once your hair is prepped, it's time to start cutting. Depending on the style you've chosen, you may need to cut your hair into layers or create specific angles. It's important to use sharp scissors and take your time, as mistakes can be difficult to fix.

If you're not comfortable cutting your own hair, it's best to go to a professional stylist who has experience with anime hairstyles.

Coloring the Hair

Many anime hairstyles involve bold, bright colors that aren't found in nature. If you want to achieve this look, you'll need to use hair dye. Be sure to choose a high-quality dye that won't damage your hair, and follow the instructions carefully.

If you're not comfortable dyeing your own hair, you can always go to a salon and have it done professionally.

Styling the Hair

Once your hair is cut and colored, it's time to style it. This may involve using a flat iron or curling iron, or simply using hair gel or hairspray to hold the style in place. Again, it's important to use high-quality products that won't damage your hair.

If you're not sure how to style your hair, you can always watch tutorials online or ask a professional stylist for advice.

Accessories

Accessories are a big part of many anime hairstyles. Depending on the style you've chosen, you may want to add hair clips, bows, or other decorative elements. Be creative and have fun with it!

Hair Care Tips

To keep your anime hair looking its best, it's important to take good care of it. This means washing it regularly with a gentle shampoo, using a deep conditioner once a week, and avoiding heat styling tools whenever possible. You may also want to invest in a silk pillowcase, as this can help prevent tangles and breakage.

FAQs

Q: Can I achieve anime hair without coloring my hair?

A: Yes! While many anime hairstyles involve bright colors, you can still achieve the look without dyeing your hair. Simply focus on getting the right cut and style, and use hair accessories to add pops of color.

Q: Do I need to use a specific type of hair gel or hairspray?

A: Not necessarily. However, it's important to choose products that won't damage your hair or weigh it down. Look for products that are labeled as "lightweight" or "non-damaging."

Q: How often should I wash my anime hair?

A: This will depend on your hair type and the products you use. In general, it's best to wash your hair no more than every other day to avoid stripping it of its natural oils.

With these tips and tricks, you'll be well on your way to achieving anime hair in real life. Remember to be patient and take your time, and don't be afraid to experiment and have fun with your new look!
