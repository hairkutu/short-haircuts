What Is The Best Curling Iron For Thick Short Hair

Written by Eveline Jun 25, 2023 Â· 7 min read

If you have thick short hair, you know that finding the right curling iron can be a challenge. You want a tool that will give you beautiful curls without damaging your hair. In this blog post, we will discuss what is the best curling iron for thick short hair and provide you with some tips on how to use it.

Description

The best curling iron for thick short hair is one that has a small barrel size, high heat settings, and ceramic or tourmaline technology. A small barrel size will allow you to create tight, defined curls, while high heat settings will ensure that your curls last all day. Ceramic or tourmaline technology will protect your hair from heat damage and promote shine.

How to Choose the Best Curling Iron for Thick Short Hair

When choosing a curling iron for your thick short hair, consider the following:

  • Barrel size: Look for a curling iron with a barrel size between 0.5 and 1 inch. This will allow you to create tight, defined curls.
  • Heat settings: Choose a curling iron with high heat settings, between 350 and 450 degrees Fahrenheit. This will ensure that your curls last all day.
  • Technology: Look for a curling iron with ceramic or tourmaline technology. This will protect your hair from heat damage and promote shine.

Step-by-Step Guide to Curling Your Thick Short Hair

Follow these steps to curl your thick short hair:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair. Apply a heat protectant spray to your hair to protect it from heat damage.
  2. Section your hair into small sections, using clips to hold each section in place.
  3. Take a small section of hair and clamp the curling iron at the base of the section.
  4. Twist the curling iron away from your face, wrapping the hair around the barrel of the curling iron.
  5. Hold the curling iron in place for 10-15 seconds, then release the curl.
  6. Repeat steps 3-5 on each section of hair, working your way around your head.
  7. Once you have curled all of your hair, run your fingers through your hair to loosen the curls.
  8. Finish with a light-hold hairspray to keep your curls in place.

Tips for Curling Your Thick Short Hair

Here are some tips to help you curl your thick short hair:

  • Use a heat protectant spray to protect your hair from heat damage.
  • Section your hair into small sections to ensure that each curl is defined.
  • Twist the curling iron away from your face to create a natural-looking curl.
  • Hold the curling iron in place for 10-15 seconds to ensure that the curl sets.
  • Run your fingers through your hair to loosen the curls and create a more natural look.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about curling irons for thick short hair:

What size barrel should I use?

A barrel size between 0.5 and 1 inch is best for thick short hair.

What heat setting should I use?

Choose a curling iron with high heat settings, between 350 and 450 degrees Fahrenheit.

What technology should I look for?

Look for a curling iron with ceramic or tourmaline technology to protect your hair from heat damage and promote shine.

Hair Care Tips for Thick Short Hair

Here are some hair care tips for thick short hair:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo to prevent your hair from drying out.
  • Condition your hair regularly to keep it moisturized.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair to prevent breakage.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often to prevent heat damage.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.

Now that you know what is the best curling iron for thick short hair, you can create beautiful curls that will last all day. Just remember to choose a curling iron with a small barrel size, high heat settings, and ceramic or tourmaline technology, and follow our step-by-step guide to curling your hair. With some practice and a little patience, you'll have gorgeous curls in no time!
