Best Hair Cut For Round Face Boy A Guide To Finding The Perfect Style

Written by Luffy Jun 13, 2023 Â· 7 min read

If you're a boy with a round face, finding the perfect hairstyle can be a challenge. You want a cut that flatters your face shape while still expressing your individuality. Luckily, there are plenty of options that can work for you. In this guide, we'll explore the best hair cuts for round face boys, how to get them, and how to maintain them.

Table of Contents

  • How to Choose the Best Hair Cut for Round Face Boy
  • Step by Step Guide to Getting the Perfect Cut
  • Tips for Maintaining Your Hair Cut
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care for Round Face Boys

How to Choose the Best Hair Cut for Round Face Boy

The first step in finding the best hair cut for round face boy is to determine your face shape. To do this, measure the width of your forehead, cheekbones, and jawline. If these measurements are roughly equal, you have a round face. Once you know your face shape, you can start looking for cuts that flatter your features.

For round face boys, the best hair cuts are those that add height and length to the face. This can be achieved through layered cuts, fades, and textured styles. Avoid styles that add width to the face, such as blunt bangs or heavy layers around the cheeks.

Best Hair Cuts for Round Face Boys

Here are some of the best hair cuts for round face boys:

  • Short back and sides with a longer top
  • Textured crop with a fringe
  • Undercut with a side-swept fringe
  • Layered cut with a side part
  • Faux hawk with a fade

Step by Step Guide to Getting the Perfect Cut

Once you've chosen the best hair cut for round face boy, it's time to get it done. Here's a step by step guide:

  1. Show your stylist a picture of the style you want
  2. Ask for a cut that adds height and length to your face
  3. Choose a length that works for your hair type and texture
  4. Ask your stylist to add layers or texture to the cut
  5. Make sure your stylist blends the cut seamlessly
  6. Ask for styling tips to recreate the look at home

Tips for Maintaining Your Hair Cut

Once you have the perfect hair cut for round face boy, it's important to maintain it. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly to keep it clean and healthy
  • Use a good quality shampoo and conditioner
  • Invest in styling products that work for your hair type
  • Visit your stylist for regular trims to keep the cut looking fresh
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as they can damage your hair

FAQs

Q: Can round face boys have long hair?

A: Yes, round face boys can have long hair. However, it's important to choose a style that adds length to the face, such as layers or a side part.

Q: What should I do if I don't like my hair cut?

A: If you're unhappy with your hair cut, talk to your stylist. They may be able to make adjustments to the cut to make it more flattering.

Q: How often should I wash my hair?

A: It depends on your hair type and texture. Generally, you should wash your hair every 2-3 days.

Hair Care for Round Face Boys

Good hair care is essential for keeping your hair healthy and looking its best. Here are some tips for hair care for round face boys:

  • Choose a shampoo and conditioner that works for your hair type
  • Avoid using too much heat when styling your hair
  • Use a hair mask once a week to keep your hair nourished
  • Visit your stylist regularly for trims and touch-ups
  • Eat a healthy diet to promote healthy hair growth

With these tips and tricks, you can find the best hair cut for round face boy and keep it looking great. Whether you choose a short back and sides or a textured crop, there are plenty of styles that can flatter your face shape and express your individuality.
