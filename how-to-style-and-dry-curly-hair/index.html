How To Style And Dry Curly Hair Tips And Tricks

Written by Eveline May 31, 2023 · 7 min read

Curly hair can be a blessing and a curse. On one hand, it has a natural bounce and volume that straight hair simply can't match. On the other hand, it can be difficult to tame and style. If you're struggling to get your curly hair looking its best, don't worry – we've got you covered. In this article, we'll go over some tips and tricks for styling and drying curly hair.

Table of Contents

  • How to Style and Dry Curly Hair
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips and Tricks
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care for Curly Hair

How to Style and Dry Curly Hair

The first step to styling and drying curly hair is to understand your hair type. Not all curly hair is the same, and different types of curls require different care and styling techniques. In general, curly hair can be divided into three categories:

  • Type 2: wavy hair with loose, S-shaped curls
  • Type 3: curly hair with tight, spiral-shaped curls
  • Type 4: coily hair with tightly-coiled, zig-zag shaped curls

Once you've identified your hair type, you can start experimenting with different styling and drying techniques to find what works best for you.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide on how to style and dry curly hair:

  1. Shampoo and condition your hair using products specifically designed for curly hair.
  2. Gently blot your hair dry with a microfiber towel or t-shirt. Avoid rubbing your hair vigorously, as this can cause frizz.
  3. Apply a curl-defining product, such as a curl cream or gel, to your hair. Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to distribute the product evenly throughout your hair.
  4. Scrunch your hair gently with your hands to encourage your curls to form.
  5. Use a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer to dry your hair. Flip your head upside down and use the diffuser to dry your hair in sections, scrunching your curls as you go.
  6. Once your hair is dry, use a small amount of hair oil or serum to add shine and tame any frizz.

Tips and Tricks

Here are some additional tips and tricks for styling and drying curly hair:

  • Avoid brushing your hair when it's dry, as this can cause frizz. If you need to detangle your hair, do so when it's wet and use a wide-tooth comb.
  • Experiment with different curl-defining products to find what works best for your hair type.
  • Consider using a satin or silk pillowcase to reduce friction and prevent frizz while you sleep.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools, such as flat irons or curling irons, as they can damage your hair and cause it to lose its natural curl pattern.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about styling and drying curly hair:

Q: How often should I wash my curly hair?
A: It depends on your hair type and how oily your scalp is. As a general rule, you should aim to wash your hair every 2-3 days. Q: How can I prevent frizz in my curly hair?
A: Use a microfiber towel or t-shirt to blot your hair dry, avoid brushing your hair when it's dry, and use a curl-defining product to help your curls hold their shape.

Hair Care for Curly Hair

In addition to styling and drying techniques, it's important to take good care of your curly hair to keep it healthy and looking its best. Here are some tips for hair care for curly hair:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner specifically designed for curly hair.
  • Avoid using hot water when you wash your hair, as it can strip your hair of its natural oils.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair moisturized.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and heat by wearing a hat or using a heat protectant spray.
  • Get regular trims to keep your ends healthy and prevent split ends.

By following these tips and tricks for styling and drying curly hair, you can achieve beautiful, healthy curls that you'll love showing off.
