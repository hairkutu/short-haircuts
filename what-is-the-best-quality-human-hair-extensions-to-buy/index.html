What Is The Best Quality Human Hair Extensions To Buy

If you're looking to buy human hair extensions, you're likely overwhelmed with the number of options available. From different types of hair to different methods of installation, there's a lot to consider. But ultimately, one of the most important factors is the quality of the hair itself. So, what is the best quality human hair extensions to buy? In this article, we'll break down the different types of hair extensions and help you make an informed decision.

Types of Human Hair Extensions

There are two main types of human hair extensions: virgin and non-virgin. Virgin hair is hair that has never been chemically processed, while non-virgin hair has been treated with chemicals to change its color or texture.

Within these two categories, there are also different types of hair, such as Brazilian, Indian, and Malaysian. Brazilian hair is known for its thickness and durability, while Indian hair is known for its versatility and natural shine. Malaysian hair is often used for its softness and silkiness.

Ultimately, the type of hair you choose will depend on your personal preferences and the look you're going for. But regardless of the type of hair, it's important to choose high-quality, 100% human hair extensions.

How to Choose the Best Quality Human Hair Extensions

When shopping for human hair extensions, there are a few key things to look for:

  • Quality: Look for extensions made from 100% human hair that is soft, silky, and free of tangles and shedding.
  • Color: Make sure the color of the extensions matches your natural hair color or the color you want to dye your hair.
  • Length: Choose extensions that are the same length as your natural hair or slightly longer.
  • Texture: Consider the texture of the extensions and choose one that matches your natural hair texture.
  • Method of installation: Decide on a method of installation that works best for you, such as clip-ins, sew-ins, or tape-ins.

By considering these factors, you can ensure that you're choosing the best quality human hair extensions for your needs.

Step-by-Step Guide to Installing Human Hair Extensions

The method of installation will vary depending on the type of extensions you choose. Here is a simple step-by-step guide to installing clip-in extensions:

  1. Start by washing and drying your natural hair.
  2. Section off your hair and clip in the extensions, starting at the bottom and working your way up.
  3. Blend the extensions with your natural hair by styling and brushing them together.

For sew-in or tape-in extensions, it's best to have a professional stylist install them to ensure they are done correctly.

Tips for Maintaining Human Hair Extensions

Once you have your human hair extensions installed, it's important to take care of them properly. Here are a few tips:

  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner to wash your extensions.
  • Avoid using heat tools on your extensions, or use a heat protectant if you must.
  • Brush your extensions regularly with a soft-bristled brush to prevent tangles.
  • Store your extensions in a cool, dry place when not in use.
  • Use a silk pillowcase to prevent friction and tangling while you sleep.

FAQs About Human Hair Extensions

Q: How long do human hair extensions last?

A: With proper care, human hair extensions can last up to a year or more.

Q: Can I dye my human hair extensions?

A: Yes, you can dye human hair extensions, but it's best to have a professional stylist do it to avoid damaging the hair.

Q: Can I swim with human hair extensions?

A: Yes, you can swim with human hair extensions, but it's best to wear a swim cap and rinse your hair thoroughly afterwards.

Hair Care for Human Hair Extensions

Overall, the best quality human hair extensions to buy are those that are made from 100% human hair, are high-quality, and match your natural hair color, texture, and length. By choosing the right type of extensions and taking care of them properly, you can enjoy beautiful, long-lasting hair extensions.

If you're unsure about the best type of human hair extensions for you, consult with a professional stylist for advice.
