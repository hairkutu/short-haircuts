What Haircuts Are Good For Round Faces

Written by Eveline Jul 03, 2023 Â· 9 min read

Round faces are characterized by their soft, curved features and often lack prominent cheekbones or jawlines. While this face shape is beautiful in its own right, it can be challenging to find the perfect haircut that flatters it. In this article, we'll share some tips on what haircuts are good for round faces and how to style them.

Description of Round Faces

A round face is characterized by a face shape that is almost as wide as it is long, with soft curves and a lack of prominent cheekbones or jawlines. The forehead and jawline are often the same width, and the chin is usually rounded. If you have a round face, you may want to choose a haircut that adds height to the top of your head, creates angles in your face, or elongates your face shape.

How to Choose a Haircut for Round Faces

When choosing a haircut for a round face, it's essential to consider your face shape and hair texture. Some haircuts will flatter a round face more than others. Here are some tips to keep in mind:

  • Choose haircuts that add height to the top of your head, such as a layered cut or a pixie cut.
  • Avoid haircuts that add width to your face, such as blunt bangs or a chin-length bob.
  • Consider haircuts that create angles in your face, such as an asymmetrical bob or a side-swept fringe.
  • Opt for haircuts that elongate your face shape, such as a long layered cut or a shaggy lob.

Step-by-Step Guide to Styling Hair for Round Faces

Once you've chosen a haircut that flatters your round face, it's essential to style it correctly. Here's a step-by-step guide:

  1. Start with clean, towel-dried hair.
  2. Apply a volumizing mousse or spray to the roots of your hair, focusing on the crown of your head.
  3. Blow-dry your hair with a round brush, lifting the roots as you go to add volume to the top of your head.
  4. Create loose waves with a curling iron or wand, focusing on the ends of your hair. Avoid curling your hair too close to your roots, as this can add width to your face.
  5. Finish with a light-hold hairspray to set your style in place.

Tips for Maintaining Your Haircut

To keep your haircut looking its best, it's essential to maintain it regularly. Here are some tips:

  • Visit your hairstylist for regular trims every six to eight weeks to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair hydrated and healthy.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as this can damage your hair and make it look dull.
  • Avoid using too many styling products, as this can weigh down your hair and make it look flat.

FAQs About Haircuts for Round Faces

Q: Can I wear a bob if I have a round face?

A: Yes, you can wear a bob if you have a round face, but it's essential to choose a longer bob that elongates your face shape. Avoid chin-length bobs, as these can make your face look wider.

Q: What hairstyles should I avoid if I have a round face?

A: You should avoid hairstyles that add width to your face, such as blunt bangs, chin-length bobs, and one-length haircuts. Instead, opt for hairstyles that create angles in your face, such as layered cuts and side-swept fringes.

Q: Can I wear my hair in a high ponytail if I have a round face?

A: Yes, you can wear your hair in a high ponytail if you have a round face, but it's essential to add volume to the top of your head to elongate your face shape. You can do this by teasing your hair at the crown of your head or using a volumizing mousse or spray.

Hair Care Tips for Round Faces

Keeping your hair healthy is essential for maintaining a beautiful haircut. Here are some hair care tips to keep in mind:

  • Wash your hair every two to three days to prevent it from becoming too dry or oily.
  • Avoid using hot water to wash your hair, as this can strip your hair of its natural oils and make it look dull.
  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and prevent breakage.
  • Avoid using too many styling products, as this can weigh down your hair and make it look flat.
  • Protect your hair from heat damage by using a heat protectant spray before using hot tools.

Now that you know what haircuts are good for round faces, you can choose a style that flatters your unique features. Remember to consider your face shape and hair texture when choosing a haircut and style it correctly to keep it looking its best. With these tips, you can rock a beautiful hairstyle that makes you feel confident and beautiful.
