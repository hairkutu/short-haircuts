How To Cut Men s Hair At Home Clippers A Step By Step Guide

Written by William Sep 12, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Are you tired of spending a fortune at the barbershop every few weeks? Learning how to cut men's hair at home clippers is easier than you might think. With a little practice and some basic tools, you can achieve professional-grade results without ever leaving your house. In this guide, we'll walk you through everything you need to know to get started.

Table of Contents

  • Tools You'll Need
  • Prepping Your Hair
  • Cutting Your Hair
  • Cleaning Up
  • Tips for Success
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care After the Cut

Tools You'll Need

Before you get started, you'll need to gather a few basic tools:

  • Clippers with various guards
  • Scissors
  • Comb
  • Cape or towel to protect your clothing
  • Spray bottle with water
  • Mirror

Once you have everything you need, you're ready to start.

Prepping Your Hair

Start by washing your hair and letting it dry completely. Comb it out so it's tangle-free and easy to work with. If your hair is particularly thick or long, you may want to use scissors to trim it down a bit before you start with the clippers.

Next, use the clippers with the longest guard to trim your hair to an even length all over. This will make it easier to work with as you start to create your desired style.

Cutting Your Hair

Now it's time to start cutting! Begin with the sides and back of your head. Use the clipper with the appropriate guard to trim your hair to your desired length. Work from the bottom up, using the comb to lift each section of hair as you go. Make sure to use light pressure and take your time to avoid any mistakes.

Once you've finished the sides and back, move on to the top of your head. Use scissors to trim any longer pieces, and then use the clippers to create your desired length and style. Again, work carefully and take your time to avoid any errors.

Cleaning Up

Once you've finished cutting your hair, it's time to clean up any stray hairs. Use the clippers without a guard to carefully trim around your hairline and the back of your neck. You can also use scissors to trim any particularly unruly hairs.

Finally, take a shower to wash away any loose hairs and freshen up. Once you're clean and dry, use a mirror to check your work and make any final adjustments.

Tips for Success

  • Start with a longer guard and work your way down to avoid any mistakes.
  • Take your time and work carefully.
  • Don't hesitate to ask a friend or family member for help if you need it.
  • Watch online tutorials to learn new techniques and get inspiration.

FAQs

Can I cut my hair with just scissors?

While it's possible to cut your hair with just scissors, it's much easier and more efficient to use clippers. Clippers allow you to create even lengths and styles with less effort and time.

What if I make a mistake?

If you make a mistake, don't panic. You can always trim a little more or use scissors to create a different style. Worst case, you can always wear a hat until your hair grows out a bit.

Hair Care After the Cut

Once you've finished cutting your hair, it's important to take good care of it to maintain your new style. Use a high-quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and hydrated. You may also want to use styling products like gel or wax to create your desired look.

With a little practice and some basic tools, you can learn how to cut men's hair at home clippers and achieve professional-grade results. Give it a try and see how much money and time you can save!
