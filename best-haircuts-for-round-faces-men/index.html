Best Haircuts For Round Faces Men In 2023

Written by William May 25, 2023 Â· 7 min read

If you're a man with a round face, finding the right haircut can be a challenge. You want a style that complements your face shape and highlights your best features, while minimizing any roundness. Fortunately, there are plenty of great haircuts for round faces men that can do just that. In this article, we'll explore the best haircuts for round faces men in 2023, with tips on how to style and care for your new 'do.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to
  • Step by Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

Round faces typically have a wider forehead, full cheeks, and a rounded jawline. The goal when choosing a haircut is to create the illusion of a more oval-shaped face. This can be achieved by adding volume and height to the top of the head, while keeping the sides and back shorter. Avoid styles that are too short or too long, as they can make your face appear rounder.

How to

When choosing a haircut for a round face, consider the following factors:

  • Your hair type and texture
  • Your lifestyle and personal style
  • Your face shape (round, square, oval, etc.)
  • Your hairline and forehead size

Consult with your barber or stylist to find the best haircut for your needs. They can make recommendations based on your face shape, hair type, and personal style.

Step by Step

Here are some of the best haircuts for round faces men in 2023:

1. Short Crop

This classic style is perfect for men with round faces. Keep the sides short and tapered, while leaving some length on top. Style with a texturizing paste or wax for a messy, tousled look.

2. Side Part

A side part can help elongate your face and create the illusion of a more oval shape. Keep the sides and back short, and comb the top over to one side. Use a pomade or gel to hold the style in place.

3. Messy Quiff

This modern style adds volume and height to the top of the head, while keeping the sides and back short. Use a blow dryer and some styling product to create a messy, textured look.

4. Faux Hawk

A faux hawk can help elongate your face and create a more angular look. Keep the sides short and tapered, while leaving some length on top. Style with a strong-hold gel or pomade for a sleek, edgy look.

Tips

Here are some tips for styling and maintaining your haircut:

  • Use a high-quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and nourished.
  • Invest in a good styling product, such as a pomade or wax, to help hold your style in place.
  • Visit your barber or stylist regularly for trims and touch-ups.
  • Experiment with different styles and products to find what works best for you.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about haircuts for round faces men:

1. Can I still get a short haircut if I have a round face?

Yes, you can still get a short haircut if you have a round face. Just make sure to keep the sides and top balanced, and avoid going too short on the sides.

2. What kind of styling product should I use for a messy, textured look?

Use a texturizing paste or wax for a messy, textured look. These products add volume and hold without making your hair look greasy or weighed down.

3. How often should I visit my barber or stylist?

Visit your barber or stylist every 4-6 weeks for trims and touch-ups. This will help keep your haircut looking fresh and stylish.

Hair Care

Proper hair care is essential for maintaining a healthy, attractive hairstyle. Here are some tips for keeping your hair looking its best:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a high-quality shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using hot tools, such as blow dryers and flat irons, too often.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and wind with a hat or scarf.
  • Eat a healthy diet rich in vitamins and minerals to promote hair growth and health.

By following these tips and choosing the right haircut for your face shape, you can achieve a stylish, flattering look that highlights your best features. Whether you prefer a classic short crop or a trendy messy quiff, there's a perfect haircut out there for you.
