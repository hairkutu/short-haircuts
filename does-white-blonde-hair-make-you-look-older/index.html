Does White Blonde Hair Make You Look Older

Written by Luffy Jun 06, 2023 · 6 min read

White blonde hair has been a popular hair color trend for years now. It’s a bold and statement-making color that can make you stand out in a crowd. But, there's a long-standing myth that white blonde hair makes you look older. In this blog post, we'll explore this myth and find out if it's true.

Description

White blonde hair is a very light shade of blonde that’s almost white in color. It’s achieved by lightening the hair to the point where it loses all its pigmentation. This process can be damaging to the hair, but the end result can be stunning. White blonde hair has been seen on many celebrities, including Kim Kardashian, Lady Gaga, and Gwen Stefani.

How To

If you’re considering going white blonde, there are a few things you should know. First, it's important to find a reputable stylist who has experience with this color. The process of lightening hair to this shade can be tricky and requires a lot of skill. Additionally, you should be prepared for regular touch-ups to maintain the color.

Step by Step

To achieve white blonde hair, your stylist will first need to lighten your hair to a pale yellow color. This is usually done with bleach. Once your hair is light enough, they will apply a toner to neutralize any yellow tones and create the white blonde shade. The process can take several hours and may require multiple appointments.

Tips

If you’re worried about looking older with white blonde hair, there are a few things you can do to keep your look youthful. First, make sure to keep your hair healthy and hydrated with regular deep conditioning treatments. Additionally, consider adding some layers or bangs to your hairstyle to create a more youthful look.

FAQ

1. Does white blonde hair make you look older?

There's no evidence to suggest that white blonde hair makes you look older. Age is just a number, and your hair color doesn't determine how old you look.

2. Is white blonde hair damaging to the hair?

Yes, the process of lightening hair to this shade can be damaging. It's important to find a stylist who uses high-quality products and takes steps to minimize damage.

3. How often do you need to touch up white blonde hair?

This depends on how quickly your hair grows and how quickly your natural color starts to show. Some people may need touch-ups every 4-6 weeks, while others can go longer.

Hair Care

If you decide to go white blonde, it's important to take good care of your hair. Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and hydrated. Additionally, use a heat protectant when styling with heat tools, and avoid over-styling or using harsh chemicals.

Conclusion

So, does white blonde hair make you look older? The answer is no. Age is just a number, and your hair color doesn't determine how old you look. If you're considering this bold hair color, make sure to find a reputable stylist and take good care of your hair to keep it healthy and vibrant.
