Men s Hairstyle For Round Face Shape Long Hair A Complete Guide

Written by Frank Mar 19, 2023 Â· 8 min read

Are you a man with a round face shape and long hair? Do you struggle to find the perfect hairstyle that flatters your features? Look no further! In this guide, we will walk you through everything you need to know about men's hairstyle for round face shape long hair, including how to choose the right cut, styling tips, and hair care.

Table of Contents

  1. How to Choose the Right Cut
  2. Styling Tips
  3. Step-by-Step Guide
  4. Tips for Maintenance
  5. FAQs

How to Choose the Right Cut

When it comes to men's hairstyle for round face shape long hair, the key is to create the illusion of length and sharpness. Avoid cuts that add volume to your face, such as blunt bangs or layers that end at your chin. Instead, opt for hairstyles that elongate your features, like layered cuts that fall below your shoulders or slicked-back styles.

Some popular cuts that work well for men with round face shape long hair include:

  • Long layers
  • Slicked-back undercut
  • Side-parted long hair
  • Textured waves
  • Loose curls

Consult with your hairstylist to find the perfect cut for your face shape and hair type. They can help you choose a style that flatters your features and suits your lifestyle.

Styling Tips

Once you have your perfect cut, it's time to learn how to style it. Here are some tips for styling men's hairstyle for round face shape long hair:

  • Invest in a good quality hair dryer to help you achieve your desired look.
  • Use a wide-toothed comb to detangle your hair gently.
  • Apply a heat protectant spray before using any hot tools.
  • Use hair wax or pomade to add texture and hold to your hair.
  • Experiment with different styles to find what works best for you.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide on how to style men's hairstyle for round face shape long hair:

  1. Wash your hair with a shampoo and conditioner that suits your hair type.
  2. Towel dry your hair gently to remove excess water.
  3. Apply a heat protectant spray to your hair.
  4. Blow-dry your hair using a round brush to create volume and texture.
  5. Apply a small amount of hair wax or pomade to your hair, focusing on the ends to create definition.
  6. Use a comb or your fingers to style your hair as desired.
  7. Finish with a hairspray to hold your style in place.

Tips for Maintenance

Maintaining men's hairstyle for round face shape long hair is essential to keep it looking healthy and stylish. Here are some tips for hair maintenance:

  • Trim your hair every six to eight weeks to prevent split ends and maintain your desired length.
  • Avoid using hot tools too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy and hydrated.
  • Avoid using too much product, as it can weigh down your hair and make it look greasy.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and other environmental factors by wearing a hat or using a hair sunscreen.

FAQs

Q: Can men with round face shape long hair wear a ponytail?

A: Yes, men with round face shape long hair can wear a ponytail. However, make sure to tie it low on the nape of your neck to create the illusion of length and avoid adding volume to your face.

Q: Can men with round face shape long hair wear a beard?

A: Yes, men with round face shape long hair can wear a beard. A well-groomed beard can help create the illusion of a sharper jawline and balance out your features.

Q: How often should I wash my hair?

A: It depends on your hair type and lifestyle. If you have oily hair or exercise regularly, you may need to wash it every day. However, if you have dry hair, you may only need to wash it once or twice a week.

Hair Care

Proper hair care is essential to keep your men's hairstyle for round face shape long hair looking healthy and stylish. Here are some tips for hair care:

  • Use a shampoo and conditioner that suits your hair type.
  • Avoid using hot tools too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy and hydrated.
  • Avoid using too much product, as it can weigh down your hair and make it look greasy.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and other environmental factors by wearing a hat or using a hair sunscreen.

By following these tips and tricks, you can rock your men's hairstyle for round face shape long hair with confidence and style. Remember to consult with your hairstylist for personalized advice and recommendations.
