How To Cut Short Men s Hair With Clippers A Step By Step Guide

Written by William Sep 01, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Cutting men's hair with clippers is a great way to achieve a short, low-maintenance hairstyle. With the right tools and technique, you can achieve a professional-looking cut from the comfort of your own home. In this guide, we'll walk you through the steps to cut short men's hair with clippers.

Description

Before we dive into the steps, it's important to understand the tools and terminology used in cutting men's hair with clippers. Clippers are electric cutting tools that come with different guard sizes to adjust the length of the cut. The guard size is indicated by a number, with a smaller number indicating a shorter cut. For short men's haircuts, you'll typically use guard sizes 1-4. You'll also need a comb, scissors, and a mirror.

How to Cut Short Men's Hair with Clippers

Step 1: Wash and Dry the Hair

Start by washing the hair with shampoo and conditioner. Towel-dry the hair and comb it in the desired direction. Make sure the hair is completely dry before you start cutting.

Step 2: Choose the Guard Size

Choose the guard size based on the desired length of the cut. For a very short cut, start with a guard size 1 or 2. For a slightly longer cut, use a guard size 3 or 4.

Step 3: Start at the Bottom

Start at the back of the head, using the clippers without a guard to create a clean line around the perimeter of the hair. Then, attach the guard and start cutting from the bottom up. Use the comb to lift the hair and run the clippers through it in a smooth motion.

Step 4: Blend the Sides and Top

Once you've cut the bottom, remove the guard and use the clippers to blend the sides and top. Start at the sides and work your way up, using a slightly longer guard size than the bottom. Use a comb to lift the hair and blend the layers together.

Step 5: Use Scissors to Trim the Top

For the top of the hair, use scissors to trim any longer strands. Comb the hair straight up and cut across to achieve an even length. Use the scissors to blend any uneven areas with the sides and back.

Step 6: Clean Up the Neckline

Use the clippers without a guard to clean up the neckline and around the ears. Use a mirror to check for any uneven areas and make any necessary adjustments.

Tips for Cutting Men's Hair with Clippers

  • Start with a longer guard size and work your way down to achieve the desired length.
  • Use a comb to lift the hair and ensure an even cut.
  • Use a mirror to check for any uneven areas and make adjustments.
  • Clean the clippers after each use to maintain their performance.

FAQ

How often should men get their hair cut?

Men should get their hair cut every 4-6 weeks to maintain the shape and style of the cut.

Can clippers be used for longer hairstyles?

Clippers are best suited for short haircuts, but they can be used to trim longer hair as well.

Do I need to use scissors to cut men's hair?

While clippers can achieve a short cut, scissors are necessary for blending and trimming longer hair.

Hair Care Tips for Men

  • Wash and condition hair regularly to maintain its health and shine.
  • Avoid using hot tools like blow dryers and straighteners too often, as they can damage the hair.
  • Use a leave-in conditioner or hair oil to keep hair moisturized.
  • Eat a balanced diet and drink plenty of water to promote healthy hair growth.

With these tips and techniques, you can confidently cut short men's hair with clippers at home. Remember to take your time and follow the steps carefully to achieve a professional-looking cut. Happy cutting!
