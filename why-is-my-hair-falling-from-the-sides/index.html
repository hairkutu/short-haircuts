Why Is My Hair Falling From The Sides

Written by Oliver Mar 31, 2023 · 9 min read

If you’ve been noticing hair loss from the sides of your head, you’re not alone. This is a common problem that affects many people, and it can be caused by a variety of factors. In this article, we’ll explore the reasons why your hair might be falling out from the sides, and what you can do to address the issue.

  • How to Prevent Hair Loss from the Sides
  • Step-by-Step Guide to Treating Hair Loss from the Sides

  • FAQs About Hair Loss from the Sides
  • Hair Care Products to Help Prevent Hair Loss from the Sides

Causes of Hair Loss from the Sides

There are several reasons why your hair might be falling out from the sides:

Genetics

One of the most common causes of hair loss from the sides is genetics. If your family has a history of hair loss, you’re more likely to experience it yourself. This type of hair loss is known as androgenetic alopecia, and it’s more common in men than women.

Hormonal Changes

Hormonal changes can also cause hair loss from the sides. Women may experience hair loss during pregnancy, after giving birth, or during menopause. Men may experience hair loss as they age, as their testosterone levels decrease.

Stress

Stress can also contribute to hair loss from the sides. When you’re stressed, your body produces more cortisol, which can lead to hair loss. If you’re experiencing a lot of stress, it’s important to find ways to manage it, such as through exercise, meditation, or therapy.

How to Prevent Hair Loss from the Sides

There are several steps you can take to prevent hair loss from the sides:

Eat a Healthy Diet

Eating a healthy diet that’s rich in vitamins and minerals can help prevent hair loss. Make sure you’re getting enough protein, iron, and vitamin C, as these nutrients are important for hair health.

Avoid Harsh Hairstyles

Avoid hairstyles that pull on your hair, such as tight braids or ponytails. These can cause hair breakage and lead to hair loss over time.

Use Gentle Hair Products

Use gentle hair products that won’t damage your hair. Look for products that are sulfate-free and don’t contain harsh chemicals.

Step-by-Step Guide to Treating Hair Loss from the Sides

If you’re already experiencing hair loss from the sides, there are steps you can take to address the issue:

Visit a Dermatologist

If you’re experiencing significant hair loss, it’s a good idea to visit a dermatologist. They can help determine the underlying cause of your hair loss and recommend appropriate treatment options.

Try Hair Growth Products

There are several hair growth products on the market that can help stimulate hair growth and prevent further hair loss. Look for products that contain ingredients like minoxidil or biotin.

Consider Hair Transplant Surgery

If you’re experiencing severe hair loss, you may want to consider hair transplant surgery. This involves transplanting hair follicles from one part of your scalp to another.

Tips for Dealing with Hair Loss from the Sides

Dealing with hair loss can be stressful, but there are steps you can take to make it easier:

Be Gentle with Your Hair

Avoid harsh brushing or styling, and be gentle when washing your hair. Use a wide-toothed comb to detangle your hair, and avoid using hot tools like curling irons or straighteners.

Try Hair Accessories

If you’re self-conscious about your hair loss, try using hair accessories like headbands or scarves to cover up the affected area.

Consider Counseling

Hair loss can be emotionally challenging, and it’s important to take care of your mental health as well. Consider seeing a counselor or therapist to help you cope with the stress of hair loss.

FAQs About Hair Loss from the Sides

Q: Is hair loss from the sides permanent?

A: It depends on the underlying cause of the hair loss. In some cases, hair loss may be temporary and can be reversed with treatment. In other cases, it may be permanent.

Q: Can stress cause hair loss from the sides?

A: Yes, stress can contribute to hair loss from the sides. When you’re stressed, your body produces more cortisol, which can lead to hair loss.

Q: What can I do to prevent hair loss from the sides?

A: Eating a healthy diet, avoiding harsh hairstyles, and using gentle hair products can all help prevent hair loss from the sides.

Hair Care Products to Help Prevent Hair Loss from the Sides

Here are some hair care products that can help prevent hair loss from the sides:

1. Nioxin Hair System Kit

This kit contains shampoo, conditioner, and a scalp treatment that can help stimulate hair growth and prevent hair loss.

2. Biotin Hair Growth Serum

This serum contains biotin and other ingredients that can help promote hair growth and prevent hair loss.

3. Viviscal Hair Growth Supplements

These supplements contain a blend of vitamins and minerals that can help promote hair growth and prevent hair loss.

Overall, if you’re experiencing hair loss from the sides, it’s important to take action as soon as possible. By following the tips and recommendations in this article, you can help prevent further hair loss and promote healthy hair growth.
