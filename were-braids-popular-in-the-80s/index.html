Were Braids Popular In The 80S

Written by Daniel Aug 05, 2023 Â· 6 min read

If you're a fan of retro hairstyles, you might be wondering if braids were popular in the 80s. The answer is yes! Braids were a popular hairstyle in the 80s, and they were often worn by both men and women. In this article, we'll take a closer look at the history of braids in the 80s and how you can recreate this iconic look today.

History of Braids in the 80s

Braids have been around for centuries, but they really took off in the 80s. This was the time of big hair, bright colors, and bold fashion statements. Braids were a perfect fit for this era, as they allowed people to express themselves and stand out from the crowd.

There were many different types of braids that were popular in the 80s. Some of the most popular styles included French braids, Dutch braids, and cornrows. These styles were often worn with colorful scrunchies, headbands, and other accessories.

How to Create 80s Braids

Creating 80s braids is easier than you might think. Here's a step-by-step guide:

Step 1: Start with Clean Hair

Before you begin braiding, make sure your hair is clean and dry. This will help your braids hold their shape and last longer.

Step 2: Choose Your Braid Style

There are many different braid styles to choose from, so pick one that works best for your hair type and length. French braids and Dutch braids are great for longer hair, while cornrows work well for shorter hair.

Step 3: Section Your Hair

Once you've chosen your braid style, section your hair into small sections. This will make it easier to braid and ensure that your braids are even.

Step 4: Start Braiding

Start braiding your hair, following the steps for your chosen braid style. Make sure to keep your braids tight and even as you go.

Step 5: Secure Your Braids

Once you've finished braiding, secure your braids with a hair tie or clip. You can also use hairspray to help keep your braids in place.

Tips for Creating 80s Braids

Here are a few tips to help you create perfect 80s braids:

  • Use a comb to help you section your hair evenly
  • Use hairspray to help keep your braids in place
  • Add accessories like scrunchies or headbands for an authentic 80s look

Frequently Asked Questions

Here are some common questions about 80s braids:

Q: Can men wear 80s braids?

A: Yes! Many men wore braids in the 80s, and it's still a popular style today.

Q: How long does it take to create 80s braids?

A: The amount of time it takes to create 80s braids depends on the length and thickness of your hair, as well as the style you choose. Generally, you can expect to spend anywhere from 30 minutes to an hour creating braids.

Hair Care Tips for Braids

Here are some tips to help you take care of your hair while wearing braids:

  • Wash your hair regularly to prevent buildup
  • Avoid using heavy products that can weigh your hair down
  • Use a satin pillowcase to prevent frizz and breakage

Overall, 80s braids are a fun and easy way to add some retro flair to your look. Whether you're attending a costume party or just want to try something new, braids are a great choice. So grab your scrunchies and get braiding!
