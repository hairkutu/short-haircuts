Do Bobs Suit Long Faces Let s Find Out

Written by Frank Jul 01, 2023 Â· 8 min read

Are you wondering if a bob hairstyle is suitable for your long face? Well, you have come to the right place. In this blog post, we will discuss everything you need to know about whether bobs suit long faces or not. We will cover topics such as what bob hairstyles are, how to determine your face shape, and what type of bob suits long faces the most. So, let's get started!

Description

A bob hairstyle is a classic and versatile haircut that has been around for decades. It is a short to medium-length haircut that typically falls between the chin and the shoulders. The bob can be worn in various styles, including blunt, layered, asymmetrical, and inverted. However, not all bob hairstyles are suitable for every face shape. Hence, it is essential to choose a bob that complements your facial features.

How To Determine Your Face Shape

The first step to finding the perfect bob for your long face is to determine your face shape. To do this, stand in front of a mirror and pull your hair back. Examine your face and compare it to the following shapes:

  • Oval
  • Round
  • Square
  • Heart-shaped
  • Long

If your face is longer than it is wide, then you have a long face. The goal of choosing a bob hairstyle for a long face is to add width to your face and balance out its length.

Step-By-Step Guide on Choosing a Bob for Long Faces

Now that you have determined your face shape let's move on to choosing a bob that suits long faces. Here's a step-by-step guide:

Step 1: Consider the Length

A long bob or a lob is an excellent choice for a long face. It should fall just below the chin or at the collarbone. This length will add width to your face and create an illusion of a shorter face.

Step 2: Choose the Right Cut

When choosing the cut, go for a layered bob as it adds volume to the hair and creates an illusion of a fuller face. Avoid blunt bobs as they can make your face appear longer. An asymmetrical bob is also a great option as it adds dimension to your hair.

Step 3: Opt for a Side-Parted Bob

A side-parted bob is perfect for long faces as it creates an illusion of a shorter face. The parting also adds volume and movement to the hair.

Step 4: Add Some Bangs

Adding bangs to your bob is a great way to balance out the length of your face. Go for side-swept bangs that fall below the eyebrows. Avoid blunt bangs as they can make your face appear longer.

Tips on Maintaining Your Bob

Now that you have your perfect bob, here are some tips on how to maintain it:

  • Get regular trims to keep your bob looking fresh.
  • Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to add volume to your hair.
  • Blow-dry your hair with a round brush to add volume and movement.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair to prevent damage.
  • Use a dry shampoo to refresh your hair in between washes.

FAQs

Q: Can I wear a blunt bob if I have a long face?

A: It is not recommended to wear a blunt bob if you have a long face as it can make your face appear longer. However, if you still want to wear a blunt bob, go for a shorter length that falls above the chin.

Q: What type of bangs suit long faces?

A: Side-swept bangs that fall below the eyebrows are the best option for long faces. Avoid blunt bangs as they can make your face appear longer.

Q: How often should I trim my bob?

A: It is recommended to trim your bob every six to eight weeks to keep it looking fresh.

Hair Care for Bobs

Here are some additional hair care tips for bobs:

  • Use a heat protectant spray before using any heat styling tools.
  • Avoid using too many hair products as they can weigh down your hair.
  • Use a hair serum to add shine and control frizz.
  • Wash your hair every two to three days to prevent dryness.
  • Avoid tying your hair up too tightly as it can cause breakage.

In conclusion, bobs can suit long faces if you choose the right length, cut, and style. Remember to consider your face shape when choosing a bob and opt for a side-parted, layered bob with side-swept bangs. With these tips and tricks, you can rock a bob hairstyle that complements your long face.
