How To Shoulder Length Hair Cut Step By Step Guide

Written by Eveline Aug 08, 2023 Â· 6 min read

If you're looking for a hair change, why not try a shoulder length haircut? This style is timeless and versatile, and it works for all hair types and face shapes. In this step-by-step guide, we will show you how to get the perfect shoulder length haircut.

How to Cut Shoulder Length Hair: Step by Step

1. Start with Clean Hair

Before cutting your hair, make sure it's clean and dry. Wash your hair with shampoo and conditioner, and let it air dry or blow dry it.

2. Section Your Hair

Divide your hair into four sections: two at the front and two at the back. Use hair clips to hold each section in place.

3. Cut the Back Section

Start with the back section. Take a small section of hair and hold it between your fingers. Cut the hair at an angle, starting with the back center and working your way towards the sides. Repeat this process until you have cut all the hair in the back section.

4. Cut the Front Section

Move on to the front sections. Take a small section of hair and hold it between your fingers. Cut the hair at an angle, starting from the center and working your way towards the sides. Repeat this process until you have cut all the hair in the front sections.

5. Blend the Hair

Use a comb to blend the hair from the back and front sections. Cut any stray hairs that need trimming.

6. Check the Length

Check the length of your hair by pulling it straight down. If any sections are longer than others, trim them until the length is even.

7. Style Your Hair

Style your hair as desired. You can straighten it or curl it, or leave it natural. Shoulder length hair is versatile, so you can experiment with different styles.

Tips for Cutting Shoulder Length Hair

Here are some tips to keep in mind when cutting your shoulder length hair:

  • Use sharp scissors to prevent split ends.
  • Don't cut too much hair at once. It's better to cut small sections and work your way up.
  • Take breaks if you need to. Cutting hair can be tiring, so it's important to rest your hands and arms.
  • Use a mirror to check the back of your hair.

Shoulder Length Hair Cut FAQ

How often should I cut my shoulder length hair?

You should cut your hair every 6-8 weeks to maintain the length and prevent split ends.

Can I cut my own shoulder length hair?

Yes, you can cut your own shoulder length hair. Just make sure you have the right tools and follow the steps carefully.

What face shapes suit shoulder length hair?

Shoulder length hair suits all face shapes. It's a versatile length that can be tailored to your individual features.

How to Care for Your Shoulder Length Hair

Once you've cut your hair, it's important to take care of it. Here are some tips:

  • Use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair hydrated.
  • Avoid using heat tools too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.
  • Avoid tight hairstyles that can pull on your hair and cause breakage.

Conclusion

A shoulder length haircut is a timeless and versatile style that suits all hair types and face shapes. With this step-by-step guide, you can cut your hair at home and experiment with different styles. Remember to take care of your hair by using the right products and avoiding damaging heat tools.
