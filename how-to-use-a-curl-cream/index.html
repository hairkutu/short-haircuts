How To Use A Curl Cream A Step By Step Guide

Written by Eveline Jun 07, 2023 Â· 7 min read

If you have curly hair, you know that finding the right product can make all the difference. Curl creams are a popular choice for those looking to define their curls and reduce frizz. In this article, we'll go over how to use a curl cream to get the best results for your hair.

Table of Contents

  • What is Curl Cream?
  • How to Use Curl Cream
  • Tips for Using Curl Cream
  • FAQ About Curl Cream
  • Hair Care Tips for Curly Hair

What is Curl Cream?

Curl cream is a styling product that helps to enhance and define curls. It typically has a creamy consistency and is applied to damp hair before blow-drying or air-drying. Curl creams work by providing moisture to the hair and helping to reduce frizz.

How to Use Curl Cream

Using curl cream is easy and can be done in just a few steps:

Step 1: Start with clean, damp hair

It's important to start with clean, damp hair when using curl cream. Shampoo and condition your hair as usual, then gently towel-dry until it's slightly damp.

Step 2: Apply the curl cream

Take a small amount of curl cream and rub it between your palms to warm it up. Then, apply the cream to your hair, starting at the ends and working your way up to the roots. Be sure to distribute the cream evenly throughout your hair.

Step 3: Scrunch your hair

Once you've applied the curl cream, use your hands to scrunch your hair. This will help to define your curls and create volume. Continue scrunching until your hair is dry or mostly dry.

Step 4: Style as desired

Once your hair is dry, you can style it as desired. You may want to add a bit more curl cream to any areas that need extra definition or moisture.

Tips for Using Curl Cream

Here are a few tips to help you get the best results when using curl cream:

  • Use a small amount of curl cream to start with, and add more as needed.
  • Avoid applying curl cream to your roots, as this can make your hair look greasy.
  • If you have thick or coarse hair, you may need to use more curl cream than someone with fine hair.
  • If you're blow-drying your hair, use a diffuser attachment to help enhance your curls.

FAQ About Curl Cream

What is the difference between curl cream and gel?

Curl cream is typically more moisturizing than gel, which can be drying for some hair types. Curl cream also tends to provide a softer hold, while gel provides a stronger hold.

Can curl cream be used on straight hair?

While curl cream is specifically designed for curly hair, it can also be used on straight hair to add texture and definition.

Can curl cream be used on dry hair?

Curl cream is best used on damp hair, as it helps to define curls and reduce frizz. However, you can apply a small amount of curl cream to dry hair to help tame flyaways.

Hair Care Tips for Curly Hair

Curly hair requires special care to keep it looking healthy and defined. Here are a few tips to help you care for your curls:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils.
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it's dry, as this can cause frizz. Instead, use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your hair when it's wet.
  • Deep condition your hair once a week to help keep it moisturized.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools, as these can damage your hair and cause it to lose its curl pattern.

With these tips and our step-by-step guide on how to use curl cream, you'll be able to achieve beautifully defined curls that last all day.
