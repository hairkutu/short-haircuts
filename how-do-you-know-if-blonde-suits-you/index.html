How Do You Know If Blonde Suits You

Written by Petter Jul 11, 2023 Â· 8 min read

Are you considering going blonde? It can be a daunting decision to make, especially if you've never tried it before. But don't worry! We're here to help you figure out if blonde is the right color for you. In this article, we'll cover everything from skin tone to hair texture to help you make an informed decision.

Table of Contents

  • Understanding Your Skin Tone
  • Considering Your Hair Texture
  • Matching Your Face Shape
  • Reflecting Your Personality
  • Taking Care of Your Blonde Hair
  • Tips for Going Blonde
  • Frequently Asked Questions

Understanding Your Skin Tone

One of the most important factors to consider when deciding if blonde suits you is your skin tone. People with warm skin tones usually look best with golden or honey blonde shades. On the other hand, those with cool skin tones should opt for ash or platinum blonde. If you're not sure what your skin tone is, take a look at the veins on your wrist. If they appear blue, you have a cool skin tone. If they look green, you have a warm skin tone.

Considering Your Hair Texture

Another consideration when going blonde is your hair texture. People with fine hair may want to avoid going too light, as it can make their hair look thinner. Those with curly or coarse hair can usually pull off a lighter blonde shade without any issues.

Matching Your Face Shape

Your face shape can also play a role in determining if blonde suits you. If you have a round face, you may want to opt for a lighter shade of blonde to add dimension. Those with more angular face shapes can pull off darker blonde shades. It's always a good idea to consult with a hairstylist who can help you choose the right shade for your face shape.

Reflecting Your Personality

Your personality can also play a role in whether or not blonde suits you. If you're outgoing and love to stand out in a crowd, a brighter blonde shade may be right for you. However, if you prefer a more understated look, a darker blonde shade may be a better fit.

Taking Care of Your Blonde Hair

Blonde hair requires a bit more maintenance than other hair colors. You'll need to use products specifically designed for blonde hair, such as purple shampoo to keep your color from turning brassy. You'll also want to avoid heat styling as much as possible, as blonde hair can be more prone to damage.

Tips for Going Blonde

  • Start with a few highlights to test the waters.
  • Choose a shade that complements your skin tone.
  • Consult with a hairstylist to choose the right shade for your face shape.
  • Invest in high-quality hair care products designed for blonde hair.
  • Avoid heat styling as much as possible.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can anyone go blonde?

While anyone can technically go blonde, it may not be the most flattering color for everyone. It's important to consider factors such as skin tone, hair texture, and face shape when deciding if blonde is right for you.

Will going blonde damage my hair?

Any major hair color change can cause some damage to your hair, but with proper care, you can minimize the damage. It's important to use high-quality hair care products designed for blonde hair and avoid heat styling as much as possible.

Do I need to lighten my hair to go blonde?

If you have naturally dark hair, you'll need to lighten it before going blonde. However, if you have lighter hair, you may be able to achieve a blonde shade without lightening your hair first.

How often will I need to touch up my roots?

This will depend on how quickly your hair grows and how noticeable your roots are. On average, most people need to touch up their roots every 4-6 weeks.

What if I don't like how I look with blonde hair?

If you're not happy with your blonde hair, you can always go back to your natural color or try a different hair color altogether!

Conclusion

Deciding if blonde suits you can be a difficult decision, but with the right considerations and care, you can achieve a beautiful blonde look. Remember to consult with a hairstylist and use high-quality hair care products designed for blonde hair to keep your hair looking its best.
