How To Style Short Soft Natural Hair Tips And Tricks

Written by Eveline Jul 03, 2023 · 7 min read
If you have short, soft natural hair, you may feel limited in your styling options. However, with the right techniques and products, you can achieve a variety of chic and trendy looks. In this article, we’ll share some tips and tricks on how to style short soft natural hair, including step-by-step guides and answers to frequently asked questions.

Table of Contents

  • How to Style Short Soft Natural Hair
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips and Tricks
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care for Short Soft Natural Hair

How to Style Short Soft Natural Hair

Styling short soft natural hair can be challenging, but there are a few basic techniques you can use to achieve a variety of looks. Here are some tips to get you started:
  • Use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair hydrated.
  • Apply a leave-in conditioner or curl cream to define your curls and reduce frizz.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your hair gently.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools as they can damage your hair.
  • Try different hair accessories, such as headbands, hair clips, and scarves, to add interest to your hairstyle.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here’s a step-by-step guide on how to style short soft natural hair:

1. Wash and Condition Your Hair

Start by washing your hair with a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner. This will help keep your hair hydrated and prevent it from becoming dry and brittle.

2. Apply a Leave-In Conditioner or Curl Cream

Apply a leave-in conditioner or curl cream to your damp hair. This will help define your curls and reduce frizz.

3. Detangle Your Hair

Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your hair gently. Start at the ends and work your way up to the roots.

4. Style Your Hair

Now it’s time to style your hair. Here are some ideas:
  • Create a twist-out or braid-out by twisting or braiding small sections of hair and leaving them in overnight.
  • Try a wash-and-go style by applying a styling gel to your damp hair and letting it air dry.
  • Create a curly fro by using a pick to lift and fluff your curls.
  • Style your hair into a sleek updo by using bobby pins and a hair tie.

Tips and Tricks

Here are some additional tips and tricks on how to style short soft natural hair:
  • Experiment with different hair products to find what works best for your hair type.
  • Use a satin or silk pillowcase to prevent your hair from becoming tangled while you sleep.
  • Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or scarf.
  • Don’t be afraid to try new hairstyles and accessories.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about styling short soft natural hair:

1. Can I straighten my natural hair?

While it’s possible to straighten natural hair, it’s not recommended as heat styling can damage your hair.

2. How often should I wash my hair?

How often you wash your hair depends on your hair type and personal preference. Some people wash their hair every day, while others wash it once a week.

3. Can I dye my natural hair?

Yes, you can dye your natural hair, but it’s important to use a dye that is specifically formulated for natural hair.

Hair Care for Short Soft Natural Hair

To keep your short soft natural hair healthy and strong, it’s important to follow a good hair care routine. Here are some tips:
  • Wash your hair regularly with a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner.
  • Apply a leave-in conditioner or curl cream to your hair to keep it hydrated.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools as they can damage your hair.
  • Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or scarf.
In conclusion, styling short soft natural hair can be challenging, but with the right techniques and products, you can achieve a variety of chic and trendy looks. Follow our tips and tricks, and don’t be afraid to experiment with different hairstyles and accessories. Remember to take good care of your hair to keep it healthy and strong.
