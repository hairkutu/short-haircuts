Hairstyles For Round Faces Over 40 A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Bardi Jul 10, 2023 · 9 min read

Are you over 40 and have a round face? Do you struggle to find the right hairstyle that flatters your face shape? Look no further as we have got you covered with our guide on hairstyles for round faces over 40. In this article, we will provide you with tips, step-by-step guides, and hair care advice to help you find the perfect hairstyle that suits your face shape and age.

  • How to Choose the Right Hairstyle for Your Round Face
  • Step by Step Guide on Hairstyles for Round Faces Over 40
  • Tips on Maintaining Your Hairstyle
  • Frequently Asked Questions
  • Hair Care for Round Faces Over 40

Description

A round face is characterized by fullness and width, with no prominent angles. As you age, your face shape may change due to factors like hormonal changes, weight gain or loss, and loss of skin elasticity. Finding the right hairstyle for your round face over 40 can be challenging, as you need to consider your face shape, hair texture, and age. The right hairstyle can enhance your features, make you look younger and more vibrant, and boost your self-confidence.

How to Choose the Right Hairstyle for Your Round Face

Choosing the right hairstyle for your round face can be a daunting task, but with these tips, you can make an informed decision:

  • Consider the length of your hair – short, medium, or long.
  • Choose a hairstyle that adds height to your face, like a high top knot or a layered bob.
  • Opt for a side-swept fringe or a deep side part to create angles and break up the roundness of your face.
  • Consider adding layers or volume to your hair to create movement and texture.
  • Avoid hairstyles that add width to your face, like blunt bangs or a blunt bob.
  • Choose a hairstyle that complements your hair texture, whether it's straight, wavy, or curly.
  • Experiment with different hairstyles and consult with your hairstylist to find the right one for you.

Step by Step Guide on Hairstyles for Round Faces Over 40

Here are some hairstyles that are perfect for round faces over 40:

Short Hairstyles

Short hairstyles are perfect for round faces as they can add height and angles to your face. Here's how to style a short haircut:

  1. Start by washing and conditioning your hair.
  2. Towel dry your hair and apply a volumizing mousse to the roots.
  3. Blow dry your hair using a round brush to create volume and lift at the roots.
  4. Finish off with a texturizing spray to add movement and texture to your hair.

Medium Hairstyles

Medium hairstyles are great for round faces as they can add length and volume. Here's how to style a medium haircut:

  1. Start by washing and conditioning your hair.
  2. Towel dry your hair and apply a heat protectant spray.
  3. Blow dry your hair using a medium-sized round brush to add volume and movement.
  4. Curl your hair using a large barrel curling iron to create loose waves.
  5. Finish off with a hairspray to hold the curls in place.

Long Hairstyles

Long hairstyles are ideal for round faces as they can add length and create an illusion of a slimmer face. Here's how to style a long haircut:

  1. Start by washing and conditioning your hair.
  2. Towel dry your hair and apply a leave-in conditioner to the ends.
  3. Blow dry your hair using a paddle brush to create a smooth and sleek finish.
  4. Straighten your hair using a flat iron to create a sleek and polished look.
  5. Finish off with a shine serum to add shine and smoothness to your hair.

Tips on Maintaining Your Hairstyle

Here are some tips to help you maintain your hairstyle:

  • Get regular trims to keep your hair looking healthy and prevent split ends.
  • Use a heat protectant spray before using hot tools to prevent damage to your hair.
  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to prevent dryness and damage to your hair.
  • Avoid using too many styling products as they can weigh down your hair and make it look greasy.
  • Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a UV protectant spray.

Frequently Asked Questions

Here are some frequently asked questions about hairstyles for round faces over 40:

What hairstyles should I avoid if I have a round face?

Avoid hairstyles that add width to your face, like blunt bangs or a blunt bob. Also, avoid hairstyles that are too short or too long, as they can make your face look wider.

What hairstyles are perfect for round faces over 40?

Short, medium, and long hairstyles can be perfect for round faces over 40. Choose a hairstyle that adds height and angles to your face, like a high top knot or a layered bob.

How can I make my face look slimmer with my hairstyle?

You can make your face look slimmer by choosing a hairstyle that adds height and angles to your face. Opt for a side-swept fringe or a deep side part to create angles and break up the roundness of your face.

Hair Care for Round Faces Over 40

Here are some hair care tips for round faces over 40:

  • Wash your hair regularly to keep it clean and healthy.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to nourish your hair.
  • Avoid using hot tools on a daily basis to prevent damage to your hair.
  • Eat a healthy and balanced diet to promote hair growth and health.
  • Stay hydrated by drinking plenty of water to keep your hair and skin healthy.

Now that you have our comprehensive guide on hairstyles for round faces over 40, you can confidently choose the right hairstyle for your face shape and age. Remember to experiment with different hairstyles, consult with your hairstylist, and follow our hair care tips to keep your hair looking healthy and beautiful.
