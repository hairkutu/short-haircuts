Best Low Maintenance Haircuts For Thick Frizzy Hair In 2023

Written by William Jul 21, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Thick frizzy hair can be a challenge to manage, but you don't have to spend hours styling your hair every day. Finding the right low maintenance haircut can save you time and effort while still looking fabulous. In this blog post, we'll be sharing the best low maintenance haircuts for thick frizzy hair in 2023.

Table of Contents

  • How to Determine Your Hair Type
  • Best Low Maintenance Haircuts for Thick Frizzy Hair
  • Tips for Managing Thick Frizzy Hair
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care for Thick Frizzy Hair

How to Determine Your Hair Type

Before we dive into the best low maintenance haircuts for thick frizzy hair, it's important to determine your hair type. This will help you choose a haircut that works best for you. Here are the different hair types:

  • Straight
  • Wavy
  • Curly
  • Kinky

If your hair is thick and frizzy, you likely have curly or kinky hair. Knowing your hair type can help you choose a haircut that will work best for your hair.

Best Low Maintenance Haircuts for Thick Frizzy Hair

Now that you know your hair type, here are the best low maintenance haircuts for thick frizzy hair:

1. Long Layers

Long layers are perfect for thick frizzy hair. This haircut adds depth and dimension to your hair while reducing bulk. Long layers are also low maintenance, making it easy to style your hair every day.

2. Bob

A bob is another great low maintenance haircut for thick frizzy hair. This haircut is perfect for those who want a shorter length without sacrificing volume. A bob can be styled in many different ways, making it a versatile haircut.

3. Pixie Cut

If you're looking for a dramatic change, a pixie cut is a great option. This haircut is low maintenance and perfect for those who want a shorter length. A pixie cut can also help reduce frizz and make your hair more manageable.

4. Shag

A shag is a layered haircut that is perfect for thick frizzy hair. This haircut adds texture and volume to your hair while reducing bulk. A shag is also low maintenance, making it easy to style every day.

5. Lob

A lob, or long bob, is a great low maintenance haircut for thick frizzy hair. This haircut is perfect for those who want a medium length without sacrificing volume. A lob can be styled in many different ways, making it a versatile haircut.

Tips for Managing Thick Frizzy Hair

Here are some tips for managing thick frizzy hair:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it's wet
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair
  • Avoid using heat styling tools
  • Use a diffuser when blow-drying your hair
  • Avoid using too much product

FAQ

1. How can I reduce the frizz in my hair?

You can reduce the frizz in your hair by using a sulfate-free shampoo, avoiding brushing your hair when it's wet, using a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair, and avoiding using heat styling tools.

2. What is the best low maintenance haircut for thick frizzy hair?

The best low maintenance haircut for thick frizzy hair depends on your personal preference and hair type. Some great options include long layers, a bob, a pixie cut, a shag, and a lob.

Hair Care for Thick Frizzy Hair

Here are some tips for taking care of your thick frizzy hair:

  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week
  • Avoid washing your hair every day
  • Avoid using too much product
  • Avoid using heat styling tools
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair

With the right low maintenance haircut and hair care routine, you can manage your thick frizzy hair easily. Use these tips and tricks to keep your hair looking healthy and beautiful.
