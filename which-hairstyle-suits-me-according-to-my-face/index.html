Which Hairstyle Suits Me According To My Face

Are you struggling to find the perfect hairstyle that complements your face shape? Look no further, as we guide you through the best hairstyles based on your face shape. Whether you have a round, oval, square or heart-shaped face, we have got you covered.

Round Face

If you have a round face shape, you want to create an illusion of length and slimness. The best hairstyles for round faces are those that add height to the crown and length to the face. Long layers, side-swept bangs, and asymmetrical cuts are great options for round faces. Avoid blunt cuts and chin-length bobs as they can make your face appear shorter and wider.

How to:

Ask your hairstylist to cut your hair in long layers that fall below your shoulders. Part your hair on the side and add side-swept bangs that graze your eyebrows. To add more height to the crown, tease your hair at the roots and spray with hairspray. Voila! You have the perfect hairstyle for your round face shape.

Tips:

  • Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to add more volume to your hair.
  • Avoid center parts as they can make your face appear rounder.
  • Experiment with different hairstyles and find the one that suits you the best.

FAQs:

Q: Can I have short hair if I have a round face?

A: Yes, you can. Just make sure that the cut adds height to the crown and length to the face.

Oval Face

If you have an oval face shape, you are in luck as almost any hairstyle will suit you. However, you want to avoid hiding your face shape with too much hair. The best hairstyles for oval faces are those that show off your face shape. Long waves, sleek bobs, and pixie cuts are some great options for oval faces.

How to:

If you have long hair, add some soft waves and part your hair in the middle. If you have short hair, try a sleek bob or a pixie cut. Keep the hair off your face to highlight your face shape.

Tips:

  • Try different hairstyles and experiment with different lengths.
  • Avoid heavy bangs as they can hide your beautiful face shape.
  • Consider getting highlights or balayage to add dimension to your hair.

FAQs:

Q: Can I have a fringe if I have an oval face?

A: Yes, you can. Just make sure that the fringe is not too heavy and blends well with the rest of your hair.

Square Face

If you have a square face shape, you want to soften the angles and add some curves. The best hairstyles for square faces are those that soften the jawline and add volume to the crown. Soft layers, side-swept bangs, and long waves are great options for square faces. Avoid blunt cuts and straight bangs as they can emphasize the squareness of your face.

How to:

Ask your hairstylist to add soft layers to your hair and cut it below your shoulders. Part your hair on the side and add side-swept bangs that graze your cheekbones. Add some soft waves to your hair to soften the angles of your face.

Tips:

  • Use a round brush when blow-drying your hair to add volume to the crown.
  • Avoid center parts as they can make your face appear more angular.
  • Consider getting highlights or lowlights to add dimension to your hair.

FAQs:

Q: Can I have a short bob if I have a square face?

A: Yes, you can. Just make sure that the cut is not too blunt and add some soft layers to the hair.

Heart-Shaped Face

If you have a heart-shaped face, you want to balance the width of your forehead with the narrowness of your chin. The best hairstyles for heart-shaped faces are those that add volume to the chin and balance out the forehead. Long layers, side-swept bangs, and chin-length bobs are great options for heart-shaped faces. Avoid blunt cuts and heavy bangs as they can make your forehead appear wider.

How to:

Ask your hairstylist to cut your hair in long layers that fall below your shoulders. Add side-swept bangs that graze your cheekbones and chin. If you prefer a shorter cut, try a chin-length bob that adds volume to the chin.

Tips:

  • Use a volumizing mousse or spray to add more volume to your hair.
  • Avoid center parts as they can make your forehead appear wider.
  • Accessorize your hair with headbands or scarves to draw attention to your face shape.

FAQs:

Q: Can I have a pixie cut if I have a heart-shaped face?

A: Yes, you can. Just make sure that the cut adds volume to the chin.

Hair Care Tips

To keep your hair looking its best, here are some hair care tips:

  • Wash your hair every 2-3 days to prevent it from getting too dry.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair hydrated and healthy.
  • Avoid using hot styling tools too often as they can damage your hair.
  • Trim your hair every 6-8 weeks to prevent split ends.
  • Eat a healthy diet and drink plenty of water to promote hair growth.

Now that you know which hairstyle suits you according to your face shape, it's time to book that appointment with your hairstylist. Experiment with different styles and find the one that makes you feel confident and beautiful.
