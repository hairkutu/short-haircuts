How To Do Cornrows For White Guys A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Eveline Apr 16, 2023 · 6 min read

Cornrows are a popular and stylish hairstyle that has been around for centuries. Originally worn by African women, these braids are now popular worldwide, including among white guys. Cornrows are a great way to keep your hair neat and tidy, especially during the summer months. If you’re interested in learning how to do cornrows for white guys, this guide will take you through the steps.

Table of Contents

  • How to Do Cornrows for White Guys
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Cornrowing
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care for Cornrows

How to Do Cornrows for White Guys

Before you get started, make sure you have all the necessary tools: a rat-tail comb, hair clips, and hair gel or pomade. You may also want to use a mirror or ask someone to help you with the back of your head.

Cornrows are created by braiding the hair in small, tight sections close to the scalp. Here are the basic steps:

Step-by-Step Guide

1. Start by parting your hair into sections. Use the rat-tail comb to create straight, even parts. Clip the rest of your hair out of the way.

2. Apply hair gel or pomade to the first section of hair. This will help keep the braid in place.

3. Divide the section into three equal parts, just like you would for a regular braid.

4. Cross the right section of hair over the middle section, then cross the left section over the middle section. This is the basic braiding technique.

5. As you continue braiding, add more hair to each section. Take a small section of hair from the outside of the section and add it to the right or left section before crossing it over the middle section.

6. Continue braiding until you reach the end of the section. Secure the braid with a small hair elastic or a knot. Repeat with the remaining sections of hair.

Tips for Cornrowing

- Start with clean, dry hair. This will make it easier to part and braid.

- Use a rat-tail comb to create straight, even parts. This will help ensure that your cornrows look neat and tidy.

- Apply hair gel or pomade to each section of hair before braiding. This will help keep the braid in place and prevent flyaways.

- For a more intricate look, try adding beads or thread to your cornrows.

FAQs

Q: How long does it take to cornrow hair?

A: The time it takes to cornrow hair depends on the length and thickness of your hair, as well as your skill level. It can take anywhere from 30 minutes to several hours.

Q: Can cornrows damage hair?

A: Cornrows themselves do not damage hair, but if they are too tight or left in for too long, they can cause hair breakage and even hair loss.

Hair Care for Cornrows

- Keep your scalp clean and moisturized. Use a mild shampoo and conditioner to wash your hair, and apply a scalp oil or moisturizer to keep your scalp healthy.

- Cover your hair at night with a silk or satin scarf or bonnet. This will help prevent frizz and keep your cornrows looking neat.

- Avoid pulling or tugging on your cornrows. This can cause hair breakage and damage to the scalp.

- Take breaks between hairstyles. Give your hair a chance to rest and recover before braiding it again.

Cornrows are a popular and versatile hairstyle that can be worn by anyone, regardless of their hair type or ethnicity. With a little practice and some patience, you can learn how to do cornrows for white guys and enjoy this stylish and practical look.
