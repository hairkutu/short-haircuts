Best Haircut For 60 Year Old Woman With Round Face

Written by Christine May 25, 2023 Â· 7 min read

As we age, our facial features change, and so do our hair needs. If you're a 60-year-old woman with a round face, finding the best haircut for your face shape can be challenging. However, with the right cut, you can accentuate your features and enhance your natural beauty. In this blog post, we'll show you the best haircut for 60-year-old women with round faces.

Description

A round face shape is characterized by a wide forehead, full cheeks, and a rounded chin. The best haircut for this face shape is one that adds length and angles to the face. It should also draw attention to your best features and create the illusion of a slimmer face.

How to

The best haircut for a 60-year-old woman with a round face is a layered bob. This haircut is versatile, easy to maintain, and can be styled in different ways. Here's how to get the perfect layered bob:

Step 1 - Consult with your stylist

Before getting your haircut, consult with your stylist to determine the best length, layers, and style for your face shape. Your stylist will be able to recommend the best haircut for you based on your hair texture, lifestyle, and personal preferences.

Step 2 - Shampoo and Condition

Shampoo and condition your hair before your haircut to ensure that it's clean and free of any product buildup. This will also make it easier for your stylist to cut your hair evenly.

Step 3 - Section your hair

Section your hair into small sections using hair clips. This will make it easier for your stylist to cut your hair evenly and ensure that all the layers are the same length.

Step 4 - Cut your hair

Your stylist will start by cutting your hair to the desired length. Then, they will add layers to create volume and texture. The layers should be cut at an angle to add length to the face and create the illusion of a slimmer face.

Step 5 - Style your hair

After your haircut, your stylist will blow-dry and style your hair. You can choose to style your hair straight, wavy, or curly. You can also add volume and texture with a curling iron or a texturizing spray.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you maintain your layered bob:

  • Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to add volume to your hair.
  • Blow-dry your hair using a round brush to add volume and texture.
  • Use a texturizing spray to add texture and hold to your hair.
  • Trim your hair every six to eight weeks to maintain the shape and length of your haircut.

FAQ

What other haircuts are good for a 60-year-old woman with a round face?

Aside from a layered bob, other haircuts that are good for a 60-year-old woman with a round face include a long bob, a pixie cut, and a shag haircut. These haircuts can also add length and angles to the face and create the illusion of a slimmer face.

Can I color my hair?

Yes, you can color your hair. However, it's important to choose a color that complements your skin tone and enhances your natural beauty. Consult with your stylist to determine the best hair color for you.

Hair Care

Maintaining healthy hair is essential for any haircut. Here are some tips to help you take care of your hair:

  • Wash your hair with a gentle shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils.
  • Avoid using hot tools on your hair too frequently to prevent heat damage.
  • Eat a healthy diet and take supplements to promote healthy hair growth.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and the elements by wearing a hat or using a protective spray.

Overall, the best haircut for a 60-year-old woman with a round face is a layered bob. This haircut is versatile, easy to maintain, and can be styled in different ways. Consult with your stylist to determine the best length, layers, and style for your face shape and hair texture. With the right haircut and hair care routine, you can enhance your natural beauty and feel confident at any age.
