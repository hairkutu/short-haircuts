Short Hairstyles For Oblong Faces Male A Comprehensive Guide

If you have an oblong face shape, finding the right hairstyle can be a challenge. However, with the right haircut and styling, you can enhance your best features and make your face appear more proportionate. In this article, we'll show you the best short hairstyles for oblong faces male.

  • Description
  • How to Choose the Best Short Hairstyle for Oblong Faces Male

  • FAQs
  • Hair Care Tips for Short Hairstyles for Oblong Faces Male

Description

An oblong face shape is characterized by a long, narrow face with a prominent chin and forehead. The goal of choosing a hairstyle for this face shape is to add width and volume to the sides of the face, and to create the illusion of a shorter face. Short hairstyles are ideal for oblong faces as they can add width and balance to the face.

How to Choose the Best Short Hairstyle for Oblong Faces Male

When choosing a short hairstyle for oblong faces male, keep the following tips in mind:

  • Choose a hairstyle that adds width to the sides of the face
  • Avoid hairstyles that add height to the top of the head, as they can make the face appear longer
  • Choose hairstyles with texture and movement, as they can add volume to the sides of the face
  • Avoid hairstyles that are too short or too long, as they can make the face appear longer

Short Hairstyles for Oblong Faces Male

Here are some of the best short hairstyles for oblong faces male:

1. Side Parted Crew Cut

The side parted crew cut is a classic hairstyle that works well for oblong faces. The sides of the hair are cut short, while the top is left longer and swept to the side. This adds width to the sides of the face and balances out the length of the face.

2. Textured Crop

The textured crop is a short, messy hairstyle that adds volume and texture to the hair. This hairstyle works well for oblong faces as it adds width and volume to the sides of the face.

3. Short Quiff

The short quiff is a modern hairstyle that adds volume to the top of the head and balances out the length of the face. This hairstyle works well for oblong faces as it adds width to the sides of the face and creates the illusion of a shorter face.

Step by Step Guide for Styling Short Hair for Oblong Faces Male

Here's a step by step guide for styling short hair for oblong faces male:

1. Start with clean, dry hair

Wash your hair with a shampoo and conditioner that suits your hair type, and towel dry your hair until it's just damp.

2. Apply a styling product

Apply a styling product, such as a pomade or wax, to your hair. This will help to add texture and hold to your hair.

3. Style your hair

Using a comb or your fingers, style your hair into the desired shape. Use a blow dryer to add volume and texture to your hair.

4. Finish with hairspray

Finish your hairstyle with a light mist of hairspray to hold it in place.

Tips for Maintaining Short Hairstyles for Oblong Faces Male

Here are some tips for maintaining short hairstyles for oblong faces male:

  • Get regular trims to keep your hair looking neat and tidy
  • Use a high-quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy
  • Avoid using too much heat when styling your hair, as it can damage your hair
  • Experiment with different styling products to find what works best for your hair type

FAQs

What is the best short hairstyle for oblong faces male?

The best short hairstyles for oblong faces male are those that add width and volume to the sides of the face, such as the side parted crew cut, textured crop, and short quiff.

Can I wear a buzz cut if I have an oblong face?

A buzz cut can work for oblong faces, but it's important to choose a style that adds width and volume to the sides of the face, such as a slightly longer buzz cut with textured hair on top.

How often should I get my hair cut?

It's recommended to get your hair cut every 4-6 weeks to keep your hairstyle looking neat and tidy.

Hair Care Tips for Short Hairstyles for Oblong Faces Male

Here are some hair care tips for short hairstyles for oblong faces male:

  • Use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy
  • Avoid using too many styling products, as they can weigh down your hair and make it look greasy
  • Protect your hair from the sun and heat by wearing a hat or using a heat protectant spray
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair looking neat and tidy

With the right short hairstyle and hair care routine, you can enhance your best features and make your oblong face appear more proportionate. Follow our guide to find the perfect short hairstyle for oblong faces male.
