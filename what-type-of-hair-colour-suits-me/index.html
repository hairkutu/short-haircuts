What Type Of Hair Colour Suits Me A Complete Guide

Written by William Apr 22, 2023 Â· 8 min read

Are you considering a new hair colour but unsure which one would suit you best? Choosing the right hair colour can be a daunting task, especially if you're not familiar with the different options available. In this guide, we'll walk you through everything you need to know about what type of hair colour suits you best. From understanding your skin tone to choosing the right shades, we've got you covered. So let's dive in!

Description

Before we dive into the details, let's start with a basic understanding of hair colour. Hair colour comes in a range of shades, from natural hues like brown and blonde to bold colours like blue and pink. The right hair colour for you will depend on your skin tone, eye colour, and personal preferences.

How to Choose the Right Hair Colour

Choosing the right hair colour is all about understanding your skin tone. Your skin tone can be cool, warm, or neutral. Cool skin tones have pink or blue undertones, while warm skin tones have yellow or golden undertones. Neutral skin tones have a mix of both.

To determine your skin tone, look at the veins on the inside of your wrist. If they appear blue, you have a cool skin tone. If they appear green, you have a warm skin tone. If they're somewhere in between, you have a neutral skin tone.

Step-by-Step Guide:

1. Determine your skin tone by examining the veins on the inside of your wrist.

2. Look for hair colours that complement your skin tone. Cool skin tones look best with cool hair colours like ashy blonde or platinum. Warm skin tones look best with warm hair colours like golden brown or copper. Neutral skin tones can pull off both cool and warm hair colours.

3. Consider your eye colour. Certain hair colours can make your eye colour pop, while others can clash. Blue-eyed individuals may want to consider warm hair colours like honey blonde or caramel. Brown-eyed individuals can play around with more vibrant colours like red or purple.

Tips for Choosing the Right Hair Colour

1. Don't be afraid to experiment. Hair colour can always be changed, so don't be afraid to try something new.

2. Consider your lifestyle. If you have a busy schedule, opt for a low-maintenance hair colour that won't require frequent touch-ups.

3. Consult with a professional. A hairstylist can help you choose the right hair colour and provide expert advice on maintenance and care.

4. Take care of your hair. No matter what hair colour you choose, it's important to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and vibrant. Use a high-quality shampoo and conditioner, and avoid using heat tools too often.

FAQ

Q: Can I dye my hair if I have sensitive skin?

A: Yes, but be sure to choose a hair dye that's specifically formulated for sensitive skin. It's also a good idea to do a patch test before applying the hair dye to your entire head.

Q: How often should I touch up my hair colour?

A: This will depend on the type of hair colour you choose and how quickly your hair grows. Generally, root touch-ups are needed every 4-6 weeks.

Q: Can I dye my hair at home?

A: Yes, but be sure to follow the instructions carefully and use a high-quality hair dye. If you're unsure about doing it yourself, it's always best to consult with a professional.

Hair Care

After you've chosen the right hair colour, it's important to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and vibrant. Here are some tips:

- Use a high-quality shampoo and conditioner that's formulated for coloured hair.

- Avoid using heat tools too often, as this can damage your hair and cause the colour to fade.

- Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a UV protection spray.

- Get regular trims to keep your hair looking healthy and prevent split ends.

Conclusion

Choosing the right hair colour can be a fun and exciting process. By understanding your skin tone and eye colour, and consulting with a professional, you can find the perfect hair colour that suits you best. Remember to take care of your hair to keep it looking healthy and vibrant, and don't be afraid to experiment with different colours!
