Mens Long Straight Hairstyles 2020 A Guide To Rocking The Look

Written by Aprilia Jun 05, 2023 Â· 6 min read

If you're a man with long straight hair, you're in luck. Long hair for men has been in trend for a while now, and it doesn't seem to be going away anytime soon. However, finding the right hairstyle can be a challenge. In this guide, we'll explore the most popular mens long straight hairstyles 2020 and give you tips on how to style them.

Description

Men's long straight hairstyles are all about embracing your natural texture and adding some style to it. These hairstyles can range from simple to complex, depending on your preference. Many men choose to let their long hair hang loose, while others opt for more structured styles like braids or top knots.

How To

The key to rocking a long straight hairstyle is to embrace your natural texture. That means avoiding harsh chemicals and heat styling tools that can damage your hair. Instead, focus on using gentle products like shampoo and conditioner that are designed for long, straight hair.

When it comes to styling, there are plenty of options. If you want to keep your hair loose, consider adding some texture with a sea salt spray or mousse. For a more polished look, try a slicked-back style using a pomade or gel. And if you're feeling adventurous, experiment with braids or a top knot.

Step by Step

1. Start with clean, dry hair. Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and shiny.

2. Apply a styling product to your hair. Depending on your preference, you can use a sea salt spray, mousse, pomade, or gel.

3. Use your fingers or a comb to style your hair. For a loose look, scrunch your hair with your fingers or use a diffuser on your blow dryer. For a more polished look, slick your hair back with a comb.

4. Experiment with different styles. Don't be afraid to try braids, a top knot, or other styles to find the one that works best for you.

Tips

- Keep your hair healthy by avoiding harsh chemicals and heat styling tools.

- Experiment with different products to find the one that works best for your hair type.

- Don't be afraid to try new styles. Mens long straight hairstyles 2020 are all about embracing your individuality.

FAQ

Q: How often should I wash my long straight hair?

A: It depends on your hair type and how oily your scalp is. Some men can go a few days without washing, while others need to wash their hair every day. Experiment to find what works best for you.

Q: Can I still style my long straight hair if it's thinning?

A: Yes, but you may need to experiment with different products and styles to find what works best. Talk to your stylist for recommendations.

Hair Care

Keeping your long straight hair healthy is essential for rocking any of the mens long straight hairstyles 2020. Here are some tips:

- Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner designed for long, straight hair.

- Avoid harsh chemicals like bleach and hair dyes that can damage your hair.

- Use a heat protectant spray if you must use a heat styling tool like a blow dryer or straightener.

- Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.

By following these tips, you can keep your long straight hair healthy and stylish.
