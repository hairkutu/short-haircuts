How To Get Tiktok Boy Hair A Step By Step Guide

Written by William Jul 12, 2023 Â· 6 min read

TikTok has taken over social media by storm, introducing new trends and challenges every day. One such trend that has gained immense popularity among boys is TikTok boy hair. It's a cool and edgy hairstyle that can instantly transform your look. In this article, we'll guide you through the process of getting TikTok boy hair in relaxed English language.

Description

TikTok boy hair is a shaggy, messy, and layered hairstyle that usually features longer hair on the top and shorter hair on the sides and back. It's a versatile hairstyle that can be styled in different ways, depending on your hair type and personal preference. TikTok boy hair has become a popular style among teenage boys and young adults because it's easy to maintain and looks effortlessly cool.

How to Get TikTok Boy Hair

If you want to get TikTok boy hair, follow these steps:

Step 1: Grow Out Your Hair

The first step in getting TikTok boy hair is to grow out your hair. You'll need at least 3-4 inches of hair on the top and 1-2 inches on the sides and back. If you have shorter hair, you'll need to wait a few months before you can get this hairstyle.

Step 2: Get a Haircut

Once your hair has grown out, you'll need to get a haircut. Ask your hairstylist to give you a layered haircut with longer hair on the top and shorter hair on the sides and back. You can also ask for a fringe or bangs on the front.

Step 3: Style Your Hair

To style your TikTok boy hair, you'll need some hair products such as hair wax, gel, or pomade. Apply a small amount of product on your fingertips and run them through your hair, focusing on the top. You can style your hair in different ways such as messy spikes, textured waves, or slicked-back look.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when getting TikTok boy hair:

  • Choose a hairstyle that suits your face shape and hair type.
  • Use high-quality hair products to avoid damaging your hair.
  • Maintain your hair by regularly trimming the ends and keeping it clean.
  • Experiment with different styling techniques to find the one that works best for you.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about TikTok boy hair:

Q. How long does my hair need to be to get TikTok boy hair?

A. You'll need at least 3-4 inches of hair on the top and 1-2 inches on the sides and back.

Q. Can I get TikTok boy hair if I have curly hair?

A. Yes, TikTok boy hair looks great on curly hair. Just make sure to use hair products that work well with your hair type.

Q. How do I maintain my TikTok boy hair?

A. Regularly trim the ends, keep your hair clean, and use high-quality hair products.

Hair Care

Getting TikTok boy hair involves growing out your hair, which means you need to take care of it. Here are some tips for hair care:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a mild shampoo.
  • Use a conditioner to keep your hair soft and moisturized.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as it can damage it.
  • Eat a healthy diet that includes vitamins and minerals that are good for your hair.

In conclusion, TikTok boy hair is a trendy and stylish hairstyle that can instantly elevate your look. By following the steps and tips mentioned in this article, you can get TikTok boy hair easily. Remember to take care of your hair by maintaining it regularly and using high-quality hair products.
