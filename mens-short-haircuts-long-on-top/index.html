Description

Mens short haircuts long on top have been a popular style for many years, and they continue to be a fashionable choice in 2023. This style features shorter sides and a longer top, which can be styled in various ways to create a unique look. Mens short haircuts long on top are versatile and can be worn by men of all ages, hair types, and face shapes.

How to Get Mens Short Haircuts Long on Top

To get mens short haircuts long on top, you will need to visit a barber or hairstylist who is skilled in this style. You can bring pictures of the style to show your stylist or ask for their recommendations. It is important to choose a stylist who has experience with this style and can give you the look you want.

Step-by-Step Guide

1. Start by washing your hair with a shampoo and conditioner that is suitable for your hair type.

2. Towel-dry your hair, leaving it slightly damp.

3. Comb your hair in the direction you want it to lay.

4. Use a hair clipper to trim the sides and back of your head, leaving the top longer.

5. Use scissors to trim the longer hair on top to your desired length.

6. Style your hair using a hair product of your choice, such as pomade or wax.

7. Use a blow dryer to set the style in place.

8. Finish by using a hairspray to hold your hair in place and add shine.

Tips for Maintaining Your Mens Short Haircuts Long on Top

Mens short haircuts long on top require some maintenance to keep them looking their best. Here are some tips to help you maintain your style:

- Visit your stylist regularly to keep your hair trimmed and shaped.

- Use a good-quality shampoo and conditioner that is suitable for your hair type.

- Avoid using too much product, as this can weigh down your hair and make it look greasy.

- Experiment with different styling products to find the one that works best for you.

- Use a comb or brush to keep your hair looking neat and tidy.

FAQs

Q: Will mens short haircuts long on top work for my hair type?

A: Mens short haircuts long on top can work for most hair types, including straight, wavy, and curly hair. However, it is important to choose a style that complements your natural hair texture.

Q: How often do I need to visit the stylist to maintain my mens short haircuts long on top?

A: It is recommended to visit your stylist every four to six weeks to maintain your style and keep it looking its best.

Hair Care Tips for Mens Short Haircuts Long on Top

Mens short haircuts long on top require some hair care to keep them looking healthy and vibrant. Here are some tips to help you care for your hair:

- Use a good-quality shampoo and conditioner that is suitable for your hair type.

- Avoid using hot tools such as blow dryers and flat irons too often, as these can damage your hair.

- Use a heat protectant spray when using hot tools.

- Avoid over-washing your hair, as this can strip it of its natural oils.

- Use a leave-in conditioner or hair oil to keep your hair hydrated and nourished.

With these tips and a skilled stylist, you can achieve the mens short haircuts long on top look that you desire. Whether you want a classic or trendy style, this look is sure to turn heads and make you feel confident in your appearance.
