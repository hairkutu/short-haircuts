How To Curl Your Hair With A Blow Dryer Brush A Step By Step Guide

Written by Petter Aug 26, 2023 Â· 7 min read

If you're looking for a way to add some volume and bounce to your hair, using a blow dryer brush can be a great option. Not only does it dry your hair quickly, but it also helps you achieve salon-worthy curls in no time. In this article, we'll show you how to curl your hair with a blow dryer brush, step-by-step. So, let's get started!

Description

Blow dryer brushes are hair styling tools that combine a hair dryer and a brush in one. They work by blowing hot air onto your hair while you brush it, which can help you achieve a smooth, frizz-free finish. By using different techniques, you can also use a blow dryer brush to create curls, waves, or straighten your hair.

How to Curl Your Hair with a Blow Dryer Brush

Here's how to curl your hair with a blow dryer brush:

Step 1: Start with clean, dry hair

Before you begin, make sure your hair is clean and dry. If your hair is wet, it will take longer to dry and may not curl as well. You can either air dry your hair or use a regular hair dryer to dry it.

Step 2: Apply heat protectant spray

To protect your hair from heat damage, apply a heat protectant spray before you start styling. This will help reduce the risk of split ends and breakage.

Step 3: Divide your hair into sections

Divide your hair into sections using clips or hair ties. This will make it easier to work with and ensure that you curl all of your hair evenly.

Step 4: Start curling

Start by taking a small section of hair and wrap it around the brush of the blow dryer. Hold the brush in place for a few seconds and then release. Repeat this process with each section of hair, working your way around your head.

Step 5: Use the cool shot button

Once you've curled all of your hair, use the cool shot button on your blow dryer to set the curls in place. This will help them last longer and prevent them from falling out throughout the day.

Step 6: Add hairspray

To finish your look, add a light mist of hairspray to hold your curls in place.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you achieve the best results:

  • Use a smaller brush for tighter curls and a larger brush for looser waves.
  • Don't use too much hair at once. This can make it harder to curl and may cause tangles.
  • Use the cool shot button to help set your curls and reduce frizz.
  • Don't hold the brush in one place for too long. This can cause heat damage and may burn your hair.

FAQ

Can I use a blow dryer brush on wet hair?

No, it's not recommended to use a blow dryer brush on wet hair. This can cause heat damage and may make it harder to achieve the desired style.

How long should I hold the brush in place?

You should hold the brush in place for a few seconds before releasing it. This will help the hair curl and set in place.

Can I use a blow dryer brush on all hair types?

Yes, a blow dryer brush can be used on all hair types. However, you may need to adjust the temperature and speed settings depending on your hair type and thickness.

Hair Care

Using a blow dryer brush too often can cause heat damage and may lead to split ends and breakage. To keep your hair healthy, try to limit your use of heat styling tools and use a heat protectant spray before styling. You can also use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to help nourish your hair and prevent damage.
