What s The Best Haircut For A Fat Face

Written by Luffy Jul 26, 2023 Â· 6 min read

If you have a round or chubby face, finding the right haircut can be a challenge. You want a style that flatters your face shape and accentuates your best features. In this article, we'll explore some of the best haircuts for a fat face and provide tips on how to care for your hair.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How To
  • Step by Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

Before we dive into the best haircuts for a fat face, it's important to understand what a round face shape looks like. A round face is typically characterized by full cheeks, a rounded chin, and a wider forehead. The goal of a good haircut for a round face is to create the illusion of length and slimness, while also accentuating your best features.

How To

When deciding on a haircut for a fat face, there are a few things to keep in mind:

  • Choose a style that adds height to your face, such as a pixie cut or a bob with layers.
  • Avoid hairstyles that add width to your face, such as blunt bangs or a chin-length bob.
  • Consider adding volume to the crown of your head to elongate your face.
  • Opt for side-swept bangs to help balance out a fuller face.

Step by Step

Here are some step-by-step instructions for achieving some of the best haircuts for a fat face:

Pixie Cut

  1. Start by sectioning off the top of your hair.
  2. Cut the sides and back of your hair short, leaving the top section longer.
  3. Texturize the top section of your hair to add volume and height.
  4. Style with a light-hold pomade or wax.

Long Layers

  1. Start by sectioning off your hair horizontally at the crown of your head.
  2. Cut the top section of your hair into long layers.
  3. Blend the bottom section of your hair with the top layers.
  4. Add side-swept bangs to balance out your face.
  5. Style with a volumizing mousse or spray.

Tips

Here are a few additional tips for styling your hair if you have a fat face:

  • Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to add body to your hair.
  • Dry your hair with a diffuser to add volume and texture.
  • Avoid heavy, blunt bangs that can make your face appear wider.
  • Work with a stylist who understands how to create flattering hairstyles for round faces.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about haircuts for a fat face:

What haircut makes a fat face look thinner?

A pixie cut or a long bob with layers can help elongate your face and make it appear thinner. Avoid blunt bangs or chin-length bobs that add width to your face.

Can I wear my hair straight if I have a fat face?

Absolutely! Just be sure to add volume to the crown of your head to create the illusion of length.

What if I have curly hair?

Curly hair can be a great asset if you have a fat face. Embrace your natural texture and consider a layered haircut to add volume and shape to your curls.

Hair Care

Proper hair care is essential for maintaining healthy, beautiful hair. Here are a few tips for taking care of your hair:

  • Wash your hair every other day to avoid stripping it of natural oils.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair hydrated.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a leave-in conditioner with SPF.

By following these tips and choosing the right haircut for your face shape, you can achieve a flattering, stylish look that makes you feel confident and beautiful.
