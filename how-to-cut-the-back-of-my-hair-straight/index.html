How To Cut The Back Of My Hair Straight A Step By Step Guide

Written by Robby Jun 22, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Cutting your hair at home can be a daunting task, especially when it comes to cutting the back of your hair straight. However, with the right tools and techniques, it is possible to achieve a professional-looking haircut from the comfort of your own home. In this article, we will provide you with a step-by-step guide on how to cut the back of your hair straight.

How to Cut the Back of My Hair Straight

Before you begin, make sure you have the right tools. You will need a pair of sharp scissors, a comb, and a handheld mirror.

Step 1: Wash and Dry Your Hair

Start by washing and drying your hair. This will make it easier to cut and will also help you to see any uneven areas.

Step 2: Divide Your Hair

Divide your hair into sections. Use a comb to create a horizontal parting at the back of your head, just above your ear. Clip the top part of your hair up and out of the way.

Step 3: Cut the Bottom Section

Starting with the bottom section, comb your hair straight down and trim any split ends or uneven lengths. Be sure to cut in small sections and take your time.

Step 4: Cut the Middle Section

After you have finished cutting the bottom section, release the middle section of your hair and comb it straight down. Use the previous section as your guide and cut any uneven areas until you reach the desired length.

Step 5: Cut the Top Section

Finally, release the top section of your hair and comb it straight down. Use the previously cut sections as your guide and trim any uneven areas until you achieve the desired length.

Step 6: Check Your Work

Once you have finished cutting your hair, use a handheld mirror to check the back of your head. Make any final adjustments if necessary.

Tips for Cutting the Back of Your Hair Straight

- Use sharp scissors to avoid damaging your hair.

- Cut in small sections and take your time.

- Use a handheld mirror to check your work.

- If you are unsure, start with a small trim and work your way up.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I cut the back of my hair straight by myself?

Yes, with the right tools and techniques, it is possible to cut the back of your hair straight by yourself.

How often should I cut the back of my hair?

This will depend on your hair type and how quickly it grows. As a general rule, it is recommended to trim your hair every 6-8 weeks.

What if I make a mistake while cutting my hair?

Don't panic! Mistakes happen, even to professionals. If you make a mistake, try to fix it by trimming the surrounding area. If all else fails, you can always visit a salon for a professional fix.

Hair Care After Cutting

After cutting your hair, it is important to take care of it. Use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner, and avoid using too much heat when styling. Regular trims will also help to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.

Cutting the back of your hair straight can be a challenging task, but with the right tools and techniques, it is possible to achieve a professional-looking haircut at home. Remember to take your time, cut in small sections, and check your work with a handheld mirror. Happy cutting!
