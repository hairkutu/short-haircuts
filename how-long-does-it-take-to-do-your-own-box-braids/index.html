How Long Does It Take To Do Your Own Box Braids

Written by Bardi Mar 22, 2023 · 7 min read

Are you thinking of doing your own box braids at home? It’s a great way to save money and express your creativity. However, one of the most common questions people have is how long it takes to complete the process. In this article, we’ll go over how long it takes to do your own box braids, as well as some helpful tips and advice.

Table of Contents

  1. Description
  2. How To
  3. Step by Step
  4. Tips
  5. FAQ
  6. Hair Care

Description

Box braids are a popular protective hairstyle that involves braiding hair into small, square-shaped sections. The process can take several hours to complete, depending on the length and thickness of your hair, as well as your skill level. If you’re new to braiding or have never done box braids before, it may take longer to complete the process.

How To

Before you begin braiding, you’ll need to gather your supplies. This includes hair extensions, a comb, hair clips, and hair ties. You may also want to use a leave-in conditioner to help detangle your hair and make the braiding process easier.

Step 1: Wash and Dry Your Hair

Start by washing your hair with a gentle shampoo and conditioner. Pat your hair dry with a towel, then use a blow dryer to fully dry your hair. It’s important to start with clean, dry hair to make the braiding process easier.

Step 2: Section Your Hair

Using a comb, divide your hair into small, square-shaped sections. The size of the sections will depend on the size of the braids you want. Use hair clips to keep each section separated.

Step 3: Attach the Extensions

Take one section of hair and attach the hair extension to the root of your hair. Divide the extension hair into three sections and begin braiding. As you braid, add in small sections of your natural hair to create the braided effect. Continue braiding until you reach the end of your hair, then secure with a hair tie.

Step 4: Repeat the Process

Continue the braiding process on each section of hair until your entire head is braided. This can take several hours to complete, depending on the length and thickness of your hair.

Tips

Here are some helpful tips to make the braiding process easier and more efficient:

  • Use a leave-in conditioner to help detangle your hair
  • Take breaks if your hands get tired
  • Use hair clips to keep each section separated
  • Start with small sections of hair to make the braiding process easier
  • Be patient and take your time to avoid mistakes

FAQ

How long does it take to do box braids?

The time it takes to do box braids can vary depending on the length and thickness of your hair, as well as your skill level. On average, it can take anywhere from 4-8 hours to complete the process.

Can I do box braids on my own?

Yes, you can do box braids on your own. However, it may take longer and require more patience and skill than going to a professional stylist.

How long do box braids last?

Box braids can last anywhere from 4-8 weeks, depending on how well you take care of them. It’s important to moisturize your scalp and hair regularly, and to avoid excessive pulling or tugging on the braids.

Hair Care

After you’ve completed your box braids, it’s important to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and prevent damage. Here are some tips:

  • Moisturize your scalp and hair regularly with a leave-in conditioner or oil
  • Avoid excessive pulling or tugging on the braids
  • Cover your hair with a satin or silk scarf at night to prevent frizz and breakage
  • Wash your hair with a gentle shampoo and conditioner every 2-3 weeks
  • Remove the braids carefully to avoid damaging your hair

In conclusion, doing your own box braids can be a fun and rewarding experience, but it also requires patience and skill. Depending on the length and thickness of your hair, the process can take several hours to complete. However, by following these tips and taking care of your hair, you can enjoy your box braids for several weeks.
