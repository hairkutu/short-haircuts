Women s Short Hairstyles For Thick Hair 2020

Description

If you have thick hair, you know how hard it can be to manage. It can be even harder to find a hairstyle that works well with your hair type. Luckily, there are plenty of women’s short hairstyles for thick hair that are trendy and easy to maintain. Whether you’re looking for a bold new look or just want to freshen up your style, there are plenty of options to choose from.

How to

Before you start picking out a new hairstyle, it’s important to consider a few things. First, think about how much time you’re willing to put into styling your hair each day. Some short hairstyles require more maintenance than others. Second, consider your face shape. Certain styles will look better on certain face shapes, so it’s important to choose a style that flatters your features. Finally, think about your personal style. Choose a hairstyle that matches your personality and makes you feel confident.

Step by step

Here are some popular women’s short hairstyles for thick hair:

Pixie Cut

The pixie cut is a classic short hairstyle that looks great on all face shapes. This style is low-maintenance and can be dressed up or down. To achieve this look, ask your stylist to cut your hair short on the sides and back, leaving a longer layer on top. You can style the top layer with product to create texture and volume.

Bob Cut

The bob cut is another popular short hairstyle that works well with thick hair. This style is versatile and can be customized to suit your face shape and personal style. There are many variations of the bob cut, including the classic bob, the inverted bob, and the asymmetrical bob.

Shag Cut

The shag cut is a layered hairstyle that works well with thick hair. This style adds texture and volume to your hair while still looking sleek and polished. To achieve this look, ask your stylist to cut your hair into layers of varying lengths.

Tips

Here are some tips for maintaining your short hairstyle:

  • Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to add body to your hair.
  • Blow dry your hair upside down to add volume at the roots.
  • Use a texturizing spray to add texture and hold to your hair.
  • Trim your hair every 4-6 weeks to maintain your style.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about women’s short hairstyles for thick hair:

Q: Will short hair make my face look rounder?

A: No, a properly styled short haircut can actually make your face appear slimmer.

Q: Can I still style my short hair for special occasions?

A: Yes, there are many ways to style short hair for a special occasion. You can add accessories, such as hair clips or headbands, or use styling products to create a more formal look.

Hair Care

To keep your short hairstyle looking its best, it’s important to take care of your hair. Use a high-quality shampoo and conditioner that’s designed for your hair type. Avoid using hot tools, such as curling irons and straighteners, too often, as they can damage your hair. Finally, protect your hair from the sun and other environmental factors by wearing a hat or using a protective spray.

With these tips and ideas, you’re sure to find a short hairstyle that works well with your thick hair. Whether you opt for a classic pixie cut or a trendy shag cut, you’ll love how easy it is to maintain your new look.
