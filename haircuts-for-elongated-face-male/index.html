Haircuts For Elongated Face Male The Ultimate Guide

Written by Oliver Aug 04, 2023 Â· 8 min read

If you have an elongated face, you know how challenging it can be to find the perfect haircut that flatters your features. However, with the right haircut, you can highlight your best features and create a balanced appearance. In this guide, we'll go through the most popular haircuts for elongated face male, how to style them, and tips for hair care.

Table of Contents

  • How to Choose the Right Haircut
  • Step-by-Step Guide to Styling
  • Tips for Maintaining Your Haircut
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care for Elongated Face Male

How to Choose the Right Haircut

When looking for a haircut that suits an elongated face, you want to create the illusion of width and volume around the sides of your face. The most flattering haircuts for elongated face male include:

1. Classic Side Part

The classic side part is a timeless haircut that works well for most face shapes, including an elongated face. The side part adds volume to the top of your head, while the tapered sides and back create a balanced look.

2. Buzz Cut

A buzz cut is a low-maintenance and stylish haircut that can help balance out an elongated face. The short length of the buzz cut draws attention away from the length of your face and towards your facial features.

3. Short Pompadour

The short pompadour is a modern take on a classic hairstyle that adds volume and width to your hair, making it a great option for elongated face male. The style features a high-volume top with tapered sides and back.

Step-by-Step Guide to Styling

Once you've chosen the right haircut, it's essential to style it correctly to achieve the best results. Follow these steps to style your hair:

1. Prep Your Hair

Start by washing your hair with a high-quality shampoo and conditioner to remove any buildup and add moisture. Towel-dry your hair and apply a styling product that suits your hair type.

2. Blow-Dry Your Hair

Using a hair dryer, blow-dry your hair in the direction you want it to go. For an elongated face, you want to create volume at the top and width around the sides. Use a round brush to add volume to the top of your hair.

3. Style Your Hair

Once your hair is dry, use a styling product, such as wax or pomade, to create your desired look. Use your fingers or a comb to style your hair and create volume around the sides of your face.

Tips for Maintaining Your Haircut

To keep your haircut looking its best, follow these tips:

1. Get Regular Trims

Regular trims are essential to keep your haircut looking fresh and tidy. Visit a hairstylist every four to six weeks to maintain your haircut's shape and length.

2. Use the Right Products

Using the right hair products can make a significant difference in how your haircut looks. Choose products that suit your hair type and desired look.

3. Protect Your Hair from Heat

Heat styling tools, such as hairdryers and straighteners, can damage your hair and make it look dull. Use a heat protectant spray before using any heat styling tools to protect your hair from heat damage.

FAQs

1. Can an elongated face male have long hair?

Yes, an elongated face male can have long hair. However, it's essential to choose the right haircut that flatters your features, such as layered long hair that adds width and volume to your hair.

2. What is the best haircut for an elongated face male with a receding hairline?

A buzz cut or a shaved head can be a good option for an elongated face male with a receding hairline. These haircuts draw attention away from the hairline and towards your facial features.

Hair Care for Elongated Face Male

Proper hair care is vital for maintaining a healthy and stylish haircut. Follow these tips for hair care:

1. Wash Your Hair Regularly

Wash your hair every two to three days to remove any buildup and dirt. Use a high-quality shampoo and conditioner that suits your hair type.

2. Protect Your Hair from the Sun

Exposure to the sun can damage your hair and make it look dull. Wear a hat or use a hair product that contains UV protection to protect your hair from the sun.

3. Eat a Balanced Diet

Eating a balanced diet that includes protein, vitamins, and minerals is essential for healthy hair. Include foods such as fish, nuts, leafy greens, and fruits in your diet to promote hair growth and strength.

With the right haircut, styling, and hair care, you can enhance your features and create a balanced appearance. Experiment with different haircuts and styles to find the one that suits your personality and lifestyle.
