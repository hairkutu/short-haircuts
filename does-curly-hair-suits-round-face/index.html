Does Curly Hair Suit Round Face

Written by Bardi Jul 17, 2023 Â· 8 min read

If you are someone with a round face, you might have wondered whether curly hair would suit you or not. Curly hair can be a little challenging to manage, and choosing the perfect hairstyle for your face shape can be even more daunting. In this article, we will explore whether curly hair suits a round face or not and provide you with some tips and tricks to help you style your curls perfectly.

Table of Contents

  2. How to
  3. Step by Step
  4. Tips

Description

A round face shape is characterized by having a circular shape with no sharp angles or prominent features. If you have a round face, you may struggle with finding a hairstyle that complements your face shape. When it comes to curly hair, the answer to whether it suits a round face or not is, it depends.

The key to finding the perfect hairstyle for a round face is to create an illusion of length and height. Curly hair can be great for this as it adds volume and texture to the hair, which can create the illusion of height and length. However, the wrong curly hairstyle can make your face look even rounder, so it's essential to choose the right style.

How to

The first step to finding the perfect curly hairstyle for a round face is to determine your face shape. You can do this by measuring the length and width of your face and comparing the two. If the measurements are similar, you have a round face shape.

Once you have determined your face shape, it's time to choose the right curly hairstyle. The key is to avoid hairstyles that add width to your face and instead opt for styles that create the illusion of length and height. Here are some curly hairstyles that suit a round face:

Long Layers

Long layers can be great for a round face as they add length and movement to the hair. This style works well with curly hair as it adds volume and texture to the hair, creating the illusion of height and length.

Side-Swept Bangs

Side-swept bangs can help to elongate a round face as they draw attention to the eyes and cheekbones. This style works well with curly hair as it adds texture and volume to the hair.

Loose Waves

Loose waves can be great for a round face as they add movement and texture to the hair, creating the illusion of length and height. This style works well with curly hair as it adds volume and texture to the hair.

Step by Step

Here are some step-by-step instructions on how to achieve the perfect curly hairstyle for a round face:

  1. Start by washing your hair with a curl-enhancing shampoo and conditioner.
  2. Towel dry your hair and apply a curl-enhancing product, such as a mousse or gel.
  3. Divide your hair into sections and use a diffuser attachment on your hairdryer to dry each section. This will help to enhance your curls and add volume to your hair.
  4. Once your hair is dry, use a curling iron to define any loose curls and add texture to your hair.
  5. Finish off the look with a hairspray to hold your curls in place.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you style your curly hair perfectly:

  • Invest in a good quality curl-enhancing shampoo and conditioner.
  • Use a diffuser attachment on your hairdryer to enhance your curls and add volume to your hair.
  • Avoid using a brush on your curly hair as it can cause frizz.
  • Use a curling iron to define any loose curls and add texture to your hair.
  • Finish off the look with a hairspray to hold your curls in place.

FAQ

What curly hairstyles suit a round face?

Long layers, side-swept bangs, and loose waves are great curly hairstyles for a round face.

How do I determine my face shape?

You can determine your face shape by measuring the length and width of your face and comparing the two.

What should I avoid when styling curly hair for a round face?

Avoid hairstyles that add width to your face and instead opt for styles that create the illusion of length and height.

Hair Care

When it comes to maintaining curly hair, it's essential to use products that enhance and define your curls. Here are some tips for taking care of your curly hair:

  • Use a curl-enhancing shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using a brush on your curly hair as it can cause frizz.
  • Use a diffuser attachment on your hairdryer to enhance your curls and add volume to your hair.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair moisturized.

In conclusion, curly hair can suit a round face as long as you choose the right hairstyle. Long layers, side-swept bangs, and loose waves are great curly hairstyles for a round face. By following these tips and tricks, you can style your curly hair perfectly and enhance your natural beauty.
