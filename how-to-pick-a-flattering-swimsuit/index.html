How To Pick A Flattering Swimsuit In 2023

Written by Bardi May 03, 2023 · 8 min read

Summer is just around the corner and it’s time to hit the beach or pool! But before you do, it’s important to find a swimsuit that makes you feel confident and comfortable. With so many styles, colors, and patterns to choose from, it can be overwhelming. In this article, we’ll give you some tips on how to pick a flattering swimsuit that will make you look and feel your best.

Table of Contents:

  • How to Pick a Flattering Swimsuit
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Finding the Perfect Swimsuit
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care Tips for the Beach or Pool

How to Pick a Flattering Swimsuit

The first step to finding a flattering swimsuit is to know your body shape. Everyone’s body is unique, but there are some general shapes that can help guide you in the right direction:

  • Pear-shaped: Your hips are wider than your shoulders.
  • Apple-shaped: Your midsection is wider than your hips and shoulders.
  • Inverted triangle: Your shoulders are wider than your hips.
  • Hourglass: Your hips and shoulders are about the same width and you have a defined waist.

Once you know your body shape, you can look for swimsuits that will accentuate your best features and downplay any areas you’re not as comfortable with. Here are some tips:

For Pear-Shaped Bodies:

Highlight your upper body with a halter top or a swimsuit with ruffles or embellishments on top. Look for bottoms that have a darker color or pattern to minimize your hips.

For Apple-Shaped Bodies:

Draw attention away from your midsection with a one-piece swimsuit or a tankini with a longer top. Look for swimsuits with ruching or shirring to help camouflage any problem areas.

For Inverted Triangle Bodies:

Balance out your proportions with a swimsuit that has a solid or dark-colored top and a patterned or brighter bottom. Look for bottoms with ruffles or detailing to add volume to your hips.

For Hourglass Bodies:

Emphasize your curves with a swimsuit that has a defined waistline or a high-waisted bottom. Look for tops that have underwire or padding for support.

Step-by-Step Guide

Now that you know your body shape and what to look for, here’s a step-by-step guide to finding the perfect swimsuit:

  1. Start by trying on swimsuits in your size and body shape.
  2. Look for swimsuits that flatter your best features and downplay any areas you’re not as comfortable with.
  3. Try on different styles, colors, and patterns to see what looks best on you.
  4. Make sure the swimsuit is comfortable and fits well. You don’t want anything to slip or ride up while you’re swimming or sunbathing.
  5. Check the swimsuit from all angles, including the back and sides, to make sure it looks good from every angle.
  6. Consider accessorizing with a cover-up or hat to complete your beach or pool look.

Tips for Finding the Perfect Swimsuit

Here are some additional tips to keep in mind when shopping for a swimsuit:

  • Try on swimsuits in natural light to get a better idea of how they will look outside.
  • Don’t be afraid to ask for help from a sales associate. They can offer advice on what styles and colors will work best for your body shape and skin tone.
  • Consider investing in a higher-quality swimsuit that will last longer and hold its shape.
  • If you’re self-conscious about your stomach, look for swimsuits with tummy control panels or high-waisted bottoms.
  • If you’re busty, look for swimsuits with underwire or thicker straps for support.

FAQ

Q: What color swimsuit is most slimming?

A: Dark colors like black, navy, and deep purple are generally more slimming than lighter colors or patterns. However, it’s ultimately about finding a swimsuit that makes you feel confident and comfortable.

Q: What should I wear if I’m not comfortable showing my stomach?

A: Look for swimsuits with a high-waisted bottom, a tankini with a longer top, or a one-piece swimsuit with ruching or shirring to help camouflage your midsection.

Q: How can I prevent my swimsuit from fading or stretching?

A: Rinse your swimsuit in cool water after each use and lay it flat to dry. Avoid wringing it out or hanging it up, as this can cause stretching. Follow the care instructions on the tag to ensure your swimsuit lasts as long as possible.

Hair Care Tips for the Beach or Pool

Don’t forget to take care of your hair while you’re at the beach or pool! Here are some tips:

  • Wear a hat or use a leave-in conditioner with SPF to protect your hair from the sun.
  • Rinse your hair with fresh water before and after swimming to help prevent damage from salt or chlorine.
  • Avoid using hot tools like blow dryers or straighteners on wet hair, as this can cause damage.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy and hydrated.

Now that you have all the tips and tricks for finding the perfect swimsuit and taking care of your hair, you’re ready to hit the beach or pool with confidence!
