How To Cut Bangs For Round Face A Complete Guide

Written by William Jul 25, 2023 Â· 8 min read

Are you tired of your same old hairstyle and looking for a change? Bangs can be an excellent option to switch up your look. However, for those with a round face shape, it can be challenging to decide on the perfect style. But don't worry, we've got you covered. In this guide, we will discuss how to cut bangs for a round face shape, including tips, steps, and hair care.

Description

Before we dive into the process of cutting bangs for a round face shape, let's understand what it means to have a round face. A round face shape has soft and curved features with a wide forehead, a rounded chin, and a cheekbone width that is similar to the face's length.

When it comes to cutting bangs for a round face, the aim should be to create a balance between the forehead and the cheekbones by adding angles to the face. The right style can help elongate the face and make it appear more oval.

How to Cut Bangs for Round Face

The first step in cutting bangs for a round face is to decide on the style that suits your face shape. Some popular options include:

  • Side-swept bangs: These are longer bangs that are swept to one side of the face. They create an angle and help elongate the face.
  • Blunt bangs: These are straight across bangs that can work well on a round face if they are cut just above the eyebrows. They create a horizontal line that can make the face appear shorter.
  • Asymmetrical bangs: These are angled bangs that can be longer on one side and shorter on the other. They create an angle that can help elongate the face.

Once you have decided on the style, it's time to start cutting. Here's how:

Step by Step Guide

Step 1: Start with clean and dry hair. Comb it thoroughly to remove any tangles or knots.

Step 2: Section off the hair that you want to cut into bangs. Use a comb to create a straight line that starts from the arch of one eyebrow to the other.

Step 3: Use a pair of sharp scissors to cut the hair. Start by cutting a small amount and gradually increase it as needed. Remember, it's better to cut less and add more later than to cut too much at once.

Step 4: When cutting, make sure to keep the scissors at an angle. This will help create a layered effect that can add volume and texture to the hair.

Step 5: Once you have cut the hair, comb it out to see how it looks. If needed, make any additional adjustments.

Tips for Cutting Bangs for Round Face

Here are some additional tips to keep in mind when cutting bangs for a round face:

  • Start with longer bangs and gradually cut them shorter. This will give you more control and ensure that you don't cut too much at once.
  • Avoid cutting bangs straight across as it can make the face appear even rounder.
  • Take your time and don't rush the process. Cutting bangs can be tricky, so it's better to take it slow and be patient.
  • Consider getting a professional haircut if you're unsure about cutting your bangs yourself.

FAQs

Q: How often should I trim my bangs?

A: It depends on how fast your hair grows and how long you want your bangs to be. On average, you should trim your bangs every 4-6 weeks to keep them looking fresh.

Q: Can I cut my bangs when they are wet?

A: It's better to cut bangs when they are dry as wet hair can shrink when it dries, making it difficult to determine the final length.

Hair Care for Bangs

Now that you have cut your bangs, it's essential to take proper care of them to keep them looking great. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your bangs regularly to prevent them from getting greasy.
  • Blow-dry your bangs in the direction you want them to sit. This will help set them in place.
  • Use a small amount of hair serum or oil to add shine and reduce frizz.
  • Avoid touching your bangs too much as this can make them greasy and cause them to lose their shape.

Cutting bangs for a round face shape can be challenging, but with the right style and technique, you can achieve a beautiful and flattering look. Follow these steps, tips, and hair care advice to get the perfect bangs for your round face.
