How To Style Shoulder Length Hair For Round Faces A Complete Guide

Written by Eveline Mar 28, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Are you looking for ways to style your shoulder length hair for a round face? Look no further! We've got you covered with this complete guide on how to style your shoulder length hair to flatter your round face. From tips to step-by-step instructions, we've got everything you need to know to achieve the perfect look.

Description

Shoulder length hair is a versatile length that can be styled in many different ways. However, if you have a round face, finding the right style can be a challenge. Round faces tend to have softer features and a wider jawline, which can make it difficult to find a style that flatters and accentuates your best features. In this guide, we'll show you how to style your shoulder length hair in a way that complements your round face and brings out your natural beauty.

How to Style Shoulder Length Hair for Round Faces

Step-by-Step

Step 1: Start with clean, dry hair. Wash your hair with a shampoo and conditioner that's suitable for your hair type.

Step 2: Apply a heat protectant spray to your hair. This will help protect your hair from damage when using heat styling tools.

Step 3: Blow dry your hair using a round brush. This will help create volume and add movement to your hair.

Step 4: Use a curling iron to create loose waves. Start at the mid-length of your hair and wrap sections of hair around the curling iron. Hold for a few seconds, then release.

Step 5: Use a comb to gently tease your hair at the crown. This will help create height and add fullness to your hair.

Step 6: Finish with a light hairspray to hold your style in place.

Tips

- Avoid hairstyles that are too short, as they can make your face appear even rounder.

- Bangs can be a great way to add definition to your face, but make sure they're not too short or too blunt.

- Layered hairstyles can help add texture and movement to your hair, which can help create a more flattering look.

FAQ

What hairstyles should I avoid if I have a round face?

Avoid hairstyles that are too short, as they can make your face appear even rounder. Also avoid hairstyles that are too blunt, as they can make your face look wider.

What hairstyles are best for round faces?

Hairstyles that are longer than chin length and have layers can be very flattering for round faces. Bangs can also be a great way to add definition to your face.

How often should I wash my hair?

This depends on your hair type and lifestyle. Some people may need to wash their hair every day, while others can go several days without washing. Experiment to find what works best for you.

Hair Care

Proper hair care is essential for healthy, beautiful hair. Here are some tips to keep your hair looking its best:

- Use a shampoo and conditioner that's suitable for your hair type.

- Avoid using too much heat when styling your hair, as this can cause damage.

- Use a heat protectant spray when using heat styling tools.

- Get regular trims to prevent split ends and keep your hair looking healthy.

By following these tips and styling your shoulder length hair in a way that flatters your round face, you can achieve a beautiful, confident look that's sure to turn heads.
