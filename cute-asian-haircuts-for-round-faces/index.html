Cute Asian Haircuts For Round Faces

Written by Petter May 27, 2023 Â· 5 min read

Are you tired of searching for the perfect haircut that flatters your round face shape? Look no further! In this article, we will be discussing the best cute asian haircuts for round faces. With proper styling and a little bit of confidence, you can rock any of these hairstyles.

Description

Round faces are characterized by a soft, circular shape with a wide forehead, full cheeks, and a rounded chin. The goal of any haircut for a round face is to create the illusion of a longer, more oval-shaped face. The best haircuts for round faces are those that add height and volume to the crown while slimming down the cheeks and jawline.

How To

Before choosing a haircut, it's important to determine your face shape. The easiest way to do this is to pull your hair back and trace the outline of your face in the mirror. Once you've determined that you have a round face, you can start looking for hairstyles that flatter your features. Consult with your hairstylist to determine which style works best for your hair type and face shape.

Step by Step

1. Start by washing and conditioning your hair. Towel dry your hair until it's damp. 2. Apply a heat protectant to your hair to prevent heat damage. 3. Blow dry your hair using a round brush to add volume to the crown. 4. Use a curling iron to add loose waves or curls to the ends of your hair for a more feminine look. 5. Finish off with a hairspray to hold the style in place.

Tips

When choosing a haircut, keep in mind that shorter haircuts tend to make round faces appear wider. Instead, opt for a medium or long length haircut that adds height to the crown. Bangs are also a great way to soften the forehead and draw attention away from the cheeks. Side-swept bangs, in particular, are a great choice for round faces.

FAQ

Q: Can I still wear my hair in a bob if I have a round face? A: Yes! Just make sure the length of the bob falls below the chin to elongate the face. Adding layers to the bob can also add volume to the crown.

Q: What hair color looks best on round faces? A: Warm, caramel tones and highlights can add dimension to the hair and balance out a round face shape.

Hair Care

Maintaining healthy hair is essential for any haircut to look its best. Make sure to wash your hair regularly with a shampoo and conditioner that's suited for your hair type. Avoid using too much heat on your hair and use a heat protectant before styling. Trim your hair every 6-8 weeks to prevent split ends and maintain its shape.

With these tips and hairstyles, you can confidently rock any cute asian haircut for round faces. Remember to embrace your unique features and have fun with your hair!
