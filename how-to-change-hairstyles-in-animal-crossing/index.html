How To Change Hairstyles In Animal Crossing

Written by Eveline Jul 30, 2023 · 6 min read

Are you tired of always having the same hairstyle in Animal Crossing? Do you want to switch things up and try a new look? Well, you’re in luck! Changing your hairstyle in Animal Crossing is easy and can be done in just a few simple steps. In this article, we’ll take you through the process of changing your hairstyle, step-by-step. But before we get started, let’s talk about why changing your hairstyle in Animal Crossing is important.

Why Change Your Hairstyle in Animal Crossing?

Changing your hairstyle in Animal Crossing is an important part of the game. It allows you to express your creativity and individuality. With so many different hairstyles to choose from, you can create a look that is unique to you. Changing your hairstyle can also help you feel refreshed and excited about playing the game. It’s a great way to mix things up and keep the game interesting.

How to Change Your Hairstyle in Animal Crossing

Changing your hairstyle in Animal Crossing is a simple process. Here’s how to do it:

Step 1: Purchase a Mirror

The first thing you’ll need to do is purchase a mirror. Mirrors can be purchased from Nook’s Cranny or from the Nook Shopping app on your NookPhone. Once you have a mirror, place it somewhere in your house where you can easily access it.

Step 2: Interact with the Mirror

Stand in front of the mirror and press the A button to interact with it. This will bring up a menu of options, including “Change Look.” Select this option.

Step 3: Choose a Hairstyle

Once you’ve selected “Change Look,” you’ll be taken to a menu of different hairstyles to choose from. Scroll through the options until you find one that you like. You can also change your hair color by selecting the “Hair Color” option.

Step 4: Confirm Your New Look

Once you’ve chosen your new hairstyle and hair color, confirm your selection. Your character will then appear with their new look.

Tips for Changing Your Hairstyle in Animal Crossing

Here are a few tips to keep in mind when changing your hairstyle in Animal Crossing:

  • Try on different hairstyles before committing to one.
  • Don’t be afraid to try something new.
  • Consider your character’s personality and style when choosing a new hairstyle.
  • Remember that you can always change your hairstyle again in the future.

FAQ: Frequently Asked Questions

How much does a mirror cost in Animal Crossing?

A mirror costs 1,500 Bells in Animal Crossing.

Can I change my hairstyle more than once?

Yes, you can change your hairstyle as many times as you like.

Do I need to go to a salon to change my hairstyle?

No, you can change your hairstyle at home using a mirror.

Hair Care in Animal Crossing

Just like in real life, it’s important to take care of your hair in Animal Crossing. Here are a few tips to keep your hair looking healthy:

  • Wash your hair regularly.
  • Use a hairbrush or comb to style your hair.
  • Avoid using too much hair product.
  • Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat.

Changing your hairstyle in Animal Crossing is a fun and easy way to express your creativity and individuality. With so many different hairstyles to choose from, the possibilities are endless. Just remember to have fun and don’t be afraid to try something new!
