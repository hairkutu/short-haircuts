Which Side Should I Part My Curly Hair

Written by Aprilia Jul 31, 2023 Â· 6 min read

When it comes to styling curly hair, one common question that arises is which side should I part my curly hair? The answer to this question is not a one-size-fits-all as it varies from person to person. However, in this article, we will provide you with some tips and tricks to help you decide which side to part your curly hair.

Description

Curly hair can be challenging to manage, and finding the right parting can make all the difference. The right parting can help to enhance your natural curls, provide more volume, and create a flattering frame for your face. However, if you are unsure which side to part your hair, it can be overwhelming.

How to

Before you decide which side to part your curly hair, there are a few things you need to consider. Firstly, you need to determine your face shape. This will help you find a parting that flatters your features.

Once you know your face shape, you can start experimenting with different partings. Start by creating a middle part and then move on to a side part. Try parting your hair on the left and then on the right side to see which one suits you best.

Another thing to consider when deciding which side to part your curly hair is the direction of your curls. You want to part your hair in a way that enhances your curls and gives you more volume.

Step by Step

Step 1: Determine Your Face Shape

Your face shape will determine which parting will flatter your features. The most common face shapes are round, oval, square, heart-shaped, and diamond-shaped.

Step 2: Experiment With Different Partings

Try creating a middle part and then move on to a side part. Start by parting your hair on the left and then on the right side to see which one suits you best.

Step 3: Consider the Direction of Your Curls

You want to part your hair in a way that enhances your curls and gives you more volume. To do this, consider the direction of your curls and part your hair accordingly.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you decide which side to part your curly hair:

  • Determine Your Face Shape
  • Experiment With Different Partings
  • Consider the Direction of Your Curls
  • Use a Wide-Tooth Comb to Part Your Hair
  • Consider Your Natural Parting

FAQ

Q: Which side should I part my curly hair?
A: It depends on your face shape and the direction of your curls. Experiment with different partings to find the one that suits you best.

Q: Can parting my hair damage my curls?
A: Parting your hair can cause friction, which can lead to breakage. However, if you use a wide-tooth comb and part your hair gently, you can minimize damage.

Hair Care

Curly hair requires special care to keep it healthy and looking its best. Here are some tips to help you care for your curly hair:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo
  • Deep condition your hair regularly
  • Avoid using heat styling tools
  • Use a diffuser when blow-drying your hair
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it is dry

Now that you know which side to part your curly hair, you can experiment with different partings to find the one that enhances your natural curls and flatters your features. Remember to take good care of your curly hair, and it will reward you with beautiful, healthy locks.
