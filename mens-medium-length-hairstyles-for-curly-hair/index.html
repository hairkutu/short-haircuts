Mens Medium Length Hairstyles For Curly Hair A Guide For 2023

Written by Eveline Jul 09, 2023 · 7 min read

If you're a man with curly hair, you know how challenging it can be to find the right hairstyle that works for you. With medium length hair, you have a lot of options that allow you to embrace your curls and create a look that's both stylish and practical. In this guide, we'll explore some of the best mens medium length hairstyles for curly hair, as well as provide tips on how to care for your curls and keep them looking great.

Table of Contents

  • How to Choose the Right Hairstyle
  • Step-by-Step Guide to Styling Your Curls

How to Choose the Right Hairstyle

When it comes to choosing a hairstyle, there are a few factors to consider. First, you'll want to think about the shape of your face. For example, men with round faces may want to avoid hairstyles that add volume to the sides of their head, as this can make their face appear wider. Instead, they may prefer a hairstyle that's longer on top and shorter on the sides.

Another thing to think about is your hair type. Mens medium length hairstyles for curly hair will look different depending on whether your curls are tight or loose. If you have tighter curls, you may want to opt for a shorter hairstyle that allows your curls to spring up. If you have looser curls, you may prefer a longer hairstyle that allows your curls to hang down.

Finally, you'll want to consider your personal style and how much time you're willing to spend on your hair each day. Mens medium length hairstyles for curly hair can range from low-maintenance to high-maintenance, so it's important to choose a style that fits your lifestyle.

Step-by-Step Guide to Styling Your Curls

Once you've chosen a hairstyle, it's important to know how to style your curls to achieve the look you want. Here's a step-by-step guide:

  1. Start with clean, damp hair.
  2. Apply a styling product, such as a curl cream or mousse, to your hair. Be sure to distribute the product evenly throughout your hair.
  3. Use a diffuser attachment on your blow dryer to dry your hair. This will help enhance your curls and prevent frizz.
  4. Use your fingers to shape your curls as desired.
  5. Finish with a hairspray or other finishing product to help hold your style in place.

Remember, the key to styling mens medium length hairstyles for curly hair is to embrace your curls and work with them, rather than trying to fight them.

Tips for Maintaining Your Curly Hair

Curly hair requires a bit of extra care to keep it looking its best. Here are some tips:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to help prevent your curls from drying out.
  • Avoid using hot tools, such as flat irons or curling irons, as these can damage your curls.
  • Invest in a good leave-in conditioner or hair oil to help keep your curls hydrated and healthy.
  • Consider getting a regular trim to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.

FAQs About Mens Medium Length Hairstyles for Curly Hair

1. What are some popular mens medium length hairstyles for curly hair?

Some popular options include the curly undercut, the messy curly quiff, and the curly fringe.

2. How often should I wash my curly hair?

It depends on your hair type and personal preference. Some people with curly hair prefer to wash their hair every day, while others opt for every few days.

3. Can I straighten my curly hair?

You can, but it's important to use a heat protectant spray and avoid using hot tools too frequently, as this can damage your hair.

Hair Care Recommendations for Curly Hair

If you're looking for products specifically designed to care for curly hair, consider the following:

  • Shea Moisture Coconut & Hibiscus Curl Enhancing Smoothie: This styling cream helps define curls and prevent frizz.
  • DevaCurl No-Poo Original Zero Lather Conditioning Cleanser: This sulfate-free cleanser helps gently cleanse curly hair without stripping it of its natural oils.
  • Ouidad Climate Control Heat & Humidity Gel: This gel helps keep curls in place while also protecting them from humidity.

By following these tips and using the right products, you can create a stylish and manageable mens medium length hairstyle for curly hair that you'll love.
