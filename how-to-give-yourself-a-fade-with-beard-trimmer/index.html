How To Give Yourself A Fade With Beard Trimmer A Step By Step Guide

Written by Robby May 06, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Are you tired of going to the barbershop every few weeks to get a fade haircut? Why not learn how to do it yourself at home? With a beard trimmer, you can give yourself a fade that looks just as good as the one you get at the barbershop. In this article, we will guide you through the process of giving yourself a fade with a beard trimmer.

Description

A fade is a popular haircut that involves gradually decreasing the length of hair from the top of the head to the sides and back. It creates a clean, sharp look that is perfect for any occasion. A beard trimmer is a versatile tool that can be used for trimming facial hair, but it can also be used to give yourself a fade.

How to Give Yourself a Fade with Beard Trimmer

Step 1: Choose the Right Beard Trimmer

The first step in giving yourself a fade with a beard trimmer is to choose the right one. Look for a trimmer that comes with different length guards and has a detachable blade for easy cleaning. Also, make sure the trimmer is fully charged before you begin.

Step 2: Start with the Longest Guard

Attach the longest guard to the trimmer and start trimming the hair at the top of your head. Work your way around the head, making sure to trim the hair evenly. Use slow, even strokes to avoid cutting too much hair at once.

Step 3: Switch to a Shorter Guard

Once you have trimmed the hair with the longest guard, switch to a shorter guard and trim the hair around the sides and back of your head. Start at the bottom and work your way up, using slow, even strokes. Make sure to blend the hair from the longer hair on top to the shorter hair on the sides.

Step 4: Remove the Guard

Once you have trimmed the hair with the shorter guard, remove the guard from the trimmer and use the blade to create a clean, sharp line along the hairline and around the ears. Be careful not to cut too much hair, as this can ruin the fade.

Step 5: Blend the Hair

To blend the hair from the top to the sides and back, use the trimmer without a guard and make small, circular motions. This will help create a seamless transition between the different lengths of hair.

Step 6: Clean Up the Neckline

Use the trimmer to clean up the neckline and create a clean, sharp line. This will give your fade a polished look.

Tips

  • Start with a longer guard and work your way down to avoid cutting too much hair at once.
  • Use slow, even strokes to avoid cutting unevenly.
  • Take your time and be patient â€“ a good fade takes time and practice.
  • Clean your trimmer after each use to keep it in good condition.

FAQ

Can I give myself a fade with any beard trimmer?

While you can use any beard trimmer to give yourself a fade, it is best to use one that comes with different length guards and has a detachable blade for easy cleaning.

Is it easy to give yourself a fade with a beard trimmer?

While it takes practice to give yourself a good fade, it is not difficult once you get the hang of it. Take your time and be patient, and you will be able to give yourself a fade that looks just as good as the one you get at the barbershop.

Hair Care

After giving yourself a fade, it is important to take care of your hair to keep it looking great. Use a quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair clean and healthy. Avoid using too much product, as this can weigh down your hair and make it look greasy.

With this step-by-step guide, you can give yourself a fade with a beard trimmer and save time and money by skipping the barbershop. Remember to take your time, be patient, and practice, and you will be able to give yourself a fade that looks just as good as the one you get at the barbershop.
