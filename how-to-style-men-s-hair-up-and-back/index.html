How To Style Men s Hair Up And Back

Written by Petter Jul 15, 2023 Â· 7 min read

If you're looking for a classic and timeless hairstyle, you can't go wrong with styling your hair up and back. This hairstyle has been popular for decades and is still a favorite among men of all ages. In this article, we'll show you how to style your hair up and back, as well as provide tips for hair care and maintenance.

Table of Contents

  • How to Style Men's Hair Up and Back
  • Step by Step Guide
  • Tips for Styling Men's Hair Up and Back
  • Frequently Asked Questions
  • Hair Care and Maintenance

How to Style Men's Hair Up and Back

Before you start styling your hair up and back, you need to make sure that your hair is clean and dry. You also need to have the right tools on hand, such as a comb, hair gel, and hairspray.

To begin the styling process, start by applying a small amount of hair gel to your hair. Make sure to spread the gel evenly throughout your hair, using your fingers to work it in. This will help to create a firm hold and keep your hair in place.

Next, use a comb to comb your hair back away from your face. Start at the front of your hairline and work your way back, making sure to smooth out any bumps or tangles.

Once you have combed your hair back, use your fingers to shape your hair into the desired style. You can create a slick and polished look by using a comb to smooth your hair back, or you can create a more relaxed and tousled look by using your fingers to create texture and volume.

Step by Step Guide

Here is a step-by-step guide to styling your hair up and back:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair
  2. Apply a small amount of hair gel to your hair
  3. Comb your hair back away from your face
  4. Use your fingers to shape your hair into the desired style
  5. Finish with a spritz of hairspray to lock in the style

Tips for Styling Men's Hair Up and Back

Here are some tips to help you achieve the perfect up and back hairstyle:

  • Experiment with different hair products to find the one that works best for your hair type
  • Don't be afraid to use a hairdryer to add volume and texture to your hair
  • Try using a pomade instead of hair gel for a more natural and flexible hold
  • Use a comb to create a sharp and polished look, or use your fingers to create a more relaxed and tousled look
  • Don't overdo it with the hair gel or hairspray - less is more when it comes to styling products

Frequently Asked Questions

Here are some commonly asked questions about styling men's hair up and back:

Can I style my hair up and back if I have short hair?

Yes, you can still style your hair up and back if you have short hair. Use a small amount of hair gel or pomade to create a slick and polished look, or use your fingers to create a textured and tousled look.

Do I need to use hairspray to keep my hair in place?

No, you don't necessarily need to use hairspray to keep your hair in place. However, if you want to ensure that your hair stays in place all day, a spritz of hairspray can help to lock in the style.

What kind of comb should I use to style my hair up and back?

You can use any type of comb to style your hair up and back, but a fine-toothed comb will help to create a more polished and precise look.

Hair Care and Maintenance

To keep your hair looking its best, it's important to take good care of it. Here are some tips for hair care and maintenance:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo and conditioner
  • Avoid using hot water when washing your hair, as this can dry out your scalp and hair
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair soft and healthy
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends and promote healthy growth
  • Avoid using harsh chemicals or heat styling tools on your hair, as this can cause damage and breakage

By following these tips and techniques, you can achieve a stylish and timeless up and back hairstyle that will never go out of fashion.
