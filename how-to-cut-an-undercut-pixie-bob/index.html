How To Cut An Undercut Pixie Bob

Written by Daniel Apr 01, 2023 Â· 8 min read

If you're looking for a bold and edgy haircut, an undercut pixie bob might be the perfect choice for you. This style features a short and choppy cut, with longer layers on top and a shaved undercut underneath. It's a great option for those who want to add a bit of flair to their look, and it's also easy to maintain.

Description

The undercut pixie bob is a versatile haircut that can be customized to suit your personal style. It usually involves shaving the hair at the nape of the neck and leaving the top layers longer. This creates a contrast between the longer and shorter sections, which adds depth and dimension to the hair. You can also add bangs or layers to frame your face and enhance your features.

How to Cut an Undercut Pixie Bob

If you're considering cutting an undercut pixie bob, here are the steps to follow:

Step 1: Section the Hair

Start by sectioning the hair into three parts: the top, the sides, and the back. Use hair clips to keep each section separate.

Step 2: Shave the Undercut

Using a clipper, shave the hair at the nape of the neck. You can choose how high or low you want the undercut to be. Make sure to use a guard that's appropriate for the length you want.

Step 3: Cut the Sides

Using scissors, cut the hair on the sides to the desired length. You can choose to leave it longer or shorter, depending on your preference. Make sure to cut in small sections to create a choppy and textured look.

Step 4: Cut the Top Layers

Starting at the crown of the head, cut the top layers to the desired length. You can choose to leave it longer or shorter, depending on your preference. Make sure to cut in small sections to create a choppy and textured look.

Step 5: Add Layers or Bangs

If you want to add layers or bangs, do so after cutting the top layers. Use scissors to create texture and movement. You can also use a razor to create a softer, more wispy look.

Step 6: Blend the Layers

Once you've cut all the sections, blend the layers together to create a seamless look. Use scissors or a razor to create texture and movement.

Step 7: Style the Hair

Once you've cut and blended the hair, style it to your liking. You can use a hair dryer and a round brush to add volume and shape. You can also use a flat iron or curling iron to add texture and dimension.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you achieve the perfect undercut pixie bob:

  • Use sharp scissors and a good quality razor to create texture and movement.
  • Cut in small sections to create a choppy and textured look.
  • Use a hair clipper with a guard to shave the undercut.
  • Experiment with different lengths and styles to find what works best for you.
  • Visit a professional stylist if you're unsure about cutting your hair yourself.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about the undercut pixie bob:

How long does it take to cut an undercut pixie bob?

The time it takes to cut an undercut pixie bob can vary depending on the length and thickness of your hair. It can take anywhere from 30 minutes to an hour or more.

What face shapes suit an undercut pixie bob?

An undercut pixie bob can suit a variety of face shapes, including oval, round, and heart-shaped. It's best to consult with a stylist to determine what style will suit your face shape and features.

How often do I need to get a trim?

It's recommended to get a trim every 4-6 weeks to maintain the shape and texture of your undercut pixie bob.

Hair Care

If you want to keep your undercut pixie bob looking its best, here are some hair care tips to follow:

  • Wash your hair with a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping the hair of its natural oils.
  • Avoid using heat tools too often, as this can damage the hair.
  • Apply a hair mask once a week to nourish and hydrate the hair.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and chlorine by wearing a hat or using a leave-in conditioner.
  • Visit a professional stylist for regular trims and maintenance.

Cutting an undercut pixie bob can be a fun and daring way to change up your look. By following these steps and tips, you can achieve a stylish and edgy haircut that suits your personal style.
