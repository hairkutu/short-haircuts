Short Back And Sides Haircut Long On Top The Perfect Style For Men

Written by Frank Mar 18, 2023 Â· 8 min read

If you are looking for a versatile haircut that is both stylish and low-maintenance, then the short back and sides haircut long on top is the perfect style for you. This hairstyle has been trending for several years now and is still popular among men of all ages. In this blog post, we will guide you on how to get this hairstyle, provide you with some tips on how to maintain it, and answer some frequently asked questions about this haircut.

Description

The short back and sides haircut long on top is a classic hairstyle that involves trimming the sides and the back of the head short while leaving the hair on top long. This style is also known as the 'undercut' as the hair is undercut at the back and sides. The length of the hair on top can vary from a few inches to several inches depending on your preference. This style is suitable for all hair types and face shapes, making it a popular choice among men.

How to Get the Short Back and Sides Haircut Long on Top

Getting the short back and sides haircut long on top is easy, and you can do it at home if you have the right tools. Here's how:

Step 1: Decide on the Length of Your Hair

The first step is to decide on the length of your hair. You can use a clipper or scissors to trim the sides and the back of your head. The length can be anywhere from a #1 to a #4 guard for a short cut or even shorter for a more daring look.

Step 2: Trim the Sides and the Back

Using a clipper or scissors, trim the sides and the back of your head. Start from the bottom and work your way up. Use a mirror to ensure that you are cutting evenly on both sides.

Step 3: Cut the Hair on Top

Once you have trimmed the sides and the back, it's time to cut the hair on top. You can use scissors or a razor to cut the hair on top. The length can vary depending on your preference.

Step 4: Style Your Hair

Once you have cut your hair, it's time to style it. You can use a hairdryer and a comb to create a sleek look or use your fingers to create a messy, textured look. You can also use a styling product like wax or pomade to add some hold and texture to your hair.

Tips for Maintaining the Short Back and Sides Haircut Long on Top

Here are some tips on how to maintain the short back and sides haircut long on top:

  • Visit your barber regularly to keep your hair looking sharp and neat.
  • Invest in a good quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and shiny.
  • Use a styling product to keep your hair in place and add some texture.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair as it can cause damage.
  • Experiment with different styles to find the one that suits you best.

FAQs

What face shape is the short back and sides haircut long on top suitable for?

The short back and sides haircut long on top is suitable for all face shapes.

What hair type is the short back and sides haircut long on top suitable for?

The short back and sides haircut long on top is suitable for all hair types.

Can I style the short back and sides haircut long on top differently?

Yes, you can experiment with different styles to find the one that suits you best.

Hair Care

It's essential to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and shiny. Here are some tips on how to take care of your hair:

  • Use a good quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair clean and healthy.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair as it can cause damage.
  • Use a hair mask once a week to nourish your hair.
  • Avoid using too many styling products as they can weigh down your hair.
  • Visit your barber regularly to keep your hair looking sharp and neat.

Now that you know everything about the short back and sides haircut long on top, it's time to give it a try. It's a versatile and stylish hairstyle that is easy to maintain and looks great on everyone. Experiment with different styles to find the one that suits you best, and don't forget to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and shiny.
