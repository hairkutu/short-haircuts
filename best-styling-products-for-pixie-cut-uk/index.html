If you've recently chopped off your hair into a pixie cut, you may be wondering how to style it. While it's true that short hair requires less maintenance, it still needs proper styling to look its best. In this article, we'll share some of the best styling products for pixie cut UK that will help you achieve the perfect hairstyle.

How to Style a Pixie Cut

Styling a pixie cut can be challenging, especially if you're used to having longer hair. Here are some steps to help you style your pixie cut:

  1. Start by washing your hair with a gentle shampoo and conditioner. Pixie cuts tend to get greasy quickly, so you may need to wash your hair more often.
  2. Towel dry your hair by gently patting it with a towel. Avoid rubbing it vigorously as this can damage your hair.
  3. Apply a styling product of your choice to your hair. We'll share some of the best styling products for pixie cut UK in the next section.
  4. Blow-dry your hair using a small round brush to add volume and texture. You can also use a diffuser to enhance your natural curls.
  5. Finish off with a hairspray to hold your style in place.

Best Styling Products for Pixie Cut UK

Now that you know how to style your pixie cut, let's take a look at some of the best styling products for pixie cut UK:

Hair Wax

Hair wax is a great styling product for pixie cuts as it can help you achieve a messy and textured look. It also adds shine to your hair and keeps it in place all day long. Some of the best hair wax products for pixie cut UK are:

  • Bed Head by TIGI Manipulator Matte Hair Wax: This hair wax provides a strong hold and a matte finish. It's perfect for creating a messy and textured look.
  • Label.men Sculpting Pomade: This pomade is ideal for adding definition and texture to your hair. It also has a natural shine and a strong hold.
  • Redken Brews Maneuver Cream Pomade: This cream pomade provides a medium hold and a natural finish. It's perfect for creating a sleek and polished look.

Hair Gel

Hair gel is another great styling product for pixie cuts. It can help you achieve a sleek and polished look or a messy and textured one. Here are some of the best hair gel products for pixie cut UK:

  • Got2b Glued Spiking Glue: This spiking glue provides a strong hold and a shiny finish. It's perfect for creating a sleek and polished look.
  • VO5 Extreme Style Matt Clay: This matt clay provides a strong hold and a matte finish. It's perfect for creating a messy and textured look.
  • L'Oreal Paris Studio Line Invisi Fix 24H Gel: This gel provides a strong hold and a natural finish. It's perfect for creating a sleek and polished look.

Hair Spray

Hair spray is a must-have styling product for pixie cuts as it can help you hold your style in place all day long. Here are some of the best hair spray products for pixie cut UK:

  • Wella Professionals EIMI Dynamic Fix 45 Second Crafting Spray: This spray provides a strong hold and a natural finish. It's perfect for creating a sleek and polished look.
  • Tresemme Extra Hold Hairspray: This hairspray provides an extra strong hold and a shiny finish. It's perfect for creating a sleek and polished look.
  • Garnier Fructis Style Ultimate Hold Hairspray: This hairspray provides a strong hold and a natural finish. It's perfect for creating a messy and textured look.

Tips for Styling a Pixie Cut

Here are some tips to help you style your pixie cut:

  • Experiment with different styling products to find the one that works best for your hair.
  • Use a small round brush to add volume and texture to your hair.
  • Avoid using too much product as it can weigh down your hair.
  • Don't be afraid to add some accessories like hair clips or headbands to your pixie cut.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about styling a pixie cut:

What is the best styling product for a messy and textured look?

Hair wax is the best styling product for a messy and textured look. It can help you achieve a tousled and natural look.

Can I use a straightener to style my pixie cut?

Yes, you can use a straightener to style your pixie cut. However, make sure to use a heat protectant spray to avoid damaging your hair.

How often should I wash my pixie cut?

Pixie cuts tend to get greasy quickly, so you may need to wash your hair more often. However, this depends on your hair type and lifestyle. Some people can go a few days without washing their hair, while others need to wash it every day.


Hair Care for Pixie Cut

Here are some tips to help you take care of your pixie cut:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair as it can damage it. Use a heat protectant spray if you need to use heat styling tools.
  • Trim your hair regularly to maintain your pixie cut.
  • Apply a hair mask once a week to keep your hair healthy and moisturized.

Now that you know about the best styling products for pixie cut UK, you can experiment with different products to find the one that works best for your hair. With the right styling products and techniques, you can achieve the perfect pixie cut hairstyle.
