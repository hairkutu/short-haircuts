Are Perms Expensive Everything You Need To Know

Written by Frank Apr 07, 2023 · 7 min read

Perms have come a long way since the '80s! Today, perms are a popular way to add texture and body to your hair without having to use hot tools every day. But are perms expensive? In this article, we'll break down everything you need to know about the cost of perms, including how to save money if you decide to get one.

Description: What is a Perm?

A perm is a chemical treatment that changes the texture of your hair. It works by breaking down the bonds in your hair and reforming them in a new shape. This new shape can be anything from loose waves to tight curls, depending on the size of the perm rods used.

How to Get a Perm

If you're interested in getting a perm, the first step is to find a stylist who specializes in perms. Not all stylists are trained in this technique, so it's important to do your research. Once you've found a stylist, you'll have a consultation to discuss the look you want to achieve and to determine if your hair is healthy enough for a perm.

Step-by-Step Guide to Getting a Perm

1. Shampoo your hair the day before your appointment. Avoid using any styling products.

2. Arrive at the salon with clean, dry hair.

3. Your stylist will section your hair and wrap it around perm rods. The size of the rods will determine the size of your curls.

4. Your stylist will apply a perming solution to your hair and let it sit for a set amount of time.

5. Your stylist will rinse out the solution and apply a neutralizer to set the new shape of your hair.

6. Your stylist will remove the rods and style your hair as desired.

Tips for Saving Money on Perms

Perms can be expensive, but there are ways to save money if you're on a budget:

- Look for specials or promotions at your salon

- Consider getting a partial perm instead of a full perm

- Opt for a looser curl, which requires fewer perm rods and less solution

- Choose a simpler style that doesn't require as much time or product

FAQs About Perms

1. How long does a perm last?

A perm can last anywhere from 3 to 6 months, depending on how fast your hair grows and how well you take care of it.

2. How much does a perm cost?

The cost of a perm can vary depending on where you live, the salon you go to, and the type of perm you want. On average, a perm can cost anywhere from $50 to $150.

3. Is it safe to get a perm?

Perms can be safe if done correctly by a trained professional. However, if the solution is left on too long or if your hair is already damaged, a perm can cause breakage and damage to your hair.

Hair Care Tips for Permed Hair

After getting a perm, it's important to take care of your hair to ensure it stays healthy and looks its best:

- Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner specifically designed for permed hair

- Avoid using hot tools like curling irons or straighteners, as this can damage your hair

- Avoid washing your hair for at least 48 hours after getting a perm

- Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair, as brushes can break your curls

- Sleep with your hair in a loose bun to prevent tangling and frizz

Conclusion

Perms can be a great way to add texture and body to your hair, but they can also be expensive. By doing your research, finding a stylist who specializes in perms, and taking care of your hair after the treatment, you can ensure that your perm looks great and lasts as long as possible. And with our tips for saving money, you can get the look you want without breaking the bank!
