How To Cut Hair In Short Layers Video A Step By Step Guide

Written by Daniel Sep 14, 2023 Â· 8 min read

Cutting hair in short layers is a popular trend that has been around for many years. This style can give your hair a voluminous and textured look, making it perfect for both casual and formal occasions. However, it can be challenging to achieve the perfect short layer cut, especially if you're doing it for the first time. In this article, we will guide you through the process of cutting hair in short layers using a video tutorial.

Description

In this video tutorial, you will learn how to cut hair in short layers using a step-by-step guide. We will also provide you with some tips and tricks to make the process easier and more efficient. The video will show you how to section your hair, choose the right scissors, and cut your hair in short layers with precision. You can watch the tutorial as many times as you need to get the hang of it.

How to Cut Hair in Short Layers Video

To cut hair in short layers, you will need the following materials:

  • Sharp scissors
  • Comb
  • Hair clips
  • Spray bottle
  • Large mirror

Once you have all the materials, follow these steps to cut your hair in short layers:

Step 1: Wash Your Hair

Before you start cutting your hair, make sure it's clean and free of any tangles. Use a shampoo and conditioner that suits your hair type to get the best results. Rinse your hair thoroughly and dry it with a towel until it's damp.

Step 2: Section Your Hair

Use a comb to divide your hair into four sections: front, back, and two sides. Clip each section using a hair clip to keep it in place. This will make it easier to cut your hair in short layers.

Step 3: Choose Your Length

Decide how short you want your layers to be. Use the mirror to visualize the final result and choose the length accordingly. It's best to start with longer layers and then cut them shorter gradually.

Step 4: Start Cutting

Take a small section of hair from the back and hold it between your fingers. Use the scissors to cut the hair at an angle, starting from the bottom and moving upwards. Avoid cutting straight across, as this can create uneven layers. Repeat this process for all sections of your hair.

Step 5: Blend Your Layers

After cutting all the sections, blend your layers by combing your hair and cutting any stray hairs. This will give your hair a natural and textured look.

Step 6: Style Your Hair

Once you're done cutting your hair in short layers, style it as you wish. You can use a curling iron, straightener, or leave it as it is. Don't forget to apply a styling product to keep your hair in place all day.

Tips

Here are some tips to make the process of cutting hair in short layers easier:

  • Use sharp scissors to avoid damaging your hair.
  • Don't rush the process. Take your time and cut your hair gradually.
  • Use a spray bottle to dampen your hair if it's too dry.
  • Watch the video tutorial multiple times to understand the process better.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about cutting hair in short layers:

Can I cut my hair in short layers if it's curly?

Yes, you can. However, it's best to cut curly hair when it's dry to avoid cutting too much. Use a diffuser to dry your hair after washing it, and then cut it in short layers.

How often should I cut my hair in short layers?

You can cut your hair in short layers every six to eight weeks to maintain the style.

What should I do if I make a mistake when cutting my hair?

If you make a mistake, don't panic. You can always fix it by cutting your hair a little shorter or blending the layers. Alternatively, you can visit a professional hairdresser to fix the mistake.

Hair Care

To maintain healthy hair, make sure you wash it regularly and use a conditioner to keep it moisturized. Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as they can damage your hair. Use a hair mask once a week to nourish your hair and prevent split ends.

Now that you know how to cut hair in short layers using a video tutorial, you can try it out at home. Remember to take your time and follow the steps carefully to get the best results. With a little practice, you'll be able to achieve the perfect short layer cut.
