How To Apply Serum To Curly Hair A Step By Step Guide

Written by Luffy Jun 14, 2023 · 10 min read

Curly hair is beautiful, but it can be tough to manage. Frizz, breakage, and tangles are common problems that can make it difficult to achieve the stunning curls you want. One solution to these issues is to use a serum designed specifically for curly hair. In this guide, we’ll show you how to apply serum to curly hair, step by step.

Table of Contents

  2. Step-by-Step Guide
  3. Tips for Applying Serum to Curly Hair
  4. FAQs About Applying Serum to Curly Hair
  5. Hair Care for Curly Hair

How to Apply Serum to Curly Hair

A serum is a hair product that helps to smooth hair, reduce frizz, and add shine. It’s a great option for those with curly hair because it can help to define curls and prevent breakage. Here’s how to apply serum to curly hair:

Step 1: Wash Your Hair

Before applying any product to your hair, make sure it’s clean. Wash your hair with a gentle shampoo and conditioner. Avoid using hot water, as this can dry out your hair and make it more prone to breakage.

Step 2: Squeeze Out Excess Water

After washing your hair, gently squeeze out excess water with a towel. Do not rub your hair vigorously with a towel, as this can cause frizz and breakage. Instead, use a microfiber towel or an old t-shirt to gently blot your hair.

Step 3: Apply Serum to Your Hair

Take a small amount of serum and rub it between your palms. Work the serum through your hair, starting at the ends and working your way up to the roots. Be sure to apply the serum evenly throughout your hair.

Step 4: Style Your Hair

After applying the serum, you can style your hair as desired. You can let it air dry, use a diffuser to dry it with a blow dryer, or use a curling iron to define your curls even further.

Step-by-Step Guide

If you’re still unsure about how to apply serum to curly hair, here’s a more detailed guide:

Step 1: Choose the Right Serum

Not all serums are created equal. Look for a serum that’s specifically designed for curly hair. These serums are formulated to help define curls and reduce frizz. They may also contain ingredients that help to protect your hair from heat damage and breakage.

Step 2: Wash Your Hair

Start by washing your hair with a gentle shampoo and conditioner. Make sure to rinse your hair thoroughly to remove any residue from the products. You may also want to use a deep conditioning treatment to help nourish your curls.

Step 3: Squeeze Out Excess Water

Gently squeeze out excess water from your hair with a towel. Do not rub your hair vigorously with a towel, as this can cause frizz and breakage. Instead, use a microfiber towel or an old t-shirt to gently blot your hair.

Step 4: Apply Serum to Your Hair

Take a small amount of serum and rub it between your palms. Work the serum through your hair, starting at the ends and working your way up to the roots. Be sure to apply the serum evenly throughout your hair.

Step 5: Comb Your Hair

After applying the serum, use a wide-tooth comb to gently detangle your curls. Start at the ends and work your way up to the roots. Be gentle to avoid causing breakage.

Step 6: Style Your Hair

You can style your hair as desired after applying the serum. You can let it air dry, use a diffuser to dry it with a blow dryer, or use a curling iron to define your curls even further.

Tips for Applying Serum to Curly Hair

Here are some tips to help you apply serum to your curly hair:

  • Use a small amount of serum to avoid weighing down your curls.
  • Apply the serum to wet hair to help distribute it evenly.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your curls after applying the serum.
  • Avoid touching your hair too much after applying the serum to prevent frizz.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair to prevent damage.

FAQs About Applying Serum to Curly Hair

Q: Can I apply serum to dry hair?

A: It’s best to apply serum to wet hair to help distribute it evenly. However, you can apply a small amount of serum to dry hair to help smooth frizz and add shine.

Q: How often should I apply serum to my hair?

A: This will depend on your hair type and how often you wash your hair. Generally, it’s best to apply serum every time you wash your hair. However, if you have very fine hair, you may want to apply it every other time you wash your hair to avoid weighing it down.

Q: Can I use serum on color-treated hair?

A: Yes, you can use serum on color-treated hair. Look for a serum that’s specifically designed for color-treated hair to help protect your color and prevent fading.

Hair Care for Curly Hair

Using a serum is just one part of a complete hair care routine for curly hair. Here are some additional tips to help you keep your curls healthy and beautiful:

  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner designed for curly hair.
  • Avoid using hot water when washing your hair.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to nourish your curls.
  • Avoid using heat on your hair too often.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your curls.
  • Avoid using products that contain alcohol, as they can dry out your hair.

With these tips and our step-by-step guide, you’ll be able to apply serum to your curly hair like a pro. Say goodbye to frizz and breakage and hello to beautiful, defined curls!
