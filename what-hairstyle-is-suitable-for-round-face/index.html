What Hairstyle Is Suitable For Round Face

Written by Eveline Mar 28, 2023 Â· 8 min read

If you have a round face, you may be wondering what hairstyle is suitable for your face shape. A round face is characterized by a wider forehead, rounded cheeks, and a rounded chin. The right hairstyle can help to elongate your face and create a more balanced look. In this article, we will discuss what hairstyle is suitable for round face and provide you with tips on how to achieve the perfect look.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to
  • Step by step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

When it comes to choosing a hairstyle for a round face, the goal is to create the illusion of length and balance. This means opting for hairstyles that add height to the crown of the head, elongate the face, and create angles around the chin and jawline. There are many hairstyles that can achieve this look, including long layers, side-swept bangs, and asymmetrical cuts. The key is to choose a style that complements your features and highlights your best assets.

How to

When choosing a hairstyle for a round face, there are a few key factors to consider:

  • Length: Longer hairstyles can help to elongate the face and create a more balanced look. Avoid hairstyles that fall at chin-level or shorter, as they can make the face appear wider.
  • Layers: Long layers can help to create movement and texture in the hair, while also adding height to the crown of the head. Avoid short layers, as they can make the face appear wider.
  • Bangs: Side-swept bangs can help to create angles around the face and draw attention away from the width of the cheeks. Avoid blunt bangs, as they can make the face appear rounder.
  • Volume: Adding volume to the crown of the head can help to create the illusion of length. Avoid hairstyles that are flat or sleek, as they can make the face appear wider.

Step by step

To achieve the perfect hairstyle for a round face, follow these steps:

  1. Consult with a hairstylist to determine the best hairstyle for your face shape and hair type.
  2. Choose a hairstyle that complements your features and highlights your best assets.
  3. Opt for longer hairstyles with long layers and side-swept bangs.
  4. Avoid hairstyles that fall at chin-level or shorter, as well as short layers and blunt bangs.
  5. Add volume to the crown of the head to create the illusion of length.
  6. Use styling products to create movement and texture in the hair.
  7. Regularly trim and maintain your hairstyle to keep it looking fresh and stylish.

Tips

Here are a few additional tips to keep in mind when choosing a hairstyle for a round face:

  • Consider your hair texture and thickness when choosing a hairstyle.
  • Use a round brush when blow-drying your hair to add volume and create movement.
  • Avoid hairstyles that are too harsh or angular, as they can make the face appear wider.
  • Experiment with different styles and products to find the perfect look for you.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about what hairstyle is suitable for round face:

Q: Can I still wear short hair if I have a round face?

A: Yes, you can still wear short hair if you have a round face. Opt for a short hairstyle with layers and volume at the crown of the head to create the illusion of length.

Q: Are bangs a good option for a round face?

A: Side-swept bangs can be a good option for a round face, as they create angles around the face and draw attention away from the width of the cheeks. Avoid blunt bangs, as they can make the face appear rounder.

Q: What styling products should I use to create volume and texture?

A: Use a volumizing mousse or spray to add volume to the crown of the head, and a texturizing spray or pomade to create movement and texture in the hair.

Hair Care

To keep your hairstyle looking its best, it is important to take good care of your hair. Here are a few tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using hot tools on your hair too often, as they can cause damage and breakage.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy and hydrated.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and heat by wearing a hat or using a heat protectant spray.
  • Regularly trim your hair to prevent split ends and maintain your hairstyle.

Now that you know what hairstyle is suitable for round face, it's time to experiment with different looks and find the perfect style for you. Remember to consult with a hairstylist and take good care of your hair to keep it looking its best.
