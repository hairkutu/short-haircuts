How To Make Short Curtain Bangs Look Good

Written by Eveline Jul 27, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Short curtain bangs are a trendy hairstyle that can transform your look in an instant. However, many people are unsure how to style them and make them look good. In this article, we'll provide you with a step-by-step guide on how to make short curtain bangs look good.

Description

Short curtain bangs are a type of fringe that is parted in the middle and frames the face on either side. They are typically cut to just above the eyebrows and can be styled in a variety of ways. This type of bangs can be a great addition to any haircut and can be tailored to suit your face shape and hair type.

How to

Here are the steps to follow to make short curtain bangs look good:

Step 1: Wash and dry your hair

Start by washing your hair and drying it thoroughly. Make sure your hair is completely dry before you start styling your bangs.

Step 2: Section your hair

Separate your bangs from the rest of your hair by sectioning it off. You can use clips or bobby pins to keep the rest of your hair out of the way.

Step 3: Use a round brush

Take a round brush and use it to blow dry your bangs. Roll the brush towards the back of your head to create a natural curve. Make sure to dry the roots of your hair first and work your way down to the ends.

Step 4: Use a straightener

Once your bangs are dry, use a straightener to smooth out any kinks or flyaways. This will help to create a sleek and polished look.

Step 5: Style your bangs

To create the curtain effect, part your bangs in the middle and sweep them to either side of your face. Use a small amount of hairspray or pomade to keep them in place.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you make short curtain bangs look good:

  • Use a heat protectant spray before blow drying or straightening your hair.
  • Experiment with different parting positions to find the one that suits you best.
  • Don't cut your bangs too short as they may be difficult to style.
  • Use a light hold hairspray to keep your bangs in place without making them stiff.
  • Consider getting regular trims to keep your bangs looking neat and tidy.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about short curtain bangs:

Can I style my curtain bangs without heat?

Yes, you can style your curtain bangs without heat by using a hair roller or a hair tie. Simply roll your bangs up and secure them in place with a hair tie or clip. Leave them in for a few hours or overnight, and then remove the roller or tie to reveal your styled bangs.

Can I wear my curtain bangs with glasses?

Yes, you can wear your curtain bangs with glasses. Just make sure that your bangs don't interfere with your glasses or get caught in the hinges.

How often should I trim my curtain bangs?

You should trim your curtain bangs every 4-6 weeks to keep them looking neat and tidy. However, if you're happy with the length and style of your bangs, you can stretch the time between trims a little longer.

Hair Care

It's important to take care of your hair to keep it looking healthy and shiny. Here are some tips to help you maintain your hair:

  • Wash your hair with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using hot tools on your hair too often as it can cause damage.
  • Use a hair mask once a week to nourish your hair.
  • Eat a healthy diet rich in vitamins and minerals to promote hair growth.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and harsh weather conditions by wearing a hat or using a protective spray.

By following these tips and tricks, you can make short curtain bangs look good and keep your hair healthy and shiny.
