How To Sixties Hairstyles A Step By Step Guide

Written by Aprilia May 26, 2023 Â· 5 min read

The sixties were a time of change, and that included hairstyles. From the bouffant to the beehive, women's hairstyles were bigger and bolder than ever before. If you're looking to recreate the look of the sixties, we've got you covered. In this guide, we'll show you how to sixties hairstyles with easy-to-follow steps and helpful tips along the way.

How to Get Started

If you're ready to dive into sixties hairstyles, there are a few things you'll need to get started. Here's what you'll need:

  • Hairspray
  • Bobby pins
  • Teasing comb
  • Round brush
  • Hair rollers (optional)

Once you have these items, you're ready to begin creating your sixties hairstyle.

Step-by-Step Guide to Sixties Hairstyles

Step 1: Tease Your Hair

The first step in creating a sixties hairstyle is to tease your hair. This will give your hair the volume and texture it needs to hold the style. Use a teasing comb to backcomb your hair at the roots, starting at the crown of your head and working your way down.

Step 2: Create a Ponytail

Once you've teased your hair, gather it into a high ponytail at the crown of your head. Use a hair tie to secure it in place.

Step 3: Create a Bouffant

To create a bouffant, use a round brush to smooth the hair on the top of your head. Then, use bobby pins to secure the hair in place, creating a bump on the top of your head. Spray with hairspray to hold the style.

Step 4: Add Curls

If you want to add curls to your sixties hairstyle, use a curling iron or hair rollers to curl the ends of your hair. Once you've curled your hair, use bobby pins to secure it in place.

Step 5: Finish with Hairspray

Once you've created your sixties hairstyle, finish it off with a generous spray of hairspray to hold it in place.

Tips for Creating Sixties Hairstyles

Here are a few tips to keep in mind as you create your sixties hairstyles:

  • Use hairspray to hold your style in place.
  • Don't be afraid to tease your hair for extra volume.
  • Use bobby pins to secure your hair in place.
  • Experiment with different styles, from bouffants to beehives.

FAQs

What are some popular sixties hairstyles?

Some popular sixties hairstyles include the bouffant, the beehive, and the pixie cut.

Do I need to use hair rollers to create a sixties hairstyle?

No, hair rollers are optional. You can create a sixties hairstyle without them.

Hair Care for Sixties Hairstyles

Creating sixties hairstyles can put strain on your hair, so it's important to take care of it. Here are a few tips:

  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often.
  • Use a heat protectant spray before using heat styling tools.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.

With these tips, you can create beautiful sixties hairstyles while keeping your hair healthy and strong.
