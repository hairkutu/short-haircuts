Description

Cutting hair at home with electric clippers can save you time and money, especially during the pandemic when many people are avoiding salons. Electric clippers are easy to use and can give you a professional-looking haircut in the comfort of your own home.

How to Cut Hair at Home with Electric Clippers

Before you start cutting your hair at home, you'll need a few things:

  • Electric clippers
  • Comb
  • Hair scissors
  • Cape or towel to cover your shoulders
  • Clip or hair tie

It's also a good idea to watch some tutorial videos to get an idea of how to use electric clippers properly. Once you have everything you need and feel comfortable using the clippers, you can start cutting your hair.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide on how to cut hair at home with electric clippers:

  1. Choose the right guard size. The guard size determines the length of the hair. Start with a longer guard size and work your way down if you want a shorter cut.
  2. Start at the bottom of your head and work your way up. Use the clippers to cut in an upward motion against the direction of hair growth.
  3. Use the comb to lift the hair and cut along the comb with the clippers.
  4. Pay attention to the sides and back of your head. Use a mirror to check the back of your head.
  5. Trim the hair around your ears with scissors.
  6. Blend the hair on top of your head with the hair on the sides and back using the clippers and comb.
  7. Check for any uneven spots and trim them with scissors.
  8. Finish with a shorter guard size for the neckline and sideburns.

Tips and Tricks

Here are some tips and tricks to make cutting hair at home with electric clippers easier:

  • Start with clean, dry hair.
  • Use a cape or towel to cover your clothes and shoulders.
  • Don't rush and take your time.
  • Use a mirror to check the back of your head.
  • Start with a longer guard size and work your way down.
  • Trim the hair around your ears with scissors for a neater look.
  • Blend the hair on top of your head with the hair on the sides and back for a more natural look.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about cutting hair at home with electric clippers:

  • Can I use electric clippers on wet hair?
  • It's best to use electric clippers on dry hair to avoid damaging the clippers and uneven cutting.

  • What guard size should I use for a buzz cut?
  • For a buzz cut, start with a #4 or #5 guard size and work your way down if you want a shorter cut.

  • What should I do if I make a mistake?
  • If you make a mistake, don't worry. You can always go back and trim the hair with scissors or use a longer guard size to fix the mistake.

Hair Care After Cutting

After cutting your hair at home with electric clippers, it's important to take care of your hair to maintain the haircut. Here are some tips:

  • Shampoo and condition your hair regularly.
  • Use a leave-in conditioner to keep your hair hydrated.
  • Trim your hair every 4-6 weeks to maintain the haircut.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and wind.

Cutting hair at home with electric clippers can be a fun and rewarding experience. With the right tools and techniques, you can give yourself a professional-looking haircut in the comfort of your own home.
