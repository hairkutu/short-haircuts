Short Haircut For Long Face Woman Tips And Ideas

If you have a long face shape and are looking for a new haircut, you've come to the right place. In this article, we'll be discussing the best short haircuts for a long face shape, how to style them, and how to care for your new hairstyle.

Table of Contents

How to Choose the Right Short Haircut for Long Face Woman

When it comes to choosing the right short haircut for a long face shape, it's important to keep a few things in mind. First, you want to add width to your face, rather than length. Second, you want to choose a haircut that flatters your features and highlights your best assets.

Some of the best short haircuts for a long face shape include the pixie cut, the bob, and the lob. These haircuts help to add width to the face while highlighting the cheekbones and jawline.

Another important factor to consider when choosing a short haircut for a long face shape is the texture of your hair. If you have thin, fine hair, you may want to opt for a layered bob or pixie cut to add volume and dimension. If you have thick, curly hair, you may want to go for a shorter, textured cut to help control your curls.

Step-by-Step Guide to Styling Your Short Haircut

Once you've chosen the perfect short haircut for your long face shape, it's time to style it. Here's a step-by-step guide to help you achieve the perfect look:

  1. Start by washing your hair with a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to add body and texture.
  2. Towel dry your hair and apply a heat protectant spray to prevent damage from heat styling tools.
  3. Blow dry your hair using a round brush to add volume and lift at the roots.
  4. Once your hair is dry, use a flat iron or curling iron to add texture and shape to your hair.
  5. Finish off your look with a texturizing spray or hair wax to add definition and hold.

Tips for Maintaining Your Short Haircut

Short haircuts require regular maintenance to keep them looking their best. Here are a few tips to help you maintain your new hairstyle:

  • Get regular trims every 4-6 weeks to keep your haircut looking fresh and healthy.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair moisturized and healthy.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair to prevent damage and breakage.
  • Invest in high-quality styling products to help you achieve the perfect look.

Frequently Asked Questions

Here are a few frequently asked questions about short haircuts for long face women:

Q: Will a short haircut make my face look longer?

A: No, a short haircut can actually help to add width to your face and make it look more balanced.

Q: How do I know if a short haircut is right for me?

A: It's important to consult with your stylist to determine if a short haircut is right for your face shape and hair type.

Q: Can I still style my short haircut if I have curly hair?

A: Yes, there are many styling techniques and products available to help you style your short curly hair.

Hair Care Tips for Short Haircuts

Here are a few hair care tips to help you keep your short haircut looking its best:

  • Wash your hair every 2-3 days to prevent over-drying.
  • Avoid using too much product on your hair to prevent buildup and greasiness.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair and prevent breakage.
  • Invest in a high-quality hair dryer and styling tools to prevent damage to your hair.

With these tips and ideas, you're ready to rock a new short haircut that flatters your long face shape. Consult with your stylist to find the perfect haircut and styling routine for you, and remember to maintain your hair with regular trims and deep conditioning treatments.
