How To Cut Dirty Bangs On A Wig A Step By Step Guide

Written by Bardi Aug 31, 2023 Â· 6 min read

If you're new to wigs, cutting bangs can be intimidating. But don't worry, it's easier than you think! In this guide, we'll show you how to cut dirty bangs on a wig, step-by-step. Whether you're looking to change up your style or just need to freshen up your current wig, we've got you covered.

Description

Dirty bangs on a wig can be a pain to deal with. They can make your wig look unkempt and messy, and no one wants that. Luckily, cutting dirty bangs on a wig is an easy process that you can do at home. With the right tools and some patience, you can have a fresh, new look in no time.

How to Cut Dirty Bangs on a Wig

Before you start cutting, make sure you have the right tools on hand. You'll need a pair of sharp scissors, a comb, and a spray bottle filled with water. Here's how to do it:

Step 1: Wet Your Bangs

Start by wetting your bangs with the spray bottle. This will make them easier to work with and help you get a more accurate cut.

Step 2: Comb Your Bangs

Using a comb, comb your bangs straight down. Make sure they're as straight as possible so you can get an even cut.

Step 3: Determine the Length

Decide how long you want your bangs to be. If you're not sure, start with a longer length and you can always cut more later.

Step 4: Start Cutting

Using your scissors, start cutting your bangs. Start at the middle and work your way outwards. Make sure you're cutting at an angle to create a soft, natural look.

Step 5: Check Your Progress

After you've cut a few pieces, stop and check your progress. Make sure your bangs are even and the length is what you want.

Step 6: Finish Up

Once you're happy with your bangs, use your scissors to trim any stray hairs. You can also use your comb to blend your bangs in with the rest of your wig.

Tips

Here are a few tips to help you get the best results:

  • Start with a longer length and cut a little at a time. It's easier to cut more than it is to fix a mistake.
  • Make sure your scissors are sharp. Dull scissors can make your job much harder.
  • Take your time. Cutting bangs can be nerve-wracking, so don't rush it.

FAQ

Here are a few frequently asked questions about cutting bangs on wigs:

Can I cut bangs on a curly wig?

Yes, you can cut bangs on a curly wig. However, keep in mind that curly hair tends to bounce up when dry, so you may need to cut them a little longer than you want them.

How do I make sure my bangs are even?

Using a comb, make sure your bangs are combed straight down. This will help you see any uneven areas. You can also use your fingers to feel for any unevenness.

Hair Care

After you've cut your bangs, it's important to take care of your wig. Here are a few tips:

  • Wash your wig regularly with a wig shampoo to keep it clean.
  • Store your wig on a wig stand to help it keep its shape.
  • Avoid using heat on your wig, as this can damage the fibers.

Now that you know how to cut dirty bangs on a wig, you can customize your look and keep your wig looking fresh. Happy cutting!
