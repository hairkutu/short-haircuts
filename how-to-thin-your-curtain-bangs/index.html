How To Thin Your Curtain Bangs A Step By Step Guide

Written by Bardi May 03, 2023 · 6 min read

Are your curtain bangs feeling too thick and heavy? Thinning out your bangs can help them lay flatter and frame your face better. In this guide, we’ll walk you through the steps to thin your curtain bangs at home.

Table of Contents

  • How to Thin Your Curtain Bangs
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Thinning Your Curtain Bangs
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care for Curtain Bangs

How to Thin Your Curtain Bangs

Thinning out your curtain bangs is a simple process that you can do at home. All you need is a pair of thinning shears and a steady hand.

What are Thinning Shears?

Thinning shears are a type of scissors that have teeth on one blade and a straight edge on the other. They’re designed to remove weight from the hair without creating blunt lines.

Step-by-Step Guide

Follow these steps to thin your curtain bangs:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair.
  2. Comb your bangs straight down over your forehead.
  3. Hold a small section of your bangs between your fingers.
  4. Use the thinning shears to cut into the section, starting at the ends and working your way up. Be sure to only cut a few strands at a time.
  5. Release the section and comb through to see the results.
  6. Repeat steps 3-5 until you’ve thinned out your bangs to your desired level.

Remember, it’s always better to start with less hair and work your way up to your desired level of thinness. You can always thin your bangs more, but you can’t add hair back once it’s been cut.

Tips for Thinning Your Curtain Bangs

Here are some tips to keep in mind as you thin out your curtain bangs:

  • Start with dry hair. Wet hair can be more difficult to cut and style.
  • Use sharp shears. Dull shears can create uneven cuts, which can be hard to fix.
  • Only cut a few strands at a time. Cutting too much hair at once can create unevenness and blunt lines.
  • Comb through your hair after each cut to see the results and avoid over-cutting.
  • Don’t cut too much from the middle of your bangs. This can create a gap that’s hard to blend in.

FAQs

How often should I thin my curtain bangs?

This depends on how fast your hair grows and how quickly your bangs start to feel heavy. As a general rule, you can thin your bangs every 4-6 weeks.

Can I thin my curtain bangs with regular scissors?

You can, but it’s not recommended. Regular scissors can create blunt lines and uneven cuts, which can be hard to fix.

Can I thin my own curtain bangs?

Yes, you can. Just make sure to use the right tools and follow the steps carefully. If you’re not comfortable doing it yourself, you can always go to a professional stylist.

Hair Care for Curtain Bangs

After thinning your curtain bangs, it’s important to take care of them properly. Here are some tips:

  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your bangs, as this can damage the hair.
  • Use a heat protectant spray if you do use heat styling tools.
  • Trim your bangs every 4-6 weeks to keep them looking their best.

Thinning your curtain bangs can help them lay flatter and frame your face better. With the right tools and techniques, you can thin your bangs at home and achieve the look you want.
