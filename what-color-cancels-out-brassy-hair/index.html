What Color Cancels Out Brassy Hair A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Aprilia Jun 10, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Brassy hair is a common problem for those who dye their hair blonde or have naturally blonde hair. It is caused by the oxidation of hair dye, which results in an orange or yellow tone. If you are struggling with brassy hair, don't worry, there is a solution. In this article, we will discuss what color cancels out brassy hair and provide tips on how to maintain beautiful blonde hair.

Table of Contents

  • How to Cancel Out Brassy Hair
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Maintaining Beautiful Blonde Hair
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care Products for Blonde Hair

How to Cancel Out Brassy Hair

The best way to cancel out brassy hair is to use a toner. A toner is a color depositing product that helps neutralize unwanted tones in the hair. Toners come in a variety of colors, but the most effective for cancelling out brassy hair are purple and blue toners.

Purple toners are best for cancelling out yellow tones in the hair, while blue toners are best for cancelling out orange tones. If your hair has both yellow and orange tones, you can use a combination of purple and blue toners to cancel out the brassiness.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here is a step-by-step guide on how to cancel out brassy hair:

  1. Wash your hair with a clarifying shampoo to remove any buildup.
  2. Towel dry your hair.
  3. Apply the toner to your hair, starting at the roots and working your way down to the ends.
  4. Leave the toner on your hair for the recommended amount of time (usually 5-10 minutes).
  5. Rinse the toner out of your hair with cool water.
  6. Apply a deep conditioner to your hair and leave it on for 5-10 minutes.
  7. Rinse the conditioner out of your hair with cool water.
  8. Style your hair as desired.

Tips for Maintaining Beautiful Blonde Hair

Here are some tips for maintaining beautiful blonde hair:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner formulated for blonde hair.
  • Avoid washing your hair too often, as this can strip the hair of its natural oils.
  • Avoid using hot tools on your hair too often, as this can cause damage and dryness.
  • Use a heat protectant spray before using hot tools on your hair.
  • Avoid exposing your hair to chlorine, as this can cause green tones in blonde hair.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about cancelling out brassy hair:

What is the difference between purple and blue toners?

Purple toners cancel out yellow tones, while blue toners cancel out orange tones.

Can I use a toner on natural blonde hair?

Yes, toners can be used on both dyed and natural blonde hair.

How often should I use a toner?

This depends on how quickly your hair becomes brassy. Some people may need to use a toner once a week, while others may only need to use it once a month.

Hair Care Products for Blonde Hair

Here are some hair care products that are great for maintaining blonde hair:

  • Joico Color Balance Purple Shampoo
  • Redken Color Extend Blondage Shampoo
  • Matrix Total Results Brass Off Shampoo
  • Olaplex Hair Perfector No. 3
  • Living Proof Perfect Hair Day 5-in-1 Styling Treatment

Now that you know what color cancels out brassy hair, you can confidently maintain beautiful blonde hair. Remember to use a toner, follow the step-by-step guide, and use the right hair care products to keep your hair looking its best.
