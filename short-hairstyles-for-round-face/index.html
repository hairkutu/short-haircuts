Short Hairstyles For Round Face

Written by Daniel Jun 06, 2023 Â· 7 min read

If you have a round face, choosing the right hairstyle is essential to make your features stand out. Short hairstyles can be very flattering for round faces, as they can help to elongate the face and make it appear slimmer. In this article, we will share some of the best short hairstyles for round faces.

Table of Content

  • How to choose the right hairstyle for your round face
  • Step-by-step guide to styling short hair for round faces
  • Tips for maintaining short hair for round faces
  • FAQ about short hairstyles for round faces
  • Hair care tips for short hair for round faces

How to choose the right hairstyle for your round face

When choosing a short hairstyle for your round face, it's important to consider your hair texture, facial features, and personal style. Here are some tips to help you choose the right hairstyle:

  • Opt for hairstyles that add height and volume to the top of your head, such as a pixie cut or a short bob with layers.
  • Avoid hairstyles that add width to your face, such as blunt bangs or a chin-length bob.
  • Consider adding side-swept bangs to your hairstyle, as they can help to create the illusion of a slimmer face.
  • Think about your hair texture when choosing a hairstyle. If you have fine hair, a short layered bob may work well for you, while those with thick hair may prefer a textured pixie cut.

Step-by-step guide to styling short hair for round faces

Styling short hair for round faces can be easy and effortless with these step-by-step tips:

  1. Start by washing your hair with a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to add body and texture to your hair.
  2. Towel dry your hair and apply a volumizing mousse to the roots of your hair, then blow-dry your hair using a round brush to add volume.
  3. If you have a pixie cut, use a texturizing pomade to create texture and definition in your hair. If you have a short bob, use a flat iron to create soft waves in your hair.
  4. Finish off your hairstyle with a light hairspray to hold your style in place.

Tips for maintaining short hair for round faces

Here are some tips for maintaining your short hair for round faces:

  • Get regular trims every 6-8 weeks to maintain your hairstyle and keep your ends looking healthy.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair soft and shiny.
  • Invest in a good quality hairbrush and comb to prevent breakage and damage to your hair.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as it can cause damage and breakage. Use a heat protectant spray before using hot tools.

FAQ about short hairstyles for round faces

Here are some frequently asked questions about short hairstyles for round faces:

1. Can I wear a bob with a round face?

Yes, you can wear a bob with a round face as long as it's a layered bob that adds height to the top of your head.

2. What is the best hairstyle for a round face?

The best hairstyle for a round face is one that adds height and volume to the top of your head, such as a pixie cut or a short bob with layers.

3. Should I avoid bangs if I have a round face?

No, you can wear bangs with a round face as long as they are side-swept bangs that create the illusion of a slimmer face.

Hair care tips for short hair for round faces

Here are some hair care tips for short hair for round faces:

  • Wash your hair every other day to prevent it from becoming dry and brittle.
  • Avoid using too much product on your hair, as it can weigh it down and make it look greasy.
  • Use a clarifying shampoo once a month to remove product buildup from your hair.
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it's wet, as it can cause breakage and damage to your hair.

With these tips and tricks, you can rock a short hairstyle that flatters your round face and showcases your best features.
