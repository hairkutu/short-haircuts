What Face Shape Do Bangs Look Good On

Description

If you're thinking of getting bangs, it's important to choose the right style that complements your face shape. Bangs can enhance your facial features and add a touch of sass to your hairstyle. However, choosing the wrong shape can have the opposite effect, making you look unbalanced and unflattering. In this article, we'll help you determine what face shape do bangs look good on and provide you with tips on how to maintain them.

How to Determine Your Face Shape

The first step in choosing the right bangs for your face shape is to determine your face shape. Here are the five most common face shapes:

Oval Face Shape

If you have an oval face shape, your face will be longer than it is wide, and your forehead will be slightly wider than your chin. Your jawline will be gently curved, and your cheekbones will be the widest part of your face.

Round Face Shape

A round face shape will have a length and width that are almost equal, and your cheekbones will be the widest part of your face. Your jawline will be softly curved, and your forehead and chin will be rounded.

Square Face Shape

If you have a square face shape, your face will be as wide as it is long, and your forehead, cheekbones, and jawline will be almost the same width. Your jawline will have a sharp angle, and your forehead will be flat.

Heart Face Shape

A heart face shape will have a wider forehead than your cheekbones, and your chin will be pointy. Your face will be longer than it is wide, and your forehead will be the widest part of your face.

Diamond Face Shape

If you have a diamond face shape, your forehead and jawline will be narrow, and your cheekbones will be the widest part of your face. Your chin will be pointy, and your face will be longer than it is wide.

Step-by-Step Guide on Choosing the Right Bangs for Your Face Shape

Now that you know your face shape, it's time to choose the right bangs for you. Here's a step-by-step guide on how to do it:

Step 1: Consider Your Face Shape

Choose bangs that complement your face shape. For example, if you have a round face, go for long, side-swept bangs that will elongate your face. If you have a square face, choose soft, wispy bangs that will soften your features.

Step 2: Consider Your Hair Type

Choose bangs that work well with your hair type. If you have fine hair, go for blunt, straight-across bangs that will create the illusion of thickness. If you have curly hair, choose longer, side-swept bangs that will blend in with your curls.

Step 3: Consider Your Lifestyle

Choose bangs that fit your lifestyle. If you're always on the go, opt for low-maintenance bangs that won't require a lot of styling. If you have more time to spare, choose bangs that require more maintenance and styling.

Step 4: Consult with Your Stylist

Consult with your stylist to determine the best bangs for your face shape, hair type, and lifestyle. They can also provide you with styling tips and recommend the right products to use for your bangs.

Tips for Maintaining Your Bangs

Once you've chosen the right bangs for your face shape, it's important to maintain them properly. Here are some tips:

  • Trim your bangs regularly to prevent them from becoming too long and covering your eyes.
  • Use a dry shampoo to keep your bangs looking fresh in between washes.
  • Blow-dry your bangs using a round brush to create volume and shape.
  • Use a flat iron to straighten your bangs if they become too curly or wavy.
  • Avoid touching your bangs too much, as the natural oils from your fingers can make them greasy.

FAQ

What face shape do blunt bangs look good on?

Blunt bangs look good on oval, heart, and diamond face shapes.

What face shape do side-swept bangs look good on?

Side-swept bangs look good on round, square, and heart face shapes.

Do bangs make your face look thinner?

Yes, bangs can create the illusion of a thinner face. Long, side-swept bangs can elongate your face, while blunt bangs can broaden your forehead and make your face appear more balanced.

Hair Care Tips for Your Bangs

Here are some hair care tips for your bangs:

  • Wash your bangs every other day to prevent them from becoming too greasy.
  • Use a clarifying shampoo once a week to remove buildup from styling products.
  • Avoid using heavy products on your bangs, as they can weigh them down and make them look greasy.
  • Use a heat protectant spray when using hot tools on your bangs.
  • Avoid brushing your bangs when they're wet, as they are more prone to breakage.

Now that you know what face shape do bangs look good on, it's time to choose the right bangs for you. Remember to consider your face shape, hair type, and lifestyle, and consult with your stylist for the best results. With the right bangs, you can elevate your hairstyle and enhance your facial features.
