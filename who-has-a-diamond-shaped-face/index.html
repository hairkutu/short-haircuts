Who Has A Diamond Shaped Face

Written by Daniel Apr 25, 2023 · 7 min read
Who Has a Diamond Shaped Face - Hair Care Tips, FAQs, and More

If you're wondering whether or not you have a diamond shaped face, you're not alone. This unique facial structure is characterized by a narrow forehead, high cheekbones, and a pointed chin. In this article, we'll dive into what a diamond shaped face looks like, who typically has this facial structure, and how to style your hair to flatter your features.

Table of Contents

  2. How to Determine if You Have a Diamond Shaped Face
  3. Step-by-Step Guide to Identifying a Diamond Shaped Face
  4. Hair Care Tips for Diamond Shaped Faces
  5. FAQs About Diamond Shaped Faces

Description

A diamond shaped face is characterized by a narrow forehead, high cheekbones, and a pointed chin. This facial structure is often seen as desirable because of its sharp and angular features. However, it can also be challenging to style hair that flatters the face shape.

How to Determine if You Have a Diamond Shaped Face

There are a few ways to determine if you have a diamond shaped face. One method is to take a full-face photo and trace your facial features onto the photo. Another method is to measure your face with a tape measure. Here are the measurements you should take:

  • Forehead: Measure the width of your forehead from one temple to the other.
  • Cheekbones: Measure the distance between your cheekbones, just below the eyes.
  • Jawline: Measure the width of your jawline from one corner of your jaw to the other.
  • Face length: Measure the length of your face from your hairline to your chin.

Step-by-Step Guide to Identifying a Diamond Shaped Face

If you're still not sure whether or not you have a diamond shaped face, here's a step-by-step guide to help you out:

  1. Take a full-face photo of yourself.
  2. Trace your facial features onto the photo.
  3. Draw a line across your forehead, just below your hairline.
  4. Draw a line from the top of your ear to the corner of your jawline on both sides of your face.
  5. Draw a line from the tip of your chin to the center of your forehead.
  6. If the lines form a diamond shape, you have a diamond shaped face.

Hair Care Tips for Diamond Shaped Faces

When it comes to styling hair for diamond shaped faces, the goal is to soften the angles of the face and draw attention away from the pointed chin. Here are some tips to keep in mind:

  • Go for hairstyles that add volume to the top of the head, like a pixie cut or layered bob.
  • Avoid hairstyles that are too slicked back or tightly pulled back, as they can emphasize the pointed chin.
  • Side-swept bangs can help to soften the angles of the face.
  • Consider adding layers to your hair to add movement and dimension.

FAQs About Diamond Shaped Faces

Q: Can diamond shaped faces pull off short hair?

A: Yes! Short haircuts like pixie cuts and bobs can be very flattering on diamond shaped faces.

Q: What hairstyles should I avoid if I have a diamond shaped face?

A: Avoid hairstyles that are too slicked back or tightly pulled back, as they can emphasize the pointed chin. Also avoid hairstyles with too much volume at the bottom, as they can make the face appear wider.

Q: Can diamond shaped faces wear bangs?

A: Yes! Side-swept bangs can help to soften the angles of the face and draw attention away from the pointed chin.

Q: What hair care products should I use if I have a diamond shaped face?

A: Use volumizing products to add volume to the top of the head. Avoid heavy products that can weigh down the hair and make the face appear wider.

Q: Can I wear my hair in a center part if I have a diamond shaped face?

A: Yes, but it's best to keep the hair loose and wavy to soften the angles of the face.
