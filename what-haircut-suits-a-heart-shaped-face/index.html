What Haircut Suits A Heart Shaped Face

Written by Eveline Apr 26, 2023 Â· 6 min read

If you have a heart shaped face, you may be wondering what haircut will suit you best. The good news is that there are plenty of options to choose from! In this article, we'll go over some of the best haircuts for heart shaped faces.

Description

A heart shaped face is characterized by a wider forehead and cheekbones, with a narrower jawline and chin. The goal of a good haircut for a heart shaped face is to balance out the proportions of the face and draw attention away from the forehead.

How to

When choosing a haircut for a heart shaped face, you should consider the length, layers, and bangs. Here are some tips:

Length

For heart shaped faces, longer hair can help balance out the proportions of the face. Long layers can also help soften the jawline. If you prefer shorter hair, a lob (long bob) can be a great option.

Layers

Layers can add volume and movement to your hair, which can help balance out a wider forehead. However, be careful not to add too many layers around the cheekbones, as this can make the face appear wider.

Bangs

Bangs can be a great way to draw attention away from the forehead. Side-swept bangs are particularly flattering for heart shaped faces, as they help balance out the proportions of the face. Avoid blunt bangs, as they can make the forehead appear even wider.

Step by Step

Here are some steps to follow when getting a haircut for a heart shaped face:

  1. Consult with your hairstylist. They can help you choose a haircut that will flatter your face shape.
  2. Consider the length, layers, and bangs when choosing your haircut.
  3. Bring a picture of the haircut you want to show your hairstylist.
  4. After your haircut, style your hair in a way that flatters your face shape.

Tips

Here are some additional tips for getting a haircut for a heart shaped face:

  • Avoid hairstyles that are too slicked back or tight, as they can emphasize the forehead.
  • Consider adding highlights or lowlights to add dimension to your hair.
  • If you have curly hair, consider a layered haircut to help control the volume.

FAQ

What length should I choose for my haircut?

For heart shaped faces, longer hair is generally more flattering. However, if you prefer shorter hair, a lob can be a great option.

What type of bangs should I choose?

Side-swept bangs are particularly flattering for heart shaped faces, as they help balance out the proportions of the face.

Should I add layers to my hair?

Layers can add volume and movement to your hair, which can help balance out a wider forehead. However, be careful not to add too many layers around the cheekbones, as this can make the face appear wider.

Hair Care

After getting a haircut for a heart shaped face, it's important to take care of your hair to keep it looking healthy and beautiful. Here are some tips:

  • Use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair hydrated.
  • Avoid heat styling tools as much as possible to prevent damage to your hair.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair looking healthy.

Overall, there are plenty of haircuts that can flatter a heart shaped face. By considering the length, layers, and bangs, you can find a haircut that will make you look and feel your best.
