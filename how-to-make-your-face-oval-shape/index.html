How To Make Your Face Oval Shape A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Daniel Apr 06, 2023 Â· 8 min read

Are you tired of your round face and looking for ways to make it oval-shaped? Don't worry; you're not alone. Many people want to achieve an oval face shape because it's considered the most balanced and attractive shape. In this article, we will guide you on how to make your face oval shape through proper hair care and styling techniques. We've also included some frequently asked questions to address your concerns.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to Make Your Face Oval Shape
  • Step by Step Guide
  • Tips for Oval Face Shape
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care and Styling Tips

Description

Before we dive into the steps, let's understand what an oval face shape is. An oval face shape has a length that is one and a half times greater than its width, with no sharp angles or prominent corners. It's often considered the ideal face shape because it's symmetrical and balanced.

How to Make Your Face Oval Shape

Now that you know what an oval face shape is, let's discuss how to make your face oval shape. There are two ways to achieve this: through makeup and hairstyle. We'll focus on the latter because it's a more permanent solution that doesn't require daily maintenance.

Step by Step Guide

Step 1: Determine your face shape

Before you can make your face oval shape, you need to know what your current face shape is. Stand in front of the mirror and observe the shape of your face. Is it round, square, heart-shaped, or diamond-shaped? Once you've determined your face shape, you can proceed to the next step.

Step 2: Choose the right hairstyle

The right hairstyle can make all the difference in creating an oval face shape. A layered haircut that frames your face and adds dimension will work wonders. Avoid hairstyles that add volume to the sides of your face, as this will only make your face appear rounder. Opt for hairstyles that create height at the top of your head to elongate your face.

Step 3: Experiment with bangs

Bangs can be a great way to create an oval face shape. Long, side-swept bangs can soften your face and make it appear longer. Avoid blunt bangs that stop at your eyebrows, as this will only emphasize the roundness of your face.

Step 4: Use hair accessories

Accessories like headbands and hair clips can also help create an oval face shape. A headband that sits at the crown of your head will add height and elongate your face. Hair clips that pull your hair away from your face can also create the illusion of an oval shape.

Tips for Oval Face Shape

Here are some additional tips to keep in mind when trying to create an oval face shape:

  • Avoid hairstyles that add volume to the sides of your face
  • Opt for hairstyles that create height at the top of your head
  • Choose a hairstyle that flatters your face shape
  • Avoid blunt bangs that stop at your eyebrows
  • Experiment with long, side-swept bangs
  • Use hair accessories to create height and elongate your face

FAQs

Q. Can makeup help create an oval face shape?

A. Yes, makeup can help create an oval face shape. Using contouring techniques, you can create the illusion of a longer, more balanced face.

Q. How often should I get my hair cut?

A. It's recommended to get your hair cut every 6 to 8 weeks to maintain your hairstyle and keep your hair healthy.

Q. Can hair color help create an oval face shape?

A. Yes, hair color can help create an oval face shape. Adding highlights around your face can brighten your complexion and create the illusion of an oval shape.

Hair Care and Styling Tips

Here are some general hair care and styling tips to keep in mind:

  • Use a heat protectant when styling your hair with hot tools
  • Avoid over-washing your hair, as this can strip it of natural oils
  • Invest in a high-quality hairbrush and comb
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy
  • Avoid using too much product, as this can weigh down your hair and make it look greasy

By following these tips and techniques, you can create an oval face shape that flatters your features and enhances your natural beauty. Remember to choose a hairstyle that works for your face shape and experiment with different hair accessories to create height and elongate your face. With a little effort and patience, you can achieve the perfect oval face shape!
