Is Straight Hair In Style 2020

Written by Aprilia Jul 13, 2023 · 7 min read

When it comes to hair trends, one question that many people ask is whether straight hair is still in style. If you're considering straightening your locks, you may be wondering if it's worth the effort. The answer is yes – straight hair is definitely still in style for 2020 and beyond. In this blog post, we'll explore why straight hair is popular, how to achieve the look, and tips for maintaining it.

Why Straight Hair is in Style

Straight hair has been a popular style for decades, and it's not going away anytime soon. There are a few reasons why straight hair is still in style:

  • Timeless look: Straight hair is a classic style that looks good on anyone, regardless of age or face shape.
  • Versatility: Straight hair can be worn in a variety of ways, from sleek and shiny to tousled and textured.
  • Low maintenance: Compared to other hairstyles, straight hair is relatively low maintenance and easy to style.

How to Achieve Straight Hair

If you have curly or wavy hair, achieving straight hair may require some effort. Here are the steps to follow:

  1. Wash your hair: Start with clean, dry hair. Use a clarifying shampoo to remove any product buildup.
  2. Apply heat protectant: To prevent damage from heat styling, apply a heat protectant spray or cream to your hair.
  3. Blow dry your hair: Use a round brush and a blow dryer to straighten your hair as you dry it. Start at the roots and work your way down to the ends.
  4. Straighten your hair: Use a flat iron to smooth out any remaining waves or curls. Work in small sections and be sure to use a heat protectant.
  5. Finish with serum: To add shine and smoothness, apply a hair serum to your ends.

It's important to note that heat styling can damage your hair over time. To minimize damage, use a lower heat setting on your flat iron, and try to limit heat styling to once or twice a week.

Tips for Maintaining Straight Hair

Once you have straight hair, you'll want to keep it looking sleek and shiny. Here are some tips for maintaining your straight locks:

  • Use a silk pillowcase: Sleeping on a silk pillowcase can help prevent frizz and breakage.
  • Avoid humidity: Humidity can cause your straight hair to frizz, so try to avoid it if possible.
  • Use dry shampoo: To keep your hair looking fresh between washes, use a dry shampoo to absorb oil and add volume.
  • Trim regularly: Regular trims can help prevent split ends and keep your hair looking healthy.

FAQs

Can I straighten my hair without heat?

Yes, you can straighten your hair without heat using a variety of methods, including braids, rollers, and hair wraps. However, these methods may not result in the same level of straightness as a flat iron.

How often should I wash my straight hair?

How often you wash your hair depends on your hair type and personal preference. However, if you have straight hair, you may be able to go longer between washes since straight hair tends to show oil less than other hair types.

Will straightening my hair damage it?

Heat styling can damage your hair over time, but using a heat protectant and limiting heat styling to once or twice a week can help minimize damage.

Hair Care for Straight Hair

Maintaining healthy hair is important for any hair type, including straight hair. Here are some hair care tips to keep in mind:

  • Use a gentle shampoo: Look for a shampoo that is sulfate-free and gentle on your hair.
  • Condition regularly: Use a conditioner that is formulated for your hair type, and apply it to the ends of your hair.
  • Avoid heat styling: Try to limit heat styling to once or twice a week, and use a heat protectant when you do style your hair.
  • Protect your hair from the sun: Wear a hat or use a hair product with SPF to protect your hair from sun damage.

Straight hair is definitely still in style for 2020 and beyond. Whether you have naturally straight hair or want to achieve the look with heat styling, be sure to take care of your locks to keep them looking healthy and shiny.
