How Can I Stop Hair Fall After Pregnancy

Written by Christine Mar 25, 2023 · 8 min read

Welcoming a new member to the family is one of the most beautiful experiences in a woman's life. However, it also comes with a lot of physical and emotional changes, including hair fall after pregnancy. Postpartum hair loss is a common issue that many new mothers face. The good news is that there are ways to stop hair fall after pregnancy. In this article, we will discuss how to prevent hair fall after pregnancy, step by step, along with some tips, FAQs, and hair care advice.

Table of Contents

  • How to Stop Hair Fall After Pregnancy
  • Step by Step Guide to Prevent Hair Fall After Pregnancy
  • Tips to Reduce Hair Fall After Pregnancy
  • FAQs About Hair Fall After Pregnancy

How to Stop Hair Fall After Pregnancy

The first step to stop hair fall after pregnancy is to understand why it happens. During pregnancy, the body experiences hormonal changes that can boost hair growth. As a result, many women enjoy thicker and fuller hair during pregnancy. However, after giving birth, the hormones return to their normal levels, causing the hair to fall out. Here are some ways to stop hair fall after pregnancy:

1. Eat a Balanced Diet

Eating a healthy and balanced diet can provide your body with the necessary nutrients for healthy hair growth. Include foods rich in vitamins, minerals, and proteins, such as leafy greens, fruits, nuts, seeds, and lean meats.

2. Manage Stress

Stress can contribute to hair fall, so it's important to manage stress levels. Practice relaxation techniques, such as meditation, yoga, or deep breathing exercises, to reduce stress.

3. Avoid Tight Hairstyles

Tight hairstyles, such as braids, buns, and ponytails, can pull on the hair and cause damage, leading to hair fall. Avoid tight hairstyles and opt for loose hairstyles that don't pull on the hair.

4. Use Gentle Hair Products

Harsh chemicals in hair products can damage the hair and contribute to hair fall. Use gentle hair products that are free from sulfates and parabens. Look for products that are specifically designed for postpartum hair loss.

Step by Step Guide to Prevent Hair Fall After Pregnancy

Here is a step-by-step guide to prevent hair fall after pregnancy:

Step 1: Consult a Doctor

If you're experiencing excessive hair fall after pregnancy, consult a doctor to rule out any underlying medical conditions.

Step 2: Eat a Balanced Diet

Eat a healthy and balanced diet that provides your body with the necessary nutrients for healthy hair growth.

Step 3: Manage Stress

Practice relaxation techniques, such as meditation, yoga, or deep breathing exercises, to manage stress levels.

Step 4: Avoid Tight Hairstyles

Avoid tight hairstyles that pull on the hair and cause damage.

Step 5: Use Gentle Hair Products

Use gentle hair products that are specifically designed for postpartum hair loss and free from harsh chemicals.

Tips to Reduce Hair Fall After Pregnancy

Here are some additional tips to reduce hair fall after pregnancy:

1. Massage Your Scalp

Massaging your scalp can improve blood flow to the hair follicles, promoting healthy hair growth.

2. Trim Your Hair Regularly

Trimming your hair regularly can prevent split ends and breakage, reducing hair fall.

3. Use a Wide-Toothed Comb

Use a wide-toothed comb to detangle your hair, as it causes less damage than a brush.

4. Protect Your Hair from Heat

Avoid using heat styling tools, such as straighteners and curling irons, as they can damage the hair and cause hair fall.

FAQs About Hair Fall After Pregnancy

Q: When does postpartum hair loss occur?

A: Postpartum hair loss usually occurs between three and six months after giving birth.

Q: Is postpartum hair loss permanent?

A: No, postpartum hair loss is temporary and usually resolves on its own within a few months.

Q: Can breastfeeding cause hair fall?

A: No, breastfeeding does not cause hair fall. However, hormonal changes during pregnancy and after giving birth can cause hair fall.

Hair Care Tips for Healthy Hair After Pregnancy

Here are some hair care tips for healthy hair after pregnancy:

1. Wash Your Hair Regularly

Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo to keep it clean and healthy.

2. Condition Your Hair

Use a conditioner to keep your hair moisturized and prevent dryness and breakage.

3. Protect Your Hair from the Sun

Wear a hat or use a hair product with SPF to protect your hair from the sun's harmful rays.

4. Get Regular Haircuts

Get regular haircuts to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends and breakage.


In conclusion, hair fall after pregnancy is a common issue that many new mothers face. However, with the right care and attention, it's possible to prevent and reduce hair fall. Follow the tips and steps mentioned in this article, and you'll be on your way to healthy and beautiful hair after pregnancy.
