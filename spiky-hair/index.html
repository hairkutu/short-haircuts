Spiky Hair A Trendy Hairstyle For Men

Written by Petter May 02, 2023 Â· 8 min read

Spiky hair has been around for quite some time now, and it's still one of the most popular hairstyles for men. If you're thinking about trying out this style, then you're in the right place! In this article, we'll cover everything you need to know about spiky hair, including how to style it, tips for maintaining it, and more. So, let's get started!

Description

Spiky hair is a hairstyle that involves creating spikes or points on the hair, usually with the use of hair gel or wax. This style can be achieved with hair of any length, but it's typically most effective on short to medium-length hair. Spiky hair is often associated with a youthful, edgy look, and it's a popular choice among men who want to stand out from the crowd.

One of the great things about spiky hair is that it's versatile and can be customized to suit your individual style. You can go for a messy, textured look, or a more structured, defined look. It's all up to you!

How to Style Spiky Hair

Styling spiky hair is relatively easy, and it doesn't require a lot of time or effort. Here's how to do it:

  1. Wash your hair with a good quality shampoo and conditioner to ensure that it's clean and healthy.
  2. Towel dry your hair and apply a small amount of hair gel or wax to your fingertips.
  3. Using your fingertips, work the gel or wax through your hair, creating spikes or points as you go.
  4. Use a comb or your fingers to refine the shape of your spikes, and add more gel or wax as needed.
  5. Once you're happy with your style, let your hair dry naturally or use a hair dryer to set the look.

Step-by-Step Guide

If you're new to spiky hair, then it can be helpful to follow a step-by-step guide to get the look just right. Here's how:

  1. Start with clean, towel-dried hair.
  2. Apply a small amount of hair gel or wax to your fingertips.
  3. Run your fingers through your hair, creating spikes or points as you go.
  4. Use a comb or your fingers to refine the shape of your spikes.
  5. Add more gel or wax as needed, and continue shaping your hair until you're happy with the look.
  6. Once you're done, let your hair dry naturally or use a hair dryer to set the style.

Tips for Maintaining Spiky Hair

Here are some tips for keeping your spiky hair looking great:

  • Wash your hair regularly to prevent buildup of product and keep your hair healthy.
  • Use a good quality gel or wax that will hold your spikes in place without making your hair look greasy.
  • If you have longer hair, consider using a blow dryer to help set your spikes.
  • Avoid touching your hair too much, as this can cause your spikes to flatten or lose shape.
  • If you're going to be spending time in the sun, wear a hat to protect your hair from damage.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is spiky hair only for young men?

No, spiky hair can be worn by men of all ages. It's a versatile style that can be customized to suit your individual look and personality.

What type of hair works best for spiky hair?

Spiky hair can work on any type of hair, but it's typically most effective on short to medium-length hair that's easy to mold and shape.

Do I need to use hair products to create spiky hair?

Yes, you'll need to use a good quality hair gel or wax to create and maintain your spikes. These products will help your hair stay in place and hold its shape throughout the day.

Hair Care for Spiky Hair

Here are some tips for keeping your spiky hair healthy and looking great:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a good quality shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using hot tools like straighteners or curling irons, as these can damage your hair.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair soft and hydrated.
  • Avoid using too much product, as this can weigh down your hair and make it look greasy.
  • If you're going to be spending time in the sun or swimming, wear a hat or use a leave-in conditioner to protect your hair from damage.

With these tips and techniques, you'll be able to create and maintain the perfect spiky hairstyle. Whether you're going for a messy, textured look or a more structured, defined style, spiky hair is a great choice for men who want to stand out from the crowd. So, why not give it a try?
