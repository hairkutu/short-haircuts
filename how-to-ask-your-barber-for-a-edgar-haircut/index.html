How To Ask Your Barber For An Edgar Haircut A Step By Step Guide

Written by William Apr 01, 2023 Â· 7 min read

If you're looking for a stylish and trendy haircut, you might want to consider getting an Edgar haircut. This haircut is named after American rapper Edgar Esteves, who popularized the style with his signature look. The Edgar haircut features a short, buzzed cut on the sides and back, with longer hair on top that is styled into a messy, textured look. If you want to get this haircut, you need to know how to ask your barber for it. In this post, we'll give you a step-by-step guide on how to ask your barber for an Edgar haircut.

What is an Edgar Haircut?

The Edgar haircut is a popular men's hairstyle that features short sides and back, and longer hair on top that is styled into a messy, textured look. It is named after American rapper Edgar Esteves, who made the style popular with his signature look. The Edgar haircut is a versatile hairstyle that can be styled in many different ways, from a messy, textured look to a more polished, slicked-back style.

How to Ask Your Barber for an Edgar Haircut

Here's a step-by-step guide on how to ask your barber for an Edgar haircut:

Step 1: Find a Barber Who is Familiar with the Edgar Haircut

The first step in getting an Edgar haircut is to find a barber who is familiar with the style. Look for a barber who specializes in men's haircuts and has experience cutting short hair. You can also ask friends or family members for recommendations.

Step 2: Show Your Barber a Picture of the Edgar Haircut

When you go to the barber, bring a picture of the Edgar haircut with you. This will give your barber a clear idea of the style you want. You can find pictures of the Edgar haircut online or on social media.

Step 3: Explain How You Want Your Haircut

Once you've shown your barber the picture of the Edgar haircut, explain how you want your haircut. Let your barber know how short you want the sides and back, and how long you want the hair on top. You can also ask your barber for advice on how to style your hair after the cut.

Step 4: Sit Back and Relax

Once you've explained how you want your haircut, sit back and relax while your barber works their magic. The Edgar haircut is a relatively simple cut, so it shouldn't take too long to complete.

Tips for Maintaining Your Edgar Haircut

Here are some tips for maintaining your Edgar haircut:

  • Use a good quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and clean.
  • Use a hair styling product, such as wax or pomade, to style your hair after the cut.
  • Get regular trims to keep your haircut looking fresh.
  • Experiment with different styling techniques to find the look that works best for you.

FAQ

How long does an Edgar haircut last?

An Edgar haircut can last anywhere from 2-4 weeks, depending on how fast your hair grows and how well you maintain the cut.

Is the Edgar haircut suitable for all hair types?

The Edgar haircut is suitable for most hair types, but it works best on straight or slightly wavy hair.

How should I style my Edgar haircut?

The style of your Edgar haircut will depend on your personal preference and the shape of your face. You can style your hair into a messy, textured look, or a more polished, slicked-back style.

Hair Care

It's important to take care of your hair after getting an Edgar haircut. Use a good quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and clean. You can also use a hair styling product, such as wax or pomade, to style your hair after the cut. Getting regular trims will also help to keep your haircut looking fresh.

Now that you know how to ask your barber for an Edgar haircut, you're ready to rock this stylish and trendy look. Remember to take good care of your hair to keep your Edgar haircut looking fresh and stylish.
