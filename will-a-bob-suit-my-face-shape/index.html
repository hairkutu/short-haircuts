Will A Bob Suit My Face Shape A Guide To Finding The Perfect Bob For You

Written by Petter Jul 11, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Are you considering getting a bob haircut but not sure if it will suit your face shape? Don't worry, we've got you covered. In this article, we will provide you with tips and tricks on how to find the perfect bob for your face shape. Keep reading to find out more!

Description

A bob haircut is a timeless and classic hairstyle that has been popular for decades. It is a versatile cut that can suit any face shape, hair type, and age group. However, finding the perfect bob for your face shape can be a challenge. A bob that looks great on someone else may not necessarily suit you. That's why it's essential to understand your face shape and choose a bob that complements it.

How to

The first step in finding the perfect bob for your face shape is to determine your face shape. There are six basic face shapes: round, oval, square, heart, diamond, and oblong. Once you know your face shape, you can choose a bob that flatters your features.

Round Face

If you have a round face, a bob that hits just below the chin or longer will elongate your face and make it appear slimmer. Avoid bobs that end at your jawline or above as they will make your face look rounder.

Oval Face

If you have an oval face, you can pull off any bob length and style. Lucky you!

Square Face

If you have a square face, a bob with soft layers and side-swept bangs will soften your features. Avoid bobs with blunt cuts and straight across bangs as they will accentuate your square jawline.

Heart Face

If you have a heart-shaped face, a bob that hits at your chin or below with layers around the face will balance your features. Avoid bobs with volume at the crown as they will make your forehead appear wider.

Diamond Face

If you have a diamond-shaped face, a bob with side-swept bangs and volume at the crown will balance your features. Avoid bobs with width at the cheekbones as they will make your face look wider.

Oblong Face

If you have an oblong face, a bob that hits at your chin or above with layers around the face will add width to your face. Avoid bobs that are too long as they will make your face appear longer.

Step by Step

Once you have determined your face shape and the type of bob that will suit you, it's time to choose a stylist and get your haircut. Here are some steps to follow:

  1. Find a reputable stylist who specializes in bob haircuts.
  2. Show them a picture of the bob you want and tell them your face shape.
  3. Trust their expertise and let them make any necessary adjustments to the cut to suit your features.
  4. Ask for styling tips and product recommendations to maintain your bob at home.

Tips

Here are some additional tips to keep in mind when getting a bob:

  • Choose a bob that suits your lifestyle and maintenance level.
  • Consider your hair type and texture when choosing a bob.
  • Experiment with different bob lengths and styles until you find the perfect one.
  • Accessorize your bob with headbands, clips, and other hair accessories.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about bobs:

Q: Will a bob suit my face shape if I have curly hair?

A: Yes, a bob can suit any hair type and texture. However, you may need to choose a bob that works with your curls and avoids the dreaded triangle shape.

Q: Can I still wear a bob if I have a double chin?

A: Absolutely! You can choose a bob that hits just below your jawline to conceal your double chin.

Hair Care

Maintaining your bob is essential to keep it looking fresh and stylish. Here are some hair care tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a shampoo and conditioner that suits your hair type.
  • Use a heat protectant spray before blow-drying, straightening, or curling your hair.
  • Invest in a good quality hairbrush and comb to detangle your hair gently.
  • Trim your hair regularly to maintain the shape of your bob.

Now that you know how to find the perfect bob for your face shape, it's time to book that haircut and rock your new look with confidence. Remember, a bob is a timeless and classic hairstyle that never goes out of style.
