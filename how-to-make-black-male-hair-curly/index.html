How To Make Black Male Hair Curly A Guide For 2023

How to Make Black Male Hair Curly - A Guide for 2023

  • How to

  • Tips

Description

Many black men desire curly hair, but they may have naturally straight hair or have had their hair chemically relaxed. There are several methods to make black male hair curly, and this guide will explore them.

How to

The key to making black male hair curly is to add moisture and manipulate the hair into curls. There are several methods to achieve this, including:

Step by Step

Method 1: Twist Out

1. Start with clean, damp hair

2. Apply a moisturizing product, such as a curl cream or leave-in conditioner, to the hair

3. Divide the hair into sections

4. Twist each section of hair tightly, using your fingers or a comb

5. Allow the hair to air dry or use a diffuser attachment on a hair dryer

6. Once the hair is completely dry, unravel the twists and separate the curls

Method 2: Bantu Knots

1. Start with clean, damp hair

2. Apply a moisturizing product, such as a curl cream or leave-in conditioner, to the hair

3. Divide the hair into sections

4. Twist each section of hair tightly, then wrap it around itself to form a knot

5. Secure the knot with a hairpin or small elastic band

6. Allow the hair to air dry or use a diffuser attachment on a hair dryer

7. Once the hair is completely dry, unravel the knots and separate the curls

Tips

1. Use a wide-toothed comb or your fingers to detangle the hair

2. Do not use a brush, as it can break the hair and cause frizz

3. Use a satin or silk pillowcase to prevent tangling and breakage while sleeping

4. Avoid using heat styling tools, as they can cause damage to the hair

FAQ

Can relaxed hair be made curly?

Yes, relaxed hair can be made curly using the same methods as natural hair. However, it is important to be gentle with the hair to prevent damage.

How often should I wash my hair?

It is recommended to wash your hair every 1-2 weeks, depending on your hair type and personal preference. Over-washing can strip the hair of natural oils, while under-washing can lead to product buildup and scalp irritation.

Hair Care

To maintain healthy, curly hair, it is important to:

- Keep the hair moisturized with a leave-in conditioner or curl cream

- Protect the hair from heat and sun damage with a hat or scarf

- Trim the ends regularly to prevent split ends

- Avoid harsh chemicals, such as relaxers or dyes, that can damage the hair

By following these tips and methods, black men can achieve the curly hair they desire while maintaining healthy, strong hair.
