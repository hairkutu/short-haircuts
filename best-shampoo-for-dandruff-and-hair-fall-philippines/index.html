Best Shampoo For Dandruff And Hair Fall Philippines A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Oliver Sep 09, 2023 · 8 min read

Dealing with dandruff and hair fall can be a real struggle, especially for those living in the Philippines where humidity and pollution can exacerbate these issues. Fortunately, there are plenty of shampoos available in the market that can help combat dandruff and hair fall. In this guide, we will be discussing the best shampoos for dandruff and hair fall in the Philippines, as well as tips on how to properly care for your hair.

Table of Contents

  • What Causes Hair Fall?

What is Dandruff?

Dandruff is a common scalp condition that causes flaking of the skin. It can be caused by a variety of factors such as dry skin, oily skin, fungal infections, and even stress. Symptoms of dandruff include white or yellow flakes of skin on the scalp and hair, itching, and redness.

What Causes Hair Fall?

Hair fall, on the other hand, can also be caused by a variety of factors such as stress, genetics, hormonal changes, and poor nutrition. Symptoms of hair fall include excessive shedding of hair, thinning hair, and bald patches.

Best Shampoos for Dandruff and Hair Fall in the Philippines

When it comes to choosing the best shampoo for dandruff and hair fall in the Philippines, it is important to look for products that contain ingredients that can help combat these issues. Here are some of the best shampoos available in the market:

1. Head & Shoulders Anti-Dandruff Shampoo

Head & Shoulders is a well-known brand when it comes to anti-dandruff shampoos. Their products contain active ingredients such as zinc pyrithione and selenium sulfide that can help reduce dandruff and prevent hair fall.

2. Nizoral Anti-Dandruff Shampoo

Nizoral is another popular brand when it comes to anti-dandruff shampoos. Their product contains ketoconazole, an antifungal agent that can help treat dandruff and prevent hair fall.

3. Dove Hair Fall Rescue Shampoo

Dove Hair Fall Rescue Shampoo is a great option for those dealing with hair fall. Their product contains Trichazole Actives, a blend of ingredients that can help strengthen hair and reduce hair fall.

4. L'Oreal Paris Fall Resist Anti-Dandruff Shampoo

L'Oreal Paris Fall Resist Anti-Dandruff Shampoo is another great option for those dealing with both dandruff and hair fall. Their product contains Arginine Essence, an amino acid that can help nourish hair and reduce hair fall.

Hair Care Tips to Prevent Dandruff and Hair Fall

Aside from using the right shampoo, there are other ways to prevent dandruff and hair fall. Here are some hair care tips:

1. Keep your scalp clean

Make sure to wash your hair regularly, especially if you have a tendency to have oily hair. This can help prevent dandruff and other scalp conditions.

2. Avoid using hot tools

Using hot tools such as blow dryers, flat irons, and curling irons can damage your hair and cause it to fall out. Try to use these tools sparingly or avoid them altogether.

3. Eat a balanced diet

A balanced diet that includes protein, vitamins, and minerals can help keep your hair healthy and prevent hair fall.

4. Manage stress

Stress can cause hair fall, so it's important to find ways to manage it. This can include exercise, meditation, or other relaxation techniques.

Frequently Asked Questions

1. Can dandruff cause hair fall?

Yes, dandruff can cause hair fall if left untreated. The itching and inflammation caused by dandruff can lead to hair fall.

2. Can hair fall be reversed?

It depends on the cause of the hair fall. If it's due to genetics, it may be difficult to reverse. However, if it's due to poor nutrition or other factors, it may be possible to reverse it.

3. How often should I wash my hair?

It depends on your hair type and how oily your scalp is. Some people may need to wash their hair every day, while others can go a few days without washing. It's important to find a routine that works for you.

Dealing with dandruff and hair fall can be frustrating, but with the right shampoo and hair care routine, you can prevent and treat these issues. Make sure to choose a shampoo that contains ingredients that can help combat dandruff and hair fall, and follow the hair care tips mentioned in this guide.
