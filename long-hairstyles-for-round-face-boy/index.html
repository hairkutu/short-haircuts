Long Hairstyles For Round Face Boy A Guide To The Perfect Look

Written by Eveline Mar 31, 2023 Â· 9 min read

If you're a boy with a round face, it can be challenging to find a hairstyle that complements your features. However, with the right long hairstyle, you can enhance your facial structure and achieve the perfect look. In this article, we'll explore some of the best long hairstyles for round face boys, along with tips on how to style them and care for your hair.

Table of Contents

  • How to Choose the Right Long Hairstyle for Round Face Boy
  • Step-by-Step Guide to Styling Long Hairstyles for Round Face Boy

  • Hair Care Tips for Long Hairstyles for Round Face Boy

Description

A round face is characterized by a soft, circular shape with no prominent angles or corners. The forehead, cheekbones, and jawline are all of similar width, creating a symmetrical appearance. To enhance your features and achieve the perfect look, you need to choose a long hairstyle that complements your facial structure.

How to Choose the Right Long Hairstyle for Round Face Boy

When choosing a long hairstyle for round face boy, you need to consider the following factors:

Face Shape

A long hairstyle should balance out the roundness of your face. You can achieve this by adding volume to the top of your head and keeping the sides and back short. A good rule of thumb is to choose a hairstyle that narrows at the temples and jawline.

Hair Type

Your hair type will also determine the best long hairstyle for you. If you have thick hair, you can go for a layered hairstyle that adds movement and texture. If you have thin hair, you can opt for a sleek, straight hairstyle that adds volume and body.

Lifestyle

Your lifestyle will also play a role in choosing the right long hairstyle. If you're active and on-the-go, you may want to choose a low-maintenance hairstyle that requires minimal styling. If you have more time to spend on your hair, you can choose a more intricate hairstyle that requires more styling.

Step-by-Step Guide to Styling Long Hairstyles for Round Face Boy

Here's a step-by-step guide to styling some of the best long hairstyles for round face boy:

Long Layered Haircut

1. Start by washing your hair with a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to add body and texture to your hair.

2. Blow-dry your hair, using a round brush to add volume to the top of your head.

3. Apply a small amount of hair wax or pomade to the ends of your hair to add texture and movement.

4. Use your fingers to separate and tousle your hair, creating a messy, layered look.

Long Side-Swept Bangs

1. Start by washing your hair with a smoothing shampoo and conditioner to tame any frizz or flyaways.

2. Blow-dry your hair, using a round brush to add volume to the top of your head.

3. Use a flat iron to straighten your hair, focusing on the ends to create a sleek, polished look.

4. Sweep your bangs to one side of your face, using a small amount of hair gel or pomade to keep them in place.

Tips for Maintaining Long Hairstyles for Round Face Boy

Here are some tips for maintaining your long hairstyle:

Regular Trims

To keep your long hairstyle looking fresh and healthy, you should get regular trims every 6-8 weeks. This will prevent split ends and promote healthy hair growth.

Deep Conditioning

Deep conditioning treatments can help nourish and moisturize your hair, preventing breakage and dryness. You can use a deep conditioning mask once a week to keep your hair looking and feeling healthy.

Protective Styling

If you're active or spend a lot of time outdoors, you should protect your hair from damage by wearing a hat or scarf. You can also use a leave-in conditioner or hair oil to protect your hair from UV rays and environmental pollutants.

FAQs About Long Hairstyles for Round Face Boy

Q: What is the best long hairstyle for round face boy?

A: The best long hairstyle for round face boy is one that adds volume to the top of your head and narrows at the temples and jawline.

Q: Can I wear my long hair up?

A: Yes, you can wear your long hair up in a ponytail, bun, or braid. Just make sure to use hair ties that don't damage your hair.

Q: How do I know if a long hairstyle is right for me?

A: You can try on different long hairstyles using virtual makeover tools or by consulting with a hairstylist.

Hair Care Tips for Long Hairstyles for Round Face Boy

Here are some general hair care tips for long hairstyles:

Wash Your Hair Regularly

You should wash your hair every 2-3 days to prevent buildup and oiliness. Use a shampoo and conditioner that are specifically formulated for your hair type.

Avoid Heat Damage

Excessive heat styling can damage your hair, causing dryness and breakage. Use a heat protectant spray and avoid using hot tools on high heat settings.

Eat a Healthy Diet

A healthy diet rich in protein, vitamins, and minerals can promote healthy hair growth and prevent hair loss. Make sure to eat plenty of fruits, vegetables, and lean proteins.

Long hairstyles for round face boy can be a great way to enhance your features and achieve the perfect look. By choosing the right hairstyle and taking good care of your hair, you can have healthy, beautiful hair that complements your facial structure.
