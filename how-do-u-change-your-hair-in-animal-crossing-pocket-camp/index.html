How Do You Change Your Hair In Animal Crossing Pocket Camp

Written by Luffy Mar 24, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Animal Crossing Pocket Camp is a popular mobile game where players can create their own campsite and interact with adorable animal characters. One of the many customization options available in the game is changing your character's hairstyle. In this article, we will guide you through the process of changing your hair in Animal Crossing Pocket Camp.

Description

Changing your hair in Animal Crossing Pocket Camp is a quick and easy process that can be done from the game's main menu. You can choose from a variety of hairstyles, including short, medium, and long hair, as well as different colors and accessories.

How to Change Your Hair in Animal Crossing Pocket Camp

To change your hair in Animal Crossing Pocket Camp, follow these simple steps:

Step 1: Open the Game Menu

From the main screen of the game, tap the menu icon located in the bottom-right corner of the screen. This will open the game menu.

Step 2: Select the Settings Icon

In the game menu, tap the settings icon located in the top-right corner of the screen. This will open the game settings.

Step 3: Choose the Appearance Option

In the game settings, select the "Appearance" option. This will open the appearance customization screen.

Step 4: Choose Your New Hair Style

In the appearance customization screen, you will see a variety of hairstyle options. You can choose from short, medium, and long hair, as well as different colors and accessories. Select the hairstyle you want and tap "OK" to confirm your selection.

Tips for Changing Your Hair in Animal Crossing Pocket Camp

Here are some tips to keep in mind when changing your hair in Animal Crossing Pocket Camp:

  • Experiment with different hairstyles and colors to find the perfect look for your character.
  • Don't be afraid to try out new accessories, like hats or headbands, to add some extra flair to your hair.
  • Changing your hair is a great way to refresh your character's look and keep things interesting in the game.

FAQ

Here are some common questions and answers about changing your hair in Animal Crossing Pocket Camp:

Can I change my hair color?

Yes, you can change your hair color in Animal Crossing Pocket Camp. There are a variety of colors to choose from, including natural colors like brown and black, as well as fun colors like pink and blue.

Can I change my hair style more than once?

Yes, you can change your hair style as many times as you like in Animal Crossing Pocket Camp. Simply follow the steps outlined above to choose a new hairstyle.

Does changing my hair affect my character's stats or abilities?

No, changing your hair does not affect your character's stats or abilities in Animal Crossing Pocket Camp. It is purely a cosmetic customization option.

Hair Care in Animal Crossing Pocket Camp

In Animal Crossing Pocket Camp, your character's hair will grow over time. To keep your hair looking neat and tidy, you can visit the salon located in the Marketplace area of the game. Here, you can get a haircut to trim your hair back to its original length.

Another way to care for your hair in Animal Crossing Pocket Camp is to use hair care items, like shampoo and conditioner. These items can be purchased from the Marketplace or received as rewards for completing tasks in the game. Using hair care items will keep your hair looking healthy and shiny.

Changing your hair in Animal Crossing Pocket Camp is a fun and easy way to customize your character's appearance. Whether you want a new hairstyle for a special event or just to switch things up, the process is quick and simple. Follow the steps outlined in this article to change your hair and experiment with different styles and colors!
