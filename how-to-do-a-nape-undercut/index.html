How To Do A Nape Undercut

Written by Frank Apr 23, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Are you looking for a trendy and edgy hairstyle? A nape undercut might be the perfect look for you! This hairstyle involves shaving or cutting the hair at the nape of the neck, creating a bold and unique look. In this article, we'll go over how to do a nape undercut, step-by-step, along with some tips, FAQs, and hair care advice.

Table of Contents

  1. How to Do a Nape Undercut
  2. Step-by-Step Guide
  3. Tips for a Perfect Nape Undercut
  4. FAQs About Nape Undercuts
  5. Hair Care Tips for Nape Undercuts

How to Do a Nape Undercut

Before we get started, it's important to note that a nape undercut is not a hairstyle that can easily be done at home. It's best to seek the help of a professional hairstylist who has experience with this type of cut. However, if you're feeling brave and want to attempt it yourself, here's a guide on how to do a nape undercut.

What You'll Need

  • Clippers or scissors
  • Comb
  • Hair ties or clips
  • Trimming cape or towel
  • Sharp razor

Step-by-Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide on how to do a nape undercut:

  1. Section off the hair at the crown of the head and clip it away from the nape.
  2. Comb the hair at the nape down and determine the length you want to cut or shave.
  3. Use clippers or scissors to cut or shave the hair at the nape to the desired length. Be sure to keep the hair at the crown of the head clipped away and out of the way.
  4. Use a sharp razor to clean up the edges and create a clean, precise line.
  5. Release the hair at the crown of the head and style as desired.

Tips for a Perfect Nape Undercut

Here are some tips to help you achieve the perfect nape undercut:

  • Make sure you have a clear idea of the length you want to cut or shave before you start.
  • Use a comb to help guide your clippers or scissors and ensure a straight, even cut.
  • Always use a sharp razor for a clean, precise line.
  • Consider using hair gel or wax to style your nape undercut and keep it in place.

FAQs About Nape Undercuts

Here are some frequently asked questions about nape undercuts:

Is a nape undercut suitable for all hair types?

Yes, a nape undercut can work for all hair types, including straight, wavy, or curly hair.

How often do I need to get my nape undercut trimmed?

The frequency of trimming your nape undercut will depend on how quickly your hair grows. On average, you should get it trimmed every four to six weeks.

Can I still wear my hair up with a nape undercut?

Yes, you can still wear your hair up with a nape undercut. However, you may need to use more hair ties or clips to keep your hair in place.

Hair Care Tips for Nape Undercuts

Here are some hair care tips to keep your nape undercut looking great:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Use a hair mask once a week to keep your hair healthy and hydrated.
  • Avoid using hot tools like curling irons or straighteners on your nape undercut to prevent damage.
  • Visit your hairstylist regularly for trims to maintain your nape undercut.

With these tips and a little bit of bravery, you can rock a stylish nape undercut in no time! Remember, it's always best to seek the help of a professional hairstylist if you're unsure about how to do a nape undercut.
