Are Square Faces Unattractive Debunking The Beauty Myth In 2023

Description

For years, there has been a beauty myth circulating that square faces are unattractive. This myth has caused many people to feel insecure about their natural facial structure, leading to a rise in cosmetic procedures and other beauty treatments.

However, the truth is that square faces are just as beautiful as any other face shape. In fact, many famous and successful people have square faces, including Angelina Jolie, Keira Knightley, and Olivia Wilde.

So why has this myth persisted for so long? It may have to do with societal norms and beauty standards that prioritize certain facial features over others. But we believe that everyone should embrace and celebrate their unique features, including their square face shape.

How to Determine Your Face Shape

Before we dive into the topic of square faces, it's important to understand how to determine your own face shape. This will help you choose the most flattering hairstyles, makeup looks, and accessories for your features.

To determine your face shape, follow these steps:

  1. Stand in front of a mirror in a well-lit room.
  2. Pull your hair back so your face is fully visible.
  3. Use a washable marker or lipstick to trace the outline of your face on the mirror.
  4. Step back and take a look at the shape you traced.

There are five basic face shapes:

  • Round
  • Oval
  • Square
  • Heart
  • Diamond

If the shape you traced is roughly equal in length and width, you have a square face shape.

Tips for Flattering Square Faces

Contrary to popular belief, there are many hairstyles, makeup looks, and accessories that can flatter square faces. Here are some tips:

Hairstyles

When it comes to hairstyles, the goal for square faces is to soften the angles and create some volume around the face. Here are some ideas:

  • Long layers
  • Soft waves or curls
  • Side-swept bangs
  • A deep side part
  • A messy updo

Makeup

For makeup, the goal is to create balance and symmetry on the face. Here are some tips:

  • Contour with a bronzer or blush to soften the angles
  • Highlight the cheekbones to create definition
  • Use neutral eyeshadow shades to create depth
  • Experiment with bold lip colors to draw attention away from the jawline

Accessories

Accessories can also help create balance on a square face. Here are some ideas:

  • Wear earrings that are long and dangly
  • Choose necklaces with curved or rounded shapes
  • Wear hats with a slouchy, asymmetrical shape

FAQs About Square Faces

Q: Is a square face shape rare?

A: No, square faces are actually quite common. In fact, they are one of the five basic face shapes.

Q: Can a square face shape change over time?

A: No, your face shape is determined by your bone structure and will not change over time.

Q: Are there any famous people with square faces?

A: Yes, many famous people have square faces, including Angelina Jolie, Keira Knightley, and Olivia Wilde.

Hair Care for Square Faces

Finally, it's important to take care of your hair to ensure that it looks its best with your square face shape. Here are some tips:

  • Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to add body to your hair.
  • Avoid heavy or blunt bangs, which can make your face look even more square.
  • Invest in a good round brush to create soft, voluminous curls.
  • Consider getting a layered haircut to add movement and texture to your hair.

Remember, your square face shape is just one aspect of your unique beauty. Don't be afraid to experiment with different hairstyles, makeup looks, and accessories to find what makes you feel confident and beautiful.
