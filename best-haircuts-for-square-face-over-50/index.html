Best Haircuts For Square Face Over 50 A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Oliver Mar 19, 2023 Â· 7 min read

If you have a square face and are over 50, finding the perfect haircut can be a challenge. A square face has a broad forehead, strong jawline, and sharp angles. It's essential to choose a hairstyle that softens the angles and flatters your features. In this guide, we'll discuss the best haircuts for square face over 50, including how to style and care for them.

Table of Contents:

  • Description
  • How to Choose the Best Haircut for Square Face Over 50
  • Step by Step Guide to Styling Your Haircut
  • Tips for Maintaining Your Haircut
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care for Square Face Over 50

Description

As we age, our face shape changes, and our skin loses its elasticity. A square face can become more angular, making it essential to choose a haircut that flatters your features. The perfect haircut can make you look younger, more stylish, and confident.

How to Choose the Best Haircut for Square Face Over 50

When choosing a haircut, it's essential to consider your face shape, hair texture, and lifestyle. Here are some tips for choosing the best haircut for square face over 50:

  • Choose a haircut that softens your sharp angles, such as a layered bob or a shoulder-length haircut with soft waves.
  • Avoid hairstyles that are too short or too long, as they can emphasize the strong jawline and square shape of your face.
  • Consider adding bangs to your hairstyle to soften your forehead and draw attention away from the angular jawline.
  • Opt for hairstyles with volume at the crown to elongate your face and balance out your features.
  • Choose a haircut that suits your lifestyle and hair texture. If you have fine hair, a short bob can add volume and texture, while thick hair can benefit from a layered cut.

Step by Step Guide to Styling Your Haircut

Styling your hair can be a breeze with the right haircut. Here's a step-by-step guide to styling your haircut:

  • Start with clean, towel-dried hair.
  • Apply a volumizing mousse or spray to the roots of your hair.
  • Blow-dry your hair using a round brush to add volume and texture.
  • Use a curling iron or hot rollers to create soft waves or curls.
  • Finish with a light hairspray to hold your style in place.

Tips for Maintaining Your Haircut

Maintaining your haircut is essential to keep it looking fresh and stylish. Here are some tips for maintaining your haircut:

  • Visit your hairstylist regularly to trim your hair and maintain its shape.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy and shiny.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as it can damage your hair and make it look dull.
  • Invest in quality hair care products that are designed for your hair type and texture.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about the best haircuts for square face over 50:

  • Q: Can I wear a pixie cut if I have a square face?
  • A: Yes, you can wear a pixie cut if you have a square face. However, it's essential to choose a style that softens your features, such as a side-swept bang or textured layers.
  • Q: Can I wear a bob if I have thick hair?
  • A: Yes, you can wear a bob if you have thick hair. A layered bob can add texture and volume to your hair and flatter your square face.
  • Q: Should I avoid bangs if I have a square face?
  • A: No, you don't have to avoid bangs if you have a square face. Bangs can soften your forehead and draw attention away from your angular jawline.

Hair Care for Square Face Over 50

Maintaining healthy hair is essential to keep it looking its best. Here are some tips for hair care for square face over 50:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using hot water when washing your hair, as it can damage your hair and make it look dull.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair and avoid using a brush on wet hair.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, such as blow dryers, curling irons, and flat irons.
  • Eat a healthy diet rich in vitamins and minerals to promote healthy hair growth.

By following these tips, you can find the best haircut for square face over 50 and keep your hair looking healthy and stylish.
