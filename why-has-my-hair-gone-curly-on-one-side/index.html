Why Has My Hair Gone Curly On One Side

Written by Petter Mar 20, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Have you noticed that your hair has gone curly on one side and you're not sure why? This can be frustrating and confusing, but it's actually a common issue that many people experience. In this article, we'll explore the reasons why your hair may have gone curly on one side and what you can do about it.

Description

Curly hair is caused by the natural shape of the hair follicle. When the follicle is oval-shaped, it produces curly hair. When it's round, it produces straight hair. However, there are many factors that can cause your hair to go curly on one side, even if your follicles are round.

How To

If you're wondering why your hair has gone curly on one side, there are a few things you can do to figure it out.

Step 1: Check Your Sleep Position

One of the most common causes of hair going curly on one side is sleeping on that side. When you sleep, your hair is pressed against your pillow, which can cause it to take on the shape of the pillowcase. If you always sleep on the same side, your hair may start to take on a curl on that side.

Step 2: Consider Your Hair Products

The products you use on your hair can also cause it to go curly on one side. If you're using a product that's too heavy for your hair type, it can cause your hair to curl. On the other hand, if you're not using enough product, your hair may be frizzy on one side and straight on the other.

Step 3: Think About Your Haircut

If you've recently gotten a haircut, it could be the reason why your hair has gone curly on one side. If your stylist cut your hair unevenly or at an angle, it can cause your hair to curl on one side.

Tips

If you've determined that your hair has gone curly on one side because of your sleep position, hair products, or haircut, there are a few things you can do to fix it.

Tip 1: Change Your Sleep Position

If you always sleep on the same side, try sleeping on your back or the opposite side. This can help reduce the amount of pressure on your hair, which can prevent it from taking on a curl.

Tip 2: Use the Right Hair Products

Make sure you're using products that are appropriate for your hair type. If you have fine hair, use lightweight products that won't weigh it down. If you have thick hair, use products that will help control frizz.

Tip 3: Talk to Your Stylist

If you're unhappy with your haircut, talk to your stylist about it. They may be able to fix it by trimming your hair or adding layers to create a more even look.

FAQ

Q: Can stress cause my hair to go curly on one side?

A: Stress can cause a variety of hair issues, including hair loss and breakage. However, it's unlikely to cause your hair to go curly on one side.

Q: Will using a straightener fix my hair?

A: Using a straightener can help smooth out your hair, but it won't fix the underlying issue. If your hair has gone curly on one side because of your sleep position or haircut, using a straightener will only temporarily fix the problem.

Hair Care

Regardless of the reason why your hair has gone curly on one side, it's important to take care of your hair to prevent further damage. Make sure you're using a gentle shampoo and conditioning regularly. Avoid using heat tools too often, and use a heat protectant when you do.

By following these tips and taking care of your hair, you can help prevent your hair from going curly on one side and maintain healthy, beautiful locks.
