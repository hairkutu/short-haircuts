What To Do If Your Bridesmaid Dress Is Too Short

Written by Robby May 06, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Being a bridesmaid is a huge responsibility, and it comes with a lot of planning and coordinating. One of the most important responsibilities of being a bridesmaid is to wear the bridesmaid dress. However, sometimes the dress you get is too short or doesn't fit properly. If you find yourself in this situation, don't panic. Here's what you can do.

Description

It's not uncommon for bridesmaid dresses to be too short. It could be due to a mistake in measuring or a manufacturing defect. Whatever the reason may be, it's important to address the issue before the big day arrives. A dress that's too short can be uncomfortable and can make you feel self-conscious.

How to

If your bridesmaid dress is too short, don't worry. Here are some steps you can take to fix the problem:

Step 1: Talk to the Bride

The first thing you should do is talk to the bride. Let her know that the dress is too short and ask if there's anything that can be done to fix it. It's important to do this as soon as possible so that there's enough time to make any necessary alterations.

Step 2: Get the Dress Altered

If the dress is too short, you can get it altered. Take the dress to a tailor and have them add some extra fabric to the hemline. This will give the dress some extra length and make it more comfortable to wear. Alternatively, you can have the tailor remove the hemline altogether and create a new one.

Step 3: Wear Leggings or Tights

If the dress is only slightly too short, you can wear leggings or tights underneath. This will not only give you some extra coverage but can also add a pop of color to your outfit.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind if your bridesmaid dress is too short:

Tip 1: Be Honest

If the dress is too short, be honest with the bride. Let her know that the dress doesn't fit properly and that you're working on getting it fixed. Honesty is always the best policy.

Tip 2: Don't Panic

Remember, a dress that's too short can be fixed. Don't panic and don't stress out. There are always solutions to every problem.

FAQ

Q: What should I do if the dress is too short and there's not enough time to get it altered?

A: If there's not enough time to get the dress altered, you can try wearing it with leggings or tights underneath. Alternatively, you can try wearing a longer jacket or cardigan to cover up the hemline.

Q: How do I know if the dress is too short?

A: If the dress hits above your knees or if you feel like you're constantly pulling it down, it's probably too short.

Hair Care

When it comes to hair care for a bridesmaid dress that's too short, it's important to keep your hair simple and elegant. A low bun or a sleek ponytail can complement the dress without taking away from it. Avoid any hairstyles that are too elaborate or that will draw attention away from the dress.

Remember, being a bridesmaid is an important responsibility, and it's important to look your best. If your bridesmaid dress is too short, don't panic. Talk to the bride and take the necessary steps to fix the problem. With a little bit of effort, you can look and feel great on the big day.
