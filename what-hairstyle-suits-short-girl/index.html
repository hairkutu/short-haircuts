What Hairstyle Suits Short Girl A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Oliver Jun 05, 2023 Â· 8 min read

If you're a short girl looking for the perfect hairstyle, you've come to the right place. In this article, we'll be discussing the different hairstyles that suit short girls and how to achieve them. We'll also be providing tips and tricks on how to take care of your hair and maintain your chosen hairstyle. Let's get started!

How to Determine Your Face Shape

Before we dive into the different hairstyles that suit short girls, it's important to determine your face shape. This will help you choose a hairstyle that will complement your features and enhance your overall look. There are six main face shapes: oval, round, square, heart, diamond, and triangle. To determine your face shape, stand in front of a mirror and trace the outline of your face with a lipstick or eyeshadow. Once you've determined your face shape, you can move on to choosing a hairstyle.

Oval Face Shape

If you have an oval face shape, you're in luck! This face shape is considered to be the most versatile, and almost any hairstyle will suit you. However, if you want to enhance your features, consider opting for a short bob or a pixie cut. These hairstyles will highlight your cheekbones and jawline.

Round Face Shape

If you have a round face shape, you should opt for hairstyles that create the illusion of length. Consider a long bob or a layered haircut that falls below your chin. Avoid hairstyles that add volume to the sides of your face, as this will make your face look wider.

Square Face Shape

If you have a square face shape, you should opt for hairstyles that soften your jawline. Consider a layered haircut with soft curls or a side-swept fringe. Avoid hairstyles that are too straight or angular, as this will accentuate your square jawline.

Heart Face Shape

If you have a heart face shape, you should opt for hairstyles that balance your forehead and chin. Consider a bob with side-swept bangs or a layered haircut that falls just below your chin. Avoid hairstyles that are too voluminous at the top, as this will make your forehead appear larger.

Diamond Face Shape

If you have a diamond face shape, you should opt for hairstyles that create the illusion of width at the forehead and jawline. Consider a short bob or a pixie cut with volume at the top. Avoid hairstyles that are too long or flat, as this will accentuate your narrow forehead and chin.

Triangle Face Shape

If you have a triangle face shape, you should opt for hairstyles that balance your features. Consider a long bob with layers that fall around your cheekbones or a pixie cut with volume at the top. Avoid hairstyles that are too long or flat, as this will make your chin appear wider.

How to Achieve Your Chosen Hairstyle

Once you've determined your face shape and chosen a hairstyle that suits you, it's time to achieve your desired look. Here are some general tips on how to achieve different hairstyles:

  • If you want a straight and sleek look, use a flat iron to straighten your hair.
  • If you want to add volume, use a round brush when blow-drying your hair.
  • If you want to add curls, use a curling iron or hot rollers.
  • If you want to add texture, use a texturizing spray or sea salt spray.

Remember to always use heat protectant products before using heat styling tools to prevent damage to your hair.

Tips and Tricks for Hair Care

To maintain your chosen hairstyle, it's important to take care of your hair. Here are some tips and tricks for hair care:

  • Use a shampoo and conditioner that suits your hair type.
  • Avoid using hot water when washing your hair, as this can dry out your scalp.
  • Avoid using too much heat styling tools, as this can damage your hair.
  • Use a hair mask or deep conditioner once a week to keep your hair healthy and moisturized.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about hairstyles for short girls:

What is the best hairstyle for a short girl?

The best hairstyle for a short girl depends on your face shape and personal preference. Some popular hairstyles for short girls include a bob, a pixie cut, and a long bob.

Can short girls have long hair?

Short girls can have long hair, but it's important to choose a hairstyle that complements your features. Consider a long bob or a layered haircut that falls just below your shoulders.

How often should I cut my hair?

It's recommended to cut your hair every 6-8 weeks to maintain your chosen hairstyle and keep your hair healthy.

Conclusion

Choosing a hairstyle can be overwhelming, but by determining your face shape and personal preference, you can find a hairstyle that suits you. Remember to take care of your hair by using the right products and avoiding heat damage. With these tips and tricks, you can achieve the perfect hairstyle for your short stature.
