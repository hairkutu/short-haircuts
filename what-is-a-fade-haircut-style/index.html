What Is A Fade Haircut Style

Written by Petter Jun 15, 2023 Â· 8 min read

Are you tired of the same old haircut and looking to try something new? Then a fade haircut style might be the perfect choice for you. This popular haircut style has been around for decades and is still going strong today. In this article, we will explain what a fade haircut style is, how to get it, and provide some tips and tricks to keep it looking fresh.

Description

A fade haircut style is a popular men's haircut that features a gradual tapering of hair length from the top of the head down to the neck. The hair is typically longer on top and shorter on the sides and back, resulting in a smooth transition or "fade" between the two lengths. The fade can be low, medium, or high, depending on how short the hair is cut on the sides and back.

How to Get a Fade Haircut Style

Getting a fade haircut style is easy, but it's important to go to a skilled barber or hairstylist who knows how to cut hair properly. Here are the steps to getting a fade haircut:

Step 1: Choose Your Fade

Decide on the type of fade you want. A low fade starts just above the ear and gradually tapers down to the neck. A medium fade starts halfway up the sides of the head, and a high fade starts at the temples.

Step 2: Choose Your Length

Choose how long you want your hair on top. This will depend on your personal style and the shape of your face. Longer hair on top will create a more dramatic look, while shorter hair will be more subtle.

Step 3: Trim the Sides and Back

The barber will use clippers to trim the sides and back of your hair. They will start with a longer guard and gradually work their way down to create the fade effect.

Step 4: Blend the Top

The barber will then blend the longer hair on top with the shorter hair on the sides and back. They may use scissors or a razor to create a seamless transition between the two lengths.

Step 5: Style Your Hair

Once the haircut is complete, style your hair using your preferred products. You can use pomade, gel, or wax to create a variety of styles, from slicked-back to messy and textured.

Tips for Maintaining Your Fade Haircut Style

Here are some tips to keep your fade haircut looking fresh:

- Visit the barber regularly to maintain the shape of your fade. - Use a good quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy. - Use a light hold styling product to prevent weighing down your hair. - Don't be afraid to experiment with different styles to find the one that suits you best.

FAQ

What is the difference between a fade and a taper?

A fade is a gradual transition between hair lengths, while a taper is a more defined line between the longer and shorter hair. A taper typically starts higher up on the head than a fade.

Can you get a fade haircut with curly hair?

Yes, you can get a fade haircut with curly hair. However, it's important to find a barber or hairstylist who is experienced with cutting curly hair to ensure the best results.

What is the best product to use for styling a fade haircut?

The best product to use for styling a fade haircut depends on your hair type and the style you want to achieve. Pomade is great for slicked-back styles, while wax is better for creating texture and definition.

Hair Care

It's important to take care of your hair, especially after getting a fade haircut. Here are some tips for keeping your hair healthy:

- Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to prevent stripping your hair of natural oils. - Avoid using hot tools like hair dryers or straighteners, as they can damage your hair. - Use a leave-in conditioner or hair oil to keep your hair moisturized. - Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends and breakage.

Conclusion

A fade haircut style is a classic and versatile men's haircut that has stood the test of time. Whether you prefer a low, medium, or high fade, this haircut is a great choice for anyone looking to switch up their style. By following our tips for maintaining your fade haircut, you can keep it looking fresh and stylish for weeks to come.
