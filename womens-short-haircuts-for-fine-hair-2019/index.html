Women s Short Haircuts For Fine Hair 2019 A Guide

Written by Eveline Jul 02, 2023 Â· 8 min read

If you have fine hair, you know how tricky it can be to find a haircut that flatters your features without making your hair look flat and lifeless. However, the good news is that there are plenty of trendy and stylish short haircuts for women with fine hair. In this article, we'll give you a guide on the best women's short haircuts for fine hair in 2019.

Description

Short haircuts for fine hair can help to add texture and volume to your hair, making it look thicker and more voluminous. However, not all short haircuts are created equal - some can actually make your hair look even thinner and flatter than before. The key is to choose a haircut that works with your hair type and face shape, and to style it in a way that flatters your features.

How to Choose the Right Short Haircut for Fine Hair

When choosing a short haircut for fine hair, there are a few things to keep in mind:

  • Consider your face shape: Different face shapes look better with different haircuts. For example, if you have a round face, you may want to avoid haircuts that are too short and flat, as they can make your face look even rounder.
  • Look for texture: Adding texture to your hair can help to make it look thicker and more voluminous. Look for haircuts that incorporate layers, choppy ends, or razored edges.
  • Avoid blunt cuts: Blunt cuts can make fine hair look even thinner and flatter. Instead, opt for haircuts with lots of movement and texture.
  • Consider your lifestyle: Short haircuts can be high-maintenance, so make sure you choose a style that works with your lifestyle and styling routine.

Step-by-Step Guide to Styling Short Haircuts for Fine Hair

Styling short haircuts for fine hair can be tricky, but with the right techniques, you can create volume and texture that lasts all day. Here's a step-by-step guide:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair. Apply a volumizing mousse or spray to the roots of your hair, and use your fingers to work it through.
  2. Blow-dry your hair upside down, using a round brush to add volume to the roots.
  3. Once your hair is dry, use a curling iron or flat iron to create waves or curls. This will help to add texture and volume to your hair.
  4. Finish with a spritz of hairspray to hold your style in place.

Tips for Maintaining Short Haircuts for Fine Hair

To keep your short haircut looking its best, here are a few tips:

  • Get regular trims to keep your haircut looking fresh and healthy.
  • Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to help add texture to your hair.
  • Avoid heavy styling products, as they can weigh down your hair and make it look flat.
  • Consider adding highlights or lowlights to your hair to create depth and dimension.

FAQ

What is the best short haircut for fine hair?

The best short haircut for fine hair depends on your face shape, hair texture, and personal style. Some popular short haircuts for fine hair include pixie cuts, bobs, and layered cuts.

Can I still have a short haircut if I have fine hair?

Absolutely! In fact, short haircuts can be a great way to add volume and texture to fine hair. The key is to choose a haircut that works with your hair type and face shape, and to style it in a way that flatters your features.

How do I add volume to my short haircut?

To add volume to your short haircut, try using a volumizing mousse or spray at the roots, blow-drying your hair upside down, and using a curling iron or flat iron to create waves or curls.

Hair Care Tips for Fine Hair

To keep your fine hair looking its best, here are a few hair care tips:

  • Wash your hair every other day to avoid stripping it of its natural oils.
  • Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to help add texture to your hair.
  • Avoid using heavy styling products, as they can weigh down your hair and make it look flat.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair looking healthy and prevent split ends.
  • Avoid over-brushing your hair, as this can cause breakage and make your hair look flat.

With these tips and tricks, you can rock a trendy and stylish short haircut for fine hair in 2019. Remember to choose a haircut that works with your hair type and face shape, and don't be afraid to experiment with different styles and techniques to create volume and texture.
