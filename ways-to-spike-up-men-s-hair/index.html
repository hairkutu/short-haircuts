Ways To Spike Up Men s Hair A Complete Guide

Written by Aprilia Mar 16, 2023 · 9 min read

If you're tired of your plain and boring hairstyle, spiking up your hair can add an edgy and stylish touch to your look. Whether you have short, medium, or long hair, there are plenty of ways to spike it up that can work for any hair type. In this article, we'll guide you through the steps of spiking up men's hair and give you some tips on how to keep your spiky hair looking great.

Table of Contents

  • How to Spike Up Men's Hair
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Spiking Up Men's Hair
  • Frequently Asked Questions
  • Hair Care for Spiky Hair

How to Spike Up Men's Hair

Spiking up men's hair is a simple process that can be done in a few easy steps. Here are the basic steps:

  1. Start with clean, towel-dried hair
  2. Apply a styling product to your hair (such as gel, wax, or pomade)
  3. Use your fingers or a comb to create spikes in your hair
  4. Finish with a hairspray to hold the spikes in place

Depending on your hair type and the look you want to achieve, you can adjust the amount of product and the size and shape of your spikes. Read on for a more detailed guide on spiking up men's hair.

Step-by-Step Guide

Follow these steps to create spiky hair:

Step 1: Wash and Dry Your Hair

Before you start spiking up your hair, make sure it is clean and dry. Wash your hair with shampoo and conditioner and towel-dry it until it is slightly damp. Avoid using a hairdryer, as it can make your hair too dry and difficult to style.

Step 2: Apply Styling Product

Apply a small amount of styling product to your hair. The type of product you use will depend on your hair type and the look you want to achieve. For a strong hold and a shiny finish, use a gel or pomade. If you want a more natural look with a matte finish, try a wax or clay. Rub the product between your palms and distribute it evenly throughout your hair.

Step 3: Create Spikes

Use your fingers or a comb to create spikes in your hair. Start at the front of your hairline and work your way back, creating spikes of varying lengths and sizes. For a more dramatic look, create spikes that stand straight up. For a more subtle look, create short, textured spikes that lay flat against your head.

Step 4: Finish with Hairspray

To keep your spikes in place all day, finish with a strong-hold hairspray. Hold the can about 8 inches away from your head and spray evenly over your hair. This will help to set your spikes in place and prevent them from falling flat or losing their shape.

Tips for Spiking Up Men's Hair

Here are some tips to help you achieve the best results when spiking up your hair:

  • Start with clean, towel-dried hair so that the styling product can be absorbed better.
  • Don't use too much product, as it can weigh down your hair and make it look greasy.
  • Experiment with different types of styling products to find the one that works best for your hair type and the look you want to achieve.
  • If you have thin or fine hair, avoid using heavy products that can make your hair look flat or greasy. Instead, use a lightweight product that will add texture and volume to your hair.
  • If you have curly or wavy hair, use a product that will help to define your curls and waves while still allowing you to create spikes.

Frequently Asked Questions

Here are some answers to common questions about spiking up men's hair:

What type of styling product should I use to spike up my hair?

The type of styling product you use will depend on your hair type and the look you want to achieve. For a strong hold and a shiny finish, use a gel or pomade. If you want a more natural look with a matte finish, try a wax or clay.

How much styling product should I use?

Use a small amount of styling product, about the size of a dime, and add more if needed. Avoid using too much product, as it can weigh down your hair and make it look greasy.

Can I create spikes with long hair?

Yes, you can create spikes with long hair. Use a strong-hold styling product and create spikes of varying lengths and sizes. For a more natural look, create textured spikes that lay flat against your head.

Hair Care for Spiky Hair

Here are some tips for taking care of your spiky hair:

  • Wash your hair every other day to prevent it from becoming too dry or damaged from the styling product.
  • Avoid using a hairdryer, as it can make your hair too dry and difficult to style.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy and moisturized.
  • Avoid touching your hair too much throughout the day, as this can cause your spikes to lose their shape.

Spiking up men's hair is a fun and easy way to add some style and personality to your look. With the right styling product and a little practice, you can create a variety of spiky hairstyles that work for any occasion. Just remember to take care of your hair and experiment with different techniques to find the look that works best for you.
