What Hairstyle Attracts Guys The Most A Guide For Women

Written by Frank Jul 22, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Are you wondering what hairstyle attracts guys the most? Look no further as we have got you covered. In this article, we will discuss the different hairstyles that men find attractive and how you can achieve them. From short hair to long locks, we have it all covered. So, let us dive in!

Description

There is nothing more attractive than a woman who knows how to style her hair. Men love women who look confident and put together, and the right hairstyle can help you achieve just that. It is crucial to understand that not all hairstyles suit everyone, and it is essential to find a style that complements your face shape and personality.

How to

The first step in finding the perfect hairstyle is to determine your face shape. There are six main face shapes, including oval, round, heart, square, diamond, and oblong. Once you have identified your face shape, you can start exploring different hairstyles that suit you.

Oval Face Shape

Women with oval-shaped faces are lucky as they can pull off almost any hairstyle. The most popular hairstyles for oval faces include long layers, bobs, and pixie cuts.

Round Face Shape

For women with round faces, it is best to avoid hairstyles that add width to your face. Instead, opt for hairstyles that add height and length, such as long layers, side-swept bangs, and asymmetrical cuts.

Heart Face Shape

Women with heart-shaped faces look great with hairstyles that add volume to the lower half of the face. This can be achieved with chin-length bobs, side-swept bangs, and long layers.

Square Face Shape

For women with square faces, it is best to soften the angles with hairstyles that add softness and roundness to the face. This can be achieved with layered haircuts, side-swept bangs, and soft curls.

Diamond Face Shape

Women with diamond-shaped faces look great with hairstyles that add width to the forehead and jawline. This can be achieved with bobs, side-swept bangs, and layered haircuts.

Oblong Face Shape

Women with oblong faces should opt for hairstyles that add width to the face, such as chin-length bobs, side-swept bangs, and layered haircuts.

Step by Step

Now that you have identified your face shape and the hairstyle that suits you, it is time to learn how to achieve it. Here are some tips:

  1. Invest in a good quality hairbrush and comb
  2. Use the right hair products for your hair type
  3. Blow-dry your hair in the right direction
  4. Use a flat iron or curling iron to add texture and volume
  5. Apply hairspray to set your hairstyle in place

Tips

Here are some additional tips to keep in mind when choosing a hairstyle:

  • Consult with a hairstylist to find the right hairstyle for you
  • Consider your lifestyle and how much time you have to style your hair
  • Experiment with different hairstyles to find the one that suits you best
  • Accessorize your hair with hairbands, clips, and hairpins

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about hairstyles that attract guys:

What are the most popular hairstyles for women?

The most popular hairstyles for women include long layers, bobs, pixie cuts, and beachy waves.

Do guys prefer long or short hair on women?

It depends on the guy's preferences, but most men find long hair attractive on women.

How often should I get a haircut?

It is recommended to get a haircut every six to eight weeks to maintain healthy hair and prevent split ends.

Hair Care

Lastly, it is essential to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and shiny. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your hair with a gentle shampoo and conditioner
  • Avoid using hot tools too often
  • Apply a hair mask once a week to nourish your hair
  • Protect your hair from the sun and environmental damage

Overall, finding the hairstyle that attracts guys the most is all about finding the right style that complements your face shape and personality. Experiment with different hairstyles and take care of your hair to achieve that confident and put-together look that men find attractive.
