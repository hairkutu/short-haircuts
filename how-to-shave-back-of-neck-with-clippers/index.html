How To Shave Back Of Neck With Clippers A Step By Step Guide

Written by Luffy Apr 20, 2023 Â· 6 min read

If you're tired of paying someone else to shave the back of your neck, or simply want to learn how to do it yourself, then you've come to the right place. In this article, we'll teach you how to shave the back of your neck with clippers in a few easy steps.

Description

Shaving the back of your neck with clippers can be a bit tricky, especially if you're not used to doing it. However, with the right tools and technique, it can be done safely and efficiently.

How to Shave Back of Neck with Clippers

Step 1: Choose the right clippers

The first step is to choose the right clippers for the job. You'll want to look for a clipper that has a small, narrow blade that can easily reach the back of your neck. You can also use a trimmer or a shaver for this task, as long as it has a small attachment that can reach the back of your neck.

Step 2: Trim your hair

Before you begin shaving, it's important to trim your hair down to a manageable length. This will make it easier to shave and prevent your clippers from getting tangled in long hair. You can use a pair of scissors or a comb to trim your hair to the desired length.

Step 3: Prepare your skin

Next, you'll want to prepare your skin for shaving. Take a warm shower or use a warm towel to open up your pores and soften your skin. This will make it easier to shave and prevent irritation and razor burn.

Step 4: Apply shaving cream or gel

Once your skin is ready, apply a generous amount of shaving cream or gel to the back of your neck. Make sure to cover the entire area that you'll be shaving.

Step 5: Start shaving

Now it's time to start shaving. Hold your clippers with the blade facing downwards and gently glide them over your skin in the direction of your hair growth. Be sure to use light pressure and avoid going over the same area too many times.

Step 6: Rinse and repeat

After you've finished shaving, rinse off any excess shaving cream or gel and check to make sure you didn't miss any spots. If you did, simply apply more shaving cream and repeat the process until you're satisfied with the results.

Tips

  • Use a mirror to help you see the back of your neck.
  • Take your time and be patient.
  • Don't press too hard with the clippers or you could end up with nicks and cuts.

FAQ

1. Can I use a regular razor to shave the back of my neck?

Yes, you can use a regular razor to shave the back of your neck, but it can be more difficult to do and may increase your risk of cuts and razor burn.

2. How often should I shave the back of my neck?

This will depend on how quickly your hair grows and how often you want to maintain your look. Generally, once a week is a good rule of thumb.

Hair Care

After shaving the back of your neck, it's important to take care of your skin to prevent irritation and ingrown hairs. Apply a moisturizer or aftershave lotion to soothe your skin and keep it hydrated.

Now that you know how to shave the back of your neck with clippers, you can save time and money by doing it yourself. With a little practice, you'll be a pro in no time!
