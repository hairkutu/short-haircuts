Shoulder Length Hairstyles For Over 50 And Overweight A Complete Guide

Written by Frank Apr 19, 2023 Â· 7 min read

If you're a woman over 50 and overweight, finding the perfect hairstyle can be a challenge. Your hair may be thinning, and you may have a round face that needs to be slimmed down. But don't worry, there are plenty of great shoulder length hairstyles that can help you look and feel your best. In this article, we'll go over everything you need to know about shoulder length hairstyles for over 50 and overweight, including how to choose the right style, tips for styling and caring for your hair, and more.

Table of Contents

  1. How to Choose the Right Style
  2. Step by Step Guide
  3. Tips for Styling and Caring for Your Hair
  4. FAQ
  5. Hair Care

How to Choose the Right Style

When choosing a shoulder length hairstyle, there are a few things you should keep in mind. First, consider your face shape. If you have a round face, you may want to go for a style that adds height at the crown and lengthens the face. If you have a square face, you may want to go for a style with layers that soften the jawline.

Second, consider your hair texture. If you have thinning hair, you may want to go for a style that adds volume and body. If you have thick hair, you may want to go for a style that thins it out a bit.

Finally, think about your personal style and what makes you feel confident and comfortable. Do you like bold, edgy styles, or do you prefer more classic, understated looks? There's a shoulder length hairstyle out there for everyone, so take the time to find the one that's right for you.

Step by Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide to creating a shoulder length hairstyle:

  1. Start by washing and conditioning your hair with products that are designed for your hair type.
  2. Towel dry your hair and comb through any tangles.
  3. Apply a heat protectant spray or cream to your hair to prevent damage from heat styling tools.
  4. Blow dry your hair using a round brush, lifting the hair at the roots to create volume.
  5. Once your hair is dry, use a curling iron or straightener to create the desired texture.
  6. Finish with a hairspray or styling cream to hold the style in place.

Tips for Styling and Caring for Your Hair

Here are some tips for styling and caring for your shoulder length hair:

  • Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to add body and thickness to thinning hair.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as this can cause damage and breakage.
  • Consider getting regular trims to keep your hair looking healthy and prevent split ends.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to nourish and hydrate your hair.
  • Try different styling products, such as mousse, texturizing spray, or sea salt spray, to create different looks.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about shoulder length hairstyles for over 50 and overweight:

What is the best shoulder length hairstyle for a round face?

A layered bob with side-swept bangs is a great option for a round face, as it adds height at the crown and lengthens the face.

How can I add volume to my thinning hair?

Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner, and consider getting a layered haircut to add volume and texture.

What is the best way to style shoulder length hair?

Use a round brush and blow dryer to create volume, and then use a curling iron or straightener to create the desired texture. Finish with a hairspray or styling cream to hold the style in place.

Hair Care

Caring for your shoulder length hair is important to keep it looking healthy and beautiful. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly with products that are designed for your hair type.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as this can cause damage and breakage.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to nourish and hydrate your hair.
  • Consider getting regular trims to keep your hair looking healthy and prevent split ends.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and wind by wearing a hat or scarf.

With these tips and ideas for shoulder length hairstyles, you can feel confident and beautiful no matter your age or body type. Remember, the key is to find a style that makes you feel comfortable and confident, and to take good care of your hair to keep it looking its best.
