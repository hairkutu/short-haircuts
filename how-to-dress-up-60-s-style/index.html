How To Dress Up 60 S Style

Written by William Jun 26, 2023 Â· 5 min read

If you're looking to add a bit of retro flair to your wardrobe, the 60's style is the perfect choice. With its bold prints, bright colors, and playful silhouettes, this fashion era can add a touch of fun to any outfit. In this blog post, we'll show you how to dress up 60's style with our step-by-step guide, tips, and hair care advice.

Description

The 60's style is characterized by bold prints, bright colors, and playful silhouettes. This era marked a significant shift in fashion, with women's clothing becoming more daring and experimental. Mini skirts, go-go boots, and mod dresses were all popular during this time, along with bold accessories like oversized sunglasses and statement jewelry.

How to Dress Up 60's Style

If you're ready to embrace the 60's style, here's how to do it:

Step 1: Start with the Basics

To dress up 60's style, you'll need to start with the basics. This means investing in a few key pieces, such as a shift dress, mini skirt, or wide-leg pants. Look for pieces with bold prints or bright colors to really capture the essence of the era.

Step 2: Add Accessories

No 60's outfit is complete without the right accessories. Consider adding oversized sunglasses, statement jewelry, or a colorful headscarf. You can also try wearing a pair of go-go boots or chunky heels to complete the look.

Step 3: Experiment with Hair and Makeup

The 60's were all about experimenting with hair and makeup. Consider wearing your hair in a bouffant, beehive, or pixie cut. For makeup, try winged eyeliner, bold eyeshadow, or a bright lip color.

Tips

Here are a few tips to keep in mind when dressing up 60's style:

  • Don't be afraid to mix and match bold prints and colors
  • Invest in a pair of go-go boots or chunky heels
  • Accessorize with oversized sunglasses, statement jewelry, or a colorful headscarf
  • Experiment with hair and makeup to really capture the essence of the era

FAQ

What were some popular 60's styles?

Some popular 60's styles included mini skirts, shift dresses, go-go boots, and mod dresses.

What types of accessories were popular in the 60's?

Oversized sunglasses, statement jewelry, and colorful headscarves were all popular accessories in the 60's.

What types of hairstyles were popular in the 60's?

Bouffants, beehives, and pixie cuts were all popular hairstyles in the 60's.

Hair Care

If you're going for a 60's look, it's important to pay attention to your hair. Here are a few tips:

  • Invest in a high-quality hairspray to keep your hair in place
  • Consider using a volumizing mousse or spray to add volume to your hair
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to gently detangle your hair
  • Consider using a hair mask or deep conditioner to keep your hair healthy and shiny

With these tips and tricks, you'll be able to dress up 60's style in no time. Whether you're going for a bold and colorful look or a more subtle nod to the era, the 60's style is sure to add a touch of fun to your wardrobe.
