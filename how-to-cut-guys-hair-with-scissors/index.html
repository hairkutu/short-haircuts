How To Cut Guys Hair With Scissors A Relaxed Guide

Written by Frank Jul 27, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Cutting hair at home has become increasingly popular in recent years, and with good reason. It's convenient, affordable, and can give you the freedom to experiment with different styles. However, cutting hair can also be intimidating, especially if you've never done it before. That's why we've put together this guide on how to cut guys hair with scissors. Below, we'll cover everything you need to know to get started, including how to prepare, step-by-step instructions, tips, FAQs, and hair care.

Table of Contents

  • How to Prepare
  • Step-by-Step Instructions
  • Tips
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care

How to Prepare

Before you get started cutting hair, it's important to gather all the necessary tools and prepare the space. Here's what you'll need:

  • Scissors: Look for a pair of sharp, high-quality scissors specifically designed for cutting hair.
  • Comb: A fine-toothed comb can help you part and section the hair.
  • Clippers (optional): Clippers can be helpful for trimming the hair around the neck and ears.
  • Cape or towel: A cape or towel can help protect the person's clothing from hair clippings.
  • Spray bottle: A spray bottle filled with water can help you wet the hair before cutting.
  • Mirror: A mirror can help you see the back of the person's head while cutting.

Once you have all your tools gathered, find a well-lit space with a comfortable chair for the person whose hair you'll be cutting. Make sure the chair is positioned near a mirror so you can see what you're doing.

Step-by-Step Instructions

Now that you're prepared, it's time to get started. Follow these step-by-step instructions to cut guys hair with scissors:

  1. Wet the hair: Use the spray bottle to wet the person's hair. This will make it easier to cut and prevent flyaways.
  2. Section the hair: Use the comb to part the hair into sections. Start by dividing the hair at the crown of the head and then work your way down to the neck.
  3. Start cutting: Hold a section of hair between your fingers and use the scissors to trim the ends. Start by cutting less than you think you need to and then gradually trim more as needed.
  4. Continue cutting: Work your way around the person's head, section by section, until you've trimmed all the hair.
  5. Trim around the neck and ears: Use clippers or scissors to trim the hair around the neck and ears. Be careful not to cut too much or nick the skin.
  6. Check for balance: Once you've finished cutting, check the person's hair in the mirror to make sure it's even on both sides.

Tips

Cutting hair can be tricky, but these tips can help make the process easier:

  • Start with small cuts and work your way up to larger ones.
  • Don't be afraid to use multiple mirrors to see the person's hair from different angles.
  • Take breaks if you need to. Cutting hair can be tiring, especially if you're not used to it.
  • Invest in high-quality scissors. Cheap scissors can make cutting hair more difficult and lead to uneven cuts.
  • Watch online tutorials to get a better understanding of cutting techniques.

FAQs

Can I cut my own hair with scissors?

Yes, you can cut your own hair with scissors, but it can be more challenging than cutting someone else's hair. Make sure you have a mirror positioned so you can see the back of your head.

How often should I cut my hair?

Most men need a haircut every 4-6 weeks to maintain their style and keep their hair looking healthy.

What if I make a mistake while cutting hair?

If you make a mistake while cutting hair, don't panic. You can always trim a little more to even out the cut. If the mistake is more severe, consider taking the person to a professional stylist to fix the cut.

Hair Care

Cutting hair is just the first step. To keep the person's hair looking healthy and styled, it's important to take care of it properly. Here are some hair care tips:

  • Wash hair regularly with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as they can damage the hair.
  • Use a hair mask or deep conditioner once a week to nourish the hair.
  • Trim the hair every 4-6 weeks to maintain the style and prevent split ends.

With these tips, you should be able to cut guys hair with scissors confidently and effectively. Remember to take your time, be patient, and don't be afraid to ask for help if you need it. Happy cutting!
