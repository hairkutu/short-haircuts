How To Cut A Man s Long Hair With Scissors

Description

Long hair can be a tricky beast to tame, especially if you're trying to cut it yourself. But with a little bit of practice and some patience, you can trim your own long hair with scissors. In this article, we'll walk you through the steps of cutting a man's long hair with scissors, from start to finish.

How to

Before you start cutting, make sure you have the right tools. You'll need a pair of sharp scissors, a comb, and some hair clips to keep your hair out of the way. If you're cutting your hair for the first time, it's a good idea to start with small snips and work your way up.

Step by Step

Step 1: Start by washing and drying your hair. This will make it easier to cut and ensure that you're working with clean hair.

Step 2: Use your comb to separate your hair into sections. Start by dividing your hair in half, from ear to ear. Clip the top section out of the way.

Step 3: Take a small section of hair from the bottom section and comb it straight. Hold the hair between your fingers and cut off the desired length. Repeat this process for each section of hair.

Step 4: Once you've finished cutting the bottom section, unclip the top section and repeat the process. Remember to comb each section straight before cutting it.

Step 5: After you've finished cutting both sections, check the length of your hair in the mirror. If you notice any uneven spots, go back and trim them.

Tips

  • Start with small snips and work your way up to avoid cutting off too much hair.
  • Use hair clips to keep your hair out of the way while you're cutting.
  • If you're not sure how much hair to cut off, start by cutting off a little bit at a time.
  • Take breaks if you need to. Cutting your own hair can be tiring and time-consuming.

FAQ

Q: How often should I cut my hair?

A: It depends on your hair type and how quickly it grows. Generally, it's a good idea to trim your hair every 6-8 weeks to keep it looking healthy.

Q: Can I use regular scissors to cut my hair?

A: You can, but it's not recommended. Regular scissors are not as sharp as hair cutting scissors, which can lead to split ends and uneven cuts.

Hair Care

After you've finished cutting your hair, it's important to take care of it to keep it looking healthy. Here are some tips:

  • Use a shampoo and conditioner that are suited to your hair type.
  • Avoid using hot tools like blow dryers and straighteners too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair looking healthy.
  • Eat a balanced diet that includes plenty of vitamins and minerals to promote healthy hair growth.

With these tips and tricks, you'll be able to successfully cut your own long hair with scissors. Remember to start small and work your way up, and don't be afraid to ask for help if you need it. Happy cutting!
