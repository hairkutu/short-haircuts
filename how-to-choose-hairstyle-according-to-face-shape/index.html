How To Choose Hairstyle According To Face Shape

Written by Petter Jul 23, 2023 · 7 min read

Choosing the right hairstyle can be a daunting task, especially when you consider your face shape. The right hairstyle can enhance your best features and make you feel confident and beautiful. However, if you choose the wrong hairstyle, it can highlight your flaws and make you feel self-conscious. In this article, we will guide you on how to choose the perfect hairstyle according to your face shape.

How to Determine Your Face Shape

Before we dive into the different hairstyles, it's important to determine your face shape. To determine your face shape, you will need to measure your forehead, cheekbones, and jawline.

Here's how you can determine your face shape:

  • Oval Face Shape: Forehead, cheekbones, and jawline are roughly the same width.
  • Round Face Shape: Forehead and jawline are rounded, and cheekbones are the widest part of the face.
  • Square Face Shape: Forehead, cheekbones, and jawline are roughly the same width, with a strong jawline.
  • Heart Face Shape: Forehead is wider than the cheekbones, and the cheekbones are wider than the jawline.
  • Diamond Face Shape: Forehead and jawline are narrow, with wide cheekbones.

Hairstyles for Oval Face Shape

If you have an oval face shape, you're in luck! This face shape is considered the most versatile and can pull off a variety of hairstyles.

Here are some hairstyles that look great on oval face shapes:

  • Long layers
  • Pixie cut
  • Bob haircut
  • Side-swept bangs

Hairstyles for Round Face Shape

If you have a round face shape, you want to create the illusion of length to balance out your face.

Here are some hairstyles that look great on round face shapes:

  • Long layers
  • Side-swept bangs
  • Pixie cut with volume on top
  • Bob haircut with volume on top

Hairstyles for Square Face Shape

If you have a square face shape, you want to soften your strong jawline and forehead.

Here are some hairstyles that look great on square face shapes:

  • Layered bob
  • Soft, side-swept bangs
  • Long, loose waves
  • Pixie cut with side-swept bangs

Hairstyles for Heart Face Shape

If you have a heart face shape, you want to balance out your forehead and narrow chin.

Here are some hairstyles that look great on heart face shapes:

  • Long layers with side-swept bangs
  • Bob haircut
  • Pixie cut with volume on top
  • Side ponytail

Hairstyles for Diamond Face Shape

If you have a diamond face shape, you want to create the illusion of width at your forehead and jawline.

Here are some hairstyles that look great on diamond face shapes:

  • Long layers
  • Bob haircut
  • Pixie cut
  • Full bangs

Tips for Choosing the Right Hairstyle

Here are some tips to keep in mind when choosing the right hairstyle:

  • Consider your hair texture
  • Consider your lifestyle
  • Bring a picture to your hair appointment
  • Consult with your hairstylist
  • Experiment with different styles

FAQ

Q: Can I pull off a certain hairstyle even if it doesn't match my face shape?

A: Of course! These guidelines are just suggestions, and you should wear whatever hairstyle makes you feel confident and beautiful.

Q: Can hair color affect the way my face shape looks?

A: Yes, hair color can affect the way your face shape looks. For example, lighter hair colors can make your face appear wider, while darker hair colors can make your face appear narrower.

Hair Care Tips

No matter what hairstyle you choose, it's important to take care of your hair. Here are some hair care tips to keep in mind:

  • Wash your hair regularly
  • Use a conditioner
  • Avoid using heating tools too often
  • Get regular trims
  • Protect your hair from the sun

Choosing the right hairstyle can be a fun and exciting experience. By following these guidelines, you can find the perfect hairstyle that complements your face shape and makes you feel confident and beautiful.
