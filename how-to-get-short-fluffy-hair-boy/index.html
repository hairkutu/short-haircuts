How To Get Short Fluffy Hair For Boys

Written by Frank Sep 01, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Short fluffy hair is a popular hairstyle for boys. It is a versatile hairstyle that can be styled in many different ways. To achieve this look, you need to have the right tools and products. In this article, we will show you how to get short fluffy hair for boys step by step. We will also give you tips on how to maintain your hair and answer some frequently asked questions.

Table of Contents

  • How to Get Short Fluffy Hair
  • Step by Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

How to Get Short Fluffy Hair

The first step in getting short fluffy hair is to have the right haircut. You should ask your barber to cut your hair short on the sides and back and leave the top longer. This will give you enough hair to work with when styling your hair. You should also ask your barber to add layers to your hair to create volume and texture.

After getting the right haircut, you need to choose the right products for your hair. You should use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to add volume to your hair. You should also use a styling product like a pomade or a wax to add texture to your hair. These products will help you achieve the short fluffy hair look.

Step by Step

Here are the steps to get short fluffy hair:

  1. Start with clean, towel-dried hair.
  2. Apply a volumizing mousse to your hair and work it through with your fingers.
  3. Blow-dry your hair using a round brush. Pull the brush through your hair, lifting it at the roots to add volume.
  4. Apply a small amount of pomade or wax to your fingertips and work it through your hair, focusing on the ends to add texture.
  5. Use your fingers to style your hair into a short fluffy style.
  6. Finish with a hairspray to hold your style in place.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you achieve the short fluffy hair look:

  • Use a wide-toothed comb to detangle your hair before styling.
  • Blow-dry your hair upside down to add more volume.
  • Experiment with different styling products to find the one that works best for your hair.
  • Don't use too much product, as it can weigh down your hair and make it look greasy.
  • Get regular haircuts to maintain your short fluffy hair look.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about short fluffy hair:

What is short fluffy hair?

Short fluffy hair is a hairstyle for boys that is short on the sides and back and longer on the top. It is styled to look fluffy and textured.

What products should I use for short fluffy hair?

You should use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner, a volumizing mousse, and a styling product like a pomade or wax.

How do I style short fluffy hair?

You can style short fluffy hair by blow-drying your hair with a round brush to add volume, and then using your fingers to add texture with a styling product.

Hair Care

To maintain your short fluffy hair look, you should follow a good hair care routine. You should wash your hair regularly with a volumizing shampoo and conditioner. You should also use a leave-in conditioner to keep your hair hydrated. Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as it can damage your hair and make it look dull. Get regular haircuts to keep your hair looking healthy and stylish.

Follow these tips and steps, and you will have short fluffy hair in no time. Remember to take care of your hair and experiment with different styling products to find the one that works best for you. With the right tools and products, you can achieve any hairstyle you want.
