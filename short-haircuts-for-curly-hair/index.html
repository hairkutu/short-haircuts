Short Haircuts For Curly Hair The Ultimate Guide

Written by William Apr 27, 2023 · 7 min read

Are you tired of your long, curly locks and ready for a change? Look no further than short haircuts for curly hair! Not only are they stylish and low-maintenance, but they can also enhance your natural curls and give you a new look. In this guide, we’ll cover everything you need to know about short haircuts for curly hair, including how to choose the right style, how to maintain it, and more.

Table of Contents

  • How to Choose the Right Short Haircut for Curly Hair
  • Step-by-Step Guide to Cutting Curly Hair
  • Tips for Styling Short Curly Hair
  • FAQs About Short Haircuts for Curly Hair
  • How to Take Care of Short Curly Hair

How to Choose the Right Short Haircut for Curly Hair

Before you head to the salon, it’s important to choose the right short haircut for your curly hair. Consider your face shape, hair texture, and personal style when selecting a cut. Here are some popular short haircuts for curly hair:

Pixie Cut

The pixie cut is a classic short haircut that works well for curly hair. It’s low maintenance and can enhance your natural curls. This cut is ideal for oval or heart-shaped faces.

Bob Cut

The bob cut is a versatile short haircut that can be customized for curly hair. You can opt for a chin-length bob or a longer bob that falls just above your shoulders. This cut works well for all face shapes and hair textures.

Shag Cut

The shag cut is a layered haircut that works well for curly hair. It adds volume and texture to your curls and can be styled in different ways. This cut is ideal for round or square-shaped faces.

Step-by-Step Guide to Cutting Curly Hair

If you’re brave enough to cut your own curly hair, follow these steps:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair.
  2. Section your hair into four parts: top, sides, and back.
  3. Starting with the back section, trim your hair using sharp scissors. Cut small sections at a time and avoid cutting too much at once.
  4. Move on to the side sections, and finally, the top section.
  5. Once you’ve finished cutting, style your hair as desired.

Tips for Styling Short Curly Hair

Styling short curly hair can be a challenge, but with these tips, you’ll be a pro in no time:

  • Use a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer to enhance your natural curls.
  • Apply a leave-in conditioner or curl cream to define your curls and reduce frizz.
  • Don’t be afraid to experiment with different styles, such as braids or twists.
  • Invest in a silk or satin pillowcase to reduce frizz and breakage.

FAQs About Short Haircuts for Curly Hair

How often should I trim my short curly hair?

You should trim your hair every 6-8 weeks to maintain the shape of your haircut and prevent split ends.

Can I straighten my short curly hair?

Yes, you can straighten your short curly hair, but be sure to use a heat protectant and a quality flat iron to prevent damage.

How to Take Care of Short Curly Hair

Proper hair care is essential for maintaining healthy, beautiful curls. Here are some tips for taking care of your short curly hair:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to prevent dryness.
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it’s dry, as it can cause breakage and frizz.
  • Apply a deep conditioning treatment once a week to nourish your curls.
  • Avoid using hot tools on your hair too often, as it can cause damage.

With these tips and tricks, you’ll be rocking a fabulous short haircut for curly hair in no time. Whether you prefer a pixie cut or a bob cut, there’s a style that’s perfect for you. Remember to take care of your hair and have fun experimenting with different styles!
