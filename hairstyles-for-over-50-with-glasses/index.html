Hairstyles For Over 50 With Glasses

Written by Petter Jun 11, 2023 Â· 8 min read

If you're over 50 and wear glasses, finding a hairstyle that complements your glasses can be a challenge. But fear not, there are plenty of hairstyles that look great with glasses! In this article, we'll show you some of the best hairstyles for over 50 with glasses, and give you some tips on how to take care of your hair.

Description

When it comes to hairstyles for over 50 with glasses, there are a few things to keep in mind. Firstly, you want a hairstyle that complements your face shape and your glasses. Secondly, you want a hairstyle that is easy to maintain and style. And thirdly, you want a hairstyle that makes you feel confident and stylish.

How to Choose the Right Hairstyle

Choosing the right hairstyle can be a daunting task, but it doesn't have to be. Here are a few tips to help you choose the right hairstyle:

Consider Your Face Shape

Your face shape plays a big role in determining which hairstyle will suit you best. If you have a round face, go for a hairstyle that adds height to your hair. If you have a square face, go for a hairstyle that softens your jawline. And if you have an oval face, lucky you! You can pretty much pull off any hairstyle.

Choose a Style that Complements Your Glasses

Your glasses are a part of your look, so it's important to choose a hairstyle that complements them. If you have bold, statement glasses, go for a simple, understated hairstyle. If you have more subtle glasses, you can get away with a more daring hairstyle.

Think About Maintenance

You don't want a hairstyle that requires a lot of maintenance, especially if you're over 50. Go for a style that is easy to maintain and style, so you can spend less time on your hair and more time doing the things you love.

Step by Step Guide to Top Hairstyles for Over 50 with Glasses

Here are some of the top hairstyles for over 50 with glasses:

The Pixie Cut

The pixie cut is a classic hairstyle that looks great with glasses. It's easy to maintain and style, and it works well with most face shapes. To achieve this look, ask your stylist to cut your hair short on the sides and back, and leave it longer on top. Style with a little bit of product, and you're good to go!

The Bob

The bob is another classic hairstyle that looks great with glasses. It's a little longer than the pixie cut, but still easy to maintain and style. To achieve this look, ask your stylist to cut your hair to chin length, and add some layers for texture. Style with a little bit of product, and you're ready to rock those glasses!

The Shag

The shag is a fun, layered hairstyle that looks great with glasses. It's a little more daring than the pixie cut or the bob, but it's still easy to maintain and style. To achieve this look, ask your stylist to add layers to your hair, starting at chin length. Style with a little bit of product, and you're good to go!

Tips for Taking Care of Your Hair

Here are a few tips for taking care of your hair:

Use a Good Shampoo and Conditioner

Using a good shampoo and conditioner is essential for keeping your hair healthy and shiny. Look for products that are specifically designed for your hair type, and avoid products that contain harsh chemicals.

Avoid Heat Styling

Heat styling can damage your hair, so it's best to avoid it as much as possible. If you do need to use heat styling tools, use a heat protectant spray to protect your hair from damage.

Get Regular Trims

Getting regular trims is essential for keeping your hair healthy and preventing split ends. Aim to get a trim every six to eight weeks, depending on how fast your hair grows.

FAQs

What Hairstyles are Best for Over 50 with Glasses?

The best hairstyles for over 50 with glasses are hairstyles that complement your face shape and your glasses, and are easy to maintain and style. Some of the top hairstyles for over 50 with glasses include the pixie cut, the bob, and the shag.

How Often Should I Get a Haircut?

You should aim to get a haircut every six to eight weeks, depending on how fast your hair grows. Regular haircuts are essential for keeping your hair healthy and preventing split ends.

Conclusion

Hairstyles for over 50 with glasses don't have to be boring or frumpy. With the right hairstyle and a few tips for hair care, you can look and feel confident and stylish at any age. So go ahead and try out a new hairstyle today!
