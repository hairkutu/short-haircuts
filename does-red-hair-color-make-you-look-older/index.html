Does Red Hair Color Make You Look Older

Written by William Mar 22, 2023 Â· 7 min read

If you're thinking about coloring your hair red, you may have heard the old adage that red hair makes you look older. But is it true? In this article, we'll explore whether or not red hair color really does make you look older, and we'll provide some tips for maintaining red hair to keep it looking youthful and vibrant.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to
  • Step by step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

Red hair has always been associated with youthfulness and vitality, but many people believe that red hair color can actually make you look older. Some people think that red hair color emphasizes wrinkles and fine lines, making them more noticeable. Others believe that red hair color simply looks more mature than other hair colors.

So, does red hair color really make you look older? The short answer is: it depends. There are a few factors that can influence how red hair color looks on an individual, including skin tone, hair texture, and the shade of red hair color chosen.

How to

If you're considering coloring your hair red but are concerned about looking older, there are a few things you can do to ensure that your red hair looks youthful and vibrant:

  • Choose a shade of red that complements your skin tone. If you have fair skin, a bright, fiery red may look too harsh and emphasize any redness or blemishes in your skin. A softer, strawberry blonde or auburn may be a better choice. On the other hand, if you have darker skin, a deeper, richer red may look more flattering.
  • Consider adding highlights or lowlights to your red hair color. This can add depth and dimension to your hair, making it look more youthful and vibrant.
  • Keep your hair healthy and well-maintained. Healthy, shiny hair always looks more youthful than dull, damaged hair. Make sure to use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner, and consider using a hair mask or oil treatment once a week.

Step by step

If you're coloring your hair red at home, follow these steps to ensure a youthful, vibrant result:

  1. Choose a shade of red that complements your skin tone. Look for a shade that is labeled "warm" or "golden" if you have fair skin, or "rich" or "burgundy" if you have darker skin.
  2. Prepare your hair by washing it with a clarifying shampoo to remove any product buildup.
  3. Apply the hair color according to the instructions on the packaging, making sure to saturate every strand of hair evenly.
  4. Leave the hair color on for the recommended amount of time, then rinse it out thoroughly.
  5. Condition your hair with a moisturizing conditioner, then rinse it out.

Tips

Here are a few additional tips for maintaining red hair color:

  • Avoid exposing your hair to direct sunlight as much as possible, as this can cause your color to fade.
  • Use a color-safe shampoo and conditioner to help preserve your hair color.
  • Consider using a color-depositing hair mask or treatment once a week to help maintain the vibrancy of your red hair color.

FAQ

Does red hair color make you look older?

As we mentioned earlier, it depends. Red hair color can emphasize wrinkles and fine lines in some individuals, but it can also look youthful and vibrant on others. Choosing the right shade of red and keeping your hair healthy and well-maintained can help ensure a youthful result.

What is the best shade of red for fair skin?

For fair skin, a soft, strawberry blonde or auburn can be a flattering choice. Avoid bright, fiery reds, as they can emphasize any redness or blemishes in your skin.

Hair Care

Maintaining red hair color requires a bit of extra care and attention. Here are a few tips for keeping your red hair looking healthy and vibrant:

  • Use a color-safe shampoo and conditioner to help preserve your hair color.
  • Avoid washing your hair every day, as this can cause your color to fade more quickly.
  • Use a hair mask or oil treatment once a week to keep your hair moisturized and healthy.
  • Avoid exposing your hair to direct sunlight as much as possible, as this can cause your color to fade.

With a little bit of care and attention, red hair can be a youthful and vibrant choice for any age.
