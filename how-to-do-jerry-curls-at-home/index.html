How To Do Jerry Curls At Home Step By Step Guide

Written by Bardi Sep 12, 2023 · 6 min read
How to Do Jerry Curls at Home - Step by Step Guide | Hair Care Tips | FAQ

If you want to achieve the iconic hairstyle of the 80s, then jerry curls might just be the perfect fit for you. Not only does it look stylish, but it’s also low-maintenance and easy to do at home. In this article, we’ll guide you through the step-by-step process of how to do jerry curls at home.

How to Do Jerry Curls at Home

Before we get started, you’ll need to gather some supplies:

  • Jerry curl activator
  • Wide-tooth comb
  • Shower cap
  • Hair clips
  • Hair moisturizer
  • Hair gel
  • Soft-bristle brush

Step by Step Guide

Follow these simple steps to achieve perfect jerry curls:

Step 1: Wash Your Hair

Start by washing your hair with a gentle shampoo and conditioner. Make sure to rinse thoroughly to remove any excess product.

Step 2: Apply Jerry Curl Activator

Apply the jerry curl activator generously to your damp hair, using your fingers or a wide-tooth comb to distribute it evenly.

Step 3: Comb Your Hair

Using a wide-tooth comb, gently comb your hair from the roots to the ends to detangle any knots or tangles.

Step 4: Cover with a Shower Cap

Cover your hair with a shower cap and leave the activator in for at least 30 minutes to an hour, depending on your hair type and desired curl intensity.

Step 5: Remove the Shower Cap and Style

Remove the shower cap and gently pat your hair dry with a towel. Apply hair moisturizer and gel to your hair, then use a soft-bristle brush to style your curls.

Tips for Perfect Jerry Curls

Here are some tips to ensure your jerry curls look their best:

  • Use a wide-tooth comb to prevent breakage and damage to your hair.
  • Don’t overuse hair products, as this can weigh down your curls and make them look greasy.
  • Use a soft-bristle brush to style your curls, as this will help to define them and give them a natural look.

FAQs

Q: How long do jerry curls last?

A: Jerry curls can last up to 8 weeks with proper maintenance and care.

Q: Can I do jerry curls on relaxed hair?

A: Yes, you can do jerry curls on relaxed hair. However, it’s important to note that using too many chemicals on your hair can cause damage and breakage.

Q: Can I sleep with jerry curls?

A: Yes, you can sleep with jerry curls. Simply wrap your hair in a satin scarf or bonnet to protect your curls while you sleep.

Hair Care Tips for Jerry Curls

Here are some hair care tips to keep your jerry curls looking their best:

  • Moisturize your hair regularly to prevent dryness and breakage.
  • Avoid using heat on your hair, as this can cause damage and breakage.
  • Protect your hair while you sleep by using a satin scarf or bonnet.

Now that you know how to do jerry curls at home, it’s time to rock this iconic hairstyle with confidence. With a little practice and the right products, you’ll have perfect jerry curls in no time.
