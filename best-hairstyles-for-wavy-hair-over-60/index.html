Best Hairstyles For Wavy Hair Over 60

Written by William Aug 17, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Are you in your 60s and have wavy hair? Do you want to know the best hairstyles that can make you look youthful and stylish? You're in the right place. In this article, we will discuss the best hairstyles for wavy hair over 60. Let's get started.

Description

As you age, your hair texture and quality change. Your hair becomes thinner, drier, and more fragile. Wavy hair is no exception. But that doesn't mean you can't have a fabulous hairstyle that shows off your natural waves. The key is to find a hairstyle that complements your face shape, hair texture, and lifestyle. With the right hairstyle, you can look and feel confident and beautiful.

How to

Choosing the best hairstyle for wavy hair over 60 requires some research and experimentation. You need to consider your face shape, hair texture, and lifestyle. You can get inspiration from fashion magazines, online tutorials, or your hairstylist. It's also a good idea to bring a photo of the hairstyle you like to your hairstylist, who can advise you on whether it suits you. Here are some tips on how to choose the best hairstyle for wavy hair over 60:

Step by Step

  • Step 1: Determine your face shape
  • Step 2: Assess your hair texture and thickness
  • Step 3: Consider your lifestyle and personal style
  • Step 4: Get inspiration from fashion magazines or online tutorials
  • Step 5: Consult your hairstylist for advice and recommendations

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when choosing the best hairstyle for wavy hair over 60:

  • Opt for a layered haircut to add volume and movement to your hair
  • Consider getting bangs to frame your face and soften your features
  • Avoid hairstyles that require a lot of maintenance or styling
  • Choose a hair color that complements your skin tone and enhances your natural hair color
  • Use hair products that nourish and protect your hair, such as a leave-in conditioner or hair oil

FAQ

What are the best hairstyles for wavy hair over 60?

The best hairstyles for wavy hair over 60 are layered haircuts, long bobs, wavy shags, and pixie cuts. These hairstyles add volume, movement, and texture to your hair, making it look fuller and more youthful.

How do I choose the right hairstyle for my face shape?

You can determine your face shape by measuring your forehead, cheekbones, and jawline. Once you know your face shape, you can choose a hairstyle that flatters it. For example, if you have a round face, you can opt for a layered haircut or a long bob that elongates your face.

What hair color should I choose for wavy hair over 60?

You should choose a hair color that complements your skin tone and enhances your natural hair color. If you have warm skin tones, you can opt for golden blonde, copper, or honey brown. If you have cool skin tones, you can try ash blonde, platinum, or burgundy.

Hair Care

To keep your wavy hair healthy and beautiful, you need to follow a hair care routine that suits your hair type and lifestyle. Here are some tips for hair care:

  • Wash your hair with a gentle shampoo and conditioner that nourish and hydrate your hair
  • Avoid using hot tools, such as blow dryers, flat irons, or curling irons, too often
  • Use a wide-tooth comb or a brush with natural bristles to detangle your hair
  • Apply a leave-in conditioner or hair oil to protect your hair from heat and environmental damage
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends and breakage

With these tips and ideas, you can find the best hairstyle for wavy hair over 60 that suits your face shape, hair texture, and lifestyle. Remember to consult your hairstylist for advice and recommendations. With the right hairstyle and hair care routine, you can look and feel confident and beautiful.
