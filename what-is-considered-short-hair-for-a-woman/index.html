What Is Considered Short Hair For A Woman

Written by Christine Mar 29, 2023 · 7 min read

Short hair can be a great way to change your look and add a bit of edge to your style. But what exactly is considered short hair for a woman? In this article, we’ll break down the different lengths that are typically considered short for women, as well as provide tips on how to style and care for short hair.

Description

Short hair is typically defined as hair that falls above the shoulders. However, there are varying degrees of short hair that women can choose from. Some women may opt for a pixie cut, which is a very short hairstyle that is typically cut close to the scalp. Others may opt for a bob, which is a shorter hairstyle that typically falls just above or below the chin. There are also variations of the bob, such as the lob (long bob) and the A-line bob.

How to

If you’re considering a short hairstyle, there are a few things to keep in mind. First, it’s important to choose a style that complements your face shape and features. A good hairstylist can help you find the perfect short hairstyle for your face. Next, it’s important to consider your lifestyle and how much time you’re willing to spend styling your hair each day. Short hair can be low maintenance, but some styles may require more upkeep.

Step by Step

If you’ve already made the decision to go short, here are some steps to follow:

  1. Choose a style that complements your face shape and features.
  2. Find a good hairstylist who has experience cutting short hair.
  3. Bring in pictures of hairstyles you like to show your stylist.
  4. Discuss your lifestyle and how much time you’re willing to spend styling your hair.
  5. After your haircut, ask your stylist for tips on how to style and maintain your new short hair.

Tips

Here are some tips for styling short hair:

  • Invest in quality hair products that are specifically designed for short hair.
  • Experiment with different styling techniques, such as using a flat iron or curling wand.
  • Consider adding layers or bangs to your short hairstyle to add texture and dimension.
  • Use a lightweight hairspray or styling cream to keep your hair in place.
  • Visit your hairstylist regularly for touch-ups and trims.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about short hair for women:

Q: Will short hair make my face look rounder?

A: It depends on the style you choose. Some short hairstyles, such as the pixie cut, can make a round face look even rounder. However, there are many short hairstyles that can complement round faces, such as the A-line bob.

Q: Can I still wear my hair up with a short hairstyle?

A: Yes! There are many ways to style short hair in an updo, such as using bobby pins or hair accessories.

Q: Will short hair make me look older?

A: Not necessarily. Short hair can be youthful and modern, depending on the style you choose.

Hair Care

Short hair requires regular maintenance to keep it looking its best. Here are some tips for caring for short hair:

  • Wash your hair every other day to prevent it from becoming too oily.
  • Use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and shiny.
  • Avoid using hot tools on a daily basis, as this can damage your hair.
  • Consider using a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair hydrated.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and heat by wearing a hat or using a heat protectant spray.

Now that you know what is considered short hair for a woman, you can confidently choose a style that works for you. Whether you opt for a pixie cut or a bob, short hair can be a fun and stylish way to switch up your look. Just remember to take good care of your hair to keep it looking its best!
