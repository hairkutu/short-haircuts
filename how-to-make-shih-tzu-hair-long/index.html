How To Make Shih Tzu Hair Long Tips And Tricks

Written by Frank May 28, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Are you a proud owner of a Shih Tzu? Do you want your furry friend to have long and luscious locks? If yes, then you have come to the right place. In this blog, we will provide you with some tips and tricks on how to make Shih Tzu hair long. Read on to learn more!

Description

Shih Tzus are known for their long and silky hair, which makes them look adorable. However, maintaining their hair can be a challenging task. It requires time, patience, and effort to keep their hair long and healthy. In this blog, we will guide you on how to make Shih Tzu hair long and healthy.

How to Make Shih Tzu Hair Long: Step by Step

Step 1: Regular Grooming

Grooming is essential to maintain the length and health of your Shih Tzu's hair. Regular grooming includes brushing, combing, and bathing. Brush your Shih Tzu's hair daily to prevent matting and tangling. Use a comb to remove any tangles or mats that may have formed. Bathe your Shih Tzu every two weeks to keep their hair clean and healthy.

Step 2: Proper Nutrition

Proper nutrition is essential for the growth of your Shih Tzu's hair. Feed your furry friend with high-quality dog food that contains the right nutrients, such as protein, vitamins, and minerals. Consult with your veterinarian to determine the best diet for your Shih Tzu.

Step 3: Regular Exercise

Regular exercise is not only essential for your Shih Tzu's overall health but also for the growth of their hair. Exercise helps to improve blood circulation, which promotes hair growth. Take your Shih Tzu for walks or play with them in the backyard to keep them active and healthy.

Tips for Making Shih Tzu Hair Long

Tip 1: Use the Right Shampoo

Use a high-quality shampoo that is specifically designed for Shih Tzus. This will help to keep their hair clean and healthy. Avoid using human shampoos as they can be too harsh for your furry friend's sensitive skin.

Tip 2: Use a Conditioner

Using a conditioner after shampooing can help to keep your Shih Tzu's hair soft and silky. It can also help to detangle any mats or tangles that may have formed. Use a conditioner that is specifically designed for dogs.

Tip 3: Trim Their Hair Regularly

Trimming your Shih Tzu's hair regularly can help to prevent split ends, which can lead to hair breakage. It can also help to maintain the length of their hair. Take your Shih Tzu to a professional groomer every six to eight weeks for a trim.

FAQ

Q1: How often should I groom my Shih Tzu's hair?

A: You should groom your Shih Tzu's hair daily to prevent matting and tangling.

Q2: Can I use human shampoo on my Shih Tzu?

A: No, you should not use human shampoo on your Shih Tzu. Use a shampoo that is specifically designed for dogs.

Q3: How often should I trim my Shih Tzu's hair?

A: You should take your Shih Tzu to a professional groomer every six to eight weeks for a trim.

Hair Care for Shih Tzu

Proper hair care is essential for the health and growth of your Shih Tzu's hair. Regular grooming, proper nutrition, and exercise are some of the things that you can do to keep your furry friend's hair long and healthy. Use the tips and tricks mentioned in this blog to make your Shih Tzu's hair long and luscious.
