How To Style Flat Asian Hair A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Bardi Jun 22, 2023 Â· 7 min read

If you have flat Asian hair, you may have experienced difficulty in styling your hair. Flat hair can be challenging to work with, but with the right techniques and products, you can achieve a voluminous and stylish look. In this article, we will provide you with the steps, tips, and hair care advice to help you style your flat Asian hair.

Table of Contents

  • How to Style Flat Asian Hair
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Styling Flat Asian Hair
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care for Flat Asian Hair

How to Style Flat Asian Hair

Styling flat Asian hair can be a challenge, but with the right techniques and products, you can add volume and texture to your hair. Here are some steps to help you style your flat Asian hair:

1. Start with Clean Hair

Before styling your hair, ensure that your hair is clean and free from any product build-up. Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner that is suitable for your hair type.

2. Use a Volumizing Product

Use a volumizing product, such as a mousse or root-lifting spray, to add volume to your hair. Apply the product to your roots and work it through your hair.

3. Blow Dry Your Hair

Blow dry your hair using a round brush to add volume and texture. Use the brush to lift your hair at the roots and blow dry in an upward direction.

4. Use a Texturizing Spray

Use a texturizing spray to add texture and hold to your hair. Spray the product onto your hair and use your fingers to scrunch and tousle your hair.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here is a step-by-step guide to help you style your flat Asian hair:

Step 1: Prep Your Hair

Start by washing your hair with a gentle shampoo and conditioner. Rinse your hair thoroughly and towel dry.

Step 2: Apply a Volumizing Product

Apply a volumizing product, such as a mousse or root-lifting spray, to your roots and work it through your hair.

Step 3: Blow Dry Your Hair

Blow dry your hair using a round brush to add volume and texture. Use the brush to lift your hair at the roots and blow dry in an upward direction.

Step 4: Use a Texturizing Spray

Use a texturizing spray to add texture and hold to your hair. Spray the product onto your hair and use your fingers to scrunch and tousle your hair.

Step 5: Finish with Hairspray

Finish your hairstyle with a light hairspray to hold your hair in place.

Tips for Styling Flat Asian Hair

Here are some tips to help you style your flat Asian hair:

  • Use a volumizing product to add volume to your hair
  • Blow dry your hair using a round brush to add texture and volume
  • Use a texturizing spray to add hold and texture to your hair
  • Try a new haircut that adds volume and texture to your hair, such as layers or a bob
  • Avoid using heavy products that can weigh down your hair, such as oils or serums

FAQ

Can I use a flat iron on my flat Asian hair?

Yes, you can use a flat iron on your flat Asian hair. However, it is important to use a heat protectant spray to prevent damage to your hair.

Can I get a perm to add volume to my flat Asian hair?

Yes, a perm can add volume and texture to your flat Asian hair. However, it is important to consult with a professional hairstylist to determine if a perm is suitable for your hair type.

Hair Care for Flat Asian Hair

Proper hair care is essential for maintaining healthy and voluminous hair. Here are some tips for caring for your flat Asian hair:

  • Wash your hair every other day to prevent over-washing and dryness
  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner that is suitable for your hair type
  • Avoid using hot tools, such as flat irons and curling irons, on a daily basis
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to nourish and hydrate your hair
  • Protect your hair from the sun and heat by wearing a hat or using a UV protectant spray

Styling flat Asian hair can be challenging, but with the right techniques and products, you can achieve a voluminous and stylish look. Follow these steps, tips, and hair care advice to help you style your flat Asian hair.
