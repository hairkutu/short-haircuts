Cutting My Own Hair Short Female A Step By Step Guide

Written by Luffy Apr 09, 2023 Â· 8 min read

Do you want to switch up your hairstyle but can't make it to the salon? Cutting your own hair short as a female may seem daunting, but with the right tools and techniques, it can be done. In this blog post, we'll cover everything you need to know about cutting your own hair short as a female, from how to do it to tips and tricks for maintaining your new 'do.

Description

Cutting your own hair short can be a liberating experience. You have complete control over your new look and can save money by skipping the salon. However, it's important to remember that cutting your own hair can be challenging, especially if you're not used to it. That's why it's essential to approach the process with patience and a willingness to learn.

How to Cut Your Own Hair Short as a Female

Cutting your own hair short as a female requires the right tools, techniques, and a little bit of courage. Here's how to do it:

1. Gather Your Tools

Before you start cutting, make sure you have all the necessary tools. You'll need:

  • Sharp hair cutting scissors
  • A comb
  • Hair clips to section your hair
  • A mirror (ideally, two mirrors so you can see the back of your head)

2. Choose Your Style

Decide on the length and style you want before you start cutting. Consider your face shape, hair type, and lifestyle when making your decision.

3. Wash and Dry Your Hair

Wash and dry your hair before you start cutting. This will help you see your natural hair texture and ensure a more accurate cut.

4. Section Your Hair

Use hair clips to section your hair. Start by sectioning off the top half of your hair and clipping it on top of your head.

5. Start Cutting

Begin cutting your hair in small sections, starting at the back of your head. Use your comb to hold the hair in place and cut with your scissors at an angle. Take your time and make sure you're cutting evenly.

6. Trim the Sides

Once you've cut the back of your hair, it's time to move on to the sides. Use your comb to hold the hair in place and cut at an angle towards your face. Remember to cut in small sections and take your time.

7. Trim the Top

Finally, it's time to trim the top of your hair. Section off the hair on top of your head and cut in small sections, holding the hair in place with your comb.

8. Check Your Work

Once you've finished cutting, check your work in the mirror. Make any necessary adjustments and trim any uneven pieces.

Tips for Cutting Your Own Hair Short

Here are some tips to help you achieve the perfect short haircut:

  • Start with small cuts and work your way up to a shorter length
  • Use hair clips to section your hair and make sure you're not cutting too much at once
  • Take breaks to check your work and make sure you're cutting evenly
  • Invest in a good pair of hair cutting scissors
  • Watch tutorials and practice before attempting a drastic cut

FAQ

Can I cut my own hair short as a female?

Yes, you can cut your own hair short as a female. However, it's important to approach the process with patience and a willingness to learn. Consider the length and style you want, gather the right tools, and take your time.

What tools do I need to cut my own hair short?

You'll need sharp hair cutting scissors, a comb, hair clips to section your hair, and a mirror (ideally, two mirrors so you can see the back of your head).

How do I choose the right style for my face shape?

Consider your face shape when choosing a new haircut. A good rule of thumb is to choose a style that complements your face shape. For example, if you have a round face, a short bob with layers can help elongate your face.

Hair Care After Cutting Your Own Hair Short

After cutting your own hair short, it's important to take care of your new 'do. Here are some tips for maintaining your short haircut:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo
  • Use a conditioner to keep your hair soft and manageable
  • Invest in hair products that work for your hair type
  • Get regular trims to maintain your haircut

Cutting your own hair short as a female can be a rewarding experience. With the right tools and techniques, you can achieve a stylish new look without leaving your home. Remember to be patient, take your time, and have fun!
