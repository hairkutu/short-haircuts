Fringes That Suit Round Faces A Complete Guide

Written by Daniel Jun 26, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Are you tired of your same old hairstyle? Do you have a round face and want to try out a new fringe? Look no further! In this article, we will discuss fringes that suit round faces, how to style them, and hair care tips to keep your new look fresh. Keep reading to find the perfect fringe for your face shape.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to Choose Fringes for Round Faces
  • Step-by-Step Guide to Styling Fringes for Round Faces
  • Tips for Maintaining Your Fringe
  • Frequently Asked Questions
  • Hair Care Tips for Fringed Hair

Description

Round faces are characterized by soft, full cheeks and a rounded chin. The key to finding the perfect fringe for a round face is to create angles and lengthen the face. Fringes that suit round faces include side-swept fringes, asymmetrical fringes, and textured fringes. These styles draw attention away from the roundness of the face and create the illusion of length.

How to Choose Fringes for Round Faces

When choosing a fringe for a round face, it's important to consider the length, shape, and texture of the fringe. The ideal length for a fringe on a round face is just below the eyebrows or at the cheekbone. Asymmetrical fringes and side-swept fringes are great options as they create angles and draw attention away from the roundness of the face. Textured fringes are also a good choice as they add volume and create movement.

Step-by-Step Guide to Styling Fringes for Round Faces

Styling a fringe for a round face is easy and can be done in just a few simple steps:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair.
  2. Use a round brush to blow-dry the fringe, directing it to the side.
  3. Use a flat iron to smooth out any kinks or waves.
  4. Apply a small amount of hair wax or pomade to the fringe to add texture and hold.
  5. Finish with hairspray to keep your fringe in place all day.

Tips for Maintaining Your Fringe

Keeping your fringe looking fresh and stylish requires some maintenance. Here are some tips to keep your fringe looking its best:

  • Trim your fringe every 4-6 weeks to keep it at the desired length.
  • Use dry shampoo to keep your fringe from getting oily and weighed down.
  • Avoid using too much product as this can make your fringe look greasy and heavy.
  • Invest in a good quality flat iron to keep your fringe straight and smooth.
  • Consider using a hair mask once a week to keep your hair healthy and hydrated.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Can I still have a fringe if I have a round face?

A: Absolutely! Fringes that suit round faces can create angles and lengthen the face, making it appear slimmer.

Q: Can I style my fringe differently every day?

A: Of course! Experiment with different textures, lengths, and angles to find the perfect style for you.

Hair Care Tips for Fringed Hair

Keeping your hair healthy and hydrated is essential for maintaining your fringe. Here are some hair care tips to keep your hair looking its best:

  • Wash your hair every 2-3 days to avoid stripping it of its natural oils.
  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and hydrated.
  • Avoid using hot tools on a daily basis as this can damage your hair.
  • Use a heat protectant spray when using hot tools to prevent damage.
  • Avoid using too much hairspray as this can make your hair stiff and crunchy.

With these tips and tricks, you're sure to find the perfect fringe for your round face. Don't be afraid to experiment with different styles and lengths until you find the perfect look for you. Happy styling!
