Will Dark Hair Make My Face Look Thinner The Truth Revealed

Written by Bardi May 25, 2023 Â· 7 min read

If you are looking for a simple yet effective way to make your face look thinner, changing your hair color can be a great option. However, many people are unsure if dark hair can actually help create the illusion of a slimmer face. In this blog post, we will explore this question in detail and provide you with all the information you need to make an informed decision.

Description

The shape of your face is determined by your bone structure and genetics, but your hairstyle and hair color can definitely have an impact on how it looks. Dark hair has long been associated with creating the illusion of a slimmer face, but is there any truth to this claim?

How to

The answer to this question is a bit complicated. On one hand, dark hair can definitely help create a more defined look and draw attention away from any facial features that you might want to downplay. However, the effect will vary depending on your complexion, face shape, and the specific shade of dark hair that you choose.

Step-by-Step

The key to making dark hair work for you is to choose a shade that complements your skin tone and face shape. In general, if you have fair skin, you will want to go for a warm, chocolate brown or a deep, espresso shade. If you have a medium or olive skin tone, you can opt for a cooler, more ash-toned brown or a rich, burgundy hue. And if you have dark skin, you can go for a jet black or a dark chocolate shade.

Once you have chosen the right shade of dark hair, it's important to consider the cut and style of your hair as well. Long, straight hair can elongate your face and make it look slimmer, while a shorter, layered cut can add volume and dimension to your hair.

Tips

Here are some additional tips to keep in mind when considering whether dark hair will make your face look thinner:

  • Consider getting highlights or lowlights to add dimension to your hair
  • Take care of your hair with regular trims and conditioning treatments to keep it healthy and shiny
  • Experiment with different hairstyles and haircuts to find the one that flatters your face shape the most

FAQ

Will dark hair make my face look thinner?

Dark hair can definitely help create the illusion of a slimmer face, but the effect will depend on your face shape, complexion, and the specific shade of dark hair that you choose.

What shade of dark hair should I choose?

Choose a shade that complements your skin tone and face shape. Fair skin should opt for warm chocolate brown or deep espresso, medium or olive skin can go for cooler ash-toned brown or rich burgundy, while dark skin can go for jet black or dark chocolate.

What haircut should I get with dark hair?

Long, straight hair can elongate your face and make it look slimmer, while a shorter, layered cut can add volume and dimension to your hair.

Hair Care

Regardless of whether you choose to go for dark hair or not, it's important to take good care of your hair to keep it looking healthy and shiny. Here are some tips to keep in mind:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo and conditioner
  • Avoid using hot tools like hair dryers and straighteners too often, as they can damage your hair
  • Eat a healthy diet rich in vitamins and minerals to promote healthy hair growth
  • Consider using hair masks or oils to nourish your hair and keep it looking its best

In conclusion, dark hair can definitely help create the illusion of a slimmer face, but the effect will depend on various factors. By choosing the right shade and cut of dark hair, and taking good care of your hair, you can achieve a look that flatters your face and makes you feel confident and beautiful.
