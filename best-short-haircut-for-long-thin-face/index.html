Best Short Haircut For Long Thin Face

Written by Eveline Apr 30, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Are you struggling to find the perfect haircut for your long and thin face? Look no further! In this article, we will discuss the best short haircuts that will flatter your features and enhance your natural beauty.

Description

Long and thin faces can be challenging to style, but with the right haircut, you can create the illusion of fuller and more defined features. Short haircuts are an excellent choice for long and thin faces as they can add volume and width to your face, giving you a more balanced and proportionate look.

How to

When choosing a short haircut for your long and thin face, there are a few things to keep in mind:

  • Choose a haircut that adds volume to the top of your head.
  • Avoid haircuts that are too short or too blunt, as they can make your face appear longer.
  • Consider adding layers to your haircut to create the illusion of fullness.
  • Consult with your hairstylist to find the best haircut for your face shape and hair type.

Step by Step

If you're ready to try a short haircut, here are some popular options to consider:

1. Bob Haircut

The bob is a classic haircut that never goes out of style. It's a versatile cut that can be tailored to suit your face shape and hair type. For long and thin faces, opt for a bob that is chin-length or longer and has layers to add volume and movement to your hair.

2. Pixie Cut

The pixie cut is a bold and edgy haircut that can be incredibly flattering for long and thin faces. It adds height to the top of your head, making your face appear shorter and more proportionate. If you're feeling adventurous, consider adding bangs to your pixie cut for an extra pop of style.

3. Layered Lob

The layered lob, or long bob, is a trendy and sophisticated haircut that can add volume and fullness to your hair. It's a great option for those who want a shorter haircut but don't want to go too short. The layers in the lob can create the illusion of fuller and thicker hair, making it a perfect choice for long and thin faces.

Tips

Here are some additional tips to keep in mind when styling your short haircut:

  • Use volumizing products to add volume and fullness to your hair.
  • Avoid hairstyles that are too sleek or flat, as they can make your face appear longer.
  • Experiment with different styling techniques, such as curling or teasing, to add texture and volume to your hair.
  • Visit your hairstylist regularly to maintain your haircut and keep it looking fresh and stylish.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about short haircuts for long and thin faces:

What is the best short haircut for a long and thin face?

The best short haircut for a long and thin face is one that adds volume and fullness to your hair. Consider a bob, pixie cut, or layered lob for a flattering and stylish look.

Can I add bangs to my short haircut?

Yes! Bangs can be a great addition to a short haircut, especially if you have a long and thin face. Consider side-swept bangs or wispy bangs to add texture and dimension to your hair.

How often should I visit my hairstylist?

To maintain your short haircut and keep it looking fresh and stylish, it's recommended to visit your hairstylist every 4-6 weeks.

Hair Care

To keep your short haircut looking healthy and beautiful, it's important to take good care of your hair. Here are some tips:

  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner that is appropriate for your hair type.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as they can damage your hair and make it look dry and brittle.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to nourish and hydrate your hair.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and harsh weather conditions by wearing a hat or using a protective hair spray.

With these tips and haircut options, you can find the perfect short haircut for your long and thin face. Experiment with different styles and techniques to find the look that best suits your personality and lifestyle. With the right haircut and hair care routine, you can achieve healthy, beautiful hair that enhances your natural beauty.
