Short Hair Makes You Look Skinnier The Ultimate Guide

Written by Robby May 26, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Are you looking for a way to look slimmer without going on a diet or hitting the gym? One simple solution is to try out a shorter hairstyle! Short hair can make you look skinnier by elongating your neck, emphasizing your bone structure, and drawing attention away from any excess weight around your face. In this blog post, we'll explore the various ways in which short hair can help you achieve a slimmer look.

Table of Contents

  • How To Get Short Hair
  • Step By Step Guide
  • Tips For Maintaining Short Hair
  • FAQs About Short Hair
  • Hair Care Tips For Short Hair

How To Get Short Hair

The first step in achieving a slimmer look with short hair is to actually get a short haircut. Before you chop off your locks, it's important to consider your face shape, hair texture, and personal style. A professional hairstylist can help you determine the best short haircut for your individual features and preferences.

Some popular short hairstyles that can make you look slimmer include the pixie cut, the bob, and the lob (long bob). These styles all have one thing in common: they fall above or around the jawline, which can help elongate the neck and create a more defined jawline.

Step By Step Guide

If you've decided to take the plunge and get a short haircut, here's a step-by-step guide to help you achieve the perfect look:

  1. Find a reputable hairstylist who specializes in short haircuts.
  2. Bring in pictures of short hairstyles that you like and discuss your options with your stylist.
  3. Decide on a length and style that complements your face shape and hair texture.
  4. Get your hair cut and styled by your hairstylist.
  5. Learn how to style your new haircut at home using the right products and tools.

Tips For Maintaining Short Hair

Once you've got your short haircut, it's important to take good care of it to ensure that it looks its best. Here are some tips for maintaining short hair:

  • Get regular trims to keep your hair looking neat and tidy.
  • Use hair products that are specifically designed for short hair.
  • Invest in high-quality styling tools, such as a good hair dryer and flat iron.
  • Experiment with different styling techniques to find the look that works best for you.

FAQs About Short Hair

Here are some frequently asked questions about short hair:

Q: Will short hair make my face look rounder?

A: Not necessarily. The right short haircut can actually help elongate your face and create a more defined jawline.

Q: Can I still style short hair in different ways?

A: Absolutely! Short hair is versatile and can be styled in many different ways, including straight, wavy, curly, and textured.

Q: Is short hair easy to maintain?

A: Yes! Short hair requires less maintenance than longer hair, and can be styled quickly and easily.

Hair Care Tips For Short Hair

Finally, here are some general hair care tips to keep in mind when you have short hair:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using too much heat when styling your hair, as this can damage your hair and cause breakage.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and other environmental factors by wearing a hat or using a leave-in conditioner.
  • Consider using a hair mask or deep conditioner once a week to keep your hair healthy and hydrated.

In conclusion, if you're looking for a way to look skinnier without dieting or exercising, consider trying out a short hairstyle. With the right cut and styling techniques, short hair can help elongate your neck, emphasize your bone structure, and draw attention away from any excess weight around your face. Follow our tips and advice to achieve the perfect look for you!
