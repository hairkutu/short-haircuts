How To Wear Your Frontal Wig In 2023

Written by Luffy Aug 28, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Frontal wigs are a great way to switch up your hairstyle without making a permanent change. They can be worn in a variety of styles and can last for months with proper care. If you're new to wearing wigs, don't worry! This guide will walk you through the process of how to wear your frontal wig.

Description

A frontal wig is a type of wig that has a lace closure that covers the entire front of the wig. This allows for a natural-looking hairline and the ability to part the hair in various ways. The rest of the wig is constructed with wefts and can be made with various types of hair, such as human or synthetic.

How to Wear Your Frontal Wig: Step by Step

Step 1: Prepare your hair

Before putting on your wig, make sure your hair is flat and secure. You can braid your hair or use a wig cap to keep it in place. If you have longer hair, you can also twist it and pin it down.

Step 2: Cut the lace

Use scissors to carefully cut the lace along the hairline of the wig. Be sure to cut as close to the hairline as possible without cutting the hairs of the wig.

Step 3: Apply adhesive

Apply a small amount of adhesive along the hairline, starting from the middle and working your way to the sides. Be sure to use a glue that is designed for wigs and skin-safe.

Step 4: Put on the wig

Place the wig on your head, starting from the front and working your way to the back. Adjust the wig to fit your head and make sure it is secure. Use bobby pins to secure the wig in place if necessary.

Step 5: Style the wig

Once the wig is in place, you can style it however you like. You can part it in the middle or on the side, or you can style it in an updo or ponytail.

Tips for Wearing Your Frontal Wig

Here are some additional tips to help you wear your frontal wig:

  • Use a wig cap to keep your hair in place
  • Trim the lace carefully to avoid cutting the hairs of the wig
  • Use a skin-safe adhesive to avoid irritation
  • Secure the wig with bobby pins if necessary
  • Experiment with different styles to find what works best for you

FAQ

How long can I wear my frontal wig?

Your frontal wig can last for months with proper care. However, it is recommended to remove the wig every 2-3 weeks to give your scalp a break and to wash and condition the wig.

Can I swim or exercise with my frontal wig?

While it is possible to swim and exercise with your frontal wig, it is not recommended as it can cause damage to the wig and your natural hair. It is best to remove the wig before engaging in any strenuous activities.

How do I care for my frontal wig?

To care for your frontal wig, wash and condition it regularly with products designed for wigs. Avoid using hot tools, such as flat irons and curling irons, as they can damage the hair. Store the wig on a mannequin head or in a wig box to maintain its shape.

Hair Care

Wearing a frontal wig can be a great way to protect your natural hair from damage and to try out new styles. However, it is important to take care of both your natural hair and the wig to prevent damage and maintain healthy hair. Here are some tips for caring for your hair:

  • Wash and condition your hair regularly
  • Avoid using heat on your hair
  • Protect your hair from the sun and harsh weather
  • Use products that are designed for your hair type
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends

By following these tips and properly caring for your frontal wig and natural hair, you can achieve healthy, beautiful hair that looks great in any style!
