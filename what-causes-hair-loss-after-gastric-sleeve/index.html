What Causes Hair Loss After Gastric Sleeve

Written by Aprilia Jul 19, 2023 · 7 min read

Are you considering getting the gastric sleeve surgery? It’s important to know that hair loss is a common side effect of this procedure. In this article, we’ll discuss what causes hair loss after gastric sleeve and provide some tips on how to prevent it.

Table of Contents

  • Causes of Hair Loss After Gastric Sleeve
  • How to Prevent Hair Loss After Gastric Sleeve
  • Step-by-Step Guide for Hair Care After Gastric Sleeve
  • Tips for Hair Loss After Gastric Sleeve
  • FAQs About Hair Loss After Gastric Sleeve

Causes of Hair Loss After Gastric Sleeve

There are several reasons why hair loss may occur after gastric sleeve surgery. One of the main causes is the rapid weight loss that occurs after the surgery. When you lose weight quickly, your body goes into a state of shock and may not get the nutrients it needs to keep your hair healthy and strong.

In addition, the surgery itself can cause trauma to your body, which can lead to hair loss. The stress of the surgery can cause your hair to enter the resting phase of the growth cycle, which can result in hair loss.

Another cause of hair loss after gastric sleeve surgery is a lack of protein. Protein is essential for hair growth, so if you’re not getting enough protein in your diet, your hair may start to fall out.

How to Prevent Hair Loss After Gastric Sleeve

While hair loss after gastric sleeve surgery is common, there are several things you can do to prevent it. One of the most important things is to make sure you’re getting enough protein in your diet. You should aim to consume at least 60-80 grams of protein per day.

In addition, you should make sure you’re getting enough vitamins and minerals in your diet. This can be achieved through a combination of supplements and a healthy diet. Some of the most important vitamins and minerals for hair health include biotin, iron, and zinc.

You should also avoid using harsh chemicals on your hair, such as hair dyes and relaxers. These chemicals can weaken your hair and make it more prone to breakage.

Step-by-Step Guide for Hair Care After Gastric Sleeve

Here’s a step-by-step guide for taking care of your hair after gastric sleeve surgery:

  1. Make sure you’re getting enough protein in your diet.
  2. Take a daily multivitamin that contains biotin, iron, and zinc.
  3. Avoid using harsh chemicals on your hair.
  4. Wash your hair with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  5. Avoid using hot tools on your hair, such as blow dryers and flat irons.
  6. Avoid wearing tight hairstyles, such as braids and ponytails.
  7. Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair.
  8. Avoid rubbing your hair with a towel after washing it.
  9. Avoid going to bed with wet hair.
  10. Consider using a hair growth serum to stimulate hair growth.

Tips for Hair Loss After Gastric Sleeve

Here are some additional tips for dealing with hair loss after gastric sleeve surgery:

  • Be patient – it can take several months for your hair to start growing back.
  • Consider getting a shorter haircut to make your hair look thicker.
  • Use volumizing products, such as mousse and hair spray, to add volume to your hair.
  • Avoid wearing hats and headbands, as they can put pressure on your hair.
  • Consider wearing a wig or hair extensions if you’re self-conscious about your hair loss.

FAQs About Hair Loss After Gastric Sleeve

Q: Will my hair grow back after gastric sleeve surgery?

A: Yes, your hair should grow back within a few months after surgery. However, it may take up to a year for your hair to fully regrow.

Q: Is hair loss after gastric sleeve surgery permanent?

A: No, hair loss after gastric sleeve surgery is usually temporary. Once your body adjusts to the weight loss and your diet, your hair should start to grow back.

Q: Can I take supplements to prevent hair loss after gastric sleeve surgery?

A: Yes, taking supplements can help prevent hair loss after gastric sleeve surgery. Some of the most important supplements for hair health include biotin, iron, and zinc.

Overall, hair loss after gastric sleeve surgery is a common side effect, but it can be prevented and treated with the right care and nutrition. By following the tips and guidelines outlined in this article, you can help ensure that your hair stays healthy and strong after surgery.
