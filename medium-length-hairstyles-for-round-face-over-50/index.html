Medium Length Hairstyles For Round Face Over 50

Written by Petter May 07, 2023 Â· 8 min read

As we age, our hair tends to thin out and lose its volume. For women over 50 with a round face, finding the perfect hairstyle that flatters their features can be challenging. Medium length hairstyles are the perfect solution for women with round faces over 50. In this blog post, we will discuss how to choose the right medium length hairstyle that complements your round face, step by step guide for styling your hair, tips, frequently asked questions, and hair care tips to maintain your medium length hairstyle.

How to Choose the Right Medium Length Hairstyle for Round Face Over 50

When choosing a medium length hairstyle for a round face over 50, it is essential to consider your face shape, hair type, and lifestyle. Here are some tips to help you choose the perfect hairstyle:

Consider Your Face Shape

For women with round faces over 50, it's best to choose hairstyles that create an illusion of length and add height to the crown. Avoid hairstyles that add volume to the sides or cheeks, as they will make your face appear wider.

Choose a Hairstyle that Complements Your Hair Type

Women over 50 usually have thinning hair, so it's essential to choose a hairstyle that adds volume and texture. If you have curly or wavy hair, go for layered hairstyles that add volume and texture. If you have straight hair, opt for blunt cuts or layered hairstyles with feathered ends to add volume and movement.

Consider Your Lifestyle

Choose a hairstyle that complements your lifestyle. If you are always on the go, opt for low maintenance hairstyles that require minimal styling. If you have the time and enjoy styling your hair, go for medium length hairstyles that allow you to experiment with different styles and looks.

Step by Step Guide for Styling Medium Length Hairstyles for Round Face Over 50

Now that you've chosen the perfect medium length hairstyle for your round face over 50, it's time to style it. Here's a step-by-step guide to help you style your hair:

Step 1: Wash Your Hair

Start by washing your hair with a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to add volume and texture to your hair. Rinse your hair thoroughly and towel dry.

Step 2: Apply a Hair Serum

Apply a hair serum or mousse to add volume and texture to your hair. Choose a product that is suitable for your hair type.

Step 3: Blow Dry Your Hair

Use a round brush to blow dry your hair, starting from the roots to the ends. Lift the hair at the crown to add volume and texture.

Step 4: Style Your Hair

Once your hair is dry, style it using a curling iron or a straightener to create waves or curls. Alternatively, you can style your hair using a hairbrush and hairdryer to create a sleek and smooth look.

Tips for Maintaining Medium Length Hairstyles for Round Face Over 50

Here are some tips to help you maintain your medium length hairstyle:

Trim Your Hair Regularly

Trim your hair every 6-8 weeks to keep it healthy and prevent split ends.

Apply a Hair Mask

Apply a hair mask once a week to keep your hair hydrated and nourished.

Avoid Heat Styling

Avoid using heat styling tools too frequently as they can damage your hair.

Frequently Asked Questions

Here are some frequently asked questions about medium length hairstyles for round face over 50:

What if I have thinning hair?

If you have thinning hair, opt for layered hairstyles that add volume and texture to your hair.

Can I still wear bangs?

Yes, you can wear bangs. Opt for side-swept bangs that flatter your face shape and add volume to your hair.

Hair Care Tips for Medium Length Hairstyles for Round Face Over 50

Here are some hair care tips to help you maintain your medium length hairstyle:

Wash Your Hair Less Frequently

Wash your hair every 2-3 days to prevent it from drying out.

Avoid Using Hot Water

Use lukewarm water to wash your hair as hot water can damage your hair.

Use a Leave-in Conditioner

Apply a leave-in conditioner to your hair before styling it to keep it hydrated and nourished.

In conclusion, medium length hairstyles are perfect for women with round faces over 50. When choosing a hairstyle, consider your face shape, hair type, and lifestyle. Follow our step-by-step guide to style your hair, and use our tips and hair care advice to maintain your medium length hairstyle.
