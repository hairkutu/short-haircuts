Thick Curly Hair Cut Short A Guide To Rocking This Stunning Look

Written by Aprilia Jul 06, 2023 Â· 7 min read

If you're blessed with thick curly hair, you know the struggle of managing it on a daily basis. While it can be a challenge, thick curly hair is also incredibly beautiful and unique, especially when cut short. In this guide, we'll walk you through everything you need to know about rocking a short cut with thick curly hair, including how to do it, step-by-step instructions, tips, and FAQs.

Description

If you're considering cutting your thick curly hair short, you're in for a treat. Short hair can be incredibly liberating, low maintenance, and stylish. With the right cut, you can highlight your natural curls and create a stunning look that's sure to turn heads. Whether you're looking for a pixie cut, a bob, or a shaggy style, there are plenty of options for you to choose from.

How to Cut Thick Curly Hair Short

Cutting thick curly hair short can be a bit daunting, but it's not as difficult as you might think. Here's a step-by-step guide to help you get started:

Step 1: Choose Your Cut

The first step is to choose the right cut for your face shape and hair texture. Whether you're looking for a classic bob or a trendy pixie cut, there are plenty of options to choose from. Consider consulting with a stylist to help you find the perfect look.

Step 2: Wash and Condition Your Hair

Before you start cutting, make sure your hair is clean and conditioned. This will help your stylist get a better idea of your natural hair texture and make the cutting process easier.

Step 3: Detangle Your Hair

Use a wide-toothed comb to gently detangle your hair. This is especially important if you have thick, curly hair, as it can be prone to tangling and knotting.

Step 4: Cut Your Hair

Using sharp scissors, your stylist will start cutting your hair to the desired length. They may use a razor or thinning shears to help create texture and movement.

Step 5: Style Your Hair

Once your hair is cut, your stylist will style it to perfection. They may use a diffuser to help enhance your natural curls or use a flat iron to create a sleek look.

Tips for Styling Short Thick Curly Hair

Here are some tips to help you style your short thick curly hair:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to keep your curls healthy and defined.
  • Use a wide-toothed comb or your fingers to detangle your hair when it's wet.
  • Apply a leave-in conditioner or curl cream to help define your curls.
  • Avoid using heat tools too often, as they can damage your hair.

FAQs About Short Thick Curly Hair

Q: Will cutting my thick curly hair short make it easier to manage?

A: Yes, cutting your hair short can make it easier to manage, especially if you have thick, curly hair. Short hair requires less styling time and can be more low maintenance.

Q: Will cutting my hair short damage my curls?

A: No, cutting your hair short won't damage your curls. In fact, cutting your hair can help it grow stronger and healthier.

Q: What should I do if I don't like my short hair?

A: If you're unhappy with your short hair, talk to your stylist about your concerns. They may be able to adjust the cut or suggest a different style that better suits your needs.

Final Thoughts

Cutting your thick curly hair short can be a bold and beautiful move. With the right cut and styling techniques, you can create a stunning look that highlights your natural curls and flatters your face shape. Whether you're going for a classic bob or a trendy pixie cut, be sure to work with a stylist who understands your hair type and can help you achieve the look you want.
