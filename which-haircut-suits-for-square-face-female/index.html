Which Haircut Suits For Square Face Female

Written by Eveline May 27, 2023 Â· 7 min read

If you are a woman with a square face, you may have found yourself struggling to find the perfect haircut that complements your face shape. But fear not, there are many hairstyles that can look fabulous on a square face. In this article, we will discuss the best haircuts for square face females, how to get them, and some tips on how to take care of your new hairstyle.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to
  • Step by Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

A square face is characterized by a strong jawline, a wide forehead, and a square chin. The goal of a haircut for a square face female is to soften the angles of the face and create balance. The ideal haircut should draw attention away from the jawline and towards the eyes and cheekbones. Below are some haircuts that can achieve this effect.

How to

Before choosing a haircut, it's important to consider your hair type and texture. Some haircuts may work better for straight hair, while others may look better on curly hair. It's also important to consider your lifestyle and how much time you can dedicate to styling your hair each day.

Short Haircuts

Short haircuts can work well for square face females. A pixie cut with choppy layers can soften the angles of the face and draw attention to the eyes. A bob with soft layers can also create a similar effect. Short haircuts are low maintenance and can be styled quickly.

Medium Haircuts

Medium haircuts can also look great on square face females. A shoulder-length haircut with soft layers can create a flattering frame for the face. A long bob, or "lob," with face-framing layers can also soften the angles of the face.

Long Haircuts

Long haircuts can be tricky for square face females, as they can sometimes accentuate the jawline. However, a long haircut with layers can create movement and softness around the face. Long hair can also be styled in loose waves or curls to soften the angles of the face.

Step by Step

Getting a haircut for a square face female is easy. Follow these steps:

  1. Choose a haircut based on your hair type, texture, and lifestyle.
  2. Show your hairstylist a picture of the haircut you want.
  3. Discuss any concerns you have about your face shape and how the haircut can complement it.
  4. Sit back and relax while your hairstylist works their magic!

Tips

  • Consider getting highlights or lowlights to add dimension to your hair.
  • Use a round brush while blow drying to add volume to your hair.
  • Experiment with different partings to find the most flattering one for your face shape.
  • Avoid hairstyles that are too sleek or straight, as they can emphasize the squareness of the face.

FAQ

What if I have curly hair?

A curly haircut with layers can create movement and soften the angles of the face. Consider getting a haircut that is shorter in the back and longer in the front to create a flattering frame for the face.

What if I don't want to cut my hair?

If you don't want to cut your hair, consider getting side-swept bangs or layers around the face to create movement and soften the angles of the face.

Hair Care

After getting a new haircut, it's important to take care of your hair to maintain its health and style. Follow these tips:

  • Use a shampoo and conditioner that is appropriate for your hair type.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as it can damage it.
  • Get regular trims to keep your haircut looking fresh.
  • Use a hair serum or oil to add shine and moisture to your hair.

In conclusion, there are many haircuts that can suit square face females. It's important to choose a haircut that complements your face shape and hair type. With the right haircut and some hair care tips, you can have a hairstyle that looks great and makes you feel confident.
