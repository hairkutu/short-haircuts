Description

The mens haircut short sides long top and back has been a popular hairstyle for years, and it's not going away anytime soon. This classic look is versatile and can be customized to suit your face shape and personal style. The style features shorter hair on the sides and back, with longer hair on top that can be styled in a variety of ways, including slicked back, messy, or textured.

If you're looking for a low-maintenance yet stylish haircut, the mens haircut short sides long top and back might be the perfect choice for you. In this guide, we'll show you how to get the look and give you tips for maintaining it.

How to Get a Mens Haircut Short Sides Long Top and Back

The mens haircut short sides long top and back can be achieved by visiting a hairstylist or barber. When you arrive at the salon, be prepared to discuss your desired look with your stylist. You can bring in pictures or examples of the style you want to help your stylist understand your vision.

It's important to note that the mens haircut short sides long top and back can be customized to suit your face shape and hair texture, so don't be afraid to ask your stylist for their professional opinion on what will work best for you.

Step by Step Guide

Here's a step by step guide to getting a mens haircut short sides long top and back:

  1. Start by washing your hair and towel drying it so it's slightly damp.
  2. Ask your stylist to trim the sides and back of your hair to your desired length. This can be anywhere from a buzz cut to a few inches long, depending on your preference.
  3. Next, your stylist will trim the hair on top of your head to the desired length. This can be anywhere from a few inches to shoulder-length, depending on your preference.
  4. Your stylist will then blend the hair on the sides and back with the hair on top. This creates a seamless transition between the two lengths.
  5. Finally, your stylist will style your hair according to your preference. This can include adding texture, using pomade or gel to create a slicked-back look, or leaving it messy for a more relaxed style.

Tips for Maintaining Your Mens Haircut Short Sides Long Top and Back

Here are some tips for maintaining your mens haircut short sides long top and back:

  • Visit your stylist every 4-6 weeks for a trim to keep your haircut looking fresh.
  • Use a high-quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and nourished.
  • Experiment with different styling products to find the one that works best for your hair type and desired style.
  • If you're using a blow dryer, use a heat protectant spray to prevent damage to your hair.
  • Avoid washing your hair every day as this can strip your hair of its natural oils.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about the mens haircut short sides long top and back:

Q: Can this haircut work for all face shapes?

A: Yes, the mens haircut short sides long top and back can be customized to suit all face shapes.

Q: What hair type is best for this haircut?

A: This haircut can work for all hair types, but it may require different styling products and techniques depending on your hair type.

Q: Is this a low-maintenance haircut?

A: Yes, the mens haircut short sides long top and back is a relatively low-maintenance haircut.

Hair Care Tips for Your Mens Haircut Short Sides Long Top and Back

Here are some hair care tips for your mens haircut short sides long top and back:

  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair when it's wet to avoid damaging it.
  • Avoid using hot tools like flat irons and curling irons too often as this can damage your hair.
  • Apply a hair mask once a week to keep your hair nourished and healthy.
  • Avoid using too much styling product as this can weigh your hair down and make it look greasy.
  • Avoid exposing your hair to chlorine and saltwater as this can dry it out and damage it.

Now that you know how to get and maintain a mens haircut short sides long top and back, it's time to book an appointment with your stylist and try out this classic and versatile hairstyle.
