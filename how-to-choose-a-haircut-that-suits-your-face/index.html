How To Choose A Haircut That Suits Your Face

Written by Bardi Aug 09, 2023 Â· 8 min read

Choosing the right haircut can be a daunting task, especially if you don't know what suits your face shape. A good haircut can enhance your features and give you a confidence boost. In this article, we'll guide you on how to choose a haircut that suits your face.

Table of Contents

  • Consider Your Hair Texture

  • Picking the Right Hair Style
  • Choosing the Right Hair Color

Determining Your Face Shape

The first step in choosing a haircut that suits you is to determine your face shape. There are six main face shapes: oval, round, square, heart, diamond, and oblong. To determine your face shape, pull your hair back and examine your face in the mirror.

If your face is longer than it is wide, you have an oval face shape. If your face is circular and your cheekbones are the widest part of your face, you have a round face shape. If your forehead, cheekbones, and jawline are all equally wide, you have a square face shape. If your forehead is wider than your jawline and your chin comes to a point, you have a heart face shape. If your cheekbones are wider than your forehead and jawline, you have a diamond face shape. If your face is longer than it is wide and your forehead, cheekbones, and jawline are all about the same width, you have an oblong face shape.

Consider Your Hair Texture

Your hair texture is also an important factor to consider when choosing a haircut. If you have fine hair, a blunt cut can make your hair look thicker. If you have thick hair, a layered cut can help to thin it out. If you have curly hair, a layered cut can help to enhance your curls.

Choosing the Right Hair Length

The length of your hair can also have a big impact on how it looks. If you have an oval face shape, you can pull off almost any length. If you have a round face shape, longer hair can help to elongate your face. If you have a square face shape, a chin-length bob can soften your features. If you have a heart face shape, longer hair with layers can balance out your features. If you have a diamond face shape, a chin-length bob or a pixie cut can accentuate your cheekbones. If you have an oblong face shape, a shoulder-length cut with layers can add width to your face.

Picking the Right Hair Style

Once you've determined your face shape and hair texture, it's time to pick a hair style that suits you. A pixie cut can be a great choice if you have a heart or diamond face shape. A bob or lob can look great on almost any face shape. If you have a round face shape, a shoulder-length cut with layers can help to elongate your face. If you have a square face shape, a side-swept fringe can soften your features. If you have an oblong face shape, a middle part can add width to your face.

Choosing the Right Hair Color

Your hair color can also have a big impact on how your haircut looks. If you have a warm skin tone, warm hair colors like golden blonde or copper can look great on you. If you have a cool skin tone, cool hair colors like platinum blonde or ash brown can look great on you. If you have a neutral skin tone, you can pull off almost any hair color.

Hair Care Tips

Once you've chosen a haircut that suits you, it's important to take care of your hair. Regular trims can help to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends. Use a heat protectant spray before using any hot tools on your hair. Use a shampoo and conditioner that is suited for your hair type.

FAQs

1. Can I pull off a pixie cut?

If you have a heart or diamond face shape, a pixie cut can be a great choice. However, if you have a round or square face shape, a pixie cut may not be the best choice for you.

2. What is the best hair length for a round face shape?

Longer hair can help to elongate a round face shape. A shoulder-length cut with layers can be a great choice.

3. What hair color suits warm skin tones?

Warm hair colors like golden blonde or copper can look great on those with warm skin tones.

4. How often should I get a haircut?

It's recommended to get a haircut every 6-8 weeks to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.

Choosing the right haircut can be a game-changer for your overall look. By considering your face shape, hair texture, hair length, hair style, and hair color, you can find a haircut that suits you perfectly. Remember to take care of your hair by getting regular trims and using products that are suited for your hair type.
