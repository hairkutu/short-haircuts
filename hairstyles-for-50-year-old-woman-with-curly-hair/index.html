Hairstyles For 50 Year Old Woman With Curly Hair A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Daniel Jul 29, 2023 Â· 11 min read

As women age, they often find that their hair changes in texture, thickness, and color. For those with curly hair, this can mean dealing with frizz, dryness, and unpredictable curls. However, with the right hairstyle and hair care routine, women over 50 with curly hair can look fabulous and youthful. In this article, we will discuss hairstyles, tips, and hair care for 50 year old women with curly hair.

Hairstyles for 50 Year Old Woman with Curly Hair

There are many hairstyles that can flatter women over 50 with curly hair. Here are some options to consider:

1. Layered Bob

A layered bob is a great choice for women who want to keep their hair short and easy to maintain. The layers add movement and texture to curly hair, and the bob length is youthful and chic.

2. Shoulder-Length Curls

If you prefer medium-length hair, consider shoulder-length curls. This style is versatile and can be worn loose or pulled back into a ponytail or bun.

3. Curly Pixie Cut

A curly pixie cut is a bold choice that can be very flattering for women over 50. The short length is easy to style and can emphasize your natural curls.

4. Curly Shag

A curly shag is a great way to add volume and texture to your hair. This style features layers and choppy ends that give your hair a messy, carefree look.

5. Curly Updo

If you have a special occasion coming up, consider a curly updo. This style can be elegant and sophisticated, and it will show off your beautiful curls.

How to Style Curly Hair for Women Over 50

Styling curly hair can be challenging, but with the right techniques and products, you can achieve a beautiful and natural look. Here are some tips for styling curly hair:

1. Start with a Good Haircut

The key to styling curly hair is to start with a good haircut. Make sure to choose a stylist who is experienced with curly hair and who can give you a cut that flatters your face shape and hair texture.

2. Use the Right Products

Curly hair requires moisture to look its best, so make sure to use a conditioner that is designed for curly hair. You may also want to use a leave-in conditioner or curl cream to help define your curls and reduce frizz.

3. Avoid Brushing Your Hair

Brushing curly hair can cause it to frizz and lose its shape. Instead, use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your hair when it is wet.

4. Embrace Your Natural Texture

Curly hair is beautiful, so embrace your natural texture and don't be afraid to let your curls shine. Use a diffuser when blow drying your hair to help enhance your curls.

Step-by-Step Guide to Styling Curly Hair

Here is a step-by-step guide to styling curly hair:

1. Wash and Condition Your Hair

Start by washing and conditioning your hair with products designed for curly hair. Rinse your hair thoroughly and gently towel dry it.

2. Apply a Leave-In Conditioner or Curl Cream

Apply a leave-in conditioner or curl cream to your hair, making sure to distribute it evenly from root to tip.

3. Use a Wide-Tooth Comb or Your Fingers to Detangle Your Hair

Gently detangle your hair using a wide-tooth comb or your fingers. Do not use a brush.

4. Diffuse Your Hair

Use a diffuser when blow drying your hair to help enhance your curls. Hold the diffuser close to your head and use a low heat setting.

5. Finish with a Hairspray or Hair Oil

Finish by using a hairspray or hair oil to add shine and hold to your hair.

Tips for Maintaining Curly Hair

Here are some tips for maintaining curly hair:

1. Sleep with a Silk or Satin Pillowcase

Sleeping on a silk or satin pillowcase can help reduce frizz and prevent hair breakage.

2. Avoid Overwashing Your Hair

Curly hair can become dry and brittle if it is overwashed. Try to wash your hair no more than once or twice a week.

3. Protect Your Hair from Heat

Excessive heat can damage curly hair, so make sure to use a heat protectant when using hot tools like flat irons or curling irons.

4. Trim Your Hair Regularly

Regular trims can help keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.

FAQs About Curly Hair for Women Over 50

1. Can I dye my curly hair?

Yes, you can dye your curly hair. However, it is important to choose a dye that is specifically designed for curly hair and to follow the instructions carefully.

2. How can I reduce frizz in my curly hair?

To reduce frizz, make sure to use a conditioner and curl cream that are designed for curly hair. You may also want to sleep on a silk or satin pillowcase and avoid brushing your hair.

3. Can I wear my curly hair in a ponytail or bun?

Yes, you can wear your curly hair in a ponytail or bun. Just make sure to use a hair tie that is gentle on your hair and to avoid pulling your hair too tight.

Hair Care Tips for Women Over 50 with Curly Hair

Here are some additional hair care tips for women over 50 with curly hair:

1. Avoid Overusing Heat Tools

Excessive heat can damage curly hair, so try to limit your use of heat tools like flat irons and curling irons.

2. Protect Your Hair from the Sun

The sun can dry out and damage curly hair, so make sure to wear a hat or use a leave-in conditioner with SPF when spending time outdoors.

3. Drink Plenty of Water

Drinking plenty of water can help keep your hair and scalp hydrated and healthy.

4. Eat a Healthy Diet

Eating a diet rich in vitamins and minerals can help promote healthy hair growth and prevent hair loss.

By following these tips and trying out different hairstyles, women over 50 with curly hair can look and feel their best. Remember to embrace your natural texture and have fun experimenting with different looks!
