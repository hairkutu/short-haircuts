How To Look Great At Age 60 Tips And Tricks

Written by Christine Jun 16, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Are you turning 60 soon and wondering how to keep yourself looking great? Don't worry! In this article, we'll give you some tips and tricks on how to look great at age 60. From skincare to hair care, we've got you covered.

How to Take Care of Your Skin

Your skin is the largest organ of your body, and it's important to take care of it. Here are some tips:

Moisturize

As you age, your skin loses its elasticity and moisture. To combat this, use a moisturizer that suits your skin type. Look for moisturizers with hyaluronic acid, which can help retain moisture in your skin.

Protect Your Skin from the Sun

As you age, your skin becomes more susceptible to sun damage. Make sure you use a sunscreen with at least SPF 30 whenever you go out. Wear protective clothing, such as hats and long-sleeved shirts, to shield your skin from the sun.

Stay Hydrated

Drinking enough water is important for your overall health, but it's also vital for your skin. Aim to drink at least 8 glasses of water a day to keep your skin hydrated and healthy.

How to Take Care of Your Hair

As you age, your hair might become thinner and less vibrant. Here are some hair care tips:

Choose the Right Shampoo and Conditioner

As you age, your hair might become drier and more brittle. Use a shampoo and conditioner that are specifically designed for aging hair. Look for products that contain keratin, which can help strengthen your hair.

Get Regular Haircuts

Regular haircuts can help maintain the health and appearance of your hair. Aim to get a haircut every 6-8 weeks to prevent split ends and promote healthy hair growth.

Consider Coloring Your Hair

If you want to cover up gray hair, consider coloring your hair. Choose a hair color that complements your skin tone and makes you feel confident.

Tips and Tricks

Here are some additional tips and tricks to help you look great at age 60:

Exercise Regularly

Regular exercise can help you maintain a healthy weight and keep your body toned. Aim to exercise for at least 30 minutes a day, 5 days a week.

Eat a Healthy Diet

Eating a balanced diet rich in fruits, vegetables, and lean protein can help you maintain a healthy weight and keep your skin and hair looking great.

Get Enough Sleep

Getting enough sleep is important for your overall health and well-being. Aim to get at least 7-8 hours of sleep each night to help your body repair and regenerate.

FAQs

Q: Is it too late to start taking care of my skin and hair?

A: No, it's never too late to start taking care of your skin and hair. Even if you've neglected them in the past, you can still make positive changes now.

Q: What's the best way to cover up age spots?

A: You can use a concealer or foundation to cover up age spots. Look for products that are specifically designed to target hyperpigmentation.

Q: Can I still wear makeup at age 60?

A: Yes, you can still wear makeup at age 60. Just make sure you choose products that are appropriate for your skin type and tone.

Conclusion

Turning 60 doesn't mean you have to stop looking great. By taking care of your skin, hair, and overall health, you can continue to look and feel your best. Use the tips and tricks we've outlined in this article to help you look great at age 60 and beyond.
