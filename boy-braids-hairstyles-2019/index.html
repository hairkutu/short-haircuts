Boy Braids Hairstyles 2019: A Complete Guide

  • Description
  • How To

Description

Boy braids hairstyles have been gaining popularity in recent years, and 2019 was no exception. This hairstyle is perfect for boys who want to add some edge and style to their look. From braided mohawks to cornrows, there are plenty of options for boys to choose from. Boy braids hairstyles are also a great way to protect natural hair and promote hair growth.

How To

The first step in achieving a boy braids hairstyle is to choose the type of braid that you want. Some popular options include cornrows, box braids, and twists. Once you have chosen the type of braid, you will need to decide on the length and thickness of your braids.

Step By Step

1. Start by washing and conditioning your hair.

2. Section off your hair into small sections using clips.

3. Take one section of hair and divide it into three equal parts.

4. Begin braiding your hair by crossing the left strand over the middle strand, then the right strand over the middle strand.

5. Continue braiding until you reach the end of your hair.

6. Repeat steps 3-5 for each section of hair.

7. Once all of your hair is braided, use a hair tie to secure the ends.

8. To maintain your braids, make sure to oil your scalp regularly and avoid pulling on your braids.

Tips

- Before braiding your hair, make sure it is clean and free of any tangles.

- Use a leave-in conditioner to keep your hair moisturized.

- To add some style to your braids, consider adding beads or hair accessories.

- If you are unsure about how to braid your hair, consider going to a professional stylist.

FAQ

Q: How long does it take to braid hair?

A: The time it takes to braid hair depends on the length and thickness of your hair, as well as the type of braid you are doing. On average, it can take anywhere from 2-6 hours to braid hair.

Q: How long do braids last?

A: Braids can last anywhere from 2-8 weeks, depending on how well they are maintained.

Hair Care

To keep your braids looking their best, it is important to take care of your hair. Here are some tips:

- Oil your scalp regularly to prevent dryness and itching.

- Avoid pulling on your braids, as this can damage your hair and cause breakage.

- Use a satin or silk scarf or pillowcase to prevent frizz and breakage while sleeping.

- Wash your hair regularly, but be gentle when shampooing to avoid loosening your braids.



Boy braids hairstyles 2019 are a great way to add some style and edge to your look. With so many options to choose from, there is a boy braids hairstyle that will work for everyone. Whether you want to protect your natural hair or simply switch up your style, boy braids are a great choice.
