How To Slick Back Black Men s Hair The Ultimate Guide

Written by Eveline Aug 29, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Slicking back hair has been a popular hairstyle for men for decades. It's a timeless look that exudes confidence and sophistication. However, achieving the perfect slick back can be a bit challenging, especially for black men who have unique hair textures. In this guide, we'll show you how to slick back black men's hair in a relaxed and easy-to-understand language.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to Slick Back Black Men's Hair
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Slicking Back Black Men's Hair
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care for Slick Back Hair

Description

Slick back hair is a hairstyle where the hair is combed back and held in place with a styling product, such as gel or pomade. It's a versatile style that can be worn in a formal or casual setting. For black men, slicking back hair can be challenging due to their hair's unique texture, which is often thick and curly. However, with the right products and techniques, anyone can pull off a sleek and polished slick back.

How to Slick Back Black Men's Hair

Slicking back black men's hair requires a few essential products and techniques. Here's what you'll need:

  • Comb
  • Gel or pomade
  • Boar bristle brush
  • Hair dryer (optional)

Step-by-Step Guide

Follow these steps to achieve the perfect slick back:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair. If your hair is wet, use a towel to dry it off.
  2. Apply a small amount of gel or pomade to your hair. Start at the back of your head and work your way forward.
  3. Use a comb to evenly distribute the product throughout your hair.
  4. Use a boar bristle brush to smooth out any bumps or lumps. Brush your hair back and away from your face.
  5. If your hair is thick or curly, use a hair dryer on low heat to help set the style. Use the brush to guide your hair back as you dry it.
  6. Once your hair is dry, use the comb to create a defined part. You can part your hair in the middle or on the side, depending on your preference.
  7. Brush your hair back again to create a sleek and polished look.
  8. Finish with a light hairspray to hold the style in place.

Tips for Slicking Back Black Men's Hair

Here are some additional tips to help you achieve the perfect slick back:

  • Use a water-based gel or pomade. These products are easier to wash out and won't leave residue on your hair.
  • Don't use too much product. A little goes a long way, and using too much can weigh down your hair and make it look greasy.
  • Experiment with different parting styles. A side part can make your face look slimmer, while a middle part can create a more symmetrical look.
  • Use a hair dryer on low heat to help set the style, especially if you have thick or curly hair.

FAQs

Can I slick back my hair if it's short?

Yes, you can still slick back short hair. Use a small amount of product and a comb to create a sleek look.

How often should I wash my hair if I slick it back?

You should wash your hair every 2-3 days to avoid buildup from the styling products.

Can I use a hairbrush instead of a comb?

Yes, you can use a hairbrush to slick back your hair. However, a boar bristle brush is recommended for a smoother and more polished look.

Hair Care for Slick Back Hair

Slick back hair requires proper maintenance to keep it looking polished and healthy. Here are some tips:

  • Use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair hydrated.
  • Avoid using too much product, as it can weigh down your hair and make it look greasy.
  • Wash your hair every 2-3 days to avoid buildup from the styling products.
  • Get regular haircuts to keep your hair looking neat and tidy.

With these tips and techniques, you can achieve the perfect slick back hairstyle for black men. Experiment with different products and styles to find what works best for you. Remember, confidence is key, so wear your slick back with pride and rock it with confidence!
