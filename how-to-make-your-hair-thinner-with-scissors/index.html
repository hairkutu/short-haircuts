How To Make Your Hair Thinner With Scissors A Step By Step Guide

Written by William May 11, 2023 · 7 min read

If you have thick hair that’s hard to manage, you may have considered thinning it out. While there are many ways to do this, one of the simplest and most affordable is to use scissors. In this guide, we’ll walk you through the steps of how to make your hair thinner with scissors. We’ll also provide tips and tricks to make the process as easy and stress-free as possible.

Description

Thinning your hair with scissors involves cutting small sections of hair at an angle to remove bulk. This technique can be done at home with a pair of sharp scissors and some patience. It’s important to note that while thinning your hair can make it more manageable, it can also cause damage if not done correctly. We recommend consulting with a professional stylist if you’re unsure.

How to Thin Your Hair with Scissors

Before you begin, it’s important to have the right tools. You’ll need a pair of sharp scissors, a comb, and hair clips to section your hair. Here’s how to make your hair thinner with scissors:

Step 1: Wash and dry your hair

Start by washing and drying your hair as you normally would. This will make it easier to see the sections you want to thin.

Step 2: Section your hair

Use hair clips to section your hair into manageable parts. Start with the bottom layer and work your way up. This will help you see the sections you want to thin.

Step 3: Cut at an angle

Take a small section of hair and comb it straight. Hold the section between your index and middle finger and cut at an angle. Cut about half an inch off the ends. Repeat this process on each section until you’ve thinned out your hair to your liking.

Step 4: Blend the sections

Use your comb to blend the sections together. This will help create a seamless look and prevent any choppy lines.

Step 5: Check your work

Once you’ve finished thinning your hair, check your work in a mirror. If there are any sections that look too thick, go back and thin them out some more.

Tips for Thinning Your Hair with Scissors

Thinning your hair with scissors can be a bit intimidating, especially if you’ve never done it before. Here are some tips to help make the process easier:

  • Start with small sections and work your way up to larger ones
  • Use sharp scissors to prevent damage to your hair
  • Don’t cut too much off at once
  • Blend the sections together to prevent any choppy lines
  • Consult with a professional stylist if you’re unsure

FAQs

Can you thin your hair with regular scissors?

While you can technically use regular scissors to thin your hair, we recommend using a pair of sharp scissors specifically designed for cutting hair. This will help prevent damage and create a more seamless look.

How often should you thin your hair?

How often you should thin your hair depends on your hair type and how quickly it grows. Generally, we recommend thinning your hair every 4-6 months to keep it manageable.

Hair Care Tips

Thinning your hair can make it more manageable, but it’s important to take care of it afterward. Here are some hair care tips to keep your hair healthy:

  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner
  • Avoid heat styling tools as much as possible
  • Use a heat protectant spray if you do use heat styling tools
  • Avoid over-brushing your hair
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends

Now that you know how to make your hair thinner with scissors, you can achieve a more manageable hairstyle at home. Remember to take your time and be patient, and consult with a professional stylist if you’re unsure.
