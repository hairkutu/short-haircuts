How To Fake Short Boy Hair A Step By Step Guide

Written by Aprilia Jul 16, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Are you looking to switch up your hairstyle and try something new? Maybe you've been eyeing short boy hair but don't want to commit to a chop just yet. Luckily, you can fake short boy hair with a few simple tricks and techniques. In this guide, we'll show you how to achieve the look in just a few steps.

How to Fake Short Boy Hair

To fake short boy hair, you'll need to create the illusion of shorter hair without actually cutting it. Here are the steps to follow:

Step 1: Gather Your Supplies

You'll need a few key supplies to fake short boy hair:

  • Hair gel or pomade
  • A comb
  • Bobby pins or hair clips
  • Hairspray (optional)

Make sure you have these items on hand before you get started.

Step 2: Apply Hair Gel or Pomade

Start by applying a small amount of hair gel or pomade to your hair. Use your fingers to work it through your hair, ensuring that it's evenly distributed.

Step 3: Comb Your Hair Back

Using a comb, comb your hair straight back. Make sure your hair is smooth and free of any bumps or tangles.

Step 4: Pin Your Hair Back

Take small sections of hair at the sides and back of your head and pin them back with bobby pins or hair clips. This will create the illusion of shorter hair at the sides and back.

Step 5: Style Your Hair

Once your hair is pinned back, you can style the remaining hair on top of your head. You can slick it back, create a side part, or leave it messy for a more casual look. Use a comb or your fingers to style your hair as desired.

Step 6: Optional: Add Hairspray

If you want your fake short boy hair to stay in place all day, you can finish with a spritz of hairspray. This will help hold your hair in place and prevent it from getting frizzy or out of control.

Tips for Faking Short Boy Hair

Here are a few tips to keep in mind when faking short boy hair:

  • Use a high-quality hair gel or pomade to ensure your hair stays in place.
  • Don't be afraid to play around with different styles to find what works best for you.
  • If you have longer hair, you may need to use more bobby pins or hair clips to create the illusion of shorter hair.
  • If you're having trouble getting your hair to stay in place, try blow-drying it after applying hair gel or pomade.

FAQ: Faking Short Boy Hair

Can I fake short boy hair with long hair?

Yes, you can fake short boy hair with long hair. You'll just need to use more bobby pins or hair clips to create the illusion of shorter hair at the sides and back.

How long does it take to fake short boy hair?

Faking short boy hair can take anywhere from 5 to 15 minutes, depending on your hair length and the style you're going for.

Do I need to wash my hair before faking short boy hair?

No, you don't need to wash your hair before faking short boy hair. In fact, it's better to start with slightly dirty hair, as this will help the hair gel or pomade hold better.

Hair Care for Faked Short Boy Hair

When faking short boy hair, it's important to take care of your hair to prevent damage and breakage. Here are a few tips:

  • Avoid using too much hair gel or pomade, as this can weigh down your hair and make it look greasy.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair gently.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools like flat irons or curling irons, as these can damage your hair.
  • Try to avoid pulling your hair back too tightly with bobby pins or hair clips, as this can cause breakage.

With these tips and techniques, you can fake short boy hair and switch up your look without committing to a drastic haircut. Give it a try and see how it looks on you!
