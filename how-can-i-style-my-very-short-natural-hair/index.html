How Can I Style My Very Short Natural Hair

Written by Christine Mar 27, 2023 Â· 8 min read
Are you tired of the same old short hairstyles for your natural hair? Do you want to try something new and fresh? Look no further! In this article, we will show you how to style your very short natural hair in different ways that are easy to achieve and maintain. Whether you have a twa (teeny weeny afro) or a slightly longer cropped cut, these styles will work for you.

  • How to Style Very Short Natural Hair
  • Step by Step
  • Tips for Styling Very Short Natural Hair
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care Tips for Very Short Natural Hair

Description

Very short natural hair is hair that is usually less than 2 inches in length. It can be a challenge to style, but there are many ways to make it look great. Whether you want a sleek and polished look or a more textured and edgy style, there are options for you.

How to Style Very Short Natural Hair

There are many different ways to style very short natural hair. Here are a few options: 1. The Tapered Cut: A tapered cut is a great option for those who want a low-maintenance style. It involves cutting the hair shorter at the sides and back and leaving a little more length on top. This creates a gradual transition from short to long hair, which looks great on natural hair. 2. The Twist Out: A twist out is a popular style for natural hair of all lengths. To achieve this style on very short hair, simply twist small sections of hair and allow them to dry. Once your hair is dry, unravel the twists and fluff your hair to create volume and definition. 3. The Finger Coils: Finger coils are another great option for very short natural hair. This style involves coiling small sections of hair around your finger to create defined curls. This style can last for several days with the right hair care.

Step by Step

1. The Tapered Cut:
  1. Wash and condition your hair.
  2. Blow dry your hair, using a comb to create a smooth finish.
  3. Cut the sides and back of your hair shorter than the top.
  4. Style the top of your hair as desired.
2. The Twist Out:
  1. Wash and condition your hair.
  2. Apply a styling product to your hair, such as a curling cream or gel.
  3. Section your hair into small sections and twist each section tightly.
  4. Allow your hair to dry completely.
  5. Unravel the twists and fluff your hair to create volume and definition.
3. The Finger Coils:
  1. Wash and condition your hair.
  2. Apply a styling product to your hair, such as a curling cream or gel.
  3. Section your hair into small sections and coil each section around your finger.
  4. Allow your hair to dry completely.
  5. Fluff your hair to create volume and definition.

Tips for Styling Very Short Natural Hair

Here are a few tips to help you style your very short natural hair: 1. Use the right products: Make sure you are using products that are designed for natural hair. Look for products that are moisturizing and help to define your curls. 2. Protect your hair at night: Use a satin bonnet or scarf to protect your hair while you sleep. This will help to prevent frizz and breakage. 3. Experiment with different styles: Don't be afraid to try different styles to see what works best for you. You might be surprised at how versatile very short natural hair can be.

FAQs

Q: Can I color my very short natural hair?
A: Yes, you can color your natural hair. However, it is important to use a color-safe shampoo and conditioner to maintain the health of your hair. Q: How often should I wash my very short natural hair?
A: It is recommended that you wash your natural hair once a week or every other week. However, this may vary depending on your hair type and lifestyle.

Hair Care Tips for Very Short Natural Hair

To keep your very short natural hair healthy and strong, here are a few hair care tips: 1. Moisturize regularly: Natural hair needs moisture to thrive. Use a leave-in conditioner or hair oil to keep your hair hydrated. 2. Trim your hair regularly: Even very short hair needs to be trimmed to maintain its shape and health. Aim to trim your hair every 4-6 weeks. 3. Protect your hair from heat: Heat can damage natural hair, so avoid using hot tools like flat irons and curling irons. If you must use heat, use a heat protectant spray and keep the temperature low. In conclusion, there are many ways to style very short natural hair. With the right products, techniques, and care, you can achieve a variety of looks that are both stylish and healthy for your hair. Don't be afraid to experiment and have fun with your hair!
