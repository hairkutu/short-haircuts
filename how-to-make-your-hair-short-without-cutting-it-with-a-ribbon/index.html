How To Make Your Hair Short Without Cutting It With A Ribbon

Written by Daniel Aug 13, 2023 Â· 8 min read

Are you tired of your long locks and want a change but don't want to commit to a haircut? Look no further than this tutorial on how to make your hair short without cutting it with a ribbon. With just a few simple steps and tools, you can achieve a shorter hairstyle without losing any length permanently.

Description

This tutorial will teach you how to create a shorter hairstyle without going under the scissors. Using a ribbon, you can create the illusion of shorter hair by tying it up in a specific way. This method is perfect for those who want to experiment with a shorter hairstyle without committing to a haircut.

How to

Follow these simple steps to create a shorter hairstyle without cutting your hair:

  1. Start by brushing your hair thoroughly to remove any tangles or knots.
  2. Pick out a ribbon that is the same color as your hair or a contrasting color, depending on the look you want to achieve.
  3. Section off the top layer of your hair and tie it up into a high ponytail using the ribbon. Make sure to leave the ends of the ribbon loose.
  4. Take the ends of the ribbon and wrap them around the base of the ponytail, securing it in place with bobby pins.
  5. With the remaining hair, divide it into two sections and bring each section to the front, tying it in a knot with the ribbon. Secure each knot in place with bobby pins.
  6. Fluff up the hair to create volume and adjust the knots and ponytail as needed to achieve your desired look.

Step by Step

Here is a more detailed step-by-step guide on how to make your hair short without cutting it with a ribbon:

Step 1: Brush Your Hair

Before you begin, make sure your hair is brush thoroughly to remove any tangles or knots. This will make the styling process much easier.

Step 2: Choose a Ribbon

Pick out a ribbon that is the same color as your hair or a contrasting color, depending on the look you want to achieve. The ribbon should be long enough to tie around your hair several times.

Step 3: Tie a High Ponytail

Section off the top layer of your hair and tie it up into a high ponytail using the ribbon. Make sure to leave the ends of the ribbon loose.

Step 4: Wrap the Ribbon

Take the ends of the ribbon and wrap them around the base of the ponytail, securing it in place with bobby pins.

Step 5: Tie Knots

With the remaining hair, divide it into two sections and bring each section to the front, tying it in a knot with the ribbon. Secure each knot in place with bobby pins.

Step 6: Final Touches

Fluff up the hair to create volume and adjust the knots and ponytail as needed to achieve your desired look. You can also add hair accessories like clips or headbands to further customize your style.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you achieve the best results:

  • Choose a ribbon that is the same color as your hair or a contrasting color, depending on the look you want to achieve.
  • Be gentle when tying the knots and securing the ribbon with bobby pins to avoid damaging your hair.
  • Experiment with different ribbon widths and textures to see what works best for you.
  • Add hair accessories like clips or headbands to further customize your style.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about making your hair short without cutting it with a ribbon:

Can I use any type of ribbon?

Yes, you can use any type of ribbon as long as it is long enough to tie around your hair several times. Experiment with different widths and textures to see what works best for you.

Will this damage my hair?

No, this method should not damage your hair as long as you are gentle when tying the knots and securing the ribbon with bobby pins.

How long will this style last?

This style can last for several hours or even a full day depending on how well you secure the knots and ponytail with the ribbon and bobby pins.

Hair Care

Be sure to take care of your hair by washing it regularly, using conditioner, and avoiding heat styling tools like curling irons and straighteners. You can also use a hair mask or oil treatment to keep your hair healthy and shiny.

Now that you know how to make your hair short without cutting it with a ribbon, you can experiment with different styles and looks without committing to a haircut. Have fun and enjoy your new shorter hairstyle!
