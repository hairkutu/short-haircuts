How To Get Thicker Hair Tips And Tricks

Written by William Sep 09, 2023 · 7 min read

Having thin hair can be frustrating, especially if you want to style your hair or try out new hairstyles. Fortunately, there are ways to get thicker hair. In this article, we’ll discuss some tips and tricks that can help you achieve thicker, fuller hair.

How to Get Thicker Hair:

1. Eat a Balanced Diet

What you eat can have a significant impact on the health of your hair. A balanced diet that is rich in vitamins and minerals is essential for healthy hair growth. Foods that are high in protein, such as eggs, lean meat, and nuts, can help to strengthen your hair and promote growth.

2. Use the Right Hair Products

Using the right hair products can also help to promote thicker hair. Look for products that are designed to add volume and thickness to your hair. Avoid products that contain harsh chemicals, such as sulfates, which can strip your hair of its natural oils and cause damage.

3. Avoid Heat Styling

Heat styling tools, such as flat irons and curling wands, can cause damage to your hair and make it look thinner. Try to avoid using these tools as much as possible or use them on a lower heat setting.

4. Get Regular Trims

Getting regular trims can help to prevent split ends and breakage, which can make your hair look thinner. Aim to get a trim every six to eight weeks to keep your hair healthy and promote growth.

Step by Step Guide:

1. Start with a Healthy Scalp

A healthy scalp is essential for healthy hair growth. Make sure to keep your scalp clean and free of buildup by washing your hair regularly.

2. Use a Thickening Shampoo

Look for a thickening shampoo that is designed to add volume and thickness to your hair. Massage the shampoo into your scalp and hair, then rinse thoroughly.

3. Condition Regularly

Conditioning your hair regularly can help to keep it smooth and shiny. Look for a conditioner that is designed to add volume and thickness to your hair. Apply the conditioner to the ends of your hair and leave it on for a few minutes before rinsing.

4. Use a Hair Mask

Using a hair mask once a week can help to nourish and strengthen your hair. Look for a hair mask that is designed to add volume and thickness to your hair. Apply the mask to your hair and leave it on for 10-15 minutes before rinsing.

5. Blow Dry Your Hair

Blow drying your hair can help to add volume and thickness. Use a round brush to lift your hair at the roots while blow drying.

Tips for Thicker Hair:

1. Massage Your Scalp

Massaging your scalp can help to promote blood flow and stimulate hair growth. Use your fingertips to massage your scalp for a few minutes each day.

2. Try Hair Extensions

Hair extensions can help to add thickness and volume to your hair instantly. Look for extensions that are made from high-quality hair and match your natural hair color.

3. Take Supplements

Taking supplements, such as biotin and vitamin E, can help to promote healthy hair growth. Make sure to talk to your doctor before taking any supplements.

Frequently Asked Questions:

1. Can I make my hair thicker naturally?

Yes, you can make your hair thicker naturally by eating a balanced diet, using the right hair products, and avoiding heat styling.

2. How often should I get my hair trimmed?

Aim to get a trim every six to eight weeks to keep your hair healthy and promote growth.

3. Can supplements help to promote hair growth?

Yes, taking supplements, such as biotin and vitamin E, can help to promote healthy hair growth. Make sure to talk to your doctor before taking any supplements.

Hair Care:

Taking care of your hair is essential for healthy hair growth. Make sure to wash your hair regularly, use the right hair products, and avoid heat styling as much as possible. Getting regular trims and using a hair mask once a week can also help to promote thicker, fuller hair.
