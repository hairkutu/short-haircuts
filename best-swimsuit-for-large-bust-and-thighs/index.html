Best Swimsuit For Large Bust And Thighs

Written by Robby Apr 27, 2023 Â· 8 min read

Are you tired of searching for the perfect swimsuit that fits your large bust and thighs? Look no further! We've rounded up the best swimsuits that will flatter your figure and make you feel confident on the beach or by the pool.

Description

Women with large busts and thighs often struggle to find a swimsuit that fits comfortably and flatters their body. Many swimsuits are designed for smaller busts and thighs, leaving larger women feeling like they can't find anything that fits properly. However, there are several swimsuits on the market that are designed specifically for larger busts and thighs.

How to Find the Best Swimsuit for Large Bust and Thighs

When shopping for a swimsuit, it's important to keep in mind your body type and what styles will flatter your figure. For women with large busts and thighs, there are a few things to look for in a swimsuit:

Supportive Tops

Women with larger busts need more support in their swimsuit tops. Look for tops with underwire, molded cups, or adjustable straps to ensure you get the support you need.

High Waisted Bottoms

High waisted bottoms can help to smooth out your tummy and are a great option for women with larger thighs. Look for bottoms with ruching or shirring to further flatter your figure.

Dark Colors and Patterns

Dark colors and patterns can help to minimize the appearance of larger busts and thighs. Look for swimsuits in solid colors or with smaller prints.

Step by Step Guide to Finding the Best Swimsuit for Large Bust and Thighs

Follow these steps to find the best swimsuit for your body:

Step 1: Measure Your Bust and Hips

Take accurate measurements of your bust and hips to ensure you find a swimsuit that fits properly.

Step 2: Determine Your Body Shape

Determine your body shape to find styles that will flatter your figure. Women with larger busts and thighs often have an hourglass or pear-shaped figure.

Step 3: Look for Supportive Tops

Look for tops with underwire, molded cups, or adjustable straps to ensure you get the support you need.

Step 4: Try High Waisted Bottoms

High waisted bottoms can help to smooth out your tummy and are a great option for women with larger thighs. Look for bottoms with ruching or shirring to further flatter your figure.

Step 5: Look for Dark Colors and Patterns

Dark colors and patterns can help to minimize the appearance of larger busts and thighs. Look for swimsuits in solid colors or with smaller prints.

Tips for Choosing the Best Swimsuit for Large Bust and Thighs

Follow these tips to find the best swimsuit for your body:

Tip 1: Look for Supportive Tops

Choose tops with underwire, molded cups, or adjustable straps to ensure you get the support you need.

Tip 2: Try High Waisted Bottoms

High waisted bottoms can help to smooth out your tummy and are a great option for women with larger thighs. Look for bottoms with ruching or shirring to further flatter your figure.

Tip 3: Look for Dark Colors and Patterns

Dark colors and patterns can help to minimize the appearance of larger busts and thighs. Look for swimsuits in solid colors or with smaller prints.

FAQ

Q: Can I wear a bikini if I have a large bust and thighs?

A: Yes! There are bikinis available that are designed for larger busts and thighs. Look for bikinis with supportive tops and high waisted bottoms to flatter your figure.

Q: What kind of swimsuits should I avoid if I have a large bust and thighs?

A: Avoid swimsuits with thin straps, bandeau tops, and small prints, as these can make your bust and thighs look larger.

Hair Care Tips for Swimming

Swimming can be tough on your hair, but these tips can help to keep your locks looking healthy:

Tip 1: Wet Your Hair Before Swimming

Wetting your hair before swimming can help to prevent chlorine damage.

Tip 2: Wear a Swim Cap

A swim cap can help to protect your hair from chlorine and keep it out of your face while swimming.

Tip 3: Rinse Your Hair After Swimming

Rinsing your hair after swimming can help to remove chlorine and other chemicals that can damage your hair.

With these tips and swimsuit options, you'll be sure to find the perfect swimsuit for your large bust and thighs. So go ahead and hit the beach or pool with confidence!
