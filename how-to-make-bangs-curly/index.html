How To Make Bangs Curly A Step By Step Guide

Written by Oliver May 21, 2023 Â· 6 min read

If you have bangs, you may be looking for ways to switch up your style. One option is to make your bangs curly! It's a fun and playful look that's easy to achieve with the right tools and techniques. In this article, we'll show you how to make your bangs curly in a few simple steps.

Description

Curly bangs are a great way to add some texture and movement to your hairstyle. Whether you have naturally straight or wavy hair, you can achieve curly bangs with a few simple steps. All you need is a curling iron, some heat protectant spray, and a little bit of patience.

How to Make Bangs Curly

Step 1: Prep Your Hair

Before you start curling, you'll need to prep your hair. Start by washing and drying your hair as you normally would. Apply a heat protectant spray to your bangs to protect them from heat damage.

Step 2: Section Your Bangs

Next, section your bangs from the rest of your hair. Use a clip to keep the rest of your hair out of the way while you focus on your bangs.

Step 3: Choose Your Curling Iron

Choose a curling iron that's the right size for your bangs. For shorter bangs, a smaller curling iron will work better. For longer bangs, you can use a larger curling iron.

Step 4: Start Curling

Take a small section of your bangs and wrap it around the curling iron. Hold it in place for a few seconds, then release. Repeat this process until all of your bangs are curled.

Step 5: Finish with Hairspray

Finish off your curly bangs with a light mist of hairspray. This will help hold the curls in place and prevent them from falling flat throughout the day.

Tips

Here are a few tips to help you achieve the perfect curly bangs:

  • Use a heat protectant spray to prevent heat damage to your hair.
  • Choose a curling iron that's the right size for your bangs.
  • Hold the curling iron in place for a few seconds to create a tighter curl.
  • Don't curl too much hair at once. Take small sections for the best results.
  • Finish with a light mist of hairspray to hold the curls in place.

FAQ

Can you curl bangs without a curling iron?

Yes, you can curl your bangs without a curling iron. You can use hot rollers or bobby pins to create curls. Simply wrap your bangs around the roller or pin, hold it in place, and release after a few seconds.

How long will curly bangs last?

Curly bangs can last anywhere from a few hours to a few days depending on your hair type and how well you maintain them. Use hairspray to help hold the curls in place and avoid touching your hair too much throughout the day.

Can you curl bangs if you have straight hair?

Yes, you can curl your bangs even if you have straight hair. Just make sure to use a heat protectant spray and a curling iron that's the right size for your bangs.

Hair Care

It's important to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and looking great. Use a high-quality shampoo and conditioner that's appropriate for your hair type. Avoid using too much heat on your hair, and use a heat protectant spray when you do. Get regular haircuts to help prevent split ends and maintain your style.

With these tips and techniques, you can easily make your bangs curly and switch up your style. Have fun experimenting with different looks and find the one that works best for you!
