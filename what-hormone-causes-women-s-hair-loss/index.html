What Hormone Causes Women s Hair Loss

Written by Aprilia Jul 25, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Women's hair loss is a common problem that affects millions of women worldwide. There are several causes of hair loss in women, but one of the most common causes is hormonal imbalance. Hormonal imbalance can cause hair loss in women of all ages, and it is important to understand what hormone causes women's hair loss in order to address the problem effectively.

What Hormone Causes Women's Hair Loss?

The hormone that causes women's hair loss is called DHT (dihydrotestosterone). DHT is a hormone that is produced naturally in the body, and it is responsible for the development of male characteristics such as deepening of the voice and growth of facial hair. However, in women, excess DHT can cause hair loss.

How Does DHT Cause Hair Loss?

DHT causes hair loss by shrinking hair follicles. Hair follicles are the tiny openings in the skin where hair grows from. When DHT levels are high, the hair follicles become smaller and smaller, until eventually they stop producing hair altogether. This process is known as miniaturization, and it is the main reason why women experience hair loss.

How Can You Tell If You Have High DHT Levels?

There are several signs that you may have high DHT levels, including:

  • Excessive facial hair growth
  • Acne
  • Oily skin
  • Irregular menstrual cycles
  • Thinning hair

If you are experiencing any of these symptoms, it is important to speak to your doctor to determine if high DHT levels are the cause of your hair loss.

How Can You Treat Hair Loss Caused by DHT?

There are several treatments available for hair loss caused by DHT, including:

  • Topical treatments: These are creams or foams that are applied directly to the scalp to block the effects of DHT.
  • Oral medications: These are medications that are taken by mouth to block the effects of DHT.
  • Hair transplant surgery: This is a surgical procedure that involves transplanting hair from one area of the scalp to another.

It is important to speak to your doctor to determine which treatment is best for you based on your individual needs.

How Can You Prevent Hair Loss Caused by DHT?

There are several things you can do to prevent hair loss caused by DHT, including:

  • Eating a healthy diet: A diet rich in vitamins and minerals can help keep your hair healthy and prevent hair loss.
  • Reducing stress: Stress can cause hormonal imbalances, which can lead to hair loss. Finding ways to manage stress, such as through exercise or meditation, can help prevent hair loss.
  • Avoiding tight hairstyles: Tight hairstyles, such as braids and ponytails, can pull on the hair and cause damage. Avoiding these hairstyles can help prevent hair loss.

FAQs

Can Hair Loss Caused by DHT Be Reversed?

Yes, hair loss caused by DHT can be reversed with the right treatment. It is important to speak to your doctor to determine which treatment is best for you.

Is Hair Loss Caused by DHT Genetic?

Yes, hair loss caused by DHT can be genetic. If you have a family history of hair loss, you may be more likely to experience hair loss caused by DHT.

Hair Care Tips

In addition to the treatments and prevention methods mentioned above, there are several hair care tips you can follow to help keep your hair healthy and prevent hair loss:

  • Avoid harsh chemicals: Harsh chemicals, such as those found in hair dyes and relaxers, can damage the hair and cause hair loss.
  • Avoid heat styling: Heat styling tools, such as flat irons and curling irons, can damage the hair and cause hair loss. If you must use heat styling tools, be sure to use a heat protectant spray.
  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner: Using a gentle shampoo and conditioner can help keep your hair healthy and prevent hair loss.

By following these tips and speaking to your doctor about the best treatment for you, you can help prevent and reverse hair loss caused by DHT.
