How To Undercut Short Hair Step By Step Guide

Written by Aprilia Apr 18, 2023 Â· 6 min read

If you're looking for a dramatic change in your hairstyle, undercutting is a great option. It's a versatile style that can be customized to suit your preferences, and it works well on short hair. In this post, we'll guide you through the process of undercutting your short hair.

Description

An undercut involves shaving the hair underneath the top layers, leaving a defined line between the two lengths. It's a popular style that's been around for decades, and it's still going strong today. The style is perfect for those who want a bold, edgy look, and it's also easy to maintain.

How to Undercut Short Hair

Undercutting short hair is a relatively simple process, but it does require some preparation. Here's what you'll need:

  • Clipper or trimmer
  • Comb
  • Hair ties
  • Scissors

Once you have your supplies, follow these steps:

Step 1: Wash and Dry Your Hair

Start by washing and drying your hair. You want to make sure your hair is clean and free of any styling products.

Step 2: Section Your Hair

Use a comb to section your hair, separating the top layers from the bottom layers. Use hair ties to keep the top layers out of the way.

Step 3: Trim the Bottom Layers

Use your clipper or trimmer to shave the hair underneath the top layers, leaving a defined line between the two lengths. Start at the back and work your way to the sides, using a comb to guide the clippers.

Step 4: Blend the Top Layers

Use scissors to blend the top layers into the undercut. You want to create a seamless transition between the two lengths.

Step 5: Style Your Hair

Style your hair as desired. You can leave the top layers down, or you can tie them up to show off your undercut.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when undercutting your short hair:

  • Start with a longer length and work your way shorter. You can always go shorter, but you can't go back once you've cut too much.
  • Use a mirror to check your progress from different angles.
  • Don't be afraid to ask for help from a friend or a professional stylist.

FAQ

Can you undercut short hair?

Yes, undercutting works well on short hair. In fact, it's a popular style for those with short hair.

Does undercutting damage hair?

No, undercutting doesn't damage hair. However, it does involve shaving the hair, which can make it feel coarser and thicker.

How often do you need to maintain an undercut?

It depends on how fast your hair grows and how short you want to keep the undercut. Generally, you'll need to maintain an undercut every 4-6 weeks.

Hair Care

Undercutting doesn't require any special hair care. However, it's important to keep your hair clean and well-moisturized. Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner, and avoid using too much heat on your hair. Additionally, you may want to consider using a leave-in conditioner to keep your hair soft and manageable.

That's it! With this guide, you can easily undercut your short hair and achieve a bold, edgy look. Remember to take your time and be patient, and don't be afraid to experiment with different lengths and styles. Good luck!
