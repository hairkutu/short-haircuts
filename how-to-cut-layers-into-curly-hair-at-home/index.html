How To Cut Layers Into Curly Hair At Home

Written by Christine Apr 07, 2023 · 8 min read

Are you tired of paying a fortune at the salon to get your curly hair cut? Want to try something new with your curls? Why not experiment with cutting layers into your curly hair at home? In this article, we'll show you how to cut layers into curly hair at home step-by-step.

How to Cut Layers into Curly Hair at Home

Curly hair can be a bit tricky to cut, but with the right tools and a little bit of patience, you can easily cut layers into your curly hair at home. Here's what you'll need:

  • Sharp haircutting scissors
  • A fine-tooth comb
  • A spray bottle filled with water
  • A mirror

Step-by-Step Guide

Here's how to cut layers into curly hair at home:

  1. Start by washing and conditioning your hair. This will help you get a better idea of how your curls will fall and where you need to cut.
  2. Once your hair is dry, comb it out with a fine-tooth comb. Make sure there are no tangles or knots.
  3. Now, divide your hair into sections. You can do this by parting your hair down the middle or into a side part, and then separating it into two sections: the top and the bottom.
  4. Take the top section of your hair and clip it up out of the way. This will allow you to focus on the bottom section first.
  5. Using your comb, take a small section of hair from the bottom and twist it tightly. This will help you see where the layers will fall and ensure that you don't cut too much off.
  6. Use your scissors to cut the hair at an angle. Start with a small amount and work your way up. Remember, you can always cut more, but you can't put hair back!
  7. Repeat this process with each section of hair, working your way up the back of your head.
  8. Once you've finished the bottom section, let down the top section and repeat the process.
  9. When you're finished cutting, take a look in the mirror and make any final adjustments to the layers.
  10. Style your hair as usual and enjoy your new layered look!

Tips for Cutting Layers into Curly Hair

Here are some tips to keep in mind when cutting layers into your curly hair:

  • Start small – you can always cut more off, but you can't put hair back on!
  • Twist your hair tightly before cutting to ensure that you don't cut too much off.
  • Take breaks if you need to – cutting your own hair can be tiring!
  • Don't cut too much off at once – it's better to take your time and cut small sections at a time.
  • Invest in a good pair of haircutting scissors – using regular scissors can damage your hair.

Frequently Asked Questions

Here are some common questions people have when cutting layers into curly hair:

Can I cut my curly hair when it's wet or dry?

You should always cut curly hair when it's dry. Wet hair can stretch and look longer, so cutting it when it's dry will give you a better idea of how the layers will fall.

How often should I cut my curly hair?

It's recommended that you get a trim every 6-8 weeks to keep your curls looking healthy and prevent split ends.

Can I cut layers into my curly hair if it's thin?

Absolutely! Cutting layers into thin curly hair can actually give the illusion of more volume and thickness.

Hair Care Tips for Curly Hair

Caring for curly hair can be a bit different than caring for straight hair. Here are some tips to help keep your curly hair looking its best:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to prevent your hair from drying out.
  • Avoid using a brush – use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your curls.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools – let your curls air dry and use a diffuser on your hair dryer to help enhance your curls.
  • Invest in a good leave-in conditioner or hair oil to help keep your curls moisturized.

Now that you know how to cut layers into curly hair at home, why not give it a try? With a little bit of practice, you'll be a pro at cutting your own hair in no time!
