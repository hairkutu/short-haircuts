What Is Graduated Bob Haircut

Written by Daniel Mar 30, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Welcome to our ultimate guide on the graduated bob haircut! If you're looking for a fresh and modern hairstyle, the graduated bob might be perfect for you. This trendy haircut is ideal for those who want to add volume to their hair and create a chic, edgy look. In this post, we'll cover everything you need to know about the graduated bob, from what it is to how to style it.

Description

The graduated bob is a type of short haircut that is shorter in the back and longer in the front. This haircut is also known as the "inverted bob" or "angled bob". The hair is cut at an angle, so the back section is shorter than the front section. This creates a graduated effect, hence the name. The graduated bob is suitable for all hair types and face shapes, making it a popular choice among women.

How to

If you're planning to get a graduated bob, here are the steps you need to follow:

Step 1: Consultation

Book a consultation with a professional hairstylist to discuss your hair type, face shape, and the desired length of your bob. This will help the hairstylist determine the best cut for you.

Step 2: Shampoo and Condition

Before the haircut, make sure your hair is clean and free of any tangles. Shampoo and condition your hair as usual, and then towel-dry it.

Step 3: Section your hair

Section your hair into four parts: the back, left side, right side, and the crown. Secure each section with hair clips.

Step 4: Cut the hair

Start with the back section and cut it at an angle, making it shorter than the front. Then, move on to the sides, and finally the crown. The hairstylist will use a combination of scissors and a razor to create the graduated effect.

Step 5: Style your hair

Once the haircut is complete, you can style your hair as desired. You can blow-dry it straight or add some curls for a more playful look.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when getting a graduated bob:

- Choose a hairstylist who has experience in cutting graduated bobs. - Bring a picture of the desired hairstyle to the consultation. - Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to add more volume to your hair. - Avoid using heavy styling products that can weigh down your hair. - Schedule regular trims to maintain the shape of your bob.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about the graduated bob:

Q: Is the graduated bob suitable for all face shapes?
A: Yes, the graduated bob is suitable for all face shapes. The hairstylist will adjust the length and angle of the cut to complement your facial features. Q: How long does the graduated bob last?
A: The graduated bob can last up to six weeks, depending on the rate of hair growth. Q: Can I style my graduated bob in different ways?
A: Yes, you can style your graduated bob in different ways. You can blow-dry it straight, add some curls, or create a messy, textured look.

Hair Care

Here are some hair care tips to keep your graduated bob looking great:

- Use a heat protectant spray before using hot styling tools. - Avoid washing your hair every day, as this can strip away natural oils. - Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair. - Apply a hair mask once a week to keep your hair hydrated and healthy. - Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a UV-protectant spray.

That's it for our guide on the graduated bob haircut! We hope you found this post informative and helpful. If you're thinking of getting a graduated bob, don't hesitate to consult with a professional hairstylist. Remember to take good care of your hair to keep it looking beautiful and healthy.
