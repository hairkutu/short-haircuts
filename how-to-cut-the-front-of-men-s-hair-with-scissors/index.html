How To Cut The Front Of Men s Hair With Scissors A Step By Step Guide

Written by Bardi Jun 16, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Are you looking to give your hair a fresh new look? Cutting your hair at home can be a great way to save money and personalize your style. But if you're new to cutting hair, it can be intimidating to know where to start. In this guide, we'll walk you through how to cut the front of men's hair with scissors. By following these simple steps, you can achieve a professional-looking cut from the comfort of your own home.

Description: What You'll Need

Before we dive into the step-by-step guide, let's go over what you'll need to get started. Here's a list of the essential tools:

  • Scissors
  • Comb
  • Hair clippers (optional)
  • Water spray bottle (optional)

Make sure you have a good pair of scissors specifically designed for cutting hair. A dull pair of scissors can result in uneven cuts and damage to your hair.

How to Cut the Front of Men's Hair with Scissors: Step-by-Step

Step 1: Start with Clean, Dry Hair

Before you start cutting, make sure your hair is clean and dry. Wet hair can be more difficult to cut and can result in uneven cuts. Use a shampoo and conditioner that works for your hair type, and make sure your hair is completely dry before you begin.

Step 2: Part Your Hair

Part your hair in the middle or on the side, depending on your preferred style. Use a comb to create a clean, straight part.

Step 3: Begin Cutting

Start by cutting small sections of hair at a time. Hold the hair between your fingers and use the scissors to cut the hair to your desired length. Make sure to cut straight across and avoid cutting at an angle.

Step 4: Check for Evenness

After each cut, check for evenness by comparing the length of the hair on either side of your part. If one side is longer than the other, make small adjustments until the length is even.

Step 5: Blend in the Sides

Once you've achieved your desired length in the front, it's time to blend in the sides. Use a comb to lift the hair on the sides and cut small sections of hair at a time to blend the front and sides together.

Step 6: Trim the Bangs

If you have bangs, trim them to your desired length. Use the comb to lift the bangs and cut them straight across. Start with small sections and make small adjustments until you've achieved your desired length.

Tips for Cutting Men's Hair with Scissors

  • Start with small sections of hair and make small adjustments to avoid cutting off too much at once.
  • Use a comb to create a clean, straight part.
  • Check for evenness after each cut to avoid uneven hair length.
  • Blend the front and sides together to create a seamless look.
  • Use hair clippers to trim the sides for a more polished look.
  • Use a water spray bottle to keep hair damp while cutting for easier handling.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I cut my hair with regular scissors?

No, you should use scissors specifically designed for cutting hair. Regular scissors can damage your hair and result in uneven cuts.

How often should I cut my hair?

It depends on your desired hairstyle and hair type. Generally, it's recommended to get a haircut every 4-6 weeks to maintain your style and keep your hair healthy.

How can I avoid cutting off too much hair?

Start with small sections of hair and make small adjustments as you go. It's better to cut off too little than too much.

Hair Care Tips

After cutting your hair, it's important to take care of it to maintain your new style. Here are a few tips:

  • Use a shampoo and conditioner that works for your hair type.
  • Avoid using hot tools like blow dryers and straighteners too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Trim your hair regularly to maintain your style and keep your hair healthy.

By following these tips and using the step-by-step guide, you can achieve a professional-looking haircut from home. Happy cutting!
