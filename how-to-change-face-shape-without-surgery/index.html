How To Change Face Shape Without Surgery A Complete Guide

Written by Frank Apr 12, 2023 Â· 10 min read

If you're looking to change your face shape without going under the knife, you're not alone. Many people want to enhance their features and achieve a more symmetrical look without resorting to surgery. Fortunately, there are several non-invasive methods you can use to change your face shape, from makeup tricks to exercises and haircuts. In this article, we'll cover everything you need to know to achieve the look you want.

Table of Contents

  • How to Change Face Shape Without Surgery
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Success
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care Tips

How to Change Face Shape Without Surgery

There are several ways to change your face shape without undergoing surgery. Here are some of the most effective methods:

Makeup

One of the easiest and quickest ways to change your face shape is through makeup. By using contouring techniques, you can create the illusion of a more defined jawline, slimmer nose, and higher cheekbones. Here are some tips for using makeup to change your face shape:

  • Use a contouring powder or cream that is one or two shades darker than your skin tone to create shadows where you want to create the illusion of depth.
  • Apply the contouring product along your jawline, under your cheekbones, and along the sides of your nose.
  • Blend the product well with a brush or sponge to create a natural-looking effect.
  • Use a highlighter product that is one or two shades lighter than your skin tone to accentuate the high points of your face, such as your cheekbones, brow bone, and the bridge of your nose.

Facial Exercises

Facial exercises can help tone and lift the muscles in your face, giving you a more sculpted look. Here are some exercises you can try:

  • Chew gum or chewy candy for a few minutes each day to work out the muscles in your jaw and cheeks.
  • Purse your lips together and suck in your cheeks to create a fish face. Hold for a few seconds and repeat several times.
  • Open your mouth wide and stick out your tongue as far as you can. Hold for a few seconds and repeat several times.
  • Place your index fingers on the corners of your mouth and press up towards your cheekbones. Hold for a few seconds and repeat several times.

Haircuts

The right haircut can also help change the shape of your face, creating the illusion of a more oval or angular shape. Here are some tips for choosing a haircut that flatters your face shape:

  • If you have a round face, opt for a long, layered haircut that falls below your chin. This will help elongate your face and create the illusion of a more oval shape.
  • If you have a square face, opt for a layered or textured haircut that softens your features. Avoid blunt cuts or straight bangs, which can make your face look boxy.
  • If you have a heart-shaped face, opt for a layered or textured bob that falls just below your chin. This will help balance out your features and create the illusion of a more oval shape.
  • If you have an oval face, you're lucky! Most haircuts will flatter your face shape. However, avoid hairstyles that are too long or too short, as they can make your face look disproportionate.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide to changing your face shape without surgery:

  1. Start by applying a primer to your face to create a smooth base for your makeup.
  2. Apply foundation and concealer as you normally would.
  3. Use a contouring powder or cream to create shadows along your jawline, under your cheekbones, and along the sides of your nose. Blend well with a brush or sponge.
  4. Apply a highlighter to the high points of your face, such as your cheekbones, brow bone, and the bridge of your nose.
  5. Try some facial exercises to tone and lift the muscles in your face. Chew gum, do fish faces, stick out your tongue, and press up on the corners of your mouth.
  6. Choose a haircut that flatters your face shape. If you're not sure what shape your face is, consult with a hairstylist.
  7. Style your hair in a way that accentuates your best features. Use a round brush to add volume or a straightening iron to create sleek, smooth locks.

Tips for Success

Here are some tips for achieving the best results when changing your face shape without surgery:

  • Practice your makeup techniques to create a natural-looking effect.
  • Be consistent with your facial exercises to see results.
  • Choose a haircut that flatters your face shape and hair type.
  • Use hair products that enhance your hair's texture and shine.
  • Don't be afraid to experiment with different styles until you find what works best for you.

FAQ

Can I really change my face shape without surgery?

Yes! There are several non-invasive methods you can use to change your face shape, including makeup, facial exercises, and haircuts.

How long does it take to see results from facial exercises?

It varies from person to person, but you can usually see results within a few weeks of consistent practice.

Do I need to use expensive makeup products to contour my face?

No! You can achieve great results with affordable makeup products from drugstore brands.

Hair Care Tips

Here are some tips for keeping your hair healthy and looking its best:

  • Wash your hair every two to three days to avoid stripping it of its natural oils.
  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid drying out your hair.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools every day. Use a heat protectant spray when you do use them.
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends and maintain healthy hair.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair moisturized and shiny.

By following these tips and techniques, you can change your face shape without surgery and achieve the look you want. Remember to be patient and consistent, and don't be afraid to experiment with different styles until you find what works best for you.
