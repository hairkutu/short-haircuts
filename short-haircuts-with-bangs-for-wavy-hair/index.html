Short Haircuts With Bangs For Wavy Hair

Written by Christine Mar 24, 2023 Â· 8 min read

Are you tired of your long wavy hair and want to try something new? Why not try a short haircut with bangs? This style is perfect for those who want a low-maintenance hairstyle that still looks chic and trendy. In this article, we will discuss various short haircuts with bangs for wavy hair and provide tips on how to care for your new hairstyle.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to
  • Step by Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

Short haircuts with bangs for wavy hair are a great way to add some edge to your look. Not only is this style low-maintenance, but it can also make your hair look fuller and healthier. There are various short haircuts to choose from, including pixie cuts, bob cuts, and shaggy cuts. The best part about this style is that it can work for any face shape and hair texture.

How to

If you're considering getting a short haircut with bangs for wavy hair, there are a few things to keep in mind. First, make sure you choose a hairstyle that complements your face shape. Second, consider your hair texture and choose a style that will work well with your natural waves. Finally, make sure you choose a length that you're comfortable with.

Face Shape

When choosing a short haircut with bangs, it's important to consider your face shape. If you have a round face, opt for a layered bob with side-swept bangs. If you have a square face, go for a short pixie cut with textured bangs. If you have an oval face, you're in luck because almost any short haircut with bangs will work for you.

Hair Texture

Short haircuts with bangs for wavy hair work well with natural waves. If you have straight hair, you may need to add some texture to your hair to make it work with the style. If your hair is naturally curly, you may need to straighten it before getting the haircut. Talk to your hairstylist about the best way to achieve the look you want.

Length

Before getting a short haircut with bangs for wavy hair, consider the length that you're comfortable with. If you're used to having long hair, a bob cut may be a good starting point. If you're feeling adventurous, a pixie cut may be the way to go. Remember that your hair will grow back, so don't be afraid to try something new.

Step by Step

Here's a step-by-step guide on how to get a short haircut with bangs for wavy hair:

  1. Choose a hairstyle that complements your face shape.
  2. Consider your hair texture and choose a style that will work well with your natural waves.
  3. Choose a length that you're comfortable with.
  4. Find a hairstylist who specializes in short haircuts with bangs.
  5. Show your hairstylist a picture of the style you want.
  6. Discuss any concerns you have with your hairstylist.
  7. Get your haircut and enjoy your new look!

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when getting a short haircut with bangs for wavy hair:

  • Invest in a good quality hair straightener to help tame your waves.
  • Consider using a texturizing spray to add volume and texture to your hair.
  • Use a heat protectant spray before using any heat styling tools.
  • Get regular trims to maintain the shape of your haircut.

FAQ

Will a short haircut with bangs work for my wavy hair?

Yes, short haircuts with bangs work well with wavy hair. Just make sure you choose a style that complements your natural waves.

How often should I get my haircut?

You should get a trim every 6 to 8 weeks to maintain the shape of your haircut.

What if I don't like my new haircut?

If you're not happy with your new haircut, talk to your hairstylist. They may be able to make some adjustments to the style to make it more to your liking.

Hair Care

Short haircuts with bangs for wavy hair are low-maintenance, but you still need to take care of your hair. Here are some hair care tips:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to prevent your hair from drying out.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often.
  • Apply a hair mask once a week to keep your hair hydrated.
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it's wet to prevent breakage.

Now that you know all about short haircuts with bangs for wavy hair, are you ready to try this trend? Remember to choose a style that complements your face shape and hair texture, and don't be afraid to try something new!
