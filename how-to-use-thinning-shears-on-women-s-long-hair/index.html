How To Use Thinning Shears On Women s Long Hair

Written by Eveline May 04, 2023 Â· 6 min read

In today's world of fashion and style, having long hair is not only trendy but also a sign of beauty. However, maintaining long hair can be challenging, especially when it comes to styling. This is where thinning shears come in handy. Thinning shears are scissors with teeth that are used to remove bulk from hair. In this blog post, we will discuss how to use thinning shears on women's long hair.

Description

Thinning shears are essential tools for hair stylists and individuals who want to maintain their long hair. They come in different sizes and shapes, but the most common ones have 30 to 40 teeth. The teeth are evenly spaced and come in different shapes, such as straight, curved, and V-shaped. Thinning shears are used to remove bulk from the hair, create texture, and blend layers.

How to Use Thinning Shears on Women's Long Hair

Step 1: Preparation

Before using thinning shears on your hair, it's essential to prepare it. Start by washing and conditioning your hair with a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner. Then, detangle your hair using a wide-tooth comb or a brush.

Step 2: Sectioning

The next step is to section your hair. Use hair clips to separate your hair into sections, starting from the back. You can create as many sections as you want, but four to six sections are ideal.

Step 3: Thinning

Take one section of your hair and comb it straight. Hold the thinning shears at a 45-degree angle and cut through the hair, starting from the mid-lengths to the ends. Repeat this process on all sections of your hair.

Step 4: Blending

After thinning your hair, it's time to blend the layers. Use regular scissors to cut any uneven strands and create a seamless look.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when using thinning shears on women's long hair:

  • Use sharp scissors to avoid damaging your hair
  • Start with small sections of hair to avoid cutting too much hair at once
  • Use thinning shears sparingly to avoid over-thinning your hair
  • Remember to blend the layers after thinning to create a natural look

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about using thinning shears on women's long hair:

Can I use thinning shears on wet hair?

No, it's not recommended to use thinning shears on wet hair as it can cause damage and breakage. Always use thinning shears on dry hair.

How often should I thin my hair?

It depends on your hair type and how fast it grows. Generally, it's recommended to thin your hair every six to eight weeks.

Hair Care

After using thinning shears on your hair, it's essential to take care of it. Use a moisturizing conditioner to keep your hair hydrated and avoid using heat styling tools too often. Also, protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a UV protectant spray.

Thinning shears are a great tool for maintaining long hair. By following these steps and tips, you can achieve a natural and seamless look while reducing the bulk of your hair. Remember to take care of your hair after thinning it to keep it healthy and shiny.
