Best Hairstyles For Small Round Faces

Written by William Apr 01, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Are you struggling to find the perfect hairstyle for your small round face? Don't worry, as we've got you covered! In this article, we'll be discussing the best hairstyles for small round faces, along with tips, step-by-step guides, and hair care advice. Let's get started!

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to
  • Step by Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

Small round faces are characterized by a shorter length and a wider width. The key to finding the perfect hairstyle for this face shape is to create an illusion of length and add volume to the crown area. Hairstyles that elongate the face and add height to the top are the best options for small round faces. Keep reading to find out our top picks!

How to

Before we dive into the hairstyles, let's discuss a few key tips to keep in mind when styling your hair:

  • Opt for hairstyles that add height to the crown area.
  • Avoid hairstyles that add width to the sides of your face.
  • Don't be afraid to add layers to your hair to create texture and volume.
  • Consider adding highlights or lowlights to create depth and dimension.

Step by Step

Now, onto the hairstyles! Here are our top picks:

1. Long Layers

Long layers are a great option for small round faces as they create the illusion of length. For this hairstyle, ask your stylist to create long layers that start at your chin and gradually get longer towards the ends. This will help to elongate your face and create movement in your hair.

2. Side-Swept Bangs

Side-swept bangs are another great option for small round faces. They help to create a diagonal line across the forehead, which elongates the face. To achieve this hairstyle, ask your stylist to cut your bangs at an angle, starting from the middle of your forehead and gradually getting longer towards the sides.

3. Bob with Volume

A bob with volume is a great option for small round faces as it adds height to the crown area. Ask your stylist to create a layered bob with volume at the top. You can also add some highlights or lowlights to create depth and dimension.

Tips

Here are a few more tips to keep in mind:

  • Avoid hairstyles that add volume to the sides of your face.
  • Consider adding a side part to create a diagonal line across your forehead.
  • Try out different hairstyles to see what works best for you.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about hairstyles for small round faces:

Q: Can I wear my hair in a middle part?

A: While a middle part can work for some small round faces, it's best to avoid it if you have a wider forehead or a rounder face.

Q: Should I avoid short hairstyles?

A: Not necessarily! While longer hairstyles tend to be more flattering for small round faces, short hairstyles can still work if they add height to the crown area.

Hair Care

When it comes to hair care, it's important to keep your hair healthy and hydrated. Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week, and avoid using heat styling tools too often. You should also trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends and breakage.

Now that you know the best hairstyles for small round faces, it's time to give them a try! Remember to keep these tips in mind and take good care of your hair for the best results.
