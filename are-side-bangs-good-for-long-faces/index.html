Are Side Bangs Good For Long Faces

Written by Eveline Mar 21, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Side bangs are a popular hairstyle that adds a touch of style and personality to any look. However, if you have a long face, you may be wondering if side bangs are right for you. In this article, we will explore whether side bangs are good for long faces, as well as some tips for maintaining and styling your hair.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to
  • Step by step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

Side bangs can be a great option for those with long faces, as they can help to balance out the length of the face. By adding some volume and framing the face, side bangs can help to create a more symmetrical and flattering look. However, it's important to choose the right type of side bangs for your face shape, as some styles may not be as flattering as others.

How to

If you're thinking about getting side bangs for your long face, there are a few things to keep in mind. First, consider the length and thickness of your hair, as well as your natural hair texture. You may want to opt for longer, wispy bangs if you have thin or fine hair, while thicker bangs can work well for those with thicker hair.

Next, think about the shape of your face. If you have a long face, you may want to avoid blunt, straight-across bangs, as these can make your face appear even longer. Instead, opt for side-swept bangs that are shorter on the sides and longer in the middle. This can help to create a more balanced and flattering look.

Step by step

Here are some simple steps for getting side bangs for your long face:

  1. Consult with a hairstylist to determine the best type of side bangs for your face shape and hair type.
  2. Decide on the length and thickness of your bangs.
  3. Ask your hairstylist to cut your bangs at an angle, starting shorter on the sides and gradually getting longer towards the middle.
  4. Style your new side bangs using a round brush and blow dryer, or use a flat iron for a sleeker look.

Tips

Here are some tips for maintaining and styling your side bangs:

  • Use a heat protectant spray before using any heat styling tools to prevent damage to your hair.
  • Trim your bangs every 4-6 weeks to keep them looking fresh and healthy.
  • Experiment with different parting styles to find the one that works best for your face shape.
  • Use a lightweight hair product, such as a texturizing spray or mousse, to add volume and texture to your bangs.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about side bangs for long faces:

Q: Will side bangs make my face look shorter?

A: Yes, side bangs can help to make your face appear shorter and more balanced.

Q: Can I still wear my hair up with side bangs?

A: Yes, you can still wear your hair up with side bangs. Simply pull your bangs back and secure them with bobby pins or a hair clip.

Q: How do I know if side bangs are right for me?

A: The best way to determine if side bangs are right for you is to consult with a hairstylist. They can help you choose the best type of bangs for your face shape and hair type.

Hair Care

When it comes to hair care for side bangs, it's important to keep your hair healthy and hydrated. Use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner, and consider using a hair mask once a week to nourish your hair. Avoid using heat styling tools too frequently, and always use a heat protectant spray when you do. And don't forget to trim your bangs regularly to keep them looking fresh and healthy!
