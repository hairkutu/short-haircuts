Which Way Do Curls Go The Ultimate Guide

Written by Aprilia Jul 01, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Curls have been a popular hairstyle for centuries. They can range from tight coils to loose waves, and can be achieved with a variety of methods. However, one question that often comes up is "which way do curls go?" In this article, we will dive into the answer to this question and provide tips and tricks for achieving the perfect curls.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How To
  • Step By Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

Curls can go in different directions depending on the desired look. Generally speaking, curls should be directed away from the face for a more open and voluminous look. However, curls can also be directed towards the face for a more romantic and intimate look.

There are different methods for achieving curls, such as using a curling iron, hot rollers, or braids. Each method will result in different curl directions, so it's important to choose the right method for the desired look.

How To

The first step in achieving curls is to choose the right method. For example, if you want loose waves, you can try braiding your hair overnight. If you want tight curls, you can use a curling iron. Once you have chosen the method, follow these steps:

  1. Section off your hair
  2. Apply heat protectant
  3. Curl or braid your hair
  4. Let your hair cool down
  5. Separate your curls with your fingers or a comb
  6. Apply hairspray or other styling products as desired

Step By Step

Let's break down the steps in more detail:

Section off your hair

Start by sectioning off your hair into manageable pieces. This will make it easier to curl or braid your hair without missing any sections. Use clips or hair ties to hold each section in place.

Apply heat protectant

Before applying heat to your hair, it's important to apply a heat protectant to prevent damage. This can be in the form of a spray, cream, or serum. Apply the product evenly throughout your hair.

Curl or braid your hair

Using your chosen method, curl or braid your hair. Be sure to follow the instructions carefully to achieve the desired result. For example, if you're using a curling iron, wrap your hair around the barrel and hold for a few seconds before releasing.

Let your hair cool down

Once you've finished curling or braiding your hair, let it cool down completely. This will help set the curls and prevent them from falling out. You can speed up the cooling process by using a cool shot button on your hair dryer.

Separate your curls with your fingers or a comb

After your hair has cooled down, use your fingers or a wide-toothed comb to separate your curls. This will create more volume and texture. Be gentle to avoid disrupting the curls too much.

Apply hairspray or other styling products as desired

To finish off your look, apply hairspray or other styling products as desired. This will help hold your curls in place and add shine.

Tips

  • For a more natural look, vary the direction of your curls
  • Use a smaller barrel curling iron for tighter curls
  • Use a larger barrel curling iron for looser waves
  • Don't forget to apply heat protectant before using any heat styling tool
  • Experiment with different methods to find the one that works best for your hair type and desired look

FAQ

Which way should I curl my hair for a more voluminous look?

Curls should be directed away from the face for a more open and voluminous look.

Which way should I curl my hair for a more romantic look?

Curls can be directed towards the face for a more romantic and intimate look.

Hair Care

To keep your curls looking their best, it's important to take care of your hair. This includes using a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner, avoiding heat styling tools too often, and using a deep conditioning treatment once a week. You can also use a leave-in conditioner or oil to keep your curls hydrated and frizz-free.

Overall, achieving the perfect curls depends on your hair type, desired look, and chosen method. With the right tools and techniques, you can create beautiful curls that last all day.
