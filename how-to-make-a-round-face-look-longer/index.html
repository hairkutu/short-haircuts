How To Make A Round Face Look Longer

Written by William May 23, 2023 Â· 9 min read

If you have a round face, you might feel like it looks a bit too full or chubby. But don't worry - there are plenty of ways to make a round face look longer and more chiseled. In this article, we'll go over some tips, tricks, and hairstyles that can help you achieve the look you're going for.

Description

A round face is typically characterized by full cheeks, a soft jawline, and a wide forehead. While there's nothing inherently wrong with having a round face shape, some people might feel like it makes them look younger or less defined than they'd like. By implementing a few simple strategies, you can create the illusion of a longer, more angular face shape.

How To

There are a few different techniques you can use to elongate your face shape:

1. Contouring

Contouring is a makeup technique that involves using darker shades to create shadows and depth on the face. By strategically applying contour powder or cream to the sides of your forehead, temples, cheekbones, and jawline, you can create the illusion of a more angular face shape. Just be sure to blend everything out well so there are no harsh lines.

2. Hairstyles

The right hairstyle can also help elongate your face shape. Try opting for styles that add volume at the crown of your head and keep your hair sleek and straight along your jawline. Long layers and side-swept bangs can also help create the illusion of a longer face.

3. Accessories

Believe it or not, accessories can also help elongate your face shape. Long, dangling earrings can draw attention to your neck and elongate your profile, while statement necklaces can create a similar effect.

Step by Step

If you're looking to elongate your face shape, follow these steps:

1. Start with a clean, moisturized face.

Before you begin contouring or applying makeup, make sure your face is clean and hydrated. This will help your makeup go on smoothly and evenly.

2. Apply foundation as usual.

Apply your usual foundation or tinted moisturizer all over your face, making sure to blend everything out well.

3. Apply contour powder or cream.

Using a contour brush or your fingers, apply a darker shade of powder or cream to the sides of your forehead, temples, cheekbones, and jawline. Be sure to blend everything out well so there are no harsh lines.

4. Apply highlighter.

Apply a lighter shade of powder or cream to the high points of your face, such as your cheekbones, brow bone, and nose. This will help create contrast and make your face look more chiseled.

5. Finish with a setting spray.

Finish off your makeup look with a setting spray to ensure everything stays in place all day.

Tips

Here are a few additional tips to keep in mind when trying to elongate your face shape:

1. Avoid round glasses.

If you wear glasses, try to avoid round frames, as they can accentuate the roundness of your face. Instead, opt for angular or rectangular frames that will help balance out your face shape.

2. Experiment with different hairstyles.

Don't be afraid to try out different hairstyles until you find one that flatters your face shape. Play around with volume, layers, and bangs until you find the perfect look.

3. Embrace your natural beauty.

Remember that there's nothing wrong with having a round face shape - in fact, many people find it to be quite attractive. Don't feel like you need to drastically alter your appearance in order to feel confident in your own skin.

FAQ

1. Can I still contour if I have a round face?

Absolutely! Contouring can be a great way to add depth and definition to your face shape, regardless of your natural features.

2. What hairstyles should I avoid if I have a round face?

Try to avoid hairstyles that are too short or blunt, as they can make your face appear rounder. Instead, opt for styles that add volume and length.

3. Should I use a matte or shimmery highlighter?

For the most natural look, it's best to use a matte highlighter that's only a shade or two lighter than your skin tone. Shimmery highlighters can look too harsh and obvious.

Hair Care

In addition to the tips and tricks outlined above, taking good care of your hair can also help elongate your face shape. Here are a few hair care tips to keep in mind:

1. Use volumizing products.

Products that add volume and lift to your hair can help create the illusion of a longer face shape. Look for volumizing shampoos, conditioners, and styling products.

2. Avoid heavy styling products.

Using too much heavy product can weigh down your hair and make your face appear rounder. Stick to lightweight styling products that won't weigh your hair down.

3. Get regular trims.

Regular trims can help keep your hair looking healthy and prevent split ends, which can make your hair look limp and lifeless.

By following these tips and tricks, you can make a round face look longer and more defined. Remember, though, that there's nothing wrong with having a round face shape - so don't feel like you need to change yourself in order to feel beautiful.
