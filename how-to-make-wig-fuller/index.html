How To Make Your Wig Fuller A Step By Step Guide

Written by Christine Jun 27, 2023 · 7 min read

Are you tired of your wig looking flat and lifeless? Do you want to add some volume and fullness to your hairpiece? Look no further! In this article, we’ll show you how to make your wig fuller with some easy tips and tricks. From teasing to using volumizing products, we’ve got you covered. Let’s get started!

Table of Contents

  • Step 1: Wash Your Wig
  • Step 2: Use Volumizing Products
  • Step 3: Tease Your Wig
  • Step 4: Add Hair Extensions
  • Tips for Making Your Wig Fuller
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care for Your Wig

Step 1: Wash Your Wig

The first step to making your wig fuller is to wash it. Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner specifically designed for wigs. Avoid using hot water or harsh chemicals, as these can damage the wig’s fibers and cause it to lose its shape. After washing, gently squeeze out any excess water and lay the wig flat to dry on a towel.

Step 2: Use Volumizing Products

Once your wig is dry, it’s time to add some volume. Apply a volumizing mousse or spray to the roots of your wig, then use a wide-tooth comb to distribute the product evenly throughout the hair. This will help to lift the hair and add some fullness. Be sure to use products that are specifically designed for wigs, as regular hair products can be too harsh and cause damage.

Step 3: Tease Your Wig

If you want to add even more volume, you can tease your wig. Take small sections of hair and backcomb them at the roots with a fine-tooth comb. This will create a fuller, more voluminous look. Be sure to tease gently, as too much teasing can cause damage to the wig’s fibers.

Step 4: Add Hair Extensions

If you want to add length and fullness to your wig, you can use hair extensions. Choose extensions that match the color and texture of your wig, then clip them in at the roots. This will give your wig a fuller, more natural look. Be sure to blend the extensions with your wig’s natural hair for a seamless finish.

Tips for Making Your Wig Fuller

Here are some additional tips for making your wig look fuller:

  • Use a wig cap to keep your natural hair flat and smooth, which will help your wig sit more securely and look fuller.
  • Avoid using heavy styling products, which can weigh down your wig and make it look flat.
  • Use a blow dryer on a cool setting to create more volume.
  • Invest in a high-quality wig that is made with real human hair, as these wigs tend to look more natural and full.

FAQs

Q: Can I use regular hair products on my wig?

A: No, it’s best to use products that are specifically designed for wigs. Regular hair products can be too harsh and cause damage to the wig’s fibers.

Q: How often should I wash my wig?

A: It depends on how often you wear your wig. If you wear it every day, you should wash it once a week. If you wear it less frequently, you can wash it every 2-3 weeks.

Q: Can I use heat styling tools on my wig?

A: It depends on the wig’s fibers. Synthetic wigs cannot be heat styled, as the fibers will melt. However, some human hair wigs can be heat styled, but you should use a heat protectant spray and a low heat setting to avoid damage.

Hair Care for Your Wig

To keep your wig looking full and beautiful, it’s important to take good care of it. Here are some tips:

  • Store your wig on a wig stand or mannequin head to help it retain its shape.
  • Avoid exposing your wig to heat and direct sunlight, as this can cause damage.
  • Use a wig brush or wide-tooth comb to gently detangle your wig, starting at the ends and working your way up.
  • Avoid sleeping in your wig, as this can cause tangling and matting.

With these tips and tricks, you can make your wig look fuller and more voluminous. Whether you’re wearing a wig for medical reasons or simply for fashion, these techniques will help you achieve the look you want. Have fun experimenting with different styles and products, and don’t be afraid to get creative!
