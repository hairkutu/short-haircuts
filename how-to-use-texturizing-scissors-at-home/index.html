How To Use Texturizing Scissors At Home A Step By Step Guide

Written by Oliver Apr 27, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Are you tired of the same old hairstyle but can't afford to go to a salon for a new look? Texturizing scissors might be the answer you're looking for! With these specialized scissors, you can add texture and dimension to your hair without having to spend a fortune. In this guide, we'll teach you how to use texturizing scissors at home, so you can achieve a fresh new look on your own.

Table of Contents

  2. How to
  3. Step by Step
  4. Tips

Description

Texturizing scissors, also known as thinning scissors, are designed to remove bulk from hair without altering the overall length. They have teeth on one blade and a straight edge on the other, which allows them to cut hair in a way that creates texture and movement. Texturizing scissors can be used on any hair type, but they are particularly useful for thick or coarse hair that tends to look heavy and flat.

How to

Before you start using texturizing scissors, it's important to have the right tools on hand. You will need a comb, hair clips, and a good pair of texturizing scissors. You can find texturizing scissors at most beauty supply stores or online. Once you have your tools, follow these steps:

Step by Step

  1. Start with clean, dry hair. You can wash and blow-dry your hair before you begin, but make sure it's completely dry before you start cutting.
  2. Section your hair. Use your comb to section your hair into manageable pieces. Clip the top layers out of the way so you can focus on the bottom layers first.
  3. Take a small section of hair. Hold the section of hair between your index and middle fingers, about 2-3 inches away from your scalp.
  4. Use the texturizing scissors. Hold the texturizing scissors vertically, with the teeth facing up. Start at the middle of the section of hair and work your way down to the ends, making small snips along the way. Be sure to close the scissors after each snip to avoid creating an uneven cut.
  5. Repeat. Move on to the next section of hair and repeat the process until you've texturized all of your hair.
  6. Blend the layers. Once you've finished texturizing your hair, use your regular scissors to blend the layers together. This will create a more natural look and prevent any choppy lines.
  7. Style as desired. Now that you've texturized your hair, you can style it as you normally would. You should notice more movement and texture in your hair, which will give it a fresh new look.

Tips

Here are some additional tips to keep in mind when using texturizing scissors:

  • Start with small snips. It's better to take off too little at first than to take off too much.
  • Work in sections. Take your time and work in small sections to ensure a more even cut.
  • Use a light touch. Texturizing scissors are designed to remove bulk, so you don't need to apply a lot of pressure.

FAQ

1. Can I use texturizing scissors on wet hair?

No, it's best to use texturizing scissors on dry hair. Wet hair is more elastic and can stretch, which can lead to an uneven cut.

2. How often should I use texturizing scissors?

This depends on your hair type and how quickly it grows. Generally, you can use texturizing scissors every 6-8 weeks to maintain your style.

3. Can I use texturizing scissors on my own hair?

Yes, but it can be tricky to use texturizing scissors on the back of your head. It's best to ask a friend or family member for help.

Hair Care

Texturizing scissors can be a great way to add texture and movement to your hair, but it's important to take care of your hair afterwards. Here are some tips:

  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy and moisturized.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as this can damage your hair.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair looking healthy and prevent split ends.

Now that you know how to use texturizing scissors at home, you can experiment with different styles and textures on your own. With a little patience and practice, you can achieve a salon-quality look without ever leaving your house!
