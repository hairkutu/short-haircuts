How To Style Long Pixie Cut Messy A Step By Step Guide

Written by Petter Sep 09, 2023 Â· 7 min read

If you're looking for a low-maintenance, stylish haircut that accentuates your features, the long pixie cut messy style is a perfect choice. This versatile hairstyle can be messy, edgy, or chic depending on your mood and occasion. In this guide, we'll show you how to style long pixie cut messy in a few simple steps. Let's get started!

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to Style Long Pixie Cut Messy
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

The long pixie cut messy style is a shorter version of the classic pixie cut. It typically features longer layers on the top of the head that can be styled in a messy or tousled look. This hairstyle is perfect for those who want to add some edge to their look while still keeping it low-maintenance.

How to Style Long Pixie Cut Messy

Styling a long pixie cut messy is easier than you might think. All you need is a few styling products and some creativity. Here are the basic steps:

Step-by-Step Guide

  1. Start with clean, dry hair.
  2. Apply a dime-sized amount of styling cream or serum to your fingertips and work it through your hair, focusing on the ends.
  3. If your hair is naturally curly or wavy, use a diffuser attachment on your blow dryer to dry your hair. If your hair is straight, you can let it air dry or blow dry it with a round brush to add volume.
  4. Once your hair is dry, use a curling iron or flat iron to add some texture to the top layers of your hair. You can create loose waves or curls, or simply add some bends to create a messy look.
  5. Use your fingers to tousle your hair and create a messy look. You can also use a texturizing spray or dry shampoo to add more volume and texture.
  6. Finish with a light hairspray to hold your style in place.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you achieve the perfect long pixie cut messy style:

  • Experiment with different styling products to find what works best for your hair type and texture.
  • Don't be afraid to use your fingers to create a messy look. Sometimes the best styles come from embracing imperfection.
  • Use a heat protectant spray before using any hot styling tools to protect your hair from damage.
  • Consider getting regular trims to keep your long pixie cut looking fresh and healthy.

FAQ

1. Will a long pixie cut messy style work for my face shape?

Yes! The long pixie cut messy style works well for a variety of face shapes, including oval, round, and heart-shaped faces. It's all about finding the right length and layers to accentuate your features.

2. Can I still style my long pixie cut messy if my hair is thin?

Absolutely! In fact, styling your hair in a messy look can actually create the illusion of more volume and thickness. Just be sure to use lightweight styling products and avoid weighing your hair down with heavy oils or creams.

3. How often should I wash my hair if I have a long pixie cut messy style?

It depends on your hair type and personal preference. If you have oily hair, you may need to wash it every other day to keep it looking fresh. If you have dry hair, you can go longer between washes. Just be sure to use a gentle shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy.

Hair Care

To keep your long pixie cut messy style looking its best, it's important to take good care of your hair. Here are some tips:

  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner that are formulated for your hair type.
  • Avoid using hot styling tools too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends and keep your hair looking healthy.
  • Eat a healthy diet and stay hydrated to promote healthy hair growth.

With these tips and tricks, you'll be able to style your long pixie cut messy in no time. Whether you're going for a casual or edgy look, this hairstyle is sure to turn heads.
