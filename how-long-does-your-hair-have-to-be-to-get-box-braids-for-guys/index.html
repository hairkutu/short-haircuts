How Long Does Your Hair Have To Be To Get Box Braids For Guys

Written by Frank Aug 10, 2023 · 6 min read

Box braids are a popular protective hairstyle for both men and women. They are low-maintenance and can last for weeks, making them perfect for those who want to give their hair a break from daily styling. But how long does your hair have to be to get box braids for guys? In this article, we will answer this question and provide you with some tips and tricks on how to care for your box braids.

Table of Contents

  • How to Get Box Braids for Guys
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Maintaining Box Braids
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care for Box Braids

How to Get Box Braids for Guys

Box braids are a versatile hairstyle that can be customized to suit your personal style. Before you get box braids, you need to decide on the length and thickness of your braids. The length of your hair will determine how long your braids can be.

If you have short hair, you may not be able to get long box braids. Typically, your hair should be at least 2-3 inches long to get box braids. If your hair is too short, the braids may not stay in place and may fall out easily.

If you have longer hair, you can get box braids of any length. However, keep in mind that longer braids may take longer to install and may be more difficult to maintain.

Step-by-Step Guide

Getting box braids for guys is a fairly simple process. Here are the steps:

  1. Wash and condition your hair thoroughly.
  2. Detangle your hair with a comb or brush.
  3. Section your hair into small parts using a rat-tail comb.
  4. Take a small section of hair and divide it into three equal parts.
  5. Braid the hair by crossing the left section over the middle section, then the right section over the middle section.
  6. Add extensions to the braid if desired.
  7. Continue braiding until you reach the end of the section.
  8. Secure the end of the braid with a rubber band or hair tie.
  9. Repeat the process on the remaining sections of your hair.

Tips for Maintaining Box Braids

Box braids require very little maintenance, but there are a few things you can do to keep them looking fresh:

  • Wrap your hair in a silk scarf or bonnet at night to prevent frizz.
  • Avoid using heavy products that can weigh down your braids.
  • Wash your hair every 2-3 weeks to prevent buildup.
  • Apply oil to your scalp to keep it moisturized.
  • Avoid pulling or tugging on your braids, as this can cause breakage.

FAQs

Q: How long do box braids last?

A: Box braids can last anywhere from 4-8 weeks, depending on how well you care for them.

Q: Can I swim with box braids?

A: Yes, you can swim with box braids. Just be sure to wear a swim cap and rinse your hair with fresh water after swimming to remove any chlorine or salt.

Q: Can box braids cause hair loss?

A: Box braids themselves do not cause hair loss, but if they are installed too tightly or not cared for properly, they can lead to hair breakage and damage.

Hair Care for Box Braids

Box braids require minimal hair care, but there are a few things you can do to keep your hair healthy:

  • Wash your hair every 2-3 weeks with a moisturizing shampoo.
  • Apply oil to your scalp to keep it moisturized.
  • Avoid using heavy products that can weigh down your braids.
  • Avoid pulling or tugging on your braids, as this can cause breakage.
  • Treat any itching or dandruff with a medicated shampoo or scalp treatment.

Now that you know how long your hair should be to get box braids for guys, you can decide if this hairstyle is right for you. Remember to take good care of your braids to keep them looking fresh and healthy.
