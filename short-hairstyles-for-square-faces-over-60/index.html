Short Hairstyles For Square Faces Over 60 A Guide

Written by Oliver Jul 25, 2023 Â· 7 min read

If you have a square face and are over 60, finding the perfect hairstyle can be a challenge. However, there are plenty of short hairstyles that can flatter your face shape and complement your age. In this guide, we will provide you with tips and suggestions on how to choose the perfect short hairstyle for square faces over 60.

Description

A square face has a strong jawline, a wide forehead, and a square chin. If you have a square face, you want to choose hairstyles that soften your features and create a more oval shape. Short hairstyles are a great option because they draw attention to your eyes and cheekbones while minimizing your jawline.

How To

When choosing a short hairstyle, you want to consider the length, texture, and layers. Here are some tips:

Length

Short haircuts that sit above the chin are ideal for square faces because they help to soften the jawline. However, you may want to avoid a super short pixie cut, as it can accentuate your square jawline. Instead, opt for a longer bob or a layered cut that falls just below the chin.

Texture

Adding texture to your hair can help to soften your face shape. Consider adding waves or curls to your hair, or opt for a choppy, layered cut. However, if you have thick hair, you may want to avoid too much texture as it can add volume to your hair and make your face appear wider.

Layers

Layers can help to create movement and add volume to your hair. However, be cautious of adding too many layers as it can make your hair appear thin and flat. Ask your hairstylist for subtle, face-framing layers that will complement your face shape.

Step by Step

Here are some steps to follow when choosing a short hairstyle for square faces over 60:

  1. Research different short hairstyles that flatter square faces.
  2. Consider your hair type and texture.
  3. Consult with your hairstylist to determine the best style for you.
  4. Bring pictures of hairstyles you like to your appointment.
  5. Ask your hairstylist for a cut that complements your face shape and age.
  6. Consider adding subtle layers or texture to your hair.
  7. Experiment with different styling techniques to find what works best for you.
  8. Maintain regular haircuts to keep your style looking fresh.

Tips

Here are some additional tips to keep in mind when choosing a short hairstyle for square faces over 60:

  • Consider your lifestyle and how much time you want to spend styling your hair.
  • Choose a color that complements your skin tone and brings out your features.
  • Accessorize with earrings or necklaces that draw attention to your face.
  • Use styling products that add volume and texture to your hair.
  • Avoid hairstyles that are too severe or blunt, as they can accentuate your square jawline.

FAQ

What is the best short hairstyle for square faces over 60?

The best short hairstyle for a square face over 60 is one that softens your features and creates an oval shape. Consider a longer bob or a layered cut that falls just below the chin. Adding texture and subtle layers can also help to create movement and volume.

What should I avoid when choosing a short hairstyle for a square face over 60?

Avoid hairstyles that are too short or too blunt, as they can accentuate your square jawline. Also, avoid hairstyles that are too voluminous, as they can make your face appear wider. Instead, opt for hairstyles that are soft and feminine.

Hair Care

To keep your short hairstyle looking its best, it's important to take care of your hair. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your hair with a gentle, sulfate-free shampoo.
  • Use a moisturizing conditioner to keep your hair soft and hydrated.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as it can cause damage.
  • Use a heat protectant spray if you do use heat styling tools.
  • Get regular haircuts to maintain your style and keep your ends healthy.

Now that you have all the information you need, go ahead and try out a new short hairstyle for your square face over 60. Remember to choose a style that makes you feel confident and beautiful!
