How To Control Hair Fall Naturally At Home In Tamil

Written by Eveline Aug 29, 2023 · 8 min read

Are you tired of seeing hair fall every time you comb or brush your hair? Don't worry, you're not alone. Hair fall is a common problem that affects both men and women. If you're looking for natural ways to control hair fall, then you've come to the right place. In this article, we'll be discussing how to control hair fall naturally at home in Tamil.

Causes of Hair Fall

Before we discuss how to control hair fall naturally at home in Tamil, let's first understand the main causes of hair fall.

1. Genetics: One of the main causes of hair fall is genetics. If your parents or grandparents have a history of hair fall, then you're more likely to experience it as well.

2. Hormonal Changes: Hormonal changes can also lead to hair fall. This is why women often experience hair fall during pregnancy and menopause.

3. Stress: Stress can cause hair fall by disrupting the hair growth cycle.

4. Nutritional Deficiencies: Nutritional deficiencies, such as iron deficiency, can also lead to hair fall.

How to Prevent Hair Fall Naturally

Now that we know the main causes of hair fall, let's discuss how to prevent it naturally.

1. Maintain a Healthy Diet: Eating a balanced diet that is rich in vitamins and minerals can help prevent hair fall.

2. Reduce Stress: Reducing stress through exercise, meditation, or other relaxation techniques can help prevent hair fall.

3. Avoid Harsh Chemicals: Harsh chemicals, such as those found in hair dyes and styling products, can damage hair and lead to hair fall.

4. Use Natural Hair Care Products: Using natural hair care products, such as those made with aloe vera or coconut oil, can help prevent hair fall.

Step by Step Guide on Controlling Hair Fall at Home in Tamil

Here's a step by step guide on how to control hair fall naturally at home in Tamil.

Step 1: Oil Massage

Massaging your scalp with warm oil can help improve blood circulation and strengthen hair roots. You can use coconut oil, olive oil, or any other natural oil for this. Simply warm the oil and apply it to your scalp, massaging gently for 10-15 minutes. Leave it on for at least an hour before washing it off with a mild shampoo.

Step 2: Aloe Vera

Aloe vera is known for its hair-strengthening properties. You can either apply fresh aloe vera gel directly to your scalp or mix it with coconut oil and massage it into your scalp. Leave it on for 30 minutes before washing it off with a mild shampoo.

Step 3: Egg Mask

Eggs are rich in protein, which is essential for hair growth. To make an egg mask, beat an egg and apply it to your scalp and hair. Leave it on for 30 minutes before washing it off with a mild shampoo.

Step 4: Curry Leaves

Curry leaves are known for their hair-strengthening properties. Boil a handful of curry leaves in coconut oil and apply the mixture to your scalp. Leave it on overnight and wash it off with a mild shampoo in the morning.

Tips for Controlling Hair Fall

Here are some additional tips for controlling hair fall naturally at home in Tamil.

1. Avoid tight hairstyles that pull on your hair, such as ponytails and braids.

2. Use a wide-toothed comb to brush your hair, as it will be gentler on your hair.

3. Don't wash your hair too frequently, as it can strip your hair of its natural oils.

4. Use a mild shampoo and conditioner that is suitable for your hair type.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Can stress cause hair fall?

A: Yes, stress can cause hair fall by disrupting the hair growth cycle.

Q: Can nutritional deficiencies cause hair fall?

A: Yes, nutritional deficiencies, such as iron deficiency, can lead to hair fall.

Q: Is it normal to lose hair every day?

A: Yes, it is normal to lose 50-100 hairs per day.

Final Thoughts

Hair fall can be a frustrating problem, but it's not one that you have to live with. By following these natural remedies and tips, you can control hair fall and promote healthy hair growth. Remember to be patient, as it may take some time before you see results.
