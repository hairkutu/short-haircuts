How To Cut The Back Of Your Own Hair A Step By Step Guide

Written by Eveline Aug 01, 2023 · 6 min read

Are you tired of spending money on salon appointments just to trim the back of your hair? Fear not! With a little practice and the right tools, you can easily cut the back of your own hair at home. In this article, we’ll walk you through the process step by step, including tips and tricks to make sure you achieve the perfect cut every time.

How to

Before you start cutting your hair, you’ll need to gather the necessary tools. Here’s what you’ll need:

  • Scissors
  • Comb
  • Clippers (optional)
  • Handheld mirror
  • Hair ties or clips

Once you have all the tools, you’re ready to start. Here’s how:

  1. Wash and dry your hair, and comb it out thoroughly to remove any tangles.
  2. Divide your hair into two sections, using a hair tie or clip to hold one section out of the way.
  3. Use your handheld mirror to check the back of your head, making sure you can see the entire area you want to trim.
  4. Take a small section of hair from the back, starting at the nape of your neck. Use your comb to hold the hair taut, and trim the ends using your scissors.
  5. Move on to the next section, working your way up your head. Remember to keep checking your progress in the mirror.
  6. If you want a shorter cut, you can use clippers to trim the hair. Start with a longer guard and work your way down until you reach the desired length.
  7. Once you’ve finished one side, repeat the process on the other side.
  8. Check the back of your head again in the mirror to make sure both sides are even.
  9. Style your hair as desired.

Tips

Here are some additional tips to help you achieve the perfect cut:

  • Don’t rush the process. Take your time to ensure you’re cutting the hair evenly.
  • Start with small sections of hair to avoid cutting too much at once.
  • If you’re using clippers, make sure they’re sharp and well-oiled.
  • Use a comb to hold the hair taut while cutting to ensure a straight line.
  • Have someone else check the back of your head if you’re having trouble seeing it in the mirror.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about cutting the back of your own hair:

  • Is it easy to cut the back of your own hair?
  • With practice, cutting the back of your own hair can be easy. It may take a few tries to get it right, but once you’ve mastered the technique, it’ll become second nature.
  • What if I make a mistake?
  • If you accidentally cut too much hair, don’t panic. You can always trim the rest of your hair to match. If you’re really unhappy with the result, you can always visit a professional stylist to fix it.
  • What if I have curly hair?
  • Cutting curly hair can be a bit more challenging, as the curls can bounce up after being cut. It’s best to cut curly hair when it’s dry, and to be conservative with the amount you’re cutting.

Hair Care

Remember that cutting your own hair can be damaging if you’re not using the right tools or techniques. Make sure your scissors are sharp and clean, and use hair-specific scissors rather than regular household scissors. Avoid cutting too much hair at once, as this can cause split ends and breakage.

Now that you know how to cut the back of your own hair, you can save time and money by doing it yourself. With a little practice and patience, you’ll be a pro in no time!
