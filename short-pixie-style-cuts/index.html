Short Pixie Style Cuts A Guide To The Latest Trend In Haircuts

Written by Luffy Jun 29, 2023 Â· 6 min read

If you're looking for a new hairstyle that is both chic and low-maintenance, you might want to consider short pixie style cuts. This haircut has been popular for several years now and shows no signs of losing its appeal. In this article, we'll give you a complete guide on how to achieve this look, as well as some tips on how to maintain it.

Description

A short pixie cut is a hairstyle that is short on the sides and back while being slightly longer on top. The hair on the top is usually styled to look messy and textured, giving the overall look a casual and carefree vibe. This cut is perfect for women who are looking for a low-maintenance hairstyle that is also trendy and stylish.

How to

Step 1: Choose the Right Length

The first step in achieving a short pixie cut is choosing the right length. Your stylist will help you determine the best length based on the shape of your face and the texture of your hair. It's important to choose a length that will flatter your features and work well with your hair type.

Step 2: Choose the Right Style

Once you've chosen the right length, it's time to decide on the style. Short pixie cuts can be styled in many different ways, from messy and textured to sleek and polished. Your stylist will help you choose a style that will work best for your face shape and hair type.

Step 3: Maintain Your Pixie Cut

Maintaining a short pixie cut is relatively easy. You'll need to get regular trims to keep the shape of the cut looking fresh, and you'll also need to style your hair daily. A good quality styling product, such as a texturizing spray or a pomade, can help you achieve the look you want with minimal effort.

Tips

Here are some additional tips to help you maintain your short pixie cut:

  • Invest in a good quality styling product
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair looking fresh
  • Experiment with different styles to find the one that works best for you
  • Consider getting highlights or lowlights to add dimension to your hair

FAQ

Q: Will a short pixie cut work for my face shape?

A: Short pixie cuts can work for many different face shapes. It's important to work with a stylist who can help you choose the right length and style for your features.

Q: Will a short pixie cut work for my hair type?

A: Short pixie cuts can work for many different hair types. Your stylist can help you choose the right style and product to achieve the look you want.

Q: How often do I need to get a trim?

A: It's recommended that you get a trim every 4-6 weeks to keep your short pixie cut looking fresh.

Hair Care

Maintaining a short pixie cut requires some basic hair care. Here are some tips to keep your hair healthy:

  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner
  • Avoid using hot tools on your hair
  • Use a heat protectant when using hot tools
  • Consider using a hair mask once a week to keep your hair hydrated

With these tips and tricks, you'll be able to achieve and maintain a short pixie cut that looks great and feels effortless. Whether you're looking for a low-maintenance hairstyle or a trendy and stylish look, a short pixie cut might be just the thing you need.
