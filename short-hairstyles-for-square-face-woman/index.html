Short Hairstyles For Square Face Woman A Guide To Flattering Cuts

Written by William May 07, 2023 Â· 8 min read

If you have a square face shape, you may feel limited in your hairstyle options. But fear not! There are plenty of short hairstyles that can flatter your features and bring out your best qualities. In this guide, we'll go over some of the best short hairstyles for square face women, as well as tips on how to care for your hair.

Description

If you have a square face shape, you have a strong, angular jawline and your forehead, cheekbones, and jaw are all roughly the same width. This can make it difficult to find a hairstyle that balances out your features. However, by choosing the right cut, you can soften your jawline and bring out your best features.

How to Choose the Right Cut

When it comes to choosing a short hairstyle for a square face, you want to look for cuts that add volume to the top of your head and soften your jawline. Here are some things to keep in mind:

Texture

Adding texture to your hair can help soften your features. Consider a layered cut or a shaggy bob to add movement and volume to your hair.

Length

Short hairstyles that hit above or below the jawline can help balance out your face shape. A pixie cut, for example, can draw attention to your eyes and cheekbones.

Bangs

Bangs can help soften a square jawline. Consider a side-swept fringe or a long, wispy bang.

Step by Step: Short Hairstyles for Square Face Woman

Now that you know what to look for in a short hairstyle, let's take a look at some specific cuts that flatter square face shapes:

Pixie Cut

The pixie cut is a classic short hairstyle that can work well for square face shapes. To achieve this look:

  1. Ask your stylist for a short cut that hits above the jawline.
  2. Add texture with a razor or shears.
  3. Consider adding bangs to soften your features.

Bob

A bob can be a great option for square face shapes, as long as it's cut to hit above or below the jawline. To achieve this look:

  1. Ask your stylist for a bob that hits above or below the jawline.
  2. Add layers to add movement and texture.
  3. Consider adding side-swept bangs.

Short Shag

A short shag can add volume and movement to your hair, while also softening your jawline. To achieve this look:

  1. Ask your stylist for a shaggy cut with layers.
  2. Add texture with a razor or shears.
  3. Consider adding bangs to soften your features.

Tips for Maintaining Your Short Hairstyle

Once you've chosen a short hairstyle, it's important to take good care of your hair to keep it looking its best. Here are some tips:

Regular Trims

Short haircuts require regular trims to maintain their shape. Visit your stylist every 4-6 weeks for a trim.

Use the Right Products

Choose hair products that are designed for your hair type and style. For short hair, look for lightweight products that won't weigh down your hair.

Protect Your Hair

Be sure to protect your hair from heat damage by using a heat protectant spray before using hot tools like flat irons or curling irons.

FAQ

What short hairstyles are best for square face shapes?

Short hairstyles that add volume to the top of your head and soften your jawline are best for square face shapes. Pixie cuts, bobs, and short shags are all great options.

Should square face shapes avoid bangs?

No, square face shapes can wear bangs! Consider a side-swept fringe or a long, wispy bang to soften your features.

How often should I get my short haircut trimmed?

Short haircuts should be trimmed every 4-6 weeks to maintain their shape.

Hair Care for Short Hairstyles for Square Face Woman

Short hairstyles require regular maintenance to keep them looking their best. Here are some tips for caring for your hair:

Wash Your Hair Regularly

Wash your hair every 2-3 days with a gentle shampoo and conditioner. Be sure to rinse your hair thoroughly to avoid product buildup.

Avoid Heat Damage

Avoid using hot tools like flat irons or curling irons every day, as this can cause heat damage. Instead, try air-drying your hair or using a diffuser attachment on your blow dryer.

Protect Your Hair From the Sun

Be sure to protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a UV protectant spray.

With the right cut and care, short hairstyles can be incredibly flattering for square face shapes. Whether you opt for a pixie cut, bob, or short shag, be sure to choose a style that adds volume to the top of your head and softens your jawline. And don't forget to take good care of your hair to keep it looking healthy and vibrant!
