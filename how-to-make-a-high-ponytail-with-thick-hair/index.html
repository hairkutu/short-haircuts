How to Make a High Ponytail with Thick Hair: Step-by-Step Guide

Are you tired of trying to make a high ponytail with thick hair that just falls flat? Look no further! In this article, we will provide you with a step-by-step guide on how to make a high ponytail that stays put and looks fabulous.

Description

A high ponytail is a classic hairstyle that is both chic and practical. It keeps your hair out of your face and is perfect for any occasion, whether it be a casual day out, a formal event, or a workout session. However, if you have thick hair, it can be difficult to achieve the desired height and volume.

How to Make a High Ponytail with Thick Hair

Step 1: Prep Your Hair

Start by washing and conditioning your hair with products that are suitable for your hair type. Apply a thickening spray or mousse to your damp hair, focusing on the roots, to add volume and texture. Blow-dry your hair using a round brush to create volume, and then apply a heat protectant spray before using hot tools.

Step 2: Gather Your Hair

Using a brush or comb, gather your hair into a high ponytail at the crown of your head. Make sure that the ponytail is secured tightly with a hair tie to prevent it from falling out.

Step 3: Tease Your Hair

Take a small section of hair from the ponytail and tease it using a comb. Repeat this process with the rest of the hair in the ponytail to create volume and height.

Step 4: Smooth Out Your Hair

Using a brush or comb, gently smooth out the teased hair to create a sleek and polished look.

Step 5: Secure Your Ponytail

Take a small section of hair from the ponytail and wrap it around the hair tie to conceal it. Secure the end of the section with a bobby pin or hair elastic.

Tips

  • Use a hair tie that matches your hair color to make it less noticeable.
  • Apply hairspray to your ponytail to keep it in place.
  • Use a bristle brush to smooth out your hair for a sleek and polished look.
  • Don't be afraid to experiment with different hair accessories, such as scrunchies or ribbons, to add some extra flair to your ponytail.

FAQ

What if my hair is too long for a high ponytail?

If your hair is too long for a high ponytail, you can try a low ponytail or a half-up, half-down hairstyle instead.

Can I make a high ponytail with thin hair?

Yes, you can make a high ponytail with thin hair. However, you may need to use hair extensions or volumizing products to add some extra volume.

Hair Care

It is important to take good care of your hair to maintain its health and appearance. Make sure to wash your hair regularly with gentle products, avoid using hot tools too often, and protect your hair from the sun and other environmental factors.

With these tips and tricks, you can make a high ponytail with thick hair that looks fabulous and stays put all day long. So go ahead, give it a try, and rock that high ponytail with confidence!
