Description

Two front strands of hair can frame your face beautifully and add a touch of elegance to your overall look. However, cutting them can be a daunting task, especially if you are not a professional hairstylist. In this step by step guide, we will teach you how to cut two front strands of hair at home without any hassle.

How to Cut Two Front Strands of Hair

Before we dive into the step by step guide, let's discuss the tools you will need:

  • Sharp scissors
  • Comb
  • Hair clips

Step by Step Guide

Follow these simple steps to cut two front strands of hair:

  1. Wash and dry your hair. Make sure it is free of any tangles.
  2. Part your hair in the middle and clip the rest of it away.
  3. Take one section of hair, starting from the crown of your head, and comb it straight down.
  4. Hold the hair between your index and middle finger, and slide your fingers down to the desired length of the strand.
  5. Use your scissors to cut the hair in a straight line. Remember to cut less than you think you need to, as you can always go back and trim more if needed.
  6. Repeat steps 3 to 5 on the other side of your head.
  7. Release the rest of your hair, and check that both strands are even in length. If not, trim accordingly.

Tips for Cutting Two Front Strands of Hair

Here are some helpful tips to keep in mind when cutting two front strands of hair:

  • Start by cutting a little bit at a time. It's easier to trim more than to fix a mistake if you cut too much.
  • Use sharp scissors to ensure a clean cut.
  • Make sure your hair is dry before cutting, as wet hair can shrink and alter the length.
  • Don't cut your hair straight across. Instead, try cutting at an angle for a more natural look.
  • If you have curly hair, straighten it first before cutting to ensure accuracy.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions regarding cutting two front strands of hair:

Q: Should I cut my hair when it's wet or dry?

A: It's best to cut your hair when it's dry, as wet hair can shrink and alter the length.

Q: Can I cut my hair at an angle?

A: Yes, cutting your hair at an angle can create a more natural look.

Hair Care after Cutting Two Front Strands of Hair

Now that you've cut your two front strands of hair, it's important to take care of them to ensure they stay healthy and looking their best. Here are some tips:

  • Trim your strands every 4-6 weeks to maintain the length.
  • Use a nourishing hair oil to keep your strands looking shiny and healthy.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Consider getting a hair mask to use once a week to keep your hair hydrated and soft.

Cutting two front strands of hair can be a fun and easy way to refresh your look. With these simple steps and tips, you can do it at home with confidence.
