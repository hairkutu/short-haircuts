How To Hide Dark Upper Lip Hair With Makeup

Are you struggling with dark upper lip hair that makes you feel self-conscious? You're not alone! Many women deal with this issue, but thankfully, there are ways to hide it with makeup. In this article, we'll show you how to hide dark upper lip hair with makeup step by step, along with some tips and hair care advice.

How to Hide Dark Upper Lip Hair with Makeup

The key to hiding dark upper lip hair with makeup is to use a combination of color correction and foundation. Here's what you'll need:

  • Color corrector in green or peach
  • Foundation
  • Concealer
  • Setting powder
  • Makeup brush or sponge

Follow these steps to hide dark upper lip hair with makeup:

Step by Step

  1. Cleanse and moisturize your face as usual.
  2. Apply a green or peach color corrector to your upper lip area. Green color correctors cancel out redness, while peach color correctors neutralize blue or purple tones. Use a makeup brush or sponge to blend the color corrector into your skin.
  3. Apply foundation all over your face, including your upper lip area. Use a brush or sponge to blend the foundation into your skin.
  4. Apply a concealer that matches your skin tone to your upper lip area. Use a brush or sponge to blend the concealer into your skin.
  5. Set your makeup with a setting powder. Use a fluffy brush to dust the powder over your face, paying extra attention to your upper lip area.

Now you're ready to face the day with confidence! If you notice any dark hairs peeking through during the day, simply touch up with a bit of concealer.

Tips

Here are some additional tips to help you hide dark upper lip hair with makeup:

  • Choose a color corrector that matches your skin tone. If you have fair skin, opt for a peach color corrector. If you have darker skin, choose a green color corrector.
  • Use a light hand when applying color corrector, foundation, and concealer. Too much product can make your makeup look cakey and unnatural.
  • Set your makeup with a setting spray to make it last longer.
  • If you have very dark upper lip hair, consider waxing or threading before applying makeup. This will give you a smoother surface to work with.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about hiding dark upper lip hair with makeup:

  • Can I use a regular concealer to hide dark upper lip hair? Yes, as long as it matches your skin tone. However, using a color corrector first will help neutralize any underlying tones that could show through the concealer.
  • Do I need to use setting powder? Setting powder helps your makeup last longer and prevents it from smudging or transferring. However, if you don't have setting powder, you can skip this step.
  • Will this makeup technique work for very dark or thick upper lip hair? It may not completely hide very dark or thick hair, but it can help make it less noticeable. Consider waxing or threading before applying makeup for a smoother surface.

Hair Care

In addition to using makeup to hide dark upper lip hair, there are some hair care tips you can follow to reduce hair growth and make hair lighter:

  • Exfoliate your upper lip area once a week to remove dead skin cells and promote cell turnover. This can help reduce hair growth.
  • Apply a homemade turmeric mask to your upper lip area once a week. Mix 1 tablespoon of turmeric powder with 1 tablespoon of milk to form a paste, then apply to your upper lip area and leave on for 15 minutes before rinsing off. Turmeric has natural bleaching properties that can help make hair lighter.
  • Consider using a hair growth inhibitor cream or serum, which can help reduce hair growth over time.

With these tips and techniques, you can confidently hide dark upper lip hair with makeup and feel your best every day.
