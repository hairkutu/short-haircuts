How To Style Layered Hair With Bangs A Step By Step Guide

Written by Eveline Apr 08, 2023 · 9 min read

If you have layered hair with bangs, you have a versatile hairstyle that can be styled in many ways. Whether you want a casual or formal look, there are a variety of styles that work well with this type of hair. In this post, we’ll take you through step-by-step instructions on how to style layered hair with bangs, as well as some tips and FAQs to help you achieve the perfect look.

Table of Contents

  • How to Style Layered Hair with Bangs
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Styling Layered Hair with Bangs
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care for Layered Hair with Bangs

How to Style Layered Hair with Bangs

Before we get started with styling, it’s important to make sure your hair is clean and dry. You can also add some styling products such as mousse, gel or hairspray to your hair to help hold the style in place. Here are some of the most popular ways to style layered hair with bangs:

1. Straight and Sleek

To achieve a straight and sleek look, you can use a flat iron or straightening brush to smooth out your hair. Start by sectioning your hair and working your way from the roots to the tips. You can also use a shine serum or spray to add some extra shine to your hair.

2. Beachy Waves

If you want a more relaxed and beachy look, you can create some waves in your hair using a curling iron or wand. Start by sectioning your hair and wrapping it around the curling iron or wand, leaving the ends out. Once you’ve curled all of your hair, use your fingers to separate the waves and add some texture.

3. Half-Up, Half-Down

A half-up, half-down hairstyle is perfect for a casual or formal occasion. Start by sectioning the top half of your hair and pulling it back into a ponytail or bun. You can leave some pieces out around your face to frame it. The bottom half of your hair can be left down or styled into waves or curls.

4. Braids

Braids are a great way to add some detail and texture to your hair. You can create a French braid, fishtail braid or even a simple three-strand braid. You can braid your bangs into the rest of your hair or leave them out for a more relaxed look.

Step-by-Step Guide

Now that you know some of the most popular ways to style layered hair with bangs, here’s a step-by-step guide to help you achieve the perfect look:

1. Section Your Hair

Start by sectioning your hair into manageable sections. Use hair clips to keep the sections separate.

2. Style Your Bangs

If you have side-swept bangs, you can use a round brush and a blow dryer to style them. Start by blow drying your bangs to one side, then switch to the other side. This will help add volume and texture to your bangs.

3. Straighten or Curl Your Hair

Depending on the look you’re going for, you can use a flat iron or curling iron to straighten or curl your hair. Work in sections, starting from the bottom and working your way up.

4. Add Some Texture

Once you’ve styled your hair, you can add some texture by using a texturizing spray or dry shampoo. This will help add some volume and hold to your hair.

5. Finish with Hairspray

To help hold your style in place, finish with a light mist of hairspray. You can also use a shine spray or serum to add some extra shine to your hair.

Tips for Styling Layered Hair with Bangs

Here are some tips to help you achieve the perfect style:

  • Use heat protectant spray before using hot tools on your hair.
  • Experiment with different styles to find what works best for you.
  • Don’t be afraid to add some volume and texture to your hair.
  • Use bobby pins or hair clips to keep your hair in place while styling.

FAQs

How often should I wash my hair?

It’s recommended that you wash your hair every 2-3 days to prevent it from becoming too dry or damaged.

Can I style my hair without using heat?

Absolutely! You can let your hair air dry or use rollers or braids to create waves or curls.

What kind of products should I use on my hair?

This depends on your hair type and the style you’re going for. You can use mousse, gel, hairspray, or shine spray to help hold your style in place.

Hair Care for Layered Hair with Bangs

Maintaining healthy hair is important for achieving a great style. Here are some tips for taking care of your layered hair with bangs:

  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner to prevent damage to your hair.
  • Avoid using hot tools on your hair too often.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week.

With these tips and steps, you should be able to style your layered hair with bangs in a variety of ways. Don’t be afraid to experiment and find the style that works best for you!
