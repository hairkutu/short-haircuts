How Long Does Your Hair Have To Be To Get Cornrows For Guys

Are you interested in getting cornrows but not sure if your hair length is suitable for it? This article will provide you with all the necessary information on how long your hair should be to get cornrows for guys.

Table of Content

  1. Description
  2. How to
  3. Step by step
  4. Tips

Description

Cornrows are a popular hairstyle for men and women of all ages and hair types. This style is achieved by braiding the hair in a continuous, raised pattern that lays flat against the scalp. Cornrows can be worn in various styles and thicknesses, depending on your preference.

When it comes to hair length, there is no strict rule on how long your hair should be to get cornrows. However, it is generally recommended that your hair is at least 2-3 inches long to ensure that it can be easily braided without causing any discomfort or breakage.

How to

To get cornrows, you will need to visit a professional hairstylist who is experienced in braiding. They will be able to assess your hair length and texture to determine if cornrows are a suitable style for you.

Step by step

The hairstylist will start by parting your hair into sections and then braiding it in a continuous pattern. The braids will be secured using small rubber bands or thread. The process may take several hours depending on the thickness of your hair and the style that you want.

Tips

If you are getting cornrows for the first time, it is important to choose a style that is not too tight or small, as this can cause discomfort or damage to your hair. It is also important to keep your scalp moisturized and oiled to prevent dryness and breakage.

FAQ

Can I get cornrows with short hair?

Yes, you can get cornrows with short hair, but it may be more challenging for the hairstylist to achieve the desired style. It is recommended that your hair is at least 2-3 inches long to ensure that it can be easily braided.

How long do cornrows last?

Cornrows can last anywhere from 2-8 weeks depending on how well you take care of them. It is important to avoid pulling or tugging on the braids and to keep your scalp moisturized to prevent dryness and breakage.

Hair Care

To maintain your cornrows, it is important to keep your scalp clean and moisturized. You can do this by using a gentle shampoo and conditioner and applying oil or moisturizer to your scalp regularly. It is also important to avoid pulling or tugging on the braids and to sleep with a satin or silk scarf to prevent frizz and breakage.

In conclusion, there is no strict rule on how long your hair should be to get cornrows, but it is generally recommended that your hair is at least 2-3 inches long. It is important to choose a style that is not too tight or small, and to keep your scalp and hair moisturized to prevent dryness and breakage. With proper care, your cornrows can last for several weeks and provide you with a stylish and low-maintenance hairstyle.
