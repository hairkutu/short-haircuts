How To Cut Shirt Into Workout Tank A Step By Step Guide

Written by Oliver Aug 16, 2023 · 7 min read

Are you tired of wearing the same old workout clothes? Do you want to switch up your routine without breaking the bank? Look no further than your own closet! With just a pair of scissors, you can transform an old t-shirt into a trendy workout tank. Follow this step-by-step guide to learn how.

How to Cut Shirt into Workout Tank

Before you begin, gather these materials:

  • an old t-shirt
  • scissors
  • chalk or a pen
  • a ruler or measuring tape

Step 1: Choose Your T-Shirt

Start by selecting a t-shirt that you no longer wear or want to give a new look. This could be an oversized shirt or one with a cool design that you want to showcase.

Step 2: Measure and Mark

Using a ruler or measuring tape, mark the desired length of your tank top. You can do this by measuring from the bottom hem of the shirt and marking a line with chalk or a pen. Then, mark the desired width of the armholes by drawing a line from the top of the shoulder to the bottom of the sleeve.

Step 3: Cut Along the Lines

Using your scissors, carefully cut along the lines you marked. Be sure to cut through both layers of the shirt if it is folded in half. If you prefer a more relaxed fit, you can leave the bottom hem intact. Otherwise, you can cut it off as well.

Step 4: Create a Racerback (Optional)

If you want to create a racerback tank top, mark a line from the center of the neckline to the middle of the back of the shirt. Cut along this line and discard the excess fabric. You can also cut the sleeves off completely for a more open back.

Step 5: Adjust and Finish

Try on your new tank top and make any necessary adjustments. You can trim the armholes or neckline to make them larger or smaller, depending on your preference. You can also add any embellishments or cutouts to make it unique.

Tips for Cutting Your Shirt into a Workout Tank

Here are a few helpful tips to keep in mind:

  • Use sharp scissors to make clean cuts.
  • Practice cutting on a scrap piece of fabric before cutting your shirt.
  • Wash and dry your shirt before cutting to prevent shrinking.
  • Experiment with different styles, such as a cropped tank top or a high-necked tank top.

FAQs

Can I use any type of shirt?

Yes, you can use any type of shirt, but it is best to use a cotton or cotton-blend fabric for a comfortable and breathable workout top.

Do I need to sew the edges?

No, you do not need to sew the edges, but you can if you prefer a more finished look. You can use a sewing machine or hand-sew the edges with a needle and thread.

How can I care for my new workout tank?

Wash your tank top in cold water and hang or lay flat to dry. Avoid using fabric softener, as it can damage the fabric.

Hair Care Tips for Working Out

While you’re working on your new workout wardrobe, don’t forget about your hair! Here are a few tips to keep your locks looking great:

  • Tie your hair back in a loose bun or ponytail to avoid breakage and frizz.
  • Use a sweatband or headband to keep sweat out of your eyes.
  • Wash your hair after every workout to remove sweat and buildup.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools on your hair too often, as it can cause damage.

Now that you know how to cut shirt into workout tank, you can create your own unique tops for your fitness routine. With a little creativity and some simple materials, you can save money and look great while you work out!
