What Hairstyle Looks Best With My Face Shape A Comprehensive Guide

Wondering what hairstyle looks best with your face shape? You're not alone! Many people struggle with finding the perfect hairstyle that flatters their face shape. But don't worry, we've got you covered. In this article, we'll be discussing the different face shapes and the hairstyles that complement them. So, let's get started!

The Different Face Shapes

Before we dive into the hairstyles that complement each face shape, let's first discuss the different face shapes. There are six main face shapes:

  • Oval
  • Round
  • Square
  • Heart
  • Diamond
  • Triangle

Each face shape has its own unique features and characteristics that determine which hairstyle looks best.

Hairstyles for Different Face Shapes

Oval Face Shape: If you have an oval face shape, congratulations! This is considered the most versatile face shape. You can pull off almost any hairstyle, from a pixie cut to long layers. However, avoid styles that add too much height to the top of your head.

Round Face Shape: If you have a round face shape, opt for hairstyles that add length to your face. Long layers, side-swept bangs, and asymmetrical cuts are great options. Avoid blunt cuts and center parts.

Square Face Shape: If you have a square face shape, soften your features with hairstyles that add volume to the top of your head. Soft waves, side-swept bangs, and layered cuts are perfect for you. Avoid straight, blunt cuts and center parts.

Heart Face Shape: If you have a heart face shape, draw attention to your eyes and cheekbones with hairstyles that add volume to the bottom half of your face. Side-swept bangs, long layers, and chin-length bobs are great options. Avoid short, full styles that add volume to the top of your head.

Diamond Face Shape: If you have a diamond face shape, balance your features with hairstyles that add volume to the top and bottom of your head. Soft waves, chin-length bobs, and layered cuts are perfect for you. Avoid short, full styles that add volume only to the top of your head.

Triangle Face Shape: If you have a triangle face shape, balance your features with hairstyles that add volume to the top of your head. Pixie cuts, layered bobs, and side-swept bangs are great options. Avoid styles that add volume to the bottom of your head.

How to Determine Your Face Shape

If you're still not sure what your face shape is, here's a simple way to determine it:

  1. Stand in front of a mirror with your hair pulled back.
  2. Trace the outline of your face onto the mirror with a bar of soap or lipstick.
  3. Step back and look at the shape you drew. Compare it to the six face shapes discussed above.

Once you determine your face shape, you can start experimenting with different hairstyles that complement your features.

Tips for Choosing the Right Hairstyle

Here are some additional tips to keep in mind when choosing a hairstyle:

  • Consider your hair texture and thickness.
  • Think about your lifestyle and how much time you're willing to spend styling your hair.
  • Consult with a hairstylist to get their professional opinion.
  • Don't be afraid to try something new!

FAQs

Q: Can I still wear a certain hairstyle even if it doesn't complement my face shape?

A: Of course! At the end of the day, you should wear whatever hairstyle makes you feel confident and beautiful. These guidelines are just suggestions to help you find a hairstyle that flatters your features.

Q: Will bangs look good on me?

A: Bangs can look great on any face shape, but the style and length of the bangs will depend on your individual features. Talk to your hairstylist about which bangs will look best on you.

Hair Care Tips

No matter what hairstyle you choose, it's important to take good care of your hair. Here are some hair care tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly, but not too often.
  • Use a conditioner that is appropriate for your hair type.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and chlorine.
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends.

With these tips and guidelines, you're sure to find a hairstyle that flatters your face shape and makes you feel confident and beautiful. Happy styling!
