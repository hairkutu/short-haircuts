Hairstyles For Shoulder Length Hair For Indian Wedding

Written by Robby May 13, 2023 Â· 7 min read

When it comes to Indian weddings, everything must be perfect. From the dress to the makeup, everything has to be just right. Hair is no exception. If you have shoulder length hair and you're not sure how to style it for your wedding, don't worry! We've got you covered. In this article, we'll be discussing various hairstyles that you can try for your big day.

Table of Contents

  • How to Style Shoulder Length Hair for Indian Wedding
  • Step by Step Guide to Hairstyles for Shoulder Length Hair for Indian Wedding
  • Tips for Hairstyles for Shoulder Length Hair for Indian Wedding

  • Hair Care for Shoulder Length Hair for Indian Wedding

How to Style Shoulder Length Hair for Indian Wedding

Before we dive into the hairstyles, let's take a look at some tips on how to style shoulder length hair for an Indian wedding:

  • Make sure your hair is clean and well-conditioned.
  • Use a heat protectant spray before using any heating tools.
  • Consider using hair extensions if you want more volume or length.
  • Accessorize your hair with flowers, jewels, or other decorative items.

Step by Step Guide to Hairstyles for Shoulder Length Hair for Indian Wedding

Now, let's get into the hairstyles:

1. Loose Waves

Loose waves are a classic and elegant look for an Indian wedding. To achieve this look, use a curling iron to curl your hair away from your face. Once you've curled your entire head, use your fingers to loosen the curls and create a more natural, effortless look. Finish off with a light hairspray.

2. Half Up Half Down

The half up half down hairstyle is perfect for those who want to show off their length while also keeping their hair out of their face. To achieve this look, take the top half of your hair and tie it into a ponytail or bun. Leave the bottom half loose and either straighten it or curl it for added texture.

3. Side Swept Curls

Side swept curls are a glamorous and sophisticated look for an Indian wedding. To achieve this look, curl your hair and then sweep it to one side. Pin the hair in place with bobby pins and finish off with a strong hold hairspray.

4. Braided Updo

The braided updo is a unique and intricate hairstyle that is perfect for those who want to make a statement. To achieve this look, start by braiding your hair into a Dutch braid. Once you've braided your entire head, twist the braid into a low bun and pin it in place. Finish the look off with some decorative hair pins or flowers.

Tips for Hairstyles for Shoulder Length Hair for Indian Wedding

Here are some additional tips to keep in mind when choosing a hairstyle for your Indian wedding:

  • Consider your dress and makeup when choosing a hairstyle.
  • Make sure the hairstyle you choose is comfortable and won't be too heavy or tight on your head.
  • Do a trial run of your hairstyle before the big day to make sure you're happy with it.

FAQs for Hairstyles for Shoulder Length Hair for Indian Wedding

1. Can I use hair extensions to add length or volume to my hair?

Yes! Hair extensions are a great way to add length or volume to your hair if you're not happy with its natural state.

2. What should I do if my hair doesn't hold curls well?

If your hair doesn't hold curls well, try using a stronger hold hairspray or using a smaller curling iron.

3. How can I make sure my hairstyle stays in place all day?

Use a strong hold hairspray and consider using bobby pins or hair clips to keep your hair in place.

Hair Care for Shoulder Length Hair for Indian Wedding

Make sure you're taking good care of your hair in the weeks leading up to your Indian wedding. This includes washing and conditioning your hair regularly, using a heat protectant spray when using heating tools, and avoiding using too many products that can weigh your hair down. Consider getting a trim to get rid of any split ends and keep your hair looking healthy and shiny.

We hope this article has given you some inspiration for hairstyles for shoulder length hair for your Indian wedding. Remember to choose a hairstyle that makes you feel confident and beautiful on your special day!
