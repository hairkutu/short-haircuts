Shoulder Length Hair With Long Side Fringe A Relaxed Guide

Description

If you're looking for a new hairstyle that's both chic and low maintenance, you might want to consider shoulder length hair with a long side fringe. This hairstyle is versatile and can be styled in many different ways, making it perfect for any occasion. The long side fringe adds a touch of femininity and frames your face beautifully, while the shoulder length makes it easy to manage and maintain. In this article, we'll explore how to achieve this hairstyle, step by step, and provide tips and advice on hair care and maintenance.


How To

The first step in achieving the shoulder length hair with a long side fringe is to make sure your hair is the right length. Ideally, your hair should be cut to just above or below your shoulders, depending on your preference. Next, you'll need to decide where you want your fringe to fall. For a long side fringe, you'll want it to be at least chin-length.

Once you have your hair cut to the right length, it's time to style it. You can opt for a sleek, straight look or add some waves or curls for a more relaxed feel. To create a long side fringe, part your hair on the side and sweep the hair on one side of your face forward. Use a flat iron or curling iron to add some texture and volume to your hair.


Step by Step

Here are the steps to achieve shoulder length hair with a long side fringe:

  1. Ensure your hair is cut to the right length, just above or below your shoulders.
  2. Decide where you want your long side fringe to fall and cut it to the desired length.
  3. Part your hair on the side and sweep the fringe to one side of your face.
  4. Use a flat iron or curling iron to add texture and volume to your hair.
  5. Finish with a hairspray or hair oil to keep your hair in place and add shine.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when styling your shoulder length hair with a long side fringe:

  • Use a heat protectant spray before using any hot styling tools to prevent damage to your hair.
  • Experiment with different hairstyles to find what works best for you.
  • Consider getting regular trims to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.
  • Invest in a good quality hair oil or serum to add shine and nourishment to your hair.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about shoulder length hair with a long side fringe:

  • Can I wear my hair in a bun or ponytail with a long side fringe?
    Yes, you can! A bun or ponytail can be a great way to show off your fringe and add some variety to your hairstyle.
  • Can I still wear my hair straight with a long side fringe?
    Absolutely! Straight hair can look great with a long side fringe, especially if you add some texture and volume to your hair.
  • What face shapes suit a long side fringe?
    A long side fringe can suit many different face shapes, but it's particularly flattering on heart-shaped or oval faces.

Hair Care

Here are some tips for keeping your shoulder length hair with a long side fringe healthy and looking its best:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using hot styling tools too often to prevent damage to your hair.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair nourished and healthy.
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends and keep your hair looking neat and tidy.

With these tips and advice, you're well on your way to achieving a chic and low-maintenance hairstyle with shoulder length hair and a long side fringe. Experiment with different styles and techniques to find what works best for you, and don't forget to take care of your hair to keep it looking healthy and beautiful.
