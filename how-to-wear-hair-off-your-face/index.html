How To Wear Hair Off Your Face A Step By Step Guide

Written by Petter Aug 30, 2023 Â· 7 min read

If you're tired of having your hair fall in your face and constantly having to push it back, there are a few ways to wear your hair off your face. Not only is it a practical solution, but it can also look stylish and trendy. In this article, we'll go over how to wear your hair off your face, including different hairstyles and tips on how to keep your hair healthy.

Table of Contents

  1. How to Wear Hair Up
  2. How to Wear Hair Back
  3. How to Wear Hair Half Up Half Down
  4. Tips for Wearing Hair Off Your Face
  5. FAQ
  6. Hair Care Tips

How to Wear Hair Up

Wearing your hair up is one of the easiest ways to keep it off your face. Here's how:

Step 1: Brush Your Hair

Before you begin, make sure your hair is brushed and tangle-free. This will make it easier to put your hair up.

Step 2: Gather Your Hair

Gather your hair at the crown of your head, as if you were going to make a ponytail.

Step 3: Twist Your Hair

Twist your hair until it starts to coil on itself.

Step 4: Pin Your Hair

Using bobby pins or hairpins, secure your twisted hair to the back of your head. Make sure to hide the pins so they're not visible.

How to Wear Hair Back

If you don't want to wear your hair up, you can also wear it back. Here's how:

Step 1: Brush Your Hair

As before, make sure your hair is brushed and tangle-free.

Step 2: Part Your Hair

Part your hair in the middle or on one side, depending on your preference.

Step 3: Pin Your Hair

Using bobby pins or hairpins, secure your hair behind your ears. You can also use hair clips if you prefer.

How to Wear Hair Half Up Half Down

If you want to wear your hair both up and down, try a half up half down style. Here's how:

Step 1: Brush Your Hair

As always, make sure your hair is brushed and tangle-free.

Step 2: Gather Your Hair

Gather the top half of your hair at the crown of your head, leaving the bottom half down.

Step 3: Pin Your Hair

Using bobby pins or hairpins, secure the top half of your hair to the back of your head. You can leave some pieces of hair loose to frame your face.

Tips for Wearing Hair Off Your Face

Here are a few tips to keep in mind when wearing your hair off your face:

  • Use hairpins or bobby pins that match your hair color so they're not visible.
  • If you have long hair, you may need to use more than one pin to secure it.
  • Experiment with different styles to find what works best for you.
  • If you're using hairpins, make sure to secure them tightly so your hair doesn't fall out.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about wearing hair off your face:

What are some other ways to wear my hair off my face?

In addition to the styles mentioned above, you can also try braids, buns, or a top knot.

Can wearing my hair off my face damage my hair?

No, as long as you're not pulling your hair too tightly or using hairpins that are too small, wearing your hair off your face shouldn't damage it.

Hair Care Tips

Wearing your hair off your face can be a great way to keep it healthy and prevent breakage. Here are some tips to keep your hair healthy:

  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner that are suited to your hair type.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends.
  • Use a heat protectant spray if you do use heat styling tools.
  • Avoid pulling your hair too tightly, as this can cause breakage.

Now that you know how to wear your hair off your face, you can try out different styles and find what works best for you. Whether you prefer wearing your hair up, back, or half up half down, there are plenty of options to choose from. Just remember to keep your hair healthy and use the right tools and products to keep it looking its best.
