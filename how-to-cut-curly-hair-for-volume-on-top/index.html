How To Cut Curly Hair For Volume On Top

Cutting curly hair can be a daunting task, especially when trying to achieve volume on top. However, with the right techniques and tools, it can be done easily. In this article, we will show you how to cut curly hair for volume on top.

Description

Curly hair is beautiful, but it can be challenging to maintain. One of the most common problems with curly hair is flatness on top. To add volume to curly hair, you need to cut it in a way that promotes lift and height.

There are many ways to cut curly hair for volume on top, but we will be discussing the most popular and effective methods in this article.

How to Cut Curly Hair for Volume on Top

The first step to cutting curly hair for volume on top is to make sure the hair is clean and dry. This will help you see the natural shape of the curls and ensure that you are cutting the hair evenly.

Next, you will need to section the hair into four parts: the front, top, sides, and back. Start with the front section, as this will be the most visible part of the hairstyle.

1. Cut the Front Section

Start by combing the hair straight down and cutting it to the desired length. This will create a base for the rest of the haircut.

Next, take small sections of hair and cut them at an angle, pointing towards the face. This will create layers and add volume to the hair.

Repeat this process on both sides of the front section, making sure to cut the hair at an angle and not straight across.

2. Cut the Top Section

After the front section is complete, move on to the top section. Comb the hair straight up and cut it to the desired length.

Next, take small sections of hair and cut them at an angle, pointing towards the back of the head. This will create layers and add volume to the hair.

Repeat this process on the sides and back of the head, making sure to cut the hair at an angle and not straight across.

Step by Step

Here is a step-by-step guide on how to cut curly hair for volume on top:

  1. Wash and dry the hair
  2. Section the hair into four parts: front, top, sides, and back
  3. Cut the front section by combing the hair straight down and cutting it to the desired length
  4. Cut small sections of hair at an angle, pointing towards the face to create layers and volume
  5. Repeat this process on both sides of the front section
  6. Cut the top section by combing the hair straight up and cutting it to the desired length
  7. Cut small sections of hair at an angle, pointing towards the back of the head to create layers and volume
  8. Repeat this process on the sides and back of the head
  9. Blend the layers together by combing the hair and trimming any uneven ends
  10. Style the hair as desired

Tips

Here are some tips to help you cut curly hair for volume on top:

  • Use sharp scissors to ensure a clean cut
  • Cut the hair when it is dry to see the natural shape of the curls
  • Start with a small amount of hair and cut more if needed
  • Cut the hair at an angle to create layers and volume
  • Trim any uneven ends to blend the layers together

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about cutting curly hair for volume on top:

Q: Can I cut curly hair when it is wet?

A: It is best to cut curly hair when it is dry so that you can see the natural shape of the curls and ensure that you are cutting the hair evenly.

Q: How often should I cut curly hair?

A: It is recommended to cut curly hair every 8-12 weeks to maintain the shape and prevent split ends.

Q: How can I style my curly hair for volume on top?

A: You can use a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer to dry your hair upside down, which will create volume on top. You can also use hair products specifically designed for curly hair to add volume and hold.

Hair Care

Here are some tips for taking care of curly hair:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it is dry, as this can cause frizz
  • Use a wide-toothed comb to detangle your hair when it is wet
  • Avoid heat styling tools, as they can damage your hair
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy and moisturized

Now that you know how to cut curly hair for volume on top, you can achieve the beautiful, voluminous curls you've always wanted. Remember to take your time and use the right tools and techniques for the best results.
