How To Make Relaxed Hair Curly Again A Complete Guide

Written by William Aug 01, 2023 Â· 5 min read

If you have relaxed hair and are looking to bring back your curls, you're in the right place. In this article, we'll be sharing how to make relaxed hair curly again. We'll cover everything from how to prep your hair for styling to tips for maintaining your curls. So, let's get started!

Description

Relaxed hair is hair that has been chemically treated to straighten it. This process can damage the hair and make it difficult to go back to its natural state. However, with the right techniques and products, it is possible to bring back your curls.

How to Make Relaxed Hair Curly Again

Step 1: Clarify Your Hair

Before you start styling, it's important to clarify your hair to remove any buildup from products. This will help your curls to form better. Use a clarifying shampoo and follow with a moisturizing conditioner.

Step 2: Apply a Curl Enhancer

Applying a curl enhancer will help your curls to form and hold. Look for a product that is specifically designed for relaxed hair. Apply the product to damp hair, starting at the ends and working your way up.

Step 3: Use a Diffuser

To dry your hair, use a diffuser attachment on your blow dryer. This will help to enhance your curls and reduce frizz. Use a low heat and low speed setting to avoid damaging your hair.

Step 4: Scrunch Your Hair

Once your hair is dry, use your hands to scrunch your hair upwards. This will help to create more defined curls.

Tips

Here are some additional tips for making relaxed hair curly again:

  • Use a satin pillowcase or bonnet to protect your curls while you sleep
  • Avoid using heat tools too often as they can damage your hair
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair moisturized
  • Try different curl enhancing products to find what works best for your hair

FAQ

Can I still relax my hair after making it curly again?

Yes, you can still relax your hair if you want to. However, it's best to wait until your curls have fully grown out to avoid damaging your hair.

How long will it take to make my relaxed hair curly again?

The process of making your hair curly again can take several months, depending on how long your hair is and how damaged it is. Be patient and consistent with your hair care routine.

Hair Care

Taking care of your hair is important for maintaining healthy, curly hair. Here are some tips for caring for your hair:

  • Wash your hair once or twice a week with a sulfate-free shampoo
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair
  • Avoid using harsh chemicals on your hair
  • Trim your ends regularly to prevent split ends

With these tips and techniques, you can make your relaxed hair curly again. Remember to be patient and consistent with your hair care routine.
