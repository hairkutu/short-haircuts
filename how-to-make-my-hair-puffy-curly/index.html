How To Make Your Hair Puffy Curly A Step By Step Guide

Written by Frank Aug 14, 2023 ¬∑ 6 min read

Are you tired of having straight, limp hair? Do you want to add some volume and bounce to your locks? Then, puffy curly hair might be the perfect style for you! In this guide, we will show you how to make your hair puffy curly in a few simple steps. But first, let's understand what puffy curly hair is.

What is Puffy Curly Hair?

Puffy curly hair is a hairstyle that features voluminous and bouncy curls. This style is achieved by using different techniques such as curling irons or rollers. Puffy curly hair is perfect for those who want to add some texture and volume to their hair.

How to Make Your Hair Puffy Curly - Step by Step

Step 1: Start with Clean Hair

Before you start styling your hair, make sure it's clean and free of any product buildup. Use a clarifying shampoo to remove any residue and follow up with a moisturizing conditioner to keep your hair hydrated.

Step 2: Apply a Heat Protectant

Before using any heat styling tools, it's essential to protect your hair. Apply a heat protectant spray or serum to your hair to prevent heat damage.

Step 3: Divide Your Hair into Sections

Divide your hair into sections using hair clips. This will make it easier to curl your hair and ensure that you don't miss any sections.

Step 4: Use a Curling Iron

Take a small section of hair and wrap it around a curling iron. Hold the iron in place for a few seconds and release. Repeat this process until all sections of your hair are curled.

Step 5: Tease Your Hair

Use a fine-tooth comb to tease your hair at the roots. This will add volume and give your hair a puffy look.

Step 6: Apply Hairspray

Apply a light hold hairspray to your hair to keep your curls in place and prevent frizz.

Tips for Making Your Hair Puffy Curly

  • Use a larger barrel curling iron for bigger curls.
  • Don't over-curl your hair. This can lead to damage and breakage.
  • Use a diffuser attachment on your hairdryer to enhance your curls.
  • Avoid touching your hair too much, as this can cause frizz.

FAQs

How long will puffy curly hair last?

The lifespan of puffy curly hair depends on how well you take care of it. If you use a good hairspray and avoid touching your hair too much, your curls can last up to three days.

Can I make my hair puffy curly without heat?

Yes, you can use rollers or braids to create puffy curly hair without heat. Simply braid or roll your hair and leave it overnight. In the morning, remove the rollers or braids and tease your hair at the roots for added volume.

Hair Care for Puffy Curly Hair

To maintain puffy curly hair, use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair hydrated. Avoid using too much heat on your hair and always use a heat protectant. Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends and breakage.

Conclusion

By following these simple steps, you can make your hair puffy curly and add some bounce and volume to your locks. Remember to take care of your hair and avoid using too much heat to prevent damage and breakage. With a little bit of practice, you can achieve the perfect puffy curly hair.
