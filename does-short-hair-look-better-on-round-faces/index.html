Does Short Hair Look Better On Round Faces

Written by Oliver Mar 27, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Short haircuts have been in trend for quite some time now. They are easy to maintain, and they give a chic and elegant look. But, the question that arises is, does short hair look better on round faces? In this article, we'll explore this topic in detail and provide you with some tips and advice on how to style short hair on round faces.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to Style Short Hair on Round Faces
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Styling Short Hair on Round Faces
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care for Short Hair on Round Faces

Description

Round faces are characterized by full cheeks and a circular shape. Short haircuts can accentuate the roundness of the face, so it's essential to choose the right cut that flatters your features. Short hairstyles can add height to the crown of the head, elongate the face, and draw attention to the eyes and cheekbones.

How to Style Short Hair on Round Faces

The key to styling short hair on round faces is to add height and volume to the top of the head. This can be achieved by adding layers to the hair, creating a textured look. Short hairstyles that are tapered at the back and sides can also create the illusion of a longer face.

Step-by-Step Guide

1. Start by washing and conditioning your hair. Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to add body and thickness to your hair.

2. Towel-dry your hair and apply a volumizing mousse to the roots.

3. Blow-dry your hair using a round brush, lifting the hair at the roots to create volume.

4. Use a flat iron to create loose waves or curls. This will add texture to your hair and make it appear fuller.

5. Finish with a texturizing spray to add definition and hold.

Tips for Styling Short Hair on Round Faces

1. Avoid blunt cuts, as they can make the face appear rounder. Opt for layered cuts instead.

2. Add side-swept bangs to your hairstyle. They can create an asymmetrical look and draw attention to the eyes.

3. Use hair accessories such as headbands or hair clips to create a focal point and draw attention away from the roundness of the face.

4. Experiment with different hair colors to add depth and dimension to your hair. Highlights or lowlights can create the illusion of shadows and make the face appear slimmer.

FAQs

Q: What short hairstyles are best for round faces?

A: Short hairstyles that add height and volume to the crown of the head are best for round faces. These can include layered cuts, tapered cuts, and asymmetrical cuts.

Q: Should I avoid short haircuts if I have a round face?

A: No, you don't have to avoid short haircuts if you have a round face. It's all about choosing the right cut that flatters your features.

Hair Care for Short Hair on Round Faces

Short hair requires less maintenance than long hair, but it still needs proper care to look its best. Here are some tips for hair care for short hair on round faces:

1. Use a clarifying shampoo once a week to remove product buildup.

2. Use a deep conditioning treatment once a month to keep your hair healthy and shiny.

3. Avoid using heat styling tools every day. Instead, let your hair air dry whenever possible.

4. Trim your hair every six to eight weeks to maintain the shape of your haircut.

In conclusion, short haircuts can look great on round faces if you choose the right cut and style. Follow the tips and advice in this article to create a chic and elegant look that flatters your features.
