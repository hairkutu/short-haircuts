How To Keep Side Bangs From Falling In Your Face Tips And Tricks

Written by Aprilia Jul 08, 2023 Â· 9 min read

If you have side bangs, you know how frustrating it can be when they constantly fall in your face. Whether you're trying to work, study, or simply enjoy your day, constantly pushing your hair out of your face can be a hassle. Fortunately, there are a few tips and tricks you can use to keep your side bangs in check. In this article, we'll cover everything you need to know about keeping your side bangs from falling in your face.

Table of Contents

  • How to Keep Side Bangs from Falling in Your Face
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips and Tricks
  • FAQ

How to Keep Side Bangs from Falling in Your Face

There are several ways to keep your side bangs from falling in your face, including:

  • Using hair accessories
  • Using hair products
  • Styling your bangs differently

Let's take a closer look at each method.

Using Hair Accessories

One of the easiest ways to keep your side bangs out of your face is to use hair accessories. Here are a few options:

  • Bobby pins: Simply twist your bangs back and secure them with a bobby pin.
  • Headbands: A headband can keep your bangs in place and add a stylish touch to your look.
  • Clips: Small hair clips can be used to secure your bangs to the side of your head.

Using Hair Products

Another way to keep your side bangs from falling in your face is to use hair products. Here are a few options:

  • Hairspray: Spraying a bit of hairspray on your bangs can help keep them in place.
  • Gel: Apply a small amount of gel to your bangs and style them to the side.
  • Dry shampoo: If your bangs tend to get oily and fall forward, dry shampoo can help keep them in place.

Styling Your Bangs Differently

If you're tired of always having to push your bangs out of your face, consider styling them differently. Here are a few options:

  • Sweep them to the side: Use a round brush and blow dryer to sweep your bangs to the side.
  • Pull them back: If your bangs are long enough, you can pull them back and secure them with a bobby pin.
  • Trim them: If your bangs are constantly falling in your face, it may be time for a trim. Ask your stylist to cut them shorter or add more layers to help keep them in place.

Step-by-Step Guide

If you're not sure how to use hair accessories or products to keep your side bangs in place, here's a step-by-step guide:

  1. Brush your hair and bangs to remove any tangles.
  2. If using hair accessories, choose the one that best suits your needs (bobby pin, headband, clip).
  3. If using hair products, apply the product to your bangs and style them to the side.
  4. Secure your bangs in place with the accessory or product.
  5. If necessary, touch up your bangs throughout the day to keep them in place.

Tips and Tricks

Here are a few additional tips and tricks to help keep your side bangs from falling in your face:

  • Use a round brush and blow dryer to style your bangs to the side.
  • Apply hairspray or gel to your bangs before styling to help keep them in place.
  • Consider getting a fringe cut instead of side bangs if you're constantly struggling to keep them out of your face.
  • Make sure your hair is healthy and moisturized to prevent flyaways.

FAQ

Why do my side bangs keep falling in my face?

Side bangs can fall in your face for several reasons, including length, texture, and hair type. If your bangs are too long, they're more likely to fall forward. If your hair is very fine or silky, your bangs may not stay in place as well.

How do I keep my side bangs out of my face without using products or accessories?

If you don't want to use products or accessories to keep your side bangs out of your face, consider styling them differently. You can sweep them to the side, pull them back, or add more layers to help keep them in place.

Can I wear a hat with side bangs?

A hat can be a stylish way to keep your side bangs out of your face. Choose a hat that fits well and doesn't push your bangs forward.

Hair Care for Side Bangs

Keeping your hair healthy and moisturized is essential for preventing flyaways and keeping your side bangs in place. Here are a few hair care tips:

  • Use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair and bangs hydrated.
  • Avoid using hot tools (like curling irons or flat irons) on your bangs, as this can dry them out and cause flyaways.
  • Get regular trims to maintain the shape and length of your side bangs.
  • Consider using a hair mask once a week to give your hair and bangs an extra boost of hydration.

By following these tips and tricks, you can keep your side bangs from falling in your face and enjoy a hassle-free hairstyle. Whether you prefer using accessories, products, or styling techniques, there's a solution that will work for you. Remember to take care of your hair and keep it healthy to prevent flyaways and keep your bangs in place.
